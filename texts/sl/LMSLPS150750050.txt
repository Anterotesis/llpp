To the Right Honourable William Bridgen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majestys Justices of Oyer and Terminer and Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex of the Prisoners therein being.

The Humble Petition of Richard Swift.

That Your Petitioner was on the 9th. day of March last past Committed to Clerkenwell Bridewell by Sr. John Fielding Knt. Charged on Oath of Thomas Faques & others for receiving from James Gost a quantity of Tin Plates tobacco Candles and other goods knowing them to be Stolen. And Detained on oath of the sd. Thomas Faques. and another for receiving 4 bibles and other Books knowing them to be Stolen Also Detained on oath of the said Thomas Faques & another for receiving a great Quantity of Stone blue his property knowing the Same to be Stolen Also Detained on Oath of George Ewbank & anor. for receiving 28 Yards of Cotton knowing the same to be Stolen. Also Detained on Oath of Thomas Lloyd for receiving 6 Dogen of Mold Candles the Property of Bull the Huntingdon Carrier. knowing them to be Stolen. And Your petitioner hath been informed that there will be Prosecutions Commenced for diverse offences of the like Nature said to have been perpetrated in the City of London and of which he is Intirely Innocent.

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays That he may (for the above Offences. as well as Such other as may be laid to his Charge) At this Present Session be Tried. Bailed or Discharged according to the form of the Statute in Such Case made and provided

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Richard Swift
