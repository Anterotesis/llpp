To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof is General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guild Hall in and for the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the [---] & Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls is the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr. Daniel Lambert Knt. & Crisp Gascoyne Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof (one being the Quorum) bearing Date the 19th. Day of August last Peter Green Ann his Wife & their Son John aged abt. 9 Yrs. were removed from the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the East London to the sd. Liberty of the Rolls as the Place of their last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and agt. the sd. Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved unto them & that the Hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for this City Libertys

Wm Chimmow agt. for the Appellts. And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
