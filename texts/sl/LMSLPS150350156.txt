To the Right: Worspfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace attheir Generall Quarter Sessions held for the City of London

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr: Peter Delme Knt: Lord Mayor: and Sr: Francis Forbes Knt: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for ye: said City (Quor un) bearing date the 23d: day of July last Edward Hurst & Elizabeth his wife were removed from ye [---] Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate London to our sd [...] Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx by the Name of the Liberty of Eastsmithfield in the Parish of St: Botolph [...] without aldgate in ye. County of Midlx as to the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That ye. Petrs: think themselves agriev'd by the sd. Order & humbly appeal to this Honble Court agt. ye same

And pray that agr: Worspps would be pleased to give them such relief in ye premises as to yr: Worspps shall seem meet.

And yr: Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc.
