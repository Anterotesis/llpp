To the Rt Honrble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and also to the Rt Worpll. the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions assembl'd

The humble petition and Appeal of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Katherine Creed church London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant unde the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London bearing date the 25th Day of April last past Elizabeth Nash wife of Thomas Nash with her two Children George and Thomas was removed from the parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said parish of St. Katherine Creed church London alleadging the said parish to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That your petitioners doubt not but to prove That the said Elizabeth Nash never had any legal Settlement in the said parish whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to yor Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of abide the determination of your Honr, Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionr Shall ever pray Etc.
