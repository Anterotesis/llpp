London ss. To the Rt. Honble Sr. Geo: Thorold Bart. Lord Mayr & ye Honble. Bench now Sitting att Justice Hall in ye Old Baily

The Humble prayer of Jno Everitt in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor petr. was Comitted to this place the 16th July last for Assaulting & Robing one Tho Burd of 16s. of wch fact he is Inocent

yr pr therefore prays that he may be tryed or Otherwise Discharged According as to ye. Lordsp. Shall Seem Meet

And yr pr as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.
