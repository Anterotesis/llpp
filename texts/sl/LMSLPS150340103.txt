To the Right Honble Sr Gerrard Conyers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his maties Justices of the peace at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Catherine Coleman London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the 14th day of June 1723 Elizabeth Balderstone Widdo. of James Balderstone and Mary Elizabeth & Anne her three Children were removed and passed [---] by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Catherine Coleman London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour and Worships there from

Yr Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that Your Honour and Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London may Appear before You & hear & abide the determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And he Shall ever pray Etc.
