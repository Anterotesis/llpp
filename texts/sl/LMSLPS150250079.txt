To the Rt:Honble Sr. Samuel Stainer Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the great within the Libties of the said City

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dates the Ninth day of July last past under the Hands and Seals of two of her late Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London (Quor unus) Alice Masters wife of John Masters Senr with Jane aged eight years John aged Seven years James aged Six years Henry aged five years Joseph aged two Years Elizabeth aged one Year & Charles aged five Weeks her seven Children were removed from the Parish of St. Sepulcher within the Libties of the said City to yor. Peticonrs. Said Parish of St. Bartholomew the great upon the Oath of the said Alice masters that about four years agoe her sd husband John Masters Senr. was a house keepr and paid parochial Duties in yor. Peticoner sd Parish, and noe Subsequent Settlemt. appearing to the said Justices, they did therefore deem & adjudge the last legal Settlemt. of the sd Alice masters & seven Children to be in yor. Peticoner sd Parish of St. Bartholomew the great

And forasmuch as yor Peticoner find themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrt. or Order of th sd two Justices, they humbly appeal agnt. the same to this Honble Court, and pray to have such relief in the premisses, as to this Court shall appear just & reasonable,

And you Peticoner shall pray etc.
