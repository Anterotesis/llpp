To the Honourable Sr Charles Duncomb Lord Major of London & ye rest of ye Justices: now sitting att ye old Bayly

The Humble Petition of Christian Voltlage:

Sheweth That your Petitioner is a Stranger a Jarmayn Corne. & by trade a woorking Goldsmith [...] did One of another stranger a brooken silver spoone or part thereof & offering ye same to sell to a Goldsmith was questioned & committed to Newgate

Your Petitioner Humbly Prayes yt he may be discharged of his imprisonment there being noe indictment against him

And as in Duty bond shall for ever Pray for your Worshipps Etc.
