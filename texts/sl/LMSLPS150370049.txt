To the right Honrble Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genrall Quarter Sessions of the peace held at Guild hall in and for the Citty of Lords now Assembled

The Humble peticon [---] and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the said Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. and Sr. Randolph Knipe Knt. two of his Maties Justices of the peace in and for the Citty of London (Quor unns) bearing date the Two and Twentieth day of March last John Fletcher and Infant aged about Sixteen month the Child of John Fletcher and Elizabeth his Wife whose Setlement are unknown lately dropt in the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London was passed to the said parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx as the place of his last legall Setlement by which order or Warrant your peticoners think themselves Aggreived and humbly Appeale therefrom

Your peticoners therefore most humbly pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers [...] of the pan of the sd parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate may attend this Court to abide the Judgment and determinacon of your Worshipps touching the meritts of this Appeale

And your petr shall ever pray Etc
