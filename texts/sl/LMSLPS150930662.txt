To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London on their General Session of the Peace at Guild Hall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Worshipfull William Plomer Lord Mayor of the said City and Robert Peckham Esquire and other of the Aldermen of the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth Day of February One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty Two Ann the Wife of Reynold Roberts was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner's conceive themselves Aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioner's therefore Pray this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determination the said Appeal. And that all Persons concerned may there be directed the Attend.

Jos: Holl Sollr. for the Appellants.
