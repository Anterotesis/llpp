To the Right Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Richard Glynn and William Alexander Esqr. Two of His Majesties Justics of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the saith day of this instant October Sarah Tock aged Forty four Years was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mildred in Bread street London to the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement.

The Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and Pray Your Worships to relieve them in the Premisses

And the Petitioners as in duty bound will pray Etc.

G Talmash Attorney for the Appellants.
