To the Rt. Honble Sr. Willm Lewen Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Baily

The Humble Peticons of Mary Kendall Ann King & Sara Dean Prisonrs in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petirs are Charg'd on suspicon of breaking Mr. Meredith Chambers in Lincolns Inn & stealing some things whereof yor Petrs. are innocent

Yor. Petrs therefore Humbly prayes this Honble Court to be Tryed this sessions unto be discharged

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc
