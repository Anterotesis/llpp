To the St Honble Sr Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Saviours Southwarke

Sheweth That one Susan Blank with her nine Children by Virtue of a Warrant under the hand and Seale of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within shew City (one being of the Quor) bearing date the Twentyh day of August last past was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the aid Parish of St Saviours Southwarke alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall settlement of the said Susan Blank

That your Petr doubt not but to prove that the said Susan Blank never had any legal settlemt in the said Parish of St Saviours Southwarke thereby they remove themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to gone Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitr therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will heare them in the Prmisses and Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate to appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs shall Ever Pray Etc
