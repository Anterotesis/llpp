To the Right Worshipfull Henry Marshall Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace now assembled.

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by virtue of an order of pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the said Henry Marshall Esquire then and now Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sir William Calvert Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of March last past a woman in the said order or pass warrant called Mary wife of Nicholas Lebar was removed from the parish of Saint Andrew in Holborn London unto the said parish of Saint Luke alledging the said parish of Saint Luke to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said order or pass warrant and therefore humbly appeal against the same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

J: Witton Soll. for the Appellts }
