To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers, of the Poor of the Parish of Trinity Minories in the Liberty of his Majesty's Tower of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal bearing Date the 29th Day of October last under the Hands and Seals of Frederick Bull Esquire and Thomas Wooldridge Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) Mary Collins Wife of Thomas Collins was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street in the City of London to the said Parish of Trinity Minories, as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Thomas Collins.

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Saml Ravenscroft for the Appellants
