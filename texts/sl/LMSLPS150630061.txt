The Humble Petition and prayer of John Norwood

Sheweth That your petitioner on the 19th. day of February last was Committed or Sent to the Poultry Compter by the worshipfull Sir Robert Ladbrook Knt. one of the Aldermen of this Honourable City on Suspicion of Stealing Lead from Barbers Hall in this City upon the oath of

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly prays that he may be Tried Bailed or discharged for the premisses according to the form of the Statute in Such Case made and provided

Jno. Norwood
