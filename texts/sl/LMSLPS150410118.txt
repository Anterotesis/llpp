To the Right Honble Sr Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Rt. Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City aforesaid in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

30/9 The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Mary At Hill Londo.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sr Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & Sr. Edward Beecher Knt. & Aldermar. [---] & two of his Maties Justices of ye Peace for the same City (Quor Unus) bearing date the thirtieth day of September last One Mary Wotost Singlewoman was Passed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Less London, to the Parish of St. Mary Athill London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. humbly conceiving themselves agreived by the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief in the Premisses

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray etc
