To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the said City assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one of whom is of the Quorum) bearing date the fifth day of December One thousand Seven hundred and Sixty one Elizabeth Scott Widow of Thomas Scott deceased and their Child Elizabeth aged five Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish, of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London as the Place of their last legal Settlement by which Order your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeal to this Honble Court and pray that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London and all Parties concerned may attend the said Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal And that your Petrs. may have such Relief in the Premisses as to the said Court shall seem meet.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
