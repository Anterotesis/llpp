To the Right Worshipfull Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord mayor and other his majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the peace now holden for the said City.

The Humble Petition and appeale of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing Date the twenty fifth day of may last past under the hands and Seals of Sir John Thompson Knt. Lord mayor of the City of London and Sr. William Billers Knt. two of his majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) Mary Bradshaw wife of John Bradshaw was removed from the parish of Saint Dionie Back Church in the said City of London unto the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement by which said Warrant or order your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeale to this Court against the same Praying such reliefe as to your Worshipps shall seem most meet

And yor Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc

Tho: Walker John Burton} Church wardr and Overseers of the poor
