To the Right Honble. Sr William Lewin Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London And to the Right Worshippfull the rest o his Maties Justices of the peace in the Genrall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows in Lombard Street in the Citty of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of A Warrant under the hands & Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within the Citty of London (one being of the Quor) beareing Date the Fourth Day of September now last past One Elizabeth Whaley was removed from the Parish of Alhallows Staining to the Said Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street alledging that the Said Parish is the Palce of the Last legall Settlemt. of the Said Elizabeth Whaley

That Your Peticoners Doubt not but to prove that the Said Elizabeth Whaley had No Legall Settlemt. in the Said Parish of Allhallows in Lombard Street whereby they conceive themselves Agreived by the Judgemt. of the Said two Justices and do Humbly Appeale to Your Honour & Worshipps thereupon

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Pray Your Honour & Worshipps will be pleased to hear them in the prmises and Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Alhallows Staining to hear and abide the Determinaton of Your Honour and Worshipps touching this Appeal

And Your Petrs Shall Ever Prays
