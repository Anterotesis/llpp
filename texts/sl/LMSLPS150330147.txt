To the Right Honble Sr Willm Stewart Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their General Quarter Sessions of the Same City Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Michaels Crooked Lane London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands and seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd City (Quor unus) bearing Date the 13th day of March last Anne Boddington Singlewoman was removed & passed from the parish of St Peters poor London unto the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Michaels Crooked Lane London Alledging the Said parish to be the places of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom.

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray Your Honour Worps. will be pleased to hear them in the premises And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St Peter poor may Appear before you and hear abide the determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
