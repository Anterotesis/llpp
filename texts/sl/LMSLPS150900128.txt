To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Old Stratford upon Avon in the County of Warwick

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of the Majestys Justices & the Peace for the City London (one being of the quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of July last past Sarah the Wife of James Crawford and William her Son aged One year and Eight months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate London to the said Parish of Old Stratford upon Avon as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petition therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray relief in the Premisse
