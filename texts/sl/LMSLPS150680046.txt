To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices in their General Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition of George Willson a Prisoner in Newgate.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner was at the last January Session holden for the said City Indicted for a Grand Larceny at the Prosecution of One Mr. John Overy

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly prays that at this present Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City of London He may be either Tried Bailed or discharged for the Larceny aforesaid pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided.

George Millson
