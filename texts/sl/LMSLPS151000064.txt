To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor Aldermen and Recorder of the City of London at their General Session assembled

The hble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the East in the said City.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the sd. City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh Day of Febry 1788 Jane Hughes Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Katherine Creechurch in the said City to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East afsd. as the place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore libly appeal to this Court agt. the sd. adjudication of the said two Justs. and their said Order or Pass Warrt. and pray that the Same may be quashed vacated and set aside And that the sd. Jane Hughes may be forthwith sent back and delivered unto ye. chrchwrdns & Overseers of the poor of the sd Psh of St. Kath CreeChurch And that they may be ordered to pay unto your Petrs. their Costs and Charges in this behalf Sustained

And your Petrs [...]
