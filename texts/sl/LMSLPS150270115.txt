To the Rt Honble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and also to the Rt. Worpll the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace for ye City at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Margaret Mosses London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals two of his Majtys Justices of the peace within ye City of London (Quox un) bearing Date the Twenty thirday of July last past Jane Kelly and Deminacon Kelly were removed from the parish of St Nicholas Coleabby London unto the said Parish of St Margt Margt Moses London alleaging the said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant and Judgmt of ye two Justices do most Humbly appeal to Yr Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly prays your Honor. & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby may appear before You [...] and hear & abide the Determinacon of yr Honr & Worps touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
