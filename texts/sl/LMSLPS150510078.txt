To the Rt: Honble: Sir John Saltar Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Warrant under the hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir George Champion Knt. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth day of June last past, Sarah Clarke Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London, unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgt London, alledging the said Parish of St. Bottolph without Aldersgate, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Peticoners conceiving yemselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order, Do appeal to your Lordship & Worps therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London aforesd. may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
