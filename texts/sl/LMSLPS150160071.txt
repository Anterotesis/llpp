To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Owen Buckingham Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to the Rest of the Justices of his Honble. Courte

The humble Peticon of George Dunn & Martha Watkins Prisoners in Newgate

Sheweth That your peticonrs: are confined for buying & receiveing of Stolen goods And your peticonrs. being wholey Innocent of the crime they are charged with & haveing Wittnesses here in Court hopes thereby to prove there Innocency

Wherefore yor. peticonrs. humbly (prayes they may be tryed at this prsent Session dead that theire prayers may be entered accordingly Als to yor. Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc

And yor. peticonrs. Shall pray Etc
