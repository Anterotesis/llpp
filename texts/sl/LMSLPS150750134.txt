To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Court of General [---] Sessions at the Guild Hall of the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order od Removal under the hands and Seals of Wm. Bridgen & F: Cokayne Esqr. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing Date the 19th. day of January now last Past Sarah Barker Widow of Henry Barker deced was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London to the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent as the Place of her Last Legal Settlement by which We think our Selves Agrieved & Appeal to this Court against the said Order

Wherefore your Petitioners Pray this Honble. Court will be pelased on the hearing of all Parties touching the said Appeal to make such further order therein as to this Court shall Seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd. Hopkins Thos Liddard} Churchwardens

Tobias Jordan Gillt Ferguson} Overseers of the Poor
