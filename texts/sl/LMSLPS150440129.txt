To the Right Honble John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twentieth Day of September last past John Nipp was removed from the parish of St. Bridget otherwise St Brides London to the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for Relief in the Premisses,

And Your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc
