To the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor and others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bridget otherwise Saint Bride London.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of William Beckford Esquire Lord Mayor and Sir John Cartwright Knt. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of June 1763, John Katesmark Mary his Wife and their Child Mary Aged one year were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London aforesd. to the said Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London as the place of their legall Settlement where by Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same.

A: Bradley Sollr. for ye. Appellants} Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray this Honoble Court will please to Grant them such Relief in the Premisses as shall be thought reasonable and Equitable.
