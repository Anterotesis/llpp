To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesties Worshipfull Justices of the peace for the City of London at the General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church- Wardens and overseas of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City

Shewth That by an order an pass warrant bearing date the 6th day of March now last past under the hands and seal of the Right Honourable James Esdaile Lord Mayor of the City of London and Nathaniel Thomas Esquires two of his Majesties of Justice of the peace in and for the said City one whereof being of the Quorum) Ursula owen singlewoman was removed from the Parish of saint Faith under saint pauls London to the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said order an pass warrant of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same

And therefore pray that you will be pleased to order the Church Wardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Faith under St. Pauls afsd. and all persons concerned to attend this Court someday this present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the Court touching the premises and the Payer Petitioners may have such releif therein as to your Worship shall seem meet
