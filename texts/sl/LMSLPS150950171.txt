To the Rt. Honble the Lord Mayor and Rt, Worshipful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sharnbrook in the County of Bedford.

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Hallifax and Sir William Plomer Knights, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum, bearing date the Twenty eighth day of April one Thousand seven hundred and eighty four. Jeremiah Coles and Elizabeth his Wife with them two Children namely Elizabeth aged Nine years and Mary aged Eight Months, were Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Bride in the City of London to the said Parish of Sharnbrook in the County of Bedford at the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jeremiah Coles, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray this Honble and Worshipful Court will be pleased to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal to Law and Justice shall appertain.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
