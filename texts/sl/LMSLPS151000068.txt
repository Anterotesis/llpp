To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall Assembled.

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of the parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of the Worshipful Brass Crosby and John Boydell Esquires Two of the Aldermen of the said City of London and Two of his Majesties of the Peace Acting in and for the said City bearing date the Twelfth day of December last past Samuel Mills was conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in Ironmonger Lane in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved And against the said Pass Warrant or orders Do Appeal to this Court

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons [---] concerned may be then directed to Attend

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd Christmas T.T. Tutt B. Baker}

Jno. Walker Peter Paul
