To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worple the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

5/12 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Puttney in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quox) bearing Date the Ninth day of December last past one Thomas Styles with Mary Styles his Wife were Sent by the Church Wardens Aldersgate unto the said Pish of puttney in the County of Surry. Alledging the said Parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of he said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to Your Honour and Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs most humbly pray your Honr and Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses And Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Pish of St Botolph without Aldersgate may appear before you and hear and abide ye Determinacon of yr Honr & Worps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
