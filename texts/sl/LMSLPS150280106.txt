To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. City in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

19/2 The humble Petition & Appeal of ye ChurchWardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St Ann within Aldersgate.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (Quon unus) bearing Date the Nineteenth day of February last past Martha Floyd Singlewoman was sent by the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in to the sd Parish of St Ann within Aldersgate Alleadging the sd Pish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petrs therefore conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of he said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to Your Honr & Worships therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Giles without Cripplgte may Appear before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of Your Honr & Worps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
