To the Worshipful This Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Plumbe and Evan Pugh Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing Date the Thirteenth day of July 1779 Judith Armstrong a Singlewoman and her Illegimate Daughter named, Ann, Aged Six Years were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex as the last legal settlement of the said Judith and Ann whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and order of the said two Justices and pray that the Hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned till the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be Holden for the said City And that your Petitioners may have such relief therein as to your Worship's shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Jno. Skull Jelut for the Parish

13 Obtr 1779
