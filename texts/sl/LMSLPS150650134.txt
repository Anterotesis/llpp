To the Rt. Honble. Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worshipful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. peace for the said City ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Lurgeshall, in ye. County of Wilts;

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of your Lordship & William Benn Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for the City of London, & the LIberties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. 31st: Day of May 1754, Elizabeth Sturgis, Widow of John Sturgis, deced, and their Child, Mary, aged eleven Months, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London, unto the said Parish of Lurgeshall, in ye. County of Wilts, alledging ye. sd. Parish of Lurgeshall, to be the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Atty for the Appelants } That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. said Warrantor Order of Removal, Do Appeal to Yor. Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be saved unto them, & ye. Hearing & Determining thereof, be Adjourned, until ye. next General Qual. Session of ye. Peace, to be holden for this City & the Liberties thereof, & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. sd. Parish of St. Giles, may then appear before you, to hear abide ye. Indgmt. & Determinacon of your Lordship & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc.
