To the Rt: Honble, Sr. Gilbert Heathcote Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London and the rest of her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the Said Citty & Liberties thereof Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poore for the Liberty of Saffron Hill Etc within the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne in the County of Midlx.

Sheweth That by a Passe Warrant Under the hands & Seales of the Rt. Honble: Sr. Gilbert Heathcote Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London and Sr. Samll. Garrard Knt. & Barrt. Justices of the Peace for the Said Citty and the Liberties thereof (quorum unus) bearing date the 25th. day of Aprill last John Mobbs and Elizabeth his wife with their son John aged about fifteen Yeares were removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborne London to the said Liberty of Saffron hill in the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborne & County of Midlx as their last legall Settlement setting forth that about term years agoe the said John Mobbs was an housekeeper and had soe been for seven Years & upwards in Brewers Yard in Chick lane in the parish of St. Andrew Holborne & County of Midlx aforesaid where he was assessed and paid to the poore during such habitacon by which Your Peticoners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved and appeale to the Judgment of this Court.

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray for an Order of this Court requiring the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of St. Andrew Holborne London to Attend this Prsent Sessions and Shew cause why their Appeale should not be allowed and they discharged of and from the Maintaining & keeping the said John Mobbs and his wife & John

And Your Peticonrs, shall pray Etc. [...] Rd. Watkins

Richd: Davison Churchwr.

Tho: Hill Dolor Slaughter} Overseers
