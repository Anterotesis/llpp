To The Right Honoble Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Crippligate London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Edward Bellamy Knt. and Sir Joseph Hankey Knt. two of his Majties. of the peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing date the Twelfth day of July 1743 Jane Benn Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West London to the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Petrs. Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray they may be relieved against the said Wartt. or Order in such Manner as this Honoble Court shall direct

And your Petrs. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
