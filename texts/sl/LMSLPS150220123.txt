To the Rt. Honrble Sr Robt Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Nicholas Acous London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hand and Seal of Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Late Lord Maior of this City bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of October last past one Richard Bragg was removed from the parish of St Katherine Creechurch London unto the Said parish of St Nicholas Acous alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Richard Bragg

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Richard Bragg never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Nicholas Acous whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment and do humby appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Katherine Creechurch may appear before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
