To the Right Honble. Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor the Recorder and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof at their General Quarter Session of ye peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church-wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the hands and seals of Slingsby Bothel and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City and Libertys (whereas one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th. day of August 1754 Elizabeth Jordan the wife of Richard Jordan (who in the aforesaid Order is sayd to be run away from her) and this Child Elizabeth Aged one Year were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate in the said City to the said parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners and Appellant apprehendg themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices do Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a Day this present session for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate and all other persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same touching this Appeal And that Your Petitioners & Appellts. may be Relieved as this Court shall be meet

And Yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Billington & Attorney for Appellts.
