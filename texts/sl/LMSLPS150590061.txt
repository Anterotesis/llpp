To the Rt. Honble Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knt. Mayor of the City of London & the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guild Hall in & for the said City assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Warden & Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents in the Parish of St Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the 14th. day of January last under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quorum unus whereby Mary Mullings & Ann her Daughter were removed from the Parish of Saint Michael Royal London to the said Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Joshua Cox Soll For the appellts. That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Dor therefore Appeal to your Worships therefore and humble pray such Relief in the Premes as your Worships shall seem meet.

April 15 1748 And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
