To The Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majestys Justices for the same City at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled for the same City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of B. Kenneth Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Thomas Hallsfax Esqr. Alderman of the said City Bearing Date the 17th day of March Last Whereby James Plumpton a Single Man was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate in the said City to the Liberty of the Rolls as the Place of his last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and Humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this Present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioner shall every Pray Etc.
