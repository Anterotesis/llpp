To the Rt. Honoble. Sr Charles Peers Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London & to the Rt. Worpll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace at the Genll. Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter to Poore London

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 29th. day of Novr. last under the hands & Seales of Sr. Wm. Thomson Knt. Recorder of the Citty of London & Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. & Alderman of the said Citty Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quorum unus) One Margaret Kite was passed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of St. Peter Le Poore as the place of her last legall Settlement.

That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the said Parish of St. Peter Le Poore Conceive themselves agreived by the said Order & therefore Appeale from the same to this Honoble. Court & humbly pray to be releived agt. the said Order.
