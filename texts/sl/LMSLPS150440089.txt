To the Right Honourable John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Master & Wardens of the Company of Sadlers on behalf of themselves & the rest of the Members of the said Company

Sheweth That Your Petitioners Hall or Mansion House standeth in the Parish of St. Vedast otherwise Foster in Foster Lane London that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish have rated and Assessed your Petitioners in respect of the Poors Rate for their said Hall the sum of fifteen Pounds and ten Shillings a Year as their Proportion towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That Your Petitioners used to be rated but Eleven Pounds five Shillings and four Pence & sometimes only ten Pounds & eight Shillings for many years heretofore and that severall other of the Inhabitants of the said Parish are not rated and assessed in the like Proportion

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved Humbly pray your Lordship and Worships that you will be pleased to relieve your Petitioners in the Premisses and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Vedast otherwise Foster and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court & produce their severall Books of Rates and Assessments and stand to and abide the Determination of this Court touching the Merritts of this Appeal

And Your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc

Robt: Lathwell Edwd Halsey John Holme
