To the Right Honble St. Willm Humfreys Knt & Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worppll ye rest of his Matys Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of St Andrew Holbourn above Barrs in ye County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrt under ye hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the Peace (one being of ye Quor) bearing date the Thirtieth day of June last One Judith Blith above Ten Years Old was sent & delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Parish of St Giles Criplegate London unto ye part of the Sd Parish of St Andrew Holbourn above Barrs in ye County of Middx & Alleadging the sd Parish to be ye place of her last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by the said Warrt & Judgment of ye said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to ye Honours & Worrpps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly Pray your Honour & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in ye premisses and order that ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Sd Parish of St Giles Criplegate London may Appear before You & hear & abide ye Determinacon of ye Honour & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray
