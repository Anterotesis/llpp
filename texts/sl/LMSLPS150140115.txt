To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Samuell Dashwood Knt. Lord. Major of the Citty of London And to the Rt. Worll. Her Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Anne Blackfryers London

Sheweth That one Katherine Palmer late wife of Edward Palmer and twoe Children by Warrant of twoe Justices of the Peace one being of the Quor were brought by the Churchwardens of St. Gyles without Cripplegate London to yor Peter to be provided for on the Oath of the said Katherine That the last Settlement of the said Edward her late Husband was in yor. Peter Parish where hee Lived an Appntice with Thomas Woodward and was by him made free of the Company of Plummers on 30th December 1689 and hath fuice had noe other Settlement Where as in truth the Said Edward was bound by Indenture dated 7th November 1682 to one Joseph Barnbow with whom hee Served in Cripplegate Parish and was made free on the 15th.January 1689 And dwelt with the said Thomas Woodward only 12 weekes about the End of the said Apprentice Shipp

Their humble Suite unto yor Lopp. and Worpps is To hear them in the Prmisses And to send the Said Katherine Palmer & her twoe Children to the Churchwardens of St. Gyles Cripplegate where hee served his Apprentice Shipp untill They can Free them Selves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And They Shall Pray Etc.
