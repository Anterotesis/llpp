To ye. Rt. Honble Sr. Owen Buckingham Knt. Ld. Majr. of ye: City of London & to ye rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in ye old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Susanna Dulton A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was in March last Comittee to Prison Charged too feloniously picking ye pockett of Katharine Baker of wch. fast yor petr is innocent

Yor Petr. therefore humble prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be admitted to bayle

And yor Petr. as in duly bound shall ever pray Etc
