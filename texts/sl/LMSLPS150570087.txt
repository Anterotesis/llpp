To the Right Honble Sir Richard Heare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and others the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace now assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave in Hartstreet London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing Date the 28th Day of January last under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Thomas Winterbottom Esquire and Alderman two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said City Ann Wilkinson Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin Outwich London to the said Parish of Saint Olave in Hartstreet as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant appeal to this Honble Court for Relief in the Premisses and humbly pray your Lordship and Worships to hear the same and make such Order and Determination therein as to you shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Chris. Robinson Sollr. for the Appellts.
