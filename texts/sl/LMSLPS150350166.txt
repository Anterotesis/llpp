To the Rt Honoble Sr. George Merttins Knt. Ld. Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll. the rest of his Majesties Justices, of the peace at their Genll. Quarter Sessions assembled a Guildhall in & for the said City of London

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by intue of a Warrt under the hands & Seales of the then Sr. Peter Delme Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London & Sr. George Merttins Knt. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said City (quor unus) dated the the 21st. of Octobr 1724 one Mary Page an Infant about three years old was removed out of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West London in, to your Petrs. Liberty of the Rolls as adjudged by the Sd. Justices to be then legall Setlemt. (as by the said pass Warrt. may more fully appeare)

For as much as that your Petrs. find themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrt. doe humbly appeale to this Court agt. the same

Humbly prays that this Court will appt. a [---] day for hearing of the same the next Genll Quartr. Session & that they may be releived

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
