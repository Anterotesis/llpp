To the Right Honrble Sr Samuell Stainer Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Pace for the said City in their Generall sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeale to Sarah De Payba Widow

Sheweth That your Petitionr is an Inhabitant in the parish of St. Katherine Creed church in London and is rated Two pounds and eight Shillings for the Workehouse in Bishopsgate Street which your Petitioner humbly conceives to be very unequall and is much more than others in the said parish under the like Circumstances are ratedat

Your Petitonr therefore most humbly Appeals to your Worpps against the said Rate and pray that she may have such releife in the prmisses as the Law in that case directs

And your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc
