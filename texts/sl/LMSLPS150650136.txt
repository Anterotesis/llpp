To the Right Honourable Thomas Rawlinson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens of the Parish of Saint Olave in the Old Jewry London.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the Hands & Seals of the sd. Thomas Rawlinson Esquire and William Allexander Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 24th. Day of June last Ann Bryant wife of William Bryant who is Run away from her & their two Children Ann aged five years and William aged two Years were removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great in London aforesd. to the sd. Parish of Saint Olave in the Old Jewry as the Place of the Last Legal Settlement of the sd. Husband of the sd. Ann Bryant.

By which sd. warrant or Order your Petition are Conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying Such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioner as in duly Bound, will Pray

Jno. Aris Alexr. Hogg Churchwardens.
