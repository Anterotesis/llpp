Appeale St Nicholas Coleabby agt St Peter Pauls Wharfe

To the Rt Honble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Nicholas Coleabby London

Sheweth That one Mary Rigg wido with her three Children Thomas Edward & Mary by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City one being of the Quorn bearing Date the 29th Day of November last past was removed from the parish of St Peter Pauls Wharfe London unto the said parish of St Nicholas Coleabby London Alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall settlement of the said Mary Rigg

That your Petitionr doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Rigg never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby whereby then conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices ad do humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Peter Pauls Wharfe may appeare before you and heare & abide the Determination of your Honr Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners shall Ever Etc
