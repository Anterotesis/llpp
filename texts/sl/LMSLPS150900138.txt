To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands and Seals of Frederick Bell & John Hart Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the 15th day of May Instant, Mary Salhill Singlewomen was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Andrew by the Wardrobe London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Salhill whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and most Humbly pray that the benefit of such their Appeal may be Saved unto them until the next General or Quarter Session of the Peace to be Holden in and for the said City of London and that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in the said Next Session of the Peace for the hearing & determining this their sd Appeal And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc

Cox Soldr. for Appellts.
