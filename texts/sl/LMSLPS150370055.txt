To the Right Honoble St. Francis Forbes Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majty's Justies of the peace for the said City & the Libties thereof to their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticoned & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Faith London

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pass Warrant under ye hands & seales of your Lords his & Sd. George merttice Rnt & Atturd of the snce City Two of His Majestis Justies of the peace for teh said City & the Libertis thereof (Quard unus) bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of May last past Elizabeth Farn Widow of Command Facer was removed from the Parish of Christchurch London unto the Parish of ST. Faith London aforesaid alledging the said Parish of ST. Faith to be the Place of her last legall settlemt.

That your Peticoners conceiving yr. selves agreived by the said order or Warrant of Removeall or aforesaid Do humbly appeall to your Lords & Worships therefrom And humbly pray that yor Lords & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Christchurch London may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of yor Lords Worps toucing this Appeall.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray etc.
