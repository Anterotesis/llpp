To the Rt. Honble Sr. George Thorold Bart. and the rest of ye Justices Now Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bayile this 27th. Aprill 1720

The humble petition of Edwd. Fenton a prisoner in in Newgate

Most humbly Sheweth That yor petitionr. was Comitted to this place the 2d. of this Instant Aprill for takeing with others from Capt. Jno Lewis a Snuff box of wch fact he is Inocent

Yor petr. therefore humbly prays that he May Called Down this Sessions & Prosecution or Otherwise Discharged & yor petr. as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.
