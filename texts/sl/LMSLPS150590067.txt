To the Right Honorable Sr. Robert Ladbrooke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Mary at Islington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of the Right Honorable Sr. Robert Ladbrooke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & Robert Alsop Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of January last John Cornwall and Ann his Wife were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the Parish of St. Mary at Islington in the said County of Middlesex

The appellants think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Therefore humbly Pray your Worships to Grant them such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

G: Talmask agent for the Appellants
