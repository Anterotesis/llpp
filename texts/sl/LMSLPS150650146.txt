To the Right Honourable Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City in their general Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Lord Mayor aforesaid and Slingsby Bethell Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of September last Sarah Baldwin Singlewoman was removed and Conoyed from the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street in the said City to the said Parish of St. Mary Islington as the place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace, appeal to this Court against the same & humble pray your Worships to hear & Determine the same.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Humphrey Elmes Attorney for the Appellts.

Jon: Boursted Charles Tapp} Churchwarden

Geo: Prince Jos: Wilkins Overseers of the poor
