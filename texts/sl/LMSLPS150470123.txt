To the Rt. Honble the Lord Major and the rest of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in the said City

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order made under the hands & Seals of St. John Williams Knt. Lord Major and Sr. Henry Hankey two of His Majties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st day of May last Anne Makepeace widow was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said City to the said Parish of St. Lawrence Jewrey as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the said Anne Makepeace

That the Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order of Removall Do appeal against the same unto this Court And

Humbly Pray that this Court will please to appoint a Day for the hearing and determining of the Said Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
