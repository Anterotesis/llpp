15/12/1731 To the Right Honrble Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worpfull his Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for the said City & Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humbly Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Le Bow in Cheapside in London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City & Libertys (Quor Unus) bearing Date the 15th. day of December last past Hannah Harvey Widow was lately removed from the Parish of St. Mary Maydelon in Old Fish street in London as the Parish of St. Mary Le Bow in Cheapside in London as the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrived by the Judgment & Warrant of the said two Justices Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worps against the sd. Order and humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to yor. Lordship & Worps shall Seem meet

And yor. Petitioners as in duty bounds shall over pray Etc
