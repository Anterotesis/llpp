To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the City of London at their Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the City of London

Shew That by Virtue of an Order of Removal bearing date the 24th. day of March 1777 under the respective Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London One being of the Quorum Rachael Harvey a Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street the said Justices Adjudging the last legal Place of Settlement of the said Rachael Harvey to be in the said Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices and do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Pray that your Lordship and your Worships would be Pleased to hear this their Appeal and that the said Order of Removal and Judgment of the said Justices may be Quashed And that your Petitioners may be further and otherwise Relieved in and about the Premisses as the nature of the Case may require

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
