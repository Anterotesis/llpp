St Dunstan in ye West agt White fryers precinct

To the Rt. Honble Sr Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Major of the City of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That One Katherine Daniel the Wife of William Daniel by Virtue of a warrant under the hands and Seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Qurom) bearing Date the Sixthteenth Day of September last past was removed from the precinct of White fryers London unto the said Parish of St Dunstan in the West London alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Katherine Daniel

That your Petitioners doubt not but to Prove that the said Katherine Daniel never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St Dunstan in the West Whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom:

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Premisses and Order the [---] Overseers of the Poore of the said Precinct of White fryers London to appeare before you and heare and Abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
