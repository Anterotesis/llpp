To the Worshippfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of The Right Honourable Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Francis Cokayne Esqr. Two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City whereof one is of the Quorum bearing date the thirteenth day of October instant Mary the Wife of Thomas Westbeer was removed from & out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said City of London to the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex as the place of her legal Settlement

The Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray Your Worships to relieve them in ye Premisses

And the Petrs. will ever Pray Etc:

G Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
