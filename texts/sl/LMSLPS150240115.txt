To the Right Honble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of her Majestys Justices of the Peace in the generall quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Bartholomew by the Exchange London

Sheweth That by Suite of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorun) bearing Date the fifth day of August last past One Samuel Linn was removed from the parish of St Margarett Lothbury London unto the said Parish of St Bartholomew by the Exchange alleadging the said parish to be the place of his last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove That the said Samuel Linn never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Bartholomew by the Exchange London whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Rent and Worpps there from

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Margarett Lothbury may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Pson & Wapps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs shall ever Pray Etc
