To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace for the sd City Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Nicho Coleabby London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt. under under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for ye City of London (Quox unus) bearing Date the 9th day of March last Sarah Meares wife of Willm Meares now a prisoner in ye Woodstreet Compter with her two Children Margaret & James were Sent & delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Gregorys by St Pauls London unto ye parish of St Nichs Coleabby London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt

That your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by th Sd Warrt & Judgment of the sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Sd parish of St Gregorys may appear before you and hear & abide ye determinacon of this Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
