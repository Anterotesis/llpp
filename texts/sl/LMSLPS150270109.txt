To the Right Honble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Nicholas Coleaby London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hand and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace (Quox unus) bearing Date ye Twenty first day of March last past One Henry Maverly Mary his wife and thre Children (Vizt) John about Eight Mary about three Years and George about Nine Months old Were Sent & deliverd by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Sd Gregory by St Pauls unto to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish of St Nicholas Colabby Alleading the Sd pish to be the place of their last Legall Settlement.

That Your Petitioners therefore Conceive themselves agriev'd by the Sd Warrt and Judgment of the Sd two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly Pray your honour and Worps will be pleasd to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens send Overseers of the poor of ye Sd. pish of St Gregory by St Pauls may Appear before you & hear & abide the Determinacon of yr honour & Worrps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
