To the Rt: Honble Sr: Willim Withers Knt. Lord Majr. of the Citty of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey,

The Humble Peticon of Mary Watts & Mary Jones Prisonrs. in Newgate

Sheweth

That yor. Petrs: were Comittee to Woodstreet Counter ye 1st: Aprill instant Charged upon Suspicon of Stealing some Muslim from Mary Thornbury & were this day brought from thence to this Prison Charge with the said fact that yor. Petrs. are wholy innocent

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prayes [---] to be tryed this Sessions or to be admitted to Baile as the Act directs

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
