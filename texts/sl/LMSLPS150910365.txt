London Ss To the Right Honorable Samuel Plumbe Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Worshipful the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and Liberties thereof at their General Session of the Pence Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Plumbe Esquire Mayor of the said City of London and Evan Pugh Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date on the Twenty seventh day of August One Thousand Seven hundred and Seventy nine Hannah Howell a Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saml. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement by which your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal Against the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal and pray such relief in the Premisses as to this Honorable Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners with ever Pray

John Greenaway

Solicitor for the Appellants.
