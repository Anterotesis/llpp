To the Right Honourable St Willm Humfreys Knt & Barrt Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worppll the rest of his Majtys Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Olaves [...] the Old Jury London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing Date the Twentieth day of September last One Thomas Phelps with Margaret his Wife and Thomas their Child aged about Eighteen months were Sen [...] d delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Bartholomew the Great London unto the Parish of St. Olaves in the Old Jury London Alleadging the said Pish to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by the sd Warrt and Judgment of the Sd two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worrpps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray Your Honours Worppl will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Pish of St Bartholomew the Great London may appear before you and hear & abide the Determinacon of your Honour & Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
