To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor of the City of London, and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City, in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents, in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of the Rt. Honorable Nathl. Newnham and Barnd Turner Esquires, two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of January last Elizabeth Clifton Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Christ Church in the said City of London to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hutton Garden and Ely Rents as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Clifton, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved, and against the said Order do appeal;

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray relief the premises and that you Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the present Session for the hearing and determining this their and Appeal

And your petitioner shall ever pray Etc.

Cox Kirby Street Hatton Garden Solr for Appellants.
