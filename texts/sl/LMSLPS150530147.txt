To the Right Honourable George Heathcote Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in the General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Gillingham in the County of Dorest

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hand and Seal of George Arnold Esquire one of the Aldermen and one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof bearing date the fifth day of May last past Jane Baling the pretended Wife of Edward Baling by the Name of Jane Baling Wife of Edward Baling and their three Children Anne Sarah and Jane were conveyed as Regues and Vagabonds to the said Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Gillingham in the said County of Dorset alledging the said Parish of Gillingham to be the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order as aforesaid Do appeal to your Lordship and Worships Herefrom and Humbly pray that you will please to hear them concerning the Premisses and that you will releive them therein as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet and agreable to Justice

And Your Petitioners shall ever Etc.
