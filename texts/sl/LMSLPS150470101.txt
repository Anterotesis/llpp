To the Right Honble. Sir John Williams Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worsipfull the rest of His Majtys's Justices of the Peace for the Said City & Libties thereof in their Genll. Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the hands & Seals of Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City & the Libties thereof (one being of the Quor) bearing Date the fourteenth Day of Septr. last past, Elizabeth Tilley Widow of John Tilley deced & their four Children (to witt) John aged Eleven years, Susannah aged Six Years, Matthew aged four years & Thomas aged Nine Months, were removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London, alledging that the said John Tilley never had any Settlement England, and that the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate is the Place of their last legall Settlement

Cha Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That Yor. Petr. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrt. or order of the Sd. Justices Do appeal to yor. Lordship & Worps there from & humbly pray Yor. Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses, and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before upon to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor. Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale.

And Yor. Peticonrs Shall pray Etc.
