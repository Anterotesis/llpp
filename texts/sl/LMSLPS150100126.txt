To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of St. James Garlickhithe

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the 19th. dayes August 1699 One Mary Hodges was brought to yor. Petrs. by the Churchwardens of St. Michaell Royall London To be provided for on the Oath of the said Mary That her last legall settlemt. was in the said parish of St. James Garlickhithe as an hired Servant with one Henry Randall a Silke dyer thereby the space of one whole yeare Whereas in truth the said Mary Hodges did not dwell as a servant wth. the said Henry Randall for the space of one whole yeare in yor. Petr. Pish

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor. and Worpps. to hear them in the prmisses and Order the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of St. Michaell Royall to appear here before you And to send the said Mary Hodges to the said Parish of St. Michaell Royall to be there kept and provided for untill they can free themselves of the charge thereof by due course of Lawe

And They shall ever Pray Etc
