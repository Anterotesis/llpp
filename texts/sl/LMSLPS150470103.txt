To the Right Honoble Sr. John Williams Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Worpll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Peter Le poor London.

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 4th. day of March last under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honoble Sir John Williams Knt. Lord Mayor and Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. & Alderman of the said City of London Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) One William Gibbs was passed from the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London to the said parish of Saint Peter le poor London as the place of his last Legal Settlement

That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Peter le poor London Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore Appeal from the same to this Court and pray to be releived against the said Order
