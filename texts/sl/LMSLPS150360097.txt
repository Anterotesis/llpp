To the Right Honoble Sr George Merttins Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the Said City & the Libties thereof in their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Woodnoth London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Sixth Day of January instant, one Jane Baker Widow, was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Aldermary London unto the said Parish of St. Mary Woodwith alledging that of St. Mary Woolwoth aforesaid to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order on Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Doe humbly Appeale to yor Lord P & Worps therefrom And humbly pray That you Lord p & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Pemisses And that the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the London may appear before your & hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of yor Lordps Worps touching this Appeale

And you Peter Shall ever pray Etc.
