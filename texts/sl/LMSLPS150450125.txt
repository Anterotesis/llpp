To the Rt. Honoble: the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwards & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order made under the hands & Seals of Sir Wm. Billers Knt Lord Mayor and Sir Richd Brocas Knt. two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said City (One being of ye Quram) One Susanna Chapple Widdow was sent and removed from the Parish of St. Buttloph without Bishopsgate in London unto the said Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry Alleadging your Petitioners said Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry to be the last place of her legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices aforesaid Do humbly appeal to Your Lopp and Worshpps therein and humbly pray they may bee heard in the premisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate may appear before you to hear the Judgment and determinacon of your Lopp and Worspps touching this appeal And to abide by the Order of this Court therein
