To the Rt: Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London & to ye [...] Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of ye Peace for the said City & Libties hereof

The humble Peticon & Appeale of ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Gregory near st. Pauls London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of yor. Lordp & St. Richard Brocas Knt. & Aldrand Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Tenth Day of July instant [...] or Woman by the Name of Mary Lee Widow of Henry Lee decd was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Old Fishstreet London unto ye said Parish of St. Gregory London alledging the said Parish of St. Gregory to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That Yor. Petr. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant Order Do appeale to yor. Lordp & Worps therefrom and humbly that your Lordp & Worp will be pleased to hear yr. in ye prmisses and Order that ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fishstreet London may appear before you to hear & abide ye Judgmt. & Determination of yor. Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Pets. shall pray Etc
