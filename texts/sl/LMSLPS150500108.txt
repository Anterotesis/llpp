To the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esquire Lord Mayor of the CIty of London and the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London one being of the Quorum bearing date the thirteenth day of December 1738. Ann Sparkes Widow of William Sparkes deceased was removed from and out of the parish of Allhallows the Great London into our said parish of Saint Thomas the Apostle London as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore humble conceive themselves Aggreived by the said order or Pass Warrant And humbly Appeal against the same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
