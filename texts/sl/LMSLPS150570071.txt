To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Rd. Hoare Knt. Lord Mayor of the CIty of London Sr. Simon Urlin Knt. Recorder of the sd. City Others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in General Quarter Session at the Guild hall in & for the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Oversrs. of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of the Rt. Honble Sr. Rd. Hoare Knt. Lord Mayor & Edwd. Davies Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 18th Day of Novr. Last Elizabeth Walker was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes where by your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrived & agt. the sd. Orderds Appeale

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

Wm. Chimmow agt. for Appellts.}

And your Petr. shall Pray etc.
