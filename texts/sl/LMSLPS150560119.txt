To ye: Rt. Honble: Henry Marshall Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worshipful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace, for the said City & the Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmonton, in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hand & Seal of your Lordship & George Arnold Esqr, two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for ye. City of London, & ye: Libties thereof, (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twentyeth Day of December 1744 Jane Ansell, by ye: Name of Jane Ansell, Wife of William Ansell, was Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London, unto ye. Parish of Edmonton in ye. County of Middx, Alledging ye. Said Parish of Edmonton, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Agent for ye. Appellants } That yor. Petitioners conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal be Saved unto them, & ye. hearing & Determining thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace to be holden for ye. City of London & Libties thereof, & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. sd. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate, may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment and Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Peticoners Shall pray Etc
