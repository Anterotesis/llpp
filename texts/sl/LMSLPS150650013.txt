To The Kt. Honble. The Lord Mayor and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices in their General Session of Goal Delivery for the City of London Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Coke.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner on the 28th. day of December 1753 was Committed to His Majestys Goal of Newgate in and for the City of London by Henry Fielding Esquire one of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex being charged before him on the Oath of Jonathan Monk house for feloniously Selling a Certain peice of Counterfeited Milled Money resembling a Moidore & not cut in peices for a less Value than by its Denomination the same did Import and was Counterfeited for Contrary to the Statute.

Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly prays that He may at this present Session of Gaol Delivery holder for the said City of London, be either Fried Bailed or Discharged for the Offence aforesaid pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and provided

Thomas Cole
