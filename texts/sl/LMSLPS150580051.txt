To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their general Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled on Monday the 13th. day of July 1747.

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of New Windsor in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by an Order made by the Right Honble. William Benn Esqr. & Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City the first day of May last Diana Haynes Single Woman was Removed from the Parish of Alhallows Barkin London to the said Parish of New Windsor

That the said Diana is not a Parishioner of New Windsor aforesaid

Wherefore your Petitioner humbly Pray your Worships that the sd Order of the said two Justices may be discharged And that your Petitioner [---] may have such further Relief in the premes as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
