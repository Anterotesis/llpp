To ye Rt. Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt: Lord Mayr. of the City of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Willm Davarall A Prisonr. in Newgate

AD 1715

Sheweth That yor. Petr was Comd to the said Prison ye 21th day of January last Charg'd with Felony whereof he is innocent

Yor petr therefore humbly prayes to be tryed this sessions or to be admitted to Baile or otherwayes discharg'd as this Honble Court shall thinck fitt.

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc
