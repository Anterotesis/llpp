10/3/1731 To the Right Honrble Francis Child Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worpsfull the rest of his Majestys Justices of ye Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions of ye Peace assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Dunstans in the last London

Sheweth That by vertue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Oath day of March Anno Dui 1731 Joseph Simpson Thomas in his Wife And their two Children vizt. Edward Aged about five years & Joseph Aged About five months were passed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London to the sd. Parish of St. Duntans in the East London as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor Petrs. humbly Conceiving themselves Agreived by the sd. Order & Judgment of ye sd. two Justices humbly Appeale to this Honrble Court for Relief in the Prmes

And Yor Petrs; shall ever pray et

Robt. Booth John Mason} Churchwardens

Jno. Rooke James Jobson} Overseers
