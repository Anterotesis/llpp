To Sr. Robert Beachcroft knt. Majr. of ye. City of London & to the rest of her Maties Justices Sitting at Justices Hall in ye: old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Benjamin Payne St Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. was Committee [---] upon Suspicon of being Confederate with another in Comittee 2 Felomies where of yor Peter is innocent

Therefore most humbly prayes to be tryee this sessions or Baylee

And yor Peter shall ever pray Etc
