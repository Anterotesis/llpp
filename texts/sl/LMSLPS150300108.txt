To the Right humble Sr. John Ward Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the most of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble petition and appearl of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the pish of St. Bridget als Brides

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrt. under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the sd day of April last past Saml. Greenway & Elizabeth his wife and one Child to wit Charlot Dean were seal and delivered by the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the precinct of White Fryers London unto the sd. Pish of St. Bridget als Brides London alledging the sd. pish to be the place of their last Legal settlement.

That your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrt. & Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices and do was humbly appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honr. and Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses & ordr. that the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Bridget als Brides may appear before You & bear & abide ye. Determinacon of the Court touching the sd. appeal.

And ye petitioners shall ever pray Etc
