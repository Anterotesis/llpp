To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and to the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter of Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the said City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Brass Crosby and John Hart Esquires Aldermen of the said City two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty ninth day of December last Elizabeth Stephens Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaff in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the said City as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Stephens Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

Carter & Simpson Sollrs. for the Appellants

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
