St Edmund the King agt St Mary Wollnoth Appeale

To the Rt Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt: Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace [---] in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Edmund the King London

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Halett wide by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Tenth day of November last past was sent and delivered by the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Mary Woolnoth London unto the said Parish of St Edmund the king alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Elizaebeth Hallett

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Hallett never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St Edmund the King whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to gone Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray you will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Mary Woolnoth May appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeale

And they shall ever Pray Etc
