To the Rt. Honoble: Sr. John Fryer Barront. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City Assembled at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace (at Guildhall) for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by on Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. William Thompson and Sr. Thomas Abney Knts: two of his Mats: Justices of the peace for the said City of London (Quor un) Bearing Date the 25th. Day of July last John Ryley a poor Boy aged about Seventeen Years was removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe whereby your Petrs: Humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggrieved.

Your Petrs: therefore most Humbly appeals to this Court against the said Order and prays such Reliefe as this Court shall Seem meet.

And Yor: Petrs: shall Ever Pray Etc.
