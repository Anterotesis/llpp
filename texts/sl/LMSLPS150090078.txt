To the Rt Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Mathew widd by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quox) was brought by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of St. Mathewes Friday Streete to yor. Petrs. to be pvided for on the Oath of the said Elizabeth That her last legall settlement was in the said Parish of St. Gyles Cripplegate where Shee lived in Sugarloafe Courte for the space of about halfe a Yeare wch. was about Nyneteene yeares past Whereas in truth your Petrs. never know any such person as the said Elizabeth Mathews Now was the said Elizabeth an Inhabitant within their said Parish

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Honors & worpps. to order the said Churchwardens of St. Mathew Friday Streete to appeare before you And to hear them all in the prmisses And to Quash the said Warrant And send the said Elizabeth Mathews back to the said Parish of St. Mathew Fryday Streete To be pvided for & mainteyned According to Lawe

And yor: Petrs shall Pray Etc
