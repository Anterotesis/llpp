To the Right Worll. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt Lord Maior of the Citty of London and others their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same Citty.

1694 The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Pish of St. Hellens London

Sheweth That by Virtue of A Warrant under the Hands & Seales of twoe of them Maties. Justices of the Peace wth. in the City one being of the Quor Dorothy Scooly Singlewooman was brought to yor. Petr. by the Churchwardens of St. Gyles wth. out Cripplegate London to be Pvided for upon her Oath That shee was last legally settled in their said Pish of St. Hellens As A hired Servant wth. one Wm. Keane after the rate of Â£3 for the Yeare whereas the said Dorothy continued wth. the said Wm. Keane in the said Pish from the 4th. of May last untill the first of June following And noe longer But then departed thence out of the said service & parish

Yor. Petr. humble suite unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is That you wilbe pleased to heare them all in the prmisses And to send the said Dorothy to the Churchwardens of St. Gyles wth. out Cripplegate from whence she came according to Lawe

And they shall Pray Etc.
