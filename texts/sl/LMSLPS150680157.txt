To the Right Honble. Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye City of London Sr. Willm. Moreton Recorder and ye rest of his Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for ye City of London and Libertys thereof at their Gent. Session of ye. Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye parish of St. Mary White Chapel in ye County of Middlesex City of London and Liberty of his Majestys Tower of London

Sheweth, That by a Vagrant Pass or Warrant under ye hand and Seal of Francis Gosling Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of ye peace in and for ye said City of Londn. Directed to John Box Constable in ye City of London and requiring him to Convey William wilder aged 13 years (apprehended as a Rogue & Vagabond in the Parish of St. Martin Contwich London) to the said Parish of St. Mary white Chapel as ye place of his last Legal Settlement

That the said Willm. Wilder hath been accordingly Removed in Pursuance of ye sd. Pass or Warrant to the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel

That Your Petrs. & Appellts. apprehend yrs chers Aggrieved by ye sd. Pass or warrant and humbly Appeal against ye same

Your Petr. and Appellts. therefore humbly pray this Court to grant them such Relief go to Your Worships Hall Seem meet and just in and touching ye premisses And that ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of the said Parish of St. Martin may (upon having Notice for that purpose) attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgmt. and Determination of ye Court touching the said Appeal

Jno. Billington, Agent for Appell

And Yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
