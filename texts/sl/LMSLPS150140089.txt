Sheweth The humble Petticon of Richd Snelling a poore prisonr on the Com Side of the Fleet Prison

The Yr Peticonr hath been actually a prisoner in the prison of the Fleet under the Custody of Willm Weeden Ford Esqr Keeper of the said Prison before the first day of Janr 1709 and is Still deteyned there at the suit of Jno Pearson Creditr for debt or damages and not haveing where wth to mainteine himself in the said Prison
