To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor and the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petr. and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Robt. Kite and M. Blackiston two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London bearing date the 30th. day of January 1767, Roger Rooe was Removed from the parish of Saint Stephen Coleman Street London to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petrs. apprehend themselves aggrieved And Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petr. therefore pray that they may have Liberty to Enter this Appeal and that this Court will be pleased to appoint some day in the next Session for the Hearing and determining thereof

And your Petrs. will pray Etc

Wm Burroughes Sollr. for Appellant
