To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the City of London and Libertys thereof in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bemondsey in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry Marshall Esqr: Lord Mayor and George Arnold Esqr: two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof bearing Date the tenth day of January instant That Mary Maverley Wife of John Maverley was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Alphage London so the said parish of St: Mary Magdalen Bermondsey as the place of her last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners finding themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Warrant, Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same, And pray, This Court will please to Grant an Order for a respit untill the next Sessions for Trying the said Appeal Your Petitioners not having had time since the removal of the said Pauper to procure their Evidences for the said Appeal.

And Your petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Scrivener Edwd Langton} Church Wardens

Joseph Clark Wm: Wilkin} Overseers of the Poor
