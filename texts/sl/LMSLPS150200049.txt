To the Rt Worpll Sr. Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Gregory London

Sheweth That one Mercy Joyce wife of Jonas Joyce with her Twoe children Mary and Christopher By Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the one and Thirtieth Day of October last past was removed from the Parish of St Ann Black fryers London to the said Parish of St Gregory London alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mercy Joyce and her Twoe Children

That yor. Petitionr doubt not but to prove That the said Mercy Joyce with her Twoe Children never had any legall Settlement in the Said Parish of St Gregory whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices And doe humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore humbly pray Your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmissed and [...] the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Ann Black Fryed [...] appeare before you And heare and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionr Shall Ever Pray Etc.
