To the Right Honble. Sr Richard Levitt Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticote & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by a Pass bearing date the 26th: day of March last past under the hands & Seale of Sd Salathiel Levell Knt & Sr Thomas Lane Knt two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City one Joseph Cooper an Infant aged about 18th Months was Sent from the parish of St Andrew Holborn London to the said parish of St Giles in the Fields as the last place of the legall Settlement of the said Infant

Your Peticoner thinks themselves [...] by the Judgment of the said two Justices & do therefore appeale to this honble. Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrew Holborn & all other parishes concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court to here & abide the Judgment & finall determination thereof touching this Appeal

And your Peticonr: (as in duty bound) shall on pry etc
