To Sir Richd. Glynn Kt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Honble. Court now assembled at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Petition of Sarah Miller now a Prisoner in his Majesty's Gaol of Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petitioner was by Mr. Fieldings Warrt. Committed to Prison for the Murder of one Martha Welch otherwise Pum being an apprentice to her Husband Saml. Miller who is also charged with the Said Murder and had the Said apprentice from the Church wardens and Overseers of Hammersmith Parish in the County of Middlesex: Yor. Petitioner therefore

Most humbly Prays yor. Lordship and the rest of this Honble. Court that yor. Petitioner may be Tryed, Bailed, or discharg'd this present Sessions for the same as yr. Lordship and the rest of this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And yr. Petitioner shall as in duly bound pray Etc

Sarah Miller
