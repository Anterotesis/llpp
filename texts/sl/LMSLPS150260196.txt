To the Right Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the less London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace (Quor unus) bearing Date the Thirteenth day of November last one Elizabeth Morehead lead of Jno Morehead was sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church London unto the said parish of St Bartholomew the less London Alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitiones therefore conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitioners most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleasd to hear them in the prmisses And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said pish of Christs Church London may appears before you & hear and abide and Determination of your Honour and Worps touching this appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
