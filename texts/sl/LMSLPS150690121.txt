To the Right Honble Sr Charles Asgill Knt. Lord Mayor Sr. William Moreton Knt. Recorder and the rest of his Maj. Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys there of at their Genl. Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in ye. County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Pass or order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th. day of April last Ann Bailey Wife of Jeremiah Bailey (a Sailor in his Majestys Service) and their two Children Vizt. Edward aged 4 Years and Mary aged 8 Months where removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said City to the said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green, as the Place of their last legal settlement.

That your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices As Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners & Appellants therefore humbly Pray that this Court will Appoint a day this Present & Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles and all other Person Jno. Billington Attorney for Appellts.

Concerned to attend this Court to here and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same touching this appeal and that Your Petitioners and Appellants may be relived as this Court shall see meet.

And Yor. Petr. & Appellts. shall ever Pray
