To the Rt Honble Sr George Thorold Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quater Sessions Assembled

23/11 The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Oversees of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & seals of two of his Matise & Justices of the peace for the city of London (Quorn Unus) bearing Date the 23d Day of November last past Mary Rand wife of Job Rand with one of Child named Eleanor aged about three yeare were removed from the Parish of St Bolotph without Aldgate London unto the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And pray that your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers & of the Poor of the said Parish may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall every pray Etc
