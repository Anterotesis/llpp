To the Right Honble Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & others his Majesties Justices in their general quarter session of the peace for the said City assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Matfellon otherwise White Chapel in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the eighth day of December last Mary Stevens singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of St. Magnus London into the said parish of St. Mary Matfellon otherwise White Chappel aforesaid

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the hearing and determining the said Appeal may be adjoined until the next general quarter Session of the peace to be held in and for the said City And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Magnus London aforesaid & all other persons concerned may attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching this [---] Appeal

Fra: Salt. Agent.
