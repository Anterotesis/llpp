To the Right Honrble Sr Samuel Stainer Knt Lord Maior of the city of London and to the Right Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the Generall quarter Sessions assembled

the humble Petition and appeale of the Master and Wardens of the Company of Merchard Taylors London

Sheweth That The Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Martin Outwich have rated your Petrs Eighteenth pounds towards the poors rate in the said parish for part of their Hall which is Scituate in the same parish Which Rate your Petitioners humbly conceive is very unequall and in reasonble and do most humbly appeal to your Honr and Worrpps [...] for releif therein

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor may appeare before you to abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps

And Yor Petitionr Shall ever Pray
