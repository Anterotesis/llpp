To ye: Rt. Honble Sr. Owen Buckingham Knt. Ld: Majr. of ye. Citty of London & to the rest of a ye: honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justices Hall in ye. old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Warman St Prisonr.in Newgate

Sheweth That yor: Petr. was Comittee the 30th. day of December last Charge for marrying a second wife his first wife living of wth. Crime yor. Petr. is innocent

Therefore humbly prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be discharged as the Act of Parliamts. directs

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc.
