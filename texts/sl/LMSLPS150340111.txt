To the Right Honoble Sr. Gerard Conyers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Augustine London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing date the fifteenth day of July last past Joanna Ives Widow of William Ives & their three Children (Vizt) Sarah aged Eleven years & upwards John aged Six years & Elizabeth three years old & upwards, were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the Parish of St. Augustine London aforesaid, alledging the Said Parish of St. Augustin to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly appeale to you Lord Ship and Worps therefrom And humbly pray that yor. Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you, & hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of yor Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale.

And you Petitioners shall pray Etc.
