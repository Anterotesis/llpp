To the Right Honourable Sr Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at the Guild Hall of the said Citty in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant dated the 20th day of April last under the hands and Seals of Sr Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor and George Arnold Esquire Alderman two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said City (one being of the Quorum) Elizabeth Pike Widow of John Pike deceased And their son Edward Pike aged thirty years were Removed from the Parish of St Botolph Aldersgate London to the said Parish of St Sepulchre London as the Place of their last legall settlement.

Your Petitioner Conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant & Judgement of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to this Court there from

Thos Witham James Tomkins} Church Wardens

Wm: Banister Richard Cave} Overseers of the Poor
