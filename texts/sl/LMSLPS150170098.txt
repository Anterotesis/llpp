To the Rt. Honrable Sr Thomas Rawlingson Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt: Worsppfull her maties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessons Assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Clemt Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt. Dated ye 4th day of July last past under the hands and Seals of St S: Lovell Recordr and Thomas Stamp Esqr twoe of her Maties Justices of the peace for the sd City of London (Quor unus) That Anne by the name of of John, and Elizabeth Carte were removed from the parish of St Bridgett als Bridges London to the sd Parish of St Clement Danes in ye Said County of Middx Alledging that the sd Pish of St Clement Danes was the place of the last Legall Settlement of the sd John Cart Father of the sd Two Children

That yor. Peticonrs conceive themselves Aggreived by ye Judgmt of the sd Two Justices and humbly appeal to ye worspps there from And humbly pray that ye worspps will be pleased to appointe a day this prsent Sessions that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of St Bridgett als Brides may Attend ye worspps & hear & abide the determinacon of ye worspps touching this appeal

And as unduly bound will pray Etc
