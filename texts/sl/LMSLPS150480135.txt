To the Right Honoble Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael in the City of Coventry

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right [...] shipfu [...] Sr. [---] hn Thompson [...] Sr Henry Hankey Knights two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 10th. day of November last William Thompson an Infant was Removed from the Parish of Saint Bennetts near Pauls Wharf London to the said Parish of Saint Michael in the City of Coventry as the Place of his last Legal Settlement

That Yor Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Yor. Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that they may be releived against the said Warrant or Order in such Manner as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And yor. Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.

Thos: Thacker James Hardway} Churchwardens

Samll: Davis Jos: Gibbons} Overseers
