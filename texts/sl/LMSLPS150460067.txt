To the Right Honble Sr Edwd Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl & the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of December last William Miles Elizabeth his wife and Wm their child aged about five years were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Sd Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch London as the Place of their last Legal Settlemt.

Your Petrs conceiving themselves aggreived by the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal for relief.
