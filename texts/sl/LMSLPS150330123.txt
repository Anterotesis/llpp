To ye. Rt. Honble Sr. Willm Stewart Knt. Lord Mayr of ye City of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in ye. Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Robt Cottrill of Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr: was at the last Sessions brought by Ha: Co: from Bristol directed to the sheriffs of London & thereby Charg'd with suspicon of altering or eracing a Bank Note & was ordered by ye. Court to Remaine in Goale till this Sessions.

Yor. Petnr. & Humbly prayes this Honble Court to be tryed now or discharged.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
