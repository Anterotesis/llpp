To the Rt Honble the Ld Mayor and the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr William Thomson Knt and Sr John Fryer Barrt two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor unus) bearing date the 6 day of March Anno Dm 1722 John Stone with Elenor his wife and their two Children Vizt Ann aged Five Years and William aged about two Years now removed and conveyed from the Parish of Allhallows on London Wall [---] to the said parish of St Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx as the last legall place of their Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore humbly think themselves aggrieved and appeale agt the order of the sd Justices and pray Relief in the prmisses of this Honble Court

And Your petitioners shallever pray Etc
