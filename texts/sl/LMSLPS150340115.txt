To the right honourable Sr. Gerard Conyers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City att the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant bearing date the eighth day of August last under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorn), one Anne Harwood the wife of Richard Harwood with Bodenham their Child aged nine weeks or thereabouts were Removed from the parish of St. Faith under St. Pauls London to the said parish of St. Sepulchre London adjudgeing the said parish to be the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Anne Harwood and Bodenham her Child

That your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices

And do humbly appeal to your honour and Worships therefrom and pray that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Faith may be ordered to hear and abide the Determination of this honourable Court touching this appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Jno: Blakie Church Wardens Willm Bott [x] Ch Warden
