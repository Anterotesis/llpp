To the Right Honourable Sr. Willm Humfreys Knt & Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Bridget als Bridge London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace (one being of the Quoy) bearing Date the Eighteenth day of August last One Martha Finnis wife of Robert Finnis together with three Children Vizt Elizabeth Aged Eight Years June Aged two Years and Susan aged six Months were sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Andrew Holbourne London unto the said Pish of St Bridget als Brides Alleadging the sd Pish to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by the said Warrt and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Worships therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray your Honour and Worpsps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses And order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Pish of St Andrew Holbourne London may appear before you & hear & abide the Determinacon of ye Honr & Worpps touching this appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc.
