11 Feb 1732 To the Rt. Honble Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace In their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens an Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for [...] City of London (Quax Unus) bearing date the 11th. day of February from the Parish of St. Magnus the Martyr London to the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitoners humbly conceiving themselves Agreived by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Honble. Court for relief in the Premisses

And yor. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
