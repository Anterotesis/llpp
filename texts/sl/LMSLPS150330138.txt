To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Wm. Stewart Knt. Ld. Mayr of the City of London And the Rt. Worll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace At the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the East London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Wm. Thomson Knt. Recorder of the City of London and Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the Said City (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of November Anno Dni 1721 One Anne Attwood was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Anne Black Freyers London To the said Parish of St. Dunstans in the East London As the place of her last legall Settlement

That yor Petitionrs. thinking themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the Said Two Justices humbly Appeal to yor. Worpps agt. the same

Wherefore yor. Petrs. humbly pray this Honoble. to hear them by their Councill against the said Order of Removall And finally determine their Appeal

And yor. Petitionrs Shall Ever pray Etc.
