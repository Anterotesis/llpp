To the Rt. Honble. Hery. Marshall Esqs. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the year of W [...] his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof General Quartor Session at the Guild hall of the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeals of the Church Wards. & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands (& Seals of St. Robt. Baylis & St. Robt. Ladbroke Knts. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th Day of January last Elizabeth Walker Wife of John Walker was Removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petitionors conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do Appeale

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be a Appointed this Present Session to hear & Determine the Matter touching the sd. Appeals

Wm. Chimmow agt. for the Appellts}

And your Petrs. shall pray etc.
