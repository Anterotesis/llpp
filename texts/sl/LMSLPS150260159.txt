To the Right Honrble St. Willm Humfreys Knt & Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Majesties of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Nicholas in the [...] City of Rochester in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrt under the hands & seals of two of his Matys Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quor) bearing Date the Twelfth day of May last past One John Emerson between Eight & Nine Years of Age and Richard Emerson between Seven and Eight Years of Age (children of Thomas Emerson and Elizabeth his wife both deceased) were sent and delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Sepulchres London unto the said Parish of St Nicholas in the City of Rochester in the County of Kent Alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by ye Warrt & Judgment of the said two Justices and do was humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray Your Honour & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses And Order that the Church Wardens & Ovrsrs of the Poor of the said Parish of St Sepulchres may Appear before You and hear & abide ye Determinacon of ye Honr & Worpps touching this Apeal

And they shall every Pray Etc
