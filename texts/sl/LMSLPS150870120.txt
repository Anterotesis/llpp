London To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles Camberwell in the County of Surry

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Wilkes Mayor and George Hayley Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing date the Eight day of May Instant, One Edward Hughes and Margarett his Wife were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Andrews Undershaft in the City of London to the said Parish of St Giles Camberwell in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and appeal against the same.

Therefore Your Petitioners humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present Session to hear and determine this their Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray.

Robert Serrell John Castle} Church Warden

Oswald Strong George Baringer} Overseers
