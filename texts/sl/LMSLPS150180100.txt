To ye Rt: Honble. Sr Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Sd. Major of ye City of London & To ye Rest of ye Justices now Setting att ye Old Bay

The humble Peticon of Joseph Holdsworth a Prisonr in Newgate}

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Peticonr was Comitted by yor Lordshp. ye 3d January 1706 Charged by Mr. Puckle for haveing Stolen goods founds upon him

Your Peticonr Most humbly Prayes yt this Honble. Benchs will be pleased to dry him this Sessions or to be Admitted to Barle

And yor Peticonrs shall Pray
