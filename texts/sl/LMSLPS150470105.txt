To the Right Honoble Sr. John Williams Knight Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull his Brothren Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Michael Royall London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of Two his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the City of London one being of the Quorum Dated the 11th Day of February last Mary Chappell Widow was passed to their said parish of St Michael Royall from the Parish of Alhallows the Great London as the place of her last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioner humbly think themselves aggreived

Therefore humbly Appeale to this Honoble Court for releife therein and pray Your Lordship and Worships will make such order thereon as to You shall seem most meet

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
