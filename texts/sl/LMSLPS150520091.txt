To the Right Honoble Daniel Lambert Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Right of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City In their General Quarter Session of the Peace of the Said City at the Guild Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the [...] of the Parish of St. Sepulchre's in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth that by Virtue of an order under the Hands and Seals of the Rt. Honoble Daniel Lambert Esqr. Lord Mayor and Sr. Robert Godschall Knight Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City Bearing Date the Eight Day of July last past Mary Pitt was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Milk Street London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchre Whereby Your Petitioners Conceives themselves to be Aggreived

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to this Honoble Court and Pray this Honoble Court Will be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal and Make order theirin and all parties Coniernd May Stand to and abide the Determination of this Court

And Yor. Petitioners shall over Pray Etc.

Octor. Sess 1741
