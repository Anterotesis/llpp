To the Right Honble Sir Richard Hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sir. Richard Hoare Knight Lord Mayor and Daniel Lambert Esq. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing date the Twenty ninth day of July last past Ann Robinson the Wife of Richard Robinson was removed from the Precinct of White Fryers London to the said Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agrieved by the said pass Warrant do humbly appeal against the same and pray such Releif in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc
