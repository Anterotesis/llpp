To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Fludyer Bart. Lord Mayor and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 12th. day of January 1762 John Stanley aged Forty three Years was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that a Day may be appointed this present Session for the hearing and Determining thereof

And Your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
