To the Right Honourable Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the Said City.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of [...] in the West in the City of London.

Sheweth That by a Warrant under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the said City and George Arnold Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing date the eleventh day of January 1742 William Carpenter and Susannah his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Bridgett vulgarly St. Brides in the said City to to the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William [...] Carpenter & Susannah Wife by which your Petitioners find themselves aggreived and humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Lordship will hear and determine the Matter of this Appeal and give Such Releif as to your Lordship shall Seem Meet

And yr. Petitioners Shall ever pray.

M: Howard Sob for Appellts.
