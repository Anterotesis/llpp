Appeale Pish of St. Gregory Agt St Gyles Cripplegate

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Gregory by St. Pauls London

Sheweth That one Thomas Sharpe and Elizabeth his Wife with their three Children by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Three and Twentieth day of February last past was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Gregory by St Pauls alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Thomas Sharpe

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove that the said Thomas Sharpe never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Gregory by St Pauls whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Worpp thereupon

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr: and worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate to appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
