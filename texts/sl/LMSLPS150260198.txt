To the Right Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx and liberties of Westmr.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace (one being of the Quor) bearing Date the Seventh day of November last one Mary Carr Widdo was sent and delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Martin Ludgate London unto the said Pish of St. Clement Danes in ye County of Middx and libties thereof Alleadging the sd Parish to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitioner most humbly prays your Honour & Worps. will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd pish of St Martin Ludgate may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honour & Worps touching this appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
