To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their general Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Buckingham in the County of Buckingham

Sheweth, That Daniel Bromwell Katherine his Wife and their Daughter Elizabeth aged Eighteen years were by Order of pass Warrant dated the 16th. day of October last under the hands and seals of John Blashford Esqr. (late Lord Mayor of the City of London) and Charles Asgill Esqr. (one of the Aldermen of the same City) two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Buckingham in the said County of Buckingham as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Therefore your Petitioners and Appellants Conceiving themselves aggreived thereby humbly Appeal to the Judgement of this honble Court against the same and pray that this Honourable Court will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal And that all Persons conserned may Abide the Judgement & Determinacon thereof And that your said Petitioners may be releived as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

W: Reade Matths Rogers Tho Bradford John Marcott
