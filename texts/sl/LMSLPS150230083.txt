To the Rt Honrble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

1712. 19/8 The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Gregory's London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majesty's Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the 19th Day of August last past One Jane Bass was removed from the parish of St. Alphage London unto the said parish of St. Gregory alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Bass

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Jane Bass never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St. Gregory whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Alphage London may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitoners Shall ever Pray Etc
