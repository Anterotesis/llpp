To the Right Honourable Thomas Rawlinson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace assembled at the General Quarter Session of the Peace held at Guildhall in & for the sd. City of London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in Southwark in the the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an order of Removeal under the Hands and Seals of the Rights Honourable Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the sd. City of London and Robert Alsop Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the sd. City of London bearing date the twelfth day of January Instant Mary Hayes Singlewoman was removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate in the sd City of London unto the Sd. Parish of St. George as the Places of the last legal settlement of the sd. Mary Hayes as appears by the sd. Order of Removal hereunto annexed

That your Petrs. apprehending themselves aggrieved by the order of removal humbly appeal agt. the same and therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to give them such Relief in the Premisses as to your Kingdom shall seem meet meet

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
