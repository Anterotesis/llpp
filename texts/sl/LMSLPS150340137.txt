To the Right Honble Sr Gerrard Conyors Knt Lord Mayor and to the Rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London

7/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of Joseph Pistana

Sheweth That by an Order of two Justices of the peace for the City of London Your Petitioner was adjudged to be the Putative father of a Female Bastard Child born of the Body of Anne Mathers Singlewoman on the 7th of November last in ye parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

That Your Petitioner Conceives himself to be Agrieved by yr sd Order & Adjudicacon of yr Saw two Justices and Therefore most Humbly Appeals to Your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly prays that Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to here him in ye premisses and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may Attend yr Honr & Worps & abide ye determinacon of [---] this Court touching ye sd Appeal

Joseph Pistara
