To ye Rt Honble Sr. Ja: Bateman Knt Ld Mayr of ye City of London & to ye rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice hall in ye old Baily

The Humble Peticon of John Fox A Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr stands Charged wth. Felony whereof he is innocent

Yor. Petr therefore prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be discharged

And he shall ever pray Etc
