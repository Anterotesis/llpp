344 Sesseme Tassere 1734

May it please your Lordship

When on Thurday the 18th: Instant I had then honour to attend your Lordship in behalf of one, Sarah Orton Step'd by your Lordship powerfull Intercession from Transportation you was then pleas'd to permitt me to remind your Lordship thereof to the end the poor Woman may be set at liberaty in pursuance to your Lordships good intention had your petitions will ener pray

John Field

P:S: as This my relation & at present dustitute & fine Onely A servant & drain'd of money on her Accounts do bigg & pray your Lordship will consider her farther consinamt. having not sufficient to discharge the Jaylors fees
