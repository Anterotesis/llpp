To the Right Honrble Sr William Humfryers Knt [...] and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the general Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petitcon and appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Allhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quorn) bearing date the 20th day of November last past one Frances Roll Wido was sent by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Catherine Creedchurch London unto the said parish of Allhallow Staining London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That your petitionrs therefore [...] Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitr most humbly pray your Honr & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Oversees of the poor of the said parish of St Katherine Creedchurch may appeare before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
