To the Right Hon'ble His Majestys Justices of Oyer & Terminer for the City of London

The Prayer or Petition of Charles Earl of Orrery

Sheweth That he was Comitted to His Majestys Tower of London by Warrant under the hand and Seal of the Rt. Honble John Lord Carteret one of His Majestys principal Secretarys of State dated the 28th day of September last for High Treason which your Petitioner is innocent of

And therefore prays that he may this present Sessions be brought to his Tryal or otherwise be Bayl'd or discharg'd out of Custody persuant to the Statute in that Case Made and provided

Orrery
