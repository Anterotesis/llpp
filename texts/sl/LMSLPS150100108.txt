To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London & To the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City in the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. James Dukes place

Sheweth That your Petrs. Parish lyeth within Aldgate London & consisteth of about 140 houses whereof not above 60 pay to the Poore and but the halfe of that number capable to bear any Office many of the Poore Jewes Inhabiting in their said Parish And having many Poore of their owne in the said Parish to mainteyne And being not able to raise moneyes amongst themselves sufficient for their releife and maintenance And having not benefitt by marriaged in Their Church As They formerly had

Their humble suite unto yor. good Honor. and Worpps. is That you will be pleased to consider of their Poore and meane condition and inability to releive their Poore And to give them such allowance out of other Parishes in London As to your Honour and Worpp shall seeme meete

And They shall Pray Etc
