To the Right Honourable William Beckford Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench in their General Session of Goal Delivery Assembled

The humble Petition and Prayer of John Prince a Prisoner

Sheweth That on the Twenty third day of September last Your Petitioner was committed Prison to the Gate house Prison at Westminster for feloniously publishing as true in the City of London a certain Bill of Exchange Purporting to be the Bill of Exchange of G:Bricklan Etc. drawn on William Orcherton for the Sum of Â£125. in Favour of him the said John Prince knowing the Same to be false forged and Counterfeited with an Intent to cheat and defraud Robert Mackoun Esqr. And now is a Prisoner confined in Newgate under the same Charge

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that at this present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be tried bailed or discharged pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided
