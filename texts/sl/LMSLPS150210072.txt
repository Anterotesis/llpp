Bassie Shaw agt. Aldgate Appeale

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Gilbert Heathcole Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in ye General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Michael Bassi Shaw London

Sheweth That one Ann Wise Wid by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorm) bearing date the 29th Day of March last past was removed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London unto the said parish of St. Michael Bassi Shaw alleadging the said Parish to be two place of the lase legall settlement of the said Ann Wise

That your Petitioners doubt no to prove that the said Ann Wise never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Michael Bassie Shaw where by they conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honrs: and Worpps to heare them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the said Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr. and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionr Shall ever Pray Etc.

Appeale allowed P consent
