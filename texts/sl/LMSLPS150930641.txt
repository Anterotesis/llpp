To the [...] Honourable William Plomer Mayor of the City of London, and the rest [...] Worshipful Aldermen his Brethren in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the City of London Assembled

The Humble Petitions and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London one being of the Quorum bearing date the Twenty Ninth day of September 1781 one Isabella the Widow of Peter Magnant a Lunatick was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East London unto the said Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London as the Peace of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner's humbly Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said order or Pass Warrant appeal against the same and pray such Relief in the Premisses as your London and the Court shall think fit

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
