To the Rt Hoble John Barber Esqr. Lord mayor of the City of London & to the Rt. Wpfull the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace on their genl. Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The hble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Olives Hart Street London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox unus) bearing date the 23d. Day of the October last one Margaret Whitaker the pretended wife of William Whitaker and Hester her daughter was passed from the parish of st. Bridgett als Bride To the parish of St. Olives Hart Street London as the place of their last Legall Settlement

Your peticoner humbly Conceiveing themselves agreived by the Warrt. & Judgement of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this honble Court for releief in the premisses

And as In Duly bound they shall Ever pray

Henry Watkinson Godf. Harries} Church ward [...]

Tho: Parr} overseers
