To the Right Honourable Sir James Esdaile Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful Justices of the Peace in the General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish Saint Martin within ludgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two at his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Second day of January last past Phillis Knott the wife of John Knott then a later in his Majesty's Service was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopgate London to the said Parish of Saint Martin without Ludgate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Yours Petitioner therefore conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and do humbly appeal against the same and pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and Worship shall ever meet

And your Petitioner's shall pray Etc
