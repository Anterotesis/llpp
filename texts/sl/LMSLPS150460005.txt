To the Right Honble. Sr. Edward Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of the Honble Bench Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey London

The Humble Prayer of Thomas Casey

Sheweth That your Supplicant was Committed a prisoner to Newgate on the fifth day of December last on suspition of picking the pockeatt of a person an brown of a Watch and no bill being preferd last sessions yor. Supplicant hath Ever since detained in Newgate.

Yor Supplicant therefore Humbly prays That the Might be Fryed this Sessions or Discharged out of Custody.

And yor. Supplicant as in duty bound shall Ever pray Etc

Janry 16.1734.

Thos. Casey
