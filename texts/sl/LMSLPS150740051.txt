To the Right honourable William Beckford Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices assigned to deliver his Majesties Gaol of Newgate on the prisoners therein being for the City of London County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Francis Parsons

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the Tenth day of June last past was by a warrant under the hand and Seal of John Goodchild Esqr. Comitted to his Majestys Gaol of Newgate being Charged upon the Oath of William Cockburn and Richard Fuller together with one John Bricklow for feloniously and privately taking from the person of the said William Cockburn one Back note of the value of Twenty pounds and other Things the property of the said William Cockburn

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays that he may be Tried at this present sessions for the offence with which he stands Charged us aforesaid or in default thereof he may be bailed or discharged for the same and your Petitioner as in duty bound shall ever pray etc.

Francis Parsons
