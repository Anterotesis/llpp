To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Peckham and Richard Clark Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. Day of December One thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty three Elizabeth Minett Single woman was removed and Conveyed from and Out of the Parish of St. Michael Royal in the said City of London to the said Parish of St Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their Order, and pray that your Worships will please to Appoint some Day in the Present Session for the hearing and Determination the said Appeal and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Michael Royal aforesaid and all Persons Concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Jos: Holl Soll. for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields
