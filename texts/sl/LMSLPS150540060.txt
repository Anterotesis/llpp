To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Pettion and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen Walbrook in the said City of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City where of one is of the Quorum (therein mentioned) bearing Date the Seventh Day of December in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Seven hundred and Forty two Elizabeth Blyth Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint Olave in Hart Street in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Stephen Walbrook as the last legall Place of her Settlement By which your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worshipps will be pleased to hear and determine the Matters touching this Appeal and give thm such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem just and meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Ivatt Agent for the Appellants }
