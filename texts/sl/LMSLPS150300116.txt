To the Rt Honble Sr John Ward Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

22/6 The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Midred in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or order under the hands and seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quom Unus) bearing date the Twenty Second Day of June last past Mary Norwood was removed from the parish of St Bennett at Pauls Wharfe London unto the said parish of St. Mildred in the Poultry alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legal settlement That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices do humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed and that the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Bennett Pauls Wharfe may appear before you and hear and abide the judgment and determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
