To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof as the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition an appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble Saml. Turner Esq. the then Lord Mayor and Barlow Trecothick Esq. one of the Alderman of the said City of London two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quroum) bearing date the 6th. day of October nor last past Sarah Wheeler (Wife of William Wheeler (gone from her) and their two Children James aged 6 Years and William aged one Year were Removed from the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Christ Church in the County of Surry are the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Concern themselves agreived by ye. said Warrant or order & humbly appeal against of same

Your Pettrs. therefore humbly pray that your Worship will be pleased to appoint a day of this Sessions that all parties may attend to [...] and the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said appeal

And Yor Pett shall Ever Prays The Strong Soler for ye said Parish of Christ Church Surry
