To the Right honoble. the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of Goal Delivery at the Sessions house in the Old Bayly

The humble Petition of Thomas Evans

Sheweth That your Petitioner upon his Pleading Guilty hath been Convicted for Stealing of Lead of the value of three shillings the property of Andrew Croft

That your Petitioner is very antient and infirme & this being his first Offence

Humby Prays he may receive Corporate Punishment.

Andw Crosse Prosecutor

And he shall ever pray & Thomas Evans
