To the Right Worshipfull Micajah Perry Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Nass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Said Micajah Perry Esqr. then and now Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr. John Saltor Knt. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London aforesaid (one Whereof is of the Quorum) bearing Date the first Day of June last past Ann Fisher Wife of Thomas Fisher (since Deceased) and Ann her Child Aged one Year and Upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said City of London unto the said Parish of Saint Luke Alledgeing the said Parish of Saint Luke to be the Place of their Last Legall Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and therefore Humbly Appeale against the same

Jno. Witton Sollr. for the Appellts.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
