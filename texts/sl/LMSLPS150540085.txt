To the Rt: Honble. Robert Willimott Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Quarter Session of the peace at Guildhall & for the said City assembled.

The [...] Petition & appeal of the [...] and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order under the hands & Seals of the said Lord Mayor and George: Arnold Esqr: two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Second Day of December last John Gould was removed from the Parish of Allhallows Staining London to the said Parish of St: Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlemt: whereby yor: Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbley Appeal to this Honble Court and pray that the sd: Court will be Pleased to [---] give your Petitioners such Relief in the Premisses as to this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And yor: Petitioners shall ever pray etc

Joshua Cox Sollr. for the Appellts.
