To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt Bart Lord Mayor of ye City of London and to the rest of His Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl. Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 17th. day of September last Anne Ashby Singlewoman was removed & passed from the parish of St Andrews Holborn London to the Said Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx Alledging the said parish to be the Place of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitionres therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hair Man in the Prmisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye Poor of ye sd Parish of St Andrews Holborn may appear before You and hear & abide the Determination of your Honr & Worps touching the said Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
