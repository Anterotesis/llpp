St Brides agt Trinity Appeals

To the Rt. Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That one Mary Trinity Spinster by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one (being of the Quorn) bearing Date the 28th day of November Anno Din 1710 was removed from the Parish of the holy Trinity London unto the said Parish of St Bridgett als Brides London alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlement Mary Trinity

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Trinity never had any legall settlemt in the said parish of St Bridgnett als Brides whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Inderment of the said Two Justices and Doe humbly appeale to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poors of the said Parish of Trinity may appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honour and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
