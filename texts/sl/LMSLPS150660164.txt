To the Right Honoble Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipfull Justices of the peace in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Chappel Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of Bridewell London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant Under the Hands & Seals of William Alexander and Francis Cokayne Eaqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of July last past William Staples Elizabeth his wife and their Three Children Hannah Aged Ten years Elizabeth Aged Six years and Johnson Aged one year were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate London to the Precinct of Bridewell as the place of their last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore Conceive themselves Agrieved by the said pass Warrant Do Humbly Appeal against the Same And pray Such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships Shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
