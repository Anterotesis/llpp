To the Right Honrble Francis Child Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Worpfull his Majesty's Justices of ye peace for the said City & Liberty's thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of ye peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St Anthony als Antholin London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the sd. City & Libertys (Quor Unus) bearing Date the 18th: Day of october last past Ann Symson Singlewoman was lately removed from the parish of [---] St. Peters West Cheap in London to the sd. parish of St. Anthony als Antholin in London As the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment & Warrant of the sd. two Justices Appeale to Yor Lordship & Worps against the sd Order & humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to Yor Lordship & Worps shall seem meet

And Yor Petitioners as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
