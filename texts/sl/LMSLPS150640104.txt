To the Right Honourable Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session assembled

That Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Thomas in Southwark in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands & Seals two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Honble City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth Day of November last one Mary Weedon was removed from the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London to the parish of Saint Thomas aforesd. as the place of her last legal Settlement. whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petr therefore humbly Pray relief in the Premes as to Yor Lordsp Shall seem meet.

And shall ever Pray Etc.

Edwd. James Soll. for Appellants.
