To ye. Rt. Honble: Stephen Theodore Jansson, Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, and to the Worpful the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of Christchurch, in the City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands and Seals of your Lordship & Sr. William Calvert, Knt, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fourth Day of December 1754, Hannah Charlsworth, Widow of Moses Charlsworth, Deceased, and their Child, Elizabeth, aged twelve Years, were Removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London, unto ye. Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging ye. said Parish of Christchurch, to be the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge, Atty for ye. Appellants } That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London may appear before you, to hear and abide ye. Judgment and Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
