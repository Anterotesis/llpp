To the Right Honble Sr Jno Fryer Bart Lord Mayor of ye City of London and to the rest of his Majtys Justices of Gaol Delivery of Newgate Assembled

The humble Petition of Elizab Walker

That yr petitioner is in Newgate by Heas Corpus from ye Marshalsea for Suspition of Felony & has given Notice to ye prosecutor to Attend this Sessions

most humbly prays ye benefit of ye Statute either to be Discharged or Baild

And She Shall ever pray Etc
