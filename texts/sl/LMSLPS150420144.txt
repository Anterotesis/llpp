To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in Genll Quarter Sessions at the Guild Hall of the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poors of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Sr William Thomson Knt and Richard Brocas Esqr two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor Unus) bearing date the four the day of August 1731 Elizabeth Glover Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Sr Mildred in Bread Street London to the said Parish of St George Hanover Square as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court against the Same & desire to be releived therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

George Carpenter Cecil Wray} Church Wardens
