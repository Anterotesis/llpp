To the Right Honourable Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the said City of their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Inhabitants of the parish of Saint Mary at Islington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by vertue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of the Right Honourable [...] Robert Ladbroke Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & Robert Alsop Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the fifth day of January last Sarah Cornwall aged Ten Weeks was Removed & conveyed from & out of the parish of Alhallows on the Wall London to the parish of Saint Mary at Islington in the sd. County of Middlesex

The Appellants think themselves aggreived by the said Order of Removal Therefore humbly Pray your Worship [...] to grant them such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

G: Talmash Agent for the Appellants
