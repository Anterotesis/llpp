To the Right Honourable William Benn Esqr. and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order under the hands and Seals of William Benn and Edward Davies Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of December last James Benbow his Wife and their two Children Mary aged Nine Years and James aged five years were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Bridget London to the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and therefore humbly Appeal against the same and pray Your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premisses as shall seem just & meet

And Your Petrs. will ever Pray Etc.

Jonathan Dean Churchwarden

Wm. Stagg Thos George Isaa Parratt John Broome} Overseers of the Poor
