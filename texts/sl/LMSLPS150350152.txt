To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace now held for the sd. City

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr. William Thomson and Sr Gerard Conyers Knts. two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for City of London (Quorn unus) bearing Date the 21st. day of July last Mary Cater Singlewoman was pass'd and conveyed from the Parish of St Bottolph without Aldgate Londo: to the said Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch as the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitionrs: do conceive themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and do therefore humbly Appeal against the same praying Such relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgment shall seem Just
