London To the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace For the Said City and Liberties Assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace For the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Foster in the Liberty of Westmr

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace For the Said City [---] and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of July one Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty one Elizabeth Ray wife of James Ray was removed From the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Foster in the Liberty of Westmr Alleding the Last Legal Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Ray to be in the said Parish of Saint Leonard Foster in the Liberty of Westmr by which Order of the said two Justices your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggrieved and Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court and pray to be Relieved in the premisses and your Petitioners will Ever pray

Cha. Bignall Attorney For Appellants
