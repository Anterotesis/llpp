To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior [...] City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of ye. Peace In ye. General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Albans Woodstreete

Sheweth That One Frances Webb By virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & Seales of two of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (Out being of the Quor) bearing date the Seaventh day of October instant was Removed from the Pish of St Nicholas Coleabby London to the said Parish of St. Albans Woodstreete Alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last legall settlement of the said Frances Webb

That yor. Petr. doubt not to prove That the said Frances Webb never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Albans Woodstreete whereby they Conceive themselves appreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices And to humbly Appeal to yor. Worpps. therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor. & Worpps. will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Overs the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby To appear here before you And hear & abide the Determinacon of yor. Honor. & Worpps. touching this Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc
