To the Right Honoble Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Right worshipfull his majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the General Quarters Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir John Barnard Knight Lord mayor of the City of London and Sir John Salter Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof bearing Date the Seventeenth day of Aprill 1738 Thomas Absalon and Anne his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street London to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant on Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray they may [...] releived against the said Warrant or Order in [...] manner as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever Pray

Phill Adams Wm Bichorton} Churchwardens

Wm Hone John Watson} Overseers
