To the Right Honourable Sir James Esdaile Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his brethren the Alderman of the sd. City

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardnes and Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint at Hackney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing Date the Twenty Seventh day of January in the year of our Lord One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Eight under the Hands and Seals of the Sir James Esdaile knight and Evan Pugh Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) Mary Green Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint Benedict otherwise Bennet Hick London to the said Parish of Saint John at Hackney' in the County of Middlesex the place of her last legal Settlement

Richard Dann against for the appellants By which said Order your Petitioner's conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Reliet in the premises is to your worship's shall seems Saints

And your Petitioner's as in Day bound shall ever Pray
