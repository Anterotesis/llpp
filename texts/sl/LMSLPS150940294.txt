To the Right Honourable Nathaniel Newnham Esqr. Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Waltham holy Cross in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Newnham Esqr. Mayor of the City of London and Evan Pugh Esqr. one of the Alderman of the said City of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing date the 12th. day of December last past John Melton was removed from and out of the Parish of saint Botolph Aldersgate in the said city of London to the said Parish of Waltham holy Cross by the name of Waltham Abbey as the place of his last legal Settlement

That your petitioners thinking them selves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or Order have therefore appealed against the same and humbly pray your

Worships to make such Order in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno. Jessopp attorney for the appellants
