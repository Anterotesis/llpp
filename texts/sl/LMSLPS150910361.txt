To the Right Honorable Samuel Plumbe Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful Justices of the Peace in their General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the second day of September instant William Groom Elizabeth his Wife and their two Children Vizt. Sarah aged thirteen Years and Hannah aged one Year and upwards were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fish Street London to the said Parish of St. Martin Ludgate in the said City of London as the Place of then last last Legal Settlement.

Your Petitioner therefore conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant And do humbly appeal against the same And pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc
