To the Rt Honble: His Majties. Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Genral Quaterly Sessions of the Peace Assembled

16/3 The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street London

Humbly Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble. Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr. Gerrard Conyers Knt. Two of His Majties: Justices of the Peace (Quorum Unus) for the said City appearing date the 16. day of March last past Joannah Rea Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Aldersgate in the said City Unto the Parish of Saint Olave Hartstreet abovesaid as the Place of the said Joannah Rea her last legall Settlement

That Your Petr. conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Doe appeale to Your Worships Against the Same and have given due Notice to the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate of this our Appeale

Wee Therefore Humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear Your Petitioners in the premises and to Have Your Worships Judgement and Determination touching the Same.

And Your Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc.
