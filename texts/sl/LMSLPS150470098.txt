To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church- wardens of the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in the said City.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order made under the hands & Seals of Sir John Williams Knt. Lord Mayor and Sr. Richd. Brocas two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th of July last Elizabeth Turner Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint Nicholas Cole Abbey in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Lawrence Jewry as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Turner

That the Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order of Removal Do appeal against the Same unto this Court and

Humbly Pray that this Court will please to appoint A Day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
