To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at the Genl. Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Clements Danes in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quox unus) bearing Date the Ninth day of January Instant Martha Tompkins was passed & removed from the Parish of St Botolph witht Aldersgate London to the Sd Parish of St Clements Danes in the County of Middx alledging the Said parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgmen of ye Sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worps. therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye sd parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate London may appear before you and hear & abide the determinacon of ye Court touching the Said Appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc.
