To the Rt. Honble Sr. John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpl the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals of Sr John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. Francis Child Knt. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of January 1739 Mary Perry Widow was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. Humbly conceiving themselves aggreived by the Warrant & Judgement of the sd two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for releif in the premisses

And Yr Petr. shall ever pray Etc
