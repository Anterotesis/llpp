To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the Rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace at their General Quarter Session assembled at Guildhall London

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Harley and Brass Crosby Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the City of London and Libertys thereof (one whereof being of the Quourm) bearing date the 25th day of May last Susanna Daniel and her Male Bastard Child Joseph Aged Two years and upwards (for Nurture) were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Luke Middlesex

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal against the same And pray that this Court will be pleased to mad such Order in the Premisses for the Relief of Your Petitioners as they shall seem meet.

J: Jenings Attorney for the Appellts:
