To the right Worppfull Sr: William Stewart Knt: Lord Mayor Sr. William Thompson Knt. Recorder and the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their genall Quarter Sessions now assembled at GuildHall in and for the Citty of London

The Humble peticon and Appeale of Ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles wth:out Cripplegate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of removall under the hands and Seales of St: William Stewart Knt. Lord Major and Sr. Gerrard Conyers Knt Two of his Majties: Justices of the peace in and for the Citty of London (Quor unus) Mary Ladwidge an Infant Aged about Three years and an halfe Daughter of Thomas Ladwidge was removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in London to the sd parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in Middx as the place of her last legall settlement her Father being aforreigner & not haveing gained any legall Settlement within this Realme and the sd Mary being born in the said parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx By which said Order your petitionr conceive themselves to be aggreieved and humbly Appeal therefrom wherefore

Your Petr: most humbly pray your worpps that they may be heard in the premisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of ye parish of St Giles wth:out Cripplegate London may attend & abide the Judgmt: and determinacon of this Court touching [...] s Appeales

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc.
