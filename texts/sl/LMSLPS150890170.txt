To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and OverSeers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of West Minister in the County of the Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Worshipfull Sir James Esdaile Knight Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Evan pay to Esquire one of the Alderman of the said City of London and two of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace nothing and for the said City of London Come whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the saith Day of February One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Eight Susannah Duttan a Lunatic was reason from the Parish of Saint Katherine Cree Church in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner's them [...] themselves aggrieved and to appeal to their Court against the names

Your Petitioner's therefore pray this Count will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and after morning of the said appeal and that all persons concerned may be taken directed to attend

And your Petitioner's shall pray Etc

Richd Ravenshaw & Gould Tomkison} Church Wardens

William Smith Geo D'Alsmaine} Over Seers of the Poor
