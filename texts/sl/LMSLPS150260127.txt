To the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Humphrys Barrt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate London.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or Ordr. dated the 22th. day of Novembr. last past under the hands and Seals of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor unus) our Mary Draper widow was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Faith under St. Paul London to yor. Peticonr. said Parish of St. Martin Ludgate as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

And for as much as yor. Peticoners said themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or Order of the sd Justices, they humbly appeal agnt. the same to this Honble Court, and pray for such releif in the prmisses, as to this Court shall seem meet Etc

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
