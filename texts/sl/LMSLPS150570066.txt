To the Right Honourable Sir Richard hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties justices of the Peace of the said City at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Richard Hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Edward Davies Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventh Day of November last past Ann Cumberlidge wife of Robert Cumberlidge the younger and their two Children namely Robert aged Twenty two Month and Richard Aged Eight Weeks were removed From the Parish of all hollows the Great London to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant Or Order and humbly appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore Humbly pray that they may be relived against the said Warrant Or Order in such manner as to. this Honourable Court shall seem meet and your Petrs. as in Duty bound shall ever pray etc.

Wm. Silley Geo. Carter} Church wardens

J Swalwell St. Roberts} Overseers
