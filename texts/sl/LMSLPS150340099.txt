To the Right Honble Sr Gerard Conyers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Right Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows in Bread Street London.

[---] That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City (Quor unus) bearing date the 21st Day of May last past One Elizabeth Morgan Wido was sent from the parish of Alhallows Barking London unto your petitioners said Parish of Alhallows Bread Street sa being the Place of her last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to Your Honours and Worps for relief therein And pray Your Honours & Worship will hear them in the Prmisses And order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Allhallows Barking to appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching this their Appeal

And they Shall ever Pray Etc
