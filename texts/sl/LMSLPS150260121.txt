To the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Humfreys Knt. & Barrt. Ld. Mayr. & the rest of the Honble Bench

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Smith

Sheweth That She was Comitted to Newgate the 20th. of this Instant Aprill on Suspicon of Stealing a Quantity of Silver Gilt Firing out of St. Pauls Church and being innocent of the fact

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that She may be either tryed this Sessions or be otherwise discharged as the Honble. Bench Shall think fitt

And She will as in all Duty bound ever Pray Etc

To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Wm. Humfreys Knt. & Barrt. Ld. Mayr. & the rest of the Honble. Bench

The humble Peticon of Eliz: Smith

Pet Beneficin Act & libtat Subdit
