To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. her Maties Justices of the Peace wth. in the same Citty

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers for the Poore of the Parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by warrant undr. the hands and Seales of Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knt. and Sr. Salathiel Lovell Knt Recordr. two of her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (quor unus) Elizabeth Hunt was past upon the sd Parish of St Bridgett als Brides from the Parish of St Sepulchers London as the place of her last legall Settlement. That yor. Petrs. conceive themselves agreived by the sd. ordr. of the sd two Justices and humbly appeale therefrom to yor. Honor. and Worpps and hope the same shall be sett aside

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray
