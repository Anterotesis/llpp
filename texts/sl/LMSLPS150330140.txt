To the Right honble the Lord Mayor and other his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London [---] led in Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Ordr. under. the hands & seales of Sr Gerard Conyers & Sr Geo: Merttins two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Citty of London dated the third day of January instant Mary Stokes the wife of John Stokes & Mary their Daughter were removed from the pish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London to the pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of their last legall settlemt.

That yr Petrs. thinke themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices & therefore due humbly appeal from the same to the Judgmt. of this honble Etc.

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps will be pleased to appoint ashort tyme for the hearing & determinacon of the sd. Appeale

And yor Petrs shall pray Etc
