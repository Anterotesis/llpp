To ye Rt: Honble Sr. Wm: Lewen Knt Ld: Mayr. of ye. City of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Sara Cloak & Mary Birt Prisonrs. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs: Stands Comitted for feloniously stealing from the person of Robt Manderly some Money of which fact they are intirely innocent

Your Petrs therfore humbly prayes this Honble Court to be tryed this sessions on to be discharged as this Honble Court shall thinck fitt

And yor: Petrs as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
