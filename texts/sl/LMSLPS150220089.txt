To the Rt Worpll Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Mary Mounthaw London.

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Taylor Wido by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quor) bearing date the Fifteenth day of March last past was removed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said Parish of St Mary Mounthaw alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Taylor

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Elizabeth Taylor never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeals to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

St Mary Mounthaw apeale agt. St Buttolph Bishopsgate
