To the Right Honble. Geo Nelson Esqr. Lord Mayr. the Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof at the Genl. Session of the peace at the Guild Hall within and for the said City and Libertys thereof Assembled

The humble petition of ye Churchwardens and Oversrs. of ye poor of the parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Order of Removal or pass Warrt. bearing Date the twenty third day of January last under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of ye peace for the said City Mary Rogers widow of Willm Rogers deceased was Removed from the parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London to the said parish of St Mary White Chapel

That Your petn. Apprehend themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrt. do hereby Appeal against the same

And Your petrs. humbly pray that this their Appeal may be adjourned Untill the next Quarter Sessn. of ye peace to be holden for the said City and Libertys in Order then and there to be heard and Determined and that all parties Concerned May be Ordd. then and there to attend to abide the Judgment of the Court upon & touching this appeal

And Your petrs. & Appellts. will pray Etc

Febry 17th. 1766 Billington Solr. for Appellts.
