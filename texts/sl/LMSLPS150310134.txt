To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt & Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl. the rest of this Maties Justices of the peace at the Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace for the Said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrt under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 19th day of May last Theophilus Drew was removed from the parish of St Bennets Pauls Wharf London to the Sd parish of St Andrew Holborn London Alledging the said Parish to be the place of [---] his last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt & Judgment of the sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray Your Honr. & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd pish of St Bennet Pauls wharf May appear before You & hear Abide the Determination of ye Court touching the Said Appeal

And they shall every pray Etc.
