To the Rt. Honoble Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of her Majties Justices of ye. Peace for the General Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hampton in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrt. Dated the Sixteenth day of Aprill Anno din 1708 under the hands and seales of Twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City of London Quor unus) One Katherine Trevor Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Vedastals Foster in London to the said Parish of Hampton in the County of Middx Alleadging the said of Parish is the place of the past legall settlemt. of the said Katherine Trevor

That yor Petr. Doubt not but to Prove That the said Katherine Trevor never had any legall Settlemt. in the said parish as Hampton in the County Middx Whereby They conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices And doe humbly appeale to yor Worpps. therefrom

Yor Petr. herefore humbly Pray yor. Worpps. will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent sessions That the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Vedast als Foster may attend yor Worpps. And hear and abide the Determinacon of yor. Worpps. touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
