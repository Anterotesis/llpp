10/1 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Stephen Coleman Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Tenth Day of January last past John Bayly a Child about five years old was removed from the parish of St Buttolph without Aldersgate London unto the said parish of St Stephen Coleman Street alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. doubt not but to prove that the said John Bayly never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Stephen Coleman Street whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your petr therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Buttolph without Aldersgate may appear before you And hear and Abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
