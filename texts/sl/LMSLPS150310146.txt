To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt & Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of this Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petitionr Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clements East Cheap London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands & Seales of two of this Majties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the first day of September last Anne Sedden with two children Margt and Anne were Removed and passed [---] by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Ethelburga London unto the Sd Parish of St Clement East cheap London Alledging the sd Parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Sd Warrt. and Judgment of the sd two Justices and doe most humbly appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye Sd. parish of St. Etheburg a London may appear before You and hear & Abide the determination of your Honour & Worps touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
