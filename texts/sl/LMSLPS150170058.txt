[...] August 1706 To the Right Honble: Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knt. Ld Mayor of the Citty of London & to the rest of the Honble: Court now sitting in the Old Bayly.

The Humble Peticon of Mathew Cates now a Prisonr. in Newgate.

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. stands Charged upon Suspition of Felony & Robbery And that yor. Peticonr. is wholly Innocent of any such Fact

Yor. peticonr. therefore humbly prays that he may be now Tryed or discharged from his said Imprisonmt.

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever Pray Etc.
