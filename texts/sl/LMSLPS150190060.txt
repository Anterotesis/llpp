To the Right Honoble Sr. Charles Duncombe Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chistlehurst for the County of Kent

Sheweth That one Robert Bamster & Hannah his wife By virtue of a Parr under the hands & seals of Two of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing date the 30th. day of August last past 1708. was Removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to the Said. Parish of Chisterhurst in the County of Kent Alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last legall settlemt of the said Robert Banister & Hannah his wife

That yor. Petr. doubt not to Prove That the said Robert Banister & Hannah his wife never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of Chistlehurst in the County of Kent whereby They Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said Two Justices And do humbly Appeal to yor. Worpps. therefrom

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor & Worpps. will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Sepulchers London To appear here before you And hear & abide the Determinacon of yor. Honor. & Worpps touching this Appeal

And yor. Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
