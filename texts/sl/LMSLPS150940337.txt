To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace assembled at the General Quarter Sessions held in and for the City of London aforesaid

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chigwell in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & seals of Sir wm. Plomer that and John Hart Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City bearing Date the 22d. day of May last past Martha the Wife of John Travells was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Master Ironmonger Lane London to the Parish of Chigwell aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal County by leave to Appeal thereto & that they may have such relief in the premisses as the nature of the care may require and the said Order set aside

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Jn. Jessop Sollr for the Parish of Chigwell
