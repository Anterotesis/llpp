To ye. Rtt. Honoble: Worspll. her Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. City of London.

The humble peticon & appeale of ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye. parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye. County of Midx.

Sheweth.

That by a warrt. under ye. hands & Seales of Sr: Char: Duncomb Ktt: Lord Majr: of ye. sd City of London & one of her Maties Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd City & Sr: Frane Child Ktt. another of her Matis Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd City (Quor unus) beareinge date ye. 10th. day of November Last past Mary ye. wife of Peter Gate wth. Mary her Younge child, who removed from the parish of St. Butolph Bishopsgate London to ye. parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch aforesd. therein alleadginge that ye. sd. Peter Gate husband of ye. sd. Mary Gate was bound an Apprentice to one George Grainger a weaver in Holloway Court in ye. sd Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch by Indenture dated ye. ord. day of Augt. 1698: & Lived therewth. his sd Master for some time afterwards.

That your Peticonr. thinke themselves agreived wth. ye. Judgmt of ye sd. Two Justices of ye. peace and humbly appeals at. ye. said Warrtt: for that your Peticon sd double not but they will make it plainly appearels this Honable. Court that ye. sd Peter Gate never Lived wth. ye. sd. George Grainger in ye. sd parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch & ipewer bound to him by Indenture was only to get a freedom & not otherwise

Your Peticonr. therefore humbly prayes your Worspr. will be pleased to grant an order that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. sd parish of St. Butolph Bishopsgate, may attend this Honable. Court this Present Sessions to heare & abide ye Judgmt. & finall determinacon of this Court touchinge this appeale

and yr. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc:

Churchwardens
