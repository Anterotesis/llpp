To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll The rest of the Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peters Cheape London

Sheweth That by virtue of A Warrant under the hands and seales of Twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Poore within the City of London One being of the Quor Dated the 31th. day of January 1707. one Elizabeth Finley Widdow was by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchers London brought to yor. Petrs. to be provided for upon the Oath of the said Elizabeth That Shee was last legally settled in the Parish of St. Peter in Cheapside London for that shee Lived Severall Yeares in the said Parish before the year of our Loid 1685. Whereas in Truth the said Elizabeth one was is as setled in yor Peticoners said Pish [---]

Wherefore yor. Petrs. humbly Pray yor Honoble. Worpps. to hear them in the prmisses And Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to Appear here before you And shew cause why They Should not provide for & maintain the said Elizabeth And alsoe to send the said Elizabeth back to the said Parish of St. Sepulchers To be by them provided for & mainteyned untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Cause of Law

And Yor Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc
