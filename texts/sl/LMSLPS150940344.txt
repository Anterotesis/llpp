To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Old Bailey in the said City assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Shaw in the County of Buks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honorable Nathaniel Newnham and Barnard Turner Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of February east part Mary the Wife of John Sidnell and her Child named Sarah aged 6 Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great within the City of London to the said Parish of Shaw as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners conceived themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present session for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of saint Bartholomew the Great and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

John Whale Churchwardens and Thos. Lovelock Overseer

Peart Est court & Miller Solicitors
