To the Right Honoble Sr. Peter Delme Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of White fryers London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order an Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quorum unus) bearing Date the fifth Day of May last past Elizabeth Barrett Wife of Daniel Barrett was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Wardrobe London to the Precinct of White fryers London aforesaid alledging the said Precinct of White fryers to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly Appeale to your Lordship & Worships therefrom And humbly pray That your Lordship & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Wardrobe London may appear before You & hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
