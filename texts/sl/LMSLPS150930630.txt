To the Right Honle. Sir William Plomer Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London, and to the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said City of London and Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the a Parish of Saint George at Hanover Square there in the Liberty of if [...] County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir William Plomer Knight and Evan Pigh Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, One being of the Quorum bearing date the Third day of May, 1782, Ann the Daughter of Roger Perryer deceased by Elizabeth his wife now Elizabeth the wife of James Jarvis aged Eleven Years and upwards was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulcher London to the [...] of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminister [...] County of Middlesex, whereby your Petitioner's think them [...] pieved and humbly Appeal to this Court [...] inst the same.

Your Petitioner's therefore humbly pray that your Worships will please to appoint same day in this present Session for the hearing and determination the [...] Appeal and that all presents [...] Appeal.

Chas. Saxon Solr. for Appellts.
