The humble Peticon of Symon Pritchard

To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richd. Hoare Knt. Lord Majr. & the rest of the Honoble. Bench

The humble Peticon of Symon Pritchard

Humbly Sheweth

That yor. Petr. was Comittee the 14th. day of March last being Charged for a felony supposed to done above Twelve Months Since, And yt. yor. petr has in Gaole ever since for the said fact this innocent of the same

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yt. he may be tryed this sessions or else be discharged

And yor Petr. will as in duly bound evr. pray Etc
