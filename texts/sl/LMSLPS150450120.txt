To the Right Honourable Sir William Billers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London, to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City & Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Colechurch in the City of London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of your Lordship & Sir Richard Hopkins Kub and Alderman Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Second Day of July instant Sarah Tipping Widow of Robert Tipping & her four Children (to wit) Mary aged ten Years, Michael aged about Six Years Elizabeth aged about four Years & Sarah aged about twelve Months, were removed from the parish of St Olave in the Old Jury London unto the Said Parish of St. Mary Colechurch London, alledging ye. Said Parish of St. Mary Colechurch to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order Do appeale to yor. Lordship & Worships therefrom, And humbly pray that yor Lordship and Worships will be pleased to hear them in the prmes and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Olave in the Old Jury London, may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of yor Lordship & Worships touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petitioners Shall pray Etc
