To the Rt. Honble Sr. James Bateman Knt & Barronett Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City Liberties thereof.

6/8 The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardn. and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Mary Delington in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of the said Sr. James Bateman the present Lord Mayor and Sr. William Withers Knt. Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City and Libties (Quorn unus) bearing date the Sixth day of August last One Daniel Rogers with Diana his wife and One Child named James about Twelve Moneths were lately removed from the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West in Fleetstreet in the said City. to the said Parish of St. Mary Dulington As the place of their last Legall Settlement As is Alleadged by the said Order.

That Your Petitioners themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant and Order And Appeale to this Court against the same.

Your Petitionrs Therefore Pray That this Court will be pleased to Appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeale And that all Parties Concerned may be Ordered to attend accordingly. And that your Peticoners may be releived therein According to Law and Justice.

And Your Peticoners As in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc.
