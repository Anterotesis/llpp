To the Right Honrble Sr. Samuel Stanier Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Madgdlen old Fish Street London

Sheweth That one Christian White aged about Twenty years by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands Seals of two his Majesties of the peace within this City (one being of the quorn) bearing Date the Third Day of July last past was removed from the parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls London unto the said Parish of St. Mary Wadgdalen old Fishs treet alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the Said Christian White never had any legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Mary Magdalen Old Fish street whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the warrant Judgment of the said two Justices And do humbly appeale to your Honr Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly pray yor Hone and worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Gregory may appeare before you And hear And abide the determination of your Hone and Worpps touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc
