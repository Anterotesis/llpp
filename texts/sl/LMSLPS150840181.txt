To the Right Honourable James Townsend Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull Justices of the Peace in their General Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Samuel Turner and William Plomer Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of February instant Ann Sleet Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West London to the said parish of Saint Martin Ludgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant and do humbly appeal against the same And pray such Relief in the premises as to your Lordship & Worships shall ever meet.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc
