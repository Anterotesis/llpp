To the Right Honble the Lord mayor & to the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace, for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the peace, at Guildhall in & for the sd City assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Clemt. Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 13th. Day of April Inst. Eliz Neat Widow. of John Neat decd. and their Child Ann aged 13 yrs. were removed from the Parish of St. Ann in Blackfryers Londn. to the aforesd. Parish of St Clemt. Danes as the place of their last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved and agt. the sd. Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of their sd. Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next general Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this City

Jno. Hampton atty for the Appillts.} And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
