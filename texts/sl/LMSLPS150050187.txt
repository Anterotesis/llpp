To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Brides London

Sheweth That yor. Petitionrs. are very much Overburthened with Poore being scituate in the Suburbs of this City And not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them Notwithstanding their Assessements are greater then ever heretofore Yett the charge of their Poore is for above their said Assessements As the same upon full examinacon hath many times Appeared to divers Aldren of this City Appointed to heare ye same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. and Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contribution of Â£50. from severall Parishes within this City for & towardes the releiveing their Poore As was last yeare given them Or such farther allowance As to yor. Honors. and Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And they shall Pray Etc.
