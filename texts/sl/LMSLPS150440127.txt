to the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and Others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands and Seales of two of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City of London one of them appointed by Act of Parliamt. bearing date the third day of Augt. last Sarah Tallant an Infant Aged Six Yeares was (upon the Oath of Elizth. Perry that Sarah Tallant the Mother of the sd. Infant did abt. fourteen Yeares Agoe live with her the sd. Elizth. Perry in the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as a yearly hired Servt. at ye Wages of four Pounds by the yeare for the Space of two yeares & upwards) removed from the Precinet of White Fryers in the City of London to the Parish of st. Clemt. Danes as the Place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Sarah Tallant the Mother of the sd. Sarah Tallant the Infant whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agreived and agt. the sd. Order do appeale.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worshipps wd. be Pleased to Appint some day this Present Sessions to hear & determine the matter touching the sd. appeale

Ro: Chimmow Sollr for the Appellants }

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray
