To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. Lord Maior of the Citty of London And to the Rt Worll. the Rest of his Maties Justices in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & overseer of the poore of the parish of St. Buttolph wth. out Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Taylor on Insant the daughter of Daniell Taylor by virtue of a warrt. under the hands & seales of twoe of his Maties. Jutices of the poore (one being of the Quox) wort brought by the Churchwardens of St. Buttolph wth. out Aldgate London to ye. Petr. To be provided for upon the oath of the said Daniell That her was never marryed to one Sarah Ellis (the mother of the said Elizabeth but only tohabited wth. her at man & wife And that the said Elizabeth & his other [...] were Bastard And that the said Elizabeth was borne in the said pish of St Buttolph Bishoppsgate whereas in tenth the said Daniell Taylor served his Apprenticeshipp in the pish of Buttolph Algate And Dwelt there wth. the said Sarah as man & wife and were [...] reputed wth. was the place of the last legall Settlement

Their humble snile unto ye. good honor & worpps is to heare them all in the Prmisses And to enough the And order made by the said twoe Justices and to find the send Elizabeth to the themselves and of the pish of St. Bottolph Algate where Daniell her father met legally Rtled untill They wm fore themselves of the charge thereof by due course of Lawe.

And ye Petrs. Shall pray Etc
