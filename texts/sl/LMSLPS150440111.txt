To the Right Honble. Jno. Barber Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipsfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin Vintry London

Sheweth That By an Order Under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the fourteenth day of December last Robert Hatchett the son of Thomas Hatchett aged about Tenn years was lately Removed from the Parish of St. Mary Mountains London to the said Parish of St. Martin Vintry as the Place of his last Legall Settlement

Your. Petrs: Therefore & thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgemt, of the Said two Justices Appeale to Yor. Lordships and Worships against the said Order and Humbly Desire This Court will Make such Order therein as to Your Lordspp & Worspps Shall seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
