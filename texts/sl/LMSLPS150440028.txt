To the Rt. Honble. John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Honble. Bench at Justices Hall in the Old Bailey.

The Humble Prayer of Elizabeth Morris.

Humbly Sheweth That Your Supplicant Elizabeth Morris was Comitted to his Maties Goal of Newgate (on the Information of Edward Powers. for buying & Receiving Severall peices of Plate knowing them to be Stoln') on the Fourteenth day of February last and has ever Since continued in Custody to the Great Detriment of your Supplicant & Poor Family.

Your Supplicant therefore most Humbly Prays that She may be Either Tryed or Discharged

And Your Supplicant (as in Duty bound shall for ever pray.

Aprill 4th. 1733

The Mark of Elizabeth [x] Morris
