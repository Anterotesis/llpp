To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and [---] Liberties thereof at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre Cree Church London.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Sir Wm. Stephenson Knight and Fredk. Bull Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberty's thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the 22d. Day of May Instant Robert Cawood was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew in Holborn London to the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church London as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving [...] themselves Aggrieved by the sd. Order of Removal or Pass Warrant do humbly Appeal agt. the same praying such Relief therein as to Your Honours shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

James Fisher Solr. for the Appellants
