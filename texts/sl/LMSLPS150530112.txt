To the Right Honrble George Heathcote Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Justices in the Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That that by Vertue of an Order or Pass Eliz Jones Widow was removed from & out of the Parish of [---] St. James Garlick Hith in London to the Parish of Lambeth as her last legal Settlemt.

Yor Petr. humble Conceive themselves Aggreived and Pray this Court to grant them Such relief in the Premisses this Honrble Court shall Seem meet

And Yor Petrs shall Pray Etc
