To the Right Worshipfull Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. Lord Mayor and other of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their Generall Sessions of the peace now holden for the sd City

The humble peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the Parish of Stepney ats [...] heath in the County of middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order bearing Date the 22d day of January last under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Francis Forbes and Sr. John Ward Knt. two of his majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor unus) Alice Miles Aged about thirteen years was removed from the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the Parish of Stepney als Stoke heath in the said Church of Middx: as the place of her last legall Settlement by which said Order your Petitionrs. conceives themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeals against the same Praying such releise in that premisses as to your worshipps shall seem Just

And yor. Peticonrs. shall ever pray Etc.
