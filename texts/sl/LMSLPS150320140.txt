To the Right Honble. Sr. John Fryer Bart. Lord Major of the City of London and to the Right Worpl. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the Said City assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 29th. day of June last Sarah Byfeild Wife of William Byfeild, was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Said parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London alledging the said parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners Conceive themselves agrieved by the Said Warrt. and Judgemt. of the said two Justices And Doe most humbly Appeal unto Your Honr. & Worps. therefrom

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly Pray your Honr. and Worps. will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of the Court touching the said Appeal.

And they Shall Ever Pray Etc.
