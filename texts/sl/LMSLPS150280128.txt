To the Right Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

31/7 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows Bread Strt London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Qurx) bearing Date the Thirty first day of July last past John Gobby and Margaret his wife with three Children John Thomas, and George Gobby were removed from the Parish of St Nicholas Olaves London unto the sd parish of Allhallows Breadstreet Alleadging the sd parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgement of the sd two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd Pish of S Nicholas Olaves may appear before You and hear and Abide the Determinacon of yr Honour & Worpps touching the sd Appeal

And yr. petrs shall ever pray Etc.
