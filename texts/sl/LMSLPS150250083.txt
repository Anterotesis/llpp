To the right honble Sr. Samuel Stainer Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London to the right worll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of ye Church Wardens & OverSeers of ye Poor of ye Parish of St. Sepulchers London

Sheweth that one Stephen Capen with Mary his wife & seven Children Vizt Anne Aged 13 years Elizabeth aged eleven Mary nine Rebecca seven Stephen five Zaccariah two ye Henry about four months old by vertue of a Warrant under the hands a seals of two of his Maties Justices of ye Peace within the City of London (one being of the Qugh) bearing Sute the one ye thirtieth Day of August now last past were removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the less London to the Parish of St. Sepulchers London alleadging the said Parish is ye place of the legal Settlement of the said stephen Gapen Ye his Wife ye seven Children

That your Peticoner doubt not to prove that the said Stephen Capen & his Wife & seven Children had no legal Setlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worships thereupon

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray your Honour & Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses & Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew ye less London to appear here before you & hear ye abide the Determination of your Honour & Worships touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc

Alex Hames Ch wardens John Habridgete Warden
