To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor, and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of F. Cokayne and Brass Crosby Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing date the Sixteenth day of September last John Barker was Removed from the Precinct of White fryers London to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the County of Middlesex as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petrs. apprehend themselves aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order of Removal And Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to your Petrs. and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City.

And your Petrs. will Pray Etc.

Wm. Burroughes & Sollr. for the Appellants.
