To The Rt. Honble. The Kings Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden fore the City of London

The humble Petition of Mary Robinson, Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That your Petitioner is detained in yr Goal of Newgate for felong expressed in yr Indictmt. depending agt. her

Yd. Petitions most humble assures this Court of her innocent & prays to be tries bailed or discharged according to the direcous of the Statute in such case made & provided

her Mary [x] Robinson mark
