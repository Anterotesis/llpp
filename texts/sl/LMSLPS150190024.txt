To the Rt: Honble Sr: Willm Withers Knt. Lord Mayrs: of the Citty of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Edward Silvester A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Peticr is now Comitted to the said prison Charged for feloniously marrying a second wife Sus first being alive

Yor. Petr: humbly prayes to be tryed This Sessions or to be bailed as the Act of parliamt. directs

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
