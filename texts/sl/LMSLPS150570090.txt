To the Right Honble Sr. Richd Hoare Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Dionis Back church. London

Sheweth That By virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of their Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of April 1746 Mary Barrett aged seven years Daughter of Jonathan Barrett by Mary his Wife both deceased was removed from the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Order of the sd Justices humbly Appeal agt. the same

And ye Petr. shall ever pray
