London ss. To the Rt. Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michaell at Quern London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr John Fryer Bart. & Sr. Wm. Thomson Knt. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (quorum unus) bearing date the 5th. day of March Anno Dni 1722 Edmond Belson Elizabeth his wife Hannah Belson Deborah Belson and Ishmale Belson their Children were removed and Conveyed from the Prish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London to the said Parish of St. Michaell at Quern in London as the last legall place of their settlement

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly think themselves aggrieved and appeale against the Order of the said Justices and pray Reliefe in the premisses of this Honble Court

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

John Cambden} Churchwardens
