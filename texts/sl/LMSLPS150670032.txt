To the Right Honble Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Commissioners of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City and for the County of Middlesex. in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Mary Jones now a Prisoner, in Newgate

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. was on the 2d. of February 1756 by Virtue of a Warrt. under the Hand and Seal of Justice Spurling Committed to Newgate for the Murder of Joslina Kidden

Your Petr. therefore most humbly Prays that She May at this Present Session be Tried, Bailed, or discharged of the Crime aforesaid according to the Form of the Statute in that Case made and provided

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc

the marke of Mary [x] Tones
