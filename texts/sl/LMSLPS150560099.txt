To the Right Honrble Sir Henry Marhsall Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Bretheen in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the sd. City assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary [...] Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order or pass warrant bearing date the first day of October 1745 under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesties of the Peace in & for the said City (one being of the Quoram) one Sarah sand ford was removed from and out of the Parish of St Bridgett otherwise saint Brides in London unto the said parish of St: Mary at Lambeth in the said County of Surry as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the sd. order or pass Warrant Humbly Appeale Against the same and pay such Releif in the premisses as to this Honrble Cort shall seem last

And Yor Petrs shall Pray Etc

Dionysias Callaghan Appellants Attorney
