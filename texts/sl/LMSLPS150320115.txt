To the right Honoble Sir John Fryer Barrtt Lord Mayor and the Worppfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace for the City of London assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Michael Cornhill Lond

Sheweth, That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace (Quox unus) for the City aforesaid bearing date the 16th day of November Anno Dm 1720 Jane Burdett Widow and Jane her Daughter were removed from the parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the parish of Saint Michael Cornhill aforesaid alledging the said parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of Robert Burdett Sen Father of Robert Burdett Jun the Husband of the said Jane Burdett the Mother,

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices and do most humbly appeale to your Lordship and Worships therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore do most humbly pray they may be heard in the premisses and that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallowes on the Wall London may appeare before you and hear and abide by the Determinacon of your Lordp and Worships touching the said appeale

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
