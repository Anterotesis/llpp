To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Alhallowes on ye wall London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. Parish is scituate were London wall And are not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive their Poore Notwithstanding their Assessements are very greate gett the charge of their Poore is above their said Assessement As the same upon full examinacon hath Appeared to divers Aldermen of this City appointed to heare the same and their Poore increaseing upon them And haveing many Allies within their Said Parish And have assessed the Inhitants to pay seaven quartrs. within the yeare past.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is that you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the contribucon of Â£12. from other Pishes wthin. this City for & towardes the releiveing their Poore as they have formerly had by reason of their greate charge & number of Poore Or such further allowance As to yor. Honors & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
