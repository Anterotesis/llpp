To the Rt. Honoble Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worpful ye. rest of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye sd. City & Libties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The hble Peticon & Appeal of ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary Cole- church, London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & ye. Liberties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Seventh Day of March, 1750 Sarah Mills, Wife of Thomas Mills, who is run away from her & their Child, Robert aged three years, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate, London, unto ye. Parish of Saint Mary Colechurch, London, Alledging ye sd. Parish of St. Mary Colechurch, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants That your Petrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye sd. Warrt. or Order of Removal; Do Appeal to yor. Lordp & Worps therefrom, and humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be Saved unto ym, & ye. hearing & Determinacon thereof be Adjourned, until ye next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace to be holden for ye. sd. City & Libties thereof, And that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate afd, may then appear before you to hear & abide ye Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor Ldp & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
