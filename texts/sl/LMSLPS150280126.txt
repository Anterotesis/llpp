To the Rt Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peac for the said City in their General quarter Sessions Assembled.

8/7 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Michael Cornhill London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the 8th Day of this instant July Ann Hawker was removed from the Parish of St Bridgett als Brides London unto the said Parish of St Michael Cornhill alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of her last legal Settlement. That your Petitionrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Therefore your Petitionrs most humbly pray your Honr & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And these the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Bridgett als Brides may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And Your Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc.
