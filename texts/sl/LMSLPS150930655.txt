London To the Right [...] and other his Mayor [...] the City of London in [...] peace in and for the [...]

The Humble [...] and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint [...] Hubbard in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Sir Watkin Lewes Mayor and William Plomer Esq. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st. day of October 1781. Abiah Blundell a Singlewoman a Lunatic was removed from the parish of Saint Nicholas Coleabby in the said City to the said parish of Saint Andrew Hubbard as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Abiah Blundell (as Alledget by the said Order pretended)

That your Petitioner's conceive themselves be aggrieved by the said Order and [---] do appeal against the same

Your Petitioner's therefore humbly pray that a short day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal

And your Petr. shall every pray Etc.
