To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said City in General Session of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the said City assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sr. Richd. Glyn Knt. Lord Mayor and F: Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberty's thereof (one of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of Novr. 1758. Mary Wells Widow of Thos. Wells deceased was Removed from the Precinct of Whitefryars London to the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
