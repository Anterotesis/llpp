To the Rt Honable Sr Thomas Rawlingson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to ye Rt Worsppfull her maties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessons Assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt Dated ye 27th day of September last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Rawlingson Mayor & Robert Bedingfeld Esqr. two of her maties Justices of the Peace for ye sd City of London (Quor Unus) That Elizabeth Glinn wife of Wm Glinn wth two Small Children were removed from the parish of St Anne Blackfryers London to the sd Pish of St Clement Danes in the sd County of Middx Alledging that the sd Pish of St Clement Danes was the place of the last Legall Settlemt. of the sd William Glinn husband of the Sd Elizabeth Glinn & father of the Sd Two Children

That yor Peticonr conceive themselves Aggreived by the Judgmts files Two Justices & humbly appeal to ye worspps there from And humbly pray that your worspps will be pleased be appointe a day this prsent Sessons that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the sd parish of St Anne Blackfryers may attend ye worspps & hear and abide the Determinacon of your worspps touching this appeal

And as no duty bound will pray Etc
