To ye Rt. Honble: Sr John Houblon, Knt Lord Major To ye Rt Worspll Sr S Lovill Knt Recorder } of ye Citty of London

Most Humbly Sheweth The humble Peticon of Charles Hardinge A Prisonr in Newgate

That yor Lordsp Peticonr was Comitted by ye Recordr for Straling from a pson unknowne 30s. wch yor Peticonr is all togeather Innocent off

Therefore in all humilty most Humbly Prayesh that this Honble. Benches would bee Pleased To Try yor Peticonr & ye his Prayer may be Entered.

And her Shall Humbly Pray.
