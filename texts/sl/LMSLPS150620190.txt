To the Right Honble and Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removed under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Francis Cockayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr Robert Ladbroke Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fifteenth day of June last past Elizabeth Wetherington Wife of Edward Wetherington who is gone from her and their two Children William aged Eighteen Months and John aged three Weeks were removal from the Parish of Alhallows the Great London to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Wm: Croft Agent for the Appellts Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
