To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George the Martyr in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Richard Outram, Jane his Wife and their three Children Sarah aged five Years, Jane aged Three Years and Ann aged nine Months, were by order or pass Warrant dated the 18th day of April last under the hands & Seals of the Right Honble. Fredk. Bull Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and J. Kirkman Esqr. and Alderman of the said City two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties there of one being of the Quorum, remov'd & Conveyd from & out of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate without London to the said Parish of St George the Martyr in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement

Thos. Wade Junr. Atty. for the Appellts Your Petrs. & Appellts. conceive themselves Aggrieved there by humbly Appeal to the Judgment of this Honble. Court against the Same and pray that this Honble. Court will be pleased to hear and Determine the sd. Appeal and that all persons con- cern'd may abide the Judgment Determination thereof and that Your Petitioners may be relieved in the premises as this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And Yor. Petitrs. shall ever Pray Etc
