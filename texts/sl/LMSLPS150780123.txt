To the Right Honble Sir Robert Kite Kt. Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull Aldermen his Bretheren in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the City of London Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London One being of the Quorum bearing date the fourth day of May 1767 Thomas Eldridge and Grace his wife were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Lothbury London unto the said Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or pass Warrant Appeal against the same And pray such Relief in the premisses as your Lordship and the Court shall think fit

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Constable Sollicitor for the Appellants.
