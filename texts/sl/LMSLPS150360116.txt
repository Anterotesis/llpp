To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace of the City of London in their Genll Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by an Order of Removall under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble. Sir George Mertins Knt. Lord Mayor and Sir Peter Delme Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of May last past Mary Philpott Wido. of Peter Philpott deceased was removed from the Parish of St Bottolphs without Aldersgate London to the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the County of Middlx as the place of the last legall Settlement

That your Peter apprehending them- selves agrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court agt the same

And therefore Pray this Court will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Bottolphs Aldersgate London and all other Parties concerned may attend this Court some time this Prsent Genl. Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the finall Determination and arbitrament of this Court touching this Appeal

And your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
