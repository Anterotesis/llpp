To the Rt: Honrble Sr G [...] Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and apepale of Richard Hopes

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order made by two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quor bearing Date the 20th Day of February last made upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London Your Petitioner is charged to be the Father of a Female Bastard Child born of the Body of one Mary Johnson and by Virtue of the said Order your Petitioner is also charged with the Payment of Two Shillings and six pence Weekely to the said Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides for and towards the maintenance of the said Female Bastard Child for so long time as the said Child shall continue a charge to the said Parish and Your Petitioner conceiving himself aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices doth humbly Appeale to your Honor. [...] Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Honr. & Worpps will be pleased to [...] them in the Prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish may appeal [...] and hear and abide the determination of [...] Honr. and Worpps herein

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray Etc.
