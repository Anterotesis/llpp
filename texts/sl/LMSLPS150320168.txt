To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Jno. Fryer Bart. Ld Mayr of ye City of London & the rest of the Honble Bench Now Sitting at Justice hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Prayer of Eliz Reeves in the Newgate

Sheweth That yor. petr. was Comd. to this place the 19th Augs. last and was with Jno. Cooper tryed in Augt Sessions for Misprison of treason & was Acquitted

That yor. petrs. Expecting her liberty on Such Acquittall was informd yt there was a Bill of Indictmt. for High treason agt her & ye sd. Cooper of wch fact Sheir Inocent

Therefore prays that yor Lordsp & ye Honbl. Bench will Consider her long Imprisonmt. & give Such Direccons that she may have her tryall this Sessions or be Otherwise Discharged as to yor Lordsps Shall Seem meet

And ye petrs as in duly bound Shall ever pray Etc.
