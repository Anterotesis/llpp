London To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace in their General Session of the peace at Guildhall in and for the City of London Assembled-

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Marylebone in the County of Middlesex-

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honorable John Burnell Esquire Lord Mayor of the said City and Brass Crosby Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of February One thousand Seven hundred and Eighty eight, Phoebe Dell the Wife of William Dell who had deserted her and her Child John aged about nine Months were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Bride in the said City of London unto the said Parish of Saint Mary lebone as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Phoebe and her said Child

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do therefore appeal to Your Worships therefrom And humbly pray that your Worships will hear them in the promises touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Thos. Martin Solr. for the Appellants No. 3 Newman Street
