To the Rt: Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Apepale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Olaves in the Old Jewry London

Sheweth That one John Biss with Elizabeth his wife and Joseph his Child by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 31st. Day of January last past was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Parish of St Olaves in the old Jewry alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the Said John Biss

That your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said John Biss never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Olaves in the old Jewry whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs humbly Pray your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the sd Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of Your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.

St Olaves Jewry agt St. Giles Cripplegate} Appeale
