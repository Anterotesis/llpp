To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the sand City at Guildhall Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mildred the Virgin in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Settlement or Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir William Stephenson Knight Lord Mayor of the said City and Sir John Cartwright Knight one of the Alderman of the said City and two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said City bearing date the fifth day of January 1765. A Woman and Child by the Name of Jane Eccles Singlewoman and her female Bastard Child Named Jane were removed from, the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate London to the said Parish of Saint Mildred as the Place of their last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive the themselves to be aggreived.

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court against the said order of the said Justices and pray this Court to make such order therein as to them shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall and pray Etc.

James Green. Wm. Raddish} Church-Wardens

Wm. Edmond Jn. Peake.} Overseers of the Poor
