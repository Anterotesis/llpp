To the Rt. Honble: Sir Samuel Fludyer Knt. & Bart. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Worshipful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. said City & ye. Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of Christchurch, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seals of Sr. Matthew Blakiston, Knt. late Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & Francis Cokayne, Esqr, one of ye. Aldermen, & Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. said City & ye. Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twentieth Day of October 1761. Elizabeth Garrett, Singlewoman, was Removed from the Parish of St. Faith, London, unto ye. Parish of Christchurch London aforesaid, Alledging the said Parish of Christchurch to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Attorney for the Appellants } That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the sd. two Justices, Do Appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom, And humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, and that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye. said Parish of St. Faith, may appear before you, to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Lordship and Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.
