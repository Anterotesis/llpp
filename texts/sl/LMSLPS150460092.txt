To the Right Honorable Sr Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said City of London Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Vedast other- -wise Foster in Foster Lane London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant Under the hands and seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the Said City out being of the Quorum Dated the 5th of September last Sarah the Wife of William Clarke and her Child Sarah aged thirteen Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Pauls London to the said Parish of Saint Vedast otherwise Foster in Foster Lane London as the Place of their last Legall settlement whereby Your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Order or Pass Warrant

Therefore Your said Petitioners humbly Appeale to your Lordship and Worships in the promisses and humbly pray such Releife therein as to Your Lordship and Worships shall seem most meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Brian Marriott Churchwarden
