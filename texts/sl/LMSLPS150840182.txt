To the Right Honble The Lord Mayor and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable William Nash Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Thomas Hall [...] Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of August One Thousand and Seven hundred and Seventy two William Thompson was Removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft, London to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieves by the said Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

S Coverty for the Appellants.
