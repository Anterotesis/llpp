To the Right honourable the Lord Mayor Aldermen and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their general Session of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlsex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the hand and Seal of I Hirkman Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London Ann Cane was [---] as a Rogue and Vagabond from the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West to the parish of Saint Martin in the Fields whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal against the said Order

Your Petrs. therefore Pray that they may be heard on then Appeal against the said Order

And your Petr. shall pray Etc

Wm. Burroughs Sollr. for the Appllts.
