To the Rt. Honble Sr. Peter. Delme Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Rt. Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary in the City of Chester

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City (Quorn unus) bearing date the 18th day of July last past One Eleanor Ball the Wife of Samuel Ball with her Six Children (vizt) Wm 14 years old Robert 12 years Old Charles Nine Years Old Judith Six Years Sarah 4 Years & John One Year & an half Old or thereabouts were sent from the Parish of St Dunstans in the West London unto Your Petrs said Parish of St Mary in the City of Chester

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the sd two Justices humbly Appeal to your Honours and Worps for relief therein And pray Your Honour & Worps will hear them in the prmisses and Order the Churchward- ens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Dunstans in the West to Appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this their Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
