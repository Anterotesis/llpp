To the Rt. Honrble Sr Gerrard Conyers Knt Lord Major of the City of London and also to the Rt Worpll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the parish of St Margarett Moses London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order bearing date the Eleventh day of December last past Jonathan Roberts with Katherine his wife and their four Children vizt Mary aged Thirteen years, Katherine aged Eleven years, William Eight years and Samuel Seven Years were removed from the parish of St Matthew Fryday Street London unto the said Parish of St Margarett Moses as the place of their last legall Settlemt:

That your petitioners conceiving them selves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or order Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom and pray that your Honr and Worpps [---] will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Matthew Fryday Street may appear before you at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps Humbuly this Appeal-

And your Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
