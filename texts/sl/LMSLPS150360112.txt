London ss To the right Honrble Sr. George Mertins Knight Lord Major of the Citty of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace of the sd Citty in their Genall Quarter Sessions now assembled

The Humble peticon and Appeale of the on Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of the Holy Trinity in the Minories

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Sr George Mertins Knt: Lord Major and Sr Gerrard Conyers Knt: Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quor unus) bearing date the Seaven and Twentieth day of Aprill last Hester Merrill Singlewooman & Lunatick was removed from the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London to the sd parish of the Holy Trinity in the Minories as the place of her last legall Settlement By which said Order your petrs humbly conceive themselves to be aggreived & Humbly Appeale therefrom and therefore

Your Peticoners pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate and all other Persons concerned may attend this Honrble Court and to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the same Court touching the matters herein conteined

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
