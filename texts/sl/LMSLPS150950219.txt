To the Right Honourable Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne Within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seals of Brass Crosby & John Hart Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof One being of the Quorum bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of December last Daniel Dupont & Sarah his Wife were Removed & Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Anne (in the said Order described by the Name of the Parish of [---] Anne Soho in the County of Middlesex) as the Place of their last Legal Settlement whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that your Worships will please to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Jno Allison Geo: Js: Soward} Charch Wardens

W Yarnold John Elcock} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
