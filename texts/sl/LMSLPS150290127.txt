To the Right Honblo Sr Willm Lewen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace a (their Genl. Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Polition and Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Vedast als Foster London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City bearing Date the Fifteenthday of April Instr Thomas Sutton with Frances his Wife and there two children were removed from Trinity parish to the Sd pish of St Vedast ats Foster alledging the sd pish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom

You Petitioners therefore most humbles pray you Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prenmisses and order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of Trinity parish may appear before Your Honour and Worps and hear and abide I Determin [...] of yr Honr & Worps touching ps Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc
