To the Rt. Honoble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humbel Petition and Appeal of the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Elizabeth Morgan Singlewoman was by an Order or Pass Warrant dated the 28th: day of May last 1757 under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honoble Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Robert Alsop Esqr. Alderman of the said City Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum, Removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holbron above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Humpy: Elmes Atty. for Appelts. Your Petrs. and appelts. think themselves aggrieved thereby Humbly appeal to the Judgment of this Honoble Court against the same & pray that this Honoble Court will be pleased to hear and determine the said appeal and that all Persons concerncerned may abide the Judgment and determination thereof and that your said Petrs. may be Relieved as this Honoble Court shall seem meet

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
