To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Newnham Mayor and John Hart Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof she being of the Quorum bearing Date the 25th day of August last John Aged Seven years and upwards and Ann Aged four years and upwards the two Children of John Harris by Elizabeth his Wife both Deceased were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the sd City, of London to the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex as the last legal Settlement of the said John and Ann

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved by the Order and Adjudication of the said two Justices Do hereby humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court from the same and pray that such relief may be now had in the premises as shall be thought meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

by Jno. Skull Sollr. for the said Parish of St Mary White Chapel
