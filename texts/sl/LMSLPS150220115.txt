To the Rt Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worppll Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General [...] Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Andrew Hubbard London

Sheweth That one Lucy Gough Widdow by Virtue [...] Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace with this City (one being of the Quoru) bearing date the One and Thirtieth day of August last was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Undershaft unto the parish of St Andrew Hubbard London alleadging the said parish to be the last legall Settlement of the said Lucy Gough

That your Petitioner [...] but to prove that the said Lucy Gough never had any legall Settlement [...] said Parish of St Andrew Hubbard whereby they conceive themselves [...] ved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do likely appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioner herefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps [...] to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Andrew Undershaft may appear before your and [...] abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray

St Andrew Hubbard agt St Andrew Undershaft} appeal
