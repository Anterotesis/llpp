To the Right Honorable Samuel Turner Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Turner Lord Mayor of the City of London and Robert Alsop Esqr. bearing date the twenty fifth day of August One thousand Seven hundred, and Sixty nine Five of her Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof Margaret Roberts Wife of James Roberts who is therein alledged to have gone from her was removed from and Out of the parish of Saint Mary Alderman London into the Parish of saint James Clerkenwell as the place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved, and Humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships that a Day may be appointed for the Hearing and determining the said Appeal and that your Worship would be pleased to make such order in the premisses as to your Worship shall seen meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray W. Johnson Solr. for the Appelr.
