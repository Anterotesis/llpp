To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of the Worshipfall Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guildhall in & for the sd. City Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That [...] under the hands & Seals of Sir Robt. Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor & Thos. Rawlinson Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 25th. Day of January last Thomas Perry Ann his Wife & their three Children Elizabeth aged Ten Years Sarah aged Eight Years & Mary aged Six months were removed from the Parish of St. Augustin London to the Parish of St. Clement Danes aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be again and agt. the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the sd. Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next Session of the Peace to be holden for this City

Wm. Chimmon Agt. for the Appeall.

And your Petrs. shall pray etc.
