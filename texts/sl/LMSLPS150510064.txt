To the right honourable Sr. John Salter Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at the General Quarter Sessions Assembled at Guild hall London

The humble Petiton & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order or Pass Warrant bearing date the thirtieth day of June last under the hands & Seals of the Sr. John Salter Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & Sr. Edward Bellamy Knight & Alderman of the said City of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace of the same City (one being of the Quorum) William Smith aged one year was removed from the parish of St. Bethnall at Pauls Wharf London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London Adjudgeing the said Parish of St. Sepulchres to be the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Smith

That your Petitioners Conceiveing themselves aggreived by the said Warrant & Judgement of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this honourable Court therefrom

Thos. Eyles Hen: Snagg} Church Wardens

Benj. Allen Thomas How} Overseers of the Poor of the abovesaid parish of St. Sepulchrs.
