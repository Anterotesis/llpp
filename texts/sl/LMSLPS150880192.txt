To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the County of London

Sheweth That by Virtue or an Order of Removal bearing Date the first Day of November One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Six under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) William Hughes and Mary his Wife were removed from the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said City of London to the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the said City the said Justices thereby Adjudging the last legal place of Settlement of the said William Hughes to be in the said parish of Saint Olave Hart Street

That your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order and the Judgment of the said Justices and Humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Pray that your Lordship and your Worships would be Pleased to hear this their Appeal and that the said Order of Removal and Judgment of the said Justices may be Quashed and that your Petitioners may be further and otherwise Relieved in the about the Premisses as the Nature of the Case may require

And your petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
