London To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor the Worshipful the Aldermen and other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and Liberties thereof At the General Quarter Session of the Peace Guildhall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Certain Pass Warrant Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honorable Thomas Hallifax Mayor of the said City and William Lee Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of February last past whereby Elizabeth Glee the Widow of Daniel Glee deceased was removed from and out of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft in the City of London unto the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Glee

Your Petitioners conceiving them selves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to this Honorable Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premise as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
