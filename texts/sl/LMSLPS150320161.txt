To the Rt. Honrble Sr. John Fryer Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That one Edward Baker being an Inhabitant in the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate and of Sufficient ability to maintain his wife and Family notwithstanding which the said Edward Baker doth neglect and refuse to provide for and maintain his wife insomuch that she is now become chargeable the said parish your Petrs being of ten forced to releive her otherwise She must have been reduced to the greatest want

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Yor Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and make such order that the said Edward Baker may be obliged to maintain his said wife so that your Petrs may be discharged from any further charge in releiving the said Edward Bakers wife

And they shall ever Pray Etc
