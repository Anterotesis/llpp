To the Right Honourable Sir Thomas Halifax Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the next of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said City of London in General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Helen in the City of London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Fredk. Bull and Thos. Wooldridge Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties whereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of April Instant Nathaniel Smith was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Helen as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Nathaniel Smith.

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said order and therefore Appeal against the same And pray relief in the premises and that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them And the hearing and Determination thereof be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Richd. Pepys Josh. Potter} Church Wardens

Jno. Fasson Wm. Portal} Overseers
