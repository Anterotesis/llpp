1691 To the Right Honoble. Sr. Thomas Pilkington Knt Lord Major of the City of London And to the Worll. their Maties Justices of the peace wth. in the same City

The humble peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Gabriell Fanchurch London

Sheweth That Thomas Arden & Elizabeth Arden twoe children of one Grace Arden deced by warrt. under the hands & seales of twoe of their Maties Justices of the peace in the County of Kent were brought from the pish of Deptford in the same County to yor. Petrs. to be provided for Then parents formerly divelling in the said parish of St Gabriell Fanchurch since which tyme [---] Grace Ardon the mother of the said children dwelt for the space of in the said parish of deptford in or publike and open mountie keeping or shopp for selling Chandlery warrt. next the streete And lodying of souldiers brought to her by the Constables And not in any private manner to comeale herselfe

Yor. petr. humbly pray by way of appeale To be heard in the prmines And that you wilbe pleased to order the over Seers of the poore of Deptford to come & appeare to this Comle to then can sewhy They should not provide for the said children according to Lane

And They shall pray Etc

Pet St. Gabr Fanchurch
