To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Genll. Quarter Sessions of the Peace at [...] Guild Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition Appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Daniel Lambert Esgr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing Date the Third day of Decr. last James Hurrst and Sarah her wife were removed from the Parish of St. Ann within Aldersgate London to the said Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the Places of their last legal settlemd. whereby your Petrs. think themselves agriesed and Appeal to this Court agt. the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to appl. a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may attend at the same time

And your Petrs will ever Pray Etc

Chas. Gresham Agent for St. Paul Covt. Gardn.
