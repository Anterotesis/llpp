To the right honourable the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace of Guildhall in and for the said City assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Eltham in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Sir Thomas Rawlinson Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London one of whom is of the Quorum bearing Date the first Day of October instant Elizabeth Scofield widow of Edward Scofield deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London to the said Parish of Eltham in Kent as the Place of her last legal Settlement by which Order your Petitioners apprehand themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this honorable Court and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Peter le Poor London and all Parties concerned may attend the said Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the said Court touching the said appeal And that your Petitioners may have such Relief in the Promises as to the said Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
