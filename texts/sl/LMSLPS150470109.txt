To the Right Honble Sr John Williams Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the Said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of May last whereby Anne the Widow of Wm Goodrich was removed from the Parish of Alhallow Barkin London to the Parish of Alhallow on the Wall London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief.

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
