To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Lamnot Stanier knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Other the Rt. Worshipfull his Matise Justice of the peace Assembled at their General Quarter Sessions held in and for the said City.

1714 29/6 The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Saviour Southward in the County of Jurry.

Gumery Sheweth That Anne Camber was by Order of two of his Mejties Justices of the peace of the said City be a ring date the 29th day of June last removed from the parish of Christ Church in the said City to the said parish of St. Savione Southwark as the place of her last legal Settlement

That Yor. Peticoners can make it Appear to this Honbl. Court the place of the last legal Settlement is not in the said Parish of St. Savior Southwark.

Your. Peticoners therefore do humbly Appeal against the said Order and pray that this Honble. Court will Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor the said parish of Chirst Church London to Attend this Court to Shew Cause why the said order should not be vacated.

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
