23/5 To the right Honrble. Sr. Peter Delme Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to the right Worpfull the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace [---] Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chobeham in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by [---] Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sr Peter Delme Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. George Merttins Knt and Alderman of the same City two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the same City (Quorum unus) bearing date the 23d day of May last Eleanor Ball the Wife of Saml Ball with their Six Children vizt Wm aged fourteen years Robt Aged twelve years Charles aged Eight Years Judith aged six Years Sara Aged three years & John Six Months old or there about were removed from the parish of [...] St. Dunstans in the west London unto the sd Parish of Chobham in the County of Surry as the place of the last legal Settlement

That Your Petrs conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Order of the Sd. two Justices humbly Appeal agt the same praying such relief in the Premisses as to your Worps shall seem Just

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
