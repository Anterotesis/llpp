To the Rt Honble: Sr. John Houblon Knt: Lord Major of the Citty of London and other the Justices now Sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

The humble Peticon of Thomas Atkinson

Sheweth That yer. Peticonr. was Comitted to the Goals of Newgate by Warrant from the Recordr: Dated 20th. of August last Charged for high treason in Cliping and Counterfeiting the Currant Corine and is Hill remaining in Goals on the said Warrt.

Humbly prayeth to bee tryed this Sessions for the fort menconed in the said Warrt. or that he may bee bailed according to the Act of year Hamt. in that Casemade

And he shall ever pray
