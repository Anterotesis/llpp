To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City in general Session of the Peace at Guild hall Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of John Sawbridge Esquire Mayor and John Wilkes Esquire two of his Majestys for the City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum bearing Date the twenty first day of November last Martha Hook the Wife of Richard Hook who hath deserted her was removed and conveyed from the Parish or precinct of Saint Ann Black Fryars in the Ward of Farringdon within in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster (in the said called and described by the Name of the Parish of Saint Anne who in the County of Middlesex) as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will please to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Potitrs. shall pray Etc

Joseph. Pelley Wm Simpson Still Wilson Thos Howard Thos Dobson Jas Marshall} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne Westmr.
