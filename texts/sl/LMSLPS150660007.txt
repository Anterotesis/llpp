To The Right Honble The Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London in the General Quarter Session of the Peace for the same City assembled.

The humble Petition of John Seal

Sheweth That Your Petitioner on the 18th. Day of September last was convicted before The Right Honble The Lord Mayor and Sir John Barnard Knt. and Alderman of selling and delivering a Ticket belonging to an Under taking in the Nature of a Lottery contrary to the Statute made in the 6th Year of His present Majesty and adjudged to prays the Sum of Â£200, and to be imprisoned for the Space of Twelve Months according to the Directions of the same Act.

Your Petitioner most humbly apprehends himself to be aggrieved by the sd. Conviction and appeals to this Honble Court and pay such Relief in the Premisses as to the Honble Court shall seem meet

John Seal
