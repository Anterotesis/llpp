To the Right Honourable and Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and Robert Alsop Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberty's thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 10th. Day of November one thousand seven hundred and fifty Margaret Selwood Wife of John Selwood was removed from the Parish of Saint James in Dukes Place London to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived and against the said Order do Appeal.

W: Croft Agent for the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
