Appeale St. Augustin agt. St Bartholomew the Great

To the Rt. Honoble. Sr Samuel Garrard Baronett Ld Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petitton and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Augustin London

Sheweth That one Mary Feild Spinster by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Sixth day of May last Past was sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London unto the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Augustin London Alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of ye sd Mary Feild

That Your Petr. doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Feild never had any lagall Settlement in the said Parish of St Augustin whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Honr. and Worrpl therefrom.

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly pray your Honr. & Worppl. will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great doe appeare before you and heare & abide the Determination of your Honr. and Worppl touching this appeale

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray Etc
