To the Right Honourable Marshe Dickinson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Honourable Bench in their General Session of Goal Delivery Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Catslick a Prisoner.

Sheweth That on the Fourth Day of November Last Your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the Poultry Compter London on a Strong Suspicion of Feloniously Stealing Twenty Pounds Weight of Sugar the Property of the Commissioners of Prizes and on the Eighth Day of the same Month of November last a Detainer was Lodged in the said Prison of Poultry Compter against your Petitioner with having Feloniously Stolen out of a Lighter on the River Thames a Parcell of Sugar the Value of Five Shillings the Property of the Commissioner of Prizes and now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate under the same Charges

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays That at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate Holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex, He may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided.

And Your Petitioner shall pray Etc

John Catsnick
