1700 To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richard Levett Knt Lord Major of the Citty of London And to the Rt. Worll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens: Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Peter the Bonds in the County of Midd

Sheweth

That by virtue of a warrant under the Hands Seales of Twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the Citty of London Dated the 27th. day of March 1700 One Thomas Purling aged (about 11 yeares) was by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Olaves hart streete in London put to yor. Petrs to be provided for whereas neither of the said Justices whoe made and Syned the invent the [...] [...] are of the Quor According to the Statute

Their humble write is to heard them in the Prmessed And to send the said Thomas purting to the Churchwarden of St. Olave hart Streete To be provided for & conteyned

And They shall pray Etc

Appeal of St. Peters the Bonds

May 1750
