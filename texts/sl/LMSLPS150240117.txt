To the Rt. Honble Sr. Richd. Hoare Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and the wst of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd City of [...]

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Mary White Chappelle in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by virtue of an order of Patt Warrt. bearing date the Sixth day of August but past under the Hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City (Quor unus) one William Green & Margaret his wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Olave in Hartstreet in this City to yor. Peticonr pd. Parish of St. Mary White Chappele wch. was adjudged by the said Justices to be the place of their last legal Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Ordr. or Putt Warrts. Judgmt. of the sd. Justices

Wherefore yrs. Peticonrs. humbly Appeale agnt. the same to this County and pray to hure [...] releif therein as to yor. Lopp & Wopp [...] shall seem just & reasonable

And yor. Peticonrs. shall prayer Etc

The Peticon & appeale of the Church wardens Etc of St. Mary white Chappell agat. St Olave Hart Street, touching He maintenane of Wm. Gran & us

Appeale to be allowed P consent
