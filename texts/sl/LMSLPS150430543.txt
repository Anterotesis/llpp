To the Rt. Honble Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London aand to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Mary Woollnorth London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 16th Day of August last Christiana Sapp a Lunatick was removed from the parish of St. Olave Hart Street London to the Parish of St. Mary Woollnorth London as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitionrs conceiveing themselves agreived by the Warrt. & Judgement of the sd two Justices humbly appeal to this Court for releif in the premisses.

And yor Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc
