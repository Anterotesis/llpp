To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richard Levett Knt. Lord Maior of the Citty of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Michaell Wood Streete London

Sheweth That one Mary Nicholson wife of Micheall Nicholson (whoe is Lately absconded by virtue of a warrant under Twoe Justices hands and Seales one being of the Quox dated 14th. June 1700 was sent with woe Children by the Churchwardens of St. John Zachary to yor. Petrs. To be provided for on the Oath of the said Mary That her husbands last legall Settlement was in the said parish of St. Michalls whereas the said Michaell Nichollson after his department out of St. Michaells parish dwelt in a house in the said Parish of St. John Zachary att Â£10. yearly Rent and paid there divers Tayes and, Dutyes to the said Parish

Yor. Petr. humble request is To heave them on their Appeale And to send the said Mary Nichollson with her Children to the Churchwardens of St. John Zachary where the said Michaell Nicholson was last setled an Inhabitant According to the Lane

And They Shall Pray Etc
