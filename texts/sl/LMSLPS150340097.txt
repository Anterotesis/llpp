To the Right Honble Sr. Gerrard Conyers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for ye Sd City at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Olaves Hartstreet London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Qour unus) bearing Date the 7th. day of May 1723 Joseph Barton with Elizabeth his wife and Mary their Child were removed and passed by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London [---] to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Olaves Hartstreet London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honour and Worships there from

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye sd Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate may Appear before You & hear & abide the determination of this Court touching ye sd Appeal

And he Shall ever Pray Etc
