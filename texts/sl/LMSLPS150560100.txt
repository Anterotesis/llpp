To the Right Honourable Henry Marshall Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majestie Justices of the Peace in Generall Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury London

Shew. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor and George Arnold Esquire two of his Majesties of the peace for the City of London bearing Date the Eleventh day of January Instant John Thompson Cool his Wife and their two Children Sarah aged Eight Years and John aged one Year were Removed from the parish of Saint Butolph Bishopgate London to the said parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury London as the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and Humbly Appeal againt the same

And your Petrs. shall ever pray & Etc

Philip Hilson James Dymock } Churchwardens

Jas. Mount Tho Plestow Jnor: Burnes Somerset Phillips Overssers
