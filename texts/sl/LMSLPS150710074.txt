To the Rt. Honble Sr. Thomas Chitty Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble Sr. Thos. Chitty Knt. Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of Febry last Mary Yardley wife of Thomas Yardley who was gone from her & their two Children Sarah aged four years & Mary aged two years were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. vedastalis Foster London to thesd. Parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of their last legal Settlemt.

Your Peter think themselves Aggrieved by ye sd. Order or Pass Warrt. of the sd two Justices of the Peace Appeale to this Court agt. the same and humbly pray your Worships to hear and Determine the same

And Yor. Peter shall ever pray

Edwd Wren Thos Davis} Churchwardens

Peter Newland Chas Hanmershey} Overseers of the Poor
