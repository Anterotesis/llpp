To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richard Levett Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace In The Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Georges Southwark

Sheweth

That one Jacob Harris with his wife and three Children by vitue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the peace one being of the Quox dated 7th. August 1700 was by the Churchwardens of the parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate London brought to yor. Petr. on the Oath of the said Jacob Harris That his last legall Settlement was in the said parish of St. Georges where hee Lived in the House of one Medralfe Whereas in truth the said Jacob Harris was neve legally Settled in the said parish of St. Georges

Yor. Petrs. therefore pray That yor Honour and worpps. would Order said Churchwardens of St. Cyles Cripplegate to Appeare before you Abd to Heare them in the prmisses And to Send the said Jacob Harris with his wife and Children to the Churchwardens of Sr. Gyles Cripplegate To be provided for untill The Cann Free themselves of the Charge thereof by die Course of Lane.

And They Shall Pray Etc.
