11/1732 To the Right Honble. John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Thomas Apostles London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under The Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorum unus) bearing Date the 30th. Day of November last past Anne Watson Single woman was removed from the Parish of St Anthony vulgarly St. Antholin to the Parish of St. Thomas Apostles London as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving them selves aggrieved by the Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for Relief in the Premisses.

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
