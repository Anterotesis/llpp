To the Right Honourable Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majestys. Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Staining London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of your Lordship & Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Two of his Majestie's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (One being of he Quorum) bearing date the Twenty third day of August last Anne Diggle Widow of Samuel Diggle deceased, was Removed from the Parish of Saint Bridget Vulgarly Saint Brides London, unto the said Parish of Saint Mary Staining London, Alledging the said Parish of Saint Mary Staining, to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order, Do Appeale to Your Lordship and Worships therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Bridget Vulgarly St. Brides may Appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Lordship and Worships touching this Appeale

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
