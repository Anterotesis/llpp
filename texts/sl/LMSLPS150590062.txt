To the Right Honble Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knt. Lord mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City at their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble petition and appeal of the Church Warden and Overseer of the poor of the Hamlet of popler and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Robert Ladbroke and Sr. Robert Alsop Knts. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of January last past Whereby Mary Harper Widow of Benjamin Harper was removed from the parish of Saint Chr Church in the City of London aforesaid to the said Hamlet of popler and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex at the place of her last legal Settlement

By Which said Warrant or Order your petitioner conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet.

And your petitionrs. at in Duty Bound will pray

Jno Brown Church Warden

Richard Smith} Overseer of the Poor
