To the Right Honble: Micajah Perry Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Honble. Bench at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Prayer of Jane Shaw

Sheweth That your Supplicant was committd: a prisoner to Newgate the Seventeenth of November last, and Indicted for Felony last Sessions But her Triall being put off by the prosecutor she was ordered to Remain in Custody

Your Supplicant therefore humbly prays that she may take her Friall this Sesions and not be kept in comfine- ment any longer

And your Supplicant as in duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc

Jane Shaw

17th. of January 1738
