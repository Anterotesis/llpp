February 1697/8 February

To the Honourble. Bench

The humble Pettition of Ann Symonds most humbly

Sheweth That your Lordships Pititioner have been committed to prison upon Suspition of picking a pockett: but knowing her self very Innocent of ye fact and being a very poor woman having two young children which are ready to starue: do humbly begg yt your Lordships will order yt she may be cryed or left upon Bayl

and your Lordships Pettr. will ever Pray Etc.
