To the Right Worship full his Majties: Justices of the peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof as their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden for the Sd: City

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseer of the poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the parish of St Dunstan Stepney als Stebunheath in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order bearing Date the Sixth day of November last under the hands & Seals of Sr Gerard Conyers Knt: Lord Mayor & Sr William Stewart Knt. two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof (Quor unus) Mary Syer Wife of Joseph Syer & Ann their Child about five years Old were removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the Said Hamlett of Bethnall Green as the place of their last legall Settlement; by which Said order your Peticoners conceive themselves Aggrieved & humbly appeals against the same praying Such relief in the premisses as to your Worships Judgment shall seem just.
