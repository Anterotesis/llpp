To the Rt Honrble Sr. Peter Delme Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

22/11 The humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorn Unus) bearing date the 22d Day of November last past Richard Clever with Dorcas his wife were removed from the parish of St Mary Staining [...] London unto the Parish of St Giles Cripplegate-London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And pray that your Hour. & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Mary Staining may appear at a certain Day to hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
