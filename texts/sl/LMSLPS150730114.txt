To the Right Honble. Sr. Samuel Fludyer Knt. & Baronet Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ealing in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Samuel Fludyer Knt. & Baronet Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th day of March last Jane Ward Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St Brides London to the said Parish of Ealing as the place of her last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrievedly the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the Peace, Appeale to this Honble. Court against the same and humbly Pray that your Worships will be pleas'd to appoint a day for the hearing and Determining the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Millward John Clarke} Church- Wardens

Richard West Tho: Berrows} Overseers of the Poor
