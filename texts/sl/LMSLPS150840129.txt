To the Rt. Honble the Lord Mayor & Aldermen of the City of London in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary at Islington in the County of Middx

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Willm. Nash Esqr. Mayor and William Stephenson Esqr. Alderman Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof One being of the Quorums bearing Date the 30th. Day of Octr. last Sophia North Wife of Edward North a Prisoner for Debt in the Poultry Compter and their Six Children to wit Thomas Aged Nine Years Edward Aged Eight Years Henry Aged Seven Years John Aged Six Years Sarah Aged Three Years and Abraham Aged Ten Months were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Mary at Islington as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Yor. Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeal therefrom

Yor. Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships that the Benefit of their Appeal may be saved to them & a Day may be Appointed in the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the hearing & Determing of their said Appeal and to make such Order in the Premes as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And Yor. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Wade Junr. Soll for the Appellts.
