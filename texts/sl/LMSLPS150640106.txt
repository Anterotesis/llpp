To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Deborah Curtis Wife of Robert Curtis (said to be runaway from her) and their Two Children Mary aged three Years and Elizabeth (then) aged five Weeks were by an Order of Pass Warrant dated the Thirty first day of May last under the hands and Seals of the Right Honoble Sr. Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and John Porter Esqr. Alderman of the said City Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) Removed & conveyed from the Parish of Saint Peter near Pauls Wharf London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the Said County of Middlesex as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your. Petrs. and Appellants think themselves aggrieved thereby Humbly Pray that this Honourable Court Will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may Abide the Judgement and determination thereof And that your said Petrs, may be Relieved as this Honourable Court shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
