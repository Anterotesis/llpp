To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order of Removall bearing dated the 12th. day of April last under the hands & Seals of the Right Honble. Robert Willimott Lord Mayor of the City of London and Henry Marshall Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London one being of the Quorum John Stevens Sarah his Wife and their three Children Mary aged nine Years Samuel aged five Years & Sarah aged one Year were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Parish of St. Mary Abchurch London as the Place of their last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners find themselves agreived by ye Order or Warrant of the said two Justices and humble appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that you will be Pleased to order the Church Wardens & overseers of the sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate and all other Persons Concerned to attend this Court this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court Touching the Premises and that your Petitioners may have such Releif therein as to Justice and Law doth appertain

And your Petitioner shall ever pray etc

Philip Evans Matw Perchard} Church Wardens

Henry Buckle John Austin} Overseers
