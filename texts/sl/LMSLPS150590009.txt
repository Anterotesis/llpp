City of London to wit} To the Right Honourable Sr. Robt. Ladbrooke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City

The humble Petition and Appeal of Robt. Gordon one of the Inhabitants of this Parish of Saint Bartholomow the less in the said City of London

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner is by a Rate or assessment made and Confirmed for the Maintenance of the Poor of the Parish aforesaid from the 19th of July to the 19th of Octo last upon the Inhabitants of the said Parish Unequally Unjustly, and Illegally charged in the said Rate

Yours Petitioner therefore Most humbly Appeals to this Honble Court and Prays to be Relieved in the premesses And Your Petitionr shall ever Pray Etc.

Robt Gordon
