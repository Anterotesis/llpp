To the Right Honble Sr George Merttins Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their General Quarter Sessions of ye peace for ye sd City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Olave Hartstreet London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd City (Quor unus) bearing Date the 18th day of December last 1724 Rachael Rotchdale and Joseph Rotchdale two children of William Rotchdale were removed & passed from the parish of Allhallows Staining London unto the parish of St. Olaves hart street London alledging the sd parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt and Judgment of the sd two Justices do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Warships therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish of Allhallows Staining may Appear before you & hear & abide the determinacon of the Court touching the sd Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
