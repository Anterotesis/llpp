To the Right Worspfull his Majeties: Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions held for the City of London

The humble Peticon of and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and seals of Sr: Peter Delme Knt: Lord Mayor: and Sr. George Mertins Knt: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor unus) bearing Date the sixth day of August last David Dunkin and Margtt: his Wife were removed from the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London to our said Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx as to the place of their Last Legal Settlemt.

That yr: Petr: think themselves Agrieved by the said Order and humbly Appeal to this Honble Court agst: ye: same

And pray that yr: Worpps would be pleas'd to give them such relief in the prmises as to yr: Worspps shall seem meet

And yrs: Petr: shall ever pray Etc
