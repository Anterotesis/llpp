To the Rt Honoble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Majestys Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish Hampton in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That One Hannah Warwick Singlewoman by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace within this City one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Eighth Day of July last past was removed from the parishes St Peter West cheap in the City of London unto the said parish of Hampton alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Warwick

That your Petitioners doubt not hut to prove that the said Hannah Warwick never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of Hampton whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Peter west cheap may Appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
