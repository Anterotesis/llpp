To the Right Honoble The Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull Justices of the City of London Assembled at the Quarter Session Held at Guild hall London the 18th. Day of Octr. 1756.

The Humble of Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Nicholas in the City of Gloucester

Sheweth That by an Order made by the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor and [...] the Worshipfull Francis Cokayne Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the City of London for the Removall of Elizabeth Stone Wido. of Joseph Stone Deceased from the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to the Said Parish of St. Nicholas in the sd. City of Gloucester

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrived by the sd. order Do Humbly appeal to this Honoble Court therefrom and Humbly Pray the Same May be Quashed
