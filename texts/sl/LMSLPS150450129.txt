To the Right Honble: Sir Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London, and to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennett Gracechurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir Richard Brocas Knt. & Alderman Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London & the Liberties there of (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of November last past Elizabeth the Wife of Thomas Lewis was removed from the Parish of St. Mary at Hill London unto the Said Parish of St.Bennet Gracechurch London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Bennet Grace- -church to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Peticonrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order Do appeale to yor. Lordship & Worships thereform And humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Mary at Hill may appear before You to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of Yor Lordship & Worships touching this Appeale.

And Your Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
