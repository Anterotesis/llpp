To the Right Honble. The Lord Mayor and the Worshipful the Alderman His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Parish Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ann Blackfryers London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble John Blackford Esqr: Lord Mayor and Edward Ironside Esqr: and Alderman two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of July last Mary Noulton the Wife of thomas Noulton who had run away from her was removed from the Parish of Christchurch on the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Ann Blackfryers London as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of her said Husband

That your Petitioners humbly thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices Appeal to your Lordship and Worships against the said order And Pray you will determine the same and make such Order therein as to your Lordship and Worship shall Seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
