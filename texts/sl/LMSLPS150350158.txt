To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their Genal Quarter Sessions now held for the said City

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St George on Botolph Lane London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr Peter Delme Knt: and Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr George Merttin Knt:, two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor unus) bearing Date the 28th. day of July last Katherine Stoner the Widdo: of Samuel Stoner deceas'd with their five Children (vizt:) Mary aged Eight Years,Sarah Six Years Samuel Three Years Francis two Years, and Katherine about Six months were pass'd and Conveyed from the Parish of St Andrew by the Wardrobe in the sd: City of London unto the said Parish of St George in Botolph Lane as the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticonrs: conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the sd: two Justices, do therefore humbly Appeal against the Same praying Such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgment shall seem just
