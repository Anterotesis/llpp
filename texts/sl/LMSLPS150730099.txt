To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled at Guild- -Hall London

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knight & Sr. John Cartwright Knight two of His Majestys of the peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 6th. day of October 1762. Ann LLoyd a Child aged four years and upwards a Bastard was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate London to the said Parish of Saint Luke Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal against the same And Pray that this Court will be Pleased to make such Order in the Premisses for the releif of Your Petitioners as they shall deem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
