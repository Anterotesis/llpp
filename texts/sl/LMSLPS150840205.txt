To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London, and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London.

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal bearing date the 12th. day of June last under the hands and Seals of the Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir William Stephenson Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum whereby Mary Tansler Wife of Robert Tansler gone from her and their Child John Robert Aged 7 Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Robert Tansler, Mary his Wife and their said Child.

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the sd Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

James Fisher Solr. for the Appellants.
