Middx to witt} To the Rt. Honble Sr. Crisp Gascoyne Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London

The Humble Petition of Wm. Meredith

Sheweth That Your Honr. Humble Petr. prays this Honble Court that he may be either Bailed Tryed or Discharged this Present Sessions, he humbly Apprehending the Law Intitles him to such favour, as Lyeing in Prison Ever since the 27th. of June last and no Indictment agt. him in this County or Elsewhere

And Yr. Petr. as in Duty Bound shall Ever Pray

William Meredith
