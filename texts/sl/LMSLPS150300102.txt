To the Right Worpfull his Majts Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Guildhall

26/3 The humble Petition and appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Sr. William Thomson Knt. Recorder and Sr William Withers Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said Citty of London and Liberty thereof (Quor un) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of March last Sarah Cotty with Four Childrens Vizt. Dennis Fourteen Mary nine Isaac four years and Sarah Eleaven Months old were removed from the Parish of St. Anne in Blackfryers to your Petrs. said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields whereby your Petrs. humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

Wherefore your Petrs. humbly [...] Appeals to your Worships against the said Order and pray your Worships to appoint a day to hear and determine the same.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

James Rainge Phill: Davis} Church Wardens
