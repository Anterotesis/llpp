To the Right Honble: Micajah Perry Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpfull the rest of His Majsty's Justices of the Peace for the Sd. City & the Liberties there of in their Genll. Quarter Session Assembled:

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Catherine Creechurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Sir Thomas Lombe Knt. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twentyeth Day of December last past Mary Eastman Sherrin, was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch London aforesaid, alledging the Said Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch to be Place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order, Do therefore Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worps therefrom, And [...] humbly pray that your Lordp & Worps will [...] pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & that [...] Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you, to hear and abide ye Judgment and Determinacon of yor Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And Your Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
