To the Rt: Honble: Sr: John Houblon Knt: Majr: of the Citty of London and other the Justices sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baly

The Humble Peticon of Mary Randall

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr: was Comitted to Woodstreet Counter by Warrant bearing date the Sixth day of this instant January Charged with Suspicon of High Treason in Cliping the Currt: Coine of this Kingdome and is new remaining a Prisoner in the said Counter

Yor: Peticonr: humbly prayeth that she may bee Removed into the County Goale and that she may bee tryed this prsent: Sessions or that she may bee admitted to Baile according as the Law in that case made Provideth And that her prayer may bee entred in this Court

And she shall ever pray Etc.
