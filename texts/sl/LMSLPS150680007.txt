To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices in their General Session of goal delivery of Newgate holden for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Elliot

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the 2st day of December last was Committed to his said Majestys goal of New Gate by John Fielding Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex being Charged on the Oath of one William King for feleniously and Burglariously breaking and Entering the Dwelling House of the said William King in the night time in the City of London and Feloniously taking and Carrying away about Twenty three Pounds in Gold three Bank Notes Value Seventy Pounds one metal Watch two Gold Rings and other things the Property of the Said William King agt. the Peace Etc.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City he may be either Tried Bailed or discharged for the felony and Burglary aforesaid Pursuant to the Statute in such base made and Provided.

James Elliot
