To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Richard Brocas Knt . Lord Mayor of the City of London and to rest of the Knt. Worppll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City.

The Humble Peticonr and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of the Knt. Honble. Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and St. Edward Bellaing Knt. Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor Unus) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of Aprill Anno Dom 1730. John Ellis and Eleanor his Wife were Removed from the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London to the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate As the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices and Humbly Appeale against the same And therefore most Humbly pray 6 be releived in the prmisses As to this Honourable Court shall seem most meet

And your Peticoners As in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc

Sam Grisold Matthew Viws} Churchwardens

Christo Nicholls Edward Tarmer} Overseers
