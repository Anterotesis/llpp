To the Right Honble. Sr John William Knight Mayor of the City of London And, the Right Worfull the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of poor of [...] parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or a pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertyes thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Nineteenth day of December last past Edmund Pettyt & Mary his Wife were removed from the Parish of St Gyles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London as the Place of their last by Settlement

Yor Petrs humbly Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justify humbly Appeal to this Court Against the same & pray to be heard thereupon

And Yor Petr shall Etc.
