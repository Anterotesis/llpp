To ye: Rt: Honble Sr: Robt Beachcroft Knt Lord Majr. of the City of London & to the rest of ye: Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of John Wigley A Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. is Charg'd for stealing a Box & some wearing Apparell of wch. fact he is innocent

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prayes to be tryee this Sessions or to be admittee to Baile

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
