To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor and Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster

Sheweth That by virtue of an order Pass Warrant bearing date the Seventh day of September One Thousand Seven hundred and eighty two, under the Hands and Seals of the At Honble Sir. William Plomer Knight Lord Mayor and one of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the City of London, and Sir. Thos. Hallifax thought Alderman and one of His Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) Mary Whippell Singlewoman, was Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Margaretin the City of Wesminister as the Place of her last legal Settlement, whereby you Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray That the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining the same pray be Adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace

Peace to be holden in and for the said City of London, and that all Parties Concerned May be then and there Ordered to attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray. Etc

Richd: Snow T: Bray} Church Wardens of St. Margaret Westmr.

John White G. Hier} Overseers of the Poor of sd. Parish
