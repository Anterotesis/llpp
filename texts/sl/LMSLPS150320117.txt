To the Rt. Honoble Sr. John Fryer Baront. Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the Worpfull the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City & the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary At hill London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London & the Liberties thereof (Quox unus) bearing Date the fifth Day of December last past Christian Blake Singlewoman, was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London unto the Said Parish of St. Mary At hill London alledging the Said Parish of St. Mary At hill to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves Agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do humbly appeale to yor Lord Ship & Worships therefrom, & pray That your Lord Ship and Worships will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses, And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of yor Lord Ship & Worships touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
