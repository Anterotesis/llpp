To the Rt. Honoble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Martins in the Feilds in ye County of Middx

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Nuttall wife of Wm. Nuttall by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing date the sixth day of Aprill instant was removed from the Parish of St Bartholomew the Less London unto the said Parish of St Martins in the Feilds in the County of Middx alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said William Nuttal

That your Petitionrs doubt not but no prove that the said Elizabeth Nuttall never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Martin in the Feilds whereby they concive themselves aggreived by the said Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St Bartholomew the less London may appeare before You and hear and abide the determination of your Honr & worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

St Martins in ye Felds Middx agt St Bartholomew the less} appeale
