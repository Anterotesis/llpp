To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by as Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble: Geo: Nelson Esqr: Lord Mayor, and F: Cokayne Esqr. one of the Alderman of the said City two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City and the Liberties thereof (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of November 1765, Mary Penny Wife of Henry Penny who is a Sailor abroad in his Majestys Service was Removed from your said Parish of St. Dunstan London aforesaid to our said Parish of Saint James as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to hear the said Appeal and to make such Judgment and Determination therein as to the said Court shall seem meet.

Thos: Thomas Solr. for Appellts.
