To the Rt. Honoble the Ld. Mayor & others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye City of Londn. Assembled in Genll. Quarter Session at Guild Hall in & for the sd. City

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of the Rt. Honoble the Ld. Mayor & Sr. Thos. Lombe Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of Londn. (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of Decr. last Gabriel Ball & Frances his wife were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Foster Lane Londn. to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order doe Appeale

R Chimmow Agent for St. Clemt. Danes}

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be [...] Appointed this Present Session to hear & determine the matter touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
