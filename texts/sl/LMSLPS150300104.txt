To the Rt. Honrble Sr. John Ward Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City in their General quarter Sessions Assembled

28/3 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of the Holy Trinity London

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the hands seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing date the Eight Twentieth Day of March last past Mary Tweedale wido with three Children Samuel, John and Martha were removed from the parish of St Mary Somersett London unto the said parish of the Holy Trinity alleadging the said parish to be the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your petrs Doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Tweedal hath not any legall settlement in their said Parish whereby they conceive themselves agrieved by the said warrant or order of the said two Justices and so humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petr therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish [...] of St Mary Somersett may Appear before you and hour and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
