To the Right Honble Sr Willm Lewen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipful the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Bridget als Brides London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quex) bearing Date the Eighteenth day of June last past Elizabeth Rivers was removed from the parish of St Andrew Holborn London unto the parish of St Brides London Alledging the sd Parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive them Selves aggrieves by the Said Warrt and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour and Worps. therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour and Worps Will be pleased to hear them in the promisses and that the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of ye parish of St Andrews Holborn may Appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of Your Honr and Worps touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petr shall ever pray Etc
