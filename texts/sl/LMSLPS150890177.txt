To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Over Seers of the Door of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (Whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth day of December 1777 Mary Nutcher the wife of Francis Nutcher. who hath deserted her, was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew in Holborn in the said City of London to the said Parish Saint Stephen in Walbrook as the last legal Place of her Settlement By which your Petitioner's conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Your Petitioner's therefore pray your worhsips will be pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this appeal and give them such Relief on the premisses as to your Worship shall seem First and meet

And Your Petitioner's shall ever Pray Etc

Wabb & Woods Agents for appellts.
