To the Right Honoble. Sr. John Fleete Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Right Worll. their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & OverSeers of the Poore of the Pish of Cleworth in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands & Seales of twoe of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of this City one Elizabeth the wife of Wm. Deighton and their Child were sent & brought by the Churchwardens of the Parish of St. Andrews undershaft to be provided for by yor. Peticoners upon the Oath of Edwd. Tilliard that the said Wm Deighton being now Souldier was an Appntice in the said Towne of Cleworth whereas in tenth the said Wm. Deighton suit he was att Clenworth was a hired servant to with Jno. Munt a Taylor dwelling in the said Pish of St. Andrews Undershaft wth. Whome he continued for a years wth. ended in October 1688 And was in tenth the place of his last legall settlement.

Their humble into to yor. good honor. & Worps. is that you will be pleased to order the Churchwardens of the Pish of St. Andrews Undershaft to Appeare before you & hear them in the Prmisses and to Quash the said Order and send the said Eliz: Deighton & her Child to the Pish of St. Andrewes under that to be pvided according to Law.

And they shall Pray Etc.
