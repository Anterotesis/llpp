To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the [---] Middle Parish in the Town of Guildford in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox unus) bearing Date the 29th day of September 1721 a female Bastard Child named Mary aged about Eleven Weeks was Soul & delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Stephen Coleman Street London to the said [...] Middle parish in the Town of Guildford Alledging the sd parish to be the place of its last Legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs conceive themselves agrieved by the Sd Warrt & Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Yr petrs therefore most humbly pray Yr Honour & Worps will be pleased to Save em the Benefit of this Appeal & Adjourn it till the next Sessions & Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd parish of St Stephen Coleman street London may then appear & hear & abide the determinacon of this Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray
