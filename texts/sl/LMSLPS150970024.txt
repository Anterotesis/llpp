London ss. To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Worshipful the Recorder and Aldermen in the General Quarter Session of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George by Saint Pauls in the said City

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Thomas Wright Lord Mayor and Sir William Plmer Knight Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties, bearing Date the Fifth Day of January instant Mariah Hill Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre without Newgate in the said City unto the said Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul's as the last Place of her legal Settlement, whereby you Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly beg leave to appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that this Honourable Court will Proceed to hear and determine Their said Appeal and make such Order therein at shall be just

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
