To the Right Honorable Sir William Stephenson the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Session of Peace in & for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Pass Warrants or order under the Hands and Seals of William Bridgen Esqr. late Lord Mayor and Sir Francis Gosling[---] Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London one being of the Quorum bearing date the 5th. day of November 1764 Abraham Vandome & Susanna his Wife &James Collins her Son and his Apprentice were removed from and out of the parish of Saint Stephen Coleman Street London to the said Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the warrant or order of the saw two Justices of the peace Do therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
