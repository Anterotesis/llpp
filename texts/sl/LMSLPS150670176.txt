To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael at Queen Hithe London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty ninth Day of October in the year of our Lord One Thousand Seven hundred and fifty five A Child baptized by the Name of Mary aged about Two years was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopsgate London aforesaid into the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queen Hithe upon the Oath of Ann Ashlaham

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Do Humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and Pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lord ship and and Worships shall seem most meet

John Fortrie, Ch: Wardn.
