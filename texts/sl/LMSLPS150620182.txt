To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr. and Sir Robert Ladbrooke Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth day of August last past Charles Tooley and Katherine his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Bridget vulgarly Saint Brides London to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the place of their last legal Settlement

The Petr. think themselves aggreived by the said Order of Removal and humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same & Pray such Relief therein as to Your Worships shall seem meet.

And the Appellants will Pray Etc.

G. Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
