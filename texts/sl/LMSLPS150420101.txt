29/1 To the right honourable Humphry Parsons Esq Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the right worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition & Appeall of the Churchwardens & Oversseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Orgars London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Jusstices of the Peace within this City (Quorum unus) bearing date ye 29 of January 1730 One Martha Strond widdow was sent & removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London unto the said Parish of Saint Martin Orgars alledging the said Parish of Saint Martin Orgars to be the place of her last legall Settlement

Wherefore your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices aforesaid Do humbly Appeal to your Lordship & Worships therein and humbly pray they may be head in the Premises and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Sepulchres may appear before you to hear the Judgment & determination of your Lordship & Wroship touching this appeal and to abide by the order of this Court therein.
