To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Fellows the Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberties of London within Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That Whereas by an Order of removeal under the Hands & Seals of Daniel Lambert Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London & St: Robert Baylis Knight one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & Liberties thereof Dated the 20th: Day of August 1741 Henry Harrison Jane his Wife & their three Children Elizabeth Henry & Ann were removed from the Parish of St: Bottolph without Aldgate in the City of London to our said Parish of St: Olave Southwark as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby we think ourselves aggrieved

Your Peticoners therefore most Humbly Pray this Honble Court will grant an [...] Order for all Persons herein concerned to appear at such Time & Place as the Honble Court shall think fitt to ap [...] in order to the hearing this appeal

And Your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc

Christ Smith} Hamilton Peters} Churchwardens

Sam: Marner} Overseers
