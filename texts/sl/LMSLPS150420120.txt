To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarters Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeales of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St [---] Katherine Coleman in Elenchurch Street London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libertye, (Quor unus) Bearing date the seventh day of Aprill Instant John Thomas & Mary his Wife was lately removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London to the said Parish of St. Katherine Coleman London as the Place of their last Legall Settlement

Your Peticoners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeale to your Lordship & Worships against the said Order & humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc.

Wm: Weaving Wm: Williams Churchw
