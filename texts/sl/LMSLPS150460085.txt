To the Right Honoble Sr Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayors of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of White Fryers London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant Under the hands and sealed of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London (One whereof being of the Quorum) dated the first of this Instant January Thomas Howell Barbara his Wife and Three Children (to wit) Elizabeth aged tenn years Jeremiah Bright aged Seven Years and John aged One year and three Months were removed from the parish of St Gregory by St. Paul London to the said Precinct of White Fryes London as the place of their last Legall Settlement

Wherefore Your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices There humbly Appeale to this Houble Court for beleife therein And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Gregory by St Paul London may abide by such order as Your Lordship and Worships shall make on the said Appeale

And Your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall over Pray Etc.

Willm Howard Nath: Brown
