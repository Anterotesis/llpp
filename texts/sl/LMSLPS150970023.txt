To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of I. Wright Esqr. Mayor and Wm. Plomer Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the 30th. day of December last Alexander Vicary was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Vedast London to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the Reaing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City.

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas: Dyson

Solicitor for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westminster.
