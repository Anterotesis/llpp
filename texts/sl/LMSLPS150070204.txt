To the Rt: Honble: Sr: John Houblon Knt: Lord Majr: of the Citty of London and other his Matie: Justices sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

The Humble Peticon of Mary Done

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. was Comitted to the Goale of Newgate by his Grace the Duke of Shrewsbury Warrt: dated the 30th: of May last Charged for High Treason in harbouring and conccerling John Counter als Rumney accused of being engaged in a designe to assassinate his Maties: pson. That yor: Peticonr: is bigg with Child and hath not above Flower teen daies to Rock on

Humbly prayeth that she may bee tryed [...] this Sessions or that the may bee barled according to an Act of parliamt. in that case made and that this her prayer may bee Recorded

And Sh: shall ever pray

Sir John Houblon was Ld. mayor in 1695
