To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Wallbrook in the City of London.

Sheweth: That by Virtue of a Pass Order under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of Peace for the said City whereof one is of the Quorum bearing Date the 20th day of December In the Year of Our Lord 1751. Rachael Ellis widow was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said City of London to the said Parish of St. Stephen Wallbrook at the last legal Place of her Settlement by which Your Petrs. conceive themselves Aggreived & Appeal to this Court agt. the said Pass Order.

Your Petitioners therefore Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to hear & Determine the Matters touching this Appeal & give them Such Releif in the Premes as to Your Worships shall Seem meet.

And your Petrs. will Ever Pray Etc

Wm. Ivatt of Walbrook London Agent for the Appellants.
