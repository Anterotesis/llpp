To the Rt. Honble: Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worpful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship & Francis Cokayne Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof. (one being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date the twenty Sixth Day of February last past, Mary Cate Single- woman, was Removed from the Parish of St. Brides, London, unto the Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, London, alledging the said Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

Chas. Baynbridge Atty for the Appellants } That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Promisses, and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Brides, London, may appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.
