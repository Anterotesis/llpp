To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble; Nathaniel Newnham Esquire Lord Mayor and Thomas Sainsbury Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberties, one being of the Quorum, bearing date the 22d. day of January last Jane Slepe Singlewoman was Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Katharine Coleman in the said City to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of the last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order, and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determination the said Appeal

And your Petrs. will pray Etc

Herbt. Robertson Solr. for ye Appellts.
