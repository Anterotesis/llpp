To the Right Honoble Sr. Francis Forbes Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Less London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Tenth Day of March last past Sarah Peel aged Eight years and George Peel seven years old or thereabout two Children of Luke Peel & Sarah his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Less London aforesaid alledging the said Parish of St. Bartholomew to be the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly appeale to your Lordship & Worships therefrom & humbly pray that your Lordship & Worships will be pleased to hear them concerning the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of your Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
