To the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr. Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of Saint Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Frances Cokayne Esquire Mayor and Thomas Rawlinson Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of January last past Mary Lee Wife of Samuel Lee and Mary their Child aged two year were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Precinct of Saint Catherine in the County aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore considering themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do Appeal to your Lordship and this Honourable Court therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Grant them such relief therein as to your Lordship and this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc
