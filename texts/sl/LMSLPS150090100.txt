To the Right Honable: and Worppll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the Citty of London

The humble peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Midlx:

Sheweth That Elizabeth Harwood wife of Ralph Harwood wth. Two Children were by a Warrants under the hands and Seales of the Right Honable: Sr: Humpfry Edwin Knt: Lord Majr: of the Said City of London and one of his Maties: Justices of the peace for ye. Said City and Sr: Thomas Stamp Knt: another of his Maties of the peace for the said City (Quoru unus) passed from the parish of St: Mary Alderman- -bury in the said City unto the Said parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch alledginge that she and her Said husband were Last Legally selled there they haveinge Lived there about Fifeteen Months

Yor: peticoners thinkinge themselves agreived the Judgmt: of the Said Two Justices; for that [...] Said Ralph Harwood nor his Said wife have Lived in the said parish of St: Leonard [...] for the Space of Fourteen yeares Last [...] upwards as will appeare to this [...] upon their Severall Oathes Hum [...] this Honable: Court and hum [...] Churchwardens and Overseeres [...] the Said parish of St: Mary [...] may be Ordered to attend this day this present Sessions, to [...] the Finall Judgmt. and determine [...] Honable; Court touching this appeal [...]

And Yor. paticonrs: shall ever pray Etc
