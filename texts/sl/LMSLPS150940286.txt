To the Right Honourable Edward Newnham Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful his Majesty's other Justices of the peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof

The humble petition of the Overseers of the poor of of the Townships of Aston Juxta Budworth in the parish of Great Budworth in the County of Chester on behalf of themselves and the other Inhabitants of the said Townships

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and John Boydell Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and Liberties of William Moore Margaret his Wife and their Five Children namely George aged Thirteen Years and upwards Harriott aged Eleven Years and upwards Mary aged Six Years and upwards William aged Two Years and upwards and Elizabeth aged Three Months were removed from the parish of Saint Andrews Holborn London to the said Township of Aston as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your petitioners Conceive that the place of the last [---] legal Settlement of the said William Moore his Wife and Five Children is not in the said Township of Aston And therefore beg lease to appeal and do hereby appeal to the said Order And humbly pray the they may have such Relief in the premises as to your Lordship and his Majesty's said other Justices may seem meet and according to Law

And your petitioners will ever pray Etc
