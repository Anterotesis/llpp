To the Rt. honble St. Charles Pears Knt. Lt. Mayor of the City of London and others the Rt: Worshpll. his majty's Justices of the peace of the said City at their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace held for the said City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Rotherhith in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Samuel Lynn and Mary his wife were since the [---] helding of the last Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace for the City of London removed by an order under the hands and seals of Two of his Majty's Justices of the peace of the said City from the parish of St. Sepulcher in the said City of London to the [---] parish of Rotherhith aforesaid as she place of their last legall settlement.

That Yor. peticonrs. can make it appear to this Honoble Court that the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Samuel Lynn and Mary Lynn is not at the said Parish of Rotherhith.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore do humbly appeal agt. the said order and play that this Honble Court will order the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchers Londonss attend this Court to shew Cause Why the Said Order should not be Vac [...]

And yor peticonrs Shall ever pray
