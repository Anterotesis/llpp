To the Right honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London the worshipful the Aldermen and other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City in their general Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Petition and Annual of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman street in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right honourable Sir Thomas Chitty Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Francis Cokayme Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the third Day of April Instant Edmund Sheppard Elizabeth his wife and their four children John aged Eleven Years William aged nine Years Edmund aged three Years and Elizabeth aged seven Months were removed from the Parish of saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said City of London to the said Parish of saint Stephen in Coleman Street as their legal settlement

That your Petitioners humbly thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices appeal to your Lordship and worship against the said Order and pray that you will be pleased to determine the same and make such Order there on as to your Lordship and worships shall seen meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
