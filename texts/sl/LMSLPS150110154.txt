To the Rt Honoble. Sr. Richard Levett Knt Lord Major And to the Rt. Worll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Lawrence Pountney London

Sheweth

That yor Peticonrs. have been formerly Charged with the payment of £4. to the releife of the Poore of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London And their said Parish of St. Lawrence being but a Small parish And haveing a greate number of Poore to maniteyne And their Assessements being much increased above what They were formerly And having not Lands nor Annuityes to Defray their Charges

Their humble Suite unto yor. good honour and Worpps. To discharge them for the future from the payment of £4. To the Paid parish of St Buttolph without Algate for the Reasons aforesaid.

And yor Petrs. Shall Pray Etc
