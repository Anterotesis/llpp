London ss To the Rt Honble Sr. Geo: Thorold Bart Lord Mayr. And the rest of the Honble Bench Now Sitting at Justice hall in the Old Baily

The humble prayer of David Wilford in Newgate.

Humbly Sheweth That yor petr. was Comd to this place the 25th Day of Augt last for picking Mr Charles Townsends pockett of a pockett book of wch fact He is Inocent

yr pr therefore prays that he may tryed this Sessions or Discharged as to yor Lordsp. Shall Seem Meet

And yor Petr. as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc.
