To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Pish of St. Gyles wthout. Cripplegt. London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very much overburthened wth. Poore being scituate in the Suburbs to this City And not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them their Parish being soe greate and their Poore soe many Notwithstanding their Assessement is greater then ever heretofore Yett the charge of their Poore is far above their said Assessemt. As the same upon full Examination hath many times Appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to heare the same And their Poroe dailey enfreaseing upon them.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is that you wilbe favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contribution of Â£90. from severall Pishes wthin. this City for & towardes the releiveing their Poore As was last yeare given to them Or such further allowance As to yor. Honors. & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And they shall Pray Etc.
