To the Right Honble Sir Edward Bellamy Knight Mayor of the City of the City of London And to the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish [---] of Saint Catherine Cree Church London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fourth day of July last Elizabeth the Wife of John Walker was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London To the said Parish of Catherine Cree Church London as the Plan of her last legall Settlement

Yor Petrs conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same
