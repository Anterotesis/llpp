To the Rt: Honble: Sr: Jno: Houblon Knt: Lord Major: of the Citty of London & other his Maties: Justices now sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

Sheweth The Humble Peticon of William and Mary Pinchard

That yor: Peticonrs: were comitted to the Goale of Newgate the 26th: day of Aprill last by Warrt: from Sr: Thomas Lane Knt: thereby Charged for Cliping the Currant Coine of the Kingdome and yor: peticonrs: are Still Remaining in the said Goale

They humbly pray that they may bee tryed this pesent Sessions or that they may bee bailed according as the Law in that case directs

And they shall ever pray Etc
