To the Rt. Honoble. Sr: William Withers Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Wortt. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Buttolph wthout Aldergate London

Sheweth That One Elizth. Kircum By virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & seales of Two of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing date the 5th. day of August last past 1708 wat Removed from the Parish of The Holy Trinity London to the said Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate London Alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last legall settlement of The said Elizth Kircum

That yor Petr. doubt not to prove That the said Elizth. Kircum never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Buttolph without Aldersgate London whereby They Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the Said two Justices And do humbly appeale to yor Worpps. there from

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray yor Honor. and Worpps will be pleased to bear them in the prmisses And Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Trinity To appear here before you And hear and abide the determinacon of yor. Honor. & Worpps. touching this Appeal

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc
