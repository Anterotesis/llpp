To the Rt. Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le Bow within the said City.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or ordr. dated the Twenty fifth day of January last past under the Hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (Quor unus) George Jackson Elizabeth his wife with George Elizabeth James & Mary their Children were adjudge to be legally settled in Yor. Peticonrs. said Parish of St. Mary le Bow, and the Sd. George Jackson was by vertue thereof brought & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to yor. Petionrs. said Parish of St. Mary le Bow to be pvided for according to Law,

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves agrieved by the Said Warrt. or ordr. of the Sd. Two Justices & humbly appeale agat. the Same to this Honble Court paying to have such relief in the prmisses at to yor. Wopps shall appear just & reasonable,

And yor Peticonrs shall pray Etc
