To Right Honorable the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guildhall of the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne Within the Liberty of Westminster in the Country of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seals of Robert Peckham Esqr. Lord Mayor and John Boydell Esqr. one other of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof bearing date the third day of February last Elizabeth the Widow of Thomas Lewis with her four Children namely Sarah Aged fourteen Years John aged Twelve years Elizabeth aged Ten years & Thomas aged Eight years & upwards were Removed and Conveyed from & out of the Parish of Alhallows in Honey Lane London to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Lewis whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices & their said Order and Pray that your Worships will please to hear & determine the same according to Law

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Jno. Allison Geo: Js: Soward} Church Wardens

John Elcock William Thomas} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
