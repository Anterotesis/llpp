To the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lad Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of ye Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace, at their General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said City assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter le Poor London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) beaing Date the nineth day of January Instant John Alper and Joannah his Wife and their three Children to wit Elizabeth William and Hanny were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate London to our said Parish of St. Peter le Poor as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment and Determination of the said two Justices do therefore humbly Appeal against the same And pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and this Honourable Court shall never meet.

Fulham & Bouno } of Kannbil

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray

John Batten Attorney for the Appellants.
