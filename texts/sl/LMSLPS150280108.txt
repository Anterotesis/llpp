To the Rt Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And also to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

27/3 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Katherine Creedchurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Pass Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quond) bearing date the 27th Day of March last past Thomas Webb with Hannah his wife and their three Children Vizt Hannah, Ann, and Mary together with Rachael his daughter in Law and two Children Thomas and James were removed from the prenict of White Fryers London unto the said Parish of St Katherine Creedchurch alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Thomas Webb with his wife and three Children nor the said Rachaell his Daughter in Law never had any legall Settlement in the sd Parish of St Katherine Creedchurch whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices And do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Prcinet of White Fryers may Appear before you to hear and abide the determination of your Honr: and Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc
