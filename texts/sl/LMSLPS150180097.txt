Pet Robt. Waldron

To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Robt. Bedingfeld Knt. Ld Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of the Justices sitting at the Old Bayley

The Humble Peticon of Robt. Waldron now a Prisonr. in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Peticonrs. was Comitted for felony on Saturday last

Yor. Peticonr. therefore Humbly prays that he may be Tryed this Sessions or admitted to Bayle

And yor. Peticonrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
