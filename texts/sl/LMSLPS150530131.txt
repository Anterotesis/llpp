To the Rt. Honble: Sir Robert Godschall Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof, in their Generall Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Barkin London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under th Hands & Seals of year Lordship & Robt. Ladbroke Esqr. one of the Aldren, & Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for the City of London & the Libties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth Day of February 1741. Margaret Lowry Wife of William Lowry, was Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London, unto the sd. Parish of Alhallows Barkin London, Alledging the sd. Parish of Alhallows Barkin, to be the Place of her last legall Settlmt:

That Your Petr. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order sa afd. Do Appeal to your Lords & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses, And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London, may appear before you, to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And Your Petitionr. shall pray Etc.
