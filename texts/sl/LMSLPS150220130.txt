To the Rt Honrble Sr Robt Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Peter Le Poore London

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 31th day of October 1711 under the hands & Seales of Sr. Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Major of the City of London & Sr. John Houblon Knt one of the Aldermen of the Same City Two of her Maties: Justices of Peace[---] for the said City & the Libertyes thereof (Quorum Unus) One Richard Bragg was passed from the Parish of St. Nicholas Acous London to the said Parish of St. Peter Le Poore as being the place of his Last Legall Settlement

That yor. Peticoners humbly Conceive themselves agreived by the said Order & doe Appeale from & against the same to this Honoble: Bench.

And Yor. Peticoners humbly pray this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing of Yor. Petrs. Appeale against the said Order

And yor Petrs. Shall Ever pray Etc
