To the Right Honble William Beckford Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of Oyer and Terminer now sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey within the Suburbs of the said City.

The Humble Petition of John Brinklow

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner stands Charged with having Committed divers Felonys and for which he most humbly prays that he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged at the present Session and your Petitioner as in Duty Bound will for ever pray Etc.

John Brinklow
