To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church warderns and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of saith Stephen Walbrook in the said City of Loudon

Sheweth That by Vertex of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (whereof one is of the Quorum there in mentioned) bearing date the twenty second day of May in the year of our Lord One thousand Seven Hundred and forty One, Mary Sherwood wife of John Sherwood and their two Children William aged Six Years and Joseph aged Two years were removed from the parish of St. James at Garlickhill with in the said City to the said Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook as the last Legal place of their Settlement By which your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order,

Your Petrs. Therefore humbly Pretr. your Worships will be pleased to Hearally Determine the Matters touching this appeal and give them such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem Just and meet,

Wm. Ivatt Agent for the Appelants }

And your Petrs shall over pray Etc
