Appeale Pish of St. Barth the less agt. St Buttolph Aldgate

To the Rt. Honrble. Sr Samuel Garrard Barntt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petitton and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the less London

Sheweth That one Sarah Stephens Wido with her Two Children by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the 25th Day of May last Past was removed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St Bartholomew the Less Alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Stephens

That Your Petitioners. doubt not but to prove that the said Sarah Stephens never had any lagall Settlement in the said Parish of St Bartholomew the less whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Honr. and Worrpl therefrom.

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly pray your Honr. and Worppl. will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate doe appeare before you and heare & abide the Determination of your Honr. and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray Etc
