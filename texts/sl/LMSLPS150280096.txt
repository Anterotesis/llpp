To the Rt Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said City in their general quarter Sessions assembled:

22/12 The humble Petition and Appeal of the ChurchWardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 22d Day of December last past Mary Scavington Widdow was removed from the parish of Allhallows the great London unto the said Parish of St Mary Abchurch London Alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Scavington never had any legal Settlement in the parish of St. Mary Abchurch London whereby the conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs. therefore most humbly pray Honr: and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Allhallows the great may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal .

And they Shall Ever Pray Etc.
