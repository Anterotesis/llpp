To the Right Honourable William Benn Esqr. Lord Mayor and John Stracey Esqr. Recorder and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of removal under the hands and Seals of St Edward Bellamy Knt. and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City of London and Libertys thereof (Whereof one is of the Quorum) Bearing Date the thirteenth day of Febry last Ann Thompson Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London City to the said Parish of St Mary White Chapel as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petrs. and Appellants Apprehend themselves to be Agreived by the said Order of the said Two Justices and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioner and Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a Day this present Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopgate London [...] and all other Presons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching this Appeal and that your Petrs. and Appellants may be relieved therein as this Court shall see meet.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Robt: Lindsay Samll. Hawkins} Church wardens

George Bardett Jno. Hill} Oversrs. of the Poor
