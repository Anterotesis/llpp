To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Session Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbour in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Hart and John Wilkes Esquires, two of his Majesty's Justice of the peace in and for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth [...] last past Sarah Parsons Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Sr. Ann Blackfriars in the said City, to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Parsons, whereby your petitioner's conceive themselves to be aggrieved, and against the said order do appeal

Your Petitioner's therefore most humbly pray that you Worship will be pleased to appoint someday in the present Session for the hearing and determining this their said appeal

And your petitioner's shall ever pray Etc

Cox Sollr for Appellts. Kirby [...] Hatton Garden
