To the Rt. Honble Sr. Saml. Stanier Knt. Lord mayor and ye. rept. of her maties Justices of the peace for ye. Citty of London.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardn. and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. John Markney in the County of Medx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrt. beareing date under the hands and Seales of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this Citty (Quor unus) the 16th day of April last past, one George Cole Mary this wife and three Children George aged Seaven Yeares, Dianah aged four Yeares and Valeantine aged two month were removed and Conveyed from & out of ye parish of Allhallows on London Wall in the Citty of London to your Petrs. Said parish of St. John Harkney as the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petr. humbly concern themselves agreived by the to order Judgemt. of the Said Justices

Wherefore your Petr. humbly appeale agt. the same to touching this Court & and pray for Such reliefe in the prmises as to this Court Shall Seeme just and reasonable.

And your Petrs. Shall pray Etc
