To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church London.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable William Nash Lord Mayor of the City of London and Robert Alsop Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City; Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th. day of March 1772 Elizabeth Treacher Single woman, was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church London as the place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant, or Order of Removal, humbly Appeal to this Court against the same, praying such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Sml: Fisher Solr. for the Appellants
