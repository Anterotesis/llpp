To the Right Honoble. Sr Jno. Fryer Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the honoble. Court now Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The prayer of Robert Brown als Booth Etc

As he stands Indicted before this honoble. Court for Felony he therefore prays he may be tryed or otherwise discharged as the Law directs.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
