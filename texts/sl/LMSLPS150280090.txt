To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worple the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

24/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Katherine by the Tower London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City of London (Quod unns) bearing Date the Twenty Fourth day of November last past one Daniel Mackey and Mary Mackey his wife were sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Stephen in Colemanstreet London unto the said parish of St Katherine by the Tower Alleadging the said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of he said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to Your Honour and Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Honour and Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses And Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Stephen in Coleman street may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of Honour & Worps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall Ever pray Etc.
