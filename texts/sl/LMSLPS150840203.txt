To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor and others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Session of the Peace for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant for Under the Hands and Seals of James Townsend Esqr. late Lord Mayor and Richard Oliver Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourth day of November last Elizabeth Garner Wife of Joseph Garner gone from her was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London aforesaid as the Place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Honble. Court will please to Grant them such Relief in the Premisses as shall be Thought Equitable.

St: Bradley Sollr. for the Appellants}
