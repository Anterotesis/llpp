To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Edwd Clerke Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace att Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble petition of Reginald Heber Mercht.

Sheweth That yor Petr. is an inhabitant in the Parish of St. James Garlickhith London and payeth Forty pounds P Ann for his house there and likewise all dutyes to the Church and poore in respect thereof And that he alsoe holdeth A little yard behind his said house in which is a Shedd or roome which lyeth in the Parish of the holy Trinity for which he payeth four pounds yearly rent

That the Churchwardens of the said Pish of the Holy Trinity have Taxed & Assessed yor. Petr. att 4 pence P weeke to the poore of their Pish in respect thereof And yor. Petr. finding himself greived thereby The same being an Extraordinary rate for such a value & not proportionable to the rest of the Parish

His humble suite to yor. Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of ye said Parish of the Holy Trinity to Appear before you wth. Their Bookes of Assessmt. last made for the Poore And to lessen the said weekly rate of 4d. To such a lesser rate As to yor. Honors. & Worpps seem meete According to the Law.

And he shall pray Etc
