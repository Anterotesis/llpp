To the Right Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Parishioners of the Parish of St. John Evangelist to London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. said Parish being very small consisting but of 23 house their Churchwarden by mistakeing in a returne to the Precept directed to them from the Right Honoble. the late Lord Maior of this City to give an account of their Poore and the charge of mainteyning them Certified £38:3s:9d as for the charge of their Poore for one Yeare which said sume was the charge of their Poore for 3 severall yeares to witt for the yeares 1695 1696 & 1697

And their said Parish being charged by virtue of the late Act. of Comon Councill to pay £19:1s:9d to the Corporation established by the said Act for setting the Poore on worke

Now yor. Petrs. in respect of the smallnesse of their Parish And the greate charge of Poore therein which They have to mainteyne desire that yor. Lopp. & Worpp. will be pleased to releive your Petrs And to order the Abatement of the said sume of £19:1s:9d to such sume in Pportion to what doth truely arise Yearely for mainteyining the Poore of their said Parish They being only releivable by this Courte

And they shall Pray Etc
