To ye Rt. Honble Sr. Robt. Beachroft knt. Sd. Majr. & to ye. rest of ye honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justices Hall in ye. old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Eliz: Greene A Prisonr in newgate

Sheweth That you Peter was Comittee 18th. June last Charg's upon Suspicon of Picking a pocket of wth she is innocent

That yor Peter humbly prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be Bailed or discharged as this honble Court shall direct

And yor Peter as in duty come shall ever pray Etc
