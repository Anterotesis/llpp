To the Kt. Honble. Sr Samll. Garrard Barrt Lord Mayr. of the Citty of London and to the Worppfull the Rest of her Maties Justices of the peace within the and City in the Generall Quatter sessions assemble.

The humble peticon of the Churchwardnes & Overseers of the poor of him parish of st Buttolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That whereas one Edward Johnson hath rented an house of Â£10 P ann in nor Petr said Parish & hath pd scott & Lott to the same & still continues an Inhabitants therein with his wife and three Children and the sd Edward Johnson she Father lately becomeing a Bankrupt & being reduced to great Necesisty and poverty is unable to Maintaine & provided for Maintained at the Expence of yor Petrs said Parish But Now soe it is that one Rebecca Jones of the parish of St. Dunstans in the East widow being grandmother of the sd three Children & of good Substance and ability ought by Law to Provide for her said three Grandchildren and not burthen yor Petr with the Charge thereof and haveing been required by yor Petrs to take care of Etc maintaine her sd Grandchildren as by Lane She ought, Nevertheless she yet refuseth so to Doe.

Therefore Yor. Petrs humbly pray yor Lorppe & Worpps to hear them in the Prmisses & alsoe order the sd Rebecca Jones forthwith to appeare before you & to shew cause why she should not provide for her sd Grandchildren as the Law in yt Case Directs and alsoe to hear & abide the determination of Yor Lorppe & Worpps therein

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc
