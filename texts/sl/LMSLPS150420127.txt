To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennett at Pauls Wharfe London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libtys (Quorum Unus) Bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of May last John Smith an Infant son of Henry Smith was lately Removed from the Parish of St. Bridgett Vulgarly St. Brides London to the said parish of St. Bennett at Pauls Wharfe London as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to your Lordships and Worships against the said order and humbly desire this Court will make such order therein at to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc.
