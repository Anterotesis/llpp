To the Right Honoble Sir John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the right worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the right honoble Sir John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23th. day of November last Sarah Litle Widow of Johugh Litle was removed from the Parish of Saint Catherine Coleman London to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your. Petrs. conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that they may be releived against the said Warrant or Order in such Manner as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.

Henry Bridgen Stephen Butler} Churchwardens

Wm. Sells} Overseer
