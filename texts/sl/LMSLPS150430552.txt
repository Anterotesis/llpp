To the Rt Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London in Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of the Right Honble Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and St Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said City (quor unsu) bearing dale the 14th day of September last One Hannah the wife of Thomas Bateson Jun & her three Children (vizt) Mary aged about Six yeares Hannah aged about four yeares & Wm aged about Seven months were removed from the said parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate and the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petrs find themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant or order of the Said Two Justices of the peace and humbly appeales to this Court agt the Same

And your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc

Robt Wyrill Tho Sayers} Churchwardens

Henry Howard Jos Gamble} Overseers of the poor
