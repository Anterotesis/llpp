1/6 To the Right Honble. Sr. Peter Delma Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull the Rest of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticonr & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majtis Justices of the Peace for the sd City of London and the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing due the first day of June last past Margret Curtis and Alice her Child Aged about three Weeks was removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the Parish of Deptford aforesd Alledging the said Parish of Deptford to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoner Conceiving themselves agreived by the sd Order or Warrant of the Old Two Justices as aforesd do Humbly Appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom And humbly may that your Lordships & Worp will be pleased to hear them Concerning to Premises And that he Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallow on the Wall London may Appear before Your & hear & Abide the Judgment & determination of ye Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal

And your. Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc.
