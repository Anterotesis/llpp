To the Right Honrble St Willm Humfreys Knt. & Bart Lard Mayor by the City of London And to ye Right Worpll ye Rest of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace in ye Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the said of the Parish of St. Faiths London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrt under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of ye peace (one bearing of the Quor) bearing Date the Twenty fourth day of May last One Eleanor Lane together with three Children Vizt Thomas about thirteen Dorothy Eleanor or halfe and Samll about Nine Years of age were sent and delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of White Fryers London Unto the Said Parish of St Faiths Londons Alleadging ye said Parish to be the Place of their last Legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agrievd, by ye Said Warrt & Judgment afrsaid two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worrppl therefrom

Your Petitioner most humbly pray your Honour & Worppl will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and Order that ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye Sd. Parish of [...] White Fryers London may appeal before you & hear & abide the the determinacon of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall Ever Pray Etc.
