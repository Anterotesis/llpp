To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor, the Recorder, and the Worshipful Aldermen of the City of London

The Humble Petition of the Journey men Narrow Weavers in the Fancy Trimming Branch

Sheweth That your Petitioners having a Book of Prices agreed to and signed by the Principal and Major part of the Masters in the above branch in the City of London, as well as in the County of Middlesex which was Confirmed at the last General Quarter Session of the Peace holden at Hicks Hall in and for the said County of Middlesex on the 13th day of January 1774

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Lordship that you will be pleased to Confirm the same

And you Petitioners will every pray

James Johnson Cornelious Cavallier John Price Archibald Johnson William Tilly
