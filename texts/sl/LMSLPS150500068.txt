To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in Genll. Session at the Guild Hall in the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. or Order under the Hands & Seals of Micajah Perry Mayor and George Champion Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Third day of [---] November last Mary Lee aged about Thirteen years Daughter of Thomas Lee deceased was removed from the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the City of London to the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your petitioners think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may attend at the same time

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Chas. Gresham Soll for Appel}

John Bladwell Jno. Oaksley Churchwardens
