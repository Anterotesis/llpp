To the Rt: Honble: Sr: John Houblon Knt: Lord Majr: of the Citty of London and other his Matie: Justice now Sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baly

The humble Peticon of Ann Jones

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: wad Comitted to the Goale of Newgate by Warrant from the Recorded Datg: October infant charged upon Oath with High [...] in Criping the Currant Coine of thio Kingdome and sd now Remaining a performer in this said Coale

Humbly prayeth that she may boe fryed this Psent Sessions or that the may bee Bailed according to an Act of Parliament in that case made and provided

And the shall ever pray
