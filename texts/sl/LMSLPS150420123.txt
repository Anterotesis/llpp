To the Right Honorble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Petersham in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Vertue and Order or Pass Warrant Under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Seventh Day of this Instant Aprill John well beloved Mary his Wife John Aged about Six Years & Benjamin Aged about two years their two Children were passed from the parish of St, Nicholas Cole Abby London to the sd. parish of Petersham in Surry as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agreiv'd by ye sd. Warrant & Judgment of the sd. two Justices Humbly Appeal to Yor Worships for Relief in the premises And pray the Hearing of ye Appeale may be Adjorned to the next Generall or Quarter Sessions of ye peace for ye City that all party's concerned may then have proper Notice to Attend And

Yor Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
