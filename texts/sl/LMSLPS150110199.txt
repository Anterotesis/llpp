To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Thomas. Abney Knt. Lord Maior of the Citty of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Michaell Queenehith London

Sheweth

That one Sara Emerston Single woman being greate with a Bastard Child by warrant under the hands and Seales of Twoe of the Maties Justices of the peace within this Citty (one being of the Quox) was brought by the Churchwardens or St. Gregoryes to yor: Petrs. To be provided for uppon her Oath That Shee lived about twoe yeares Since with Mr: John Cox of the parish of St. Michaells Salter for the Terme of one whole years and upwards as a hired Servant Att Â£3.10s.- P Annum whereas in Truth The said Sarah Dwelt with the said Mrs. Cox nott above 6 months or there abouts and for had not settlement in their said Parish

Their humble suite unto yor. Lopp and Worshipps is to heare them in the premisses And to Send the said Sarah Emerston to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Gregoryes to be by them pvided for and mainteyned untill They Caun Free themselves of the Charge there by due course of Lawe
