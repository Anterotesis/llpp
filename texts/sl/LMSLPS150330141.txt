To the Rt. Honrble Sr William Stewart Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of the of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Qurn Unus) bearing date the First day of February last past Richard Farleys with Elizabeth his wife were removed from the parish of St Bridgett ats Brides London unto the said parish of St Mary Abchurch London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And pray that your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Bridgett als Brides may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc:
