To the Right Honoble Sr. Samuell Stanier Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London and the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace att the Quarter Sessions held for the said Citty

1714 22/6 The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Benntts Pauls Wharfe London

Shew That by a Pass Warrt: under the hands and Seales of Sr Peter King Knt. and Sr. Thomas Abney two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quorum unus) upon the Complaint of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of Allhallowes Lombard Street London the last Legall Settlement of Sarah Watts was adjudged to be in the said parish of St. Bennetts Paules Wharfe and thereby the said Sarah Watts was sent to yor. Peticoners to be taken care of according to Law

By which said Adjudicacon yor: Peticoners do Conceive themselves to be agreived And therefore do appeale from the Same

And humble Pray yor. Lorpp and Worpps to appoint a day for hearing of the sd. Appeale

And yor. Peticoners shall Ever pray Etc
