To the Rt: Honble Sr. Wm: Lewen Knt: Ld. Mayr. of the City of London & to the rest 9h ye Honble Bench of Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Margaret Thomas A Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. is Comittee to Newgate for Felony for picking a pocket whereof she is innocent

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prayes to be this Session or be discharg'd upon Baile or otherwayes as this Honble Court Shall thinck fitt

And yor Petr as in duly bound shall ever pray
