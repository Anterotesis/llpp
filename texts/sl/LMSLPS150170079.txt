To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Severall Parishes of St Gyles without Cripplegate St Buttolph without Aldgate St Andrewes. Wardrobe St. Katherine Coleman St. Andrew Holborne St. Sepulchers St Brides Alhallowes on the Wall St Buttolph Aldersgate & St Buttolph without Bishoppsgt.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. are very much Overburthened with poore And not able to Raise Sufficient Sumes of money amongst themselves to Releive them notwithstanding their Assessments are greater than heretofore Yet the Charge of their Poore is farr above their Assessments As the Same upon full Examination hath divers tymes appeared to Severall worth Aldermen of this City appointed to hear the Same And their Poore daily encreasing upon them their Said Parishes lying Some of them near to the Suburbes and others of them in the Suburbs of this City

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contributions of other Parishes within this City for and towards the Releiving their Poore as they have formerly had which are Pticularly hereunder menconed Or such further Allowance As to yor. Honor. & Worpps. Shall Seeme meete

And They Shall Pray Etc

St. Gyles without Cripplegate £90 St. Buttolph without Aldgate £50 St. Andrew Wardrobe £40 St. Katherine Coleman 12. St Andrew Holborne 7. St Sepulchers 44 St. Brides 15. Alhallowes on the Wall 12 St Buttolph without Aldersgate 15. St Buttolph Bishoppsgate 60.
