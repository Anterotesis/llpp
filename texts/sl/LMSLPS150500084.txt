To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their General, or Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Norton Folgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties Thereof (One being of the Quorum) Bearing Date the Twelfth Day of February last James Boucher Sarah his Wife with Their Six Children John Aged Thirty One Years James Aged Twenty Years William Aged Eighteen Years Elizabeth aged Fifteen Years Robert Aged Nine Years and Samuel aged. Seven Years Were Passed from the Parish of St Botolph Without Bishopsgate London to the said Liberty of Norton Felgate in the County of Midlesex Aforesaid as the Please of their Last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Humbly Conceiving Themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court for Releif in the premises.

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever pray and so Forth.

Marshe Dickenson Agent for the Appellants

Wm. Gilding Jno. Luke Landon} Over Seers.
