To the right honble. Sr. William Humphries bright and Barronett Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the right worll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace in the generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchers London.

Sheweth that Hester Warren wife of Michael Warren with Maria Warren her Child aged about three year's and an halfe by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing date the first day of July instant was removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the great London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last legall Settlement of the Sd. Michael Warren and whither his said wife Hester Warren & Maria he said Child ought to be removed.

That yor. Peticonr. doubt not to prove that the said Michael Warren had no legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London, whereby they Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Sd. two Justices and doe humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worships thereupon

Yors. Peticoners therefore humbly pray your Honour and Worships will be pleased to hear then in the Prmisses. and order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the great London to hear and abide the determinacon of your Honour and Worships touching this Appeal

And your Peticonrs shall ever Pray Etc.

John Ashbridge Chwarden. Jno Edden Ch:wardens.
