To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one whereof us of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of March last past Sarah Phelps aged about five years was removed from the Parish of Saint Ann Blackfryers London unto the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx as the Place of her Last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of pass Warrant And therefore Humbly Appeals against the same

Tho: Bray Sol. for the Appealts.
