To the right honble Sr. Charles Duncombe Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to the Eight Worth her Majties Justices of the peace of the said City in the generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of thee Churchwardens and Overseers of the Borne of the Parish of St. Alban Wood Street London

Sheweth That ye Virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & seales of two of her majties Justices of the peace within this City upon Complaint to them made by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of City Butolph Aldgate London One Elizabeth Christmas Widdow of Thomas Christmas deceased and Eliz: Christmas her daughter aged abt the yeare had been sent and delivered to Gane Petrs. to be provided for by the parish of St. Alban Wood street aforesaid

That your Petrs. conceive they are not lyable by Law to provide for the said Elizabeth Christmas and her daughter for that the said Thomas Christmas and the said Elizabeth his wife have many yeares Since gained a legall settlement in the said parish of St. Buttolph Aldgate

You Petrs. therefore humble pray that you Honr and Worps. will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish of St. Buttolph Aldgate may appeare before you [...] and that you will be pleased to her you Petrs. in the prmisses and order the keeping of the said Elizabeth Christmas and her daughter according to Law.

And you Petrs. shall pray Etc.
