To the Right Honble William Benn Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace now Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ethelburga London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty third Day of December last past Rachael Jennings Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London to the said Parish of Saint Ethelburga London as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices appeal to your Lordship and Worships against the said Order and humbly desire such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners (as in Duty bound) shall ever pray Etc

William Hussey Solicitor for the Parish of Saint Ethelburga London
