To the Right Honoble Sir Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in Southwark in the County of Surry.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Westley Knt. and Bernard Esqr two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing date the Tenth day of March 1743. William Harvey Mary his Wife and three Children were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate to the Parish of St. George in Southwark in the County of Surry as the place of their last legall Settlement

That Your Petrs Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly Appeal against the same.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray they may be releived against the said Warrant or Order in such Manner as this Hononble Court shall direct.

And yor. Petrs. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
