To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poore & Parishionrs. of the Parish of St. Ethelburgh within Bishoppsgate London

Sheweth That the Clarke of yor. Petrs. Parish by mistake in a Returne to the prcept directed to them from the Right Honorble the late lord Maior of this City to give an account of their Poore and the charge of mainteyining them hee made a returne of the sume of £133. for one yeare which was the totall sume of all the pavments made in every respect for & concerning the same Whereas in truth the true charge of their poore amounts to no more than about £45. yearely.

And their said Parish being charged by virtue of the late Act of Comon Councill to pay 66:10:2 to the Corporation of established by the said Act for setting the poore on worke

Now yor: Petrs. in respect of the smallnesse of their Parish And the greate charge of Poore therein wch. They have to mainteyne Define that yor. Lopp. & Worpps. will be pleased to releive your Petrs and to order the abatement of the said sume of £66:10s:2d: to such sume in proportion To what doth truely arise by their assessement for mainteyning the Poore of their said Parish They being only releivable by this Courte

And They shall pray Etc.
