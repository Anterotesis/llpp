To the Right Honourable Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Right Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary at hill in the City of London

Shewth. That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Benn Esquire late Lord Mayor of the City of London And William Whitaker Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of October last past Elizabeth Cooper Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Criplegate London unto the said Parish of Saint Mary at Hill in the said City alledgeing the said Parish of Saint Mary at Hill to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom

And Humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses And Order that the said Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate may Appear before you to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of your Lordship and Worships touching the premisses

And your Petitrs. shall ever pray Etc
