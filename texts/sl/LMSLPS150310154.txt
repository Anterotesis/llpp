To the Rt: Honbles: the Lord Mayr: & other by Maties: Justices of the peace for the City of London now Sitting at Guildhall

The Humble Peticonr and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Matfellon als White Chappell in the Sd. County.

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor un) bearing date ye sd. day of Augt: last Eliza. Dyke wife of Abram Dyke with three Children (Vizt.) Samuel aged four Years and an half Elizabeth aged there years and Hannah aged about fourteen Clothes were removed from ye Parish of St. Katherine Coleman London to the said Parish of St.Mary Matfellon als White Chappell as to their last legall Settlement

That yr. Petrs: think themelves agrieved by ye said Order and Humbly appeal to this Honrble. Court agt. ye same.

And pray that Yor. Worpps would be played to give them such relief in the premises as to ye. Worpps shall seem meet.

And yr. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
