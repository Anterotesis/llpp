To the Rt. Honble: Sr William Lewen. Kt Lord Maier of the City of London And others his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at their Genll. Quarter Sessions Holden at Guild Hall in the sd. City

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr William Withers Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the twenty Secored day of February last. Anne Fowler Widd. was removed from the Parish of St Dunstan in the West in the said City of St James Westmr. as to th [...] place of the last legall Settlement of the said Anne Fowler

That your Petitioners think themselves agrieved by the said Order and humbly Appeal to this Honble. Court Against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that yr Lordship and this Honble Bench Would be pleased to Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the saidparish of St Dunstan in the West and all other Persons concernd to attend this court some Day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeale

And yr. Petrs. Shall Ever pray Etc
