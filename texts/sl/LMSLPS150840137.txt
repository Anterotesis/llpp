To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London At their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Fyfield in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass order under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of June last past Jane Seymore wife of John Seymore, gone from her, and their two Children, to wit, Hannah Aged three Years and Sarah Aged One Year were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Fyfield in the said County of Berks as the last Legal Place of their Settlement By which Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Skinn & Stirling Agents for the Appellants} Your Petitioners therefore Pray your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this Appeal and give them such relief in the Premisses as to Your Worships shall seem Just and Meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
