To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor of the City of London, and the rest of the Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dionis Back church London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honorable Robert Peckham Lord Mayor and John Boydell Esq: one of the Alderman of the said City, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum dated the twenty fifth day of March 1784 Mary the Wife of William Dunn (who hath deserted her) with her Child named William, aged two months, were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Bennett Grace church as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order, Do appeal against the same to this Honorable Court, and pray such Relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

[...] Hall} Church Wardens

James Green Robt: Gosling} Overseers
