To the right honble Sr. Wm: Gore Knt sd. Mayr. and the rest of her Maties Justices att the old Bayly

The humble Peticon of Mary Sheston

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comitted to Newgate on the 14th. of Aprill last upon abare Suspicon of being Consederate in a Felloney, that the hath been contained in Goal ever fined without any prosecucon and is very Innocent in the & [...] whereof the is accused;

Therefore humbly prays this honble: Court either to discharge or bring her to a Tryall this Sessions;

And yrs. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
