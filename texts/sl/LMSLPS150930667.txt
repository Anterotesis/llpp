To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Watkin Lewis and Mayor and Nathaniel Newnham Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in & for the City of London bearing Coate the fifth day of November naw last past Charlotte Tipping Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of Saint Alban in Wood Street in the City of London to the said parish of Saint George And the said Justices in and by the said Order of Removal Adjudged the last Legal Settlement of the said Charlotte Tipping to be in the said Parish of Saint Geroge

That Your Petitioner's are advised and Believe that the last Legal Settlement of the said Charlotte Tipping is not in the said parish of Saint George As by the said Order is Supposed and therefore they Conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order and Determination of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Worship's against the same

Your Petitioners therefore as well as the part and behalf of themselves as the other Inhabitants and Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint George Do Humbly pray that the said Order of Removal of the said Charlotte Tipping may be Quashed And that Your Petitioners may be further and otherwise Relieved in the premises as to your Worships shall seen Meet.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Major Wright Sollicitor for the Appellants
