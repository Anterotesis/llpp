To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in & for the City of London at their General Session of this Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Chuchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of and for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty second Day of October last Mary the Wife of Thomas Styles a Soldier in his Majesties Service and her four Children vizt. Ann Aged six Years Hester Aged four Years John aged Two Years and upwards and Mary aged nine Months and upwards were removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Sepulchre London to the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great at the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said Order and pray that the Benefit of such their Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden in and for the said City of London and that your Worships would be pleased to appoint some Day in the next Session for the Hearing & Determination of this their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Railton Solicitor for the Appellants

exd
