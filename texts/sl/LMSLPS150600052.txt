To the Rt. Honble. Sir William Calvert, Knt. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Worpful ye. rest of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace, for ye. sd. City, and ye. Libties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seals of Sir Daniel Lambert, Knt. & Marshe Dickinson Esqr, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & ye. Liberties thereof, (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty ninth Day of June last past, John Squire, Elizabeth his Wife, & their three Children, Vizt, Anne aged nine Years, Richard aged eight Years, & Katherine agd one Year & upwards, were Removed from ye. Parish of Alhallows Breadstreet, London, unto ye. said Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, London, alledging ye. sd. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Cha: Baynbridge Atty for ye. Appellts. That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agreived by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worships therefrom, and humbly pray, That you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, And that ye. Church- -Wardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of Alhallows Breadstreet, may appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor. Lordship & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc
