To the Rt. Honble Sr. John Thompson Kt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London & the rest of the Worpll. Justices of the Peace at the Genll Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Assembled

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. John Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of St. John Thompson Kt. Lord Mayor and Robt. Godschall Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London One being of the Quorum bearing Date the Ninteenth day of Aprill 1737. whereby May Onslow Wife of Richd. Onslow was removed from and out of the Parish of Alhalows on London Wall in to our said Parish of St. John Southwark as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Your Peticoners therefore humbly conceive themselves Agrieved by the Said order or Pass Warrant and humbly appeals agt. the Same

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
