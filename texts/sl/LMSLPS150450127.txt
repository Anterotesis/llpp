To the Rt. Honble: Sir William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Humbly Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the said Sir William Billers and Sir Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London. and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Twentieth day of September 1734 Elizabeth Debney wife of James Debney was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Olave London. to the said Parish of St. James as the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Debney Whereby you Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

And Therefore humbly pray this Court with please to Hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law and Justice

And Your Petitioners Shall pray

Wm Wayte} Churchwarden

James Whiffen Bess Bayly Richd Norris} Ocerseers of then Poor

Jos: Dunning John Smith
