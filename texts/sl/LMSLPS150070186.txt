To ye Rt. Honr. Sr. Jno Houblon Knt Lord Major. of London to ye Rest of ye Judges & Justices now Sitting att ye Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Nicho: Nolan of Prisonr in Newgate.

That yor Peticonr before ye Last Sessions was Comitted to Ye Goal of Newgate for Refuseing to take ye oathes &

your Peticonr most humbly Prayeth that hee may be tryed or Discharged this Sessions & that his Prayer may be Recorded

And yor. Peticonr. Shall pray
