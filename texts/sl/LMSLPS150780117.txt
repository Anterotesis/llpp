To the Right Honble Sr. Robert Kite Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Right Worshipful the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof at their general Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement near Eastcheap London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & seales of Francis Cokayne & Samuel Turner Esqr. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said City & the liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourth day of March last William Fosh was removed from the Parish of St. Edmund the King London to the Parish of St Clement next Eastcheap London alledging the said Parish of St Clement near Eastcheap to be the place of his last legal Settlement.

That your Petrs. conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of Removal Do appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises & that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Edward the King may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Wordships touching this appeal And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
