To the Right Honble Robt. Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City & the libertys thereof in their Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Saviours in Southwark in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order & Pass Warrt. under the Hands & Seals of Your Lordship & Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libertys thereof (One being of the Quorum) Bearing Date the 20th Day of September 1752 Elizabeth Ettery Wife of Isaac Ettery Was removed from the Pish of St. Batholomew the Great London to the sd. Parish of St. Saviour Southwark Alledging the sd. Parish of St. Saviour to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the sd. Warrt. or Order of Removal do Appeal to Your Lordship & Worships there from & Humbly Pray that you will be Pleased to hear them in the Premes & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great may Appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of Your Lorshp and Worships touching this Appeal.

Geo Ellis Attorney for the Appellants

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc
