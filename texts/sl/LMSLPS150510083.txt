To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace fot the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the said [...] Westmr. [...] Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Rt Honble Sr. John Satler Mayor and Sr William Rows Knt. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 10th. Day of May [---] last Jane Tancock Wife of William Tancock and their four Children Mary Anne aged [...] Jeremiah aged one year and upwards were removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres London to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray such releif in the Premisses may be granted your Petitioners as to this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And your Petitionrs. will ever pray Etc.

[...]

John Brindley John Gretton} Overseers of the poor
