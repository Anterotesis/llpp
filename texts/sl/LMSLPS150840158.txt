To the Right Honble Wm Nash Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Majestys Justices of ye peace for ye same City at their Genl. Qr. Session of ye peace assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. George the Martyr in Southwark

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of ye Quorum) bearing date ye 10th Day of Sepr. last, Elizth. Cowdell Singlewoman & her Female Bastard Child Susanna aged five Months were removed from the parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate London to ye sd. parish of Sr. George Southwark as ye place of her last legal Settlemt

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye order of ye sd. two Justices, humbly appeal agt. ye same, & pray that this Honble. Court will please to hear & determine their sd. appeal & make such Order thereon as shall be Just

And your Petitionrs. will ever pray Etc
