To the Right Honble. Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London Sr. Wm. Moreton Knt. [---] Recorder of the Same City and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City and Libertys thereof at their Genl. Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnel Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under ye. hands and Seals of two of His Majesty's of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof (whereof our is of the Quorum) Ann Revel Wife of Paul Revel who is a Sailor in his Majesty's Service and their three Children John Ann and Thomas were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate in the said City to the said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves to be argrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners & Appellants thereof Pray that this Court will appoint a Day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate and all other Persons Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same touching this appeal and that Your Petitioners and Appellants may be Relieved as this Court shall See meet.

And Your Petitioners and Appellants shall ever Pray Etc.

Octor. Sessn. 1756.

Jno. Billington Agent for Appellts.
