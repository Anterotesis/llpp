To the Right Honoble Sr. Gerard Conyers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City and the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticion & Appeall of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Twster Lane London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London and the Libties thereof (Quor un) bearing Date the 22d. Day of November last past Dorothy Luther Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Stephen Colemanstreet London unto the Said Parish of St. Leonard Foster Lane London alledging the said Parish of St. Leonard Foster Lane to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving them- -Selves agreived by the Said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices Doe humbly appeale to your Lord Ship & Worps. there from, And pray That yor Lordship and Worships will be pleased to hear them concerning the promisses, And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Stephen Coleman Street may appear before, you & hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of you Lordship & Worp touching this Appeale

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
