To the Rt. Honble: Robert Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worpful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for the sd. City, & the Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal, of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor, of the Parish of Christchurch, in the City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under ye. Hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble. Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. late Lord Mayor, & yor. Lordship, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & the Liberties thereof (one being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty fifth Day of April 1752, Sarah Bunce, Widow of John Bunce, deced, was Removed from the Parish of St. Martin Orgars, London, to ye. Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging the said Parish of Christchurch, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. sd. Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Martin Orgars, may appear before You, to hear & abide ye. Judgmt. & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

Chas: Baynbridge Atty for the Appellants.}

And yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
