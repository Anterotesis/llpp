To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Newington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City of London (whereof are is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 4th. Day June instant, Ruhamah Roberts, Jane his Wife and Mary their Daughter were removed from the Parish of St. Anns Black Fryars London to the said Parish of Saint Mary Newington as the last legal Place of their Settlement By which your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said pass Order.

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worships Will be pleased to hear and determine the Matter touching this Appeal and give them such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem just and meet.

And your Petioners shall ever pray

Thos. Mason Solicitor for the Church wardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Mary Newington
