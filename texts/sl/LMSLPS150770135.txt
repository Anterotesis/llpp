To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in General Quarter Sessions of the peace at Guildhall in and for the said City Assembled The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace of the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing date the Eleventh day of December One Thousand Seven Hundred and Sixty five Mary Curtis Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Stephen Coleman Street London to the parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement together with her two Bastard Children for Nurture whereby the Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and Against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Matter of the said Appeal may be heard and that they may have such Relief therein as to Your Worships shall Seem meet

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Carter & Woodgate for the Appealants
