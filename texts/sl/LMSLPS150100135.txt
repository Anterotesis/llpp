To the Right Honble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Hamlett of Milend old Towne in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Midds.

Sheweth That one Richard Taylor being lunatick by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of Quor) was brought by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of St. Martins Ludgate London to yor. Petrs. to be pvided for on the Oath of Sarah his mother That his last legall settlement was in the said parish of Stepney in Middx where hee served his Appnticeshipp with Captaine Robert Deane of the said Parish & County Whereas in truth yor Petrs. never knew any such Pson as the said RIchard Taylor Nor ever heard or knew of any such Pson as Robert Deane To be an Inhabitant within their Hamlett in the said Parish and County

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Honors. & Worpps. to order the said Churchwardens of St. Martins Ludgate to appeare before you And to hear them all in the prmisses And to Quash the said Warrant And send the said Richard Taylor backe to the said Parish of St. Martins to be pvided for an mainteyned

And they shall Pray Etc
