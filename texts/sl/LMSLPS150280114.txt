To the right honourable Sr. James Bateman Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the right worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

6/ [...] The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Henson by vertue of a warrant or under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within the City of London one being of the Quorn bearing date the 6th. day of May last past was Removed from the Parish of St. Bridget ats Brides London to the parish of St. Sepulchres London alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Henson That your Petitioners doubt not to prove that the said Elizabeth Henson had no Legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Sepulchres London whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices And do humbly appeal to Your honour and Worships thereupon

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray your honour & Worships will be Pleased to hear them in the premises & Order the Church:wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London to hear & Abide the determination of Your honour and Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs Shall ever Pray Etc.

Jno: Seaper: Ch:wardn Ch: Honmond Ch: Warden. John Petche Overseers
