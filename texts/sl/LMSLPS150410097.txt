1730 23/2 To the Right Honble Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City London & to ye Worpfull the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libtys thereof in their Genal Quarter Sessions Assembled

The hble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant unto the hands & Seals of your Lordship and Sr. Richard Hopskins Knt. and Aldran two of his majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libtys thereof (Quor unus) bearing date ye. twenty third day of Febry last past Martha Nixon widow of Jno. Nixon deced was removed from the Parish of St. Catherine Coleman [---] London unto ye sd. Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London alledging the sd. Parish of St Andrew Undershaft to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That Yr. Petrs. conceiving themselves Agrieved by ye sd Warrt. or Order do Appeal to your Lordship & Worships thereform and humbly pray yr your Ldp & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmes & order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Catherine Coleman London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determination of ye Lordsp & Worps touching this Appeale

And yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
