To the Rt. Honble the Ld. Mayor and others his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mildred Breadstreet London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City (Quor unus) bearing dase the Eighth day of March last past One Sara Newman with John her Son aged about Fourteen were sent from the Parish of St James Garlickhith London unto Your Petitioners sd parish of St Mildred as being the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving them selves agreved by teh Judginent of the sd two Justices humbly Appeal to Your Worps for relief in the Prmisses

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
