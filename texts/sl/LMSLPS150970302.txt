To the Right Honorable Richard Clark Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Lawrence Jewry London united to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Milk Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and William Plomer Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fifth Day of August last Joseph Peacock and Mary his Wife with their two Children Mary Aged Seven Years and upwards and Margaret aged Eight Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of saint Lawrence Jewry as the last legal Settlement of them the said Joseph Peacock and Mary his Wife and their said Children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves greatly aggrieved.

Wherefore your Petitioners do hereby appeal against the said Order of Removal and humbly pray your Worships to Quash the same and to grant them such other Relief in the premises as shall be just

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
