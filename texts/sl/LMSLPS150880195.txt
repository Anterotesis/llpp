London To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull the Aldermen of the City of London his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at the General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guildhall of the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Coleman in the said City of London

Sheweth. That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Thomas Hallifax Knight Lord mayor of the said City and Robert Alsop Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City Dated the Twentieth Day of December last Jane Richards and her Illegitemate Son Thomas Aged Two months and upwards for Nurture were removed from the Parish of Saint Catherine Creechurch in the said City of London to the Parish of Saint Catherine Coleman in the said City of London as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Jane Richards by which your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray your Lordship and Worships that they may have Liberty to appeal to the said Order of Removal

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray.
