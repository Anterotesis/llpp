6/6 To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rt. Wortt. his maties justies of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens of uno Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry London

Sheweth That by virtue of Warrant veder the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justies of the peace within this City (Quor Unus) bearing date the 6th. day of June last part One Elizth. the wife of John Roberts with William Roberts a Child of about apear old were sent and removed from the Parish of St. Botophs without Aldersgate London unto the said Parish of St. Laurence Jewry London alleadging your Peticons said Parish of St. Lawrence Jury to be the place of her last legall Settlement

Wherefore your Peticonrs conceiving themselves agreives by the judgmt of the said two Justies aforesaid Do humbly appeal to your Lordspp & Worpps therein and humbly pray they may be heard in the Premissed And taht the Churchwards: & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Butolph without Aldersgate may appear before you to hear the judgemt. & determinacons of yor Lopps worpps touching this appeale And to abide by ye order of this Court therein

And yor Petrs shall ever pray etc.
