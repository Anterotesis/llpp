To the Right Worpll. Her Majtys. Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of London

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of the Parish of Woolwich in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under ye. hands & Seals of Sr. Peter King & Sr. Thomas Abney Knts. two of her Majtys Justices of ye. peace for the City & Liberty of London (Quor unus) bearing date the fourth day of May last there was passed & removed into their said parish from ye. Parish of St. Botolph without Algate London Daniel Jones aged abt. Twelve years & Jane Jones aged abt. Eight years And yt. yor. peticonrs. by reason thereof find themselves agrieved And humbly prays to be heard upon ye. sd Appeal agst. ye Order of the sd two Justices And yor. peticonrs shall pray Etc

Samll Fletcher William Loyd} Churchwardens

John Osborne John Shermgars} Overseers

parish of Woolwich in kent appeal agt St Buttolph Aldgate
