1713 26/7 To the Right Honble: Sir John Barnard Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir William Billers Knt. & Aldran Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Sixth Day of July last past, Morgan Meriter Morgan & Elizabeth his Wife, were Removed from the Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street London, unto the Parish of Alhallows Staining London aforesaid, alledging the sd. Parish of Alhallows Staining to be the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do appeal to yor Lordship and Worp. therefrom, And humbly pray that you [...] will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, [...] that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor [...] the Parish of St. Stephen Colemom Street London appear before you, to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worps touching this Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
