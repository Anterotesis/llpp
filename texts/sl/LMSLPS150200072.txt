To the Rt. Honrble Sr Charles Duncombe Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of St. Andrew Holborne in the County of Middx

Sheweth

That one Francis Byfeild and Elizabeth his wife with their three Children Elizabeth James and Francis By Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the 19th day of February 1708 was removed from the parish of St. Gregory by St Paul London the parish of St. Andrew Holborne, in Middx Alleadgeing the said parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Francis Byfeild and Elizabeth his wife with their said Three Children

That your Petitionr doubt not but to prove That the said Francis Byfeild and Elizabeth his Wife with their said Three Children never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St Andrew Holborne whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Twoe Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Worpps there from

Your Petitionr therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmissed and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St. Gregory by St. Paul to appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionr shall ever Pray Etc
