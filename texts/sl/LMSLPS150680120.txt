To the Rt. Honble: Marshe Dickinson, Esqr, Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, and to ye. Worshipful ye. rest of his Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye sd. City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seals of your Lordship and Francis Cokayne, Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. thirteenth Day of January last past, Letitia Price, Widow of John Price, deceased, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph, Aldersgate, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great, London, alledging ye sd. Parish of St. Bartholomew, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Atty for the Appellants. That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. sd. Order or Warrant of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph, Aldersgate, afd, may appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petitioners shall pray Etc.
