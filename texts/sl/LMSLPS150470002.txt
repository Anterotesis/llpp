ss To the Right. Honble. Sir John Williams Kt. Lord Mayor of the City of of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace at the Guidl Hall of the said City in Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of Culverwell Needler.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner is the Person intended & mentioned by the Name of Culverwell Needler in the Order or Adjudication hereunto annexed and that the Facts Charged upon him by Mary Mackbride in the said Order or Adjudication mentioned are untime and your petitioner humbly concieves that he is much injured & grieved by the said Order or Adjudication.

Your Petitioner therefore Appeals from the said Order or Adjudication to your Worships to be relieved touching the Premes as to Your Worship Shall Seem meet and your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.

Culverwell Needler
