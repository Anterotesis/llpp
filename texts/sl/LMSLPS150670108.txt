To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices in their General Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate for the said City of London assembled

The Humble Petition of James Clowes

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the Eleventh Day of August last was committed to the said Gaol of Newgate by Thomas Beach Esquire Coroner of and for the said City of London for the Willfull Murder of Sarah Wheeler by Poisoning her.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays that at this Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City of London he may be either Tryed Bailed or discharged for the Crime aforesaid Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc James Clowes
