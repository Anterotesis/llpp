To the Right Honoble. Sr Francis Child Knt. Lord Major [...] of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Severall Parishes of St. Gyles without Cripplegate St Buttolph without Aldgate St. Andrew Wardrobe St. Katherine Coleman St. Andrew Holborne St. Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the wall St. Buttolph Aldersgate St. Buttolph wth. out Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs are very much Overburthened with Poore and not able to vaite [...] sumes of money amongst themselves to Releive them Notwithstanding their Assessments are greater than heretofore yett the charge of their Poore to farr above their laid Assessments as the came upon full Examination hath many times appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to hear the same And their Poore daily Encreasing upon them Then said Parishes lying once of them neere to the suburbs and others of them in the suburbes of this City

Their Humble Suites therefore unto yor good Honor & Worpps. to That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them The Contribucons from other Parishes within this City for and towards the Releiving their Poore As They have formerly had which are pticularly hereunder menconed or such further Allowance as to your Honor. and Worpps. shall seeme meete
