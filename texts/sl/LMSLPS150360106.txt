To the Right Honrble Sr: Geo: Merttins Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the City of London at their Genalll Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble peticon and appeal of the Church [...] [...] and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborne London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removall bearing date the 13th. day of March 1724 under the hand, of two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the sd City Anne Elwood the widow of John Elwood her late husband deced with then two Children Vizt. Wm. abt 8 years old & Anne 6 years Old or thereabouts are removed from St. Anns Black fryers London to St Andrew Holborne London as the place of their Legall Settlemt.

Therefore the sd. parish of St. Andrew Holborn do appeal agt the sd order & pray (in as much as they Are thereby agrieved) then the said Appeal may Receive a hearing and be argued by Councill

And your Petr as in Duty Bound Will ever pray
