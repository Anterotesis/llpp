To the Right Honble Sr. Samuel Stainer Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right worpll the Rest of her Majesties of the Peace for the same City in the Generall quarter Sessions assembled

1/2 1714 The humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Thomas ye Apposite London

Sheweth That one Mary Allen widdow by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majesties Justices of the peace within this city (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the first Day of February last past was removed from the parish of St Ann Black fryers London into the said parish of St Thomas the Apostle London alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal of the said Mary Allen

That your petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Allen never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Thomas the appostle whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Hour and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Ann Black fryers may appeare before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeale.

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
