To the Right Worpll Sr Richard Brocas Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City Assembled

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant or order under the hands & Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said City (quor unus) bearing date the 27th. day of this Instant June Sarah Henshaw aged about Seventeen yeares was passed from the peace of St. Andrew by the Wardrobe London to the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices Humbly appeale to your Worps for releife in the premises, And process that the hearing of the said Appeale may be adjoined to the next Genall qur Sessions of the peace for this City & that all Parties concerned may be ordered to attend then.

And Your Petrs Shall pray Etc

}Churchwardens
