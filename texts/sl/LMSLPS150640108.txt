To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in General Quarter Sessions at the Guild Hall of the said City.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Rt. Honble Crisp Gascoyne Lord Mayor of the Said City and Francis Cokayne Esqr two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of November last past Jane Tickeridge the wife of James Tickeriedge (who is run away from her) was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Alhallow the Great London to the said Parish of St Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

Whereby Your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same and pray to be relieved therein

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.

Thos. Huddle Wm. Witton Wm. Payne Thos. Mare} Overseers Churchwardens
