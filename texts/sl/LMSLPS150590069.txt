To the Right Honrble Sir Robert Ladbrock Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to thereof of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the Said City of their General Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Michael Bassishan London.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Ladbrok Knight and Robert Also & Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (One being of the Quorum) bearing doth the fifteenth day of June One thousand seven hundred and forty Eight Whereby Jane Price Single Woman was removed from the parish of Saint. Stephen in Coleman Street in the City of London aforesaid to the said parish of Sant Michael Bassisham London as the plane of her last Legal Settlement.

By which said Warrant or Order your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against of the same praying Such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall Seem meet.

And your petitioners as in Duly bound will pray

John Saunders Danil Waldern} Churchwardens

William Keene Wm. Kendrick} Overseers of the poor.
