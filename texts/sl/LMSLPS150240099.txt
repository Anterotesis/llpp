To the Rt. honoble. the Lord Mayor the Rt. Worppll the Recorder & the rest of her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

1713 24/4

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers for the poor of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant undr. the hands and seales of Sr. Peter King Knt. & Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. two of her Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. City of London quor) Unus) bearing date the 24th. day of Aprill 1713 Elizabeth Cave Singlewoman was past on yor Petrs. from St. Peters parish Cornhill as the Place of her last legall Settlement for that about 26. years agoe she was a hired Servant above on year in the parish of St. Brides London with William Weeks apothecary on Ludgate hill in the sd. Parish of St. Brides

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggreived by the ordr. of the sd. two Justices and humbly appeale therefrom to yor Honor. & Worpps

Therefore yor Petrs. humbly hope yor honor. & Worpps will hear yor Petr by then Cornhill agt the sd Order

And yor. Petrs Shall Pray Etc
