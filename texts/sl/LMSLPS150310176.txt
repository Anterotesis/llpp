To the Rt. Honble Sr. Geo: Thorold Bart. Lord Mayr & the rest of the Honble. Bench Now Sitting att Justice Hall in ye Old Bayly.

The humble prayer of Rose Smith in Newgate

Sheweth That yor petr. was Comitted to this place 29th. of Sepr. for Stealing from Mr Rupert Clarke a Diamond Ring of which fact She is Inocent Therefore Humbly prays that yor pet. may be tryed this Sessions or otherwise Discharged as to yor Lordsp. Shall Seem Meet
