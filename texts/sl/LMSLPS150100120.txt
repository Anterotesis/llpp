To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London & to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Dunstans Stepny in Midd.

Sheweth That Thomas Doe and Richard Doe the Sonnes of one Thomas Doe decd by Warrant from twoe Justices of the Peace dated 13th. day of June 1699 were brought by the Churchwardens of St. Gyles without Cripplegt. London to yor. Petrs. to be provided for on the Oath of one William Ray that their Mathews last legall settlement was in the said Parish of Stepny in Middx where he Served as an Appntice for about three yeares with one Mr. Hilton near Ratcliffe crosse whereas in truth the said Thomas & Richard Doe the children were bastards And not borne in their said Parish of Stepny

Their prayer is that yor Honor & Worpps would hear them on their Appeale And cause the said Church wardens of St. Gyles Cripplegt. to appear before you And to Quash the said Order And send the said Thomas & Richard Doe the Children backe To the said Parish of St. Gyles Cripplegate To be Pvided for according to the Lawe

And They shall Pray Etc.
