To the Rt: Honble Sr. George Merttins Knt: Ld Mayr: of the City of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Peticon of Quilt Arnold A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. was Comittee for that he had feloniously rescued from a Constable Roger Johnson a Convicted Felon knowing him to be such

Yor. Petr. humbly Prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be admitted to Baile or otherwayes to be discharged as this Honble Court shall think fit

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc

his marke Quilt [x] Arnold
