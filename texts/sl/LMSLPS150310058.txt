To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt & Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of Edward Filewood of Rygate in the County of Surry Higler.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of two of this Majesties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing Date the 15th day of March last Your Petitioner Adjudged to be the Putative father of a female bastard child born of the body of Ann Richardson Singlewoman in the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London and is become chargeable to the said parish.

That altho your Petitioner had Notice and did attend upon the said Justices at the time of making the said Order Yet Your Petitioner was [...] heard or examined in the premisses to defond himself against the said Charge

That by Vertue of the said Order Your Petitioner is directed to pay to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish the Sum of Â£9:2s:10d by them already expended in maintain [...] the said Bastard Child and other incident charges and the further Sum of 3s:6d Weekly from thenceforth So long as the said Child shall continue chargeable to the said parish

That Your Petitioner doubts not but to prove the said Charge to be false and Malitions and that the said Anne Richardson has upon Oath Charged another person to be father of the said Bastard Child before the making of the said Order, Whereby your Petitioner conceives himself aggrieved and dos most humbly Appeal to Your No [...] and Worps agt the sd Order.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray [...] Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear him in the premisses And that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St Giles with Cripplegte with the Said Ann Robinson may Attend before four Honr & Worps & hear & abide the Determinative of this Court touching this Appeal,

And he shall ever pray Etc
