To the Rt: honble: Sr: James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and other his Majties Justices of the peace Assembled in General Quarter Sessions of the peace held in and for the said City.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ Church London.

Humbly Sheweth That Mitchell Wheeler wife of John Wheeler and her Two Children were by Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majties Justices of the peace of the said City bearing date the Twenty third November last removed from the parish of St. Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Christ Church as the place of their legal Settlement.

That your Peticonr. humbly conceive [---] they can make it Appear to this Court that the last legal Settlement of the said Mitchell Wheeler and her Children is not in the said parish of Christ Church.

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray this Court will please to hear their said Appeal.

And yor. Peticonr. Shall ever pray Etc
