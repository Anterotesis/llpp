To the Right Honble St. John Ward Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Matys Justies of the peace of use Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

11/9 The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows in the Town of Stamford in the County of Lincoln.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majtys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Eleaventh day of September last Rith Whitby wife Willm Whitby now a prisoner in ye Fleet London with Willm Crawford Whitby her son aged abt Six weeks were sent & delivered by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of ye Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London unto the Chuchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of [...] Allhallows in the Town of Stamford in the County of Lincoln Alledging the sd Parish to be ye place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That your Petrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrant & Judgmt of ye sd two Justices and do th [...] humbly appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in ye premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the Parish of St Botolph witht Aldgate may appeal before yr honr & Worps and hear & abide & Determinacon of this Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
