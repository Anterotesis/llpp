To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall in the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Francis Cokayne Esqr Sr: Robert Kite Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (one of whom is of the Quorum) for the said City of London bearing date the Twenty ninth day of March One Thousand Seven hundred and Sixty five Abel [---] Trot was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopgate London to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the saw Abel Trot whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

Joshua Box Solr. for the Appellts Therefore they Humbly Appeal to this Honble Court and Pray that the said Court will be pleased to Order that the Church- Warden and Oversees of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Botoph Bishopgate and all persons concerned do Attend this Court some day in the next Sessions of the Peace for the said City to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determine of the said Court touching she said Appeal And that the Benefit of hearing the said Appeal at that Sessins may be saved to your petitioners

And Yr. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
