To the Right Honble. Thos. Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor, the Recorder and the rest of this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church-wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Thos. Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the said City and William Alexander Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof (whereof one is of he Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of April 1754 Ann Reynolds Aged Thirteen Years was Removed from the Parish of Saint Magnus in the said City to the sd. Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners and Appellants Apprehend themselves to be Aggriesed by the said Order of the said two Justices and do Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a Day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Magnus in the said City and all other persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide by the Judgment and Determinating of the same touching this Appeal And that your Petitioners and Appeallants may be Relieved as this Court shall be meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Billington Attorney for Appellts.
