To the Right Honoble. William Benn Esqr. Lord Mayor and the Right Worshipfull the Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and the liberties thereof in their general session assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands Seals of the Right Honoble. William Benn Esqr. and Francis Cokayne Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the said City and the liberties thereof bearing Date the Twenty first Day of July last Elizabeth Webb widow of Rice Webb Deced whose settlement (is alleged by the said order to be unknown was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Catherine Coleman London into the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate aforesaid as the place of her last legal settlement

Your Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order humbly appeal to this court against the same and pray such relief in the premes as this Honoble. Court shall seem meet

James Clare Sollr. for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate
