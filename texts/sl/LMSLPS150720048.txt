To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Majesties Justices of Oyer and Terminer and Goal Delivery of Newgate Holden for the said City

The Humble Petition of John Perrott

Shewth. That your Petitioner Stands Committed of his Majesties Goal of Newgate for Felony Expressed in the Warrant, of which your Petitioner is Intirely Innocent

Your Petitioner most humbly Prays this Honrble Court that he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged this Sessions According to the form of the Statute in that Case made and Provided

And your Petitioner will for ever Pray

John Perrott
