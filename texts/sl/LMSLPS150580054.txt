To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Popler and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable William Benn Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and Edward Davies Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of December in the Year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and forty Six Alice Cockeram singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London aforesaid to the Hamlet of Popler and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

By which said Warrant or Order your petitioners conceive themselves agrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the pemisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your petitionrs. will Ever pray Etc

John Brown Church Warden

John Smith} Overseers of the Poor
