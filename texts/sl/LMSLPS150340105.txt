To the Right Honble Sr:Gerard Conyers Knt: Ld: Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the other of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrt: or Order under ye hands and Seales of said Sr: Gerard Conyers and Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt: Two of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye City of London (Quor Un) bearing date the Eighteenth day of June last Sarah the Widow of Henry Corney Deced with her Three Children Edith Sarah and Ebiezer were Removed from ye Parish of St: Katherine Coleman London unto ye said parish of St: Nicholas Colebby as ye place of their last legall Settemt:

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by ye said Warrt: or Order of ye said Two Justices Doe humbly Appeale to your Worshipps there from And pray that you will be pleased to hear them in ye premes And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of ye said Parish of St: Katherine Coleman may appeare before you at a certain day to hear and abide ye Judgemt: and Determination of your Worpps touching this Appealety

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
