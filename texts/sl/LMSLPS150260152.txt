To the Right Honrble Sr. Willm Humfryes Kt. and Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worppll the Rest of his Matys Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Geoville als will in the County of Somerset

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrt. under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quon) bearing Date the Twenty first day of March last past One Edward Brockay and Charity his Wife with their two Children Willin about Nineteen Years Old And Mary about Eleaven Years Old Were Sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said Parish of Yeoville als Evill in the County of Somerset Alledging the said Parish to be the Place of their last Legall Settlement.

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrt. and Judgment of ye said two Justices And to most humbly Appeal to your Honours and Worppl. therefrom

Your Petitoners most humbly pray your Honour and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate may appear before you and hear & abide the Determination of Yr Honour & Worppr. touching this Appeale

And they Shall ever Pray Etc
