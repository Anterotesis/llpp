To the Rt Honrble Sr William Stewart Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That Mary Watts the Daughter of Timothy Watts and Elizabeth his wife is left in the said parish of St Giles without cripplegate and is already become a great charge thereunto and unless timely prevented is likely so to continue, which said Father and mother of the said Mary Watts are both dead But one Edward Buxton of the said parish Distiller and John Moore of the parish of St Olave Hartstreet London Winercoper are Grand fathers of the said Mary Watts and either of them persons of Sufficient Ability to maintain her, and not to burthen your Petitioners with the charge thereof which said Edward Buxton and John Moore being required by your petitionr to maintain their said Grandchild according to the direction of the statute in that case made and provided do altogether refuse and neglect so to do

Your Petitionrs & therefore most humbly pray your Honr & and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in they prmisses and make such Order therein as to your Honr & and Worpps Shall seem meet

And Your Petrs. & shall ever Pray Etc
