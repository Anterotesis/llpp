To the right honourable St. William Humfreys Knight & Baronet Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the right worll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace in the generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That one Esther the wife of Michael Warren with Maria her daughter aged three years or thereabouts by Vertue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of this peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing date the 20th. day of July now last past were removed from the parish of St. Bartholomew the great London to the Parish of St. Sepulchers London alleadging the said Parish is the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Esther Warren & Maria her daughter

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove that the said Esther Warren & Maria her daughter had no legall Settlement in the parish of St. Sepulchers London aforesd. whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices and Do humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worships thereupon.

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray your Honour & Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premises & order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bartholomew the great London to hear & abide the determinacon of your Honour & Worships touching this appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

John Ashbridge Chwarden Jno Edden Ch.Warden.
