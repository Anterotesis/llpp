20/9 To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the said for the City of London of their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Hellen for the sd. City

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Int. at Hackney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the twentieth day of Septemr last under the Hands & Seals of Sr. William Thomson & Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. to of his Majesties Justices of the come for the City of London (Quorum unus) Sarah Bartrum Wife of Joseph Bartrum with Joseph their Child aged about two Years were passed from the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of Hackney in the sd. County of Middx: as the place of the last Legall Settlement by which sd. Order your Petitioners Cameives themselves Aggrieved & Humbly Appeal against the same Praying such releif in the Premisses as to Your Worship is shall Seem Just

And yr Petitionrs. shall ever Pray
