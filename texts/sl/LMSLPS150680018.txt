To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices in the General Session of Goal Delivery for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition of William Adams a Prisoner in Newgate.

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the fifteenth day of February Instant was committed to His Majesty's Goal of Newgate for the said City and County of Middlesex by John Fielding Esqr One of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County being charged before him on the Oaths of Samuel Baldwin and Humphrey Bache for feloniously Publishing as true in the said City a certain false forget and counterfeited Receipt for the Payment of Money for Over Entry of Wines in his Majesty's Custom house in the Port of London and thereby defrauding his said Majesty of the sum of two Hundred and fifty pounds and upwards contrary to the Statute &

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that at this present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City of London He may be either tried (Bailed or discharged for the Felony aforesaid Pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and Provided.

William Adams
