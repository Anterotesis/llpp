Jon. Thompson pet

To the right Honble. Sr. Edward Clarke Knt Lord Mayr: for the Citty of London & to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Court

Sheweth The humble Peticon of John Thompson a Prisonr. in Newgate

That yor peticonr: is accused for picking of a pockett when in Truth he is noe wayes guilty of the Same Your peticonr. haveing wittnesses at this Sessions to prove his innocency

Therefore yor: peticonr humbly prayes he may be tryed at this Prsent Sessions and that his prayer may be Entered accordingly as to yor Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc

And yor: peticonr: shall pray Etc.
