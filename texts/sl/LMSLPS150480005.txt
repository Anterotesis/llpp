To the Right Honrble. Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of the Honble. Bench at Justicehall in the Old Bayly.

The Humble Prayer of Richard Eves & Margaret his wife

Humbly Sheweth. That your Supplicants were Committed to his Majestys Goal of Newgate the 11th. of December last on Suspicion of being Concerned in the Murder of one Captain Innys. of which fact your Supplicants are altogether Innocent.

Your Supplicants Therefore humbly Pray That they May be either Tried this Sessions or Discharged out of Custody

And Your Supplicants as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc.

14 January 1736.

Richd. Eves

The Marke of Margt. [x] Eves
