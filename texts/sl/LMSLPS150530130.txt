To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Gent Quarter Session of they [---] Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant for Order under the hand & Seals of the Rt. Honble. Sr. Robert Godschall Knt. Mayor of the said City of London and George Arnold Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City aforesaid bearing date the Second day of March 1741 Sarah Brown Singlewoman and [...] Parish of Sr. Sepulcher London to the Parish of Sr. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court Against the same please to grant [...] such relief in the

Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet And Your Petitionrs. will ever pray Etc

S. Parry Sollr for Appellants
