4/3 To the Rt. Honble: Humphrey Parsons, Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City assembled at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said City at Guild Hall.

The Humble Petition of he Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Bromley in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That By Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the said Rt. Honoble the Lord Mayor of the City of London & Sr. Robert Baylis Knight, two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor un) bearing date on or about the fourth Day of March last Samuel Osborne & Elizabeth his wife, and their son Joseph aged about Seventeen Years were Removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Leonard Bromley as the place of their last Legal settle- -ment, whereby Your Petitioners most Humbly conceive they are Wronged and Aggrievely.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly prays that this Court will be pleased to hear and Determine their Appeal this present Sessions, And that they may Obtain such Relief in the Premises as to your Wisdoms shall seem Meet

And Your Petrs shall Pray Etc.
