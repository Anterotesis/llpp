To The Right Honble Sir Francis Child Knight Lord Mayor and The Rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the Citty of London at their Quarter Sessions Assembled

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petitionr. Freemen of this Citty haveing been Severally Imployed by Daniel Holborough Thomas Hannam George Davis Senr. Benjn. Wilkinson Benjamine Barnes Joseph Taverner John Yerbury George Davis Junr. Willm. Cooper William Farrer & others as Journeymen Cloathworkers in the most Laborious Branch of the Woollen Manufactrue for a Considerable time and for wages so Inconsiderable as readers them Unable to Support themselves & Familys Insomuch that they will soon be reduced to beggary unless timely assisted.

Your Peticonr. therefore humbly pray your LordShips & Worships will be pleased to take their Case into Consideracon and enforce the Severall Statutes made for setling the Wages of Labourers and Artificers that thereby their said Masters may be compelled to give them a reasonable allowance for their Work, or to give them such releife as to this Court shall seem Just

And your Peticonr. Shall ever pray Etc

George Powell Rodert Bigg Ant Adams. Richard Sheperd Thomas Hodgson, Richard Platt his mark [x] Osmond Jones his mark [x] Lodwick Loyd Do [x] Benjamin Varney do [x] Francis Watson John Tomson Joseph Straccon Edward Stone Thomas Leimar Robt Young Tho: Kibbell

William Peal
