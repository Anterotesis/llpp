To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richards Levett Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the severall parishes of St. Gyles Cripplegate St. Buttolph without Aldgate St. Andrewes Wardrobe St. Katherine Colamen St. Andrews holbourne St. Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the wall St. Buttolph Aldengate And St. Buttolph without Bishoppgate

Sheweth

That yor. Petrs. are very much over burthened with Poore and nott able to Raise Sufficient Sumes of money amongst themselves to Releive them Notwithstanding their Assessments are greater than therefore Yett the Charge of their poore is farr above their said Assessements As the Same upon full Examination hath many times appeared to divers Aldren of the Citty appointed to heare the same and their poore daily Encreasing upon them Their said Parishes lyeing some of them neare to the Suberbes and others of them in the Subcriber of this Citty

Their Humble Suites therefore unto yor. good honor Worspps. is that you will be favourable pleased to graunt unto them the Contribucons of other Parishes within this Citty for and towards the Releiving their poore As They have formerly had which are particularly hereunder menconed or such further Allowance As to yor. Honr. Worpps. shall Seeme meete

St Cyles without Cripplegate £90 St. Buttolph without Aldgate £50 St Andrew Wardrobe £40 St. Katherine Coleman £12 St Andrews holbourne £7 St Sepulchers £44 St Brides £15 Alhallowes on the wall 12 St. Buttolph Aldersgate 15 St. Buttolph Bishoppsgate 6o
