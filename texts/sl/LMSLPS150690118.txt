To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Court of General Quarter Sessions for the said City at the Guild Hall of the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent.

Sheweth Unto this Honble Court That by Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands & Seals of Sir Charles Asgill Knight and Sir Robert Ladbrooke Knight bearing Date the Fourteenth day of March last William Melton and Mary his Wife were Removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent as the Place of their last Legal Settlement by which We think our Selves Aggrieved, and Appeal to this Court against the said Order.

We therefore your Petitioners Pray this Honble Court will be pleased to Grant an Order for all Parties to Attend this Court in Order to hearing the Complaint & Defence of both Parties by Evidence and to make such further order thereon as the Court shall think proper

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc

Richd. Hopkins Richd. Harford} Church Wardens

Earl Newland} Overseers of the Poor
