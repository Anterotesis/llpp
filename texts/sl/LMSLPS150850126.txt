To the Right Honourable The Lord Mayor and others His Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London at the Quarter Session of the peace Assempled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Matfelon otherwise White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of The Right Honourable Frederick Bull Lord Mayor of the City of London and John Wilkes Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fifth day of June 1774 Elizabeth Revell and Mary her Bastard Child aged one Year who is with her for Nurture were removed from the parish of Saint Olave Silver Street in the City of London to the parish of Saint Mary at White Chapel in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Revell

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Coverly for the Appellts.
