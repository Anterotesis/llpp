To the Right Honourable Sir John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Margaret Lothbury London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof one being of the Quorum Elizabeth Washington was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopgate London to the said parish of Saint Margaret Lothbury Alledging that to be the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order.

Your Petitioners do therefore appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom and humbly pray to be heard in the Premisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopgate may appear before year to hear and abide the Judgemetn and Determination of your [...] Lordship and Worships touching this Appeal.
