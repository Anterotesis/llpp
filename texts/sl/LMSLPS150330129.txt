To the Right Worshipfull Sr. William Stewart Lord Major Sr. William Thompson Knight Recorder and other of his Matys Justices of the peace for the City of London at their Genall Quarter Sessions now assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of John Ralphs

Sheweth That your Petitioner is an Inhitant of the Parish of St. Ann Black Fryars within this City & by the Rates for the releife of the Poor for the said parish your Petitioner is asesed for two Houses & hath hitherto paid for them altho' one of the said Houses is tell by Lease & occupied by a proper Tenant By wch sd. Rate your Petitioner conceives himselfe to be aggrieved & humbly appealy therefrom

Wherefore your Petitioners prays that the may be heard & Releived in the prmises & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish may attend & abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the same

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
