To the honoble: the Lord Mayor and to the Right Worpll: the rest of her Majts: Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London at their Generall Quarter Session at Guildhall London.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the ChurchWardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth. That by an order bearing date the Fifteenth day of June last under the hands and Seales of your Lorp and Sr. Jno Parsons two of her Majts. Justices of the peace for the said Citty. one Jonathan Large upon man was removal from the parish of St. Bartholomew the less London to your petrs said Parish. whereby your Petrs. humbly Conceived they are wronged & aggrievell

Yor. Petrs: therefore humbly appeale to your Worps. and pray your Worps to hear and determine the same

And yor: Petrs. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
