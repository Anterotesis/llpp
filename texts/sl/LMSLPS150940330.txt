To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and other his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City London

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Nathl. Newnham and Barnd. Turner Esqr. [...] date the 5th day of July 1783 Sarah Wood Singlewoman and her Illegitimate Child named Elizabeth are [...] removed and conveyed from and our of the Parish of Jane Botolph Bishopgate in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Wood

Saml. Malbon Sollr. for the said Parish of Shadwell
