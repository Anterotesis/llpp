1724 To the right honble. Sr. Peter Delme Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (Quorn unus) bearing date the 21st. day of July last one Hannah Hodges Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Said Parish of St. Sepulchres London Adjudgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Hannah Hodges

That your Petitioners doubt not to make it appear that the said Hannah Hodges had no legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London Whereby they Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices

And do humbly appeal to your honour and Worships therefrom and Pray that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate may hear and abide the determinacon of this honble. Court touching this Appeal

Richd: Hewett Joshua. Wallfree} Church Wardens

Joseph Sterry Edward Coster} Oveseers
