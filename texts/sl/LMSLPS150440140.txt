To the Right Honorable ye Lord Mayor and others his Majesties Justices Peace for ye City of London Assembled in Quarter Sessions

This humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under ye hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of ye Peace for the Citty of London and ye Libertys thereof (Quor Unus) bearing Date ye 25th Day of October last Sarah Rosengrave Widw. was removed from ye Parish of St. Giles Without Cripplegate London to ye Sd. Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to ge Agreived

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray your Worshipps wd. be pleased to appoint some day this Present Sessions to hear & determine the matter touching this Appeale.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

We have given Notice of this Appeale
