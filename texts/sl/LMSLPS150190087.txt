To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Gyles in Reading in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of Her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London one being of the Quor dated the 10th. day of December Anno Dm 1707. Our Elizabeth Harpud late wife of John Harpud with John Harpud her Seine was by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Barthll. the Greate London sent to yor. Petr. to be pvided for and maintained upon the Oath of one Robert Longbottome That the last legall Settlement of John Harpud decd was in yor. Petrs. parish where he was an Apprentice by Indenture to and lived without Moses Willis A Collar maker dwelling in London Streete in yor Petrs. Parish Whereas in Truth the Said John Harpud Decd never was bound an appntice by Indenture to the said Moses Willis

Wherefore yor Petrs. humbly pray yor. Honor. & Worpps. to hear them in the Prmisses And Quash the said Warrant made by the said twoe Justices And Order the Said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Barthll. the greate to Appeare here before you To shew Cause why they should not provide for and mainteyne the said Elizabeth Harpud and her some And alsoe send the said Elizabeth Harpud and her some to the said Parish of St. Barthll. the Great To be by them Provided for and maintained untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And They Shall Ever Pray Etc
