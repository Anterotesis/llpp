To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the several Persons whose Names are hereunto Subscribed, Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London.

Sheweth That An Assessment has been lately made upon the Inhabitants of the said Parish for the Relief of the Poor, by which Assessment your Petitioners apprehend that they are Charged with the payment of greater Sums for the Relief of the Poor than they ought to be In regard that Parnell Nevill, Richard Shrapnell, John Adlam, Ebenezer Sull John Webber, Joseph Stone, John Lattimer and Daniel Waldron, Inhabitants of the said Parish who are Householders and ought as your Petitioners conceive to be assessed to the Payment of the Poors Rate of the said Parish, are not Rated to the said Assessment whereby your Petitioners are respectively Rated more than their Just proportion to the said Assessment.

Wherefore In regard the said Several Inhabitants abovenamed are Omitted in the said Assessment, And that your Petitioners by reason of such Omission are rated to the said Assessment for the Relief of the Poor more than ought to be, Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Assessment and Pray that the same may be Quashed, On that your Petitioners may has such other Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships upon Hearing shall seem Just and reasonable

And your Petitioners shall Ever Pray, Etc

Francis Cooper Jno Whiteham Robert Marsh Wm: Baker Henry Smart Richd Yerbury Wm. Rogers Sam. Nicholson
