To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session at the Guild Hall in and for the said City assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble. Steph. Theod: Janssen and Wm. Alexander Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing Date the 28th. day of August 1755 Thomas Bartly was removed from the Parish of St. Brides London to the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Jno. Hampton Attorney for ye Appellts.} Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the matter of the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
