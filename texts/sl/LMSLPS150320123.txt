To the Rt. Honrble Sr. John Fryer Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Garlickhithe in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quorm unus) bearing date the twenty third day of January last past, Elizabeth the wife of Christopher Denton was removed from the parish of St Nicholas Cole abby London unto the said Parish of St James Garlickhithe London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And pray That your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Nicholas Cole abby may appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.
