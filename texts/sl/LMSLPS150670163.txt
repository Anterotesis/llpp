To the Right Honble Marshe Dickinson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rest of the Honble Bench in their Generall Session of Goal Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Coe a Prisoner

Sheweth That on the Twentieth Day of November last Your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to his Majestys Prison of Newgate London for Feloniously Buying and Receiving Two Parcells of Shalleck the Quantity of Seventy Pounds and Upwards of the Value of Forty Shillings and Upwards Stollen out of the Lighte is in the River of Thomas knowing the same to be Stollen And now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate aforesaid under the same Charge

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays That at this present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate Holden for the CIty of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed Bailee a Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case Made and Provided

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc.

John Coe
