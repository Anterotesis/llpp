To the Rt. Honble St. William Stewart Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpt the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

23/8 The humble petition and Appeal of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate London

Sheweth That by a pass Narrt. or Order under the hands & seals of Two of his Maties Justies of the Peace which the City of London (Quard unus) bearing date the Twenty third day of August last past That Richd Collins wth. Sarah his Wife were removed from the parish of St. Buttolph wth.out Aldersgate London unto yer. Petr. parish of St. Martins Ludgate London alledging that yer Pets. parish is the place of their last legal Settlement your Peth. therefore conceiving themselves aggreived by the said warrant & judgment of the said Two Justices Humbly Appeal to yor. Honors & Worpps. for redress therein

And humbly pray you'l he pleased to hear them touching the prmises and order the Church wardens & overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Buttolph wth.out Aldersgate to Appear before you to hear & abide the judgment & determination of yor. Honor. & Worpss. upon this their Appeal

And yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
