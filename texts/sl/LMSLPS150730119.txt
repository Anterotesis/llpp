To the right honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace in & for the same City at their general Quarter Session of the peace now assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sir Samuel Fludyer Barronet Lord Mayor of the City of London and Francis Cokayne Esquire Alderman two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty sixth day of February last past Rebecca Robinson wife of Motley Robinson (who is therein said to be gone from her) was lately removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the said Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Middlesex

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the peace appeal to your Worships against the said Order and humbly desire the Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Hearing & determining hereof and make such Order in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc:

By Order of the Overseers of the Hamlet of Ratcliffe }

Jon. Seacome Agent for the Appellants
