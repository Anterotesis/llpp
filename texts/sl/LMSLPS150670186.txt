To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of or the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Bassishaw in London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of October in the year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and fifty five Deborah Mathews Widow of Herbert Mathews Deceased was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall in London aforesaid into the said Parish of Saint Michael Bassishaw

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved thereby Do humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and Pray Such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem most meet.

Benjamin Walker Church Warden
