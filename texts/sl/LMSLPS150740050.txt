To the Right honourable William Beckford Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices assigned to deliver his Majesties Gaol of of Newgate on the Prisoners therein being for the City of London and County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Francis Parsons

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the Tenth day of June last past was by a warrant under the hand and Seal of Sr. John Fielding Knt. Comitted to his Majesties Gaol of Newgate being Charged on the Oath of Richard Hulier and others for feloniously stealing and Carrying away one Bank note of the value of Fifty pounds one Ditto of the value of Twenty five pounds one Ditto of the value of Ten pounds and one tod Morotto Pocket Book and other things the property of Joseph Sheppard

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays that he may be Tried at this present Sessions for the offence with which he stands Charged as aforesaid or in default thereof he may be Bailed or Discharged for the same and your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Francis Parsons
