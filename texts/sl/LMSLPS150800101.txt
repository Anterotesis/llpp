To the Right Honble Samuel Turner Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Brethren Justices of the Peace for the said City of London at their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdulen Old fish Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City (One being of the Quirum Dated the Twenty fifth Day of March last Sarah Brookes Singlewoman did was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulcher in the said City to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Old Fish Street as the Place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Humble Court

Pray Predress herein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray etc
