To the Right Honourable Marche Dickinson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the Rest of the Honble Bench in their Quarter Session of Goal Delivery Assembled.

The Humble Petition and prayer of Matthew John A Prisoner

Sheweth That on the Twenty First Day of September last year Petitioner was Committed Prisoner together with One James Sleep to Clerkenwell, Bridewell in the County of Middlesex by John Fielding Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County as Oath of William Backhouse for Mrs Prision of High Treason in Counterfeiting a Certain Piece of Gold Coin called a Moidore And now is a Prisoner can fined in Newgate under the same Charge

[...] Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly Prays That at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Care Made and Provided.

And your Petitioner Shall Pray Etc

Matthew Johnson
