To the Right Honble. Sir William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worshipfull the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Margarett Pattons London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the nineteenth Day of February one thousand seven hundred and thirty three Elizabeth Gravener the Wife of Manister Gravener (whose Husband hath left, her and her two Children to witt Ann aged two Yeares and upwards and Elizabeth Six Months or thereabouts were passed from the Parish of Saint Buttolph without Aldgate to the said parish of Saint Margarett Pattons as the Place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Order and Judgmt. of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this honble Court for Releife in the Premises

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Wern Anthy. Mett} Church: Wardens
