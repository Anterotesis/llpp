To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poore of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very Poore And not able to raise sufficient Sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them Notwithstanding their Assessements are greate then heretofore Yett the charge of their Poore is above their said Assessement told the same upon full Examinacon hath many times Appeared to divers Aldremen of this City appointed to heare the same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them the said Parish lyeing in the Suborbs of the City.

Their humble Suites therefore to yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is that you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contribution of Â£15. from other Parishes wth. in this City for & towardes three haveing their Poore As they have formerly had by reason of their greate charge & number of Poore Or such further a Allowance as to yor. Honors. & Worpps. shall [...] seeme meete.

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
