To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Genl. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled at the Guildhall London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Saviours Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir Willm. Stephenson Knight Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of May last William Williams Isabella his Wife and their two Children William Aged Two years and Benjamin Aged One Year were Removed and Coneyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Helen near Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Saint Saviours Southwark as the Place of their Last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceived themselves Aggrived by the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the said Appeal may be heard and determined as this present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace and that your Petitioners may be relieved against the said Order

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
