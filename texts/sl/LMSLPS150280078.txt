To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. James Bateman Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London & to the Rt. Worpll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace at the Genll. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter le Poore London.

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 23th. of October last under the hands & Seales of Sr. Charles Peers Knts. late Lord Major of the Citty of London & Sr. William Withers Knt. & Alderman of the said Citty Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quorum Unus) One Rebeccah. Paine & John Paine her Son were passed from the parish of St. Mary Stayning London to the said parish of St. Peter le Poore as the place of theire last legall Settlement.

That the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of She said parish of St. Peter le Poore Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order And therefore Appeale from the same to this Honoble. Court & humbly pray to bee relieved against the said Order.
