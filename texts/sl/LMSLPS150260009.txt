To the Right Honrble: the Ld: Major the Justice & other of the Honrble Bench Siting att Justice Hall in the Old Bayliff London

The Humble Peticon of Robt: Tiping Prisoner in Newgate Comited upon his own confescon of haveing two wives both being Liveing

Humbly Prayeth yr Peticoner may be Brought to his tryall for the Same this Sessions or be Admitted to Bayle or other Wife Discharged as to this Honrble: Bench shall seem most meet & ye Peticoner as in Duty Bound Shall Ever pray Etc:

23o Febry 1714
