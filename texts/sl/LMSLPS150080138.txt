To the Right Honble Sr. Edward Clarke Knt. Lord Mayor: for the Citty of London And to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Court

Sheweth The humble peticon of John Willis als Powell a prisonr. in Newgate

That yor. peticonr.is accused for High Treason in Counterfeiting the Currant coyne of this Kingdome And your peticonr. haveing Wittnesses here in Court to prove his Junocemy

Therefore most humbly prayes he may be tryed this present Sessions And that his prayer may be Entered accordingly As to yor Lordpps shall seem meet Etc

And yor. peticonr. Shall Ever pray Etc

pet fo Jno. Willis als Powell
