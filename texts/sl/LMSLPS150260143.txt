To the Right Worll: his Mats: Justices of the peace for the City of London sitting at Guild Hall

The Humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of Bromley St. Leonards in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Whereas Rebecca Daniells was by order or Warrant Dated the first day of February last under the Hands & Seales of Sr: Wm: Humfrey Barrt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & Sr: Wm Withers Knt: two of his Matie: Justices of the peace for the said City & Liberties thereof (Quor un) Removed from the Parish of St Mary Colechurch London to the said Parish of Bromley St. Leonards in the County of Middx aforesaid.

Yor: Petrs. finding themselves aggreived by the said order of the said two Justices Appeale to this Honoble. Court against the same & pray that they may be releived in the premises.

And Yor: Petrs. Shall pray Etc

Wm. Kinson Elias Russell Ed Benson} Churchwardens Overseers

The Rice} Ovrseers of ye poor
