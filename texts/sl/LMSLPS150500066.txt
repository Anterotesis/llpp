To The Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City [...] in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pari [...] Saint James in Dukes P [...] London

Sheweth That Grace Cue Singlewoman was by Order or Pass Warrant dated the tenth day of this Instant October under the hands & seals of you the said Micajah Perry Esquire & of Sir John Barnard Knight two of his Majestys Justices of the said City & Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate unto the said Parish of Sr. James in Dukes Place.

That your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Appeal to this Honourable Court for such Relief in the Parish as this Court shall think proper.

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.
