To the Rt. Honble St. John Ward Knt. Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The humble peton and appeal of the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the Pish of Allhallows the Less London.

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrt. under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Sixth Day of June last past Elizabeth Walker a Female infant were sent and delivered by the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Dionis Backchurch London unto the said Pish of Allhallows the Less London alledging the sd pish to be the place of its last Legal Settlement.

That your petrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt & Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps with be pleased to hear them in ye premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the sd Pish of St. Dionis Backchurch may appear before you and hear & Abide the Determinacon of your Honr & Worps touching the said Appeal.

And they shall over pray Etc.
