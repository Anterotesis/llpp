To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Sepulcher in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Wilkes Esqr. Mayor of the City of London and Frederick Bull Esqr one of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London bearing date the 28th: day of January 1775 William Garfield Symmonds a Lunatic and Catherine his Wife were removed and Consused from and out of the parish of Saint James at Garlickhith as the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre as the place of their legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass warrant of the said Mayor and Jurtice Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the came may be heard [...] neet. Sessions and that a time may be now Appointed for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will were pray Etc
