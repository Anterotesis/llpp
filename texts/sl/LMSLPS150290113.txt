To the right honble. Sr. John Ward Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the right worshipfull the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of St. Martin Ludgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing date the twenty (second day of October now last past One Elizabeth the Wife of James Rivers was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew in Holborne London, to the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate London alleadging the said Parish is the places of the last legal Setlement of the said Elizabeth Rivers

That your Petitoners doubt not but to prove that the said Elizabeth Rivers had no legall Setlement in the said Parish of St. Martins Ludgate London Whereby they Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Honour and Worships thereupon

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Pray your Honour & Worships will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrewb in Holborne London to hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honour and Worships touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners shall even Pray Etc

John Badcock Ch: Warden John Lyons. Ch: Warden
