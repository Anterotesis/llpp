To the Right Honourable Theodore Jansen Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate in Session Assembled

Sheweth The Humble Petition of Benjamin Jones a prisoner in Newgate

That your Petitioner Stands Indicted for a Rape Sworn to be Committed on the Body of one Sarah Robinson

Your Petitioner Therefore humbly prays to be Tryed Bailed or Discharged at this Present Session pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray

Benj: Jones
