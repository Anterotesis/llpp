To the Rt Honrble Sr Peter Delme Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: The rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

18/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace fro the City of London (Quorn Unus) bearing date the Eighteenth day of November last past Richard Woods with Mary his wife and Elizabeth their Child were removed from the Parish of St Andrew Holborn London unto the said parish of St Giles without Criplegate London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And pray That your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwarden and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn may appear before you at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgments Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
