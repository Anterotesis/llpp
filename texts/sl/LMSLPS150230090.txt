To the Right Honrble: Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the General quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Gregory by St Pauls London

Sheweth That one John Austin and Margarett his wife by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Thirteenth Day of November last pass was removed from the parish of St. Sepulchre London unto the said parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said John Austin

That your Petitioner: doubt not but to prove that the said John Austin never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St Gregory by St Pauls whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Sepulchre may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
