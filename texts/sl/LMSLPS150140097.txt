To the Rt. Honoble. Sr Samuell Dashwood Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to ye. Rt Worll. Her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of Samuell Fleming Arthur Bunyon Thomas Muscatt Daniell Peacock John Bellass Thomas Kirby Thomas Waggett William Syms John Guy Richard Fellowes Wm. Jones Constables in the ward of the Bridge London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. have Executed the said Office in the said parish ever sicne the beginning of January last And liveing in the said Ward neer the Bridge gate which is Adjoyning to southwarke in the County of Surry have passed and Conveyed many Psons sent to them by divers Warrants through this City according to the late Act of Pariliament in such be halfe And yor. Petrs haveing expended already severall sinues of Money in conveying them the wright this City

And there being noe such person as a high Constable in London to receive or have any publick moneyes in his hands on the Accompts of Gaols & March also a money mentioned in he said Act to Remburse yor. Petrs the moneyes by them Expended as aforesaid And the Justices in their Quarter Sessions being impowered by the said Act to doe the same

Their humble suite unto yor. good Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be pleased to Order such reimbursemt. of the Charged expended & to be Expended by then in such manner As to yor. Honor. & Worpps. shall seeme meete

And they shall pray Etc
