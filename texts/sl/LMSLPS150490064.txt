To the Right Honble Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor and she [...] rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions held in & for the same City at the Guild hall of the Said City on the tenth day of April in the year of our Lord 1738.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Willsdon in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of the [...] Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor and Sir Thomas Lombe Knight and one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the Said City Jane Story Widow and Elizabeth Story were removed from the said Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street to the said Parish of Willsdon.

That yor Petrs. conceive the said Parish of Willsdon agrieved by the said Order and do appeal thereof and humbly pray [...] your Lordship will be pleased to appoint such time for the hearing this Appeal as to your Lordship shall seem meet.

And your Petitrs shall pray.

Thomas Niccoll Churchwarden Daniel Wes [...] Overseer
