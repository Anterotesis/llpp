To the Rt Honrble Sr Samuel Hanier Knt Lord major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of her Majestys Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Edmond the King London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quorn bearing late the fifth day of November last past one Margaret Reynolds was removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said parish of St Edmond the King alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Margaret Reynolds never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Edmond the King whereby they conceive themselves aggreved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear abide the Order of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Petitionrs: Shall Ever Pray
