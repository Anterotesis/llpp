London To the Right Honble. Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of the Right Worshipfull the Aldermen his Bretheren in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace in & for ye. sd. City of London Assembd.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London on behalf of themselves & the rest of ye Parishioners of ye. sd Parish

Humbly Sheweth That Ann Bumford aged three years & upwards the Daughter and Child of John Bumford now of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London be gotten on the body of Mary Magdalen Bumford his Wife was (by a certain order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Thomas Moore & Nicholas Jeffreys Esqrs. Justices of the peace in & for the County of Middx. one being of the Quorum bearing Date ye. 24th. Day of March 1742) removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London as the Place of her last Legal Settlement wth sd. order or Pass Warrt. afterwards to wit at the General Quarter Session of th Peace held for the County of Middx. by Adjournmt. at Hicks hall in St. John Street in the sd County on Thursday ye. 14th. Day of April 1743 was by the sd. Court Ratifyed & Confirmed That the sd. Ann Bumford was at the time of such Removal & hath ever Since continued & now [...] is a poor & Impotent Person & not able to work to relieve & maintain herself and hath been for all the time aforesaid & still is maintained at the Expence of your Petitioners That the sd John Bumford the Father is aparishioner of the sd Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate & of Sufficient Ability to Relieve & maintain such his sd Child but he hath refused & still doth refuse so to do

Your Petr. therefore humbly Appeal to this Honble. Court & Pray that ye. sd. John Bumford may be ordered to pay so much money to your Petrs. as they have necessarily expended in the maintainance of ye sd. Ann Bumford & also theif Costs in this Application & that such further order may be made by this Honble. Court upon the sd John Bumford for the future maintainance of the sd Child as to this Hoble. Court shall seem meen pursuant to the Statute in that Case made & Provided & that your Petrs. may have such other Relief in the Premes. as this Court shall think just And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

Willm. Salt Sollr. for ye. Applts.
