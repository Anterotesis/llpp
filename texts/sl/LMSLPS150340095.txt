To the Rt Honrble Sr Gerrard Conyers Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churhcwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of st. Bartholomew the great London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorn Unus) bearing date the 24 Day of April last past Elizabeth Roberts wife of William Roberts was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn London unto the parish of St. Bartholomew the great London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Peter humbly comening themselves aggrieved by the said warrant and Judgment of the said her Justices do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps there four And pray that your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisces And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Andrew Holborn may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Honr and Worpp touching this Appeal

And Yor Petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
