London ss: 17/9 To the Right Honble St. Wm. Stewart Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worptfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liblies thereof in their Generall Quarter sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Stephens Coleman street London

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majty's & Justices of the Peace for the said City of London the Libties thereof (Quord unus) bearing Date the seventeenth Day of September last past John Leach with Grace his Wife & their three Children vizt Sarah aged Eight years William aged three years and John aged Six Weeks were removed from the Parish of St. Botoloph without Bpsgate London unto the said Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street London alledging the said Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street to be the Please of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said & Warrant or order of the said Two Justices Do humbly appeale to your Lordship and Worps therefrom, & pray that your Lordship & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Prmisses And that the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worps touching this Appeal

And yrs. Petts. Shell pray Etc.
