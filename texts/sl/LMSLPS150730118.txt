To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London the Worshipful the Aldermen and other his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said City in their Generall Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Alban's Woodstreet in the City of London.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Mathew Blakison Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Francis Cokayne Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth Day of February Instant Jane Barnes Widow of Thomas Barnes and her two Children John Aged four Years and Charles Aged ten Weeks were removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Alban's in Woodstreet as their legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners humbly thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices Appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order And pray That you will be pleased to determine the same and make such Order thereon as to this Honourable court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Greenhow D Crury} Church Wardens.
