To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Knt Baronett Lord Major of the City of London And also to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quour Unus) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of December last past Richard Blackfann Elizabeth his wife and three Children Mary 12, Susan 3 and Eliza: One year and half old were removed from the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said parish of St Botolph without Aldgate as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petrs humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Lordpp and Worpps therefrom And pray That your Lordpps & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And yor Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc
