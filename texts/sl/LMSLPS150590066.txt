To ye: Rt. Honble: Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Worpful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye said City and ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of ye Parish of St. Gregory in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of ye & Lordship & Francis Cohayne Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justies of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & Liberties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twentyeth Day of November 1747. Hannah Deloff, Widow of Richard Deloff desed, & their Child Richard, aged seven Years, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate, London, unto ye. said Parish of St. Gregory, London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Gregory, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That your Petitionrs. conceiving themselves Agrieved by ye. said Warranter Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lords & Worps therefrom, and humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear ym. in ye. Premisses, & that ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate, London, may appear before you to hear & abide, & Judgment & Determinacon of Yor. Lords & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And your Petrs. shall pray etc.
