London To the Right Honourable Sir William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Guild hall now assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant undr. the hands and Seals of Sir William Billers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Robert Baylis Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City one being of the Quorum bearing date the twenty seventh day of September last past William Davis and Amey his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Faith under St. Paul London to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the said Order or pass Warrant and therefore humbly Appeal against the same And pray that you Worships will appoint a day this Sessions for hearing and determining the same And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said

Parish of Saint Faith under St. Paul may be ordered to Attend this court to hear and Abide by the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor. petrs. as in duly bound shall ever pray Etc.
