City of London to wit} To the Right Honourable Sr. Robert Ladbrooke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City

The humble Petition and Appeal of Geo: Whitmore one of the Inhabitants of this Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Less in the said City of London

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner is by a Rate or Assessment made and Confirmed for the Maintenance of the Poor of the Parish aforesaid from the 19th. of July to to the 19th. of Octor. Last upon the Inhabitants of the said Parish Unequally, Unjustly, and [...] Charged in the said Rate

Yours Petitioners therefore Most humbly Appeals to this Honble Court and Prays to be relieved in the premess. And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.

Geo: Whitmore
