To the right honourable Sr. John Williams Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled at the Guild hall of the said City

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order of pass Warrant bearing date the fourth day of August last under the hands and Seals of the right honourable St. John Williams Knight Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sir Henry Hankey [...] two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) Sarah Hoskins was removed from the Parish of Saint Buttolph Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchres adjudgeing the said parish of Saint Sepulchres to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Hoskins

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices Therefore humbly appeal to this honoruable Court therefrom

John Kings Thos: Cromwell} Church Wardens

Willm Bird Jno Chatfeild} Overseers of the Poor
