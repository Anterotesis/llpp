To the Rt. Honrble. John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London, And to the Honrble. Mr. Baron Thomson Recorder of the said Citty and the rest of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That Frances the wife of Jonathan Wood Anthony their Son an infant of the Age of Four years and Mary their Daughter aged two years were by Order of Pass Warrant dated the Sixteenth day of March last under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Sir William Billers Knt. Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said Citty of London (Quor Unus) removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph's Bishopsgate in the Citty of London to the said Parish of St. John of Wapping as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removall humbly Appeal to this Court against the same; and pray a day of hearing may be Appointed this present Quarter Sessions; And that all Persons Concerned may be Ordered to Attend, to hear and Abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
