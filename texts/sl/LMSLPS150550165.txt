To The Right Honoble Henry Marshall Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in Generall Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Robert Westley Knt. and Sir George Champion Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London bearing date the 23d. day of October last Elizabeth Field Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Magnus London to our said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves agreived by the said Pass Warrant or Order and Humbly beg leave to appeal against the same

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Fras. Strong Wm. Silley Chruchwdns.

Jno. Perry John Winter} Overseers
