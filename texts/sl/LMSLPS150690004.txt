To the Right Honble. Sir Charles asgill Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, Sir Wm. Moreton Knt. Recorder of the sd. City & to the rest of the Honble. Bench.

The Humble Petition of Mary Bates a Prisoner in his Majesties Goal of Newgate

Most Humble Sheweth

That your Petitioner (by the Name of Mary Clarke) is most wickedley & Unjustley accused with returning from Transportation for an Offence Pretended to be Committed many Years ago.

That your Petitioner is not the Person and is only a Malicious and Unjust Accusation

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Preys that she may be back or Discharged

And Your Petitioner as in Duty will Ever Pray Mary Bates
