To the Right Honourable Robt. Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor and to the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Robt. Alsop Lord Mayor & Thos. Chitty Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 8th. Day of June 1752 Joshua Kedden & Jane his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Andrew in Holborn London to the Liberty of the Rolls in the Parish of St Dunstan in the West in the County of Middx as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the sd. Order do Appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this City & Liberties

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
