To his Majestys Justices [...] of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of The Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in Southwarke

Sheweth That Robert Southam Jane his Wife of their three Children to wit Dina aged ten Years William Aged nine Years and Jane Aged two Years, were by an Order dated 15th Decr. 1762 under the hands & Seals of Sr. Charles Asgill & Sr.Wm.Stevenson Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of ye Peace for ye. sd. City, removed from the Parish of Allhallows ye Less London to our said Parish of St. George, whereby we think our selves agrieved

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly beg leave to enter an Appeal agt the said Order & desire to be heard by Councell thereupon

And your Petitionrs. will ever pray Etc
