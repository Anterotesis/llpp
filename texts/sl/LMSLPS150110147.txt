To the Rt: Honble: Sr: Richd: Levett Knt Lord Major and to the Rt. Wortt: the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseeres of the poore of the Pish of St. Mary Le Bow London.

Sheweth That yor. peticonrs. have been formerly Charged with the yearely paymt of Tenn pounds to the releife of the poore of the Parrish of St. Giles without Cripplegate. And their Said Pish of St. mary Le Bow Consisting but of about one hundred houses, and having above Twenty Children to pvide -for and alsoe Six pencenrs. And their Assessmts. being nere Treble To what they were Formerly: Being rated this yeare Fifteene pence in the pound, for the rents of their Dwelling houses, and the Last yeare above twenty pence in the pound.

Therefore their humble suite to yor. Good Honour & Worpps. is To Discharge them for the future from the paymt of the Said Tenn Pounds P Annum to the sd Pish of St. Giles Cripplegate

Joseph Webb Geo: Vyne Tho: Conor Hum: Corwall

And yor. Petrs. Shall Pray Etc John Browne Timothy Batt 1700} Comtt on Counedl Men

Robert. Greens Obad Grevith
