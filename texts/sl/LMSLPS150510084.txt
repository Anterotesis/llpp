To the Right Honble Humphry Parsons Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Wpfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City as their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Coleabby London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir John Salter Knight and Sir John Barnard Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City (being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d day of October last past Elizabeth the wife of Clayton Royston and their three Children Elizabeth Aged Six years Edward Aged two years and Ann Aged one Month were lately Removed from out of the said Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Old Fish Street London to the said Parish of Saint Nicholas Coleabby London as the Place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeal to your Lordship and Worships Against the said Order and humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to your Lordship & Worship shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
