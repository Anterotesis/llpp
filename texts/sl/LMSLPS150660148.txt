To the Rt. honble: Stephen Theodore Janssen, Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worshipful ye. rest of his Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. City & the Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye. Parish of Christchurch, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of your Lordship, and Francis Cokayne, Esqr, Two of his Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Second Day of October 1755, Sarah Holles, Widow, was Removed from the Parish of St. Augustin London, unto the Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging ye. Sd. Parish of Christchurch, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Atty for ye. Appellants.} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. Said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordship & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be Saved unto ym, & ye. hearing & Determining thereof, be Adjourned, until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace, to be holden for ye. sd. City of London, & ye. Liberties thereof, & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Augustin, London, may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgmt. & Determination of yor. Lordp & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray Etc.
