To the Rt. Worll. Sr. Edwd. & Clerke Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of ye Peace Att the Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Peticon and Appeale of he Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Christchurch London

Sheweth That one Anne Wright by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace wthin this City Dated 9th. June 1697 was brought & Delivered by the Churchwardens of St. Sepulchers London to yor. Petr. upon the Oath of one Margaret Chadwicke That the said Anne Wrights legall settlement is in yor. petrs. Parish of Christ- Church Where her Father was an Appntice Whereas in truth her said father was an Appntice to John Chadwicke But the said Chadwicke was not a settled Inhabitant in yor. Petr said Parish of Christchurch According to the Law

Their humble suite to yor. Honors. and Worpp. is That you with pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Quash the said Warrant And send the said Anne Wright to the Churchwardens of St. Sepulchers To be by them provided for untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Law.

And They shall Pray etc.
