To ye: Rt. Honble Sr. Charles Thorold Bart. Lord Mayr. of ye City of London & to ye rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in ye old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Ann Ross a Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. is comitted for picking Edward Fentons pcket of a Guinea of wth. fact she is innocent

Yor. Petr prayes this Honble Court to be Tryed this Sessions or discharged

And yor. Petr shall ever pray Etc
