To the Right. Honble: Sr. Samuell Garrard Baronett Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to the right Worshipfull the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in ye general Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of Parish of St. Michaells Cornhill Lonodn

Sheweth That Abigail Heddin Widow late wife of John Heddin deceased and John Heddin and Thomas Heddin in her two children by Virtue of A Warrant under the hands and seales of two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace within this Citty London (One being of the Quorn) bearing date the Sixth day of March Last past was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Michaells Cornhill London alledgeney the said Parish of St. Michaells Cornhill to be the place of the Last legall settlement of the said Abigail Heddin and her two children

Yor. Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate may appear before you and heare and abide the determinacon of your Honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
