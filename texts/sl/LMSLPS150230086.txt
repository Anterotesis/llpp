To the Right Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the the City of London and also to the Right Worpll: the Rest of her Majesties Justices of the Peace in the General quarter Sessions Assembled

24/10 1712 The humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Rother hite in the County of Surry

Sheweth That one Sarah Uzell wide by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majestys Justices of the peace (within this City one being of the Quor) bearing Date the four and twentieth Day of October last past was removed form the parish of St. Olave Hart Street within the City of London unto the said parish of Sr St Mary Rotherhith in the County of Surry alleadging the said parish of St. Mary Rotherhith to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Sarah Uzell never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Mary Rotherhithe whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr: and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Olaves Hart Street may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petioners Sall ever pray Etc
