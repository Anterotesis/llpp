To the Right Honble: Sr Richard Levet Kent: Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Send City

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr Salathiel Lovell Knt. & Sr Edward Clarke Knt two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said City Anne Warren widow was lately passed from then pish St. of Brides als Bridnet London to the Said pish of St Giles in the Feilds as the last place of her legall Settlement

Your Peticonrs. thinke themselves aggreived with the Judgement of the said Two Justices doe therefore Appeal to this Honble. Bench & humbly pray that in regard all parties concerned herein are now attending in Court that you will be pleased to hear & examine the matter & to make such finall Order & Determination touching this Appeal as to your great Wisdom shall seem meet

And your Peticonrs: (as in duty bound) shall en pray
