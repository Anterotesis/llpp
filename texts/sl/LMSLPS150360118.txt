To the Right Honble Sr George Merttins Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt Worpl the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at their General Quarter Sessions of ye peace for ye sd City Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Anne Black fryers London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for ye sd City (Quor unus) bearing Date the Third day of June Instant Mary Whitton wife of John Whitton deceaced was Sent & delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James Garlickhithe London to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Anne Blackfryers London Alledging the said parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Honr and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James Gailick hiths may appear before Your Honr & Worps & hear and abide the determinacon of the Court touching the Said Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
