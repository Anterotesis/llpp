To the Right Honoble Henry Marshall Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof in Generall Quarter Session at the Guildhall of the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Oversrs. of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass under the Hand & Seale of Sr. Edwd. Bellamy Knt. one of her Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London bearing Date the 8th. Day of Febry last Sarah Green Singlewoman was Passed as a Vagrant from the Parish of St. Andrew on Holborn London tot he sd. Liberty of the Rolls whereof your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Pass do Appeall

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short Day May be Appointed this Present session to hear & Determine the Matter touching the sd. Appeall.

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appellts.}
