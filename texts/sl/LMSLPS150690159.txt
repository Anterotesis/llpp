To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guild hall in & for the sd. City assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of [...] Burbridge in the County of Wilts

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under she hand & seal of Sr. Joseph Hankey Knight one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 2nd. day of March 1758 Prudence Hollick was passed as a Vagrant (with Elizth: her daughter aged one Month) from the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in the City of London to the Parish of Burbridge in the County of Wilts as the Place of their last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Pass Warrant or Order do appeal

John Aske Agent for the Appellants} Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the Matter of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc
