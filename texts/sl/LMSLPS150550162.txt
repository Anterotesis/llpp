To The Right Honoble the Lord Mayor and the Best of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberty's thereof

The Humble Peition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Passwarrant Under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 24th. Day of February 1743 Sarah Dilla Barnadera and her two Children Richard Aged Nine years and Michael Aged four years were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London To the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate London Aforesaid Adjudging the Last Legal Settlement of the Said Sarah Dilla Barnadera and her said two Children to be there And Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves Agrived by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal there from and humbly pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet and Convenient.

Burr Attorney for Bpsgate Parish}

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
