To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt & Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

3/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Nicholas Coleabby London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Third day of November last Dorothy Grantland Widdow was removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St Nicholas Coleabby alledging the said parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt & Judgmt of ye sd two Justies do most humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Giles without may appear before ye Honour & Worps & hear & abide ye Determinacon of ye Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
