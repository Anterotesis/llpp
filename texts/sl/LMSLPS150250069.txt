To the Rt. Honrble St. Samuel Stanier Knt. Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of he Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General quarter Sessions assembled.

1714 7/6 The humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bennett Gracechurch London

Sheweth That one Rebecca Sherwood with her daughter Rebecca Sherwood by virtue of a warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majesties Justices of th peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the seventh Day of June instant were removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate unto the parish of St. Bennett Gracechurch aforesaid alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Rebecca Sherwood

That your petrs. doubt not but to prove That the said Rebecca Sherwood never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St. Bennett Grace church whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly pray your Honr and worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may appeare before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And yor petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.
