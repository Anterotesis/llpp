London ss To the Rt. Honble Sr. Gerrard Conyers. Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London And the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said Citty in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Ethelburga London

Sheweth That by Order under the hands and Seales of Sr. William Thompson Knt. and Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said Citty of London (Quor Unus) bearing date the 9th day of October Anne Dm 1722 Thomas Hare wth Ann Hare his wife and their three Children (Vizt) Charles aged four Yeares Ann two Yeares, and Hannah three months were Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of St. Ethelburga London as the last legal Place of their Settlemt.

Yr Petrs. therefore humbly think themselves aggreived and Appeal against the said Order of the Said Justices and pray that Yr. Hones. will be pleased to hear their Said Appeal And grant Yr. Peter Such resert in the Prmisses as to Yr. Honrs. Shall Seem Meet

And Yr Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
