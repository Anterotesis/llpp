22/10 To the Rt: Honble the Lord Mayr: & other of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the City of London now Sitting at Guild Hall.

The Humble Peticon & appeal at the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Mary Matfellon als White Chappell in ye County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the sd. City (Quor unus) bearing date the 22d: day of October last Arabella Fewterell Singlewoman was removed from ye Parish of St: Katherine Creedchurch in ye sd. City so our sd. Parish of St: Mary Matfellon als Whitechappell as to the place of her last legall Settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. therefore think themselves agrieved & humbly appeal to this Honble Court agt. ye same.

And pray that you would be pleased to order that they may be relieved in ye premises as to you shall seem meet upon the hearing of all parties

And yr. Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc.
