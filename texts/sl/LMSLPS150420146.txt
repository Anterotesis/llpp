To the Rt. Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt Worpl the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City Assembled at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens adn Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Benedict als Bennett Fink London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the Seventh day of October instant Anne Cacy Wife of Henry Cacy & their Child aged about Nine Weeks were sent from the parish of St. Brides London to the Parish of St. Benedict als Bennett Fink London as the Place of their last legal Settlemt.

Your Petr humbly Conceiving themselves agreived by the Warrt. & Judgmt. of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief in the premisses

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
