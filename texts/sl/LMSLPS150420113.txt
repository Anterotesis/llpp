29/3/1731 To the right Honoble Humphey Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Beststreet in the City of Norwich

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quorum Unus) bearing date the 29th day of March last One Joseph Horth & William his son aged is Months were passed from the Parish of St Peter le poor London to the parish of Beststreet in the City of Norwich as the place of the last legal Settlement of the Joseph Horth

Yor Petitioners humbly conceiveing themselves aggrieved the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court for relief in the Prmisses

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray etc
