That your Petitioners think themselves agrieved by [...] Order of severall of the said Two Justices aggrived [...] the same

Your Petitioners and Appellr therefore Pray that [...] will appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Botolph wth. out [...] in the said City and all other Persons concerned to Attend [...] Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners and Appelts. may be received therein accoording to Law and Justice
