To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesties Justices for the Citty of London

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church. Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London-

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble: Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of this Citty and Sr: Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quor Un) bearing date the Sixteenth day of February Anno Dom 1731 Matthew Seegor and Mary his Wife were Removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate, London to the said Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Matthew Suger and Mary his Wife

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeale against the same

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray that your Lordship & Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day of this Present Sessions that all Parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and determmacon of this Court for and touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs: as in duty bound shall ever pray.
