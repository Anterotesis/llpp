To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in the City of London.

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the 19th of day of May last under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Sir John Salter Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Edwd. Bellamy Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London one being of the Quorum Elizabeth Adderley widow of Samuel Adderly deced was removed from the parish of Allhallows the Great London to the parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners find themselves aggreived by the wardens or order of the said two Justices and humbly appeal agt. the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that you will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Allhallows the Great and all other psons concerned to Attend this Court this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the premisses And that your Petitioners may have such releif therein as to Justices & said doth Appertain

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Browne} Church wardens

W: Tyson W: Perchard} Overseers.

Edwd. Wade Attorney for the Applts.
