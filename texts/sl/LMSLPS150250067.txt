To the Rt Honrble Sr Samuel Stainer Knt. Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Majesties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quor) bearing date the right and Twentieth Day of May last past One Gabriel Desmond a young Lad about Seven years and an half Old son of John Baptist Desmond was removed from the parish of St Martin Orgaes London unto the said parish of St Bridgett als Brides alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Gabriel Desmond

That your petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Gabriel Desmond never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Bridgett als Brides whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr an Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Martin Organs may appeare before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionr Shall ever Pray
