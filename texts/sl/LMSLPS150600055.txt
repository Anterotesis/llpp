To the Rt. Honble: Sir William Calvert, Knt, Lord Mayor, of ye. City of London, and to the Worpful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace, for ye. said City, & ye. Libties thereof, in their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal, of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor, of ye. Parish of St. Helen near Bishopsgate, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt, under ye. hands & Seals of yor. Lords & Francis Cokayne Esqr. Two of His Majtys Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty ninth Day of April, 1749, John Brown, Frances his Wife, & their Children, John aged Six Years Sarah aged four Years, Anne aged three Years, & Jane aged four Months, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Stephen in Coleman Street, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Helen near Bishopsgate, London, Alledging ye. said Parish of St. Helen, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge Sollr. for ye. Appellts.} That Yor. Peticoners Conceiving them- -selves agrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Ldp & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear ym. in ye. Premisses, & yt. ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor, of ye. Parish of St. Stephen in Coleman Street, London, may appear before you, to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determination of yor. Ldp & Worps touching this Appeal; And yor. Peticoners shall pray Etc.
