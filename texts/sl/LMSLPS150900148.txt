To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Samuel Plumbe Esqr. Mayor and Evan Pugh Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of March last past Martha Dancey the Wife of Willm. Dancey a Soldier in the Middlesex Militia and her two Children Vizt: William Aged One Year and Upwards and James aged Six Months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the City of London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Martha Dancey and her said two Children, Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and most humbly pray that the Benefit of such their Appeal may be Saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said City of London and that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some day in the said next Session for the hearing and determination of this their said Appeal

And Your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.

Cox. Sollr. for Appellt:
