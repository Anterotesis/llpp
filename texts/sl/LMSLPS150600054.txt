To the Rt. Honble: Sir William Calvert Knt. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worshipful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. City, & ye. Libties thereof, in their General Quar. Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal, of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch in ye. City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship & Francis Cokayne, Esqr, Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye. City of London, & ye. Libties thereof, (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Second Day of May 1749, Sarah Buckell, by ye. Name & addition, of Sarah Buckles Wife of John Buckles, who is run away from her, & their four Children, Anne aged nine Years, Sarah aged four Years, Elizabeth aged two Years, & Mary aged four Months, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate, London, unto ye. sd. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, London, Alledging ye. sd. Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Cha: Baynbridge Agent for ye. Appellts } That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. sd. Warrt. or Order of Removal, Do appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, That you will be pleased to hear them in ye premisses, and that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate, London, may appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
