To the Rt. Honble. Sir Edward Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honble. Sir Edward Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Gerrard Conyer Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof bearing date the 19th. day of June 1735 one Henry Smith was removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Sr James astle place of his last legal Settlemt. Where by Your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved.

And therefore humbly Pray That Your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal According to Law and Justice

And Your Petrs. Shall Ever pray

Benj Foster Church Warden

Peter Henry Hen: Scott} Overseers
