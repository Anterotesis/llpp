To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Ch [...] Magna in Somersetshire.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of July last 1743 Ann King Aged Nine Years Elizabeth King aged Seven Years Charles King aged Six Years and Francis King aged four Years Children of Abraham King by his Wife both deceased were removed upon the Oath of one Thomas LLoyd and other Circumstances from the parish of the precinet of Whitefryers London to the said parish of Chute Magna in Somersetshire as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant and Humbly Appeal against the same And pray the Benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved unto them and further Grant Your Petitioners such other Releif in the premisses as your Worships shall seem meet

And Your petitioners shall pray Etc
