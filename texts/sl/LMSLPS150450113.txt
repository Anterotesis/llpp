To the right Honble William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the right Worshipfull the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Foster Lane London.

Sheweth That by Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Sr. William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And Sr. Richard Brocas Knight and Alderman of the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventeenth day of May In the Year of our Lord 1734 Mary Taylor Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the great London to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Foster Lane London as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petitioners Conceiving they are Aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for Releif

and Yor. Petitioners shall ever Pray
