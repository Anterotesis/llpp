To the Right Honourable Sir Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipful his Majestys Justice of the Peace for the said City and Liberties in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East London

Sheweth That by an Order of Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Walter Bernard Esquire and Alderman two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Second Day of July last past James Davidson was lately removed from the Parish of Saint Mary at Hill London to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East London as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices Humbly appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order and pray such Relief in the Premisses as to You in your Judgment shall seem most meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Jno Sellon Cha: Townley} Churchwarden

John Woodbridge Jno. Smith} Overseers.
