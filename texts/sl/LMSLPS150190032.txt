To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the rest of Her Maties: Justices of the Peace in the General [...] Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Severall Parishes of St. Gyles without Cripplegate St. Buttolph without Aldgate St. Andrews Mardrobe St Katherine Coleman St Andrew Holborne St Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the Wall St. Buttolph Aldersgate & St Buttolph without Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very much over burthened with Poore & not able to Raise Sufficient Sumes & money amongst themselves to releive them notwithstanding their Assessments are greater than heretofore Yet the Charge of their poor is farr above their Assessments As the same upon full Examination dath divers tymes appeared to Severall worthy Aldermen of this City appointed to hear the same And their poor daily encreasing upon them their said Parishes lyeing some of them near the Suburbes & others of them in the Suburbes of this City

Their humble suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. & Worll. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contributions of other Parishes within this City for and towards the Releiving their poor As they have formerly had which are pticularly hereunder menconed Or such further Allowance As to yor. Honor. & Worpps. shall seeme meete

And They Shall Pray Etc

St Gyles without Cripplegate £90. St. Buttolph without Aldgate £50. St. Andrew Wardrobe 40 St Katherine Coleman 12. St Andrew Holborne 7. St. Sepulchers 44. St. Brides 15. Alhallowes on the Wall 12. St. Buttolph without Aldergate 15. St. Buttolph without Bishoppsgt. 60.
