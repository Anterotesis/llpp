To the Right Honourable Sir Robert Kite Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London in their General Session of the peace Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Robert Kite Knight and Thomas Hallifax Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of July last Margaret Sheldon Wife of Joseph Sheldon gone from her was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Joseph Sheldon and Margaret his wife Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Pass Warrant or Order and Pray that the Benefit of the said appeal may be saved to your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Benjn. Gee Geo: Soward, John Milbourne John Dorman Richd. Nowlson} Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
