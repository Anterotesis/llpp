To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesties Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the fourth day of October now last past under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable John Sawbridge then Lord Mayor of the City of London and William Plomer Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said City (One Whereof being of the Quorum) Ann Gorden otherwise Ann Garden Newlan the Wife of John Gordon otherwise John Gordon Newlan who had deserted her and her two Children James aged Two Years and upwards and Charles aged two Months were removed from the parish of Saint Lawrence Jewry in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners Apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same

And therefore pray that you will be Pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Laurence Jewry aforesaid and all Persons concerned to Attend this Court same day this Present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the Premises And that your Petitioners may have such Releif therein as to your Worships shall seem meet
