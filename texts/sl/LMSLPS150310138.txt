To the Rt. Honrble Sr George Thorold Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and also to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City of their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Petr of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing date the Sixth day of July last past George Nurse was removed from the parish of St Martin Vintry London, unto the said parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appear to your Honr. and Worpps therefrom And pray that your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to hear then in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Martin Vintry may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and determination of you Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc.
