To the Rt Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

1713 27/5

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Magdalen Milkstreet London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quom) bearing date Seven and twentieth day of May last One Sarah Rand with her two Children Martha Rand and Edward Rand were removed from the parish of St Stephen Colemanstreet London unto the said Parish of St Mary Magdalen Milkstreet alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legall settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Sarah Rand never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St Mary Magdalen Milkstreet whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Stephen Coleman Street may appear before you and hear and abide the determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionr Shall ever Pray Etc
