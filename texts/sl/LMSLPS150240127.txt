To the Rt. Honble St. Richd. Heare Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and the rest of her Maties Justices of the Bare for the sd Citty [...]

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn London.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Ordr. or Patt Warrt. bearing date the Seventh day of this instant October undr. the hands & Seals of two of her Maties of the Peace for the sd. City Quor suns one George Pi [...] with Fortune his wife & and Child names Mary were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen old pish street London to yor. Petrs. sd. Parish of St. Andrew Holborn wch. was adjudged by the said Justices to be the place of last legal Settlemt. of the sd. George Purchase.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrt. & Judgmt. of the sd Justices

Wherefore they humbly appeale agnt. the Same to this Court and pray for such relief therein, as to yor. Lpp & Wrpp shall Seem just & reasonable.

And yor. Peticonr. shall Pray Etc

The Peticon & Appeale of the Church Warden & of St. Andrew Holborne London agat. St. Mary Magdalen old Fishstreet touching the settlemt. of George Purchase & wife & daughter.

Adjorned till next Sessions wth of Maintenance to goe as Court that adjudge the Settlemt. to be

Appeal allowed.

Cost of maintenance to pd. Sane lost Sessions
