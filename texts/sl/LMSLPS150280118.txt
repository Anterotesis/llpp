To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace for the City of London in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

8/5 The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Ealing in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox nuns) bearing Date the Eighth day of May last past one Hannah Tickner wife of Thomas Tickner was remover was removed from the Parish of St Bartholomew the less London unto the Said Parish of Ealing in the County of Middx Alleadging the said Pish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore [...] most humbly pray your Honour & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Pish of St Barthw the less may appear before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honour and Worps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
