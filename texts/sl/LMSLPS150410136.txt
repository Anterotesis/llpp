To the Right Worshipfull Sr. Richd. Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London

The Humble Peticon of Richard Henson

Sheweth That Your petr. was this present Sessions At the Old Bayly Tryed for a Robbery pretended to be comitted in the Street and was [...] Acquitted thereof but by your Lordships Order was Ordered to remaine in Custody while the next Ensueing Sessions

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays that in Case your Lordship thinks fitt your Petn. should appear the next Sessions in this Court that your Petr. may be Admitted to give Security now in Court for his appearance at the next Sessions

And your petr. as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc
