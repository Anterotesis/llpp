To the Rt Honble: Sr: Wm. Humfrey Knt. & Barrt. Ld. Mayr. & the rest of the Honble. Bench

The humble Peticon of John Rye

Sheweth That he was comitted to Newgate the 31th. of May last for a ryott & Assault one one Mr. Gee

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that he may be tryed this Sessions or be otherwise discharged

And he will as in all Duty bound ever pray Etc.

To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Wm Humfrey Knt. & Barrt. Ld Mayr. and the rest of the Honble. Bench

The humble Peticon of John Rye.
