To the Right Honourable Sr. Charles Asgill Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the sd. City Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Edmunds Bury in the County of Suffolk

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hand and Seal of Wm. Bridgen Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City bearing Date the 15th. Day of March 1758 Alice Jackson was removed from the Parish of Saint Christopher London to the sd. Parish of St. Edmunds Bury in the said County of Suffolk by the Name or Description of the Parish of Bury Saint Edmonds in the county of Suffolk as the Place of her last legall Settlement

your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves Agreised by the said Pass Warrant or Order & do humbly Appeal against the same & Pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners Etc
