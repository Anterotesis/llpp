1/1734 To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London in their Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Sir William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Richard Brocas Knight & two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of this Instant January whereby Elizabeth Kettle Singlewoman was removed from the parish of St. Sepulcres in the City of London to the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your peticoners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices [---] humbly appeale to this Court against the same praying such reliefe in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And yor peticoners will Ever pray

Thomas Bayly Churchwarden

Abr: Letudois Jno Went} Overseers
