To the Rt. Honble Sr George Thorold Barrt. Lord Mayr. & ye rest of ye Honble Bench Now Sitting at the Old Bailey.

The Humble Prayer of Rebecca Carter a prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor petr was Comd. to this place the 11th. March last for Stealing a P of washed Stockings out of ye Shop of Joseph Marriot of wch fact she is Innocent

Yor petr therefore prays That she may be Called Down this Sessions & tryed or otherwise Discharged

& yor petr. as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc.
