To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Barlow Trecothick Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. Richard Glynn Baronet Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 28th. Day of August last, Elizabeth Odell aged fourteen Years was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew holborn above Bars as the place of her last legal Settlement

Yor. Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved to Your Petitioners And that the hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for the said City and the Liberties thereof

And yor. Petrs. will ever pray Etc

Thos: Wade Junr. Sollr for the Appellts 13th Septr. 1770
