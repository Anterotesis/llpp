To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London in General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Olave in the City of London.

Sheweth. That by an Order of Removal bearing Date the Sixth Day of December last under the Hands and Seals of Francis Cokayne Esquire and Sir Mathew Blakiston Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof One being of the Quorum Daniel Kelly and Rachael his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe London to the said Parish of Saint Nicholas Olave London as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners find themselves agreived by the Order of the said two Justices And humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships That you will be pleased to order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe And all other persons concerned to attend this Court this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the Premisses, And that your Petitioners may have such relief therein as to Justice and law doth appertain

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Robert Champante Attorney for the Appellants

Jabez Bloxham George Atwood} Church Wardens

Charles Buxton Wm. Humfrys} Overseers
