To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St Matthew Fryday Street London.

Sheweth That Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (Quor unus) bearing Date the first day of October Instant One Mary Darvall widdow was removed from the Parish of St Peter Poor London to the Sd Parish of St Matthew Fryday Street Alledging the Sd parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worppll therefrom
