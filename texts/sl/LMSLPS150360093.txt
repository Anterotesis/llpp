London Ss To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye Poor of ye Parish of St. Michael at Quern in London

Sheweth That by order under the Hands and Seals of Sr George Merttins Knt. Lord Mayor of this Honble City of London and Sr John Fryar Barrt: two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye said City of London (Quorum Unus) bearing date the tenth Day of December, Anno Dni 1724 Katherine ye widdow of Samuell Stonehall decesd wth five children Mary aged about eight Years, Sarah about Six Years, Samuell about three Years, Francis abt two Years and Henchman about nine Months were removed and conveyed from the the Parish of St. Andrew Wardrobe, to ye sd: Parish of St Michael at Quern in London as the last Legall place of their Settlement

Your Petitioners there fore Humbly think themselves aggrived and appeal against ye Order of the said Justices and pray releif in the Prmisses of this Honble Court.

And Yor Peticoners shall ever pray
