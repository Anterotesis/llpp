To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & Libertys thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twelfth Day of May 1749 Robert Williams and Hannah his wife were removed from the Parish of St. Lawrence Pountney London to the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London aforesaid Adjudging the Last Legal Settlement of the sd Robert Williams and Hannah his Wife to be there, And your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order & Judgment of the said two Justices Do Humbly Appeal therefrom and Humbly pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet and Convenient

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.

Burr Attorney for Bishopgate Parish}
