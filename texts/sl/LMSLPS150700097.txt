To the Right Honble: Sir Richard Glyn, Knt. and Baronet, Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Rt. Worshipful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye said City & Liberties thereof, in their Genal Quarter Session assembled.

The humbe Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Michael in Cornhill, London.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals, of Francis Cokayne, Esqr. & Sr. Samuel Fludyer Knt. two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. fourth Day of October 1759, Susanna Bloom was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Michael in Cornhill, London, alledging ye. sd. Parish of St. Michael, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Worlidge, Sollr. for ye. Appellants} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by ye said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worship therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be Saved unto them, & ye. hearing & Determinitig thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next. General Quarter Sessions of ye. Peace, to be holden for ye. sd. City of London, & Liberties thereof, And that ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. sd. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate afd, may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.
