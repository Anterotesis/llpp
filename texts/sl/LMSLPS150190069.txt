To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In ye. General Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Vedosti. als Fosters

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Wilkins what of Robert Wilkins deth wth. 3 children By virtue of a Warrt. under the hands and Seales of Two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing date the Fifth day of August 1708 was removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Greate to the Said Parish of St. Vedosti als Fosters Alledging the said Parish is the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth

That yor. Petrs. doubt not but to Prove That the Said Elizabeth never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Vedosti als Fosters whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices And do humbly appeal to yor. Worpps. therefrom

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor. & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the greater To appear here before you And hear & abide the Determinacon of yor. Honor. & Worpps. touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc
