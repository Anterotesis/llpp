To the Right Honble: Sr John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir Robert Baylis Knt. Two of His Majtys's Justices of ye Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of July last, Eleanor Howard Single- -woman, was Removed from the Parish of St. James in Dukes Place London, unto the said Parish of St. Catherine London, Alledging the said Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch to be the Place of her last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order, Do appeal to yor. Lordp & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the said Parish of St. James in Dukes Place may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor Lordp & Worships touching this Appeal.

And Yor. Petr. Shall pray Etc.
