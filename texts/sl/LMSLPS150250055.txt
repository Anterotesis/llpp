To the Right Honble St Samuel Stanier Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Right Worple. the Right Worpll the Rest of her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the same City in the general quarter Sessions assembled

1714 13/2 The humble petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under his hands and Seals of two of her Majesties Justices of the peace within this City one (being of the Quorn) bearing date the Thirteenth day of February last past One Sarah Ball a Girl aged thirteen years was removed from the parish of St Peter poor London unto the parish of St. Alllia cows the wall London Alleadying the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the and Sarah Bill

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Sarah Ball never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Alllhallows the Wall whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St poser poor may appeare before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs: Shall ever Pray Etc
