To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Katherine Coleman London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. Parish is scituate wth. in Algate And are not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive their Poore Notwithstanding their Assessements are greate And the Charge of their Poore is above their said Assessement As the same upon full examination hath appeared to divers Aldren of this City Appointed to heare the same and their Poore increaseing upon them And haveing many Allies within their said Parish.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. and Worshipps. is that you wilbe favourably pleased to grant unto them the Allowance of Â£12. from other Parishes within this City for & towards the releiveing their Poore As they have formerly had by reason of their greate charge & number of Poore Or such further Allowance As to yor. Honors. & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And Yor. Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc.
