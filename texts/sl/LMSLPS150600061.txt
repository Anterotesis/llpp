To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and to the rest of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof in General Quarter Session of the peace at Guildhall in and for the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Settlemt Calvert Knight Lord Mayor and Fras. Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) dated the 19th. Day of January last whereby Eliz. Dall Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Botolph Aldgate in the sd. City of London to the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humble Appeal agt. the Sd. Order of Removal & Pray your Worships to Grant them such Releif in the Premises as shall Seem Just and equitable

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Chimmow agt. for the Appellts.
