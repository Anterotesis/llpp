To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard shoreditch in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or order of Removal bearing date, on or about the Fourth day of June now last past under the hands and seals of William Plomer Esquire and William Gill Esquire Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City of London and Liberties thereof) one being of the Quorum) Mary Davidson singlewoman and her Illegitimate Child named mary aged three weeks for nurture of were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish, of Saint Stephen Coleman street London, to the said Parish of Saint Leonard shoreditch in the county of Middlesex as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Davidson, Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved, and humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court against the said order and adjudication.

And there fore your petitioners humbly pray that the Court with be pleased to order their said Appeal to be heard at some day to be Appointed in their present Sessions, And that the Church Wardens, and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Stephen Coleman street London, and all Parties concerned may Attend and abide by the order of this Honourable Court Touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.

Jno. Fearr Solr. for the Parish of St. Leonard shoreditch Hoxton Town
