To the Right Honble Sr. Humphry Edwin Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worll his Maties. Justices of the same City in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the Hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quox) bearing date the 14th day of May 1698 John Younger Jane his wife & James his son were by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Ethelbury London sent to yor Petr to be provided for upon the Confession of the Churchwarden of yor Petrs Parish that their last legall Settlement was in yor Petrs said Parish [...] here as in truth the Churchwarden of yor Petrs Parish never confest any such thing and can further prove that the said John Younger Jane his wife & James his son lived in att alley in the Parish of St Ethelbury were the said Jane younger hung out a Board at the end of the alley to give notice to people that she could help Servants to places

Wherefore yor Petrs humbly pray yor. Honors & Worpps. to hear them in the prmisses And order the Churchwardens of the Parish of Ethelbury to Appeare before you And to Quash the said Warrant and send the said John Younger Jane his wife and James his son back to the pish of Ethelbury to be by them kept & pvided for untill they can free themselves of the charge thereof by due course of Lawe.

And yor Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
