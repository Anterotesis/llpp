To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quar Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. John Barnard & Sr. Thomas Lombe Knights two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d day of November last Leonora Lucy Carr was removed from the Parish of St. Dunstan in the Evant in the said City to the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx & as the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr. think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the peace appear to this Court against the Same [...] and humbly prays your [...] worshipps will be pleas'd to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing & Determining the Same

And yor: Petitioners shall ever pray etc.
