To the right Honble: the Bench of Sessions Sitting at Guildhall in London.

The humble peticon of Humfrey Dyer of the parrish of the Holy Trinity in London Painter

Shewing That yor: Petr. is Assessed in the sd Pish to bee worth £50: P Annm

Yor. Petrs. therefore make his humble Appeale to this Honble: Bench to bee Releived in the prmisses.

And hee shall ever pray Etc

Jur in Cor Not worth £200. To be abated the £50. P Annm
