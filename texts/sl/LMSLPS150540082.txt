To the Rt: Honble the Lord Mayor and other his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The Humble Peticon of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Robt: Willimott Esqr: Mayr and George Arnold Esqr: two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libtys thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of Novemr: last Isaac Glynn Sarah his Wife and their 2 Childn: Sarah and Parish of Alhallows Staining London to the said Parish of St. Olave Southwark as the place of their last Legall Settlement by which Order or Warrant your Peticoners finding themselves agrieved humbly begs relief in the Pmes,

And as in duly Bound shall ever pray Etc

Alexr Hay Sam: Marner} Church Wardens

Henry Dinning Jos Towle} Overseers of the Poor
