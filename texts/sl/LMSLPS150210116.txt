Appeal Waltham Abby agt St Giles Cripplegate

To the Rt Honoble Sr Saml Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

To humble Petition [---] an appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of Waltham Abby in Essex

Sheweth That Mary Dell by Virtue of a Warrant under ye hands & seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of [...] Waltham Abby in Essex Alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of her last legall settlemt

That your Petitionr Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said Two Justices And doe humbly appeale therefrom

And Yr. Petitionrs shall ever Pray
