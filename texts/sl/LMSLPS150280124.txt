To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the right Worpl: the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

21/6. The humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Michael Cornhill London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant from two of his Matys Justices of the peace with in the City of London (one being of the Quoz) bearing date the 21 day of June last One John Lucas with Mary his Wife & Thomas their son were removed from ye Pish of St Giles with Cripplegate London to ye sd Pish of Sr Michael Cornhill alleadging the sd Pish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt and Judgmt of ye sd two Justices and do most humbly appeal to Your Honr and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worps [...] hear them in the premisses And Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd Pish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you [...] [---] And hear & abide the determinacon of yr Honr and Worps touching ye sd Appeal

And Your Petrs Shall ever Etc.
