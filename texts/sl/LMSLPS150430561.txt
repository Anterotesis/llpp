To the Right Honourable John Barber Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seales of the Right Honourable John Barber Esqr Lord Mayor and Sir William Billers Knight Two of his Majesties Jutices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (Quorum unns) bearing date the Fifteenth day of November 1739, Robert Hambleton Anne his wife and their five Children Vizt. Anno aged 11 yeares Francis aged 9 Years Sarah aged 6 yeares, Robert aged 8 years, and Isabella aged 15 months wereby the said Order removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Robert Hambleton Anne his Wife an Ann Francis, Sarah Robert and Izabell their five Children Your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That this Honourable Court will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal And Your Petitioners may be releived as unto this honourable Court shall seem meete

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
