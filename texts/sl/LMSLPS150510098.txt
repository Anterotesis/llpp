To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden for the said City and Liberties in Guildhall assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass or Order under the Hands & Seales of the Right Honourable Sir John Salter Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Edward Bellamy Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of April last past Mary Colebatch Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Andrew in Holborn London to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace and pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeale

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

J Godfrey Agent for the Appellts:
