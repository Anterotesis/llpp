To ye Re Honbee Sr Richard Levett Knt Ld Major To ye Re Worspl Sr Salathiell Lovell Knt Recorder} of ye Citty of London

To ye Rest of ye Judges Now Setting att ye Old Bailey

The humble Petion of [...] Mayor [...] Jno Littahall A Prisonr. in Newgate

Most humbly Sheweth That yor. Peticonr was ye Last Session To find Baile to Appear this Sessions for a Misdemeant butt Could not Find Baile haveing no Freinds Left wer haveing non Remained in Newgate Ever Since

Therefore Most humbly Prayes that this Honble. Benchs would bee pleased to discharge yor Poor Peticnr this Sessions & that this Prayer may be Registered

And yor Peticor Shall Pray
