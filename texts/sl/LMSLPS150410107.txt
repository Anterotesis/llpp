To the Rt Honoble the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace for the City of London in their Genll Quarter Sessions of the Peace Att the Guild Hall now Assembled

12/5 The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchre in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of A Warrant or order under the Hands and Seales of the Right Honoble Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the Said City & Sr Francis Child Knt. & Alderman Two of his Majties of the Peace for the said City (Quor Unus) Bearing Date the Twelfth Day of May last past Aris Mary Care was removed from the parish of St. Martin Ludgate London to the Said Parish of St. Sepulchre Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Honoble Court Praying that they will hear Examine and Determine the sd appeale and Will please to make order thereupon for the Releif of Your Peticoners as to this Honoble Court Shall Seem Meet

& Yor. Petrs. Shall Etc
