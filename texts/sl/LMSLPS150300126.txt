To the Right Honble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Mayor of ye City of London and to the rest of his Matys Justices of ye peace at ye Genl. Quarter Session's of ye peace assembled

21/8 The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Dunned St Andrews in the County of Wilts.

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant under the hands Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within ye City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 21 day of August last Richard Hooker was sent and delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Mathew in Fryday street London unto the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye sd Pish of Domin St Andrews in ye County of Wilts Alledging the sd Pish to be the place of his last Legal Settlement

That your petitioners conceive themselves aggrievd by the said Warrt & Judgment of ye sd two Justices and do most humbly appeal to yr Honr & worps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps will be pleased the hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye sd Pish of St Matthew in Fryday street may appear before you and hear & abide the determinacon of yr Honr & Worps touching the said Appeal.

And yr petrs shall ever pray Etc.
