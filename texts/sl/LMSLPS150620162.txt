To the Rt. Honble Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worshipful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anthony Vulgarly Antholin, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under the Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship & Robert Alsop Esqr, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & the Liberties thereof, (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fourth Day of April instant, Francis Knighton, aged ten years, & Richard Knighton aged seven years, Children of Richard Knighton by Martha his Wife both deceased were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Anthony Vulgarly Antholin, London, alledging the said Parish of St. Anthony, to be the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Atty for ye. Appellants} That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. sd. Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lords & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be saved unto them, & ye. Hearing and Determining thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace, to be holden for ye. sd. City of London & ye Liberties thereof And that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph afd. may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petitoners shall pray Etc.
