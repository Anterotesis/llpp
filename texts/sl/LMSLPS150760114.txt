To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor the Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of ye City of London and Libertys thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden in and for the said City at the Guild hall Assembled on Monday the Fourteenth Day of October in the Year of our Lord 1765

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Oversrs. of ye Poor of ye. parish of St Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order dated ye. Seventeenth day of August last under ye. hands and Seals of her of His Majesty's Justices of ye peace of the said City Mary Curtis with her Illegitimate Children both Infants of tender Years one named Mary and aged three Years and the other named George and aged one year were Removed from the Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street in that said City to the said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the place of the Legal Settlemt. of the said Mary and the said two Children with her for Nurture

That Your Petrs. Apprehend themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrt. and the Appeal to this Court against ye same

Your Petrs. & Appellts. therefore humbly pray Your Worships that the said Order or pass Warrant may be Vacated & Quashed And that this thin Appeal against ye same may be Allowed with Maintenances

And Your Petrs. & Appellts. will pray Etc

By Order J Billington Sol. for Appellts. Octr. 14. 1765.
