To the Right Honorable Sir Richard Hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Alhallows in Lombard Street London

Sheweth That by virtue of An Order or pass warrt. under the Hands & Seals of The Knt. Honble Sir Richard Hoare Knt. sd Mayor of the City of London & Edward Davies Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourteenth day of Febry last George Stevens, Ann his Wife and their Two Children Mary aged Two Years & Hannah aged one Year were removed from the Parish of St. Catherine Coleman London to the said Parish of Alhallows in Lombard Street London as the place of their last legal settlement.

That your Petitioners [...] Concieve themselves aggrieved by the said Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that they may be relieved in the premisses in such manner as this Honourable Court shall seem meet.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
