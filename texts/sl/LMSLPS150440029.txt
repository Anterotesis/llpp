To the Rt. Honble. John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Honble. Bench at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey.

The Humble Prayer of Jane Morris [...]

Humbly Sheweth. That Your Supplicant Jane Morris was Comitted to his Maties Goal of Newgate (on the Oath of Edward Powers for Receiving Several Stol'n Goods knowing them to be Stol'n) on the Sixteenth day of February last & has ever Since Custody to the great Prejudice of Your Supplicants Health.

Your Supplicant therefore Most Humbly Prays that she may be either Tryed or Discharged

And Yor. Supplicant (as in Duty bound) shall for ever pray.

April 4th 1733.

The Mark of Jane [x] Morris
