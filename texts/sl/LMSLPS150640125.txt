To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennet Sherehog in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 20th. Day of April in the Year of our Lord 1753 Sarah Wilkinson Wife of James Wilkinson who is a Prisoner for Debt in the Poultry Compter and their Child Elizabeth Aged one Year were removed from the Parish of St. Sepulcher in Coleman Street London to the said Parish of St. Bennet Sherehog as the last legal place of their Settlement by which your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to Hear and Determine the Matter touching this Appeal and give then such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

D. Webb of Walbrook London Agent for the Appellts.]
