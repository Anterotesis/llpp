To the Right Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace at the Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of Sarah De Paiba [...] of St Catherine Creechurch Widdo

Sheweth That Your Petitioner is an Inhabitant in the Sd Parish of St Catherine Creechurch London and is Rated at Four pounds five Shillings yearly to the poor of the sd parish by which your Petitioner Conceives her selfe greatly griev'd, the same Sess or Rate being very unequall and is much more than others in the sd Parish under the like Circumstances a re Rated at

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays your Honour & Worps to relieve her in the premisses And Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor to Appear & produce the sd present rate, and then cause why the sd rate Shou'd not be Quash'd & yr Petitioner eas'd in in the premisses

And Your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc.
