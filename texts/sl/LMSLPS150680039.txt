To the Right Honble. the Comrs. of Gaol Delivery for the City of London.

The Humble Petition of Catherine Walker a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That Your Petitioner stands Committed to this Gaol for Felony

Yor. Petitioner prays this Court to be tried bailed or discharged at this Session according to the Statute

And Your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Catherine Walker
