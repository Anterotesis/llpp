To the Right Honble: Sr. Humphrey Edwin Knt: Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt: Worll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace of the same City in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poore of the Sevrall Parishes of St. Gyles without Cripplegate Buttolph wthout. Aldgate St Andrew Wardrobe St. Kathine Colemon St Andrew Holbourne St Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the wall St Buttolph Aldersgate & St Buttolph without Bishoppsgate.

Sheweth That yor Petrs. are very much over burthoned wth. poore and notable to raise Sufficient Sumes of Mony amongst themselves to releive them Notwthstanding. their Assessmts. are greater then heretofore Yet the charge of their poore is far above their said Assesmt. As the same upon full Examination hath many times appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to heare the same and their Poore daily encreasing upon them Their said Parishes lying some of them noore to the Subvrbes & others of them in the Subvrbes of this City.

Their humble suites therefore unto yor good Honr: & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased toy cant unto them the contribucons from other parishes wth, in this City for & towards the releiving their Poore As they have formerly had wch are pticulerly hereunder menconed or such further allowance as to yor Honr. & Worpps. shall seeme meete

St Giles wthout. Cripplegt £90 St Buttolph wthout Aldgate £50 St Andrew Wardrobe £40 St Katth. Coleman £12 St. Andr Holborne £7 St Sepulchers £44 St Brides £15 Alhallowes on ye wall £12 St Butt Aldersgt £15 St Butt Bppsgate £60

And yor Petr Shall ever Pray Etc
