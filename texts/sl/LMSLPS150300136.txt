9/10 To the Right honble the Ld. Mayor Recordr. & other his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Citty of London

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of Sr. Wm. Thomson [...] Wm. Withers two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Citty of London & the Libtyes thereof dated the 9th. day of October last Elizth. Snow was removed from the Pish of St. Botolphs without Aldersgate to the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her legall Settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly appeale from the sd. Ordr & pray yor Worpps Judgemt. touching the sd. Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc.
