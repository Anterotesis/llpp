To the Rt. Honble: Daniel Lambert Esqr. Lord Mayor the City of London, & to the Worship full the rest of [...] Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City at the Liberties thereof, in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of [...] Church [...] the Parish of St. Botolph without [...] Aldersgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order a Pass Warrant the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir George Champion Knt. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the [...] for the City of London & the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Fifth Day of August last past, Mary Seers was Removed from the Parish of St. Ann Blackfryers London, unto the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London, alledging the said Parish of St. Botolph to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Baynbridge fro ye. Appellts. That yor. Peticonr. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removall as afd. Do Appeal to Yor. Lordship & Worships there from, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parise of St. Ann Blackfryers afd. may appear before you, to bear and abide the Judgement & Determination of your Lordship & Worship founding this Appeal.

And your Peticoners Shall pray [...]
