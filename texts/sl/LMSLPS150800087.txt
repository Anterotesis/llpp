To the Right Honorable Samuel Turner Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of Samuel Turner Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir James Esdaile Knight two of His Majestes Justices of the Peace for the City if London and Liberties thereof Mary Binfield Wife of Thomas Binfield (who is therein alledged to have deserted her was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Pauls London into the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the please of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your worships that a day may be appointed for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that your Worships wou'd be pleased to make such Order in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray

W: Johnston Solr. for the Appellts.
