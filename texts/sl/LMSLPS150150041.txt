To ye. Rt. Honble Sr. Jno. Parsons knt. Sd. Majr. of the Citty of London & to ye rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in ye. old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Eliz: Johnson als Martha Rogers A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Chargd in Custody in Newgate [...] the 19th day of October last [...] with having Comittee sevall Felonies

Yor. Petr. prayes this Honble Court to be tryed his Sessions or otherwayes discharged

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc

December 1803

Mary Elderts n als Goodman Pet beneficin Act Pshbta Subdit

Petiton of Eliz: Johnson als Martha Rogers
