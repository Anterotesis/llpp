To the right honble. Sr. Gilbert Heathcott Knight Lord Major of the City of London, and to the right worpfull the rest of her Maties, Justices of the peace in the generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulcher London

Sheweth That one Thomasin Baily wife of William Baily with three Children vizt) Grace about eight years old, William about Seaven years old, And James about four years old by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of two of her Maties. Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of January now last past were removed from the parish of St. Anne Aldersgate London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London alleadging the said Parish is the place of the legall Settlemt. of the said Thomas in Baily & her said three Children;

That your Peticoners doubt not to prove that the said Thomas in Baily and her 3 Children had no legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Sepulcher London whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices, and doe humbly appeal to your honour & Worships thereupon

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray your honour and Worps wilbe pleased to hear them in ye Pr.misses and order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Anne Aldersgate London to appear here before you and hear 3 Abide the determination of your Honors. & Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
