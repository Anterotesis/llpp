To the Right Honble Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd City in the their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Sr. Olave in the Old Jury London

Sheweth That Eleanor Cambier Widow of jno Cambier by virtue of and Order or pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox Unus) bearing date the 27th. day of June last was sent from & delivered by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Botolph without Aldergate London To the said parish of Sr. Olave in th Old Jury London as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Peticoners humbly conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Warrt. & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal agt. the same And pray such relief therein as to this Honble Court shall seem just.

And Your petrs. shall ever pray Etc
