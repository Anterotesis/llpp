To the Rt Honble Sr. George Merttins Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Rest of the Rt. Worppll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City all the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That By Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of the Rt. Honble Sr George Merttins Knt. Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr John Fryer Bart. Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor Unus) bearing date the Twentieth Day of Aprill Anno Dom 1725 George Welch and Jane his Wife with their Children Vizt George Elizabeth and Adrian were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London As the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said George Welch & Jane his Wife & their three Child

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Humbly Appeale against the same and most humbly pray to be releived in the premisses in such manner As to this Honble Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc.

John Addison} Churchwardns Wm Simpson} Overseers
