To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of the Right Worship full Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order dated the 13th. day of April instant under the hands and Seals of William Bridger Esqr. Mayor and Sir Charles Asgill Knight two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London, Martha Page Wife of William Page who is gone from her was removed from the Parish of Saint Peter near Pauls Wharf London to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London at the Place of her last legall Settlement

Yours Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal, Do there few humbly appeal to this Court against the Same Praying Such Releif in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet And that the benefit of this Appeal May be saved to them to the Adjournment of the present Session

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Fisher Atty for the Appellants
