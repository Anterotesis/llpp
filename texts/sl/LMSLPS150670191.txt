To the Rt Honble Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord mayor of the City of London Sr. Wm. Moreton Knt. Recorder of the Same City and the Rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City and Libertys thereof at their Genll Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Andrews Hubbard London

Sheweth That by a Pass or order of Removal under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City and Libertys thereof (whereof one is of the Quorum) Esther Hazleham Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Brides London to the said parish of Saint Andreww Hubbard [...] place of their last legal Settlement

That ye Petr. and Appellants apprehendery themselves to be aggreived by the said order of the said two Justices do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petrs. & Appellts therefor pray that this Court will [---] this Save to them the benefit of this appeal to ye Petrs. and adjourn the determination [...] thereof to the next Genll. Quarter Sessns. of the peace for this City and grant such other Relief to ye. petrs. as to this Court shall Seem Meet

And yr. Petrs. & Appellts. shall Ever pray Etc.

Oct Sessns. Edwd. Alban Sollr. for the Petrs.
