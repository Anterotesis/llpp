To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace for the City of London in the General Quarter Session Assembled

18/5 The humble Petition afsd Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Mary le Bow London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of It Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date ye Eighteenth day of May last past A Male Infant was removed from the Pish of St Vodast als Foster London unto to the parish of St Mary le Bow London Alleadging the said Pish to be the place of [---] its last Legal Settlement

That Your Petrs conceive themselves aggriev'd by the said Warrant and Judgment of the sd two Justices and Do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humble pray Your Honr and Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd Pish of St Vedast als Foster may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of yr Honour and Worps touching this Appeal

And Your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
