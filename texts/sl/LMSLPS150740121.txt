To the Right Honourable William Beckford Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir William Stephenson Knight and Francis Cokayne Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 12th. day of December last John Dunn was Removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the sd. City of the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of his last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrt. of the said two Justices of the peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing & Determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

John Morsan Thos. Lewis Churchwardns.

Thos. Marfield Richd. Chappell Overseers of the Poor
