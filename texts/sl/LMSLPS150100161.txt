To the Rt Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth

That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) dated the 14th. day of Aprill Anno Dni 1699 Mary Tuffnell an Infant Daughter of William Tuffnell was brought to you Petrs. to be pvided for became her said Father formerly dwelt in your Petrs. said Parish of St. Buttolph whereas in truth the said Mary by Indenture of Appnticehood dated the 20th. day of Aprill 1698 was legally bound to one John Abney of the Parish of St. James Duker place Taylor for the Terme of nyne yeares As by the same Appeareth And is legall settled in the said Parish of St. James Duties place as aforesaid

Therefore your Petr. humble Pray your Honr. & Worll. to hear them all in the prmisses And to order the Churchwardns. of St. James Dukes place to Appear before you and to Ralph the said warrant made by the said two Justices And candle said Mary Tuffnell to the said Parish of St. James taken place where shee is legally settled to be by them kept & paid as for untill they can Free themselves of the charge thereof by due course of Lawe

And They Shall Pray Etc.
