To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace at their General Session assembled at Guildhall London

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Kite and Sir Thomas Rawlinson Knt: two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the City of London and Libertys thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st day of July 1767. Elizabeth Proudfoot Widow of Andrew Proudfoot deceased was removed and conveyed from and out of the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St Luke Middx.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that this Court will be pleased to make such Order in the Premisses for the relief of your Petitioners as they shall seem meet.
