To the right honourable the Lords Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

9/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That one Nathanael Tills with Catherine his wife & Susanna their Child by Vertue of a warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace of the said City one being of the Quored bearing date the ninth day of November now last past was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn London to the Parish of St. Sepulchers London aforesaid Adjudgeing the said Parish to be the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Nathanael Sills and of his wife and Daughter.

That your Petitioners doubt not to make it Appear that the said Nathanael Tills had no legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London whereby they Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this honble. Court will be pleased to hear them in the premises And order the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holbn. London to hear and Abide the Determination of the Court touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc

Edw: Leapedge Chill Willm. Bott [x] Churchwardens
