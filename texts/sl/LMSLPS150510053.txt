[...] Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir John Barnard Knt. & Aldran. Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date Eighteenth day of J [...] Elizabeth Copland Wife of Edward Copland was Removed from the Parish of Alhallows on London Wall London unto ye. Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London aforesaid alledging the said Parish of St. Botolph to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That Yor. Petr. conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removall as afd. Do Appeal to Yor. Lordp. & Worships thereform and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear ym. in the Premisses, and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of Alhallows on London Wall may appear before You, to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And Your Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.
