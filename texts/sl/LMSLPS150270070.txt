To the Rt: Honble Sr: Charles Peers Knt: Ld. Mayr. of the Citty of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Fludd A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. stands Comitted by his Maties Coror. for the said City of London Charged for murder of wch: Crime yor. Petr. is wholy innocent

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be admitted to Baile or otherwise discharged as to this Honble Court shall seeme meet

And yor Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
