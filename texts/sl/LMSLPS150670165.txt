among 1756 papers

To the Right Honble: the Lord Major and Worshipfull Gentlen: of the Court

Your humble Petitioners

Sheweth that being Poor men and familys residing in ye Country but hapening to be prefent when ye unlucky Affair of the Prisoner at ye. Bar's Accident hapened are Oblidg'd to attend ye. Honble: Courts of Justi. and being at great Expence and loss of time Most humbly beg your Honrs: if Possible to grant them Some redress and your Petitioners Shall always be in Duty bound to Pray Etc

Thos. Smith Wm. Yarbury
