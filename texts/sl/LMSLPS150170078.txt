To the Rt. Honble. Thomas Rawlinson Knt Lord Major of the Citty of London And to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Court

And Humble Peticon of John Macsmith

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. was robbed of Seaventy two pounds by Jonathan Shipton his Lodger for wch he was comitted to Newgate on Sunday Last And Yor. Peticonr. haveing an Castle others remote cannott wth conveniency try him this Session, wthout prejudice

Therefore Your Peticonr Humbly prmes Yor. Lordpps will please to continue him in Newgate till Next Sessions And to Mr. Turnner May Enter the Order Soe to doe this to yor Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc.

And yor: peticonr: Shall pray Etc

Pet of Jno. Mackeith
