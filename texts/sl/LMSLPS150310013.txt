To the Rt Honrble Sr George Thorold Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of Sarah Atkinson Widdow-

Sheweth That yor Petr late husband James Atkinson in his life time had a lease of a Messuage or Tenement in the parish of the holy Trinity London which he afterwards converted into a meeting house for protestant Dissenters which said Tenement never was lett for more than Two & Thirty pounds P Annu before it was so converted into a meeting house as aforesaid That Since her said husbands Decease your Petr is become the Proprieter of the said meeting house, for which She is now rated and charged to pay to the Poor of the said Parish of the holy Trinity the Sume of Two Shillings and Six pence weekely which your petr. most humbly conrieves is unequal and much more than Other houses of the same or greater value in the said Parish are rated and charged-

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays Your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear her in the prmisses and grant her such relief therein as to yor Honr and Worpps Shall Seem meet

And She Shall ever Pray Etca-
