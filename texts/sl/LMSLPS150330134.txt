To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Wm. Stewart Knt. Ld. Mayr. of ye City of London And the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace At the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Wansworth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrt under the hands and Seals of Sr Wm Stewart Knt Lord Mayor and Sr Thomas Abney Knt two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor unus) bearing date the 6th Day of November 1721 One Jane Marlis Wido and her son & was Richard aged about sixteen Years were removed from and out of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the sd. parish of Wansworth in the County of Surry as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your petitioners thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices humbly appeal to Your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners humbly pray this Worpss Court to hear them by their Council against the said Order of removall and finally to determine this Appeal.

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
