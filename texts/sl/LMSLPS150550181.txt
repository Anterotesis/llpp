To the Right Honourable Henry Marshall Esqr. Lord mayor of the City of London and the Best of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace at the Guildhall in and for the said City assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatten Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrews Holbourn in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Sir Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor of the said City and Sir William. Billers Knight two of his majestys of the peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the twenty sixth day of October last James Hands and Charity his wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Bridgett Vulgarly Saint Brides London to the said Liberty of Saffron hill Hatten Garden and Ely Rents as the place of their last Legall Settlement Whereby your petitioner conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore your petitioners humbly appeal to this Honourable Court and pray such relief in the premises as to your Lordships and worships Shall seem meet

Joshua Cox Sollr for the appellts.
