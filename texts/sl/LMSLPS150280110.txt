To the Right honble the Lord Mayor of the Citty of Londns

The humble peticon [...] Appeale of the Churchwardens & Ovrsers of the poor of the parish of St. Clemts. Danes

Sheweth That by ordr. of undr. the hands & Seales of Sr Wm Thompson & Sr. Wm Withers two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Citty of Londn. (Quor unus) bearing Date the 4th. day of Aprill Instant Jane Lewis abt. Eight Elizth. Lewis six & John Lewis one yeare & three Quarters old were removed from the prcint of the White Fryers were removed to the Pish of St. Clemts. Danes in Com Middx as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor petrs. thinke themselves aggreived by the ordr of the two Justices doe appeale therefrom to this honble Cort
