To the Rt: Honble. Sr. John Houblon Knt. Lord Maj. of the Citty of London and other the Justices now Sitting att Justice Hall in the old Baley

The humble Peticon of William Walbanck

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner was Comitted to the Goals of Newgate the 20th: of March last by Warrt. from the Rt Honble the Lord Majt. charged to bee guilty of High Treason for Conspiring the Death of his Matre: and is now remaining in the said Goale

He humbly prayeth to bee tryed this Sessions For what he is Charged with or that he may bee bailed according to the Act of Parliament in that Case made and provided

And he Shall ever pray
