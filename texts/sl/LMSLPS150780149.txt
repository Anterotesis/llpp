To the Right Honble Sir Robert Kite Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipful the Aldermen his Bretheren; his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London at their General [---] Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Swithin in the said City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quourm) dated the thirteenth Day of December last Past, Martha Adderley Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Swithin as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this honble Court against the same and

Pray that this Honble Court will be pleased to hear and determine their said Appeal and make such Order therein as shall be Just.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
