3/4 To the Right Honble: Sr. Richd: Brocas Knt . Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said City & liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Matys. Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libertys (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Third day of this instant April Sebella Tarne Wife of Edward Tarne was latley removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne London to the said Parish of Allhallowes Staining as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices of the peace to appeal to your Lordship & Wordships against the said Order and humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to Your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And Yor. Peticoners Shall Ever pray Etc

Jere: Goodrich{ Church Warden
