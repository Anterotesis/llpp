To the Right Worshipfull his Maities Justices of the peace for the City of London in Generall Quarter Sessions at the Guild Hall of the said City Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Le Strand in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble. Sr. Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor of the said City and Robert Alsop Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (where of one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of February last James Newman Ann his wife and their Infant Son was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate [...] the said parish of St. Mary Le Strand in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legall Settlement

Your Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgemt. of the said two Justices of the peace appeal to this Court against the same and desire to be relieved therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
