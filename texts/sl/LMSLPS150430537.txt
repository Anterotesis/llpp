To the Right Worps his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London assembled in Generall [---] Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlx

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands and Seals of two of his for Majesties Justices of the peace of the City of London (quor unus) bearing dixte the Twenty first day of Aprill last Mathew Seeger was removed from the parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate London to the Said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate as the place of his last legall Settlement

Your Petrs thinking themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the Said two Justices of the peace and appeale to this Court against the Same, and humbly prays your Worps will be pleased to appoint a day to hear & determine the Same

May 19 1732 And your Petrs Shall prays Etc

Robt Wyrill Thos Sayers} Churchwardens

Henry Howard Jos Gamble} Overseers of the poore
