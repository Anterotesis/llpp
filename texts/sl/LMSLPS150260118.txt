To the right honble. Sr. William Humphrys Knt. and Baront. Loed Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace at the Sessions house in ye Old Baily

The humble Peticon and Prayer of Mary Barnaby als Saunders

Humbly Sheweth Thal your Peticonr. now lye's in the [...] Compten being Charged by William Bryan upon Suspicion of stealing a Ring a pair of Silver Spurrs and a Silver Snuff Rose.

That She hath lain in Prison over since the ninth day of March last in a very miserable Condicon and is innocent of the fact with which she is Charged.

Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly Pray's this honble. Court will be pleased to order her to be brought upon her Fryall this Sessions or admitted to Bail according to a late Act of Parliament Comonly called the Habeas Corpus Act.

And Yor. Peticonr. shall ever Pray Etc.
