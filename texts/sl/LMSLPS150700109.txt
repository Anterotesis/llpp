To the Rt Honble: Sir Charles Asgill, Knt. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Rt. Worshipful ye. rest of His Majty's Justices of ye Peace for ye sd. City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fish Street, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of Sir Robert Ladbroke, Knt, & William Stephenson, Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty ninth Day of September 1758, Mary Bawden, Wido. was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Mary Magdalein in Old Fish- Street, London, alledging ye sd. Parish of St. Mary Magdalen to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge Atty for ye. Appellants } That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agrieved by ye sd. Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal be Saved unto them, & ye. Hearing & Determining thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next Genl. Quarter Session of ye. Peace to be holden for this City, & that ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Gregory aforesaid, may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And Yor. Petitioners shall pray Etc.
