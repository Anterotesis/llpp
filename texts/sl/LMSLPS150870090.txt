To the Right Honourable John Wilkes Esquire Mayor and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their General Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kenderchurch in the County of Hereford

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of the Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifth day of June last Sarah Smith widow of James Smith and her three Children James aged Nine years, William aged seven Years and Ann aged five Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London to the said Parish of Kenderchurch in the County of Hereford (by the Name of Kynechurch in the County of Hereford) as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same and pray such relief in the Premisses unto this Honourable Court shall seem just and equitable

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc
