To the Rt: Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon and apepale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth London

Sheweth That Sarah Corbett Singlewoman by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quoru) bearing dat [...] 25th day of January last past was removed from the Parish of St James [...] place London unto the Parish of St Mary Woolnoth alleadgeing the said Parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of ye Said Sarah [...] tt.

That your Petitioner dou [...] prove that the said Sarah Corbett never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Mary Woolnoth whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs [...] bly Pray your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to heare [...] m in ye Prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the sd Parish of St James Dukes Place may appeare before you and hear and abide the determination of Your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale.

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.

Appeale St Mary Woolnoth agt St James Dukes place
