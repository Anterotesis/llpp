To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London and the nest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and the Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin within Ludgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Pass Warrant bearing date the 16th. day of June last under the hands and Seals of two of His Majestys of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) Charles aged Nine Years, Joseph aged seven Years and Mary aged four Years the Children of the late John and Mary Saul both deceased were removed from the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the City of London to the said parish of Saint Martin within Ludgate as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass warrant humbly appeal against the same and pray such relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seen meet

And your Petitiners shall ever pray Etc.
