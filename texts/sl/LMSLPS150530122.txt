Octor Sess 16th Geo: 2d To the Rt Honoble Sr George Heathcote Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Past of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guild Hall now Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under [...] and Seales of Your Lordship and W: Bernard Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the Said City (one being of the Quorum) Edward Frevil and Margaret his Wife were removed from the Parish of St Sepulchure London to the said Parish of st Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Edward Frevil by Which order of Removal Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

They therefore humbly Pray to be heard by their Councell on the Said Appeal and that this Honoble Court Will hear and determine the same the Church Wardens etc of St Sepulchre's London being Served With timely Notice of this Appeal

And Yor Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
