To the Right Honoble Francis Cockayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of James Hodgson.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner at the time of making of the Several Rates herein after mentioned was and Still is an Officer and Servant of the Governours of Christs Hospital (to wit) Master of the Royal Mathematical School of the said Hospital which is and for near 200 Years last past hath been a House of Charity and is & Standeth within the Parish of Christ Church London

That Yor. Petr. for doing his Duty and Service afsd. doth reside and Inhabit in a certain House or Building part of or belonging to the Governours of the sd. Hospital & Situate in the Parish aforesaid And your Petioner so residing and Inhabiting was by a Rate or Assessment made on the 15th. Day of November 1750 for the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish of 13 Weeks Comencing from the 15th. Day of July then last past to the 14th. Day of October Exclusive and by another like Rate or Assessmt. made on the 7th. Day of Febry 1750. for the like Relief for 13 Weeks Comencing from the 14th. Day of Octcober then last past Rated and Assessed with other Inhabitants of the same Parish towards the sd. Relief for and in Respect of your Petrs. said Residence & Habitation whereby yr. Petr. conceiveth himself Aggrieved And doth humbly Appeal agt. the same

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays yr. Lordship and this Honble. Court to take the Premes into Consideration and to Grant him such Relief therein as to Yr. Lordship and this Honble. Court shall seem meet.

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
