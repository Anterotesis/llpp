To the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their [...] Generall Quarter Session assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Barking London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Your Lordship and Sr Robert Ladbroke Knt two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourth Day of June [---] 1751 Rachael Sharnbrook Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint giles Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Allhallows Barking as the place of her last legall settlement

That Your Petrs humbly conceive Themselves aggreived by the Judgment and determination of the said two Justices And Do humbly appeal against the same and pray such releif in the premisses as to Your Lordship and This Honourable Court shall seem Meet

And Foras Much as your Petrs have not been able to give due Notice for the hearing and determination thereof and are not ready with their Witnesses Your Petrs humbly pray that the benefitt of this their Appeal may be saved unto Them and that the hearing and finall determination thereof may be adjourned unto the next Generall Quarter Session of the peace to be held for this Honourable City and to have an Order that all parties may then attend to hear and abide the same

And Your Petrs shall ever Pray

Sam Wilson Trowd: Cowell} Church Wardens

Thomas Stavley George Hill} Overseers
