To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hand and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th. Day of February 1747 John Smith Was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London to the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London aforesaid Adjudging the Last Legal Place of Settlement of the said John Smith to be there and Your Petr. Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal therefrom and humbly Pray Such Relief the Premises as to Your Lordship and Worship Shall seem meet and convenient

Burr Attorney for Alhallows Parish}

And Your. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
