To the Right Honoble. Sr. Thomas Lane Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the severall Parishes of St. Gyles wth. out Cripplegate St. Buttolph wth. out Algate St. Andrew Wardrobe St. Katherine Coleman St. Andrew Holborne St. Sepulchers St. Brides Allhallowe on the Wall St. Buttolph Aldersgate St. Buttolph wth. out Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That yor. Petitionrs are very much Overburthned wth. Poore And not able to raise Sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them Notwthstanding their Assessemts. are greater then heretofore yett the charge of their Poore is fare above their said Assessmt. As the same upon full examination hath many times Appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to heare the same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them their said Parishes lying some of them neere to the Suburbs & others of them in the suburbs of this City.

Their humble suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. and Worpps. is that yor. will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the contributions from other Parishes wth. in this City for and towards the releiveing their Poore As they have [...] formerly had wth. are Perticularly here under menconed or such further allowance as to goe. Honor. & Worps. that seem to meete

And yor. Petitionr. shall ever pray Etc

St. Gyles wth. out Cripplegate £90: St. Buttolph wth. out Algate 50: St. Andrew Wardrobe 40: St. Katherine Coleman 12: St. Andrew Holborne 7: St. Sepulchres 44:

St. Brides £15: Alhallowes on ye wall 2: St. Buttolph Aldersgate 18: St. Butt wth. out Bishopsgt:50:

1.9.95 Allowed & ordered according
