To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt. & Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to ye rest of His Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl .Quarter Sessions of ye Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye parish of St Bridget als Brides London.

Sheweth That [---] By Virtue under ye hands & Seals of two of this Maties Justices of ye Peace (Quor unus) bearing Date ye 15th day of August last James Rivers (now Lunatick) was removed from ye Pish of St Martins Ludgate London to ye said parish of St Bridget als Brides London alledging ye sd Parish to be the Place of this last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish may Appear before You and hear & abide the determination of ye Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray
