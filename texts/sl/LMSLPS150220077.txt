To the Rt: Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That one Joyce Sowle Widdow by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Queen) bearing Date the first Day of December last past was removed from the Parish of St Helens London unto the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of ye Said Joyce Sowle

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Joyce Sowle never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore humbly Pray your Honr. & Worpps will be pleased to heare them in ye Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the sd Parish of St Helens may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of You Honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs Shall Ever Pray Etc.
