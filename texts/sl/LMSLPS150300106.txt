To the Right Honble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Mayr of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace there General Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled

21/4 The humble Petition and Appeal of Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Parnerasin Pancras Lane London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of Quor) bearing Date the twenty first day of April last pase Mary Heathorne Singlewoman was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Undershaft into the said Parish of St. Pancras in Pancras Lane alledging the sd parish to be the place of her last Legal settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to from Honour & Warps therefrom

Your Petetioners therefore most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in ye premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Andrew Undershaft may appear before you & hear & abide the Determinacon of ye Honr & Worps touching ye sd appeal

And he shall never pray
