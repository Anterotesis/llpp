To the Right Honourable Wm. Benn Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands [---] and Seals of the Rt. Honrble Wm Benn Esqr Lord Mayorand Thomas Rawlinson Esqr two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the City of London and libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the tenth day of October [...] Instant Elizabeth Metcalf wife of John Metcalf who is run away from her and their Two Children Elizabeth Aged Six years & William Aged three Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx as the Places of their last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves Aggrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for the said City

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

Wm. Chimmons agt. for the Rolls Libty.}
