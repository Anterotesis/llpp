To the Right Honble Fras. Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor & to the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass under the hand & Seal of the Right Honble Fras. Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London dated the 11th. Day of July last Jane Watts Singlewoman was passed as a Vagrant from the said City of London to the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved and agt. the said Pass do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the sd. Appeal and be Saved unto them & that the hearing & Determination thereof may be adjd. to the next Genl Qur. Session of the peace to be held for this City & Libertys} [---]

Jno. Hampton Atty. for the Appellts.}

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
