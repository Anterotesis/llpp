To the Right Honoble. Sr Edward Bellamy. Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Michael Woodstreet in the said City of London

Sheweth That Whereas Frances the Wife of Samuel Davisby an Order dated the twenty third day of December last 1734 under the hands & Seals of your Lordship & Robert Alsop Esq. and alderman two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (One being of the Quorum) made upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Gyles without Cripplegate London is adjudged to has her last legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Michael Wood Street to which she hath been conveyed by Vertue of the said Order

That your Petitioners do humbly apprehend them- -selves to be agreived by the said Order for that the last Legal Settlement of the said Frances the Wife of the said Samuel Davis is not in the said Parish of St Michael Wood Street

Therefore and for other matters appearing in the said Order

Your Petioners do humbly appeal to this Honble Court against the said Order & pray the same may be quashed

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc
