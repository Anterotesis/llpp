Eliz Syms peticon

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Edwd: Clarke Knt: Lord Major of the Citty of London and other his Maties Justices now sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley.

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Syms

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr: was Comitted to the Poultry Counter the First day of June last by Sr. Robert Gefferys Warrant and charged for offering 29s: of new mony which was too light

That yor: Peticoner is there deteined whereby She is not in a Capacity of haveing the benefitt of the Habeas Corpus act by makeing her prayer the First day of the Sessions she not being in the County Goale

Therefore She humbly prayeth that she may bee brought Forthwith to the County Goale and also prayeth that she may bee tryed this Sessions or that he may bee admitted to baile as the Law in that Case directs And that this her prayer may bee Recorded

And she shall ever pray
