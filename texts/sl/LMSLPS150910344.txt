To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor And to the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London At their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition And Appeal of the Churchwardens And Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Without Bishopsgate in the Said City of London.

Sheweth That by An Order or Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seals of B. Kennett Esquire Mayor of the City of London And Thomas Hallifax Esquire Alderman of the said City two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of May last Ann the Widow of Robert Smith deceased Was Removed And Conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Botolph Without Bishopsgate in the Saith City As the Place of the last legal Settlement of the Said Ann Smith Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Carter & Simpson Sollrs. for the Appellants Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray Your Worships to Appoint a day in this Present Session to hear And determine their said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
