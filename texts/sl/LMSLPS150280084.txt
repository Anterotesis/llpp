To the Rt Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quartor Sessions assembled:

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Bartholomew the great London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Nineteenth Day of December last past Mary Willford an Infant about Six months Old was removed from out of the parish of Allhallows the great London unto the Said parish of St Bartholomew the great London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly conceiveing themselves aggrieved by the said warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices do most humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom.

Your Petr. therefore most humbly pray you Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said parish of Allhallows the Great may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr: and Worpps [---] touching this Appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc
