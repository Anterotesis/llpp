To the Right Honble The Lord Mayor of the City of London and others His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Diouis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of George Heathcote Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London an [...] never Ladbroke Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of September last Mary Braban Widow of Robert Braban was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the parish of St. Diouis Backchurch London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. humbly conceiving then of Aggrieved by Warrt. and Judgmt. of the said be so Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for relief in the Premisses

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
