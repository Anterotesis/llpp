To the Right Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poors of the Parish of St. Michaell Royall London

Sheweth That Elizabeth & Jane Selby the Children of William Selby by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quox) were brought by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of St. Martins Vintrey London to yor Petrs. to be pvided for on the Path of Isaac Grevill & William Ashwin That the said William Selby was charged with & paid the Polltaye for severall yeares past in the said Parish of St. Michaell Royall And therefore Suggested to be his last legall settlement Whereas in truth the Pavment of the Polltaye is only a psonall taye And not a publick taye or levy of the said parish whereby to gaine a legall Settlement in any parish

Yor. Petrs. therefore humble pray yor. Honors. & Worpps. to order the said Chruchwardens of St. Martins vintrey to appeare before you And to hear them all in the prmisses And to Quash the said Warrant And send the said Elizabeth & Jane Selby backe to the said parish of St. Martins Vintrey where the said William Selby dwell & inhabited To be pvided for & mainteyned According to Lane And have this charged allowed them wch. They have wrongfully Insteyned by keeping the said Children

And yor: Petrs. Shall Pray Etc
