To the Rt. Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And also to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

8/10 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Mary Colechurch London

Sheweth That one Ann Stockwell Singlewoman by Virtue of Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the city of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Eight Day of October instant was removed from the Parish of St Bennett at Pauls wharfe London unto the said parish of St Mary Colechurch alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That your petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Ann Stockwell never had any legal settlement in the said parish of St Mary Colechurch whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Bennett at Pauls wharfe may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etca
