To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Master Wardens & Assistants of the Company of Coachmakers & Coach harnesse makers London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. Hall for the manageing the publicke Affaires of their Company is Scituate in Noble Streete in the Parish of St. Mary Steyning Where is a Small Dwelling for their Clarke. That yor. Petrs. are Charged and Assessed to the Poore of the Said Parish with the Sume of 40s.8d. in respect thereof Quarterly which is a very greate Rate And more than the Inhabitants in the Same Parish in proporcon doe pay

Their humble Peticon to yor. Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens may appeare before you with their bookes of Assessments And That you will be pleased to abate the Said Assessement to the Proporcon of other Inhabitants within the Said Parish

And They Shall Pray Etc
