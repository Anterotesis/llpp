To the Right Honble Wm Nash Esqr Lord Mayor of ye City of London & to ye Worshipful the Alderman his Majestys Justices of ye Peace for ye same City at their Genl Qr Session of ye Peace assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St. Geo. the Martyr in Southwarke

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under ye hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of ye Peace for ye sd. City of London (one whereof being of ye Quorum) bearing date the 16th day of Septemr last Elizth Parker Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Bridget London to ye sd. Parish of St George Southwark us ye Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye order of ye sd. two Justices, humbly appeal agt the same, & pray that this heatle Court will please to hear & determine their sd. appeal & make Such Order thereon as shall be just

And yr Petitioners shall pray Etc
