To the Right Honourable Sir Williams Thomas Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Majesty's Justice of the peace assembled at the Guildhall in and for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace on Monday the fourteenth Day of October in the Twenty second year of the Reign of him Majesty King George the 3d.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulcher London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal under the hand and seals of Sir William Plomer knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Thomas Hallifax Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London and liberties thereof one whereof being of the Quorum being date the twenty Sixth day of September One Thousand seven Hundred and Eighty two Elizabeth the Widow of William Gibson was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of saint Ann Black Friars to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of her Legal settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal at of the said two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the said appeal may be adjourned to the next General Sessions of the Peace
