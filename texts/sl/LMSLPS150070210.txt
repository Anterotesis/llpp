To ye Rt. Honble. Sr Jno Houblon knt. Lord Mayor. And To ye Rest of ye Just now Setting att ye old Baily

The humble Peticon of Robert Weldrum a Prisonr in Newgate

Most Humbly Sheweth That yor Lordsps Peticonr Peticoned this Honble. Bench ye Last Sessions To bee Tryed a Bailed, since wch time yor Peticonr hath Suffered much hardship by Reason of his Confinemt haveing nothing Lost to Subsist

Yor Lordsps Peticonr A game most Humbly Prayer To bee Tryed Bailed or Discharged & that his Prayer may bee Recorded

And yor Peticonr. Shall Pray
