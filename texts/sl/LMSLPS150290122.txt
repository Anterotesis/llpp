To the Right Honble Sr Willm Lowen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpl the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace for ye sd City Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Magnus by London Bridge

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Sents of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Fourth day of September last past one Samuel Lynn was Removed from the parish of St Sepulchres to the Said parish of St Magnus by London Bridge Alledging the said parish to be the place of his last Legal Settlemt

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Sd two Justices And do most humbly appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honour and Worps will be pleased to hear them in the promisses and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd Pish of St Sepulchres may appear before You and hear and abide the Determicon of the Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc
