To the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor. and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libert ye thereof

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor. of the Parish of St. Botolph Without Bishopgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys therof (one being of the Quorum) bearning Date the Tenth day of January 1744 Elizabeth Thompson Wife of James Thompson was removed from the Parish of St Michael Crocked Lane London to the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopgate London Adjudging the last Legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Thompson to be there and your Petrs. Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly pray such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem Meet and Convenient

Burr Attorney for Bishopgate Parish}

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
