To the Right Honourable Sir Matthew Blakiston Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Honourable Bench in their General Session of Goal Delivery for the City of London Assembled

The Humble Petitioner and Prayer of Samuel Lee a Prisoner

Sheweth That an the Tenth Day of July last your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner by John Fielding Esquire to his Majestys Goal of Newgate Within the Suburbs of the City of London on Oath of Matthews Braugham and Another for Forging and Counterfeiting a Bill of Exchange Purporting to be the Bill of Benjamin Sullen for Forty Pounds Payable to Rogers Ruding a Account of Thomas Poughfey and for Imblishing the same as True, Knowingst to be False, Forged, and Counterfeited with Intent to Defrand John Price and is now a Prisoner Confined in Newgate under the Same Charge

Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly Prays that at this Present Sessions of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case Made and Provided

And Your Petitioner shall pray Etc, Saml Lee
