To the Right Honoble: the Lord Mayor and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Saviour in Southwark in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of the Right Honoble. Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 10 day of January Instant Ann Pujelow (Wife of John Pujelow gone away from her) was removed from the Parish of Allhallows London Wall in the City of London to our said Parish of [...] St. Saviour in Southwark as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby We think ourselves aggrieved

We therefore humbly appeal against the said Ordered pray that this Court will now be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal the Respondents being ready and desirous of having the same now heared & determined

And as in duty bound Your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Rd Carpenter Richd: Hale} Churchwardens

Jno Frietrams Willm. Jorden} Overseers
