To ye Right Honble Sr. Samuel Garrard Bart. Lord Majr. of ye City of London & to ye rest of the Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Katharine Speed ats Miller

Sheweth That yor. Petr. is now A Prisoner in Newgate Charged for Felony

Therefore Humbly prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be admitted to Baile or Ayerweyd discharged as this Honble Court shall think fitt

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
