To ye. Honable: & Worspll: her Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. [...] the Ann humble peticon & appeales of ye. Chu [...] Overseers of ye. poor of ye. parish of St. Leonard in ye. County of Midlesex

1704 10/4 Sheweth That by a Warrtt: or Order under ye. hands & Seales of Two of her Majties. Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd City of London (Quor unus) bearing date ye Tenth day of Aprill 1711. One Grace Bayley about Nine yeares old daughter of William Bayley & Thomas in his wife was removd from ye. parish of St. Anne Alderlgate in ye. sd. City of London ye. Said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch therein sittings forth that ye sd Gracets agley was borne there & that ye sd parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch is ye. Legall Setlemt. of ye. sd. Grace Bayley;

Your peticoners therefore findinge themselves agreived by ye sd Warrt. or order & Judgmt. of ye. sd. two Justices of ye. peace humbly appeales to this Honable: Court against ye Same Your peticoners not doubling but it will plainely appeare that ye Last Legall Setlemt. of ye sd. Grace Bayley is not in ye. sd parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and humbly pray your Worsps. will be pleased to heare & determine this appeales your peticoners leaveinge given due Notice to ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye. sd. pish of St. Anne Aldersgate of ye sd. appeale.

And your peticoners shall ever pray Etc.

Wallis Deane Ralph Harwood}
