To ye Rt. Honble Sr. Samuell Garrard Bart. Lord Majr. of ye Citty of ye Citty of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Jane Bench A Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comd. A Prisonr ye 14th. of November last Charged for Stealing a Hankerchiefe from Edward Phillips

That Yor. Petr. is annocent of the Fact charged agt. Her

Therefore Humbly prayes to be dryed this Sessions or to be admitted to Baile otherways discharged as this Honble Court shall thincke fitt

And yor. Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
