To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the use of this Maties. Justices of the Peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepny in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (Quor) unus bearing date the 7th. day of July Anno Dm 1699 One Anne Gregory of Female Bastary Child was sent to yor. Petrs. to be provided for upon the Severall Oathes of Mary Mills Mary Glynn & Elizth. East that the said Female child is a Bastard And was borne of the Body of one Hanna Lea in the Hamblett of Ratcliffe aforesaid in yor. Petrs. said Parish and was there baptized by the name of Elizabeth Whereas in truth the said female Bastard child now sent to yor. Petrs. is not the Child of the said Hanna Lea Nor was ever borne of her Body in yor. Petrs. said Parish

Yor. Petrs. Humbly pray yor. Honor. & Worpps. to hear them in the prmisses & Order the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Gregoryes to appeare here before you And send the said Female Bastard child to the said Parish of St. Gregoryes To be there kept & provided for untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And They shall ever Pray Etc
