To the Rt. Honble Sr: George Merttins Knt Ld. Mayr: of the City of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Peticon of Jonathan Wild A Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Peticr: stands Comitted for feloniously rescuing from a Constable one Roger Johnson a Convicted Felon knowing him to be such whereof yor. Peticr. is innocent

Yor Petr: therefore humbly prayes this Honble Court to be tryed this sessions or to be discharged upon Baile or otherwayes

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Jonth Wild
