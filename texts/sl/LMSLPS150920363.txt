The Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Brass Crossby and John Hart Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing Date the twenty third day of February last past Elizabeth Grace Lawson Wife of Olive Lawson and their Daughter Sophia Aged Eighteen Months were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Moses in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Mary Matfellon otherwise White Chapel as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal Agst the said Order and pray this Court that their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the Hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be held for the said City and that Your Petitioners may then have such relief herein as shall be thought meet

Mr. Skutt sold for Appellants Goodmans Fields 23d April 1781

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
