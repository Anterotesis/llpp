To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Alderman of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and the Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bow London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant bearing date the Eleventh day of November now last past under the Hands and Seals of the Lord Mayor of the City and London and John Boydell Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) Mary the Widow of Robert Williams and her illegitimate Child named William aged Twelve Months and upwards were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Katherine Cree Church in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bow London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Williams whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humble Appeal against the said Order of pass Warrant and pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them; And that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City of London

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.
