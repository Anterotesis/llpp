To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of February last past Margaret Biggs wife of John Biggs, gone from her, and their four Children, to wit, John George Aged Seven Years Charles Aged five Years, Anthony Aged two Years, and Sarah Aged Six Weeks were removed from the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street London to the said Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook London as the Last Legal Place of their Settlement By which Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court Against the said Pass Order

D: Webb Agent for the Appellants Your Petitioners therefore pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this Appeal and give them such relief in the premisses as to Your Worships shall seem Just and meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
