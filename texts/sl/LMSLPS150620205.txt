To the Right Honble. John Blachford Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace at their general quarter sessions of the peace in and for the said City assembled.

The humble petition and appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Norton Falgate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the eighth day of October 1750. Ann Powell aged forty Six was removed from and out of the parish of St. Andrew in Holborn in the said City of London into the Liberty of Norton Falgate in the County of Middx as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the peace Do therefore humbly Appeal against the same and pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next general quarter Sessions of the peace to be held at Guildhall in and for the said City and that all parties may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of the said Appeal

And your petitioners shall every pray Etc.

Wm. Carter Attry. for Appellants
