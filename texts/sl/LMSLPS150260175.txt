To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their Generall quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon and appeale of the Overseer of the poor of the precinct of the old Artillerry Ground in the Libty of the Tower of London

Sheweth That by an order or Pass Warrt: under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for this City and Libertys thereof (quor unns) bearing date the 25th. day of July last one Anthony Chapman was removed from the parish of St: Gregory by St. Pants in London To the Overseers of the Poor of the Tower Hambletts in the Liberty of the Tower of London as to the place of his last legall Settlement and left wth: your petrs; to provide for him and that by and other order or pass warrt: under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties: Justice of the peace for the Sd. City and Libertys thereof (Quorum unus) bearing date the 28th. day of July last the said Anthony Chapman was againe removed from the said Parish of St. Gregory by St: Pauls in London to the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Tower Royall, and againe left wth: your Petr to provide for him the said Anthony Chapman

Your Petrs: findeing himselfe agreived by both the orders abovemenconed humbly appeals to this honrble Court against the same

And prays that yor. Worships would be pleased to grant your Petr Such releife in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet upon hearing both parties.

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
