To the Right Honourable Sr. John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor St. John Strange Knt. Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof in their Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petiton and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order [...] under the hands and Seals of the said Sr. John Salter and George Champion Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof Bearing date the Twenty Second day of September 1740 Ann Tole said to be the wife of Francis Tole was removed from the parish of Saint Sepulchres London to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel to the Place of her last Legal Settlement By which said pass Warrant or Order of Removal your Petitioners apprehend themselves agrieved and Do appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore your Petr. and appellants pray that this Court will Please to Appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Sepulchres and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgment and & Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal and that [...] Petrs. and Appellants may be released therein according to Law and Justice

And yr. Petr. Etc

Wm Polackshaw Jno. Ashton} Church ward & Overseers of Wt. Chapell
