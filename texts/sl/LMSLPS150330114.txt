To the Right Honble Sr Wm Stewart Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers at the poor of the parish of St George Botolph Lane London

Sheweth That [---] for many years past the Sum of Six pound has yearly been ordered to be paid at the General Quarter Sessions for London [...] to the parish of Allhallows on the wall London for the relief of their poor of ye sd parish

That Your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Order their poor being much increased and their Rates much higher then at the time of making [...] the sd Order Several of their Inhabitants being gone to decay & the Charge of the sd parish much increased and therefore most humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

And therefore humbly pray this Court will Order that the consideration hereof may be Adjourned to the next general or Quarter Sessions and all parties concerned may then Attend & abide the determinacon of the Court touching the sd Order

And they Shall ever pray Etc
