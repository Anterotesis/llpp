To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the City of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of Sr. Jno. Fryer Mayor and Sr. John Ward two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing date the 29th day of August One thousand Seven hundred and Twenty one [...] one John Davis and Susanah his Child Aged about Eleven Months was removed from and out of the Parish of Allhallows Staining London to the Said Parish of St. Margt. Westminster as the place of their Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petitioners thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Worll. Court to hear them by their Councill against the said Order of removall and finally to determine this Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc
