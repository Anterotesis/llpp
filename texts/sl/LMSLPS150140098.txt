The Peticon & Appeale of Owen Loyd

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Sam: Dashwood Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Wor her Maties. justices of the Peace in teh Genall Quarter sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of Owen Loyd

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was by an order under the hands of yor. Lopp & Sr. Francis Child Knt. Charged to be the Father of a female bastard child lately bound on the body of Elizth. Cotton Spr in the Pish of St. Dunstans in the West Whereby yor. Petr. was charged to pay unto the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the is Pish the nune of Five pounds the charge & Expence in keeping the sd Elizth. Cotton

Whereas in truth [...] Yor Petr. never had any knowledge or acquaintance wth. the sd Elizth. Cotton

His humble into unto yor. [...] Honr. & Wopps. is That yor. wilbe Pleased to heare yor. Petr in the Prmissed and discharged the said order of twoe Justices [...]

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
