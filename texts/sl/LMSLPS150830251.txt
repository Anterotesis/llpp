To the Rt, Honble the Lord Mayor and Rt, Workshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under they Hands and Seals of the Rt, Honble William Nash Esqr, (Lord Mayor) and Thomas Hallifax Esqr, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing Date the 21st, day of March 1772, John Elkin was Removed from the Parish of st. Mary at Hill in the City of London to the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster as the Place of his last Legal Settlement, whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays Your Lordship and this Worshipfull Court will hear and determine this Appeal according to Law

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Wm Gibbard Thos. Hatels Church Wardens of St. Margaret Westmr.

Thos Hudson Thos. Greenaway} Overseers of the Poor of the same Parish.
