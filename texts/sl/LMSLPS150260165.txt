To the Rt: Honble: the Lord Mayor or & Aldermen & the rest of His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the City of London assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon. & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le Bones in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands & Seals of William Thomson Esqr: & Sr: Thomas Abney Knt. two of His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City of London Quor unus bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of June last Ann Fryer wife of Eleazer Fryer was passed from the parish of Alhallows on the Wall in the said City to the said Parish of St. Mary le Bone as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the said Ann Fryer

Yor: Peticonr. think themselves aggrieved wth. then Judgmt. of the said two Justices, appeal to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Alhallow on her Wall & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
