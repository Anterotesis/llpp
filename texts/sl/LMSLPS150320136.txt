To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace for the Same City Assembled.

The humble Petitions & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands & Seals of two of his Maty's Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox unus) bearing Date the 4th day of April Inst Elizabeth Mortarshell Wido of Richd Mortarshell was sent & delivered by the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Somerset London unto the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish of St Nicholas Coleabby London Alledging the sd Parish to be [---] place of her last Legal Settlement.

That your Petrs conceive themselves aggrieved by by the sd Warrt and Judgment of the sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worpsl. therefrom

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray Your Honour & Worpl will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd parish of St Mary Somerset May Appear before you & hear & abide the Determinacon of the Court touching the Sd Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
