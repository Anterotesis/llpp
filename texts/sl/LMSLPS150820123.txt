To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull has Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Brass Esqr. Crosby Mayor and Thomas Hallifax Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (one being of the [...] ) bearing Date the fifteenth day of December last past three Bastard Children to Wit Ann Aged five years Edmund Aged three Years and Robert Aged two years all Born of the Body of ann Baker spinster who hath deserted them were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish, of Saint Botolph Bishopgate in the said City of London to the said Hamlet of Mile End New Town as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and therefore Appeal to this Honourable Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premises as to the Court Shall see in meet

Dated the 19th day of Janry 1771}

Francis Redhead} Churchwarden

Wm. Lanson Isaac Boyd} Overseers
