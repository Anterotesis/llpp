To the Right Honrble Sr. R Hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace of the sd. City in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of Decer. 1745 Peter Jones & Eliz his Wife Were removed from & out of the parish of St. Botolph with out aldgate Within London in to our Said parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex aforesaid as the place of their Last Legall Settlement

Your petitioners Conceving themselves is Agreived by the Said order or pass Warrant do humbly appeal against the Same and pray Such Relief in the premisses as to this Honrble Court Shall Seem Meet & your peter. shall ever pray Etc.

Robert. Turner Thos. Robinson} Church Wes.

Mr. Terement Robt. Day} overseers
