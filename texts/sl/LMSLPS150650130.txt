To the Rt. Honble: Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knt. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Worshipful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of Ye. Peace, for ye said City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Moses, in ye City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship & Thomas Rawlinson Esqr, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & the Liberties thereof (one being of Ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Sixteenth Day of July 1753, Robert Curtice, Deborah his Wife, & their two Children, Mary, aged about three years, & Elizabeth aged about Six Months, were Removed from ye. Parish of Saint Peter near Pauls Wharf, London, unto ye. Said Parish of Saint Margaret Moses, London, alledging ye sd. Parish of St. Margaret Moses, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Atty. for the Appellants } That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal; Do Appeal to your Lordp & Worps therefrom & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal, be served unto them, & the Hearing & Determining thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace to be holden for ye. said City of London & the Liberties thereof, And that ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Peter near Pauls Wharf, London, may then appear before you, to hear and abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Lordp & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.
