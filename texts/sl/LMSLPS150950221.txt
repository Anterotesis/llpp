To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and other his Majesty's Justices of his Peace at their General Quarter Sessn. for the sd. City assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen old Fish in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Brass Crosby and John Hart Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the sd. City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of Decr. 1783 Agnis Larkin was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Paul in the sd. City [...] as the Place of her last legal Settlemt. wheby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court agst. the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a Time for hearg. and determining the said Appeal & that all Persons concerned may than attend

Janry 1783
