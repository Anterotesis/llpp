John Rt Honable Sr. Francis Forbes Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl the rest of this Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the several Parishes of St Giles wihtout Cripplegate St. Botolph without Aldgate St. Andrew Wardrobe St. Catherine Coleman St. Andrew Holbourn St. Sepulchres St. Brides Allhallows on the Wall St. Botolph Aldersgate & St. Botolph with on Bishopsgate

Sheweth That your peticoners are very much over burtheiced with poor and not able to raise sufficient sumes of mony amongst themselves to relieve them notwithstanding their assessments are greater them heretofore yet the Charge of the poor is far above there assessments as the same upon Examination hath divers times appeared to several worthy Aldermen of this City appointed and their poor daily increasing upon their sd Parishes some of them lying near the suburbs and others of them in the suburbs of this City

Their Humble Sait thereof for your Honour & Worps is That your will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contributi- ons of the other Parishes within this City for and towards the rest of their Poor as they have formerly had which are perticularly hereunder menconed or such further allowance as to your Honour & Worps shall seem meet
