To the Right Honoble Sr. John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of [...] Right Honoble Sr. John Salter Knight Lord Mayor of the City aforesaid and [...] Edward Belamy Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the same City and Liberties thereof bearing date the Ninth day of June Instant Mary Woodall Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of Saint Buttolph Bishopsgate London to our said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. conceive themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore Humble Pray they may be Releived against the said Warrant or Order in such Manner as this Honoble Court shall seem meet

And your Petrs. as in Duly bound shall ever Pray Etc

John Hammond Thos. Davis} Church Wardens

Wm. Lovejoy Wm. Budgen} Overseers
