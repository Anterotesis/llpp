To the Rt. Honble: Sr Charles Duncombe [...] tnt. St. Majr. [...] the City of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Willm Felewood A Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That ye Petr. was Comittee to Prison in December last Charged for selling a grett of plate stolen from Mr Shiles & was afterwards Charged in Custody for breaking his house of wch. fact he is [...] ment

Yor Petr. therefore prayer to be Tryed this Sessions or that this Honble Court would be pleased to admit him to Baile

And yor Petr: Shall ever pray Etc.
