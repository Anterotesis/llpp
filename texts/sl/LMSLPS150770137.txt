To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor & the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the twenty sixth day of April last under the hands & Seals of George Nelson Esqr Mayor & Sir William Stephenson Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Liberties thereof Ann Carter the Wife of John Carter gone from her & their Child Ann aged three years were removed from the Parish of Trinity in the sd. City of London to the said Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of their last legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitioners find themselves aggreived by the said Order or Pass Warrant & Do therefore humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that You will be pleased to Order the Church wardens & overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Trinity & all other Persons concerned to Attend this Court this Present Sessions, & to hear & abide the Judgment of & determination of this Court touching the Premises and that Your Petitions May have push relief therein as to Justice of Law doth appertain

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

John Portor Jno. Thacker} Church Wardens

Saml Kingston} Overseers
