To the Right Honourable Stephen Theodore Janssen Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Edmunds Bury in the County of Suffolk

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hand and Seal of Francis Cokayne Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing Date the 17th. Day of February 1755 Elizabeth Armstrong was removed from the Parish of Saint Michael Cornhill London to the said Parish of Saint Edmunds Bury in the said County of Suffolk as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and do humbly Appeal against the same and Pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners Etc.

Wm. Chapman Sollr. for the Parish of St. Edmunds Bury in the County of Suffolk
