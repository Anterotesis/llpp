To the Right Honourable [...] Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City at their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in Ironmonger Lane London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty third Day of July last Martha Thomas Widow of Evan Thomas deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Martin in Ironmonger Lane as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them, and the hearing and Determination thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said City
