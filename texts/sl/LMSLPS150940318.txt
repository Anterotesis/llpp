To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Benjamin Wheatley and Margaret White (by the description of Margaret his Wife) and their two Children namely Ann aged twelve Years and upwards and Elizabeth aged four years and upwards were by Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London bearing Date the First Day of August last removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners and Appellants conceive themselves aggrieved by such part of the said Order of Removal as Respects the Settlement of the said Margaret White and the said two Children and humbly appeals to the Judgement of this Court against the same and pray that they would be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may abide the Judgment and Determination thereof And that Your Petitioners may be relieved in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Wade Junr. Sollr. for the Appeallants

No. 33. Lambs Conduct Street
