To the Lord Mayor and Aldermen his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the City

The Humble Petition and Appeal or the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Molser in the County of Buckingham.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Ladbroke Knight and William Plomer Esquire Aldermen and two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Sixth Day of November one thousand Seven hundred and Seventy two Mary Tame and Joseph Tame her Infant Child were Removed from the Parish of Saint Ann in Black Friars in the said City of London to the said Parish of Molser in the said County of Buckingham As the place of then last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and Do Crave Leave to appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
