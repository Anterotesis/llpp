To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions of ye. Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London One being of the Quox bearing date the Fourth day of November last One Anne Sheppard Widdow with Eight Children were by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the doore of the Parish of St. Mary Aldermary London Sent to yor. Petrs. to be Provided for upon the Oath of Elias Baker That the Said Anne Sheppard lived in and kept an house in the Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle London of about £15 P Ann Rent for above twoe Yeares after her husbands Death Whereas in Truth the Said Anne Sheppard Since Shee Lived in yor. Petrs. Parish hath taken an house of above £x yearely Rent in the Parish of St Mary Aldermary

Wherefore yor. Petrs. humbly pray yor. Honors. & Worpps. to heare them in the prmisses And Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St.Mary Aldermary London To appeare here before you And to Shew Cause why the Said Warrant made by the Said twoe Justices Should not be Quashed And the Said Anne Sheppard Sent to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Said Parish of St. Mary Aldermary London To be by them provided for and maintained untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And yor. Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
