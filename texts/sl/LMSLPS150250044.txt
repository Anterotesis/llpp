To the Rt. Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London.

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le Bow within the sd. City.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or Ordr. dated the twenty third day of March last past undr. the Hands & Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (Quor unus) one Sarah Baldock was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchrs. London to the said Parish of St. Mary le Bow as the place of her legal Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or Ordr. of the said Two Justices, and humbly appeale agat. the same to this Honble Court,

And yor. Peticonrs. humbly Pray to have such relief in the prmisses as to yor. Wopps shall appear just & reasonable Etc

And they shall ever pray Etc
