To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Robert Stevenson Aged Three Years & Catherine Stevenson Age done Year Children of William Stevenson Decd. by Ann his Wife whose Settlements could not be found were by Order or pass Warrant dated the Second Instant under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knt. & Alderman of the said City Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the place of their last legal Settlement

Jam Bolton Atty. for the Appeall Your Petrs: and Appellants conceive themselves Aggrieved thereby Humbly Appeal to the Judgement of this Honourable Court against the same and pray that the Court will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal And that all persons concerned may Abide the Judgement & Determination thereof And that your sd. Petrs. may be Relieved as this Honoble Court shall see meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
