To the Right Honble: [---] Sr. John Fryer Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Magnus the Martyr London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quox unus) bearing Date the 17th day of September last Mary Russell wife of Thomas Russell with Miles their Child aged four years & an half were Sent and delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows Staining London to the Said parish of St Magnus the Martyr London alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrt & Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honr & Worps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye sd Parish of Allhallows Staining may appear before you and hear and abide the determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
