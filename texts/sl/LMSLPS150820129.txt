To the Rt. Honble: Brass Crosby Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and The Rest of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by a Vagrant Pass or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Wm. Stephenson and Richd. Oliver two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing Date the 9th. day of July last Peter Paul a Lunatick was Removed from the Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Pauls London to the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent as the Place of his last Legal Settlement by which We think our Selves Agrieved And Appeal to this Court against the said Pass or Order.

Wherefore your Petitioners Pray this Honble. Court will be Pleased on the hearing of all Parties touching the said Appeal to Grant them such Relief in the Premises and to make such further Order therein As to this Court shall Seem Meet

And Yor. Petitioners shall pray Etc

Abrm Dalton Wm Hales} Churchwardens

Woodhead Bruce Overseers of the Poor
