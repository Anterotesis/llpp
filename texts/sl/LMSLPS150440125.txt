To the right honble John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City now in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sr. Mary Samerset London

Sheweth That Elizabeth Peete Wife of William Peete & their two Children ( that is to say) Mary aged abt. two years & Rebecca aged four Months & upwards were by Order or Pass Warrt. dated the 25th. day of August last under the Hands & Seals of you the sd. John Barber Esqr. & 2st Richd. Broras Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the sd. City & the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) removed & conveyed from the Parish of st. Andrew by the Wardrobe London unto the sd. Parish of St. Mary Somerset London

Dat 2d. Oct 1733. That yr. Petrs. humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Appeal to this honble Court for such Releife in the premisses as this Court shall think proper,

And your Petrs. shall ever pray et.

Henry Edwards, Geo Deacon} ChurchWardens

Jo: Chitty Jonth: Manrop Aug. Meadons} Overseers of the Poor
