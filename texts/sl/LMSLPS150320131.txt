To the Right Honble Sr John Fryer Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the rest of his Maties Justices of ye peace at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Bridget als Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 4th day of March last Mary Smalley wife of John Smalley with her child named Sarah aged about Twelve Months were Sent & delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the [---] Parish of St Anne Blackfryers London unto the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish of St Bridget als Brides London alledging the Sd parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt & Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom.

Your Petionrs therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye Sd parish of St. Anne Blackfryer may Appear before you and hear & abide the Determinacon of this Court touching the Sd Appeal

And they Shall ever pray
