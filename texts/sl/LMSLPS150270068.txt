To ye: Rt. Honble Sr: Charles Peers Knt. Ld. Mayr. of ye. City of London & to ye. rest of ye honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Carny A Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comitted to the said Goale Charge with ye felonious comitint. of a Rape of wch. he is a innocent

Yor. Petr. therefore prayes to be tryed this Sessions or to be discharged

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
