To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guildhall Assembled

1409 The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable St. Henry Marshall Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and St. Robert Willimot Knight two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing Date the tenth Day of July last past Elizabeth therein said to be the Wife of Robert Hobbs a Prisoner Confind in Goal was removed from the Parish of St. Bridget in the City of London to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved And Appeal to this Court againt the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the said appeal and that all Parties Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

John Tovey Vestry Clerk of the Parish of St. Martin in the Feilds 7th. October 1745
