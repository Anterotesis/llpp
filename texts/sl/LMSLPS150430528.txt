To the Right Honble:Francis Child Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye: Hands & Seals of Yor Lordship & Humbly Parsons Esqr one of the Aldermen of this City & two of his Majty's Justices of the Peace for the same City & the Liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Oath the Twenty second Day of December last past Elizabeth Carter single- woman was removed from the Parish of St Katherine Coleman London unto the said Parish if St. Andrew Under Shaft London alledging the said Parish of St. Andrew Under Shaft to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That yor. Petitionrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do appeale to yor: Lordship & Worps therefrom, and humbly pray that yor: Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear ym in the prmes & Order That ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye said Parish of St Katherine Coleman may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgement & Determinacon of yor: Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale

And Yor: Peticonr. shall pray Etc
