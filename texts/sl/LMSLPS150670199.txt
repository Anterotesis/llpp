To the Rt. Hon Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord Mayor & the rest of the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in ye. County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrt or Order of Removal under ye. Hands & Seals of Thomas Rawlinson & Robert Alsop Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. said City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 15th. Day of April last David Boovey was Removed from the Parish of St. Peters in Cornhill in ye. said City of London to the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel aforesaid as the Place of his last legal Settlement.

By which said Pass or Order of the said two Justices Your Petitioners and Appellants Apprehend themselves Aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same

Mr. Billington Agent for ye. Appellts. Apr. 26th. 1756

Therefore Your Petitioners and Appellants Pray that this Court will Please to Appoint a Day this Present Session for ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish and all Person Concerned to Attend this Court to hear & abide & the Judgment & Determination of ye. Court touching the said Appeal & that your Petitioners & Appelants may be Relieved in ye. Premes. as this Court shall see meet

And your Petitioners & Appellants shall ever Pray
