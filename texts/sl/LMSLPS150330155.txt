To the Right Honoble Sr. William Stewart Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the worthfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City the Libties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Olave Silver Street London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seales of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Thirteenth Day of third last past Sarah Street Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Mary Aldermary London unto the Said Parish of St. Olave Silverstreet London alledging the said Parish of St. Olave Silver Street to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That part Petitionrs. Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices Do humbly appeale to yor Lordship and Worps therefrom, And pray that Yor. Lords & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Aldermary aforesaid may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of yor Lords & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
