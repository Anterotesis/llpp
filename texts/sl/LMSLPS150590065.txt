To the Right Honble Sr. Robt. Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guild hath in & for the sd. City assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of there Poor of the Parish of Stelemt. Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by in Order under the hands & Seals of Sr. Robt. Ladbroke The Lord Mayor and Thos. Robinson Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties there of (one of the Quarum) bearing Date the 6th. Day of May Last Margt. Bothamley Wido. was removed from the Parish of St. Bridget London to the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx as the Place of her lastleg at Setlemt. whereby your Petrs conceive themselves agrieved and agt. the said Order do appeal

Your Petrs therefore humbley pray that a shortday may be appointed this present Session to hear & determine the [...] said appeal

Wm. Chariman for the Appels

And your Petr shall pray etc.
