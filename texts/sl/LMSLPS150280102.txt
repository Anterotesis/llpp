To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll. the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace in the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

14/2 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Bridget as Brides London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the fourteenth day of February last past John Norton son of Elizabeth Norton Wido was Sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Precinet of White Fryers London unto the Said parish of St Bridget ads Brides London Alledging the said parish to be the place of his last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitionrs therefore conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of he said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitionrs most humbly pray your Honour and Worships will be pleas'd to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Precinet of White fryers London may appear before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of Your Honour & Worps touching this Appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc.
