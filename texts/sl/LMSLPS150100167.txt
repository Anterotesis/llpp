To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Mary of the City of London & to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Saviours

Sheweth

That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands of Seales of twoe Justices of the Peace wth. in this City dated the 3d. of June 1699 (one being of the Quor) a female Bastard child borne of the Body of Sarah Greenway widd in the parish of St. Saviors as is therein alledged was sent to yor. Petrs. to be pvided for Whereas the said Child is unknowne to yor. Petrs. P was not borne in their said Parish of St. Saviours

Yor. Petrs. therefore Pray yor. Honors. and Worpps. to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens of St. Fosters may be ordered to appear before you And that the said Warrant be Quashed And the said Female Bastard be sent to the Churchwardens of St. Fosters To be pvided for according to the Lawe

And They shall Pray Etc.
