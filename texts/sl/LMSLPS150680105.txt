To the Right Honble. Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor Sr. Willm. Moreton Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices off. peace for ye City of London and Libertys thereof at their Genl. Session of ye peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in ye County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under ye. hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of ye peace for ye sd. City and Libertys thereof (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of July last Flora Omash wife of James Omash who (in the said Order is sayd to be a Sailor in his Majestys Service) and their five Children to wit Elizabeth aged ten years Frances aged Eight years James Aged five years John Aged two years and Joseph Aged two Months were Removed from the parish of St. Botolph Aldgate, London to ye said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said James Omash ye Father

That Your Appellts. apprhending themselves to be Agrieved by the said Order of Removal Do Appeal to this Court against ye same Your Petrs. & Appellts. therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to take ye premisses into Consideration and grant Your petrs. and Appellts. such Relief as to your Worships shall seem just and that ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye sd. Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate may (in pursuance of Notice for that purpose) attend this Court and hear and abide by ye Judgmt. thereof touching the said Appeal

Fyl. Billington for Appellts Sepm. 12th 1757.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
