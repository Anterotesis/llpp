To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the said Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Ethelburga London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City and the Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum Dated the 4 day of Novr. last one Mary Jones a Singlewoman was Passed from the Parish of St. Martins Vintry London to the said Parish of St Ethelburga's within the said City as the Place of her last Legall Settlement

Whereby yor. Petr. humbly conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices Therefore humbly [...] to this Court for Releife in the premisses

And yor. Petrs. shall Ever pray
