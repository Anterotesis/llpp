To the Rt. Honrble Sr. Charles Duncombe Knt: Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Annwell in Hertford share

Sheweth That One Elizabeth Archer Spinster By virtue of a Warrant undo. the hands and seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (One being of the Quorm) bearing Date the 24th. Day of March 1708 was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said parish of Annwell in the County of Hertford Alledging the said parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Archer

That yor Petrs doubt not but to prove That the said Elizabeth Archer never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of Ann well whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices And doe humbly Appeale to your Worpp there from

Yor Peter therefore humbly pray your Honrs and Worpps: will be pleased to hears them in the Prmisses And order the Churchwardens and overseers of the poore of the said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate To appeare here be fore you And hear and Abide the Determination of you Hone and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionr: Shall Ever pray Etc
