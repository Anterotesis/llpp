To the Rt. Honrble Sr. William Humfryes. Knrt Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in the general Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Bartholomew the great London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the handsand seale of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quom) bearing date the fifth Day of April last past one Eleanor Port with Edward Port her son was removed from the parish of St Gregory by St pauls London unto the sd parish of St Bartholomew the great London alleadging the said parish to be the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. doubt not but to prove That the said Eleanor Port never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Bartholomew the great whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens: Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Gregory by St Pauls may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And yor Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc
