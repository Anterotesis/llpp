To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook is the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass, Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth Day of July last past Abraham Royce a Lunatick was removed from the Parish of Saint Helen near Bishopgate in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook London as the Last legal Place of his Settlement By which Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court Against the said Pass order

Your Petitioners therefore Pray Your Worships will be Pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this Appeal and given them such relief in the Premisses as to Your Worships shall seem Just and meet

And Your Petitioners shall every Pray Etc
