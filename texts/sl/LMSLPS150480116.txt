To the Rt. Honble. Sir John Barnard Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City & the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Peass Warrant under the Hands, Seals of your Lordship & Sir John Salter Knt. and Alderman, Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth instant George Newbolt, [...] by the Name & addition of George Newbolt Sarah his Wife & their two Children (vizt.) George aged three Years & a female Child aged Six Weeks (not baptized) were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London, unto the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That Your Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order, Do appeal to yor. Lordp & Worps therefrom, And humbly pray yt. yor Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London may appear before you, to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
