To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Sessions of the Peace at Guild hall in and for the said City assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Chitty Knight and William Alexander Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London one of whom is of the Quorum bearing date the 16th. day of July 1762 Elizabeth the Wife of Joshua, Slater was removed from the Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street London to the said Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London as the Place of her last legal Settlement by which Order your Petitioners think themselves aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Honourable Court And Pray that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street London, and all Parties concerned may attend the said Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal And that your Petitioners may have such Relief in the Premisses as to the said Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
