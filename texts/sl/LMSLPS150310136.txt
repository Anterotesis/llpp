To the Right Holloble Sr. George Thorold Knt. & Baronet Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worpfull the rest of this Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Hellen London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Sixteenth day of June last past Elizabeth Burkett Wife of William Burkett with her Three Children (vizt) William about Six years Anne about Two Years and Edward four Months old or thereabouts were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St. Hellen London, alledging the said Parish of St. Hellen London to be the Place of their last Legall Settlment.

That Your Peticoners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices, Do humbly appeale to yor Lordship & Worps therefrom, & pray that your Lordship & Worships will be pleased to hear them concering the Premisses, And that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor Lords & Worps touching this Appealer

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
