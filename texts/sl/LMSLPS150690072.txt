To the Right Honble Sr. Charles Asgill Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices in their General Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Carrier a Prisoner in his Majesties Goal of Newgate

Shewth. That your Petitioner is now a Prisoner in Newgate Charged on Suspicion of Publishing an Order for the Payment of One Hundred and Eighty Pounds with a forged Acceptance in Order Defraud one William Cooper Your Petitioner Knowing the said Acceptance to be forged

Your Petitioner most humbly Prays that at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City he may be either Tryed, Bouled or Discharged for the Felony aforesaid pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and Provided

John Carrier
