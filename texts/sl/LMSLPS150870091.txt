To the Right Honourable Sir Thomas Hallifax Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipful the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London, bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of October last past Joshua Hepworth was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn within the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same And pray that this Court will be pleased to proceed to the hearing and determining the said Appeal, and make such Order thereon as shall be just

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
