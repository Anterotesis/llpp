To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberty thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth day of April One thousand seven hundred and fifty three Robert Camberlidge Ann his Wife and their three Children Vizt. Robert aged Nine Years Thomas aged three years and Arthur aged One your were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Somerset London to the Parish of Saint Botolph Without Bishopsgate London aforesaid Adjudging the last legal Settlement of the said Robert Camberlidge Ann his Wife and their said three Children to be there And Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Two Justices Do humbly appeal therefrom and Humbly Pray such Relief in the Premisses as to Your Lordship and Worship shall seem meet and Convenient.

Burr Attorney for Bishopsgate Parish

And Yr. Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
