To the Rt. Honrble Sr. Samuel Stanier Knt. Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll And Rest her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition and Appeals of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of swithin London

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Thireteing Widow with her son Robert by Virtue of a warrant under the hands and Lease of two of her Matice Justice of the peace within this City one being of the Quour) bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of april last past was removed from the parish of St Sepulchre London unto the parish of St Dunstain London alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal settlement of William Thackeray late the Husband of the said Elizabeth

That your petition doubt not but to prove That the said Elizabeth Thackeray never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Swithin who way they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Hwn and Worpps thereform

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Seputitice may appeare before you and heare and abide the determine- -nation of your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
