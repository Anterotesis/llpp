To the Rt. Honoble: Sr: Wm: Stewart Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City Assembled at their Generall Quart: Sessions of the peace (at Guild Hall) for the said City.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Limehouse in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. William Thomson and Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said City of London (Quor un) bearing Date the Fifth Day of February last George Priddin in with Rebecca his Wife and William Mary Henry, Anne and Asiah there five Children were removed from the parish of St Bridget als Brides London to the said Hamlett of Lime house whereby your Petrs. humbly Conceive they are wrong at and aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Appeals to this Court against the said Order and prays such Relief as this Court shall seem meet

And Yor: Petrs: shall Ever Pray & Etc
