To the Right Honeble Sr George Merttins Knt. Lord Mayer of the City of London & to the Worpfull His Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libties thereof (Quour unus) bearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of January last past one Edward Jones was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook London aforesaid Alledging that of St Stephen Wallbrook to be the Place of his last legall Settlement

That your Petioners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the Said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly Appeale to your Lordp & Worps therefrom And humbly pray That your Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale.

And you Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
