To the right Worship full his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. That by a Warrant, Order, or Vagrant Pass under the hand and Seal of Thomas Hallifax Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London bearing date the Fourteenth day of March One thousand seven hundred and Seventy Seven Catharine Smith Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Woolchurch have in the City of London aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Catharine Smith

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or Vagrant Pass, Do humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seen meet

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

Jn: Pearr Solr. for appellts.
