To the Right Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London & to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within this City dated the first of May 1699 (one being of the Quor) one Elizabeth Wright Singlewoman was sent to yor. Petrs. to be provided for upon her Oath That her last legall Settlement was in the said Parish of St. Buttolph Aldgate where shee had been a hired Servant by the yeare to one Mr. Turner or Butcher att Â£3 Wayed about 5 yeares past And time hath had noe other legall Settlement Whereas in truth the laid Elizabeth by the name of Elizabeth Kirke by a Warrant under two Justices hands daced 21 November 1698 was by virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties Justices of the Peace conveyed by your Petrs. to the said Church wardens of St. Buttolph Bishoppsgate to be pvided for and mainteyned whoe never made any Appeale to the said Warrant And Soe by the Lawe are to pvide for her According to the Judgement thereby given And further the said Elizabeth Ewelt with the said Turner as a servant not above halfe a yeare in the said Parish of St. Buttolph Aldgate

Yor. Petrs. therefore Pray your Honr. and Worpps. to hear their in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens of St. Buttolphs Bishoppsgate may be Ordered to appeare before you And the said late Order be Quashed And the said Elizabeth sent to the Churchwardens of St. Buttolphs Bishoppsgate To be pvided for According to the Law
