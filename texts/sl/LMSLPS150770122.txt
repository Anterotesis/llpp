To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Session of the peace assembled. The humble Petition and Appeal of Henry Darquitt

Sheweth That on the Twelfth day of March in the Sixth year of His present Majestys Reign Your Petitioner was Convicted Before The Right Honourable George Nelson Esquire Mayor of the City of London and one of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City for buying and receiving about Fourteen Ounces of Piedmont Silk from Elizabeth Ford intrusted to wind the same the consent of the Employer not being first had against the Statutes

Your Petitioner conceiving himself Aggrieved thereby Doth humbly Appeal against the said Conviction as being illegal and Prays such Relief in the Premises as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet.

Henry Darquitt
