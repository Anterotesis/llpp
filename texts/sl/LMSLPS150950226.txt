The Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Newnham and John Hart Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof bearing date the fourth day of November 1783 John aged Seven Years and upwards and Ann aged four Years and upwards the Tw [...] Legetimate Children of John Harris by Elizabeth hos Wife both deceased are removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street on the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said John and Ann

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the adjudication of the said Two Justices and therefore Appeal to this Court agt. the same

S Malbon Sollr. for the said Parish of Shadwell
