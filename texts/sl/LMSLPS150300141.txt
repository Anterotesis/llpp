22/10 To the Right Worrll. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London at their Generall Quarter Sessions att Guildhall London

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Martin in the feilds in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an order under the hands & Seales of Sr. John Ward Knt. Late Lord Mayr & Sr Wm Withers Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Citty of London (Quor un) bearing date the two & twentieth day of October last Elizabeth Carpenter Widow & her two Children were removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres without Newgate London to your Petr said Parish of St. Martin in the Feilds whereby your Petrs humbly conceive they are wronged & aggreived

Your Petrs therefore humbly Appeale to your Worshipps And pray that your Worshipp will be pleased to hear and Determind the same

And your Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc

Phill: Davis Giles Harris} Churchwardens

Adam Long John Wilson John Bayley} Overseers of the poor
