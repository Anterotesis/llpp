To ye: Rt. Honable: & Worspll: her Maties: Justices of ye. peace for ye. City of London.

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye. churchwardens and Overseers of ye. poor of ye. parish of St: James Clerkenwell in ye. County of Middx:

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order under ye. hands & Seales of Two of her Maties. Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd City of London, (Quor unns) beareinge date ye 25th: day of September 1711: Elizabeth the wife of John Hudson, wth. Two children; Thomas aged about Two Yeares, & John aged about one yeare, were removed from ye. parish of St: Dionis Backchurch, in ye. sd. City of London to ye. parish of St: James Clerkenwell aforesd. therein Settinge forth ye. said John Hudson was bound Apprentice, to one John Poysor a Cane Chairmaker Liveinge in ye. Sd. parish of St: James Clerkenwell wth. whome he Lived for ye. Spate of one Yeare & upwards;

That your Peticoners, thinke themselves agreived wth. ye. Judgmt. of ye. Sd. Two Justices of ye. peace, humbly appeale to this Honable: against ye. Sd. Warrant or order, for that your peticoners doubts not but that it will appeare to this Court; that ye. Last Legall Setlemt. of ye. Sd. John Hudson is not in ye. Sd. parish of St. James Clerkenwell

Your peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worsps will be pleased to heare & determine ye. Sd. appeale and to give Such Judgmt: & finall determinacon. touchinge ye. Same, as to your Worsps. Shall Seem meet Your peticoners haveinge given due Notice, to ye. Church- Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. sd. parish of St: Dionis Backchurchof this appeale

And your peticoners shall ever pray Etc:

Tho: Elford Church Wardens}
