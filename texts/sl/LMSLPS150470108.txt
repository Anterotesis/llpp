To the Right Honble Sr John Williams Knight Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Sd. City in their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows the Great London

Sheweth [...] bearing date the Eleventh day of June 1736; under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) Lydia Barber Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Alhallows the Less in London to the said Parish of Alhallows the Great in London as the Place of her last Legall Settlement

Yor Petrs humbly Conceiving themselves Agrieved by Vertue of the sd. order or Pass Warrant Do humbly Appeal against the Same and pray such Relief in the Premisses as this Honrble Court Shall think just & Meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
