To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the severall Parishes of St. Gyles without Cripplegt. St. Buttolph without Aldgate St. Andrewes Wardrobe St. Katherine Coleman St Andrew Holborne St. Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the Wall St Buttolph Aldersgate & St. Buttolph without Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very much overburthened with Poore and not able to Raise Sufficient Sumes of money amongst themselves to Releive them notwithstanding their Assessements are greater than heretofore yet the Charge of their Poore is farr above their Assessements As the same upon full Examination hath divers [...] mes appeared to severall Worthy Aldermen of this City appointed to hear the same And their poore daily encreasing upon them their said Parishes lying some of them near to the Suburbes and others of them in the Suburbes of this City

Their humble suites therefore unto yor. good Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to Grant unto them the Contributions of other Parishes within this City for and towards the Referring their Poore as they have formerly had which are Pticularly hereunder menconed Or such further Allowance As to yor. Honor. & Worpps. shall seeme meete
