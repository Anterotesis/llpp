To the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor & the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof In their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberty thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 22d. Day of November 1748 Mary Walburge called in the said Order Mary Dampier and by that Name was removed from the Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch London to the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London aforesaid Adjudging the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary to be there and your Petrs. Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do Humbly appeal therefrom and humbly pray such relief in the premises as to your Lordships and Worships shall seem meet and Convenient and

And Yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
