To the Right Honble Sr. John Barnard [...] Lord Mayor of ye City of London & to the Wor [...] the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace for ye [...] City & the Liberties thereof in their Genll. Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition & appeal [...] Churchwardens & overseers of ye poor of the Parish of Ware in ye County of Hertford

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Warrt under the hands & Seals of ye Lordship & Sr. Thos Lombe Knt two of his majties Justice of ye peace for ye City of London & ye Little thereof one being of ye Quorum [...] bearing date the 9th. day of Febry last past Ann Copping Widow of John Copping was removed from ye parish of St Mildred in the poultry London unto ye par of [...] in ye Com [...] four [...] Settlement

That ye Petitrs Conceiving themselves Aggreiv'd by ye sd. Warrt or order do appeal agt. ye same & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premes & that ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye sd par of St. Mildred in ye Poultry London may appear before you to hear & abide ye Judgement determination of ye Lordsp & Worshipps touching this Appeal & ye petitrs shall ever pray
