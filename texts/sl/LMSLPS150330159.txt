To the right Worpfull Sr. William Stewart Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London Sr. William Thompson Knight recorder of the said Citty and the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace of the same in their Genall Quarter Sessions now Assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St James Dukes place London

Sheweth That by a pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of the said Lord Mayor and Sr. Charles Peers Knt. Two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said Citty of London (Quor unns) bearing date the second day of this Instant only Eleanor Jakeman wife of Richard Jakeman with her three Children (Vizt) Benjamin Hannah and Richard therein named were removed from the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said parish of saint James Dukes place as the place of their last legall settlement By which order on warrant your peticoners conceive themselves to be aggreived and humbly Appeale therefrom

Your peticoners therefore humbly pray that they may be heard in the prmisses by this Court and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of the sd. parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate may attend their Sessions and abide the Judgment and determination of this Honrble Court touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
