To the Right Honble: Robert Willimott Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Rell of his Majesty's Justices of Oyer & Terminer & General Goal Delivery for the City of London their Gentll: Sessions of Oyer & Terminer [...] Severally [...] the said City Adj [...] all in the [...] Daily Assembled.

The Humble Petition of John Waite Prisoner Confined in His Majesty's Goal of Newgate

Sheweth That Yor: Petitioner on the Twenty Seventh Day of Janry: 1712 was Comitted A prisoner to his Majesty's Goal of Newgate for Feloniously Take in ye Secreting and Carrying Away from the Bank of England Severall East India Bonds belonging to the Suitors of the High Court of Chancers with An Inters Defrand the Proprietons thereof.

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly prays that at this present Session now held for the City of London An Justice Hall [...] Old Daily on Wednesday Day the 23rd [...] of February 1742 He may be Try Builed on Discharged

And Yor:Petr: shall Ever pray Etc

John Waite
