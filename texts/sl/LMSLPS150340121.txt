To the Right Honoble Sr. Gerard Conyers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of ye. Peace for the Said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter the Poor London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the 15th. Day of November last past William Measure & Ann his Wife with their Two Children Vizt Martha aged about Twelve years & Heredia aged Ten years were removed from the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall London unto the said Parish of St. Peter the Poor London alledging the Said Parish of St. Peter the Poor to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Order or Warrant of the Said two Justices Doe humbly appeale to your Lordship and Worps therefrom, And pray That your Lordship & Worps will be pleased to hear them cencerning the Premisses. And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London aforesaid may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Lordship and Worps touching this appeall.

And yoe Petnrs Shall ever pray Etc.
