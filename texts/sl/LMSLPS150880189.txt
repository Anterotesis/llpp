To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and the worshipfull Justices of the peace for the City of London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Frederick Bull Esquire and Sir Watkin Lewes Knight two of his Majestys Justices of the City of London (one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the 3rd, day of March 1777 Ann Williamson a Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and one of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of her Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved and that a day may be appointed at this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
