To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & to the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of the Rt Honble F. Cokayne Esqr Ld. Mayor & Sr Robt. Ladbroke Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 31st. Day of May last Ann Young Wife of Wm. Young who [...] from her was removed from the parish of St Bridget vulgarly St Brides London to the sd. parish of St Clement Danes as the place of the last legal Settlemt. of her sd. husband whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the sd. Appeal may be Saved to them & that the hearing & determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be held for the sd. City

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Wm Massey John Harding} Ovrs. of the Poor
