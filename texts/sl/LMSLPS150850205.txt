To the Right Honourable and Worshipful Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of James Townsend Esqr. then Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Barlow Trecothick Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the said City Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quourm) bearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of October last Mary Wales Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West London to the said Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to your Petitioners And that the Hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for this City

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos: Wade Jnr. Lambs Condt. Street Soll for the Applts.
