To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and the Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bow London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal bearing date the Thirtieth day of March last under the Hands and Seals of the Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Thomas Hallifax Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum whereby Mary Williams Widow of Robert Williams deceased and her two Children Sarah aged Five years and Elizabeth aged three year were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate without in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bow London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Williams and her said two Children

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Ordered of Removal do humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Nix Sollicitor for the Appellants
