To the Rt Honrble Sr Peter Delme Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and also to the Rt: Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

15/1. The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under the hander Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quoris Unns) bearing date the Fifteenth day of January last past Ann Dangerfeild wife of John Dangerfeild with their Six Children Vizt Richard aged Twelve years, Elizabeth Ten Years Mary Eight Years, Sarah Six Years, John three years and Jane one Years were removed from the [---] parish of St Ann within Aldersgate London unto the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs humbly conceiveing themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your [...] Honr: and Worpps there from And pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Ann within Aldersgate may appear before you and hear and abide th Judgment and Determinacon of you Honr and Worpps touching this appeal-

And Your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
