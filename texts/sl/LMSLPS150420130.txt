To the Right honourable Humphry Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City att their Generall Quarter Sessions att Guildhall Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & and Overseers of the Parish of St Hollens London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of the said Humphry Parsons Esqr and Sir Edward Becher Knt two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor un) bearing date the 18th day of May 1731 Susanna Chapple was by the said Order removed from the Parish of St Botolph witht Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of St Hollen neare Bishopsgate London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Susanna Chapple Your Petrs. humbly Conceive they are wronged

Your Petrs. therefore Humbly pray Your Lordshipp and Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day to heare and determine the said Appeale And Your Petrs. may be releived as unto your Lordshipp and Worshipps shall seem meete

And Your Petrs shall ever pray Etc
