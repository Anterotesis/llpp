To the Rt. Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties of the peace for the said City in the General quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two [...] Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City whereof one is of the Quor bearing date the Two and twentieth Day of December last [...] Hugh Rowe an Infant aged about three years and an half was removed and passed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate ate London unto the said parish of St. Bartholomew the great alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hugh Rowe.

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Hugh Rowe never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Bartholomew the great whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Order of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom.

Your Petrs. therefore pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appears before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeales

And your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
