To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor Recorder and Aldermen of the City of London and the rust of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled,

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Claines in the County of Worcester

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable John Burnell Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Brass Crosby Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (One of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th. day of February last Ann Dyer Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre without Newgate in the said City to the Parish of Claines in the County of Worcester as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Do therefore humbly Appeal against the same And humbly Pray that this Honourable Court will grant them relief in the premises;

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

J. Symonds Devonsheriff Sol. for ye [...]
