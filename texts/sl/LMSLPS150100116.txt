To the Right Honble: Sr: Francis Child Knt: Lord Mayr: of the City of London and to ye: rest of the Honble: Bench of Justices sitting in the Old Bayly.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Pilpott prisoner in the Gaol of Newgate.

Sheweth. That yor: Peticoner being comitted upon suspicon of Stealing a horse from one Mathew Lloyd and being altogeather innocent of the Fact of which he stands charged and being alsoe willing and desirous to justify his innocency according to Law

Therefore most humbly prayes the favorite of this Honble Court to be tryed some time during this present Sessions pursuant to Law

And yor: peticoner as in duly bound shall ever pray Etc
