To the Right Honble Sr Willm Lewen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to Worpl the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Anthony als Antholins London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unns) bearing Date the 28th day of July last Jane Rawlings a Lunatick was removed from the pish of Christ church unto the said parish of St Anthony als Antholins alledging the send parish to be the place of her last Last Legal Settlement

That Your Said petitionrs do most humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worps therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour and Worpswill be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Christ Church London may appear before You and hear and abide the Determinicon of yr Honour and Worps touching the said Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
