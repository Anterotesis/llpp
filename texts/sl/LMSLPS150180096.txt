To the Rt Honble. Sr. Robt. Bedingfeld Knt. Ld. Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of the Justices sitting at the old Bayley

The Humble peticon of Thomas Smith now a Prisonr. in Newgate.

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. has been a Prisonr. in Newgate for Six month: last past & that prsently after yor. Peticonrs. Comittmt. he was Charged for a fact Comitted two years since & was never exsecuted upon the Indictmt for the said fact

Yor. Peticonr. Therefore humbly prays that he may be Tryed for the said Fact this Sessions or be discharged from the said Indictmt.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
