To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in General Quarter Sessions by Adjournment at the Guild Hall of the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George's Hanover Square in the County of middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Francis Cokayne Esqr. and Sir Matthew Blakiston Knt Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th. day of December 1764 Henrietta Wallbey, wife of Benjamin Wallbey who is a Patient in Saint Thomas's Hospital was removed from the Parish of St. Olave in Hart Street in the City of London to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Benjamin Wallbey and Henrietta his Wife.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this court against the same and desire to be relieved therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Richd Long Boston} Churchwardens

Thos. Morgan Mark Warcup} Overseers of the Poor
