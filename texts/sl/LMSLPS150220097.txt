To the Rt. Honrble. Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bartholomew the less London

Sheweth That a Female Bastard Child named Elizabeth Beachamp by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quoru) bearing date the Thirteenth day of Aprill last past was removed from the parish of Allhallows the Great London unto the said Parish of St Bartholomew the less alleadging the said Parish of to be last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Beachamp.

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Elizabeth Beachamp never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the less whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the said Parish of Allhallows the Great may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etca.

St Bartholomew ye less agt Allhallows the Great}
