To the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Pennant Knt. Ld. Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of [...] Christ Church in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of your Lordship and [...] Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the said City being [...] Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof [...] of the Quorum) bearing Date the 26th. Day of January last Mary Channon, said to be the Widow of William Channon deceased whose settlement could not be for was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripple [...] London unto the said Parish of Christ Church as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners do humbly Conceive them selves aggrieved and humbly Appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the [---] said Judgment may be set aside and that they may have such relief in the Premes as to this houble Court Shall seem meet.

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

Edw. James Sollr. for the Appellts
