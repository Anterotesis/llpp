To the Right Honourable Daniel Lambert Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipfull the Aldermen of the same City his Majesties Justices of the Peace Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Michael in Cornhill in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble: Daniel Lambert Esqr: and Sir Robert Baylis Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of August last past Elizabeth Parsons (by the Name of Martha Parsons) Widow was Removed from the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St: Michael in Cornhill as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking Themselves Agreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices of the Peace, Appeal to this Court, against the Same And humbly pray your Worships will be pleased in this present Sessions to hear and determine the same

And your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
