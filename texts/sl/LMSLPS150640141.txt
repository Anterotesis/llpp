To the Right Honble Sr. Crisp Gascoyne Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in Qur. Sessions at the Guildhall in and for the Sd. City assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwards. & Oversr. of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 24th. Day of Febry. last Eleanr. Osborn Widow. of Jasper Osborn was removed from the Parish of St. Bridget Vulgarly St Brides London to the aforesd. Parish of St. Clement Danes as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners concieve themselves agrieved and agt. the sd. Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that the Benefit of the Sd. Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for this City

And yor. Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
