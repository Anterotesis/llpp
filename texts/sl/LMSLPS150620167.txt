To the Right Honourable Francis Cokayne Esqr: Lord Mayor of the City of London And To the Rest of the Right Worpful The Aldermen his Brethren in their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in London

Sheweth That on the seventh Day of June last past Yor Petrs were served with the order hereunto Annexed which Your Petrs: Apprehend to be illegall upon the Face of it It Appearing that the Bastard Child therein named was born in Holland and not in the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in London

Yor Petrs therefore humbly pray that the sd. Order may be Vacated & Quashed

And Yor Petrs shall pray Etc

W Salt Sollr for Comptts
