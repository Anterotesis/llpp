To the Right Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Major of the City of London and also to the Right Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the general Quarter Sessions assembled

Tho humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardene, Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) one Sarah Rann wife of Job Rann with her two Children has removed From the parish of St Stephen Colemanstreet London unto the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Sarah Rann never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Peticonr therefore most humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to bear them in the & Emissed and that the Churchwarden Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Stephens Coleman Street may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
