London ss To the Right Woppfull Sr. Gerrad Conyers Knight Lord Mayor of this Citty St. William Thompson Knight Recorder of the same Citty and he rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace in end for ye sd City now Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bartholomew the Less within this Citty

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of the said St. Gerrard Conyers and Sr. [...] George Mertins Knt. Two of his Maties Justice Justices of the peace in and for this [---] Citty Quorum unus bearing date the Twenty first day of Junnary [---] last Richard Soame with Margarett his Wife and Two Children Nathaniell Aged about five years and Margarett aged about Five months [...] [---] were removed from the parish of Saint Sepulchres London to ye Said parish of Saint Bartholmew the Less as the place of their last Legall Settlement By wth. said Order or pass Warrant your peticoners think them Selves agreived and humbly appeale there from and

Therefore your peticoners Humbly pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish of Saint Sepulchres may attend and a bide the Judgmt. and determinacon of the Honble Court touching the merritts of this appeale and that your peticoners may be releived Therein

And your peticoner shall ever pray Etc
