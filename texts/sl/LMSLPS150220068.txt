To the Rt: Honble: Sr Gilbert Heathcoate Knt: Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to the Worppfull the rest of her Maties: Justices of the Peace within the same Citty in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Buttolph Aldersgate London.

Sheweth. That One Elizabeth Hopkins being about 76 Yeares of age and haveing been an Inhabitant in yor. Petrs Parish for severall yeares last past is reduced to great Necessity and poverty and now become burthensome to yor Petrs. said Parish and notwithstanding one Thomas Paine who married the said Hopkins Daughter is of goods substance and Ability and ought by Law to provided for his said mother and not burthen yor Petrs said parish with the Charge thereof get the refuses soe to doe altho he hath been thereof required.

Wherefore yor Petr. humbly pray yor. Lordship and Worpps to heare them in the Prmisses and also order the said Thomas Paine to appeare before you and shew cause why he should not provided for his said Mother as the Law in that Case directs and also to hear ad abide the determinacon of yor Lordship and Worpps therein.

And Yr Petrs, shall ever pray Etc.
