To the Right Honoble: Sr: John Barnard Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Right Worshipful the Aldermen Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Quarter Sessions at Guild hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonards Foster Lane London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of the said Sr: John Barnard Kt: and Sr: Thomas Lombe Kt: two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of August 1738 Mary Crook Widow of Henry Crook deceasd. and her two Children Henry aged Thirteen Years and Mary Aged Six Years were by the said Order removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Leonards Foster Lane London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Crook Widow and her two Children whereby your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged and Agreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray Your Worships will be Pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal and your Petitioners releived as to your Worships Shall seem meet

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
