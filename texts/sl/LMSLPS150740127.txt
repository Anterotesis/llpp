To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City of London at Guildhall in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Matthew Friday Street in the City of London

Sheweth That the Right Honble Willm. Beckford Esqr. Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esqr: two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London by an Order under their respective hands and Seals bearing date the 23d. day of December 1762 directed to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Pauls London Did thereby require them to remove and Convey Matthew Pryce Patience his Wife and Elizabeth Wilmot Pryce their Daughter aged about eight Years into the said Parish of Saint Matthew Friday Street.

That Your Petitioners apprehend and are advised that they are greatly aggrieved by the said Order of Removal And

Therefore Your Petitioners humbly Appeal against the same and pray that this Honourable Court will make such Order therein as shall be just & reasonable

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
