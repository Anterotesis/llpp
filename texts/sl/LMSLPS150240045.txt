To the Rt Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the several Parishes of St Giles without Cripplegate St Buttolph without Aldgate St Andrew Wardrobe St Katherine Coleman St Andrew Holborn St Sepulchre St Brides Allhallows on the Wall St Buttolph Aldersgate and St Buttolph without Bishopsgate

Sheweth That your Petitionrs: are very much overburthened with poor & not able to raise sufficient sumes of mony amongst themselves to releive them notwithstanding their Assessments are greater then heretofore Yet the charge of the Poor is far above their Assessments as the same upon their Examination hath diverse times appeared to several worthy Aldermen of this City appointed to hear the same And their Poor Dayly increasing upon them their said Parishes some of them lying near the suburbs and others of them in the suburbs of this City

Their humble petition therefore unto your good Honr and Wapps is that you will be favourably pelased to grant unto them the Contributions of other parishes within this City for & towards the relieving the poor as they have formerly had which are particularly hereunder menconed as such further Allowance as to your Honr and Worpps shall seem meet
