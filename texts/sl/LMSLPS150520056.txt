To the right Honourable Humphrey Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor Sr: John Strange Knight Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petiton and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the said Humphrey Parsons Lord Mayor [---] And Sr: Edwarrd Bellamy two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London and Libertys thereof Bearing date the third day of December 1740 Elizabeth Walker said to be the wife of William Walker and their Child Susanna aged about Eleven Years were Removed from the Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry London to the said Hamlet of Mile End Old Town as the place of their last Legal Settlement By which said pass Warrant or Order of Removal Your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore Your Petitioners and Appellants pray that this Court will Please to appoint a day this present Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mildred and all other Persons Concerned to attend this Court to Hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court Touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners and Appellants may be Relieved therein according to Law and Justice.

And your Petrs:

John Redman Benj Jones.} Churchwardens and Overseers of Mile End Old Town.
