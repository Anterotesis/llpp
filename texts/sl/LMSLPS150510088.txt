To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of London in General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Kingston upon Thames in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of Sir John Salter Knight and George Champion two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberty Dated the Eleventh Day of Aprill last past Elizabeth Bunny Singlewoman is Removed from the Parish of St. Martin Outwich in the said City of London to the said Parish of Kingston upon Thames as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Your Petitioner finding themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removall of the said two Justices humbly Appeale to this Court against the same

And humbly Pray that the same may be Heard thus Prsent Sessions, and that Your Petitioners may be Releived therein according to Law.

And Yor Petitioners shall Pray Etc.

Wm: Nettlefold Tho: Franks} Church Wardens.

Thomas Ledger John Penner} Overseers
