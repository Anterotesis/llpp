To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in general quarter Sessions at the Guild Hall of the said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of their Right Honble. Sir Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor of the said City and Robert Alsop Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eleventh Day of February last past Mary the Wife of John Boswell was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Alhallowes Staining in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace appeal to this Court against the same and desire to be relieved therein

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
