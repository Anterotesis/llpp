To the Right Honble Sr. Richard Glyn Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Eastcheap London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Sr. Charles Asgill Knt. late Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Robert Alsop Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of November 1758 Isaac Pack and his two Children to wit Isaac aged time years and Eleanor aged Seven years were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth London to the Parish of St. Clement Eascheap London alledging the said Parish of St. Clement Eastcheap to be the place of their last legall Settlement

The Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of Removall Do appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom and

Humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Promises & that the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Woolcoth London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment and determination of your Lordship and Worships touching this Appeal

And Your Petitrs. shall pray Etc

Timo: Sutton Soll for the Appellants
