To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in General Quarter Session at the Guild Hall of the said City.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Rt. Honble Francis Cokayne Lord Mayor and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of February last past Susanna Brown Singlewoman, with her two Bastard Children, for nurture Vizt. Susanna aged four years and James aged four months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres London to the said Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex the said Susanna Brown as the place of her last legal Settlement, and her said two Bastard Children for Nurture.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices of the peace Appeal to this Court against the same and desire to be relieved therein

Thos Huddle Wm. Wilton} Churchwardens

Wm. Smith G: Squire} Overseers of the Poor
