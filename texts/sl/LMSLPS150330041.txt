To the Rt Honble Sr William Stewart Knt: Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of Abraham Lusciena

Sheweth That by an Order and adjudication made under the hands & seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the city of London (Quoru Unus) bearing date the Twentieth day of March last past your Petr Abraham Lusciena was adjudged to be the putative Father of a Female bastard child born in the Parish of St Olave Hart Street London of the body of Elizabeth Atkins singlewoman which is become chargeable to the said Parish And was thereby likewise ordered to pay unto the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish Forty Shillings being money by them expended in maintaining the said child, and also the weekely Sum of Two Shillings and Six pence for the further maintenance of the said child

That your Petr conceiving himself agrieved by the said Order and Adjudication of of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps there from and pray That you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Olave Hartstreet may appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petr shall ever pray Etc
