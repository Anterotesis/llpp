To the Right Honourable William Nash Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City. at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty fourth Day of September last. Elizabeth the Wife of James Nash. who hath deserted her and their Child Elizabeth Frances, were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices, humbly appeal against the same, and pray that this Honourable Court will please to hear and determine their said Appeal and make such Order thereon as shall be just

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc.
