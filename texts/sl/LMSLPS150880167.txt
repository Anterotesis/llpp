To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in for the City of London in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Oversees of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Thomas Hallifax[---] and William Lee Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of February last past, Mary Audley a Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said City of London to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of the Legal Settlement of the said Mary Audley Whereby your Petition Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved, and against the said Order to Appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray That your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in the next Session for the Hearing and Determining of their said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Coax Sollr. for the Appellants.}
