To the Right Honrble Micajah Perry Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace of the said City in their General Quarters Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Newington in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Ninth day of February 1738 Whereby Martha Page Wife of Thomas Page and her three Children were removed from and out of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London into our Said Parish of St. Mary Newington in the County of Surry as the Place of their last legall Settlement

Yor Petrs Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant & humbly Appeal agt. the same and Pray such releif in the Premisses as to this Honourable Cost Shall seem meet

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc
