To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Genl. Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said City of London

Sheweth That by an order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of the Right Honble John Sawbridge Esqr. and Samuel Plumbe Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of June last past Ann Melton the Wife of Thomas Melton who hath deserted her and her Son Frances aged three years & upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said City of London as the place of the legal Settlement of the said Ann Mellon whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrived and against the said order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the next Sessions for the Hearing and determining of their said appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Cox Sollr for Appellts
