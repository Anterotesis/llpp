To the Right Honoble: Sir Gerrard Conyers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties of the peace for the said City and the libties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Michael Cornhill London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of on Order or Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said City and liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing date the 27th day of July 1723 Dorothy Gyvar and Mary Gyvar her Daughter were removed from the parish of Allhallowes on London Wall to the parish of Saint Michael Cornhill aforesaid alledging the said parish of Saint Michael Cornhill to be the place of them last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Order do humbly appeale for to your Lordship and Worships therefrom and humbly pray they may be heard concerning the premisses and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Allhallowes on London Wall may appear before you to hear and abide by the Judgmt. and determination of your Lord ship and Worships touching this appeall

And yor Petrs shall pray Etc
