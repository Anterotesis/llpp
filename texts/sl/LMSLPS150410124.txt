To the Rt honble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London & to ye. rest of ye. right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye. sd. City

20/10 The humble Petition & appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of Alhallows Barkin London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under ye hands & Seales of the Rt. honble Sr. Richard Brocas Knt & Sr. Richard Hopkins Knt two of his Majesties Justices of ye. Peace for ye sd. Citty (Quor unus) bearing date ye. twentieth Day of October Anno Dom 1730 Mary Wottost was removed from ye. Parish of St. Barthole mew the less London to ye. sd Parish of Allhallows Barkin London As ye. place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Petitionrs conceive themselves Aggreived by ye. sd. Warrant or order of ye. sd. two Justices & humbly Appeale against ye. same & therefore most humbly pray to be releived in ye. prmisses as to this honble Court shall seem most meet.

And Yr. Petitionr As in duty bound hall ever pray Etc

John Lane Rt. Obson} Churchwardens

Rich: Romman William Reads Richard Owen Joseph Salway } Overseers
