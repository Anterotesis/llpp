To the Right honble the Lord Mayor and Other His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London in Open Session assembled

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. John Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Ordr. or Warrant under the hands and Seals of [---] Robert Ladbrooks Knt. Lord Mayor and Robt. Alsop Esqr. two of This Majesties Justices of the peace for Cty of London bearing date the 26th. day of March Last Isaac Gray was removed from the Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street London to the sd. Parish of St. John Southwark in the County of Surry as the place of his Last Legall settlement to hereby two think our Selves agrieved therefore humbly Appeal & Pray that this Court Will make an Order for all Partyes to attend to hear and determine the said Appeal Dated the 18th. day of April 1748

Ed. Langord Isaac Lee} Church Wardens

Char. Devon Thos: Brown} Overseers etc.
