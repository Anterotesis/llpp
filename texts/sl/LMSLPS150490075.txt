To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor and other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in Generall Quarter Session at the Guild Hall in and for the said City.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of the Rt Honble the Lord Mayor and Sr. Thos Lombe Knt two of his Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for ye sd City London (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th. day of July last Grace Bird [...] was removed from the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London to the said Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agreived and against the said Order in Appeale.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Short day may be appointed this Present Session to hear and determent the matter touching the said Appeale.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

R Chimmow Sollr. for the Appellants
