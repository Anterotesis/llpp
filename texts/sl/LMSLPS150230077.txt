To the Rt Honrble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of her Majestys Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bartholomew at the Exchange London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one (being of the Quorn) bearing date the twentieth day of June last past One Mary Winbold Singlewoman was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn London unto the parish of St Bartholomew Exchange aforesaid alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Winbold

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Winbold never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Bartholomew at the Exchange whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Andrew Hollow may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr And Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall Ever pray Etc
