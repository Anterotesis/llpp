To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Bennett Shear Hog in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass order under the hands and seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Piace for the said City of London whereof one in of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of November last past Sarah Warwick the Widow of Thomas Warwick and her Bastard Child Margaret Draper and Infant aged Eight Months for Nurture were removed from the parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate without London to the said Parish of Saint Bennet Shear Hog London as the last Legal Pleae of their Settlement By which your Petitioner & think themselves Aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worships will be pleased to him and Determine the Matters touching this appeal and five them such relief in the premisses as to Your Worships shall seem Just and meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Webb & Woods for the Applts
