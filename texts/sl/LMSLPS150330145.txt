To the right honourable the Lord Mayor and to the right Worshipfull the Aldermen his Brethren and Justices of the Peace as the general Quarter Sessions assembled at Guildhall London

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Old Jewry London

Whereas by an Order or Warrant of Removall bearing Date the Twenty third Day of February now last past under the hands and Seals of the right honourable Sr. William Stewart Knight Lord Mayor & the right Worshipfull Sr: Charles Pears Knight & Alderman two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (quorum Unus) Rachael Poey Widow with her Child Maria Aged about Five Years have been sent and removed from the Parish of St: Stephen Colemanstreet London to the Parish of St: Olave Old Jewry London as to the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices humbly Appeale to this Court against the same and Pray the Judgement and Determination of this Court concerning the same Appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
