To the Rt Honrble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Michael Bassishaw London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorm Unus) bearing Date the 31st Day of December last past Matthew Langton with Elizabeth his wife and one Child named Matthew were removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Parish of St Michael Bassishaw as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petr doubt not but to prove that the said Matthew Langton never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St. Michael Bassishaw whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petr therefore most humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment & determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
