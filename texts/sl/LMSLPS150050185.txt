To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lrod Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Sepulchers London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very much Overburthened with Poore being scituate in the Suburbs of this City And not being able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them Notwithstanding their Assessemt. is greater then heretofore Yett the charge of their Poore is forr above their said Assessemt. As the same upon full examinacon hath many times Appeared to divers Aldermen of this City Appointed to heare the same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them.

Their humble suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contribucon of Â£50. from severall Parishes wth. in this City for & towardes these liveing their Poore as hath been formerly givene them Or such further allowance As to yor. Honors. & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And they shall Pray Etc.
