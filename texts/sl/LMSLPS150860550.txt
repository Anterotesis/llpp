To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in the their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of John Lamb an Inhabitant of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London.

Sheweth. That by a certain Taxation Rate or Assessment lately made by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London and County of Middlesex or the greatest Part of them for the Relief of the Poor of that Part of the said Parish which lies in the City of London and Ward of Farringdon without in the said City from the Twenty fourth Day of June last to the End of three Months Your Petitioner is taxed and Assessed towards the said Relief in the Sum of Thirteen Shilling 1 & 6 for and in Respect of his Dwelling House.

That Your Petitioner apprehends himself to be aggrieved by the said Taxation Rate or Assessment and to be illegally and unequally taxed and Assesed thereby and therefore humbly appeals to this Honourable Court against the same and prays Relief in the Premises and that the said Taxation Rate or Assessment may be quashed or that the same may be amended in Respect of Your Petitioner as to this Honourable Court shall seem most meet.

William Chetham Solicitor for the Appellant }

J Lambs
