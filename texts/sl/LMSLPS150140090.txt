To the right Honrble Sr Saml Dashwood Knt Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and to there right [...] Worshipll the Justices of the Peace of the generall quartr Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of Sr Isaack Kirby a poore prisonr on the Comn side of the Fleet Prison

Sheweth That Yor Peticon hath been actually a Prisonr in the Prison of the Fleet under the Custody of Willm Weeden Ford Esqr Keepr of the sd Prison before the first day of Janr 1701 and is Still deteyned there of the suit of Thomas Speight Creditr for debt or damages and not haveing where in to maintaine himselfe in the Sd Prison

22.3.1702 Charged Wth. above Â£20. Yor Petitionrs suit therefore is that this Cor will be pleased to order the sd Willm Weeden For the kept of the said prison of [...] Certaine day and time to bring yor Peticonr before yo with a Coppy of the Causes of his [...] prisomnt that he may take the Oathe menconed in the late act of Parliamt made for releife of Poore Prisonr for debt, and that the said Creditr may be ardered then and there alsoe so appear and shew cause why the said Isaack Kerby should not be discharged from his imprisonmt according to the said act and that he may be discharged accordingly

And Yor Peticonr will ever Pray
