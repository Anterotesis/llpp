To the Rt. Honorble. Sr: Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poore & Parishionrs of the Parish of St. Ethelburgh within Bishoppsgate London

Sheweth That the Clarke of yor. Petrs. Parish by mistate in a Returne to the Precept directed to them from the Right Honoble. the Late Lord Maior of this City to give an account of their Poore and the charge of mainteyning than hee made a returne of the sume of £133. for one yeare which was the totall sume of all the Pavements made me every respott for & concerning the same Whereas intruth the true charge of their Poore amounts to no more than about £45. Yearely

And their said Parish being charged by virtue of the late Act of Comon Councill to pay 66:10:2 to the Corporacon established by the said Act for setting the Poore on worke

Now yor. Pertrs. in respect of the Smallnesse of their Parish And the greate number of Poore therein which they have to mainteyned Desire that yor. Hopp. and Worpps. will be pleased to releice yor. Petrs. and to order the abatement of the said sume of £66:10s:2 to such sume in & Pencon To what doth truely a wise by their assessment for mainteyining the Poore of their said Parish They being only releivable by this Courte

And they Shall Pray Etc
