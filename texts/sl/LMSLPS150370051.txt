To the Rt. Worshipfll His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Hartstreet in the Said City.

Humbly Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or order, under the hands & Seals Of the Rt. Honble: Sr Francis Forbes Knt Lord Mayor of the said City of London & Sr John Fryer Bart. Two of his Majtis: Justices of the Peace (Quor unus) for the said City, bearing date the 4th. day of May last, Sarah Bill Widow was removed from the Parish of Alhallows Barking in the said City, unto the Parish of St. Olave Hartstreet abovesaid, as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petrs. conceiving themselves Agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do Appeale to Your Worships against the same and haveing given due notice to the said Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Alhallows Barking of this Our Appeale Humbly Pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear Your Petrs. in the premises and to have your Worps Judgement and Determination touching the same

And Your Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc.
