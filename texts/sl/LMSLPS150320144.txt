To the Right honourable Sr. Francis Forbes Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace att Guildhall in their generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchers London

Sheweth. That by Vertue of an Order or pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (quorn unus) bearing date the 11th. day of this Instant July one Anne Salisbury Widow of Abel Salisbury was removed from the Parish of St. Martins Ludgate London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London Adjudgeing the said Parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Anne Salisbury

That your Petitioner humbly Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Appeal to this honourable Court and Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the prmises

And your Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc

Edwd Lambeth Thos. Maynard} Church wardens

Richard Grundy Tho Ward } Overseers of the poor
