To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace at their General Session Assembled at Guildhall London

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knight and Thomas Hallifax Esqrs. Two of This Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the City of London and Libertys thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing [---] date the 24th day of March last Mary Ginn Widow of Lawrence Ginn deceased was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the said Parish of Saint Lawrence Jewry to the said Parish of Saint Luke

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices Do humbly Appeal against the same and pray that this Court will be pleased to make such Order in the Premisses for the Relief of your Petitioners as they shall seem meet

J: Jenings Attorney for the Appellants
