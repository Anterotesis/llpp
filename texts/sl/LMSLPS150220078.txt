To the Right Honble: Sr. Gilbert Heathcot Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and to other her Majties: Justices of the Peace of the Said City in Quarter. Sessions assembled.

The humble Petitions of and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Saviour Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of her Majties: Justices of the peace of the City of London One Rose [...] Webb with her Female Bastard Child was Passed from the Parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate in the City of London to the Parish of St. Saviour Southwark in the County of Surrey as the place of her last legal Settlement Gained by her late husband John Webb Serving this Apprenticeship there

That your Peticonrs. can make it appear to this honble: Court that the said John Webb Served [...] his whole apprenticeship in the parish of St. Olave Southwark and never lived as an apprentice in the said Parish of St Saviour [...] Southwark

Your Peticonrs. therefore most humbly Appeals against the said Order and prays that your Worships will please to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate may appeale in this honble: Court this Prsent Sessions to there cause why Rose Webb Should not be repassed to the Said Parish of St Gyles without Cripplegate

And yor. Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc.
