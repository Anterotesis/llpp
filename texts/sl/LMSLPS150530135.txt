To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor The Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Trinity Minories in the Liberty of the [...] London

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals by Williams and Robt. Ladbrooke two & his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the fourteenth day of July now last past Elizabeth Howard widow of Samuel Howard deced was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Hubbard in the said City of London to the sd. Parish of Init. Mi [...] Widow as the place of her last legal Settlement.

By which said Pass or Order of Removal of the said two Justices your petrs. apprehend themselves agrieved and appeal to this Court against the Same.

Therefore your Petrs. and appellants pray that this Court will appoint a day this present session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor the Sd. Parish of St. Andrew Hubbard and All other persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of Court touching the said Appeal and that [...] and appellts. may be relieved therein According [...] Law and Justice

Thos. Cooke Wm Croger Church Ward.

Rich Langley Over seers

And Yr. Petr. Etc
