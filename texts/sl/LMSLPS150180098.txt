To ye Rt Honble. Sr. Robt: Bedingfeld Knt Lord Major & To ye Rt. Worspll. Sr Salathiell Lovell Knt. Recorded & To ye Rest of ye Justices now setting yt ye Old Baily

The humble Peticon of Jno Roberts a Prisonr in Newgate

Most Humbly Shewth. That yor Peticonr. is Comitted to Newgate by Sr Owen Buckingham for Stealing from Wm. Emery A Perriwigg in of Queens Highway

Your Peticonr most Humbly Prays yor Lordsp that he may be Tryed this Sessions

And yor Peticonr shall Pray
