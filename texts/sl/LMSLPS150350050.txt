To the Rt. Honble Sr Peter Delme Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Several Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate Sr Botolph without Aldgate St Andrew Wardrobe St Catherine Coleman St Andrew Holbourn St Sepulchers St Brides Alhallows on the Wall St Botolph Aldersgate and St Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner are very much over burthened with poor & not able to raise Sufficient sums of Mony amongst themselves to relieve them notwithstanding their assessments are greater then heretofore yet the Charge of the poor is far above their assessments as the same upon Examinacon hath divers times appeared to several Worthy aldermen of this City appointed and their Poor daily unceasing upon their said Parishes some of them lying near the Suburbs and others of them in the suburbs of this City

Their humble Suit therefore to Your Honour & Worp's that you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the Contributions of the other parishes within this City for and towards the relief of their Poor as they have formerly had which are particularly hereunder menconed or such further allowance as to your Honour & Worps shall seem meet.
