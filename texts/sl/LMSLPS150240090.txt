To the Rt. Honble. Sr Richd. Hoare Knt. Ld. Majr. & the rest of the Honoble. Bench

The humble Peticon of Ben: Payne

Humbly Sheweth

That Yor. Peticoner was Comitted the Sessions before last on Suspicon of Felony And was entered to remaine till this Sessions

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prays yt. he may be tryed this Sessions or else to be otherwise discharged.

And yor Petr. will as in duty bound ever pray Etc.

The humble Peticon of Ben Payne.
