To the Rt. Honrble Sr George Thorold Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and also to the Rt. Worpll his Maties. Justices of of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of John Smart

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Adjudication under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Seventeenth day of September last past your petitioner was charged and adjudged to be the putative Father of a male last and Child born of the body of Mary Sillito Singlewoman in the parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London and become chargeable thereunto by which said Order your Petitioner is ordered to pay unto the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish the sume of Five pounds and Ten Shillings being by Them already Expended and the weekly sume of Two Shillings and Six pence for the maintenance of the said male bastard Child so longs as the said Male bastard Child shall continue to be chargeable to the said Parish.

That your Petition conceiving himself aggrieved by the said Order and Adjudication of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal is your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear him in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate London may appear before you this Prsons general quarter Sessions of the peace to hear and abide the judgment and determination of Your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc Jno. Smart
