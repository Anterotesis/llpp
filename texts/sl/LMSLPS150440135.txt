To the Rd Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the field in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Sr. William Billers & Sr. Richard Browns Knights two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum bearing date the Eighth day of November last Many Carr an Infant was removed from rent of the parish of st. Buttolph without Aldersgate London to the said parish of st. Giles in the fields as the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs therefore thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justics Appeale to this Court against the same to humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to hear & Determine the same

and yor Petr shall ever pray etc

Charles Fairchild Thomas Beere } Churchwardens

Jonathan Hughes Thomas Gilbans} Overseers of the Poor
