To the Right Honourable Sir Watkin Lewes Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Assembled at the Guild hall in and for the said City at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace on Monday the fifteenth Day of October in the twenty first Years of the Reign of King Geroge the third

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulcher London

Sheweth That by [---] Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Sir Watkin Lewes Lord Mayor of the City of London and John [...] Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace of the [...] the City of London and Liberties thereof [...] being of the Quorum bearing Date the [...] October One Thousand Seven Hundred and [...] Knight Single Woman was Removed a Conveyed from and out of the Precinct of White Friars to the said Parish of Saint Sepulcher in the City of London as her Legal Place of Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said [---] Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and Humbly pray the said appeal may by [---] be adjourned to the next General Sessions of the peace
