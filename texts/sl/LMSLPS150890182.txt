To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesty's Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the 18th day of February now last past under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable James Esdaile Lord Mayor of the City of London and Evan Pugh Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said City one whereof being of the Quorum Frances Moore the wife of Charles Moore who hath Left her and her two Children Vizt. Ann aged three years and upwards and a male Infant Child not Christened a native of Warwickshire but whose Settlement is not known were removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopgate London to the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the exchange in the said City as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner's apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said order on pass Warrant of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same

And therefore pray that you will be pleased to order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopgate aforesaid and all persons can carreid to attend this Court some day this present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination
