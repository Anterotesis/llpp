To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwdns and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the Co. of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Tho: Hallifax Mayor and Robt. Peckham Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing [...] the twenty sixth day of September last Carolina Barton [...] of George Barton who hath deserted her & her two Children vizt. Sarah age & four Years and upwards and Henry aged three Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London to the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the [---] County of Middlesex as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Carolina Barton and her two Children

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will appoint a day in the next Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. will Pray Etc
