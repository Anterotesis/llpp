To the Rt Honble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Session Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Thomas the Appostle London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quour Unus) bearing Date the 29th Day of December last, Past Phebe Williams wife of William Williams with three Children, George Elizabeth and David were removed from the Parish of St. Michael in crooked lane London unto the said Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petr. doubt not but to prove that the said Phebe William and her three Children hath not any legal Settlemt. in the said Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant of the said two Justices And do humbly appeal to your Honour and Worpps there from

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Michael crooked Lane may appear before you and hear & abide the Determination of your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
