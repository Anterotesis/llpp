To the Right Honourable Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor Baron Thompson Recorder and the Rest of this Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberty thereof in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removeall under the Hands and Seals of Sir John Barnard Knt: Lord Mayor and Humphry Parsons Esqr: Two of his majestys Justices of the peace for the said City (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Fifth day of April last Mary Mills aged about Fifteen Years was Removed from the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said City of the said parish of Saint Mary White Chapel an the place of her Last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Apprehends themselves to be Agrieved by the said Order of Removeall of the said Two Justices and Appeals to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellants Therefore prays that this Court will Appoint [...] day this present Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the aid City and all other persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the determind [...] of this Court touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners may be Relieved therein According to Law and Justice

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

Wm Hamilton Humpy. Wright
