To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London Assembled in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guild Hall of the said City.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Slingsby Bethell Lord Mayor of the said City and William Stephenson Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace fot the said City of London bearing date the twentieth Day of August one thousand Seven hundred & Fifty Six Thomas James (being one of the People commonly called Quakers) Mary his wife & their four Children Anne aged Ten Years Thomas & Sarah aged three years each and Priscilla aged one year were removed from the Parish of St.Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middx. as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court against the same and desire to be relieved therein

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Wm: Boother Thos. Huddle} Churchwardens

John Upton Thos. Nicholl} Overseers of the Poor
