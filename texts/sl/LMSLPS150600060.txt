To the Right Honble. Sr. Willm. Calvert Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. or Order under the hands and Seals of the said Sr. Wm. Calvert and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of November last Mary Jones wife of John Jones a Sailor in His Majesty's Service and their child James aged Nine Years were removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Jones and child

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices of the peace, Appeal to this Court against the same & humbly pray that yor Worships will be pleas'd to appoint a day for the hearing & Determining of the same

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Dan Bolton Agent for the Appellts.
