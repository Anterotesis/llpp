To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Elizabeth Burges Singlewoman was by an Order or Pass Warrant Date the 13th. day of Septr. last under the Hands and Seals of the Rt. Honoble Sr. Richard Glyn Bart. Lord Mayor of the City of London & William Bridgen Esqr. Alderman of the sd. City two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum, Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Therefore your Petitioners & Appellants conceive themselves Aggrieved thereby humbly Appeal to the Judgment of this Honoble Court against the same & Pray that the Court will be pleased to hear & determine the said appeal and that all Persons concerned may abide the Judgemt. & determination there of And that your Petrs. May be relieved as this Honoble Court shall seem meet

And your. petrs. shallever Pray Etc.

Humpy. Elmes Atty. for the Appellts.
