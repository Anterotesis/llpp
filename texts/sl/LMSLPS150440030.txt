To the Right Honble. John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Master and Wardens of the Fraternity of the Art or Mistery of Haberdashers in the City of London

Sheweth That your Petitioners Common Hall is scituate in the Parish of St. Mary Staining London part of which Hall your Petitioners have appropriated to the use of dwelling Houses for their Clerk & Beadles and are known reputed and taken to be the property of your Petitioners and the Taxes for the same have been paid by your Petitioners. That a Rate now made by the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the said parish for the Relief of the poor is very unequall by which your Petitioners conceive they are aggrieved and over rated in proportion to John Hall Foxall Isaac Sierra and others of the Inhabitants of the said Parish

Therefore your Petitioners humbly appeal to this Court for such Relief as to this Court shall seem meet.
