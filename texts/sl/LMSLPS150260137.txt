To the Right Worll: his Mats: Justices of the peace for the City of London sitting at GuildHall

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor for the Hamlett of Stratford lee Bow & Oldfoord in the Pish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Whereas Rebecca Daniells was by Order or Warrant Dated the 20th: day of December last under the hands and Seales of Sr William Humphries Barront: Lord Mayor of the said City and Sr: Samuel Garrard Knt. two of his Mats: Justices of the peace for the said City & liberties thereof (Quor un) Removed from the Parish of St. Mary Colechurch London to the said Hamlett of Stratford lee Bow & Oldfoord (by the name of Bow in the Parish of Bow)

Yor: Petrs: finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Appeale to this Honoble: Court agat: the same and prays that they may be releived in the Prmisses And ye Petr: Shall pray Etc

Timothy Kent Hen Tilbury} Churchwardens
