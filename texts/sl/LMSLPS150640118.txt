To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Sessions of the Peace for the Said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Norton Falgate to in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of Crisp Gascoyne Lord Mayor and Thomas Chitty Esqr Two of his Majesty;s Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Fourth Day of January One Thousands Seven Hundred and Fifty Three Isaac Baker aged Forty Four Years was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopsgate London to the Liberty of Norton Falgate in the Said County of Middlesex as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves by the Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices of the Peace do therefore humbly appeal to the Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray

John Winn Francis Nevett} Overseers
