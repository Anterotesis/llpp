To the Rt. Honble Sr Edwd Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worps the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dionis Bothchurch London

Sheweth That By virtue of a Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justice of the Peace for the said City & Liberties (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of March last Edward White Sarah his wife and Elizabeth John & Mary their three Children were removed from the Parish of St Martin Vintry London to the said Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London as the Place of their last legal Settlemt.

Your Peters. Conceiving themselves aggreaved by the Warrt. & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal for relief

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
