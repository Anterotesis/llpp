To the Rt Honble and Rt Worll. the Ld Mayor and Court of Aldermen and others his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled

7/3 The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Margarett Westmr in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr William Thompson and Sr. Thomas Abney two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London bearing date the Seventh day of March 1719 John Bishop with Ann his Wife and two Children Vizt. Ann Aged Seventeen Years and Mary Aged Nine Years were Removed from and out of the Parish of St Batholomew the Great London to the Parish of St. Margarett Westmr. as the place of their lawfull Settlement.

That yr Petrs. thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worps Against the Same

Therefore Humbly pray your Honr and Worps will please to Ordr. That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Bartholomew the Great London may attend this Court Some Day this Quarter Sessions to here and Abide the Final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yr Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
