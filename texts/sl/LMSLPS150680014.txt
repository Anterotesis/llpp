To the Right honourable the Lord Mayor of London and the rest of the Justices of Oyer & Terminer and General Goal Delivery of Newgate at their session at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey in the suburbs if London assembled

The Humble Petition of Richard Hughes

Sheweth That your Petr. was on the 19th. day of January last comitted to the said Goal of Newgate by John Fielding Esqr. one of his Majestie's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, being charged with feloniously publishing as true three false & forged Letters of Attorney, as appears by the copy of Comitment hereunto annex'd

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that Yor. Lordships will be pleased to Order that Yor Petr. may be tryed bailed or discharged as this present session pursuant to the statued in such Case made & provided

And Yor. Pet. shall ever pray Richd Hughes
