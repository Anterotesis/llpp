To the Rt. Honble Sr: Charles Peers Knt. Ld. Majr. of the Citty of London & to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Margaret Wright A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr. was Comitted to the said Goale on the 13th. day of December last Charg'd with Felony

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prayes to be tryed this Sessions or other wayes to be discharged

And yor petr shall ever [...] pray Etc
