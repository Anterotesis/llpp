To the Right Honourable Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Saviour in the County of Surry.

Sheweth. That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of January one thousand seven hundred and fifty six Mary Barber aged five years (as therein named) was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate London unto the said Parish of Saint John as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Do humbly appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordships and Worships shall seem [---] meet

And Your Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc.

Edw. James Soll. for the Appellts.
