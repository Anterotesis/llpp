To the Rt. Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace In their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church -wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Hubbard London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing date the 28th. day of January last one Frances Smith Singlewoman was passed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegt. London to the parish of St. Andrew Hubbard London as the place of her last Legal Settlemt.

Your Peticoners humbly conceiving themselves agreived the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for relief in the premises

And Your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc
