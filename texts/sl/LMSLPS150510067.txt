To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said City and Liberties in Guild Hall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir John Salter Knight Lord Mayor ofMicajah Perry the City of London and Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth Day of June last past William Warren and Sarah his wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Ann in Black Fryars London to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said Justices of the Peace and pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeale

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

J Godfrey Agent for the Appellts:
