Indicted July 1745 Sentenced Sept. To the Rt. Honrable: Henry Marshal Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Honrable: Bench

May it please Year Worships. I find I stand Indicted for an Assault with an intent to Committ. [...] Therefore Jurors humbly pray that I may be Tryed at this prsent Session or be Admitted to Bail. And as in Duly bound I shall ever pray

Samuel Goupil
