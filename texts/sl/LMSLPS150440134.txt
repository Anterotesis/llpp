To the Right honoble the Lord Mayor & others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London assembled in Quarter Sessions as GuildHall in & for the sd. City

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in ye County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the once for the City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th day of Octr: last Frances Humphreys Widw. of Benjn Humphreys and her three Children (To witt) Frances aged 4 Yeares Edwd Aged three Yeares & Elizth aged abt five Months were removed from the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt Danes as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs Conceive themselves to be agreived and agt. the sd. Order doe Appeale

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present to hear & determine the matters touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs shall ever pray
