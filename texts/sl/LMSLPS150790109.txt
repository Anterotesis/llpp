To the Right Honourable The Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middx.

Sheweth. That by virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant bearing date under the Hands and Seals of Your Lordship and Mrs. Alderman Plumbe the fourteenth Day of January last One Ann Wilkinson [---] Singlewoman was sent and conveyed from the Parish of St. Vedast Foster in the said City to the said Parish of St James as the place of he last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrt. and pray relief in the Premes.

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the premes may be taken into Considn. and that the same may be determined in such manner as to your Lordship & this honble. Court shall seem meet

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
