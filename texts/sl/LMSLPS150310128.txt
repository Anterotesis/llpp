To ye. Rgt. Honable. his Matis: Justices of ye: peace, for ye: City of London

The humble peticon & appeale of ye. Churchwarden and Overseer of ye. poor of ye. Hamlet of Bethnall Green in ye parish of St: Dunstan Stepney in ye. County of Middx:

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order under ye. hands & Seales of Two of his Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd City of London beareinge date ye: Ninth day of Aprill. 1720: Elizabeth Gifford widow of Laterall Gifford; was removed & past from ye. parish of St: Giles Cripplegate London, to ye Sd Hamlet of Bethnall Green therein Settinge forth that ye. Sd Hamlet of Bethnall Green in ye. Last Place. of ye. Legall. Setlemt. of ye. Sd Elizabeth Gifford widow

your peticoners findeinge themselves agreived with ye. Judmt: of ye. Sd Two Justices of ye. peace Humbly appeals to this Honable: Court agt. ye. Sd Warrant on order; & prayes ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Sd parish of St: Giles Cripplegate May attend this Court, to heare & abide ye. Judgmt. & finall determinacon of this Court touching ye. Sd appeale

And Yr: peticonr: Shall ever pray.

Wm: Church Churchwarden Jos: Gosdin Overseer of ye. poor
