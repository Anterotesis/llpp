To ye. Rt. Honble Sr. Wm: Lewen knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London & to the nest of the honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticons of Margaret Pheazant & Susanne Moss prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs: stane Comittee to Newgate Charg'd with felony where of they are both innocent

Yor Petr. humbly prayes this Honble Court to be tryed this Sessins or to be discharged upon Baile or otherwayes as this honble Court shall thinck fit

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray etc
