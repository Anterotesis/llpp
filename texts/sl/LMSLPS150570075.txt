To the Rt. Honble. the Lord Mayor and the rest of the this Majestys Justices of the peace Assembled

The Humble [...] Session Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George Stanover Square

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Handsand Seals of the Rt. Honble. [...] Sir Richard Hoare Mayor and Walter Bernard Esqr. to of the Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 18th day of April last a Child named John Churchey aged two years son of John churchey by Other his wife was removed from the Parish of Christ Church London to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the Place of his last legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggreived and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitr: therefore humbly pray this Court will please to Appoint a limit for hearing & Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may them attend

And Your Peter shall ever Pray etc.

J Parry for St. George Hanmore Square
