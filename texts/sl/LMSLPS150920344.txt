To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majesties Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the 21st. day of October now last past under the hands and seals of the Right Honourable Brackley Kennett Lord Mayor of the City of London and Evan Pugh Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said City (One whereof being of the Quorum) Whereby Catherine Jones a singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City as the place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same

And therefore pray that you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London afsd And all persons concerned to attend this Court some day this present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the Premises And that Your Petitioners may have such relief therein as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners will ever pray
