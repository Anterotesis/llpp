To the Right Honrble Daniel Lambert Esqr [...] Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Worp- full this Majesties Justices of the Peace to the [...] their General Quarter Session of the Peace [...] the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal [...] Church Wardens and Overseers of The Poo [...] the Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise B [...] the said City.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant Under the Hand and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Ouorum) bearing date the Ninth day of October in the Year of Our Lord One Thousand Seven hundred and Forty one Jane Sinks or Sinke was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Anns Black Fryers London into the Parish of Saint Bridget other wise Bridges in London aforesaid at the Place of her last settlement

Your Petitioners therefore Conserning themselves Aggreived by the said Order a Pass Warrant Humbly Appeal against the same and Pray such r ceif in the Premisses as to this Honorable Court shall seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
