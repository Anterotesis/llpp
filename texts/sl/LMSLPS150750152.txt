To the Right Honourable William Bridgen Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchers without in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Suburbs thereof (one being of the Quorum bearing date the Twenty Second day of December last Mary Payne Wife of Edward Payne who is gone from her was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchers without in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Warrant or Order and humbly Pray such relief in the Premises as to your Lordship and this Honb Court Shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pay Etc.

John Hyde P G
