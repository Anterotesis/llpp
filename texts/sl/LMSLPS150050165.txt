To the Right Honoble: Sr. Thomas Lane Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties. Justices att the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

17th October 1694 The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Eyling in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of A Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of their Maties. Justices of the Peace for this City (one being of the Quor) Dated the 17th. of October last one Robert Hammond son of William Hammond was sent to yor. Petr. to be provided for upon the Oath of one John Hartwell that Wm. Hammond the father lived att Bramptford in the Parish of Eyling in the said County for Five or Sixe Yeares as A housekeeper where the said Robert was born whereas in truth the said Wm. Hammond for about yeares past did live in the said Parish but afterwardes in the yeare 1686 dwelt in the Parish of St. James Garlick in the London As Appeares by a Certificate under the hand of the Minister and Churchwardens in order to obteyne the said Wm. Hammond to be admitted into the society of Porters London the better to enable him to make provision for his family whoe was admitted accordingly and never since had any residence in the said Parish of Eyling

Yor. Petrs. humbly Pray yor. Honors. and Worpps. to Swar them in the Prmisses and to can so the said Robert Hammond to be sent to the said Parish of St. James Garlickhith where his father Wm. Hammond was last legally settled there to be provided for According to the Law.

And they shall Pray Etc.

Appeale allowed
