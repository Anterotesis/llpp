To the Right Honrble Sir Robert Westley Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of Lonon and the rest of the Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order of Vagrant Pass Warrant under the Hum and Seal of Sir Robert Westley Knight Mayor of the City of London one of the Justices of the Peace for the said City (being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of May 1744 Sarah Jones aged about fourteen years was lately removed. as a Rogne and Vagabond by the aforesaid Lord Mayor unto the said Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides London as the Place of her last Legal Settlemt.

Yor Petr Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order or Vagrant Pass Warrant humble Appeal agt. the same. And Pray such relief in the Premes as Your Lordship and Worships shall see in meet

And Yor Petes shall Pray Etc
