To the Right Honourable James Townsend Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general Session of the pease assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the hand and Seal of the right Honourable James Townsend Lord Mayor of the City of London one of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City bearing date the twelfth day of January last Mary Selling Wife of Jacob Selling gone from her whose Settlement cannot be found and their Child Sarah aged four Months apprehended in the parish of Saint Gregory London was conveyed as a Rogue and Vagabond with her said Child to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your petitioners therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the said pass Warrant And pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law and Justice

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Rey: Grignion Jno Gerard} Churchwardens

and

Richd West Wm: Williams} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
