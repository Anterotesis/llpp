To the Right Honrble Sr. Richard Hoare Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City in the general Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Gregory by St pauls London

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant under the hands Seals of hoe of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorm) bearing date the Thirteenth Day of August last past Eleanor Lane Wido with her two Children William and Elizabeth was removed from the parish of St Andrew Holborn London into the said Parish of St Gregory by St Pauls alleadging the said parish to be the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Eleanor Lane never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to yor Psonr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Andrew Holborn may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs: Shall ever pray Etc
