To the Right Honble Stephen Theodere Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesties Justices of dyer and Terminer for the Goal of Newgate

The Humble Petn. of James [...] Flowers a prisoner in Newgate Hbly

Sheweth That yor Petr. is Charged with high Treason for raising Septers on Shillings in imitation of Guineas And with Uttering the Same Which yor Petr. hopes to prove his Innocence.

Yor Petr. therefore hbly prays yor Lord Shp That he may be Try'd Bailed or Discharged at this presence Sessions And Yor Petr. as in Duty Bound will Ever Pray.

James Flowers
