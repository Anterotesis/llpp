To the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint [...] in the said City of London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and [...] of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City one being of ma [...] bearing date the Ninth day of August In the Year of our Lord [...] Clarke Wife of Joseph Clarke and her Child William aged three years removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of St. Botolph with in the said City of London into the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in [...] City of London as the place of their last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Humbly Appeale against the same and pray such Relief in the Premises as to this Honourable Court shall seem Meet.
