To the Right Honourable Sir William Billers Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty Justices of the Peace for the Said City att their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of Sir William Billers Kmt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Richard Brocas Knt. and Alderman two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London, and the Liberties there of, (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Seventh day of July last, Ann Newey Singlewoman was removd from the Parish of Saint Bottolph without Bishopgate London to the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London Adjudying the last legal Settlement of the Said Ann Newey to be in the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London That Your Petitioner conceive themselves aggreived by the Said Order of the Said two Justices, And therefore Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court to be Reliev'd in the premisses, And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc

Thomas Barnes Jacob Randall{ Churchwardens & Overseers
