To the Right Honble: Sr. John Fleete Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll: their Maties. Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Lesse London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the handes & seales of twoe of their Maties. Justices of the peace wth. in this Citty a certeyne male child aged an about 3 yeares found wandering in or neere St. Bartholmews Hospitall wthin. the said pish was sent & delivered to yor. Petitioners to be provided for and mainteyned by the said Parish which yor. Petrs. receive They are not by Lawe to provide for & mainteyne The said Child being left wth. in the an Prmint of the said hospitall The Governors. whereof are Patrons of the Church these And pay nothing towards the releife of the poore And who are to provide for the poore in their Hospital or to be left therein As formerly they have done

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray That yor. Honors. and Worshippe would be pleased to order that the Trer of the said Hospitall may appeare before yor. And that you will be pleased to heare yor. Petrs. in the prmisses And [...] order the keepeing of the said Child according to Lawe.

And they shall Pray Etc
