To the Rt. Honoble. Sr Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worll: The rest of her Maties: Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That one William Ralfesh a young Child by virtue of a Warrant Dated 19th. May 1707 under the hands and seales of twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quox) who brought by the Churchwardens of the Parish of St. Michaell Queenehithe to yor. Peticonr. to be provided for upon the Oath of George Young That George Preston Father in Law of the said William Ralfesh was an Appntice with one Mr. Evans a Clothworker in the Said Parish of St. Buttolphs Whereas in truth the Father of the said William Ralfesh dwelt in the said Parish of St. Michaells Queenehithe Severall yeares where the said William was Borne And where his Father dyed and was an Inhabitant Setled by Lawe in the said Parish of St Michaells

Yor. Petrs. humbly Pray That their Appeale may be heard And that the said William Ralfesh may be sent back to the said Parish of St. Michaells Queenshithe where hee was borne And was the place of his own Fathers last legall settlement To be there provided for by them according to the Lawe

And They shall Pray Etc
