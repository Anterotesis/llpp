To the Right Honble: Sr. John Fryer Bart. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl. the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the Said City assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Matew in Fryday Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of our Order or pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the 5th. day of July inst. [...] Susanna Bates was removed from the Parish of St. Benedict vulgarly Bennet Fink London to the said Parish of St. Mathew in Fryday Street London alledging the said parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners Conceive themselves agreived by the said Warrt. and Judgemt. of the said two Justices And Doe most humbly Appeal unto Your Honr. & Worps. therefrom.

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly pray Your Honr. & Worps. will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Benedict vulgarly Bennet Fink may Appear before You & hear and abide the Determinacon of the Court touching the said Appeal.

And they Shall Ever pray Etc.
