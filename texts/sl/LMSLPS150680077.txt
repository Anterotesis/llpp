To the Right Honoble Marshe Dickinson Esqr and the rest of the Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex at Justice Hall in the Old Baily London

The Humble Petition of Jemima Wilcox

Sheweth That your Petr. being Charged on the Oath of Nicholas Kemp [...] and James Blackburn for High Treason and Felony expressed in the Warrants of Committment Yor. Petr. stands Comitted to Newgate

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays to be Tryed Bailed or Discharged this present Session's pursuant to the Statute in that behalf

And Your Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Jemima Willcox
