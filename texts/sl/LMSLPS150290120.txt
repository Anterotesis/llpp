To the Right Honble Sr Willm Lowen Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to ye Rt Worpl the rest of his Matys Justices of ye peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Eaton in the County Bucks.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands and Seals of two of his Matyr Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quorunns) bearing Date the 16th day of September Anno Dni 1718 George Cooper was removed from the parish of St Mary Woolnoth London unto the parish of Eaton in the County of Bucks alledging the Said parish to be the place of his last Legal Settlement

That Your Petiticoners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour & Worps Will be pleased to hear them in ye prmisses and order that the Church Wardens Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Woolnoth London may apper before you and hear & abide the Determinacon of your Honr & Worps touching the sd Appeal

And they Shall Ever pray Etc
