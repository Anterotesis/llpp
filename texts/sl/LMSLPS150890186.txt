To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bennet Shorehog in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of September last past Elizabeth Kemp a Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin within Ludgate in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Bennett Shorehog as the last legal place of her Settlement, By which your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order

Your Petitioners therefore Pray your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the matters touching this Appeal and give them such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem just and meet

Webb & Woods Agents for the Appellants. And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
