1699

To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Richard Levett Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Michael Queenhith

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of this Matyes. Justices of the Peace within this Citty (One being of the Quor) Dated, the 29 January 1699 End Mary Punnall was brought by the Churchwardens of Alhallowes Barking to yor. Petrs. to be mainteyned upon her Oath That her last legall Settlement was in Yor Petrs. Parish of St Michaell where Shee lived as an hired Servant for the have of a whole yeare with Mrs. Anne Carfoote widd Whereas in tenth Shee twed with the said Mrs Carfoote not above 10 months

Yor. Petrs. Pray They may be heard on their Appeale And that the said Mary may be Sent to the Said Parish of Alhallowes Barking to be by them provided for untill They can free themselves of the charge thereof by due Course or Law And They may be Allowed the Charges They have Susteyned by the Remove all aforesaid

And They Shall Pray Etc
