To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Wm. Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll the rest of her Maties. Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled:

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the pish of St. Sepulchers wth. out Newgate in London

Sheweth That one Mary Thompson widdow together wth her three Children named John Thomas & Mary By virtue of a Warrt. under the Hands & Seales of Two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace wth. in the City of London one being of the Quor) bearing date the 7th. day of September 1708 was removed from the pish of St. Mary Woolworth London to the said parish of St. Sepulchers London Alleadgeing the said parish is the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the said Mary Thompson

That yor Petr. doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Thompson never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgemt. of the said Two Justices And do humbly Appeal to yor. Worpps. therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor Honor. & Worpps. will be pleased to bear them in the prmisses And order the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Mary Woolworth To Appeare here before you And hear and abide the determinacon of yor. Honor. & Worpps. touching this Appeale

And yor. Petr Shall Ever pray Etc
