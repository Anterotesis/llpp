To the Rt. Honble. the Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the sd. City assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church- Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Francis Cockayne Esqr. & Lord Mayor of the sd. City & Robert Alsop Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof (on being of the Quorum) bearing Date the tenth day of November last Thomas Nolton and Mary his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Nolton & his Wife Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived

Joshua Cox Sollr for the Appellts Yor Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrt or [---] Order of the sd. two Justices of the Peace Do therefore humble appeal agt. the [---] same and pray that Uppleaing and determining thereof may such adjourned [---] the general Quarter Session of the Peace to be held at Guildhall in the sd. [---] City and that all parties may then attend to hear and abide the Judgmt. [---] and determination of the said Appeal

And yor. Petrs shall ever pray Etc
