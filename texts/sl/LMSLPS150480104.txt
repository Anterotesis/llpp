To the Rt. Honourable Sir John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worshipfull ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City & Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch in the City of London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under ye. hands and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the First of this instant Aprill John Maslin and Sarah his Wife were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St. Catherine Creechurch London alledging the said Parish of St. Catherine [...] Creechurch to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoners Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do Appeal to yor. [---] & Worps there from And humbly pray that yor. Lordship & Worships will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor. Lep & Worps touching this Appeal

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray etc.
