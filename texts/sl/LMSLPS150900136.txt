To the Right Honourable Samuel Plumbe Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at the General Sessions of the Peace for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Stands and Seals of the Right Honourable Samuel Plumbe Esquire Lord Mayor and Evan Pugh Esquire Alderman two of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. day of May 1779 Catherine the Widow of John Hunt deceased was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London to our said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Do humbly Appeal against the said Order or pass Warrant and Pay that this Court will be pleased to appoint a day at this present Session of the Peace for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish Saint Andrew Holborn and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
