To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John the Baptist in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of July last past Ann Smith Widow of Richard Smith Deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Swithin at London Stone in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint John the Baptist in London as the Last legal place of her Settlement By which Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Pass order

Your Petitioners therefore Pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this appeal and give them such relief in the premisses as to Your worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall every Pray Etc
