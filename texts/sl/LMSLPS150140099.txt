To the Rt. Hoble Sr. Samuell Dashwood Knt Lord Major of the City of London And other Rt. Worll. Hen Maties Justices of if Peace In the Generall Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Faithes London

Sheweth That one John Wilson with Anne his wife and 2 Children being an Inhabitants in yor. Peticoners Parish the said John Wilson haveing beene married about 18 yeares to the said Anne his wife And by profession is a Surgcon and of good Ability to provide for his Family Yet not with standing the said John Wilson does refuse to provide for and mainteyne his said wife and Children as by Law he ought to doe And hath left them to the Care of Yor. Petrs Parish to be provided for And the said John Wilson being bound over to appear this sessions To answer yor. Petr. Complaint

Yor. Petrs therefore most humbly pray yor Honour & Worpps. To hear then in the Prmisses And to Order the said John Wilson to provide for his wife and Children as by Law he ough to doe And Free Yor. Petr parish from the charge and Burthen thereof

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
