London ss. To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Geo: Thorold Bart. the Rt. Worpll the Record and the Rest of the Honble Bench now Sitting att Justice Hall in ye Old Bayly.

The Humble prayer of Anne Stone Now a prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor petr. was Comitted to this Place the fourteenth of July last for picking the Pocket of one Mr Purdue of which fact She is Inocent

yor petr therefore humbly prays that She [...] be tryed This Sessions for the said fact [...] Otherwise Discharged According as he yor Lordsp. and this Honble. Bench Shall Seem Meet

And yor petr. as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc.
