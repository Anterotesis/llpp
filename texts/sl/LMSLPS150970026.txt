To the Right Honble The Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at Guild Hall in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennet Sherehog in the City of London

Sheweth That the Right Hoble Thos. Wright Esqr. Lord Mayor and Henry Kitchin Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London by an Order under their Respective hands and Seals Bearing date the 28th day of January 1786 Directed to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London Did thereby Requi [...] them to Remove and Convey Mary (in the said Order Called) The Widow of John Creaswell deceased unto the said Parish of St. Bennet Sherehog

That you Petitioners Apprehed and aer advised That they are greatly aggrieved by the said Order of Removal And

Therefore your Petitioners humbly Appeals against the same and Prays That this Honble Court will make such Order therein as shall be Just and Reasonable

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
