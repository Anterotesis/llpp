To the Right Honble Sr. Richard Hoare Knt Lord maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the rest of her maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the general quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the parish of St Faith the Virgin London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (no being of the Quorn) bearing date the tenth Day of August last past Elizabeth Armitage wife of Samuel Armitage with her two Children were removed from the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London unto the said parish of St Faith the Virgin alleadging the said parish of St Faith to be the place of their last legall Settlement

That your petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Elizabeth armitage never had any Legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Faith whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the poor of the said Parish of St Bridgett als Brides may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc.
