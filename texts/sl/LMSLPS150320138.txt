To the Rt. Worll. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Qr. Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals of the Rt Honble Sir John Fryer Lord Mayor of the said City of London & Sr. Gerrard Conyers Knt. two of his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the Said City bearing Date the 27th. day of April last Jonathan Showers Abigail his Wife and their Six Children were removed from and out of the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of St Margaret Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves thereby agreived most humbly pray this Worll. Court that they may be heard by their Council against the said Ordr. of Removal & in favour of their appeal, And that this Court will accordingly Determine the same.

And yr Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
