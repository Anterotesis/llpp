To the Right Honoble. Sr. John Fleete Knt. Lord Marion of the City of London And other their Matyer. In Justices of the place of the same City

The humble Petition & appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poore of the pish of St Georges Buttolph Lane London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Wales of boot of their Majestyes Justiceces of the peace wth. in this City one Henry Bates with his wife & twoe Children were sent to yor. Petr. to be Pvided for upon the Oath of Mary Bates the wife that the said Henry her Husband was last legally settled in yor. Petr. pish for about Five yeares where they said severall Taxes to here as in tenth the said Henry Bates had never any legall Settlement in the said Pish but was last legally Settled in the Pish of St. Buttolph Bishoppsgate

Yor. Petrs. humbly prayes yor. Honors. & Worpps. to hear them in the Prmisses & Quash the Order made by the said hood Justiced And send the said Henry Bates to be Pvided for in the Pish of St Buttolphs according to Law.

And they shall Pray Etc.
