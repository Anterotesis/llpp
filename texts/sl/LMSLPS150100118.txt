To the Rt: Honrbl Sr: Francis Child Lord Major. And to the Rest of his Majts: Judges now Asembled at the Old Bayley

The humble Petitio of John Keats now Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petitioner was maliciously Comited to Newgate for that hainous Cryme of Buggrey propogated and Instegated by dwell minded people: as yor. Petitiones shall prove by Worthy Just honest men: therefore your petitioner heartyly desires to be tryed or Left upon Baill Acordinge as the Law directs In such Cases

And yor. Petitioner Shall ever pray

Peticon Jno. Kents
