To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor Aldermen and Recorder of the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church -Wardens and Over seers of the Parish of saint Bartho comers the Great in the said City. Sherveth,

That by an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right, Honorable Robert Peckham Mayor and John hart Esquire Alderman two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City, one where of being of the Quorum, bearing Date the first Day of May, one Thousand seven Hundred and eighty four- Thomas Martin Mary his Wife and their four Children namely Thomas aged ten years, Elizabeth aged seven Years, James aged five years, and Joseph aged there year were removed from the Parish of Saint Glies without Cripplegate in the said City, to the said Parish of Saint Bartholourew the Great to be the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved,

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly appeal to the Court against the said Adjudication of the said Justice and their said order- And your Petitioners shall ever may Ee

John Evans Solicitor for the Appellants.
