To the Right Honorble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Parishioners of the Parish of St. Mathew Friday Streete London

Sheweth That the of yor. Petrs. Parish by mistake in a returne to the Precept directed to them from the Right Honoble. the late Lord Maior of this City to give an account of their Poore and the charge of mainteyning them hee made a returne of the sume of £79:15s:6d for the Yeares charge in 1697 wch. was the totall sume of all the Payments made in every respect for & concerning the same Parish Whereas in truth the true charge of their Poore amounts to noe more Then about £52:16s.

And their said Parish being charged by virtue of the late Act of Comon Councill to pay £39:10s to the Corporation established by the said Act for setting the Poore on worke

Now yor. Petrs. in respect of the smallnesse of their Parish And the greate charge of Poore therein which they have to mainteyne Desire that yor. Lopp. & Worpps. will be pleased to releive yor. Petrs. And to order the Abatement of the said sume of £39:10s. to such sume in proportion To what doth truely arise by their assessement for mainteyning the Poore of their said Parish They being only releivable by this Courte

And they shall Pray Etc
