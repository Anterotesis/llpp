To the Right Honourable John Barber Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful his Majestys of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ethelburga Lodon

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of January last past James Bignall and Ann his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the Parish of Saint Ethelburga London as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Honourable Court for Relief in the Premisses.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray and so forth
