To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their general Session of the peace assmebled.

The Humble Petition of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Vagrant Order under the hand and Seal of Benjamin Hopkins Esqr. one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing date the twenty first day of October one thousand seven hundred and seventy four Elizabeth Francis was removed from the Parish of Saint Mildred Poultry in the said City of London to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the County of Middlesex as the place of the legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Francis, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved, and against the said Order do appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that the Benefit of their appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the City of London aforesaid And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the said next Sessions for the hearing and determining of their said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Cox Soltr. for Appellts.

crd J.C.
