To the Rt honble Sr. Charles Peers of Knt Ld. mayor of the Citty of London & the rest of the honble Bench of Justices.

The humble Peticon of John Bridett

Sheweth that yr. peticonr was Comitted to Newgate for feld the 15th day of Sepembr. 1715 & has laid there since.

May it therefore please yr. Ldpp & this honble Bench Court that yr Peticonr may be tryed this Sessions or be discharged & he will Pray Etc.
