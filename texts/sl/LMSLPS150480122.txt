To the Rt. Honble Sr John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpl the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Sr John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr John Salter Knt. & Alderman of the same City two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within the sd City (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of October 1737 George Doncaster Katherine his Wife and their five Children Vizt. Mary Margaret, Susanna, John & Katherine, were removed from the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the parish of Alhallows on the Wall London As the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves Agreived by the Warrt. & Judgment of the sd two Justices humbly Appeal for Relief

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray etc
