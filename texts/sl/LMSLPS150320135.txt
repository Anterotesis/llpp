to the Right honble the Lord Mayor & Recorder & other his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Citty of London Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church- wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of Sr. Peter Deline & Sr. George Merttins two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London bearing Date the 31th, day of March last Sarah Wilcocks the wife of John Wilcocks with her Child Charles Wilcocks year old or thereabts. were removed from the Pish of St. Anne Blackfryers in the Citty of London to the Sd. Pish of St. Clemt. Danes at the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petr. thinke themselves aggrieved by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices

And therefore doe humbly appeale to this honble Cort for yor Judgemt. touching the sd. Appeale
