To the Right Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of her Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows in the [---] Town of Cambridge

Sheweth That One Mary Creed Widdow by Virtue of a Warrant under the hand and Seals of two of her Majesty's Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quom) bearing date the Sixth day of November last was removed from the parish of St. Sepulchre within the said City of London [---] unto the said parish of Allhallows in the [---] Town of Cambridge alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Creed

That your Petitionr doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Creed never has any legal Settlement in the said parish of Allhallows Cambridge whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgmt: of the said two Justices And do humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitonr: therefore most humbly pray That your Honr: and Worpp will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Sepulchre may appear before and hear and abide the determination of your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
