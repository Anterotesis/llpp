To the Right Honorable Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in their general quarter Session of the peace for the said City assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Matfellon otherwise White Chappel in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing Date the twenty third day of March 1753 Nicholas Hamblin Elizabeth his wife & their child Benjamin aged six years were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of St. Bridget otherwise Brides London into the said Parish of St. Mary Matfellon otherwise White Chappel

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or warrant do humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray such relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet.

F. Salt. Atty. for Appellants
