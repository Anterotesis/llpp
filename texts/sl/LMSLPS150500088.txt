To The Right Worshipfull Micajah Perry Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace now. Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the ChurchWardens, and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the said Micajah Perry Esquire then and now Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sir Robert Baylis Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London aforesaid (one whereof is of the Quorun) bearing Date the 16th: Day of February last past Martha Thacker; Widow of Richard Thacker deceased and her two Children, (to with) Hannah Swift aged five Years, and Katherine aged one Year were removed from the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the said City of London unto the said Parish of Saint Luke Alledgeing the said Parish of Saint Luke to be the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners Humbly Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order, or Pass Warrant, and therefore Humbly Appeale against the same

J Witton Sollr: for the Appellts:

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc

Peter Verstille Tho: Southby} Churchwardens

Mac Routlidge Thos. Harris} Overseers of the Poor
