To the Right Honoble. Sir Richard Hoare Knt. Lord Mayor and the Right Worship full the Recorder and the left of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said city and the Liberties thereof in their general quarter session assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of st. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of the Right Honoble. Sir Richard Hoare Knt. Lord Mayor and Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the said city and the liberties thereof bearing date the eighteenth day of June instant Jane Price wife of Samuel Price (who is alleged by the said order to be at sea) was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of saint Andrew in Holborn London into the said parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said warrant or order humbly appeal to this court against the same and pray such relief in the premes as to this honoble. court shall seem meet

James Chare Sollr. for the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of st. Botolph without Aldgate in the county of Middx.

And your Petr. Shall ever pray etc.
