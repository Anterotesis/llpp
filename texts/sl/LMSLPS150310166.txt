To The Rt. Honble. Sr. George Thorold Barrt. Lord Mayor & ye rest of ye Honble. Bench Now Siting at Justice Hall in the Old Baylie.

The humble petion of Mary Granger now a prsoner in Newgate,

Humbly Sheweth That yor. petrs. was Comitted To This place the the 27th. of Decemr last for takeing out of the hand of Saml. Colthurst 20s. (of wch fact She is Inocent

Yor petr. therefore humbly beceeches yor Lordsp. & the honble. bench That She may be tryed for the sd Fact (or discharged according to Law) This Session and

Yor. petrs. (as in duty bound) Shall Ever pray Etc.
