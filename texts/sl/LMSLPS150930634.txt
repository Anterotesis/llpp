To the Right Honorable William Plomer Mayor of the City of London And the Worshipful the Aldermen of the said City at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hand and seals of William Plomer Esquire Mayor of the City of London and Robert Peckham Esquire one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date on the Eighteenth day of January last Martha the Widow of Andrew Peart was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the said City as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby you Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Therefore humbly Appeal to his Honorable Court against the same And pray that this Honorable Court will make such Order therein as may be just

Martha Petitioners will every pray &c April 1782
