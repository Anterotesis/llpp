To the Right Honrble Sr Charles Duncombe Knt: Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Leonard East cheape London

Sheweth That one Mary Tomlin with her Child about a Month old By Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Fourth day of June last past was removed from the Parish of St. Bennetts Grace church London to the send Parish of St. Leonard Eastcheape alleadeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Tomlin

That your Petitonrs doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Tomlin never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Leonard Eastcheape whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Twoe Justices And doe humbly Appeale to your Worpps there from

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the premisses And Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Bennett Grace church to appeare before you And heare and abide the Determine of your Hone and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
