To the Rt Honble: Humphry Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majties Justices of the City of London

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Pauls Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City (Quor Unus) bearing date the thirtyeth day of June last one Jane [...] Whicherly Wife of Thomas Whicherly (who is gone away from her) was Removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchers in the said City of London to the sd. Parish of St. Paul Deptford Adjudging the sd Parish of St. Pauls Deptford to be the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Jane Whicherly

That your Petiocners Conceiving themselves Agreived by the sd. Warrant & Judgment of the sd. two Justices

Humbly Appeal to this Honble: Court therefrom

Thos. Watson} Overseers of the Poor of St. Pauls Deptford
