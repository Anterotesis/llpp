To ye Rt. Honble. Sr Francis Child Knt. Ld Major of ye Citty of London To ye Rest of ye Justices now & Settinge att ye Old Baily

The humble Peticon of Edward Procter A Prisonr in Newgate.

Most Humbly Sheweth That yor Peticonr. was Comitted by Sr Richard Levett Knt. Charged for not appearing according to his Recognizance hee Standing Indicted att Old Bayly for picking John Bryans Pockett of three Pound Fifteen Shillings.

Your Lordsps. Peticonrs most Humbly Prayeth that yor Peticonr may bee Tryed, Bayled or discharged this Sessions

And yor Peticonr Shall Pray
