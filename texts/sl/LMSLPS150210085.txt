Appeale St Bennett Finck agt St Thomas the Apostle

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices all the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Bennett Finck London

Sheweth That one Lydia Pitches and Thomas Pitches by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Third Day of February last past was sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Thomas the Apostle to the said Parish of St. Bennett Fink alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Lydia Pitches

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove That the said Lydia Pitches never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Bennett Finck whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Worpp therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr: & Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said parish of St. Thomas the Apostle may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr. and Worppsl touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
