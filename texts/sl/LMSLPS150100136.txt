To the Right Honoble. Sr Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Magnus the Martin London

Sheweth That John Younger with his wife & child by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) were brought by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall unto yor. Petrs. to be provided for on the Oath of the said John Younger that he was last legally settled in the Parish of St. Magnus where he lived as an Appntice with John Garland Tallow chandler whereas in truth the said John Younger lived as an Appntice with one John Garland an Inhabitant in the said Parish of Alhallowes about 5 yeares And then the said Garland removeing into the Parish of St. Magnus the said John Younger was with him about a moneth And then went for a Souldier And hath beene since releived by the Churchwardens of Alhallowes as a Poore Inhabitant of their Parish

Yor.Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Honors. & Worpps. to order the said Churchwardens of Alhallowes on the wall to appear before you And to hear them all in the prmisses And to Qush the said Warrant And send the said John younger wth. his wife & child backe to the said Parish or Alhallowes on the Wall To be pvided for & manteyned untill they can the three themselves thereof by due course of Lawe

And yor. Petrs. shall Pray Etc
