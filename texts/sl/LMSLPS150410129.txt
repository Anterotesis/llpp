2/12/30 To the Right Honble: Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London Sr. Wm. Thompson Knt. Recorder and the rest of the Aldermen and Justices of the peace at their General Quarter Sessions now Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Humphry Parsons Esqr and Francis Child Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Second day of December last Elizabeth Catlin a Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborne London as the Place of her last legall Settlement By which Order of the said two Justices Yor. Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agreived and humbly Appeal therefrom

And Therefore Yor. Peticoners pray that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn London & all other persons concerned may attend this Court this present Sessions & stand to & abide the determinacon of this Court touching the Merrits of this Appeal.

And Yor. Peticoners shall Ever Pray Etc.

Cha Dodd Mathw Cox Wm Withers
