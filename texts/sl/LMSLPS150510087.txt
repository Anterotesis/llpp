To the Rt. Honoble. Sir John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Right Worpll. the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margaret Lothbury London

Sheweth That by an Order Dated the Twelfth day of March last under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honble. Sr. John Salter Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. John Williams Knt. and Alderman of the said City Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) one Thos. Wiltshire Mary his wife and their Two Children were passed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said parish of St. Margaret Lothbury as the place of their last legall Settlement

That the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margaret Lothbury Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore Appeal from the same to this Honble. Court and and humbly pray to be releived against the same
