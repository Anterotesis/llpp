To the Rt Honoble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That one William Bayly Junr Son of William Bayly by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within that City (one being of the Quoru) bearing date the tenth day of April last past was removed from the parish of St Ann Aldersgate London unto the said Parish of St Buttolph Bishopsgate London alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Bayly Junr

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said William Bayly Junr never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate whereby they conceive themselves agreived by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr. and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Ann Aldersgate may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etca

St Bishopsgate appeale agt Ann Aldersgate
