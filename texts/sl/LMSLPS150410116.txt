To the Rt Honble. Sr. Richard Brocas Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Rt: Worppll His Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City All the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwards and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order the hands and Seales of the Rt: Honble: Sr. Richard Brocas Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr Edward Bellamy Two of his Matis Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quory Unus) bearing date the First day of August last past Elizabeth the Wife of Augustus Frederick and their Two Children Valentine and Catherine were Removed from the Parish of St Bottolph without Aldersgate London to the said Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate As the Place of the last legall Settlemt: of the said Elizabeth Frederick and her said Two Children

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves aggreived by the sd Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices humbly Appeall against the same and most humbly Pray to be releived in the Prmisses in such manner As to this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And Yr Peticoners As in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray
