To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Samuell Dashwood Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt Worll. Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens of St. John in Wapping in Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx one being of the Quox dated 18th an May 1703 [...] Winckles and Miriam Winckles Spinsters the Daughters of John Winckles Weaver decd were brought by the Churchwardens of St. Buttolph Aldgate London to yor Peter To be provided for on the Oath of John Winckles the Senne of the said John Winckles decd That the last legall Settlement of his Father was in the Parish of St. John Wapping where hee lived Severall yeares as a House keeper and particularly in the yeare 1685 in Wiltsheire lane there And had noe settlement according Lawe Since But now whereas the Said Susanne was since that tyme a hired servant in Baseing hall streete London for near the Space of 2 yeares And the Said Miriam an hath been a hired Servt. in the said Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate for above a year And have been both releived & maintained by the Churchwardens of the said Parish of St. Buttolphs

Their humble Suite unto yor. good Honor. and Worpps is That you will be pleased To heare their Appeale in the permisses And to send the said Susanne and Miriam back to the said Churchwardens of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London To be provided for untill They can free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And They Shall Pray Etc
