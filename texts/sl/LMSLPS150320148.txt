To the Rt. Honoble: Sr: John Fryer Barront: Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said City Assembled at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace (at Guildhall) for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Limehouse in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. William Thompson and Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. two of his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (Quor un) Bearing Date the 25th. Day of July last Anne Stoddard wife of John Stoddard with her two Children (vizt.) Mark aged about Three years and John about Eighteen Months were removed from the Parish of St Anne within Aldersgate London to the said Hamlett of Limehouse whereby your Petrs, Humbly Conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

Your Petrs: therefore most Humbly Appeals to this Court against the said Order and prays such Reliefe as this Court shall seem meet.

And yor: Petrs: shall Ever pray Etc.
