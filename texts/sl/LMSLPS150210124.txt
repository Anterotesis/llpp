To the Rt Honrble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Helens London

Sheweth That one Mary Slater Singlewoman by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City one being of the Quorn bearing Date the 18th Day of November last Past was removed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said Parish of St Helens alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall settlement of the said Mary Slater

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Slater never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Helens whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in ye Prmises and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate may appeare before gone and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
