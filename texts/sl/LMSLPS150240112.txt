To the Rt. Honble St. Richard Houre Knt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London, and the rest of her Maties Justies of the Peace for these City

The humble Peticon & appeals of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London,

Shers, That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrt. bearing date the fourth day of July past under the hands & Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Sd. City (Quor unus) one Rachel Gardens als Hill was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Stephen Coleman street in this City to yor. Peticonrs. Sd Pish of St. Michl. Bassishaw wth. was adjudged by the sd. Justices to be the place of her legal Settlemt. as being Corn there.

That yor. Peticonrs. Conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Ordr. or Pass Warrt. & Judgmt. of the sd. Justices

Where fore they humbly appeal agal. the same to this Court and pray for such relief here in as to yor. Lopp e Woppll shall seem just & reasonable.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall Prayer

The Peticon & appeale of the Church wardens Etc of St Michael Bassishaw London agnt. St. Stephen Coleman Street touching the settlemt. of Rachell Gardner als Hill

Appeal allowed
