To the Right Honble. Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John at Hackney [...] the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the 6th day of this Instant January under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Lambert and Samuel Pennant Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) Mary Clare the wife of William Clare was removed from the Parish of Saint Markin Vintrey in the said City to the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement

By which said Warrant or Order your Peticoners Conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And yor Petitioners will Ever pray

Jos. Sureties} Church Wardens
