To the Right Honble Frederick Bull Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and the Liberty's thereof

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Whitwick in the County of Leicester

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship Nathaniel Thomas Esquire Alderman two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date on or about the 3d. day of March now last past and made upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Ironmonger Lane in the City of London the said two Justices did adjudge the last legal Settlement of Susanna Travell Wife of John Travell and her two Children Abigail Travell aged Ten years and Thomas Travell aged Five Years in the said Order respively mentioned to be in your Petitioners said Parish of Whitwick in the County of Leicester and did thereby Will and require the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin Ironmonger Lane in the City of London forthwith to Remove and Convey the said Susanna Travell and her two Children Abigail Travell and Thomas Travell from and out of their said Parish unto your Petrs. said Parish of Whitwick in the County of Leicester and them Deliver unto your Petitioners there or to some or one of them together with the said Order and thereby required your Petitioners to receive and provide for them according to Law

That the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin Ironmonger Lane in the City of London thereupon Caused the said Susanna Travell and her two Children Abigail Travell and Thomas Travell to be forthwith Removed and Conveyed from their said Parish and delivered unto your Petitioners together with the sd. Order of Removal

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal and therefore

or

Your Petitioners humbly Appeal from the said Order of Removal Etc

Tho: Hood Sollr. for the Appellants
