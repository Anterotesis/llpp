To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St John Zachary London

Sheweth That be Warrt: Dated the 11th day of June [...] Instant under the hands and seales of S: Lovell and Thomas Rawlinson Knts. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty of London (Quor Unus) Edward Davis were Removed from the pish of St Anne wth in Aldersgate London to the said pish of St John Zachary London Alleadgeing the said pish is the place of the Last Legall settlement of the said Edward Davis That yor petr doubts not but to prove that the said Edward Davis never had any Legall Settlement in the said pish of St John Zachary London Whereby they Conceive themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices And Doe humbly appeale to yor Worshipps therefrom

Yor Petr. therefore humbly pray yor Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day this Prsent Session that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of St Annsworth Aldersgate aofresaid may Attend yor Worships and heare and abide the determinacon of yor Worships touching this Appeale

And yor Petrs Shall pray Etc
