To the Right Honourable Brass Crosby Lord Mayor of the City of London and Others his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Brass Crosby Lord Mayor of the City of London and James Rossetter Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (two being of the Quorum bearing Date the third Day of June One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy One Elizabeth Linnard Aged three Years and upwards was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief therein as to this Court shall seem mat

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

James Fisher for the Appellants.
