To the Right Honble St Robert Godschall Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worshipfull the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Hble Petion & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of December last past Thomas Lindsay was removed from the Parish of St Andrew Hubbard London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London alledging the sd Parish of St Andrew & undershaft London to be the place of his last legal Settlement.

That your Petrs Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said has Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Lordship and Wordships therefrom and humbly pray that your Lordsp and Worships will be pleased to save to them the benefit of this Appeal over the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for this City & the Libertys thereof And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Andrew Hubbard London may then appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment and determination of your Lordsp & Worships touching this Appeal

And Yr Petrs shall over pray Etc
