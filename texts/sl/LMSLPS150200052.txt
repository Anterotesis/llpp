To the Rt. Woppfull. her Maties Justices of the Peace now holding their Quartr.Sessions of the Peace at Guild Hall in and for the City of London.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens of the Parish of St. Olave in the Old Jewry London

Sheweth That the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poore of the Pish of all Hallows on London wall within this City or some of them have by virtue of an order. or pass Warrt. bearing date the 11th. day of August last past undr. the Hands and seales of Sr. Charles Duncomb Knt. & Lord Mayr. and Sr. Robt. Beachoroft Knt. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this City & Libties thereof Conveyed one Judith Jewry from the said Parish of all Hallows on London Wall into yor. Peticonrs. sd Parish of St. Olave in the old Jewry to be maintain'd Etc by reason where of yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves agreived,

Wherefore yor. Peticonrs. humbly Appeale to this woppfull Court for Redress, and Pray that yor. wopps will be pleased to appoint a time to hear & determine the same Etc

And yor. Peticoner shall ever Pray Etc
