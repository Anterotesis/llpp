To the Rt. Honble. St. Wm: Humfreys Knt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and others his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Same Citty Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Chappell Warden and Overseers of the poore of the precinct of the savor in the County of Midlx

Sheweth That by Warrt. under the hands' and Seales of the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Humfreys Knt Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and Sr. Wm. Withers Knt. Justices of the Peace for the said Citty (Quorum Enus) bearing date the twentieth day of December last John Starfe together with Ruth his wife and two Children (Vizt) Elizabeth thirteen and Susann about tenn years Old were passed from the parish of St. Giles Criplegate London to the said precinct of the Savor as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said John Starfe

Your Peticoners thinck themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the an Said two Justices doe humbly Appeale to this Court and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St. Giles Criplegate London may be ordered to Attend this Court to house and abide Your Honour & Worships & determinacon touching this Appeale

And your Peticoner shall pray Etc
