To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St Edmund the King London

Sheweth That one Dulcibella Peace Widdow by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Three and Twentieth day of March last past was removed from the parish of St Bridgett ats Bridges London to the said Parish of St. Edmund the King alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Dulcibella Peace

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove That the said Dulcibella Peace never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Edmonds the King whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Worpp therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Honr. and Worppl will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Bridgett ats Bridges may appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr. and Worppsl touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.

Appeale St Edmund the King agt St Brides

Appeale Allowed

Pd
