To the Rt: Worpll her Majties Justices of the Peace at their Quarter Session at Guild hall London.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwns. of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields.

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the, Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Coleman Street London or some of them have by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the Tenth day of August last under the hands and Seales of Sr. Charles Duncombe and Sr. Robert Beacher of two of her Majesties Justices of the peace Conveyed Ann Scott and her Child Benedictus from the said Parish of St. Stephen Coleman Street into Your Peters said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields to be maintained Etc By meanes where of Your Peter him they are wronget and Aggreved

Your Peter therefore humbly appeale to this Worpfull Court and pray that this Court will be pleased to hear and determine the same As to Right and Justices doth Appertaine

And Your Peter shall ever pray Etc

Jno Holylyn Jor Witchwood
