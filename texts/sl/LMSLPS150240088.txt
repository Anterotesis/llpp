The petition of Sarah Johnson.

To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Richd Hoare Knt. Ld Mayr. and the rest of the Honble: Bench

The humble Peticon of Sarah Johnson

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comitted to Newgate abt. the 22d. of August last on Suspicon of Stealing a pair of Stockings

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prayt yt she may be tryed this Sessions or else be discharged

And yor Petr. will as in duly bound evr Pray Etc
