To the Right Honourable James Townsend Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Somerset London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the sixteenth Day of January last Samuel Self and his two Children to wit Sarah aged ten Years and Charles aged seven Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhith London to the said Parish of Saint Mary Somerset London as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same And pray that this Honourable Court will please to hear and determine their said Appeal and make such Order therein as shall be just

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
