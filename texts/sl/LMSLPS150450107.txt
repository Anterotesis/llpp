3/1734 To the Right Honrble. Sr. William Billers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London The Honrble. Mr. Baron Thomson Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Sarah Hartle, Michael [---] Collins and John Collygun Children of Elizabeth Collygun were by Order of Pass Warrant dated the 15th. day of March last under the Hands and Seals of St. Wm. Billers Knt. and Sr. Richard Brocas Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London to the said Parish of St. Paul Shadwell as to the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves Agrrieved thereby and Appeal against the same and pray a day of hearing may be Appointed this present Quarter Sessions for the hearing and determining the same And that all Persons Concerned may be Ordered to attend to hear and Abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
