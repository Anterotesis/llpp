To the Rt. Honrble the Lord Mayor and the Rest of the Justices of Jayle Delivery of Newgate

The humble Petition of Peter dela Fountain

Sheweth That he Stands Indicted for Forgeing a Note of one John Baptista zannier

That he hath Severall Witnesses and Councell to Attend this Honourable Court in order to make a propper Defence

And most humbly prays the Court well please to appoint Saturday after noon for his Tryall

And as in duty Bound shall ever pray

De Lafontaine
