To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London, At their Genll. Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 16th. Day of June last past Mary Bambury Widow of Edwd. Bambury deced was Removed from the Parish of Saint Christopher le Stocks in the said City of London to the said Parish of St Stephen Walbrook as the last legal Place of her Settlement By which Your Petitioners Humbly conceive themselves aggrieved & Appeal to this Court against the said Pass Order.

Your Petrs. therefore Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Hear & determine the Matters touching this Appeal, and give them such Relief in the Premes as to Your Worships Shall Seem just & Meet,

And yr Petrs shall ever Pray Etc.

D. Webb Agent for the Appellts 9. July 1757.
