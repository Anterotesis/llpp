To the Right Honble Frederick Bull Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful Justices of the Peace in their General Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin at ludgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty sixth day of January last past Eleanor Warden Wife of John Warden who had deserted her was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Martin at Ludgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be saved to them and that the Hearing and Determination thereof may be adjourned over until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Wm Cooling Vestry Clk & [...] for Appellants.
