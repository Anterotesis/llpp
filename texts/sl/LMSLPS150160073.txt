To ye. Rt. Honble Sr: Owen Buckingham Lord Major: of ye Citty of London & to the rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justices Hall in ye old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of John Cousins of Prisonrs. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comittee ye 4th. of November last for Robbing on ye. Highway

Yor Petr. therefore prays to be tryed this Sessions or to be bailed as ye Tht of parliament directs

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
