To the Rt Honrble Sr Charles Duncombe Knt Lord Maier of the City of London and to the Rt Worptt the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Ann Blackfryers

Sheweth That one Jane Gregory the Wife of Sampson Gregory the Elder by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn)
