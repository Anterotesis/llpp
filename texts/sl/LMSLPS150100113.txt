To the Right Honble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Mayr. for the Citty of London and to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Court

The humble Peticon of Richard Backer a prsoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor, Peticonr. is confined in Newgate for high Treason wch he is noe wyes guilty of and he having Wittnesses Ready in Court hopes thereby to prove his inocency

Therefore yor. Peticonr. humbly prayes he may be Tryed at this Prsent Sessions & that his prayer may be entered accordingly as to yor. Lordpps shall seem meet Etc

And yor. Peticonr: shall pray Etc
