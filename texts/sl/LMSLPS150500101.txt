To the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of ye Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the sd. City of London.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of ye peace for the said City One being of the Quorum bearing date the Eleventh day of September In the year 1739. Mary Maynard Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Alhallows Barkin in the said City of London into the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said City of London as the place of their last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Humbly Appeal against the same and pray such Relief in the premisses as to this Honourable Court shall Seem Meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
