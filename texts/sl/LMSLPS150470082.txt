To the Right Honrble: Sr. John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London Sr. Wm. Thompson Knt. Recorder of the said City and the Worshipfull the Aldermen of the said City and Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex now assembled at the Sessions of Oyer and Terminer and Generall Goal Delivery of Newgate

The Humble Petition of James Mould

Sheweth That your Petr, some time in the Month of July last past was Comitted by Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. to his Majestys Goal of Newgate on Suspicion of haveing possoyned one Edward Stone a Tidewater belonging to the Port of London

That Your Petr. being wholly Innocent of the fact and no Indictment being found against him for the said (Supposed Offence) at the last Sessions was discharged upon his own Recognizance Appear at this present Sessions

That Your Petr. is the Mate a Ship and hath a Wife and three small Children to provide wch. must Inevitably Perish unless your Petr. can go to Sea and follow his Business of a Marriner to foreign Parts to maintaine and provide for himself and his Family

Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays (as no Indictment has been preferred agt him for the said Supposed Offence) for so long a tome as aforesaid that your Lordships and this Honble. Court will discharge your Petr. from his said Recognizance or further releive him as to Your Lordships and this Honble. Court shall seem meet

And Your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

James Mould
