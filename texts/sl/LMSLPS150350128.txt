3/2 To the Right Honoble: Sr: Peter Delme Knight Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appealle of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Michael Woodstreet London.

Sheweth That Whereas your Lordship and Sr: Francis Forbes Knt. and Alderman two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said Citty by Order Dated ye. 3d: of February 1723 upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Leonards Foster Lane London have adjudged the last Legall Settlement of John Deacon in the said Order Named to be in the parish of St: Michael Woodstreet as haveing abt: Twenty five Yeares Since served an Apprenticeship of Seaven yeares to and with One William Hall in that parish Joyner and there upon have Ordered the said John Deacon and Elizabeth his Wife to be removed to (and to be provided for by) the parish of Saint Michael Woodstreet

That your Petitioners Appretiend themselves Agreed by the said Order for that the said John Deacon hath no Legall Settlement in the said parrish of Saint Michael Woodstreet

And therefore Do Humbly Appeale to this Court Against the said Order, And pray the same may be Discharged Or your Petitioners Otherwise releived as this Honoble: Court shall think fitt.

And yr: Petitioners shall pray Etc
