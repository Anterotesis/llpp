London To the Right Honble. Robert Willmott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of the Right Worshipfull the Aldermen his Bretheren in their General Quarter Session of the Peace in & for the sd. City of London Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the ChurchWardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London on behalf of themselves & the Rest of the Parishioners of the sd Parish

Humbly Sheweth That Mary Magdalen Bumford Wife of John Bumford now of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London was (by a certain order or pass Warrts. under the hands & Seals of Thomas Moore & Nicholas Jeffreys Esqrs. Justices of the peace in & for the County of Middx. one being of the Quorum bearing Date ye. 24th. Day of March 1742) removed from the Parish of St. Giles in teh Fields in the County of Middx. to ye. sd Parish of Botolph without Aldgate in London as the place of her last Legal Settlement which sd. order or pass Warrt. afterwards to wit at the General Quarer session of the Peace held for the County of Middx. by Adjournmt. at Hicks hall in St. John Street in ye sd County on Thursday the 14th. Day of April 1743 was by the sd Court Ratifyed & Confirmed That the sd Mary Magdalen Bumford was at the time of such Removal & has ever since continued & now is a poor & Impotent Person & not able to Work to Relieve & maintain herself & has been for All the time aforesd & still is maintained [---] at the Expence of your Petrs. That the sd John Bumford is a Parishioner of the sd Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate & of sufficient Ability to Relieve & maintain such his sd Wife but hath Refused & still doth refuse so to do

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Appeal to this Honble. Court & Pray that the sd John Bumford may be ordered to pay so much Money to your Petrs. as they have necessarily expended in the maintainance of the sd Mary Magdalen Bumford his Wife & also their Costs of this Application & that such further order may be made by this Honble. Court upon the sd John Bumford for the future maintainance of his sd wife as this Honble. Court shall seem meet pursuant to the Statute in that Cave made & provided And that your Petrs. may have such [---] other Relief in the Premes as this Court shall think just

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Willm. Salt Sollr. for ye. Applts}
