To the Rt. Honble. Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of Maties. Justices of the peace for The said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seales of the Rt. Honble. Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. Richard Hopkins Knt. Alderman Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said City (Quro Unus) bearing date the Twentieth day of December Anno Dom. 1731 Timothy Hickes Mary his Wife & their Child Anne Aged Fifteen Years were Removed from the parish of St. Catherine Coleman London to the said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate As the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Timothy Hickes and his family

That your Peticoners Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices and Humbly Appeale against the same and therefore most Humbly pray to be releived in ye prmises As to this Honble. Court shall seem most meet

And Your Peticoners as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc.

Matt Vines Tho:Barchett Churchwardns.

Jno:Lacey Hon:Bridgen} Overseers
