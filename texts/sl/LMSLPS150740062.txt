To the Right Honble William Beckford Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices assigned to deliver his Majestys Gaol of Newgate on the Prisoners therein being

The Humble Petition of John Clarke

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the 2d. day of this Instant September was detained by a warrant under the hand and Seal of Sr. John Fielding Knt. in his Majestys Gaol of Newgate for feloniously taking three Bank Notes of the Value Â£250. The property of Theodore Goodwin privily from his person

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays That he may be Tried at this present Sessions for the offence with which he stands Charged as aforesaid or in default thereof he may be bailed or discharged for the same And your Petitioner as in duty cound shall ever pray etc

John Clarke
