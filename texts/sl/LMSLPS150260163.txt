To the Right Honble. St Willm Humfreys Knt & Bar Lord Mayor of the City of London and to ye Right Worppll the Rest of his Matys Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St Mary Woolnoth London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant Under ye hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace within this City (one bearing of ye Quorn) bearing Date ye First day of June last one Elizabeth Parratt singlewoman was sent & delivered by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Peter Cornhill London Unto she Said Parish of St Mary Woolnoth London Alleadging ye Said Parish to be the Place of her last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners therefore Conceive themselves agriev'd by the Said Warrt. and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honours & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly Pray Your Honour & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd Parish of St Peter Cornhill may Appear before you and hear & abide the determinacon of your Honour & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
