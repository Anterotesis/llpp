To the Right Honourable The Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petition & Appeal of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint John the Baptist in peterborough in the County of Nothampton

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of removal under the hands and seals of Brackley Kennett Esquire and Evan Pugh Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Nineteenth day of October last Sarah the Widow of Daniel Jamblin and her Daughter named Sarah aged thirteen Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Precinct of White Friars in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint John the Baptist in peterborough as the place of their last legal Settlement by which Your petitioners conceive themselves agrieved

Therefore humbly appeal and pray for such Relief in premisses as to this Honourable Court shall Seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
