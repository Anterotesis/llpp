The peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That William & [...] Sarah Taylor the children of one Daniell Taylor by virtue of a warrt. under the hanes & Rates of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the peace wth. in the City of London (one being of the Quox) ented the 28th. December 1700 were brought to yor. Petr. by the Churchwardens of the Pish of St. Buttolph wth. out Algate London.
