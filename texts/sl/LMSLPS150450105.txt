3/1734 To the Right Honoble Sr William Billers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull in Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Dunstan in in the East London

Sheweth That by an order under the hands and seales of two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said City One being of the Quorum bearing Date the Eighth Day of March last Mary Fox Widow of Jacob Fox Deced is removed from the [...] Princinct of White Fryers London to the said parish of St Dunstan in the East As the Place of [---] her last Legall settlement

Yor Petitioners therefore thinking them- selves aggrieved by the Judgemt of the Said two Justices appeale against the sd order and humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to Your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet

And Yor Petitionrs shall ever Pray and so forth
