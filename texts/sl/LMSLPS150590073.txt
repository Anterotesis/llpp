To the Rt. Honble Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & therest of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals the Rt. Honble Sr. Robert Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Two of is Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of January 1747 Sarah Johnson Widow of William Johnson deceased was removed from the Parish of St. Augustine London to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint some Day this present Session for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend

And your Petitionrs that ever pray

Harry agent for appell.
