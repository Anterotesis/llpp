To the Rt Honble Sr. William Humfreys Knt. & Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in the General quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn London.

Sheweth That Martha Rogers wife of William Rogers Junr with Mary her child by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the 25th day of August last past was removed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London unto the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn alleadging the said parish to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Mary Rogers with her Child never had any legal Settlement in the Said parish of St Andrew Holborn whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr. Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear there in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeal.

And your Petrs: Shall ever Pray Etc.
