To the Rt. Honble Sr William Lowen Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions assembled

29/8 The humble Petition & Appeal of the Chuchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Allhallows Barkin London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quom Unus) bearing date the Nine & Twentieth Day of August last past one Francis Letherwood with Ann his wife and two Children Elizabeth and Mary were passed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Parish of Allhallows Barkin alleadging the said Parish to be the place of their past legal Settlement

That your Petrs: conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr: and worpps therefrom and Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your [---] Honr: and Worpps touching this appeal

And they shall ever pray Etca.
