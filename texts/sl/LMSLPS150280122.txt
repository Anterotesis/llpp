To the Rt. Honble. Sr. James Bateman Barrt. Lord mayor of the City of London and others his Majties Justices of the peace of the said City in Quarter Sessions assembled.

29/5 The humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ church in the said City of London

Humbly Sheweth That Sarah Crook Widow was, since the holding of the last General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the said City, removed by an Order under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said City removed from the parish of St. Sepulcher in the said City of London to the said parish of Christ Church as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Peticoners humbly hope they can make it appear to this honble Court that ye said Sarah Crook is not legally settled in the said parish of Christ Church

Your Petitioner's therefore do most humbly appeal against the said Order and pray such Relief in the Prmisses as to this Honble. Court Shall Seem meet.

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
