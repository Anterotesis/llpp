To the Rt. Honble: Francis Child Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Wordfull the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the Said City & the Liblies thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of yor. Lordship & Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. & Alderman Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties thereof (Quax unus) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of February last past John Woodworth & Sarah his Wife & their two Children vizt. John aged one year & Mary aged ten years, were removed from the Parish of Alhallows Breadstreet London unto the Said Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street London, alledging the said Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or order Do appeale to your Lordship & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray that your Lordship will be pleased to hear them in the premisses, & order that the Churchwardens & Overfeers of the Poor of the said Parish of Alhallows Breadstreet London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Lordship & Worps touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
