To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Kathrine Creechurch London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the 4th. day of Octobr 1699 One Dorothy Mathewes an infant the Daughter of one Jno. Mathewes was brought to yor. Petrs. by the Churchwardens of Alhallowes Steyning London on the Oath of Mary Mathewes the mother That the said Dorothy was borne in yor. Petrs. Parish Whereas in truth the said John Mathews the Father never was an Inhabitant settled by Lawe in their said Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor. and Worpps to hear them in the prmisses And Order the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Pish of St. Alhallowes Steyning to Appeare here before you And to send the said Dorothy Matthewes the Infant to the said Parish of Alhallowes Steyning to be there kept and provided for untill They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due course of Lawe

And They shall Ever Pray Etc
