To the Right Honorable Samuel Plumbe Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London And the Worshipfull the Alderman his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City and the Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guildhall of the said City Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint saith under Saint Paul in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Robert Alsop and Evan Pugh Esquires Two at him Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) Dated the fifteenth day of June One thousand Seven hundred and Seventy nine Margaret Humphreys a Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Foster Lane in the City of London to the Parish of Saint Faith by Saint Paul in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Margaret Humbhreys by which your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal and pray such relief in the Premises as to this Honorable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Green away

Solicitor for the Appellants

No.8 Exeter Court Strand
