To the Right Honrble Sr. Charles Peers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Majtys Justices of the peace at the Generall Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Helen near Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace (one being of ye Quor bearing Date the Thirteenth day of December last one Elizabeth Luck wife of Willms Luck with Frances her daughter aged about two Years and [...] were sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Ethelberga London unto the Sd parish St Helen near Bishopsgate London Alleadging the Sd parish to be the place of her last Legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd the Sd Warrant and Judgement of the Said [...] Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour & Worships thereupon

Your Petitioners most humbly pray Your Honour & Worpl. will be pleased to hear them in the premisses And order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd Parish of St Ethelberga London may appear before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of your Honour & Worpl. touching this Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
