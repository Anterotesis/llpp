To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London in there General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Watkin Lewis Knt. and John Hart Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23d day of October last Ann Browning Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of Christ Church Newgate Street London unto the said Parish of Saint George Southwark as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner's Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore Appeal to your Worhsips thereupon and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Christ Church Newgate Street may appear before [...] on to and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner's shall ever pray

Vincent Solr. for the Appellts.
