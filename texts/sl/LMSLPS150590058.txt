To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under [...] and Seals of Sir Robert Ladbroke Knight and [...] Alsop Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London one whereof is of the Quorum bearing date the nineteenth day of January last Belinda by the name of Malinda Foster widow & Elizabeth her Child aged fifteen Years were removed & Conveyed from & out of the parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate to the parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

That the Appellants think themselves aggrieved as to the removal of the said Belinda and the [...] humbly appeal to this Honourable Court again [...] the Same and that your Worships will grant them such Relief in the Premisses as in your Wisdoms shall seem meet

And the Appellants will ever Pray Etc:

G: Talmash Agent the Appellant [...]
