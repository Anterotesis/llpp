To the Rt: Honrble: Sr. Gilbert Heathcote [...] Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwar- dens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Nicholas Cole abby London

Sheweth That Mary Lever wife of Richard Lever by Virtue of a warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the First day of December last past was removed from the Parish of St Augustin London unto the said Parish of St Nicholas Cole abby alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Lever.

That your Petitionr doubt not but to prove That the said Mary Lever never had any legall Settlemt. in the said Parish of St. Nicholas Cole abby whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Pray your Honr and Worpp with be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Augustin doe appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of you Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Peticonr. Shall Ever Pray Etc.
