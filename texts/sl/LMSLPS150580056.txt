To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor & other his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guild hall in & for sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the [---] Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by [...] Order under the hands & Seals of the Rt Honble Mr Bean Esqt Lord Mayor & Thomas Rawlinsen Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London Libertys thereof (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 3d. Day of Octr. Just Thomas Baldwin aged abt 12 Years & Sarah Baldwin aged about Ten Years Children of Jno: Baldwin by Mary his Wife both deceased were removed from the Parish of St Mary Magdalsn in Old Fish Street to the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx as the Place of their last Legal Settlement whereby your Petrs. Conceive themselves to be Agrieved & agt. the Sd Order do Appeal

Year Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the sd. Appeal may be Save to them & that the Hearing & Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace

Wm. Chimmoners agt. for the Rolls Libty} And Your Pets shall ever pray
