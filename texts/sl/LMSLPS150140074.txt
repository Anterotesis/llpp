To the Rd. Honoble. Sr. Samuell Dashwood Knt. Lord Major of ye City of London. And to the Rt. Worll. Her Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall att Quarter. Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Soverall Parishes of St. Cyles without Cripplegate St Buttolph on without Aldgate St. Andrewes Wardrobe St. Ratherine Coleman St. Andrewes Holborne St Sepulchers St. Brides Alhallowes on the Wall St Buttolph Aldersgate and St. Buttolph without in Bishoppsgate

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. and very much Over burthened with poore and not able to raise sufficient Sunnes of Money amongst themselves to releive them notwithstanding their Assessements are [...] than heretofore Yet the marge of their poor this farr above them said in Allegedments As the same upon full examination hath many times appeared to divers Worthy Alderman of this City appointed to hear the same And their poore daily arresting upon them their said parish lying some or them near to the suborbes And others of them in the Sububes of this City

Their humble suites therefore unto yor. Good Honor. & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant out other the Contribucous of other Parishes within this City for and towards the of releiving their poore As They have formerly had which and particularly hereunder menconed Or such further Allowance as to yor Honor. and Worpps shall seeme notice

And They shall Pray Etc

St Cyles without Cripplegate £90 St Buttolph without Aldgate £50. St Andrew Wardrobe £40. St Ratherine Coleman £12. St Andrews Holborne £7. St Sepulchers £44. St Brides £15. Alhallowes on the Wall £12. St Buttolph Aldersgate £15. St. Buttolph Bishoppsgate £60.
