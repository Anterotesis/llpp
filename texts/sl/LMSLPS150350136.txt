To the Rt Honrble Sr Peter Delme Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the same City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

4/4 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quorn Unus) bearing Date the Fourth day of April instant Jane Willson Widdow was removed from the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said Parish of Allhallows on the Wall as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs conceineing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom And pray that your honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc
