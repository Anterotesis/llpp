To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll: the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace for the said City of London in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

8 [---] The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Peter near Pauls Wharfe London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quox) bearing Date the Eighth Day of May last past one Mary Jones was removed from the Parish of St Mary Magdalen in Old Fish Street London unto the Said Parish of St Peter near Pauls Wharfe Alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last Legall Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St Mary Mary Magdalen Old fish Street may Appear before You and hear & abide the Determinacon of your Honour & Worps touching this Appeal.

And Yr petrs shall ever pray Etc.
