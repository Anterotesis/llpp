To the Right Honble Sr. James Bateman Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of his Matys Justices of ye peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Vedast als Foster London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quox bearing Date the Ninth day of November last One Anne Gudgeon Spr was sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said parish of St Vedast als Foster London Alledging the said Parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitiners therefore conceive themselves aggriev'd by the [---] said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour and Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray your Honour and Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of ye said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear and abid the Determinacon of Your Honour & Worps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
