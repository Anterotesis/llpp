To the Right Honble. Sr. William Humfreys Kt. Barrt. Vizt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and others his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the said Citty Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Brayles in the County of Warwick

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Sr. William Humfreys Kt. Barrt [...] Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and St. Thomas Abney Knt. two of his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the said Citty and Liberties thereof (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Ninth day of March last, one Jonathan Rimell with his fower Children (Vizt) Sarah Rimell aged about eight years' Richard Rimell aged between Six and Seven Years' Mary Rimell aged between four and five years' and Elizabeth Rimell aged about fifteen Months were passed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate within the an Libertie's of the said Citty of London to the said parish of Brayles in the County of Warwick as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices do humbly Appeale to this Court and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate and all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to Attend this Sessions, to heare and abide the Judgment ye determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Your Peticonrs as in duty bound shall pray Etc
