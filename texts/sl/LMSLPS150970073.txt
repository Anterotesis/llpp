To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said City

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by an order under the hands and Seals of T. Wright and R. Clark Esquire two of his Majesty's in for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Fifth day of January One thousand Seven hundred and eighty six James Khaoa aged eleven Years and upwards the Son of John Khaoa Removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend
