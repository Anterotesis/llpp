Basieshaw agt St Giles Cripplegate appeale

To the Rt. Worpll. Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in ye Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens Overseers of this Poore of the parish of St Michael Basieshaw London

Sheweth That on Lydia Cooks Wido with her two Children Mary and Edward by Virtue of a warrant under the hands & seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quor) beareing date the 20th day of Octobr. last past was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Parish of St Michael Basieshaw alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall settlement of the said Lidia Cooke

That your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Lydia Cooke never had my legall settlement in the and Parish of St Michael Basieshaw whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr. therefore humbly pray your Honr & Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc.
