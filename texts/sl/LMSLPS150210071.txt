Appeate Pish of St. Andrew Holborne agt St Martins Ludgate

To the St. Honble. Sr. Samuel Garrard Barnett Lord. Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll. her Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the peace of the parish of St. Andrew Holborne above ye Barrs in the County of Middx

Sheweth That one Ann Shaw wife of Richard Shaw the Elder with her five Children by virtue of a Warrant under the Handles and Seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Two and Twentieth day of March last past was removed from the Parish of st. Martins Ludgate London to the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx alter adjoining the said Parish told the place of the last legall Settlement of the [...] Ann Shaw

That Your Petitioners doubt but is prove that the said Ann Shaw never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your worpps their from

Your Pettitoners therefore humbly pray Your Honr. and Worppl will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwarden and [...] Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Martin Ludgate may appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr. and Worpps. touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
