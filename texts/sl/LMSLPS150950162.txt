To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the City of Westminster in the Country of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Peckham Esquire Mayor and John Hart Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 14th. day of July last whereby Johannahthe Widow of George Jones with her Child namely Alexander Solea aged five years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeal may come on at this present General Session.

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc.
