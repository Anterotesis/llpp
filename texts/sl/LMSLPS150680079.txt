To the Right Honoble Marshe Dickinson Esqr and the rest of the Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the City of London at Justice Hall in the Old Baily London

The Humble Petition of Joseph Wood

Sheweth That your Petr. being Charged on the Oaths of Nicholas Kemp and Thomas Donne [...] Your Petr. stands Comitted to Newgate for High Treason expressed in the Warrant of Committment

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays to be Fryed Bailed or discharged this present Session pursuant to the Statute in that behalf

And Your Petr: as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Jos Wood
