To the Right Honourable Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City, assembled in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George Bloomsbury in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Thomas Rawlinson and William Alexander Esqr. two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 2d. day of June last Thomas Atkinson aged four years son of John Atkinson by Sarah his wife both Deceased was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Nicholas Olave in the said City to the said parish of St. George Bloomsbury as the place of his last Legall Settlements.

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace, appeal to this Honourable Court against the same, and Humble may your Worships to appoint a day for the hearing & Determining of the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Jonan: Hodgson Tho Davies} Churchwardens

C Danl Read Ambrose Denn [...] } Overseers of the Poor
