To the Right Worshipful the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of Saint Catherines near the Tower in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of the Right [...] Worshipful Thomas Hallifax Lord Mayor and William Lee Esquire one of the Aldermen of the City of London bearing date the twenty sixth day of November last Catherine Judd formerly Connolly the Wife of David Judd who is a [...] of the Kingdom of Sardinia who hath deserted her and her two Children Francis aged two years and upwards and Mary Ann aged thirteen Months and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in London aforesaid to the said Precinct of Saint Catherines as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Catherine Judd formerly Connolly

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Order of Removal do humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
