Appeale St. Mary Stanning agt St. Peter Cheap

To the Rt. Honrble Sr. Samuell Gerrard Barnell Lord Maior of the Citty of London and to the Rt. Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Apprentice of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Peace of this Parish of St. Mary Stanning London

Sheweth That one Eliza. Skeafe wido. and Millicent her Daughter by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & sales of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Tenth Day of June last Past was removed from the Parish of St. Peter Cheap London unto the sid Parish of St. Mary Stainings London alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Skeafe

That your Petitioners doubt not nut to poore That the Said Elizabeth Skase never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Mary Staining whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitoners therefore humbly Pray your Honr. and worppl will be pleased to hears them in the Prmisses and Order the Churchwardens and Ovrseers of the Poore of the Sd Parish of St. Peter Cheape to appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of Your Honr. and Worppl touching this Appeale.

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.
