To the Rt. Honble. William Benn Edqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the Said City at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Pricinet of St Ketherine was the Tower of London in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order dated the Second day of December last under the Hands and Seals of the said St honble. William Benn Esqr Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Edward Devies Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said City, (One bring of the Quorum) Thomas Parker and Mary his wife were removed from the Parish of Saint James in Dukes place in the said City of London to the said Precient of Saint Katherine as the place of their last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Parker and Mary his wife

That Your Peticoners was Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Order of the Said two Justices and Appeal against the Same

That your Peticoners have given due Notice of the Appeal to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St James in Dukes place

Therefore most humbly pray this Court that they way be heard in the premisses us to their Said Appeal

And Yr. Petrs Etc

Saml. Cowper Attorney for the Appellants Well Close Square

John Marchant John Jones} Overseers of the Poor
