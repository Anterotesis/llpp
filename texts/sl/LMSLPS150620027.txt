To the Rt: Honble the Lord Mayor and the Rest of the Honble Judges of Oyer & Terminer & General Goal Delivery of Newgate Holden at Justices Hall in the Old Bailey in and for the City of London

The humble Petition of John Carr

Sheweth That your Petr. was on the 21st. day of January last by Warrant under the Hand and Seal of Mr. Alderman Asgyle Comitted to Woodstreet Compter for Uttering as true; a false and Counterfeit Letter of Attorney & thereby Endeavouring to Receive Prize Money tho. believed he knew the Same to be false and Counterfeit

That your Petr. in Order to Manifest his Innocence has Cause Several Persons of Reputation & Charles to Attend on his behalf at this Sessions

Your. Petrs. therefore most humbly Praus your Lordship that the may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged this Sessions Pursuant to the Statute

And Your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

John Carr
