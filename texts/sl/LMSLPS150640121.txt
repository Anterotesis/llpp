To the Right Honourable Crisp Gascoyne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hornsey in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Crisp Gascoyn Esqr. Lord Mayor and John Porter Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one being of the Quorum)bearing date the fourth day of July Instant William Walker and Katherine his wife were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the Liberty of the said City to the said Parish of Horney as the place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the Sd. Pass Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this Present Sessions to hear and Determine the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray

Jno. Let Joseph Petty} Church Wardens

Hay Coff John Willson} Overseers of the Poor
