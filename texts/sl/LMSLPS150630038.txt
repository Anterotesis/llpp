To the Right Honble. Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majestys Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate in their Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Prayer of Aaron Cardose

Sheweth That your Petitioner was lately apprehended and Committed to the County Gaol for Surry on Suspicion of Receiving Goods in the City of London knowing them to be Stolen, and your Petitioner has since been Removed by Habeas Corpus therefrom to his Majestys Goal of Newgate where he now remains Confined

That your Petitioner well knowing his Innocence in the premisses Most humbly Prays that in pursuance of the Habeas [...] Corpus Act He may be either Bailed Tryed or Discharged at this present Session

And your Petitioner as in Duty bound will pray

8th. April 1752 the [x] mark of Aaren Cardego
