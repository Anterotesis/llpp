Appeale St. Augustin agt. St Giles without Cripplegate

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samll Garrard Barntt Lord Maior of the City of London and to ye Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St Augustin London

Sheweth That one Mary Killgore Singlewoman by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the 15th day of Aprill last past was sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Augustin alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall settlement of the said Mary Killgore.

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove That the said Mary Killgore never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Augustin whereby they conceive themselves agreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Honr. & Worppl therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Honr. and Worppl will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate doe Appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr. and Worppl touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.
