To the Right Honble. Sr. Saml. Fludyer Bart. the Lord Mayor Sr. Willm. Moreton Recorder and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof at their General Session at Guild hall within and for the said City Assembled on Monday the twenty fourth Day of May in the year of our Lord 1762

The Humble Petition and Appeal of ye Churchwardens & Oversrs. of ye poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel within the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrt. or Order of Removal dated on the third Day in ye. Month of April last under the hands & Seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace of the said City and Libertys Mary Davis Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London within the said City or Libertys to the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel as ye. place of her last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. Apprehend themselves to be agrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal and therefore do Appeal to this Session against ye same

Your Petrs. therefore do Appeal to this Session against ye above Order of Removal or pass Warrant of the said two Justices And humbly pray that this their Appeal against ye same may be Adjourned Until the next General Quarter Session to be held in & for the said City & your Petrs. then Relieved

May ye. 24th. 1762 Billington

And Your Petrs. will ever pray Etc. Sollr. for Appellts.
