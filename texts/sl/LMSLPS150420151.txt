To the Rt. Honoble. Francis Child Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rt. Worpll. the rest of Said Maties. Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Peter be Poor London

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 2d November last under the hands of Seals of the Rt Honble Francis Child Esqr Lord Mayor of the Citty of London of Sr. Edwards Bellamy Knt. of Alderman of the said City Two of his, Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor Unus) One Mary Howard Singlewoman was passed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London To the said parish of St. Peter be Poor as the place of her last legal Settlement

That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the said parish of St. Peter le Poor Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order and therefore Appeal from the same to this Hononble. Court and humbly pray to be relived against the said Order
