To the Honoble. Sr. Thomas Lane Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Georges in Southwarke in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace wth. in this City (one being of the Quor) Dated the third of May last one Mary Biggmore was sent to yor: Petitionr. to be pvided for on the oath of the said Mary that shee was last legally settled in the said Parish Whereas in truth the said Mary Biggmore was servant to one Christmas about seaven yeares past in the Parish of St Thomas Appostles who then removed into the Mint in the said Parish of St. Georges where hee continued about a yeare and then returned into the said Parish of St. Thomas Appostles again wth. whome the said Mary hath Dwelt and continued as a Servant for Diver's yeares since

Yor. Petr. humble request Therefore unto yor. Honors. and Worpps. is to heare yor. Petr. in the prmisses' And to send the said Mary to be provided for by the Churchwardens of St. Thomas Appostles where shee was last legally settled As a Servant According to Law

And They shall Pray Etc
