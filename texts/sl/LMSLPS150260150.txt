To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London at their Generall Quarter Sessions held for the said City at the Guildhall

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Aldermary in the City of London.

Sheweth That by an order or precept made the 17th. day of March last by Sr. Wm. Humphrys Barronet Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr. Samuel Garrard Knight two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (quorum unus) directed to the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Margarett Moses and Saint Mary Aldermary They the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Saint Margarett Moses were required to take passe and carry away John Dobson from and out of the said Parish of Saint Margarett Moses unto the said parish of Saint Mary Aldermary and there deliver him unto your Petr. or to some or one of them who and there by likewise required the said John Dobson to receive and provide for as for other poor of your Petrs. Parish

That for as much as the said John Dobson neither had nor hath any Legall Settlement in Your Petrs. said Parish.

Your Petrs. humbly Appeal to your [...] Woships against the said order or precept and from and against the provideing for the said John Dobson and humbly pray the said order or precept may be discharged as to your Peticoners and their said parish and for such Costs as your Worships shall think fit.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
