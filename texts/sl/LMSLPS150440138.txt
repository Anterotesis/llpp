To the right Honourable Sir William Billars Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions at Guildhall Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of All Hallows Staining in the said City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the right Honourable Sir William Billers Knight the present Lord Mayor of this City and Sir Francis Child Knight two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourteenth day of November last Mary Stacey wife of John Stacey was Removed from the parish of Saint Andrews Holborne in the said City of London to the said Parish of All Hallows Staining as the Place of her last legall Settlement where by your Petitioners Conceive themselves to Bellgreived.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Judgment of he said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint Some day this present Sessions to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeal.

And your petitioners Shall ever pray and So forth
