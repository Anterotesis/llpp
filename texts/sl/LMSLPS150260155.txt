To the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Humfreys Knt. and Barrt. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Hart Street London.

Sheweth, That by virtue of an Ordr. or Patt Warrt. bearing date the sixth day of this instant April under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libties thereof (Quorn unns) one frances Robt widow of John Robt her late husband deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Katherine Creedchurch in the sd. City to yor. Peticonrs. sd Parish of St. Olave Hart Street as the place of her last legal Settlemt:

That yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by said Ordr. or Pass Warrt. and Judgmt. of the said Two Justices, and humbly appeale agnt. the same to this Court,

Wherefore yor. Peticonrs. humbly Pray to have such releif in the prmisses, as to yor. Wopps shall appear just and reasonable,

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
