To the Right Honourable Sir Richard Glyn Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London in their General Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of your Lorship and Francis Cokayne Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty Second day of December one thousand seven hundred and fifty Eight Elizabeth Burnell widow of Thomas Burnell deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary in Aldermanbury London unto the said Parish of Saint Luke alledging the said Parish of Saint Luke to be her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and therefore humbly appeal against the same

And your Petitiners shall ever pray Etc

James Barwick Soll. for the Appellants.
