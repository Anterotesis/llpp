To ye. Rgt: Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye: Peace for ye: City of London

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye: poor of ye parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye. County of Middx:

Sheweth That by a Warrt: or Order under ye. hands & Seals of Two of his Maties. Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd. City of London bearinge date ye: 15th. day of July 1720: [...] Mathew Langton wth. Elizabeth his Wife & Two children (Vizt.) Matthew aged about Three Yeares & John aged about fourteen Months were removed from & out of ye. parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate: London, to ye. Sd parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch therein Settingeforth that ye. Sd Mathew Langton dwelh in a Lease hold Estate of a Eleven pounds P: amd. rent that he had for a Longe Terme of Yeares in right of his wife in ye. sd parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch; being ye. place of his Last Legall Settlemt.

Your Petriconers findinge themselves agreved within the Judgmt, of ye Sd Two Justices of ye. peace humbly appeales to this Honable: Court against ye. sd. Warrant and prayes that ye churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. sd. parish of St. Giles wthout: Cripplegate may attend this Court to heare & abide Yr. Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching ye. sd. appeale they heaveing had due Notice of this Appeale

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc

John Cann Jeremiah Wright Churchwardens}

Benja: Holloway John Jewks } Overseers of ye. poore
