To the Rt. Honble: Sr. John Houblon Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to ye Rt. Worll: his Maties: Justices of the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Olaves Silver Streete London

Sheweth That one Zachary Read & his wife by virtue of a warrt. under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the peace (one being of the Quor) were by the Churchwardens of St. Albans Wood Streete brought to yor. petr. to be pvided for upon his oath That for Twenty & one yeares together hee had dwelt in their said parish of St. Claves wch. They acknowledge to be true But that about twoe yeares last past The said Zachary went out of the said Parish of St. Olaves And tooke a dwelling & prmisses in the Parish of St. Albans Wod Streete att the went of Â£15. yearly went wch. They can prove whereby the last legall settlemt. of the said Zachary Read was in the said Parish of St. Albans Wood Streete
