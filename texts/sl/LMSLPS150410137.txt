AD 1729 30 598

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Richd: Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Justices assembled at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The hmble Peticon of Edward Cahill

Sheweth That Your Peticoner was about five Weeks agoe taken upon a Warrant and hath been brought before Your Lordship and this Honble. Court, but no prosecutor hath appear'd against him to prove the fact.

Therefore humbly Prays Your Lordship & this Honble Court either to Discharge him or admit him to Baile

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc.
