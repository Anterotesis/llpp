To Sr. James Bateman Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Honble Bench

The humble peticon of William Hodges now a prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petr was Comitted to the Said prison upon Oath for the Fellonious takeing a peice of Ingott Silver of the Value of three Pounds

He therefore most humbly prays yor honor would admitted to be tryed for the said fact this present Sessions or for want of prosecucon he may be accquitted therefrom Dated this 1st day of May 1717
