To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor & others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their general Quarter Sessions of the Place for the said City at Guildhall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mildred the Virgin in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by an Order of Settlement or Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Samuel Plumbe Esquire Lord Mayor of the said City and Evan Pugh Esqr. one of the Alderman of the said City and two of the Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said City bearing date the 3d. Day of March 1779 Honnor Taylor the Widows of John Taylor deceased was remove from the Parish of Allhallows London Wall London to the said Parish of Saint Mildred as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Crowne Ther nicols Inn Sall. for the Appllts. Therefore they humbly appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order of the said there Justices and pray this Court to make such Order therein as to them shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray
