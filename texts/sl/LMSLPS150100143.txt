To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Savious Southwarke in the County of Surry

Sheweth That one Anne Warner Spr daughter of John Warner deced by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) was brought by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Sepulchers unto yor. Petrs. to be provided for on the oath of Elizabeth Marifeild That her last legall Settlement was in the Parish of St. Saviours where shee was borne and where her father was a House keeper and paid Parish dutyes Whereas in truth the said Anne Warner lived as a hired Servant with one Edward Sweetapple in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers for twoe yeares & an halfe untill about 6 monethes past and had her last legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Sepulchers

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Honor & Worpps. to order the Churchwardens of St. Sepulchers to appear before you And to hear them all in the prmisses And to quash the said Warrant And send the said Anne Warner backe to the said Parish of Sepulchers whereas a hired Servant shee was setled by the Lawe

And they shall Pray Etc.
