To the Right Honble Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of Gaol Delivery of Newgate for the City of London in Session assembled.

The Humble Petition of John Bell a Prisoner in His Majesty's Gaol of Newgate.

Sheweth That Your Petitoner stands committed by Henry Fielding Esqr. one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex on Suspicion of High Treason expressed in the Warrant

Your Petitoner most humbly prays this Honble Court that he may be tried bailed or discharged this Session according to the Directions of the Statute in such Case made and provided

And Your Petitoner shall for ever pray
