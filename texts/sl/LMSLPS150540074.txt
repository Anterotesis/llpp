To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden for the said City and Liberties in the Guild Hall thereof assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass under the Hand and Seal of the Right Honourable Robert Willimot Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and Liberties thereof and one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties bearing Date the twenty fourth Day of May last past Sarah Catheral who as in the said Pass is alledged was apprehended in the City of London wandring abroad and begging as a Rogue and Vagabond was conveyed to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners are aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeale to this Court against the said Pass and pray that they may have Relief in the Premisses

And your Petitioners will ever pray and so forth

J: Godfrey Agt: for the Appellants
