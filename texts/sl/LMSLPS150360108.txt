To the Rt Honoble Sr George Merttins Knt. Ld Mayor of the City of London And the Rt. Worll the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margarets Lothbury London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant Under the Hands & Seals of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace wth. in this City (Quor Unus) Braring date the 9th. Day of Aprill Anno Dui 1725 Anne Oulton Widow was passed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Botolph Bishoppsgate London to yor. Peter said Parish of St. Margaret Lothburys as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That yor. Petrs Conceiving themselves aggreived by by the Warrant & Judgment of the said Two Justices Humbly Appeal to Yor. Honors & Worpps therein Praying Such Releif in the prmisses as in yor. Worpps Judgments Shall Seem meet

And they Shall Pray Etc
