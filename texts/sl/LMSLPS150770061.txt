To the Right Honourable George Nelson Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Fellows Justices in and for the City of London and County of middlesex of Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Petition of John Mahone now a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That your Petitioners was on the first day of march last Committed to Westminster Gatehouse by Thomas Miller Esquire by Virtue of a Warrant upon the oath of Bridget Cullum Widow on suspicion of Feloniously making forging Counterfeiting and publishing a Certain Instrument purporting to be the last will and Testament of John Cullum a Marriner deceased late belonging to his Majestys Ship the Panther with an Intent to Cheat and detrand his majesty knewing the same. to be forged and Counterfeiting Contrary to the Statute Etc

Therefore your Petitioner most humbly Prays your Lordship and the rest of this Honourable Court taking the Premises into your Consideration That your Petitioner might may be this present Session Tried Bailed or discharged for the same And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall Ever pray Etc

John Mahon
