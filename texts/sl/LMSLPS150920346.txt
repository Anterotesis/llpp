To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in the in General Session of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hand [...] and Seals of Sir Walkin Lewer Knight Mayor and Hen Kitchen Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of April last, John William, the Illegitimate Son of Ann Kempton who hath deceased him aged Two years was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London to the said parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex of the place of the last legal Settlement of the said John William By which your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order and Adjudication of the said Two Justices and pray your Worships will Appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc.

Mr. Tears Sellr. for Appellts.
