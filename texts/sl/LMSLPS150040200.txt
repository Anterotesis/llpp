April Sessions 1693

To the Right Honoble. & Right Worll. their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx.

The humble Petion & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers; of the poore of the parish of Alhallowes' Lumberd Streete London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of two of their Maties. Justices of the peace wth. in the City one Anne Cheyney was sent to yor. Petr. to be provided for upon her Oath That shee was brought upp & mainteyned in & by the said Parish from her infanny and was there last legally settled & never any where else where in tenth in the yeare. 1693 the said Anne Cheney was putt forth an Appntice by the then Churchwardens of the said Parish to one Francis Chapman of the Parish of St Gyles wth. out Cripplegate London & Mary his wife for the terme of nine yeares wth. whorne thee served accordingly And hath ever since the expiracon of the Indentured dwelt in the said Parish of St. Gyles Cripplegate being her legall settlement.

Their humble suite unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is to hear yor. Petr. in the Prmissed And to Quash the said Order made by the said two Justices And to send the said Anne Cheney to the said Pish of St. Gyles Cripplegate There to be provided for according to Lawe.

And They shall Pray Etc.
