To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lrod Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Pish of St. Buttolph wth.out Algate London.

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are very much overburthened with Poore And not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongest themselves' to releive them Notwithstanding their Assessements are greater their heretofore Yett the charge of them Poore is far above their said Assessemt. As the same upon full examination hath many times Appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to heare the same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them the said Pish lyeing in the Suburbs of the City.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. good Honors. & Worpps. is That you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the contribution of Â£50. from other Pishes within this City for and towardes the releiveing their Poore As they have formerly had by reason of their greate charge and numbers of Poore as aforesaid Or such further allowance As to yor. Honors. & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And yor. Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc.
