To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Thomas Chitty Lord Mayor of the City of London and Francis Cokeyne Esqr. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof bearing Date the Twenty first day of March One Thousand seven Hundred and Sixty one Mary B [...] man are removed from the Parish of Saint Ann in [...] Fryers London to the Parish of Saint Pauls at Shadwell

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly Appeal against the same Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that your Wordships will be pleased, to appoint a day of this present Sessions that all Parties may attend to hear and ab [...] the Judgment and, Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno Turpin Wm Green} Church Wardens

Osd Osmondson Fr: Clarkson Jno King Thos. Mathews Marton} Overseers
