To the Right Honourable Robert Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael at Queenhith London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant bearing date the 7th. day of April 1752 and being under the hands and Seals of The Right Honourable Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. Late Lord Mayor of the City of London, and the Right Honourable Robert Alsop Esqr. the present Lord Mayor of the said City then being two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City (one whereof then being of the Quorum) Susanna Shelley aged about thirteen years was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Michael in Cornhill London into the said parish of St. Michael at Queenhith

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and prays such Relief in the Premisses as your Worships shall seem meet

Fras. Salt Attorney for Appellts.
