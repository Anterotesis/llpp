To the Right Honble Sr Willm Lowen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpl the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter sessions of ye peace for ye said City assembled.

16/9 The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Watfeild in the County of Suffolk.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justies of ye peace within the City of London (Quor unus). bearing Date the 16 day of September last Charles Bless was removed from the parish of St. Bartholomew by exchange London unto the Parish of Watsfeild in the County of Suffolk alledging the said Pish to be ye place of his last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do must humbly appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therfore most humbly pray your Honour and Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Bartholomew by Exchange London may appear before you and hear abide the Determinacon of your Honr & Worps touching the sd Appeal

And he Shall ever pray Etc.
