To the Right Honorable Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices in their general quarter session of the peace for the said City assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Margaret Lothbury London

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London aforesaid (one where of being of the quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of August last Elizabeth Archer Wife of Charles Archer who is gone from her and their Child John aged about seven Weeks were removed and conveyed from & out of the parish of St. Bridget London into the said parish of St. Margaret Lothbury

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said order or pass Warrant do humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the hearing and determining the said Appeal may be adjoined until the next general quarter session of the peace to be held in and for the said City And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Bridget London and all other persons concerned may attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

F Salt Agent.
