To The Rt: Honble Sr. Francis Forbes Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London the Rt Worshipfull Sr Wm: Thomson Knt Recorder of the same and the Rest of the Maties Justices of the peace for the said City in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Taylor of the Tower of London

Sheweth That by a Judgment of Outer under the hands of Sr: Francis Forbes Knt and Ld: Mayr. of the City of London and Sr: Gerard Conyers Knt: two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of April last your Petitioner is Adjudged the Reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child born in the Parish of St Leonard Eastcheap me the sd City on or about the 4th: of April last begotten on the body of Eliz: Cox Singlewoman That your Petitioner think, himself agrieved by the said Judgment and Order Humbly Appeals against the same

And Prays this Court would be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Leonard Eastcheap and all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment of the same touching the said Appeal

And your Petitionr shall ever pray

John Taylor
