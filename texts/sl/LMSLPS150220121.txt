To the Rt Honble Sr Gilbert Heathcote Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the general Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Templer Spr by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quoru) bearing Date the fifth Day of October instant was removed from the parish of St John Zachary London unto the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legall Settlement

That your petitioners [...] not but to prove that the said Elizabeth Templer never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices And do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St John Zachary may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Cripplegate agt St John Zachary} Appeale
