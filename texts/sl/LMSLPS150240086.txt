To ye. Rt. Honble St. Richard Hoare Knt Ld Majr of ye. City of London & to ye. rest of ye. Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Sara Ridgway als Windar Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr is Comittee to Newgate Charge for feloniously marrying a second Husband her first being alive

Yor. Petr Humbly prayes to be tryed this sessions or to be admitted to Bails or otherwayes to be discharged as this Honble Court shall think fit

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
