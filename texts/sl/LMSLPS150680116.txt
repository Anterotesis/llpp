To the Right Honble: Marshe Dickinson, Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, and to the Worshipful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye sd. City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Alphage, in ye. City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of Slingsby Bethell Esqr, and Francis Gosling, Esqr, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London and Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Eighteenth Day of October 1756, Sarah Palmer, Widow, was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London to the Parish of Saint Alphage, London, alledging ye. sd. Parish of St. Alphage, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge, Atty for the Appellants.} That yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by ye sd. Warrt. or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray, that ye. Benefit of this Appeal be Saved unto ym. & ye. hearing & Determining thereof be Adjourned until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace, to be holden for ye. sd. City, & yt. ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, may then appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor. Ldp & Worships touching this Appeal. And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc.
