To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

19/2 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Leonard Eastheap London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace within this City (Quor unus) bearing Date the Nineteenth day of February last One Anne Smith Single woman was Sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto the sd Parish of St Leonard Eastcheap London Alledging the said parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves aggriev'd by the said Warrant and Judgmt of the said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour and Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses And Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Precinct of White Fryers London may appear before You And hear and Abide the Determinacon of Your Honr & worps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
