To the Right Honrble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of St Andrew Wardrobe London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majtys Justices of the peace within this City (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twenty Ninth day of November last Thomasine Amos wife of Benjamin Amos was sent by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrew Holborn London unto the sd pish of St Andrew Wardrobe Alleadging the Sd parish to be the place of her last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by the sd Warrant & Judgement of the Sd two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worps. therefrom.

Your Petitiners most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses And O0rder that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Sd pish of St Andrew Holborn London may appear before you and hear & abide the Determinacon of your Honour and Worps touching this Appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc.
