To the Rt. Honble Sr. Samuell Garrare Bart. Lord Majr. of the City of London & to the Rest of the Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Petitcon of Nichlas Millechap A Prisonr in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Committee ye: 15th of August last Charged for Felony That Yor. Petr: is wholy innocent of the Crime Alleadyed agt. him

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prayes that this Honble Court would be pleased to Try yor. Petr. this Sessions or that he may be otherwages discharge from his Jurprisonmt.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
