London To the Right Honourable Robert Willimot Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys Assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberty's thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 26th day of August last, Mary Cowley Wife of William Cowley who is run away from her, and their Child John Aged one Year were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London Adjudging the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Cowley and her said Child to be in the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street by which said Order of the said two Justices your Petitioners apprehend themselves Aggreved. And therefore Appeal therefrom

Yor. Petrs: therefore humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court to be releived in the Premisses as to Your Lordship shall seem meet and Your Petitioners will over pray

Henry Kettle John Swift} Church Wardens
