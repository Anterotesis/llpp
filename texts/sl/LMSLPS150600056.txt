To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Brethren the Justices of the Peace in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of White Fryers London

Sheweth. That by Vertue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Sr. William Calvert Knt. Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull Francis Cokayne Esqr one of the Aldermen of the sd. City Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the sd. City and Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 17th. Day of May 1749 Susanna Murray Widow was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London to the said Precinct of White Fryers as the Place of [---] Her legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves Agrieved by the said Order Therefore

Humbly Appeal to this Court and pray that the said Order may be Quashed and that they may have such Other Relief as to this Court shall seem meet
