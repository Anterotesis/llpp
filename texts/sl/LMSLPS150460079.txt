To the Right Honble Sir Edward Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble. Sr. Edward Bellamy Knt. & Robert Alsop Esq two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London and the Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of January last John Wilkison was removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London to the said parish of St. Ann by the Name of the parish of St. Ann Soho in the Liberty of Westminster & County of Middx as the place of his last legal Settlement when by your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived.

Therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the adjudgement of the said two Justices & pray your Worships will be pleased to hear & determine the said appeal According to Law & Justice

And shall ever pray Etc.

14th. April 1735.

Jno. Hodson Richd Sutton} Churchwardens of the parish of St. Ann Westmr.
