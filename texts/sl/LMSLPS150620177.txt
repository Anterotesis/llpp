To ye. Rt. Honble. Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London and to the Worpful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye. said City & ye. Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye. Parish of Christchurch, in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of your Lordship, & Robt. Alsop, Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace, for the City of London & ye. Liberties thereof (One being of ye Quorum) bearing Date ye. Tenth Day of November 1750, Ann Willoughby, aged about Sixteen Years, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate, London, unto ye. Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging ye. said Parish of Christchurch, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Attorney for the Appellants } That your Peticoners conceiving themselves agrieved, by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to your Lordship & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & that ye.Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate, London, may appear before you, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
