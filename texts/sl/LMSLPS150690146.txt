To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Session at Guild Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlsex

Sheweth That by virtue of A Pass Warrant or Order under the hand and Seal of Wm Stephenson Esqr one of his Majesty's Justices for the City of London bearing date the first day of December last Rosamund Dowling A Rouge and Vagabond was Conveyed from the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in the City of London to the Said Parish of St. James Westminster whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to Your Worships Against the Pass Warrant or Order of the Said Justice and Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be Saved to your Petitioner untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for this City and that the hearing of the Said Appeal may by your Worships be Adjourned until the Said next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of St Lawrence Jewry and all Persons Concerned do Attend the Court of the Said next General Quarter Session to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of the same Court Touching the Said Appeal

And your Petitioner Shall Pray Etc.

Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St James Westminster{ John Taber James Palmer Jno Gregory
