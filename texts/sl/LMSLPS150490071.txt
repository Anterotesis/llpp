To the Right Worshipfull Sr John Barnard Knt Lord mayor and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bethnal Green in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the third day of last past under the hands and Seals of Sr. John Barnard Knt Lord Mayor and John Salter two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum Morgan Shea Martha his wife and their two Children namely) Mary Aged twenty seven Years and David twenty four years old [...] removed from the [...] without Bishopsgate in the City of London to the [...] of Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

By which said Warrant or Order your Petitioners conceive themselves Agrieved and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief as to your Worships shall seem most meet

And your Petitioners will Ever Pray

Thos. Walker Churchwardn and Overseer of the Poor of the said Hamlet
