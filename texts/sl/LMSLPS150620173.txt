To the Rt. Honble Francis Cokayne Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the sd City in their general Quarter Session Assembled

The humble petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Francis Cokayne & Kingsby Bethell Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City aforesd. (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of April las Elizabeth Ashton Widow of James Ashton deced and her four Children Jane aged seventeen Years Ann Nine Years Susanna seven years & George William aged four Years were removed & Conveyed from & out of the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London into the said parish of Christ Church

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said order or Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court agt. the same & pray that the bearing and determining this Appeal may be adjorned untill the next general quarter session of the peace to be holden for this City and that the Court will appoint a day in the said next Session In the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London & all other persons concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

W. Salt Agent
