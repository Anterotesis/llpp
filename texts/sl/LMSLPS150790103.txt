To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof At the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant as Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Thomas Harley Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Charles Asgill Baronet two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties there of bearing Date the third day of May one thousand Seven hundred and Sixty Eight One Elizabeth Mc.Quan Aged Eleven Years was removed from the parish of Saint Bridget London to the parish of Saint Paul at Shadwell

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly appeal Against the same Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day of this present Sessions that all parties may Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Wm Murton } Church Wardens

Malbon Sallr Shadwell Church
