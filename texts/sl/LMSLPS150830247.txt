To the Right Honourable James Townsend Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull the Aldermen His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City at their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Mounthaw London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty ninth day of October last Eleanor Ellison widow of Christopher Ellison deceased and their Four Children to wit Christopher aged Nineteen Years, Robert aged Twelve years, Eleanor aged Eight Years and Sarah aged seven Years were removed from the parish of The Holy Trinity London to the said Parish of Saint Mary Mounthaw as the Place of their last legal settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same and pray that this Honourable Court will please to hear and determine their said Appeal and make such Order therein as shall be just.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
