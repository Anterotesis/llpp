To the Right Honble Sr. Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Bridget als Brides London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace (One being of the Quor) bearing Date the Thirteenth day of December last One Thomasine Amos was sent and delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St Andrew by the Wardrobe London unto the Said pish of St Bridget als Brides London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to your Honour and Worships therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly Pray your Honour & Worps will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish of St Andrew by the Wardrobe London may appear before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of you Honour & Worppll. touching this Appeal.

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
