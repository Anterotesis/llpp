To the Right Honble Sr Peter Delme Knt Ld Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

11/11 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of Kingsthorp in the County of Northampton

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quox unus) bearing date the Eleventh day of November 1723 Sarah Dickenson Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Andrew Wardrobe London to the said parish of Kingsthorp in the County of Northampton aforesaid Alledging the said parish of Kingsthorp to be the Place of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order do humbly appeal to Your Lordship and Worp therefrom and humbly Pray they may be heard concerning the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Andrew Wardrobe London may appear before your to hear and abide by the Judgment and determination of Your Lordship and Worps touching this Appeal

And Your Petrs shall ever pray Etc
