To the Right Honble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worpll. the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City Quox unus) bearing Date the Thirteenth day of March last One Isabella Gibson Widdow of Ralph Gibson deced was sent and delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Botolph without Algate London, Unto the Sd Parish of St Andrew Undershaft Aleadging the Sd Parish to be the place of her last Legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves agriev'd by the Sd Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Worrps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray your honour and Worrps will be pleasd to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens And Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish of St Botolph without Aldgate may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of your honour and Worrps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
