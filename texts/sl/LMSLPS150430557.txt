To the Right Honble [...] John Barber Lord Mayer of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Bace for the said City now in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Sarah Meredith widow of John Meredith (deceased) was by order of Pass Warrant dated the twenty ninth day of November last past under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble. [...] John Barber Lord Major of the City of London and Sir Richard Brocas Knt. and Alderman of the Same City two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Libertys thereof (Quorum Unus) removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Dunstans in the West to the said Liberty of the Rolls.

That your Peticoners humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and appeal from the Judgement of the said two Justices to the Judgement of this Honoble Court And desire that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said parish of Saint Dundtans may appear before you to hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Honoble Court upon and touching the said Appeal

And Your Petr Shall ever pray Etc
