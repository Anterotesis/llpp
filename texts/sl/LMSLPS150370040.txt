To the Rt Honble Sr. Francis Forbes Knt Mayr of the City of London & to the rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticonr of Thomas Jefferson At Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That Yor. Petrr. was Comitted Prison ye. 4th. of this instant Augt. on Suspicon of Felony

Yor. Petr. therefore prayes ye benefit of the Statute to be tryed this Sessions or othrewise discharged as this Honble Court shall think fit.

And yor Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

Tho. Jefferson
