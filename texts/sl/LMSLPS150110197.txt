To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. Lord Major of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Gyles wth. out Cripplegt. London

Sheweth That one Mary Deebins widdow being greate with a Bastard Child by Warrant under the hands and Seales of twoe of his Maties Justices of the peace within this Citty (one being of the Quor) was sent by the Churchwardens of St. Mary Steyning to yor. Petrs. to be pvided for upon her Oath That Shee lised a whole yeare as an hired Servant with Abraham Norton of the parish of St. Gyles Cripplegate Turner about for 5 yeares Past Att wyh P Annum whereas in Truth The said Mary Deebins dwelt with the said Mr. Norton as a servant nott about 6 Months & soe had noe Settlement in their said Pish

Their humble suite unto yor. Lott. and Worpps. is To heare them in the promises And to send the said Mary Deebins to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Mary Steyning to be by them pvided for untill They can free themselves of the Charge thereof by due course of Lawe

And they Shall Pray Etc
