To the Right Honoble the Lord Mayor and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry in the said City

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order made under the hands and Seals of Sir John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor and Sir Henry Hankey Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of January instant Mary the Wife of James Cowell otherwise Mary Johnson was removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate in the Said City to the Said Parish of St. Lawrence Jewry as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Mary Cowell otherwise Johnson

That the Petitioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order of Remove all do Appeal against the same unto this Court and

Humbly pray that this Court will please to Appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said Appear

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc
