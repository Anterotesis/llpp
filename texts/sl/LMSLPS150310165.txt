To the Rt. Honble. Sr. George Thorold Barrt. Lord Mayor & the City of London Etc & the rest of the Honble Bench now Siting at Justice Hall in the Old Baylie

The Humble peticon of Stephen De la Hoss now a prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth, That yor. petr. was Comitted to this place the 23d. of Decemr last for returning into this Kingdome (of wch fact he is Innocent)

Yor peticonr. therefore humbly beceeches yor Lordsp. & the Honble Bench that he may be Tryed (or dischargd according to law) this Sessions &

Yor. Petr. as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.
