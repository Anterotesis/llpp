To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions at Guild Hall assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esquire Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sir John Salter Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberties of London one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty sixth Day of June last past Elizabeth Hampton Widow of James Hampton deceasd whose Settlement was unknown was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Michael in Cornhill London to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeale to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices and pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the Matter touching the Said Appeale

Wm. Head Sollr. for the Appellts.
