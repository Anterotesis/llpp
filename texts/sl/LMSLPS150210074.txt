The 17th Aprill 1710

Bridewell Prcinct agt St Andrew Holborne London Appeale

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samll Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in ye General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of Bridewell Prcinct London

Sheweth That one Mary Brandreth Wido. with her Two Children William and Henry by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorm) bearing date the Three and Twentieth day of January last past was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne London to the said Precinct of Bridewell London alleadging the said Prcinct to be two Place of the last legall settlement of the said Mary Brandreth

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove that the said Mary Brandreth never had any legall settlement in the said Precinct of Bridewell where by they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr: and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborne London doe appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
