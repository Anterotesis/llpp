To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Benedict Fincke London

Sheweth That one Margarett Brittaine Widdow of Ralph Brittaine Decd by virtue of a Warrt. Dated 28th. May 1707 under the hands and Seales of twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quox) was brought by the Churchwardens of the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall to yor. Petrs. to be provided for on the Oath of one Rachell White als Brittaine That the said Ralph Brittaine Served his Appnticeship in the said Parish of St Benedict with one John Brittaine Decd Whereas in Truth the said Ralph Brittaine was made a Freeman in 1675. And since dwelt with his wife in the Parish of Alhallowes on the wall as a Lodger for the Space of about twoe Yeares before May 1685. And hath had we Residence since in yor Petrs. said Parish of St Benedict

Yor. Petrs humbly pray That their Appeale may be heard And that the said Margarett Brittaine may be sent back to the said Parish of Alhallowes on the wall being the place of Her last legall Settlement To be provided for by them according to the Lawe

And They shall Pray Etc
