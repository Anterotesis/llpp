To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in their General Sessions of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition of James Dunn a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That your Petitioner was Committed on a Strong Suspicion of Forging a Bill of Exchange for the Payment of money knowing the same to be Forged with an intent to defraud

Your Petitioner most humbly Prays that at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate aforesaid he may for the Forgery aforesaid be either Tried Bailed or Dicharged Pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and Provided

James Dunn
