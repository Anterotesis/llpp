To the Right Honble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of his Matys Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled.

20/12 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Tottenham high Cross in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing Date the Twentieth day of December last past One Amsie Humfreys wife of John Humfreys with her son John Eighteen Years or thereabouts were sent by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrew Holborn London unto the said Parish of Tottenham High Cross in the County of Middx alledging the said Pish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement.

That your Petrs conceive themselves aggriev'd by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your However & Worps therefrom

Your Petrs most humbly pray you Honour and Worps. will be pleas'd to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Andrew Holborn may appear before your and hear and abide the Determinacon of Your Honour and Worps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
