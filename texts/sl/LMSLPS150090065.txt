AD 1697-8 To the right honble Sr. Hump: Edwin Knt. Sd. Mayr. of the City of London & the post of his Maties Justices att the sessions held att she Old Beyly London.

The humble Peticon of William Argent als Jon. Warner

That whereas yor. was comitted to Newgate upon a false Accasacon, & was bayled out since, but being now come into Custody & ready to answer his Accasers

542

Humbly pray this honble Court will bee pleased either bring him to his Tryall or Discharge him this Sessions.

And yor Petr. shall pray Etc
