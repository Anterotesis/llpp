To the Right Honoble Marshe Dickinson Esqr and the rest of the Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx at Justice Hall in the Old Baily London

The Humble Petition of Joseph Wood

Sheweth That your Petr. being Charged on the Oaths of Nicholas Kemp and Thomas Donne [...] for High Treason expressed in the Warrant of Committment your Petr. stands Comitted to Newgate

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays to be Tryed Bailed or discharged this present Session's pursuant to the Statute in that behalf

And Yor. Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Jos Wood
