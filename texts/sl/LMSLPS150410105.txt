To the Rt. Honble: Sir Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of your Lordship & Sir William Billers Knt. & Aldran Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Twenty fourth Day of Aprill last past Catherine Beacon Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby London unto the said Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls London, Alledging the said Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls to be the Place of her last legal Settlemt.

That your Peticonrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do appelale to yor. Lordship & Worps therefrom & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premes & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Nicholas Coleabby London may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of Yor. Lordp & Worships touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petrs: Shall pray Etc.
