Appeale St Martins Orgars agt St Sepulchre

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samuel Garrard Barntt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Martins Orgare London

Sheweth That one Samuel Bedford and Frances his wife and Two Children by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Eighteenth day of Aprill last past was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchers London to the said Parish of St Martin Orgars London alleadging the said Parish to be the place of the last legall settlemt of the said Samuel Bedford his wife & Children

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove That the said Samuell Bedford never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Martin Orgars whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honr. and Worppl therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr: and Worppl will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Sepulchers doe appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr. & Worppl touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
