To the Right Honourable William Bridgen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the peace

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hampstead in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Francis Cokayne Esqr. and Sr. Matthew Blakiston Knt. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth, day of December last Ann Golding Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Swithin in the said City to the said parish of Hampstead as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices of the peace, Appeal to this Honourable Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Time for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

John Reynolds Robt Fryer} Church wardens

Geo Bussee Wm Bovingdon} Overseers of the Poor
