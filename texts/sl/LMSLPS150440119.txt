To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and others his Majesty's Justices of the Pence for the City of London Assembled in Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London one of them appointed by Act of Parliamt bearing date the 13th day of July last Thomas Fay and Anne his wife were removed from the Parish of St Gregory by St. Paul in the City of London to the sd. Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of their last Legall Settlemt. [...] whereby Your Petrs conceive themselves to be Agreived and against the sd. Order doe appeale

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worshipps would be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions to hear & determine the mater touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray

Ro: Chimmow Sollr. for the Appellants}
