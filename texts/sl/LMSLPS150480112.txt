To the Right Honourable Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the rest of the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace new Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth day of October last Henry Moreland with Mary his Wife and their Child James Aged one Year (since deceased) were removed from the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said City of London unto the said parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex Alledging the said parish of Saint Luke to be the place of their last legall Settlement.

Jno: Witton Sollr. for the Appellts: } That Your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant & therefore humbly Appeale against the same.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
