To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the peace for teh said City in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petitition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant bearing date the twenty sixth day of October last under the Hand and Seal of Brass Crosby Esqr. then Lord Mayor of the said City Jane Hukey Singlewoman a Rogue and Vagabond apprehended in the parish of St. Dunstan in the West in the said City was conveyed therefrom to the said parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Hickey there to be provided for according to Law

Your Pettioners think themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

Wm. Leech John Hurst Wm Almond W: Clarkes} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the fields
