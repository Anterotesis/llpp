To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Samuell Dashwood Knt Lord Major of the Citty of London And tot he Rt Worll. Her Maties Justices of ye Peace in the Generall Quartr Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peters the Poore London

Sheweth That one William Bubb and Lucy Bubb his wife by Warrant of Twoe Justices of the peace One being of the Quox were brought by the Churchwardens of St Stephen Coleman streete London to yor Petrs To be pvided for on the Oath of the said Lucy That the Last legall Settlement of William Bub was in Austin Fryers in yor Petrs parish where Hee Lived and was partner with Robert Rodway for about 4 yeares and paid Tenne pounds Kent P Annm and Taxes for the poore Whereas in truth the said William Bubb never was a Partner with the said Mr Rodway Nor ever paid the Rent of Â£10.
