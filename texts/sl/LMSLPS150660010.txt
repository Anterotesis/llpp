To The Right Honble. The Lord Mayor & Aldermen of the City of London in the General Quarter Session of the peace for the Same City assembled

The Humble Petition of Richd. Bulkley

Sheweth That Your Petitioner on the 18th of Septr. last was convicted before The Rt. Honble. The Lord Mayor and Sr. Jno. Barnard Knt. & aldn. of setting and delivering to one Henry Rafter a Ticket belonging to an Undertaking in the Nature of a Lottery contrary to the Statute made in the 6th. Year of His present Majesty & adjudged to pay the Sum of Â£200 & to be imprisoned for the Spane of twelve Months according to the Direcons of the same Act

Your Petitioner most humbly apprhends himself to be aggrieved by the said Conviction & appeals to this Honble. Court [...] and prays such relief in the prsses as to this Honble. Court shall seem meet,

Richd. Bulkley
