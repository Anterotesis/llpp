To the right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The hble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter was Pauls Wharf in the said City of London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order bearing date the 26th. day of July last under the hands and Seal of the Right Honble Micajah Perry Esqr. Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Robert Westley Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City one being of the Quorum Sarah Wallington (said to be the Wife of Crispiana Wallington) and her Child Crispiana Aged Six Year were removed and passed from the Parish of Allhallows Barking in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Peter near Pauls Wharf as the Place of their last Legall Setlement

That your Petitioners find themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the Sd. Two Justices and have given due Notice to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhollows Berking aforsd. of appealing aga [...] Sd. Order this Present Sessions

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships that you will be pleased to Ordd that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Alhallows Barking may [...] Allowd this Court some day this Present Quarter Sessions to hear and abide by the judgement and Determination of their Court toutching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray
