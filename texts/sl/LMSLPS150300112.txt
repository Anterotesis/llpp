To the Right Worpfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys therefore their Generall Quarter Sessions at Guildhall

3/6 The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands & Seales of Sr. John Ward Knight Mayor and Sr. William Withers Knight Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Citty of London and the Libertys thereof (quor un) bearing date the third day of June last, Elizabeth Ross was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Wardrobe London to your Petrs. said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields, Whereby your Petrs. humbly conceive they are wronged & aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeal to your Worships, and pray Your Worps to appoint a time to hear & determine the same.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Phill. Dave Giles Hain} Churchwardens

Henry Coumbe Wm. Nine John Bayley} Overseers of the Poor
