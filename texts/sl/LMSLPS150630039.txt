To the Right Honble: Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. Mayor of the City of London And the rest of this Majestys Justices in their Session of General Goal Delivery for the said City at Justice Hull in the Old Bailey Assembled

The Humble Petition & Prayer of Samuel Wilson

Sheweth That your petitioner on the Sixth day of March last past was Committed to the Custody of the Keeper of Newgate by the aforesaid Mayor of the City of London being Charged by the Oath of James Lundin for being Concerned in the Teloniously Destroying the the Ship Elizabeth and Martha of which Ship your said Petitioner was (said to be) an Owner against the Statute in such Case made and Provided

Your Petitioner therefor most Humbly Prays that at this present Session of General Gaol Delivery holden for the City of London aforesaid your said petitioner may be Tired Bailed or Discharged for the aforesaid Offence, according to the form of the Statute in such case made and Provided

Sam Milson
