To the Right Honble John Wilkes Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of his Majestys Justices at this present Sessions of Oyer and Terminer and Goal Delivery of Newgate held at Justice Hall in the Suburbs of the said City the 12th Day of July 1775

The Humble Petition of Thos. Judd a Prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner was committed the 20th Day of June last upon on Indictment found Against him at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden of the City of London in the Month of April 1774 For feloniously buying taking and receiving from William Or me the Younger Six Hundred Peices of false and Counterfeited Copper Money made to resemble the Copper Coin of this Kingdom called an half Penny not then being Melted or cut in pieces at a lower rate and Value than the said Counterfeit peices of Money did by their denomination Import and were Counterfeited for Against the Statue Etc

That your Petr. doubts not upon his Tryal but to prove his Innocence of the above said Charge alledged Agt. him in the sd Indictment

Wherefore you Petitioner most Humbly Prays That he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged at this present Sessions.

And your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc Thos: Judd
