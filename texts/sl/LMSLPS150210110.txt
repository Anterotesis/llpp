Appeale Alhallows Lombard street St Mary Woolchurch

To the Right Honoble Sr Samuel Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Allallowes Lombard Street London

Sheweth That one Robert Blacknell with Mar [...] his wife with their three Children by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City one being of the Quorm bearing Date the Eleaventh day of July last Past was removed from the Parish of St Mary Woolchurch London unto the said parish of Allhallowes Lombard Street London Alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall setlement of the said Robert Blackwell

That your Petitioners doubt no but to prove that the said Robert Blackwell never had any legall settlement the said Parish of Allehallows Lambardstreet whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices and doe humbly apeale to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to here them in he Prmisses and Order the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Mary Woolchurch to appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc
