To ye: Rt. Honble Sr: Ja: Bateman Knt Ld. Mayr of ye City of London & to ye. rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Baily

The Humble Peticon of Sarah Thompson A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr. stands Comitted for a Felony whereof she is innocent

Therefore prayes to be tryed this Sessions or otherwayes discharged as this Honble Court shall thinck fit

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc
