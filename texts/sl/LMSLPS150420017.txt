To the Rt. Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London Sr. Wm Thompson Knt, Recorder of the Same & one of the Barons of his Majesties Court of [...] & the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices at their Sessions Held at ye Guild Hall of ye Sd City on the 13o. of Janry 1730.

The Humble Peticon of John Eyers now a Prisoner in his majesties Goal of Newgt.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is in very Poor & Low Circumstances & in an ill State of health That he was the last Sessions order'd for Bail as by Copy of ordr. Annexed & now have [...] in Court Sufficient Bail

He therefore Humbly pray that this Honble Court will take the premisses into Consideracon & order the Clerk of ye Peace to take Bail in Court in order for yr Peticoners Inlargemt. & in pursuance of the sd. annex'd order

And as in Duty Bound your Peticoner Shall Ever Pray

John Eyers
