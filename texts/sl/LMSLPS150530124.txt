To the Eight Honble George Heathcote Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipful the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the sd. City & the Libties thereof in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew [...] Exchange London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of St Robert Baylis & Sr. William Rons Knts. & Aldermen & two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London & the Libties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of Octor. Instant, Mary the Wife of Robert Finch [...] [...] (gone to sea) was removed from the Parish of St. Clement East Cheap London unto the Parish of St. Barthew by Exchange London, Alledging the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew to be the Place of her last legal Settlemt.

Cha Baynbridge sollr. for ye. Appellants.} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removed as afd. Do appeal to your Lordship & Worships therefrom, And humbly pray that yor. Lordship & Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premises, And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement ed Cheap may appear [...] you to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Lordship & Worships touching this Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall pray &c.
