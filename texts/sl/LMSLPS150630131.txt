To the Right Honble and Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourth day of January 1752. Elizabeth Ethelstone Widow of John Ethelstone deced was removed from the Parish of Saint Ann within Aldersgate London to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived and against the said Order do Appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that a Short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the said Appeal

W: Croft Agent for the Appellants

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
