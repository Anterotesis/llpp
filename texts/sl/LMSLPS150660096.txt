To the Right Honourable Stephen Theodore Janssen Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London, and to the Rest of the Honourable Bench in their General Quarter Sessions of Gaol Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Harris a Prisoner

Sheweth That on the Thirteenth Day of September last your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the Woodstreet Compter London on Suspicion of Stealing in Spittlefields in the County of Middlesex a Piece of Hair Shag of the Value of Twelve Pounds the Property Alexander Watson And now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate Under the same Charge.

Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly Prays That at this present Sessions of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex, he may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that [...] Made and Provided.

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc

John Harris
