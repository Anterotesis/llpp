To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Halifax Knight and Frederick Bull Esqr two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London bearing Date the twelfth Day of March One thousand seven hundred and Seventy Seven Elizabeth Lowrey the Widow of Hugh Lowrey Deceased was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London to the said parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Lowrey

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves Agrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore humbly Appeal Against the same and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London and all Persons Concerned to Attend this Court

to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal And that Your Petitioners may have Such relief therein as to Your Worships shall seem Meed.

John Shaw John George} Churchwardens

G Shelley James Easter} Overseers
