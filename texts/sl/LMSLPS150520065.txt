To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Guild Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition Appeal of the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrt. or Order under the Hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and The Right Honble Sr. Robt. Baylis Knt. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City bearing Date the Sixth Day of February last Elizabeth Seymour wife of Richard Seymour was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres London to the said Parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved and Appeal to this Court agt. the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Prays this Count will Please to appear time for the said Appeal and that all Person Concerned may attend at the same time

And your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc

Chas. Gresham Agent for St. Paul Convent Garden
