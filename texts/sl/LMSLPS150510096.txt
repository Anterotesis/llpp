To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London assembled Genll. Quarter Session at Guild Hall in the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Lord Mayor of the City of London and Seals of the Right Honble. Sr. John Salter Knt. Micajah Perry Esqr. two of his Maities Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of January last Mary Colbatch Siglewoman was removed from the parish of St. Andrew in Holborn in the City of London to the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays this Court will Please to appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may attend at the same time

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Chas. Gresham Soll for Appels.}

John Bladwell Richd. Fryes Denis Moueque} Churchwardens
