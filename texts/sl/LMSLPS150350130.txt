To the Rt Honrble Sr Peter Delme Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

21/2. The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Barkin London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the hand ap Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quorn Unus) bearing date the 21st Day of February last past Robert Pearson with Jane his wife were removed from the Parish of St Dunston in the East London unto the said Parish of Allhallows Barkin London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitionr conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom And Pray that you will be pleased to hear them is the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Dunstan in the East may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Honr: and Wopps touching this appeal

And they shall every pray Etc:
