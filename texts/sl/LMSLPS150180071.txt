To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Robert Bedingfeld Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the less London

Sheweth That one Anne Brockist By virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace in London Dated the 25th. June 1707. One being of the Quor was brought by the Churchwardens of the Parish of St. Antholins to yor Petr. to be provided for upon her Oath That Her late Husband Henry Brockist was an Housekeeper Att the tyme of his Death in the said Parish of St Bartholomew the lesse att the Rent of £16. P Annu being Her last legall Settlement Whereas in Truth The said Henry Brockist took the said House in yor. Petrs. Parish att £16. a yeare And dwelt there in abt. a moneth & then dyed which yor. Petrs. Conceive to be noe legall Settlemt. for the said Anne whoe went thence within 3 weekes next ensueing

Yor. Petrs. humbly Pray yor. Honor. & Worpps to hear them in the prmisses And to send the said Anne to the said Parish of St. Antholins To be by them Provided for untill They can Discharge themselves thereof By due Course of Lawe

And They shall Ever Pray Etc
