To the Right Honourable Sr: Francis Child Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Humbly Sheweth That by a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and seals of the said Sr. Francis Child and Sr. Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Twenty Ninth day of September 1732 Jane Baldwyn Singlewoman was Removed from and Out of the Parish of St. Bridgett Als Bride London to the sd. Parish of St. James As the Place of the last legall Settlement of the sd. Jane Baldwyn Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved

And Therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to Hear and Determine the said Appeal According to Law and Justice

Saml. Seddon, Agent for the said Parish of St. James.
