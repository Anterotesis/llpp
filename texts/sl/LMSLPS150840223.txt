To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London and the Liberties thereof at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church London

Sheweth That by a Vagrant Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seal of the Right Honourable James Townsend Lord Mayor of the City of London bearing Date the twenty third Day of November last Mary Coombs Widow of Richard Coombs deceased was conveyed as a Rogue and Vagabond from the Parish of Saint Mary Woolchurch haw to the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church as the Please of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Vagrant Order or Pass Warrant Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

James Fisher, for the Appellants.
