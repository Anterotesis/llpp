To the Rt. Honble. Daniel Lambert Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London, & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said [...] City & the Liberties thereof, in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petrs. [...] and Age [...] Churchwardens & Overseers of the Petrs. of the Parish of Chipping Barnett in [...] County of Hertford.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order Pass Warrant in in Hands and Seals of your Lordship & Sir John Salter Knt. & Alderman, Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libities thereof, (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eight Day of July last past, John Rotherham aged about Sixty three Years was Removed from the Parish of St. Benedict Vulgarly, Bennett Frick London into the Parish of Chipping Barnett in Hertfordshire, according the said Parish of Chipping Barnett, to be the Place of his last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge for ye. Appts: That your Petitonrs. conceiving themselves a [...] by the said Warrant or Order of Removall as [...] Do Appeal to Yor. Lordship & Worpll therefore, and humbly pray, That you will be pleased to heart and in the Premisses, and that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Benedict Vulgarly, Bennett Finch London may appear before you, to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of Yor. Lordp & Worsp touching this Appeal

And yor. Peticoners shall pray Etc.
