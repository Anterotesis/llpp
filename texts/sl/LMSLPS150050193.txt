To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborne London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. have many Poore to pvide for being scituate in the Suburbs of this City And not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive them their Parish being soe greate and their Poore soe many Notwithstanding their Assessemt. is greater then heretofore Yett the charge of their Poore is above the said Assessemt. As the same upon full examinacon hath many times appeared to divers Aldermen of this City Appointed to heare the same And their Poore dailey increaseing upon them.

Their humble Suites therefore unto yor. Honors. and Worpps. is that you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the contribucon of seaven Pounds from severall Parishes within this this City for & towardes the releife of their Poore As was last yeare given them Or such other allowance as to yor. Honors and Worshipps shall seeme meete.

And they shall Pray Etc.
