To the Right Honble: Sr. Richard Glyn Knight Lord Mayor Sr. William Morton Recorder and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberty thereof at their General Quarters Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Your Lordship and Francis Cokaym Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fifteenth Day of March last Isaac Salmon Aged ten Years and Eleanor Salmon Aged Nine Years were Removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth in the said City to the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners and Appellts. Apprehending themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Do Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellts. therefore Humbly pray that this Court will Appoint a Day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth and all other Persons concerned to. Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners and Appellts. may be Relieved as this Court shall see meet

And your Petitioners and Appell shall ever pray Etc.

April ye. 23d. 1759 Jno. Billington Solr. for Appellt.
