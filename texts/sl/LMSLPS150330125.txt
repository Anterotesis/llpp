To the Right Honble. His Majestys Justices of Oyer & Terminer for the City of London

The Prayer or Petition of William Lord North & Grey.

Sheweth That he was Comitted to His Majestys Tower of London by Warrant under the hand and Seal of the Rt: Honble. Charles Lord Viscount Townshend His Majestys Principal Secretary of State dated the 29th. day of September last for High Treason which your Petitonr is innocent of

And therefore prays that he may this present Sessions be brought to his Tryal or otherwise be bailed or discharged out of Custody persuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided

North & Grey.
