To the Right Honourable Robert Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary in Aldermanbury London

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of the right honble Robert Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Thomas Chitty Esqr. One of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of June 1752 Ann Longsdon aged thirty three years was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Less London into the said Parish of St. Mary in Aldermanbury aforesaid

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said order or pass Warrant humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the hearing and Determining the said appeal may be adjorned until the next general quarter session of the peace to be held in & for the said City And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the less and all other persons concerned may then attend this Court to hear and abide the judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

Fra: Salt. Atty.
