To the Right Honble: the Lord Mayor and the rest of his fellows Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberties of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Saviour Southwark.

Humbly Sheweth That Whereas by an Order of removeall under Hands & Seals of Micajah Perry Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and John Salter Esqr. one of the Aldermen of the said City. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties thereof bearing Date the 23d. Day of November 1738. Richard Ash, Deborah his Wife & their Child, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Att Hill in the City of London, to our said Parish of Saint Saviour Southwark in the County of Surry as the Place of their last legall Settlement whereby we think ourselves aggrieved.

Your Pettioners Therefore most Humbly Prays this Honorable Court will Grant an Order for all persons herein Concerned to appear at such Time and Place as this Honourable Court shall think fit to Appoint in Order to the hearing this Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Clemt. Lee Churchwn. Thos. Curtis Overseer
