To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Saml Garrard Barrt. Ld. Mayr. of the City of London And to the Rt Worll the wst of her Matis. Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the pish of St Margt. Lothbury London

Sheweth That one John Badger Jane his Wife and Mary John Katherine Xpofer and Jane their Children by Virtue of a Warrt under the Hands & Seales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the Peace wch in the City of London (one being of the Quox) bearing date the 4th. day of November last past was sent and delivered by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the pish of St Andrew Holborne in London to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said pish of St. Margt. Lothbury alleadging the said parish to be the Place of the last legall settlemt. of the said John Badger his Wife & Children

That yor Petrs doubt not but to prove That the said John Badger his Wife & Children never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Margt. Lothbury whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgemt. of the said Two Justices And doe humbly Appeale to yor Honor & Worll therefore

Yor Peter there fore humbly pray yor Honor & Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the prmisses And That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the sd Pish of St Andrew Holborne doe Appeare before you and hear & abide the determination of you Honor & Worpps touching this Appeale

And you Peter Shall Ever pray.
