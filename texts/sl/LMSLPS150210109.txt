To the Rt Honoble: Sr Samuel Garrard Baronett Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled:

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Hamlett of Spittlefields in the Parish of St Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That one Jane Bruton Widdow by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the First Day of August last past was removed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate unto the said Hamlett of Spittlefeilds in the County of Middx alleadgeing the said Hamlett to be the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Jane Bruton

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Jane Bruton never had any legall settlement in the said Hamlett of Spittlefeilds whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices And doe humbly appeale to your Worpps there from

Yor Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Premisses and Order the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishops- gate to appeare before you and heare and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionrs shall Ever Pray Etc
