Their humble petition herefore unto your good Honr and Worpps is that you will be favourably pleased [...] grant unto them in the Contributions of other parishes within this City for and towards the reliveing the poor as they have fomerly had which are particularly her under menconed or such further Allowance as to your Honr and Worpp Shall sein meet
