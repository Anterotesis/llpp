To the Rt. Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at the Guild Hall of the Said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of St. Sepulchre lyeing in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seales of Sir John Salter Kt. Lord Mayor of the Said City of London and Humphrey Parsons Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said City (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Seventh Day of November last past Elizabeth the Wife Peter Tattersell with Elizabeth their Child Aged about Two years were Removed and Conveyed from that part of the Parish of St. Sepulchre which is within the City of London to that part of the Said Parish of St. Sepulchre which is in the Said County of Middlesex as the Place of their last Legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving they are Aggrieved by the said Warrant Order of the Said Two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court for reliefe

And your Petitioners shall for Ever pray Etc

John Wills Agent for St. Sepulchred Middlesex 14th. January 1739
