To the R. Honble. the Lord Majr. of the Citty of London & to the rest of this Majtyes Justices of Gaol Delivery of Newgate

The humble peticon of Jasper [...] Roth a Prisoner in this Majestyes Gaol of Newgate

Sheweth That ye petitonr is Comitted for High Treason in Corresponding with the Kings Eximyer, a Cryne of White ye Peticonr is allogether [...] Innocent.

Wherefore ye peticonr humbly prayeth the benifitt of the Statute made for the Liberty of the Subject & to be dyed or Bayles this Psent Gaol Delivery

And yor Peticonr shall pray Etc.
