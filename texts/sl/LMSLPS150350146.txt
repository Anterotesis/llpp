To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the City of London at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the Peace now holden for the said City

16/6 The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church- Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. or Order under the hands and Seals of Sr. William Thomson Knt. and Sr. John Fryer Knt. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 16th. day of June last Mary Lee Wife of John Lee with her two Children (vizt.) Ann aged about twenty months and Mary aged about tenn Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate and unto the Said Hamlett of Bethnall Green as the Place of their last legall Settlement

That Your Peticonrs. conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and do thereof humbly Appeal again the same. Praying Such relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgement Shall Seem just.
