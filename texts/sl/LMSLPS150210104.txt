St Andrew Holbone Middx agt St Buttolph Bishopgate

To the Rt Honrble Sr Samuel Garrard Barntt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx

Sheweth That one Margarett Wills wife of Richard Wills by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the five and Twentieth day of July last past was removed from the parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London unto the said Parish of St Andrew Holborne in the County of Middx alleadgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last Legall settlement of the said Margarett Wills

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Margarett Wills never had any legall settlement in your Petitioners said Parish whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgment made by the said Two Justices and doe humbly appeale to your Honour and [...] Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionoers therefore humbly Pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London to appeare before you and to heare and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeale

And your Petitionr shall ever Pray Etc
