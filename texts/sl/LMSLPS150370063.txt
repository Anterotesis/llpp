[...] To the Right Honble Sr. Francis Forbes Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull this rest of His Majesty's Justies of the peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Barthers by Exchange London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under the Hands and seales of yor. Lord his & Sr. Peter John Knt. one of the Aldren of the same City Two of His Majty's Justies of the Peace for the said City City & the Liberties thereof (Quord unus) bearing date the Twenty fifth Day of June last past Thomas Shellon was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Abchurch London unto the said Parish of St. Bartholomew by Exchange London alledging the said Parish of St. Barthew to be the place of his last legall settlement.

That yor. petrs conceiving them blvr agreived by the said Order or warrant of Removed Do humbly appeale to your Lords his of Worps therefrom And humbly pray that your Lords & Worps will be pleased t hear them wintering the Parish of And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the the said Parish of St. Mary Abchurg London may appear before you to hear & abide the udgment & Determinacon of Yor Lords & Worps touching this Appeall

And yor Petrs shall pray etc.
