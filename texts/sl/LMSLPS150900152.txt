To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Samuel Plumbe Esqr: Mayor and William Plomer Esqr. Two of his majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 4th day of March last past, Sarah the Wife of John Mulhall (who in the said Order is said to have Deserted her) was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Christ Church in the said City of London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said City as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Mulhall, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore Appeal agt. the said Order and most humbly pray Relief in the premes, And that Your Worshipful will be pleased to Appoint some day in this present Sessions for the Hearing and determining this their said Appeal

Cox. Sollr: for Appellts.
