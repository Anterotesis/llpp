To the Rt. Honrble Sr Charles Peers Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worpll: the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in the General quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate ale London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under her hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London one being of the (Quorn bearing date the 25th. Day of September last past Christian Row Single woman was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London unto the said parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legal settlement.

That your petrs doubt not but prove that the said Christian Row never lead any legal settlement in the said parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr. and Worpps therefrom

Your Petr therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Pairsh may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
