To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the City of London in their present General Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which has above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Burnell and Nathaniel Newnham Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 25th. Day of April last Mary Robinson Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Katherine Creechurch in the said City to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to Your Worships against the same and humbly request you will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal And that you will order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Katherine Creechurch to appear before you to abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc

Stable for the Appellants
