To the Rt Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Majesty Justices of the Peace in the General quarter sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Martin Ludgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of avo of her maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Sixteenth Day of June last one Sarah Bainbridge wife of William Hainbridge with her daughter ann were removed from the parish of St Austin London unto the said parish of St Martin Ludgate alloadging the said parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Bainbridge the husband

That your Peticonrs doubt not but to prove that the said William Bainbridge never had any legal Settlement in yor Peticonrs said Parish of St Martin Ludgate whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr & Worpps therefrom

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Austins may appeare before you and hear and abide the dece [...] of your Honr. and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs shall ever Pray
