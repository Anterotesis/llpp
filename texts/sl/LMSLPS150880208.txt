To the Right Honble. Sir Thomas Hallifax Knt. Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Session if the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir. Thomas Hallifax Knight Lord Mayor and Frederick Bull Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum, bearing date the Eleventh day of July last Sarah aged Sixteen Months or thereabouts (the illegitimate Daughter of Susannah Jesson, a Singlewoman who had deserted her) was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Ajudication and Order of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Herbt. Robertson Sollr. for the Appellants
