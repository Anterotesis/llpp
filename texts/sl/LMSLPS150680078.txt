To the Right Honoble Marshe Dickinson Esqr and the rest of the Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the City of London at Justice Hall in the Old Baily London

The Humbly Petition of Jemima Wilcox

Sheweth That your Petr. being charged on the Oath of Nicholas Kemp and James Blackburn for High Treason and Felony expressed in the Warrants of Commitment Your Petr. stands Comitted to Newgate

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays to be Tryed Bailed or Discharged this present Sessions pursuant and to the Statute in that behalf

And your Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Jemima Willcox
