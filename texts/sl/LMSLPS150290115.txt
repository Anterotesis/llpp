To the Right Honble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Mayor of ye City of London and to the rest of his Maty Justices of the peace at the Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Brides London

Sheweth That By Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quor) bearing Date ye Twenty Second day of October [...] cost past Samuel Lynn was removed from the parish of Sr Sepulchers London to ye parish of St. Brides London alledging the Sd Pish to be the place of his last Legal Settlement

That Your petsconrs come we themselves agrieved by She Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to Your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honour and Worpswill be pleased to hear them in the premisses & Order that the Church Wardens & Oversrs of the poor of ye sd parish of St Sepulchres may appear before You & hear & abide the determinacon of ye Court touching the Said Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
