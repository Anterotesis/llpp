To the Rt Honble Sr. John Ward Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of the Rt Worppll His Maties Justices of the peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Appeale of William Mason of the Parish of St. Margarett Lothbury London Taylor

Sheweth That the said Appellant stands Convicted by Sr Thomas Abney Knt One of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City for having caused to be made and Sett upon divers Suites of Cloaths or Garments severall Quantities of Buttons and Button holes of the same Stuffe of which such Suites or Garments were respectively made And that he hath been Adjudged or Ordered by the said Justices to pay the Forfeitures thereby Incurred. And forasmuch as the said Appellant is aggreived thereby

He therefore Humbly Appeales unto this Court against the said Conviccon Anvell for that the same is Insufficient As that the said Appellant is not in any wise Chargeable with the offences therein Contained, or any of them and prays to be discharg'd therefrom in such manner as this Court shall think fitt.

And Your Peticoner As in Duly bound Shall Ever Pray Etc
