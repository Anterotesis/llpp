To the Right Honble: Sir Francis Child Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Christopher in the City of London

Sheweth That by VIrtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of yor. Lordship & Sir William Billers Knt. & Aldran Two of His Majtys Justices of the Base for the City of London & Libties thereof (Quorn unus) bearing Date the Tenth Day of August last past Obediah Smart & his four Sons, vizt. John about the Age of nine Years, Obediah about the Age of Eight years, David about the Age of Seven years & George about the Age of four years, were removed from the Parish of St. Benedict vulgarly Bennet fink London, unto the said Parish of St. Christopher London, alledging ye. sd. Parish of St. Christopher to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Peticonrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do appeale to your Lordship & Worps therefrom & humbly pray, that your Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, Order yt: the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Bennet vulgarly Bennet fink London, may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor. Lordp & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
