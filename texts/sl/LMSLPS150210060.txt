Petiton St. Brott Aldgate

To the Right Honoble. Sr. Samuell Gerrard Barnll. Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll. the Rest of her Maties. Justices of the Peace [...] the same Citty in the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petiton of the Appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That Whereas one Edward Johnson is new and for sometime past hath been an Inhabitant with his wife and three Children in your petitioners said parish of St. Buttolph Aldgate And whereas the said Edward Johnson by severall lessed and misfortune is reduced to great Usufully and Poverty into much that he is not capable to maintaine and provide for his said more Children but hath left them to be provided for and mantained at the expence of your Petitioners said Parish But now so it is that one Rebecca Jones of the Parish of St Dunstans in the East Wid. being Grandmother of the said three Children and of good substance and Ability ought by law to provide for her said three Grand children and not burthen your Petitoners with the Charge therof and haveing been required by your Petitioners to take care of and maintain her sd Grandchildren as by law she ought St [...] eatheless the yett refusth so to doe.

Wherefore Your Petitionrs. humbly Pray Your Honr. & Worpps. to hear them in the Prmisses And alsoe Order the said Rebecca Jones forthwith to appeare to before you and to Shew cause why they should not Provide for the sd Grandchildren as the Law in that Case direct to And also to heare and abide the determination of Your Honr. & Worpps. therein

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.
