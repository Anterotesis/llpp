To the Rt Honoble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the right worll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City of the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

1712 3rd July The humble Petition appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Katherine Creechurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant understands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City of being of the Quorm) bearing Date the Third Day of July last past Richard [...] dye with Frances his Wife were removed from the parish of St Mary Abchurch And unto the said parish of St. Katherine Creechurch London alleadging the said to be the place of the last legall settlemt of the said Richard Rootledge

That your Petitioners doubt [...] prove that the said Richard Rootledge never had any legal Settlement the said Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch whereby they conceive themselves [...] reived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and [...] appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Pet [...] therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps [...] leased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish [...] Mary Abchurch may appear before you and hear and all the determination of your Honr & Worpps touching this apeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

St Katherine Creechurch agt St Mary Creechurch} Appele
