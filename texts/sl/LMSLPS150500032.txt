To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & [...] of the Honble: Bench of Justices at the Old Baily

The humble Petition of Andrew Reid

Sheweth

That the Right Honble. the Lords Commissioners of is Majesty's Treasury having been pleased by Articles dated the 5th. of April last to contract with your Petitioner for transporting all such Felons as shall be Sentenced for Transportation by this Court, & he having accordingly made the necessary preparations for transporting as soon as conveniently may be, those who are now lying in Newgate under Sentence of Transportation, on may fall under the Same during this present Sessions.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that he Court will be pleased to order an Assignments of the abovesaid Felons to made unto him in order for this immediate Transportation her being ready to give the necessary Securities.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Andr: Reid

London June 6th. 1739.
