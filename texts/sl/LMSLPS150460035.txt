To the Worshipfull Bench of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye City of London

The Humble Petition of Joseph Ady of Little Moorfields in ye Parish of St: Giles's Cripplegate Dyer

Sheweth By way of Appeal to ye worshipful Bench that he is Rated Eighteen Shillings P Annum, to ward the Maintenance of the Poor of this Parish by wch he is very much Aggrieved his whole Estate, Household Goods & Chattels, do not Amount to Twenty pounds (his Debts being paid) wch. he is ready to make oath of

Therefore he hopis this Honble Court will take it unto Consideration

And ye Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

10 October. 1735
