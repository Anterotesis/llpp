London ss To the Right Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertyes thereof

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Chappel Warden and Overseer of the Poor of the Prcinct of the Savoy in ye County of Middx

Sheweth That by an ordr dated the 3d. day of August last past undr. the hands & Seals of Sr. Saml Garrard Barrt Lord Major of London and Sr. Robert Beddingfield Knt. two of her Majties Justices of the Peace of the sd City (Quor Unus) Elizabeth Elston widow of Charles Elston decd was rembed from the Parish of St Bridgett als Brides London to the Prcinct of the Savoy aforesaid alleadging the same to be the place of her last legall Settlement;

That yor Peticonr conceiveing themselves to be aggrieved by the aforesd Judgment of the sd two Justices doe humbly Appeal to this Court therefrom

Yor Petrs therefore humbly pray that this Court will be Pleased to ordr. that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish of St Bridgett als Brides May forthwith attend this Court and bear & abide the Judgement & determinacon thereof touching this Appeal;

And Yor Petrs shall Pray Etc

Jno Natt Ralp Chase } Chappell Waden Overseer of ye Poor
