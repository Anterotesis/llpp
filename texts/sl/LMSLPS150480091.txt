To the Right Honble Sir John Barnard Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Honble Bench at Justices Hall in the Old Bailey in the Suburbs of the said City of London

The Humble Prayer of John Webster.

Sheweth That your Supplicant was on the 14th. of October last Committed a Prisoner to Newgate being Charged with Wilfull & Corrupt perjury but no bell being preferr'd your Supplicant was by this Honble Court Order'd to Remain in Custody.

Yor Supplicant Therefore Humbly Prays That he may be tried bailed or Discharged this Sessions as Shall Seem Most meet to this Honble Court

And yor. Supplicant as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc

Entred 7th. December 1737 being the first day of ye Sessions

John Webster
