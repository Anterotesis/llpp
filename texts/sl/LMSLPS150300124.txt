To the Rt Honrble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions assembled

14/8 The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St James Dukes place London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (Quous unus) bearing Date the fourteenth day of August last past Sarah De Costa wife of Isaac Decosta with Elizabeth their Daughter was removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London unto the said Parish of St James Dukes place London alleadgeing the said Parish to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said warrant and Judgment of the said Justices To appeal to your Honr: and Worpps therefrom and most humbly pray your Honr: and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the primisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Allhallows on the Wall may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment & determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etca.
