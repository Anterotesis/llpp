To the Right Honble St Edwd Bellamy Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpl the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the sixth day of June last Margt. the wife of Leonard Biggins and Sarah their daughter aged three years were ref [...] eved from the Parish St. Bridgett otherwise St Brides London To the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopgate London as the place of their Last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the the Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the same
