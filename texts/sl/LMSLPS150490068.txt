To the Right Honourable Sir John Barnard Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of His Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the said Sr. John Barnard and Sr. Thomas Lombe Knt. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Fifteenth Day of June 1738 Ann Allen and her four Children George Aged Eight years Jane Judith five years Hester three years And Margaret two Months were removed from the Parish of St. Olave Hart Street London to the said Parish of St James as the Place of their last Legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner think themselves Aggrieved

And Therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please [...] Hear and Determine the said Appeal According to Law and Justice.
