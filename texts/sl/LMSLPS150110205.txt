To the Rt. Honoble: Sr. Richard Levett Knt. Lord Manor of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll: the Rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Session Assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of Sr. Martins Cutwich

Sheweth That one Hester Tyles Singlewoeman by virtue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seales of twoe of this Maties. Justices of the Peace One being of the Quoy date the first day of Januray 1699 was brought by the Churchwardens of St. Sepulchers London To yor. Petrs. to be provided for and mainteyned For that her last legall Settlement was in their said parish of St. Martins Outwich As by a Certificate under the Hand of the minister for sending her thence to St. Thomas Hospitall for her Cure They would have her to be Settled with them Whereas in truth the laid Hester Tyles was not in their said parish of St. Martins as a Servant above the space of Fower mone thes. And had not legall Settlement therein

Their humble suite unto your Honour and Worpps. To heave yor. Petrs. Appeale in the premisses And to lend the said Hester Tyles to the Churchwardens of St. Sepulchers To be by them provided for untill They can three themselves of the charge thereof by due Course of Lawe

And They shall Ever Pray Etc
