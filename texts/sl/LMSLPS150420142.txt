To the Rt Honble Humphry Parsons Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt Worpl his Maties Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City Assembed

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Alhallows on the Wall London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (Quor Unus) Robt. Hamilton Anne his Wife & their 5 Children Vizt. Anne John Frances Sarah And Isabella were sent from the Parish of St. Ethelburga London to the parish of Alhallows on the Wall London as the place of their last Legal Settlement.

Your Peticoners humbly conceiving themselves agreived by the Warrt. & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for such relief in the prmisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
