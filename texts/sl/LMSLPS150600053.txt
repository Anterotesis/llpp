London To the Right Honourable Sr: William Calvert Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties Assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the peace for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Le Quern

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirty first day of may one Thousand Seven hundred and Forty nine Susanna George Wife of Edward George who is run away (as is alledged) was removed from the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London to the said Parish of Saint Michael Le Quern London alledging the last Legal Settlement of the said Susanna George to be in the said Parish of Saint Michael Le Quern London By which Order of the said two Justices your Petitioners apprehend themselves Aggrieved and appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court & Pray to be in relieved in the Premisses and your Petitioners will pray}

Geo: Chapman Church Warden}
