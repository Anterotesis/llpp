To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt: Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worll. The rest of His Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St. Saviours Southwarke in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Warrt Dated the 5th. June 1708 under the hands and Seales of Twoe of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London (Quor unus) One Anne Rowley als Morgan was Removed from the pish of St. Sepulchers London to the pish of St. Saviour in Southwarke Alleadging the said Pish is the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Anne Rowley als Morgan That Yor. Petr. Doubt not but to prove That the said Anne Rawley als Morgan never had any legall Settlemt. in the said Parish of St. Saviours Southwarke [...] Whereby They conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgemt. of the said Two Justices And doe humbly Appeale to Yor. Worpps. therefrom

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray Yor. Worppl. will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London to Appeare herebefore you And hear and abide the Determinacon of Yor. Honr.& Worpps. touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc.
