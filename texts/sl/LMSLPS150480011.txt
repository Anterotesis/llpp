To the Right Honourable Sr. John Thomson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Honourable his Majesties Justices of Oyer and Terminer and Goal Delivery for the said City of London and County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of John Farmer

Sheweth That your Petr. being declared a Bankrupt he went thro' his Examination at the third Sitting but was about three Weeks ago Committed to the Gate House for a pretended Concealment to the Value of Twenty Pounds and upwards (of which your Petr. is entirely Innocent) and is now removed to Newgate in ordr. as your Petr. apprehends to be tryed for the same

That your Petr. is ready to take a Tryal but is justly apprehensive his Prosecutors will affect a Delay therefore to prevent his being kept in Prison until another Session thro' any delay of the said Prosecutors

Your Petr. most humbly Prays that at this Session he may be tryed Bailed or Discharged for that your Petr. may be entirely ruined for want of his Witnesses at an Ensuing Sessions.

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Farmer
