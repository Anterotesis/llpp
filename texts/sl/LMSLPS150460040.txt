London To the Right Honourable Sr. Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor and the rest of the worshipfull the Justices in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon and appeale of Thomas Ansell

Sheweth That Yor. Petr on the 14th day of June Instant was Convicted of keeping a Comon Alehouse not being thereunto lawfully Lycenced whereby Your petitioner conceives he is Aggreived

Yor. Petitioner therefore most humbly Appeales to Your Worships in this behalfe & prays reliefe in the premisses as to Yor. Worships Shall Seem meet.

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
