To the right Worppfull Sr. George Thorold Knt. Lord Major of the Citty of London and the rest of his Maties Justices at their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace now assembled at Guildhall in and for the Citty of London

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the 16th. day of Augt last under the hands and Seales of Sr. William Thompson Knt. Recorder of the said Citty and Sr. William Withers Knt. two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this Citty and liberty thereof (Quor unus) Elizabeth Gearey Spinster was removed fout of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London into that part of the said parish of St Giles Cripplegate called ye Lordshipp by which said Ordr. your Petr. think themselves agreived and humbly appeale therefrom

Yo Petconr therefore humbly [---] pray to be heard & releived in the Prmisses and the Churchwardens. and Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish in London may hear & abide the Judgmt. of this Court

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc.
