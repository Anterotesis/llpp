To the Right Honoble Sr. John Fryer Bart. Lord Mayor of London One of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London And to the Worshipfull The Justices of the Peace for the said City in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dinois Back Church in London

Sheweth That Whereas in and by one Warrant under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Sir Samuel Stainer Knt. and Alderman of the said City bearing Date the 14th: Day of September Anno Dm 1721 Sarah Lane Singlewoman was removed fand passed from the Parish of St. Olave Silver Street to the said Parish of St. Dionis Back Church as her last Legal place of Settle ment and was Accordingly by the said Warrant Removed thereto

That the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Dionis Back Church with Great Submission Apprehending that they Shall be able to make it Appear to Your Lordship and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace that the said Sarah Lane never Obtained any Legal Settlement in the said Parish Have therefore Humbly Appealed from the said Warrant And Hope they Shall be Admitted to be heard by their Councill Against the Same

And Your Petrs: Shall Ever Pray
