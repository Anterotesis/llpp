To the Right Honble George Nelson Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worshipful the Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Flude Anthony Pearce and George Fishwick Inhabitants in the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said City

Sheweth That by the last Rate and Assessment made for the Relief of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles your Petitioners are severally rated in a Large Sum of Money

That your Petitioners thinking themselves greatly overcharged and Aggrieved by the said Rate, humbly Appeal against the same

And pray that this Honble Court will take their Cases into Consideration and quash the said Rate or otherwise after or amend the same in such manner as may effectually relieve your Petitioners

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
