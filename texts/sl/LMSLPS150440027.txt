To the Worshipfull Bench of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the City of London

The Humble Pettition of Peter Boursott of the Parish of St. Botolph Aldersgate London

Sheweth By way of Appeal to the Worship- full Bench that He is Rated three Pence Pr Week to wards the Maintainance of the Poor of this Parish, His whole Estate and Houshold Goods & Chattels does Not Amount to the Value of fifty Pounds His Debts being Paid Which he is Ready to take Oath of

Therefore he hopes this Honbl: Bench Will take it into Consideration

And Your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray

This being the fourth Time of Appealing and have had No. Relief

January ye 12th 1732
