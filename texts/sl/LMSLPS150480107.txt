To the Right Honourable Sir John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the General quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Righte Honourable Sir John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Richard Brocas Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Sixth Day of September last John Toney and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchres in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that they may be releived against the said Warrant or Order in such manner as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Stephen Braly.

Phill Adams} Churchwardens

Wm Cocking Simon King} Overseers
