To the Right Honourable William Bridges Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Honble Bench in their General Session of Gael Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition and Prayer of Elizabeth Smith a Prisoner.

Sheweth That the Eighteenth Day of February Instant your Petitioner was Committed to his Majestys Gaol of Newgate upon the Oath of George Read on Suspicion of being Concerned with others Unknown in Feloniously Stealing Sixteen Iron Barrs the Property of the said George, Eight of the said Iron Barrs being Found in her Possession And now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate under the Same Charge

Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly Prays That at this Present Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex She may be Tryed, Bealed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided.

And your Petitioner Shall Pray Etc.

Eliz Smyth
