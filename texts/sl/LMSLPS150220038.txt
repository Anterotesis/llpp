The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parishes of St Giles without Cripplegate St Buttolph without Aldgate St Andrew Wardrobe St Katherine coleman St Andrew Holborn St Sepulchres St Brides Allhallows on the Wall St Buttolph Aldersgate and St Buttolph without Bishopsgate

Sheweth That your Petitioners are very much overburthened with Poor and not able to raise sufficient Sumes of Mony amongst themselves to releive them not with Stand= =ing their Assessments are meater than heretofore Yet the Charge of the poor is far above their Assessments as the same upon their Examination hath divers times appeared to several Worthy Aldermen of this City appointed to hear the same and their poor daily increasing upon them their said parishes some of them lying near to the Suburbs and others of them in the Suburbs of this City

Their humble Suited therefore into your Good Honr and Worpps is that you will be favourably pleased to many unto them the Contributions of other Parishes within this City for and towards the releiving the poor as they have formerly had which are pticularly hereunder menconed or such further allowance as to your Honr and worpps shall seem meet
