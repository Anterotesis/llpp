London To the Right Honourable Slingsby Bethell Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Honourable Bench in their General Quarter Session of Gaol Delivery assembled

The Humble Petition and Prayer of Samuel Arrowsmith a Prisoner

Sheweth That on the Twenty Fifth Day of March last Your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the Poultry Compter London for Publishing four Bills of Exchange for the Payment of Money and also forged Note for the Payment of Money with Intention to Defraud William Griffiths He the said Samuel knowing the Same to have bee forged and Counterfeit against the Statute, and now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate under the Same Charge.

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays That at this Present Session of Gool Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided.

And Your Petitioner shall Pray Etc Saml Arrowsmith
