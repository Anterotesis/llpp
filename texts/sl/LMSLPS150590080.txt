To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Guildhall in and for the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Sr. Robt. Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor & Edward Ironside Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23d. Day of Novr. last (Eliza. Shelton Wife of Mathias Shelton (who is ran away from her) was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopgate London to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby year Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd. appeal

Wm. Chimmons agt. for the Appellrs.

And your Petrs. shall pray etc.
