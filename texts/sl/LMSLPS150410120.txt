30/9 To the Right Honble: Sir Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticond & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Vedast als Foster London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of yor Lordship & Sr Wm. Billers Knt. & Aldrand Two of His Majtys Justices of ye Peace for this City & Libties thereof (quor unus) bearing Date the Thirtyeth Day of September last past Elizabeth Callin Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn London into the the said Parish of St. Vedast als Foster London alledging the said Parish of St. Vedast als Foster to be ye. Place of her last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or order Do appeale to yor. Lords & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray that yor. Lords & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmes, & order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor. Lords & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray etc.
