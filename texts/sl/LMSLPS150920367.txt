To the Worshipful the Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London His Majesty's Justices of the Race for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Pan of the Parish of Saint Mary at Hill London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Benjamin Kennett Esquire Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sir Watkin Lawes Knight Alderman of the same two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City one being of the Quorum bearing date the Twenty sixth day of June one Thousand seven hundred and Eighty Sarah Goddard Widow was removed and conveyed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the said City from and out of their said Parish to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Hill as their Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves, to be aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant humbly Appeal against the same to your Worship and pray Relief in the premises and that a day may be appointed for hearing and deterning the said Appeal.
