To the Right Honble Sr. Robert Wostley Knt. Lord Mayor Sr. Simon Urlin Knt. Recorder And the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Robert Horne

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the abovesaid Sr. Robert Westley and Sr. Joseph Hankey Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London and Libertys thereof (One being of the Quarum) bearing date the Fourth day of May last Your Petr. is Adjudged the Reputed father of a Male Bastard Child there in mentioned to be Born on the Body of Sarah Harper Singlewoman in the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the said City of London on or about the Twenty Sixth day of Febry then last.

That your Petr. is there in Ordered to pay unto the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate on some or one of them the Sum of Four Pounds one shilling and Six pence said to be by them Expended for and towards the Maintenance of the said Male Bastard Child And also you Petr. is thereby Ordered on Fryday in every Week from and after the Date thereof so long as the said Male Bastard should be Chargeable to the said parish to pay to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish the Sum of Two Shillings and Six pence.

Your Petr. therefore Apprehending himself Agrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices humbly Appeals to this Court against the same and pray such Relief in the premisses as this Court shall see meet.

And your Petr. Etc.

Robt. Herne
