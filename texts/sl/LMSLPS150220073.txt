To the right Honble. Sr. Gilbert Heathcote Knight Lord Mayor of of the City of London and the rest of her Majesties Justices Assembled att the Sessions in the Old Baily

The humble Petition of Thomas Waples now a prisoner in Newgate.

Humbly Sheweth that your Peticonr. hath been confined in Goal for a considerable time and hath suffered great hardships during his Imprisonment.

That no body hath preferred and till of Indictment against him since his Imprisonment

Your Peticonr. therefore humbly prays your honour and Worships wilbe pleased to discharge him from his Imprisonment or admitt him to bail

And Your Petr, (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc.
