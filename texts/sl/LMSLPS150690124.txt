To the Rt. Honble. Sir Charles Asgill Knt. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worshipful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. City of ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of Ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fishstreet London.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of Slingsby Bethell & Francis Cokayne, Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & ye. Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing date ye. Twelth Day of January last past, Sarah Hubbard, Wife of William Hubbard, a Sailor in His Majesty's Service, & their three Children, Sarah, aged five Years, Wm. aged three years, & Elizabeth aged about two Years, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London, unto ye. Sd. Parish of St. Mary Magdalen, alledging ye sd. Parish of St. Mary Magdalen, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

P Baynbridge Sollr. for the Appellants. That yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved by ye sd. Warrt. or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordp & Whorships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear ye. in ye. prmisses & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, may Appear before on, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordp & Worps. touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petitioners Shall pray Etc
