Janry Session 1783. To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Fellow Justices of the Peace for the said City in Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St. Catherine in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Pass Order under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Newnham Knight Lord Mayor and John Boydell Esquire Citizen and Alderman two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberties thereof bearing date the third day of January Instant Ann Brand Singlewoman removed from and out of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the said City of London (as likely to become Chargeable to the same) to the said Precinct of St Catherine in the County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of the Peace humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate aforesaid and all other Person concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Clare Sollr. for St. Catherine}
