To the Right Honoble Sir Gerard Conyers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Right Worpfull the Rest of His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Warrt. under the hands & Seales of two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties thereof (Quor unus) bearing date the nineteenth day of June In the year of four Lord Christ One hundred seaven hundred twenty and three Joanna Ives Widow of William Ives deced with their three Children Sarah John and Elizabeth of different aged were removed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London to Your Peticoner said Parish alledging the same in the Judgmt of the said Justices to be the Place of their last legal settlement.

That your Peticoners conceiving them selves aggreived by the said Order or Warrt of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to Your Lorp & Worps therefrom And humbly pray they may be heard concerning the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Buttolph & with. Aldgate London may appear before You to hear & abide by the Judgmt. & Determinacon of Your Lorp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And Your Peticoners shall ever pray etc.
