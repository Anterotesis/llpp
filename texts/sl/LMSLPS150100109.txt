To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt. Worll. his Matice Justices of the Peace of the same City in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Christchurch London

Sheweth That by virtue of a warrant under the hands & Sealed of twoe of his Maties. Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Qnox) dated the 10th. day of February Anno Dm 1698 A male child about three moneths old laid downe within the Hath leading out of the Cloysters up into the Girles ward in Christs Hospitall on the 7th. February 1698 was sent to your Petrs. to be pvided for which yor. Petrs. conceive They are not Obliged by Lawe to Doe the Hospitall being a distinct house of Charity from the said Parish And the Governors have used to pvide for the Poore found therein And whoe pay nothing to the releife of the Poore of the said Parish in respect of their said Hospitall on greate house scituate in their Parish

Therefore your Petrs. humbly pray your Honor and Worpps: would be pleased to hear them all in the prmisses And to order the Treasurer or some of the Governors of the said Hospitall to Appear here before you And to Quash the said Warrant made by the said twoe Justices And send the said male child to the said Hospitall to be by them kept and pvided for untill they can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due course of Lawe

And They shall Pray Etc.
