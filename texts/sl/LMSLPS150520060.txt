To the Right Honoble Daniel Lambert Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their Quarter Session of the Peace for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Epsom in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of His Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of September 1741 John Coomes and Ann his Wife were removed and Conveyed From and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate within the liberties of the said City to the sd aforesaid Parish of Epsom in the County of Surry aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petitioner Therefore conceiveing themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass warrant Humbly Appeal against the same and pray such Raleif in the premises as to this Honoble Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno. Ireland Geo: Worsfold Wm Stacy
