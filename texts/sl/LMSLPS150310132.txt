To the Right Honble Sr George Thorold Knt. & Bart Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace at for the sd City at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of ye parish of St. Bennet Shearhog London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 13th day of May last That Mary Chapman with Thomas Mary & James Chapman her three Children were removed from the psh of St Botolph with Bishopsgate London unto ye sd. Pish of St Bennet Shearhog London Alledging the Sd Pish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

The Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt & Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Honr. & Worps therefrom.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Pray Given Honr & Worps well be pleased to hear them in ye prmisses & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate may appears before Y [...] r & abide [...] of your Honr & Worps touching the said Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc.
