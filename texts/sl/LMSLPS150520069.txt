London. To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the Ward of Farringdon without in London aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of Humphry Parsons Esqr. late Lord Mayor of the City aforesaid and also one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Same City deceased and Sir William Rous Knt. Alderman of the Same City Another of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City aforesaid bearing date the Fifth day of February last past James Copen Aged four Years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of same Sepulchre London to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in London aforesaid as of his the said James Copen's last legal place of Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of Peace humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court will appoint a Day this Present Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre aforesaid and all other [...] concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc Joes Stephens Churchwarden

Joseph Lewendon Nicholas Langford} Overseers
