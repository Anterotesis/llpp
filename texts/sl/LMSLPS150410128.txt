To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeall of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Faith under St. Pauls London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace forthe said City and Libertys (Quor Unus Bearing date the Twenty Fourth day of October last Past. Abell Rockall was lately Removed from the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate London to the said Parish of St Faiths under St. Pauls London as the Place of his Last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking them- selves Aggreived by the Judge- ment of the said Two Justices of the Peace, Appeall to your Lordship and Worship's Against the said order and humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to your Lordship and Worship's Shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray etc.

James Brooker John Suffield} ChurchWardens
