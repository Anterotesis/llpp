To the Right Honoble Sr. Peter Delme Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty & Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Session & Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennet at Pauls Wharfe London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of July last past one Jane Bashervile Widow of Edward Bashervile Carpenter was removed from the Parish of St. Elhelburga London unto the said Parish of St. Bennet at Paulswharfe London aforesaid alledging the said Parish of St. Bennet at Paulswharfe to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

Therefore your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly appeale to your Lord Ship and Worps therefrom And humbly pray that your Lordship & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Elhelburga London may appear before you & hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your LordShip & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
