To the Right honble. Sr. Thomas Atney Knt. Ld. Mayr. of the City of London & the Rest of his Maties Justices at Justice hall in the Old Bayly London

The humble Peticon of William Franklin [...] new a prisonr. in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Perr. having been malisiously swore Against was Comitted above a month agoe and is now in an state of health

And therefore humbly prays that he may bee cald to a Tryall this Sessions or otherwise bee discharged

And your petr. as in duty Bound shall ever pray Etc
