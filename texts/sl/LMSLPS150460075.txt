To the Rt Worspll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petitioner Appeale of the Church- -wardens Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Sr. Edwd Bellamy Knt. John Thompson Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of February last Ann Barton otherwise Abudente Wife of Gideon Abudente was removed from & out of the parish of Saint Sepulchres in [---] the City of London to the parish of St Giles in the Fields aforsaid as the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. therefore Conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices appeale to yor. Worps against the same & humbly hope yor. Worsps will to pleased to hear & Determine the same

Danl Bolton Soldr & the Appells.

And yor Peters. shall ever Pray Etc

John Baker Thos. Cogging} Churchwardens
