To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin Vintrey London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys (Quor Unus) [...] Bearing date the Eighteenth day May last Mary Langley and her three Children vizt. James aged two years and a half Edward and John Langley Twimss aged about Eight Months was lately removed from the Parish of St. Mary Somerset London to the said Parish of St. Martin Vintrey London as the place of Their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioner thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to Your Lordship and Worships against the said order and humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem most meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
