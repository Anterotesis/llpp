The Humble Peticon Pat. Coffy and Katherin his wife.

To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Jon. Houblon Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Judges of Oyer and Terminer at Justice Hall in the Old Bayly

The humble Peticon of Patrick Coffy and Katherin his wife Prisonrs. in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That your Petr. stand Committed for High Treason for Counterfeiling the Coyne of this Kingdome as by the Honble. mt Records Warrt Appeares dated the 21 febry. 1695 that your Petrs. humbly pray the Benefitt of the Habeas Corpus Act for the Liberty of the Subject and that this their Prayer may be Entred in this Court in order to be tryed or Bayled psuant to the said Act

And yr Petrs. as in duly bound Shall ever pray Etc
