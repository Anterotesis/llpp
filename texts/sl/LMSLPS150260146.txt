To the Right Honrble St Willm Humfryes Kt. Baronet Lord Maior of the City of London And to the (Right Worshipl the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Midx.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quor bearing Date the fifteenth day of March last Past One John Mayson an Infant was sent [...] by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Katherine Coleman London unto the Said Parish of Kensington in the County of Midx Alledging the said Parish to be the place of his last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioner therefore conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Honours and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs most humbly pray your Honour and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the [...] of the said Parish of St Katherine Coleman may appear before you And hear and abide the Determination of Your Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray
