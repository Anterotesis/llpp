To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City assembled at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace (at Guildhall) for the said City.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Limehouse in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor and Sr. Richard Hopkins Knt. two of his Majtys Justices of the Peace for the Said City of London (Quor un) bearing date the Second day of April last Rebecca Gill wife of Thomas Gill was removed from the Parish of St. Botolphs without Aldgate London to the Said Hamlet of Limehouse whereby your Petitioners humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly appeals to this Court against the said Order and prays such releife as this Court Shall Seem meet.

And Yor. Petitrs. Shall ever pray Etc
