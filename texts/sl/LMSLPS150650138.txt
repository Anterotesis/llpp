To the Right Honourable Thomas Rawlinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Brother the Alderman of the said City

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John at Hackney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing Date the Thirty first Day of January one thousand seven hundred fifty and four under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Rawlinson & John Porter Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) Jane Middleton wife of Henry Middleton was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate to [---] the Parish of St. John at Hackney in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement

By which said Order your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived and humbly appeal to this Court agt. the same praying such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem just

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Ric: Dann Agent for the Appellants
