To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said City of Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Tottenham High Cross in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a certain Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Sir William Plomer Knight and William Gill Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the sixteenth day of December one thousand seven hundred and eighty five Mary the Widow of Joseph Bell was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Mounthaw in the City of London to the said Parish of Tottenham High Cross by the description of the Parish of Tottenham in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices and Therefore humbly Appeal against the same.

Chetham. Solicitor for the Appellants } Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said City of London.
