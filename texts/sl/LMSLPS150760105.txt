To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled in & for the said City

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Dane in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands & Seals of the Right Honble Wm Stephenson Lord Mayor & Sr Samuel Flydyer Knight and Baronet two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (One of the Quorum) Bearing Date the 24th of Novr. 1761 Mary Atkinson Widow was Removed from the Parish of St. Andrew in Holborn London to the said Parish of St. Clement Danes as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners concieve themselves to be, aggrieved & against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitions therefore humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing & Determinig there of may be Adjourned untill the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City of London

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc
