To the Sr. George Thorold Knt. & Baront. Ld. Mayor of the City of London And alsoe To the Rt. Worshipfull rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Sd. City Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeals of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew holborne for the Liberty above the Barrs

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr. William Thomson Knt. Recorder of the City of London & Sr. William Withers Knt. one of the Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (Quorun unus) bearing date the twentieth day of September last Amilia Predman & Bridget Predman Daughters of Jonas Predman by Martha his wife were passed from the parish of St. Bridget als Brides to the said Parish of St. Andrew hoborne above the Barrs as the place of the said last legall Settlement of the said Jonas Predman

Your Peticoner [...] Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and pray that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Bridget als Brides & all other Parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners (as in duly bound) shall pray Etc.
