To the Right Honrble Sr: Robert Godschall Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the rest of the Worshipfull Aldermen his Brethren in their General Quarter Sessions [...] of the Peace for the said City Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John Baptist London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and London bearing date the twelfth day of December 1741 [...] One Samuel Gibbens & Alice his Wife were removed from & out of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in London into our Said Parish of St John Baptist in London as the place of their last Legall Settlement

Yor Petrs: Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order of pass Warrant Do humbly Appeale against the same And pray such Relief in the premisses as this Honrble Court shall think just

And yor Petr as in Duty bound shall pray

John Dodd Ch warden
