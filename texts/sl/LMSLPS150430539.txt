To the Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the, parish of St. Mary Le Bon in the sd City of London

Shew That by Ordr. Dater the 3d. day of May 1732 Under the hands & Seals of Fran: Child Mayr Sr. William Billers Knight two of his maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City (Quarn Unus) Daniel Wakeham was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Michl: Queen hish London to the sd Parish of St. Mary Le Bow

Yor. Petrs. humbly Conceiving themselves Aggreived thereby Do Appeal from the sd. Judgmt. of the sd. Two Justices of the peace to the Judgmt. of this Honrble Court & Crave a day of hearing

and yor. Petrs Shall ever pray
