To the Right Honourable Slingsby Bethell Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said City at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Or Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Slingsby Bethell Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and Thomas Rawlinson Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixteenth day of July last past A Child Named Arthur Baynes aged about Five years was Removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London To the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant and humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray That they may be relieved against the said Order Or Warrant in Such manner as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

John Savell James Bonner} Churchwardens

Tho: Carleton James Jones} Overseers
