To the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Withers Knt Ld. Majr. of the Citty of London and to the rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

[---]

That Humble Peticonr of Wm. Lavender of Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth

That yor petr. was Comittee this 8th: day of September last (being the First day of the last sessions) Charg'd upon Suspicotr of Stealing sevall Horses

That yor Petr. did remaine all the last Sessions and no psecucon agt him

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prayes this Honble Court to be tryed this Sessions or that he may be discharged

And yor. petr shall ever pray Etc
