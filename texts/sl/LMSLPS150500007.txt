To the Right Honourable Miraijah Perry Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Raw of his Honrble Brothron the Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate now in their General Sessions of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the City of London at Justice hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Chetwood a Prisoner in his Majesties Goal of Newgate.

Sheweth The Your Petitoner was on the fifteenth day of December last Committed for Stealing a Parcell of Brass Patterns of Branches the Property of William Alexander

That Your Petitoner hath been Confined in Goal over Since for the said Offence and hath lately been Informed an believes that the said Mr: Alexander the Prosecutor intends not to try Your Petitioner at this Sessions but to have Your Petitioner detained untill the next Sessions of Goal Delivery to be hold for the said City of London for the said Offence

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that (not being Accused with any Other Fact) he may at this Present Sessions of Goal Delivery of Newgate be Fryed bailed or Discharged

And Your Petitioner Shall ever Pray James Chetwood
