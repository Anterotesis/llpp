To the Rt. Honble St. John Ward Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

This humble Petrs and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Mary Magdalen in Mill street London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Court under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestyes Justices of his peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Second day of May last past Mary Roberts widow of Thomas Roberts) were sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate London unto the sd. Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Mary Magdalen in Millstreet London alledging the sd. Pish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petenrs. conceive themselves aggrived by the sd Warrt. and Judgmt of the sd two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your petitioners therefore most humbly pray Etc Honr. and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and Ordr. that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear & abide the Determinacon of the Court touching the sd. appeal.

And yr. petonrs. shall ever pray Etc.
