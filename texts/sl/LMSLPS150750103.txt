To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of His Majesty's Justices of Goal Delivery in their General Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City and County of Middlesex Assembled

The Humble Petition of Thomas Warwick and John Warwick

Sheweth That Your Petitioners on the fifth day of October One Thousand seven hundred and Sixty four were committed to New Prison Clerkenwell in the said Country of Middlesex by Joseph Girdler Esquire one of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County being charged before the said Joseph Girdler on the Oath of Samuel Phene for feloniously taking and carrying away a stripped Satten Counterpane the property of Samuel Phone and Thomas Jones against the Peace Etc

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City and Country aforesaid They may be either Tryed Bailed or discharged for the Felony aforesaid pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and Provided

Thos. Warwick Jno. Warwick
