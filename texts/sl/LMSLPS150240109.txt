To the Rt. Honrble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and also to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of her Majestys Justices at the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Margarett pattons London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seals of two of her Majesty's Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorun) bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of Time last past One Agnes Whittey Wido was removed from the parish of St Peter in Cornhill London unto the said parish of St Margarett Pattons alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement.

That your petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Agnes Whitbey never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Margarett Pattons whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr. and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionr therefore most humbly pray's your Honr. and Worpps will Copleased to hear them in the Prmissed and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Peter Cornhill may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal

And yor Petitionrs: Shall ever Pray Etc.
