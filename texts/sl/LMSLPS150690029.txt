To the Right Honourable Sir Charles Asgill Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London, and to the Rest of the Worshipfull Bench in their Present Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Martindall a Prisoners

Sheweth That on the Twenty fourth Day of January Last Your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the House of Correction at Clerkenwel in the County of Middlesex for Feloniously taking and Carrying away one Bill of Exchange value Twenty four Pounds, Ten Shillings, the Property of Charles Greenwellers, and now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate under the same Charge.

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly prays that at this Present session of Goal Delivery of Newgate Holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex, We may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the statute in that case made and provided.

22d. February 1758,

And your Petitioner shall pray etc. John Martindale
