To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices in their General Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the County of Middlesex Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Richard Mason Clerk.

Sheweth That by Bill of Indictment found at the Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the County of Middlesex in February Session last Your Petitioner stands Indicted for Feloniously Solemnizing Matrimony against the form of the Statute in that Case lately made and Provided.

That Your Petitioner hath for several Months last past been a Prisoner Charged with Debt in the Kings Bench Prison and hath caused himself to be removed by Virtue of His Majesty's Writ of Hebeas Corpus to the Goal of Newgate in order to Surrender and take his Trial on the said Indictment.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the County aforesaid he may be either tryed Bailed or Discharged for ther Felony aforesaid Pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and Provided.
