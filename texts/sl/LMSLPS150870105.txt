To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City now in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Sarah Andrews the Widow of Richard Andrews Deceased was by Order or Pass Warrant dated the 21st. Day of June last under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable John Sawbridge Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and Fredk. Bull Esqr. Alderman of the said City Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libertes thereof (One being of the Quorum) Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Vedast alias Foster in the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners & Appellants conceive themselves aggrieved thereby humbly appeal to the Judgment of this Honourable Court agt. the same and pray that they would be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may abide the Judgment and Determination thereof And that Your petitioners may be Relieved in the Premises as this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos Wade Junr Sollr. for the Appellts.
