To the Rt: Honble: St. William Stewart Knt: Ld. Mayor of the City of London & to the Worshipfull the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberty's thereof in their Generall Quarter Session Assembled

1/9 The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton garden & Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew holborne in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a pass warrant or order under the hands & seales of the Rt: Honble: Sr. William Stewart Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and St. Francis Forbes Knt. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quorum unus) bearing date the first day of September last Richard Baily and Susannah his wife were removed and passed from the Parish of Saint Gregory by St. Paul London to the said Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton garden & Ely Rents as the place of the last legall settlement of the said Richard Baily

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justies humbly appeal to this Court again the same and pray to be releived in the prmisses as to your Worships shall seem meete

And Your Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
