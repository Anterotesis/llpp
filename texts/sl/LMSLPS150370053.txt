To the Rt:Honble: St: Francis Forbes Knt: Lord Mayr: & the rest of his Majties Justices of the peace for the City of London

Sheweth The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblet of Spittlefeild in the County of Middx.

That by Virtue of an Ordlunder the Hands & Seals of Sr: Francis Forbes Knt: Lord Mayr: & Edward Boocher Esqr: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the sd: City of London 12 nov. [...] bearing date the Eleventh day of May last Mary Cutler & her three Children (vizt) William aged about Nine Years Peter aged about five years & Thomas aged about One Year & a half were removed from the Parish of St: Botolph without Bishopsgate London to our Sd: Hamblet of Spittlefield as to the place of their Last Legal Settlemt:

That Yor: Petrs: think themselves agreived by the sd. Ordr: & Hambley appeal to this Honbl: Court against the same

And pray that yor: Worspp would be pleased to give them Such Relief in the prmi [...] as to yr: worspp shall seem Meet

And yor Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
