To the Right Honoble. Sr. William Ashhurst Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Right Worll. the rest of their Maties. Justices of the Peace of the same City.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Andrews Wardrobe London

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. Parish is scituate never broken wharfe And are not able to raise sufficient sumes of money amongst themselves to releive their Poore Notwithstanding their Assessements are very greate gett the charge of their Poore is above their said Assessemt. As the same upon full examination hath sundry times Appeared to divers Aldren of this City appointed to heare the same and their Poore increaseing upon them.

Their humble Suites unto yor. good Honors. and Worpps. is that you will be favourably pleased to grant unto them the contribution of Â£40. from other Parishes wthin. this City for and towardes the releiveing their Poore As they have formerly had for divers yeares by reason of their greate charge & number of Poore or such other allowance As to yor. Honrs & Worpps. shall seeme meete.

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
