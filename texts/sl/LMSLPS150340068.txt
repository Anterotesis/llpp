To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. Gerard Conyers Knt. Ld. Mayr. of the City of London And the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of William Leister

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt. under the Hands Sr. Gerard Conyers Knt. St. Mayr: of the City of London and Sr. George Merttins Knt. Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace wth. in the same City yor. Pett was did judged to be reputed Father of a Female Bastard Child born on the Body of Jane Nailer Singlewoman and was orders to pay unto the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Peters the poor where the sd. Child was born Six Pounds & ten pence for Charges expended & paid out in the maintenance & providing for the sd. Female bastard Child And also to pay weekly from the 14th. day of May being the date of him said Warrt. to the said Churchwardens two Shillings & Six pence for so long time as the sd Child shall continue a charge to the said parish

yor. Petr. thinks himself aggreived by the said adjudis ration of his sd. two Justices And humbly Appeals to yd. Housr. for Releife therein and Prays to be heard touching the plumies And that the Churchwardens & overseers may be ordered to [...] Two show Cause with the sd. Warrt. & adjudication of the sd. two Justices [...] not be quarters And yor Psh. [...] therefrom

And he shall ever pray Etc.
