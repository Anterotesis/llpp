To the Right Honble Sr. Willm Lowen Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worpl ye Rest of his Matys Justices of ye peace at ye Genl. Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled

1/10 The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St Bennitt ats Fink London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maty Justices of ye peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date ye first day of October Instant Giles Davies was removed from ye Pish of St Giles without Cripplegate to ye sd Pish of St Bennit Fink Alledging the sd Pish to be ye place of his last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrant and Judgment of ye sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray your Honr & Worps will be pleased to heard them in the premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of ye Court touching ye sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
