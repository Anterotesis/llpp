To ye: Rt. Honble Sr: Samll Dashwood knt. Ld: Majr: of ye: Citty of London & to ye rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in ye old Bayley

The Humble Peticon of Francis Dushaw A Prisonr

Sheweth That Yor. Petr was Comitted to Newgate the beginning of August last Charged on suspic of breaking Edwd. Crowders house & stealing his goods whereof Yor Petr. is wholy imbront

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Prayes to be tryed this sessions or bailed as this honble Court shall thinke fit

And Yor Petr: shall ever pray Etc
