To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worpfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Garlick hith London

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys (Quor Unus) Bearing date the 25th. day of July last past Rebecca Baker aged 3 years or Upwards Daughter of Marmaduke Baker Deced was lately Removed from the Parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. James's Garlick hith London as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking them selves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeal of your Lordship and Worships against the said order and humbly desire this Court will make such order there was to your Lordship and Worships shall Seem most meet

And your Petitioners as in duty bound shall ever pray
