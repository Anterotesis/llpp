To the Right Honble the Lord Mayor & the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & the Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 17th. Day of August one Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty two Ann Reeves (otherwise Elizabeth Reeves) Widow was Removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate London afd. adjudging the last Legal Settlement of the said Ann Reeves to be there and Your Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal therefrom and Humbly Pray Such Relief in the Premisses as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet and Convenient

Burr Attorney for Bpsgate Parish

And Your Petrs shall ever Pray Etc.
