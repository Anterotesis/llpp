To the Right Honble Sr Henry Marshall Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, And to the Rest of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said City in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary At Hill London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble Sr Henry Marshall Knt Lord May and George Arnold Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of September 1745 Anne Hargrove Widow of Richard Hargrove deced and their child Sarah aged seven years, were removed from the parish of St Dunstans in the West London, to the parish of St. Mary At Hill London as the place of their last legal settlement

Your Petitioners humbly conceiveig themselves aggrieved by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court for relief against the same

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Jno North Att for St Mary Hill
