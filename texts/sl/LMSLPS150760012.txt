To the Right Honourable Sir William Stephenson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Richard Holmes Prisoner in his Majesties Goal of Newgate

Sheweth That your Petitioner was Committed the 26th. day of December last by Sir John Fielding Knight on Oath of Isaac Dupree and John Lewis Assignees of the Estate and Effects under a Commission of Bankrupt awarded against your Petitioner and on Oath of Elizabeth Cane and others for feloniously Concealing part of his said Estate and Effects to the Amount of Twenty pounds and upwards with intent to defraud his Creditors

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays to be Tryed Bailed or Discharged at the present Sessions

Richard Holmes
