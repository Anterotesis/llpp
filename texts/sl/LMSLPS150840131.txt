To the Right Honoble James Townsend Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Right Worshipful the Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said City & Liberties thereof in their General Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a warrant or order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honoble. William Nash Esqr. late Lord Mayor and Sir Robert Ladbroke Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the Said City and the Liberties thereof bearing Date the Sixth day of November last Mary Blower aged Twelve years Sarah Blower Aged Ten years and Frances Ann Blower aged Seven years Children of William Blower by Mary his wife both deced were removed & Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew in Holborn London into the Said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

Bampfield Sollr for the Churchwns. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph witht Aldgate } Your Petitionrs Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or order do humbly appeal to this Court against the Same and pray Such Relief in the Premes as to this Honoble. Court Shall Seem meet

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
