To the Rt. Honble. Sr. Gerrd. Conyers Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Honble Justices on the Bench now sitting att Justice hall in the Old Bily.

The humble Peticon of Richard Pollard now a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner was on the twenty fourth Day of this Instant August [...] Comitted to Newgate by Sr. Fra: Forbes Knt. for having bought & reced severall Quantityes of Tobaccoe & other goods Stolen from Several persons unknown of a Considerable Value of which yer. peticoner is Innocent.

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly prays that hee may bee Fryed or Discharged this sessions

And hee as in Duly bound shall Ever pray Etc.
