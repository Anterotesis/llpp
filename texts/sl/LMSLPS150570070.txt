1745 To the Right Honourable Sir Richard Hoare Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest Worship full the Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrant undr. the Hands and Seals of the said Sr. Richard Hoare Knt. then and now Lord Mayor of the Said City of London and Daniel Lambert Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London aforesd said (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of December last past a Female Bastard Child aged about two years Baptized by the name of Ann Staples was removed from the Parish of saint Andrew Holborn London unto the said Parish of Saint Luke alledging the sd. Parish of St. Luke to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That yor. Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Order or Pass Warrt. and therefore humbly Appeal against the same

And yor. Petitioners shall ever Pray etc

J Witton Sollicitor for the Appellants}
