3/2/31 To the Right Honoble Humphry Parsons Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City & Libertys thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeall of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by an order under the hands [...] & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libertys (Quor unus) Bearing date the Third day of February last Martha Phillips Widdow of Jacob Phillips Deced was lately removed from the parish of St. Giles's without Cripplegate London to the said parish of alhallows Staining London as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices of the peace Appeall to your Lordshipp & Worships against the said order & humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoners shall ever pray et.

Robincon [x] Appeall
