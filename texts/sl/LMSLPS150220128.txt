To the Rt Honrble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish St Katherine Coleman London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quoru) bearing Date the 25th Day of October last past Baynard Bridgman was removed from the parish of St Sepulchre London unto the said parish of St Katherine Coleman London alleadging his said parish to be the place of his last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Baynard Bridgman never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St Katherine Coleman whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish of St Sepulchre may appear before your Honr and Worpps and hear and abide your determination therein

And your Petitioner Shall ever Pray
