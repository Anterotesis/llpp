To the Right Honourable Nathaniel Newnham Esquire Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful Alderman his Brethren in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the City of London Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Orgars in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th. day of December 1782 one Mary Webb Single Woman was Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Catherine Colman in London unto the said Parish of Saint Martin Orgars as place of the last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Appeal against the same and pray such Relief in the premisses as to your Lordship and the Court shall think fit

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
