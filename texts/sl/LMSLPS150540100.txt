To the Right Honoble Robert Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwdns. & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honoble Robt. Willimott Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Wilm. Calvert Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties thereof bearing date the 22d. day of April 1743 Hannah Smelt Wido. of John Smelt deced was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchers London to the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves agrieved [---] by the said Warrt. or Order and Humbly Appeal against the Same

Your Petrs. therefore Humbly pray they may be relieved against the said Warrant or Order in such manner as this Honoble Court shall seem meet

And Yr. Petrs. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.

Simon King Fras. Strong} Churchwdns

Thos. Green Wm. Paine} Overseers
