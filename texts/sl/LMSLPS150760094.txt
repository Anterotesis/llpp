To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor the Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace of the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace at the Guildhall of the same City assmbled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and [---] Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Olave in Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrant dated the first day of April instant under the hands and seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City Mary Cooper Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Katherine Creechurch within the said City to the said Parish of St. Olave in Southwark in the County of Surry as the place of her last legal settlement

That your Petitioners apprehend and are advised that the lat legal settlement of the said Mary is not in the Parish of St. Olave Southwark And therefore apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or Order of Removal and do appeal to Your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore humbly pray that your [---] Worships will please to proceed to the hearing of this their Appeal whereof the Respond unto have Notice And that this those appeal mary he allowed with Cost.

And You Appellants will pray Etc.

Gunter Sollr. for Appellts.
