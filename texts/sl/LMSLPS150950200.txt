To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and to the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the said City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Plomer and John Hart Esquires Aldermen of the said City two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty second day of January last William Payn was removed and conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Ann in Black Friars in the City of London t the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate in the said City as the palace of the last legal Settlement of the said William Payn whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your worships to appoint a Day in this present Session to hear and determine their said appeal

Carter & Simpson Sollrs. for the Appellts.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
