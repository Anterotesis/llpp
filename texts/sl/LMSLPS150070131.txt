To the Rt. Honble: Sr. John Houblon Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to ye Rt. Worll: his Maties: Justices of Gaole Delivery of Newgate of the City of London & County of Midd in the Generall Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon of Jno. Child

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was comitted by the Rt. Worll. Sr. Salathiel Lovell Knt. Record. of this City on the 6th. of this instant July [...] for hight reason in counterfeiting the current mony of this kingdome

His humble suite unto yor. good Honr. & Worpps. is That yor. Petr. may be tried this Prsent Sessions for the same or otherwise admitted to fine baile for his Psonall appearance at ye next Sessions of Gaole Delivery of Newgate to be holden for this City according to the Lane.

And yor. Petr. shall or Pray Etc

The humble Peticon of Jno. Child
