To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor and the Right Worshipfull the other his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof

The Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Michael at Queenhithe London

Sheweth That on Complaint made the 15th day of Febry 1743. to Sir Wm. Billers Knight and George Arnold Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof one being of the Quorum by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the East London that Mary Myers Widow of David Myers deceased were then lately come to reside in their said Parish not having obtained any legal settlement there and were likely to become chargeable thereto unless timely prevented which Complaint the said Justices did Judge to be true and for as much as it appeared to them by the Oath of the said Mary Myers that about one and twenty Years ago the said Mary Myers's said Husband David Myers rented a House in the said Parish of St. Michael at Queenhithe London of the Value of Â£10 a Year & lived in the same for several Years and no other Subsequent Settlement appearing unto them they the said Justices did adjudge the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Myers to be in the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhithe London and by Warrant under their Hands and Seals bearing date the said 15th day of Febry 1749 did Cause the said Mary Myers to be removed out of the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the East into the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhithe as her last legal Settlement

That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhithe are advised that the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhithe is not the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Myers

And therefore the Churchwardens & Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Michael at Queenhithe do Appeal against the said Order so made as aforesaid for the removeal of the said Mary Myers into the said Parish of Saint Michael Queenhithe and pray to be reimbursed the Costs they have been at on Account of providing for the said Mary Myers since her said removeal together with the Costs sustained by them the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Michael

Michael at Queenhithe on Account of this present Appeal And the said Appellants also pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Dunstans in the East may be ordered to attend at this Quarter Sessions or such other time as to Your Lordship and the other Justices shall deem meet and may observe such Order as Your Lordship and the other Justices shall think fit to make in the Premisses

And Your Appellants shall ever pray Etc
