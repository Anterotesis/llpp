To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticonr of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne and Agnes within Aldersgate

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor and Sir Francis Forbes Knight dated the Sixteenth day of February now last Abigail the wife of John Hewett with their two Children were lately removed from the Parish of St. George in this City to the said Parish of Saint Anne and Agnes

Your Peticoners finding themselves aggreived by the said Order do humbly appeale against the Same and Pray the same may be discharged and that they may be releived in the Prmisses.

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc
