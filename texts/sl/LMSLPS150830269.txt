To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, in their General Session of the Peace at Guildhall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Worshipfull Brass Crosby and Samuel Plumbe Esqruires Alderman of the said City of London and two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said City of London and Liberties thereof (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth Day of June One thousand seven Hundred and Seventy two Ann Hanson (Wife of Robert Hanson who had deverted her) was removed from the parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul's London to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in the present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may be then directed to Attend

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Jerh. Hawkes R Ravenshaw Greenwollers} Churchwardens

Wm: Jones James Ray Overseers of the Poor
