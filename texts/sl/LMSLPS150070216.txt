The Humble petr. of Tho. Holiland & his wife

Tho Holyland

To the Rt. Honble. the Lord Maior & the Rest of his Majsts. Justices of ye peace in this Honrble Court at ye Sesions house in the old Baily

The Humble petr. of Tho: Holiland & Margarett his wife

Humbley sheweth That yor. petrs. Being Comitted on Suspision for Cliping & Coyning Etc.

Yor Petrs. Humbley prays that they may be brought to tryall this Sessions or discharged as this Honrble Court shall think Fitt

And yor petrs Shall pray
