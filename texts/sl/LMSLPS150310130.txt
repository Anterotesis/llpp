To the Rt. Honrble Sr George Thorold Knt and Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the aid City in their general quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Bartholomew the great London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of two peace for the City of London (Quor Unus) bearing date the twelfth day of April instant Elizabeth Heathcote a Lunatick was removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London by the name of the parish of Saint Giles Criplgt extra London) unto the said Parish of St Bartholomew the great London alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petrs Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom and pray That your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Criplegate may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this appeal

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
