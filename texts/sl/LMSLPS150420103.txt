To the Rt Worspll. His Majesties Justices of the peace for Citty of London in their Generall Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlx.

Sheweth That by an Order & under the hands & Seales of Sr. William Thomson & Sr. William Willers, Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. Citty of London (quorn. Unus) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of January last, William Hall & Hannah his Wife wer Removed from the parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London, to the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in ye. County of Middlx. as the place of their Last Legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves agreived by the Judgmt. of the said Two Justices of the peace & Appeale to this Court against the Same; & Humble Pray Yor. Wroshps. will be pleased to appoint a day to hear & Determine the Same

And Yr. petrs. shall ever pray Etc
