To the Right Honourable James Townsend Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Liberties thereof in their General Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bromley in the County of Hent

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of William Nash Esquire and Sir Richard Glyn two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Liberties thereof bearing Date the 15th Day of October last past Edward Payne aged about Ten Years was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great London to the said Parish of Bromley as the Place of his last legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and humbly appeal against the said Warrant Order

Therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present General Sessions to hear and determine this our Appeal.
