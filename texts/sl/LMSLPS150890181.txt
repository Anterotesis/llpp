To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London at the General [---] Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens Over Seers of the Poor of the [...] of Mile End New Town in The Parish of Saint Dunstan Stephney otherwise steben health in The County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Selas of the Right Honourable Sir James Esdaile Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and Tho Wright Esqr. one of the alderman of the said city bearing Data the Twenty Second Day of January One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Eight Dorothy Thompson aged Seven years and upwards and Charlotte Thompson aged five years and upwards the two Daughters of Joseph Thompson who hath ordered them by Elizabeth his wife of deceased were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Stephen without Aldersgate London to the Said Hamlet of peace End New Town as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Dorothy Thompson and Charlotte Thompson

That Your Petitioner's is conceive themselves aggrieved by the said adjudication of the said Two Justices and the said order, and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and humbly [...] That this Honorable bourn will be pleaced to appeal seventh Day of the Present Session for the hearing and Determination of the said appeal and to who that the Church Wardens and the Over Seer of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Boloph with in the and also present concerned to them said the Court
