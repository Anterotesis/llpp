To the Right Honble Sr Richard Hoare Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Oversees of the poor of the parish of St Dinonis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hand and Seale of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quorum bearing date the Second Day of July last pass Joanna Medison wife of Thomas Medison was removed from the parish of St Buttolph without Bishopsgate London [...] into the said parish of St Denis [---] [...] Backchurch alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legall settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to this court therefrom
