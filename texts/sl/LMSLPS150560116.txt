To the Right Honoble Henry. Marshall Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of [...] his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London & Libertys thereof in General Quarter Session at the Guild Hall of the sd. City Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by as Order under the hands & Seales of The Rt. Honoble Henry Marshall Esqr. & Sr. Wm. Billers Knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eleventh Day of April Instant Mary Boyd Wido. of John Boyd was removed from the Parish of St. Faith London to the sd. Parish of St Clemt. Danes as the Place of her last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and agt. the sd. Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session to hear & Determine the Matter touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Wm Chimmow Agt. for the Appellts.}
