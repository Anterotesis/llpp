To the Right Honble Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesties Justices in their General Session of Goal Delivery for the County of Middx Assembled

The Humble prayer of David Davids

Sheweth That the said David Davids together with Abraham Davids and Thomas Doyle by Warrant under the Hand and Seal of Saunders Welch Esqr. one of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx bearing date the 20th. day of this Instant October were Committed to his Majesties Goal of Newgate charged on the Oath of Thomas Crossland and Joseph Woolf for feloniously taking and carrying away out of the Warehouse of the said Thomas Crossland a Quantity of Rum to the Amount of Forty Gallons and upwards the Property of the said Thomas Crossland against the peace Etc

That the said David Davids is intirely Innocent of the sd. Offence

The said David Davids therefore humbly prays that he may be Tried Bailed or Discharged at this present Session of Goal Delivery according to the Statute in that case made and provided

David Davis
