To the Right Hon Marshe Dickinson Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and To the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same City in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chigwell in the County of Essex.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of your Lordship and the Rt Worshipful Robt. Alsop Esqr One of the Aldn. of this honble City dated the 15th. Day of December last Joseph Josiah Dugard Elizabeth his Wife & their two Children James Aged two years and John aged nine Weeks as therein mentioned, were removed from the Precinct of White Fryers London to your petitioners said Parish as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners humbly Concieving themselves agrieved by the said Order of removal Appeal to this Honble. Court against the same

And humbly pray such relief in the Premes as to Your Lordp shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Mr. Southouse Milk Street Agent.
