To the Right Honble: The Lord Mayor and the Rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at Guildhall in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Walbrovk in the City of London.

Sheweth That The Right Honble: Robert Peckham Esqr. Lord Mayor and Thomas Wright Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London by an Order under their Respective hands and Seals bearing date the Twenty third Day of February 1784 Directed to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Bothaw in Dowgate in the City of London Did thereby require them to Remove and Convey Martha the Wife of Henry Brooks into the said Parish of Saint Stephen in Walbrook.

That your Petitioners apprehend and are advised that they are greatly aggrieved by the said Order of Removal, And

Therefore your Petitioners humbly Appeal against the Same and pray That this Honble: Court will make Such order therein as shall be just and Reasonable.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
