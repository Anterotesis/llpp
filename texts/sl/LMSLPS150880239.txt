To the right Worship full his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their Generall Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County Of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a warrant, Order, or Vagrant Pass under the hand and seal of William Lee Esquire One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said City of London bearing date the Eleventh day of March One thousand, and Seven hundred and Seventy seven Mary Ball was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Michael Bassishaw in the City of London aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlements Of the said Mary Ball.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order Or Vagrant Pass. Do humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc

Jn Peare Solr. for appellts.
