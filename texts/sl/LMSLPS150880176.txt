To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of the Justices of the City of London

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchers in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order as Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable John Sawbridge Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sir Walter Rawlinson Knight two of this Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (bearing date the 5th day of July 1776 Thomas Broker and his three Children Jane aged thirteen Years Mary aged Eleven Years and Sarah aged Eight years were removed and conveyed from and out of the [...] Precinct of White Fryers in the city of London to [...]

legal Settlement Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said order on Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be [...] adjourned and that a day may be appointed in the next Sessions for the hearing and [...] ing thereof
