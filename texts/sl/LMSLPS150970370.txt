To the Right Honorable the Lord Mayor Recorder and Aldermen at their General Quarter of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter near [---]

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Brass Crosby and Thomas Skinner two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof dated the fifth day of September 1786 Mary Phmur Singlewoman upon the Oath of the said Mary did adjudge the last legal Settlement of the said Mary to be in the said Parish of Saint Ann in Blackfriars.

That by virtue of such last mentioned Order the said Mary Plomer was accordingly removed to the said Parish of Saint Ann Blackfriers

Your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the above mentioned Order humbly pray Your

That the said Order may be quashed.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
