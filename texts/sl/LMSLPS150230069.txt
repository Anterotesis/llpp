1712 21/11 To the Rt. Honble the Lord Mayr. & the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Giles's Cripplegte. London

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Ordr. dated the 21th. day of January last past under the Hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor unus) Robert Allcock Carpentr. with Mary his wife & Anne their daughter abt two years of age were removed from the Parish of St. Bridgett ats Brides London to the said Parish of St. Giles's Cripplegate as the place of last legal Settlemt. of the said Robert Allcock

That yor. Peticoner find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or order of the said Two Justices,

Werefore yor. Peticonrs. doe humbly Appeale agnt the same to this Honble Court, and Pray for such relief therein as to this Court shall seem meet Etc

And yor Peticonrs Etc

St. Gyles Cripplegate Appeale agt St. Brides.

Adjorned to next Ten

14:9: Appeal Allowed Churchwardens St. Bridges not appearing
