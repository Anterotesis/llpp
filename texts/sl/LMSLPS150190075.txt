To the Rt. Honoble. Sr. William Withers Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of ye Peace In the General Quartr. Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of Alhallows the Greate

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Hunt wth. her child by virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace wth. in the City of London (One being of the Quor) bearing date the 21th. day of May 1708 wors removed from the parish of St Andrew wondrobe to the said parish of Greate Alhallowes Aleadging the said parish is the place of the last legall settlement of the said Elizth. wth her late husband Edward Hunt whoe hood as a servt. in the sd. parish

That yor petr doubt not but to prove That the said Edward Hurst never had any legall settlement in the said parish of Greate Allhallowes whereby They remove themselves aggrived by the indgemt. of the said two Justices And doe humbly appeale to yor. worpps.. therefrom

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray yor. Honor. & worpps wilbe pleased to heare them in the prmisses And order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poors of the said parish of Gt. Andrew Wardrobe To appeare here before yor. And heare & abide the determinacon of yor. Honor & Worpps touching this Appeal

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
