Bridewell Prcinct agt. St. John Zachary Appeale

To the Rt. Honrble Sr Samuell Gerrard Barnoll Lord Maior of the Citty of London and to the Rt. Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Chappell Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of Bridewell Prcinct London

Sheweth

That one Andra. Collins Widdow and by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & sales of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Second Day of February last Past was removed from the Parish of St. John Zachary London to the sid precinct of Bridewell London alleadgeing the said Princt to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Andra Collins

That your Petitioners doubt not but to prove that the Said Andra Collins never had any legall Settlement in the said Prcinct of Bridewell whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Twoe Justices and doe humbly appeale to yor Worpps therefrom

Your Petitoners therefore humbly pray your Honr. and Worppl will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Sd Parish of St. John Zachary may appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of Your Honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale.

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.
