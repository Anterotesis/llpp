To the Rt Honorble: Sr. Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of Her Majestys Justices of the Peace of the same City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Olaves Hartstreet London

Sheweth That one Richard Jennings an Infant aged about four years by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one King of the Quorn) bearing Date the Thirteenth Day of May last past was removed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate unto the said parish of St Olaves Hartstreet London alleadging the said place to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Richard Jennings

That your petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Richard Jennings never had any legal settlement in the said parish of St. Olaves Hartstreet whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate may appear before you an hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Wopps touching this appeal

And they Shall ever Pray Etc
