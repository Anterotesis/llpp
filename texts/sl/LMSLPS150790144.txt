To the Right Honourable Thomas Harley Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesy's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hand and Seal of the Right Honourable Thomas Harley Lord Mayor of the City of London one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace fot the said City bearing Date the twenty third Day of November last Elizabeth Sermon a Rogue and Vagabond was conveyed from the City of London to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement Where by Your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to Your Wordhips against the said Pass Warrant And Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal according to Law and Justice

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc:

Benjn. Gee W Williams} Church Wardens

Geo: Soward John Dorman John Milbourne Richd. Nowlson Overseers of the Poor
