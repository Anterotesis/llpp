To the Right Honourable Robt. Alsop Esqr. Lord Mayor and to the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Thos. Winterbottom Esqr. late Lord Mayor & Thos. Chitty Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 20th. Day of May last Priscilla Wordan wife of Edward Wordan who is run away from her and their Son Charles aged fifteen Years were Removed from the Parish of St. Bennet Pauls wharf London to the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx whereby your Petitioner Conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do: Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the sd. Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this City and Liberties

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
