To the Right Honble Sir Watkin Lewes Knight Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Sales of Sir Watkin Lewes Knight Lord Mayor and Evan Pugh Esquire two of his Majety's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and Liberties thereof, one being of the Quorum, bearing date the Twentieth day of July last Mary the Daughter of John Low a Soldier by Mary his late Wife was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Bennett Pauls Wharf in the said City to the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner's are, as they conceive, Aggrieved

Your Petitioner's therefore humbly Appeal in this Court against the [---] Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. will pray Etc

Herbt. Robertson Sollr. for the Appellts.
