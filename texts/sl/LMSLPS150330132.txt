To the Right Honoble Sr. William Stewart Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpfull the rest of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

Sheweth. The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Atthill London

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Liberties thereof (Quor un) bearing Date the Twelfth day of October last past Sarah Geary Wife of Robert Geary with her Two Children (vizt) Richard aged about three and Sarah about Two years were removed from the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London unto the said Parish of St. Mary Atthill London Alledging the said Parish of St. Mary Atthill to be the place of their last legall Settlement.

That yor. Peticoners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of ye. said Two Justices, Do humbly Appeale to your Lords and Wops therefrom, And pray That your Lords and Worps will be pleased to hear them concerniing the Prmisses. And [...] that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London may appear before you, and hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of yor Lords & Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
