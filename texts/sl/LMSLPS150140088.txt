[...] the right Honrble. Sr Saml Dashwood Knt Lord Major of the Citty of London and to the right Worshipll the Justice. of peace in the generall quarter Sessions Assembled the

Sheweth Humble Peticon of Alvernia Jackson a poor Prisonr on the Com [...] side of the Fleet Prison

Theof yor Peticonr hath been acutally a prisonr in the prison of the Fleet under the Custody of Willm Weeden Ford Esqr the keeper of the said Prison before the first day of Janr 1709 and is still deceyned there at the suit of Jonas Turner Creditor for debt or damages And not haveing where wth to manteine himselfe in the sd prison

3.22.1702 Charged wth. above 320 Yor Peticonr suit therefore is that this Court will be pleased to order that the said Willim Creeden Ford the keep of the said Prison of a Certaine day and time [...] to bring yor Peticonr before you with a Coppy of the Causes of his Imprisenmt the she may take the oath undercond in the late Act of Parliamt made for releife of poore Prisonrs for debt, And that the said Credict may be ordered then and there alsoe to appear and shew cause whythe said Alveria Jackson should not be discharged from her Imprisonmt according to the said act and that he may be discharged accordingly

And yr Peticonr will ever pray Etc
