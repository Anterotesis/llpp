To the Right Honoble. Sr. Thomas Rawlinson Knt. Lord Maior of the City of London. and to the Rt. Worll. The rest of Her Maties. Justices of the Peace In the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petiton and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Austins London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and seales of Twoe of Her Maties Justices of the Peace (Quox unus) dated the 3d. day of May Anno Din 1706 One Mary Hounsell Widdow was Sent by the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Alhallowes onthe Wall London to Yor. Petr. to be provided for upon the Oath of the Said Margarett That Shee Lived in Yor Petr. Parish Severall days before and after May 1685. and hath not had any legall Settlement since

Whereas in Truth the said Margt. Hounsell was never 40 days in yor. Petr. Parish before May 1685. But hath Since Lived Severall Yeares in the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall where Shee was Rated to Church & Poore

Yor. Petr. humbly Pray Yor. Honor. & Worppl. to hear them In the Prmisses And Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall London To appear here before You To shew Cause why they should not Provide for the Said Margaret Hounsell And alsoe to send the said Margaret Hounsell to the said Parish of Alhallowes on the Wall To be by them kept and provided for till They can Free themselves of the Charge thereof by due Course of Law.

And Yor. Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
