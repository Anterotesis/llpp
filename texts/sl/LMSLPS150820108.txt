To the Right Honourable Brass Crosby Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful Justices of the Peace at the Quarter Session of the Peace now assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the [...] of the Parish of Saint Maryle Bew London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London (our being of the Quorum) bearing date the 11th. day of January Instant Jane Harding Singlewoman was removed from and out of the Priest of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London into the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone London as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant And humbly appeal against the same And pray that this Honourable Court will be pleased to adjourn the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeal until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said City and to make such Order in the Bruises as shall seem meet.

John Nix Vestry clerk & Sollicitor for the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone.
