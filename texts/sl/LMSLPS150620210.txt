To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th. Day of August 1750 Charles Coventry and Alice his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Ann in Black Fryars in the said City to the said Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook as the place of their last legal Settlement By which your Petitioners Humbly conceive themselves Aggrieved And Appeal to this Court against the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the Matters touching this Appeal and give them such Relief in the Premises as to Your Wrships shall seem Just and Meet

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc
