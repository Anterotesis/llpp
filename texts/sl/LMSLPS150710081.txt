To the Right Honoble. Sir Thomas Chilly Knight Lord Mayor and the Right Wordshipful the Recorder and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honoble: Sir Thomas Chitty Knight Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for she said City and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Twenty Ninth day of last past Ann Johnson (who is alleged by the said Order to be Ann Murray wife of James Murray and gone from her) was removed and Conodged from and out of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great London into the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate as the place of her last legal settlement.

James Clare Sollr for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate }

Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray such relief in the Premis as to this Honble. Court shall seen meet

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
