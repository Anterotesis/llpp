To the Right Honble Marshe Dickinson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London Sr. William Moreton Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Trinity Minories in the Liberty of This Majestys Tower of London

Sheweth That by a Vagrant Passor Warrant under the hand and Seal of the said Lord Mayor of the City of London Directed to John Ebthorp of the London Workhouse and requiring him to Convey Ann Martin Singlewoman (apprehended as a Rogue and Vagabond in the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate London) to the said Parish of Trinity Minories (in the said Pass said to be in the County of Middlesex) as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That the said Ann Martin hath been accordingly removed in Pursuance of the said Pass or Warrt. to the said Parish of Trinity Minories.

That Your Petitioners and Appellts. apprehend themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass or Warrant and do Appeal agt. the same

Your Petitioners & Appeallts. therefore humbly Pray this Court to grant them such Relief as to your Worships shall seem just in and touching the Premisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate may (upon having notice for that Purpose) attend this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Jno. Billington

Att. for Appellts. Octr. ye. 24th. 1757.
