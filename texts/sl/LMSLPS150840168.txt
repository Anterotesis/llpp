To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Nash (Mayor) and John Shakespear Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty third day of June last One Sarah Young aged Nine years was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and Appeals against the same

Therefore your Petitioners humbly pray [---] your Worships to appoint a Day in [---] this present Session to Hear and Determine this their' Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno. Pratt} Churchwarden

John Hewitt Js. Jones Js. Showell} Overseers
