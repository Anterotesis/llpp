3/2 To the Rt Honoble Sr. George Thorold Knt. & Baront. Lord Mayr of the City of London, And to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the City in their Genrall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Third Day of February last past, Mary Wright Widow with one Child named Luke about Fifteen Months old were Removed from the Parish of Alhallows on London Wall unto the said Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London, Alledging the said Parish of St Andrew Undershaft he be the Place of their past legal Settlement.

That yor Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices, Do appeale to yor Lord & Worps therefrom & humbly pray That your Lordp & Worps with be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses, And that the Churchwarderns & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Alhallows on London Wall may appear before you, & hear and abide the Judgmt. & Determination of yor Lordp & Worps touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
