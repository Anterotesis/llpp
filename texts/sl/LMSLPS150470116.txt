To the Right Honoble Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll. the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter le Poor London

Sheweth. That by an Order Dated the Thirteenth day of November last under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honoble Sr. John Thompson Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and Sr. Henry Hankey Knt. and Alderman of the said City Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) One Jane Blake Widdow and Wm. Blake her Son aged four Years and Mary her Daughter aged One year and a halfe Were passed from the parish of St. Bottolph without Aldgate London to the said Parish of St. Peter le Poor London as the place of their last legall Settlement

That the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of St. Peter le poor Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore Appeal from the same to this Court and humbly Pray to be releived against the said Order.
