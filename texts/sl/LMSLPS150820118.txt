To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the City of London Assembled at their General Session holden at Guild Hall London in and for the said City.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden's and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order of Removal bearing date the Ninth Day of May one Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy One under the Hands and Seals of William Stephenson and Thomas Hallifax two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) Ann Gilbert Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopgate London to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal do humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said, Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopsgate London, and all other person's concerned to attend this Court some Day the next Session, to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal And that your Petitioners may have such Relief therein as to Justice and Law doth Appertain.

And your Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc

Jno. Hodgson [...] B Mangles} Church Wardens

Tho Preswick Thos. Willis} Overseers
