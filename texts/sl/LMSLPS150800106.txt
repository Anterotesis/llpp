To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the City of London in their Session of the Peace assembled at the Guild Hall London

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble Samuel Turner Mayor of the said City and William Nash Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof, one of the Quorum, bearing date the Eleventh Day of February One thousand Seven hundred and Sixty Nine William Ingram was removed from the Parish of Saint Alban in Woodstreet[---] London to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved And appeal to this Court against the same And desire to be relieved therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray etc Jno. Austin Atty. for the Appellts.
