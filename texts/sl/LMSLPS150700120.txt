To the Right Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant on Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Richard Glynn and Francis Cockayne Esqr. two of HIs Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of August last past Margaret Fitzgerald aged twelve years Edward Fitzgerald aged Ten Years Elizabeth Fitzgerald aged five Years and Jane Fitzgerald aged Three Years Children of Edward Fitzgerald deceased by Margaret his Wife (who is run away from her said Children) were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West London to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

The Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray your Worships to Relieve them in the Premisses

And the Petrs. as in duty bound will Pray Etc:

G Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
