To the right honble. Sr. Robert Beachcroft Knight Lord Major of the City of London and To the right worshipfull the rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

26/11 1712 The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchers London

Sheweth That one Elinor Fletcher singlewoman by vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quor) bearing date the six and twentieth day of January 1711 was removed from the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London to the said Parish of St. Sepulchers London alleadging the said Parish is the Place of the Legall Settlement of the said Elinor Fletcher.

That Your Peticoners Doubt not to prove the said Elinor Fletcher had no Legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Sepulchers London whereby they Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices and Doe humbly Appeal to Your Worship therefrom.

Your Petitioners Therefore humbly Pray Your Honour and Worships well be Pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London to appear here before You and hear and abide the Determinacon of Your Honrs. and Worships touching this appeall

And Your Petitioners (as induty bound) Shall ever Pray Etc
