To the right Honoble Sr. John Williams Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City at the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled at the Guildhall of the said City

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of [...] Christ Church in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order pass Warrant bearing date the twenty second day of September last under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sr. John Williams Knight Lord Mayor of the said City of London and Sr. Henry Hankey Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) William Sexton Elizabeth his Wife and Elizabeth their Daughter aged Ten Weeks were removed from the parish of St. Buttolph Bishopsgate London th the said Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex adjudging the said Parish of Christ Church [...] at the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said William Sexton Elizabeth his wife and Elizabeth their Daughter.

That Your Petitioner conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Order or pass Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Therefore humbly Appeale to this Honourable Court Against ye. Same
