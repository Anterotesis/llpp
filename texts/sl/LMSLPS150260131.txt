To the Rt. Honble Sr. Wm. Humfreys Knt. & Burront. Lord Mayr. of the City of London and to the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal green in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth, That by virtue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrt. dated the Second day of Decembr. last under the hands & Seales of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City (Quor unus) William Price with a woman therein call'd Mary his wife and a Male Child named John aged five Months called his son; were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London to yor. Peticonrs. said Hamlet of Bethnal green as the place of their last legal Settlement.

And forasmuch as yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrt. or Ordr. of the said Justices. they humbly Appeal agnt. the same to this Honble Court, and pray for such releif in the prmisses as to this Court shall seem meet Etc

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
