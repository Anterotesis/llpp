To the Rt. Worpll her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the [...] Citty of London at their Generall Quarter Session

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Margt: Westmr:

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date 26th. of August last under the hands Seales of St: Richard hoare and Sr: William Withers two of her Majts: Justices of the peace for the Citty of London & Libertys thereof One Lucy Stone and [...] John Stone her son were removed from the Parish of St: Giles Criplegate London unto your Petrs Said Parish as the place of their last legall Settlemt: by wch Order your Petrs: humbly conceive, that they are wronged

Your Petrs humbly appeal to your Worps against the said Orders and pray your Worps to appoint a time to hear & determine the same

And yor: Petrs: shall pray Etc

Emery Angus Henry Priest John Lony} Overseers of Poor
