To the right honble. Sr. Charles Peers knight Lord Maior of the City of London, and to the right worll. the rest of this Maties Justices of the Peace in the generall Quarter Sessions asembled.

The humble peticion and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ther Parish of St Sepulchers London.

Sheweth That one Eleanor Harris by vertue of Warrt. under the hands and seals of two of his Maties. Justices of the peace with in the City of London (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Tenth day of May now last past, was removed from the Parish of St Andrew Holborne London to the parish of St Sepluchres London alleading the said parish is the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Eleanor Harris.

that yor, Petrs. Doubt not prove that the said Eleanor Harris had noe legall Settlement in the said Parish of St Sepulchres London whereby they Conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgemt. of the said two Justices, And doe humbly appeale to yor. Honor. and Worps. thereupon

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly pray yor, Honor. and Worps. will be pleased to here them in the prmisses and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Andrew Holborne London to hear & abide the Determinacon of yor. honoble. & worps. touching this Appeale.

And yor. peticonrs shall or pray Etc

Walter Bott Chillard or Daniell Hetey Ch Wardens Jno Leaper Overseer
