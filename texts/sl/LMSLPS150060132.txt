To the Right Honoble. Sr. Thomas Lane Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And alsoe to the Rights Worll. the rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace att the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of Wapping Stepney in ye Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Thomas Taylor son of Phebe Taylor who was the wife of Richd. Taylor was by Warrant or Pass under the hands & Seales of twoe of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London one being of the Quor Passed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Buttolphs Algt. in the same City to the Hamlett of Wapping aforesaid suggesting yt. the said Thomas Taylor was borne there Who was in truth the said Richard Taylor the Father was never settled in the said Hamlett or Parish.

Yor. Petr. humbly Conceiveing themselves Aggreived by the said Warrt. and Doe humbly Appeale to this Honoble. Courte and Pray and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Buttolphs Aldgate may Attend this Honoble. Courte to try the said Settlement.

And yor. Petr shall Ever Pray Etc.

Warrant confirmed
