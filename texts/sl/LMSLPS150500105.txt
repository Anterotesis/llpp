To the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esq Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Worspfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the sd. City & Liberties thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael le Quern London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberties (Quorum unus) bearing date the first day of August last past Ann Orme Widow of Edward Orme deceased & her Seven Children Mary Aged Fourteen years Richard Aged Thirteen years Sarah Aged Twelve years John Aged Four years James Hyatt Aged Three year Ann Aged One year & Joseph Aged One Month were lately removed from the Parish of Saint Gyles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Michael le Quern, London as the place of their last Legall Settlement

Your Petrs. thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to your Lordship & Worships against the said Order & humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to your Lordship & Worships shall seem meet

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc,
