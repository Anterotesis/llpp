To the Right [...] Honble Sr John Thompson Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of ye Worpfull the Justices of the peace At the General Quarter Sessions of ye Peace now Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St: Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London one being of th Quorum bearing Date the 17th: day of December 1736: Whereby Mary Poston Widow was removed from & out of the parish of St: Michael at Queenhith London into our said parish of Saint Mary Abchurch London as the place of her [---] legall Settlement

Yor Petrs: therefore himself Conceive themselves Aggrived by the sd. Order or Pass Warrant And humbly Appeal agt. the Same

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray
