To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesties Justices of the peace of the said City At their next General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Little Warley in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Sir Watkin Lewes Knight and Evan Pugh Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of April Instant Mary (therein called) the Wife of William Mathews a Sailor and their three Children Namely Mary Ann Aged Ten Years William Aged Seven Years and Margaret Aged two Years were removed from the parish of Saint Andrew Hubbard London unto the said Parish of Little Warley in the County of Essex as the places of their legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order, Appeal to this Honourable Court against the same And humbly Pray Your Worships to Appoint a day for to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
