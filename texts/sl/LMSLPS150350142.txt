1/6 To the Right Honable Sir Peter Delme Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpfull the rest of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anne Black fryers London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London the Libties thereof (Quorum unus) bearing Date the first Day of June last past, Thomas Hutchenson an Infant aged about Six Years as removed from the Parish of St. Mary Somersett London to the Parish of St. Anne Blackfryers London aforesaid alledging the said Parish of St Anne Black fryers to be the Place of his last legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly Appeale to your Lordship & Worps therefrom And humbly pray that yor Lordship & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Somer Sett London may appear before you & hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of yor. Lord & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
