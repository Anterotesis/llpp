To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof, at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace now assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Spittle Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Sr. George Merttins Knt: Lord Mayor, and Sr. Edward Baker Knt: two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 6th day of March last Martha Guidney the wife iof Edward Guidney and their two Children (Vizt.) Joseph aged about three years, and Elizabeth aged about one year were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticonr. conceive themselves aggrieved by the order and Judgment of the said two Justices, and do therefore humbly appeal against the same praying such relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgement Shall Seem just
