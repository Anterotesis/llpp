To: the Right Honble Sr: Richard Hoar Knight Lord maior of the City of London and to the Right Worpll the rest of her majestys Justices. of the peace in the generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

the humble petition and appeale of the Chur- ch wardens and overseers of the parish of trinity parish London

Sheweth That moses greenaway and his wife and three Children by virtue of a warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her majesties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quom bearing date the thirtenth day of october 1712 was removed from the parish of St Michael at Gnern London into the Said parrish of trinity London aleadging ye said parrish to be the plane of ye Last legall Settlement of the said moses green away wife and Children

That your petitioners doupt not but to prove that the said moses greenaway wife and Children never had any legall Settlement in ye said parishes of trinity whereby they conceive themselves agrevied by ye said warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Hoard and worships therefrom

Your Petitioners therfore most humbly pray your Honr. and worpps will be pleased to hear them in the premesses and that the owersers of the said pari [...] of St. Michael at Quern may appear before your Honrs: and worpps touching this appeale

And your Petionars Shall ever pray Etc
