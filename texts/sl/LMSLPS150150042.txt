To ye Rt. Honble Sr. Jno: Parsons Knt: Ld Mayr: of ye. Citty of London & to ye rest of ye. Honble Bench of Justice sitting at Justice Hall in ye old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Mary Elderton als Goodman A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That yor petr. was ye 6th. of November last Comitted to Newgate Charg'd to have marryed on Jno. Dickins her former husband Living

Yor Petr. prayes this Honble Court to be tryed this sessions or [...] admitted to baile as ye Act of Parliamt. directs

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Prayer of Mary Elderton als Goldman
