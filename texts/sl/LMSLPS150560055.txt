1745 [---] To the Rt. Honble: Henry Marshall Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at the Guildhall in and for the said City assembled

The humble Petiton & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said City

Sheweth That William Pawley of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex Vintner hath unlawfully begotten on the body of one Elizabeth Hill Singlewoman a Male Bastard Child which hath been lately born a Bastard in the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn and is become chargeable to the Parishioners of the said Parish and that the said William Pawley the of sufficient Ability to maintain such Bastard Child refuses to do

Yor. Petitioners therefore Appeal to this Honble. Court And may such Releif in the Premisses as to your Lordship & Worship shall seem meet

Thos: Maning Church Warden}

John Kerry Ben Munn} Overseers of ye Poor
