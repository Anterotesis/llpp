To the Rt. Honble Sr. Owen Buckingham Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London The humble Petition of Elizabeth Lucie.

Humbly Sheweth That Sometime past your Petr was by the Worpll Justice Constable Comitted to How Prison [...] where the now Remaines For Holloing upon the Oath of One Martha Williams.

That your Petr hath Four Children to mainteyne and was never concerned in any such matter of which the sd. Martha [---] Williams has Sworne And forasmuch as the sd [...] Williams being a Notorius Offender & a pson knowne to be perjuced & haveing formerly Robbed your Petr. and hath Sworne agt. your Petr.

Yor. Petr therefore most humbly Prayes yor Honour wil be pleased to Grant that the may this pvdt Sessions take her Tryall [...] the sd. [...]
