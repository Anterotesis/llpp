To the Rt. Honble. Slingsby Bethell Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of ye Comissioners of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the 5d. City and for the County of Middx in Session Assembled.

Jan Session 1756 GD S.M 123 The Humble Petitioner of Alexander Thompson now a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was on the 20th day of December 1755 by Virtue of Warrant under the hand and Seal of Saunders Welch Esqr. Committed to New Prison the said Warrant Setting forth that Yor. petr: had not Surrendered himself to the Comissioners named in a Commission of Bankruptey awarded against your petr. within the time mentioned in the Gazette for that Purpose with intent to defraud the Creditors of your sd. Petitioner against the Statute

And on the 23d. day of the said Month your petr. was Detained by Warrant under the hand and Seal of John Fielding Esqr. for feloniously Wilfully & maliciously Setting fire to a Certain Dwelling house in Berry Street in the parish of St. James Westminster

That Yor. Petitioner hath since been removed from the said New prison to his Majestys sd. Gaol of Newgate where he now remains to take his Trial for the said Offences

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that he may at this present Session be Tried Bailed or Discharged of the Offences aforesaid according to the form of the Statute in that Case made and provided.

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray &.

Alwr. Thompson
