To the Right Honourable Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the same city their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew by the Wardrobe [...] London

Sheweth That by the Order or Pass Warrant hereunto annexed Ann Lawrence therein named together, with the said three Children therein also named were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph [---] Aldersgate London into Your Petitioners said Parish as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners Concieving themselves Aggireved thereby do humbly Appeal against the same And pray Such Relief in the Premises as to your Lordship & this Honr Court shall seem meet

And forasmuch as the Said Warrant was [...] not shewn & delivered to Your Petitioner. until the Day of this Present Sessions and are not now ready with their Witnesses Your Petitioners humbly Pray that the hearing & determination of this their said Appeal may be adjourned unto the next General Quarter Session of the Peace for this Honble City

J Jefferson for Appellts.

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
