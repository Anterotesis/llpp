To The Right Honourable The Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the same City at the General quarter Session of the peace now assembled.

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Sir Samuel Fludyer Bart. & Sir Thomas Chitty Knight two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City & Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 17th. Day of September 1762. Titus Goldsmith aged Eleven Years Was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen in Old Fishstreet London to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order And therefore Humbly Appeal against the same And Pray this Honourable Court to Grant them such relief in the Premises as shall be thought most Equitable

And your Petrs. shall ever pray
