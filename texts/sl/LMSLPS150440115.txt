To the Right Honourable the Lord Major [...] and others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and the Libertys thereof in Quarter Sessions at the Guildhall of the said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Strand in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John [...] Barber Esqr. Lord Major of the said City and Sr. Richard Brocas Knight and Alderman two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Libertys thereof (Quorum unus) bearing Date the Fifth Day of March last Sarah the Daughters of Nathaniel Tallant was removed from the Prescinct of White Fryers London to the said Parish of St. Mary Le Strand as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court agt. the same and desire to be Relieved therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
