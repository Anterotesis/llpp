To the Rt Honrble St William Humfryes Knt & Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the same City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margarett Newfishstreet London

Sheweth That one Martha Broughton by virtue of a Warrant made the hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quom) bearing date the 26th Day of January last past was sent and delivered unto the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Margarett Newfishstreet London alleadging the said parish to be the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Martha Broughton

That your Petitrs doubt not but to prove That the said Martha Broughton never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Margarett Newfishstreet whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Yor Petrs therefore most humbly prays your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmises and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish St Peter the poor may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honr and Worpps touchng this appeal.

And Your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
