To the Right honble the Lord Mayor and the Worshipfull Aldermen his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace held at Guild hall in and for the City of London Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Hubbard in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir Charles Asgill Knight Late Lord Mayor of the said City and Thomas Cokayne Esqr. Alderman of the said City Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City (both being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of October Last John Mount was removed from the Parish of Christ Church London to the said Parish of Saint Andrews Hubbard as the place of his Last Legal, Settlement Whereof your Petitioner Conceive themselves to be Agreived

Therefore they Humbly appeal to Yr. Worships against the Judgement of the sd. Two Justices and pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoints some day this present Sessions to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeal And Your Petitioners shall ever pray etc.
