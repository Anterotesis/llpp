To the Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City of London at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. Tha by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Alsop and Francis Cokayne Esquires two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing Date the Seventh day of May last Benjamin Stanley, Esther his Wife and his two Children Mary Aged thirteen Years by a former Wife and Benjamin aged Nine Months by the said Esther were Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate London to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and do therefore Appeal against the Same and Pray your Worshipps to make such Order touching and concerning the Premisses as to your Worshipps shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

G. Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
