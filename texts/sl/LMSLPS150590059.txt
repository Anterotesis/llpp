London To the Right Honourable Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace For the said City and Liberties thereof Assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of St Olave Silver Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of August one Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty Eight Mercy Richards Widdow of William Richards deceas'd and their Child William aged three years were removed From the Parish of St Alban Woodstreet London to the said parish of St Olave Silver Street London Alledging the last Legal Settlement of the said Mercy Richards and her said Child to be in the said parish of St Olave Silver Street London by which Order of the said two Justices your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggrieved and Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court and pray to be Relieved in the Premisses and your petitioners will pray

Churchwardns Overseers} Richd. Smith John Lewgrove William Allan Hen Gretton
