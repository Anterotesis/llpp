To the Rt. Honoble Sr. Wm. Stewart Knt. Mayor & the Rt. Worll. the rest of his Maties. Justices of the Peace At the General Quarter Sessions Assembeld.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St. Bennet Pauls- wharfe London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrt. or Order under the Hands & Seals of Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace wth. in the City of London (Quor unus) bearing date the Thirtieth day of Time last past One Elizabeth Moor widdo. of Roger Moor wth. her two Children Elizabeth aged Six years and upwards and Mary aged three years & Upwards were removed from the parish of St. Buttolph wth. out Algate London unto the parish of St. Bennitts Paulswharfe London alledging that yor Petr. parish is the place of their last legal Settlement

yor Petrs. concerning themselves aggreived by the sd. Warrant & judgment of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal to yor. Honors & Worpps for Releif therein. And Pray they may be heard in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Buttolph wth. out Algate London be ordered to Appear before yor Honors. & Worpps. to hear & abide the judgment & determination of yor. Honors. touching their Appeal
