To the Right Honourable James Townsend Esqr. Lord Mayor and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of James Townsend Esquire and Sir Charles Asgill Barronet Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum bearing date the second day of February One thousand seven hundred and Seventy three Thomas Curtis aged thirteen years was removed from out of the Parish of Saint Ann in Blackfryers London unto the said Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Warrant humbly appeal against the same unto this Court and pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premisses as shall be thought most equitable

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Wm. Drew Solr. for Appellants.
