To the Right Honourable Micajah Perry Esqr Mayor of the City of London & the Rest of the worpfull Alderman his Brrthren in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the City of London Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Lawrence Pountney in London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Poore for the City of London one being of the Quorum bearing Date the twenty seventh day of March 1739 one Sarah James Widow of Daniel James was removed from and out of the parish of St: Michael in Crooked Lane London unto the said parish of St. Lawrence Pountney in London as the Place of here Last legall Settlement

Your Petrs. humbly conceiving them selves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Appeal Against the same and pray such Relief in the premisses as yor Lordshipp & the Court shall think fitt

And Yor Petrs shall pray Etc

Will Salt Sollr for Appellants
