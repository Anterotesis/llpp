To ye. Rt. Honble: Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to ye. Worpful ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mildred in ye. Poultry in ye. City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship & Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London, & ye. Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. twenty fourth Day of May, last past, Thomas Kilmister, aged fifty Seven Years, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London, unto ye. Parish of St. Mildred in ye. Poultry London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Mildred, to be ye. Place of his last legal Settlement.

Chas Baynbridge Atty for the Appellants } That your Petitioners conceiving themselves Agrieved, by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordship and Worships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, and that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate, London, may appear before you, to hear and abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worps, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray Etc.
