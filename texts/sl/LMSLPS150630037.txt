To the Right Honourable Thomas Winterbottom Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Best of his Majesty's Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and prayer of John Norwood

Sheweth That your petitioner on the Nineteenth day of February last was Committed or Sent to the Poultry Compter by the Worshipfull Sir Rober Ladbrooke Knight one of the Aldermen of this Honourable City on Suspition of Stealing Lead from Barber's Hall in this City upon the Oath of

Your Petitioner these fore humbly prays that he may be Tryed bailed or discharged for the Premisses according to the form of the Statute in such Case made and Provided

John Norwood
