13/8 To the Right Honoble Sr J [...] Ward Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the [...] st of his Matys Justices of the peace at the [...] Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Michael Wood Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals other of his Matys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the Thirteenth day of August last John Wilcox with Mary his wife were removed from the [---] Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London unto ye sd Pish of St Michael Wood Street London alledgeing the said Parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt & Judgment of the sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honour & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the sd Pish of St. Giles witht Cripplegate London may appear [...] force your Honr & Worps & hear & abide the Determinacons of this Court touching ye sd Appeal

And your petrs shall ever pray Etc.
