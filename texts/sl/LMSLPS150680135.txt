To the Right Honble. Marshe Dickenson Esqr Lord Mayor Sr. Wm. Moreton Knt. Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of removal under the hands and Seals of the said Lord Mayor and Robt. Alsop Esqr bearing date the seventh day of December one Thousand seven hundred and fifty Six William Kent Lucy his Wife and their two Children William aged Eight Years & Benjamin aged six Years were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate in he said City to the said Parish of St. Mathew Bethnal Green as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves to be agreived by the said Order of the said Lord Mayor and Robt. Alsop do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore humbly pray this Court will Appoint a day this present Session for the sd Churchwardens and Overseers of the [...] St. Botolph Aldgate and all other Persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the same Court touching the said Appeal and that Your Petitioners and Appellants may be relieved as this Court shall see Meet.

And Your Petitioners and Appellants shall ever pray.

Jno. Billington Agent for Appellts. January ye. 12th. 1757.
