To the Rt Honrble Sr Robert Beachcroft Knt Lord Maior of the city of London and to the Rt Worpll the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace within this City (one being of the Quoru) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of October last past One Mary Parnell Wido was removed from the Precinct of white Fryers London unto the said Parish of Allhallows Staning alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners doubt not but prove that the said Mary Parnell never had any legall Settlement in the said Parish of Allhallows Staining whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and that the Overseers of the Poor of the said Precinct of Whitefryers may appeare before you and hear and abide the determination of your Honour and Worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
