To the Right Honoble Sr. Peter Delme Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London & to the Worpfull the rest of his Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City & the Libties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter in Cheapside London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Libties thereof (Quor unus) bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of September last past John Willoughby, Sarah his Wife & Sarah their Daughter about five Months old, were removd from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London unto the said Parish of St. Peter in Cheapside London aforesaid, alledging the said Parish of St. Peter in Cheapside to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

Therefore your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the said Two Justices as aforesaid Do humbly Appeale to your Lords & Worps therefrom And humbly pray that yor. Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Premisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London may appear before you & hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Lordship & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
