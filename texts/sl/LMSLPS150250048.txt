To the Rt: Honrble Sr Samll Stanier Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of her Matres Justices for the City of London and Liberties thereof

5/12 1713 The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor on behalfe of themselve and the rest of the Inhabitants of the parrish of St Dunstans in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and seales of Sr Peter King Knt Recorder of the City and Sr Wm Withers Knt & Alderman of London two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quoram unus) under their hands and seales dated ye 5th day of December last that Ann Tomlins was a serve to one Mr Gower an Apothecary in the said parrish for a yeare and that the sd Ann Tomlins is become chargeable to the Parrish of St. Andrew's London being aggreved by the said Order doe appeale from the said Order

Yor petr humbly implores your Lordship to order the said Appeale to be heard sometime this Sessions and that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of St Andrews Holborn London may attend this Court and above such Order as shall be made by this Court

And yor Petrs shall ever Pray
