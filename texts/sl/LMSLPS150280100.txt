To the Rt Honrble Sr James Bateman Knt Lord Maior of the City of London And to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City in their general quarter Sessions assembled

14/1 The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Allhallows Barkin London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London one being of the (Quorn) bearing Date the fourteenth Day of this instant, January One Mary Rogers Singlewoman was removed from the the Parish of Christchurch London unto the parish of Allhallows Barkin aforesaid alleadging the said parish to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Your Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Order of the said two Justices Do most humbly Appeal to your Honr. and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Christchurch may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Honr and Worpps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall Ever pray Etca.
