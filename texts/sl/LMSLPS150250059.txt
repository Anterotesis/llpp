To the Right Honrble Sr. Samuel Stanier Knt Lord Major of the city of London and to the Right Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace in the general quarter sessions Assembled

1713 72/3 The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Alban in Woodstreet London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City one being of the Quorn bearing Date the 23d Day of March last past one Thomas Burtless was removed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London unto the said parish of St. Alban in Wood Street London alleadging the said parish to be the place of his last legall Settlement

That you petitioners doubt not but to prove that the said Thomas Burtless never had any legall settlement in the said Harish of St Alban Wood Street whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Honr and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Buttolph without Algate may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of ye Honr and worpps touching this Appeale

And your Petr Shall ever Pray Etc.
