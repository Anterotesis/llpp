To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor & the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace of the City of London at their General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street in the City of London

Sheweth That Thomas Renoldson and Charles Renoldson Grand Children of Thomas Renoldson of Alderman Bury in the City of London Cabinetmaker are poor impotent Children and not able to get their Living (being totally deserted) whereby they are become Chargeable to the Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street aforesaid

That the said Thomas Renoldson is the [---] Grandfather of the said Paupers and of sufficient Abilities to maintain them

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that this Court will be Pleased to make such Order in the Premisses for the Relief of your Petitioners as they shall seem meet

J. Jenings Attorney for the Petitioner
