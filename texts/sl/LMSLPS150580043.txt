To the Rt. Honble: William Benn Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London, & to the Worshipfull ye. rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. said City & ye. Liberties thereof, in their Generall Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of Christchurch in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of your Lordship & George Arnold Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City of London & the Liberties thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twentyeth Day of December last past, Mary Taylor Singlewoman, was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London, unto the Parish of Christchurch London, alledging ye. said Parish of Christchurch, to be ye. Place of her last legall Settlement.

Cha: Baynbridge Sollr. for the Appellants } That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removall, Do Appeal to your Lordship and Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, and yt. ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London may appear before you, to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of your Lordship and Worships, touching this Appeal.
