To the Worpll: her Majties Justices of the Peace at their Session at Guildhall, London

The humble Peticon of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Christian Palmer, John Palmer, Mathew Palmer Ann Palmer, and Cheynes Palmer all in fants were by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Sr: Selathiel Lovell & Sr: John Hublon two of her Majts: Justices of the peace for the sd Citty of London dated the 12th: day of June last removed from the parish of St: Mary Woolworth London to the said Parish of St: Martin in the Fields being adjudged by the said Justices to be the place of their last legall Settlemt

Wee the said Churchwardens and Overseers do think Overseers aggrieved by that are Ordr and therefore do humbly appeal from the said Order to this humble Court and pray for a time to be heard

Any yor: petr: shall ever pray
