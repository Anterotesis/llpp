To the Right Honble Sr John Ward Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to rest of his Majesties Justices of the peace at the Genl Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Michl Wood Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace within the City of London (Quor unus) bearing Date the 15th day of January last past Dorothy Beele with Patrick her Son aged about Twelve Months were Sent and Delivered by [---] the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London unto ye sd Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St. Michael Wood Street London Alledging the said parish to be place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and to most humbly Appeal to Your Honour & Worps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Your Honr & Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Bartholomew ye Great may Appear before You and hear & abide the Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
