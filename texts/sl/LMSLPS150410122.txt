2/10 To the Right Honourable Sr. Richard Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Apeall of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Garlikhill London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties (Quorum unus) bearing date the Second day of the Instant October Elizabeth Massey Wife of George Massey was lately removed from the Parish of St. Giles's without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. James Garlikhill London as the [---] Place of her Last Legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeale to your Lordship and Your Worships Against the said Order and humbly desire this Court will make such Order therein as to your Lordship and Worship's Shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
