To the Right Honourable Stephen Theodore Janssen Esquire Lord Mayor and the rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Hubbard in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of The Right Honourable Stephen Theodore Jansson Esquire Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City of London bearing date the Twenty Eighth Day of February last Ann Price Wife of Thomas Price was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to the said Parish of St. Andrew Hubbard as the place of her last legal Settlement whereof your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agreived by the said Order

Therefore they humbly Appeal against the same and Pray your Worships will be pleased to Grant them such Releif in the Premises as shall be thought most Equitable

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
