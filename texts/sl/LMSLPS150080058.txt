peticon of Jno. Wright

To the Rt. Honble: Sr: Edwd: Clarke Knt: Lord Major of the Citty of London and other his Maties Justices now sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

The humble Peticon of John Wright

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: was Comitted a Priosnr: to the Goale of Newgate by Warrant from the Worll: the Recordr: dated the Tenth day of this instant may thereby Charged on suspicon of High Treason in Counterfeiting the Coine of this Kingdome and is still remaining a Prisonr: in the said Goale

He therefore humbly prayeth that he may bee tryed (for the Crime where with he (lands charged) this prsent Sessions or that he may bee admitted to Baile as by the Law in that case is provided and that his prayer may bee Recorded

And he Shall ever pray Etc
