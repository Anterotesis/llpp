To the Rt. Honble Sr. Richd Brocas Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll the rest of his Maties Justices of the peace in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardn. and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth That your Peticoners not beng able to provide for and maintain their own poor have for about 50 Years last past by Order of this Court been releived yearly by the several parishes in the several sumes following Vizt.

£ s d

St. Michael le Quern in the sume of 5:10:= St. Stephen Wallbrook 12:=:= St. Peters Cornhill 10:=:= St. Pancras Soaper Lane 4:10 St. Mary Woolchuch 7:=:= St. Antholius 5:=:= St. Vedast als Foster 5:=:= St. Andrew Undershaft and 5:=:= St. Christophers 6:=:=

£ 60:=:=

Amounting in the whole to the sume of £60 P Ann Which Assessmts. the sd Parishes have not complained of to this Court as not being able to coninue their relief to your Petrs sd Parish And Your Petrs being now in much worse Circumstances then they have been for many Years last past. by the daily increase of the poor & other incident charges that have lately accrued and being upwards of £1200 in debt has obliged Your Petrs to rate the Inhabitants above double what they used to be rated Assessed which hath Caused several of the Inhitants to remove out of Your Petrs Parish which greatly lessens the Number of Inhitants that are able to pay to the relief of the poor their being 1700 Familys living in the Parish and not above 700 that are able to pay to this relief of the Poor

Turn over

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray in Consideracon of the Prmisses That this Court will please to order them the same relief from the sd Parishes as they have heretofore received from them.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
