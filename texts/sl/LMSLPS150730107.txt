To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Guildhall in and for the said City assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Fludyer Bart. Lord Mayor and F: Cokayne Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of March 1762 Zachariah Seaton Ann his Wife and their Child Sarah aged five Months were removed from the Parish of Alhallows Barkin London to the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved and against the said order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them & that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden For the said City of London

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
