To the Right Honble Marshe Dickinson Esqr Lord Mayor Sr.Wm. Moreton Knt. Recorder and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Vag [...] rant Pass or order under the hand and seal of the said Lord Mayor bearing date the fifteenth day of Novemr. one Thousand seven hundred & fifty six Ann Juniper Single Woman was removed from the Parish of St. Michael Corn hill in the said City to the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners and Appelts. Apprehending themselves to be agrieved by the Said Order of of the said Lord Mayor do Appeal to this Court against the Same.

Your petitioners & Appealls. therefore humble pray that this Court will Appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Michael Cornhill & all other Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement & determination of the same touching the said Appeal and that Your Petitioners and Appealls. may be relives as this Court shall see Must

And Your Petitioners & Appealts shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Billington Agent for Appellts. Decr. 1st. 1756
