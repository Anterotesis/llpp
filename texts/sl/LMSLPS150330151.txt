To the Rt. Honoble Sr. William Stewart Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Worpfull the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libties thereof in their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

Sheweth The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Augustine als St Austin London

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London & the Liberties thereof (Quor un) receiving Date the Thirtyeth Day of May last past Elizabeth Tarrott Widow of John Tarratt was removed from the Parish of St Bennedict St. Bernett Pauls wharf London unto the said Parish of St Augustin als Austin London alledging the said last menconed Parish to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the sd. True Justices Do humbly Appeale to your Lordp and Worps therefrom, and pray that yor Lordp & Worps will be pleased to hear them concerning the Prmises And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Augustine ats Austin London may appear before you and hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of yor. Lord & Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
