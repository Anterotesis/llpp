To the Right Honourable Sir Robert Westley Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipfull Justices of the peace for the said City in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of Your Lordship and George Arnold Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said City bearing date the tenth Day of July last past Frances Catherine Crosby Wife of John Crosby a Prisoner for Debt in the Poultry Compten and their three Children Miles aged five Years Joseph aged three Years and John aged one Year were removed from the precinct of White Fryers London to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the said City of London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners affrehending themselves aggreived by the said Removal humbly appeal to your Lordship and this Court for such Releif in the premes as to Your Lordship shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Howard Sof for Appellt.}
