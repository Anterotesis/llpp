To the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Pennant Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Holy Trinity within the Liberty of His Majesty's Tower of London in the County of Midd.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 16th. Day of March last Elizth. Ambrose therein alledged to be the Widow of James Ambrose deced who had no Settlement in England was removed from & out of the parish of St Both Bishopsgate London, into the said Parish of Holy Trinity (by the description of the Parish of Trinity in the Minories in the County of Midd) as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petr. humbly Conceive them [...] Aggrieved

Your Petr. therefore humbly pray such Relief in the Premes as to your Lordp and this honble Court shall seem meet

And your Petr. shall ever pray

Bolton Agent for Appelts.
