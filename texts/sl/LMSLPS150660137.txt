To the Right Honble. Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor the Recorder & the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof at their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the hands & Seals of the said Stephen Theodore Janssen Esqr. Lord Mayor and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberty's (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth Day of November 1754 Ann Brooks Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said City to the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Do Appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners & Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Giles Londn. & all other Persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of the Same touching this Appeal And that Your Petitioners & Appellants may be Relieved as this Court Shall See meet.

And your Petrs. & Appellts. Shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Billington Attorney for Appellts.
