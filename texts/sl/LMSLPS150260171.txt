To the Right Honrble. Sr Willm Humfreys Knt & Barrts Lord Maior of the City of London And to ye Rt Worppll ye rest of ye Majesties Justices of the peace at ye Generall Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeals of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of St Andrew Holbourn above Barring County of Middx

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrt under ye hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the Peace (one being of the Quor) bearing Date the Second day of July last one Elizabeth Blith and Mary her daughter about Nine Years Old Were sent & delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles Criplegate London unto that Poors of the said Parish of St Andrew Holbourne above Barrs Alleadging ye said Parish to be Place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Petitioners therefore conceive themselves agriev'd by ye said Warrts & Judgment of ye said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to ye Honour and Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioner most humbly pray your Honour and Worpps will be pleased to hear them in ye Premisses And order yt ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye Sd Parish of St Giles Criplegate London may appear before you & hear & abide ye Determinacon of ye Honr & Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc.
