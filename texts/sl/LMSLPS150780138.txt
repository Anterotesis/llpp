To the Right Honble George Nelson Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said [---] City at their General Quarter Session of the peace for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churhchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of your Lordship and Fras: Cokayne Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace (one being of the Quorum) For the said City and the Liberties thereof bearing date the Eighth day of September last. Elizabeth Douglass Widow of John Douglass deceased ad their Two Children, Ann aged Thirteen Years and Mary aged Seven Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great London to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly appeal to this Honble Court against the said Order or pass Warrant and pray such Releif in the Premises as to this Honble Court shall seem meet.

And yr Petitioners Etc.

Joshua Cox Sollr for the Appellts.}
