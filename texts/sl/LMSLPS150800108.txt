To the Right Honorable Samuel Turner Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Turner Esqr. Mayor, and Wm. Nash Esquire Alderman two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of March last Elizabeth Mary Foster aged ten Years was removed out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London to the said Parish of Saint James as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant humbly appeal against the same, And pray that the benefit of the said Appeal, and the Hearing and determining thereof may stand Adjourned until the next General Sessions of the Peace for the said City of London, And that Your Petitioners may have such further or other Relief in the Premisses, as to this Honorable Court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

T: Thomas Solr. for Appellts.
