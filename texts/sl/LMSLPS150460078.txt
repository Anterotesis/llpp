To the Right Honble Sr. Edward Bellamy Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Matys Justices of the peace for the Said City of London in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of the Rt. Honble Sr Edward Bellamy and St Wm. Billers Knts. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the 21day of January last Elizabeth the wife of Jonathan Millington Jonathan their son aged Eight years James Millington aged five years & Christian Millington aged two years there other two Children were removed from the said parish of St Andrew by the Wardrobe London unto the said parish of St Luke Alledgeing the said parish of St Luke to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Petrs conceiving themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Doe therefore appeale to your Worps therefrom and humbly pray that your will be pleased to [---] hear them in the premises, and hear the Judgement & determination of your Worps touching the said Appeale.

And your Petrs Shall pray Etc

Ralph Mansell Church Wardens

Saml. Burchall} Overseers of the poore.
