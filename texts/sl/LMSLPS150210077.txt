Appeale Pish St Swithin agt St Martin outwith

To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Samuell Garrard Baronett Lord Maior of the City of London and to the Rt. Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the peace in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of Parish of St Swithin London

Sheweth That one Israel Wright widdow. and her Two Children by Virtue of A Warrant under the hands & seals of Twoe of her Maties Justices of the peace within the City of London (one being of the Quorm) bearing Date the Four and Twentieth day of February last past was removed from the Parish of St. Martin Outwith London to the said Parish of St Swithin alleadgeing the said Parish to be Place of the last legall settlement of the said Israel Wright.

That your Petitioners doubt not to prove that the said Israel Wright never had any legall settlement in the said Parish of St. Swithin whereby they conceive themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices and doe humbly Appeale to your Worpp therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Honr: and Worpps will be pleased to heare them in the Prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Martin Outwith London doe appeare before you and heare and abide the Determination of your Honr. & Worpps touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
