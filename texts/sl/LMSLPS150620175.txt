To ye. Rt. Honble. Francis Cokayne, Esqr. Lord Mayor of ye. City of London & to ye. Worpful the rest of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. said City & ye. Liberties thereof in their Genl. Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye. Parish of Christchurch in ye. City of London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of yor. Lordship, & Thos. Rawlinson, Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace, for ye. City of London, & ye. Liberties thereof (One being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. twentyeth Day of December 1750, Sarah Blake, Wife of John Blake, who is run away from her, & their two Children, Thomas aged eight Years, & John aged Six Years, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, London, unto ye. Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging ye. said Parish of Christ Church, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Baynbridge Atty for the Appellants.} That yor: Peticoners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of Removal, Do Appeal to yor. Lordp & Worps therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, may appear before You, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of yor. Lordship & Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
