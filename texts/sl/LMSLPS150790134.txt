To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace of the City of London at their [...] General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchres in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Ann Hanson is a Poor Old & impotent Person not able to work or get her Living whereby she is become Chargeable to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre & is now in the Workhouse belonging to the said Parish

That the said Ann Hanson hath a Son named James Hanson a Carpenter & Builders who keeps a great House and Timber yard on the West side of the Fleet market London & another Timber yard in the said Parish of St. Sepulchres & carrys on a very extensive Trade & who is of sufficient Ability to maintain his said Mother

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to make such Order in the Premisses for the relief of Your Petitioners as they shall seem meet.

J. Jenings Attorney for the Petrs.
