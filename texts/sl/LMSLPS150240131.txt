To the Rt Honble Sr Samuel Stanier Knt Lord Major of the City of London and to the Rt Worpll: the Rest of her Maties Justices of the Peace within the same City in the General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Ab church Londn:

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of her Maties Justices of the peace within this City (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the Thirtieth day of November last past one Alice Norwood Singlewoman was removed from the parish of St Marti Vintry London unto the said parish of St Mary (abchurch alleadging the said parish to be the place of her last legal settlement

That your Petitionrs doubt not but to prove that the said Alice Norwood never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Mary Abchurch whereby they conceive on themselves aggreived by the said Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeale to your Honr and Worpps therefrom

Your Petionrs therefore most humbly prays your honr & Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Martin Vintry may appear before you and hear & abide the Determination of your Honr & Worpps touching the Appeale

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc
