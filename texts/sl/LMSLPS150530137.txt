To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hornsey in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of The Right Honble. George Heathcote Esqr. Mayor and Richard hoare Esqr. two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of July last Elizabeth sinkfield Widow of Richard sinkfield Deceased was removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Bridget vulgarly St. Brides in the said City to the said parish of Hornsey as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

Yor Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices of the peace, Appeale to this Court against the same and humbly pray your Worship will be pleasd to appoint a day for the bearing and Determining the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray etc.

Dan Bolton Agent for the Appellts.
