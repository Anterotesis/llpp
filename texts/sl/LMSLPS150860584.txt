To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and other his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the City of London in Quarter Session of the Peace of the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or adjudication Under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable John Wilkes Esqr Lord Mayor and George Hayley Esqr. one of the Aldermen two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said City and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th day of September last Thomas Bloodworth Ann his Wife and their four Children Namely Elizabeth aged Seven years, Ann aged Six years, Hannah aged four Years and Thomas aged two years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Michael in Cornhill in the said City to the said Parish of Saint John the Evangelist as the place of their legal Settlement

That your petitioners conceive themselves agreived by the said Order or adjudication and appeal to this Honourable Court therefrom and

Your Petitioenrs therefore Humbly Pray that this Honourable Court will please to Grant them such Relief in the Premises as the Nature of their Case may require And your petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Geo: Lee Sol for the appeallts.}
