To the Right Honourable Sir William Calvert Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the said City In general Quarter Sessions Assembled,

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in ye West In the City aforesaid

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under ye hands and Seals of your Lordship and Francis Cokayne Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said City one being of the Quorum bearing Date 31st Day of Augt. last past Ann Price Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St: Botolph Bishopsgate London to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the sd City of London as the place of her last Legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners Apprehending themselves aggreived by the said Removal humbly Appeal to your Lordship and this Court for such Releif In the Premes as to your Lordship shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

M: Howard Soll for Appellts.
