To the Right Honourable Sir Robert Ladbroke Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace of the said City in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace now Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Royal London.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said City of London (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23d day of January last Thomas Hutchings and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Bennett at Pauls Wharf to the said Parish of Saint Michael Royal as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices Appeal to your Lordship and Worships against the said Order and humbly desire such releif therein as to your Lorship and Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc.

Chris: Robinson Sellrs. for Appellts.
