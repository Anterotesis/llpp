[---] 25/5 To the Right Worshipll his Matyes Justices of the peace for the City of London & libertys thereof

The humble petition & appeall of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of Enfield in the sd. County

Sheweth That by wort. under the hands & seals of William Stewart Mayor & Frs. Forbs Esqr. two of the Maties Justices of the pease for the City & the libertye thereof one James Moore & Sarah his wife was lately passed from the Pish of St. Gyles Criplegate London to the Pish of Enfield aforesd. as the place of their last legall settlemt.

That yd peticon ordr findeing themselves agreed by the sd. Wart. ordr. of the sd. two Justices doe himbly appeale against the same

And pray yr worships would be pleased to ordr. that the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the Pish of Enfield of ordr May appeare in their Houble Court this prsant Sessions to shew Couple why ye sd. James Moore & Sarah his wife should not be repassed to the sd. Pish of Criplegate

And yr. peticoners shall over pray Etc.
