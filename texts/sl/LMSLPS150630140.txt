To the Right Honble. Francis Cokayne Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull the Aldermen his Majestie's Justices of the peace in and for the Said City of London in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honble. the said Lord Mayor and Robert Alsop [...] Alderman two of his Majestie's Justices of the peace for the said City of London and the Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventh day of September last, Thomas Blackbourne was removed from the Parish of Allhallows on the Wall London to the Said Parish of Saint Stephen Coleman street London as the place of his last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices Appeal to your Lordship and Worships against the said Order and pray You will determine the Same and make such Order them as to your Lordship and Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc.
