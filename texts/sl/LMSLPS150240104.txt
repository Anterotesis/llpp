To the Rt. Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx.

Sheweth, That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. Baring date the 8th. day of June last past under the hands and Seales of two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London (Quor unus) one Mary Marshall wife of Robert Mashall was removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate London to the [---] Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch by the name of Mary Guillard widow of Clandius Guillard deced as the place of her last legal Settlement

That yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Orderr or Pass Warrt. of the said Justices, and therefore humbly appeale against the Same to this Honble Court.

And yor. Peticonrs. humbly pray that the Sd. Appeale may be heard this present Settlement and that yrs. Peticrs. may have such relief in the premisses as to yor. Wopps shall been just.

And yor. Peticonrs. Etc
