To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session for the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary at Hill London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. day of May last 1743 one Elizabeth Nash Widow of Samuel Nash deceas'd was removed upon her Own Oath from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Hill London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Yor Petr therefore Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant and humbly Appeal agt. the same And pray the Benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved unto them and further grant Yor Petrs such other Releif in the Premisses as Your Worships shall Seem meet

And Yor Petrs shall Pray Etc

I North Soll for the Appellts.
