London To the Right Honourable Sir John Williams Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties assembled at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberties thereof (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 17th day of May last Bethell Blower Elizabeth his wife and Female Child then unBaptiz'd were remov'd From the Parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate London to the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London Adjudging the last legal Settlement of the said Bethel Blower Elizabeth his wife and said Female Child to be in the said Parish of Saint Olave Silver Street London by which said Order of the said two Justices. your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggreived. and therefore Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court and Pray to be relieved in the premisses and Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray

Emanuel Jacob Robert Woodward{ Church Wardens
