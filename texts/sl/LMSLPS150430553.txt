To the Right Honoble John Barber Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace forr the said City and Libertys thereof in their generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humbles Petition and Appeall of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Mildred in Breadstreet London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Libertys thereof (Quor Unus) bearing date the 24th day of October last past Whereby William, Smith was Removed from the parish of St. Andrews Holborn London to the said Parish of St. Mildred in Breadstreet London as the place of his last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices, Appeal to your Lordshipp worships against the Order And humbly desire this Court will make such order therein as to your Lordshipp & Worshipps shall Seem meet

And Your Petitioners (as in duly bound) shall ever pray
