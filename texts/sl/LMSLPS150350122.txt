To the Rt Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace at their Genll Quarter Sessions at Guild Hall London

18/11 The humble Petition of ye Churchwar- dens and Oversrs: of the Poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Field in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Ann Bartlett Wife of John Bartlett with her two Children (Vizt) Alice Aged about 2 Years and Charles Aged about 7 Weeks were by an Order under the hands and Seals of Sr William Thomson and Sr John Fryer Knt: Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of London bearing date the 10th day of November last past rechoved from the Parish of St Andrew Holborn London unto Your Petitioners Said Parish to be Maintain'd and Provided for by wch. Said Order Your Petrs: thinks themselves aggreiv'd

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the Said Order and pray that a day may be Appointed to hear and Determine the same

And Your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
