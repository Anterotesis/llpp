To the Rt. Honble Etc Rt. Worll. her Maties Justies of the Peace for the City of London in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Parish of St: Margtt. Westmr.

Sheweth That William Lutwych & Elizabeth his Wife with their three Children Frances, Benjamin & Nathaniel were Removed by Virtue of a Pass Warrt: Sign'd by Sr. Samuel Garrard Barrt. & Sr: Thomas Stampe Knt. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for ye City of London out of ye Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate London & brought into ye Sd: Parish of St: Margaret Westmr: as ye place of their last legall Settlemt:

That yor. Petr. think themselves Agrived by the Adjudgemt: of ye Sd: two Justices.

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly Appeale & pray this Honble Bench to grant yor. Order to ye Church Wardens or Overseers of ye Poor of ye sd: Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London to Answer ye sd: Appeale

And Yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
