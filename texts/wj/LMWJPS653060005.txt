To the Right Worpll: their Maties. Justices of the peace for the City & Liberties of Westmr.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the High wayes [---] for the parish of St. Martin in the Fields within in the Liberty of westmr.

Sheweth That the Highwayes and pavements to be repaired by the said Parish are now so broken and defective, that they cannot be inffinently amended and repaired for the year ensuing from the Feast of the Nativity of our Lord God last past at less charge than Two hundred Fifty pounds

Therefore yor. most humbly pray an order of this Court to enable them to repayre the same, as by the Statute is provided & appointed

Ander.
