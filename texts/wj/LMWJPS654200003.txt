To Ye. Worshipfull Bench of Justices for ye: City and Liberty of Westminster

The humble Petition of Richd: Cobley

Sheweth That yr: Petitioner, this Quarter Sessions of ye. Peace, Indicted, one Thomas Harrison for an Assault, and Your Petitioner is Inform'd that ye. Said, Thomas Harrison is Discharged from ye: Said Indictment without giving Notice of Triall to yr: Petitir.

Therefore ye: Petitioner most humbly Prays that yrs. Worshipful Bench, Will be pleased, to take ye: Said Indictment into Consideration

And he will Pray Etc.

Richd Cobley
