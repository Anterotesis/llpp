To the Worshipfull his Mats. Justices of the peace for the Citty & liberty of Westmr. att the Generall quarter Sessions then & there held the first day of April 1719

The humble peticon of Anne Allen Spinster

Sheweth That yor petitioner being Some time agoe bound as an Apprentice to Elizabeth Rose Milliner by the Trustees of the Charity School of St. Martins in the Fields that her sd Mrs does refuse to allow her Sufficient Meat Drink Washing & Lodging & does not take care to instruct her in her Sd business which if not reminded by yor Worspps it will end in the ruin of your Sd Peticoner

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worshipps to take the premisses into Consideration grant her such reliefe as your Worshipps shall think fitt

And yor Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
