To The Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr. in their Genral Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Walter Brett Keeper of the House of Correction

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. was at the Genral Quarter Sessions told the Poore held for the City & Liberty of Westmr on Thursday the 30th. day of June in the 11th. Year of his Present Majte. Chosen & Appointed by yor Worships Master or Governour of the House of Correction of Tottle Fields with a Sallary of £50 P Ann wth. Yor. Peticon constantly received from the Sevral Parishes within the sd. City & Liberty

That by the Statute of the 12th. Year of the Reign of his Present 1 Majte: Intituled an Act for the more easy assessing Collecting & levying of County Rates It is Quarted That He Justices of the peace for the sd City & Liberty shall have full Power to appoint the Govr. or Master of the House of Correction within the sd. City & Liberty who shall have the sum of Money Yearly as both been accustomed for & towards The Support & Maintenance of the Priisoners in his Custody etc (not exceeding £50 by the Year) and direct the Treor or Trent of the County of Middx & City & Liberty of Westmr. to Obey all orders wth. shell be made by the sd. Justices of the peace of the sd City & Liberty or the great part of then Assembled in their Genral Quarter Sessions For the Payment of any sum of sums of Money for the allowance allotted to such Governour or Master of the House of Correction

That There is due to yor. Peticonr £25 for Half Years Sallary Four Justices to Lady day Last

Yor Peticonr & therefore most humbly Pray Yor Worships to order Wr. John Higgs Feers of the County fo Middx to Pay to Yor. sd. Peticon The sd. sume of £25

And Yor Peticonr shall ever pray

Waller Brett
