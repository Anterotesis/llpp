To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr. in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said City & Liberty at Westmr Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwdens. and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St. Anne Westmr.

Sheweth That the Highways & Pavements to be amended & repaired by the Inhabitants of the said parish are at present so broken & foulty that they can not be Sufficiently amended & repaired for this present year from the the past Day of the Birth of our Lord Christ now last past at less Charge then the Expence of one hundred Pounds And that the money being not to be raised without an Order of the General Quarter Session

It is prayed that this Court will grant them such Order whereby they may be Enabled to raise the said Sum into Defray the Charges before mentioned

And shall ever pray Etc.

Edm: Byron of St. Ann's Vestry
