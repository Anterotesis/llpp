To this Maties Justices of the Peace in Quarter Sessions Assembled in Westminster Hall

The humble Petition of Booth Chaderton

Sheweth That Your Peticoner is Aged about 64 and hath been Clerk to Several Justices of the Peace for 40 Yeares, & is well affected to the Prsent Governmt.

He therefore prays Your Worships Consideration & favour towards him, & to appoint him Keeper of Bridewell in the Roome of Thomas Evans deceased.

An he shall over Pray Etc

Booth Chadertons
