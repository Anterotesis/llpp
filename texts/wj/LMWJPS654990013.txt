To the Rt. Worshipfull her majesties Justices of Lee Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Mansfield widow.

Sheweth, That Hanna the wife of Shadrack Grantland of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County Year Stands indicted at this prsent Sessions of the Peace for Cheating your Peticoner out of five pounds

That the said Hanna [...] was bound over to appear at this Sessions, & has include default Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly prays that the Recognizance entred into by the said Hanna is baile may be forthwith estreated. whereby [...] may be awarded agt. the Baile, and that yor. Peticonr. may have a warrant for the taking and apprehending of the said Hanna to answer the premises, & that for her Contempt as aforesaid, She may be obliged to plead to & try the said Indictmt. the next Sessions.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
