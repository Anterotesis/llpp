To the Rt: Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled att Westminster

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St George Hanover Square

Sheweth That the Surveyors of ye Highways of the said Parish of St George Hanover Square for the Year last Past have not made up and Passed their Accounts relating to their said Office as the Law directs in Such Cases.

Your Petioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to make such Order for the said Surveyors to pass their Accts. as to your Worships shall Seem meet

George Carpenter. Cecill Wray} Church Wardens

Robt: Owen Willm: Campbell} Surveyors
