To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westmr.

Sheweth That the Surveyors of the Highways of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square for the year 1745 have not made up and Passed their Accounts relating to their said Office as the Law directs in Such Cases.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will be pleased to make such Order for the said Surveyors to Pass their Accounts as to your Worships shall seem meet.

Willm. Timbrell} Churchwardens

John Pratt Benja Benwick Dano [...] } Surveyors of the Highways
