To the Wortt: the [...] of the Peace for the City & [...] of Westmr: in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & appeal of Wm: Wurea [...] in behalf of himself & others Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Margt Westmr.

Sheweth That Your Petrs: are over rated in the Coors Rate made for the said Parish Notwithstanding the frequens Complains they have made to the Officers of the said Parish.

Wherefore they humbly pray this Worll. Bench will grant Such Order herein as may Effectually relieve Your Petrs. touching the said Rate.

July ye iith 1717

And Your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc

William Murray Shil. Mathews Join Thurston
