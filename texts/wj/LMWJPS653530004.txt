To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace Assembled by Adjournment on thursday the [...] 20th of August 1719

The humble Petition of Ann Evans [...] Widdow of Thomas Evans late keeper of the [...] house of Correction

Humbly Sheweth That it being the Custom for the Widdows of former keepers of the house of Correccon to hold the said House of Correccon untill such time as another [...] keeper shall be elected by this Court

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that she may be admitted to the same favour

And as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc
