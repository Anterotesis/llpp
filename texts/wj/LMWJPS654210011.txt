To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace at their General Quarter Session Assembled at Westminster

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster & County of Middx.

Sheweth That the Highways Couseways and Pavements to be repaired by the said Parish are no so broken Court of repair that they cannot be Sufficiently amended & repaired for this present Year Commencing from Christmas last at less Charge than Four hundred Pounds & Upwards which may be raised by a Rate of Two Pence in the Pound upon The Inhabitants Owners & Occupiers of Lands Houses Tenements & Hereditaments within the said Parish

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray Your Worships to Grant an Order of this Court to enable Your Petitioners to make an Assessment of two Pence in the Pound upon all & every the Inhabitants of the said Parish for repairing of the the Statute in that behalf made and Provided

Edward Hody} Churchwarde

Benj. Berwick Wm. Hood} [...]
