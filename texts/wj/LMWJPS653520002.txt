To the Worshipful Bench of Justices

The humble Petn of the Widdo Lawrance

Sheweth That yr: Petnr: is Sixty five Years of Age, and has been a Hous keeper this Thirty four Years and has paid alby duties of the Parish, but is now by Age and other misfortunes rendred uncapable to pay any Longer, and hopes therefore that your Worships will Take it into consideration, and excuse her for the future from paying to the Poor, She being a very great object of Charity her Self.

And your Petnr: as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc
