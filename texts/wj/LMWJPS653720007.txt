To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Westmr:

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St: George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster [...] County of Middlx

Sheweth That the Highways Causeys and Pavements to be Repaired by the said Parish are now so broken and out of Repair that they cannot be Sufficiently amended and Repaired for this present Year Commencing from Christmas last at less charge than five hundred Pounds and upwards which may be raised by a Rate of four Pence in the Pound upon this Inhabitants Owners and Occupiers of Lands Houses Tenements & Hereditamt. within the said Parish

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships to grant your Petitioners an Order of this Court to Enable your Petitioners to make an Assessment of four pence in the Pound upon all and every the Inhabitants of the said Parish for Repairing the said Highways Causeys Streets and Pavements, as by the Statute in that behalf is Provided and appointed

And your Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc

Carpenter Wm: Herbert} Churchwardens

Mat. Tomlinson Roger Williams} Surveyors
