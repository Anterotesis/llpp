To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Miles.

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof your Petr. Stood Indicted in this Honble Court this Prsent Sessions Your Worspps were pleased to Fine him £10. and for Newpaymt. thereof to Comitt him unto Custody where he Now Remaines

That your Petr. is very sorry for the sd. Offence and Resolves never to Offence againe in the Like Nature and forasmuch as the remainds of your Petrs. Adversary being £25.10s Which your Petrs. ran by noe Meanes comply with In Regard he hath a large Family to Mainteyne Soe that your Petrs. is not Able to pay the sd. Fine and humbly on builting Himself to your Worspps Great Goodness and Mercy.

P Tho. Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore Most Humbly Prayes Your Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the sd Fine As put your Worspps Shall Seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc John Miles his [x] mke
