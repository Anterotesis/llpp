To the Worship. Bench of His Majties: Justices of ye Peace assembled in Quartr. Tessions for ye City & Liby. of Westmr

The humble Petition of Walter Hardie

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr. Recognizance which shou'd have been drawn the last Quarter Sessions, was forfeited thro' his misunderstand- ing the direction of ye Clerk of ye Court occasion'd by the Adjournmt. of ye Sessions: And the Prosecutor & Yor. Petr. being fully Agreed

Yor: Petr: humbly prays his Recog -nizance may be now withdrawn
