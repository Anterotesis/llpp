To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in the General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said City and Liberty at Westmr. Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St Anne within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That the Highways and Pavements to be amended & repaired by the Inhabitants of the said Parish are so broken and faulty that they cannot be sufficiently amended and repair'd for this present year from the feast day of the Birth of our Lord Chirst now last past at less Charge that the Expence of One hundred Pounds And that the Money being not to be raised without an Order of the General Quarter Session

It is prayed that this Court will grant them such Order whereby they may be enabled to raise the said Sum to defray the Charges before menconed

And shall ever pray Etc

Dan: Jones Vest Cl.
