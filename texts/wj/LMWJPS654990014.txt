To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Anne Miller

Sheweth That for the Fart whereof Yor. Petr. (have Indicted in this Honble. Court [...] this prsent Session Your Worpps were pleased to Find her 5th. And for Conpayment thereof to Comitt her to New Prison in this County Where the New Remaines

That your Petrs. very Sorry for the Offence and being very Poor is not able to pay the sd. Find and hath Offence an Agreemt. with her Adversary But he will not Comply And humbly submitting herselfe to your Worspps Goodness Mercy

P Tho:Nash Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly Prayes your Worspps [...] he pleased to Mittigate the sd Fine Or Grant that She may be Admitted to Bagle Coventry and [...] shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc
