To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the Citty and Liberty of Westmr. Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of John Johnson of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden Mercer

Sheweth That by Order Dated the third day of Aprill last under the hands and Seales of John Tully and John Ellis Esqrs. two of his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx and Liberty of Westmr. Your Peticoner is adjudged to be the reputed father of a Female Bastard Child borne in Walkers Court in the Parish of St. James Westmr. on the body of Mary Bell Single woman on the Second day of the Said Month of Aprill last which Said Childe is already (as is alledged) become chargeable to the Sd parish
