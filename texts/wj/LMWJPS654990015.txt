To the Right Worshppll her Matys Justices of the Peace for County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Emanuell Fuller

Sheweth That yor. Petr. Stands indicted last Sessions held for this County for an assault upon one Mary Newton and to wth. indicted yor. Petr. pleaded not guilty And thereupon put in Bayle to try his Traverse this prsent Sessions And the said Mary Newton being also then indicted for an assault upon yor. Petr. and having not appeared as yet and pleaded not thereunto

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to respite his Recognizance untill the next Sessions against wch. time yor. Petr. intends to obleige the said Mary Newton by Warrt. from this Court to appear to his Indictment in order that both indictmts. may be tryed together

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
