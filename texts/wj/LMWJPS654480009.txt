To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace now assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden at the Courthouse near Westminster Hall in and for the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an order or pass warrant under the hand & seal of Daniel Gach Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster aforesd. bearing date the twelfth day of April in the year of our Lord one Thousand Seven Hundred & Forty Nine after writing that Elizabeth Hudson was apprehended in the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty aforesd. in the sd. County of Middlesex as a Rogue and Vagabond (vizt.) wandering abroad and Lodging in the Open Air and not being able to give a good account of herself it was ordered by the sd. Justice by the sd. order or pass warrant that the said Elizabeth Hudson Should be Conveyed from the sd. Parish of St. Martin in the Fields to the sd. Parish of St. George in the County of Surry at her Last legal place of Settlement

That the sd. Elizabeth Hudson by virtue of the sd. order or pass warrant hath been conveyed from the sd. Parish of St. Martin in the Fields to the sd. Parish of St. George where She now remains Chargeable to the same Parish Lastmentioned

Yor. Petrs. apprehending themselves aggreived by the sd. order or pass warrant made by the sd. Justice as aforesd. do therefore humbly appeal against the same

Edwd.Cooke Jos. Bayley} Churchwardens

Wm. Tovey Saml. Carter} Overseers
