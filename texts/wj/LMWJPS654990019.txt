To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticonr of John Walter

That are of Yesterday upon a Hearing in this Honble Court Betweene your Petr. and One John Miles touching an Assault Your Worspps were pleased to Fine the sd. John Miles wh. and to Order that he should Agree the Matters in Difference

That your Petr. Demands were noe More then 8th. which the sd. Miles absolutly Refuses to pay But alledged that before your Petr. Shal be. pair Two pence He will pay the sd. Fine And threatene your Petr. with More Actions att Law.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Beggs such Releise in the prmises as unto your Worpps shall seem meet

P. Tho: Nash

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

John Walker
