To the Worpll. the Bench of Justices now Sitting in Quarter Session for the City & Libty of Westminster.

The humble Petition of Thomas Turner.

Sheweth That Mr. Petr Stands bound [...]

These are to Certifye That Thomas Turner liveing in Brick Street And Alice his wife and their Children are now upon the Charges of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields and are relieved by us as Poor of the said Parish Given under our hands the Tenth day of February Anno Doniun 1718.

Raro [...] Barnett Mark England} Overseers of the Poor of St. Martin in the Fields.
