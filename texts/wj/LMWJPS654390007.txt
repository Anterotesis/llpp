To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westmr.

Sheweth That the Highways Cause ways and Pavement to be repaired by the said Parish are now so broken & out of repair that they cannot be sufficiently amended and repaired for this present year 1746 commencing from Christmas last at less Charge than Four hundred Pounds and upwards which may be raised by a Rate not exceeding Two pence in the Pound [...] upon the Inhabitants Owners & occupiers of Lands Houses Tenements & Hereditament within said Parish.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to Grant them an Order to enable your Petitioners to make a Rate or Assessment not exceeding Two Pence in the Pound upon all and every the Inhabitants of the said Parish for repairing the said Highways Cause way said pavements as by the Statute in that case is made and provided.

Willm. Timbrell} Churchwarden

John Prat Benjn Benuick Dan: Vials} Surveyors of the Highways.
