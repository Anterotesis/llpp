To the Worpll. the Bench of Justices now Sitting in Quarter Session for the City & Libty of Westminster

The humble Petition of Thomas Turner

Sheweth That yor. Petr. stands bound Over to this Worpll. Bench, for Playing at Scettler at high Parke Gate.

That he is very Sorry he should any ways act Contrary to the Laws, whereby to in curr Yor. Worps. Displeature But he did it for meer bread, being in years, and having Six children, all now lying ill, as also yor Petr.

Therefore he humbly begs Yor. Worship's Compestion in Pardoning this offence, & he with take Care never to do the like again

But Shall always Pray Etc
