To the Right Worsph. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Still.

Sheweth That your Petr. was lately imployed in the Service of one Daniell Stacy of Hackney in the said County Brewer as a day labourer and that Jeremiah Hatchet one of the Headborough of the said parish did lately take him by force to the Savoy in order to compell him into the Matys Service without bringing him before threet of her Matys Justices of the peace for the said County as the Law directs. That yor. Petr. in a housekeeper and pays all parish dutys and hath a wife and six children to maintaine and is of an honest life and conversacon which he is ready to make appeare to this Honble Court.

P Westhy Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worsps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from his said Imprisonment.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
