Westmr Ss: To the Worshipfull the Justices of Peace For the City and Liberty of Westmr.

The Humble Peticon of William Smith now an Apprentice to William Spencer of R- Bedford Street [...] vent Garden Cordwainer

Sheweth that your Peticoner have served the [...] William Spencer upwards of Six yeares and that for Four yeares last past your Petitioner have wanted all master of necessaries fitt and convenient for him (vizt.) Ment Drink and Lodgeing and that the said William Spencer refuses to provide for your Petitioner

Therefore your Humble Petitioner prays your Worshipps to take the Premisses into your Consideracon and as in Duty [...] bound Your poor Peticoner shall ever pray Etc

Willam Smith
