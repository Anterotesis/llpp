To the Worshipfull his Mats Justices of the Peace at this present Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of Richd Fletcher

Sheweth That Your Peticoner was by Order of this Honourable Bench ordered into Custody of the Keeper of the Gate house for Speaking disrespect full words agt this Honourable Court which Words Your Peticoner ought not to have Spoak therefor Yor Peticoner knowing himself in a very great fault which Yor Peticoner did do through mad vertency humbly begs this Honourable Courts Pardon for the same And hopes that Yor Worshipps will release Yor peticon out of Custody

And Yor Peticoner as in Duty Bound Shall ever pray Etc

Richard [...] lephey
