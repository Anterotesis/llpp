To the Worpll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the City and Libty of Westmr. in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon of James Macleane

Sheweth That on the Seventh of August 1722 Your Petr was bound an Apprentice to One William Warran of the parish of St. Margaret Westmr. Barber & Peruke maker, That Your Petr. Said Master and Mistress have often beat and abused Your Petr. in a very Extraordinary manner without any Just case, and have also Encouraged their Journeyman so to do, That Your Petrs, said Master hath not provided Sufficient Meat or Cloths for Your Petr. Pursuant to his Agreement,

Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays this Worpll. Court Will take his Case into Consdieracon and Discharge him from his Said Indenture of Apprenticehood.

And Your Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.

James Macleane
