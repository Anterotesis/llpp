To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of James Stedman Carpenter William Tufnell Bricklayer & Thomas Burt Smith.

Sheweth That the Petrs. have performed the Sevl. Works at the House of Correction in Tothill Feilds Westmr. according to an Orders of this Worll. Court Dated the Eighth of October 1729. amounting to the same of Thirty two Pounds and tenn Shillings.

Therefore Your Petrs. most humbly prays your Worships to give directions for the Payment of the Same.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.

£ s d
