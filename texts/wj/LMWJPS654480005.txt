To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in their General Quarter Session of the Peace by Adjournment Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster.

Sheweth That the Surveyors of the Highways of the said Parish of Sant George Hanover Square for the year last past have not made up and passed their Accounts relating to their said Office as the Law directs in such Cases.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to make such Order for the said Surveyors to Pass their Accounts as to your Worships shall seem meet.

Fra: Burton Jos. Patterson} Churchwardens.

Cornelius Vendestop Charles Durham Wm: Perritt} Surveyors.
