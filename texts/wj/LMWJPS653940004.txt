To the Right Worshipfull the Chairman and other his Majties Justices of the peace for the City & Libty of Westmr. in their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Robert Cotes of the Parish of St. Mary Lestrand in this Libty Apothecary

Most Humbly Sheweth That Your Peticonr. Did by an Indenture bearing Date the 26th. Day of February 1730 take one Anthony Carrafa to be an Apprentice to Learn the Art and Mistry of an Apothecary that During the time the sd. Carrafa hath Served Year Peticonr. As Such Apprentice he hath behaved himself as an Idle of & Disorderly Aprentice And hath Assaulted Beat and Bruised year Peticonr. in a Violent Manner and hath Done other Great Wronges to your Petionr.

Your Petitionr. haveing Caused the Sd. Carraffa to be bound over to this Present Quarter Sessions to Answer your Petionrs. Sd. Complaint

Therefore your Peticonr Humbly Beggs this Court to Order & appoint a day when the Matters may be heard before this Court and that this Court will be pleased to Punish the sd. Apprentice & releive your Petionr. in the Premisses as to this Court Shall Seem meet

And your Peticonr. shall for Ever Pray Etc.

Robt Coles
