To the Rt: Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their Genl. Quarter Sessions Assembled at Westmr.

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens & Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster & County of Middx.

Sheweth That the Highways Causeways & Pavements to be repaired by the said Parish are now so broken & out of repair that they cannot be Sufficiently amended & required for this present year Comencing from Christmas last at less charge than four Hundred Pounds and upwards which may be reasied by a Rate not exceeding two pence half penny in the pound upon the Inhabitants Owners and Occupiers of Lands Houses Tenements & Hereditaments within the said Parish

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray Your Worships to grant your Petitionr. an Order of this Court to enable Your Petitioners to make an Assessmt. not exceeding two pence half penny in the Pound upon all and every the Inhabitants of the said Parish for repairing the said Highways Causeways and Pavements as by the Statute in that behalf made & Provided

Henry Talbot Blundell} Churchwardens

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Berysl. Berwick David Willanme} Surveyos. of the High- -ways
