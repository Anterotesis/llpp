To the Worll: His Mats: Justices of the Peace for the City & Libty of Westmr: in Quarter Sesss: Assembled.

The humble Peticon of Edwd: Jenkins

Sheweth That he being bound an Apprntice to Mr: Richd: Redman Tripe Man of the Pish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. his sd: Master hath some time Since absconded from business & left Your Petr: Unprovided for & Destitude of all Necessaries

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays Your Wors. won'd please to Discharge him from his Said Apprnticesp.

And yor Petr: Shall pray Etc.
