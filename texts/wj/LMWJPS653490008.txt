To the Rt. Worpll. His Majties. Justices of the peace in Quarter Sessions Assembled Etc

The humble peticon of Christian Simmerwell Widow

Sheweth That your petr. hath been a Housekeeper in the parish of St. Martin in the fields upwards of forty Years, and hath paid and upwards, and am uncapable of getting my bread, and must utterly parish of Now Forasmuch as there was a pention settled upon me for three Years and upwards Cut for four Months last past have not received any

Most humbly Beg your Worships will be pleased to take my distressed Condicon into your tender Consideracons and order the Continuance of Pay pention

And your petr. as in Duty bound (will ever Pray Etc
