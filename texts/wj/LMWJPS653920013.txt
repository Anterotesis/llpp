To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled att Westmr.

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westmr and County of Middx.

Sheweth That the comon Highways Causeys and Pavements to [...] repaired by the said Parish are now so broken & out of Repairs that they Cannot be Sufficiently Amended and Repaired for this present Year Commencing from Christmas last att less Charge than four hundred Pounds and Upwards which may be Raised by or Rate of three pence in the Pound upon the Inhabitants Owners and Occupiers of Lands Houses Pavements & Here ditaments within the said Parish

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to Grant Your Petitioners an Order of this Court to Enable your Petitioners to make an Assessmt of Three Pence in the Pound upon all and Every the Inhabitants of the said Parish for repairing the said Highways Causeys and Pavements as by the Statute in that behalf made and provided

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray
