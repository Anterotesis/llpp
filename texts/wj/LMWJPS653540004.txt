To the Worshipll. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in Quarter Sessions & Assembled.

The humble Petition of Thomas Lloyd.

Sheweth. That By the Death of Mr. Thomas Evans late Governour of the House of Correction that places is now Vacant.

That Your Petr. hath been an Housekeeper in the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster near Thirty Years.

That Your Petr. hath a Wife and Six Small Children wholly Unprovided for.

May it therefore please Your Worsps. to Admit him Governour of the Said House of Correction Vacant as aforesaid

And Yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Thomas Lloyd.
