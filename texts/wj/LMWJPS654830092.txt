To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the City and Liberty of Westminster in their Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace for the Said City and Liberty assembled.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Hugford.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner hath been Annually favoured with your Worship Licence to keep a certain Room called Hickford Great Room Situate Brewers Street in the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster for Publick Concerts and other Musical Entertainments of the like kind for several Years last past and unto this present time

That Your Petitioner hath taken the Utmost care and so will, continue for the future to preserve good Decorum. And that every thing therein shall be conducted with decency sobriety and good manners, and a strict conformity to the Act of Parliament in that behalf.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that your Worships would be pleased to grant time. License for continuing the said Room for the purpose aforesaid for the ensuing year.

And Your Petitioner as in Duty and bound shall every Pray Etc.
