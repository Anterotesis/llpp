To the Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of John Witherington and John Baker St

Sheweth That Your Petr. are Bayle for one Nathaniel Taylor [...] Stands indicted for a fraud in Taking half a Guinea on which Indictmt. he was to try his Traverse the first of this Session but being so very much incumbred with Debt the Dares not Stir abroad as the Wife of the said Taylor now in Court in ready to make Affidavit

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray this Court will grant them in respite till the next Session

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray. John Witherington John Baker
