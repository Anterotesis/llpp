To the Worll. his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the City of & Liberty of Westmr. in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of Thos. Hyhorne

Sheweth That your Petrs. was a Greencoat Boy of ye. School founded by King Charles ye first in Tittle Fields Westmr. out of which he was bound about Three years ago to Mr Isaac Stevens of St. Margaret Westmr. Undertaker as his Apprentice for 7 years.

That your Petrs. said Master Dyed about Six Months ago, since which him your Petr. hath been unprovided for, Mrs. Rose his said Masters Administrative refusing to admit him into her Service.

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays this Worll. Court. to take such Ordr. for ye relief of ye. Petr. (in ye Premises) as to ye. Worsps. Wisdom & Justice Shall Seem most meet.

And yr Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Thomas Hyhorne
