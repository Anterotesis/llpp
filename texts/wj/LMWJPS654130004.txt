Westmr. to wit These are to Certify that the Statute Law now in force to oblige the Inhabitants to amend the Highways Six days in the year is not Sufficient to repair and amend the Highways of the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Libty. without an order of the General Quarter Sessions of the peace held for the said Liberty to make a rate & assessment on the Inhabitants of the said parish for raising the Sum of four hundred Pounds and upwards for and towards the repairing and amending the Highway's belonging to the said Parish And we do hereby Certify the Contents of the within Petition is true

June 21th: 1738

Carpenter Thos: Rea

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Survey [...] of ye Highway of ye parish [...] St. George Hemr. Square
