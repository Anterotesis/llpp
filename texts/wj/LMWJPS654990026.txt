To the Rt. Worshippl. her Majties Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble peticon of Wm. Margill on ye behalfe of his Son in Law Egles Green

Sheweth yt yor pet ye 16 March 1703 bound his sd Son Apprentice to one John Bounbridge of [...] Street were red lyon Square of ye pish of St. And one Golbored in this County Perukemaker for 7 years to leave ye sd Trade

That yor Pet Son has served 3 years & 7 Months of his Apprentice Hood dueing wth. time he hath undergon great [...] in Geing in unlearne & fowle Ledding & been often immediately beaten & brussed by his sd Master & his wife without any Pvocacon & yt ye sd Master has used his utmost endeavour to put ye said Apprentice off to dry Pson yt would give ye most money for him by haggleing wth. him to severall Psons unknown to yee pet & gold Masters hath not instructed [...] Apprentice in her sd Trade & hath likewise Endeavoured to put yor. petrson of to some Comandr in ye affirm Company or to waite upon any gentle & this declared to hath not fulness for your Petrson

Petr. he caused yr. Petr. said Son to be apprehended and carryed before Ralph Bucknall Esq One of her Majties Justices of the peace for this County and therein yr. Son was comitted to New Prison att Clarkenwell in this County where he now remaines

E Stanton Yor Petr. humbly prayes that ye said matter may be heard [...] examined by this Courte & that yr. Worships will be pleased to order that the said John Bainbridge may be ordered to attend this Courte to shew cause why yr. Petr. said Son should not be discharged from his said Indenture of Apprenticehood and yr Petr. may be relieved here in as to ye. Worshps. shall Seem meet

And yr. Petr. as in duty bound shall ever Pray
