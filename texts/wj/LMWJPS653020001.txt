To the Rt. Worll. the Justices for the Citty and Libties of Westmr. in Session.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margtt. Westmr.

Sheweth. That Whereas upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of St. James Westmr. one Ann Cooper and her Son Charles Cooper were passed the 12th. of June 1691. to the parish of St. Margtt. Westmr. by John Tully and Andrew Lawrence Esqrs. two of their Mats. Justices for the said Citty and libtie And Whereas the said Ann Cooper & Charles Cooper were never Inhabitants or had any legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Margarett.

May it therefore please yor. Wors. to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of St. James be sumoned to appear before this Bench this Session to shew cause why the said woman and her Son ought not be repassed to them, and to pay such charge as yor. Petrs. have bin att in releiving them since they have bin passed as aforesaid.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray
