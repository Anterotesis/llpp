To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of William Boulton the Younger Your Worshipps Cryer:

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was at the last General Quarter Session of the Peace holden at Westmr. in & for the said City & Liberty elected chosen & appointed Cryer of the Court of General Quarter Session of the Peace holden for the said City & Liberty in the room place & stead of William Boulton his Father who Surrendred the said Cryers place.

That your Petitioner is one of the Clerks in the Post Office and his Constant Attendance requiring him to attend to Sort and give out the Letters to be Sent and given to his Majesties Subjects of great importance to the Publick.

Your Petitioner humbly prays Your Worshipps as Your Petitioner is Oblidged to attend the Publick Service in the Post Office That Your Worshipps will be pleased to excuse Your Petitioner from Personal Attendance in the Said Office of Cryer and be Pleased to Admitt Thomas Barber who has for Several Years last past Executed the Said Office of Cryer to Execute & Officiate in the Place Stead and absence of Your Petitioner

And Your Petitioner in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc:

William Boulton
