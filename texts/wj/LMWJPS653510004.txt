To the Worll: this Majts: Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westme: in Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of Edward Thoras

Sheweth That your Petr being one of the Constables of the said City & Liberty, did some him since being some Prisons who were in his Custody, to be Examined before several Justice of the Peace who were then siting at a Taverne in Cevill Court.

That your Petr. did not indeed then know Mr. Justice Burdens to be a Gentleman in the Comission of the Peace, & Is heartily sorry for his Offence, & begs Pardon of all the Worll. Gentleman in General, & of Mr. Justice Johnson & Mr. Justice Burdus in Parfion [...]

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worll. to accept this his Suspect Submission & to pardon his ill Behaviour which has given so just Offence.

And Your Petr. shall ever pray Etc. Edward Thomas
