To the Petr. Worspl. hev Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth The humble Petition of Anne Adams

That For the Fark whereof Your Petr. stood Indicted in this Honble Court this Prsent Sessions Your Worspps Were pleased to Fine her 10th. And for Nonpayment thereof to Comitt her to New Prison in this County That your Petr. is very sorry for the sd. Offence and Resolves never to Offend againe in the Like) nature But Imediately to Leave her house And for as much As your Petr. being very Poor and her Landlord haveing lately [...] seized On her Goods for [...] of Kent and the haveing One Child and her husband being in her Maties service Soe that The is noe Waies Able to pay the sd. Fine and humbly submitteing herselfe to yor. Worpps Great Goodness Mercy.

P Tho Nash Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly Prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the [...] Fine as unto yor. Worspps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc
