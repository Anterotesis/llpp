July 1745

To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal, of [...] John Rogers.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner being an Inhabitant of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westmr. is Rated and Assessed to the Poor of the said Parish for his dwelling house at the Yearly Rent of Â£30. by which your Petitioner thinks himself Aggrieved

Therefore humbly Prays this Court to appoint a time this present Sessions for the hearing of his appeal, and that all persons concerned may attend

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc

John Rogers.
