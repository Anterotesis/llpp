To the Right Honourable Samuel Fromer Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Hoble Bench of Justices in their Present General Session of God Delivery Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Prayer of Thomas Webb a Prisoner.

Sheweth That at the Twenty fifth Day of January last year Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the New Prison of Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex fro Stealing Thre Deal Beards of the Value of Three Shillings the Property of Alexander Hume and now is a Prisoner Confined in the Lame Prison Under the same Charge.

Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly Prays that at this Present Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed, Bailed a Dishcarged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case, made and Provided.

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc

Thos. Webb.
