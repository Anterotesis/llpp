To the Right Honble. Kingsby Bethel Esqr Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Commissioners of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City and for the County of Middlesex. in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Mary Jones now a Prisoner in Newgate.

Sheweth That Yor. Petitr. was on the 2d. day of February 1756 by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hand and Seal of Justice Spurling Committed to Newgate for the Murder of Joshua Kidden

Your Petr. therefore Most Humble Prays that She may at this Present Session be Tried, Bailed or discharged of the Crime aforesaid according to the Form of the Statute in that Case made and provided.

And Your Petr. shall ever pray Etc

the Marke of Mary [x] Jones
