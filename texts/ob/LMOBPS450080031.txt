To The Right Honourable William Bridgen Esqr. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Commissioners of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey.

The Humble Petition of Patrick Coyle.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner together with three others was on the 17th. day of this Instant February Committed to His Majestys Gaol of Newgate by John Hawkins and Saunders Welch Esquires upon a Charge for riotously and tumultuously Assembling before the Door and Entering the Dwelling House of the Excellency the Morrocco Ambassador and Committing violences and assaults therein of which said Charge Your Lord ships Petitioner is Entirely Innscent and which he hopes fully to make appear to this Honble Court at time of His Trial.

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays Your Lordship and this Honourable Court that He may at this Present Session be Tried Bailed or discharged of the said Offence Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and Provided

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc Patrick Coyle

his [x] mark
