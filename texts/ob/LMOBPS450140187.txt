To the Right Honourable Samuel Turner Esquire Lord Myor of the City of London and to the rest of his Majesties Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal Delivery now held at Justice Hall in the Old Baily within the Suburbs of the City of London

The Humble Petition of Patrick Tullis

Sheweth That at the last Sessions of Oyer and Terminer held at Hicks Hall in and for the County of Middlesex a Bill of Indictment was preferred and found against your Petitioner for a Capital Felony for Stealing Goods Etc. out of a Ship in the River Thames

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that he May be Tried for the same at the present Session Pursuant to his Notice given the Prosecutor for that Purpose or Bailed or discharged and as in Duty Bound your Petitioner will ever pray Etc.
