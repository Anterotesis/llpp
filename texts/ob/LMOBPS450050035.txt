To his Majestys Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal Delivery holden for the County of Middlesex at Justices Hall in the Old Bailey in the Suburbs of the City of London at this present Session assembled

The Humble Petition of William Pentlow and William Darwell.

Sheweth That Your Petitioners Stands Charged with an Indictment for Murder and are now detained in his Majestys Goal of Newgate prisoners for the Same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Lord Ships that they may at this present Sessions be Tried Bailed or discharged for the said Offence According to the Act of Parliamt. in that Case made and provided

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

William Penltors Wm. Darvall
