To the Right Honble Barlow Frecothick Esqr. and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of Oyer Terminer and General Goal Delivery for the County of Middlesex now Sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey.

The Humble Petition of John Brannon now a Prisoner in Newgate and Charged with the Wilful Murder of Mary Cuddy

Wherefore your Petition most humbly Prays that he may be Tryed for the same at this Present Session or Bailed or Discharged and your Petitioner as in Duty Bound will ever Pray Etc

John Brannon
