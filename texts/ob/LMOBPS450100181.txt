To His Majestys Justices of Goal Delivery for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of Joseph Loyd a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That Your Petitioner stands comitted & detained in his Majesty's Gaol of Newgate for felony expressed in ye Warrt. of Comitment

Your Petr. most humbly prays this Honble. Court that he way be tried bailed or discharged this Sessions according to the Statute in that behalf

the Mark of R Joseph Loyd

Wittnes John Huskins Bayles
