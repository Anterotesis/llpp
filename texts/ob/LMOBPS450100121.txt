To The Right Honble. The Lord Mayor of the City of London & others His Majestys Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx

The humble Prayer of Margaret Clough

Sheweth That Your Petitioner stands comitted to His Majesty's Goal of the Gale house for felony and stands also indicted for the some

Your Petitioner meet humbly prays this Honble. Court that she may be tried further same at this present Sessions or that she may be discharged of the Same as this Honble. Court shall think proper & that She may have the benefit of the Stable in that behalf.

Margrat Cleugh
