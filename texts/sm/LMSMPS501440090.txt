To the Rt Worpll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx now Assembled at their Genall Quarter Session of the peace held by Adjournmt. this 17th day of Febry 1714

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Celia Feinnes Spinster

Sheweth That yor. Petr. is an Inhabitant in the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the Said County and is rated Seven Shillings & Six pence P Month towards the releif of the poor of the said parish which is more in proporcon than Severall other Inhabitants of the same parish do pay to the poors rate aforesaid who rent houses of greater yearly value whereby Yor. Petr. conceived her self Aggreived.

Therefore humbly prays yor. Worpps. will be pleased to order yor. petr. such releif in the prmisses [...] as to yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
