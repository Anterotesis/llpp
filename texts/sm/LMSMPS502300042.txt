To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genll. Quarter Sessions at Hicks's hall Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of St. Helens London.

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Ellis and Richard Gifford Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor un) Bearing date the 25th. Septr. 1725 Ann Kislinbury the Widow of Richard Kislinbury Was by the said Order removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Great St. Helens in the City of London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Ann Kislinbury Whereby yor. Petrs. humbly conceive they are wronged

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray Yor. Worshpps will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal And yor. Petrs. may be releived as unto your Worshpps Shall Seem meet

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
