To the Right Worpt. the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Anne Hardman of the Liberty of the Rolls.

Sheweth That your Peticoner hath lived in the Paid Liberty these Nine yeare and upward And hath not as yet been chargeable thereunto Phit in regard the being very poore and [...] distracted and being formed to lye in the Streets And the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poore of the said liberty refuseing to allow your Peticoner any thi [...] towards her releife & mainteynance [...] oe that he is likely to perish unless comisterated and releived By yor. worpp Charity

ye. Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to allow her such Charity towards her releife and mainley name as unto yr. worpp Shall Seem meet

And ye Petr. Shall ever pray et.
