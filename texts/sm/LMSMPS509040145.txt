To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St. Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Revd. Herbert Mary D.D. and Wm. Blackmore Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Thirty first day of October last, Susannah Burges Widow and her Six Children namely Mary Ann aged Sixteen Years or thereabouts, Esther aged Ten Years or thereabouts, Margaret aged Eight Years or thereabouts, Thomas, aged Six Years or thereabouts, Elizabeth aged Four years or thereabouts, were removed from the Parish of St. George in the County of Middlesex to the said Precinct of St. Catherine as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or orders Do. humbly Appeal against the same And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in the next General Quarter Sessions that all Parties concerned may attend to hear and abide the Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jas. Clare} Solr. for the Appellants
