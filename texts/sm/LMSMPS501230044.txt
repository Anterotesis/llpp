To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the Said County

Sheweth That Elizabeth Pope widow is lately passed from the parish of St Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Edward Le [...] & William Conn Esqrs two of Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westmr [...] Quor nuns to the parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the Said County as the place of the legall Settlement of the Said Dorothy Pope

Your Peticonrs: think themselves aggrieved wth: the Judgment of the Said two Justices do appeale to this Court and humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Anne within the Liberty of Westminster & all other persons concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide Judgment & finall determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc

Midd ss Ad Genalem Quarterial Session pacis Due Regine tent P Com Midd apnd Hicks Hall in St John street in Com pred die lune (Scilt) Scdo die July Anno Rin Due ure Anne Dei gra mmc Regine Magne Britannie Etc decimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of st Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in this County have notice of this Appeale and that they & all other parties concerned doe attend this Court on Fryday next at Ten of the clock in the forenoon to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of the Court touching the Sd appeale

P Cur Harcourt
