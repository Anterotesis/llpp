To the Right Worship full His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James Dukes Place in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Thomas Robe & I Poulson Esqrs: Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of December last past Robert Dunston, Frances his Wife & two Children Frances and Anne, Anne aged about one Year & an half, were removed from the Parish of St. Luke in Old Street in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. James Dukes Place London, alledging the said Parish of St. James Dukes Place to be the Place of their Settlement.

That your Peticonrs. conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warranter Order of the sd. Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yor. Worps therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses, & order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Luck in Old Street in the said County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners will Pray

C Baynbridge & Agent for the Appeallants
