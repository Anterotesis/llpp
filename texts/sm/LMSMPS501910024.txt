[...] Worsppll his Matys Justices of [...] [...] County of Middx

The humble Peticon John Hewitt Constable [...] Endfield in the Sd County

Sheweth That there is one Quarters Sallary decide [...] to yor Petr at Christmas last for passing and relieving Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds which yor Petr standing in need of

Humbly prays this Court will be pleased to order he may be paid the same as to yor Worspps shall seem meet

And yor: Petr shall Ever pray Etc Jon Hewitt
