To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middsx

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwarden & Oversees of the poor of the Hamblett of Hampton wick in the parish of Hampton this County

Sheweth That by order dated the 20th day of January last under the hands & seals of John Bateman and Leonard Hammond Esqr two of the Maties Justices of the peace for this County a certaine male infant Child was lately removed from that part of the sd parish of Hampton called the Towne of Hampton to the other part of the Sd. parish called (Hampton wick as the place of the Last Legall settlemt of the said Child

That your petrs find themselves aggreived by the said order of removall of the Sd male Infant Child as aforesd & humbly appeal to this Court agt the same

Yor petr therefore humbly pray yor Worpps would be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of [...] Hamply Towne aforesd & all other Psons [...] concerned to [...] attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgemt & determinacon of this Court upon & touching the Sd appeal, And ye petrs Shall pray etc.
