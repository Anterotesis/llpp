To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the county of Middx on their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above Me barrs in the said County

Sheweth

That by an order under the hands and seals of Robert Dennett and Richard Richards Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (Whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of June last Past whereby Charles Clark the Younger aged about theirteen years was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the barrs in the same County as the Place his last legall Settlement

Rd. Watkins, Agent for the Appellts. Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the Peace appeal to the Court against the same and Pray the Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining the same

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray
