To the Worsshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hick's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Doddington in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Arrtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Leand and Seal of Richard Chamberlaine one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid bearing Date the Twenty third day of February now last past Martha the Wife of Henry Mexted was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Said Parish of Doddington as the last Place of the Legal Setttlement of the said Henry Mexted

That your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order And the Judgement of the said Justice And do therefore Humbly appeal unto this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners thereofore Humbly Pray that this Court will be Pleased to appoint Some day in this Present General Quarter Sessions for this County holden to hear and Determine the Matter touching their Said Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
