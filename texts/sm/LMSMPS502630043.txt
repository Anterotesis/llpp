The Humble Peticon of Andrew Park against the Poor Rate

Exhibited 13th: October 1729

[---] Crippelegate

Poore rate

refr to petty Sess: in Finsbury Div:

1729

Middx ss: Ad Genal Quartial Session paris Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd P adjorn die Lune Scilt decimo tertio die Octobris Anno regni Dui Georgy Scdi nunc Regis magne Britannie Etc tertio.

Ordered by this Court that it be and it is hereby recomended unto his Maties. of the peace for this County residing in Finsbury Division to be assembled petty Sessions to be holden in and for the Same Division to Sumon before the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles [...] Cripplegate in this County and to examine into the truth of the peticoner saint and to certify their opinions touching the premisses within menconed [...] d Maties. Justices of the peace at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace held for this County.
