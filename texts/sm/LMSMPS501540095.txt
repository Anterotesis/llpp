To the Right Worpll his maties Justices of The peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and appeale of William Crook of the parish of St Giles without Crippl Gate in the said County

That yor Petr is rated towards the releife of Poore in the parish one hundred pounds for S [...] Which is twelve Shillings & Six pence per An [...] Which yor Petr is not able to pay yor. Petr. thinks Self agreved by the said assesment
