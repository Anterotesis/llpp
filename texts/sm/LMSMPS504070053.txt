To the Workship full His Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in and for the said County now Assembled.

The humbled Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Jacob Harvey and Richard Chamberlayne Esqrs. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace (Where of one is of the Quorum) for the County of Middlesex aforesaid bearing Date the first day of November last John Thomas Colemor, Mary his Wife and their two Children name by Elizabeth aged twelve Years and Martha aged about Eight Years and Six Months were removed from the Parish of St. James Clerken Well in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London Whereby your Petitioners think them selves to be Aggrieved

Joshua Cox Sollr. for the Appell. }

Therefore they humbly Appeal to This Honourable Court And pray that the said Court will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens & Over seers of the poor of the Sd. Parish of St. James at Clerkenwell and all Person concerned Do attend this Court some day in this present Session of the Peace for the said County to lear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal Etc.

Appear Petitioners shall ever Pray
