To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Nares and James Stratton Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Thirteenth day of March now last past Elizabeth Harding Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Harding

That in as much as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and the Removal and Conveyance of the said Elizabeth Harding in Pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly pray your Worships to grant than such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
