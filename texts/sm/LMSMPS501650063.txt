The Humble peticr of John Oder

After wth: Ordr. that the pire be considered of above Scavrs

Ch:wden. & Ov: to attend Fr: 10th to she: cause why they Should not make a just equall & legall Rate upon Inhitants of Chelson according to Stat 43o. Eliz: for maintenance of poor, and that Heathfeild doe atte [...] [...] at Same time to She: cause why he being elected & duoly appted. one of the Ov: of the poor of the parish of Chelsea refuses to take upon himselfe & Execute the office,
