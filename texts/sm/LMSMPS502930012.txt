To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Hble Petition, and Appeale of William Wallton, of the parish of St. Mary White Chapple in the County of Middx Wheelright

Sheweth

That your Petitioner Rents a Small house of Four Pounds a Year in the Said parish and is Charged Two Shillings and Two pence p Month for and Towards the Necessary Releife of the Poor of the Said Parish That yor: Petr. hath, a great Family to Maintain and in very Low Circum- -stances and hath, Nothing but What he getts by his Labour and Industry whereby he is Intirely unable to pay the Said Rate And yor Petr. further setts forth, that he never was Assessed More than One Shilling and Six Pence Pr Month, And that Severall of his Neighbours who Live in houses of Greater Rents and in Better ability do not pay so much, as he now Stands Assessed.

Therefore Yor. Petr. thinks- Himself Aggreived and Humbly Appeals against the same and- prays to be releived in, the- Premisses as yor Worships- Great Wisdome and Justice Shall seem meet.

And yor Petr. as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc Wm Wallton
