[...] the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticion of John Gray junr. of the Hamlet of Wappin Stepney in the sd. County.

Sheweth, That yor. Peticoner is rated two shillings & Six pence P Month besides the overrate, towards the relief of the Poor of the sd. Hamlet, which is more in Pporcon than his neighbours with respect to his and their abilities,

Wherefore yor. Peticoner humbly Prays yr. Wopps would please to order the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Hamlet to attend this Court Some day this present Sessions, and that yor. Peticonrs. complaint may be heard and he have such relief in the premisses as to yor. Wopps shall appear just & reasonable

And yor. Peticonr. shall pray Etc.
