To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden at the Session House or Clerkenwell Green in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of St. John The Evangelist in the City of Westminster and County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Stephen Cole, and W, Heckford Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex, bearing date the 11th day of October 1787. Elizabeth Barrett Widow of John Barrett who was the Son of James Barrett with her Child named James aged 4 Years, were Removed from the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. John the Evangelist Westminster as the place of their last legal Settlement, whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal, may be saved unto them, and that the hearing and Determining of the same may be Adjourned until the next General Session.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Chas. Clarke Wm. Davis} Churchwardens of St. John the Evangelist Westminster

John Morris} Overseer of the Poor of the said Parish.
