To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Ascue [...]

Sheweth

That abt. 29 o. Decemb 1705 One Mary needham made Oath before Roger Smith Esqr. One of her Matys Justices of the Peace for this County That She was Pregnant with Child or Children which when Borne would be bastards and that One Jeremiah Booty a Single man was the true Father of the same

That in March following the sd. mary needham was Deliver and [---] in March last she made affidavit before the sd. Roger Smith Esqr. that your Petr. was the Father of the sd. Child she was for Delivered whereupon your Petr stands bound for his appearance in this honble Court this prsent Sessions and forasmuch as there being noe Order made upon your Petr. there upon and the sd. Mary Needham being a Marryed Woman and your Petr being a house keeper

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes your Worpps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd. Recognizance.

And yor. Petr shall Ever pray etc John Haskor

P Tho: Nash
