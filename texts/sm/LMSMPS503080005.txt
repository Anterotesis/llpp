To John Martin Esquire One of his Majesties Justices of the Place for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Mathew Powell an officer belonging to the Sheriff of the said County

Humbly Sheweth That the last October Sessions held by Adjournment for wth drawing Recognizances your Petitioner was fined for not giveing his due Attendance That your petr. at that time being A very young officer and not knowing it might be Any neglect inocently incurred the Displeasure of this Court for wch he humbly Craves Pardon and promises for the future to take Care not to Commit the like or any other offence beleiving he had no occasion to wait

any longer after the sessions week}

Therefore Most humbly prayes yors. Worship to interseed that his fine may be remitted

And yor. petr. as in all Duty Duty bound shalll ever pray Etc
