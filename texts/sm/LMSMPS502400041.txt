To ye Rt Worshipll His Maties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midd assembled in their Genll Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of Eltham in ye County of Kent

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Thomas Colby & Thomas Sutton Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for ye sd County of Midd (Quorn unus) bearing Date the 7th Day of November last Elizabeth Stoaks Single Woman (then big with Child was removed from the parish of Kensington in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Eltham as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

That the said Elizabeth Stoakes is now in Labour & not [...] able to appear this prsent Sessions

Yor: Petrs. think themselves aggriev'd by the Indgmt. of the said two Justices of the peace Appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the benefit of there said Appeale may be saved to Yor: Petrs. and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Kensington & all other Psons concern'd may be Ordered to attend this Court some adjornmt. day this or some Pay next Sessions to hear & abide the Indgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
