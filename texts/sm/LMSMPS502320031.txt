To the Right Worship full his Majties. Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled

She humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of thr Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Ellis Esqr Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (quorum) bearing date the four and twentieth day of December last John Rice and Mary his wife were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legall Settlement whereby your petitioners Concerive themselves to be agreived

Therefore they humbly appeale, for to Your Worships against the Judgemt of the said Two Justices and pray you Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions to here and determine the matter touching the said Appeale

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
