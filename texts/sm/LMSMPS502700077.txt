To the Right Worshipll. his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeale of Samuel Rayson of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the sd. County Gentl

Sheweth That your Petr. is charged towards the releife of the poor and Scavengrs. Rates of the Said parish much more than he ought to pay. Therefore he thinks himself aggrieved by the said Rates and Appeale agt. ye Same

And pray's your Worps. to appoint Some day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor And Scavengrs. of ye. sd. Parish to attend yr. Cort. to hear & abide ye. Judgemt. & Determinacon of ys. Court touching ye sd. appeale

And your Petr. shall pray.

Samll Rayson
