To ye Worshipfull ye Justices now Sitting upon ye Bench at Hicks Hall

The Humbly Petition of Sophiah David

Sheweth That yor. said Petitioner is willing to take two Guineas in hand & a Bond for [---] [...] eight Guineas more which is but a trifle to what charge & damage ye Said Petitioner has been at

Therefore Yor. Petitioner leaves it to yor Worshipfull discretions not doubting but yor said Worships will releive her as much as in yor Worshipfull power ly's And Yor. sd. Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray
