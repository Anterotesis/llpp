To the Right Worshipful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlesex At their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Margaret Chambers how a Prisoner In Clerkenwell Bridewell In the Said County

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petr. being Subpen'ad on the behalf of Phillip Goldrick and his Wife who hath been Convicted and Sent to Goal and your Petr. having Giving her Evidence and making a Disturbance In Court was there upon Comitted your Petr. being at Sometimes Disorderd In her Sensses Occaisioned the said Disturbance your Petr. having now a Sickly Husband and Two Small Children unprovided for your Petr. most humbly pray your Worsps would be pleased to Comiserate her unhappy Case and to Order that She may be Discharged

December 5th: 1754

And yr; Petr. Shall Ever Pray Margritt Chamber
