To the Rt: Worsppth: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Rebecca Stafford

Sheweth

That about Three Weekes agee [...] your Petitr: was by vertue. of a Bench Warrant granted by this Court taken up and Carryed to New Prison in this County and soone after gave Bail before the Worshippfull Justice Fuller to appear this present Sessions

That your Petitr now lyes in a Sick and Weak Condicon. Confined to her Bedd Jusoe much that She is not able to attend this Sessions The truth of which is ready to be. attested upon Oath to this Court

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays Your Worspps willbe pleased to Respite her. said Recognizance untill next Sessions And Yor: Petitr shall Ever pray Etc.

Rebecca Stafford-
