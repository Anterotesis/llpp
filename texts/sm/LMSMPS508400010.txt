To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble [---] Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Mich: Downs and William Tyler Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of October Instant Charles Aged Twelve years and Agnes aged Ten years the Children of John Foothead gone away by Ann his Wife deceased were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said Order And pray your Worships will appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Richd. Christmas Tho. Byrnes James Baugham G Pressey} Churchwardens & Overseers
