To the Worspll: His Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Paddington in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of John Ellis and Thomas Woodcock Esqrs: two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd. (Quor unus) bearing date the 13th: day of July last past Martha Adderley aged 50 Years Widow of Ralph Adderley and their two Children Vizt: Catherine aged 16 Years and Ralph aged 15 Years were removed from & out off the parish of St: James Westminster in the said County into the Said Parish of Paddington as the place of their last legall. Settlement.

That thereby yor. Petr. humbly Conceive themselves to be Agrieved.

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly appeale to yor. Worsps agt: the Order of the said two Justices & pray yor. Worsps will be please to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St: James Westmr: and, all Psons concerned do attend this Court some day this Generall Session to hear & abide the Judgemt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Yor. Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc

Danl Bolton
