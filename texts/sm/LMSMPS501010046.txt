To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices

The Humble Peticon of John Barns of the Pa- -rish of St Giles without Cripplegate Labourer

Sheweth That your Petetioner being bound over to appear the last sessions of the Peace was taken up by a Constable & secured in New Prison, and therefore could not appearthen to withdraw his Recognizance; and Your Petetioner being very poor, humbly prays Your Worshipps; that he may now with- -draw his Recognizance, paying no more, then if he could have done if the last sessions

And Your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
