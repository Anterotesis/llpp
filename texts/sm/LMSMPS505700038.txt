To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Samuel Peirson and Thomas Miller Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of this Instant April Hannah a Female Child aged near 3 Years was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlements Whereby Your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Margaret and all persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

B Wood Saml. Agar Geo: Joward, Jno Dorman Ricd. Nowlson} Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish. of Saint Anne
