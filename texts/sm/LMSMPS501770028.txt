To the Right Worrpll: His Maties: Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Bushey in the County of Hertson

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr William Ashhurst Knt & Marmaduke Bealing Esqr two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing date the twenty eighth day of January last Richard Perreman Mary his wife & Amy their daughter were passed from the parish of Finchley in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Bushey in the County of Hertford as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Richard Perreman

P James Fyton Your Peticonrs: think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices do appeal to this Court and humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Finchley & all other persons concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor: Peticonrs: (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc
