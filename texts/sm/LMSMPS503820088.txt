To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in the sd. County now assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Stephen Beckingham and Vigirus Edwards Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing Date the fourth day of December instant Nathaniel Middlebrook Alice his wife and their two Daughters namely Margaret aged about sixteen Years and Jane aged about eight Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the sd. County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn London Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Joshua Cox sollr for the Appeallts. Therefore they humbly appeal to this Honourable Court And pray that the said Court will he pleased to order that the Church Warden and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the sd. County of Middlesex and all Persons concerned Do attend this Court some day in this persent Sessions to hear and abide the Judgemt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal And yor. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
