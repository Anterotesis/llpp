To [---] the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace now holden at Hicks Hall in St John Streets in the said County

The Humble Petition of Francis Lafarge of the Hamlett of Spittlefields Barber Surgeon

Sheweth That your Petitioner's Apprentice Simeon Rabi was this day Croughts before Isaac Tillard Esqr one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex by Vertue of a Warrant from the said Justice for running away from your petitioner and absenting [...] his service and being an Idle and Disorderly Person whereupon the said Justice did bind by Recognizance [...] your Petitioner's said Apprentice to answer the same at this General Quarter Sessions of the Peace And your said petitioner together with his said Apprentice [...] being now presents and being both willing to be Discharged the one from the other most humbly prays that this Court would now Discharge your Petitioners said Apprentice [...] from his said Apprenticeship or make such order touching the Premisses as this Court shall think fit

And your Petitioner Shall ever Pray Etc
