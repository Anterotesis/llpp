To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for this County of Middx

The Humble peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish of Tottenham high Cross in the said County of Middlx

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated the seventeenth day of November last under the Hands & Seals of Thomas Pindar & W. Heyton Esqr.two fo his Majties Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx.(Quor unus) one Jonathan Ackers & Joyce his wife were removed & conveyed from the parish of St. Mary Islington in the sd. County. to the said parish of Tottenham high Cross as the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That your Petrs. appr. hend themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the sd two Justices, and doe Appeale to this Court agt the same

Yor. Petrs. therefore pray this Court would be pleased to appoint same Day in this pr.sent Sessions for the Churchwardens and Over seers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Mary Islington and all other persons concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd Appeale, And that your petrs may be releived there in according to Law.

And Yor. Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
