To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All Saints in the City of Norwich.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Mr. Buckley Clerk and John White Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth Day of August 1797 Mary aged five Years and Elizabeth aged two Years the lawful Children of Benjamin Christopher by Mary his late Wife deceased the said Benjamin Christopher having deserted them were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of All Saints in the said City of Norwich as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining this their Appeal And that You will order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone and all other Persons concerned to attend the same

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
