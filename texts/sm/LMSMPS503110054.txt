To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble petition and appeal of the Churchwards. and Overseers of the poor of the parish of saint John at Hackney in the said County.

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or order bearing date the Sixteenth day of this Ingtant August under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Chandler and Ralph Harwood Esquires tow of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) whereby Eleanor Raper was removed from the parish of Saint Leonard Shereditch in the said County to the parish of Saint John at Hackney in the same County as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the Judgment and Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same praying such relief in the Premisses as to your Worshipps shall seem meet.

And your petitioners will her pray Etc
