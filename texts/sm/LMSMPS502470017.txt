To The Worshipfull his Majtes: Justices of the Peace of ye City of London

The Humble Petition of Joseph Brown of ye Parrish of St: Stevens Wallbrook London

Sheweth

By Way of Appeal [---] this Worshipfull Bench that he is Rated Ninetene Shillings & Six pence a yeare That he is unable to pay towards the Maintainance of ye Poor all his Estate Either in Goods house hold stuff or Money in his Possession (his debts being first paid) Doe not amount to the Summ of Thirtye Pounds as ye Petitioner is readey to make Oath of tharefore humbly prays to be discharged thare from

Octbr: ye: and yor: Petitioner 16 day 1727 shall ever Pray
