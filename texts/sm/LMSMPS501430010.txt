To the Rt. Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Mary Bates of the parish of st Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Wife of Richard Bates

Sheweth

That your petitus allowed Eight Shillings p Month as a pention from the said Parish But for as much as your Petitrs: said Housband having lately had the misfortune to break his Leg For the Cure of which he is now in st Thomas Hospitall and your Petitr: having five small Children to Mainteyne and being turned out of Doors for Nonpayment of her Rent Must Inevitably. starve without a further Allowance from the said Parish

Your Petitr therefore humbly Prays Your Worspps will be pleased to order that her said pention may be Augmented As onto your Worspps shall seeme Meeke

And Yor Petitr shall ever pray Etc:

Meary Bates
