To the Rt. Worshipll. His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the- Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petr. of William Sumers

Sheweth That yer. Petr. was bound by Indr. bearing date the 1st. day of May 1723 to Mathew Nicholls of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County Gilder for the Form of Three years.

That his Said Master has absconded himself for Nine Weeks last past & upwards and never Appears But on Sundays and takes no Care of Your Petr. either to teach him his Trade or provide Necessaries for him according to the Articles of his Indre.

Yor. Petr. there fore humbly- prays that this Cort. will be pleased to Discharge yor. petr. from his said Apprenticeship [...]

And yer. Petr. shall ever pray etc

Willm. Summers
