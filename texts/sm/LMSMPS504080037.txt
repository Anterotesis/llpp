To the Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Milner & Philip Dyer Esqrs two of his Majestye's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date ye. Ninth day of January instant Sarah Mongomery Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Luke Alledging the Said Parish of St Luke to be the place of her last legall Settlement

Jno. Witton Agent for the Appellts. } That your Petitr. conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the Sd. the two Justices, Do Appeal to your Worhips therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields may appear before you at a certain day to be Appointed to hear & abide & the Judgement & determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petitr. shall ever pray etc
