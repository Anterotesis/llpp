To the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the overseers of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of J: Hale and William Hyde Exqr: two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth Day of September 1779 Susannah Price Wife of John Price ( now in his Majestys Sea Service)and their two Children to Wit Elizabeth aged four Years and Sarah aged two Years were pernarved and conveyed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid from and out of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields to the said Liberty of the Ralls as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the paid Order or Pass Warrant humbly Appeal against the same to Your Worships and Pray relief in the Promises and that a Day in the present Session may be appointed for the hearing and determining the said Appeal
