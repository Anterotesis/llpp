To the Richt Worth: their Maites: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St Giles in the found in ye said County

Sheweth That William Forster & Hannah his wife were lately passed by Warrant under the hands & seas of Robert Constable & George Bohun Esqrs two of their Maties Justices of the deare of the said County from the Pish of St [...] Mary white Chappell in the said County to the said Pish of St Giles in ye Feilds thereby alleadgen that they were last legally Settled there haveing heed in a house of Sixteen Pounds Panne rent & paid parish all Duties of all fords

Mary it please this worll: Bench the said William Forster & his wife never had any legall settlement in there Pish of St Giles in the Feilds as hath been allend [...]

Your Peticonrs therefore Appeals from the said Order & humbly pray that you would be pleases to Order the Churchwardens & Over- seers of the poor of the Pish of white Chappell to appeare in the Court att a day retaine to shew Cause why the said William Forster & his wife should not be repassed to the said Pish of White Chappell

And Your Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc
