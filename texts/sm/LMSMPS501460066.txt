To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matyes Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Salsbury Constable of South Mims in the said County

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr at Midsumer last for passing and Believing of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Term Pounds.

That your Petitr hath beene at great charge and Expence in Passing and Believing the said Vagrants And being a poor Man and Standing in Great want of his said Moneys.

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to order that he may [...] be paid the said Moneys As unto [...] your Worspps shall Seeme Meete

And yor Petitr shall ever pray etc.

John Salsbury
