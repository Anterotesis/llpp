To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of George Mercer and J. Mercer Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of September 1793 Mary Nicholas, Single woman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Marylebone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement where by your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their sad Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal and your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

Thos: Wallis P Richardson Willm. Morris Thos Gleaves Ferdinand Ballhorn Joseph Whitaker Thos Cockings Jna. Dutton Edwd Hayley} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor
