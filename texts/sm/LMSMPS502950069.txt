To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx in Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Hble Petion and appear of Ann Burton of the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County Wido.

Sheweth That Your Peticoner Rents a house of Eighteen pound a Yeare and is Charged three Shillings and three pence a Month towards the Releife of the poor of the sd. parish That Severall of Your Peticoners Neighbours who live in houses of Greater Rent and Better Ability do not pay so much Therefore

Your Peticon [...] thinks herself Agreived and Humbly appeals against the Same and prays to be releived in the premises as your Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoner as in duty bound shall ever pray and so forth

G: Meidel Attorney

Ann Burton
