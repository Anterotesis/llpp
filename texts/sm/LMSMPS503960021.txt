To the Worshipfull the Chairman of the Bench at Hicks Hall and the rett of the Worshipfull Justices there

The Humble Peticon of Richard Smith this Day Convicted [---] for an Assault on Catherine Daven Port and fined one shilling

Sheweth That your peticoner is a very poorman and not able to pay his fees but is willing to pay so much as in his power

Therefore yr [---] peticoner & prays such relief in the premisses as to your worship [...] shall seem meet & he shall ever pray Etc

Richd: Smith
