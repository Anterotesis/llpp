To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition of Thomas: Gosden of the Hamblett of Wapping Stepney in the said County.

Sheweth That in March last your Petr was Passed from the Parish of St Olave Southwarke to the aforesaid Hamblett as the place of this case Legall Settlement That upon application made by yor Petr to this Court for relief that the Churchwarden promised to allow your Petr. Eighteen Pence P weeke Pention But hath not allowed him anything And forasmuch at yr petr by reason of his great age is rendred uncapable of Doing any. thing for a Lively hood and being Lame must perish in the Streets unless relieved by the goodness of this Court.

Yor Petr: most humbly prays this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this Sessions for the Churchwardens to attend to there cause why your poor petr: should not be allowed some relief for his Support in this Extretialy

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc Tho: Gosden
