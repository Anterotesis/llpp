To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx in Generall Quarter Session assembled,

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx

Sheweth That by a Warrt: or Order of removall under the Hands and Seal of R Midford and Johna Milner Esqrs: two of Adj. Maties Justices of the Peace of the County (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the tenth day of this Instant October, Joshna Coston Mary his wife and Elinor their Child aged about ten Months was removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County aforesaid to the parish of saint Botolph without Aldgate in the Sd. County as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Peticoners think themselves Agreived by the Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices and Humbly Appeal against the same)

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the fields and all toher persons Concerned to Attend this Court some day this Present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the Premises And that Your Peticoners may have smith whife therein as to Justice and Law doth Appertain

And your Peticoners Shall every pray Etc

Wm Barrow Samll Jordan} Churchwarden

P: Meidel Attr for the Applls }
