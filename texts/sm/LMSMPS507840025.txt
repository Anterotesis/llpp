To the Worshipfull his matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the Sessions of the Peace of our Lord the King holden for the said County

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Martin Outwich in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrt under the hands & Seals of David Wilmot & Chas Trequet Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of June 1784 Nathaniel West was removed & Conveyed from & out of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the sd County unto the sd Parish of St Martins Outwich in the City of London the sd Justices thereby Adjudging the last legal Settlemt of the sd Nathl West to be in the Parish of St. Martins Outwich

That yr Petrs. apprehend the Settlemt. of the sd Nathl West is not in the sd Parish of St. Martin Outwich & therefore your Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the sd Order of Removal or Pass Warrant

Your Petitioners do therefore Appeal

from the said Order of the sd two Justices & humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Sessions to hear & determine the sd Appeal
