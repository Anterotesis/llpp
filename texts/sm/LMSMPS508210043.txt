To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass warrant or order under the hands and Seals of D: Walker and Philip Dyot. Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being [...] of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second day of January last John Warnes was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the said order and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Session for the hearing and determining the said appeal

And your petitioners shall pray [...]

Richd Christmas Theos. T Tutt Thos. Bourdillon} Churchwardens

Geo: Leigh John Toms} Overseers
