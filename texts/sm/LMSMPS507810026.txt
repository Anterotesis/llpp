To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in your General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows London Wall in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under [...] Hands and Seals of S. Hawley and Robert Smith [...] two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said [...] of Middlesex bearing Late the Twentieth day of [...] One thousand seven hundred and eighty four Mary [...] Thompson Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of [...] Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said [...] of Middlesex to the Parish of Allhallows on London [...] in the City of London as the Place of the said Mary [...] Thompson's last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners [...] conceive themselves aggrieved

Therefore Humbly Pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present General Quarter Session to hear and determine their said Appeal
