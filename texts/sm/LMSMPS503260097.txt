To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph wth. out Aldgate in London

Sheweth- That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrt. Under the Hands Seals of Wm. Hayton Esqr & Wm: Browing Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for ye said County of Middlsex One Where of is of the Quorum bearing Date the Wth: Day of September 1737 In the Eleventh Year of his Prsent Majestys Reign One Richard Garrett & Elizabeth his Wife were removed from & out of ye Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middlesex into the sd. Parish of St. Botolphs without Aldgate London as the Places of the last Legall Settlement

Yor Petitioners therefore Humbly conceiving themselves Aggreived by ye sd. Order or Pass Warrt. Do Appeale Agt. the Same & Pray yt. this Honrble Court will Please to Order ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the sd. Parish of St. Luke and all Parties Concerned to Appear Some Day at this prsent Sessions to Hear & Abide ye Indgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd. Appeal
