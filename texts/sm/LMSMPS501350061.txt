The Humble Peticon And appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seales of Rich [...] Woodward and John Crawford. Esqrs Two other [...] Justices of the peace for the said County bearing Date [...] and Twentieth day of August last John Gill a poor Man [...] removed from the Parish of Fulham for Fulham side [...] this County unto yor Peticoners said Parish as the place of his last legall settlement by which Order yor Peticoner Con [...] they are wronged and Aggreived.

Yor Peticoners therefore humbly Appeal to yor [...] against the said Order and pray yor Worshipp [...] Appoint a Day to hear and Determine the same

And yor Peticoners shall Ever Pray Etc
