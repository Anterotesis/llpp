To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of Thomas Pruce

Sheweth

To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

Sheweth The humble Peticon of the Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Katherine in the sd. County.

That in December last One Nicholas Hayes of the sd. parish was by Warrant under the Hand & Seale of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County comitted to the house of Correccon in the sd. County for running away & leaving his Wife & Three Children to be mainteyned att the Charge of the sd. Parish

That June the sd. Hayes has been Soe comitted as aforesd his sd. Wife is dead & the sd parish have Expended & Disbursed the Sum of 6s. or thereabouts in & about the mainteying & keeping the sd Hayes Wife & Children and forasmuch as the sd Hayes not haveing [---] as yell male any proposecon for fulempinfyed the sd. Parish from his sd. Children or to pay the Money by them disbursed & [---] your Petr. being still att [...] awal charge with the sd. Children Oare likely to be foe for the future.

P Tho. Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humble Pray ye Worspps taker pleased to Grant that the sd Hayes may [---] be continued in Custody untill he has pd. the Money disbursed by the sd parish & Seven Security for fulempinfying the sd. Parish from the maintey name of his said Three Children

Emamens Hudson

And Yor. Petr Shall ever Pray Etc/Charles Luddon
