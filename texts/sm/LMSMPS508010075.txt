To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of W. Blackmore and Herbt. Mayo two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the Seventeenth Day of January last past Mary Robson Wife of William Robson now at Sea and her two Children Vizt. Elizabeth Robson aged two years or thereabouts and Mary Robson Aged three Months or thereabouts were Removed and Convey'd from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Robson and her two Childrens

Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and pray Your Worships will appoint a Day in the present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

Middlesex At the General Quarter Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden at the Session House in and for the said County on Middlesex on Monday the 10th.. Day of May in the 25th. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the third and in the Year of our Lord 1785

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex have Notice of the within Appeal and that they and all Persons concerned do attend this Court on Thursday next at 9 o' Clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court
