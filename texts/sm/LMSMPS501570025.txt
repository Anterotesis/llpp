To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of John Levett Edward Walker, Moses Bull Alexander Cruikshank George Fisher, Daniel Thomas, Edward, Steed, John Whiten, John Podemead, Henry Hemrick, Eliz: Clifton, Thomas Gray, John Charriott, Henry Oliver & Firinand Simmon Inhabitants belonging to the precinct of St. Katherine in the said County.

Sheweth. That your Petrs. are this year raised to the poors Rate of the said precinet( [...] [---] and particularly John Levett who is this year charged twelve pounds twelve Shillings to the said Rate where as he paid but Seven pounds the last year

That Severall of the Inhabitants of the said precinet who are of as good abillity as your Petrs. are not charged so Much but on the contrary much less then they paid last year and for Severall years last past

That the present Rate was not assessed and made as the Rates for forty Years last past have usually been made for wth: reason your Petr. are very much agreived by the said Rate and humbly appeale to this Honrble: Court against the same

And Pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order the Overseers of the poor of the said Precinet to Attend this Court with the said Rate some day this present Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Richard Cuey Tho: Gray his mark John Chariott Henry Oliver F [...] Sirinond Sinmon

Geo. Fisher Dan: Thomas Edward Stead John Whiton Jno. Deamead Henry Hemrick Eli Clifton

[...] Edward Walker Moses Bull A Cruikshank
