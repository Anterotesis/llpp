Eve Salmons peticon

1686

Feb 1690/1

To the Worpffull: Bench of Justices

The humble Peticon of Eve Salmon

Sheweth That your Peticonar is an Apprentice to Rich Atkinson and his Wife for the Space of Eight Yeares to learne the Art of Houswifery your Peticonar haveing recd Severall abuses through want of Food & Cloathes which has been the onely instrument to make her Frequent ill companey and yor petr can provide for her selfe wthout being a charge to any Pson and her said Mat & Mrbrefuse to a entertaine her any longer

She therefore humbly beggs to be discharg ed From her Said Servitudd

And as in duly bound She Shall ever Pray
