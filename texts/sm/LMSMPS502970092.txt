To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Priestley and Henry Dodson Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of December One thousand Seven hundred and thirty three Sarah Winington Aged about five Years and Margarett Winington Aged about two Years and a half Children of Francis Winington and Jane his Wife were Removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Winington and Margarett Winington

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint a day of this present Sessions that all parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and determination of this Court for and touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. as in duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.
