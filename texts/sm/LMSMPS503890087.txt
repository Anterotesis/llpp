To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in General Quarter Session at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street in and for the sd. County [---]

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Long Aston in the County of Somerset

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of D: Hech stretter and Rd Hassell two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 9th. Day of June last John Holder Ann his Wife and their Three Children (to wit) Joseph aged Seven Years Jacob aged upwards of three Years and Ann aged upwards of one Year were removed from the Parish of Finchley in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Long Aston in the County of Somerset aforesd. as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and agt. the sd. Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a short Day may be Appointed this present Session to hear and Determine the matter touching the sd. Appeal

John Adderley of Clemts. Inn Agt. for Long Aston}

And your Petr. shall pray Etc
