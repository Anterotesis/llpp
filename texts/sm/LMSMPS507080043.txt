To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St: John Street in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary at Hill in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Darling Knt: and Wilmot Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex, (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 3d: day of November last Elizabeth Haro Single Woman were removed and conveyed from the Hamlet of Poplar of Blackwall in the County of Middlesex aforesaid do the said Parish of Saint Mary at hill in the City of London as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal do this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order And pray that the benefit of this there Appeal may be saved unto them And that the hearing and Determining thereof may he adjourned unto the next Session of the Peace to be held for this County to hear and determine the same And that your Worships will be pleased to make your Order for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Poplar and all Persons concerned then to Attend

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

William Elliot Sollr. for the Appellts.
