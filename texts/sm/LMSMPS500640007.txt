To the honrble the Bench of Justices

The humble Peticon of Griffith Wooton

Sheweth

That your Peticoners wife haveing bound him over to the peace and by your honr order have this Sessions continued his recognizance the matter against your petr. being onely vexations and a Frivilous and cann proceede onely from some malitious people that Evilly advised his wife soe to doe

Your Peticoner humbly beggs your honr to appointe what time his sd wife shall shew cause to this honrble Bench why his Recognizance are continued

And your petr: shall Ever pray
