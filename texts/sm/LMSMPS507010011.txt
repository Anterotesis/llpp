To ye Right Worspll. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Henry Whitch of Wm: Bulley Richard Wink feild Thomas Odell Edmd. Cooke on behalfe of themselves and divers other Inhabitants and Land holders in the parish of Finchley in the said County

Sheweth That of late an addiconall rate of £4 P pound was made towards the better releife of the poor of the said parish and afterwards another addiconall rate of £6 P pound was also made towards the better releife of the poor of ye sd parish.

That yor. Petrs. are well informed and Satisfyed that the said Addiconall rate of £4 P pound is Sufficient for the releife of the poor of the Said parish for the Year last past Soe that yor. Petrs. conceires themselves aggreved by the sd addiconall rate of £6 P pound

Yor: petrs therefore humbly pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may be discharged from paymt. of the addiconall rate of £6 P pound

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
