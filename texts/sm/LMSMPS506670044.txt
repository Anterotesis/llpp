To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Sessions Assembled at Hickes Hall in St. Johns Street in the County of Middlesex

The Appeal of William Collison of the Parish of Chelsea in the County of Middlesex Baker

Sheweth unto your Worships

That your Appellant was on [---] the 28th day of August last convicted before Thomas Miller Esqr one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for this [...] County in the penalty of 20 for [...] one half P [...] 3 guines and [...] [---] maker so of then forever needs.

That your Appellant Apprehends himself Aggrieved by such Conviction and Appeals (as He is Advised) for the Judgment of the Court in the behalf

Your Appellant therefore humbley Prays your worship [...] to appoint or day for [...] Said Appeal [---] and give such Judgment and make such Order as to Your worships [---] shall seem Meet

And Your Appellant shall ever Pray

Wm: Collison
