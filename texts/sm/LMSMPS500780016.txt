To the Rt. Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticonr appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St John Wapping in the said County

Sheweth That James Vandevell & Katharine his wife and Mary James & Dorithy their Children was by Warrt under the hands and Senles of Robt Bateman & John Webber Esqrs, two of his Masties Justices of the Peace for the said County lately past from the Hamlett of Wapping to the parish of St John Wapping

That the said James Vandevell was never married to the said hath his pretended wife till the 16th. day of March last which was after the birth of her last Child Soe that the said three Children are illegitimate and none of them borne or Christened in the said Parish of St John Wapping which said last Child was born in the Said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney

Your Petr. therefore appeale from the Judgment of the said Justices and must humbly Pray Your Worpps to order that the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamlett may appeare at such time and place as Your Worsps shall direct to shew cause why the said Warrt should not be Sett a side

And your Petr shallen pray Etc
