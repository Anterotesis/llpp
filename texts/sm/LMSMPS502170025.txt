To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of William Robson of the Parish of Hampstead in the said County

Sheweth

That on the 2d: of September last a Poor Hackny Coachman Getting up on his Coach box on Hampstead Heath had the Misfortune to receive a Kick from one of the Horses which broke one of his Legs where he lay in a Deplorable condition ready to Perish untill your Petitioner (who lived near ye. place the said Accident happen'd) in pitty and compassion to him took him into his house and Gott proper relief for him

That upon Application made to the Churchwarden of Hampstead of this sad accident He ordered the poor Hackny Coachman to be taken care of which was accordingly done And Your Petr: at his own Expence Supplied him in his Extremity with Meat Drinke and all Necessarys for the Space of Twenty Weeks That your Petitr hath applied to Mr John Spinke the Churchwarden for sattisfaction but he refuseth to give him any or to Sattisfy the Surgeon

Your Petitr therefore humbly hopes this Court will be pleased to order ye sd Churchwarden to attend this Sessions to shew cause why your petr: should not be relieved in the premises:

And Yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc Wm: Robson
