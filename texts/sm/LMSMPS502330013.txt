To the Rt: Worpfull His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bennet Gracechurch in the City of London

Sheweth That a Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Sir Isaac Tillard Knt. Alexander Pitfeild Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace the County of Middx (Quon unus) bearing Date the Twelfth Day of February last past Sarah Holmes [...] Widow was removed from the Hamlett of Stevunheath in the County of Middx unto the sd Parish of St. Bennet Gracechurch London, alledging the said Parish of St: Bennet Gracechurch to be the Place of her last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petioners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Tow Justices Do therefore Appeale to yor. Worps therefrom And humbly pray that yor. Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses & Order That the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Spittle feilds in the said County of Middx may appear before at a certain Day by yor. Worps to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgmts. & Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
