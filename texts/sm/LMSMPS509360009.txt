To the Worshipful his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of [...] Sunbury in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the hands and coals of John Collick & John Hatchett Esquires how of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of March 1797 Martha Meares wife of Alexander Meares (Insane) was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Sunbury in the said County Alledging the said Parish of Sunbury to be the Place of the said Martha Meares last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant order of the said two Justices Do therefore appeal to your Worship therefrom and humbly pray that you will please to hear them in the Premises & order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Field may appeal before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of your worships touching this appeal And your is Petitonas & hall ever pray Etc

Rd Tollemach solr for the appellts
