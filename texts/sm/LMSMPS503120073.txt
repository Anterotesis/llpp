To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Peticon of Samuel Bellus Constable of the Liberty of Glass house yard in the parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate in the said County.

Sheweth. That the Inhabitants of the said Liberty hath at a meeting nominated one John Ashton an Inhabitant of the same, to serve the office of Constable in the room of your Petr. for the year ensueing, but he refuses to be Sworn into the said office, Therefore your Petrs thinks himselfe aggreived and prays this Honble Court to releive him in the premises

And your Petr shall pray Etc.

Samll Bellus
