To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Matfelon otherwise WhiteChapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Charles Friquet Joseph Faikney Esquires. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in 7 for the County of Middlesex, One whereof being of the Quorum, bearing Date the 4th. Day of November, Benjamin Hipwell, Ann Hipwell his wife Robert Hipwell his Son. Aged about 9 Years were remove'd & Convery'd from & out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary Matfelon otherwise White Chapel as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggriev'd

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court againt the Adjudication of the said Two Justices, and Their said Order and Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear & determine the said appeal

And Your Petitioners will Pray

Skull & Greene Sollr. for the Appelts
