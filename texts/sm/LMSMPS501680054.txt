To The Rt: Worppll. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hanmore Magna In the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order dated ye. 20th day of January Last under the hands & Seales of Nicholas Philpott & William Buchnall Esqrs Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx One Hugh Peplo & Mary his wife and their five Children [---] were removed from the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in the said County to the said Parish of Stanmore Magna as the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That Your Peticoners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Judgmt. of the said Two Justices And Doe Appeal there from Your Peticoners therefore pray that Your Worpps will be pleased to Appoint a Short day for the hearing of the said appeal

And Your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
