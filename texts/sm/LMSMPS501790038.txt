To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petetion and Appeale of John Davis John Radclift John Rose Richard Ambler and Benjamin Combs of the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate

Humbly Sheweth That yor: Petrs: are all over Charged to the poors Rate of the said psh: Vizt: John Davis Richard Ambler, John Rose are Severally Charged for Two Hundred pounds prsonall Estate Each that yor Petitionr: John Radclift is charged for three Hundred Pounds Prsonall Estate whereas yor Petetionr: hath no Such Prsonall: Estate and that Yor: Peter: Benjamin Combs is Asest for Sixteen Pounds pr And Rent whereas he holds but 14th pr Ann Rent

Yor Petr: therefore Humbly prays that then may be Releived as you in yor Great wisdom shall see meet

And yor. prs. shall ever pray
