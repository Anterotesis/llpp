Middx: To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in the General Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of Robert Pilkington one of the Constables for the Parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth

That Yor. Peticonr hath Served the Office of Constable in the said Parish of Kensington Upwards of One Year, And that Yor. Peticonr: is Credibly informed by the Bailiff of Abbotts Kensington that there will be no Court Leet held this Year for Chooseing of Officers.

Henry Vincent Therefore Yor. Peticonr. most humbly Prays that he may be Discharged of his Officer and [...] nother Person Sworne in his Stead.

And Yor. Peticonr: as in duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc. Robt: Pilkington-
