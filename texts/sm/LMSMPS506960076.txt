To the Right Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now sitting at Hicks hall in the said County

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of ye: Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Pancras soper Lane London

Sheweth That by an order made the 19th: of May last John Bond and John Coggs Esqrs. two of her Maties: Justices of the Bane for the County of Middlx (Quor nunc) upon ye: Complaint of ye: Church Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. pish of St. Clements Danes in ye. said County of Middx your Petitioners are required to receive & Provide for Elizabeth Prosser Spinster as they do other their poor

Great your Peticoners do find themselves aggrieved by the said order And do humbly Appeal from ye: same to your worshipps now sitting in open Quarter Sessions of ye: Peace for the said County be pleased therein as do your Worpps shall seem meet

And your Petitioner and Appeal unto shall ever pray Etc

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dne Worpprs Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St John Str in com prd die Lune scilt Septimo die July Annexed Due Anne is Rne Mayne Britannie Etc sexto

it is ordered by this Court that ye. Church wardens and Overseers of ye: poor of ye. pish of St. Clements Danes in this County have notice of this Appeal & that they under all other parties concerned do attend this Court on Fryday next at tenn of the Clock in ye. forenoon to leave & abide ye: Judgmt and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

p Cur Harcourt
