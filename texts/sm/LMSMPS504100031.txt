To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of John Upton and Francis Burton Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of March 1751 Mathew Gray Ann his Wife and their two Children Mary Aged thirteen Years and Alice Aged twelve years were removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover square in the Liberty aforesaid to the said Parish of St James as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved And Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore [...] humbly Pray that this Court will be Pleased Appoint a time for hear ing and Determining the said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned May then Attend

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Saml. Seddon Agent for [...] James's
