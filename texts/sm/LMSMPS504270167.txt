To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnall Green in the said County

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant and is the Hands & Seals of Benjamin Cax & John Fielding Esqr two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of October last Elizabeth Shepard Wife of William Shepard (who is gone away left his said Wife as mentioned in the said Order was [...] Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnall Green as the Place of her last legall Settlem [...]

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrid [...] by the said Order or Pass Warrant Appeal to this Court against the same humbly pray that the Benefit of the [...] [...] Westmr Served to your Petition [...] that the hearing of Determining [...] thereof may be Ad found untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be held for this County

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

Dan Bolton Attr. for the Appellts.
