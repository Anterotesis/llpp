To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justice the Peace for the County of Middlesex Asse [...] in General Sessions at Hicks's Hall in the S [...]

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchward [...] Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hano [...] in the County aforesaid

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or under the Hands and Seal's of R: Manning and John Laro two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Liberty Westminster (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date Thirteenth Day of November last Sarah Floyd and her Patrick aged about four Years were removed from the Pa [...] St. James in the LIberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx: Parish of St. George Hanover Square, As the Place of the legall Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think the Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly [...] this Court will please to Appoint [...] hearing the said Appeal and that all [...] Concerned may Attend at the same

And Your Petitioner's shall ever pray [...]

Danis Bond James Spilman} Churchwardens
