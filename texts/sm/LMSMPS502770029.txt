To the Worshipfull his Majess Justices of the Peace att Hicks Hall

Sheweth That Your Poor Petitr Prudence Pebworth has been A prisoner in Bridewell a long time but is acquitted Two months agoe Only paying his fees and being She has no friends is forced to lye there and is almost Starved for want of proper Sustenance and humbly Beggs You [...] take it unto Consideration So as She may be Discharged & as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc
