To the Worpll: Bench of Justices at Hicks's Hall

The humble Peticon of Thomas Jeffreys & al

Sheweth. That Yor: Peticoners [...] Employed Mr. Henry Denton practiconer in this Court in a Cause which was Tryed last Sessions before [...] Honble Bench. And the sd Mr. Denton having Delivered yor Peticoner [...] Extravagant & [...] reasonable Bill as yor: peticoners [...]

Your peticoners humbly pray That this Worppfull Bench would be pleased to Appoint Mr: Hardisty or some other Officer of this Court to Tax the said Bill.

And yor peticoners shall pray Etc

Thomas Jeffreys [...] Blake [...] Cornish
