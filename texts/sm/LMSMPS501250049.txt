To the Right Worshipfull her Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Trinity in the Freedom of London

Sheweth That by Order dated the 20th. day of August last past under the hand & Seales of Roger Smith and John Stuart Esqr two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Sd County of Middx (Quorn unus) one Elizabeth Harris Widdow was lately removed from the Humbles of Spittlefields in the Sd County of Middx to the sd Parish of Trinity as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Harris

That your Peticonrs finds themselves Agreved by the Said Order of the Sd two Justices of the Peace And Appeale against the Same the Sd Elizabeth Harris not haveing any Legall Settlemt in the sd Parish of Trinity

Your Peticonrs pray that this Court will Order a day in this Sessions for all Ptyes to Attend & to heare & [...] abide the Judgmt and determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale
