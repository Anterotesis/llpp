To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Generall Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alton in the County of Hants

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of St. Samuel Gower. Knt & Robert Dennett Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 9th. day of July last Mary Clements Wife of Thomas Beason Clements (who is gone for a Soldier) and their two Children Joseph aged about four Years & Mary then Aged about nine Months were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middx to the Parish of Alton aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived and against the said Order do Appeal

R Philps Agent for the Appellants

Your Petitioners Therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear & determine the Matter touching the sd. Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Middlesex At the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middx at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid by Adjournment on Monday the tenth day of October in the 22d. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain Etc. It is Ordered by this Court that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel this County have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Persons concerned do attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court Waller
