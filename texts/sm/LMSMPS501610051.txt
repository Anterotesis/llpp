To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace (Assembled in Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall) For the County of Middx.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Paul Covent Garden in the said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated ye. 1st: day of April last Under the hands and Seals of Michael Crake and William Conne Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor. Unns) William Whitlow was removed from the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St: Paul Covent Garden as the Place of his last Legall Settlement

That Yor: Petrs: find themselves Agreived by the Sd. Warrant or Order of the said two Justices. And humbly Appeal against the Same to this Honble. Court.

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray Your Worps. will be Pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Martin in the Fields And All other Persons Concern'd to Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determmacon of this Court Upon and touching the said Appeal, And that Yor: Petrs: may have Such relief here in As to Yor: Worps. Shall Seem meet Etc.

And Yor: Petrs: Shall Pray Etc.
