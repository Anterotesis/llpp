Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Race for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Joseph Ridgway of Islington in the County of Middlesex Builder

Sheweth That by a certain Order in Writing under the Hands and Seals of William Bleamire and Aaron Graham Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 19th. day of February now last Past your Petitioner was Ordered and Appointed to Pay unto Charles Beagley of the Parish of Christ Church Surry the Surveyor and Supervisor appointed under and by virtue of a certain Set of Parliament made and Paper in the Fourteenth year of the Reign of his present Majesty Entitled

"An Act for the Further and better Regulation of "Buildings and Party Walls and for the more Effectually preventing "Mischiefs by fire within the Cities of London & Westminster & "the Liberties thereof and other the Parishes Precincts and "Places within the Weekly Bills of Mortality the Parishes of "Saint Mary le Bone Paddington Saint Pancras and "Saint Luke at Chelsea in the County of Middlesex and "for Indemnifying under certain Conditions Builders and "other Persons against the Penalties to which they are or "may be Liable for Erecting Buildings within the Limits "aforesaid Contrary to Law" to the Parish of Saint James and Saint John Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex the Sum of Twenty three Pounds two Shillings Awardes by them the said Justices to him the said Charles Beazley as a Satisfaction for his Trouble and in Viewing and Surveying Eight certain Buildings and Six certain Additions to Buildings att of the Fourth

Fourth Rate or Class of Building specified in the said Act and within the Limits thereof "to wit" Seven Dewelling "Houres which did not each Exceed three Square, and an half of "Building on the Ground Plan thereof One Building not being a "Dwelling & oure which did not Exceed one Clear Story above "Ground and Six Adaitions to Six Several Dwelling House "which said Dwelling Houses did not each Exceed there Square "and an half of Building on the Ground Llyn thereof" of which said servant Buildings and additions your Petitioner stood charged Summonse and Proved before the said Justices as being the Builder thereof and Persons causeing the said Buildings to be Build-

That Your Petitioner Conceiving himself to be Aggrieved by such Order and appointment Entered into a Recognizance with Sureties to appeal against the same at this present Quarter Session pursuant to the Directions of the said Act-

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly begs leave to appeal against the said Order and that the hearing and Determining thereof man be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex-

And your Petitioner shall Every Pray Etc.
