To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in and for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Thomas Kynaston and John Goodchild Esqr. two of this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County one whereof being of the Quorum, bearing date the 19th. day of April 1768. Frances Smith aged Seventy two Years and upwards Widow of William Smith deced, was Removed from the Parish of Kensington in the said County to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westminster in the said County as the place of her last Legal Settlement, whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived, and Appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them, and that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in the next Session for hearing and determining the same, and that all Parties concerned may then attend

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Titus Wilson John Shipman} Churchwardens of St. Margaret Westmr.

Rd. Godfree Maris Marsault} Overseers of the Poor of the Same Parish
