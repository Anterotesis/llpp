To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Petersham in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Han [...] of John Derbyshire Birkheadand John Goodchild Esqr. [...] Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (whereof One is of [...] bearing date the 30th. day of June last past John Read Elizabeth his Wife and his Two Children (to wit) John Aged Three Years and upwards and Sarah Aged One Year and upwards were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Twickenham in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Petersham as the place of their last Legal place of Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices of the peace Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray this Court will Appoint a day at the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be Holden for this County for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Petersham and all other Persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.
