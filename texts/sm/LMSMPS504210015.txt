To the Right Honble. Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London, and to the rest of the Justices of Gaol Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex, in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Jane Flowers.

Sheweth that your Petitioner [...] by Warrant under the Head and Seal of Richard Ricards Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the Sd. County of Middlesex was on the 1st. day of August last Committed to Clerkenwell Brldewell for being Concerned with others in making and Counterfeiting the Current Coin of this Kingdom and has been Since removed to his Majestys Gaol of Newgate in Order to take her Tryal for the said offence.

Yor. petitioner therefore most humbly prays That she may at this present Session be either Tried Bailed or Discharged.

And yor. petr. Shall ever pray

Jane Flowers
