To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warranter Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Ricards an Robert Donnel Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Nineteenth day of December last Barbara the Wife of Miles Rains and her Children (that is to say) William aged about four years and Miles aged about one year were removed from the Precinct of Saint Katherines in the said County to the parish of Saint George Hanover Square as the place of the is last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioner think themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concern'd may Attend at the Same time

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray and so forth

S. Parry Sollicitr. for ye Parish
