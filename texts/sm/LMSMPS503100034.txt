To the Right Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of Charles Marsh Apprentice.

Sheweth That by Indenture bearing date the thirteenth day of October in the year of our Lord 1731 your Petr. was bound Apprentice to Samuel Crowther of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Said County Chair & Cabinet maker for the Term of Seven years to learn his Act in Consideracon whereof the said Sam Crowther reced the Sume of ten pounds

That yor. Petrs. said Master does not, nor has any other person or persons by his Direction Instructed yor. Petr. in the Art or Trade of a Chair & Cabinent maker nor has [---] yr Petr learnt any part of his Trade.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays that yor. Worsps will be pleased to Discharge him from his said Apprenticeshood And direct the said Samuel Crowther to return Such [---] part of money as [---] yor. Worps shall think meet in Order to Enable yor Petr. to get another Master

[---]

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc

Chas. Marsh
