To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of Anne Harding of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the sd County Widow.

Sheweth That your peticoner is this year rated in the poors Rate of the said Parish.

That your Peticoner rents two Roomes in the said Parish at the yearly rent of Fourty Shillings and noe more and for these two yeares past hath by her hard labour kept two Grand children who otherwise must have become chargeable to the said Parish and being very poor Appeald against the same

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Order that She may be wholly Discharged from paying towards the sd Poors rates

And Yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

Anne Harding
