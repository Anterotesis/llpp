To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Honover Square in the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Joseph Moser and H Cosser Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of September last Mary Jennery the wife of James Jennery (gone from here) Was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this court against the said Order And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Suge and Chandless

Sollrs. for the said Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
