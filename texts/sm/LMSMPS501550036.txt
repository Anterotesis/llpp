To the Rt: Worpl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poore of the Hamblet of Spittle Feilds in the sd County

Sheweth

That by Warrt under the hands & Seales of Felix Feast & John Dey - Esqr Two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County Dated the 26th July 1716 Rich Skanfield and Anne his wife were removed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate to the sd Hambt .the said Justices Deeming the Sd Hambt. their last legall Settlemt. But Your. Peticoners hope to prove the Contrary

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray of day may be Assigned that all Ptier concearned may Attend & Abide your Worpps Determinacon touching this Appeal

And Shall pray Etc

Tho: Ward Chur: War:

P Jno: Knowles Cleerk

Danl. Dymock

Benj: Waters
