To Ye. Rigtt: Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middx:

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Parish of St: Leonard Shereditch in the Sd County

Sheweth That by a Warrtt: under ye. hands & Seales of Thomas West & Samll. Jones Esqrs. Two of his Matis. Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd County (Quor unus) beareinge date ye. Ninth day of July 1720. Anne Thompson Widow was removed from ye. Hamlet of Lyme house in ye. Parish of Stepney in ye. Sd. County to ye. Sd Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch; as ye. place of her Last Legall Setlemt.

Your Peticoners findeinge themselves agreived wth. ye. Judgmt: of ye Sd Two Justices humbly Appeales agt. ye. Sd Warrtt: and humbly [...] prayes that ye. benefitt of ye. Sd Appeale was be Saved unto ye. Sd Appell unto untill ye. next Generall Sessions of ye. Peace to be holden for ye. Sd County for that Your Peticoners Cannot be prepaired to Prosecute the Sd Appeale this Prsent Genell: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace by reason ye. Sd Anne Thompson in ye. Sd Warrtt: Menconed was not brought unto Your Peticoners untill ye. Thirteen day of this Just July.

And ye: peticonrs. shall ever pray Etc:

John Cann. Jeremiah Vright} Churchwardens
