To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition And Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph With out Bishopgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrent under the hands and Seals of Samuel Gower Esqr. and Thomas Quarrell Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One Where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date to the Sixth Day of November 1747 Darius Humfrey was Passed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex To the Parish of St. Botolph with on Bishopgate London aforesaid Adjudging the last Legal Place of Setlement of the said Darius Humfrey to be in the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate and Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly Pray Your Worships

Barr Attorney for Bishopgate Parish} Would appoint some Day this Present General Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County to hear this Appeal and that your Worships Would make an Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all Parties Concerned in and about the Premises May then Attend hear and abide the Final Determination of your Worships

And Ye. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
