To the Right Worpll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Qr Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of John Ward of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch Vict

Sheweth That your Peticoner is charged for the last half year of this present Year 1725 She Sume of [...] Nineteen Shilling & Six pence towards the Releife of the Poor of the Sd Parish Your Peticoner finding himself aggreived [...] by the sd Rate Humbly Appeals against the same.

And Prays that this Honble Court Won be pleased to Order him Such Releife the prmses being considered as in your Worps grave Wisdoms shall seem [...] Proper.

And your Peticoner as in Duty bound shall pray Etc

John Ward
