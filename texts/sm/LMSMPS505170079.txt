To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, in their General Session Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw, in ye. City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under ye. Hands & Seals of Benjamin Cox & Thomas Kynaston, Esqrs., Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. City & Liberty of Westminster, in ye. County of Middlesex, (One whereof being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye.

Elizabeth Rose, Singlewoman, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Martin in ye. Fields within ye. Liberty of Westminster, in ye. County of Middlesex, unto ye. said Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw, in ye. City of London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge Atty for Appellts.} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of ye. said two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom; and humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & Order that ye. Churchwardns. & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of St. Martin in ye. Fields, may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petitioners shall pray Etc.
