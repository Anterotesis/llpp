To the right Worshipfull his Mgesties Justices of the Peace in Sessions Assemble at Hick's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Henry Rogers Constable of Ealing in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Yor. Petitioner haveing obtained the Order hereunto annexed did Shew the Same unto John Tadpole therein mentioned and told him the contents thereof but the said John Tadpole refused as he now do [...] to obey the same or any Part thereof And your Petitioner having executed the office of Constable in the said Parish of Ealing as Setforth in his former Petition and the said Order hereunto Annexed

Yor. Petitioner most humbly prays your Worships the Premisses considered to discharge Yor. Petitioner from his said Office or make such further or other order herein as to your worships shall Seem meets.

And Yor. Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc.
