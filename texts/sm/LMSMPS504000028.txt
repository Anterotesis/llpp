To the Rt Worshipful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Chuchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westmr. in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of William Withers and Henry Feilding Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the [---] county of Middlesex bearing Date the 30th: day of November 1749 Mary Nixon Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of St. Margt Westmr: as the place of her last Legal Sittlement whereby Your Petitrs. Conceive themselves aggrieved & Appeal to this Court against the Same.

Yor: Petitrs therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to Appoint a time for hearing and Determining the Said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend

And Yr. Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Heary - Agent for St Margarets

Middx Westmr At a General Quarter Session of the Place of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the County [---] aforesaid on Monday the Fifteenth day of January in the 29d Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Ld: King of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex have Notice of this petition and Appeal & that they and all persons concerned to attend this Court on Thursday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

By the Court.

Waller
