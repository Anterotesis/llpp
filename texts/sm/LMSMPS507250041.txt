To the right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of David Walker and Thomas Brooksbank two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 16th day of February 1780 George Benson was removed and Conveyed from the [---] Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint John Southwark in the County of Surrey as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said George Benson

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of removal of the said two Justices

Do appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray

Tho: Hearne Alexr Robbins} Church Wardens

Thomas Blackstone Richd. Bowzer} Overseers
