To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Williams Esqr. and the Revd. H. Reynett D.D. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 16th. day of May last Mary English Widow of Benjamin English deceased was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County to the said Part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices, appeal to your Worships against the same and request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County and that all parties concerned may be then ordered, to attend to abide the judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc Arthur Griddon Sollr for Apptts
