To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Thomas Chambers

Sheweth That Your Petr. was abt. 4 Years ago bound Apprentice to Thomas Lanes of the Parish of St. George the Martyr to learn with him the Art of a Carpenter

That about 2 Months ago Your Petrs. said Master Abseconded from his Home and has not since been heard of and has Gave Your Petitioners Indentures to a [...] other Person who will not Give up the same unless he Pays the Sum of 5 Guineas

Your Petitioners therefore prays Yours Worships to Grant him such Relief in the Premes as to Your Worships shall seem Meet

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc

Thomas Chambers.
