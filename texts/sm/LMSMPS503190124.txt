To the Rt. Worpll His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals Gideon Harvey & Owen Davies Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second day of September last Mary the Wife of Phillip Wallis was removed from the Parish of St. Margarets in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middx to the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petrs. humbly conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same

And Your Petrs, shall ever Pray Etc.

Jno. Northy Soll for Alhallows on the Wall
