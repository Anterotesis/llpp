To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing Date the Seventh Day of November last John Bomford Alice his Wife and their two Children Vizt. William aged seven Years and Samuel aged about two Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne As the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their [...] Order and pray that Your worships will please to a [...] Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining [...] said [...] to October [...] of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields an [...] all persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitr. shall Pray Etc.

Isaac Baldwin Richd Craser} Church Warden, and

James Fregent George Bayant} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
