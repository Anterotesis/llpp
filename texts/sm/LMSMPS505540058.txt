To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth day of November last Winifred Dennison Wife of Philip Dennison (who is gone away and not to be found) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last Legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

And

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all Persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Samuel Smith John Troller Thos. Thompson Thos. Williamson} Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Anne
