To the Rt: Worppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of Basings to be in the County of Southton

Humbly Sheweth

That the child of one Mary Golfe who dyed lately att Kensington in this County was by Warrand under the hands Seales of Thomas Allton Esqr & Edwd Chamberlain Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the peace of this County passed from the Sd pish of Kensington to the Sd pish of Basing Stoke in the sd County of Southton whereas ye Sd child now the parents of the [...] had any legall Settlemt there wth yor peticonrs can [...] are ready to make appeare to this Honble: Bench.

P CW: John Alen Church Warden of Prose my [...] Yor. peticonrs therefore humbly pray yor. Worp: to be pleased to order that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poore of the Sd pish of Kensington may appe in this honble Cort Some day in this prsent Sessions to Show cause why ye to Child Should not be repased to the Sd pish of Kensington yor. Peticonrs discharged from ye maintenance thereof.

And yor. Peticonr: Shall pray Etc
