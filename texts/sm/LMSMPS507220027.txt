To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the Saint John Street in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of and Order under the hands and Seals of William Blackborow and David Walker Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventh Day of December last Ann Hill Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Ann as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that the benefit of this appeal may be Saved to them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Session of the peace to be holden in and for the County

And Your Petitrs: shall pray Etc

Thos Brooke John Dorman Jas: Fisher} Churchwardens and

Robt. Jefferson} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
