To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poorof this Parish of Christ Church in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtues of a Pass Warrant on Order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrill and John Staples Esquires Two of his - Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of Octomber Instant Jane Hilton Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary White chapel in the said County to the said Parish of Christ Church as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Hilton Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved And

Therefore they - Appeal to This Court against the Adjournment of the said have Justice and the said Order And Pray That the Benefit of their their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned untill the Next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County And That Your Worships will be pleased to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chaples and all Persons Concerned do them a Hend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination thereof touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitions shall ever pray Etc F Parnell} Sollicitor for the said appellants
