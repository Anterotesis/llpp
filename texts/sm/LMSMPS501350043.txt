To the Rt Worspll her Mats Justices of the Peace for the County Middx

The humble Peticon of John Howett Const of Enfeild in this County

Sheweth That there is due to yor Peticoner at Michellams [...] The Sum of Fifteen pounds being one Quarter For Passing of Vagarants That your Peticoner haveing disbursted some much money in Passing the said Vagarants That hees a Fraid of being putt to trouble and Charge

Wherefore yor Peticoner humbly prays your Worspps will bee pleased to Order the paymt of the said [...] out of the money soe Appropriated for that use

And yor Petr as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.

John Howett
