To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Quarrill and Thomas Smith Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of February last Catherine Smith Singlewoman was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnall Green in the said County of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Humphy. Elmes Atty. for the Appellts. Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
