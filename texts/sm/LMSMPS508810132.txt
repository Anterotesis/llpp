To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of A Graham Esquire and William Bleamire Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirtieth day of November one thousand seven hundred and ninety two Elizabeth Burnam aged eight years or there abouts Daughter of John Burnam lately deceased by Elizabeth his late wife was removed from and out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London the said Justices adjudging the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Burnam to be in the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved and Humbly appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant.

Wm Chetham Solr. for the Appellants } Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex.
