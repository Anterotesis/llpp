To the Right Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Giles in ye fields in ye sd County

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant under the handl & Seals Alexander Pitfield & Samuel Benson Esqrs two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the thirtieth day of May last past Thomas a Bastard child aged about a year & ten Months was passed from the Parish of Leonard Shorditch [---] in the said County to the Said parish of St Giles in the Fields

Your Peticonrs: thinke themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices do appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Leonard Shorditch in the said County may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & [...] abide the Judgment & determinate of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Peticonrs: (as in duty) Shall ever pray Etc
