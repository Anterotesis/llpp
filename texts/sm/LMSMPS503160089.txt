To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Paul's in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourteenth day of April last past, Mary Strong the Wife of Thomas Strong and Sarah Strong their Daughter aged about seven years were removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the Said County of Middlesex unto the Said Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul's London Alledging the said Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul's to be the Place of their last Legall Settlement.

Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Doe there fore Appeale to your Worships there from, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, and Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke in the said County may Appear before you at a certain day to be Appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of Your Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.
