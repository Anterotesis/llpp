Febry Session 1755

To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London & others His Majesty's Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Drury Bailey a, Prisoner in his Majesty's Goal New Prison

Sheweth That your said Petitioner Hands Comitted to the said Goal for [...] Felony of which he is intirely Inocent

Your Petitioner most humbly prays this Honble Court that he may have the benefit of the Habeas Corpus Act & that he may be Tried Bailed or discharged this Session According to the Directions of the same Statute

And your said Petitioner shall ever pray

Drury Bailey
