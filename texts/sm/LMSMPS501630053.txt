To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx:

The humble Peticon of William Brittain on the behalfe of William Brittain his Son

Sheweth That your Peticoners said Son hath been Confined a Prisoner in New Prison in this County ever Since the 17th of January last to give Evidence against severall persons Concerned in severall Burglaries and Fellonies

That your Peticoners St Son hath compleated his Evidence and hath Convicted three persons on his Evidence and is now referred to the Comitting Justice to discharge him being Justice Ellis who refused soe to Doe

That Yor Petr hath beene Obliedged to mainteyne his said Son ever since his Imprisonment who otherwise must have Starved and your Peticones having three other Children to Mainteyne cannot any longer provide for his said Son Who is very willing to goe to Sea

Yor Petitr therefore humbly prays his sd Son may be Discharged from his Sd Imprisonmt and Yor Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc

Wm Brittan
