To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices Siting [...] att Hicks- Hall.

The humble Peticon of Jane Palmer

Sheweth. That her Father William Palmer is a very aged & de [...] -crepid man & not able to work, having a legal Settlement in the parish of Paddington, & upon application to the sd parish the Church wardens & Overseers refuse to give any Sustenance to your Petr. Said Father, & have refus'd to ap. -pear to Majr Hanway's Summons to Shew cause why they wou'd not.

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays that She may have an Order of Session to have her Said Father's case consider'd, he being almost Starv'd with Hunger & cold. Etc

the mke [x] of Jane Palmer
