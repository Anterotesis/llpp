To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seals of Stephen Martin Lake & Thos: Exelbee Esqrs. two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the 8th Day of January Instant John Hoit & Mary his wife & his two Children Vizt. John aged about Twenty Years & one quarter & Mary aged about fifteen Years & one quarter were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Ann in the County of Middx to the said parish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London alledging therein that the said Parish of St Andrew Undershaft is the Place of the last Legal Settlemt. of Sd. John Hoit Mary his wife & John Mary his Children

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants Yor Petrs: thinking themselves aggreived by the aforesaid Order of the Sd two Justices humbly Appeal against the same to this Court And forasmuch as yor Petrs. cannot be ready to Psecute their said Appeal this Prsent Sessions humbly pray that the hearing & determining thereof may be adjorn'd untll the next Genll Sessions of the peace. And that your Worps. wou'd be pleased to appoint same Day therein for all parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs. as in duty bound shall pray Etc.
