To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in Generall Sessions at Hicks's Hall in the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Ellis and Miles Harper Esqrs: two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of August Instant Frances Dexter Widow of William Dexter was removed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square As the place of her last legall Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray & so forth

Rich: Whitworth} ChurchWarden
