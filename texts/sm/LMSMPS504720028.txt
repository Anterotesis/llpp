Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Francis Jackman and Henry Trent Esqrs. [...] of [...] Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of June 1758 Jacob an Infant aged about Seven years and David an Infant aged about five Years Children of David and Sarah Nichols both deceased were removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners shall every pray Etc.

Thos. Huddle Wm: Boorcher} Churchwardens

Jno Sheppard Robt Hayes} Overseers of the Poor
