To his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarters Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Castle Hedingham in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and William Hacks Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Jonathan Wood and Elizabeth his Wife and their three Children (to Wit) Ann aged almost Six Years Elizabeth aged almost four Years and Robert aged about Six Weeks were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlsex to the Parish of Castle [...] Hedingham in the County of Essex

That Your Petitionrs. apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Order Humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court

Therefore Your Petitioners Humbly Pray that a Short Day may be fixed this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal And that they way be Relived in the Premisses in such manner as this Court shall think fitt

And Your Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc.

Geo: Unwin Agent for the Inhabits. of Castle Hedingham
