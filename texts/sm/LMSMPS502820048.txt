To the RightWorshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of th Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Richd. Manley and W: Haydon Esqrs two of his majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quar unus) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of February Anno Dom 1731 Sarah Shaw was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London As the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Shaw

That your Petrs Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day of this present Sessions that all Parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court for and touching the said Appeal

And yor Petrs. as in duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.
