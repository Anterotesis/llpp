To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of [...] Assembled

These are to Certify that Bishop Johnson bound by recognizance before D'Oly Michell Esqr. one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County to appear at the next Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the sd. County after Frances Richardson for [...] of the last Child [...] the then count with at Hicks Hall in St. John Street to answer the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Mary White Chapple in the sd County for begetting the sd Frances Richardson with Child of a Male Bastard Child wth which hath since been born in the sd. Parish, hath giving Sufficient Security to our said parish to underpinse it from any charges as shall true thereby And we therefore pray yor. Worships will be pleased to dishcharge him from his recognizance Wittness our hands the 20th day of Mar 1727

Test Sam Sadleirs

Mr Beaumont Jno Wilson

Church wardens
