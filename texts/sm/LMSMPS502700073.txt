To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx in Quarter Sessions Assembled,

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Abram Castle of the Parish of St Mary Whitchappell in the County of Midx.

Sheweth That your Peticoner Rents a House but of Seven pounds ten Shillings per Annum and is rated two Shillgs P Month besides Over Rates towards the releif of ye Poor of ye sd Parish Your Peticoner therefore thinks himself agreived and humble Appeal against the same.

Your Peticoner therefore prays to be releived in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seen meet

And Your Peticoner shall Ever pray Etc

Abraham Castel
