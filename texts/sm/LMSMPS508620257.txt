To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman: and the Rest of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their adjourned Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of William Stokes Surveyor of the Parish of Christ Church Middlesex the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and the Hamlet of Mile End new "Town in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitioners was ordered by John Staples Esqr and the Revd. Herbert Mayo D D two of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex residing in the Parish of Saint George Middlesex to survey the Building in the said Parish since the Death of Mr. Thomas Dade the late Surveyor And on examining the same found a Number not certified and Numbers of others erected contrary to the Statute in that Cases made and provided that you Petitioner hath caused several of them to be alter'd and others to enter into Recognizances to alter the same agreeable to the said Statute within times not as yet expired, and others have begun to be built under the Inspection of your Petitioner

Therefore Humbly Prays your Worships to permit him to Certify those that he hath caused to be alterd and also those began under the Inspection of you Petitioner

And as in Duty, bound will ever pray

William Stokes
