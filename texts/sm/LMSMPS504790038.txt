To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Hale and John Lane Esqrs. Two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh day of this instant January Mary the Widow of Benjamin Park was removed & conveyed by the name of Mary sparks (without any addition) from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement

The Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of removal & therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premisses as shall be thought just & meet

And the Petrs. will ever Pray Etc:

G Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
