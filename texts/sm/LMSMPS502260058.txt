To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Southmims in the County aforesd.

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the Hames [...] John Troughton & Nich Booth Esqrs two of Ma [...] Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the first Day of March last past Elizabeth Crathern wife of Samuel Crathern & David her Son of the Age of one Year & upwards peace removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Pancrass in the County of Middx to She [---] Parish of Southmims in the County [...] the place of their last Legall Settlemt.

[...] finding themselves aggreived by the Order of the Sd. two Justices humbly Appeal against the same [...] pray that this Court would appoint some Day this Present Sessions for all Parties concerned to attend to heard & abide the Judgmt & Determi- nacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc.
