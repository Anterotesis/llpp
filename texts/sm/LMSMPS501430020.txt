To the Worshipfull. His Maties Justices of the Peace for tho. [...] County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Whitton

Sheweth

That Your Peticonr: the last Sessions of the Peace held for the County of Middx was fine [...] [...] five Pounds

That she is very poor and altogether incapable of Payeing the Said sumr and must if not releived by your worships great Goodness inevitably Perish.

Your Peticonr. therefore prayes this honble. Court will take her case into Considration and mitegate her said fine as to Your Worshipps in your great Goodness and Charity shall seem meet

And your Peticonr. shall Everpray

Your Peticoner has five small Children to provide for.
