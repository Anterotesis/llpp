To the Worshipful Thomas Lane Esquire Chairman, and the other Worshipful Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The Petition of John Turner Keeper of Clerkenwell Bridewell,

Humbly Sheweth. That your Petitioner having four Small Children, and being greatly in debt by misfortune of marrying a Woman abandon'd to extravagance & vice, has no way of providing for his children, nor of keeping himself from a prison, if he be removed from his place; But if Suffered to remain in it is now in hopes of paying his debts and bringing up his children, She being gone from him.

Therefore your Petitioner implores your Worships patience & Compassion that he may be continued in his place, And he promises to exert his utmost endeavours in the discharge of his duty.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.
