To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in this General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bromley in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Timbrell and William Mantle Two of his Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster dated the 9th. day of September now last past Mary Hornsby Singlewoman were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. George, Hanover Square, within the Liberty of Westminster, in the County of Middlesex to the Said Parish of Bromley as the Place of her last legal Settlement

your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Order or Warrant Do humbly Appeal against the Same And Pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be an Order of him Court be on Such day at the on next general Sessions as Shall be deemed Proper for that Persons And that the Said Appellants may be deliver in the Premisses as the Court Shall them Seem Meet

And your Petitioners will Pray Et

R: Kent Selr. for the Appellants.
