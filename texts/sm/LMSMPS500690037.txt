To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace held for the said County

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary Redclife als Ratcliffe in the City of Bristoll

Sheweth That by order dated the 21th day of July last past under the hands & seals of Samll Buck Esqr and German Ireton Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quoy Unns) one Eliza: the Relict of Peter perry and their Children; Mary aged 6 years, Henry aged 3 years & Francis aged 12 weeks or there abouts were lately removed from the parish of St Andrew Holborn in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St Mary Redciffe als Ratcliffe in the said City of Bristoll

That your Peticonr have made strict inquiry & find that the said Peter Perry had noe legall Settlement in the sd pish of St Mary Redcliffe als Ratcliffe as is Suggested by the said warrt of removall and are ready to make the same Appear to the Ho [...] County

Your peticonr therefore finding themselves aggrieved by the removall of the said Elizabeth perry & his said three Children from the sd pish of St. Andrew Holborn to the sd pish of st Mary Redcliffe als Ratcliffe doe appeal agt the said order of the said two Justices and pray that a day may be appointed for all psons concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the judgmt and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal And that your peticonr may be relieved as to your Worppl shall seem meet

And yor petr shall pray Etc.
