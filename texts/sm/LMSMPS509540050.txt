To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the peace in and for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies Esquire and the Revd. Henry Reynett two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty seventh day of October last Six Persons therein described as follows vizt. "James Minihan Margaret his "Wife and four Children namely Elizabeth aged about Ten "Years Catherine aged about Eight Years Thomas aged about "Six Years and Ann aged about four Years Lawful Issue" were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order, and do appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a day may be appointed in this present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Saml. Wegener. Sollr. for the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping
