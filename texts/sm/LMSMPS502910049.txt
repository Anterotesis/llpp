To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bow in the City of London

Shew That by Order Dated the Seventeenth day of March last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Dummer and John Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex

William Binfield was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles's in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone as the place of his last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agreived thereby Do Appeal from the said Order of the said Two Justices to the Judgment and determination of this Honourable Court and Crave a day of Hearing

And your Petr shall ever pray

Moses Sievra Agent for Said Mary Le Bow parish
