To the Right worshipfull his majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Generall Sessions assembled

The humr petition of Edward Crane late Constable of the parish of St. Mary Stratford Bow in the sd. County

Sheweth that yor sd Petition has at severall times laid out disburst the sum of three pounds seventeen shillings and seven pence in passing severall Vagrants Pursuant and according to the Direcion of several Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Charles Peers Knt. and Elias Russell Esg two of his majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County yor petiti [...] therefore prays this honourble Court will make such Ordr to the reimbursing him the sd sum of to them shall seem meet

NB an Ordr for twelve shills signed by Elias Russell Esqr bearing date the 31st of of augst last not menconed in the petition was produced

And yor Petitionr as in Duty bound shall ever pray
