To the Justices of the Goal Delivery for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Thomas Cunningham a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That Your Petitr. Stands Committed to Newgate for Felony Expressed in the Warrant of Commitmt.

Your Petitr. therefore Most hbly prays to be Tried Bailed or discharged this Sessions pursuant to the Act of Parliamt. in that Case made & provided

Thomas Cunningham
