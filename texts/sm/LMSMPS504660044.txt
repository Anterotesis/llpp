To The Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of November last past under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Thomas Hutchinson and his two Children namely Jane Aged about Seven Years and upwards and John Aged about five Years and upwards Were Removed from the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rent in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbourn in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of their last legal Settlement

J: Witton Attorney for St. Lukes} Your Petitionrs therefore Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Judgement of the said two Justices do humby appeal to Your Worships therefrom And pray that the church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents may be Ordered to Attend this Court this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Determination of Your Worships touching this Appeal And your Petitioners shall were pray Etc.
