To the Right worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of the Church wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the Sd. County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. undl. this hands & feales of Wm. Gore & Danll Combe Esqrs two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Sd. Com of Middx (Quor unus) dated the 23d day of Augt. last Anne Warwick wife of John Warwick was removed from the pish of St. Martyn in the Feilds in the Sd. County wilst pish of St. Clemt. Danes of the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the Sd. Anne Warwick

That yor. Petrs. think themselves aggrieve by the Ordr.of the Sd. two [...] pastires & doe appeale there from to the Judgemt. of this honble Cort

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray Yor. worspps to appoint some day this present Sessions to leave & determine the matter touching this Appeale

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
