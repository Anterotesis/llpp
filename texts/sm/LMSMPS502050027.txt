To the Worshipfull the Charman and the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Charnock

Sheweth That yor. Peticor Stands [---] comitted in Custody of the keeper of New Prison Upon a Fine for 20lb for an Assault and Batery that yor Petio. hath New Satisfyed and Agreed with the Prosecutor and that Releases are Executed between them Ready to be produced to the Court

Therefore yor. Pertior. most Humbly praoys yor Worships That he may be discharged and Sett at liberty [---] by a millgacon of the Sd Fine

Yor. Petior as in Duty Bound will ever pray Etc
