To the Rt: Worsppll: his Majtyes: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of East Greenwith in County of Kent

Sheweth That by Warrant of Order dated the fifth day of this instand July under the hands and Seals of Bast Johnson and Robert Kyrby Esqrs. two of his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) Elizabeth Hayes Widow was Removed from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the Parish of St. Dunstans Stepney in the County of Middx to the said Parish of East Greenwich, As the place of the last legall Settement of his said Elizabeth Hayes

That your Peticonrs Conceived themselves agreived by the said order of the said two Justices and Appeal against the same

Your Peticonrs. therefore Humbly pray a day may be appointed this present sessions for all parties to attend, to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Peticonrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Gabriell Garrett William Ransom} Churchwardens
