To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Anne Graves

Sheweth That Yesterday yor. Worpps were pleased to order the Churchwardens of the Parish of St Martins in the Feilds to pay yor. Petr. all arreares, due to her at 2s p weeke for the nursing of Mary Ayres she being borne in the sd. Parish and also from henceforth to provide for the sd. Mary Ayres at the charge of the sd Parish Yet nevertheles the Churchwardens doe refuse to pay to yor Petr the sd Arrears or to provide for the sd. Child Alledging that they are noe waies obleidged Soe to doe unless the sd Child was passed to the sd. Parish

Yor Petr therefore most humbly prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to order that the sd Mary Ayres may be forthwith passed to the sd Parish & that yor Petr may be satisfyed he sd arreares according to the order of this Honble Cort

And Yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
