To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esquires two of his Majesties Justice of the Peace for the Said County Dated the Nineteenth day of October 1737 Honour Reddall Widdow of Thomas Reddall was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Olave Southwark as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Honour Reddall

Your Petitioners finding themselves agreived by the said Order or Warrant of the Said Two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same [...] Pray this Court would appoint a day at this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all Parties Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petitioners as in Duty Bound Shall ever Pray Etc

Ralph Hilditch Church Warden Christ: Smith overseer Samll. Gregory Overseer
