This to the worship full bench of Justices at Hiksess Hall the humble petistion of Elizabeth Tendor

Your humble petistioner having lane mon [...] in prison thes foreteen weekes and being all perishin beges that the hounerable bench will senden order of Court for me for I went doun last Sessions and what referd till this Sessions and your humble petistioner [...] lieng in prison and all most Stareed to deation begs that your worships will take it to Consideration and your petistioner Shall ever pray
