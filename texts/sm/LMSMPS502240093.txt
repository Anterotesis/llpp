To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genal Quarter Session of the peace now holden for the Said County

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church- -warden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamble of Spittle Fields in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney ats Stebun heath in the Sd: County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands & Seals of C: Bourne and H: Gough Esqrs: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the 30th. day of November last Susannah the Wife of Benjamin Walle and Elizabeth Walle and John Walle their Children (as in the Said Order named) were passed and conveyed from the Parish of Endfield in the Sd: County, unto the said Parish of St: Dunstan Stepney as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Sd: Peticonrs: conceives themselves aggrieved by the Order of the sd: two Justices, and do therefore humbly appeal against the Same praying Such relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgment Shall Seem just.
