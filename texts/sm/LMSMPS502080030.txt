To ye: Rt: Worshipll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd assembled in their Generall Quartes Sessions of ye Peace

The Humble Peticonr & Appeale of ye Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Hillingdon in the sd: County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Samuel Perry and D'oyly Michel Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) beating Date the 18th. Day of December last past Martha the wife of Adam Hannah & her three Children vizt: Mary aged about nine years William aged about two years & Hester aged about two months were remov'd from the parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the Said County to the Village of Cowley in the said parish of Hillingdon as the place of their last legall Settlemt:

Yor Petr. think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices of the Peace appeale to this Court & humbly pray that Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd: parish of St. Mary White Chapell & all other parties conersn'd may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
