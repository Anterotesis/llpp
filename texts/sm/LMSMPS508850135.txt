To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of M Conant and John Scott two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the fifth day of March One thousand seven hundred and ninety three Elizabeth Hudson wife of Christopher Hudson (then and now absent from her) and her two Children namely Elizabeth Aged ten Years and upwards and Hugh Aged Eight Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of MSaint George Hanover Square as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in the next Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Inse & Chandless sollr. for the Appellants
