To the Worshipful Justices now Assembled in their General Sessions of the Peace at Hick's Hall

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and seals of Paul Vaillant And Thomas Kynaston Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of November last Ann Carey Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said Liberty of Westminster to the parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order humbly Appeal against the same And pray such releif as to your Worships shall Seem Meet

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

H Drew Sollr. for sd. Petitrs.
