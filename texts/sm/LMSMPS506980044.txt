To the Worshipll: her Maties: Justices of ye peace of the County of Middlesex

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of ye parish of St Saviour southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands & seales of two of her Majesties Justices of the peace of this County One Isabella Hutchins was passed from the parish of st Mary White Chappell in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St Saviour Southwark us the place of her last Legall Settlement

That your peticonr: can make it appear to this honble: Court that the sd Isabel Hutchins was never legally settled in the sd parish of St.Saviour Southwark And finding themselves agrieved by ye sd order of removall

yor Peticonr therefore most humbly [...] appeal against the sd order and pray your worships to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of st Mary White Chappell may appear in this honble Court this present sessions to shew Cause why the sd Isabella Hutchins should not be repassed to the sd parish of st Mary White Chappell

And yor Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
