To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Richmond in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of John Staples Esqr and Herbert Mayo doctor in Divinity [---] two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearmis date the 5th. Day of December 1794 Ann Watts as Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Richmond as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication or the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said appeal

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

Rd. Tallemach Solicitor for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Richmond
