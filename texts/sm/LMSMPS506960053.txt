To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of John Williams

Sheweth That by Order Dated the 11th. day of June last Under the hands & Seales of John Plumer & Germain Ireton Esqrs. two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace for this County of Middx (Quor unus) Your Petionr. as adjudged to be the [...] reputed Father of a Female Bastard child born on the body of Elizabeth [...] Washborn prtended Widow in the Liberty above barrs in the pish of St. Andrews Holborne in this County which is (as is alleadged) become chargeable to the said Liberty

And thereby Yor. Peticonr is Ordered to pay or cause to be paid to the Overseers of the poor of the said pish & Liberty the Sume of Â£02:14s:6d Already Disbursed for Maintainance of the said Bastard child from its Birth And other Necessary Expences for the Lying In of the said Elizabeth Washborn and alsoe Weekly & Endy Week from the time of his receiveing Notice of the said Order Soe long as the said child Shall be chargeable to the said Liberty, to pay or cause to be paid to the Overseers of the poor of the said pish & Liberty for the time being [...] Some or one of them the Sume of two Shillings & Six Pence for & towards the Keeping & Educacon of the said Bastard child And Lastly that yor. Petr. Should become bound wth Sufficient Security in forty pounds to the prsent Overseers of the poor of the said Liberty to pforme the said order

That your Petr finds himselfe agrreived by the sd ordr of the said two Justices and humbly appeals agt the same

Jo. Rolfe Your Petr therefore humbly prays yor Worpps would be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd pish & Liberty [...] & all other parties concerned to attend this Court [...] some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court upon & touching the said appeal & that your petr may be releived herein Etc

And yor Petr shall pray Etc
