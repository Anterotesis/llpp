To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth That on the twenty third day of October last Elizabeth Orrill a child aged about five Months was passed from the parish of St. Giles wthout Cripplegate in the said County to the Said parish of St Giles in the Fields by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Nathaniel Manlove & Joseph Short Esqrs. two of Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) as the place of the legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Orrill

p J Tysons Your Peticonrs: thinke themselves aggrieved wth the Judgement of the said two Justices do appeal to this Worpll: Bench & humbly pray the prmises considered that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said parish of St. Giles wthout Cripplegate & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And your Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc.
