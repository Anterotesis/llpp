To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace assembled in their Quarters Sessions in & for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of Kingsbury in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrt. or Order of Removal bearing date the Twenty third day of June 1762 under the hands & Seals of William Baker & Sam: Tufnell Esrqs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Sd. County of Middx (One whereof being of the Quorum) Hannah Francis was Adjug'd to be last legally settled in the Parish of Kingsbury aforesd. and was Ordered to be removed & convey'd from the Parish of Isleworth in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. Parish of Kingsbury.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the

said two Justices of the Peace humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray that this Court will Appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Isleworth afd. and all other Persons concerned to Attend the Court to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal
