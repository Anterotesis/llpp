To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrill and John Staples two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the ninth day of June last past Grace Conway Wife of Philip Jacob Conway who is now in Gaol) and her [...] Male Bastard Children (Vizt) John aged about Six Years Edward aged about three Years and Philip Jacob aged about fifteen Months were removed and conveyed out of the parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Grace Conway.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your worships will appoint a Day in the present Session for the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc

Saml. Malbon Sollr. for St. Paul Shadwell
