To Right Worpll her Maties Justices of the County of Middx

Sheweth The Humble Peticon of William Hopkins of the parcis of St. Bottolphs without Aldgate in the sd. County

That yor. Petr. is an Inhabitant of the aforesd. parish of St. Bottolphs without Aldgate & rents a house of Â£18 P And for which he is charged to the poors rate 3d P Month yor. Petr. thinking himselfe agreived humble appeales to this Honoble Court against the sd: rate and prayes that yor. Worshepps would be pleased to Ordr. the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the aforesd. Parish to attend this Court some day this present sessions to shew Cause why yor. Petr. should not be releived in the Premisser

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc. Wm: Hopkins [...]
