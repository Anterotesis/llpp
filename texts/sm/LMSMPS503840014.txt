To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx: in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Rance one of the High Constables of Stanes Division in the Hundred of Spelthorne in this County

Sheweth That Your Petitioner hath Served the Office of High Constable for the Space of two Years and that he is very Infirm and greatly Afflicted with the Gent and is thereby render'd incapable of doing his Duty therefore desires to be Discharged from the same And he returns Edward Hastler of the Parish of Stanes Grocer and William Lewis of the same place Inn: holder to serve the sd. Office in his stead.

Your Petitioner therefore begs that one of the above persons may be appointed in this stead and that he may be Discharged and as in Duty bound he shall ever pray Etc

Midlx: to Wit We His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County Acting for the sd. Division Do approve of the above Petition Septr. 11th. 1747

Jno. Rance

Kinnoull Geo: Tash
