To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Robert Archer Constable of Endfield in the said County

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your peticoner at Midsumer last for passing & relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds.

That your peticoner has been at great charge in Conveying vagrants this last Quarter & Stands in want of his said Moneys [...]

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays he may be paid his said Quarter Sallary

And Yor: petr Shall ever prayer Robert Archer
