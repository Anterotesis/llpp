To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General [---] Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint. Anne within the LIberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of George Wright and Thomas Edwards Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty third day of May last Mary Jagger Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved. And

Therefore they humbly Appeal to the Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Pass Warrant or Order And Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal May be saved unto Your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this County.

And your Petitioner shall Pray Etc

I Ellis Sollr. for the Appellts.
