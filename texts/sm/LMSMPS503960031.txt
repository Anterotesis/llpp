To the Worshipfull the Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Bayley of Isleworth Gardener.

Sheweth

That by an Order bearing date the Eleventh day of April last under the Hands and Seals of Robert Tothill and Francis Hole Esqrs. two of Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County Is was Adjudged and Declared That your Petitioner was the Reputed Father of a Female Bastard Child Born of the Body of Ann Evans Singlewoman-And that your Petitioner should forthwith Pay or Cause to be Paid unto the Church Wardens or Overseers of the Poor of the time being of the Parish of Heston in the said County or to some or one of them the Sum of three Pounds three Shillings- and Seven Pence for the Maintenance of the said Child for the Space of Four Weeks and the other Legal and Necessary Expences of the Officers of the said Parish And also that your Petitioner should likewise Pay or Cause to be paid to the said Church Wardens or Overseers Weekly & every Week the Sum of Two Shillings and Six Pence for and towards the Releif and Maintenance of the said Child for so long time as the said Child should become Chargeable to the sd. Parish of Heston

That your Petitioner Conceives himself to be Aggreived by the said Order and humbly Appeals therefrom to your Worships Judgment and Prays such Releif in the Premisses as to your Worships shall Seem Meet
