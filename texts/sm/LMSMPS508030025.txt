To The Worshipul His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

Sheweth The humble Petition and appeal of The churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Shalburn in the County of Berks.

That by virtue of a certain Order or Pass warrant bearing date the Thirteenth day of September in the year of our Lord One thousand seven hundred and eighty five under the Hands and Seals of Henry Lambe Esquire and Thomas Fellows Esquire two of His Mjesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex and one of them of the Quorum John Blundy and Sarah his wife and their four children namely Ann aged Eighteen Years John about Twelve years of Age, Major Seven years of Age and Jane about Eighteen Months old were removed from and out of the Toun of Uxbridge in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Shalburn in the County of Berks as the place of the lawful Settlement of them the said John Blundy and Sarah his Wife and their said four Children Ann, John, Major and Jane whereby Your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved.

Chetham Solicitor for the Appellants } Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex
