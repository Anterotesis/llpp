To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Clarke and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty eighth Day of July last Henrietta Deletang (in the said Order mentioned and described to be the) Widow of William Deletang deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County as the place of the last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore the humble Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order of the Church= Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And your Petitioners Shall Pray &c

Grantham B Dorsett Richd Hereford John Knight Peter Mounior} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
