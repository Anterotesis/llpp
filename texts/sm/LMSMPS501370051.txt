[...] [...] That yor [...] towards the releife of the Poor of the Hamblets [...] That Yor Petr is extreme poor, and being aged 80 yrs. and upwards So that he is altogether past his Labour.

That yor Petr. was taken of paying anything to the poor nor any other taxes in ye year 1695 As will appear by the Certificate hereunto annexed Therefore Your Petr. think to himselfe much aggreiv'd thereby that he Should be now assessed.

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that he may be releifed in the sd pl [...] And Yor P shall ever pray Etc.
