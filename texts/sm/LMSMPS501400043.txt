To the Rt Worsppll her Maties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The humble Peticon. of Robert May Constable of End field in the Said County

Sheweth

That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr: at Midsumer Last for passing and Relieving of Vagrants Amounting to the Sum of Fifteen Pounds

That your Petitr: hath been at great Charge and expence in Conveying the said Vagrants

Therefore humbly Prays yor Worspps will be pleased to order that he may be paid the said Mony As unto your Worspps shall seeme Meete

And yor Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Robt: May:
