Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor Parish of Staines in the the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or Pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of Stephen Cole and William Heckford Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of January now last post Robert West was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Isleworth in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Staines as the place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners concieving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore appeal to your Worships there from and humbly Pray that you will Please to hear them in the Premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Isleworth may appear before Your at a Certain Day to be appointed by your Worships to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of Your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall pray Rd. Tallemach Sollpr for said Petitioners
