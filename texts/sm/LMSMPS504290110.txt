To his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Inhabitants of the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex whose names are hereunto Subscribed on behalf of themselves and diverse other Inhabitants of the said parish.

Sheweth That a Rate hath been lately made by the Overseers of the poor the said parish of Saint George upon the Inhabitants and Occupiers of Lands Houses and Gardens within the Lower Division in the same parish for the Relief of the poor thereof in this present Year One thousand seven hundred and fifty four which Rate your Petitioners are Advised his unequal Oppressive and Unjust for that several of the Inhabitants of the said parish who are persons of great Substance and Ability are undercharged in the said Rate and that several other Inhabitants of the same parish of less Substance are over Assessed in the aforesaid Rate

That your Petitioners are very much Aggrieved by the aforesaid Rate and humbly Appeal to his Court against the same

And pray that the said Rate may be quashed and Vacated and that the said poors rate may be produced before this Court at such time as the Court shall Appoint for the hearing and determining the Matter of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Willm. Smith Philip Robert
