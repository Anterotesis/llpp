To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Richmond in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Scott & Philip Neve requires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 20 day of June 1793 Jane Cavendish the wife William Cavendish was removed & conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middle to the said Parish of Richmond as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved

Your petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjuderation of the said two Justices & their said Order & pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear & determine the said appeal

And your Petitioners will [---] ever pray Etc

Rd. Tallemack Solicitor for the Church wardens & overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Richmond
