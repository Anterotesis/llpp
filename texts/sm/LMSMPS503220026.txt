To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the Pish of Saint Clements Eastcheap London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or pass Warrant Under th Hands & Seals of John Mercer Esqr & Thos: Dummer Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx One Whereof is of the Quorum bearing Date the 15th. Day of April 1737 In the Tenth Year of his present Majesty or One Mary Glyn Widw. of Jno Glyn Decd was removed from & of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the sd. County of Middx into the sd parish of Saint Clements Eastcheap London As the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggreivd by ye sd. Order or Pass Warrt. Do Appeale Agt. the Same & pray that this Honrble Court will please to order to Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of ye sd. parish of St. Clement Eastcheap and all parties concerned to appear Some day at this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

Will Salt Agent for ye Appellants

And your Petrs: Shall ever Pray Etc
