To the Right Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Parishoners of the Parish of Whitchurch in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Jeffery Saunders and John Worley Esqrs two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor Unus) Elizabeth Taverner Singlewoman in removed from the Parish of St. Clements Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Whitchurch as the Place of her last legall Settlement

That the said Parishoners of Whitchurch Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and doe humbly appeal therefrom and pray a day may be appointed at this prsent Sessions for hearing the said Appeale

And yor Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
