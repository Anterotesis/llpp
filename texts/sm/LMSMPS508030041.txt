To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of I. Beete and Jno. Griffiths Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of December last Past James Hill and Jane his wife were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said James Hill and Jane his Wife.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrivered by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a day in the Present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners Etc

Saml: Malbon Solr. for St. Paul Shadwell
