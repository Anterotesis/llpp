To the Right Worspl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon and Appeale of the- Churchwardens of the parish of St. Katherine Cree Church London

Sheweth

That Anne Lloyd was by Warrant under the hands and seales of two of their Maties Justices of the peace for the said County lately passed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County to the sd. parish of St. Katherine Cree Church

That the sd. Anne Lloyd never had any Legall Settemt. in the sd. Parish of St. Katherine Cree Church which yor. petrs. are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court

Yor. petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. worspps to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd. Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds may appeare in the Honble Court the put Quarter Sessions be shew cause why the sd. Anne Lloyd should not be repassed to the sd. pish of St. Giles in the Feilds.

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever Pray etc.
