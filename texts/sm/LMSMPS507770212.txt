To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of John Woodham Esqr. and Peter Greene Esqr. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the this County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourteenth day of January Instant Joseph Studd and Rebecca his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of there last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a day may be appointed the hear and determine the matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then Attend

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Tudman & Wegener Sollrs. for Appellants
