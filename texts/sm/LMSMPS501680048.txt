To The Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon. & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Harrow on the Hill In the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order dated ye. 21st. day of January last under the hands & Seals of Rupert Browne Whitlock Bulstrode John Venner Esqrs: Three of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx One John Richards with his wife & four Children were removed from the Parish of Hanwell in the said County to the said Parish of Harrow as the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That Your Peticoners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Judgmt. of the said Three Justices And Doe Appeal there from

Your Peticonr. therefore pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Short day for the hearing of the said Appeal

And Your Peticonrs. Shall ever pray Etc
