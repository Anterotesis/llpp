To the Rt: Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeal of Mary Wilkinson of the parish of St Mary Islington in the said County Widdow.

Sheweth

That your petitr is rated to the poors Rate of the said parish for the house & Lands she Rents after the Rate of Four and Fourty Pounds p Ann.

That your petitr. pays noe more rent then Seventeen pounds p Ann Wherefore she Conceived herselfe and Appeals against the same.

your petitr. therefore humbly prays such relief in the promises As to this Court shall be. thought meete.

And yor petitr shall Ever pray Etc.

Mary Wilkinson
