The Humble Petition of Nichls: Stoker

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner having a wife and family wch: will be Starved It you Send him to Bridewell, Therefore would beg of the Honourable Bench to take it into Consideration and to respitt his punishment for this time and your Petitioner

as in duty bound Shall Ever pray

from Your most Humble: & Obedient Servt: to Command Nicholas Stoker

aprill ye 14 1716
