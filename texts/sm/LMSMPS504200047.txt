To the Right honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Comissioner of Goal Delivery of Newgate at Justice Hall in the Old Baily in the Suburbs of London Assembled

The humble Petition of George Ebenezer Pentherer

Sheweth

That your Petitioner was at the Last Session of Goal Delivered for the County of Middlesex Indicted as an Accessory to a Fellony-

That your Petitioner was thereupon Removed from the Fleet Prison and Committed to Newgate as by the Copy of the Order of Sessions hereunto annexed Appears-

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays- that he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged out of Custody at this Session Pursuant to the Statute in Such Case made and Provided

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray Etc.

Geo: Ebenezer Pewterer
