The Humble Peticon of William Day a [...] Newgate

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: it [...] for a fellony & Burglawry When in truth so it nod wayes guilty of the Same And yor. peticonr. having witness here in Court hopes to prove his Innocency

Therefore yor.peticonr: humbly prayes he may be Viged at this prsent Sessions and that his prayer may be Entered accordingly as to Sd. Lordpps Shall seem meet Etc

And yor.Peticonr: Shall Ever Pray Etc
