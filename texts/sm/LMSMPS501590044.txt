To the. Rt : Worpl his Maties Justices of the peace for the. County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Representacon of Abrah: Debren of this Hamblett of Spittle Feilds Baker

Sheweth

That Joseph Brackhus was Lately put to Apprentice to your Peticoner by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of Westham in Essex That he is & A pilfering Boy and was ordered to Bridewell by 3 of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County but by Application the sd Justices were pleased to Send him to his mother att Stratford

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays that the sd Appentice may be rele [...] from his sd Apprenticeship

And he shall en Pray Etc

P Jno: Knowles
