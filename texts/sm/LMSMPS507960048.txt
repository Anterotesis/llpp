To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Tho: Collins and Jno: Barnfather Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of February 1785 Hannah Wesley aged fourteen years or thereabouts are illigitimate Child was removed from the Parish of St Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex unto the Parish of Saint George Southwark in the said County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of St George Southwark to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefroms and humbly Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Vincent Solr: for the Appellants
