To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Jane Ralphs.

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner was comitted about ten daies ago to the house of Correccon in Clerkenwell by the Worspll. Justice Fuller Esqr. upon Oath made by one Cole her Land lady for taking out of her perfett the sum of Seven pence.

That yor. Worspps poor peticoner hath ever since the said Comittment been in the said House of Correccon and this morning she was brought before this Honble. Bench but the said Cole never appear'd against yor. Petr. however the Worsppll. Justice Fuller with other his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said County were pleased to order that yor. petr. should return again to Bridewell and there receive the Correccon of the Said Gaol and to remain there a Fortnight to hard Labour.

That severall [---] Neighbours which live near [---] this Honble Court did go this afternoon to the said Justice Fuller and did speak in yor. petrs. behalfe. Upon which his Worspp was pleased to order them to make this peticon to your Worspps in your petrs. behalfe. Wch. Neighbours are now in Court.

That yor. petr. is actually Lunatich and a great object of yor. Worspps Charity.

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to consider her condicon and to order that he may be discharged from her said Imprisonmt. without Correccon

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.
