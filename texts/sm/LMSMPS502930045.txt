To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Ann Westmr in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of John Ellis and Miles Harper Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (Quorum unus) bearing date the Sixth day of June Instant Elizabeth Watts Widow and her four Children Vizt. George upwards of Seven years Francis aged about Six Years Elizabeth about four years & Samuel about Six Months were removed from the parish of St. James Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Ann whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived

Therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudgment of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchward and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James Westmr & all persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And shall ever pray Etc

Jo Kendall Al & Appellts:
