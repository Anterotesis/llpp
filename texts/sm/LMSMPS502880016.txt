To the Worshipful the Justices of ye Peace at the Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of [...] Trotter of the Parish of Lelam in the sd County Victualer

Humbly Shewth. That yr Petr. hath kept a Publick House in an orderly Manner in the sd. Parish for about Eight years last past & Sr. Thos. Reynolds for Reasons unknown to ye. Petr. hath lately refused granting a Licence to ye Petr.

Yr. Petr. therefore humbly prays yr. Worps to take his Case into ye. prudent Consideracon yt. he may be relieved in the Prmisses.

And ye Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

The Mark of Cha: Trotter
