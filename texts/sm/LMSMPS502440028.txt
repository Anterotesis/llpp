To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Henry Mackris one of the High Constables of the Hundred of Edmonton in the sd County

Sheweth That at Lady Day last Past there was due and owing to yor. Petr. the Sume Sixty Pounds for one years Salary for his loss of time and Expences in Releiving Passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Prays this Honble. Court would be Pleased to Order the Payment of the said sixty Pounds due to yor. said Petr. for the said Service

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

Henry Mackris
