To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of D'oyley Michel and Robert Dennett Esqrs two his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quoru unus) bearing date the twelfth day of December last past Thomas Chickwood and Sarah his wife were removed from the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds in this County unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs: conceving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom And Pray that your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamblett of Spittlefields may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc.
