To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of New Windsor in the County of Berks.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Stand and Seal of Thomas Balack Esquire One of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty Sixth Day of April 1762. Ann Lee Widow of Robert Lee deceased Apprehended in the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex as a Rogue & Vagabond (Videlicet) wandring and Lodging in the Open Air Contrary to the Statute in that Case made and Provided was removed from the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of New Windsor in the said County of Berks as the last Legal Place of Settlement of the said Ann Lee the Vagrant above named Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a day in this Present Session for the Hearing & Determining
