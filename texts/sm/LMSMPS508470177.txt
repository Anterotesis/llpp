To the Worshipfull William Mainwaring Esqr and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Brasted in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by a certain Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of G: S: Bradshaw & J: Barnfather Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex and the City and Liberty of Westminster, bearing date the 22 Day of April last, whereby One Ann Smith & her Female Child Mary Ann aged nine Weeks were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Brasted in the County of Kent aforesaid, by which your Petitioners conceived themselves agrieved & humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Day may be appointed for the hearing & determining the said Appeal & that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Clerkenwell may be Ordered to Attend the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that such Order may be made in the Premises as to your Worship may seem Meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc, Etc.

7th July It is ordered that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex do attend this Court on Thursday the 9th day of July Instant to hear and abide the Judgment of the Court upon the above Appeal
