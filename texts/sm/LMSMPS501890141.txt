To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor [...] of Rumford in the County of Essex

Humbly Sheweth That By an Order under the hands & Seales of Laurence Saints & Gwynn Vaughan Esqrs two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx. Grace Wingfield widow & William Wingfield her child aged about five years were removed from the parish of Staines in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to Rumford aforesaid as the Place of their last legall Settlement wth. said Order beares date the tenth day of September last

Yor Peticors thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale therefrom And Pray that this Court will Please to Order a day for the Sd. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Staines to Attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon thereof touching this Appeale

And Yor Petr as in Duty Found Shall ever Pray Etc
