To the Worsippfull the Bench of Justices Assembled at Hicks's Hall

The Humble Petition of Andrew Bath

Sheweth

That Your Worships petr. was Cound by his Worship Mr Justice Lonth To whome this honble. Bench did referr Yr Petr. ye last Sessions

That Yr. Petr. hath ever Since been Confind to the great hardship & utter rain of You Petr. he having No friends to Com Near him in So Much that Yr Petr. Will inevitably perish Unless Dischargd by this Honble. Bench

Wherefore Yr Poor Petr most humbly beggs that he May be Discharged by this Worship full Bench And Yor Petr will ever pray &Ca.
