To the Rt. Worpll: his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeall of the Church: wardens and Overseer of the poor of the parish of St. Ann Westmr: in the sd. County.

Sheweth

That Thomas Hall aged about Seven Yeare, John Hall aged about Four years, and William Hall aged about Twelve Months Children of Thomas Hall and Anno his wife. were by Order Dated the 21st: day of Decembr: 1716, under the Hands and Seals of John Milner & and John Richardson Esqrs. Two of his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor Unus) removed from the parish of St. Gyless in the Fields in the sd County, to the sd parish of St. Ann. And Since their Removall into the sd Parish of St: Ann, Your Petiors: have carefully enquired according to the reasons aledged in the sd Order but can not find's that the sd Thomas Hall Father of the sd Children was legally setled in the parish of St. Ann aforesd

Your Petrs: therefore humbly conceives them- selves agrieved and appeals from the Judgment of the sd Justices to the Judgment of this Honble: Court and crave a day of hearing, And that the Church wardens and Overseers: of the poor of the sd Parish of St. Gyles in the Fields, may then attend so that the sd Thomas Hall, John Hall, and Willm Hall may be so Setled as this Honble's: Court in their wise Judgment upon hearing shall think fitt

And Your Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc.
