To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of Jeremiah Boreman

Sheweth

That yor. Peticoner having been Keeper of New prison at Clerkenwell for two years past, hath during the said time disbursed a considerable sum of money in the Necessary occacons of the said house the loss of which sum will tend to the imediate ruin of himselfe and family, and he being willing to obey such Orders as shalbe made by this Court for the Admission of the New Keeper

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to take his deplorable state into your serious Consideracon and to appoint him a Compensation for his said disbursments in such manner as to your Worpps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. (as in Duty bound) shall ever Pray Etc.
