To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in the General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in & for the said County.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Exelbee and Alexander Genett Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx aforesaid (whereof one is of the Quorum) Ann the Wife of John Flannigan an Irish man and her two Children (that is to say) John aged three years and James aged two years the lawfull Issues of the said John Flannigan and Ann his Wife were removed from the Hamlett of Mile end new Town in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your peitioners Conceive themselves agrieved and against the said Order do appeal.

Therefore your Petitioners humbly prays that a Short day may be appointed this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal,

And your Petition as Etc

Wm. Clarks Tho Harrison
