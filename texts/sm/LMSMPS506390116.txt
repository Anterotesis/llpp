To the worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of Lord Dudley and Ward and William Rugge Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th: day of March 1774 Elizabeth Cradock Wife of William Cradock was removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners concieve themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeal to Your Worships against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to order that the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square and all Persons concerned do attend this Court Some Day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
