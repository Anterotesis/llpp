To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Taplow in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Paul Vaillant and William Heckford Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 18th. day of October 1794 Mary Cage Widow of John Cage with her three Children the eldest named Susannah aged 4 Years the second named Sarah aged and Year and nine Months the youngest named Sophia aged nine Weeks were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Taplow as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that you will be pleased to appoint same day in their present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners etc

P Carsons Solls. for Appellants
