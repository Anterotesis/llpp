Middx Ss To the Right Worll: his Maties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions held at Hicks Hall

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Thomas Omoriarty of the Pish of St. James Westmr. in this County

That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Lawrence St. Loe Esqr. & Anthony Mann Esqrs. Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for Ye Sd County bearing date the first. day of June 1717 [...] yor. Petr. is Charged & adjudged as the reputed a Father of Female Bastard Child born of the body of Mary Readfearn & is shereby ordered to Indempinfye the Sd Pish & pay to ye Church Wardens & overseers the sume of two shillings & six pence P week as long as the Sd Female Bastard Child shall Remaine chargeable to ye Sd Pish & to give such further security as this Court of Justice at ye Quarter Sessions shall think proper as by the Sd Crdr. May appear By Wet Sd. Order yor. Petr. humbly conceives himselfe to be aggreived & doth appeale [...] to this Court against the said Order.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor: Worpp to appoint a Day to hear & Determine the Prmisies and that yor. petr. may be releived therein according to Justice & Equity

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc Thomas Omoriarty
