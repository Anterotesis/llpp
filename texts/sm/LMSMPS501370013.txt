To the Rt: Worsppll: her Matys Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon and Appeal of Mary Harris: Widdow

Sheweth That in December last your Petitr: upon the Information of one Anna Ridge was convitted before John Fuller Esqr: one of her: Matys Justices of the Peace for this County for Selling a Half penny Wheaten Roll of Bread to John Ridge Junr: of Shadwell Markett wanting one Ounce & Eight Drams of the Assize then Sett forth: And Since that Distresse hath been made on your Petitr: Goods. and are Deteyned from her

That your Petitioner humbly conceives herselfe aggrieved by the sd [...] Conviction Appeales against the Same.

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays this Court. will be pleased to order a Day in this Sessions: for all parties to Attend & hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court to [...] thing the said Appeal:

And yor Petitr: shall ever Pray etc.
