To the Right Worpll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of a Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the [...] Overseers of the Poor of the Libty of the Rolls in the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West in the County of Middlesex aforesaid

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands & Seales of Alexander Ward & Matthew Hewitt Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth day of March last past Elizabeth Sheppard wife of Rowland Shepperd with their three Children Virt: Ann their Eldest Daughter Aged about the Six Years John their [...] of Son aged about Four Years Elianor their removed Daughter aged about two Yeares & three months were removed from the Libty of Saffron hill & in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbern in the County of Middx to the said Liberty of the Roas as the Place of their last legall Settlementy

Yor. Petr. thinking themselves aggrivead by the Judgment of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal there from And pray that this Court will Pliase to Order the Church warden and Over seers of the Poor of the said Liberty of Saffron hill as & all Parties concerned to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court of Touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.
