To the right honoble Sr. Tho: Abney Knt. Lord Mayr. & the rest of his Maties Justices att the Sessions in the Old Bayley London.

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Price now a Prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth.

That yor. Petr. is a very poor Woman hath 4 small Children, upon a false accusacon hath been confined for above 4 Weeks;

Therefore humbly prays yor honble. Bench wilbe pleased to call her to a Tryall this Sessions or admitt her to bayle or otherwise discharge her as you Shall think for

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
