The Humble Petn. of Griffith Wooton

contd to next Sess.

Midd Ss: Ad Genal Session paris Dui Regis tent & Com Midd apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Streete in Com prd die Lune (Scilt) Vicesimo Septimo die February Anno RRs Gulielim Pcy mmc Augl Etc undecimo.

Itt is ordered by this Cort that Maddm Wooton wife to the petr: doe attend this Cort. on Fryday next att tenn of the clock in the forenoone to shew cause why the petr. Should not be discharged of his Recoginzance to Keepe the peace towards his sd. wife

P Cur Harcourt
