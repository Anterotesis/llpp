To the Worshipfull his Maty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Genll Quar Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petitnr. Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anne Westmr in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Hand & Seals of Willm. Ellis and Bremfield Esqr two of his Maty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 6th. Day of June last Priscilla Fowles widdow of John Fowles & their two Children Vizt [...] aged about nine years and Susanna aged about five year were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Le Strand in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. Anne Westmr as their last legal Place of Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved

Therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudgement of the said two Justices Pray year Worps will be pleased to order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary Le Strand & all persons concerned do attend this Court some Day on the Present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Shall Pray Etc

4th. July 1732. Jokendall Sol p Appeal.
