To His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the holding the general Quarters Sessions in and for the County of Middlesex-

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor for the Parish of Bray in the County of Berks

Sheweth

That he Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex did by Virtue of an Order under the Stands and Seals of John Scott and [---] Nashe Conant two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 23rd. day of February last remove Thomas Johnson a Pauper from the said Parish of Saint George- Hanover Square to the aforesaid Parish of Bray where the said Thomas Johnson has no Settlement whereby your Petitioners are aggrieved

That on the 23rd. day of March last

last your Petitioners caused Notices to be duly served on the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square of their Intention of appealing at the next general Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex against the said order

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the appeal may be set down to be heard pursuant to the above Notice
