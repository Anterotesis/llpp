To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hardin in the County of Hertford

Middlesex That by warrant or order dated the fourth day of May [---] last and under the hands and seals of Richard Birson Esqr. and John Bathbone Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the [---] County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) William Hogan Son of William Hogan by Jane his Wife aged near two Years was passed and removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Hardin in the County of Hertford under a pretense as your Petitioners are informed of its being the pl [...] of [...] his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order of the said two Justices of the Peace and humbly appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore your said Petitioners most humbly pray a day to be appointed sometime this present or the next General [...] Sessions of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex for the hearing and determining this their Petition and appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Theod: Johnson Sollr for the Apellants
