To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Sarah Blane [...] of the Hamblett of Spittlfields said County Widdow

Sheweth That by an order of this Court made in September last the Church wardens & overseers of the poor of the said Hamble [...] were ordered to allow your peticoner five Shillings p Mou [...] towards her Mainteynance to geather with the arrears of her pencon & to Continue the same untill further Order of this Court But the Church wardens & Overseers refuse to obey the same & stand in Contempt there of And for as much ad your peticoner being in great want ready to edrish

Your Petr there fore humbly prays this Con [...] will be & [...] sed to order the Churchward & Overseers to attend some day this Sess [...] to shew Cause why they refuse to obey the said Order

And yor Pt. Shall Ever pray Etc
