To the Rt. Worpll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of William Bilson On the behalf of william Bilson his Son

Sheweth That abt. 14th. March 1703 Petrs. Son became an Appentice to John Bartholomew of the Pish of St. Johns Wapping in this County Marriner For the Term of Five Years & a halfe as by the Judge of Appentishipp Appeares

That the sd. John Bartholomew is lately come home from a Voyage to sea & hath not Only barbourously used yor. Petrs. Sd. Son in such his Voyage but hath Left in parts beyond the Seas And forasmuch as your Petr. paying 20 Guineas 14 Pold to the sd. Bartholomew with his sd. Son

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly prayes your worshipps to be pleased to Order that the sd. John Bartholomew may Appear in this Honble Cort this put Sessions to give an Accmpt of she sd. Matter and that yor petr. Son may be Discharged from his [...]

P Tho Nash
