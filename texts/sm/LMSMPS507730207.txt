To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. george Bloomsbury in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and Charles Triquet Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one where of being of the Quorum) [...] date the Thirteenth day of October instant Joseph Hardy Mary his [...] and their three Children namely Mary aged Sixteen years Letitia [...] aged [...] years and Elizabeth aged Nine years were removed and conveyed [...] Court of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County [...] said Parish of St. George Bloomsbury as the place of their last legal [...] whereby your Petitioners are as they conceive aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will Pray Etc

Wor [...] Robertson [...] Sollr for the Appellants
