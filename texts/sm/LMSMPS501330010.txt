Middx May 1713.}

To [...] upfalle her Majts Justices of the peace for the Said County

The humble Peticon of James Hickson

Sheweth That yor Peticon is Malitionsly Prosecuted by Hannah Storie by the investigation of Mr Row; and that yor Peticon is an evidence for her Majtice against the Said Row; yor Worships were pleased to order him to plead to his indictment this afternoone;

Yor Peticonr therefore humbly prays the said Hannah Stone may be produced in Court she being Very Well in health altho

Mr Row may prsend the contrary

And yor Petcion as in duty bound shall pray
