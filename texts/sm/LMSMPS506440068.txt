Sept 1774

Honourd Sir I beg leave to lay the case before you of Thomas windsbury who is now in Clerckenwell Baidwell and have cum Taid before your Honour and he have Sufferd is punishment according to the order of the Court notwithstanding all this the said keeper refuses to Discharge him altho is time is out for to be Dischargd. according to the Late as he was last by he most Humbly begs that your Honour Gave goodness will be pleased to gams an order for is releasment as he at this time as no allowance of Barad and no friend to give him any and as get a Wife and Familey [...] sing and your Petioner as in Duty bound was to so pay Thomas Windsbury
