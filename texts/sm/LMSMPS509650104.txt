To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or order under the Hands and seals of N Conant and John Scott Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of September 1799 John Gravett was removes and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Marylebone in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West in the City of London

That your Petitions conceiving themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrant or order Do humbly appeal against th same and pray this Court that the Benefit of the S. Appeal be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the sd. County of Middlesex and that they and all persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the judgm and determination of this Court touching the sd. appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
