To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, in General [---] Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of the Westminster in the County Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue and in pursuance of an Order under the hands and seals of Thomas Gordon and Philip Neve Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 11th instant Elizabeth Hanmore was Removed and conveyed by the Name of Elizabeth Ann More from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said Two Justices, and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present sessions for the hearing and determining thereof and that all Persons concerned therein may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

George Barber Richard Willans H. T. Hodgson John Betteley Francis Glossop.
