1724 To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Jutices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of John Geary Keeper of New Prison in Clerken well in the said County

Sheweth That Yer. Petitconr upon John Sheppards breakeing out of the sd. Goal repaired and Strength ened that part of it but their being severall other parts Weak and out of repair he humbly prays Yor. Worships will be please to appoint a Committee to take a View and give directions for the repair of the same

And Yor. Petitions as Duty bound Shall [...]

Jno: Geary
