To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Uffington in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an [---] Order under the Hands & Seals of two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th day of August last past Mary Trinder wife of Edmund Trinder Bricklayer impressed into His Majesty's Service & their two Children namely Elizabeth aged about two Years & James aged about Seven Months were removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn to the said Parish of Uffington in the said County of Berks as the last legal Place of their Settlements by which your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved & appeal to this Court against the said [...] order

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worships will be pleased to hear & determine the matters touching this appeal & give them such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners shall for ever pray
