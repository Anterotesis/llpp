To William Manwaring Esqr. and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at the General Sessions of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard in East Cheap

Sheweth that by an order of Removed under the Hand's and Seals of Mr. Adington and W Barrett Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Elizabeth King Widow of Archibald King (who has been dead near Nine Months) and their Child John Archibald King were removed from the Parish of Saint Margarets in Westminster To the Parish of Saint Leonard in East Cheap as their last legal Place of Settlement by which order of Removal Your Petitioners think themselves agrieved.

Your Pettioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships to take the said order of Removed into Consideration and give Your Petitioners such releif as to your Worships shall seem meet And Your Petitioners as in duly bound shall ever Pray.
