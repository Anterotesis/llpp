To the Right Worspll his Maty. Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Leonard Mills of the parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the said County

Most Humbly Sheweth That yor Petr. is charg'd 6s. p Quartr. towards the releife of the said Poor of the said parish. That yor. petrs rent is but seven pounds p Annm and that Yor. petr. is aged 70 years and having a wife aged 63 years and both very crazy and reduced to extream poverty And upon Nonpayment of the said Assessment The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the aforesaid parish were pleased yesterday to restrain yor. Petr. goods So that he thinks himselfe very much aggreived thereby

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worpps to be pleased to consider his poor Condicon and to relieve him in the said prmisses

And yor. Petr. three ever pray Etc Leo: Mills
