To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Sessions in and for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr: in the said County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Peter Elers and John Poulson Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One of them being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 2d. day September 1748 Mary Mascall Widow was Removed from the Parish of Chelsea in the sd: County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Margt. Westmr: as the place of her last Legall Settlement Whereby your Petr Conceive themselves Aggreived and Against the said Order do Appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a day may be Appointed this Present Session to hear and Determine the matter touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc.

J. Feary Agent of the Appellants:
