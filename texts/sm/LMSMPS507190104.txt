Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assem [...]

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint [...] Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Rugge and Charles Mark Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster [...] [...] bearing. Date the Twenty fourth Day of November last past Elizabeth the wife of John Jekyll was removed and conveyed from the [...] Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint [...] James as the place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humble Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said order and pray that your worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the Hearing and determining this Appeal and to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish

of Saint George Hanover Square and all Persons concerned to then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Jas Dyson Solicitor for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westminster
