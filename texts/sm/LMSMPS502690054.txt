To the Rt. Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Ann Yorkson apprentice To Thos. Priduax of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the Said County

Sheweth That Your Peticonr: was by Indenture of apprenticeship bearing Date the Seventh Day of February last bound apprentice to Serve the Said Thos. Priduax for the Space of Two Years During which Space Your Peticonr. has been frequently Bent & very much misused & not been Supplied with Sufficent Necessary's

Your Peticonr. therefore most humbly pray that your Worsps would be pleased to Order that She may be wholy Discharged from her apprenticehood or Such other Releif in the Premises as to your Worsps Shall Seem meet

And your Peticonr. Shall Ever Pray Etc

Ann Gockson
