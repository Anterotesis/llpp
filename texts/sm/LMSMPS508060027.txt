To His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County in the Month of December 1785 Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Colechurch in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Chars Triquet and D Wilmot Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex afore said (One where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Sixth Day of October One Thousand Seven hundred and Eighty five Ann Wiltshire Single woman was removed and Conveyed by the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Norton Falgate in the County of Middlesex from and out of the said Liberty to the said Parish of Saint Mary Colechurch in the City of London. as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived

And Humbly Appeal to the Court against the same and pray that a Day might be Appointed for the hearing and Determining this Appeal at the present Session And that. the Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty of Norton Folgate and All other Persons. Concerned may be Ordered to Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. will ever Pray Etc
