To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Dicey

Sheweth That your Petr. hath served the Office of Constable for the Parish of Kensington for the space of one Yeare that there hath not been any Court Leet lately holden nor likely to be holden for a considerable time for the Manner wherein the said Parish doth lye.

Yor Peticoner therefore humbly prays that this Court would be pleased to discharge him from his said office of Constable & that your Worps; would be pleased to appoint Daniel Mr Phunn of the same parish Victualler to serve the sd office of Constable in your Petrs. Room & stead the sd Mr Phunn being a Substantial householder in the said Parish

And your Petr. in Duty bound shall pray Etc. Thos Dicey

April 1735 Birch
