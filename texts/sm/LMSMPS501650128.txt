To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genll: Quarter sessions Assemble

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of John Gumley, Thomas Edwards and Charles Smith Esquires of the Parish of Istleworth in the said County

Sheweth That Your Peticoners are Rated for their respective Messuages or Tenements in the said Parish of Istleworth for the Releife of the Poore of the Same Parish for the yearly Rent of Twenty pounds more than the same ought to be Whereby they Conceive themselves Aggreived and Appeale against the same

Your Peticoner therefore Humbly Pray they may be Releived in the sd. Premisses as to this Court Shall Seem Meet

And Your Petitionrs: Shall ever pray

John Gumley Tho Edwards. Cha Smith
