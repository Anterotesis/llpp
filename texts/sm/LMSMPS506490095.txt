To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, at their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Lane and George Merier Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of December last, Jacob Trout and his Child namely Sarah, aged Eight Years, was removed and conveyed from the- Parish of Saint James, as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved;

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session,- for the Hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint- Mary Le Bone, and all persons concerneds do then attend this Court

And your Petrs: shall pray Etc.

Jas: Dyson Sol. for the Appellants
