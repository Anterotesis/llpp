To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middx in their Genl. Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn which lies below the Bars within the Liberties of the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and Alpaham Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of January, last William Rodway and four Children therein described to be his four Children by the Names of Anthony George Rodway aged near 9 Years John Rodway aged near Six Year Sarah Ann Rodway aged about 3 Years and Nine Months and Joseph born on or about the 12th. day of Jany then Instant were removed and conveyed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx to that part of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies below the Barrs within the Liberties of the City of London as the place of the last legal Setlemt. of the said Wm Rodway and the said 4 Children before named Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitiners therefore appeal agt. the said Order and humbly pray relief in the Premes & that the Benefit of this their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in & for the sd. County

And your Petitioners shall ever pray [...]

Cox Hatton Garden Sollr. for Appellants Feby. 20th. 1794
