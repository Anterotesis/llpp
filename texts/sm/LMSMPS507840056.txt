To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General quarter Session of the peace in and for the sd. County assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish Saint John of wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and seals of William Blackmore Esquire and Herbert Mayo Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the tenth day of June last Cicely Eden singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint George in the sd. County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the sd. Order and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court agt. the same

And Yr. Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Tudman & Wegener Sollr. for appellts.
