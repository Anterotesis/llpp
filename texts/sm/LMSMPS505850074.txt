To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows in Lombard Street London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Robert Pell and Richard Hare Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of June One Thousand seven hundred and Sixty eight Mary Hayward Wife of Wilmer Hayward (who was gone from her and left her destitute and unprovided for) was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street as the place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day at this present General Quarter Session of the Peace for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all other Persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal.

Joseph Barber Upper

Churchwarden of the said Parish of Allhallows in Lonbard Street.
