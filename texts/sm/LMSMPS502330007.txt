Middl ss To the Right Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Hble Peticon of Edward Downes Late an apprentice to Richard Margetts of [...] Hamblet of poplar in the sd County Ropemaker [...]

Sheweth To [...] Petr. was by Indenture of Apprenticeship bearing date the 12th: day of February 1720 to ye: sd: Richard Margetts for the space of Seven Years to [...] his of a Ropemaker

That yr. Petrs. said Master hath been Dead abt: Eight Months last past And his Widow Esther Margarets doth not follow the sd: Trade so that yr. Petr. hath been since improvided for And the sd: Esther Margarets refuses to turn him over to a proper Mast [...] that he may be Taught & instructed in the sd. Buisness, under a Large sume of Money

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly pray that yor. Worspps would be pleased to Ord: that to may be Discharged of [...] his sd: Apprenticeship.

And yor: Petr: shall as in duty bound ever pray Etc:

Edward Downes.
