To the Right worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the sd. County.

That one Robt. Dast of the Parish and County aforesaid was (by the worspll Justice Webber Esqr.) comitted to New Prison (where her now remaines) for not taking care of and providing for Tomasin his now wife as by Law he ought to doe That the Said Tomasin being for this long time past very sick & weak and reduced to extreame poverty is since become chargeable to the Said Pish.

To whom She has bin a Charge for abt: two months last past.

P L Westley

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly prays yor. worsps to Order that the paid Robt. Dast may not be discharged out of the Said Prison without the knowledge of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poore of the sd Parish And may be Ordered by yr worsps to provide for his wife free from the Charge of the Pish

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Benin Knowles Ch: wd:
