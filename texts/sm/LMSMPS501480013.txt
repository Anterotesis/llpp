To the Worshipfull The Justices of the Peace for the County of Middelsex

The Humbel Petition of Diogenes Nichols of Sheweth that your Petitioner being an Inhabitant near old Street in the parish of Cripplegate and being an Inmate paying but four pound P annum Rent not with Standing is charged by mistake Considerable more to the Poors Rate; and your Petitioned [...] a family and being but in Indifferant Sarcomsta [...] in the world Humbly in treats Your Worships that the may be holy Releved from the Said Poors Rate and your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray

Diog' Nichols
