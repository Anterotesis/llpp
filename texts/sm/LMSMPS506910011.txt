To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon of Rowland Cambridge.

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof Yor. Petr. Stood Indicted in this Honble Cort this psent Sessions Your Worspp were pleaded to Fine him 40s. and for Nonpaymt thereof to Comitt him to New Prison in this County where he was Remains

That your Petr in a very Poor man & not able to pay the sd. Fine and humbly In bail being himselfe to your Worspps Great Goodness Mercy

P. Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes your Worspps To be pleaped to Mitigate the sd Fine as unto your Worspps shall Seem meet

and Yor. Per shall a [...] Pray Etc
