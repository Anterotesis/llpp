To the Rights Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Wright Church warden of Mile end old Towne. in the Parish of Stepney in the said County:

Sheweth That William Blackway over Seer for the Poor of the said Hamblett for the year 1699 hath in his hands the Sume of Forty Six pounds Fifteen shillings ane Nine Pence, over & above what he hath Disburst and paid for the Pse of the poor of the said Hamlett as appears upon the Ballance of the Accots being allow'd under the hands and seales of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County (Quorum Vnus) And that he refuseth to pay the said Sum to your Peticoner, altho the Same hath beene Seval times Demanded.

Yor. Peticonr therefore humbly prays that the said Willm. Blackway who is ordered [...] to attend the honble court this ofternoon upon another order may then [---] shew cause why he refares to pay your Peticone the said Money

Humfrid Elmes. Script

Ams Yor Petconer shall pray &ra. Tho: Wright
