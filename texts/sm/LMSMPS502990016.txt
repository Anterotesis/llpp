Aprill 22 1734

The Humbly Pertition of Elizabeth Evens Humbly Sheweth that your poor pertiticoner is an Pbgeck of pity and has been Confined in the goal called Clarkingwell This Mounth and Nasat hard Laber till I was sick and Olmost Harving for want I was Confined for a Quarell but am Discharged And have No friend in the World to Subsist. Me in my Extremety for hop your Honerable benth Will be so good as to Order Me to be turned out of that Misfortunate Place or Elce I Shall perick and your Pertithoner Will in Duty bound Ever To pray for you and your Good familys
