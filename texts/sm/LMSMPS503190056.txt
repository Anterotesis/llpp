October ye 12 1736

The humble perticton of Easter Grubb To the honourable Cort Being a very poor object of pitty She being formerly bund over before Justis Messer which he Know to be a honest poor woman and She Drawned her Recognise the 18th day of Last October which She Can Satisfy the Cort when She Come before ye honorable Bench Hoping you Gentlemen will take it in Conseederation of my seventy So I Rest

your Humble pertitioner

Easter Grubb
