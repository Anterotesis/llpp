To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of Philip Dyot & Stephen Clarke Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 14th: day of November 1760 a Female Bastard Child named Maryaged one Month born of the Body of Mary Hocknall otherwise Thomas Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St: George Hanover Square in the said County as the place of the said Childs last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a Time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then Attend And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc,

Wm. Barlow Wm. Perritt John James James Watkins} Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor
