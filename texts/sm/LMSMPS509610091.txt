To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of John Owen Paper Harying Manufacturer and Stationer and James Westbrook Gentleman Inhabitants of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor and also the Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch at a Meeting held on the 26th. day of April 1799 in the Vestry Room of the said Parish did make a certain Rate or Assessment for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish And for other the Ends and purposes of a certain Act of Parliament made and passed in the fourteenth year of the Reign of King George the third for the better relief and Employment of the Poor within the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and that they the said Church Wardens and overseers and also the Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch have omitted in such Rate or Assessment the names of the Reverend Mr Rose, Williams Mansfield George Greswell, John Sharrate, Elizabeth Lewin, Rolfe, Clark James Brown, Samuel Crompton, Jeremiah Smith Inhabitants of the said Parish and liable to be rated and included in the above Rate and to be assessed towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish and who so were at the Time of making the said Rate And also the names of several other Inhabitants of the said Parish who are or at the time of making the said Rate or assessment were liable to be rated and assessed towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That the said Rate or assessment was on the said 26th. day of April last ratified and confirmed by John Floud Esquire and William Brodie Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said rate or Assessment Do appeal to your Worships therefrom humbly pray that you will he pleased to hear there in the Premises and that the said Rate may be quashed And Order that the Church Wardens and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and such others whom it may concern may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worshipps touching the Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
