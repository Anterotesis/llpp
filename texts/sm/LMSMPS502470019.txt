To the Rt: Worsppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Gerras Quarter sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & appeal of [---] William Bartholomew appnr: :tice to Chelton Clark of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the sd: County Marriner

Sheweth.

That yor: Peticonr: was by Indenture bearing date the 29th: day of November 1722 bound an Appnr: to the sd: Chelton Clark for ye: Term of Seven Years to learn ye: Art of a Marriner.

That yor: Peticonr: sd: Master hath beat Prinses & otherwise misused yor: Peticonr: & for ye: space of Three Months last past hath not provided any Manner of New paries for yor: Petr:

Yor: Petr: therefore thinking himself Agrieved, humbly Appeal's to this Honble Court [...]

And prays that yor: Worspps would be pleased to Order that yor: Petrs: Said [...] a [...] May attend this Court some day this present Sessions to shew cause why yr. petr. should not be discharg'd from [...] R [...] & to hear & abide, the Judgmt: & determinacon of this [---] Court touching ye Sd: complaint

And yor: Petr: shall Ever Pray Etc.
