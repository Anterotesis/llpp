To the Right worspll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticonr of [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Dunstans in the [...] West London.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and sealed of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County Josuah Geering the Child of Thomas Geering of the age of Two Yeares or thereabouts was passed from the Liberty of the Rolls to your Petr.

That the Father of the Sd. Child never had any Settlmt in the Sd. Parish of St Dunstans in the West See that your Peter humbly conceive themselves not by able to provide for the Sd. Josuah Geering

Sheweth Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor Worsed to be Cased to Order that the said Josuah Geering may be repassed from the Sd Parish of St Dunstans in the West London to the Liberty of the Rolls aforesd

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.

Jos: Wilson Church Wd.
