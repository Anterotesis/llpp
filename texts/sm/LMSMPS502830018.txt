To the Right Worshipful the Bench of Justices at Hicks's Hall.

Ann Whitchead Nurse to Mrs: Mary Morgan the Wife of Henry Morgan. came this day before me and made Oath that the aforesaid Mary Morgan her Mistress now lying in and being very ill & weak thro: the fright She recd by the violent assault from Faithful Isham is utterly uncapable of giving her attendance at Hicks's Hall this Sessions

Most humbly prays that Faithfull Isham may be continued upon her recognisances till next Sessions.
