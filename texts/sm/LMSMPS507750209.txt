To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of R. Davies and I Hawley Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One where of being of the Quorum bearing date the Tenth Day of November last Margaret Robinson the Wife of Robert Robinson who is now absent from her at Sea was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate, in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields, Westminster as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some day in the present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate And all Persons concerned do then attend this Appeal and your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Jos: Holl. Sold. for the Appeallants
