Peticon of Ann Alprest

to be dd

December 1699

To the Rt. Worshippfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Ann Allprest

Sheweth That yor. peticonr: presented Jane Chatterton & Mary Pemberton for felloniously Stealeing Sevall goods of Value from yor. peticonr. wth goods are Now in the Custody of John Hooke one of the Headborough of St. Sepulchers wth. in the this County who Refuseth to Restore them to yor. peticonr. notwithstanding [...] secution
