To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Dr. Thomas Bennett Vicar of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County

Sheweth That your Petr is rated towards the releife of the poore of the Said parish the Sume of thirteen pounds and fifteen Shillings P ann for his Tythes, Therefore your Petr. thinks himselfe aggreived and appeales agt the Same

Your petr prays your Worp to releive him in the prmes as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And your Petr Shall pray Etc.
