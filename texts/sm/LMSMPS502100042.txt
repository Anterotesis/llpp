To his Worships ye Chairman Pure ye Worshipfull Bench of Justices now Setting at Hicks hall

The Petition of Thomas Chattaway of Hounslow House Keeper & Baker Most Humbly

Moveths's That yor Worships Petitioner may be admited to Baile two Melicious Bills being brought against him he not being Ready to try them & his bail being goad men & Ready in Court & your Worships Petitioner Shall Ever Pray
