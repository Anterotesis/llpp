Middx To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled this 12th day of January 1762

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Shenley in the County of Hertford

Sheweth Whereas by an Order under the Hands and Seals of P.L Perrin and Charles Welch Esqrs Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster Dated 21st day of November last Thomas Aged Twelve Years John Aged Six Years And William and Mary Aged five Months Twins therein said To be Children of William Hughes gone Away by Margaret this late Wife Deceased Were Removed from the Parish of St. James in the Sd [...] Liberty of Westminster To our sd Parish of Shenley as the Place of their [...] Lawfull Settlement Your Petitioners Thinking Themselves Agrieved by the sd Order And Removal Do Appeal To this Court Against the Same And Begg this Court Will Please to Fox a short Day To Hear and Finally Determin the sd Appeal And

Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray and so forth

Jno: Smith The Mark [x] of John Fensom} C:Wardens

The Mark of Joseph Shelton William Shelton} Overseers of The Poor
