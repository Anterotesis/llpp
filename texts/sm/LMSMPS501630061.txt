To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth This by Virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quord) bearing date the Seventh Day of July instant Martha Moorey and two Children Vizt Martha and Thomas Moorey were removed from the Parish of St James Westminster in the County of Middx aforesaid unto the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate London alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of their last legal Settlement. That your Petitionr comeing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Worpps there from

Your Petitioners therefore west humbly Pray Your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmised and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St James Westminister may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worpps touching the Appeal

And your Petitionrs Shall ever Pray Etc
