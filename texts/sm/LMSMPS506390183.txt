To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum of bearing date the Twenty fifth day of February 1774. Elizabeth Wilkes Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre as the place of hers last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitoners conceive themselves to be Aggreived

Therefore humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a Day in this Present Session to hear and Determine their Said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Jas. Noakes Sollr for Appelts.
