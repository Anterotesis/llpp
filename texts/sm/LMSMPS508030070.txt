Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Christ Church in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and John Spiller Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid one whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the Sixth Day of September last Elizabeth Wilson Widow was removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Christ Church in the City of London

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices do appeal to your Worships there from and humbly Pray that you will please to hear than in the Premises and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John Hackney may appear before you on a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
