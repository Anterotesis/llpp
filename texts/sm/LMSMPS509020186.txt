To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex Assembled at their General Quarter Session of the Peace begun and holden the Twenty seventh day of October in the Thirty fourth year of the Reign of King George the Third

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of R. Davies and H. Reynell [...] DD Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 28th. day of July 1794 James Bandage was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the Place of his last legal Settlement and your Petitioners were required to Provide for hum according to Law whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly Pray relief in the Premises and that your Worship will be Pleased to Appoint some day in the Present Session for the hearing and determining this their Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
