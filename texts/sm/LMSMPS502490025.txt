To the Rt. Worshipll. His Majties Justices of the Peace for the County [...] now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the sd County of Middx

Sheweth

That Richard Goddard was by Order dated the 10th. day of January Instert under the Hands and Seales of Nicholas J [...] freys and John Troughton Two of His Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said County Middx (quor unus) removed and [...] the parish of St.Andrew [...] Barrs in the same County [...] of St. Giles in the Fields as the legal Settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. humbly on themselves aggrieved there Appeale from the Judgment sd Two Justices to the Judgment this Honble Court and detire a Day of Hearing and that all Persons concerned may be ordered to attend and abide the Determination of this Court.

And yor. Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
