To the Right Worshipll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx Assembled in their Generalls Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Andrew Holborne above barrd in the County of Midlx

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Mich: Crake and W: Conn Esqrs. two of his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Second day of this instant Month of February William Gladwell aged about forty eight Years and Ailce his wife with their two Children (Vizt) William Gladwell aged about Fouerteen Years and Robert about thirteen Years were passed from the parish of St. Martin in the fields in the Said County to the St. parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs as the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said William Gladwell the elder

Your Peticoners thinck themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices doe humbly appeale to this Count and pras that the Church- -wardens and overseers of the poore of the Said parish of St. Martins in the fields and all other parties concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to heare and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Comt [...] touching this appeale

And Your Peticoner (as in duty bound) Shall pary Etc.
