To the Rt. Worpll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Middx ss The Humble Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter Le Poore in London

Sheweth. That by an Order dated the 6th. of July 1720 Under the hands & Seals of John Metcalfe & Wm. Dewe Esqrs. Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) by which said Order Robert Stopford als Stopforth with Elianor his Wife & their Son Robert an Infant aged about 9 months were passed from the parish of St. Andrew Holbourn above Barrs in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Peter Le Poore London as their last Settlement

That the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter Le Poore humbly Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order [...] pray an Appeale to this Honoble. Bench

Your Petrs. Humbly pray this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing of yor. Petrs. Appeale agst. the said Order

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
