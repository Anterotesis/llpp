To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled at Westminster.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of W. Kelynge and Petr. Planck Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 5th. Day March 1768 Elizabeth Barber a Female Child aged upwards of Two years was removed from the [...] Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County as the place of her last legal, Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time in this present Session for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may then Attend.

Austin Atty. for Appellts.
