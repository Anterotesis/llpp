To the Rt. Worspful his Maties Justices of this peace for the County of Midlx. at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Joseph Walden

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of William Hucks & John Ellis Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing date the fourth day of December last[---] your Peticonr. was Adjudged to be the Putative father of a Male Bastard bound on the body of Catherine Tod Widdow in the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the Said County

your Peticonrs. thinking himself agreived thereby Humbly a [...]

And pray there your [...] to give him Such refers in the Preiving your Worsps Shall Seem meet & the the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles's in the fields & all other Persons concerned may be Ordered to Attend Same day the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be held for the said County to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court

And your Peticonr. Shall Ever Pray Etc

Joseph Walden
