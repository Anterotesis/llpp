Ordrd that Pratten [---] attend on Thursday afternoon at 3.

27 May 3 W [...] 1691

To the Right Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of John Smith & Robert Circuit.

Sheweth That yor. Petrs have Served as Watchmen Under the late Constables for the Dutchy Liberty & their wages is Still owing them wch. they refuse to pay yor. Pets.

That yor. Petr. John Smith being an Old man & past his labour & very poor.

Yor. Petrs therefore humbly pray yor. Worshipps to order the Said late Constables Mr. Pratten & others to pay them their wages: And yor. Petr. John Smith humbly prayes yor. Worships to order him Some way for his future Subsistence to prserve him from perishing as to yor. wrps in yor. wisdome shall seeme meete.

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc:
