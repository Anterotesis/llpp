To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Scott and Nath Conant Esquires. Two of His Majesty Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th Day of June 1795 Elizabeth Pearce was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order & pray that the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day for the hearing & determining the same

And your Petitioners will pray Etc.

W. Atkinson Solr. for Appellants
