To the Worshipful the Justices at Hicks's Hall in St: John Street in and for the County of Middlesex Assembled

The humble Petition William Hiner

Sheweth That Your Petrs stands Indicted for an Assault and Riot George Shields the presecuts

That George Shields the aforesd prosecut was convictd at Maidstone Assizer for Willful and Corrupt perjury and is transported

Your Petr therefore humbly Imptores that Your Worships grant that Your Petrs may draw his Recognizance

And Yr Petr will pray Etc

William Hiner

N B That Yr Pet has been greatly harrsed and ruined has a Family and every Poor and tryed one Traverse Thomas Osborne prosecutr on same presented Assault & [...] And acquitted
