To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of A Graham and W. Kinnard Esquires- two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth Day of February last Sarah Alexander Widow (otherwise the Wife of John Alexander) was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John Hampstead in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of her legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal to this- Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be heard this present Session and that a time day be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof
