To the Right Worpl His Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St George Botolph Lane London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr Isaac Tillard Knt & Alexandr Pitfeild Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 15th day of February Inst 1723. John Chambers aged abt twelve Years & Sarah Chambers aged abt Nine or ten Years the Lawfull Issue of John Chambers who is not to be found were Sent and delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the County of Middx unto the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St George Botolph Lane London alledging the sd parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt

That your Petitionr conceive themselves aggrieved by the Sd Warrt & Judgmt of ye sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Worps. therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Worps will be pleased to Appoint a day in this present Sessions for hearing the Sd Appeal and that the Churchwarden & Overseers of ye poor of ye Sd [---] Hamblet of Bethnall Green & all other parties concerned may then Attend to hear & abide the determinacon of the Court touching the Sd Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc.
