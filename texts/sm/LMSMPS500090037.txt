To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Page late Constable of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell and William Pakeman late Headburrough there.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. in the time of their sd. Officer did disburse the Sume of Â£3.13s.4d in passing and releiving Cripples and Vagabonds As by a Bill now ready to be produced may appeares.

That yor. Petr have oftentimes requested the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the said parish to reimburse yor. Petrs. the sd. moneys Soe by them disbursed as aforesd but they refuse Soe to doe without an Order from this Honble Court

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poore of the sd. parish may reimburse yor. Petrs. the sd. moneys Soe by them disbursed as aforesd. by such were & meanes as unto yor. Worpps shall seem meet

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
