To the worpl his Maties. Justices of the peace of the County of Midd

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poore of the pish of St. Brides London

Sheweth That by warrt. under the hands & seales of Sr. Charles Lee Kt. & [...] Samuel Buck [...] Esqr two of his maties. Justices of the peace of this County (Quoy unus) Dated the 30th. day of May last, Anne Grantham an infant of the age of seaven years was passed from the pish of St. Andrew Holborne [...] this County unto the said Pish of St. Brides alledging that William Grantham father of the said Anne Grantham was last legally settled in the said Pish of St. Brides

yor petr [...] themselves agreived with the Judgmt. of the [...] humbly Appeal to this Cort & [...] ray that [...] wardens & overseers of the poore of the said [...] Andrews Holborne & all other [...] this Cort Some day this pse [...] to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determine [...] Cort. touching this Appeal
