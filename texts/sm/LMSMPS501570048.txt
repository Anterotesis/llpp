To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Tho: Smith Surveyour of the Highways of the Lower side of the parish of Eling in the said County.

Sheweth That Yor. petr. did indict last October in this Honble Court (amongst other persons Charles Beasley Henry Hiller percival Chapman Andrew Down John Frost John Fouch And William Collins Inhabitants of the said Parish For refusing to pay to the Highways therein

That the said Persons did appear before this Honble Bench when indicted and did pay their severall and respective Fees of this Honble Court But by Neglect some where or other they did not pay yor. Petr.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps that he may be releiv'd in the said prmisses

And yor. Petr. call ever pray Etc.
