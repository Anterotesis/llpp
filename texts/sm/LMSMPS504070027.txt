Middx. Decemr. Sessions 1750 To the Worshipful thom [...] Esgr. and Others his majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their general Session of the peace now Assembled

The Humble Petition of Charles Bray

Sheweth That your Petr. was at this present Session Tryed and Convicted for making an Assault on one Jane Buckley.

That for such his offence, the Court was pleased to direct that he should be fined 5. and suffer six months Imprisonment

That your Petr. is a very Poor man, a maker of Dolls by profession, has a Wife and family to maintain and cannot Satisfy and pay the said time without disposing of his implements in Trade, the doing of which will be attended with his and their utter Rum, more Especially that if he should Continue that time in Prison his Business wontd be intirely Lost.

That your Petr. has made Application to her prosecutor to make an End with him, but his Demands are no less than 30 [...] . which all your Petr. has in the World will not (if he was ever so willing)Enable him to pay.

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worships (the premises considered) to be pleased to release him from his said Imprisonment and mitigate his said Fine

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Charles Bray
