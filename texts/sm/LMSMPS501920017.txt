Midd ss: Ad Genal Session paris Dui Regis tent P Com Midd apnd HicksHall in St. John Street in Com prd die Lune scilt vicesimo Septimo die February Anno rui Dui uri Georgy Dei gra mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Septimo.

It is Ordered by this Court that it be And it is hereby recomended unto Thomas Milner William prestley John Richardson John Cleeve John Metcalfe and Alexander Ward Esqrs Justices of the peace for this County or any one of them to sumon before them the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Pancrass in this County And to examine into the truth of the peticoners complaint And to make such Order for releif of the peticoner as to the said Justices or any one of them shall seem meet.
