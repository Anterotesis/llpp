To the Rt: Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Jane Carrier of the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County Widow

Sheweth That your Petr. keepes a Victualling house in [---] Church Lane in the sd. Parish and att the Last Petty Sessions held att Bloomsbury for Renewing of Lycences for keeping Victualling houses Your Petr. Lycence for that purpose was then & there suppressed and your Petr. was then Ordered to make her Application to this Honble Cort this prsent Sessions

That your Petr. was borne & hath Ever since Lived in the sd. Parish where your Petr. hath allwaies Demeaned & behaved herslee civilly & honestly & kept a well governed & Orderly house

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes such Releife in the prmises from this Honble Court as unto your Worspps shall seem meets

And yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc

Jane [x] Carrier her make
