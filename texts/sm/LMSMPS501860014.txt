To ye Honble, the bench of Justices now sitting att Hicks's Hall

The Humble. Peticon of Joseph Benson

Whereas Yor poor peticoner now lying in New prison in a Starving Condition being referd to the Worship full Justices Fuller & he not being in town and Your poor petitioner having been Comitted ever since the eighth day of March & no prosecution, Humbly beggs the Honble, bench will be pleased to take it into [---] Consideration & grant his Discharge for which

Your Peticoner as in Duty bound Shall ever pray
