To the Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace now assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon of William Newman High Constable of Harrow in the Hundred of Gore

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. has Served the Office of High Constable for the full Terme of three Yeares, & has sevrall private affairs of his own to Transact wth: said Office linders yor. Peticonr: very much of Transacting his own Affairs & buissniess

Yor. Peticonr has paid & discharged all the Publick money in his hands

Yor. Peticonr. humbly hopes yor. Worshipps will dismiss him from the said Office of High Constable for the sd Hundred of Gore and humbly recommends Mathew Farmer of Pinner John Alwing of the same place & Henry Finck of Harrow for yor Worshipps Approbacon & Choise of one of them in yor. Peticonr Room

And yor Petr. in duty bound shall ever pray Etc William Newman

We approve & recommend. as a fitt & proper Pson to be chosen High Constable for the Hundred of Gore in the Room of Wm. Newman
