To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Woodburne of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate in the County of Middlesex Pattin maker

Sheweth. That your Petr: was Sworn unto and Executed the Office of Constable of the said Parish for the Year Last past.

That Yor: Petr: having Served for the Space of One year and upwards and no Court Lect having been held for the Mannor wherein the said Parish is

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Prays that he may be Discharged from his said Office and that one of the Inhabitants here under named may be Sworn in his Room.

Henry Man of the Parish and County aforesaid Master Coachman and William Rogers of the same Master Coachman

And yor. Petr. as in duty bound shall ever Pray etc.

The Mark of John [x] Woodburne
