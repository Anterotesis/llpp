To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the the peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble. Peticon and Appeal of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margaret Moses in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands D'oyly MitchelAlexander Pitfeild Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the Twentieth Second day of January last past John Bramhal and Elianor his wife were Removed from the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the sd. County of Middx unto the sd. parish of St. Margaret Moses London Alledging the said parish of St. Margaret Moses to be the place of their last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoners Conceiveing themselves Agreived by the said [...] said Two Justices [...] therefore [...] to Your Worships of the from, And humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premisses And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the sd. County of Middx May Appear before you at a Certain day by Your Worships to be Appointed to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determinacon of Your Worships touching this Appeale

And Your Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
