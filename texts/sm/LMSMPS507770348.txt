Middx Janry. Session 1784} To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of Jenkin Jones & David Wilmot Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx one Whereof being of the Quorum bearing rate the ninth day of of January 1784 Elizabeth Taylor described as in Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Luke in the Said County to the Said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Thats our Petitionrs Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the sd [...] tant or order do humbly Appeal against the Same and Pray the [...] Worships Will be Pleased to Appoint a day this present [...] han that all Parties Concerned may Attend to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal and your Petition & shall ever Pray Etc

Clare & Bamptick} Sollr for Appellts
