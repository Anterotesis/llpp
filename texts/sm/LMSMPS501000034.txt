To the right honourable Sr. William Withers Knight Lord Major & other her Maties. Justices at the Sessions

The humble peticon of John Hasling Hosier

Humbly Sheweth that whereas John Crofts stands convicted this Sessions of Felony and Burglary in the house of the Peticoner.

The the Peticoner lost Goods to the value of near fifty pounds and hath been at ten pounds charges to find out and prosecute the said Crofts.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays this honourable Court will admitt him to the Reward given by Act of Parliament for convicting Burglaes and house breakers or soe much thereof as you shall thinke reasonable

And yor. Peticoner (as in duty bound) shall ever Prayer
