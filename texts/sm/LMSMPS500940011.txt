To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Mary the Wife of Thomas [...] ll

Sheweth That for the [...] of your Petr found Judicted in this Honble Cort [...] Last Sessions your worshpps were pleased to Fine [...] and for Nonpaymt. thereof to Comitt here to New Prison in this County where she still Remaine

That yor Petr is very Poor [...] able to pay the sd Fine and forasmuch as She haveing One Child to Maintey [...] also [...] with Child and humbly Submitteing herselfe to the Goodness [...] of this Honble Cort

Yor Petr therefore most humbly Prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the sd. Fine as unto your Worspps Shall Seem meet [...] her matys court of [...]

P Tho Nash

And Yor: Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc
