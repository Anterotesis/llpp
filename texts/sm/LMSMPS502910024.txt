To the Worship full William Cooper Esqr Chaireman & the Rest the Justices of the peace at the Quarter Sessions of the peace now Holden at Hicks's Hall

The Humble pettition of Patrick Sloman

Sheweth that yor pettr was Bound for one James Leary, upon a Comon Assault most Humbly Beggs that yor pettr may be Called Soe as he may Draw his Recognizance

And yr pettr well pray
