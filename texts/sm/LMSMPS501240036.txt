To the Right Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace fo [...] the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Joseph Hollowey an [...] Thomas Hill.

Sheweth That your Petrs. did serve the office of Surveyors of the Highwaies within the parish of St. John Wapping in the [...] county for the year Last past. That your Petrs. have disburssed more then they have re [...] by vertue of the Rate made for reparing the Highway within the [...] parish of St. Mary Whitechappell. That your Petrs. have delivered the Said Rate to the Wors [...] Justice Feamton who together with the Worspl. Justice [...] Johnson have directed your Petrs. to pay the sum of two pounds to the present Surveyors of the Highwaies of the Said parish And there being Severall Sums of money Uncollected of the said Rate and the said Justice Fram and Justice Johnson refusing to redeliver the said Rate [...]

P L Westley Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Worspp [...] to be pleased to order that the said Rate or Assessment may be redelivered to them Soe that [...] may receive the said Severall Sums of money for Uncollected as aforesaid in order that their accompt [...] may be Settled.

And yor: Petr: shall ever Pray Etc.
