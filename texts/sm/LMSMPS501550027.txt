To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Gregorys by St Pauls London

Sheweth

That by Order or Warrant Dated the Third day of August last under the hands and Seales of Thomas Rand and Richard Browne Esqrs: two Of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor un) Elizabeth Rydder Widow was Removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County to the aforesaid Parish of St Gregorys by St Paul as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Rydder

That your Peticoners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and Appeat against the Same.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Session for all parties to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
