To the Right Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Richmond in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by Warrant dated 31 day of October last past under the hands and Seales of Sir Godfrey Kneller Kt. and Thomas Vernon Esqr. Anne Ellott was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Twickenham in this County to the Parish of Richmond aforedsaid as the place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Peticoners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Ordr. humbly appeal against the same and pray a day may be appointed this prsent Sessions for all Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of the Court touching this Appeal

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
