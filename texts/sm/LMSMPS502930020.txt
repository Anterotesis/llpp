To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of Robert pidgeon against the poors Rate of the parish of Saint Mary Whitechappell in the Said County

Sheweth.

That your petitioner is this year Rated- and Assessed the Sume of Two Shilling and Two pence per month towards the poor Rate of the said parish and that their is Severall persons who live in houses of greater Rent and abillity who are Not charged So much.

Your petitioner thinking himself agreived by- the Said Rate humbly appeal against the same

And pray that Your Worships would be pleased to give him Such releif in the premises as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray

B: Pidgeon.
