Janos Burtons Petion done dd

To the honorble she Bench of Justices for the County of Middx sitting at Hicks hall

The humble peticon Jane Burton

Sheweth That ye. Petr. Stands. comted to Newprison being sentineed to stand upon the pillory & fined Six pounds thirteen Shilling in [...] fore. Peace upon being converted of a misdemeanor

That yr. Petr. is big wth. child & has nothing to help herself wth. all & must in all probability parish in prison unless relievd by this honble. Court

Therefore humbly prays this honorble. Court to mitigate or Streate her said fine as to yor. worsps. Shall seen meet

And ye. Petr. (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray, Etc a
