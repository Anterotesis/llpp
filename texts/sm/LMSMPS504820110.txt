To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace, for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye Poor of ye. Parish of Alhallows, Staining in ye. City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, and the Hands and Seals of Thomas Quarrill & Thomas Smith, Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye: County of Middlesex (one whereof being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of February last past, Elizabeth Vizer, Widow, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Mary, Whitechapel, in the County of Middlesex, unto ye sd. Parish of Alhallows, Staining, in ye. City of London, alledging ye. said Parish of Alhallow's, Staining, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Cha. Baynbridge, Attorney for the Appellants } That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom, and humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the Promisses, and Order, That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary, White- -Chapel may appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.
