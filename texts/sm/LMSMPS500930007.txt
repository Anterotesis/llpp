To the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Mary Thorowgood

Sheweth That One Mahon & Morphy stand indicted which Indictmt: ought to have been traversed last Sessions But yor. Worships were pleased to Order it to be respited. untill this Day 10: a Clock That yor: peticoner & her Witnesses have attended ever since 10: untill now, And the said [...] have not appeard according to the Said Order [...] humbly prays yor. Worps. direccon whether she shall attend with her Witnesses any longer or not they living near 20: Miles from London.

14 Janr [...] [...]

And yor Peticonr: will pray. Etca.
