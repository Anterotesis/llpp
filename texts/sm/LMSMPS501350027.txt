To the Right Worship full her Majties. Justices of the [...] assembled att their Gennerall Quarter Sessions [...] for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Canning Leicester of the Parish of Saint Clements Danes in the County of Middx Baker

Sheweth ss: That your said Peticoner was Convicted before John Fulley Esqre: one of her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx, upon the informacon exhibitted agst: [...] by Samuell Cooke [...] for selling to John Ridge Junior Shadwell markett one halfe penny wheaten Lowfe of Bread wanting twelve Dracluns of the Assise then sett

That your said Peticonr: Humbes himselfe aggre [...] the said Conviccon of the said Justice, and humbly appeals to this Worshipfull Bench,

P Henricum Denton 12th: October 1713}

Wherefore your said Peticon: [...] humbly prayes a day to be appa [...] for the hearing and determining this his Appeale

And yor Peticonr shall pray Etc
