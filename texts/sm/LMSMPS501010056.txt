To the Right Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of William Dickman of ye Parish of Cheswick in ye said County

Sheweth

That your Petitioner was Disabled by the Lose of ye use of his Left Ancle in an Engadgement by Sea in ye Service of her Majestie Queen Ann on board her Majesties Ship Cumberland under ye Commd. of Captain Richard Edwards & after Prisoner in France Ten Months where he endured Great Hardship

Your Petitioner therefore humble Prays your Worships to be pleased to Grant him such Pension as Yor. Worships Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr: Shall Ever Pray Etc.
