To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Warden Carpenter

Sheweth That your petitioner hath laid out about the sum of Eighty Pounds for Repairing the house of Correction at Clerkenwell in this County but it being referred to John Fuller Alexr. Ward John Ellis of Denmark Street Thomas Pindon and Mathew Hewitt Esqr. Justices of the peace for this County or any three of them to View the said Work which they did and have only reported due to the Petitioner Thirty five pounds Seven Shilling and Seven Pence halfe penny Exclusive of Carpenteres & of Plumbers work which they say they cannot discern where the same is done

That Your Petr. Finding he is likely to loose a Consider; able sum of Money by the said report he therefore most humbly this prays this Court will please to appoint Captain Boswells on any other Competent Judge to Estimate the work of all kinds and to upont to yer. Worships what he Deserves for the Same and Yor. Petitioner shall readily agree to the same

And us in Duty bound shall ever pray. John Warden
