To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St. Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples Esqr and Rupert Clarke Esqr two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Nineteenth Day of January last, Catherine Ford Widow of John Ford Deceased and her Fire Children lawful issue by her said late Husband Vizt. John Elizabeth, Francis, Mary and Ann, as also a Daughter named Catherine Letgen by her former Husband Martin Letgen, were removed from the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the said Precinct of St. Catherine as the place of their last legal settlement

That Your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order do humbly appeal against the same and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions that all parties concerned may attend to leare & abide the judgment and Determination of this court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jas Clare} Solr for the appellants
