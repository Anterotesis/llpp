To the Right Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembeld

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hendon in the Said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Farmer and Clifford William Phillipps Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (one of them being of the Quarum) made the Second Day of March in the year of our Lord One thousand, Seven hundred and thirty five Upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chappell in the County aforesaid Sarah Sutton (widow and Relict of Richard Sutton) together with Sarah their Daughter Aged Five years and ten months Richard their Son Aged four years and Eleanor their Dans Aged Eighteen months were removed and conveyed from the Said Parish of Saint Mary White Chappell to the Said Parish of Hendon as the Place of their Last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive the Said Parish of Hendon Agreived by the Said Order and Do humbly Appeal there From.

Your Petitioner Therefore humbly Pray that a Day may be Appointed for hearing the Said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners Shall Pray

Tran: Peters Soll: for Hende [...]
