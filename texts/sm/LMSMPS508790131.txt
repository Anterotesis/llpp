To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the liberty of the Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and seals of Nathaniel Conant and Philip Nese Esquires two of his majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th day of December last Richard Berry was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the said liberty and County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of this last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof and that all Persons concerned may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

George Lockett Jonathan Buttall Richard Moorley Thomas Knight William Pitts William Richardson
