May it please yor: Worships:

The humble Petition of Jane Tiffeny wife of Joseph Tiffeny Mat: in ye Parish of St: Jnos Wapping Stepney Redinn Lane

Sheweth That yor: Petitioner is a poor Sickly weak Woman not capable to Work for her bread and her husband Some where abroad and has two Small Childrean & must unavoidably Starve if not relieved in Some measure, She has Apply'd to the Church Wardens of the Parish but will give her no reliefe

Therefore She humbly prayr your Worships to have Compassion on her miserable Condition and Order Some thing for the Support of her two Small Childrein and yor poor Petitr: Shall ever pray Etc:
