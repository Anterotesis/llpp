To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James [...] Clerkenwell

Sheweth That by are Order under the Hands and Seals of I Floud and W [...] ne Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) be any date the eleventh day of September One thousand seven hundred and ninety four Thomas Savage and Mary his wife and their three Children lawful Issue namely Sophia aged nine years and upwards Sarah aged Seven Years and George aged four years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saml Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners [...] themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly May their Ca [...] will please to appoint a time be hearing and deternming the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may their Attend
