To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx in their Genll: Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the said County.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removall [...] Date the Second day of September Last past under the hands. [...] Seales of John Fuller and Alexr. Ward Esqrs: Two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) Winfred Ipsom was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the place of her Last Legall Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices And Humbly [...] Appeal against the Same to this Hononrable Court

Your Petr: therefore Humbly Pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint Someday this prsent Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal And that Your Petr. may have such Releife herein as to your Worship's shall Seem Meet etc.

And Yor: Petr: shall Pray Etc
