To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of South Fleet in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Smith and George Garrett Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of April last past Mary Stephenson Wife of Solomon Stephenson (a Soldier abroad in his Majesty's Service) with her Child named John aged upwards of Sixteen years were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary White Chappel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of South Fleet the said Justices there by Adjudging that to be the Place of their last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices and humbly Appeal there from,

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Pray that your Worships would be pleased to Appoint someday

In this Present Session to hear this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chappel and all parties Concerned in and about the Premises may then and there hear and abide the Determination of Your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno: Garland Thos. Colyer} Churchwardens

John Colyer Jno Venner} Overseers of the Poor
