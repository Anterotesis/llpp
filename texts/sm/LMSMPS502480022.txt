To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices For the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Fan.

Sheweth That your Petitioner as Formerly Keeping the Hicks Hall Coffee House under your Worships has Since Been afflicted wth. the Rumatishim for three Quarters of a year and Having two Children to Maintaine all that White [...] Drove to Such a Necessity that is Oblidged to Trouble this Honourable Court for their Charity Towards Sending her Down in the Country to her Relations and then will Never more be Troublesome

And Yor Petitr. [...] ll in Duty Bound Ever Pray
