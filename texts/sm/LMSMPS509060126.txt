To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr. and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at the Quarter Session of the Peace now holding at the new Session-houses Clerkenwell Green in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by a certain Order date the 8th. day of November 1794 under the hands and Seals of Paul Vaillant and Mr. Heckford Esquire two of his Majestys her has of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) made on the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex one William Ellis therein named hath been removed from the said Parish of Isleworth to the said Parish of Christ Church in the said County of Surry as his lawful Settlement whereby your Petitioners and the rest of the Inhabitants of the said Parish Christ church and much aggrieved as your Petitioners humbley conceive the said William Ellis near obtained any legal Settlement in the said Parish of Christ Church

Your Petitioners do therefore Appeal to your worship against such Order of Removal and humbly Pray that they may be heard

thereon And that an Order may be directed to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Isleworth to appear before your Worships to hear and abide the Determination of you Worships touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
