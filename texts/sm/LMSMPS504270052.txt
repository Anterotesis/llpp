To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant dated the Thirteenth Day of thes instant April under the Hands and Seals of John Bever and King Gould Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Ann Cadell aged about 15 or 16 years was removed from the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Lambeth as the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petrs. apprehending themselves aggrieved by said removal humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray that a Day may be appointed this Sessions for the Hearing and Determining thereof

And your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.

Middlesex At the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid by Adjournment on Wednesday the Twenty fourth Day April in the Twenty Seventh year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain Etc.

It is Ordered by this Court that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Hammersmith have Notice of thes Appeal And that they and all persons concerned do attend this Court on Fryday next at Ten of the Clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same Court touching the said appeal.

By the Court Waller
