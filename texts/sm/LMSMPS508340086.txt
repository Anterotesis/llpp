To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session house for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of Ann Order under the Hands and Seals of Wm Quarrell and Saml Hawkins Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid) the whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th day of April 1788 Elizth Pope Widow of Thos Pope was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Hamlet of Mile End Old Town as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships Will please to appoint some day in this present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned do them attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall Pray

Phil Chalk} Church Warden

Robt Duchesne Jas. Anderson} Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town.
