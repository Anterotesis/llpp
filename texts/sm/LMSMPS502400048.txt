To the Right Worpl his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Allhallows Staining London

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sr Willm Thomson Knt Recorder of the City of London and John Milner Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 17th day of December 1726 Anne the Wife of Paul Okey with her two children Mary aged two Years and Paul aged Ten Weeks were removed from the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx to the said parish of Allhallows Staining London Alledging the Said Parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Worps therefrom

And therefore humbly pray Your Worps would be pleased to appoint a day in this present Sessions for hearing the said Appeal And Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of [---] Bot without Aldgate in ye sd County and all other Parsies concerned may then Attend and hear and abide the determinacon of this Court touching the Sd Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
