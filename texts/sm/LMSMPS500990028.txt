The Humble Petr of Wm Jeanes

5s. R H

To att & Sh: cause Th: 10 cl

5o July 1708 Served Dowdeswell It [...] P:

Sr. Jon Bennet refr to Mr. Coggs Mr. Worley or 2

by consent of ptys

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session Paris Due Regine tent & Com Middx apud Hickshall in St. John Street in Com prd. die Lune scilt quinto die July anno regin Due Anne mmc Regine Magne Britannie Etc Septimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Present Churchwardens and the late Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in this County have notice of this peticon, and that they doe attend this Court on Thursday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to shew cause why they Should not pay unto the petr. the within menconed sume of four pounds three shillings & ten pence, as they & every of them will answer the contrary at [---] their Peril

P Cur Harcourt

Jonathan Dowdeswell Edwd Smith } Prsent Chwdens.

Bayly Edward How Thos: Sage Withers &} Ov: for the ye. 1707
