Sheweth The humble Peticonr [...] a [...] sixteene Pence P Ann [...] Rated towards the Releife of the Poor of the Sd Parish the sume of one Pound & to the Scavengr Rate Eight Shillings against be which Rates he humbly Appeales to this Court

your Peticoner humbly Prays that the said Rates may bee metigated as to your Worshipps shall seeme meet

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
