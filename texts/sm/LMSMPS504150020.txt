To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peice for the County of Middx in their Generel Serious of the Peace Assembled

The Humble petition of Margaret Matthews

Sheweth that Your Petr at this present Sessions was tryed upon an Indictment for a misdeneonor [...] in thretning to Set Fire to a house and found Guilty and Sentened by your Worships to be Pub lickly Whiped for the said offence and is now in the Custody at the Keeper of New Prison which Sent ence she Confesses to be most Just That your Petr has five small Children to provide for which by her own Industry She has kept from the parish Several Years and has no other way of Getting her lively hood but by Serveing News Papers

That if your Petr undergoes the Sd. Sentence it will intierly Ruin your Petr and her Said Chilldren that Humbly beg that your worships will be pleased to take her Care into Considiration and Mitagate the Sd Sentence as to your worships Shall Sum meat And your petr Shal Ever pray M: M [...]

we whose Names are Subscribed So humbly Certify to your worships that the Petr [...] a verey honest Industri ous woman whom we have known some years and that She has five Small Chilldren which the main tains by her one Labour and Industry
