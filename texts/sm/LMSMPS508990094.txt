To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and John Scott Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventeenth day of June last Mary Brothers the Wife of Charles Brothers (gone from her) and their Two Children namely George aged Three years and James aged one year were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said part of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and pray that you will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that you will Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Mary le bone to appear before you to abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc.

Arthur Gliddon Sollr for the Applls
