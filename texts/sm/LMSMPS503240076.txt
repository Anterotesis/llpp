To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeale of the Overseers of the Poor of the old Precinct of the Tower without

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Owen Davies and Richard Mauler Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of westminster (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty first day o June Last Past Ann Watson Single woman was removed from the Parish of Saint margaret [...] in the said City of westminster in the County of middlesex to the Old Precinct of the Tower without as the Place of her Last Legal Settlement

By which said Warrant or Order your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeale to this Court against the Dance Praying such relief as to this Court shall seem most meet

and ye Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc

By order of the Overseers of the Poor of the said Precinct

Sam Sadler their Agent
