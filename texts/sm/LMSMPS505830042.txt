[...] Worshipful the Chairman and the Rat of his [...] Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex [...] General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The [...] Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and over seers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the said County

Sheweth That [...] or Order under the Hand sand and Seals of [...] melch and George Fried Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th day of april 1768 [...] Elizabeth Mathews Singlewoman was Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Christ Church in the said County Alledging the said Parish of Christ Church to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

16th may 1768 Your Petition bearing themselves aggrieved by the Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the Peace Do therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall Settlement.

And your Petrs shall ever pray etc

Wm Carter So Wm Vistry Clerk of the Parish of Christ Church

Middx
