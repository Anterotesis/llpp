Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace assembled in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden in and for the said County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of James Duffin of the Parish of Saint Ann Soho in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the third day of November last summoned to appear before the sitting Magistrate at the Public Office in Hatton Garden to answer a certain information exhibited by one Martha Bunting against your Petitioner for that your Petitioner did after the Twenty fourth day of June One Thousand seven Hundred and Thirty Nine to wit on the fourth day of September One Thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety Seven at a certain House in Leicester Street in the Parish of Saint Ann in the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster unlawfully play at a certain fraudulent Game to be determined by the chance of Dill under the denomination of the Game of Hazard

That your Petitioner in obedience to such Summons appeared before the sitting Magestrate at the Public Office aforesaid on the ninth day of November last when your Petitioner was convicted by Aaron Graham Esquire of the said offence and adjudged by him to pay the sum of Fifty Pounds for such supposed Offence.

That your Petitioner then thought and still thinketh himself aggrieved by the Judgement of the said Majestrate in as much as your Petitioner is not Guilty of the Offence so charged upon him by the said information

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly pray he may be heard by himself or his Counsel in the nature of an appeal against such Conviction as aforesaid.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray &. James Duffin
