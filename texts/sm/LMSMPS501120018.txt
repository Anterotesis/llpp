To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Samuell Morley

Sheweth

That your Petitr stands bound for his Appearance in this Honble Court this present Sessions To Answer the Complaint of Ann Morley (A pretended Wife of yor Petitr her Name being Anne James) As by the Recognizance may Appeare.

That yor Petitr is now in her Majesties Service In the Capacity of A Lieutenant In the Regiment Commanded by the Honourble Major Generall Sabin And that your Petitr hath upon this Occasion only Staid in Towne Since Saturday Last When the Express orders. from his Superiour Officer were to the Contrary Soe that he lyes Lyable to be Suspended And forasmuch as there being noe Indictment against yor Petr:

P Tho: Nash The therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Grant that he may be discharged from the said Recognizance

And your Petitr Shall Ever pray Etc

Sm. Morley
