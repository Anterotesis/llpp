To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices for the County of Middlx now in Session

The Humble Petition of Mary Kitt

Sheweth That your Petitioner is very Poor & now bigg with Child & not able to pay [...] One penny, That your Petitioners Husband is now in Goal upon this Prosecution Therefore your Petitioner humbly beggs this Worshipfull Bench will remitt the Fine laid upon your Petitioner And your Petitioner shall ever pray

Mary Kite
