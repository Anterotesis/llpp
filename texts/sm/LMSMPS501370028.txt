To the Rt Worppll her Mats Justices of Ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Anthony Buckmaster Constable of South Mims in this County

Sheweth That your Petitioner has Due to him the Sum of Tenn Pounds at Christmas Last Past for one Quarter of a Year's Passing of Vagrants

That your Petitioner Standing Indebted to Severall Person is in great Necessity and want of money to Discharge the same.

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly prays your Worsps wil bee pleased to Order him the said Sum of Tenn Pounds out the moneys Collected for that Purpose

And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall Ever pray Etc
