To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the PeaceAassembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alton in Hampshire

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex ( one whereof being of the Quorum ) bearing date the eighth day of January Inst. Elizabeth Pickernell wife of William Pickernell and her two Children were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Alton as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved humbly Prays that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that Your Worships would appoint some day in the next Session for the hearing and determining thereof Etc.

Your Petitioners will over Pray Etc
