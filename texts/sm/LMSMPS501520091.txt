To the Worll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of Richard Bealing & Thomas Moody Esqrs. Justices of the Peace of & for the City and Liberty of Westminster and the said County of Middx beareing Date the 11th. Day of February last: Elizabeth Markvey a Bastard Child then aged about two Months was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Leonard in Foster Lane in the Liberty of St. Martins Le Grand in the Said Liberty of Westmr. to the said parish of St. Margaret Westminster as the place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That your Petrs. thinking themselves agreived by the Adjudgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to yor. Worsps. against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worps. would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Leonards Foster Lane London in the said Liberty may attend this Court Some Day this Quarter Sessions to heare & abide the Judgment and final Determination of this Court touching this appeal

And Your Petrs: Shall ever pray.
