Middx To the Right Honourable Sir Mathew Blakiston But Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of the Honourable Bench in their General Session of Goal Delivery [...] for the County of Middx Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Grant now Prisoner in Newgate for Felony

Sheweth That on the Thirtieth Day of June last your Petitioner was Committed Prisoner to the Gatehouse Westminster by George Wright Esquire as Oath of William Law for Privately Stealing form his Person a Canvas Bag Containing Twenty Guineas his Property And now is a Prisoner Confined in Newgate Under the same Charge.

Your Petitioner therefore Most Humbly prays That at this Present Session of Goal Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case Made and Provided.

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc.
