To the Right Honble Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the Cis: of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex In Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Oliver Mc.Allester a prisoner in is Maj [...] Gaol of Newgate

Sheweth. That your petitioner on the twenty second Day of A [...] last was committed to His Majesty's Goal of New Prison of [...] Henry Fielding Esqr One of His Majesty's Justices of the [...] for the County of Middlesex for felony expressed in the [...] Warrant and is now detained thereon in His Majesty's Gaol of Newgate.

Your petitioner most humbly prays this Honourable County that He may be tried bailed or discharged this Session according to the directions of the Statute in such Case made and provided.

And your Petitioner shall for ever Pray Oliver Mc: Allester
