To the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The Petition & appeale of Timothy Ireland of the hamb [...] of Ratclif in the parish of Stepney

Most humbly shewe [...]

That he is very much over rated in the tax for the poor, and most humbly prays your Worships will be pleasd to consider his Case and

Your Petitioner will as in Duty bound Ever prays Etc
