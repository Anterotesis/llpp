Middx To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of Peace for the Said County in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of Samson Barnett on of the Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middx Gentleman

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner is of the Age of Sixty Seven & Greatly afflicted with the Gent other disorders by Reason of which he several weeks in the year Confined to his bed and without the Assistance of others not able to helps himself

That the Inhabitants of the parish of St. Luke aforesaid have Put your Petitioner in Nomination to Serve as one of the Scavenger of the said parish for the Present Year from which Office under Your Petitioner Circumstances & Age he humbly hopes he Shall be Excused from the Service thereof as the Same may tend greatly to the Destruction of his Health & the Shortning his Days & the Inhabitants be Injured by reason of the unavoidable neglect of his duty if appointed there to for the reasons before mentioned

Your Petitioner therefore Prays for the reasons above Offerd that he may be Excused from the Service of the Said office or to give him Such relief as to your Worships Shall seem meet

And your Petitioner Shall ever Pray

Sampson Barrett
