To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex in this present Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Powsey in the County of Wilts.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hand and Seals of Nathaniel Collant and John Scott Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Dale the 14th Day of May Instant Mary Jordan and her five Children Namely Harriot Jordan aged 12 Years and upwards William Jordan aged 10 Years Ann Jordan aged 7 Years and upwards Hannah Jordan aged 5 Years and catherine Jordan aged 3 Years were removed and conveyed how and out of the said Parish of Hanwell to the said Parish of Pewsey as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to you Worships against the same and humbly request that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be reserved to them and that a day may be appointed in the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex for the hearing and determining the same And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Hanwell be then and there ordered to attend to hear & abide the Judgement and Determination of the County touching the said Appeal And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
