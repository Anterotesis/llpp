To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in- General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers- of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Lewis and John Hatchett two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirtieth day of October last Jane Lissemore Simmons Singlewoman was Removed and conveyed from the Parish of Kensington in the County- of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive Themselves to be Aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

Jas. Dyson

Sollr. for the Churchwards. and of Overseers of the said Parish of St. James
