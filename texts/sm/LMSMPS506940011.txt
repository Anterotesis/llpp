Middx: To the Rt. Worppfull a her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon of Jno Nelson

Humbly Sheweth. That yor. Petrconr. hath served the office of Pette Constable in the Parish of Wilsdon in the Sd. County for the space of one year last past [...] [---]

Yor: Peticonr there fore humbly prays Yor Worpps to Order Saml. Bretteridge jun of Wilsdon aforesd. to appear to before [---] [...] of her Maties Justices of the peace of the [...] County at this Session [...] tion to take the Oath of Constable of the Sd. Parish in the Roome & Stead of Yor. Peticonr.

And yor. peticoner shall pray Etc.
