To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Session of the Peace held at Hick's Hall in & for the sd County.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor & Parishoners of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Richard Newton & 6: Moore two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 6th. day of this instant April, Mary Boyter Widow was Removed from the Parish of St. Clements Danes in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London as the place of the last Legal Settlement of her the said Mary Boyter by which Order & Removall your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved & humbly Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order a Short Day for all parties to Attend your Worships on this Appeal & to abide by the Order & determination of your Worshipps touching the same

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Richd Berry Sollr. for the Appellts.
