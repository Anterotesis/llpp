To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John's Street in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bridget otherwise Bridge in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Rea [...] Henry Trent Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one of the Quroum) bearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of June Last Elizabeth. Needum Singlewoman Was Removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Bridget otherwise Brides as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceivs themselves to be Agrieved and Against the said Order do Appeal.

Wm. Gason Attorney for the Appellants }

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray- that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session To hear & Determine the Matter Touching the Said Appeal-

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
