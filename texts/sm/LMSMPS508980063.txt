To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Sessions of the peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Alban in the County of Hertford

Sheweth; That by an Order or pass warrant under the hands and seals of Sir W. Gibbons Rent and Thomas Wood the younger Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty first day of September last past whereby George Cottrill Hannah his wife and their four Children (namely) John aged about eight years George aged about six Years and Mary aged about five years and Ann aged about three years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Hanworth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Alban as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal so made by the said two Justices Appeal to your Worships against the same and request you will be pleased to respite the hearing and determination of the said Appeal until the next Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex

And as in Duty Etc Jno. Cowper Sollr. for the Appellants
