Middlx ss. To the Right Worpll. her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden [...] and Overseer [...] of the poor of the Parish of Greenford in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Wart under the hands and Seales of Nicholas Goodwm and James Hawley Esqrs. two of her Maties Justices of the peace for the send County (Quor unus) Bearing date the 5th day of December Cap. Thomas Shephard was removed from and out of the Parish of Chiswark in the same County to the Parish of Greenford aforesd. therein Setting for [...] that he the said Thomas Shephard about Nine or ten Years agoe was hyried or a Covenant Servt by the year to one Francis Heath of Greenford aforesaid Gentsmem. at two pounds per Ann Nages.

Yor Peticoners therefore finding themselves agreived with the Judgmt of the sane two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this honble Court against the said Warts. And pray that yor. Worpps: will be pleased to grant an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Chiswark above said may attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and finall Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal Etc.

And yor Peticonrs shall over pray Etc as Simon Ravenor Churchwarden Tho: Betwell overseer of the poor
