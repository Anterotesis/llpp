[...] To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex [...] Quarter Sessions of the Peace at the Session House in the said [...]

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of on Order under the hands and Seals of David Walker and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Tenth Day of September last samuel Elliott aged about thirteen years (in the said Order described by the Name [...] Samuel Ellard [...] removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint [...] in the Field in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint [...] the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby year [...] conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitoners there fore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determinning the said appeal and to Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Field and all persons concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitir shall Pray Etc

Jno: Allison G:J soward} Church wardens

and

John Elcock William Thomas} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint anne
