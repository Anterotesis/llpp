To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of George Mercer and J: Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth Day of November last Mary Byron was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Count against the Adjudication and Order of they

the said two Justices and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof and that all Persons concerned may have Notice to attend the same

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

P Sydenham Rd Murthwait} Churchwardens

Charles Bond William Main Jas Bisfield} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Arme

James Ranken
