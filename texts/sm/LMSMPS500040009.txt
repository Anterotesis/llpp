To ye Rt Worshipfull their Maties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Lee in behalfe of her husband Lucey Lee of St Giles in the Feilds

Sheweth That your Peticoner is bound by recognizance to appeare this Sessions to Answere what should be objected against him for being a Roman Catholick

That your Peticoners husband is a poore distempred man and not of a sound minde memory or rationall partes and aperson altogether unable and unfitt to Attende the Sessions as by the annexd affidat may appeard

Wherefore your Petr most humbly prayes this Hounoble Bench to Comisserate his poore and misserable Condicon and be pleased to discharge the Sd recognizance

And (as in Duty bound) she shall ever pray Etc.
