To the Worshipful this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish os St. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Peter Planck and Thomas Kynaston Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th. day of January last Margaret Buckle Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the said City and Liberty to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the sd. two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will he pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for hearing determining the Said Appeal

Richd: Remnant Edwd. Birch Thos. Chalmers Saml. Baker} Ch Was Overseers of the Poor
