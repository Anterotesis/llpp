To ye: Rt: Worshipfull His Maties Justices of ye: Peace for ye: County of Midd assembled in their Generall Sessions of ye; Peace

The Humble Peticon & Appeals of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye. parish in Hayes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Samuel Perry & Samuel Newton Esqrs: two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 28th. Day of November last Martha the wife of Adam Hannah & her there Children Viz Mary aged about nine years & eight months William aged about two years & Hester aged about two months the Lawfull Issue of the said Adam Hannah & Martha his wife were removed from the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County to the sd. parish of Hayes as the place of the Cash legall Settlemt. of the said Martha Hannah (the sd. Adam Hannah being a Foreigner & his Settlemt: unknown)

Yor Petrs: think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the sd: two Justices appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. parish of St: Mary White Chappell & all other Psons concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt: & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor: Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
