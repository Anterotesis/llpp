To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Oveseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in old Fish street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of I Poulson and Thomas Exbe Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middledsex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Pearing Date the Nineteenth Day of June 1735 John Shaller an Infant was removed from the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fish street London, Alledging the Said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in Old Fish street to be the Place of his last legall Settlement.

That Your Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeal to yor Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear ym. in the Premisses, and Order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Loberty of Saffron till Hatton Garden Ely Rents in the sd. Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain day take appointed to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of yor. Worships touching this Appeal.

Cha Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. }

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray Etc.
