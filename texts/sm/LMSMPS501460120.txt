To the Rt. Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of at Hickshall

The humble Petition and appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. under the hands and Seales of John Milner & Jno. Richardson Esqrs. Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County bearing date the Tenth day of June last whereby Mary Holloway aged about Eight years Daughter of Thomas Holloway & Elizabeth his wife was removed from the parish of St Gyles in the Fields in the said County to your Petrs. said parish of St. Martin in the Fields whereby your Peter humbly conceive they are wronged and agrieved

yours Petr therefore most humbly pray yours Worps to appoint a day to hear & determine the same.

And your Peter. shall ever pray & c.

Will Braman Churchwarden Wm Harr overseers
