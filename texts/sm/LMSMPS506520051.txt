To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of Joseph Loyd of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex Chandler

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was on the 21st. day of March last [---] Convicted before John Sherwood Esquire one of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County for Unlawfully Selling three Loaves of Wheaten Bread not marked with a Roman W. And was adjudged to pay and forfeit for the same the Sum of Eight pounds

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeals to this Court against the said Conviction and prays that this Court will be pleased to Order that All Persons concerned Do Attend this Court some day in this present Sessions of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal And that Your Petitioner may be otherwise releived in the Premises as the said Court shall see fit

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

J. Jenings Soll. for the said Joseph Loyd
