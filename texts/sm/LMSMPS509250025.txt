To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Brixworth in the County of Northampton

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of John Spiller and George Reid Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22nd. day of March 1796 Mary Denton Wife of James Denton was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Brixworth as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudicated of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
