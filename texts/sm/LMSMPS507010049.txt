Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull the Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex att Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Richd. Leister Inn Keeper

Sheweth That Your Peticonr. has Served the Office of Head borough for the Parish of Kensington in the said County by the Space of one whole Yeare & upwards And there is noe Court kept wherein a Head borough can be Chosen not like to bet. of some Considerable time

Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly prayeth that he may be Discharged and that Edward Whitehall Victualer or James Holland Baker Indifferently Chosen by ye Inhabitants of the said Parish may one of them by the Order of this Court be Sworn in his Place And yor. Peticonr. will ever pray.
