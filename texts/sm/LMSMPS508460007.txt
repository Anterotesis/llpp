To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrews Holborn which lives above the Bars in the Said County

Sheweth That by an Order of pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Cogan and Charles Friguet Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof is of the Quorum bearing date the 23rd. day of May Instant Catharine Lawson Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County aforesaid to the said part. of the said parish of Saint. Andrew Holborn as the place of her last. legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that your will he pleased to hear them in the premisses and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Mary Islington Mary appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Etc

Stable Sol for Appets.
