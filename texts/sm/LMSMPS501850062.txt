To the Right Worshippfull Bench of Justices at Hick's Hall etc

The Humble Petition of Catherine Revell liveing in the Parish of St Leonard Shorditch in the County of Middlesex Widow

Humbly Sheweth that yor. Petitionr. is a Poor widow Woman and keeps a little shop to sell Chandlery ware towards my maintenance and being told I must pay to the Poor which Sum not able to do, Most humbly be such your Worships, will please to take my case into your consideration and Order that I may be cas'd of the Same not being able to pay etc.

Worthy Srs and Yor: Poor Petitionr. As in duty bound shall ever Pray

Catherine Revell

Justices report on ye other side
