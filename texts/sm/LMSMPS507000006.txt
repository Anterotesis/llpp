To the Right Worspll. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of John Stacy

Sheweth That yor. having executed ye Office of Keeper of Ludgate. Prison in the City of London for near twenty yeares past and therein behaved himselfe to the Satisfaccon of the Severall Magistrates for the time being, and by reason of his Experience being (as he humbly hopes) well qualifyed for any place or Stacon of the like nature

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to admitt him into the place of Keeper of New prison now vacant by the Death of Mr. Wm Wicks.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray.
