To the Right Worshipfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of N Boothe and William Hucks Esqr. Two of It is Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one where of being of the Quor) bearing Date the fifth Day of July last past, Elizabeth: the Wife of Thomas Price & their three Children (to wit) Thomas Price aged about five Years, Sarah Price aged about three Years & Elizabeth Price the Younger aged about Six Months, were removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn above the Barrs in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London Alledging the Said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate to be the Place of their last legall Settlemt:

Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants } That Yor: Petrs: conceiveing themselves agrieved by the sd. Warrt. or Order of the sd. Two Justices, Do therefore appeal to yor. Worships therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & Order yt: the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn above the Barrs in the County of Middx aforesd. may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed. to hear & abide the Jndgmt. Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall pray Etc
