To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of James Lane of the Parish of St. George in the East in the Said County of Middlx:

Sheweth That your Peticoner rents a House of ten Pounds p ann in the aforesaid Parish and is Rated in the Summe of two Shillings P month for and towards the necessary Respect of the poor of the same: that Sevall of you peticoners Nergbours who rent Houses of Greater Value and who are in better Circumstances than your Peticoenr pay but One Shilling and Six pence: and that Your Peticoners House never was rated So much before

Yor. Peticoner threrfore thinks himself Agreived by the said Rate and humbly prays that this Houble Court would Give [...] Sati [...] as to your Worshipps Shall Seem meet

And your Peticoner Shall Poor pray Etc
