To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Edward [...] Wheeler

Sheweth That your Peticoner about Four years agoe by Indenture of Apprenticehood became an Apprentice to [---] Daniel Harvy Turner for the Terme of Seven years.

That the said Master hath Beat & abused him & not Learnt him his Exade nor allowed him Necessarys Insoemuch that your petitrs friends have been at the Expence of near Twenty pounds to Subsist him To answer which the said Master stands bound by recognizance before the worshipfull Justice Com his appearance this prsent Sessions.

Yor. Petr. therefore prays his said Master may attend this Court to shew cause why he should not be Discharged from his Apprenticehood to his said Master

And yor: Petr: shall Ever pray Etc

E: Wheeler
