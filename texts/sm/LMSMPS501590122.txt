To the Rtt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the Pea [...] for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon and Appeal of the Churchwar- -dens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrt: under the hands and Seals of Tho: Sewell and Doyly Michel Esqrs: two of by Maties. Justices of the peace for this County (Quor unus) bearing date ye. 24th: day of October last Wm Smith & Elizabeth his wife and one Child named William Smith aged about nine years (the lawfull Issue of the said William Smith and Elizabeth his wife) were removed from the parish of St: John of Wappin in this County to ye. said parish of St: Botolph wth: out Aldgate as to ye. place of their last Legall Settlemt:

That yor petrs: think themselves agreived thereby and humbly appeale to this Honrble: Court agt: the same And pray that your worships would be pleased to Order the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: John Wappin to attend this Court some day this Quarter Sessions to heare and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said appeale

And your petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
