To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace in Sessions assembled at Hicks's Hall in St John street in & for the County of middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Heathwat now a Prisoner in New prison for the sd County

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. hath been confined in New prison aforesd upwards of nine weeks on a pretender assault on one Ann hott and being this day discharged by you worshipps for want of any prosecution and your Peticoner being upwards of 74 years of age & not able to pay ther fees of this Court nor the fees of the sd Goal haveing only a pention of gd. P wee [...] for her maintainance from the Pish of St George in the East in ye sd County payd

Yor Petr. most humbly Prays yor. worsps the mennes considered to order yor Petr. to be discharged witness paying any fees or to make such Other order herein as to yor. Worship Shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
