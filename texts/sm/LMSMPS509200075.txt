To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Dukes Place London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removal under the hands and Seals of the Reverend Henry Reynett D D [---] and Robert Smith Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County, of Middlesex one being of the Quorum, bearing date the Seventeenth Day of November 1795 Esther Baren therein called the Wife of David Baren and Martha her Child aged about Six Years and Sarah her Child aged about Five Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County of Middlesex into the said parish of Saint James Dukes Place as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order appeal against the James to his Honourable Court and pray such Relief in the promise as to this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno. Land Jno. Baker} Church Wardens

John Stocker John Chapman} Overseers
