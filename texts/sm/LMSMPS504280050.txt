To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the peace for the same County Assembled

The humble petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Alverstoke[---]in the County of Southampton

Sheweth That by an Order or Vagrant pass [...] Dated the 12th. Day of April 1754 under the [...] of Boulton Mainwaring Esqr. Rachel Miller and her two Children Vizt. Samuel & Charles Miller were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of saint George in the County of Middx aforesaid to the parish of Alverstoke aforesaid

Your petitioners concerning themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Vagrant pass [...] do humbly appeal to to this Court against the same And pray that she hearing and determining of the same Appeal may adjorned untill the next General Session of the peace to be held for this County And that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish St. George aforesaid and all other other persons concerned may then attend this Court to hear and abide the final and and determination of this Court touching the same Appeal

And your petr. shall ever pray

J Fell Soll for the Appellants
