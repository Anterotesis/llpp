To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace at the Court of the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Machin and James Croft Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifth day of October One thousand seven Hundred and Seventy nine Jane Burnet Wife of James Burnet (a Soldier) and her Child namely James aged one Year and Eleven Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County to our said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby Do humbly appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day at this present Sessions of the Peace for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
