To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the [...] and Scals of John Cox and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth day of January 1768 Elizabeth Wilkinson aged four Years and five Months And Hanah Maria Wilkinson aged one Year and Nine Months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the [...] in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby [...] [...] mers conceive themselves [...]

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this [...] the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said [...] And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to Your Petitioners And that the hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for this County

And Your petitioners Shall pray Etc

Benjn. Gee W. Williams Geo: Soward, John Dorman} Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
