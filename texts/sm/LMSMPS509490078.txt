To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Ricd Davies Esqr. & The Revd. Henry Reynett D.D. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifteenth Day of March last Jane Carrovan Singlewoman was removed & conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day that all Parties may attend to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of this Count touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
