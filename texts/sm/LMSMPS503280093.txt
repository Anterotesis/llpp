To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall in the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Lane and Robert Midford Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of October last Eleanor Evans Singlewoman was removed form the parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the Pish of St. Paul Covt. Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved and Appeal to this Court agt. the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may attend at the same time

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

D: Hughes Soll for appel}

James Gordon Paulin} Churchwardens
