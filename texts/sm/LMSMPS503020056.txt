To The Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Mary Wilson Spinster was by Order of Pass warrant dated the 4th day of June 1734 Under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Removed & Convey'd from the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the same County to the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the barrs as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners Humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and appeal from the judgmt of the said two Justices to the Judgment of this Honourable Court and desire a day of hearing may be appointed this present Sessions And that all persons concern'd may be ordered to Attend to hear and abide the Judgmt and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray
