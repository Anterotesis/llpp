To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers. of the poor and other ancient. Inhabitants of the Parish of Heston in the Said County

Sheweth

That last Sessions your Petitioner were Indicted for not having Trested a Cage Whipping Post and Stop in the Towne of Hounslowe.

That your Petitrs. have since at yr owne proper. costs and Charges Erected a Substantiall Cage. Whipping Post and Stop in the most convenient place in Heston aforesaid

Wherefore yor: Petitrs. humbly pray they may be Discharged of the aforesd Indictments.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc
