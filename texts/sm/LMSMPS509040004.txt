To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hand and Seal of Nicholas Bond Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty fourth day of October now last past [...] the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes are ordered to pay to Emelia Empson the Wife of Thomas Empson a Substitute serving in the Westminster Regiment of Militia for William Crighton of the Said Parish of Saint Anne towards the support of herself and child a weekly allowance of three shillings out of the rates for the relief of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes which said allowance is to be comfected from the 24th. day of April 1793 and by which said order the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne from time to time when and as of [...] as they should be thereto required by the Overseers of the [...] of the said Parish of Saint Clement Daues are ordered to rein burse and pay to them all such sum and sums of money as they shall advance in pursuance of the order made as aforesaid Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

They therefore hereby appeal to this Court Against the Order of the Said Justice and pray that your worships will appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining thereof and that all persons concerned therein may have notice to attend the same.

And your Petitioners will ever pray

Willm. Hopkins Wm. Bailey Wm M Ream Jn Preeds
