To ye Rt Worshpll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of William Hall

Sheweth That yor Petr Stands Bound for his Appearance in this Honourable Court this present Sessions for Assaulting one Martha Smith as by the Recognizance may Appeare

That the matter Indifference between Yor Petr and the sd Martha Smith is fully Determined and Agreed As by a generall Release Executed by the said Martha Smith to your Petr more plainly Appeares

P Tho: Nash [---] Your Petr therefore most humbly prays your Worshipps to be pleasd to Grant that he may be dischargd from the said Recognizance

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc William Hall
