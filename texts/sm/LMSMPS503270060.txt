To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the said County

Humbly Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex whereof one is of the quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of November; One the said Seven hundred and thirty Seven James Mercer otherwise Messer Ellinor his Wife and their Three Children James aged about Fourteen Years, Joseph Aged about Four Years and Ellinor Mercer aged about Three Months were Removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in this County to the said Hamlet of Ratcliff as the Place of their last Legall Settlement

Therefore your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humblly Appeal to this Court against the same

And pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day this Present Sessions that all Parties may attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court for and touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall Pray Etc

Thos. Martin Agent for the Appellants
