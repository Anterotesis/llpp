To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at the Geneal Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in and for the County of Middlesex on Monday the fifteenth day of January 1749.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Datchet in the County of Bucks.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble the Lord Kinnoull and John Stevens on Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fourth day of December last William Wansell and Sarah his wife were removed from and out of the parish of Bedfent in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Datchet in the said County of Bucks.

And that your petitioners think in themselves aggreived by the sd Order of removal humbly appeald against the same

That your Petitioners have given due Notice to the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Bedfent of their Intention to Appeal at the prsent Sessions against the said Order

Therefore your Petitioners humbly hope that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing of the said Appeal and that the said Order of the said two Justices way be discharged And your petrs shall ever pray Etc.

J Tyrrell agt for Appellants
