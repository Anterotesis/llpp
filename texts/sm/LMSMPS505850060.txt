To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Surveyors of the Highways within the Parish of Greenford.

Sheweth. That the Highways aforesaid have been repaired within the present Year 1768. at a considerable Expence.

That your Petitioners have already disbursed the Sum of Seventy Pounds or thereabouts on that Occasion.

That the Composition Money (now of the Inhabts. chusing to do Labour) Amounts to about the Sum of Â£40.

That it will require at Rate of Six pence in the Pound to compleat the said Roads and Reimburse your Petitioners their Expences

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray a Rate of 6d. in the Pound to be made on the Inhits of the said Parish

and your Petr. shall ever Pray.

A Martham Nathl. Ravenor Juner
