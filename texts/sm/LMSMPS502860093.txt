To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of R. Parsons and Thos. Rea Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Quorum unus bearing Date the 12th. Day of August last Sarah Titchbourne Widow of Edmond Titchborn aged about Twenty Nine Years was removed from the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County of Middx. to the said parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeal to Your Worships against the said Pass warrant and Judgment of the said Two Justices and pray that the Benefit. of the said Appeal may be saved unto them untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for the County of Middlesex.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pay Etc
