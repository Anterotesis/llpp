To the Right Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon of William Atwick Constable of Shepperton. in the said County.

Sheweth That Your peticoner hath served the Office of Constable for the Parish of Shepperton for the space of Two years. whith hath been Expensive & fa [...] ting to him

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays he may be Discharged from Serving any longer and one of the two under named person those in his room

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

Wm Atwick

Alex: Battle John Hatchett
