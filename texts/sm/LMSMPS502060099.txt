Middx ss To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Sd. County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the pish offt. Clemt. Danes in the Sd. County.

Sheweth That by an Ordr. midl. the hands & heales of two of his Matyes Justises of the peace of th Sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) dated the Sixth day of Sebtembr last John Smith and Hannah his Wife [...] [...] were removed from the pish of St [...] James Clerkenwell in the Sd. County to the. pish of St. Clemt.. Danes as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That Yor Petrs. thinke themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices and humbly appeale there from to the preqeant. of there Cort

Chimmow Yor. Petrs therefore most humbly pray yor. Worspps to Ordr. all partyes to attend Yor Worspps Some day this Sessions in Ordr. to abide the Judgemt.. of this Cort Waching this Appeale

And yor Petrs. Shall pray Etc.

Mr Shadle a Butcher upon the Green by the Redsion Ch:W Mr.Jno. Ward [...] next door [...] Overseer
