To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions Att Hick's Hall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seales of John Ellis & Miles Hayer Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Second day of September 1730 Henry Reeves aged about forty Eight Years together with Elizabeth his Wife aged about twenty Seven Years and William Reeves their Son aged about five Years were Removed & Convey'd from & out of the Parish of St. James Westmr. in ye said County of Middx to the Parish of St George Hanover Square in ye said County as the Place of their last legall Settlement Whereby the said Parish of St George Hanover Square think themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Prays this Court will Appoint a day [...] ye said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned may Attend at the Same time

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc
