The Petition of Mary Lincoln

Prisoner in Newgate1 [...] 90/91

To the Worshipful William James Esqr one of their Majies Justices of the Pence for the County of Middx.

The humble Petn of Mary Lincoln Prisonr in Newgate.

Sheweth That yor Petr is fitly qualified for the Benefit of the late Acts of Parliament, for the Reliefe of poor [...] Prisonrs for Debt

Therefore your Petior humbly prays your Worship to grant yor Warrant to the Sheriffe of Middx: and to the Keeper of their Maties Goal of Newgate for the said County thereby requiring them to Bring yor Petr before yor Worship to take the Oaths required by the said Acts in order to your Petrs Discharge.

And your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc:
