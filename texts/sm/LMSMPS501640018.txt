To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of

Middx

The humble peticon of Mary Crawford

Sheweth That your peticoner Standing Indicted in this Court Imployed one Mr. John Nuby of St. John's Wapping to be her Sollicitor to assist her in Traversing the said Indictmt [...] For which the said Nuby hath brought your peticoner in a Bill of Five pounds Seventeen Shillings & Eleaven Pence Which Bill your Peticoner is informed to be very Unreasonable & Exorbitant

Your Petitr: therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Order that the aforesaid Bill may be Taxed by the Clerken [...] of the peace of this County

And Yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc

Mary Crawford
