To the Right Worps. their Mates Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster and County of Midds.

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Thomas Saunders High constable of Finsbury Division in the Hundred of Ossulston in the said County.

That at the generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County in October 1689 your Peticoner was elected to Serve the said Office of High Constable For the said Division.

That your Peticoner hath been lately visited with a great Fitt of Sickness almost to the hazard of his life and he being still very infirme and crazy in body See that he is not longer able to everute the Said office.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays your Worpps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from the said Office of High constable and that one of the persons hereunder named may be elected to Succeed your Peticoner in the Same Office.

And yor. Peticoner shall ever Pray Etc.

William Genson of St. John Streete Stephen Cleeve of Whitecrosse streete John Evans of the same Edward Godfrey of Clerkenwell } Persons Fill to Succeed
