To the Rt: Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the peace for Ye. County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Henry Hodges One of ye petty Constables of Southmins in this County.

Sheweth That at Lady Day last past there was due and owing unto yor. Peticonr. Twenty pounds for halfe a years Salary for his Loss of time Charges & Expences in releiving passing and conveying of Vagrants through this County

And that yor. Petr. having been at great Cost and Expences in performing the sd Service, and having a great Family to maintain.

Most Humbly prays this Honble; Court would be pleased to Order the speedy payment of the sd Twenty pounds unto yor. said Petr.

And /as in duty bound/ he shall ever pray Etc.

Henry Hodges.
