To the Right Worspd. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd. The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Towne of Norwood in the said County.

Sheweth

That one Roads als parsons widow was by Warrant under the hand and seales of Nicholas Price Esqr. and Richard Shoreditch Esqr. two of their Maties Justices of the peace for the sd County lately passed from the parish of Hayes in this County to the sd Towne of Norwood

That the Sd. Road als Parsons never had any legall Settlemt. in the Sd. Towne of Norwood & that she was Last legally Settled in these parish of Hayes which yor. petrs. hope to make appeare to this Honble Court

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that these Road als Parsons may here passed to these Parish of Hayes.

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

Margret

Richard Bicks [---] dur Morr: [...]

Henry Nellunn
