The Humble apeall of Micah Willkinson to this Honourable Bench.

Being chose headborough of the liberty of old Street in the Parish of St Gille's Chriplegate, and is in the Sixty Second year of his Age. I haveing been uncapable of Working at his own busyness for severall years by lameness of the Rhumatisme therefore is by his age and in firmeness of body uncapable of answering the ofice Either by person or Finne therefore humbly desires your Worships Consideration
