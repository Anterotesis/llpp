To the Right Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of he Parish of Trinity in the Citty of Chester in the County of Chester

Shews That by an Order dated the 9th. day of Decemr last under the hands and Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor Unus Sarah Urmson Widow and her four Children (Vizt.) Anne aged Six Yeares Thomas four Yeares Samuel two Yeares and Richard a Month were removed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in this County to the said parish of Trinity in the City of Chester in the County of Chester as the parish and place of the last legall legall Settlement of the Said Sarah Urmson and her four Children

That yor. petrs. think themselves aggreived by the Order of the Said two Justices and Appeale therefrom to yor. Worshipps And therefore and in regard to the distance of the Said Parish of Trinity from this County

Yor. Petrs. therefore pray you would be pleased to appoint Some day in the next Easter Sessions to heare and determine the said Appeale

And yor petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
