To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church ward [...] & Overseers for the poor of the Parish of St. Burgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands and Seals of Henry Lowth and Doyly Mishel Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (quor unus) dated the 13th. day of July 1726. Sarah Bleech Wido. of John Bleech & her three Children James William & Sarah were past on ye. Petrs. from the Parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate Middx as the peace of their last legall Settlement

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggrevid by the ordr. of the sd. two Justices one humbly appeale to yor. Worpps therefrom

Wherefore yor Petrs humbly Pray Yor Worpps to appoint a day to heare & determine the Sd. Appeale

And yor. Petrs Shall pray Etc
