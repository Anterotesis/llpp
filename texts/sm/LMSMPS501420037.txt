To the Rt Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon of Lewis Colebrand of the parish of St Mary Islington in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitr. hath lived Fourty Yeares in the said Parish & never Received any relief there from But being aged above Fourscore yeares one past his labour and having all his Goods seized on was obliedged to apply himselfe to the Churchwdens of the said parish for Relief Who Refuse to allow him any without an order from this Court

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays he may be allowed such a Pention from the said Parish of unto yor Worspps shall seeme Meete.

And Yor Petitr shall ever pray Etc Lewis Colebrand
