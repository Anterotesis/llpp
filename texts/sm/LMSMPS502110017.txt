To the Rt. Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The Humble Peticon of Anthony Buckmaster one of the petty Constables of Southmims in this County.

Sheweth That at Lady Day last past there was due and owing unto yor. Petr. the Sume of Twenty pounds for halfe a yrs Salary for his Loss of time & Expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants thro' this County

And that yor. Petr. is again sworn into the sd Office of petty Constable for the year ensuing; And well approved off by the Inhabts. of the sd Pish of S. Mims to Releive and Convey such Vagrants for the said your

Yor. Petr. there fore most humbly prays this Honble: Court to Order the paymt. of the sd Sume of Twenty pounds as due to yor. sd Petr. And that he may be Establisht in the like Salary for such Service for ye ensuing year

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Anthony Buckmaster
