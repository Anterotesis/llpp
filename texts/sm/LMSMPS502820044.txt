To the Rt Worspll. His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Richard Manley & Richard Browne Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quar unus) bearing date the13th: Day of March last Anne Wright warrant removed & conveyed from & out of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said parish of St Giles in the fields as the place of the last Legall Settlement

That the Esqr yor Petrs Conceive themselves to be Agrieved & Appeale to yor. Worsps against the Same.

Dan Bolton Yor Petrs therefore humbly pray that yor Worsps will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the said appeale

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
