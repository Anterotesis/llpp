To the Rt: worspll his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The Humble peticon of Thomas Martin one of the petty Constables of Southmims in this County

Sheweth That at Michmas last past there was due & owing unto yoe Pete the same of Ten pounds for his less of time Charge and Expences in passing and Conveying of Vagrants thro' this County

Yor Peticonr therefore most humbly prays this Honble Court would be pleased to Order the paymt. of the sd Ten pounds to yor sd petr

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc Thos: Martin
