To ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Ann Lubonisky

Sheweth

That One Susannah Nayler, Stands Indicted as of last Session for An Assault upon Yor. Petitioner And was Alsoe Bound over to Appeare the Same Sessions But She having not As yet Appeared her Recognizance is forfeited

P Tho Nash Your Pet of therefore most humbly prays your Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that the Recognizance may be forth with Extreated

And Your Petr shall ever pray Etc
