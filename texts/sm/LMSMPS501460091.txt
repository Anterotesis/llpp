To the Constable Brick al HicksHalls

Where as your Worships poor petitioner who Has Brick Beford nor this tone Sisons: and Surs in every In constable and storving Confution and Having Frains [...] to super strong constables wants & they for your Honors poor petitioner Humbly Implors your worships to took at into Consideration and grant me my discharge serving & Have no person to apaier against me: and in so doing & your worships poor Juplora the petitioner shall for Ever be Bound in July to pray for your worsh

Wallter Cannody
