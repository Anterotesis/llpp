To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Cooke

Sheweth That your Petitioner is a Householder in the Parish of South Mims and Aged Fifty Nine years and upwards Is a [---] Widower and has three Children And prays the Licence of this Court to be common Badger buyer and Seller of Corn Butter Cheese and other dead Victuals for one year

And your Petitioner shall every pray [...]

his John [x] Cooke mark

Witness Hlopps
