To the Worshipful this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Henry Trent and John Ludbey Esqrs. Two of This Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th day of December 1747 Anne Bornier; single woman, was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore Humbly Pray your Worships will Please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeale and that all Persons Concerned may then award

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray, Etc.

St. Parry, agent for St. George Hanover Squard
