To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of John Bowitt Constable of the Parish of Endfeild in the sd. County

That there is Due to Your Petr. att Midsomer lasts a Quarters Sallary for Passing and Releiving of [...] Vagrants the sume of 15th. And Your Petr. haveing Borrowed Moneys And is lyable to be troubled if not Soon Paid

P Tho: Nash He therefore Humbly prayes Your Worspps wilbe pleased to Order him the sd. 15th. and 5th More of his old Arreares. Of Your Worspps Soe thinke fitt

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

John Howett
