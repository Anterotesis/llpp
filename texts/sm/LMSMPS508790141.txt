To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of said Gregory by Saint Paul in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Addington Esquire and The Reverend Herbert Mays Doctor of Divinity two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth day of November one thousand seven hundred and Ninety two Jane Lee Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex and delivered to your Petitioners being the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul in the said City of London (the said Justices adjudging the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Lee to be in the said Parish of Saint Gregory whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggreived

Wm Chetham Solr. for the Appellants } your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said Order of Pass Warrant and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and Determination thereof.
