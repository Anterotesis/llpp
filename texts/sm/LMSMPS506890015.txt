Middx ss To the Worshipfull her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County now Sitting at Hicks Hall.

The humble Peticon of Nicholas Case and Anne his wife.

Sheweth That your Petrs. about 25 years Since they Rented a little Tenant. in the parish of Christ Church in the County of Surrey of 3d. P And where they Continued about 3 years but never paid one penny either Scott or lolt,

That ever Since your Petrs. have lived as Lodgers in the parish of St. Clements Danes in the said County and your Petrs. husband was Imployed as a Watchman in the said parish till his Labor was Ended

That your Petrs. being very antient and not able to get their bread the Parishoners under presence they were Setled in [...] Christ church parish sent them thither with a pass and from thence they were repast to St Clemts.

That the last Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for the County of Surrey a Peticon was delivered for releife as by Peticon annext appeares and order thereupon

That Since a Peticon has been delivered to Vestry of St. Clemts. for releife who referred your Petr. to appeale to this Worshipfull Court for that they would not releive them,

That your Poor Petr. being So passed & repassed from one parish to ye other as aforesd. by meanes whereof your Petr. are ready to perish in the Streets for want of a Settlemt. and Some Support oath of them being above Sixty years of Age and Lame &decripped

Your Petrs. therefore (for Gods sake) most humbly Implores your worshipps to take their Sad and helpless Condicon in to your tender and Chairtable Consideracon before whome their redress lyes, to the End they may be Settled in One parish or the other & have releife that they may not utterly parish whirl at prsent they are almost at present ready to doe

And yr Petrs. shall ever pray
