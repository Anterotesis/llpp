To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Precinct of Saint Catherine in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order of removal bearing date the Twenty Fourth day of August last Under the hands and seals of Ant Wroth and W. Booth Esqrs. two of his Majties justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Rachel Harley and her Children Namely Edward aged Eight Years EIizabeth aged Seven Years Mary aged Five Years Jane aged two years and Joanna aged ten Months was removed from the parish of Saint Luke in the County aforesaid to the said Precinct of Saint Catherines as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners find them- Selves agreived by the warrant or Order of the said two Justices and Humbly appeal against the Same

Your Peticoner therefore Humbly Pray you would be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the premises and that Your Peticoners may have Such releife therein as to Justice and Law doth appertain

And Your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc

Wm: Benham Jno: Crowther} Overseers of the poor
