To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the- County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled-

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers- of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wappin in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by a Pass Warrant or Order dated the fourteenth day of April last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Quarrill and Nathaniel Duckinfield Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) John Baldwyn and his Child named Sarah Aged about Eleven years were removed form the Parish of Saint Mary White Chappelin the said- County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wappin as Place of the last Legall Settlement of the said John Baldwyn and his said Child

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Agreived by the said Order of- the said two Justices and Appeal against the same That your Petitioners have given due notice of this Appeal to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chappell.

Thos. Cotton in Red Lyon Street Wapping Attorney for the- Appellants }

Therefore your Petitioners humbly pray this Court that they may be heard in the Premises as to their said Appeal in his

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Mattw. Dove Mattw. Arnold Wm Kickham} Churchwardens

Wm. Timbrell} Overseers of the Poor
