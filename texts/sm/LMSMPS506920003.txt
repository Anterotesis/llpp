To the Rt. Worpll. her Majestices Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of John Gauden on the behalf of Sarah his Daughter

Sheweth That Yor peticoners sd Daughter was bound- to one Alice Smith for Five years that yor- peticoners said Daughter hath been Frequently- beaten by her said Mistress & she now stands- bound to appeare this Sessions to answer yer- peticoners Complaint

Yor petitoner there fore humbly prayes that the said alice Saint he may attend this Honble Court to abide yor- Worpps Determination and he shall ever pray

John Gauden
