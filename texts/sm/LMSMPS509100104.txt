To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and R. Clarke Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace (one whereof is of the Quorum) being Date the 24th Day of April 1795 David Hastings aged about 25 Year (who was then Insane) was removed and conveyed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the said County of Middx to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioner therefore appeal against the said order and humbly pray Relief in the Premes and that the Benefit of this their said appeal may be saved unto them until the next Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in & for the said County

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Joshua Cox Sow for Appellts May 20th 1795
