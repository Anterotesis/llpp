Middx ss To the Worshipfull: her Maties Justices of the Peace att the General Quarter Sessions of the peace holden at Hicks Hall and for the Said County of Middx ye 28th day of June 1714

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Overseers of the Poore and other Parish She Inhabitants of the Parrish of Twickenham in the said County

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant or pass under the hands and and Seales of Christopher Clitherow Esqr: and James Hawley Esqr. Two of her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx John Tribbick and Mary his Wife and four Children mere Lately brought lby the Churchwardens or Overseers of the Poore of gresson in the Said County as Inhabitants Settled in our parish of Twickenham

That Your Petitioners h [...] Examined into the Matter doe Conceive and are informed that the Said Tribbick and family are noe payes Intituled to a Settlement in our Said parish of Twickenham

Wherefore Your petitioners are advised to appeale against the Said Warrant and Order and doe humbly pray this Honoble Bench will grant them an Order to the Parish where of Hesson to attend this Honoble Bench to make their defence on Such a day this Sessions as your Worrships Shall please to appoint and think fitt

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.

Timothy Child Tho: Jackson Jasper Payne

Tho: Gray Tho Morris} Overseers
