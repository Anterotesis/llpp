To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices at Hick's Hall

The humble Petition of John Gibbs

[...] eth That Your Petitioner being a poor Child inhabiting in the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the Liberty of Suffron Hill and Eley Rents was put out an Apprentice by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor for the said Parish and Liberty one Samuel Elwick of St. James's Clerkenwell Mohair Twister

That Your Petitioners said Master having defrauded Severall Parish of their money by taking poor Boys Apprentices Vizt. ten or Eleven at once and having not work for them or doth he allow them Bread to Subsist nature is (as Your Petitioner is informed) in Newgate for such frauds

May it therefore please Your Worshipps to Order Your Petitioners said Master to Attend this Court and that Your Petitioner may be releived as to Your Worshipps Shall seem most meet

And Your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc. John Gibbs

Agent P [...] er
