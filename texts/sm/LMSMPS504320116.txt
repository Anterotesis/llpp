To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order dated the 15th: day of October 1754 under the hands and Seals of John Fielding Esquire and Philip Dyot Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum Ann Dixon Wife of Richard Dixon and Mary their Child were removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields to the said Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry

By which Warrant or Order Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners as in Duty bound will pray

Leader Cox Matthew Best} Churchwardens

Isaac Cockerton John King Thos Curtis} Overseers
