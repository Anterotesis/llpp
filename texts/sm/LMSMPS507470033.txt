To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of J. Slade Esquire and Henry Lambe Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace Acting in and for the said County (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of November new last Sarah Malbrough and her three Children Namely William Malbrough Aged about Five Years Sarah Malbrough Aged about three Years and Edward Malbrough Aged about Five weeks were Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Wilsdon in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kensington as the place of their Lawfull Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc.

John Chipperfield} Church Wardens

J Plowman Wm. Butler} Overseers
