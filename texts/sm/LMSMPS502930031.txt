To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace in Sessions assembled at Hicks's Hall in St John Street in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of John Brown & Anthony Mackanella now Prisoners in the Gatehouse Westmr.

Sheweth That Your Petitioners having been Comitted to the Sd. Prison Sometime before last Sessions on Suspition of uttering a false Six pence piece and haveing Sewed the Prosecutor with notice of Tryall on this day and haveing preferred their humble Petition to your worships thereby Praying they might be brought [...] into this honoble Court to take their tryall on the Indictment Preferred against them this present Sessions and the Order thereon for that purpose being not drawn up Your Petitioners are likely to remaine in the said Goal untill the next Session unless Your Worshipps will be pleased to Order them to be brought into Court this Day

Yor. Petitioners therefore most humbly pray Yor. Worships to order that the Keeper of the sd. Goal may forthwith bring your Petitioners into this honoble Court in order to be tryed

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.
