To ye. Rt. Worshipfull this Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Oliver Lambart and John Ludbey Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr. (whereof one is of the Quor) bearing Date the Thirtyeth day of October last past Mary Lankslow Widow was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London, alledgilng the said Parish of St. Barthew the Great to be her last legall Place of Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the sd. Two Justices Do therefore appeal to yor. Worps therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses & Order yt: the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. James may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear & abide the Judment and Determinacon of yor. Worships touching this Appeal

Cha Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants }

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc
