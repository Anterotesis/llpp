To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Thomas Catt of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in ye said County

That yor. Petr. is already a Receiver of 3s. 6d P month towards his Releife

That Yor. Petr. being aged 70 yrs. and having lost his left Arm and being reduced to extream Poverty and the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish refusing to allow him any further pencon Soe that he must inevitably perish unless releived by yor. Worspps. his son being forced into her Matys Service

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that he may be allowed Such further pencon towards his mainteynance as unto yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray.
