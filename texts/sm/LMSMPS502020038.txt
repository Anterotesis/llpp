To the Right Worpll. his. Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The Humble peticon to Appeale of John Prosser of ye. parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County Tire Smith

Sheweth That Yor. Petr Hath by Warrant under the Hands & Seales of John Richardson & John Mercer Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the peaces for the said County Quarunues bearing Date the 31th day of March last been(as a Substantiall householder in the said Parish) [...] appointed nominated Overseer of the poor there for this Precent Year

Yor. Petr. being no housekeeper in the said parish humbly conceives himself to be aggrieved by the sd. Nominated and Prayes that this Court (for the said reason) will be pleased to Discharge him forthwith from the said Office

And Yor, Petr as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
