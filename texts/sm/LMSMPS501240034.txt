To ye Rt Worspll her Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of William. Scurfield of the Parish of St Andrews Holbourne In the sd County

Sheweth That Your Petr hath Lived many Yeares In the said Parish And paid all Taxes and Parish Dutys Impesed on him But your Petr (through Sicknesse and many Loses and Misfortunes In the World) being now reduced to as Poor and Lowe Condition and hawing A Wife and two Sick Children to Mainteyne And his Goods Distrained for Nonpayment of Rent Is ready to Perish for Want of Relief

P Thos Nash Your Petr therefore humbly pray [...] Your Worspps will be pleased to Grant That he may be allowed such Pension from the sd Parish As unto your Worspp Shall Seeme Meete

And yor Petr shall Ever prayer
