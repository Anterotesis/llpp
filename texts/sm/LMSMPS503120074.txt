To the Right Worshipfull Richd. Newton Esqr. and others his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Session at Hicks Hall in St: Johns Street in and for the sd. County

The humble Peticon of Percivall Haslam of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx Hosier.

Sheweth That your Petr. is retorned by the Constables for the Liberty of the Dutchy of Lancaster in the County of Middx as a Person qualifyed to serve on Jurys at Westmr. Hall

That your Petr. is neither Freeholder Copyholder or Leaseholder within the sd. Liberty of the Dutchy of Lancaster or within the sd. County of Middx and therefore your Petr. Apprehende he is not Qualifyed to serve as a Juryman according to a late Act of Parliamt. in that case made and wch your Petr. is read to make Oath of.

Percival Haslam Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays that his Name may be struck out of the Freeholders Book or the Book where in such Qualifyed person are Entred

And your Petr. shall pray
