To the right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden at Hickes Hall in the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Great Gaddesden in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by an order under the hands & Seals of Philip Dyot & Francis Bedwell Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middlesex dated the sixth day of September last past Ann Fitzpatrick the Widow of Constant Fitzpatrick deced with her two Children (vizt) Mary aged three years or there abouts and Margaret aged ten months or there abouts were removed from the parish of Saint Gyles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Great Gaddesden as th [---] last legal place of their Settlement at which place they the said Ann [...] Fitz Patrick and her Children Mary and Margaret are now ill of the small pox.

That your Petitioners humbly apprehend they are aggreived by the said Order, Do therefore appeal there from to your Worships and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Gyles in the Fields do appear before your Worships at a certain day to be appointed and to stand to and abide the Judgment & determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
