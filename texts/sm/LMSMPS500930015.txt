To the Right Worshippll. her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Mitchell of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above Barrs:

Sheweth [...] Peticonr hath been an Inhabitant [...] five yeares in wch. time he [...] [...] all Taxes and Parish Duties whatever [...] your Peticonr. is rated foure shillings a Yeare, more then he Ever yett Payd: and more then any yett Payd who lived in the sayd house before your Peticonr

Your Peticonr therefore most humbly Prayes your Worshipps to take his hardship into yor. Consideracon and be pleased to grant an Order out of this Court that he may pay no more then what he and severall others payd for Severall yeares last Past who lived in the sd house before

And yor. Peticonr. will Ever Pray Etc
