To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their adjourned Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of thePrecinct of Norwood in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of James Clitherow and Paul Naillant Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of August in the year of our Lord one thousand Seven hundred & Seventy four John Lott his Wife Ann and their four Children one named Sarah aged about Ten Years another named Mary aged about Seven years another named Elizabeth aged about four years and the other named Richard aged about five weeks were removed from the Parish of Hanwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Precinct of Norwood in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners Therefore pray this Court will appoint a day in this Session for the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeal and that all parties concerned may then Attend

J. Janes Sollicitor for the Precinct of Norwood by order of the Petitioners
