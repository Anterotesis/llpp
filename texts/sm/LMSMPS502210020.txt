To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Sessions or Sitting at Hicks's Hall in & for the said County.

The Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor [---] of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order bearing date the 21th. day of July last Christian Ogdon with her Child Anne are Removed from the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Dunstans in the West

That yor. Petrs. [---] thinking themselves thereby Agrieved Appeal to this Court against the said Order

Therefore Your Petrs. humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Parish of Kensington & the parish of St. Dunstans in the West may attend this Court some day this Session in Order that the said Appeal may be [...] heard & determined

And Your. Petr. will ever pray. Jo: Burr Jno Lewis} Churchwardens

Wm. Courcy Nicho Lambert} Overseers
