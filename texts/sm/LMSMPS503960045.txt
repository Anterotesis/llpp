To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James within the Liberty of Westmr in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and seals of Thomas Quarrill and N: dukesfield Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourteenth day of March 1748 James Harrison aged about six weeks a Bastard Child was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St James of the place of his can legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore humbly Pray your worships will Please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all Persons Concerned may the Attend

and your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc S Seddon asent for B James
