To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and Phillip Neve Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the eighth Day of April last Maria Crouch Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County, to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices, and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof, and that all Persons concerned may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

Tho. Willows James Unsworth} Churchwardens

}John Vincent Charles Bond Byfeld Jacob Welsh Overseers of the Poor
