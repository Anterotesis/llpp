To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General quarter Session of the Scace Assembled

She Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the Stands and Seals of Thomas Kynaston and Peter Planck Esquires two of him Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date she [---] Nineteenth day of September last Rose Dillon wife of Charles Dillon (gone from her) and their three Children to wit Charles aged Right years and upwards Catherine aged one year and upward and Ann aged ten Months [---] were removed and Conveyed from and cut of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Giles the paid as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the Said Rose Dillon and her three Children

Years Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray your worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determin- -ing the said appeal

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc

Chas. Harris Wm. Leech} Church wardens

Jas: Bull John Cranage} Overseers of the Poor
