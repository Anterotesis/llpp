To the Honble. Chairman And Worshipfull Magistrates of the County of Middlesex Asumby in Sessions

The Humble Petitioner Ezekiel Russell

Sheweth Your Petitioner having labors under Imprisonment for Bastardy for these Six Months and Upwards and at January Sessions last was Broughts into Court and ordered to find Bail which he assures Your Worships He is not able to Procure, but is Willing to take the Cognizars one Simsely and will Immediatly inter into the State of Matrinony, so us to present that Buther she nor Yet Her Infant shall by any Means Become troublesome to any Parish the hopes Your Worships will take these Matters Into Sircous Counderation For Which An shall allways be Bound so Pray
