To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Peticon of Miles Lee of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County aforesaid Glove colourer

Sheweth That your Peticoner is assessed One penny per Weeke to the Poors Book of the said Parish which according to the number of Quarters comes to about twenty shillings per Ann. and your Peticoner haveing a Sick Wife which hath layce Long upon his hands by which yor Peticoner is much impoverished and is in Soe poor a condition that he is not able to pay the said Rote: Therefore most humbly prays to be releived in the premises by this Honble: Court, by such ways & means as yor Worpps: in yor. grave wisdoms Shall see meete

And your Peticonr as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc

Mile Lee

Peticon of Miles Lee
