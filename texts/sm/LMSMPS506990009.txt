To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Mary Mould Abraham Crouch & Roger Wreeh of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the said County Victuallers.

Sheweth That your Peticoners and Severally Indictet for Keeping Gameing houses, And yor Peticonrs: are very Sorry for such term offence humbly begging parder and Submitting them Selves to the humble Court neither ever will offend aganis in the her times

Yor Peticonrs therefore most humbly pray your Worpps: to consider the Condition of your poor Peticoners and Familys And to grant yor Peticonrs such releife in the premises as in yor grave Wisdome Shall Seeme meete

And yor Peticoners as in Duly Count Shall ever pray Etc

Ed Smith Robert hemin Chris [...] : Priddith Constable} Church Wardens

Wee the Churchwardens Constable and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in he said County doe hereby humbly hereby that the Peticonrs above Named and very [...] Industrious presons (the but in mean Conditions. And that upon Strant Search and enquirg by as made we never found or heard of any disorders kept in their respective houses In witness whereof we have hereunto Subscribed our Named this 14th day of January 1707

James Long Alex Bowcher} Overseers
