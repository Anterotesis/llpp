To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Court of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the Session House an Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The humbled Petition and appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of The Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarill and John Staples Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty sixth Day of August last, Charlotte Seaman aged about Eleven years and William Seaman aged about. Nine Years the Children of William Seaman deceased were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet Mile End New Town as the Place of the legal Settlement of the said Charlotte Seaman and William Seaman Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

And therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjusication of the said Two Justices and the said Order And pray That your Worships will to pleased to appoint some Day in persent to session for the Hearing and Determination of the said appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
