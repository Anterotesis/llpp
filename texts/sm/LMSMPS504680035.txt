To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Paddington in the sd. County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of Francis Jackman and William Pickering Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th. day of February 1758 Charles Pruce Eleanor his wife and their Child Stephen aged about Six Years were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster to the said Parish of Paddington as the place of their last Legall Settlement

Humphy. Elmes Attorney for the Appellts. Your Petitioners therefore think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humby pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
