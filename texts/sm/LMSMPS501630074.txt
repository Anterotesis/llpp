To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Olave Silver Street in ye City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx one (being of the Quorn) bearing date the with day of May last Past Jane the Wife of Edward Powers with two Children of the said Edward Powers Vizt Isebella aged about thirteen years and Edward aged about Eight Years were removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St Olave Silver Street London alleadging the said Parish to be the place of their last legal Settlement That your Petr: conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices Do most humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

And Pray That your Worpps will order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate to Appeare before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs: shall ever pray Etc
