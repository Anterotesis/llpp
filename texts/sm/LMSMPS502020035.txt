Middx ss Ad Genal Quartial Session paris Diu Regis Tent & Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John Street in Com prd ye adjorn die Javis Scilt quinto die Aprily Anno regin Din Georgy Dui gra mmc Regis Magne Britannie xp octavo

Itt is ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County have notive of this peticon & appeal, and that they & all other psons concerned doe attend the Court on Saturday next att term of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the petr. shall not be discharged [...] of her from serving the office of Overseers of the poor of the said parish for the prsent year, and some other folt person nominated & appointed to serv the said office in his [...] said stand & place

P Cur Harcourt

The Humble Peticon of John Greenwood of Old Street Liberty in Cripplegate Middlx 1722

petr. in Coer Mr. Long Ch: wden. in Coet to att: Sat 10th

petr. to be dischd. being 74 yrs of age
