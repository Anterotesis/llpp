To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds The humble Peticon of Ann Shaw

Sheweth That One John Tibbs stands Bound for his Appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to Answer your Petr. Complaint as by the Recognizance may appeare

That your Petrs. Wittnesses not being Ready and she goeing is danger of her Life.

P Thos Nash Yor. Petr therefore most Humbly prayes your Worspps To be pleased to Grant that that the sd. John Tibbs may be Continued Bound untill the next Sessions.

And yor. Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

Ann Shaw
