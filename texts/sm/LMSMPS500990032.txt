Peticon of John Pether

Wed 10 cl

To the Rt. Worshipfull her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx:

The Humble peticon and Appeal of John Pether of the Hamlett of Hamersmith Watreman.

Sheweth That by an ordr. dated the 9th. day of June last past ordr. the hands & Seals of Phillip Howard and John Tully Esqrs. two of her Majtes: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx yor. petr. was Adjudged to be the reputed Father of a Male bastard Child borne on the body of Jane Evans on the 5th. day of June last. in the Parish of St James Westmr in this County, and yor petr is thereby ordred to pay to the Chuchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the se Parish the Sume of Eleaven Shillings Six Pence a week for the first four Weeks from the birth of the Sd Child & two Shillings & Six peace for Soe long time as the Same Shall be [...] aftwards chargeable to the Sd Parish, That yor. petr conceneing himselfe to be Aggreivd by the Adjudicacon [...] of the Sd two Justices doe humbly Appeale to yor Worships therefrom Yor petr therefore humbly pray yor. Worshipps will re Pleased to appoint a day for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St James Westmr. aforesd. to attend this Court for the Judgement thereof touching this Appeal.

And yor Petr Shall Pray Ect Jno Pether
