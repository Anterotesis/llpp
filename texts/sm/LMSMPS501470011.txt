To the Rt. Worpll. & Honble Bench of Justices

The humble petition of Alice Warner

Humbly Sheweth That your petition or was sent to New Prison, on Fry day cast on Account of assaulting one Margaret Tarrant, for the which she is Fyned Tenn pounds,

She therefore most humbly Implores the Honble Bench as the is a poore distressed [...] person having four Children to maintain, and now ready to be delivered of an other and having not where with to pay the said Fyne that you would be Mercifull pleased to alleviate or Lesson her Fyne, otherwise she must Innevilably parish for want

And she as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc 10th Sept.
