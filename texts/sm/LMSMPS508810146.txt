To their Workships the Majestrates of the County of Middlesex in Sessions assembled

The Memorial and Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Woodford in the County of Essex

Sheweth that by an Order of Removal bearing Date the Eleventh Day of January last under the hands & Seals of N. Connant Philp Neve Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County Middlesex Elizabeth White Widow of Alexander White and her three Children by her said late Husband are removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Woodford And we your Petitioners conceiving that the last legal place of the said Elizabeth and her said three Children or of any or either of them is not in the said Parish of Woodford

Wherefore your Petitioners on Behalf of themselves and the other Inhabitants of the said Parish of Woodford pray your Worships that they may be at Liberty to enter an appeal against the said order of Removal and to proceed thereon as to Law doth appertait And your petioners will ever pray Etc. Dated 15th Day of Jany 1793

John Moxon Churchwarden

Henry Cools Overseers
