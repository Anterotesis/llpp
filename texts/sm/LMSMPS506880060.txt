To the Rt: Worspll: her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx-

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey-

Sheweth

That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Edwd- of her Majties: Justices of the peace for the Said County- of Middx One Edward Pennery Son of David Pennery- Aged about Two yeares & Six Months was passed from the parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx to the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey as the place of his last Legall Settlement

J Harrer That your Peticonrs: can make it Appear to this Honble: Court that the Said Edward Pennery was never Legally Settled in the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey-

Yor: Peticonr. therefore most humbly pray [...] Worships will be pleased to Order that the said Parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate may Appear in this Honble: Court this prsent Sessions to Shew cause why the sd: Edwd: Pennery should not be repassed to the Sd: parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate

And yor: Peticonr: Shall ever pray Etc.
