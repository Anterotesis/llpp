To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew in the City of Canterbury

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth day of December last Constance Obree was removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew The said Justices thereby Adjudging that the last legal Place of Settlement of the said Constance Obree was in the said parish of Saint Andrew

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices And do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint someday in this present Session to hear this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all Parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there hear and abide the Determinations of your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray etc. Geo. Jemmett Solicitore for the Appellants
