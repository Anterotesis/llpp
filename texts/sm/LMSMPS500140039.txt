To the right Worspfull. Their Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Leonard Scott gent

Sheweth That Whereas yor. peticonr is bound by Recognizance to appear before yor Worspps. this Sessions. Yor. peticon being arrested & in Custody, cannott appeare

Wherefore yor peticonr humbly prayeth That the Recogn may bee respited till next Sessions

And yor Worps. peticon shall pray
