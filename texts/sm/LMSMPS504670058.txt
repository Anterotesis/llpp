To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Upton and William Timbrell Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th. day of December 1757 Nathaniel Stephen Dossett was removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said Liberty of Westminster to the said parish of Saint James as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same And

Therefore Pray this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal, And that all persons Concerned may then and there Attend

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

John Taber Jno Bennett Jas. Palmer} Churchwarden and Overseers of the parish of St James Westmr.
