To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices [...] the peace for the County of Middlesex at General Quarter Session of the Peace in [...] the Said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of [...] Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the [...] Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W. Blackmore Esquire and Wm. Clappeson Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the Said County of Middlesex bearing Date the 15th. day of October Instant Grace Green Wife of Henry Green then at Sea) and her three Children namely William and Nine Years or there abouts Mary aged 3 Years or thereabouts and Henry aged 16 Months otherabout were removed or conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint George in the Said County of Middlesex to the Said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the Place of their lawful Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Order and do appeal against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be appointed in this present Session to hear and Determine the Matter of the Said Appeal and that all parties con [...] may them Attend

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Jame Wegener Soll for the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping
