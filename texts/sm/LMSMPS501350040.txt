To the Honrble Bench of Justices of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Edwd Broffe Humbl

Sheweth That yr Petitioner is Charged to the Poor of Criplegate not withstanding his great Poverty among a sick and numerous family Whose industry hath prevented his being burdensome to the parish tho he hath a legal Settlement

That he may be exempted as he ever hath been is al he desires

And yr Petitioner shal ever pray [...]
