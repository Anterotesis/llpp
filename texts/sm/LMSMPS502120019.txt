To the Honoble the Chairman & the rest of his Matys Comrs. of Oyer & Terminer in & for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Walter Cahoun

Sheweth That yor. Petr. being Convicted of Extorting the sume of five Shillings & Six pence hath since obtained a [...] Release from the Pror. Bennett Herle

That Mr. Grantland the sd. Prors. Sellr. is gone from home having the sd Release in his Custody, wherey Yor. Petr. is deprived of the hene fitt of proving the Same altho' yor. Petr. friends who witnessed the due Execucon of the sd. Release now attend to prove the same

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayers yor. Honrs. to Metigate his Fine & yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

Walter Cahoun
