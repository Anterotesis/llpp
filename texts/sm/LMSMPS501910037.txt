To The Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Genll. Qr. Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon of James Walthoe late Church Warden of the parish of St. Giles without Cripple gate for the year 1719

Sheweth That your Petr. during the time of his said Office of Church Warden of the parish Aforesaid did Disburst and lay out for the use of the poor of the said parish over and above the money by him received or Collected upwards of Eighty pounds and that by reason of the great number of poor which were that year over sickly your petr. did Contract Severall other Debts for the use of the said parish amounting to upwards of Eighty five pounds for which Debts your Petr. is dayly Threatned to be arrested.

That there being a Considerable Sum of mony in the hands of the Treasurer of the Mortuary money and Mr. Champ the present Churchwarden of the said parish.

Therefore your Petr. most humbly prays this Honble. Court will be pleased to take his Case into Consideration and Relieve him as in their great Wisdom and Justice that be thought meet & Convenient

And your petr. as in Duty bound will ever pray
