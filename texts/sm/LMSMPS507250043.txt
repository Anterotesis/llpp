To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrill and John Staples Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County bearing date the Eighteenth day of February last; Philip Mullins who is insane was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Philip Mullins

Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a Day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

Robt. Tudman Sollicitor for the Appellants
