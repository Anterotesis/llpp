To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace held for the said County in October 1796 assembled

The humble Petition of James Harding of the Parish of Edmonton in the County aforesaid Vintner.

Sheweth

That last year your Worships granted a Licence for a certain House or Place for Publick Dancing, Musick or other Entertainments of the like kind called or known by the Name of the Assembly Room at the Angel in the said Parish.

That the said House or Place is now in the occupation of your Petitioner who will take the utmost care to preserve good decorum and that every thing therein shall be conducted with decency, sobriety and good manners and in strict conformity to the Act of Parliament in that behalf

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays, that your Worships would be pleased to grant him a Licence for the purpose aforesaid for the year ensuing.

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc.

James Harding
