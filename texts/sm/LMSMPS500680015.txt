To the Right honble. Sir Francis Child Lord mayor of London and the Justices of Oyer and Terminer for the County of middlesex now sitting at the Sessions house in the old [...] ayly

The humble petition of John Bellingham

your petitioner now remains committed in Newgate forging or Counter seizing exchequer notes altho ough Petitioner hopes to make his innocence appear his tryall

In order to which your petitioner humbly prays that he may be tryd or admitted to bayl according to law

And your petitioner shall ever pray Etc
