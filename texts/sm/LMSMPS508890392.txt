To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of P Colquhoun Esqr and H Reynett as [---] two of his Majestys Justices of the peace Acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third Day of October Instant William Crank alias Ellison (aged about two Years the illegitimate Son of Sarah Crank was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County of middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Crank alias Ellison

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a Day in the next Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

James May Sevr. for Appellants
