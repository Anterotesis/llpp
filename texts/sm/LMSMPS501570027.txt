To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx:

The Humble Peticon of William Gardiner Ebenezer Nickoletts Richard Castle and Thomas Lygoe late Scavengers of the High Street Precinet within the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the sd. County

Sheweth That your Petrs. were Chosen in the Office of Scavengers at Easter 1715 which office was by them faith fully Discharged.

That on the Sixth [---] of June last your Petrs. were by Warrant Called to an Account and appeared accordingly to the Auditors appointed by the said Warrant And your Petrs. did then make it appeare to the Worshipfull Justice Sewell and Justice Wickham that they had disbursed the Sume of Â£7: 18s: 11d more then they could Collect of the Inhabitants Where upon in order to Reimburse your Petrs. and Discharge the Parish of the aforesaid Debt The Parishoners were pleased to grant the Present Scavengers a Rate (sufficient as hath been customary when such Small Deficiencys has happened) But they have refused so to doe or give your Petor. any Satisfaction

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to order the present Scavengers of the aforesaid precinet to attend this Court some Day this Sessions to Shew Cause why they refuse to Reimburse your Petrs. Or that they may be otherwise Releived in the premisses As to this Court shall be thought meete.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc Wm Gardiner R: Nicolets Richd: Castle Tho: Ligo
