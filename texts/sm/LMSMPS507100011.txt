To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Brooksbank and David Walker Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty eighth Day of December last Mary Cusheroe Widow was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars and all Persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitrs: shall pray Etc

Isaac Baldwin Richd Creser} Church Wardens and

George Bryant James Tregent} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
