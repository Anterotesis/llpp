Middlesex to wit To the Worshipful Williams Mainwaring Esquire and the Rest of the Justices of the Peae Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex in Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and seals of Richard Clerk and Ann Graham Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said county Maria Jones hath been Removed From the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the aforesaid Parish of Edmonton

That your Petitioners Conceived themselves agrievd by the said Order And therefore humbly appeal against the same and pray that the your Worships will please to appoint some do in this present Sessions for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
