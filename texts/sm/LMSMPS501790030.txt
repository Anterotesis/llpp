To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of his Peace for ye. County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Petition of Elizabeth Hall of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the said County Widow

That your Petitr is allowed Eighteen Pence & Weeke as a Pention from the said Parish But forasmuch as Your Petr: being very sickly and not able to worke cannot Subsist on the Psention

Your Petitr: there fore humbly prays such an Angmentacon of her sd Pention As to this Court shall Seem meet

And Yor: Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc Eliz: Halls
