To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions at Hicks's hall Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middx

Sheweth, That by Virtue of a Pass Harrant under the hands and Seales of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs: Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing date the fifteenth day of October last, Mary Clarke Widow was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlemt: Whereby your petitioners humbly Conceive themselves to be agrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeale to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices, And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day- this present Sessions to hear & Determins the Matter touching the said Appeale

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
