To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order Dated the fifth day of April 1754 under the hands and Seals of John Upton Esquire and Samuel Thresher Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum Elizabeth Griffith Widow was removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mildred in the Poultry aforesaid

By which Warrant or Order Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premisses As to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petrs: as in Duty bound will Pray

Leader Cox Matthew Best} Churchwardens

Jones Cockerton John King Thos Curtis} Overseers
