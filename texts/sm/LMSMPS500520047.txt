To the Right Worpps Maties. Justices of the Peace

for [...] County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of Northolt in the said County

Sheweth That lately Ann Shepherd the wife of Joseph Shepherd wth two Children was [...] passed from the Pish of Acton in the said County to the said Pish of Northolt [---] by warrant under the hands & Seals of Henry Hawley Nicholas Grice & Christopher Clitherow-Esqrs three of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County thereby alleadging that the said Joseph Shepherd was last legally Settled at Northolt

Your Petnr. thinks themselves Acreived wth. the Judgment of the Son [...] Justices & do therefore Appeal to this Worll. Borns & humbly pray the ye [...] Considered that the [...] Court Counting the Appeal

P Jones Tysons

And yr. Petr. as in duty bound Shall over pray Etc
