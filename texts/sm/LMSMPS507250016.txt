To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Wm. Quarrill Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the third Day of May instant Isabella James Widow of Charles Jarvis deceased was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication & Order of the said two Justices Therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a Day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

[...] Herbt. Robertson Solr. for the Apellts.}
