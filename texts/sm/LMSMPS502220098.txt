To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the Rest of the Jutices all Hicks Hall being the Sessions of the [...] the County of Middx

The humble Petr. of Marth Battey late Wife [...]

Sheweth That your Petr. is abode threescore Age and hath allmost lost her E [...] lost the Use of one of her Arms [...] [...] been an Inhabitant in Shadwell as like her Husband a Great many years and being no [...] in a very deploreable Condition

Where fore Yor: Petr. Humbly Implores your Worships consideration and a further Ordr. that your Petr. May have her Rent paid the Churchwardens Disobeyeing yor: Worship former Ordr. and likewise humbly beggs that her Pention may be Enlarged being not able to Subsist D

And yor: Petr Shall ever pray Etc
