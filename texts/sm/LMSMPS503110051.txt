To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genl: Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Feltham in the said County

Sheweth

That by Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Edmund Elwill Bart: Grantham Andrews Esqr. St. Thomas Reynell Bart: and Brent Reynell Spiller Esqr: Four of His Mats. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx One being of the Quorum bearing Date the 8th: Day of August Instant One Daniel Becham was Removed from the Parish of East Bedfont in the said County of Middx to the said Paish of Feltham as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Daniel Becham

Yor: Petrs: thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said Justices humbly appeal therefrom

Jas Tyton Agent for ye Appellts

And Pray that this Court will please to appoint some day this Sessions for all Persons Concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determinecon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor: Petr: shall ever Pray
