To the Right Worspll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Sessions.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St George Bloom s bury in the said County.

Sheweth That by on Order under the hands & Seals of Richard Manley & Thomas Jones Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County [...] bearing date the 15th: day of July last Elizabeth Wadley the Widdow of John Wadley Deced with three Children (Vizt:) Elizabeth Wadley aged Eleaven Years, George, aged Six years and William Wadley aged One Month were removed from the parish of st George in the said County to the Said parish of St George Bloomsbury as the place of there last legall Settlement

Yor: Petr: think themselves agreved by the Judgemt: of the said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the salve & humbly pray yor Worps will be [...] per today for hearing and Determining the same

And yor: Petr: shall ever prayer

Dan Bolton
