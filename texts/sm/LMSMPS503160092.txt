To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in the General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall in and for the said County.

The humble Petitcon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals [...] John Priestly and Henry Dodson Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of April last John Heardman Potter, and Ann his Wife (who is a Lunitick) and Ann Heardman their Child Aged about three years and half a year, were Removed from the Parish of Saint John [...] Wapping in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the peace of their last legall settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves agrieved and against the said Order do Appeal.

Therefore your Petitioners humbly pray' [...] that a short day may be appointed this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners Etc.

Henry Southerll Wm. Bryan
