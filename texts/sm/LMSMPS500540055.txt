To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate within the Freedome of the City of London

That by Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County Eliz Cooke Widow late Wife of John Cooke Dent with her Foure Children (vizt.) Katherine Jane William and James Cooke was passed from the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in this County to your Petrs.

That the sd. Eliz: Cooke nor her sd. late husband was never legally Settled within the sd. Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate London Soe that your Petrs. humbly conceive themselves not lyable to mainteyne & provide for the sd Eliz Cooke & her Foure Children

P Tho: Nash.

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poore of the sd [...] Parish of St. Mary Whitechappell may appeare in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to Shew cause why the sd Eliz Cooke with her 4 Children should not here passed to the sd Parish of Mary Whitechapell

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
