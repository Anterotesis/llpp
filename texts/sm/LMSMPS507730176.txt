To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genl. Quarter Session. of the peace in and for the sd. County assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St John of Wapping in the Co: of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of Wm. Blackmore Esqr. & Peter Greene Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the sd. County (one whereof being of the Quroum) bearing date the 19th day of Septr last Martha Gray aged 5 years & upwards & Jane Gray aged 4 years or thereabouts were removed for the sd parish of St George [...] in the sd. County of Middx to the sd Parish of St. John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlemt

That yr. Petrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Order and do appeal agt. the same

Yr. Petrs. therefore humbly prays that a day may be appointed of the present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the sd. Appeal and that all parties concern'd may then attend

And yr.. Petrs. shall ever pray [...]

Tudman & Wegener Sollrs. for Appellts
