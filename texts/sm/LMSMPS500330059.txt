To the Right Worspl. their Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens of the parish of St. Katherine Cree Church London. That Anne Lloyd was by Warrant under the hands and sealed of two their Maties Justices of the pease for the said County lately passed from the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County to the sd. parish of St. Katherine Cree Church.

That the sd. Anne Lloyd never had any Legall Settlmt. in the sd. parish of St. Katherine Cree Church which yor. petrs. are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court.

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly pray yor. worsppl to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of these parish of St. Giles in the Feilds may- appeare in this Honble Court this put Quarter Sessions to shew cause why the sd. Anne Lloyd should not be repassed to the sd pish of St. Giles in the Feilds.

And yor. Petrs shall die pray etc.
