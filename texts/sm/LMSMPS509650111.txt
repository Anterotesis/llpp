To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appear of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Scott and James Herbert Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of October one thousand seven hundred and Ninety nine Charlotte Wall wife of William Wall (who is now Absent from her) and her Male Child named Thomas aged about One year and eleven Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Charlotte Wall and her said Child Thomas Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly beg leave to Appeal from the said Order and pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be at this present Session now holden for the said County and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all other concerned may be Ordered to attend: And that your Petitoners may have such Relief therein as the Court shall seem meet.

W. Tellkamp H Solicitor for Appellants

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc Etc
