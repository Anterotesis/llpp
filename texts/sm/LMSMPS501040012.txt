To the Worshipfull her Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at Hick's Hall assembled

The humble petition of Edward Hicksford on the Behalfe of his Apprentice Charles Beverly.

Sheweth That yr. petitioner Apprentice was Impress by One Lieve Middleton [...] Belonging to the East India Company and Carried to One Captaine Mac Mohan's prison in the Savoy and is there Detained as a prisoner (agt. the will of yr petitioner) in Order to be Carried to the East Indies, on the Account of the East India Company.

Yr. Petitir therefore humbly prays Releive in the prmisses

And as in Duty bound he shall ever pray etc
