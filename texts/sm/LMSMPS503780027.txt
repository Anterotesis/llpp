Middx to witt} To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition of John Whittenberry and Sarah his Wife Servant to Henry Wallbanck Keeper of Clerkenwell Bridewell

Humbly Sheweth. That your Petr. has been a Servant to the sd. Henry Wallbanck for severall Years last past as Task master and your Petrs. Wife who has always heed in the Prison and been of great Service to the Prisoners and as the place of Matron is new become Vacant by the Death of Mrs. Cavenagh, of your Worships shall think proper to appoint her to be Matron will take the utmost Care that's possible to do every thing in Regard to that Office in the most Discreet manner that any Body can do.

Therefore your Petr: humbly beggs your Worships will take it into Consideration and give such Orders as to your Worships shall seem meet

4th. June 1747 Clerkenwell Bridewell}

And your Petr: shall ever pray Etc

John Whittenberry her Sarah [x] Whittenberry Mark
