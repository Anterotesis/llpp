To the Right Worppll: her Majties Justices of the Peace in the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Edward Fletcher [...] of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County

Sheweth. Tha your Petr is rated 3s per Quarter towards the Releife of the poore of the St parish and that by reason of his poverty and Lameness in his Limbes and great losses by Seamen & nothing whar with to maintane himself & wife makes him unable to pay the sd Rate

Therefore humbly prayes yor: Worpps to discharge him from the St Assesment

And yor: Petr shall pray Etc
