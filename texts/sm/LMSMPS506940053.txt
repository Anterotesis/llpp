To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of midds.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Walter

Sheweth That for the Year last past your Petr. served the Office of Constable for the Towne of Harrow On the Hill in this County and that there being this Year noe Court Leet held and kept for the Mannor of Harrow aforesd. Your Petr. cannot be Discharged from his sd. Office

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes your worshpps to be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd Office of Constable and that the Persons hereunder Named Or One of them may for the Year Ensueing serve the If your Worspps for think & fitt

Tho: Read Jno: Armstrong

and your Petr. shall Ever pray Etc

Thomas Walter
