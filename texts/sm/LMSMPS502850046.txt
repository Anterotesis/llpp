To the Worshipfull his Majtyes. Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx assembled in Generall Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for ye sd. County.

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye Church wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye parish of St. Clemt. Danes in ye County aforesd.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrent under ye hands & Seales of two of his Majestyes Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx (Quor Unns) bearing date ye Eighteenth day of Augt. instant Catherine Dukewitts wife of Clause Dukewitts & their son Willam Aged abt. four yeares upon ye Oath of ye sd Clause Dukewitts were removed from ye Parish of St. Andrew Holborn [---] above ye Barr's in this County to ye sd Parish of st. Clement Danes as the place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs conceive themselves to be agreived.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worshipps wd. be pleased to appt. some day this prsent Sessions to hear & determine ye matter touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
