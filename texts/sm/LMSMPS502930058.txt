To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John at Hackney in the Said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by on Order of removal under the hands and Seals of Ralph Harwood and Joseph Goddard Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of April last past Mary Morris Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the same County as the Place of [...] her last legal Settlement by which order or Judgment of the said two Justices of the peace your Peticoners conceive themselves Agrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the Prmisses as to this Court Hall seem meet

And your Peticoners will Ever pray
