To the Worshippfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order bearing Date the third day of Aprill 1754 under the hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and John Fielding Esqrs. two of this Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Sarah the Wife of Joseph Jennings and John their Son Aged two Years were Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and therefore humbly Crave leave to Appeal against the Same

And your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

G. Talmash Attorney for the Appellants.
