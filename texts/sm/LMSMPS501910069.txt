To the Right Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers for the poor of the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Richard Beating & Stephen Brice Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (quor Unus) bearing date the Seaventeenth day of December 1720 Mary Elizabeth Dumay aged about 50 Yeares wife of Thomas Dumay was past on yor. Petrs. from the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the sd. County of Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the order of the sd two Justices and appeale to yor. Worpps therefrom

Yor. Petr therefore humbly pray yor Worpps to appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And Yor. Petrs shall ever pray
