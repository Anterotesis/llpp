To the Rt. Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of st. Bartholomew the Great London

Shew That by Warrt. or order rates the eighth day of August last past under the hands and Seales of Peter Lavigne & John Roydhouse Esqrs. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Libty of Westrn. in this County (Quor anns) one Sarah Vyalls wife of Nathaniel Vyalls (who was absconded) was removed from the Parish of st. Martin in the Fields in the said Libty & County to the said Parish of sd Bartholomew the great was being the place where the said Nathaniel Vyalls was last legally settled.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt. or ordr. of the sd. Justices. & humbly appale agt. the same to this Honble Court.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to ordr. the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the sd. Parish of st. Martin in the Fields & all other p [...] conversed to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court upon & Touching the sd. Appeale, and that yor. Peticoner. may have such relief here in as to yor. Wopps shall seem meet [...]

And yr Peticonrs. shall pray Etc

She is sim delivers a male Child Baptized Nathl. in st. Barthlow Pish
