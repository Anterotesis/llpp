To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

Most humbly Sheweth The humble Peticon of Thomas Leonard.

That one Dennis Davison did the latter end of Febry last robb yor. Petr. of divers goods of Value.

That Yor. Petr. last Thursday by good Fortune did take the sd Davison and the Worspll. Justice Evans [---] was pleased to Comitt him to New prison.

That since his said Consinemt. Yor. petr. his found severall goods wch. the sd Davison did pawn at the Brokers.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that he may not be discharged this day by Reason Yor. Petr. is inform's of a great many more goods wch. he hopes to find out before the next Sessions Then he designes to prosecute the sd Davison

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Thos. a Mke of Thomas Leonard
