To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseer of the poore of the the Pish of Ealing in the sd County.

That upon a former Order of this Court this Honble Bench was pleased to Order that Charles Weaver and Judith his wife should be mainteyned & provided for at the charge of the sd. parish.

That upon yet Petr. appeale to this Houble Cort as of this prsent Sessions yor petr. are there upon discharged from provideding for the sd. weaver and his wife. That yor. petr. have disbursed the sum of Â£8.0s.0d and upward in releiving the sd weaver and his Wife and otherwise about the sd matter which was occaconed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Pish of Kensington who procured the sd. Weaver and his Wife to be passed from theme to yor. petr. whereby yor. petr. are much appreived

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Ovrseers of the poore of the parish of Kensington [...] Kensington may retinburse y [...] petr. the sd. moneys soe by them disbursed as aforesd

And yor. petrs. shall ever pray etc.
