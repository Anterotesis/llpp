To the Right Worspl his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Genall quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon of Francis Oliver and John Taylor Constables of the Liberty of Glass house yard in the Said County

Sheweth That your Petr. Francis Oliver dureing the Office of Constable passed Severall Vagrants through this County to the Towne of Chipping Barnett in the County of Hertford which he hath been allowed by Severall of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County the Sume of £2.9s.6d.

That your Petr John Taylor dureing the Office of Constable passed Severall Vagrants through this County to the Said Towne of Chipping Barnett which he hath been allowed by Sewall of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County the Sume of £0.16s.6d as by Severall Certificates will appeare

Therefore your Petr. prays your Worspr to [...] releive your Petrs in the premisses.

And your Petrs Shall pray Etc.

Francis Oliver John Taylor
