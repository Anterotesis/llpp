To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in [---] Generall Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Trinity Minories in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrt or Order bearing Date the Twenty Fifth day of July last under the hands and Seals of Clifford William Phillips and Richard Former Esqs. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) James Herring and James aged Eight Years and John aged four Years the Children and Lowfull Issue of the said James Herring by Martha his lase wife Deccased was removed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chappell in the sd. County to the parish of Trinity Minories as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners find themselves agreived by the Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices and Humbly appeals against the same

G: Midel Altorn for the Applls. Your Peticoners therefore Humbly pray you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of White Chappell and all other persent Concerned to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the premises And that Your Peticoners may have Such releife there in as to Justices & Law doth Appertain:

And your Peticoners as in Duty bound Shall pray Etc.
