To the Right Worll: Her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of William Meres Esqr

Sheweth

That by an Order dated the Sixteenth day of May last past under the hands & seals of Francis Negus Esqr and Peter Lavigne Esqr two of her Masties Justices of the peace for the said County yor peticonr. is adjudged the reputed father of a Male Bastard Child born of the body of Martha Griffis singlewomman [...] in the parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the said County

J Rolfe

Yor: peticonr: being agrieved [...] by the adjudication of the said Two Justices humbly Appeales to this Worll: Bench & Prayes (the premises considered) that the Churchwardrens & Overseers of the poor of said parish of St Martin in the Feild & all other persons concerned herein way be Ordered to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to here & abide the Judgment & finall determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Peticonr. as in duly bound shall pray Etc
