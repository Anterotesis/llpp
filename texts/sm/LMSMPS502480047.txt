To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Sessions

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hillington in the said County

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seales of John Weedon and William Bowyer Esqrs. two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor nuns) bearing date the Seventh day of November last past John Nicholls and Sarah his wife with John Nicholls Thomas Nicholls and Sarah Nicholls their Children were removed from the Town of Uxbridge in the said County to the said Parish of Hillington as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order appeale to this Court against the same

And humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to Appoint a day this present Sessions for hearing and determining the said Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
