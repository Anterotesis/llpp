Middx To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices now Sitting at Hickses Hall

The Humble Peticon of William Champ long of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn:

Sheweth That your Peticoner being Indicted And A Bill found Against him in this Honble: Court; But Since he has Agreed and made an end with his Peitt And Generall Releases Drawer between them; But Your Peticoner being dangerously Sick Soe that he is not Able to come Abroad And Draw his Recognizance as is to be Testifyed by his Surgeon Mr Hambleton who is here, Therefore Your Peticoner humbly Desires A Respite may be Granted him till next Sessions:

And Yor. Peticonr: as in Duty bound Shall over pray Etc
