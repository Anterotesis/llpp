To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices [...] the peace for the County of Middlesex In their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London.

That by Virtue of all Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Richd. Ricard Esqr. & N: Dukinfield Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the County of Middx (one Whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the 22d. Day of March 1747 Mary Colvert (in the said Order Called) Widow of Thomas Colvert Deceased formerly Wife of Thos. Jones Deceased And her Daughter Ann Colvert by her said late Husband Thomas Colvert Were Removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Alhallows on the Wall London aforesaid Alledging the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Colvert and Ann Colvert to be there and Your Petr. Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly pray Your Worships

Burr Attorney for [...] llows Parish} Would Appoint some day this present General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County to hear this Appeal and that your Worships Will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel and All Persons Concerned In and about the premises may then Attend hear and Abide the Final Determination of your Worships

And Yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
