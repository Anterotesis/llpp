To the Rt. Woppfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw in the City of London.

Sheweth, That by Warrant or Order dated the tenth day of January last under the hands and Seals of John Fuller and Richd. Browne Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor nuns) Katherine Day widow was removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County to yor. Peticonrs. said Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or ordr. of the said two Justices, and humbly Appeale agat. the same to this Court.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to ordr. the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Sepulchers and all other persons concerned to attend this Court some day this Prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the sd. Appeale And yt. yor. Peticonrs. may have such relief herin as to yor. Wopps shall seem meet Etc.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
