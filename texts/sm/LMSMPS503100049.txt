To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General at Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Ri: Farmer C: W: Phillipps Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the 12th. Day of June 1735 Susannah Hodges Spinster was removed from the parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. James as the place of her last legal Settlemt. Whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

And Therefore your Petrs humbly Prays that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray

Saml. Seddow Verstry Clerk of St. James's
