To the right worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the Fifteenth day of January last under the hands and Seals of Richd. Richard Esqr. and Jr. Sam Gower Knt. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Elizabeth Tennant aged about eight years the Daughter and Lawful Issue of George Tennant deced was removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in this County to the Said Parish of St. Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner's conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Order of the Said two Justices do appeal against the Same

And humbly Pray that this Court will be Pleased to appoint a Day this Present Session for all Parties to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And the Petrs will pray Etc

G: Talmash attorney for the Appellants
