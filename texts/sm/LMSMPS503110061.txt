To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the Said County

Sheweth That by warrant or Pass under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majasties Justices of ye Peace for the Said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Dated the Seventh Day of July Last that Cathrine atkins aged one year & Elizabeth the Mother wife of Wm. atkins was Passed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields to the Parish of Christ Church as their Last Place of Legall Settlement

Whereford Your Pettionrs humbly Concoveing themselves aggrieved by the sd. order or Pass Warrant and Judgment of the Said Two Justices Therefore humbly Appeale to this Court for Releife in the Promisses and Pray a Day for the hearing & Determing the Sam

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc

Robt. Day Soll, by order of the Said Church Wardens & overseers
