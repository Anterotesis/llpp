To the Right Honble. the Lord Majr: and to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble Bench

The humble Peticon of Sarah Walker a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That your peticoner: is consined for picking the Pockett of one Edward Booth 10th your peticonr: [...] nod wayes guilty of But hath her Wittnesses Ready to provd her innocency

Yor: Peticoner: therefore most humbly prayes that she may be tryed at prsent Sessions and that her prayer may be entered accordingly as to yor. Lordpps shall seem under Etc.

And yor peticoner: shall pray Etc.
