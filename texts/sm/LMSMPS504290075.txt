To the Rt Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. GeorgeHanover Square within the Liberty of westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and J Fielding Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14 day of May 1754 Jane Smith an Infant aged three years and upwards the true and lawfull Issue of William Smith by Jane his wife was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Field within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Smith Whereby your Petitiones think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determinning the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Jno Newton Asent for the Appealts.
