To the Dr. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble peticon of Thomas Young of the pish of St. Martins in the Feilds in the sd. County.

That yor. Petr. hath Served as Cooke of her Matys Yatcht the Fubbs for the peace of 14 Years under the Comand of Capt Richard Biron and in other [...] Shipps of warr and behaved himselfe very Soberly and Obedient to Comand But on the 19th. June [...] last being Deprived of the use of his Right Hand was for that reason Discharged from his sd Imploymt. As by a Certificate under the Hand of the sd. Capt Biron hereunto annexed appear and forasmuch as your Petr. being now Rendred wholly uncapeable of any Imploymt. and being noe Penconer of the Chest at Chatham n haveing a Family to mainteyne.

Wee whose names are Subscribed doe beleive the contents of this peticon to be true & doe recomend the peticonr of a fitt object of yor. Worpps charity: Tho. Yates Curate

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor Worspps to be pleased to Order him this County Pencon for the Support of himselfe & Family

7 o Apr 1703

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc

Ed: Chapman Church Warne Richd Prestege
