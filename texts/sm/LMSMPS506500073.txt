To the Worshipful Justices now Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by and Order under the Hands and Seals of George Wright and Thomas Edwards Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of January last Sarah Cheere Widow of Matthew Cheere deceased [---] and their three Children to wit Eleanor aged Six Years Sarah Aged four Years and Mary Aged two Years were removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surrey as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order humbly Appeal against the same And Pray such Relief as to Your [---] Worships shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc Wm. Drew Sollr for Appellts
