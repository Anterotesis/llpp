To Ye. Rt. Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx I Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of John Wilson in the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That yor peticon is rated to the poors and Scavengers rates of the Said parish for Twenty five pounds pr. Ann for the house he lives in altho': he Rents but Twenty pounds pr. Ann

Yor. Peticon therefore thinks himself agreeved by the Said Assessment and Appeales against the Same

Yor. peticon therefore humbly prays for Such releife as Your Worshipps shall See meet

And Yor. Peticon shall ever pray Etc
