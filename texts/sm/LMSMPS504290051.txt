To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and George Errington Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the sixth day of this instant July Margaret Connolly aged about two Years in the said Order mentioned to be the lawfull Issue of John Connolly by Mary his Wife both absconded was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the place of her last legal Settlement

The Pets. think themselves aggrieved by the said Order & therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same & Pray Your Worships to grant them such Relief in the Premisses as shall be thought meet

And the Petrs. will ever Pray Etc:

G: Talmash Attorney for the Appellants.
