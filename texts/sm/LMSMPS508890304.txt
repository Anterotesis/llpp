To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows the Great in the City of London.

Sheweth That under and in Pursuance of an Order made by two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex under their Hands and Seals bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of July now last past Ann Leech Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the Said County to the said Parish of Allhallows the Great as the Place of her last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said order and humbly pray that your Honours will be pleased to appoint a Day during the present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

John Jackson Wm Walduck} Churchwardens

M Johnson W Harwood } Overseers.
