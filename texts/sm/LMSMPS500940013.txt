To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Jones

Sheweth That Sometime Past yor Pet. was comitted to the House of Correccon in this County upon Complaint [...] of his master and that the matters upon which Such Complaint was made being Determined and your Petr. haveing Entred himselfe On Board her Matys Shipp Royale Soveraigne On Board which Shipp he is this Night Ordered to Repaire.

Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from his sd. Imprisonmt

P Tho Nash.

And yor Petr. Shall Ever pray etc
