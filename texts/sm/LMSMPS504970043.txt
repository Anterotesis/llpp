Stanes Division in Spelthorne Hundred in the County of Midlx:} To The Rt. Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of James Froud one of the High Constables of the said Division

Sheweth That Your Petitioner hath served the Office of High Constable for the said Division upwards of two Years, and That he hath cleared and settled all his Accounts with Mr. Higgs the Treasuer and is desirous to be Discharged from the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that he may be Discharged from his said Office and he doth return Thomas Pott of the Parish of Stanwell Farmer and John Fitzwater of the Parish of Ashford Farmer to serve the said Office in his Stead; And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray etc. .

James Froud

Midlx: We his Majestys Justices of the Peace Residing in and Acting for the said Division Do Approve off and allow of this Petition P Musgrave Thomas Wood Vanst: Hudson
