To the Rt. Worpsll his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Quartr: Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westmr

Sheweth

That Susannah Sparrow als Passaroe was by Order dated the 20th: day of Decembr: last, under the Hands and Seals of Richard Bealing & Brill Esqrs: Two of his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the Sd County of Midd (Quor Unus) removed from the Parish of St. Pauls Covent Garden in the Sd County to the aforesd Parish of St. Ann.

Your Petiors: humbly concerves them Selves agrieved thereby. And appeals from the Judgment of the Sd, Justices to the Judgement of this honble: Court and crave a day of hearing. And that the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Sd Parish of [...] St: Pauls Covent Garden may then attend, So that the sd Susannah Sparrow als Passaroe may be So Setled as this Honble: Court upon hearing may think Fit.

And Your Petr: Shall ever Pray Etc
