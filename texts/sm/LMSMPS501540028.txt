To the Right Worpll. his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Giles Quinny Governour of the House of Correccon in the said County.

Humbly Sheweth. That at Midsummer last there was due unto yor. Peticonr the Sume of Thirty pounds for the last yearly payment of Sixty pounds Sallary allowed him by this Honoble. Court

Your Peticonr therefore humly prays yor Worpps will be pleased to order the said halfe yearly payment of thirty pounds due att Midsumer last to be paid to your Peticonr as to your Worpps shall seem meet

And yor Peticoner as in duty bound shall pray Etc.

Giles Quinny
