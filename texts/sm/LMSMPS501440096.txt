To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeale the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Harrow in the said County.

Sheweth

That by Order Dated the Seaventh Day of February Anne Dom 1714 under the Hands and Seales of John Metcalfe & N: Boothe Esqrs: two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor nuns) Sarah Moore a Bastard Child was removed from the parish of St Andrews Holbourne above Barrs in the said County to the said Parish of Harrow as the place of the Legall Settlement of the said Sarah Moore

That your Petitrs: conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order of the said two Justices and appeal against the same

Yor Petitrs: therefore humbly pray yor. Worspps will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Andrews Holbourne above Barrs to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And Yor. Petitr. shall Ever pray Etc.
