To the Right Worpfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Hesset in the County of Suffolt.

Sheweth

That by warrant or order dated the thirteenth day of April last under the hands & Seals of Samll. Jones & Philip Wilshire Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middlx one Anne Pett Widdow of James Pett deced & their two Children Elizabeth aged about three years & an halfe and Joseph aged about eighteen months which were become chargeable to the hamblett of Mile end old Towne in the Parish of Stepney in the Said County of Middlx (as was alledged in the Said order) were removed & conveyed from the said Hamblett to the Said Parish of Hesset (by the name of the Parish of Haset) in the Said County of Suffolk, as the place of the last legal Settlemt. of the Said James Pett, his Said wife & Children

That your Peticoners find themselves aggreived by the Said order of removeall & appeal to this Court against the Same

Jor Rolfe Yor. Peticoners & appellants liveing at a great Distance & not being ready with their witnesses to have the Said Appeal heard & determined this prsent Sessions humbly Pray that this their Appeal may be now reced by the Court & the hearing & determineing thereof may be adjorned to Such day of the next genal quarter Session of the peace to be held for this County as the Court Shall think fitt to appoint & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said Hamblett of Milend Old Towne & all other P fons concerned may be ordered to attend this Court accordingly to hear & abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court upon & touching the Said appeal & that your peticoners may be releived herein according to Law & Justice

And yor. Peticoners Shall Pray Etc
