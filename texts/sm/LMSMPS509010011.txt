To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session Assembled.

The humble petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Cranley Thomas Herby and James Pettit Andrews Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being for the Quorum) bearing date the first day of this instant September Jane Hampstead Widow of Charles Hampstead deceased and her Child Jane aged two Years and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint John the Evangelist in the City of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

They therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication and order of the said two Justices and pray that your worships will appoint a day in the present Session for hearing and determining thereof and that all persons concerned therein may have notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

Willm. Hopkins Wm McNewan Josh. Preedy John Hubbald
