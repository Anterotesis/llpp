To the Rt: Wosppfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate in ye. sd. County

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and seals of Jo: Colbatch & Wm: Gore Esqrs: two of his Majtes. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Midlx (Quor un.) bearing date the 6th. day of Febry. inst: Jane Fitz Gerald Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in ye fields in ye. sd. County to our sd. Parish of sd. Botolph without aldgate as to ye. place of her last Legal Settlemt.

That yr. Petr. think themselves agriev'd by ye. sd. Order and humbly appeal to this Honble Court agt: ye. same.

And pray yt. yr. Worsps would be pleased to Ordr. that ye. Church -wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Martin in ye. fields and all other persons concerned may attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear & abide ye. Judgemt. & determinacon of this Court touching ye. sd. appeal

And yr. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
