To the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, new Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth, in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the Second day of June last under the Hands and Seals of Nicholas Bond, and Samuel Tolpey Esquires, two of his and Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex, Frances Baker Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields, in the said County of Middlesex, to the said Parish of Lambeth said to the her last Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceiving the said Frances Baker to have no legal Settlement in the said Parish of Lambeth;

Humbly Pray that an appeal against the said Order may be entered: and that the same be respited, until the next Sessions to the holden in and for the said County of Middlesex
