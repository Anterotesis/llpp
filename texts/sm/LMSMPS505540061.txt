The Worshipful His. Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex [...] general Session of the peace holden at Hick's Hall in Saint John street and for the County of Middlesex assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Old Street in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of [...] John Fielding Knight and John Spinnage Esqr. 2 of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex afore said (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th day of November 1765 Catherine Mullaly Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Strand in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Luke as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Justices of the peace Do therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same end pray that this Court will appoint a Day in this present general Session of the Peace for the hearing and determining of the said. Appeal and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Strand and all other Persons Concerned may then attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

I Jenings Attorney for the Apellants
