To the right Woppfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or Order dated the 19th. day of February last past under the hands and Seals of Felix Feast and John Pery Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorn unns) one Matthew Taylor and Kinsbury his wife were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County unto the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of last legal settlement of the said Matthew Taylor.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or Order of the said Two Justices and humbly appeale against the same to this Court

Yor: Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray yor. wopps will be pleased to Ordr. the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the sd. County and all other persons concerned to attend this Court some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court upon & touching the said Appeale, and that yor. Peticonrs. may have such relief herein as to yer. Wopps shall seem meet Etc.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
