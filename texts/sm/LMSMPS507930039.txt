To the Worshipful: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the Now Session House Clerkenwell Green Assembled.

The Hamble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Gilbert and Thomas Gordon Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace Acting in and for the City of and Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of February one thousand Seven hundred and Eighty five Thomas Bert was removed from the Liberty of the Rolls within the said County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all persons concerned any be then directed to attend

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd Christmas Jno French Jas Sidgwick} Churchwardens

A Chaplin Gould} Overseers of the Poor
