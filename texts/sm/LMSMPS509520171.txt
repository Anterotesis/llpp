To The Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples Esqr. and Rupert Clarke Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Third day of August last Margaret King Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order do humbly appeal against the same, And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions that all Parties Concerned may attend to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
