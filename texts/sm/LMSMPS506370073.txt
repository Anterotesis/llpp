To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of D: Wilmot Esquire and Burford Camper Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. Day of November 1773 Two Children one of them of the Age of Nine Years or thereabouts called by the Name of Richard Young and the other of the age of Seven Years or thereabouts called by the name of Mary Young Were removed and conveyed from the Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jno Coverly Sollr. for the Appellants
