To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in the general Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition of James Willder of Lothbury London Attorney at Law

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath ever since Lady Day last attended the Gentlemen appointed by Order of The Lords of This Majesties most Honourable Privy Council to take care of the distempered Cattle in the Said County of Middlesex, as their Clerk and hath ever since done all the Business relating thereto

That your Petitioner hath received no Reward or Satisfaction for Such his Trouble and Attendance; An Account whereof your Petitioner hath delivered to the Gentlemen appointed by the Privy Council as aforesaid

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to take the same into your Consideration and to allow him such Relief in the Premisses as to Your Worships shall think proper

And your Petitioner shall ever pray James Willder
