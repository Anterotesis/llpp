To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petitioner and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margaret Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hand and Seals of Nicholas Jeffreys and Stephen Beckingham Esq two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Searing Date of 13th. day of February 1746 Mary Roots Widow of Thos. Roots Deced was Removed form the parish of St Andrew Holbourn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St, Margaret Westmr. as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petrs. conceive themselves to be aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petits. therefore Pray this Court will Place to appoint a time for hearing and Determining this said Appeal and that all Persons concerned do them attend.

And Your Petitrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Middx. to wit At the Genl Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King held by adjornment in & for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the County aforesd. on [---] Tuesday the 28th. day of April in the 20th. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second of Great Britain King Etc:

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn above the in this County have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all other Persons do attend this Court on Friday next as ten of Clock in the forenoon to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court

Walter
