To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Phillipp Holden of the Parish of Kensington in the sd. County

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. was Elected by the Court Leet One of the Headburroughs of the sd. Parish for the Manner of Abotts Kensington att Easter 1704 and that since there hath been noe Court Leet held for the sd. Mannor.

That the Vestry of the sd. pish have Returned One Thomas Elver to Serve the sd. Office of Headburrough for the sd. Mannor.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd. Office of Head burrough and that the sd. Thomas Erver may be Sworne in his Roome

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

Phillip Holden

P Tho: Nash
