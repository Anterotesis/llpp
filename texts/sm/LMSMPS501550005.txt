To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Anne Collins Daughter of Thomas Collins

Sheweth

That your Peticoner by her Indenture of Apprenticehood Dated the Twenty Seaventh Day of February last became an Apprentice to one Robert Marsh of Stepny Dyer for the terme of Seaven Yeares comencing from the Date thereof As thereby may Appear

That your Peticoners Masters hath been Gone from his Habitacon for above these two Months. past and where your Peticoner doth not know and Your Peticoners Mistresse Phillis Marsh is now in Clerkenwell Bridewell on Suspicon of Theft soe yt your Peticoner is taken noe manner of Care on and wth is likely to tend to her utter Ruine

Yor: Petr therefore humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to order the may be Discharged from her Apprenticehood to the said Robert Marsh

And Yor: Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

Anne Collins
