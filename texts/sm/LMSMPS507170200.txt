To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in general Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty fourth Day of August last Ann Holding was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly, Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to Your petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next general Session of the peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex

And Your Petitrs: shall pray Etc

Thos Mooke John Dorman} Chuchwardens and

Jos. Fisher Adam Dunford} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
