To the Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx.

The Humble Petition of William Garrett Constable of the Down of Hamsted Lee in the said County

Sheweth

That [...] there was a Watchouse Lately built in the said Down which Stands very conveinent for that purpose being very near the Round homes But Your Petitionr. cannot enjoy any Bonefitt of the Paine by reason Some of the Inhabitations of the lower Order of the said Parish are unwilling to Pay an equall proporcon of the charge of building the Same

Your Session there fore most humbly prays that yor. Worships will be pleased to Order that an equall rate or Assessment May be Made and Leoyed on all the Inhabitant of the said- parish of Hamsted to defray the charges of Building the said Watchhouse

And Yor. Peticonr. Shall Over Pray Etc.

William Garrett
