To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Green of the Precint of St. Catherine in the said County

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Dennett and Richard Ricards Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd: County one being of the Quorum bearing Date the 13th. day of January 1734 Yor. Petr. Stands Adjudged the Father of a Female Bastard Child born of the body of Sarah Bills Widow in the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

That yor: Petr. humbly Conceives himself agreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and Prays that a day may be appointed in this present General Quarter Sessions for Vacating the said Order and that all persons Concerned may be Ordered to Attend

And yor. Petr: as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc.

[...] Thos. Green
