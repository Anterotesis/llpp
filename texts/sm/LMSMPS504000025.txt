To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace in their Quarter Session of the peace holden at Hicks Hall in & for the County of Middx Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Dionis Back Church in the City of London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & seals of Henry Norris & John London Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the eleventh day of January 1749 William Mallett Stevenson Ann his Wife & their two Children Ann aged about two years & Charles about three Months were sent and delivered by the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Mile end Old Town in the said County of Middx unto the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Dionis Back Church in London aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby humbly appeal against the same and pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjorned until the next Quarter Session of the peace to be held for the said County

And your petitioners will ever pray Etc
