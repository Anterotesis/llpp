To the Right Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of Ewelme in the County of Oxford

Sheweth

That by a Passe Warrant bearing date the Nine and twentieth day of October last Under the hands and Seales of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlx (Quorum Unus) Amy Button Widdow of Thomas Button deceased was removed from the parish of Hornsey in the Said County of Midlx to the Said parish of Ewelme as the place of the Said Thomas Button. last legall Settlement

Your Peticoner's finde themselves Aggrieved by the Indgment of the Said two Justices Doe appeale to this Court and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Said Parish of Hornsey and all other person's concerned may be Ordered to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching this appeal

And Your Peticoner's us in duty bound Shall pray Etc
