Honoured Gentelmen

your Humble Petisinor being ye poore Mother of be wicked and ungodly Child, who by her Vicious and wicked Cources hath gott the foule distemper to a high deegree, and I beeing verey Poore and not Able to helpe her Made help my Address to ye Churchwarnds off St. Pulchers, who tell me planley they wil not Truble themselves to put her Into ye Hospitall Humbly begg upon would bee graciously, Pleased to take it into Consideration that my Child may not perrich for which In duty shall be bound to Pray Etc.

Yours Ann James
