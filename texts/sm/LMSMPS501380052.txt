To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Robert Parkin of the Parish of St Gyles without Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Rated towards the Releife of the Poor of the Sd Parish Eighteene Shllings of quarter & then hath beene fewer quarters already & beleives them will bee at Least on quarter more Aganist which Rate he humbly appeales to this Court; And your Peticon further Sheweth that his house he Lives in is 8h P Ann & that he drives noe Traid & hath noe Psonall Estate Save house held goods & money to Supply her necessityes

And Prays such Releife in the Prmisses as to your Worshipps shall seeme meet

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
