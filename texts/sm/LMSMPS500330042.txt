To the Right Worspll. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of George Jervis of the parish of St. Mary Islington in the said County.

Sheweth.

That your Peticoner is elected to serve the office of Constable for the said parish for the yeare ensueing And that Joseph Foreman hath lived in the said parish longer then your Peticoner Soe that of right, & by ancient Custome he ought to serve the said office of Constable before your Peticoner.

confirmd. Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly prays this Honble Court to be pleased to Order that he may be discharged from serving the said office & that the said Joseph Foreman may be elected to serve the said office of Constable.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray etc.
