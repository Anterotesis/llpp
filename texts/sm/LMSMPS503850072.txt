To the Right Honoble Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Majesties Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx at Justice Hall in the Old Baily London.

The Petition of Richard Morris.

Most hbly Sheweth That your Petitioner on the Eleventh day of this Instant April was committed by Nathaniel Duckin field Esqr. one of his Majesties Justice of the Peace for the said County of Middx to the House of Correction at Clerkenwell He being charged on the Oath of One Samuel Unwin for threatning him whereby he went in danger of his Life or some Bodily harm he would do or cause to be done unto him

That your Petitioner was by a Warrant under the Hand of the said Justice bearing date the same day detained in the said Prison he being also charged on the Oath of the said Saml. Unwin as standing Indicted in London for Felony to which your Petr: had not appeared or taken his Tryal as appeared to the said Justice by virtue of a Warrant under the Hand & Seal of the said Sir Robert Ladbroke

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays your Lordshipps that he may be either Tryed Bailed or discharged this present Session for the said Offence pursuant to the direction of the Habeas Corpus Act

And your Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc Richard Maurice
