To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Howett Constable of Endfield in the said County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary due to your Petitr for Passing and relieving of vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen Pounds

That yor Petitr hath been at great charge in Conveying Vagrants & Stands in great need of his said Moneys.

Yor. Petr therefore humbly Prays he may be paid his sd: Quarters Sallary.

And Yor. Petr Shall Ever Pray etc.

J: Howett
