[...] Rt Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace [...] County of Midds in the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Margaretts Pattons in London

Sheweth

[...] of a warrant under the hands & Seales of twoo of his Maties. [...] the Peace of this County (one being of the Quor) dated the Fifth day [...] instant one Henry English with his wife and Child by the Churchwardens [...] of the Poore of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepney [...] were brought to your Petrs. to be pvided for upon the Oath of the said [...] That hee was last legally settled in the Parish of St. Margarett [...] as a Covenant servant by the assignemt. of an Indenture of [...] the same Parish Cord wey nor and lived with him [...] he was in truth about Tenn yeares past the said Henry English was [...] Appntice with one Stanly Johnson in the Parish of St. Paull Shadwell [...] lived about First yeares And then the said Stanly Johnson owing some money [...] Stretton The said Henry English by an agreement betweene them came [...] Stretton to worke with him with whom hee continued about nine moneths [...] And being now runne away & his wife lately decd The child named Mary [...] left wth. yor. Petrs. to be pvided for where the said Henry English her Father had

Therefore yor. Petrs. Pray that the Churchwardens of the said Parish & Hamblett may be ordered to appear before you & that you will be Pleased to hear them all in the prmisses And to Quash the said Warrt. And send the Child named Mary - English to the Churchwardens of the said Parish & Hamblett to be pvided for untill They can Free themselves of the charge thereof by due course of Law

And They shall Prayer
