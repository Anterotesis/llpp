To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for Ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Harford.

Sheweth That There is due to ye Petr for Wages homone Nicholas Copping of Whiliffe Crosse in this County Woodmonger The Sum of Â£5:13s:05d which the said Mr. Copping refuseing to pay the Stands Bound for his Appearance in this Honourable Court this present Sessions And forasmuch as yor Petr being a Poor Man & haveing a Family to Mainteyne Stands in Great Want of his said Wages

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to Order that he may be paid his said Wages as unto ye Worshipps shall Seeme meete

And Yor. Petr shall ever pray Etc Jos Harford [x] his Mke
