To the Right worshipfull his majesties Justices of the peace for the County of middlesex at then General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esquires two of his majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Qurum) bearing Date the thirteenth day of march last past [...] William Camden and Jane his wife were removed from the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement

By which said Order and Judgment of the [---] said two Justices your petitioners conceive themselves agrieved and humbly appeale to this court against the same praying such Relief i the premises as to your worships shall seem meet

And yr petitioners will Ever pray

Sand Sadlen agent for the said Churchwarden and Overseers
