To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kington upon Thames in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of James Fraser and Edward Ashe Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th day of November 1750 Margaret Goodburn Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kingston in the County of Surry as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved and against the said order do Appeal

Charles Berry Attorney for the Appellants Your petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal And that all parties Concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc.
