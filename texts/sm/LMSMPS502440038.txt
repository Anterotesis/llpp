To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Dee of the Liberty of Holywell Street in the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your Peticoner being (at a Vestry held for the said Parish) Chose one of the Said Scavengers for the Said Liberty and being a Person in Years and Cann Neither write or Read and his wife not being a Person Capable in his absence to look after the Shop by reason of some Afflictions which has fallen upon her and taken away her understanding and Sences that she Cann Neither buy or Sell soe that it will be to Continue in this Place a great detriment to your Peticoner in his trade and business & Livelyhood

Therefore your Peticoner humbly Prays (for the reasons above Menconed that Yor. Worshipps would discharge him from the Office of Scavenger and Order the Inhabitants to Chewse an other Person in the Place of Yor. Peticoner

And Yor Peticoner as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray
