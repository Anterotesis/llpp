To the Rt. Worppll his Matys Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West London

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order dated the twenty first day of July last Under the Hand & Seals of Simon Parry & Jno. Mercer Esqrs. two of his Matys Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) Richd. Jones was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the Sd. County to ye sd. Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London As the Place of ye Last Legall Settlemt. of ye Sd. Richd. Jones.

That Your Petr. Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the sd. Order of Ye sd. two Justices & Appeale against the Same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleas'd to Appoint a Day this Sessions for all Parties to Attend to hear & abide the Judgemt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal.

And Yor Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
