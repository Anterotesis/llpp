To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, in their General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ= Church, in ye. City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of George Howard and Richard Manley Esqr Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirty first Day of October now Last past, Elizabeth Burnett Widow, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Margaret, in the City of Westmr. unto the said Parish of Christchurch London, Alledging the said Parish of Christchurch, to be the Place of her last legal Settlemt.

Thos Baynbridge Agent [...] Appellants } That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to yor. Worps therefrom; & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the Prernisses, & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Margaret, Westmr, May appear before you, at a certain Day to be Appointed, to hear & abide ye Indgmt & Determination of yor. Worships touching this Appeal
