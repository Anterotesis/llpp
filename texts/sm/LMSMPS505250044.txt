To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of Thomas Hopkins

Your Petitioner most humbly prays your Worships Votes and Interest to succeed John Turner as Keeper or Governour of Clerkenwell Bridewell

Your Petitioner has the Honour of being known to Lord Northumberland for many Years, and as long to many of your Worships, to whom he most humbly refers for his Character.

Your Petitioner having a larger Family, he most humbly hopes that it will be an Inducement with Your Worships to vote in Favour of your Petitioner, who is so happy us to be Chosen, he will make it his Constant Study to writ your Approbation by discharging his Duty with Assiduity, Fidelity and Humanity, and as shall become

(Your Worships) most dutiful Servant and Petitioner Thos Hopkins
