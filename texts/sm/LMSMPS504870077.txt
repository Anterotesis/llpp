To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Edm. Byron and Jno. Machin Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th. day of August 1760 Grissel Greer Wife of Alexander Greer (who is an Irishman and hath not gained any Settlement in this Kingdom and is now in the Lock Hospital in Kent Street Southwark) was Removed from the Parish of St. Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middx to the [...] Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the same Liberty and County as the place of her last legal Settlement Where by Your Petrs. conceive themselves to be Aggrieved, and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that this Worshipful Court will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining- this Appeal and that all partys concerned may then and thew attend.

And Your Petrs. will ever pray Etc.

W. [...] t Sellr. for the Parish of St. Martin in the fields
