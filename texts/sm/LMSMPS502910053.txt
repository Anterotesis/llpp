To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the [---] Parish of Saint Andrew Under shaft in the City of London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of Nicholas Jeffreys and Barwell Smith Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is mentioned to be of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty seventh day of February last Mary Bell was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft [...] in the City of London called in the said Order the united parishes of St Mary Ax and Saint Andrew Undershaft alledging therein that the said [---] Parish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London is the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Bell

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggreived by the aforesaid Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same unto their Court And Forasmuch as your Petitioners cann be ready to prosecute their said Appeal this present Sessions Humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to adjorn the hearing and determining the Said Appeal untill the next Generall Sessions of the peace & that your Worships would appoint some Day therein for all parties concerned to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeas

Thomas Elmes Agent for the Appellants

And your Petitioners shall pray and so forth
