To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the precinct of Saint Catherines in the said County:

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order of removall bearing Date the Twenty fourth day of August last Under the hands and Seals of Ant. wroth and W: Booth Esqrs: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Rachel Harbey and her Children Namely Edward aged Eight years Elizabeth aged Seven Years Mary aged Five years Jane aged two years and Joanna aged ten Months was removed from the parish of Saint Luke in the County aforesaid to the said Precinct of Saint Catherines as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners find themselves agreived by the warrt. or order of the said two Justice and Humbly appeal against the same

Your Peticoners therefore Humbly pray you would be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all other persons Concerned to Attend this Court some day this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the premise And that Your Peticoners may have such releife therin as to Justice and Law doth appertain

And your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc.

Wm: Benham John Crowther} Overseers of the poor
