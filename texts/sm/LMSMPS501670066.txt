To the Petr Worpll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Middx ss The humble Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter Le Poore London

Sheweth. That by an Order dated the 2d. day of Novr. 1717 under the hands of Robert Kyrby & Bastwick Johnson Esqrs. Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) by which said Order Thomas Waite & Margarett his Wife with their Four Children Vizt. Susanna Waite aged abt. 11 years Elizabeth aged abt. 9 years Mary aged about 6 years & Thomas Waite aged abt. 3 years (all the lawfull issue of the said Thomas Waite & Margaret his Wife) were passed from the parish of St. John of Wapping in the County of Middx, to the said parish of St. Peter le Poore London as the place of their last legall Setlement

That the said Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of St. Peter le Poor most humbly Conceive themselves agreived by the said Order, & pray an Appeale to this Honoble. Bench

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly rave this Honoble. Court, to appoint a day for hearing of yor. Petrs. Appeale agts: the said Order

And Yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc
