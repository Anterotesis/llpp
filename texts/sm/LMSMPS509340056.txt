To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the Sessions Houses in the said County in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen Walbrook in the City of London

Sheweth That Thomas Lockwood Esquires and Thomas Collins Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex by an Order under their Hands and Seals respectively bearing date the Tenth Day of December One Thousand Seven hundred and Ninety six directed to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex did thereby require them to remove and Convey Margaret Hubbard a Singlewoman into the said Parish of Saint Stephen Walbrook

That your Petitioners Apprehended and are Advised that they are greatley aggrieved by the said Order of Removal And

Therefore your Petitioners Humbly Appeal against the same and pray that your Worships will please to make such Order therein as shall be just and Reasonable

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
