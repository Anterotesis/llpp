To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Mary Cook of the Parish of St. Ann Westm in the sd. County

Sheweth That your Petr. hath Lived in the sd. Parish these Nineteen Yeares past and is now Reduced to as very Poor and lower Condicon and having been for some time past and [...] much Distempred and Indisposed in Body which Renders her very uncapable of getting a Livelywhood Soe that the most Inevitably Perish unless Releived in the prmisses by this Honble Cort.

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Order her such Releife from the sd. Parish as unto your Worspps shall seem meets

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
