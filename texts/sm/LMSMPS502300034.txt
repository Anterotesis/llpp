To the Rigt. Worspll. his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clement Danes in the sd. County [---]

Sheweth That Anne Sherburn Singlewoman was by [...] Order dated the 6th. of August last under the hands & Seales of John Milner and John Ellis Esqrs. Two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quorum Unus) removed & conveyed from the Pish of St. Giles in the Fields to the Pish of St. Clement Danes in the sd. County [---]

Your Petrs. thereby humbly conceives themselves agreived & appeales from the Judgemt. of the sd. Two Justices to the Judgemt. of this Cort & crave a day of hearing Etc

And your Petrs. shall ever pray
