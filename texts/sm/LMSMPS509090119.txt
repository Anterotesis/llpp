To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John at. Hackney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Martha Shobrooke Spinster Daughter of William Shobrooke of the said Parish Esquire is a Parishioner of the said a Parish hath been for a long time past and still continues to be a Poor, impotent, infirm Person, not able to work and for a long time past hath been, and still continues to be totally maintained at the charge of the said Parish in the Workhouse of the said Parish that the said William Shobrook her said father is of sufficient ability to maintain and provide for her and although frequent application hath been made to him for the purpose he always refused and still refuses so to do.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that he may be assessed in such weekly sum of money as your Worships shall think proper for the relief and maintenance of the said Martha Shobrooke his Daughter to be paid to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish for the time being for so long time as the shall be chargeable to the said Parish

And Your Petitioners as in duty bound will ever pray Etc

John Adamson Conrad Loddiges } Churchwardens

John Meek John combs} Overseers
