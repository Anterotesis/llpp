To the Right Worspl: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: sitting at hickshall

The humble petion and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the hamblet of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Kyrby and Bastwick Johnson Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the first Day of This Instant Month of February James Dicks an Infant Child aged about three Years was Conveyed from the parish of St. Pauls shadwell in the said County of Middx to the hamlet of Ratcliffe aforesaid as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Infant Child

Febry: 25: 1717/18 Your Petrs: finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of removall of the said two Justs: humbly Appeale against the Same and pray your Worsps will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determineing of the said Appeale this present Sessions and that your Petrs: may be releived as unto your Worsps shall Seem meete

And Yor: Petrs: shall us: pray Etc
