To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Clarke and John Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh day of this Instant April Susannah Akenhead Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

B Wood Saml Agan Geo: Howard, Jno Dorman Richd Howlson } Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Annes
