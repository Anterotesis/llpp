To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Genl. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of Joshua Walker Keeper of his Majesties Goal oalled New Prison att Clerkenwell

[---] That the said Goal of New Prison is very much out of repair and that your Petitioner is daily in great danger of the Escape of some of his Prisoners one whereof not long since broke out in the day time by Clambring over his door case which your Petr. humbly hopes this Court will be pleased to order the said door case to be Pulled down and to give such further Directions towards repairing the said Goal as to this Court shall seem meet to Prevent the Escape of any of his Prisoners

And Yor: Petrs. shall ever pray Joshua Walker
