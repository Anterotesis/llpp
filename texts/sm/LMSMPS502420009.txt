To the Right Worppll. his Majestyes Justices of the peace at the Quarterly Sessions holden for the County of Middx at Hick's Hall

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers for the poor of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth

That your Petitioners being [...] fourth day of this prstant Aprill under the hands and Seals of the Worpll Jno. Milner and Jno. Ellis two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) by the ChurchWardens and Overseers for the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in ye sd. County of Middx ordering them to remove Sarah Speek wife of Thomas Speek from & out of their Said Parish of St. Giles to your Petrs. Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides who were thereby ordered to Provide for ye. said Sarah Speek at their Perill your Petitionrs therefore do think themselves aggreived & the said Sarah Speeks settlemt. not being in their Parish as your Petrs. [...] not prove

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that your Worpps will be pleased to [...] appoint a time for the hearing of both Partyes and to abide by Such Judgment as the Court shall think fitt

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
