To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Adam Prince and Abigal Hind

Sheweth That the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County have assess'd your petrs. (vizt.) Adam prince Six shillings P Quartr. & Abigall Hind Seven shillings a Quarter towards the Releife of the poor of the said parish for the year ensuing Wch. said Rates are after the Rate of Twelve pounds and Fourteen pounds P Annu at two shillings in the pound Wch. said Assessmt. is charg'd upon the Inhabitants in the parish aforesd.

That yor. petr Princes rent is but Seven pounds P Annu & the sd Hinds but Twelve pounds So that they both think themselves aggreiv'd by the sd Assessmts.

Your Petr: therefore most humbly pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may severally be releiv'd in the sd prmes

And Yor. petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
