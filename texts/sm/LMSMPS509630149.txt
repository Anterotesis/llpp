Middlesex To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in arid for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removal under the Stands & Seals of R. Johnson and John Willcock Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting and for the said County Dated the second day of Septr. Tnott. Mary Hooper was removed from the Parish of St. James Within the liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kensington as the last legal Settlement of her the said Mary Hooper Whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived end do appeal to your Worships against the said order

Wherefore your Petitioners humble pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day in the present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal & that all parties Concerned may be ordered to attend to abide and perform such order as your Worships shall please to make and that your Worships will please to give your Petitioner such Relief in the Premises an shall be just And your Petitioner shall pray

Wm Smith} Church Wardens

Wm Warwick Geo. [...] mans} Oversees of Poor
