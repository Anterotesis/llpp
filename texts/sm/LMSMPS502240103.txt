To the Rt. Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden at Hicks Hall in and for the sd: County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of th poor of the Parish of pancras in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Wart. or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Tuller and Wm. Cotesworth Esqrs: two of Cui Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorum) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of December last Sarah Collier with her two Children Vizt. Anne Seven years Old and Richard aged five Years were Removed from the Parish of Hornsey in the County of Middx: aforesd: to the said parish of Pancras as the place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby Your Petitionrs. Humbly Conceive they are Wronged and aggrieved.

Your Peter. therefore Humbly prays your Worpps would be pleased to appoint A Day for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal this present Sessions, and that your petrs. may be Relieved as unto your Wisdom Shall Seem Meet

And Your Petitionrs Shall Ever pray Etc
