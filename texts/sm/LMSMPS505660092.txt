To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Ethelburga in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Christopher Scott Esquire and George Garrett Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of December instant a Certain Woman therein Called Sarah the widow of John Campbell deceased was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Said Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Ethelburga as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Campbell

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the Benefit of this their appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing may be adjourned till the next General Sessions of the peace and that your Petitioners may be relieved against the said Order

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Hin Corrock } Church warden

Saml Lindsay Overseer of the poors
