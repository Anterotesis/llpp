To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Sessions of the peace for said County Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Wickham Esquire and H. Reynett D.D. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty third day of January last whereby Christopher Goujon aged about Ten Years and William Goujon aged about Seven Years the lawful Issue of Christopher and Sophia Goujon were removed and conveyed from and out the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of the their last legal Settlement

Your Petitoners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and request that you will be pleased to appoint same day in the present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And as in Duty Etc Arthur Gliddon for Appellants
