To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of W, Bleamire and W, Kinnard Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of December One thousand seven hundred and Ninety five Sarah Siddaway Spinster was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Christ Church called in the said Pass Warrant or Order The Parish of Christ Church Spital fields" as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Siddaway Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and the said Order And Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint same day in this present Session for the bearing and determination of the said Appeal And to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell and all Persons conceived do then attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Hugh Parnell Sollicitor for the appellants Church Street Spital fields
