To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled-

The Humble Petition and Appeal of several of the Inhabitants and substantial Householders of Lands House Etc within the Parish of St. George in the County of Middlesex whose Hands are hereunto subscribed for & on behalf of themselves & other Inhabitants & Householders of the said Parish

Sheweth That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. George Middlesex in the said County of Middlesex have made & obtained a Rate or Assessment on all the Inhabitants Householders of Lands House Etc for the Support & Maintainance of the Poor of the said Parish for the Year 1751 for raising of the Sum of £658,,4s,,2d which is called at Eight Months Rate

That there is in the Hands Custody or Possession of the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor or in the Hands Custody or Possession of Trustees appointed for that Purpose for the Use of the Poor of the said Parish in Government Securities and Money £1093,,8s,,7d and which has been raised on the Inhabitants & Householders of the said Parish for the Support and Maintainance of the Poor of the Same Parish

That Your Petitioners Apprehend that no Sum or Sums of Money ought or should be raised on the said Inhabitants & Householders of the said Parish for the Support and Maintenance of the Poor of the said Parish untill all such Sum and Sums of Money which is now in the Hands Possession or Custody of the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor and Trustees as aforesaid of the said Parish were expended applied and laid out for the purposes for which such Sum and sums of Money were raised

Your Petitioners most humbly Prays Your Worships that the said Rate or Assessment for the Reasons above specified may be Quashed and Vacated

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Joseph Crowcher

Wm Reemes Willm Smith E Arlington Cha Header John Mason Wm Printon Beni: White Sr Roberts Heny: White Tho Leach David Mulle Andw. Loughnan Jeffry Burton
