To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a certain order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Reverend Herbert Mays [...] DD. and John Staples Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the Twenty Eighth day of March 1794 Frances Lipman Singlewoman was conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft in the City of London as the place of her lawful Settlement whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in the present Session for the hearing and determination thereof
