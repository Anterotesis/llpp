To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said County One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of February last Thomas Quissenbrough, Martha his Wife and their two Children(that is to say) Elizabeth Aged Eight Years and Thomas Aged Six Years the lawfull Issue of the Sd. Thomas Quissen brough and martha his Wife are removed from the Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the said County to the sd: parish of St. Andrew Undershaft as the place of their last legal settlement

Your Petitioners Therefore Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices Do Humbly Appeal to this Court for such releife in the premisses As to Your Worships shall seeme meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

Samuel Butler Agent for the Appellants}
