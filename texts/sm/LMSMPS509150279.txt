To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwans and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Andrews and H. I. Pye Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th, day of October 1795 James Abberley Elizabeth his wife and their Three Children vizt. Elizabeth aged five years John aged Three years and Benjamin aged One year and an half were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that your Worshipes will be Pleased to appoint some day in this Present session for the hearing and Determining this their Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray. Etc.
