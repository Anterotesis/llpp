To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Cornelius Martin and William Martin Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the First Day of this present Month of February a Woman and Child in the said Order called and described by the Names and Descriptions of Susanna young Widow of James Young and their Child George aged about five Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint James and all persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitrs. shall pray Etc

Thos. Williamson J. Ayliffe} Church Wardens and

Wm Milbourne W Draycott Geo: Lockett D Evans} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
