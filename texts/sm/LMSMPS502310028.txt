To the Rt: Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Sessions

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Heston in the sd. County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Rupert Brown & John Venner Esqr. two of His Maties. Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the 24th. Day of November last past Elizabeth Symms Singlewoman was removed from the parish of East Bedfont in the said County to the said parish of Heston as the place of Her last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the sd two Justices humbly appeal therefrom And pray that the Church- -wardens & Oversrs of the poor of the sd. parish of East Bedfont and all persons Concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeall

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
