Middlesex To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under Hands and Seals of [...] The Right Honourable Thomas Harley. [---] Lord Mayor and Henry Banker Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof one being of the Quorum bearing Date the Twenty fourth Day of February instant Michael Daniel Mary his wife and their two Children (that is to Day) John aged three Years and James aged Eight Months were removed from the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London to the said parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said pass warrant or Order do appeal to this Court.

Your Petitoners therefore in at humbly pray that your worships will be pleased to order that the Benefit of their said appeal be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be held for this County in [...] notice thereof to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London.

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

I: Lane Sollicitor for the appellants
