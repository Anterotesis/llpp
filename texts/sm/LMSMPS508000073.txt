To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of St. Sampson Wright and Edward Naish Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth Day of July last Thomas Henry Selwood Arnold Mary his Wife and their three Children (to wit) Anna Maria aged Five Years Naomi aged three Years and William Thomas aged Two Months. was Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the said Liberty and County to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields. within the Liberty and County aforesaid. as the place of their last Legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and Deter mining the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Jos. Holl Solr. for the Appelts.
