To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Benjamin of and Thomas Balack Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Nineteenth day of January last Ann Roberts Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Anne as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrived

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices And pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal And to Order that all Persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitrs. Shall pray Etc

John Horne Wm Trigg N: Parkers Ottewill Timmiss} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Anne
