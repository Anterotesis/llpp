To the Rt. Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace of their General Quarter Sessions holden at Hicks hall for the County of Middlx

The Humble Peticonr and Appeal of Benjamin Coher- of the Hamlett of Lyme house in the said County Cowkeep

Sheweth

That your Peticoner is rated more towards the reliefe of the Poor of the said Hamlett this year then what the paid the last yeare Tho he rents no more Land nor is he is better Circumstances And for that there being no General- Rise through the said Hamlett your Peticoner thinks himselfe Agreivd by the said Rate and humbly Appeals agt the same

Therefore most Humbly hopes yr. Worspps will give him Such reliefe in the prmisses As to yr. Worspps Shall Seem meet

And yr. Pet shall ever Pray Etc
