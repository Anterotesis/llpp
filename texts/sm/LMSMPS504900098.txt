To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble petition of the Overseers of the poor of the precinct of St. Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of removal bearing date the thirteenth day of November last past Under the Hand and Seals of Christopher Scott and Thomas Smith Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace- for the said County of Middlesex aforesaid Ann Elliott Widow of Edward Elliott deceased and her three Children (to wit) Edward aged about six years Mary aged near three years and Martha aged about Ten Month were removed from the parish of St. John Wapping in the said County of Middlesex to the precinct of St. Catherines in the said County

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves agrieved by the said Order of removal do humbly appeal against the same and pray that a Day in this present Sessions may be fixed for the hearing and determining of the said appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

James Clare [...] Vestry Clerk
