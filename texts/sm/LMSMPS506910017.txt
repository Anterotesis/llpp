To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Lydia Simpson

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof yor. petr. Stood Indicted in this Honble Cort this prsent Sessions your Worspps were pleased to Fine her 20s. and for Nonpaymt. thereof to comitt her into Custody in the Bayle Dock where she now Remaines

That your Petr. is very Sorry for the sd. Fact and being a very Poor Woman and haveing a poor antient & almost Blind husband to mainteyne Soe that your Petr. is not able to pay the sd Five and humbly on builleing hereof to the Great Goodness & Mercy of this Honble Court

P Thos Nash

yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the sd. Fine [...] unto yor. Worspps Shall seem meet.

yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc
