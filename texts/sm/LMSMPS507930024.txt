To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the New Session House Clerkenwell Green Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the hand and Seal of the Worshipful D. Walher Esquire one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Action in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Tenth day of January one thousand seven hundred and Eighty five Ann Morgan Singlewoman a Rogue and Vagabond was conveyed from the Parish of Saint [...] Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of has last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved And against which pass Warrant or order Do Appeal to this Court.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determination of the said Appeal And that all parties concerned may be then directed to attend

And your Petitioners shall pray &

Richd. Christmas Jno French Jno Sidgwick} Churchwardens

A. Chaplin Gould} Overseers of the Poor
