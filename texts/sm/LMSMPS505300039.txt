To the Right Worshipfull the Justices in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Hastings

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was this day Tried along with one Vaudecast upon an Indictment for an assault and forceable on and that Your Petitioner was Convicted for an Assault only and Committed to Bridewell for one Month

That Your Petitioner is Sorry for his Imprudence in the affair but does beg leave to assure Your Worships that his only Motive to give what due assistance he could to a Constable that was calling out Murder not knowing any thing of the Nature or Meaning of a Supersedeas

That Your Petitioner as a Man of a good and peaceable Disposition and for the reasons above mentioned doth for the sake of his Wife and great famely most Humbly implore that Your Worships will be pleased to Commisserate his Case and order such Metigation of his Sentance as to Your Worships it shall seem most meet

And Your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc

James Hastings
