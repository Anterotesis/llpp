To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Pish of Stocken Pelham in the County of Hertford

[...] weth That Roberta Westwood Wife of James Westwood and One Child named Thomas Westwood aged four Years were by an Order dated ye 24th. [...] of July last Past under the Hands & Seales of Stephen Martin Leak and P: Wiilshire Esqrs Two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) removed and Conveyed from the Hamlett of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Stocken Pelham in the County of Hertford

Yor. Petrs humbly Conceive themselves agrieved thereby And do Appeal from the Judgement of the said Two Justices to the Judgement of this Honble Court and Crave a day of Hearing

And Yor Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
