To the Right Worship full his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the (Church wardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands and Seales of John Fuller and Ward Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx( [...] ) bearing date the Nineteenth day of February last Thomas Gilles with Mary his wife and their three Children (vizt) Thomas John and Mary were Past on your petrs. from the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate as the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves Aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices and appeale to your Worshipps there from

Yor. Petrs: there fore humbly Pray your Worshipps will appoint a day for the hearing and de [...] ining the said Appeale.

And your Petrs: Shall pray Etc
