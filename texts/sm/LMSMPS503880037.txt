To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Maties Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal Delivery held at Justice Hall in he Old Bailey for the County of Middlesex

The humble petition of Jacob Carter

Sheweth That your Cotr. on the 28th. day of June last was Committed to the Common Goal of Suffolk being charged by an Information of having been guilty on the eighth of October 1746 with being together with divers other persons Armed with fire Arms in Order to be aiding and Assisting in the running and Landing of an customed Goods

That Your Cotrs. in Order to manifest his Innocence has been at the Expence of bringing up two Witnesses from the County of Suffolk to give Evidence on behalf of Your petrs at this Sessions

Your petrs. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court will be pleased to Order that your petr. may be tryed Bailed or Discharged this present Sessions

And Your petr. shall Ever pray
