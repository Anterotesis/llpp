To the Right Worspl. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon & appeal of Thomas Waters of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated two shillings P month towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for this present year.

That your Peticoner conceives himselfe aggreived by the said assessment For that he is rated more in proporcon then divers of the Inhabitants of the said parish who ought to be rated in equallity with your Peticoner

p Swestley Wherefore he humbly Prays your Worspps to be pleased to mittigate his said Rate in Such manner as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yor. Petr: shall ever pray Etc.
