To the Rt Worshippfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of Isaac Davis of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County Distiller

Sheweth That your Peticoner is Charged to the poor and [...] Rates within the said parish after the Rate of Twenty pormes P ann although in truth your Petitioners pays no more the Tenn pound a year for the same to hereby thinks himself Apprived and appeals to this Court agst. the same

Wherfore your petitioner prays this Court will be pleaced to grant him such releife in the premis is [...] as to Your Worship shall seem Mat & Convenient

And your Petr Shall ever pray of Isaac Davis
