To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session [---] Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Martin

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petitioner has attended as Door Keeper & Messenger to this Honourable Court for one year ending at this present Session

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly prays that your worships would be pleased to order him such Salary or Gratuity as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioner will ever pray Etc

John Martin
