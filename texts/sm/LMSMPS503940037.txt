Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hick's Hall in & for the said County now assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Liberty of Saffron-hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the st. County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Boulton Mainwaring & Richard Ricards ;Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (one where of being of the Quorun) bearing date the 1st. day of March 1748 Flora Tabb Widow was removed from the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx to the said Liberty of Saffron hill Heatton Garden & Ely Rents as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Flora Tabb whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Joshua boxsoll for the Appelets. Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Honble Court and Pray that the sd; Court will be Pleased to Order tha the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Ratcliffe and all Persons concerned do attend this Court Some day in this Present Session of the Peace for the said County to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of the Said Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
