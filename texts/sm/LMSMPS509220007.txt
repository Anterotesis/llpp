To this Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Bay ley of Kennington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the Twenty Eighth day of December last past Convicted by Williams Addington Esquire one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex of having at a certain Turnpike Gate at Rogmove Lane in the parish of Saint Mary abbot kensington in the county aforesaid on the Twenteeth day of December lasts taken of and from one John Andrews a Greater to be for a Single horse passing through the said Turnpike Gate than what he is authourised to do, contrary to the Statute in that case made and provided And [---] your Petitioner conceiving himself affrieved thereby doth humbly pray to be permitted to appeal from such conviction and that you will please to appoint a day for that purpose.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray &c Thos. Bailey.
