To the Worshipll: his Majties; Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Gennerall Quartere Sessions Assembled

She humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish [...] Woborn in the County of Bucks

Middx ss, Sheweth; That by order are Warrant dated the second day of this Instant October and under the hands and Seales of Reginald Marriott and George Howard Esqrs: two of his sd, Majties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) Mary Burcatt and her three Children (vizt) Charles about Six Years old, Mary foure, and Willi [...] Two Years old, were lately removed from the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middx, unto the said Parish of Woborn in the said County of Bucks.

Therefore your Petrs. humbly conceive themselves aggreived by the said ordere and appeale agst: th [...]

P Hen Denton octavo die Octobris 1719 } Wherefore yor: Petrs: humbly [...] day to be appointed for the hearing and determining this their Peticon and Appeale, Sometime this present Sessions
