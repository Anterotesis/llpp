To the right Worpl. her Majties. Justices of the peace Sitting [---] The Honorble Bench at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of Robert Shall & Elizabeth his wife humbly Sheweth

That whereas the Petrs. Stand Indicted before this Honorble. Bench touching a Bill in Chancery in matter depending between Mary Carter now wife of Francis Westman Seedsman and the same being to be heard this day the Petrs. maine materiall Witness being out of Towne & not to returne dureing this Sessions

The Supplicants humbly prayers yor. Worpt. may please to respite the present hereing untill next Sessions

And the Petrs. shall were pray Etc
