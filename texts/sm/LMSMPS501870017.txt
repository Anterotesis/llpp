The humble peticon of John perrochan Apprentice to John Bevan

to be hd 1st. day of next sess: 11 cl

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent p Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John street in Com Prd P adjorn die sabti scilt decimo sexto die Julij anno regin Dui Georgij mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc sexto

It is ordered by this Court that the within named John Bevan have notice of this peticon, and that he doe attend [...] his Maties. Justices of the peace at the next Generall Sessions of the peace to be holden for this County on Monday the fifth day of September next at eleven of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the within named John Perrochon should not be discharged from his apprenticehood to the said John Bevan,

P Cur Harcourt
