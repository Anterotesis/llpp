To the Rt. Worpll. his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in the Genall Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Finchly in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a passwarrt. under the hands and Seales of William Dobyns and John Nicoll Esqrs Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx af [...] (Quor nunc) beareing date the Nineth day of January last Robert Marsh and Jane his Wife and four Children (vizt) Robert Marsh aged Eight Year's Jane Marsh aged Six Year's Richard Marsh Three Year's and Samuell Marsh Three Quarters of a Year were passed from the parish of Hendon in the said County of Middx to the sd. parish of Finchly in the Sd. County of Middx aforesd. as the last place of ye. last Legall Settlement of the said Robt. Marsh

Yor. Peticonrs. think themselves agreeved wth. the Indgemt. of the sd. Two Justices doe appeal to this Court and humbly pray that the Churchwardens and over Seers of the poore of the parish of Hendon and all other parties concen'd herein may be order'd to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgemt. and determination of this Court touching this appeal

And yor. Peticonrs Shall ever pray Etc.
