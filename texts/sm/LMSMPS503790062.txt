To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Burdus and William Jones Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster[---] (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th. day of June 1747 Mary Hicks, Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in this County to the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray, Etc

Tho South Thos; Huddle} Churchwardens

Thos: Whitehead Wm. Malin} Overseers of the Poor
