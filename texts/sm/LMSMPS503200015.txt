To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: Assembled in General Sessions at Hicks's Hall in the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Squa [...] in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of apass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of James Fraser and Val: Hilder Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first Day of November last Mary Lewis the Widow of Joseph Lewis Deced and Mary Lewis aged about Fifteen Years (Daughter of the said Joseph and Mary Lewis were removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx: to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square, As the Place of their last legall Settlement, Whereby Your Petitioners then themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Peac [...] this Court will please to Appoint [...] atime for hearing the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And Your Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc

Denis Bond James Spilman} Churchwardens.
