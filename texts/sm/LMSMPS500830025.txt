Sheweth That your Peticoner was Sumonsed to appeare in this Court this morning in order to Serve on the Grand Jury this present Sessions.

That your Peticoner did not appeare time [...] enough in this Honble Court in discharge of his said Sumons Soe that your Worspps were pleased to fine him 40s. And your petr. being Sorry for the Said contempt.

P Westley He humbly therefore prays your Worspps to be pleased to discharge him of the Said Fine

And yor. petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

Thomas Tidman
