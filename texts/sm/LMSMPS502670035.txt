To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace Asemble at Hicks's Hall

The humble peticon of Tho: Bosswell prisoner in Clarkenwell Bridwell

Sheweth That Your Petitioner being in a Laguishing Condition in the sd. prison having been Confined therein for the Space of Six weeks the time Specified in my Sentence Being Unable to pay the fees Demanded most humbly prays that Yor Worships would be pleas'd to Com misserate my Unhappy Circumstances together with my familys who have been sick and been upon ye parish Since my Confinement And Order him to be Discharg'd from prison and Your Petitioner as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc
