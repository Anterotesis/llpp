To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Northgate in the City of Canterbury.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Selas of D. Wilmot and Chas. Friguet Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one Whereof being of the Quorrum) bearing date the day of Phineas Obree and his four Children lawful Issue namely Phineas William aged Seven Years Mary Elizabeth aged five Years James aged three Years and Apisoby aged two Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Northgate in the City of Canterbury as the place of their last legal Settlen at

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the aid two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worshipsh will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Henry Arnold Solr. for the Appellants
