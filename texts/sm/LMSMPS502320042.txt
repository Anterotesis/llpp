To the Worshipfull his Maities Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions [...] assembled

The humble petition of Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of [...] the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Daniel Houghton & Thos. Scott Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace of the sd County of Middx (Quor un) Mary Rider wido & Sarah her Daur are by the sd. Warrt. required to be removed from the Parish of St. George Hannover Square to the sd. Parish of Edgeworth as the Place of the last legall Setlemt. of the sd. [---] Mary & Sarah her Daur,

That ye Petrs. appretiend Themselves agreived by the sd. Warrt. & [...] [---] appeal there from & humbly pray ye Worsfull [...] appoint a Day for the hearing of the Mrc of this Petition

And ye Petrs. shall pray Etc.
