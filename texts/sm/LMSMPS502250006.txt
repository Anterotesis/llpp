To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble petition and appeal of Joseph Storey of the parish of Hornsey in this County

Sheweth

That your Petr. is Charged to the poor's Rates in the said Parish for the House the lives in abt Six pounds per Annum more than what the Realy any night to be [---] for the Same, and your petr. therefore Thinks himself Agrieved, and Humbly Appeals against the said Rate.

May it therefore please your Worshipps to Consider the premisses and to make such Order for your Petrs. Relief as to this Court Shall Seem meet.

And your Petr. shall ever Pray

Joseph Storey
