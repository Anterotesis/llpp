To the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Qr Sessions for the Peace Assembled

The Petition of Several Protestant Dissenters Whose names are hereunto Subscrib'd Humbly Desiring that the House of Mr. Robert Austin in the Parish of Bromely St Leonard in the Sd County may be Allow'd & Recorded in this Honourable Court as a Meeting place for Religion Worship by Protestant Dissenters of the Denomi nation of Independents & that they may have a Certificate thereof

Signd July 4th 1737

Robert Austin Tho Yeoman Richard Lewis John Osland George Jakeman James Man
