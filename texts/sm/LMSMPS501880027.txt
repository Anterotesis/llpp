To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Richard Habberly.

Sheweth

That there is due to your Peticoner for one years. Wages from one me John Higgs the Sum of Three pounds which he refuseth to pay And forasmuch as your Peticoner being a poor man and standing in great want of his said Wages

Yor Petr: therefore humbly prays. the said John Higgs may attend this Court some day this Sessions to Shew Cause why he refuseth to pay your petitioner his said Wages.

And yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

R. Habberly.
