To the Right Worpfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of All Saints in the Town of Hertford in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order dated the 24th. day of November last under the Hands & Seales of D'oily Michell Esqr. & Alexander Pitfeild Esqr two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (Quor unus) one Elizabeth Bates the wife of John Bates and John her son (aged about one year) the lawfull Issue of the Said John Bates & Elizabeth his wife (as is alledged in the Said Warrant or Order) were lately removed & conveyed from the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the Said County of Middx to the Said parish of All Saints in the Said Towne & County of Hertford ad the place of the last Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Bates & her Said sown

The Appellants finding themselves agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the Same

Jno. Batty Attorny for the Appellts: And humbly pray this Court will please to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch & all other persons concerned to attend this Count this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court upon & touching the Said appeal and that the Appellants may be releived herein according to Law And your peticonerd Shall pray Etc
