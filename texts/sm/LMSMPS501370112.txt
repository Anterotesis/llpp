To the Right Worpll her Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Samuel Hanson of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County. Baker

Sheweth That yor Petr. is Assessed Thirteene shillings & Six pence per Quarter towards the releife of the poore of the Sd. parish hath a Wife two Childring And for as much as yor: Petr: is much in debt and is Confind in his house about 12 months for feare of being Arrested & carried to Grate & haveing but a Small trade which makes your Petr, not able to pay, the sd Rate

Therefore humbly: prayes your Worppll to take his Case into Consideracon & grant him & Such releife herein as in yor: Wisdom shall seem meet

And yor: Petr: shall pray Etc

[...] the [...] at St Giles Cripple gate Middlx

about the poors rate

[...] to be hd. 22d. Feb: 9 cl

rate to stand
