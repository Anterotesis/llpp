To the Rt. Worspd. her matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth.

The humbled Peticon of Daniell Waite Robert Tredwell and Richard Towersey Three of the prsent Ovseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the sd. County Arthur Davis late Churchwarden there of and Joshua Squire Robert Wheeler and John Towersey on the behalfe of themselves [...] thereof of the Antient Inhabitants of the North and South sides of Cow Crosse and the Hill Liberty of St. John Streete in the sd. Parish.

That upon the decease of Archibald Lovett the Place of Head Beadle of the sd. pish became vacant and the Churchwarden thereof lately called a Seleck Vestry unknowne to Sewall of yor. Petr. and then chose One Robert Raymond Head Beadle of the sd. pish in the place of the sd. Archibald Lovett Decd. [...]

That the Elector. of the sd. Robert Raymond were not above Twelve in number and as yor. petr humbly comeiveeny Inhabitant of the sd. pish who payes to Church & Poore has a free vote for the Chaseing a Head Beadle thereof.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor. worspps to be pleased to Order that to new Choise may be made for the Electing a Head Beadle of the sd. Parish that eny Inhabitant thereof who payes he Church & Poore may have a Free Vote in such Eleccon

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

P Tho: Nash
