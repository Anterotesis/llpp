To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middsex

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Westham in the County of [...]

Sheweth That Your Petitioners Concerva [...] themselves Agreived By an Order [---] dated the 27th Day of September last [---]Under the Hands and Seales of Doyly Mitchell and Robert Dannett Esqrs Justices of the Peace of the County of Middsex Whereby Elizabeth Murrray Wife of Nicholas Murray was removed From the Parish of St: Mary Mattfellons als White Chappell in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Westham in the County of Essex as the Place of the Legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Murry And that your petitioners Appeale against the said Order unto this Court: And Humbly

Pray that this Court [---] will appoint a Day for hearing and determining the matter of the said Appeale

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
