The Humble Peticon & appeal of Joseph Green served Thursday abt. 4 afternoon

To Morris Overseer of ye. Poor of Hackney in Pson To Hamlen Overseer of ye. Poor of Bethnall Green in Pson To Tapper Ch: Warden of Shoreditch in Pson [---] Jas. Tytons Junr:

On oath of service as above Petr. dischd.

to be hd. Fr: 10 cl 2s. 6d.

Midd ss Ad Genal Quarteral Session pacis Dom Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St John street in Com pred [...] P adjorn die Mercury (scilt) vicesimo Septimo die Aprilis Anno regin Dui wri Georgy Dei gra mmc Regis magne Britannie Etc pricno

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the severall parishes if St. Leonard Shoreditch & St. John Hackney & the hamblett of Bethnall green in this County have notice of this appeale, and that they doe attend this Court (if they shall think fit soe to doe) on Friday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the petr. should not be discharged from paymt. of the moneys assessed upon him in the respective rates made for the releife of the poor of the [---] respective parishes & hamblett above menconed.

P Cur Harcourt
