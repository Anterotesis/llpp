To the Worshipfull Thomas Lane Esqr Chairman and the rest of the Worshipfull Bench of Justices Assembled at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of James Ellmore

Humbly Sheweth That your Worships being pleased To Appoint your Petitioner as Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell during the Suspension of Mr William Turner your Worships Petitioner Continued as Such from the 24th Day of February to the 19th. Day of May both Inclusive

That your Worships having been Pleased To Appoint a Salary of Fifty Pounds per Annum to the Keeper of the House of Correction your Worships Petitioner Humbly Begs Your Worships order to the Treasurer of the County, for the Payment of the Salary due to him, for the time he Officiated as Keeper

6 December 1763

And es in Duty Bound Your Worships Petitioner Will Ever Pray Etc

James Ellmore
