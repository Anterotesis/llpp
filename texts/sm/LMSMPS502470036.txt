To the Rt: Worsppl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass under the hands and Seals of Phillip Willshire and Saml Jones Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the said County (quor unus) bearing date the 9th day of September last past Elizabeth Williams now Wife of John Williams formerly Elizabeth Matthews widd of George Matthews with her three Children (vizt) Mary Matthews aged about 7 year's & Eliz Matthews aged about Six year's and William Williams aged about 18 Months were removed from the Hamlett of Rattclife in the said County to the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney abovesaid as the place of their last legal Settlements

That Your Peticoners think themselves aggrieved by the said Pass and Judgment of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Honoble. Court against the same And humbly pray that their said Appeal may be Adjourn'd over to the next Sessions And that your Worships would be pleasd to appoint a day for the hearing the same And that all parties concerned may then Attend to hear and abide the determinacon of this Honoble. Court touching the said Appeal

S. Cowper

And Yr. Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
