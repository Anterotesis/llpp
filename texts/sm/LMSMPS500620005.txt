To the Right Wospll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Frances Williams wife of John Williams of the Pish of St. Martins in the Feilds in the sd. County

That yor. Petr. paid Husband hath lived with One Anne Strowd for Some time past as her husband and will not live with yor. Petr. nor allow her & her child mainteynance as by Law he ought to doe but did of late beat Yor. Petr. in a very Barbarour manner.

P Livestyley The Names of the Willeses Mr. Gllixander Mr. Jenkenson Mr. Booden Mr. Russell Mr. Cox Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worsps to be pleased to Order that her paid husband may allow her & her Child Such releife and mainteynance as unto yor. worsps Shall Seeme meete.

And yor. Pr. Shall on pray Etc
