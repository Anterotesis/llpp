To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of N Conant and John Scott Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th day of October 1799 Elizabeth Smally aged about Eighteen years Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the fields may appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to Abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray
