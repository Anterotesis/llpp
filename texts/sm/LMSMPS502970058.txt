To the Right Wospll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of James Fraser & William Jones Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (whereof one is the Quorum) bearing date the Fourteenth day of November last James Bowen aged about twenty one years and Catherine his wife were removed from out of the parish of St Martin in the Fileds in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of their last legall Settlement.

Yor. Petrs. therefore thinking themselves agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray yor. Worsps will be pleased to appoint a day for hearing & determining the same

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton
