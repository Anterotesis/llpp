To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden Within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Neve & Nathaniel Conant Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the said County (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the second day of January instant John Evans was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the Place of his last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court that your Worships will be pleased to save the benefit of their Appeal and to adjourn the hearing and determining thereof until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in & for the said County And that all Persons concerned may be directed then and there to attend

And your Petitioners shall every pray Etc

Hassell Hutchins J But [...] Geo: Leigh} Churchwardens

Charles Wiple Henry Garling} Overseers
