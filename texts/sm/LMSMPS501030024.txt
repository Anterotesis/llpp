To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Petition of William Rowse Marriner Belonging to her Majestys Shipp Rumneys

Sheweth That your Petr. tando Bound before the Worspll Justice Steward for your Petrs. appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions for assaulting of One John Streete and Breaking his Head near Ratcliffe Crosse in this County As by the Recognizance may appear.

That your Petr. is Ready by Severall Creddible Wittnesses to Make appear to this Honble Court that att the Same time the said assault was Made and the aforesd abuse [---] Petr. was at the Sweeds Armes in St. Katherine which is above a Mile Distance from the place where the said assault and abuse was Made ye given and for asmuchas your petrs. Tickett being out On accompt of which he is Obleidged forthwitts to Repair On Board the sd. Shipp and there being yoe Judictmt. preferred again it your petr. in this Honble Court On Accompt of the sd. Matter and he being Buformed neo such wilbe.

Tho: Nash Your Petr. therefore most humbly prayes yor worspps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd Recongnize On that the Same may be Respited untills next Sessions as unto your Worspps shall Seem meets.

And your Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

William Rous
