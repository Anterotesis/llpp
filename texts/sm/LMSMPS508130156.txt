To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of D: Walker and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid whereof (one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. Day of July one Thousand seven hundred and eighty six Mary Lock (therein mentioned to be Wife of John Lock) gone from her (together with her Male Bastard Child named William aged two Years and upwards for Nurture) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barr in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Lock and Mary Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barr may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Thos. Martin Solicitor for the Appellants Newman Street.
