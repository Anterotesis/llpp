To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Generall Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Harefeild in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order Under the hands and Seals of Mark Frecker and William Miller Esqrs two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the third Day of October last past that Mary Perryman the Wife of Thomas Perryman lately Deceased and her two Children, John Aged Seven years and Thomas Aged three years were Removed from the parish of Fulham in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Harefeild in the same County as the place of their last Legall Setlement

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal there from and pray that this Court will be pleased to order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Fulham to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

James Hampton Agent for the Appealants
