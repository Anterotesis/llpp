To the Worshipfull his Majestics Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx at their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Bennet and John Harding

Sheweth That there is due to Your Petitioners the sume of Four Pounds & Six Shillings as appears by Certificate under the hands of Sir George Cooke Knight James Rogers and John Weedon Esqrs: Three of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex hereunto annexed.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that Your Worshipps will be Pleased to give an Order to Edward Scott One of the High Constables of Uxbridge Division to pay Your Petitioners the said Sume of Four Pounds and Six Shillings

And Your Petitioners in Duty Bound Shall ever Pray Etc:

John Harding & for James Bennett
