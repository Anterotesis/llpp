To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Tempsford otherwise Thamesford in the County of Bedford

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Peter Greene and R. Smith two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the sixth day of September One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty three. William Riseley a Lunatic was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Thamesford as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby the said Churchwardens and Overseers of Thamesford otherwise Tempsford conceive themselves aggrieved.

Therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present General Quarter Session to hear and determine their said Appeal.
