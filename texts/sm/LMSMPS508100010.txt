To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Sampson Wright Knight, and William Kitchener Esquire Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 10th: day of April 1786. Sarah Wilson aged 30 Years or thereabouts and her Sister Elizabeth Wilson aged 16 Years or thereabouts, were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement and in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Margaret, as the Place of their Last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices, therefore Humbly Appeal to this count against the same, and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will Pray Etc

Jas: Toplis Jno. Cartwright} Churchwardens of Saint Margaret Westminster

Robt: Askey Geo: White} Overseers of the Poor of the same Parish
