[...] Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the [...] of Middlesex at their General Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticonr and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Richard Farmer and Thomas Exelbee Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the Said County (One being of the Quorum) dated 17th day of August 1734 Mary Lester and Anna Maria her Daughter aged ten years and whose were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the Said County to the Parish of St. George in the Said County as the Peace of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petr think themselves agrieved by the Said Order of the Said two Justices and most humbly Appeal to Your Worspl against the Same

Therefore most humbly prays your Worspsl Will Appoint a day this Sessions for the hearing and determining the Same And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Cheppel abovesaid and all other parties may then Attend

And yr. Petr Shall ever Pray Etc

Sam: Cowper Attorney for Appellants
