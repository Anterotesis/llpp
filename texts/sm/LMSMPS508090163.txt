To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the sd. County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seal of G: Mercer & John Croft Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of February 1786 Jane Peat an Infant [...] of the Age of 4 Years or there about was removed conveyed from the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell as the place of her last Agal Settlement whereby your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all person concerned may there attend
