The humble Peticon of Mary Champlin

St. Andrew's Holborne above barrs for rel

refr to Mr. Metcalfe Mr. Booth Mr. Foot or 1

May 4 Geo I 1718

Middx ss: Ad General Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd. die Martis Scilt vicesimo Septimo die May Anno regni Dui Georgij mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quar [...] .

It is ordered by this Court that it be, And it is hereby Recomended unto John Metcalfe Nathaniel Booth and Francis Foot Esqrs. Justices of the peace for the said County or any one of them to Sumon before them or any of them the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in this County, and to Examin into the truth of the Petrs: complaint, and make such order for the further Releife of the petr. and her children as to the said Justices of the peace or anyone of them Shall Seem meet.
