To the Worll his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seats of Thomas Woodcock and A: Mann Esqrs Justices of the Peace of and for the City and Libty of Westmr. and Said County of Middx bearing Date the 26th. Day of April last Ann Stapleton als Smith Singlewoman was removed from and out of the [---] Parish of Saint James's Westmr in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of Saint Margaret Westmr. as the place of her Lawfull Settlement

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worsps. against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worsps. would be please to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in this County may attend this Court Some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and final Determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
