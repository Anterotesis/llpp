To the Right Worshipfull Justice Busburn

Humbly Sheweth that your Poor Pettr. Thomas Headen hath at this present time his two Children lying in the small Pox they being Orphans their Mother being Dead this three years part & your Poor Pett Coming heare to drap his Cognisance being A little Disguised & thinking no harm is Ordered into Costody therefore hoping your Worspih will take into Consideration to Release me or my Poor Children must inevetably Perish therefore hoping your will take Comparsion on my Chilldren and your Poor Pett will for Ever Pray
