To the right Worpfull their Majestyes Justices of the peace, att Hick hall

The humble Peticon & Appeal of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poore of ye parish of Yeovill in the County of Sommersett

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & feilds of two of their Majesties Justices of ye peace for this County barling Date the twenty third day of April last made upon the Compll of the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of the [...] Hon [...] of Bow in the parish of Stepney that yeard [...] & her three children were likely to being chargable to ye sd. Hamlett & for that Henry [...] of he sd. [...] ard was Case legally Selled in ye sd. Parish of yeomill The sd. [...] and & her three children were by ye sd. Order Sent to Yr Petitioner as Church wardens & Overseers of Ye Poore of Ye sd. parish of Yeomill by [...] Order Yr petitioner doe [...] the Parishoner of Ye sd. Parsih [...] agrieved & ye sd. parish wrongfully charged and doe therefore humbly appeale from Ye sd. Order

Wherefore Yr Petitioners humbly pray That [...] the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Bow may how cause why ye sd. [...] ard & her three Children should not be repaired

And yr Petitioner of in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
