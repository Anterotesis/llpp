To the Right Honourable Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And to the Rest of the Honble Bench in their Quarter Sessions God delivery Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Prayer of John Watkins a prisoner for Felony

Sheweth. That on the Twenty Ninth day of December last year petitioner was Committed to New prison in the County of Middlesex for Assaulting and Ravishing Rebecca Haslehurst. against her Consent. and your petitioner now is a prisoner confined in Newgate under the Same Charge.

Your petitioner therefore most Humbly prays That at this present Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the City of London and County of Middlesex he may be Tryed, Bailed or Discharged pursuant to the Statute in that Case Made and provided.

And your petitioner shall pray Etc.

John Watkins
