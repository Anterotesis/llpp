To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the Rest of the Justices Sitting on the bench

The Humble Petition of Lewis Griffis

Humbly Sheweth

That your Petitioner before Septembr: Sessions Last became Bound in Recogn for the appearance of One John Barber who Stands Indicted for an Assault on Mary Heckman

That your Petitioner is ready to Surrender the Said John Barber to the Court

May it therefore please your Worships to discharge your Petitioner of his Said Recogn on the Surrender of the Said John Barber and your Petitioner as in Duty bound will Incessantly pray

Lewis Griffis
