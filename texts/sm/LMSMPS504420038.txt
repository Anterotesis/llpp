To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster in County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fifth day of June. Mary Sears Aged about twenty four Years was removed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea Adjudging the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Sears to be in the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea.

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves to be greatly Aggrieved by the Said Order & Determination of the said two Justices do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore do humbly Pray that Your Worship will be pleased to Appoint some day of this present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the hearing of this their said Appeal And that Your Worships will make an Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James Westminster and all Parties Concerned in and about the Premisses May then and there hear Abide the Final Determination of your Worships therein.

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.

Geo: Champ John Randal} Church Wardens

Peter Ingham Nath Betts} Overseers of the Poor

Josh: Clarkson Attorney for the Appellants }
