To the Rt Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Pursuant to the Order of the General Quarter Session of the Peace within Written we whose hands are hereunto Subscribed two of his Maties Justices of the Peace, for this County in the said Order named haveing this day taken upon us the hearing and Examination of the Complaint of the Petitioner John Whetham Esqrs herein named the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor as also the Scavengr. of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell being present and upon hearing of all P lies and duely Considering the Reason's and Allegations given [---] tend made on all sides, Wee report our Opinions to this Honble: Court that the said John Whetham Esqr [...] ought not be charged to the Churchwardens Rate Poors Rate and Scavengers Rate in the said Parish for Two Hundred Pounds Personall Estate Given under our hands this Elleaventh day of February 1719.

John Fuller Richd Browne
