To ye Right Worshipll. ye Bench of Justices Sitting At Hixes Hall The hunble Peticon of Richard fale Sheweth that yr Petisoner is a very poore Man A wever by trade Cannot gett eight Shilling per wick And [...] haveing Seven in familey, is verre hard putt to it for to Liue in the world And peaing but three Pounds A yeare Reint- And being nowe feased in the Poores Books And not being able to Pay I Beg most humbly that Yor Worships will take it into Consideracon that I may be ffreed And I Shall ever be bound to Pray for yr Worshipe Prosperety
