To the Worpll: their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of John Roberts

Humbly Sheweth That your Petr. by his Indenture beareing date the Eleventh day of April 1689 Did bind himselfe Apprentice to Anthoney English for the Terme of Seaven yeares wherein the Said Anthony English did oblidge himselfe to find your Worships Peticoner with Meat, drink washing lodging Apparrell and all things necessary dureing the Terme Now your Peticeners Master haveing not provided Cloaths for your Peticoner applyed himselfe to the Worshipll: Coll: Johnson for his Reliefe whereupon his, Worship was pleased to order your Peticoners Master to provide your Peticoner Such Cloaths and other things as was necessary But your Peticoners Master did not pursue his Said Order by which neglect and want thereof your peticoner is Rendred very lame in his Limbs.

Therefore your Petr: most humbly prays your Worships that you would be pleased to Summon his Said Master to discharge your Petr. from any further Service.

And your Petr. as in Duty bound Shall over Pray Etc
