To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble. Peticon of Richd. Adderley & William Dunckley

Sheweth That the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County have assess'd your peticoners severally and respectively Five shillings P Quarter towards the Releife of the poor of the said parish for the Year ensuing Which said Assessmts. are after the Rate of Ten pounds P Annu (at two shillings in the pound) Wch. said Rate is charg'd upon the Inhabitants of the said parish towards the Releife of the poor aforesaid That your peticoner Adderleys Rent is but Eight pounds P Annu, and your petr. Dunckleys Rent is but Seven pounds P Annu So that they severally think themselves very much aggreiv'd by the sd Assessmt:

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly Pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may be severally releiv'd in the said prmisses

And Your Peticoner shall ever Pray Etc.
