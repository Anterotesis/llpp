To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd.

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Anderson of the Parish of St. Dunstane Stepney in the sd County

That Yor. Petr. is Rated Â£1.4s. P annm towards the Releife of the Poore of the sd. Pish and for nonpaymt. thereof the Ovseers of the poore of the sd. Parish have made Dispescon your Petr Goods.

That yor. Petr. had a house of her Owne in the sd. Parish but her husband Mortgageing- the same and is time gone away from your Petr. who not being able to Redeem that Mortgage the Mortgagee has taken possession of the sd. house & yor. Petr. Rents a Roome of noe more then 40d. P annm therein And being a poore Woman is not abler to pay the sd. Rate.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes Yr. Worsppe to be pleased to Order that the [...] Goods soe Dischequed may be Delined to her- againe & that the sd. Rate may be inttigated as unto yer. Worspps shall seem meet

And Yor. petr. shall Ever pray etc. Elizabeth Anderson
