To the Right Worship full his Majtes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx ss;

The Humble peticon of Thomas Warner Apprintice to Jno. Harrison of the parish of St Leonard Shoredich in the County off or said Chocolate Maker

Sheweth That by Indentur bearing date the Sixtenth day of November 1723 Your Petr pras Bound Apprentice to the John Harrison to serve him for the Terme of seven years

That your Petr. said Master hath ten times beat Brused Wonded and Discharge your Pete. in a most Crer [...] and Barbarous Manner so that he is not at to Continue In his Serves

That your Petr. Master stands Bound by Recognizance to Appear in this Court thus present Sessions to answer the same

Your Petr. Therefor most humbly Prays that yor Worship,s Would bestileajd to Order that ye Petr Sd. Master may attend this Court some day this present Sessions to shew Cause why your Petr should not Bed Discharged From his Sd. Apprenticehood
