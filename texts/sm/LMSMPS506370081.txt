To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Cheshunt in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Sherwood and Robert Pell Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty first day of August in the Year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and seventy [...] three the last legal Settlement of Rebecca Renolds (Wife of Thomas Renolds) and her three Children to wit Sarah aged five years Fanny Aged three Years and Charles aged one Year or thereabouts was adjudged to be in the Parish of Cheshunt in the County of Hertford And by Virtue of the same Order the said Rebecca Reynolds and her said Children Sarah Fanny and Charles were removed from the Parish of Enfield in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Cheshunt in the County of Hertford as the Place of their last legall Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved

John Smart Solr. for Appelts

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order and pray that the Court will be pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Enfield and all Persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Session of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners may be otherwise relieved in the Premises as the said Court shall see fit

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
