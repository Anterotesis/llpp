To the Rt. Worshpll: his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of Henry Billingay of the parish of St: Mary [---] Clerkenwell

Sheweth That Yor: Petr: is Over Charged to the poors rate of the said Parish being Assest for Occupying £300 pr. ann whereas he Occupies but £215 pr. ann yor Petr: Therefore appeales against the same

Yor: Petr. therefore Humbley Pray that he may be releived etc.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
