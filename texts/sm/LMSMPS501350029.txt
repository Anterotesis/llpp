To the Rt Worsppll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of George Wray of the Liberty of Norton Folgate in the said County

Sheweth That on or about the four and Twentyeth day of September Last Yor petitr: was by the Inhabitants of the said Liberty Malicously [...] to Serve the Office of Headborough for the Said Liberty

That your Petitr. is a very Poorman having lately Susteined [...] great Losses and being & very Ailding Is not able to Serve the Said Office

Wherefore your Petitr: humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to take his Poor Condition into yor Serious Consideration & to order that he may be Discharged from Serving the said Office and that such other Prson may be Chosen in his Roome As unto yor Worspps shall Seem [...] Meete

And yor Petitr: shall Ever pray [...]
