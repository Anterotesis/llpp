To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County of Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the John Scott and Philip New Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex(One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th day of December 1796 Thomas Morgan and Catherine his Wife and his Two Children Namely Thomas Aged Five Years and Elizabeth Aged One Month were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that their Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray etc

Will Butler Josn. Stutley Chr. Gill Thos. Granger John Green Saml. Instone John Moir Geo. Hithcock Wm. Vernon Thos. Taylor Bignold} Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor
