To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of this Parish of St: Mary White Chappel in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Robt: Kyrby and Saml: Jones Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the sd: County (Quor unus) bearing date ye. 7th. day of May last, Alice Smith was removed from the Parish of St: John of Wapping in the said County, to the said Parish of St: Mary White Chappell as to the Place of her last legall Settlement

That your petrs think themselves agreived by the Jud [...] humbly appeal to this Honble: Court against the same

And pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of st: John Wapping and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court Some day this present Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale

And Your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
