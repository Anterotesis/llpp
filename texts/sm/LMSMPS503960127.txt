To the Right Honble Sir Wm. Calvert Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Kings Justices in their Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the County of Middx Assembled

The humble Petition of John Wilson

Most humbly Sheweth That Your Petitioner was on the third day of July Instant Committed to the said Gaol of Newgate for the Offence mentioned in the Commitment and two Severaly Detainers here to Annexed

That as there is no Indictment before this Honrble Court as Yet agt. Yor. Petr. for the Supposed Crime he is Committed for

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Prays that this Honrble Court will Please to permitt him to take his Trial at this Present Session upon any Indictment that shall be found agt him within the Jurisdiction of this Honble Court or be Admitted to Bail or discharged or otherwise relieved as this Honble Court Shall seem meet

And Yor humble Petr. will ever Pray Etc

John Willson
