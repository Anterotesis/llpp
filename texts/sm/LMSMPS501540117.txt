To the Rt Worp this Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Isaac slow of the parish of St. Leonard shoreditch, in the said County. Labourer

Sheweth That your Peticonr: is a poor Labouring man And is rated four pence half penny per week for the standing, poor, And Nine shillings and Nine pence more for half a years over rate, in this present Year, And forasmuch as your Peticonr: and his wife are both Aged and poor, and his rem but six pounds per Annum, and he having had Many Losses, and thinks himself aggrieved by the said rate, Humbly appeals to this Honoble Court, Against the said Assessment.

Your Peticonr: therefore most humbly prays your Worps to be pleased to Mittigate the said rate as unto your Worps shall seem meet

And your Peticonr: shall Ever pray etc [...]
