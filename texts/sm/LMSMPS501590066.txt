To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeall of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als [...] des London

Sheweth

That by Warrant undr. the hands and Seales of John Metcalfe and J Liddell Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County (quor unus) bearing date the Nineteenth day of November Anno Dui 1716. Elizabeth the wife of Francis Parents and their three Children Edward aged 13. Yeares, Elizabeth tenn Yeares and Margt. Six Yeares were past on yor Petrs. from the parish of St. Andrews Holborne above barrs in the sd County as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Francis

That yor. petr. conceive themselves aggreived by the ordr. of the sd. two Justices and appeall to Yor. Worspps therefrom

Yor. Petrss therefore humbly pray Yor Worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeall

And Yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
