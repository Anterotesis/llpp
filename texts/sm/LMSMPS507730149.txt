To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of The Reverend Sir George Booth Baronet and William Blackborow Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the second Day of September 1783 Catherine Townsend was removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Midddlesex to the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great aforesaid Lodging the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great to be the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determining of this Appeal And to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre aforesaid and all persons concerned Do then Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of your Worships in the Premises And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Evans Solicitor for the Appellants.
