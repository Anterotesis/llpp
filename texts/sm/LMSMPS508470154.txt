To his Majesties Justices of the Peace Assembled at the General Quarter Sessions to be held at Hicks Hall in and for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of Appeal of George Bower

Sheweth That by an Order of Session dated the 7th. day of May 1789. Under the hands and Seals of John Brettell & N. Conant Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting and inhabiting in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex and the Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the said County Your Petitioner and Appeallant was adjudged the Putative Father of the Male Wastard Child born of the Body of Mary Child formerly Mary Champer Single Woman and was thereby Ordered to Pay to the Overseers of the Poor of the above Parishes the Sum of Four Pounds Nine Shillings and Six Pences by them alledged to be expended for the releif of & Maintenance of the said Child and was thereby also Ordered in Future to Pay the said Overseers the weekly Sum of three Shilliings & Six Pence towards the Maintenance of the said Child so long as the same should remain charged the

That Your Petitioner and Appeallant conceiving himself to be materially injured and agrieved by the said Order of the said Justices is advised to appeal therefrom And therefore

Your Petitioner & Appeallant humbly Prays that such his Appeal may be received and Entered at the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace or the adjournment thereof and that a day may be appointed for hearing and determing the same

And Your Petr. shall ever Pray
