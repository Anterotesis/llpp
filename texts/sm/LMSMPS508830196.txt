To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Peals of Patrick Colquhoun and J. Hole Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 30th Day of March 1793 Susannah Bird Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

Jospeh Gremill Thos Wallis Wm Dodds Joseph Knight John Strachan John Arnold [...] John Scott Robt Tabor Tho Bates} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor
