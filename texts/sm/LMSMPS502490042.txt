To the Right Worshipfull His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of John Milner & John [...] of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of [...] (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twelfth Day of this instant January, Anne Wilson the Widow of Andrew Wilson deceased was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx aforesaid unto the Parish of St. Bottolph without Bishopgate London alledging the said Parish of St. Bottolph without Bishopsgate to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Peticoner Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to your Worps therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, and order That the Churchwardn: & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parrish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day by your Worps to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgment [...] your Worps touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc.
