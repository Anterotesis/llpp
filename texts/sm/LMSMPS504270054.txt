To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or order Under the hands & Seals of Boulton Mainwaring and Thomas Jervis Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Twenty Second day of March last Elizabeth Kinchin and her two Children Giles & Mary Kinchin were removed and Conveyed from and out of the sd Parish of Saint George in the County of Middx into the sd Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx as the place of their last legal settlement.

Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved of by the said Order of the said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the Same And pray this Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. George aforesaid in the sd. County of Middx And all other parties Concerned to Attend this Court to hear & abide the judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petr shall ever pray Etc

J. Clare Sollr. for the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph witht Aldgate Middx
