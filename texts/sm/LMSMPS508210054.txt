To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Gordon and William Addington Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) being Date the Twenty Ninth Day of March last John Miller was removed and conveyed from and out of the liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

F. Booth, Solr for Appellants
