To the Right Worshippfull theire Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth

The humble peticon of Eliz Kaywork a Very poor wooman of the Parish of St. James Clerken well in this County

That one Johannah Ross wth. two Small Children She being bigg wth. another child (were passed) from out of the Sash of St James Clerken well in this County Feo the Parish of St Sepulchers wthout in the County aforesaid By Warrt. Under the hands & Seales of two of theire Matie: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx and accordingly were Received by Christopher Pound Constable of the Sd. Parish of St. Sepulchers wch Sd. Constable came and Sollicited yr Peticonr. to give them Entertainment for Some Small time purseing you peticonr. paymt. for ye Same wch hath not been pformed by the Sd. Mrs Pound the Constable to the great damage of Jno. Peticonr.

That Since the aboad of the sd. Johannah Ross: (at ye peticonrs house) She hath Miscarryed log a feemale child wch Imediately dyed Hoe She could not be removed, by wch means all the three persons wholey Relyes Upon ye. Peticonr for Support and Maintenance wthout wch they [...] must Parish, they haveing noe releife as was praised by the Sd. Constable, by wch ill doeings of the Sd. Constable & other Officers, Your poor peticonr: is out of pockets three pounds as by bill annexed may Justly appeare

Therefore yr. poor peticonr: most humble beggs of his Honble. Court (the premisses Considered) to grant an Order for ye Sd. Christopher Pound the Constable to appeare here to their cause why he Should not pay or Satisfye Your poor peticonr. for her charge and honbled above Specifyed or otherwise to Order Releife herein as your Worshipps Shall Seem meet Etc

And your poor peticonr. as in duly bound Shall pray Etc
