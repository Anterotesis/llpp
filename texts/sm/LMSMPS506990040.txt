To ye: Worspll: her Maties: Justices of ye: peace For ye. County of Mids:

The humble peticon & Appeal of ye. churchwardens and Overseers of ye. poor of ye. parish of Loughton in ye. County of Essex.

Sheweth That by in Warrants under ye. hands & Seales of Robt: Constable & Rogr: Smith Esqrs Two of her Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) bearinge date: ye. Nineteenth day of December Last Margaret Wroth wife of Charles Wroth was removed from & out of ye. parish of St. Botolph Aldgate in ye. Sd. County of Mids. to ye. parish of Loughton aforesd. therein alledging that her Sd. husband Charles Worth was Last Legally Setled there & where he was borne & Lived many yeares:

That your peticonrs: thinks themselves agreived wth: ye. Judgmt: of ye. Sd. Two Justices of ye. peace & humbly appeal against ye. Sd. Warrant: For that your peticoners doubles not but they with make it plainely appeare to this Honable: Court that ye. Sd Charles Wroth hath gained as Legall Setlemt. in mother parish the Since his Liveinge ye. Sd. parish of Loughton.

Your peticonrs: therefore humbly pray your Worsp will be pleased to grant on Order of this Honable: Court that ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. parish of St. Botolph Aldgate aforesd. May Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to heare & abide ye Judgmt: & finall determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And your peticoners shall ever pray Etc:

Script: P: J: Story

John Wheeler} Church Warden
