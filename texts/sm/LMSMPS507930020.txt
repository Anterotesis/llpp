To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petitioner and Appeal of the Churchwardesn and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Strand in the Liberty of Westminster and said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of pass Warrant made under the Hands [...] of Sir Sampsn Wright [...] & I hearing Esqr two of this [...] of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex [...] of the Quorum) bearing date the [...] Instant - Mary Ireland was Removed and Conveyed four and out of the parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary le Strand as the place of the last legal Settlement of her the said Mary Ireland Whereby Your Petitioner Conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitoners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc

T. Youd Sollr for the Appeall
