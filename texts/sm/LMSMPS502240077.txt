Middx ss To the right Worppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace at their genall Quarter Sessions now assembled in and for the said County of Middx

the Humble petcon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Michael Wood Street London

Sheweth That by virtue of apuss Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of John Milner and R Gifford Esqr Two of his Matyes Justices of the peace of this County (Quor unus) & bearing date the Fourth day of November last Bernard Reeves Son of John Reeves was removed from the paris of Saint Giles in the Feilds in the said County unto the parish of of saint michaell wood street [...] aforesaid as the place of his last legall Setlement By which Order your peticoners conceive themselves Aggreived and humbly appeales therefrom

Yor petnonr therefore pray this Honrble Cant to appoynt [...] day for the hearing the Merritts of this Appeale and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the said parish of Saint Gyles in the Feilds may Attend and abide the Determinacon and Judgment of the Court touching the same

And your petr. shall ever pray Etc.
