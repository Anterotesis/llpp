To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petion and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Buckland in the County of Berks.

Sheweth That by Virtue of and Order under the hands and Seals of John Upton and William Timbrell Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster dated the 25th. day of May last John Mills Mary his Wife and their three Children (Vizt.) Mary aged four Years Elizabeth Aged upwards of two Years and William Aged about a Year [...] were removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Buckland by which Order Your Petitioners Apprehend the said Parish of Buckland are agreived

Your Petitrs. therefore humbly Pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing of their said appeal And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
