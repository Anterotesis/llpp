To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Generall Quarter Session

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Cowley in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order Under the hands & Seales of John Ellis and Oliver Lambert Esqrs. two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlx ( [...] one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d. day of this Instant June Anne Omer Singlewoman was removed from the parish St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the Said County of Middx to the said parish of Cowley in the Same County as the place of her last leg at Settlement

Yor. Peticoners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeale there from and pray that this Court will be pleased to Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster to Attend the Court Some day this Sessions. to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
