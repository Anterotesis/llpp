To the Rt: Worpll: his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Genall Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Peticion and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Ann in the Libty of Westmr: within this County of Middx:

Sheweth That Mary Hutchinson Widdow late wife of Wittin Hutchinson deceased, and Two Children (Vizt) Thomas aged about Ele [...] yeare and William Hutchinson aged about Eight years, were by Order dated the 24th: day of June last 1723: under the hands and Seals of Phillip Willshin and Stephen Martin Leakt Esqr. Two of his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middx(Quor unus) removed from the Hamlet of Lime house in the Parish of Stepney in the sd County to the aforesd Parish of St: Ann in the sd Libtye of Westmr.

Your Peticonr: thereby humbly conceives themselves agrreied and appeal from the Order of the sd Justices of the peace to the Indgment of this Honbls: Court and crave a day of hearing so as the St. Mary Hutchinson & her sd Two Children may be so Settled as upon hearing this Honble: Court shall think fitt:

And Yout Petr: shall ever pray Etc:
