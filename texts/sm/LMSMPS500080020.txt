The Right Worple the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of the Constables Headbouroughs and other Ancient Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County.

Sheweth [...] noe Watchhouse within the said Parish wherein to Shelter [...] (when on their Watch) from the extremity of the weather [...] nt of a Watchhouse in the said Parish hath already occasioned [...] of severall of the Inhabitants thereof Soe that your Peticoners [...] not Constable duly to execute the said Offices without hazard of their [...]

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Pray your Worspps to be pleased to Order that a Watchhouse may be erected in the said parish neere the Whiping post there.

Any your Peticoners Shall ever Pray etc.
