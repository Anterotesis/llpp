To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex assembled at their General Quarter Session of the Peace began & holden the 13th day of January in the 34th Year of King George the third

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and John Scott Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fourth day of December 1793 Jane Hunt Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement and your Petitioners were required to provide for her according to Law whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal Against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises and that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in these present Session for the hearing and determining this their Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
