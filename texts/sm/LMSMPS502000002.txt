To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Petition of George Anderson

Humbly Sheweth

That as of last Sessions your Peticoner was- Indicted by the Name of Guy Anderson and convicted for Keeping a Disorderly house For which Offence Your Petitioner was fined five pounds and Comitted to the House of Correction. at Clerkenwell to hard Labour for the space of Three Months

That Your Peticoner hath Wife and Two small Children to Mainteine Who are Destitute of any friends or have they any Substance to support then but must Inevitably starve if your peticoner longer Imprisoned and hindred from working for his said Family

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Consider the Calamity of his Family and to be pleased to discharge him from his said Imprisonment.

And yor. petr. Shall Ever pray Etc. George Anderson
