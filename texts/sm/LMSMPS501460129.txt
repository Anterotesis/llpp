To the Right Worl: his Maes: Justices of peace for the County of Middx Sitting at His

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poore for the Hamlett of Sune he in the Pish of Stepney in the said of Middx

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the Hands & Seales of Robert Kyrby & Bastwich Johnson Esqrs two of his Mais: Justices of the peace for the Said County of (Quos un) bearing Date the 4th: day of this instant Months of July Hannah Sattimore wife of Hugh Sattimore three Children the Eldest names Hannah Sattimore nine years & a halfe the next names Hugh Sattimore aged three yeares & a halfe the Youngest names William Sattimore aged four Months were Conveyed From that Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney in the said County to the Hamlett of Linphour of said as the place of their Last Legall settlemt:

Four Petrs: Finding themselves aggreived by the Said Order of [...] of the said two Justices Humbly appear agat: the Same & Pray your Worpp will be pleased to appoint a day this present Sessions for the Hearing and

Determining of the said appeale And that your Petrs: may be releived in the premises in Such manner as unto yr Worpps: Shall Seeme meet

And yr: Petrs: Shall ever pray
