To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of st Andrew Holborn in the city of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the quoru) bearing date the Fourth day of March Anno Dui 1716/17 Ann Humfryes Widdow and John Humfryes her Son aged seventeen years or Upwards were removed from the parish of Tottenham High cross in the said County unto the said parish of st Andrew Holborn alleadging the said parish to be the Place of their last legal Settlement That your Petrs: conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Order of the said two Justices do most humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitionrs therefore most humbly pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Tottenham High cross may Appear before you and hear and abide the Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs: Shall ever pray & ca.
