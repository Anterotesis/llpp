To the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Fludger [...] Baronet Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of His Majesty's Justices Assigned to deliver the Goal of Newgate of the Prisoners therein being

The humble Petition of John White

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the 15th. Day of December last was by Virtue of a Warrant of Commitment under the Hand and Seal of Sir John Fielding Knight one of his Majesties Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex Committed to his Majesties Goal of Newgate charged upon the Oath of the Revd. Mr. Lewis Hughes and Martin Lewis upon Suspicion of having feloniously taken and Carried away one Diamond Ring one Indian Bond and other things the property of the said Mr. Hughes

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that he may be brought to his Tryal at this present Session for the Supposed Felony whereof he stands charged Or that he may be Bailed or Discharged pursuant to the Statute in such Case made and provided

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray

John White
