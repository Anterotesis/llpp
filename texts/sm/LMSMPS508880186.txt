To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseer of the poor of the parish of Lacton otherwise Latton in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands & seals of N. Bond and A. Mitchell Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of May 1793 Elizabeth Cook singlewoman being then Pregnant with Child was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of Lacton otherwise Latton as the Place of her last legal settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises and that your Worships will be pleased to Order that the benefit of their said Appeal Shod. be saved unto them and the hearing and determining thereof [---] be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex and that a day should be Appointed for that purpose

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
