To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of Emannel Johnson of the parish of Edmonton in this County labourer

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Hassell and Honoral Smith Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the second day of July 1734 Yor. Petr. Emannel Johnson was adjudged to be the putative Father of a female Bastard Child lately born of the Body of Elizabeth Pegram of the parish of Fryern Barnet in this County singlewoman, which for some time was chargeable to the said parish; And that the said Justices by their said Wart. did Order yor. Petr. Forthwith to pay to the Churchwardens & Oversers of the poor of the sd parish of Fryern Barnet the Sume of two pounds four shillings and ten pence towards the lying in of the sd Elizabeth, and burying the sd Bastard, and other Charges

That Yor. Petr. finding himselfe aggrieved by the sd Order and Judgment of the sd two Justices humbly Appeales agt. the same

And prays yor. Worships would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of Fryern Barnet may have Notice of this Appeale, and that they and all parties concerned may attend this Court some day this pr.sent qr. ter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor. Petr. shall pray [...]

Emall Johnson
