To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above Barrs in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Arthur Chaddock was by Order dated the first day of September now last past under the hands and Seales of William Burdon and Thomas Scott Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorum Unus) removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Libty of Westmr: in the said County to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above barrs

Your Peticonrs. humbly conceives themselves aggrieved thereby and appeal from the Judgment of the said two Justices to the Judgment of this Honourable Court & Crave a day of Hearing

And Your Peticonrs: Etc
