To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of John Broster

Sheweth. That your Petr. was last Sessions Indicted in this Honrble: Court for defrauding Ann the Wife of William Batlin of a Guinea.

That yor. Petr. being a Poor man and not haveing wherewith to Try his Traverse did Yesterday [...] plead Guilty to the said Indictment and was Fined Two Pounds three Shillings.

That your Petr. hath made the Prosecutor full Satisfaction and hath a Release to produce to this Court to testifie the same.

Yor. Petr: there fore humbly Prays that yor. Worspps will be pleased to Mittigate his Fine in such manner as to yor. Worspps shall seem meet

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
