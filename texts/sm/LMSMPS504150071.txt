To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of White Waltham in the County of Berks

Sheweth Hely Attorney for Appellants That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Milner Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourteenth day of November One Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty One Mary Hester Widow of West Hester deced and her Two Children by her said late Husband Vizt. Sarah aged Two Years and upwards and Frances aged about Six Weeks was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx to the said Parish of White Waltham as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Holy Attorney for Appellants Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgment of the said two Justices And Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all Persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
