To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Butler of Barrowswell Green near Winchmore Hill [---] the Parish of Edmonton in the County of Middlesex Higler.

That your Petitioner hath dwelt in the said County upwards of Three years last past Is a married man and an Householder and of the Age of Thirty years and upwards. and three Children now livg.

That your Petitioner having a Family to maintain is desirous of using the Trade or Business of a Badger Higler or Buying and Selling of Butter Cheese Poultry and such like kind of Victuals for their Support

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to grant him a Licence for that purpose

And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray

The Mark of Wm. [x] Butler

2d May 1767.
