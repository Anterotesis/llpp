To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor and Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of George Wrighte and John Spinnage Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the third day of this Instant June Mary Wells Spinster aged about thirty years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said pass Warrant or Order and Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Benjn. Gee W Willaims John Milbourne Richd Nowlson} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
