To the Right Worpl. there Matier Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Peticon of William Parkin

Sheweth

That in sometime past yor Petr became bayle for the appearame of Frances Hinton in this Honble Court as of this put Sessions As thereby may appeare That yor. Petr. was yesterday comitted to Newprison by the Worpl. Justice Perry where he still remainer for giving the sd Frances Hinton liberty to goe about her lawfull occacons And the said Frances Hinton being now in the bayle Dons

Yo Petr. therefore most humbly prausye Worpps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from the sd. Imprisonmt.

And yor. Petr Shall over pray Etc.
