To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order, under the Hands and Seals of John Sherwood and Jasper Clarke Esquires two of his. Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County (one whereof being) of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Second Day of September last Mary Pigeon was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Pigeon whereby your Petitioner Conceive themselves to be agrieved

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication and order of the send two Justices And Pray your Worships- will Appoint a Day this present Session for the hearing & determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioner will pray Etc

Jno: Skutt. [...] [...]
