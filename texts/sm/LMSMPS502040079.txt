To the Right Worpl his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrews Holborn London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt under the hands & Seals of Matt [...] Hewitt & Wm Cotesworth Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 26th day of May 1722. Mary Vallet was removed & passd by ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of [---] the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx unto the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Andrews Holborn London Alledging the Said Parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Peticoners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices And do most humbly Appeal to Your Worships therefrom

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly pray your Worps. will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Sd Parish may appear before you on Such day as this Court Shall appoint within is Sessions to hear & abide the Determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Your Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc
