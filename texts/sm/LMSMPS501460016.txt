To the Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Henry Randall and Joseph Pettitt.

Humbly Sheweth

That your Peticoners are the Prsent Scavengers of and for the Liberty of Church End and Hexton in the Parish of St. Leonard Shoredith in the County of Middx that the officers and Inhabitant have rated and attested a book for your Peticoners to Collect, but the Same being not attested according to a pound Rate the greatest part of the Inhabitants refuse to pay

The Prmisses Considered your Peticoners humbly pray your Worpps to grant them Such releife therein as to your grase wisdoms Shall Seen meet

And your Peticoners as in duty pound Shall pray etc
