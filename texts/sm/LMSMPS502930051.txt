To the Right Worspl. His Majestys Justices of the Peace at Their General Quarter Sessions assembled for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the Said County

Sheweth That by a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Clifford William Phillips and James Brain Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the Said County of Middx (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d day of May 1733 Mary Simpson the Widdow of John Simpson deceased was remov'd from the Parish of Saint Mary (White Chappel) in the Said County to the Said Parish of Saint George as the place of her last legal Settlement

That You Peticoners think themselves Aggriev'd by the Said Pass Warrant or Order And humbly appeale to this Court Against the same

Therefore Your Peticoners humbly pray this Court to Appoint a day in this General Quarter Sessions that att Parties may then Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of the Court touching this Appeal

Sam Cowper Attorney for Appellents

And Your Petr. Shall ever Pray
