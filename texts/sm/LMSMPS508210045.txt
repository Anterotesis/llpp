To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Court of Quarter Sessions at the Sessions House in and for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth Unto this Court that by Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Herbt Mayo Doctor in Divinity and W Blackmore Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fifteenth day of January last past Ann Bois Widow of Henry Bois deceased And her two Children Vizt. Henry Bois aged five years and Ann Bois aged fifteen Months were removed from the Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint NIcholas Deptford in the said County of Kent as the Place of their last legal Settlement by which we think ourselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Order

Wherefore your Petitioners pray that this Honorable Court will be pleased to grant an Order for all Parties to attend this Court in order to Hearing the Complaint and Defence of both Parties by Evidence and to make such further Order thereon as the Courts hall think proper

And your Petitioners shall over pray Etc.

Jas. Elliott Anty Smith} Church Wardens of the said Parish of St Nicholas Deptford

Richd Batty Jno. Denickson} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish
