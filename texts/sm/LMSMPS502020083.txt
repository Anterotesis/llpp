To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeale of Mottershead of Muzzle Hill in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Your Petitioner lives at MuzzleHill aforesaid and Rents Some Land belonging to the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County for which the Offices have Charged your Petitioner to the Scavengers Rate in the said Parish, That your Petitioner Conceives he Ought not to pay the said Rate and therefore [...] Appeale agst the samee

And humbly prays Such releife in the Prmises at this Honble Court shall think meet

And Yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
