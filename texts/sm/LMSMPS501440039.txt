To the Rt Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of William Goodwin Constable of Endfield in the said County

Sheweth

That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr at Lady day Last for Passcith one Relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds.

That your petitr. hath been great Charge in Conveying Vagrants the Last Quarter & is a poor man And hath a great Family to mainteyne and stands in great want of the said Money.

Your Petitr. therefore humbly prays Yor Worsppll. will be pleased to Order that he may be paid the said Money As unto Your Worsppl Shall Seeme meete

And Yor. Petitr shall ever pray Etc.

Wm Goodwin
