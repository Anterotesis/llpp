To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Cheshunt in the County of Hertford.

Sheweth

That by Vertue of a Warrt. or Order under the hand and Seals of Thomas Exelbee & Alexander Garrett Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) dated the nineteenth day of May last William Pett Mary his Wife and their two Children namely William aged eight years and Edward aged about three years were removed and conveyed from the parish of Christ Church in the said [...] County of Middx to the said parish of Cheshnut in the county of Hertford as the last legal place of Settlement of the said William pett Mary his Wife and their two Children

That your petitioners think themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of Removal and appeal to this Court against the same.

your petitioners therefore humbly pray that this Court will appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And your petitioners Shall pray Etc

Churchwardens{ Edw Casdell Samll Woodward

Overseers{ Edwd: Jones Joseph Noon
