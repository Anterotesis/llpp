To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Quarrill and John Staples Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of April last Peter Oyens and Jane his- Wife were Removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved and Humbly- Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be Pleased to hear them in the Premises and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel may Appear before you at a Certain day to be Appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc M. Wright Sollr for the Applts.
