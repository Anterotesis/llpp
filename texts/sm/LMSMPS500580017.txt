To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of ye. Peace for ye County of Middx.

Sheweth The humble Petition of his appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Stephen in the City of Bristol

That day Warrant under the Hand & Seales of two of his maties Justices of the Peace for this County one Thomas Cox an Infant Son of Thomas Cox Dent was passed from the parish of St. St. Paul Shadwell in the Sd. County to the Sd. Parish of St. Stephen upon the Oath of Johanna Twilt that the Sd. Thomas Cox the Father was last legally Settled in the Sd. Pish of St. Stephen where he was by Indenture an appentice to one Mr. Bateman a Sailemakers in the Sd Pish of St. Stephen.

That the sd. Thos. Cox the Father was were legally Settled in the Sd pish of St. Stephen as yor. Petr. can make appeare by Severall credible Wittnesse Soe that they humbly conceive themselves not by able provide for the Sd Tho: Cox the Son

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor Worspps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Sd Parish of St. paul Shadwell may appeare in this Houble Court this Prd Sessions So that cause relay the Sd Tho Cox the Son Should not be repasse [...] Sd Pish of St paul shadwell

And Yor. Petr. [...] Pray Etc
