To the Right Worpll his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Great Shelford in the County of Cambridge.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seals of Thos Tolboys & Ralph Harwood Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 8th Day of February [---] Instant Susana Burnett Widdow late wife of Barnett als Barnaby Barnett [...] Deced as fishman wth: her two Children (Vizt) Eleanor Aged abt. two Years & Barnet aged abt eight Weeks the lawfull Issue of the sd Barnet als Barnaby Barnet [...] Deced & the sd Susana Barnett were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx to the Parish of great Shelford in the County of Cambridge alledging [...] therein that the sd Parish of Great Shelford is the Place of the last Legall Settlemt. of the sd Susana Barnett & her two Children.

Thos Elmes Agent For the Appellants Yor Petrs Finding themselves aggreiv'd by the Order of the sd two Justices humbly Appeale agt the same.

And Forasmuch as Yor Petrs cannot be ready to prosecute their sd Appeal this prsent Sessions humbly pray that your Worps. wou'd be pleased to save to your Petrs. the benefitt of the sd Appeal & that the same may be adjorned ever untill the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace for this County Yor Petrs. therefore humbly pray that all parties concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some Day the next Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor Petrs. as in Duty bound shall pray Etc
