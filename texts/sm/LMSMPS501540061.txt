To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of martha Staper of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell In the sd County Widow

Sheweth That your peticoner is rated Twelve Shillings this present year towards the relief of the poor of the said Parish

That your peticoner is a poor Woman & Destitute of any Manner of Busynesse & is not Able to pay the said rate or any part thereof & appeals against the Same.

Yor Petr of therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to order that she may be wholly Discharged from paying to the sd Poors rate.

And Yor: Petr Shall Ever pray etc Martha Staper
