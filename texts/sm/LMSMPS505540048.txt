To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John Zachary in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cowley and John Spencer Colepeper Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of September last Ann Elcomb Single Woman was Removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint John Zachary in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Elcomb

Your Petitioners apprehending themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do humbly Appeal against the same And pray that a Day in this present Session may be appointed for the Hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray [...]

John Beard Attorney for the Appellants
