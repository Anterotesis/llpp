To the Rt. Worpll: his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now in Quartr: Session assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church: wardens and Overseers. of the poor of the parish of st. Ann in the Libtyr of Westmr.

Sheweth That Willm Clincot, Elizabeth his wife with two Children vizt: Ann aged about Nine Years, and Mary aged about Nine Months, were by Order dated the 30th: day of November last under the Hands and seals of J Hayne and Richd Newton Esqrs. Two of his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middx (Quor unus) removed and conveyed from the parish of St: Clement's Danes in the aforesd County of Middx to the parish of St. Ann in the sd Libtye of Westmr.

Your Petionr thereby humbly conceives themselves agrieved, and appeals from the Judgment of the sd Two Justices to the Judgment of this Court, and crave a day of hearing Etc.

And Your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
