To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace now Assembled.

The Humble Petition of John Cousins

Sheweth That on Easter Monday last Mr. John Townsend one of the Present Constables of the Parish of St. Mary Islington gave Notice to your Petitioner who is an Inhabitant of the said Parish within the Preband Liberty thereof that he your Petitioner was designd. by the said Townsend to be put [...] Nomination to succeed him in that Office for the year next Ensueing

That your Petitioner is of the Age of Fifty Six Years very Lame Week & Infirm and not Capable of Executing the Office of Constable should he be there to Chosen being Exe [...] ly think of [...]

Mr. Townsend was Requested by Several of the Inhabitants to Nominate the said Mr. Con [...] who was at that time a mear Stranger to him And had Mr. Townsend at the time he did so Nominate him known of his Infirmitys he had not have put him in Nomination

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worships for the Reasons aforesd. that he may be Excused form Serving the said Office.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

John Cousins

His now Nomination Joseph Markland Thos. Blunt
