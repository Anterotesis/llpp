To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Gewall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Stephens Walbrook in the City of London,

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqrs Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unns) bearing Date the Eighteenth day of July last past Marth [...] Taylor Wife of Peter Taylor with their three Children (Vizts) Thomas Six years old Robert three years old and Peter abt one year & a half old were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid unto the Said Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook to be place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to your Worships therefrom And humbly pray That your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain day by your Worships to be appointed and hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc
