To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the said County [...]

Sheweth That on the twenty Seventh day of October last John Harding a third aged Seve [...] years was passed from the parish of Hornsey in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sd. Edward Gould Knt & Alexander Ward Esqr two of Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) as the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said John Harding

Your Peticonrs: thinke themselves aggrieved wth the Judgement of the Said two Justices to Appeal to this Worpll: Bench and humbly pray the prmises considered that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Hornsey & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

P J Tyson

And yor Peticonrs: shall ever pray Etc.
