To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Smith of the Parish of St. Pancras in the sd. County Widow.

Sheweth That for the Space of Four yeares past your Petr. was allowed 8sh. month as a Pencon from the sd. Pish But very Lately Paymt. is Stopt there of By reason of Which your Petr. must Inevitably Parish Haveing wholly lost the first of One of her Eyes Dim of the Other ya most [...] lost the use of her Limbs

Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly prayes your Worspps to be Pleased to Order that She may be allowed such Pencon from the sd. Pish As but your Worpps shall seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray etc

Eliz: Smith her & mke

P Tho: Nash
