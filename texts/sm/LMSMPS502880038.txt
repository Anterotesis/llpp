To the Right Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Dated the 20th. day of December last under the Hands and Seals of Stephen Martin Leake and James Brain Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx One John Holmden and Christian his wife were removed from the Hamlett of Ratcliff in the said County to the abovesaid Parish of St. Paul Shadwell as to the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petrs. humbly conceive themselves Aggrieved [...] by the said Order of Remove all and pray a Day for hearing the said Appeal

And shall pray Etc.
