To the Right Worll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the pish of St Clement Danes in the County

Sheweth

That on the thirteenth day of May last past one Susanna Haines being [...] of the small pox was passed from the pish of St Mary White- Chappell in the said County to the Said pish St Clement Danes by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Robert Bateman & John Webber Esqrs two of his Maties. Justices of the peace of the said County thereby alleadging that the said Susanna Haines was last legally settled as a hired Servant by the year wth one Sarah Wright in Duke Street in the said pish of St Clement Danes

Your petrs: thinke themselves agrieved wth the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeale to this Worll: Bench & pray that you will be pleased to Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of White Chappell to appear in the Court some day the next quarter sessions to hear & abide the finall Judgment & determinacon of this [...] Bench touching this Appeale.

And yor petr shall er pray Etc

Mic: Lucas} Churchwardens

John: Dorrills John Bussel
