To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of [...] Chelsey in this County

Sheweth

That by order dated the 28th day of October last under the hands & seals of St John Elwes Kt and Richard Woodward Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County (quor unus) one Thomas White & Lidia his Wife were removed from the pish of Fullham in this County to the said Parish of Chelsey the said order alledging that the said Thomas White was last legally setting [...] the sd pish of Chelsey where he lived a hired servt by the yeare after the rate of Â£7 p ann with one Christofer Grinsted als Grinston a gardiner about 5 years since & served his said master about three months & then was impressed for his maties service & hath not had any other settlemt since as aforesd

Yor petrs find [...] themselves aggreived by the order of the said twoe Justices & removall of the said Thomas White & his wife from the said pish of Fulham to the said pish of Chelsey and therefore appeal agt the sd recited order of the said two Justices & pray a day may be appointed for the hearing & detmineing of the sd appeal & that all psons concerned may be ordered to attend accordingly to hear & abide the judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the sd appeal

P J Rolfe

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc
