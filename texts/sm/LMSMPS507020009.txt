To the Rt. Worpll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Deverell and Thomas Cooper

Sheweth That for the Facts whereof your Petrs. stood Indicted in this Honourable Court this present Session Your Worshipps were pleased to Committ them both to New prison in this County Where they now remained

That [...] Satisfaction hath been made to the Prosecuter on Account of the said Fact committed by the Doorelle Cooper And for as much as they being both Apprenties And haveing noe Friends on Whome to Depend in Towne And humbly Subwriting themselves to your Worshipps Great Goodness Mercy

P Tho: Nash

Your Petr therefore most humbly pray your Worships to be Pleased to Mitigate the Said Fines as unto Your Worshipps Shall Seem meete

And yor Petrs shall ever Pray etc
