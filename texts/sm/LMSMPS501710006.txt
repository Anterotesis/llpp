To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and appeale of Deborah Edge of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County Widdow.

Sheweth. That your Petr. is rated towards the releife of the poor of the Said parish Fifty pounds for Psonall Estate And alsoe for her house which She rents in the Said parish Ten pounds P ann when She pays but Eight pounds for the Same, and that part of the house which is new built, is less then before when the old appartment was Standing

Therefore your Petr thinks herselfe aggreived by the Said Rate and appeales agt the Same.

Humbly prays She may be releived in the premises at your Worps Shall Seem meet

And She Shall pray Etc.
