To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices Now sitting at Hicks hall for the [...] County of Middx

The Humble petition of Ebeneazer Green aver poorman now in Clarkenwell bride well on Comittment by the worshipfull Justice Miller to Hard Labour & lyes for his Fees

Therefore your poor [...] petitioner Humbley prays that the Honrable bench will Commisseritt yr petitioner Case haveing Neither Frends or Money & lyes here on the bare boards in a Starveing Condition Your poor petitioner begs yr. Honrs. will be pleased to ordr yr petitioner his Mr Large -ment and your petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray
