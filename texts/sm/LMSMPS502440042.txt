To the Rt. Worppll. his Majtys: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of East Greenwich in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by warrant or Order dated the 21st: day of Aprill 1727 under the hands & Seals of Francis Sorell and Alexander Heirdine Esqrs two of his Majty: Justices of the Peace for the Said County and City & Liberty of Westmr (Quor un) Elizabeth Ward with her two Children Mary and Jonathan were Removed from the Parish of St. Margaretts Westminster to the said Parish of East Greenwich as the place of the last legall Setlemt. of the said Elizabeth Ward & her said two Children

That your Petitionrs: humbly Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order of the said two Justices & Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray a day may be Appointed this present Sessions for all parties to Attend to hear & abide the Judgment order & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc

Thomas Martley Edmd Stagg} Churchwardens
