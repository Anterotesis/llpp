To the [...] Citty of London [...] the Justice [...] att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

The humble Peticon of Henry Arrow smith att Hunt

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: was comitted to the Goale of Newgate the 25th. day of June last by Warrt: from St. Michaell Cole Warrt: charged with Murdering of Christopher Ladbroke and that yor. Peticoner is now a Prisoner in the said Goale

The humbly prayeth to bee tryed this Sessions for the Start he Stands charged with or that he may itt admitted to Baile as by the Law is provided and that this his prayer may bee Recorded

And he Shall ever pray Etc
