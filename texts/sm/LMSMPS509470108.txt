To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Bleamire, and Aaron Graham, two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster and County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th: day of November last Mary the Illegitimate Child of Elizabeth East Singlewoman aged upwards of twelve Months, was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above Bars in the aforesaid County to the parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices

Justices and their said Order And pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to the Petitioners, and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to the next General Session of the peace to be holden in and for this County to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

W Yarnold

Alexr Gordon Isaac Hitchin William Reid
