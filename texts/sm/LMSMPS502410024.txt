To the Right Worpll his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Peter Glagor Son of Stephen Glagor of the Parish of St Anns Westmr.

Sheweth That by Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing date the twenty first Day of January 1723 Yor Petr. became an Apprentice to Abraham Foisseau Engraver for the term of Seaven Years Commencing from the Date of the sd Indenture as thereby Appeares.

That your Petrs. Master hath greatly neglected Instructing your Petrs. in his sd Art Mistery & provideing for him as an Apprentice as menconed in the sd Indenture

To answer which Compt. the sd Foisseau stands bound by Recognizance before John Ellis Esqr one of his Maties: Justices of the peace for this County.

Therefore your Petr humbly prays that this Court wou'd appoint some Day this Sessions for his sd Master to attend & shew Cause why your Petr shou'd not be discharged from his sd Apprentice hood.

And your Petr as in Duty bound shall pray etc

Peter Glagor
