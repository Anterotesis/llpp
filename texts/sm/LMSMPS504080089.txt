To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pas Warrt. or Order under the hands and Seals of James Fraser and Edward ashe Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in this Court (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th. Day of January 1750 Mary Hester Widow (of William Hester deceased) and her Child (to wit) Sarah aged one Year and ten Months were removed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays this Court will please to save them the benefit of this appeal and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned untill the next general Quarters session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex. and that all persons concerned may then attend

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Wadm Wyndham Churchwardens

John Wharton S Wilson} Overseers of the Poor
