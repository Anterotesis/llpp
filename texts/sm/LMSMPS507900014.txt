To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace, Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Pass Warrt. or order under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Hawkins and John Staples Esquires two of [...] Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date Twenty fifth day of November last. Diana Fuller Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner conceiveing themselves aggrieved by the said warrant or order Do Humbly appeal against to the same And pray Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session that all parties concerned may attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc

[...] Sollr. for the Appellts

Middlesex At the General Session of the peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden at the Session House in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid on Monday the sixth day of December in the Twenty fifth year of the reign of our Sovereign Lord george the third King of Great Britain Frances and Ireland Defender of the Faith next

It is Ordered by this Court that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex have Notice of this Appeal and that they And all persons Concerned Do attend this Court on Three day next Nine [...] o Clock in the forenoon to hear and a bid the Judgment and [...] ation of this Court Touching the said Appeals

By the Court. Selby.
