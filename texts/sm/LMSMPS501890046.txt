To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and appeale of James Worgaine of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth That your Petr is rated towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for One hundred and fifty pounds personall Estate when he hath no Such. There fore your Petr think himself aggreived by the Said Rate and appeals agt the Same.

Your Petr prays your Worps to releive him in the prmes as your Shall Seam meet

And your Petr Shall pray Etc. James Worgaine
