To the Right Worpll. His Maties. Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chiswick in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seales of John Birch John Smith Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the Seventeenth day of December last past John Brown Ann his wife and their Children (Vizt) Elizabeth Brown aged about thirteen [---] John Aged about twelve Years, Henry aged about Nine Yeares Mary Aged about five Yeares, Benjamine Aged about three Yeares And Joseph under the Age of One Yeare were removed from the Hamlet of Hamersmith in the parish of Fulham in the said County to the said Parish of Chiswick as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr thinking themselves aggrieved wth the Judgmt of the said two Justices humbly appeale therefrom [...]

Jas. Tyton And pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamlett of Hamersmith & all parties Concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to Shew Cause why Yor. Petrs. should not have Such releife in the premises as to Yor. Worships great Wisdom shall Seem meet

And Yor Petrs. as in Duty bound shall ever pray
