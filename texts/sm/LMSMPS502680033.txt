To the Right Worpll. His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll. Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hanwell in the County of Middx aforesaid

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands & Seales of John Milner & Richard Gifford Esqr. two of His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County Quor unus bearing date the fifth day of May instant Elizabeth Hill aged tenn yeares Thomas Hill aged about three yeares Mary Hill aged about two yeares Anne aged five yeares Children of William Hill and Mary his wife Deced were removed from the Parish of St Giles in the fields in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said parish of Hanwell as the place of their last legall Settlement

J Tyton Sol & appe [...] . Yor. Petr. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said Justices appeale therefrom and Pray that this Court will Please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Giles in the Fields & all persons Concerned to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
