To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Enfield in the said County

Sheweth That on or about the twenty first day of July last one Elizabeth the wife of Thomas Wakefield was found Wandring and begging in the said Parish And being sent to the Workhouse there was delivered of a Female Child baptized by the Name of Ann whereby She and her said Child became Chargeable to the Sd. Parish

That Yor Petrs. did detain the said Woman in their Workhouse until the 12th. day of August last when they Conveyed her before Merry Teshmaker Esqr. one of His Majesty's Justices of ye. peace for this County who upon Exam- ination of ye. sd. Vagrant did by a Pass under his Hand & Seal order & direct her to be Conveyed to the Parish of Jarro in the Bishoprick of Durham being the place of her last legall Settlement

Humphr Elmes for ye Petrs. Yor Petr. therefore humbly Pray that Yor. Worships will be Pleas'd to order the Trear of this County to pay yor. Petrs. such a Sum of money as you shall Adjudge a reasonable Satisfaction for the Charges the sd. Parish has been put unto on Accot. of the lying in of ye sd. Elizth Wake fold And Yor Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
