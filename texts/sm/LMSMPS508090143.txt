Middlesex September Sessions To William Mainwaring Esqr and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for these County of Middlesex

The Petition of John Tiler and Richard Mead Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of he Parish of Saint Mary Aldermary London

Sheweth That Whereas By an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Gascoigne and James Croft Esquires two of his Majesty Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex, Pryce Devereux Prosser, Finances his wife and their two Children to wit John Aged man two years and Catherine aged one Year and upwards were removed from the send Parish of Saint Anne within the liberty of Western in the said County of Middlesex, to your Petitioners said Parish of Saint Mary Aldermary London to be by your petitioner's received and provided for according to Law

Your Petitioners and conceive as themselves aggrieved by such Order And therefore appeal there from and crave such relief herein as to your Worships shall seem Proper. And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc..

Exd.
