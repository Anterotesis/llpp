To the Right Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr. and other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition of Edward Hartwright

Sheweth That your Petitioner is heartily sorry for the trouble he hath given to their Worships respecting his Garden Ground and that he is willing to embrace the kind Offer which their Worships have already made, and humbly pray the same may be speedily compleated

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc

Edward Hartwright

March 5th. 1784
