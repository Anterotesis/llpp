To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices [...] Peace for the County of Middlesex [...] of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell [...] Green Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseer of the Poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of St. Hamley and Sd Greene Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex One where of bring of the Quorum bearing Date the Twenty second day of November last Susannah Brown the Wife of John Brown who Absents himself from her and [...] to Adminster any relief to her for the support of herself and Children together with [...] Children and lawful Issue by her said [...] Namely John aged about Sixteen Year and Sixteen Years and Timothy aged about three Years and half were Removed and Conversed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Ratcliff as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Susannah Brown and her said two Children John and Timothy Whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved Aged

Therefore They appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justice and the said Order and pray That your Worships will be please to Appoint some Day in the [...] Session [...] the Hearing and Determination of the said appeal and to Order That the Churchwardens and Overseer of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate and all persons concerned do [...] then attend this Court.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
