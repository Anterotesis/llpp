To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of William Mark:

Sheweth That your Petr: stands Indicted in this Court for Keeping a Gaming Table

That your Petr: Ignorantly was Imployed by Thomas Johnson to look after a Play board and never received any benefitt thereby and was guilty of the said Offence thro Ignorance and being heartily sorry for the same and resolving never to Offens agains in the like nature and being a poor Sickly man

Yor. Petr: humbly prays this Court will be pleased to consider his poverty and Case and to Estreat his fine for the said Offence

And Yor: pet Shall Ever pray et

Wm Mack.
