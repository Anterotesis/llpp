To the Right Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions [...] Assembled.

The humble peticon & appeale of the Church -wardnes & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the feilds in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order made the hands & Seals of Thomas Robe & William Hayton Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County ( Quormns) bearing date the fourth day of this Instant August Flora Wilson Daughter of David and Flora Wilson was removed from the Liberty of Saftron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of [...] her last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves agrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray yor Wor [...] will be pleased to appoint [...] bearing & Determination the Same

And Yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

D [...] boltam

Middx Ss: Ad Genal Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Midd apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com Prd & adjorn die martie scilt Vicesimo Nano die Augti [...] Dui Georgy [...] nunc Regis magne Britan Etc Sexto.

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Libty of Saff on Hill Hatton garden & Ely [...] in the parish of St. Andrew Holborne in the County have notice of Vizt Peticon & Appeale & that they & all parties concerned matter [...] Thursday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to hear & abide the Judgement & Determination of this court touching the said Appeale

P cur Walter
