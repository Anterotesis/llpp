To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Maria Wife of James Dalbiao.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was on or about the 13th. day of February last Convicted before Nathaniel Conant Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of the Middlesex For purchasing a certain pair of Tan Leather Glover Subject to the Stamp Duties Imposed by the Statute in such Case made and Provided without the Stamp Ticket directed by the said Statute to be Affixed to the same being so Affixed as by the said Statute is directed Contrary to the Statute Etc. And your Petitioner was declared and adjudged to have forfeited for her said Offence the Sum of Ten Pounds Whereby Your Petitioner conceives herself Aggrieved.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly begs leave to Appeal to this honourable Court against the said Conviction and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session.

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray to.

Maria Dalbiad
