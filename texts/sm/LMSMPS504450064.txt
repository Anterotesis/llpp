To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order under the Hands and Seals of Bartholomew Hammond and Francis Bedwell Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of September last whereby Sarah Macdonald and her four Children Namely Catherine aged about ten Years Michael aged about Seven Years John aged about four Years & Robert aged about fourteen Months were removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the peace of their last legall Settlement.

Your Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the said order appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray Your Worships will Appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing & Determination of the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc. Danl. Bolton atts. for ye. Appellts.
