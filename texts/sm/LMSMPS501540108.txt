To ye. Rgt: Worspll: his Maties: Justices of ye peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeale of Henry Williams of ye. parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye sd County Cooper

Sheweth That Your peticoner is rated Six pence p: weeke to ye. Rate made for ye. releife of ye: poor of ye. sd parish and that by reason of ye. many Rates that are ussually reade and ye. great Losses he hath Lately had together wth. a great decay in his traide he is not able to pay ye. Same,

Your peticoner therefore humbly prayes an Order of this Honable: Court, that he may be eased in ye. sd Rate, as to your Worsp [...] shall Seem meet

And Your peticoner Shall ever pray Etc:

Middx ss: Pursuant. to ye. Order of ye. Genrll: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace wch. in Written, We Whose names are hereunto Subscribed Two of his Matis Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd County in ye. sd order named, haveing taken upon us ye. heareinge & examinacon of ye. appeale & Compll. of Henry Williams ye. peticoner & appeltt. above named, ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor & Severall other of ye. Inhatants of ye. sd pish of St. Leonard Shoreditch beinge prsent, & upon hearinge of all ptyes & duely Considering ye. reasons & allegacons given on all Sides, We report our oppinions to this Honable: Court, that ye. sd Henry Williams ought to be eased & abated one penny p: Weeke to ye. Last Rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye sd pish upon his payinge of all arrears to any former rate or rates made for ye. Same purposs & for ye. future to pay [...] five pence p: Weeke & noe more Given under our hands this 31: day of May and Dom 1716: all ptyes accquiessinge

Alex: Pitfrild. Sam Benson.
