To the Right Worpll His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the Peace for the Said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sd Peter in Cornhill London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of R: Misford and H: Broad head Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th. day of April 1737 Thomas Rest was Removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. Peter in Cornhill London as the place of his last legal Settlemt.

Your Petitioner Conceiving they are Aggrieved by the Warrt. & Judgment of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court for Relief

Jno North P Agent for St. Peter's Cornhill

Middx At the General Sessions of the Peace of Our Lord the King holden for the County of Middx at Hicks hall in St. John Street in the Said County on Tuesday the 24th. day May in the Tenth Year of the Reign of Our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in this County have Notice of this Petition of Appeal And that they & all persons concern'd do Attend this Court on Friday next at nine of the clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court upon & touching the said Appeal

By the Court Walter [x]
