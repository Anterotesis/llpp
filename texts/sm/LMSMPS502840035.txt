To the Rt. Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petion & Appeal of John Francis agt. the Poors Rate of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the Said County

Sheweth

That your Peticoner is this year Rated & Assembled the Sum of Thirteen peace P Month towards the poors Rate of the sd. parish P your Peticoner being in very mean Circumstances is not able to pay the Said Rate

Your Peticoner therefore most humble Pray that yr worsps would le pleased to give him such- Releif in the Prmises as to your Worsps Shall Seem meet

And your Peticoner Shall Ever Pray Etc

John Francis
