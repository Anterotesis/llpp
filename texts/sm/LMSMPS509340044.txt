To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their Genl. Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hamover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and W Kinnard Esqr Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex(one whereof being of the Quorum)bearing date the Twenty Second day of November one thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety Six Hannah Pitt aged about Twenty three years Single Woman was removed and Conveyed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Pitt by which order of Removal the said Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Order or Warrant of Removal

Your Petitioners therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to receive this Appeal and as the said Hannah Pitt was only a few days since brought to bed and therefore at present too weak to attend to be examined by the Court your Petitioners humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the Matters touching this appeal at such time as you will please to Order and direct and give your Petitioner such relief in the Premises us to your Worships shall seem Just and meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Saint George Hanover Square

Inge & Chandlip Sollrs. for the Appeals

& Chand lep Juge Solr for the Appellts
