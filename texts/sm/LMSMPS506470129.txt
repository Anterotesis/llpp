To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Burford Camper and John Sherwood Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of November last William Lindsay and Elizabeth his Wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray That the benefit of this Appeal may be saved to Your Petitioners And that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this County.

And Your Petitr: shall pray Etc

Charles Storey Churchwarden

Geo. Chalmers Thos. Key Wm Hall Robt Desbrone} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
