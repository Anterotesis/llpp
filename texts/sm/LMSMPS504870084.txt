To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant Or Order under the Hands and Seals of Paul Mombray and Robert Hale Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 4th. day of August 1760- Elizabeth Urwin aged Six years and upwards Daughter of Michael Urwin by Jane Anne his late Wife both deced was removed from the parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

That your petitioners Conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly appeal against the same

Your petitioners therefore humbly pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of this present Session, that all parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Stagg Sollr. for the Applls.
