Middx ss To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church- Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the pish of St. Mary to Bow in the Citty of London

Sheweth

That by an Ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx & Libty of Westmr (Quor unus) bearing date the tenth day of september last Sarah Thomas widw. was removed from the pish of St. Margarett & Citty of Westmr. in the Sd. County to the sd. pish of St. Mary Le Borne in the sd. Citty of London of the plate of her last legall Settlemt.

That yor Petrs. think themselves aggreived by the Ord. of the Sd. two Justices & humbly appeale therefrom to the Judgemt. of this Cort

Chimmow Yor Petrs. there fore most humbly pray yor Worspps to appoint all Plyes to attend yor. [...] Worspps some day this sessions in Ordr. to heare & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Cort touching this Appeale

And yor Petrs Shall pray Etc.
