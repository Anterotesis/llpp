To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Thomas Webb of the pish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County On the behalfe of Eliz Webb his Daughter

That in October 1698 Yor. Petr. sd. Daughter became apprentice to Jane Gibbins of the pish of St. Paul Covent Garden in this County Milkwoman for the Tenn of Seaven Yeares comeating from the day of the Date of her Judre of Apprntishipp as thereby may appeare That the sd. Jane Gibbins hath Frequently abused Barbourously Beaten yor. Petr. sd. Daughter and hath not allowed her such necessarys fitting for an apprntice where upon the sd. Gibbins stands bound before the Worspl. Justice Tracey for her Appearence in this Honble Courts this pdt Sessions And for as much as your Petr. being a Poore Watchman of the sd. pish having Sewall Children to mainteyne and the sd Gibbins being a wy troublesome Woman

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes ye Worsppd to be pleased to Grant that his sd. Daughter may be discharged from her sd. Apprntishipp.

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

Thomas Webb
