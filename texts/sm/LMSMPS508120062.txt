To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Tottenham High Cross in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a certain Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Bosworth Esqr. and Richard Noate Clerk two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County and one of them being of the Quorum bearing Date the fifth Day of April One Thousand Seven hundred and Eighty six Stephen Brown aged thirty four Years Elizabeth his Wife and their Son namely George aged about Eighteen Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Finchley in the sid County to the said Parish of Tottenham High Cross by the Description of the Parish of Tottenham in the County of Middlesex at the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Stephen Brown Elizabeth his Wife and their said Child

That Your Petitioner apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal against the same.

Chetharn Solr for the Appellant} Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of this their Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the Hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County.
