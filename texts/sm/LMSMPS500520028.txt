To the Right Worll: his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Chappell wardens & Overseers of the poore of the Town of New Brentford in this County

Sheweth That [---] one John Mundy was lately passed from the Pish of Martin in the feild in the said County to the sd Towne of New Brentfore by Warrant under the hands & Seals of James Dewy & Francis Negus Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace of this County thereby alleadging that the said John Mundy was last begally settled att said Town of Brentford

James Tyson Yor Petr think themselves [...] wth the Judgment of the sd two Justices appeal to this Worll Bench & humbly [...] the Churchwardens [...] [...] Martin in the Feilds & all other pties concerned may attend this Court some day this sessions to heare & abide the Judgement & finall determinacon of this Court touching this appeale

And yor petrs Shall ever pray Etc

James Jennings Chapelwardens George Butler
