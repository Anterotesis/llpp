To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St; George the Martys in the Said County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Ellis Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the Twelfth Day of May now last past Elizabeth Harrold [...] was removed from the parish of St Giles in the Field [...] in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. George the Martyr as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Whereby your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves agrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Judgment of the said Two Justices and pray your worships will be pleased to apporil, some Day this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal

And your petitionr: shall pray Etc
