To the Worshipull the Chairman and the Rest of the worship full Bench of Justices Assembled at Hicks hall in St. John Street in the County of Middx to be held there by Adjournment for the County Aforesaid

Sheweth The humble petition of Giles King

That your Petr. on the 28th. day of April in the year of our Lord 1729 became bound as an Apprentice by An Indenture unto Samuel Price of the Parish of St. Martins in the Feilds in the County of Middlesex Carpenter for the Term of Seven yeare And whereas by the said Indenture your Petrs. Master was to find and provid your Petr. with wearing Apparrel meet Drink washing and Lodging During the of said term of Seven years and at the End and Expiration thereof to give a Suit of new Cloath unto your Petr. as by the said Indenture now Ready to be produced to your Worships will more fully Appear

And that whereas your Petrs. said Master about three month agoe went a way from his Sd shop and left your Petr. improvided for And his wife since lett the Shop and Left your Petr. Destitute of All manner of Provisions and newsarys

Your Petioner therefore most humbly prays your worshipsl that he may be discharged from his Said Master

And your Petr. as in Duty bound shall Ever pray Etc

Giles King
