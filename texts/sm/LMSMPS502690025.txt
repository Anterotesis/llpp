Peticon of Rebecca Beesley Cripplegate Poor Rate

refr to petty Sess: for Finsbury Division to ex & cert

The Humble Peticon of Rebecca Beesly Widw.

Middx ss: Ad Genal Quartial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apud Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd P Adjorn die Mercury Scilt vicesimo Sexto die Augusti Anno regin Dui Georgy Scdi nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto.

It is Ordered by this Court that it be audit is hereby recomended unto his Maties Justices of the peace for this County residing in Finsbuty Division to be assembled- at their petty Sessions to be holden, in & for the Same Division to Sumon before them the Churchwardens and Overseers of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in- this County and to examine into the truth of the peticoners complaint And to certify their Opinions touching the premisses within menconed unto his Maties Justices of the peace at the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County.
