To the Right worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kemsing in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hand and Seal of Peter Planch Esqr. One of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of westminster within the County of Middlesex bearing date the Sixteenth Day of September in stant Jane Trigg Widow of James Trigg deceased and their Two Children to wit Ann aged Four years and Robert aged Two Years were apprehended in the Parish of Saint Martin in the Feilds in the said County of Middlesex as Vagrants and removed and Conveyed to the said Parish of Hemsing in the said County of Kent it appearing to the said Justice to be the Place of their last lega Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justice and to humbly appeal therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore Most humbly Pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session to hear this their Appeal and to Make an Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Martin aforesaid and all Partys concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there hear and abide the Determination of your worships therein.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Rd Trippe} Church warden

John Turner} Overseer.
