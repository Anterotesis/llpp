To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace in Sessions assembled at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street and for the County of Middx

The huble Petition of John Cattle of the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx

Sheweth That yor. petr. prferred his humble Petition to be honable Court praying releife when Yor. Petr. was [...] directed to apply to the parish offices for releife which yor. Petr Accordingly did but the said offices refuse to releive your Petitioner-unless Your Petitioner can produce an Order from this honoble Court for that purpose.

That yor: Petr: is a very poor man not able to to work haveing not the use of his himbs and almost Pished for want

Therefore yor. Petitioner most humbly prays yor. honr the pranes considered to grant unto Yor. Peter Such releife herein as to yor honrl Shall Seem meet

and Yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc
