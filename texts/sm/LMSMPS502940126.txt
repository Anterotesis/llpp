To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in Generall Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwdns: & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ann Westminster in the said county of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of samuel Grey and Thomas Deveil Esqr two of this Majestys Justices of the peace for the City & Librty of Westminster bearing late the Fourteenth day of July last Mary Allen aged about Thirty five years Wife of John Allen gone from her and their three children (that is to say) Elizabeth aged about thirteen years Henrietta Eight years & Ann upwards of two years were removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the Liberty of the City of westmr in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish Saint ann whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the adjudgment of the said two Justice & Pray your Worships will be Pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the fields & all Persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this Present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And shall Pray Etc. Jo: Kendall sol for Appern.
