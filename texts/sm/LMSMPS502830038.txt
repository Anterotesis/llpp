[...] he Right worshipfull his Majesties Justices Peace for the County of Middx in their [...] al Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing date teh 1st. day of April instant [...] dith Ward was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles's in the Feilds in the County of Middx aforesd. to the sd. Parish of St. James as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Edith Ward Whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

And therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the sd. Appeal and that allpersons Concerned May Attend at the Same time

And your Petr. Shall pray Etc

Middx ss: Ad General [---] Saml. Seddon Vestry Cl Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John street in Com prd P adjorn die Martis scilt Vicesimo tertio die May anno regui Dui Georgy scdi nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quinto

It is Ordered by this Court that the hearing & determining of the matter of this appeale be and the same is hereby adjourned Untill the next General Sessions of the peace to be holden for this County And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in this County doe (Upon [---] service of a copy of the sd. appeale & of this order) attend the Court of the said next General Sessions to be holden for this County by adjornment on Friday the twenty Sixth day of May instant [...] at ten of the clock in the forenoon to heare & abide the Judgment & determinacon of the same Court touching The said appeale,

P Com Walter
