To the Worshipful is Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled-

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of George Mercer and J.. Mercer Esquires two of his Majestiy's Justices of the Peace in and for the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of November last. Frances Baker Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved

Saved to the Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for this County to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Willams

Besfeld C Bond
