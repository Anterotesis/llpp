To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of [---] Alexander Ward and John Metcalfe Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (quor Unus) bearing date the twenty eight day of November 1720. Elizabeth Day was past on yor. Petrs. from the parish of St. Andrews Holbourne above barrs in the sd. County of Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. apprehend themselves aggreived by the order of the said two Justices & appeale to yor Worpps therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor Worpps to appoint a day to heare and determine the Said Appeale.

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
