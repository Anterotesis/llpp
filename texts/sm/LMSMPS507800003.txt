To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Session at Hicks Hall assembled.

The humble Petition of William Thomas

Sheweth That by an Act of Parliament pass'd in the Reign of his present Majesty King George the third, for the further and better regulation of Buildings and Party Walls, and for the more effectually preventing Mischiefs by fire, within the Cities of London and Westminster, and other precincts and places therein men =tioned, Certain persons skilld in the Art of Building are to be nominated and appointed Surveyors to see the rules and regulations in the said Act mentioned, well and truly observ'd,

The your petitioner has been yearly brought up in the Building branch and perfected in that Art under the Tuition and in the Service of the Messrs. Adam of the Adelphi Architects

That your Petitioner humbly presumes himself qualified for undertaking and performing the intended employment of a Surveyor under the Said Act,
