To [---] Right Honble. Sir Robert Ladbroke Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of his Maties Justices of Goale Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx

The humble petition of William Gray

Humbly Sheweth That your petitioner [...] Maties Golale [...] at Maidstone for the County of Kent being charged on the oaths of Richard Stander & Benjamin Snelling for being armed with Fire Arms & other offensive Weapons in order to be Aiding & assisting in the running of Uncustomed goods since which Committment of your Petitioner to ye said Goale of at Maidstone aforesaid your petitioner was on the 31st day of January last brought to his Maties Goale of Newgate for the sd Offence of which your petitior is entirely innocent

Yr Petitioner therefore humbly prays that yor petitioner may be either Bailed tryed discharged this Sessions as to yr Lordships shall seem meet According to the Statute

And yr petitioner shall [...] pray Etc

William Gray
