To his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon & appeale of Simon Michell Esq

Sheweth That your petnr. is aggrieved (as be humbly conceives) by a Charge on him in the rate for the releife of the poor of the parish of Fulham in this County for this present yeare and therefore appeales therefrom to this County and prayes to be releived against ye same as to yr worpps Shall Seeme meet

And yor. petr Shall ever pray Etc

S: Michelle
