To the Right Worspl. there Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of William Byrd of the Parish of St. Mary Islington in the Sd. County

Sheweth

That at Easter 1693 yor. petr. was elected to serve the Office of Constable for Prebben Liberty for the yeare Last past which yor. Petr. executed accordingly and that upon Easter Tuesday Last according to ancient custome Mr. John Holland was elected to serve the Sd. Office of [...] Constable for the Sd. Liberty for the yeare ensuines yett nevertheless the Sd. Mr. John Holland refusech to execute the Sd. Office

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worspps to be Pleased to Grant that the he may be discharged from Serving the Sd. Office & that the Sd. Mr. John Holland may be compelled to events the Same.

And yer. petr shall ever Pray Etc.
