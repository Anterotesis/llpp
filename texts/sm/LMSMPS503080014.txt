To the Worshippfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Anne Westminster in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Milner & Ben Burroughs Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of this Instant April Richard Gostelian & Katherine his Wife were removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlx to the said parish of Saint Anne Westminster whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived.

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the aforesaid Pass Warrant and Adjudgement of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields & all persons concerned do attend this Court on next adjournment day to hear & abide the Judgemt. & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your petitioners shall pray Etc.

Jo. Kendall Agent for the Appellts.
