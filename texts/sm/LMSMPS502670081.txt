To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (quorn unus) bearing date the sixteenth day of January last one Sarah Wilson aged about Eleven Years and Richard Wilson aged about Nine Years the law full House of Richard Wilson Senior and Sarah his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of St. Sepulchres in the City of London adjudging the said Parish of St. Sepulchres to be place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Wilson and Richard Wilson.

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices

Humbly Appeal to Your Worships therefrom and pray that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch may be ordered to attend and hear and abide the Determinacon of this Court touching this appeal.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

Henry Radford [...] Anderson} Church Wardens

Murdock Broomer Wm Dew} Overseers of the Poor
