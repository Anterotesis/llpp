To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall in the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of B. Burroughs and John Milner Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Sd. County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth Day of August last Charles a Male Bastard Child was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby of our Petitioner think themselves agrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Prays this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may attend at the same time.

And Your Petitionrs, willever pray Etc.

Thos Rea Wm. Compbell} Churchwardens

S. Parry Lett
