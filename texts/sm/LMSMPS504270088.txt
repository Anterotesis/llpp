To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Newport Pagnel in the County of Bucks

That by an Order or Pass Warrant dated the 16th day of March 1754 under the Hands and seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx Ann Jefferies Wido. was removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx aforesaid to the Parish of Newport Pagnell aforesaid

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray that this Court will make such [---] Order therein as shall seem Meet

And Your Petr: shall ever Pray Etc

Jon Higgs sold for the the appellants
