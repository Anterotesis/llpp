To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Surrey.

Sheweth That by an order under the hands and Seals of David Wilmot Esqr. and John Benleaze Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Fourth day of Aprill 1776 Elizabeth Loft widow of Thomas Loft and her Two Children lawfull there (namely) Thomas Aged six Years and upwards and Elizabeth aged Two Years and upwards were Removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the paid County of Middlesex into the Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Survey as the place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order and have appealed there from

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray That you will be pleased to appoint a day for hearing and [...] of the said [...]

And yr: Petitioner [...]
