To the Rt. Worshipl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of William Ellis and Jo: Colbatch Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx & (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the Fifteenth Day of September 1727 One Ann Mooney the wife of Peter Mooney and her two Children Mary aged four Years and upwards and Eleanor Aged three Years were removed from the Parish of St. Mary le Strand in the Liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs as the place of their last legall Settlement

Whereby Your Peticoners Humbly Conceive themselves agrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeale to your Worships against the Judgment of the said two Justices And Pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some Day this Present Sessions to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeale

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etca.
