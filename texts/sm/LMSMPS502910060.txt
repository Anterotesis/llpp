To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by as Order or warrant under the Hands and Seals of James [...] Stuart and Bowater Vernon Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing date the thirtieth day of March last [...] John Bolton the Son of John Bolton Aged about Eight Years was removed from the parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surry as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the aforesaid Order and Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And yr petitioners will Ever Pray
