To the Worshipfull his Majestys [...] Justices of the pence for the County of Middx Assembled in Genll. Sessions at Hicks Hall in for the sd. County

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the Pence for the sd. County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th. day of April last Mary Gilbert Aged atmost Nine Yeares was removed from the Parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County of Middx to the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the same County as the Place of her last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be Agreived & agt. the sd. Order doe Appeale.

Ro: Chimmow Sollr. for the Appellts }

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Sessions to heare & Determine the mattor touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray
