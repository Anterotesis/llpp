To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace, The humble Petition of Joshua Garlands

Sheweth That your Petitioner humbly prays that the Court would be pleased to take of his Fi [...] lay'd on him last Sessions for not being in proper time on the Travers Jury his having no intent to give the Court offence, and having duly attended as a Grand Juryman ship Sessions humbly hopes they will consider his Case

We the rest of the Grand Jury himself prays that your would grant him his request
