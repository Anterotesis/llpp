To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Burf Camper and John Sherwood Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of December 1773 Ann Goddard Widow of John Goddard deceased and their Two Children Vizt John aged One Year and upwards & Benjamin aged One Month and upwards were removed to the parish of Saint Giles by Cripplegate in the City of London as the place of their last legal Settlement from the parish of St. Mary White Chapel in ye County of Middx

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day of this present Sessions That all parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

Thos. Strong Sollr. for the Appellts
