To the Right Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Ov [...] of the poor of the Pish of Fleetwick [...]

That by order dated the 29th day of November last [...] the hands & seals of Thomas Smyth Esqr & Morgan Ryan Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd County of Midds one Richard Fisher a Poor Pson was lately removed from the Pish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County of Midds to the said Pish of Fleetwick in the sd County of [...] Bedford as the place of his last settlemt

That yor petrs find themselves aggreived by the removall of the sd Richard Fisher to their said Pish for that they hope to make out that the sd Richard Fisher hath noe legall settlemt in their sd Pish of Fleetwick & therefore humbly appeal agt the said order of the said two Justices
