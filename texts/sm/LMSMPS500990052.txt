To the Right Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx:

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne Westmr. in the County of Middx:

Sheweth That by an Order dated the forth day of June last past under the hands & Seale of Sr. Harry Dutton Coult. Bart. and Jonathan Chase Esqr. two of her Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx One James Thomas a Child about the age of three Years was removed from the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the County to Parish of St. Anne aforsaid as the Place of his last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceiveing themselves to be Aggreved by the Judgmt. of the Said Two Justice doe humbly Appeale to yor. Worships therefore

Yor. Peticoners therefore humbly Pray yor. Worships would be pleased to appoint a day that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James aforesaid may attend this Court to heare & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon thereof touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners Shall Pray Etc
