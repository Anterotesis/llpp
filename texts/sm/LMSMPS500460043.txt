To the Right Worsp his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Countey of Middelsex Sheweth the humble Sessions of [...] Aeled Curre Youer worships wear pleased to [...] Fienshur to grots and for not pay in thease of the Con [...] hur to new Prison Whear She now remains that yours petisoner is now almost ready to for is for want be in a widdow and harfin thre smole Cheldren and youer peticoners thear foor most humbly prays youer Worships the sad and de storabel Condicon Whear in She now langueseth for Want youer peticoner humbely begs youer Worships will be pleasd to Consider hur and Youer Peticoner Shall ever Pray for you

Ales Curre
