To The Right Worshipfull This Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: now Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That By Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Richard Bealing and Edward Hawks Esqrs. Two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace (Quorum nuns) Bearing date this Eighteenth day of February: 1714 Ann Graham was passed from the Parish of St. Anne Westmr: in the said County to the Parish of St. James aforesaid as the place of her Last Legall Settlement By which your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and Appeale to this Court.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Anne and all other persons Concerned may attend this Court to heare and abide the Judgment and determination of Your Worships touching this Appeale

And Your Petitionrs: Etc
