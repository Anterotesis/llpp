To the Right Worpll his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon & appeale of widdow starr being so poor that he is not able to maintaine her family desire you to excuse her, & Willm Beddoss being a very poor man past his labour desire that you would be pleased to excuse him Heleing [---] not able to pay

And your petirs shall ever pray
