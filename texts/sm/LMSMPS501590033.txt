To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Howett Constable of Endfield in the said Countys

Sheweth

That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to yor Peticoner at Christmas last for passing & relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds.

That your peticoner hath been at great charge & Expence this last Quarter in passing of Vagrants and stands in want of this said Moneys

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order that he may be paid the said Quarters Sallary

And Yor: petr Shall ever pray Etc John Howett
