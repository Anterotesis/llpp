To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Sampson Wright Knight and William Addington Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 17th. Day June 1789 Edward Dawson Aged 68 Years and Diana his Daughter Aged 36 Years [---] were removed and conveyed from that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the Place of [---] their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then Attend
