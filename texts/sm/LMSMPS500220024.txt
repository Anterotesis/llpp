To the [...] Worpll: their Maties Justices of the [...] the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of [...] St Bridget als Bridget London

Sheweth

That of late Anne Blow William Blow and Robert Blow Children of John Blow were by Warrant passed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn in this County to the said parish of St. Brides.

That the said John Blow Father of the said Children was half Legally settled in the said parish of St Andrew Holborn in the said County Where he was assessed to the publiqr Rates within the said parish and from time to time paid the same, which your Peticoners are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court.

Your Peticoners therefore most humbly Pray your Worspps to be pleased to grant that the said Children may be repassed to the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn in this County.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
