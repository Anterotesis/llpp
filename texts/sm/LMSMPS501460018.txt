To the Worpll his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Henry Randall and Joseph Pettitt

Humbly Sheweth That your Peticoners are the prsent Scavengers of and for the Liberty of Church End and Hexton in the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlx That the Officers and Inhabitants have rated and Assessed a Booke for your Peticoners to Collect but the Same being not assessed according to a Pound Rate the greatest part of the Inhabitants refuse to pay

The prmisses Considered your Peticoners humbly pray your Worpps to grant them Such releife therein as to your grave Westmr Shall Soemr meet

And your Peticoners as in duty bound Shall pray etc
