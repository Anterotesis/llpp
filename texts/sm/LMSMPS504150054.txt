To the Rt: Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, in their General Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of Christchurch in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under the Hands & Seals of Philip Dyott and John Milner, Esqrs, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye 2d: Novr. 1751, Mary How, Singlewoman, was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in ye. Feilds, in the County- of Middlesex, unto the Parish of Christchurch, London, alledging the said Parish of Christchurch, to be the- Place of her last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Atty for ye. Appellants} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, Do Appeal to yor. Worships therefrom, And humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in the Promisses, & Order, that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Giles in the- Feilds afd, may appear before You, at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide ye. Judgment and- Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
