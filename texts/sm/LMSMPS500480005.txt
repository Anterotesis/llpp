To the Rt: Honoble & Rt Worppll his Masties: Justices of the peace Sitting in the bench at Hicks Hall.

The humble Peticon of Henry Downe

Sheweth That the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of [...] belonging late to Well Close in the parrish of St John Wap [...] g Did putt and Henry Smith a poor child to yor peticoner [...] nursed, Butt since the 16th day of June 1696 (Some diffrence happening Beweene the Sd Churchwarden & Overseers of Well Close And the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of St John Wapping) your Peticoner h [...] not beene paid for nursing the Sd child, neither can prvaile with either or any of them to pay for the same
