The Humble Petition of Samson Barrett

to be hd. Fr: 10 cl Petition dismissed 9s dismiss

Middx At the General Quarter session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middx at Hicks hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid by adjornment on Wednesday the fifth day of April in the twenty second year of the reign of our sovereign Lord George the second Kind of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor and antient Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Luke in this County have notice of this Petition and that they [---] do attend this Court on Friday next at ten of of the clock in the forenoon to show cause why the within named samson Barrett the Petitioner should not be excused and discharged from being scavenger of the said Parish,

By the Court Waller
