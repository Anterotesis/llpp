To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their adjourned Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by in Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of [...] Walker & Sam Foyster Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex [...] whereof being of the Quorum (bearing date the third day of April Instant Ann Aged three Months or thereabouts the Illigitimate Child of Ann Hodgetts Single Woman was Removed and conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London aforesaid was the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

[...] Simpson Sollr. for the Appellts Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships to Appoint a Day in this present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
