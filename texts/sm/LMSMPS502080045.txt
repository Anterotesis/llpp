Middx ss To the Rt: Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarr: Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Langley Marsh [...] in the County of Bucks

Sheweth

That by Warrant under the Handsand Seals of George Walter and Samuel Benson Esqrs: two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for this County bearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of October last Henry Lobley was removed and Conveyed Out of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in this County into the said Parish of Langley Marsh in the County of Bucks aforesaid (as not haveing any Legall Settlement in the said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch) But legally Settled in the Parish of Langley Marsh.

Yr: Petitionrs: therefore Humbly think themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said Justices & Appeal to this Court agt. the Same And Humbly Pray Yr. Worships to Appoint a Day for All Parties to Attend this Court to Abide the Determination of Ye: Worships touching Your Petitionrs Sai Appeal and to Grant them such Other Reliefe in the Prmisses as to Your Worships Shall [...] Seem Meet.

Ye. Petitionrs: Shall Ever Pray Etc.
