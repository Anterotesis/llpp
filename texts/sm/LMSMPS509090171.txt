To the Worshipful the Chairman & the Justices of Middlesex in Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition of the Journeymen Weavers in the Silk Crape, Silk Muslin, Foot Figured & Flower'd Branches

Sheweth That your Petitioners & the, Masters on the above branches have agreed upon Certain Prices to be paid to them the Journeymen for different Works in the Silk Crape, silk Muslin, Foot Figured & Flower'd branches the particular of which with the Prices are annexed hereto

Your Petitioners therefore Humble pray that your Workships will be pleased to settle regular Order & declare the Wages & Prices of Work of the Journeymen Working within this County in the Silk Crape, Silk Muslin, Foot Figured & Flower'd Branch aforesaid agreable to the Statute in that Case made & Provided

And Your Petitioners as in Duty Bound Shall ever Pray

Signd Thos Box

Janry 10. 1795 Jas Bresson Secretary

James Faunham Wm Till Antony Reed Samuel Reed Thos Hayward Francis Rose
