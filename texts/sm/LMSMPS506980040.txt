To the Right Work her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The Humble Petition of the Constables of the Parish of Hamsted in the said County.

That there was never no conveinent Watchhouse Erecled and Built in the said Parish soe yt yor Petitnors and Persons before them in the like Office have been Allways exposed when upon their Duty to the Extermity of the Weather

P Tho: Nash

Your Petitrs therefore most humbly Pray yor Worspd. to be Pleasd to Order yt a Watchhouse may be Erected & Built in such conseinent Part of Ye said Parish as shall be Adjudged Most Nessesary

And Yor Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
