To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in your General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Eltham in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Cox and Stephen Clarke Esqr: two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fourth day of May 1767. Ann Ellsom Widow of William Ellsom Labourer deceased was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Pancras in ye. sd. County of Middx to the Parish of Eltham in ye. County of Kent as the Place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day in this Present General Quarter Sessions to hear and determine their said Appeal

Thos: Williams Sollr: for the Appellants
