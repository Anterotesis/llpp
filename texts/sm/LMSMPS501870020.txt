To the Right Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions now Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Samuell Hutton John Hocker William Collier Richard Wall & Henry Quintam Parishioners of Yr. Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County in behalfe of themselves & diverse other Inhabitants of the said Parish

Sheweth That your Petitioners in January Sessions last Exhibited their Petition to this Court against William Cuxon Thomas Gibson & Thomas Shadd Surveyors of the High Ways for the year 1719 for the said Parish to Account for such Moneys as remained in their hands at the Expiration of their said Office & thereupon a Comittee of Justices of the Peace for this County were Ordered & appointed to Inspect & Examine the said Accounts of the said Surveyors & Report their Oppinions thereof to this Court at this imediate Sessions that such further Order might be made there in as should be just

That in pursuance of such appointment the said Comittee were Attended by your Petitioners & also the said Surveyors & Allegations were heard on both sides & a Report being now Offered to this Court whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggreived & for that Your Petitioners have not had either a view or Coppy of the said Report

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly pray that they may have a Day appointed by this Honourable Court to make their Objections to the said Report by Councell

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
