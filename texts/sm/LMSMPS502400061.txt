To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Fuller and Wm. Cotesworth Esqrs: two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twenty seventh day of October last past, Jane Wilks Wife of Andrew Wilks and Jane their Danl: aged abt: Six Month were removed from the Parish of St: James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St: Martin in the Fields as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgement of the said two Justices, And pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. James Clerkenwell and all persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petts: Shall pray Etc
