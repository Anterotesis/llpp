To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of All hallows on London Wall

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of George Bourne and Abraham Addams Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace sd: [...] County of middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 22d day of June last past one John a bastard Child aged about two years was removed from the parish of Enfeild in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of All hallows on London Wall as the place of his last legal settlement

That your petition conceiving themselves aggrieve by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Wopps there from and pray your worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Enfeild may appear before you at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgment & determinacon of your worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
