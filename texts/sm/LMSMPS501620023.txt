To the Rt. Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon and appeale of ye Churchwardens & overseers of ye poor of the parish of Maidstone in ye County of Kent

Sheweth. That by Warrant or order Dated the Sixth day of May last under ye hands & Seales of Samuel Jones & Thomas West [...] two of his Matys Justices of ye peace for ye Sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) Isabella Barker widow & two Children Vizt. Henry Barker Aged about nine yeares & Daniel Barker aged about Six yeares were removed from ye Hamlett of Ratcliffe in ye parish of stepney in this County to the aforesd. parish of Maidstone in Kent as the place of ye last legall settlement of the sd. Isabella Barker & her two Children.

That your peticoners humbly Conceive themselves aggreived by the Sd. Order of ye Sd two Justices & appeal agt ye Same.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray your worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day in this sessions for all parties to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching ye sd. appeale

And yor Petr. shall Ever pray etc.
