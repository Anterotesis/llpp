To the Right Worspl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of David Wilcard & Francis Collins late [...] for Wentworth Streets in the parish of St Mary [...] appell in the Sd. County.

Sheweth That upon Auditing yor. Petr. Accompts. the appeared to be due to your Petr. the Sume of Three pounds and Seaven pence As by a note now nearly to be produced may appears That yor. Petr. have demanded the said moneys of the Churchwardens of the Sd. Parish but they alledge they will not pay the Same without at Order of this Houble Court

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worsps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens of the said Parish was pay Your. Petr the Sd. Moneys

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc

David Wickant [...]
