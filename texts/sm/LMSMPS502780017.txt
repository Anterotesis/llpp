To the Worspll. his Matyes Justices of the Peace Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing Date the Second day of October Instant Mary Garnett the wife of Richd. Garnett & her some Richd. Garnett were removed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the Pish of St. Dunstan in the west in the West in the sd. County to the sd. Pish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petrs. thinks themselves aggreived by the Order of the Sd. two Justices doe Appeale there from to your Worshipps & humbly Prays a Short day may be Appointed for hearing the Same

And your Petr. shall Pray Etc
