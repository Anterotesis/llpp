To the Rt. Worshipll. his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Genll. Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchward- -ens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrt. or Pass Order under the Hands and Seales of Two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlx bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of June Last Two Children of John Roades Deced Vizt. Mary Aged About Twelve Years and Joseph aged about Nine years was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in Said County unto the Said parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the Said County Alleadging the Said Parish to be the Place of the Last Legall Settlement of the Said Two Children that Yor Petrs. Conceiveing themselves agreeived by the Said Warrt or Pass Order of the Said Two Justices do most Humbley Appeale agt: the Same

Yor. Petir: therefore Pray Yor Worshipps will be pleased to here the me in the Premises & order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch may Appeare before you and hear and Abide the Determination of Yor Worships Touching this appeale

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray
