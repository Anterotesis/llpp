To the Rt: Worspll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County at Hicks hall assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Kyrby and Bastwick Johnson Esqrs: two of his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorun) bearing Date the [...] 31st: Day of May last, Christopher Martin and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middx: to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe- whereby your Petitionrs: humbly Conceive they are Wronged and aggrieved.

Yor. Petitionrs: therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order of Removall of the said two Justices and pray your Worshipps will be Pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal this present Sessions, and, that yor: Petrs: may be Relieved as unto your Worshipps shall seem meet.

And Yor. Petrs: shall Ever Pray Etc
