To the Honble: Bench of Justice's

The Humble Petition of Eliz: Cuins

Sheweth That Your poor petitioner is Confin [...] in Bridewell for her fees and hath been dischargd. This Month and almost Starved to Death & being put in for no Crimes but only Words Humbly begs that yours honours will take my Starving Condition into Your Consideration and order me to be Discharged having no on [...] friend in the World to Do any Thing for me

And your petitioner as in (Duty bounds) shall Ever pray Etc
