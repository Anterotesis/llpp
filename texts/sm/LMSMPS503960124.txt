To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor of the City of London and the rest of the Commissioners of Goal Delivery of his Majesty's Prison of Newgate at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey in the Suburbs of London aforesaid Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Hetherington now a Prisoner in Newgate aforesaid

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was Committed by Henry Fielding Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex on the Third day of July Instant to the Prison of Newgate aforesaid being Charged upon the Oath of Peters Wood for unlawfully Assembling with divers others Persons in a riotous and tunneltuos Manner and being so Assembled for beginning to pull down a certain Dwelling house the Property of the said Peter Wood against his Majestys Peace and also against the Form of the Statute Etc

That a Warrant of Detaines from the same Justice is lodged against your Petitioner with the Keeper of the same Prison granted upon the Oath of Joseph Stantrope Charging Your Petitioner with High Treason in Levying War against his Majesty by riotously and routously Assembling with divers other Persons in order to Suppress and Pulldown all Bawdy Houses and in Execution of which [...] your Petr is charged that he with the sd other person begin to pulldown a certain Dwelling house of the said Joseph Stanhope against the Peace and the Form of the Statute Etc

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays your Lordship That your Petitioner may be tryed Admitted to Bail or Discharged at this Present Sessions pursuant to the Statute in such Case provided.

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray

James Hetherington
