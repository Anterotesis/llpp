To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genal Quarter Sessions, now holdon for the Sd County.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Leonard Hemun if the Parish of St: Leonard Shore- ditch in the Sd County Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticonr is rated at twenty Six Shillings for and Towards the necessary Releif of the Poor of the Sd Parish for this present Year 1725

That your Peticonr is but in mean Circumstances, being only a Journy man & haveing nothing but his Labour to [...] Port his Family, and apprehends he is charged more than in proportion he ought to be his Neighbours of much better at lily & Circumstances not paying more than he is charged [...] and therefore conceives himself aggreived by the Sd Rate & humbly appeals agt the same praying such Releif as to your Worships Judgment shall seem just

And your Peticonr shall ever pray &c
