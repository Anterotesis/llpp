To the Rt. Worpll: her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticons p Appeale of the Church- wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of Christ Church London

Sheweth

That by Warrant dated the Three & Twentieth day of May last under the hands & seales of Ra: Bucknall & Richd. Dyot Esqr two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds (quor unus) John Howell & Margeret his wife were lately removed from the parish of St. Gyles in the Feilds in the Said County to the Said parish of Christ Church London as the place of there last legall settlemt.

That yor. petr. funds themselves agrived by the Said Order of removeall & humbly Appeale against the Same And pray that this Court coned please to appoint a day this prsent, Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Said parish of St. Gyles in the Feilds & all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon there of upon & touching the said Appeale & that yor. petrs. may be releived herein

And yor. petrs. shall pray Etc
