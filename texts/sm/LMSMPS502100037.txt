To the Right Worspp his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of James Walters of the Parish of St. Sepulcher in the said County.

Sheweth That at Easter last your Petr: was by the Officers of and Inhabitants of the said parish Elected and Chosen to serve the Office of the Head borough for the Year Ensuing

That Your Petr is aged Sixty Seven Years and very Lemer and hath noe Trade [...] for his Support and is not able to Execute the said Office

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order he may be Discharged from serving the said Office and another fitt person Chosen in his roome

And yor: Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

J. Walters
