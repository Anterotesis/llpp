To the Rt Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Tucker of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourne in the sd. County

Sheweth

That at Easter last yor. petr. was chosen Scavenger for the Libertys of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden Ely Rents in the sd. parish.

That by a misinformacon the Inhabitants of the sd. pish chose yor. Petr. Scavenger of the sd. Libertys but [---] alledged they could not discharge him and thereupon advised your petr. to prfere his Peticon to this Honble Court and forasmuch an yor. Petr. being a very poore man y haveing a Family to mainteyance

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayers yor Worspps to be pleased to Grant that the may be discharged from the sd. Offices

And yor. Petr. Shall Everpray etc.

Thomas Tucker
