To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their general Session of the peace now assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Saint Dunston Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth that by an Order under the hands and Seals of Burford Camper and John Sherwood Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seven [...] day of November last past Hannah Nicholas Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Hamlet of Ratcliffe at the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners shinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the peace do therefore appeal to your Workships against the said Order of removal and humbly [...] the Court will appoint a day this present Session for the hearing and determining hereof & make such further under in the premises as to your worships shall weremeet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Je Seacome Agent for the Appellants.
