To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary North Gate in the City of Canterbury

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and Penleaze Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 16th. Day of November 1781 John Taylor Elizabeth his Wife and their two Children Namely Thomas aged five Years and Elizabeth aged one Year and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex To the Parish of Saint Mary Northgate in the City of Canterbury as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Taylor Elizabeth his Wife and their two Children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners Therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices and that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be held for the said County

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
