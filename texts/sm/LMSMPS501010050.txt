To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of James Fuller And John Stockton

That Sometime past Yor Petr became Baile for the Appearance of One Thomas Kater in this Honourable Court for An Assault As by ye Recognizance may Appeare

That the sd Rater hath Agreed with ye Prosecuter But hath not this Sessions Appeared in this Honourable Court in Discharge of the said Recognizance

P Tho: Nash Your Petrs -therefore most humbly prays Yor Worshipps to be pleased to Respite the sd Recognizance untill next Sessions

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Jeames Faller John stockton
