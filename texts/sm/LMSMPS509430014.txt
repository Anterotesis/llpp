To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Midddlesex in their Present General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said- County Assembled..

The Humble Petition of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in- the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N Conant and John Scott Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date Fourth day of October, Instant Mary Gatton Widow and her Two Children namely John aged- about Eleven Years, and Sarah aged about Six Years, were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last, legal Settlement

Your Petitoners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Wroships against the same and request that the Beneift of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County and that all parties concerned may be then ordered to attend to abide the judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal.-

And Your Petitioners Etc..

Arthur Gliddon Sollr, for Petitioners
