To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Castle Henningham in the County of Essex.

Sheweth That by a certain Pass under the Hand and Seal of Boulton Mainwaring Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the second day of February One thousand seven hundred and fifty one Mary Davis (mentioned in the sd. Pass to be Wife of Thomas Davis) and her Child Sarah Davis aged two years were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middx as Vagabonds apprehended in the same Parish wandering and begging and were conveyed to the said Parish of Castle Hemingham in the sd. County of Essex as the place where the Father of her the Sd. Mary Davis was born

Yor. Petitioners receiving themselves aggrieved by the sd. Pass of the said Justice of the Peace humbly appeal to this Court agt. the same and pray that the Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George aforesd and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

P. Burton Sollr: for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the parish of Castle Henningham
