To ye Worshipfull her Majties Justices of ye Peace now Setting att Hicks Hall in St John Street in ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Sarah Allen widow

Sheweth

That yor Peticonr. did make her applicacon to this Court & yor Worships were pleased to make An Ordr & referred ye Same to Justice Talley Justice Perriman & other her Majties Justices or any two of them to Sumon ye Churchwarden & Overseers of ye poor [---] of St James Westminstr & to Examininto ye South of ye Peticonrs Complaint wth war to be Allow'd 8s P moneth whereas they Allow'd her but Six unless Unto sd yor Peticonr will goe to A Parish Nursse who is A very Ill woman & will spend ye Allowance & 4s P Moneth more & paece yor Peticonr wch Ordrs & peticon ye Churchwardens & Overseers keep the ye Justices Ordred them to pay yor Peticonr & Say they will give her nothing

Hen: Rowel Yor Peticonr therefore humbly prays to be releived in ye prmises & yt ye Churchwardens & Overseers may be Orded to attend this Crt to Shew Cause why they Should not obey the former Ordr. & yor. Peticonr receive her pension herselffe or as itt Shall Seem meet

And as in duty bound Shee Shall ever pray Etc Sarah Allen

Middx ss Ad Genal Session paris Dme Regine tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St John Street in Com ye dith P Adjornmt die lune & silt Sexto die Marty Anno Regine Domnd Anne Dei gra mmc Regine Magne Britannie Etc Octave

Itt is Ordrd by this Crt that ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St James Westminstr in this County doe attend this Crt on Thursday next att tenn of ye Clock in ye Fourenoon to Shew Cause why they have not paid unto ye Peticonr A pencon of Eight Shillings & moneth pursuant to An Ordr of ye Justice of peace wth in menconed & why they should not pay unto ye Peticonr ye sd pencon of Eight Shilling P moneth forgo

Halure

P Cur Harcourt

The Peticoner and ye Overseers was before and upon ye Examanacion found no Just Cause of Complant
