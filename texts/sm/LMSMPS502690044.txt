The Humble peticon of Thos Banbury apprentice agst. John Whaley of Sy Leonard Shoreditch for abuseing his Sd apprentice

Middx ss Ad Genal Session pacis Dni Regis tent P Com Middx your Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd die Mercury Scilt Vicesimo Sexto die Augusti Anno regni Dni [...] Georgy Side nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto

It is ordered by this Court that the within Named John Whaley have Notice of this peticon complaint & that he (& all other persons concerned) die attend this Court on Monday next at tenn of the Clock in the forenoon to Shew cause why the Peticoner Should not be discharged of his Indenture of apprenticehood to the Said John Whaley

p Cur Walter
