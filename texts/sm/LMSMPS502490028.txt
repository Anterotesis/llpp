To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices as the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Parishes by the Exchange in the City of London

Sheweth,

That by Virtue of [...] any other the Hands & Seales of Robert Jackson [...] Osborne Esqrs. Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor nuns) bearing Decte the Third Day of this instant January Margaret Brownrigg the Widow of Jeremiah Brownrigg & her three Children (vizt) Jeremiah aged about Nine Years, Frances aged about four years & Sarah aged about Nine Weeks, the lawfull Issue of the sd. Jeremiah Brownrigg & Margaret his Wife, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the County of Middx unto the Parish of St. Bartholomew by the Exchange London, alledging the said Parish of St. Bartholomew to be the last place of their legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners concerning themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore Appeale to yor. Worps therefrom & humbly pray that you be pleased to bear themin the [...] Order [...] the Poor of the said Parish of [...] in the said County of Middx worshipp you at a certain Day to be appointed to he [...] abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor. Worshipps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray Etc.
