To the right Worshipfull his Majties Justice of the peace for the County of Midlx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the old Artillery Ground in the Liberty of the Tower of London

Sheweth That by a warrt. on order of removal bearing date the Ninth day of this Instant October Under the hands and Seals of Richard Ricards and Richd Farmer Esqrs. two of this Majtes Justices of the peace for the sd County one being of the Quorum Robert Hunt Elizabeth his wife and their two Children that is to Say Frances aged about three years and Mary aged about Seven Months the Lawfull Issue of the said Robt. Hunt and Elizabeth his wife was removed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chappell in the said County to the Liberty of the Old Artillery Ground as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your peticoner think themselves agreived by the warrt. or order of the said two Justices and Humbly appeal against the same.

Your Petrs: therefore humbly pray you would be pleased to order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of white Chappell and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the permises and that Your Petrs. may have such releife therein as to Justice and Law doth appertain

And your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

Thos: Teaton Peter Aubar} Overseers of the poor
