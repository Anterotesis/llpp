To the Right Worspll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. That one Joms Delany wife of Solomon Delonay by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of two his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing docse the Seventh day of May last was removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the said County to th said parish of St Luke Alledgeing the said parish is the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Jane Delomy That your Petr doubt not but to prove that the Said Jane Delomy had no legall Settlement in the Said parish of Saint Luke whereby they Canceive themselves aggreived by the Indgment of the said two Justices and doe humbly Appeal to your Worps thereupon

your Petrs therefore humbly prays your Worps will be pleased to appoint a day to hear them in the premises and Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the Sd County to hear & abide the determinacon of your Worps touching the Said Appeals.

And your Petr shall pray Etc

Chas Robinson John Bayles} Churchwardens

Thos Everatt Edwd Curtis} Overseers
