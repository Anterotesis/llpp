The Humble Petition of Mary Jones

That was Sent To hard Labour, for 2 Months for Selling Spirituous Liquors, Contrary to ye. Act of Parliament & Not Knowing ye Rules thereof She humbly Desiring to be Released, having 2 Young Children, Which will be Starv'd, as belonging to No Parish, She Lying on yt. boards & having but a half pinny Worth of bread & Water for 24 times & her Children Must of Nessesity Starve; having No friends then her
