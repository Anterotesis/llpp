To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Gabriel Fouace & Henry Fielding Esqrs. two of His Matie's Justices of the Peace for the City & LIbty of Westmr. in the sd. County of Middx (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st day of March last Alice Carne was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty aforesd. in the said County to the said Pairsh of St. Giles in the fields as the Place of her last legall Settmt.

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggreived by the Order or Pass Warrt. of the sd. two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court agt. the same and humbly Pray that yor. Worships will be pleas'd to appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Dan Bolton soll for the Appellts.
