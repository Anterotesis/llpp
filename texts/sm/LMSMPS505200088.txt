To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace hold in and for the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of January 1763 Margaret William Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Christ Church in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement By which said pass or order of the said two Justices your Petitioners and Appellants apprehend themselves aggrieved.

Therefore your Petitioners and Appellants pray that this Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Christ Church and all Persons concerned to Attend And that your Petitioners and Appellants may be relieved in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners and Appellants shall ever pray

Febry 26th. 1763

James Praed Attorney for Appellants}
