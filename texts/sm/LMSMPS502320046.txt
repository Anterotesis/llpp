To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of John Milner & John Ellis two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace Quorum unus bearing date the 24th. day of December last Anne Singer Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds to the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London as the Place of her last Legall Settlemt.

That your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the sd two Justices & hope to make it Appear that the sd. Anne Singer hath not any Legall Settlemt. in the Sd. Parish of St Dunstan in the West And therefore do appeal from the sd. Order to the Judgmt. of this Court

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day this present Sessions to hear & determine the matter touching this Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray
