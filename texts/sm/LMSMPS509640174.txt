The Petition of Solomon Hart

Sheweth That your Petitioner as appears by the Copy of the Conviction hereto annexed was on the eighth day of October instant convicted in the Penalty of ten pounds for carrying to sell and Exposing to Sale as in Conviction stated Your Petitioner having on Such Conviction Extend into a Recognizance to appeal at the present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex at the New Sessions House Clerkenwell Green but the Conviction not having been filed with th Clerk of the Peace till after the Commencement of this present Sessions. Your Petitioner could not prosecute the same Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays that he may be permitted to enter his Appeal and that the Recognizance may be respited to the next Sessions
