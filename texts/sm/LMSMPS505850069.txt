To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Hill London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. Day of April last John Brown Coffeeman Esther his Wife and Esther their Daughter aged about nine years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Maryhill London as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order and pray that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned unto such day in the next general Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for this county to hear and Determine the same and that your Worships will be pleased to make your Order for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs and all Persons concerned to then Attend

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc. William Elliot Sollr. for the appellants
