To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens of and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint sepulchre in the City of London

Sheweth That by Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sampn Wright and John Machin Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the Nineteenth day of April 1775 Elizabeth Adams widow of Thomas Adams deceased and Amos aged 10 Years Hannah aged 7 Years Mary aged 6 Years or thereabouts and Thomas aged 6 weeks or thereabouts Children of the said Thomas Adams were removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the City of London as the place of their legal Settlement

Your Petitioner thinking themselves (Aggrieved by the said Order or pap Warrant of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved and that a day may be appointed at this new [...] Sessions of the for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

And your Petitioners will over pray Etc
