To his Majties. Justices of the peace now Sitting al Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Paul shadwell in the said County

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order Under the hands and Seals of Ralph Harwood and Alexander pitfeild Esqr two of his Majties. Justices of the peace of the said County Quor Un Margarett Goodacre and her two Children Richard aged about 6 years and Jane aged about two years were passed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch to the said Parish of St. Paul Shadwell as the place of their last legall Settlement by which Order your Petitionrs. themselves agreived and Humbly appeal against the same

And pray that yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the [---] poor of the said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch to attend this Court and abide the finall determination thereof

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
