To The Worshipfull The Chairman and The Bench of This Majesties Justices of Peace at Hicks's Hall

The Humble Petition of Ephraim Brooks Enqine Maker

Most Humbly Sheweth

That Your Petitioner being Committed by the Worshipfull bench Yesterday Humbly Sollicitis a Discharge from Prison for which shall be for ever bound to Pray

for Your Worships Health and Happiness

Who am Your Worships Most Obedt

Febry 15th 1774} Dutifull Humble Servant Ephraim Brook

P:S:And I doubt not but my liberty if your Worships think & proker will he of more Importance to the publick than being here

Idleness is the root of all Evil and what I Rbher
