To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrews Holborn above Bars in the said County

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of N. Conant and P. New Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. March last Maria Key and her two infant Children born in lawful Wedlock, namely William aged Seven years, & John Henry aged five Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of St. Andrew Holborn above bars as the place of there last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Sessions of the peace to the holden for the said County and that all parties concerned may be then Ordered to attend to abide the judgment and determination of your Worshipful touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner Etc
