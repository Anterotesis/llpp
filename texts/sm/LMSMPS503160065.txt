To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwd. & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westmr. in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Rich: Peirson & Jno. Rathbone Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (One where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 31st. Day of March last Robert Usher & Elizabeth Usher his Wife were removed From the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Ann Westmr. whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the Adjudgemt. of the said Two Justices and pray Your Worships will be pleased to order that the Churchwdns. & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary Le Bone and all persons Concerned Do Attend this Court some Day in this present Session to hear & Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc
