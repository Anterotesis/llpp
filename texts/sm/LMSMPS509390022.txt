Middlesex to Wit General Quarter Session 1797

To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices Assembled to keep this Peace Etc the in and for the County of Middlesex at this Present Session of Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Stanton near winchcomb in the County of Glencester

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Patrick Colquhoun and William Brodie Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex whereof one is of the Quorum Acting in and for the said County bearing date the 27th day of March 1797 Esther Chambers Singlewoman Was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Stanton as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Esther Chamber

That in as much is your Petrs. do conceive themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or order and the removal and conveyance of the said Esther Chambers in pursuant thereof

Your Petitioners therefore appeal Parish the said Pass Warrant or order of removal and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that your worship will be pleased to appoint some day in the present Session for the having and determining this their said Appeal

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
