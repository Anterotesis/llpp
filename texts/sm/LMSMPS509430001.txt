To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Vitue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of G. Mercer and J: Mercer Esqures two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of November last Mary Ann Hodges Singlewomen was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London.

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly Appeal against the same and pray this Court that the Benefit of the said Appeal be saved into them until the next Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all Persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray
