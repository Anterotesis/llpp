To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardns. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westmr. in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Robt Jackson and R. Dennett two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Second day of November Anno Dui 1725. Sarah the Wife of [...] William Parish and her three Children (Vizt) Sarah Aged about seven Years, Elizabeth Aged about two Years and a half and Wm. Aged about three Quarters of a Year were removed from and out of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechapel in the said County of the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as place of their Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves agrevied by the Judgement of the said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worship against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worships. will please to Order; That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the said County may attend this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appear.

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
