To the Worspll. Her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of Charles Wapshott

Sheweth That Yor. Petitionr. stands convicted before Yor. Worships for a Trespasses for which Yor: Worps. were pleased to Fyne him Ten pounds and to stand comitted to New Prison untill he pay the same, where he now remains

That Yor. Petr. is in a very Poor & low Condition having sustained great Losses, and hath not where wth. to maintain his Family, much lesse to pay so great a Fine: And whilst he continues in Prison for the same, his Family must starve.

That the damage done to the Prosecutor was Not of the Value of One Shilling, there being only a Staple o fa Door drown

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays Yor Worps. will be pleased to mittigate his said Fine, as to Yor. Worps. shall seem meet.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
