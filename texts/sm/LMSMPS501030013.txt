To ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Mary Essington

That About the Third of August last yor Petr became on [...] Apprntice to Henry Stafford & Elizabeth his wife for the formed of Four Yeares Concerning From the day of the date of her Indenture of Apprnticeshipp as thereby may Appeare

That the sd Stafford & his Wife have not Allowed yor Petr. such necessarys as are Fitting for an Apprntice But yor Petr said Misstress hath Frequently were & Pawned yor Petr Cloathes Most of the best of wth now lye Pawned by yor. Petr. said mistress And for as much as the sd Henry Strafford Standing bound for his Appearance in this Honorable Courtt this Prsent Sessions

P Tho Nash

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to Groult that She may be discharged from her sd. Apprnticeshipp

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc Mary Essington her Mke
