To the Right Worshipfull his Majestres Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church in the said County

Sheweth

That by Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of Philip Dyot and Jo Hucks Esquiers two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventeenth day of September Last past Whereby Mary Brown Wife of John Brown was removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County to the parish of Christ Church in the said County as the place of her Last Leg at Settlement

your petitioners conceving themselves agrieved by the Warrant or order of the Said Two Justices of the peace do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court Shall seem Meet

and your petitioners shall ever pray Etc,

Wm. Lane Lewis Gilbert} Church Wardens

Claude Croque Joseph Howse} Overseers of the poor
