To the Worshipfull the Chairman and others his Majesty's Justices of the place, at this Quarter Sessions Assembled The Humble Petition of Thos. Westbeer

Sheweth That Your Worship's Pets. is charged in Custody of the Keeper of New prison, by Order of Court, but not Absolutely committed on the Complaint and priveance of One Susannah Bedwell on Account of Receiveing half a Crown for writeing a Seller as was supported By Evidence in Court

The therefore most humbly begs that he may be Tryed Bailed on discharged or otherwise as to this Honble Court shall seem meet and bess to be referred

And Your Petr: as in Duty Bound will Ever Pray

Thos: Westbeer
