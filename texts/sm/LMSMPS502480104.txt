Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Jutices of the peace at the Genrall Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Over Seers of the poor of the Precint of White Fryers in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands & seales of [...] of his Matys Justices of the Peace within the said County of middx (Quor Unus) bearing Date the 17th Day of novr last whereby Alice Hall Widdow & her s Children Viz Elizabeth aged about 18 Years Rebecca aged about 16 Years of Temperance aged about 10 Years were sent of removed from the parish of st James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx unto the Reciect of white Tryers in the City of London Allead geing Yor Petrs said Precinct of white Fryers to be their last Legall settlement

Whereofre your Peticoners humbly conceiving themeselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices Do Appeale to Yor Worships thereon & humbly Pray they may be he ard is the Prmes and that the Churchwardens of overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St James Clerkenwell May appear before iron to hear the Judgment of Determinacon of Your Worshipp touching this appeale of Abide by the order of this Court therein

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc
