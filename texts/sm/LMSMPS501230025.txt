To the Right Worpll for Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon & appeale of William Rich.

Sheweth That by order dated the 26th day of May last under the hands & Seales of Roger Smyth & John Steuart Esqrs two of her Maties Justices of the peace for this County (quor nuns) Your Petr is adjudged the Father of a Female (pretended) bastard child named Martha bor [...] on the body of Margarett Needes wife of George Needes, in the parish of St Leonard Shorditch in this County which child hath (as is alledged) allmost ever Since the birth thereof been [...] chargeable to the Said parish and So likely to continue.

That your Petr is thereby ordered weekly to pay to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the poor of the Said parish for the time being Soe long as the Said Child Shall be chargeable to the Same, the Sume of two Shillings & Six pence weeke towards the Nurseing & bringing up of the Said child And also to pay to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the poore of the Said parish Forty Shillings for the charge of lyeing in Nurses Wages Midwife & other necessaries And the Sume of two Shillings & Six pence weekly untill the Said child Should attain the Age of Ten years, And that your Petr Should give Security to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Said parish for the time being & their Successors to discharge & keep the Said parish harmless from all charge concerning the keeping or releiveing of the Said child.

That your Petr finds himselfe aggreived by the Said order of the Said two Justices & humbly appeales against the Same.

Your Petr therefore humbly prayes your Worpps will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St Leonard Shoreditch & all other persons concerned to attend this Court this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale And to be releived herein & [...]

And your Petr Shall pray Etc.
