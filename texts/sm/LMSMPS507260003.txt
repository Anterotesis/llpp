To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor in the Parish of Saint James the Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of this Order of Removal under the Hand and Seal of David Walter said Phillip Dyot Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and For the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quroum) bearing Date the foreteenth Day of Gentl last whereby Margaret Chanter Wife of William Chanter Gone from then and there service in their Middlesex Street in as a Substitute for John Guildsroy of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County forced her two Children by her said Husband Vizt Catherine aged about 3 Years and William aged about one Year and seven Months was removed and conveyed from the said parish of Saint Giles in the Fields to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of the last legal Settlement of the Said William Chanter and or his said Wife and two Children Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court apt the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appear same Day in the present Session for the Wearing and determining the said Appeal and to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the field and all Person conceived do then attend this appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc Jas Dyson Soliciter for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westiminster
