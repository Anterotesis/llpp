To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of George Howard and Richard Manley Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing date the 20t. Day of November 1747 Jane May Widow was removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westmr. in this County to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid as the place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend

And Your Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc S Parry Agent for Appellants.
