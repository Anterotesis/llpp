To the Rt: Worppll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon. of Thomas Fensham.

Sheweth. That your Peticoner by his Indenture of Apprenticeship bearing [...] the Third day of September Anno Dom. 1712. And in yr. Eleaventh of her now present Maties: Reigne became an Apprentice to [...] Hanson of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in [...] Baker for the Terme of Seaven Yeares Comemeing from [...] Recited Indenture. At thereby may more att large Appeare.

That Your Peticoners. said Master hath very frequently beat [...] bruised him in a most barbarons and [...] mmane marnner with Cause and hath not allowed him sufficient necessaries In so much that [...] drank nothing but Water three Weeks togeather.

That your Peticoner about three Weeks agoe went to the Worppll: Justice Fulle [...] and the made abovesaid Complaint who granted his Warrant against [...] said Samuell Hanson his Master is order to have brought him before some other of her Maties. Justices of the peace to answer yr abovesaid Complt and to be further dealt with according to Law But your Peticoner freinds and yr. Constable having been very diligent [...] to take his said Master. And he Absconding and hidering himse [...] Justice

Therefore humbly prays Your Worpps that Conside [...] the premises. you will be pleased to grant an Orde [...] the said Master to shew Cause why he should not discharged from his said Indenture of Apprentices and to appeare some day this present Sessions to [...] said cause or that Your Worpps would be pleas [...] discharge him from the same. for that he [...] be taken.

And Your Peticoner As in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray [...]
