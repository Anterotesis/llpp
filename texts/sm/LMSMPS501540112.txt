To ye. Rgt. Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeale of Ellen Dawson of ye. parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch. in ye. sd County Widow

Sheweth That Your peticoner is rated 7s:7d: p: Quarter to ye. rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd parish and that by reason of her Low Circumtances she is not able to pay ye. Same She haveinge but very Smale Bussieness whereby to maintaine her Selfe

Your peticoner therefore humbly prayes an Order of this Honable: Court, that She may be releived in ye. premisses as to Your Worsps. shall Seem meet.

And Your peticonr. shall ever pray Etc:

Ellen Dawson.

Middx ss: Pursuant. to ye. Order of ye. Gen [...] Quarter Sessions of ye. peace wth. in written [...] Whose names are hereinto Subscribed Two up his Matis. Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd of ye. appeale & Compll. of Ellen Dawson above named; ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor [---] & Severall. other of ye. Inhitants of ye. sd pish of St. Leonard Shoreditch. beinge prsent. & upon heareinge all ptyes & duely Consideringe ye. reasons & allegucons on all Sides We report our oppinions to this Honble: Court that ye. sd Ellen Dawson ought to be eased & abated one penny p: weeke to ye. Last Rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd pish, upon her payinge all arrcars to any former rate made for that purposs. & for ye. future to pay Six pence p: weeke & noe more, Given under our hands this 31: day of May ann Dom 1716:

all ptyes accquiessinge

Alex: Pitfeild. Sam Benson
