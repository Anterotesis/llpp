To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Christopher Clitheroe Esqr.

Sheweth That yor Petr is rated and Assessed for & towards the Releife of the poor of the Parish of Ealing otherwise Zealing in this County after the rate of £20. P ann for his Estate note in his own Occupacon

That the said Estate hath been hitherto charged at £8. P ann only. and hath been Lett by Lease at the yearly Rent of £8. 10s. and no more.

Your petr therefore finding himself aggrieved by the said Rate, Appeals to this Court against the same. And prays, the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish, may be Ordered to [...] this Court some day this Prsent Sessions to show cause why yor petr shall not be relieved in the Prmisses.

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc
