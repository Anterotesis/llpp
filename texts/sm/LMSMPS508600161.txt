The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Brides in the City of London.

Sheweth That Hanna Prosser Singlewoman was by Order or pass Warrant Dated the fourth day of January One thousand seven hundred and ninety one under the Hands and Seals of G. Mercer and J. Mercer two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County Removed and Conveyed from and out of he Parish of Saint Marylebone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Bridget otherwise Brides London aforesaid as the place of her last Settlement

Your Petitioners and Appellants conceiving themselves aggrieved Humbly Appeal to the Judgment of this Honourable Court against the same and pray that this Honourable Court and be pleased to hear and Determine the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may abide the Judgment and Determination thereof And that your Petitioners may be Relieved in the Premises as this Honourable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
