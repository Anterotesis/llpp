To the Rt. Worpll His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poor of the Parish of Juer in the County of But

Humbly Sheweth That by An Order under the hands & Seales of John Fuller and William Cotesworth Junr Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx Quor Unus [...] [...] bearing Date ye. twentieth Day of December last past John Hutchins wth Ann his wife and their Son about One Year and a half Old were Removed from the Parish of Hornsey in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of poor in the County of Bucks as the place of the last legall Settlements of the said John Hutchins

Yor Petr. thinking themselves aggrieved wth the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeale there from & Pray that the Church- wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Hornsey may be Ordered to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yr Petr. as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc
