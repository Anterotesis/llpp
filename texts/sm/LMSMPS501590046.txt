To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble petition of Jacob Wilkins of St Catherines near the Tower of Londn. in the said County Marriner

Sheweth

That your petitioner lost his legg and half his thigh in an Action on board the Sea horse Man of Warr in the Year one Thousand Seaven hundred and two and having- a wife and four Small Children [...] by which loss and the great charge of his family and being out all manner of buisness is rendred thereby to extream poverty and want

He therefore humbly prays your Worships the Consideration thereof and being an object of pity to grant him among others a certain same or County pention towards the support of himself and poor family and as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

Wee whose hands are here und set Inhabitants of St Catherines aforesaid do hereby Certifye and recommend the above petitioner to be and object of Charity and beleive the above petition to be very true

R. Ley. Minister;

Jonath: Dennis Edwd : Whitaker} ouer sear of Ye poore
