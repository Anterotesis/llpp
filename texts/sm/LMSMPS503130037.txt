To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx [...] in General Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Saviours Southwarks in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order of removal under the hands and Seals of Anthony Wrath and Thomas Rohe Esqrs: Two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bareing Date the Eighteenth Day of October 1735 Jemima Ingram Singlewoman aged about fourteen years was removed from the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St Saviours Southwark in the County of Surrey as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves agrieved and Humbly appeal against the Said Warrant or Order and prays that your Worships will appoint a day this Sessions for the hearing and determining the Same and that the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St Luke and all other personl concerned be Summoned to attend this Court to hear and abide by the Judgment and Determination of the Same

And your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Nathl: Payntel Agent for St. Saviours Southwark
