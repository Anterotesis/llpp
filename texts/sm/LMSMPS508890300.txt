Middlesex Octr. Sessions 1793} To the Worshipsful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary abbotts Kensington in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Tyler and Willm. Jones Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in the said County (One hereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of September last Henrietta White the Daughter of William White by Jane his Wife, both deceased, was Removed from the Parish of St. James Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kensington as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order, and do Appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Day may be Appointed of this present Session to hear and determine the said Appeal, and that all Parties concerned may them and their Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Stokes } Churchwarden

Wm. Wride Wm. Newell} Overseers
