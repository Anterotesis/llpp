To ye: Rt: Worshipll. His Maties Justices of the Peace for ye: County of Middx assembled in their Generall Sessions of ye Peace

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of ye Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County aforesd:

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Matthew Hewitt & Thomas pindar Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 17th Day of July last Ruth Hollyman wife of wm Hollyman wth. their two Children vizt. Mary aged about Seven years & Withers aged about a quarter of a yeat were remov'd from the parish of St. Sepulchre in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of their last legall Settlemt:

Yor; Petr. think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the Sd. two Justices appeal to this Court & humbly pray that yr: Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd: parish of St. Sepulchre & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide ye judgmt & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeall

And Yor Petr: shall ever pray Etc
