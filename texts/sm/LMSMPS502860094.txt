To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth.

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Cliffd. Willm. Phillips and Richd. Ricards Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx- (Quorum unus) being Date the Thirty first Day of July last Hannah Dean widow of John Dean & three Children (Vizt.) Elizabeth Dean aged Eleven years Eleanor Dean aged Seven years & Ann Dean aged Eighteen were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last Legal Settlement whereby Yor. Petrs. conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to yor. Worships against the said Pass warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices & Pray that the Benefit of the Said Chppeal may be Saved unto them untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the County of Middlesex.

And Yor. Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc.
