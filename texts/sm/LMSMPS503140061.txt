To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in there Generall Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Snitterfeld in the County of Warwick

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Thomas Cotton and Owen Davies Esqr. two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (Whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of Augt. last past John Jordon Margtt. his Wife and their two Children (that is to say) John aged upwards of three years and Thomas aged upwards of One year were removed from the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist in the City of Westminster in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of Snitterfeld Alledging the said Parish of Snitterfeld to be their last legall place of Settlement

That your Petrs. conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to yor. Worshipps therefrom And humbly Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses And Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. John the Evangelist may Appear before you at a Certain day to be Appointed to hear & Abide the Judgment & determinacon of yor. Worshipps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Dovey Agent for the Appellants}
