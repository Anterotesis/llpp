To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Dennett and Richard Ricards Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourteenth day of June last past Francis West and Hannah his wife and Francis West their Child aged about One and Twenty Months were Removed and Conveyed from the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the same County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceiveing themselves Aggrieved thereby and Appeal from the Judgement of the said Two Justices to the Judgement of this Honourable Court And desire a day of hearing may be Appointed this present Sessions and that all Persons Concerned may be Ordered to Attend to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray.
