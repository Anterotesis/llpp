To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds.

The humble Peticonr of Lydied Partiace of the Parish of St Giles Cripplegate In the said County.

Sheweth That your Petr is Allowed Nine Pence p Weeke as a Pension from the said Parish Out of which She payes Six Pence p Weeke for Lodging And She not being able to Subsist on that Pension to pay her Lodging And for her Maintynence hath run In Debt for rent to her Land Lady and is turned out of her Habitaton Soe that She is Ready to Parish

p Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore humbly prays. such an Augmentacon of her sd Pension as unto yor Worsppsll Shall See me Meete

And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc. Lydia Partrick her [x] Mke
