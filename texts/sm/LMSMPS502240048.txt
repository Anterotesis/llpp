To the Worpll. the Justices of Peace for the County of Middlx in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Science & William Seavern [...] & James Glascock. & Henry Fox

Sheweth That in a Rate lately made for reliefe of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the sd. County Your Petnrs. are ever rated not only beyond a proporcon with others in the same rate but even beyond their rents

Your Petnrs. therefore humbly Pray to be relieved in the premisses as to Your Worpps shall seem under

And Your Petnrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Thos: Science Will. Seaverne James Glascock
