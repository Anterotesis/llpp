Middx ss. To the right Worppfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace at their Genall Quarter Sessions now Assembled in and for the Said County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michaell Wood Street London.

Sheweth: That by Virtue of [...] pass Warrant or Order under the hands and seales of John Milner and R. Gilford Esqrs. Two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace of this County (Quor unus) & bearing date the Fourth day of November last Bernard Reeves Son of John Reeves was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the Said County unto the Parish of St. Michaell Wood Street a foresaid as the Place of his last legall Settlement By which Order your Peticoners conceive themselves Aggreived and humbly Appeales therefrom.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore Pray this Honeble. Court to appoint a day for the hearing the Merritts of this Appeale and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Said Parish of Saint Gyles in the Feilds may attend and Abide the Determinacon and Judgment of this Court touching the Same

And your Petr. Shall ever party Etc.
