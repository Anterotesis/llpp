Midd ss: To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace, now holden att Hickes Hall for the Sd County the humble Peticon of John Angle most Humbly Seweth.

May it Please Yor Worships, Your Peticonr who is an Inhabitant in the Parish of St Gyles's Cripple Gate without, and being a poor man, and being Rated to pay to the Poor which he apprehends himself not able to pay

Your Peticonr therefore humbly prays Such relief in this affair as Yor Worsps in Yor great Wisdom & Goodness Shall Se meet

And Yor Petr

Will Ever Pray
