To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thos. Gordon and W. Addington Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th. day of November last past Ann Chantres was removed and conveyed by the Names of Ann Gibson from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Chartres.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a Day in the present Session for the Wearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc.

Saml: Malbon Solr. for Saint Paul Shadwell.
