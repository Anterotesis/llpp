To the Right Worsr: her Majtys. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition of Jacob Bonnell Citizen & Vintner of London

Sheweth. That your Petitioner hath been Conversant in busines in General as well in foreigne Parts as in this Kingdom, whereby he humbly hopes he hath gained such Experience as to be thought fitty Qualifyed for yr: Worpps: Service

And your Petr: being at present our of Imployment

Your Petr: therefore humbly prays yr: Worpps: to bestow on him the Post of Keeper of New Prison now vacant, which Trust, if Your Worspps: shall think fit to confer on him, he will endeavour with all Duty & faithfulnes to Discharge, to the entire, satisfaction of the Honourable Bench

And yr: Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
