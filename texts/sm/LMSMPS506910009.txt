To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midds.

The Humble peticon of Susanna peirce of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County aforesd Widow

Sheweth That yor. Petr. last Sessions did peticon yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish that they might allow yor. Petr. some pencon towards her releife and two Children.

That yor. Worspps were pleased to referrr the matter to Joseph Short and John Crosbie Esqrs. two of her Matys Justices of the peace for the sd County in order to Examine into yor. petrs. said Complaint Wch they did and were pleased to order that your petr. should have 12d. P week Which the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor doe refuse to allow yor. Petr. Soe that yor. petr. must inevitably perish unless releived by this Honble bench

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to be [...] ted [...] to order the [...]
