To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and John Scott Edquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th day of August last, Hannah Edwards Wife of John Edwards and their Child aged about eight Years, were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the said. County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudications and Order of the said two Justices, and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof, and that all persons concerned therein may have Notice to Attend the same

And Your Petitioners will ever pray George Barber Joseph Fishey. Richard Willan John Betteley Haygarth Tayler Hodgson
