To the Worshippfull her Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of Egbert Guede Francis Rich, Phillip Martin Francis Cremer, and Thos. Herbert late Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County

Sheweth That yor petrs being elected Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Clement Danes for ye Yeare 1710. have expended and laid out for the necessary Releife and maintenance of the poor of the said parish the Sume of Two hundred [...] eighty four pounds fourteen shillings and four pence farthing more than they have Collected and received for the use of the poor of the said Parish as by their Accounts stated and allowed by the Vestry of the said Parish and afterward Examined and allowed Richard Bealing and John Smith Esqrs two of her Matyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx may appeare

Ros Chimmows Therefore yor. petrs. humbly pray yor. Worspps to take their Case into yor. Confideracon And to Order the Churchwardens and other the antient Inhabitants of the said Pish of St. Clemt. Danes to make a rate on the sevall Inhabitants of the said Parish to reimburse yor. petrs. the said Sume of Two hundred eighty four pounds fourteen shillings and four pence farthing due to them as aforsesaid

And yor. petrs. shall forever Pray Etc
