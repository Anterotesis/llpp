Middx ss. To the Rt. Worspll. his Majtes. Justices of the Peace for ye sd County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Anthony Buckmaster one of ye petty Constables of southmims in his County

Sheweth That at Midsumr. last past there was due & owing unto yor. petr. the sume of ten pounds for a qrters. Salary for his loss of times & expences in passing and conveying Vagrants through this County.

That yor. Petr. has a very great family of Childrn. and has been at a great expence in Pforming the sd Service.

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the payment of the said ten pounds.

And yor. Petr. shall pray Etc.

Anthony Buchmaster
