To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gregory in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Sir Fisher Tench Barronet & Thomas Robe Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirty first Day of January last past, John Ondy and Sarah his Wife were removed from the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborne in the County of Middlesex unto the Said Parish of Saint Gregory London alledging the Said Parish of Saint Gregory to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

C Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrantor Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to your Worships therefrom, and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses, and order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Liberty of Saffron hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbourne in the Said County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeale.

And your Petitioners shall pray
