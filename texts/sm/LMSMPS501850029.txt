To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Stevenson of the Parish of St. Mary Islington in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petitr at Easter last was chosen to serve the office of one of the Overseers of the poor of the aforesaid Parish for the year Ensuing

That your Petr: is troubled with Fitts in soe much that two or three times in a day he has been taken up for dead and hath remained soe for a considerable time and is now under ye Care of Physicians Whereby he is rendred. uncapable of serving the said Office

Yor. Petr therefore humbly prays he may be discharged from serving the said office & a fit prson chose in his roome

And Yor Petr Shall Ever Pray etc.

J Stevenson
