To the Regt: Worpl: the Bench of Justices assembled together at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon of Margarett Fillow. & Elizabeth Grimes

Sheweth

That yor. peticonr having Sworne the peace agt: one Isabella Anderson and being bound over by Recognizance for ye Same to this Sessions of peace. and ye Said Isabella Anderson Still threatning ye life of yor. peticonr.

Yor. peticonr therefore humbly prays yor. Worpl: would be pleased to ordr. yr the Sd Isabella Anderson may Stand continued upon her Sd Recognizance

And yr. peticonr Shall ever pray.
