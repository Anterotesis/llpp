To the Worll. bench of Justices at Hicks's Hall, held for ye County of Middex

The humble Peticon of Mary Berry Widdow.

Humbly Shews That your Petr. hath a Son lying in Clerken: well: Prison at the Suit of his Mastr. but having had his discharge from ye Worpl. Justice Rotherham, he now lyes in the aforesd Prison for his Fees. Yor. Petr. Therefore being very Indigent and not able to assist him with Money to pay his Pay Fees.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays that you Worps. will be pleased to grant him his Fredom.

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
