To the Worll: her Mats: Justices of the Peace in Quarter Sessions at Hickshall.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church= Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Margtt. Westminster

Sheweth That Mary Broadgate Widdow is removed by Virtue of a Pass Signed by the Worp: John Worsley and Germain Ireton Esqr: two of her Mats: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx, out of the Parish of St: Clements Danes in the said County. And brought into the said Parish of St: Margtt: Westmr: as her last Legall Settlement

That yor: [...] think themselves Agg [...] [...]

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Appeal and Pray this Worp: Bench to grant yor: Order to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Clement Danes to Answer the said Appeale

And yor: Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc
