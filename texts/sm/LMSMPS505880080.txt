To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr John Fielding Knt. and Philip Dyot Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of September last Five Children of Archibald Marr and Mary his wife (both deceased) namely Mary aged twelve Years and upwards Archibald aged Eleven years and upwards John aged Nine Years and upwards Barnard aged Seven Years and upwards and William aged four years and upwards were Removed and conveyed from the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St Andrew Holborn in the said County to the Parish of St Giles in the Fields as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said five Children

Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices, there- fore humbly appeal to this Court against the same, And pray your worship will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Thomas Edward Tho Simmonds Thos Hanks
