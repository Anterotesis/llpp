To the Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster in their General Session of the Peace for the sd County City and Liberty assembled.

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Foster lane in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Addington and & Mitchell Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said City and liberty. (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the 27th day of May 1793. Jane Partin wife of John Partin. (gone to sea) was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Leonard Foster lane in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Partin and Jane his wife whereby your Petitioner's conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioner's therefore humbly pray that the benefit of their sd appeal may be reserved to them & that a day may be appointed in the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the sd. County for the hearing & determining the said appeal & that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields he then [...] there required to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt & determination of the Court touching the sd appeal
