To the right Worll: the Justices of the Peace for the County of middx assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County.

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Silver Street London.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seale of Joseph Short and Benjamin Hilton two of her Majtices. Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx & (Quor Anns) upon Complaint made to them by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County One mary Bartholomew supposed to be the Widdow of William Bartholomew deceased with their two Children Virt. Thomas about five yeares old and Catherine about two yeares and an haffe old have been lately sent and delivered to your Petrs. to be provided for and maintained by them.

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselves not lyable to provide for or Maintain the said Mary Bartholomew nor the said two Children for that their said Father had a wife living at the time of his Marriage with the said Mary

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly appeale agt. the Order and pray yor. Worpps. to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County may appeare before you and that yor. Worpps. will be pleased to hear yor. Petrs. in the premisses and order the keeping of the aforesaid Mary Bartholomew and her two Children according to Law.

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.

Richard Chadwick Robt. Peake} Churchwardens.

John Barren John Land} Overseers.
