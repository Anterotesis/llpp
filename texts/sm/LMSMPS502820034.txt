To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in the said County

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens Overseers & Parishoners of the Parish of St Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands & Seals. of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs. bearing date the 17th. day of March last Jane James Widow of Evan James & their Five Children (vizt) John Aged thirteen years & upwards, Mary Aged Eight years, Jane Aged four years, Elizabeth Aged two years, and Letitia. Aged Six Weeks were Removed from the parish of St Giles in the Feilds to the sd Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London as the place of their last Legall Settlement By which Order your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

Your Petr. therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order a short day for the hearing and Determining the Appeal of your Petrs. to such Order

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
