To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of the Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Low Church called in the Order of Removal Saint mary High Church in the Town of Kingston upon Hull.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller and H. Jackson Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth Day of November last past Ann Robinson Wife of Thomas Robinson (now at Sea) and her Female Bastard Child named Ann of the Age of Six Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shore ditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Low Church by the Description of Saint Mary High Church aforesaid in the said Town of Kingston upon Hull as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Robinson and her Female Bastard Child where by your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order, and humbly pray Relief on the Remises, and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in the Present Session for the Hearing and determining this their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Allen's Furnivals Inn Sollicitors for Appellants
