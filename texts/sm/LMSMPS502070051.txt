To ye: Rt. Worshipll. His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd assembled in their Generall Sessions of ye: Peace

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye: parish of St: Giles in the Fields in ye sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Daniel Combes & William Gore Esqrs: two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date ye 26 Day of November last Abigail Balland widow of William Balland Deced was removed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the sd. County to the said parish of St. Giles in ye fields as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

Yor: Petrs: think themselves aggreived by ye Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd: parish of St: Martin in the fields & all other Psons concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgmt: & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor: Petr. shall ever pray Etc
