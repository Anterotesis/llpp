To the Rt. Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall [---] Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon of Thomas Raby of the pish of St. Giles in the Feilds

Sheweth That your Petr. became bound by Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing date the 23d Day of January 1729 to John Ward of the pish of St Giles in the Feilds in the County aforesd Peruke maker & Barber for the fersh of Severn Years of Warn his Art.

That your Petrs. said Master died about Christmass last past That your Petr hath since serv'd [...] Hannah Ward Sister & Adminstratrix to your Petrs. sd late Master.

That yor Petrs. Mistress hath imploy'd one William Warman as her Journyman to instruct your [---] in the sd trade

That the sd William Warman hath struck & beat your Petr. wth Instruments [...] not proper to correct yor Petr. & hath bar barously beat & abused yor Petr. & swears that he will never teath your Petr. his trade

And although yor Petrs. sd Mistress knows & hath been acquainted by your Petr of this ill Ussage hath not thought proper to prevent it.

yor Petr. therefore most humbly prays that your Worps wou'd be pleased to appoint some Day this present Sessions for the sd [...] Hanna Ward yor Petrs Mistress to attend this Honble Court to shew cause why yor Petr shod. not be discharged from his Sd Indenture of Apprenticehood

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall pray Etc Thos Raby
