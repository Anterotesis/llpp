To The Robt Worshipll. their Maties Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of the Churchwarden of the Hamlett of Mile end Old Towne in the Pish of Stepney in the Sd County

Sheweth

That one Ann Cullum was by warant under the hands & Seales of two of their Maties Justices of the peace for the said County lately passed from the pish of St Paules Shadwell in ye Sd County to ye said Hamlett

That the Sd Cullum was soe passed to ye said Hamlett upon Oath of the Beadle of the Sd Pish of St Pauls Shadwell that the Sd Cullum was resident in the said Hamlett about Seaven Years past when infrote the Sd Cullum has not been resident in the Sd Hamlett this 8 Years past & upwards

Yor Peticonr Therefore most Humbly Prayes yor Worshps to be pleased to grant that the said Hamlett may be discharged from the Mentaineance of the Cullum

And yor. Peticonr shall ever pray Etc

A true Copy
