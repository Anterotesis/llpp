To the Rt: Worpll: his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Quartr. Sessions Assembled:

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church: wardens and Overseer: of the poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westmr.

Sheweth That Henry King an Infant, was by Order Dated the Seventh Day of Septembr. 1716: under the Hands and Seals of Richd. woodward and Moses Good year Esqr: Two of his majtyes Justices of the Peace for the County of midlx (Quor Unns) removed and conveyed from the parish of Chelsea in the sd County unto the sd parish of St: Ann. That your Petr. have carefully enquired according to the reasons aledged in the sd Order but can not finde that the sd Henry King hath a legall settlement in the sd Parish of St. Ann.

Yor Petr. there fore humbly conceives them selves agrieved and appeals from the Judgment of the Two Justices of the peace to the Judgmt: of this Honble. Court and crave a day of hearing and that the Church wardens, and Overseers: of the poor of the parish of Chelsea may then attend so that the sd Henry King may be so setled as this Honble: Court in their wise Judgment shall think fitt.

And Yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
