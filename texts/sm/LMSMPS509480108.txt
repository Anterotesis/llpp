To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex Assembled at their General Quarter Session of the Peace begun and holden the Eighth day of January in the 38th year of King George the Third

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwns and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Wm Bleamire & Aaron Graham Esqrs Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex (One of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th. day of Decr. 1797 Ann Evans (with her illegitimate Child named Elizth. aged about 12 Months for Nurture) was removed and conveyed from & out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly Pray relief in the Premises And that your Worships will be Pleased to Order that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them that the herring & determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace for the said County of Middx and that some day in the same Session may be appointed for that Purpose

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
