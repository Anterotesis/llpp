To the Right Worsps. his Maties Justice of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Salmon

Sheweth

That this Gent Sessions your Petr. Alversary Jno. Corney is obliged to try the Traverss upon his Indictmt. for abuseing your Petr. in very barbourous manners.

That your Petr. is very poore Curt haveing money to Fee [...] & the sd. Corney haveing cause your Petr. to imstarry

P the Nash.

Yor Petr. there fore most hum [...] Prayes your Worsps. to be pleased to [...] her aggreivanced un [...] Worsp shall seem meet

And yer Pr. Shall ever Pray Etc
