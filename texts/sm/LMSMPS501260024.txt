To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County [...] in St John Street in Com pre die Lune (Scilt) tertio die Decembris Anno Rui Due ure Anne Dei gra mmc Regine magne Britannie Etc decimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hornsey in this County have notice of this peticon & appeale, and that they & all other parties concerned doe attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to heare & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court touching the sd appeal

P Cur Harcourt
