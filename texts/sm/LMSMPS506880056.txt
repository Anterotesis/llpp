To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon Elizabeth Deale

Sheweth

That On Satturday last One Eliz: Moore als Tite was by the Worspll Justice Perry comitted to New Prison in this County for privately stealing from your Petr. sewall Goods of value.

That your Petr. being att this time very same came of appeare this prsent Sessions in this Honble Cort and forasmuch as your Petr. most Metewall Wittness in this case cannot give Evidence this Sessions.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor Worspps to be pleased to Grant that the sd. Eliz Moore als Tite may be continued in New Prison aforesd. untill next Sessions

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc.

P Tho: Nash
