To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire Chairman & the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the [...] of the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County

That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the [...] Seals of Sir George Booth Baronet & William Blackborow Esqr. two of His [...] Justices of the peace in & for said County one whereof being of the [...] bearing date the 26th day of January 1784 John Ford Aged about 12 years [...] & Conveyed from & out of the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said [...] Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the place settlement of the said John Ford

Your petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant humbly Appeal against the same to your Worships & pray relief in the premises & that a day in this present Session may be appointed for the hearing & Determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will pray Et

Jno Skutt Solr. for appellants

Goodmans Fields 23d Feby 1784
