To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon of Hellen Penn of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County Widdow

Sheweth That your peticoner was allowed Six Shillings P Month as a pention from the said Parish But of take the Same has beene wholly taken away from her

That your peticoner is very much in Debt for Rent and is threatned Daily to be Troubled for it and hath a Small Child to Mainteyne Which she is not able to doe without Relief from the sd Parish

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays She may be allowed [...] Pencon from the sd Parish As to yor Worspps Shall See me Meete

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Hellen Penn

July 31st. 1716

Present John Fuller Tho Rand Esqr. not Received
