To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at the Sessions house on Clerkenwell Green in the sd. County of Middlesex assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church= =wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth. That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Cranley Thomas Kerby and Henry James [---] Pye Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in & for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 16th. day of February 1793. William Bates and Mary his Wife with their four Children vizt. Thomas aged about 12 Years Elizabeth aged about 11 Years John aged 6 years and Joseph aged 4 Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist Westmr. in the said County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of Saint James as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court agst. the said hdpidication of the said two Justices and their sd. Order & pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear & determine the sd. Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc James Dyson Sollr. for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westminster.
