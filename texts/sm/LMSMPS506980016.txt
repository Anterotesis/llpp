The Peticon of Jno King Constable of Old Street Liberty and other the ancient Inhabitants of the Same, in St Giles Cripplegate Parish in this County

Octobr Sessions 1707

To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John King Constable of Old street Liberty in the Parish of St Giles wthout Cripplegate in the St. County on the behalf of himself and divers others ancient Inhabitants of the sd Liberty

Sheweth.

That one Daniel Maynard an Inhabitant in the sd Liberty is chosen to serve the Office of Head borough for the year ensueing but refuseth to be Sworn and to take upon him the Execucon of the same Office.

Therefore yor Peticonr humbly prays an Order from this Honble Co [...] for the sd Daniel Maynard to attend yor Worpp to Shew cause why he refuseth to be Sworn into, and take upon him the execucon of the said office

And yor Peticonr are in duly bound Shall ever pray Etc

Fr: Th: 10 cl
