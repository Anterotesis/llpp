To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of ye Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in ye County of Middx

Sheweth That by vertue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of John Milner & Robert Hucks Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx bearing late the fourteenth day of December last Cary Senner aged about Nineteen Years was Removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the the Fields in the Said County to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of his last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved And appeale against the said Pass warrant or Order unto this Court

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to save them ye Benefitt of this Appeal untill ye next Quarter Sessions & Adjourn ye Same [---] Accordingly

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

J Parry Vestry Clerk
