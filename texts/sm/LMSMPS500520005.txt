To the Right worspll. his Maties Justices [...] Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Robt. Newman of Fryers Bar [...] in the County aforesd. Constable

That yor. petr. did in the time of his sd. Offices disburss the Sum of Â£2.13s.7d in passing and releiveing Cripples & vagabonds & otherwise As by yor. Petr. account ready to be Pencee may appeare.

That yor. Petr. hath often requested the Churchwarden of the sd. Parish to reimburss yor. Petr. the said moneys see by him disbursed as aforesaid but he refuses soe the doe & he being a very Poore man now Stanes in great need of his sd. moneys.

P Westley

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worrps to be pleased to Order that he may be satisfyed the said moneys see by him disbursed as aforesed. by Such waies & meanes as unto [...] worrps hall Seem met.

And yor. Petr. Shall en pray Etc.

Robert Moreman
