To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of John Tyser of the Parish of St: Mary Mat fellon alias White Chappell in the said County Fallow Chandler.

Sheweth That by a Judgement or Order under the hands & Seales of Lawrence Saintlow & Anth Mann Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace of this County (Quor unus) bearing date the twentieth day of May last your Petr. is charged to be the reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child born in the Parish of St: James's Westminster on the nineteenth day of May last on the body of Sarah Roberts Singlewoman That your Petr: thereby thinks himself agrieved and Humbly Appeals to this Honable: Court against the same.

And prays that your Worshipps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: James's Westminster in the said County and all other Parties concerned to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to heare and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale.

And Your Petr: Shall ever pray Etc
