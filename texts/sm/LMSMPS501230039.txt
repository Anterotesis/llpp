To the Right Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Robert Fawdery and Edmund Feribee Church wardens of the Parish of St. Ethelburga London.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated the Fourth day of June last under the Hands and Seales of Philip Howard and John Tully Esqrs. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middx and Libty of Westmr. (Quor unus) one John Watson and Anne his wife were removed from the Parish of St. James at Westminstr in the County aforesd. to the said Parish of St. Etherburga as the place of last legall Settlemt. of the said John Walton.

That Yor. Peticoners find themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Ordr. of the said Two Justices, and humbly Appeale agat. the Same to this Honble. Court,

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to Order the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Said Parish of St. James at Westminstr. and all other persons somerned to attend this Court Some day this Prsent Sessions to heare and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court upon & touching the sd Appeale, & yt. yor. Peticonrs. may have Such releife herein as to yor. Wopps shall Seem meet Etc.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall Pray etc.

Robt: Fawdery Edmund Teribee
