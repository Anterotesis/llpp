To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of St. Thomas Clarges Bart. & S: Steuart Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Four [...] day of February last Jane the wife of George Scarlet aged about thirty years together with Elizabeth aged about One years, and Catherine aged about ten weeks, her Children by her said husband were Removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx aforesd. to the sd. Parish of St. James As the place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggreived

And therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the Sd. Appeal and that all persons Concerned may attend at the Same time

And your Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc
