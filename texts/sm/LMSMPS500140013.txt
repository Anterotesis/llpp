To the Rt: Worspll: their Majesties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The Humble: Peticon of Mary Rosseter

Sheweth That your Peticonr presented one Anne French the last Sessions for buying Clippings Your Peticonr being at great Expence and haveing a Child to maintaine, Went to Benjamine Overton Esqr; who referr'd her back to your Worpp

Where fore your Peticonr. Implores your Worps: would be pleased to Order your Peticonr. her Charges agt the said prosecution or as to Your great Wisdoms Shall think most meete

And Your Peticonr. Shall ever pray Etc.

Peticon of Mary Rosseter

referred Mr Overton to pay 10s a money [...] pender by her do [...] for her trouble [...] of vinr Sr
