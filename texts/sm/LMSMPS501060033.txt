To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Jane Gregory Widdow

Sheweth

That the Place of Matron of the House of Correction att Clerkenwell in this County being vacant And in your Worshipps Disposall And your Petitr: having beene Housekeeper of the said House of Correction for Seaven Yeares Last past and upwards And hath alsoe Officiated and Acted as Matron thereof during the Sickness of Phebe Rawbone Formerly Matron And in the Absence of Mary Weekes Late Matron of the sd House of Correction And having noe other Imployment and Busyness

P Tho: Nash

Your Petr humbly Prays your Worshipps would be Pleased to Bestow the said Place of Matron of the sd House of Correction upon your Petr

And yor Petr: Shall Ever Pray Etc
