To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & seals of William Caslon & Samuel Gower Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh day of February instant Thomas Ratleaf & Elya Blanchia his Wife were removed from the parish of Saint Leonard in Shoreditch in the said County of Middx unto the said parish of Saint Luke in the same County Alledging the said parish of Saint Luke to be the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Petitrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do appeal to Your Worships there from & humbly pray That you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses & order that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Leonard in Shoreditch may Appear before you at a certain day to be [...] Appointed to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

Jno. Witton Agent for ye Appellts.} And your Petitrs. Shall pray Etc
