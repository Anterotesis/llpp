To the worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows the Great in the city of London

Sheweth That under and in Pursuance of an Order made by two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex under their Hands and Seals bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of November now last past Susannah Bullin Wife of John Bullin deceased and their four Children, viz, Elizabeth aged about fifteen Years, Susannah aged about thirteen [---] Years, Esther aged about eight Years, and John aged about five Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County to the said Parish of Allhallows the Great as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray that Your Honours will be pleased to receive their said appeal and adjourn the same to be heard and determined at the next general Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County, Your Petitioners not hearing given due Notice thereof, for the same to be heard and determined during the present Session

John Jackson Wm Walduck} Churchwardens

Mattw Johnson W Horwood} Overseers.
