To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace forth County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of West ham in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hand s and Seals of George Booth and William Blackborrow Esquire, Two of he Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex One being of the Quorum bearing late the Ninth day of February instant Mary Newby and her three Children Vizt. Sarah Aged Thirteen Years and upwards Elizabeth aged Four Years or there about Marianne Aged Two Years or there about were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint James at Clarkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of West ham in the County of Essex at the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order [...] the Removal of the said Elizabeth and Marianne Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray your Worships to Appoint a day for to hear and determine the same
