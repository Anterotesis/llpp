To the Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx at the Generall Quarter Sessions held for the said County

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens And Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. John of Wappin in the said County of Middlx

Humbly Sheweth

That by vertue of an Order under the hands and seals of Bastwick Johnson and Robert Kyrby Esqrs. Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx (Quor unus) one Katha. Blew was removed and Passed from the Hamblet of Wapping Stepney in the said County of Middlx to the parish of St John of Wappin above said as the place of her last legall Setlement whereby the said parish of St. John of Wappin think themselves very much aggreived she not having any legall Setlement there

S: Bass Therefore humbly pray yor. Worshipps will be pleased to admit and appoint some day in this present Quarter Sessions to hear and determine the said Appeal as in your just Judgement shall Seem right

And yor Petitionrs Shall pray Etc
