To the Worshipful his Majties Justices of the peace of the County of Midlex in Quarter Sessions Assembled in & for the Said County

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of ChristChurch in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Anne Bird was (by Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majties Justices of the Peace of the Said County of Midlx bearing Date the 25th. day of November Anno Dom 1720) removed from the parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of ChristChurch as the place of her last legal Settlement That Yor Peticoners humbly conceive that they can make it appear this Court that the place of the last legal Settlement of the Said Anne Bird is not in the Said Parish of ChristChurch

Yor. Peticoners therefore humbly Pray Yor. Worships will be Pleased to Order the Churchwardens And Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Clemt. Danes to Attend this Court for the hearing the Said Appeal at Such time as to Yor. Worships shall Seem Meet.

And Yor. Peticoners Shall ever Pray.
