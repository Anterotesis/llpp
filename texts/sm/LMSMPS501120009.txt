To ye Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Ellis on the behalfe of William Ellis his son.

Sheweth

That the sd Wm: Ellis by his Indenture of Apprenticeship bearing date the tenth day of February in the sixth year of her now Matys reign Annoq Dui 1707 Did put himselfe Apprentice to Richard Shory of the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in ye County of Midds Cordwegner for the term of Seven years comencing from the day of ye. date of the same Indenture as thereby may appear.

That the sd Richard Shory having been often times arrested and being now imprisoned in the Marshalsea for Debt and your Petr. not knowing when he shall gain his Liberty and yor. Petr. being obliged to keep his sd son and that his sd son having no other business to doe but to attend his sd [---] Masters Children so that your petr. is well satisfyed that his sd son will never gain his sd trade.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that his sd son Wm. Ellis may be discharged from his sd Apprenticeship

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
