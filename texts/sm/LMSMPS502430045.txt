To the Right Worpl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrt. under the hands and Seals of Thomas Railton and Henry Trent Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quox unus) bearing Date the 6th day of March 1726 Willm Chapman a Male Bastard Child was sent & delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margarets Westmr. in the County of Middx to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish St. Botolph without Aldgate London Alledging the sd. Parish to be the place of his last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. and Indgment of the said Two Justices And do most humbly appeal to your [...] Worshipps there from

Yr. Petr. therefore most humbly pray your Worps will be pleased to save to them the benefit of this Appeal and appoint a day in this present Sessions for hearing the sd Appeal and Order that the Church wardens & Overseers of ye poor of St. Margarets Westmr and all parties concerned may then Appear and hear & abide the Indgment & determinacon of this Court touching ye sd appeal

And they shall ever pray Etc
