The Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary, Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies Esquire and the Reverend Henry Reynett Doctor in Divinity two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the second day of July 1794 Jane Lansdown Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court from the said Order and Adjudication and pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Sessions and that all Persons concerned may be then ordered to attend

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

John Smith Sollr for Appellants 18th July 1794
