To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Session of the Peace in and for the said County-Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by an order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies Esqr. and Robert Smith Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty seventh day of January last Mary Webster (Widow of George Webster deceased) together with her five Children and lawful Issue by her said Husband namely Mary Aged about Seven years John Aged about ten years and Eight Months Joseph Aged about Seven Years and one Month Elizabeth Aged about three Years and ten Months and a Male Child not yet Baptized Aged about Eleven Months were removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph with out Aldgate in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceives themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humble Pray that a day may be appointed of the Present Session to hear and determine Matter of the said Appeal and that Parties concerned may then Attend

And Your Petitioners ever Pray

S. Wegener Sollr. for Appellants
