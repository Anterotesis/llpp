To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Taylor one of the Constable of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in the County of Middx

Sheweth That your Peticoner did serve the Office of Petty Constable for the year 1719 within the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in this County in which Office he has expended and paid in passing Vagrants the sume of Three pounds as appear's by the severall Certificates of Allowances under the hands and seales of divers of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said County residing in or near the said Parish

Your Peticoner most humbly Pray's your worships to grant him an Order of this Court for Mr. John Mills late Treasurer for Holborne Division to reimburse and pay him the said money soe by him expended as aforesaid or otherwise releive him in the Premisses as to your worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoner (as in duty bound) shall Pray & etc
