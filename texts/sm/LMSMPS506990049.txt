Who the Right Worpfull he Majtyes Justices of for the County of Middx at Hicks Hall assembled

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Cowley in the sd County

Sheweth That by [---] order mader the hands & Seales of the Honble James Bartie Esqr John Jenyur Esqr two of her Matyes Justices of ye peace of ye sd County (Quoy unus) John Sadler & his wife were sent from the Parish of Harmondsworth to the Parish of Cowley in the sd County of the [...] place of the last legall Settlemt of the said John Sadler whereas the said Parish of Cowley is not the place of the last legall Settlemt of the said John Sadler

Your Peticonrs. therefore appeale from ye said order of the said two Justices and desire some day may be appointed for ye hearing and determing of the laid matter And yor Peticonrs shall ever pray
