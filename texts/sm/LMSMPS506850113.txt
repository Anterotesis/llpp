To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County by Adjourn into Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardnes and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George a Hanover [...] within the Liberty of Westminster in this County

Sheweth That to Virtue of [...] Warrant or Order under the [...] Seals of David Walker and Thomas Brook bank Esqrs Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and fid the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum be [...] Dale the [...] 3d [...] October 1778 James Collins [...] Patty his Wife were removed and conveyed from and one of the parish Saint [...] the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parsih Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in [...] said County as the place of their [...] legal Seal [...] where your Petitioner [...] monts besides aggrived and humbly Appeal to this Count against the same

Your Victumens [...] therefore humbly pray that your Westmr will release to ar she has Ben of of the said Appeal may be samed unto then and this the hearing and determination hereof be Adjourned unto the news general Sessions of this Peace to be holden for the said County said that all Persons concerned and be [...] then to read [...]

And your Petitioners will pray etc

Thos. Thornwaite John [...] ison Wm Clarke} Churchwardens and Overseers
