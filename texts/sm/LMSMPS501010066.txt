To the Worshipfull her Maties. Justices of peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Aston in the County of Hertford

Sheweth The by warrant under the hands and seales of Daniel Brattell and Bernard Halfepenny Esqrs. two [...] of her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quo P unus) beareing date the nineteenth day of June last John page and his wife and Sarah, Mary; Ann, James, Joseph & Wm. their children were passed from the parish of Enfeild in the County of Middx unto the said parish of Aston on prtence that John Bigg & Michael Ireland Churchwarden & Overseer of the said parish of Aston had by their Certificate of the tenth day of June in the yeare of our L [...] owned the said John Page and his wife to be Inhabitants legally settled in their said parish And that the said Certificate was allowed of by Sr: Ralph Radcliffe & Richard Helder Esqrs. 2 of his then Maties. Justices of peace for the County of Hertford And that the sd. John page his wife and children were become chargeable to the said parish of Enfeild And that they nor any of them had since the date of the said Certificate obtained any other legall settlemt. That yor. Petrs: conceive themselves aggreived by the said warrt. And humbly appeale therefrom to yor. Worshipps Judgement and determinacon

And therefore humbly pray yor. Worpps. will appt: some short day for the heareing & determinning of the sd. Appeal

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
