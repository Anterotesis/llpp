To the Right Worshipfull her Maties: Justices of the Peace now Sitting at the Sessions held at Hicks Hall in the County of Middex.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Woodman Cobler now a Prisoner in New Prison.

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. was Indicted this present Sessions for an Assault committed agt. Henry Seamour Shoomaker; and for Seditions words. Whereunto yor. Petr. pleaded guilty.

That the sd Seamour gave Informacon to yor. Worshipps That yor Peticonr. being Porter to my Lord Petre, he was able to pay a Fyne of Twenty pounds.

Whereupon this Worshipfull Bench thought fitt, and did Fyne yr poor Peticonr. in five pounds, for which he was forth with committed to the sd new Prison; where he is like to perish for want of bread.

That yor. Petr. does confess he was Portr. to my Ld. Petre untill this unluckey accident happened, when his Lordpps Badg was taken from yor. petr. to his great prejudice.

That yor. Petr. has a helpless wife, & two small Children, who have nothing to depend on but yor. Petrs. labour: and therefore they must of course be putt upon ye Parish

Wherefore yor. Petr. most humbly boggs That this Worshipfull Bench will be pleased to consider the poor condicon of yor. Petr. & his Family; And be pleased to mittigate his Fyne Suitable to his poor condicon.

And Yor. Petr. (as duty bound) shall ever pray Etc
