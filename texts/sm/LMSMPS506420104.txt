Friday July Session 1774

The Humble Petr

William Martin

Infavour of his Son who was sentence to be hamported for felony This Culprit before was apprehended under the Fallow miking prockets during the execution of come offenders,Two hand kerchiefs were found upon him conuated in his Brucher being wrapped round his thighs; and the only thing he had to allidge in his defence was that the handker were his property and that being in [...] coud where he knew he pick pocketing was going forward he placed them there for safety.
