To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Dated the Fourth Day of May Instant David David was removed from the Parish of Hornsey in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate in the place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Honourable Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing and Determination thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Robt. Holbert Soltor appellts.
