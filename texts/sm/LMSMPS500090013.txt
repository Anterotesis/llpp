To the Honorble. the Bench of Justices Sitting at Hicks hall

The humble petion of Mary Coppinger St Giles

Sheweth That yr. petr. standeth comted: by this [...] Cort. to new prison being fined five marks upon pleading guilty to an Indictmt. for keeping a disorderly house

That yr. petr. is so poor that unless this said Fine be mitigated or estreated She must continue in prison for ought she knows for her life

Therefore humbly prays this honorble. Court to mitigate or estreate her said fine at to yor. honrs. shall [...] meet otherwise yr. petr is in great danger of [...] in prison

And yr. petr (as in duty bound/shall ever pray Etc
