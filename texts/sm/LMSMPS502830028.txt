To the Honerable Chaireman

Wheras their is an Indightment Against Eliz King at the Suite of Abraham Hutichins: and divers Persons are to make Affidavit against the Sd. King, are persons of not Carrecter and cannot give any Account of there place of abode begg [...] this Court will pleas to Inquire into Same, and your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray
