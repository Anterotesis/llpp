To His Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Brooksbank and David Wather Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and forth County of Middlesex aforesaid (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of September last past Ruth Singleton [...] was removed from the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County to the said Parish of Christ Church in the City of London as the last Legal Place of Settlement of the said Ruth Singleton whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived and against the said Order do appeal.

Your. Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the said Appeal may be received and the hearing and determining thereof [...] adjourned over until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden at Hicks Hall in and for the said County of Middlesex And that Your Petitioners may have such Relief in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Wm. Cooling Solr. for the Appellants.
