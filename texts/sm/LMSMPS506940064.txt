To the Right Worshpll: her Maties: Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Saviour Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands and Seales of two of her Maties: Justices of the peace for this County One Mary Davis Widow was passed from the parish of St: Gyles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St: Saviour Southwark as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Peticonrs: can make it Appear to this Honble: Court that the said Mary Davis was never legally setled in the said parish of St: Saviour Southwark

Your Peticonrs: therefore most humbly Appeal against the said Order and pray your Worships to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: Gyles in the Fields may Appear in this Honble: Court this prsent Session to Show cause why the said Mary Davis should not be [...] the said parish of St: Gyles in the Fields

And Yor: Peticon: shall ever pray etc.
