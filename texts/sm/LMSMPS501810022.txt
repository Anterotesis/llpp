To ye Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Heweth Constable of Endfield in the said County

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary due to yor Petr. at Michaelmas last for Passing and relieving of Vagrants to amounting to the Sum of Fifteen Pounds Which Money yor. Petr [...] ding in need of

Humbly prays this Court will [...] pleased to order he may be paid became [...] to this Court [...] Meet

And Yor: Petr shall Ever pray Etc Jon Howett
