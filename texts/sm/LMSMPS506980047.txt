To the Worll: her Mats: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Margtt: Westmr

Sheweth That Jane Soan Widow was removed by Virtue of a pass Signed by John Cogg and John Bond Esqr: two of her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx out of the Parish of St: Clement Danes in the said County and brought unto the said Parish of St: Margtt: Westmr: as her last legal Settlemt. the passe bearing date the 23th. day of May last

That yor: Petr: think themselves Agrieved by the Adjudgement of the said Justices

Your Petr: therefore humbly Appeal and pray this Worll: Bench to grant your Order to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: Clement Danes to Answer the said Appeal.

And yor: petr: shall ever pray Etc
