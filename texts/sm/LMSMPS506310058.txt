Middlesex To the Worshipfull Sir John Hawkins Knt Chairman and the Rest of the Honourable Justices of the Court for the County Aforesaid

The Humble Petition of Samuel Hayward

Your Petitioner Humbly Prays to be Executed of Serveng att the office of Headborrough as & your Humble [...] has served in the office for fourteen Months Past,

[...] David Davis is Chosen as headborrough under Wm. Samuel Taylor Counstable of Wood, Close Liberty Clerkenwell

And Yr. Petitioner Bound in Duty

David Davis of Compton Street Woods Close Smith
