To the Rt: Worppll; his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate- London.

Sheweth

That By Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Thomas Sutton and James Cardonnel Esqrs Two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the send County (Quor Unus) bearing date the Third day of December last One Mary Banister Singlewoman was- lately removed from the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London As the place of her last legall Settlement As is Alledged by the said Orders

That your Peticoners find themselves Aggreived by the said- Warrant or Order. And Appeale to this Court against the same

Your Peticoners therefore pay that this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeale- And that all parties Concerned may be Ordered to Attend Accordingly And that your Peticoners may be- releived therein According to Law and Justice.

And your peticoners as in Duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc

John Addison Tho: logan} Churchwardns,

John Mabbat Will: Simpson} Overseers
