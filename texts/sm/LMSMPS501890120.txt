Middx ss To the right Worpfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx att their Genall Quarter Sessions now Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Battersea in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order bearing date the 6th: day of September last under the hands and Seales of Robert Kirby and R Dennett Esqrs two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx ( Quorum unns) that Mary Smith the Widdow & relict of John Smith Labour deced with two Children (Vizt) Joseph Smith aged about three Years Ambrose Smith aged about four [...] Months were Removed from the Parish of St. John att Wapping in this County unto the Said Parish of Battersea as the place of their last legall Settlement by which Warrant or Order Yor Peticonrs conceive themselves to be aggreived and humbly appeale there from

Your Peticonrs therefore pray your Worspps to hear them in the premisses and that there Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John Wapping may attend this Court att a retaine day to hear and abide the Judgemt & Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeale

And Yor Peticonrs shall ever pray Etc
