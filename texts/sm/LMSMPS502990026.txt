To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Joseph Cook late Petty Constable of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was Petty Constable for the said Parish of St. Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex for the Year One Thousand Seven Hundred & Thirty

That Your Petitioner Expanded & laid out for the passing releiving & Conveying of Several Vagrants while he was in the Said Office of Constable the Sume of One Pound Fifteen Shillings as appears by Severall Allowances made by Sir Charles Peers Baronet one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays Your Worshipps to Order your Worshipps Treasurer to pay Your Petitioner the said Sume of One Pound fifteen Shillings

And your Petitioner in duty bound Shall ever Pray
