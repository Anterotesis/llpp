Middlesex (to wit) To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Tooling Graveney in the County of Surry-

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under their hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and John Scott Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fifth day of November one thousand seven hundred and ninety six Susannah Court Single woman and her Female Bastard Child named Caroline aged one year and upwards were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Sooting Graveney (in the said order named Tooting) as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggeieved by the said Warrant or order of the said Two Justices Do thereof appeal to your Worships therefrom And humbly play that your will please to hear them in the promises And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone may appear before you at a certain day day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching this appeal

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc. Turner & Seymour Solicitor for the Appeallants
