To Ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices Ye Peace for Ye County of Middx

The Humble Petition of John Bristoe on the Behalfe of Jon Bristoe his Sonn

Sheweth That your Petr Said Sonn is this Morning brought downe before this Honourable Court for Committing a Rape upon the Body of Mary Robbinson Aged about Ten Years

That your Petr and his Wife are both Crazey and Aged and have a great Dependance upon their Said Sonn for Assistance And he being Aged about Fourteen yeares and willing to goe into her Matys Service

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays your Worspl to be pleased to have a regard to the tender years of his sd Sonn

And yor Petr Shall ever Pray Et

P Tho. Nash
