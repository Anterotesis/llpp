The Humble Petition of Eliza. Muscovy that your Petitioner Humbly beggs that you. would forgive ye Court fees your Petitioner being very Sick & very Big with child un Provided for and her Husband being in the Marshallses for Debt and your Petioner Shall ever Pray

Elizabeth Muscovy

Mr Hardistys office [---] nomber one in Elme Court in the Temple
