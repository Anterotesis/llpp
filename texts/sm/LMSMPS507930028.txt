To the Worshipful His Majestiy's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of St. Sampson Wright Knight and I Reading Esquire Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County one hereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th. Day of February Instant Mary Hawley was Removed and Conveyed from and but of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last Legal Settlement

Your Petititoners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Two Justices therefore. humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Whorships will be pleased it Appoint a Day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determination the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

Jo. Holl. Sold. for the Appellants.
