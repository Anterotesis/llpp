To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their May General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of the [...] them [---] in the County of Norfolk.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of the Right Honble John Lord Viscount Dudley and Ward and William Timbrell Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifth day of May 1770. Anne Winwood Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said Liberty to the said Parish of East Wietham by the name of the Parish of Rettam near Thetford in the County of Norfolk as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Anne Winwood Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Wherefore your Petitioners humbly hope your Worships will be pleased to save the benefit of this their Appeal unto them, and appoint some day in the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be held for this County to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Hunt Wm Wade} Churchwardens

Thos Bidwell Jos Mowl} Overseers of the Poor
