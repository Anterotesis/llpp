To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex in general Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Tottenham High Crops in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Quarril Esquire and John Staples Esquire was of hid Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirtieth day of September One thousand seven hundred and ninety One Samuel Harvey the Infant Child of Thomas Harvey Aged One Year and upward was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Totterham High Cross in the Said County the said Justice adjudging the [---] Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Samuel Harvey to be in the said Parish of Totterham High Cross in the said County Whereby Your Petitioners Apprehend themselves to be aggrieved and Humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Pray that the benefit of this Their Appeal may be Saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned until the General Session of the Peace to be Holden for the said county of Middlesex

Chethem Solr. for the Appellants}
