To the Worshipfull the Justices in and for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Cotton Most Humbly sheweth

That your poor petitioners Husband being Cast for Transportation for being Accessed with Shaling some Lead And being the first time that he were was guilty of any Misdemeanor and did it out of pure necessity Support me and this Three small Children from starring and having no parish in London nor any Friends most Humbly prays for the Mercy of this Favourable Cover and in Duly Bound She shall forde [...] Pray Etc

Eliz Cotten
