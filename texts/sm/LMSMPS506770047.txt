To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Cad: Coker Esqr. and Jenkins Jones Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Seventeenth day of November One thousand Seven hundred and Seventy Seven William Oxhart aged about three Years therein mentioned to be the lawful Son of William Oxhart and Sarah his Wife was removed from the Parish of Saint Lukes in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street as the Place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order do humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitiones therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present Sessions for hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
