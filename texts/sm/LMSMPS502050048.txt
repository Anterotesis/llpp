To the Wort his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth That Anne the wife of Josia Snowden and their three Children Vizt Edward Elizabeth and Hester were by passe Warrt under the hands and Seals of Doyly Mitchell Esqr and Samll Perry Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County (Quor unus) dated the 28th day of May 1722 removed from the Hamblett of Limehouse in the Said County to the Said parish of St Giles in the fields

From which passe Warrt yor Petitioner finding themselves agreived do humbly appeal and pray that a day may be appointed and all parties have notice to attend this present Session to heare and abide the Judgement determination of this Court touching the same

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc

Jos: Wootton Will: Gilmore } Churchwardens
