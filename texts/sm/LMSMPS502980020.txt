To yr: Worshipfull Bench of Justices for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition Augustin Osborne

Sheweth That yr: Petr: Stands Indicted by ye: Parish of St: Spulchers Without for having a Child Born in his house which Child was taken (by ye: Mother which is Since dead) from ye. house of yr: Petir: about a year and a half agoe: but as yr: petir: hath given in Bail to appear this General Sessions to ye: sd: Indictment

Your Petitioner most humbly Prays that ye: Worshipfull Bench will be so pleased to Respite his Plaed till ye next Quarter Sessions in Order to make the offear up with ye: sd: Parish

And yr: Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever Pray & [...]

Augustine Osborne
