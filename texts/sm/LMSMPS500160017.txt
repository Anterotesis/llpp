To the Right Worpl [...] Maties. Justices to the Peace for a County of Middlx

The humble Perccon of Thomas Moreing of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlx Brewer.

Sheweth

That your Peticoner with others hath made on of a certaine pond of water called Dame Agnes to Heers, in the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the said County for about Twenty yeares last past And that of late the Churchwardens wth order the anneal Inhabitants of the said parish have onested your peticoner 4d per peace to the poor of the said parish, for by one of the said water, which was never done before, the said water being fave for any person that will one the same [...] and no person rated or anesd & for the Same your peticoner therefore humbly dewes your Worpps. to discharge him from paying the said Rate,

And he [...] in duly bound shall our pray Etc

Peticon of Thos Moorey Rate confirmd
