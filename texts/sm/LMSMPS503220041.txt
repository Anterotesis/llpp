To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Genral Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by a Pass or Order of Removeall under the hands and [...] of Clifford Wm: Phillipps and Richd: Farmer Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the [---] said County of Middx (Where of one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 16th: day of [---] March 1736 Margrett the wife of Isaac Chalkley and Rachael aged about Six Years And Isaac Aged about Eleven Months the Children and Lawfull Issue of the Said Isaac Chelkly and Margt. his said Wife were Removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermendsey in the County of Surry as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Yor Petitioners therefore think themselves Agreived ley [...] the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices and [...] humbly appeal agst: them same

Yor. Petitioners humbly prays that the Churchwardens [...] and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in the County of Middx and all persons Concerned in the Premisses have notice of this Petition [...] and appeal that they may Attend this Court Some Day this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc:
