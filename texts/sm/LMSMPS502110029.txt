To the Right Worship full his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx assembled in their Genll Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Stratford Bow in the said County

Sheweth that bv Warrt under the hands and Seals of St Gilford and John Ellis two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorum Anns) bearing date the first day of March last Samuel a Male Bastard Child aged three years and upwards was removed from the bamblet of Hammer Smith in the County aforesaid to your Petitioners as the peace of legall Settlemt of the said Samnel

That your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order and humbly Appeal agt the same

Yor Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray your Worships will appoint a day to hear and diterman the said Appeal

And your Petitionrs Shall Pray Etc
