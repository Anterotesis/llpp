To the Rt. Worpfull. his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church- -Wardens and Overseers of the poor [...] the parish of St. Dunstan in the East in the City of London

Sheweth That whereas one Anne Cottman was by Warrt. Under the hands and Seales of Sammell Jones and Thomas Wilson Esqrs. then and yet two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (Quorned) bearing date the 29th. day of March 1717. passed from the hamlet of Popler and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney in the Said County of Middx to the Said parish of St. Dunstan in the East alledging That the Said Anne Cottman was last legally Settled in the Said parish of St. Dunstan in the East in the said City of London.

Yor. petrs. finding themselves agreived with the judgment of the said two Justices of the peace Humbly appeal to this Honobles. Court and do therefore beg yor Worps. woud be pleased to appoint a day for the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of popler and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney aforesaid to attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to hear and abide the final end and determinaton of this Court touching this appeal

And yor petrs. Shall ever pray Etc. Nich: Batchelor Church- -Warden
