To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Walford ad Cad: Coker Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty sixth day of August One thousand seven hundred and seventy four Susanah Frankis Singlewoman and her three Bastard Children Namely Sarah aged about Six Years Susanah aged about Four Years and John aged about Eighteen Months (who are with her for Nurture) were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Susannah Frankis

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Order of Removal Do humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Releif in the premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jno Coverly Sollr. for the Appellants
