Middx ss To the Worshipfull the Chairman and Other his Majties Justices of the Peace in their Genll Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Now Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Samuel Godwin Churchwarden of the parish of St Sepulchre in Ye Said County

Sheweth That it has been A Costoms in the Said parish for the Churchwarden to Reimburse the Constable for the time being the Charge attending Vagrant Passes and take the Certificate for the Vaucher of Such payment And Said Samuel Godwin hath Five Such Certificates for which & his predecessours have Reimbursed the Constables of Amounting in the whole to Â£1:7s:6d

To Therefore Humbly prays this Honoble Court to Make Order that the Treasurer of the County May reimburse the Charge Attending the passing such Vagrants the whole Amounting to One pound And Seven Shilling & Six pence

& Yor Peticoner Shall ever Pray Etc

Saml Godwin

15th Octor 1731
