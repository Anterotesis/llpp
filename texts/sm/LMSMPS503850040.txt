To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Le Strand within the Liberty of Westmr. And in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Danl. Gach & Wm. Jones Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said Liberty of Westmr. (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th : day of March 1747: Elizabeth Mc. Kertny aged upwards of 5. Years was removed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the said Liberty of Westmr: to the said parish of St. Mary Le Strand as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Mc. kertny Whereby your petitioners think themselves Agreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore humbly pray your Worships will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all partys concerned may Attend

And yor. Petr. will Ever Pray Etc

Hodson Sollr: for the Appellants
