To The right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Pease for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Inhabitance of the parish of Stammore Magne in the same County

Sheweth That by a Warrant under the hands & Seales of the right Worshipfull Sr. Charles Grace Bart. & Thomas Stru [...] [...] of his Maties Justices of the [...] date the 27th day of November last Over [...] women was removed from the parish of Harrow in the St. County to the sd. parish of Stannmore magne as the last late of her legall Settlement Whereas in truth She was [...] [...] legally Settles in the said parish of Stammore Magner.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray an order of this Court reprising all parties concerned therein to attend & hear the finall determinacon of the premisses at such day as four Worshipps Shall appoint.

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.

Fran: Peters Junr.

Robt Martin
