To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the County of Midd

The humble Petition of Charles Loxton & Sarah his Wife

Sheweth

That your Petitioner Sarah Loxton Standing indicted [---] the Malicious Prosecution of One Timothy Joynes & mary his Wife, for buying of Stolen Goods, & your Petitioner being altogether Innocent of the Matter; Your Petitioner thereto Pleaded not Guilty & given Notice of Tryall for Fryday the Tenth of this Instant January, but your Petitioner being very Poor, & having a great many children; in great danger of becoming Chargeable to the Parish, are not able to taken out a Venie or Prosecute ye Traverse

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that your Petitioner Sarah may be admitted to prosecute her Sd. Traverse in form a Paupere

And your Petitioner Shall ever Pray Etc Hun Wright Ed Lindy} Churchwardens

John Chesm John Foster Richard Ashley

John Willson over Seare

Jno. Gattley Bruce Randall John Long Tho Sevell
