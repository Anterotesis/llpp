The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the pish of St. Mary Le Savoy als Strand agt. St. Clemts Danes

To the Right Worshipfull His Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at HIck's Hall

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Le Savoy als strand in ye Libty of Westmr

Sheweth That Catherine Crump Widow was removed from the parish of St. Clements Danes in the County of Middx to the said parish of St. Mary Le savoy als strand by Warrant under the hands & Seales of John Worley and Jeffery Saunders Esqs. two of His Majties. Justices of the peace of she said County bearing date the Seventeenth day of January last past thereby Alleadging that the said Catherine Crump was last legally Settled in the parish of St Mary Le Savoy als Strand

yor. Petr. thinking themselves aggreived by the Jndgmt. of the sd. Two Justices Do therefore Appeale to this Court And humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of ye said parish of St. Clements Danes And all other partyes concerned may be Ordered to attend some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Jndgement & final Determination of this Court touching this Appeale

And yoe Pete. shall pray Etc Thomas White Ja: Fulller Saml Burrow{ Church wardens & Overseer

J: Corbrt Sollt.
