To the Rt. Worshipll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genll: Quarter Sessions at Hickshall Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth

That William Bodle Elizabeth his Wife and their three Children Vizt Thomas aged about Eleven years Robert about five years and Catharine about three Quarters of a year Parishioners of the said parish of St. Martin in the Fields are Chargeable to and relieved by the said Parish being Poor and unable to Work towards their Maintenance

That Mary Scott of Hackney in the sd. County of Middx: Widow and mother of the sd: Elizabeth Bodle and Grandmother of the said three Childrens is of a Sufficient Ability to relieve and maint [...] them

Your Petrs: therefore pray your Worships would be pleased to order the said Mary Scott to Contribute towards their Reliefe and Maintenance

And your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
