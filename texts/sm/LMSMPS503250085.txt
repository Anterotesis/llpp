To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in the City of Canterbury in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That [---] an Order of Removall under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Exelbee and Richard Farmer Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) Dated the Sixteenth Day of July 1737 was made for the removing of Frances Legrand and [---] Mary wife of Thomas Sier lately deceased and her three Children (to wit) Mary Sier Aged Seven Years Hannah Sier Aged Four Years and Sarah Sier Aged Two Years [...] from the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the County of Middx To the said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in the City of Canterbury aforesaid In the said County of Kent.

That your said Petitioners humbly apprehend they are Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal And Appeal against the said Order to this Court

Yor. Petitioners humbly Pray your Worshipps to appoint some short Day for the [...] Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the said County of Middx and all other Partys concerned to Attend the said County [...] hear the said Appeal,

And yor. Petitionr. shall ever pray Etc

Isaac Fagg. Sollr. for the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen in the City of Canterbury.
