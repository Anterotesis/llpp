To the Worshipful this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County by adjournment at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Ensor of High Street Saint Mary le bone Pawnbroker

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the Eleventh day of March last convicted in the Penalty of Five Pounds by Nathaniel Conant C. John Scoft Esquires [...] two of the Justices of the Peace for the said County upon an Information exhibited and prosecuted by James Pegler charging that your Petitioner using the trade of a Pownbroker did in High Street in the Parish of saint Mary-le-bone in the said County on the 12th. day of June 1793 take and receive of John Crossley on redu [...] ning the pledge therein after mentioned One Penny by was of profit upon two Shillings on the Seventh day of May in the Year 1793 lent upon a Pledge of a Silver Tea Spoon to the said John Crossley contrary to the Statute Etc.

That your Petitioner conceiving himself aggrieved by such conviction is advised to appeal against the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to permit him to enter his Appeal so that the same may be heard at this present General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County and that your Worships will appoint a day for hearing and determining the same

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.
