To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant middx the hands and Seals of Thomas Balack Esqr. and Peter Planck Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing date the twenty Second day of the February one thousand Seven hundred and Sixty eight, Rachael, Decastro [...] Wife of Jacob Decastro (gone from her) was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Katherine Cree Church as the Plane of her last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices, Do therefore humbly appear [...] this Court against the same. Praying such Releif in the premisses as to this Court Shall Seen meet

And your Petitioners Shall ever peace Pur Fisher for the Appellants
