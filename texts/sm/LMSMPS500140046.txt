To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for The County of Midds The humble Peticon of John Sedgwick

Sheweth That for the Fact where of yor. Petr. stood indicted in this Honble Court yor. Worpps were pleased to Fine him 5l.

That yor. Petr. is a very Poore man and did the Fact through Ignorance & is not able to pay the sd Fine but is willing to Submitt him self to the Clemency of this Honble Court.

Yor Petr therefore most humbly pray to yor. Worpps to be pleased to Wittgatetle D Fine as unto yor. Worpps Shall Seem met

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
