To the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace for the the County of Middlesex now Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the Twelfth day of October instant, under the Hands and Seals of William Tylor and John Willock Esquires, Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of Westminster in the County of Middlesex Elizabeth Dunkin was removed from the Parish of Saint James's in the said City and Liberty to the said Parish of Lambeth, said to be her last Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving the said Elizabeth Dunkin to have no legal Settlement in the said Parish of Lambeth

Humbly Pray that an Appeal against the said Order may be entered, and that the same be respited untill the next Sessions to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex
