To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church= Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands Seals of Joseph Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires tow of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Second day of June last Rose Anne Forassassi an Infant was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of St. Mary Islington in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Rose Anne Forassassi

Yr. Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Thomas Edwards Thos. Simmonds Thos Hanks} Church wardens & Oversrs. of the poor
