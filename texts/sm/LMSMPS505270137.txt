To the worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Caston and Robert Darling two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex dated the twenty third day of September last Sarah Roberts Singlewoman with her Child William aged about thirteen Months a Bastard born of her Body out of lawful Matrimony were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Peter le Poor as the Place of their last legal Settlement

J: Batter Sollr. for the Appellts Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that the Hearing and Determination thereof may by an Order of this Court be on such Day of this present Session as shall be deemed proper for that purpose And that the said Appellants may be relieved in the Premisses as to the Court shall then seem meet

And your Petitioners will pray Etc.
