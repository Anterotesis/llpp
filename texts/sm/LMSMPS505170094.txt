To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [...] Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Nicholas Spencer and Samuel Pierson Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 12th day of November last Mary Stonhill Spinster was removed from the Parish of St: Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of St: Anne as the Place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselve to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices And Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to Order that all Persons concerned may then Attend

And Your Petitrs. Shall Pray Etc

John Horne Wm Trigg N. Parkers Joseph Tolley} Church warden And Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Anne
