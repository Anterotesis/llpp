To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justies of the Peace of Middlesex in Generall sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church-Wardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Mary le Bow London

Sheweth That by Order dated the Sixthday of this present Month of may under the hands and seals of Henry Norris and Samuel Tyssen Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Susannah Lad widow was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary of Hatford Bow in the said County to the said Parish of St. Mary Bow in the said City of London the place of her last legal settlement &

Your Petitioner humbly concerning themselves greived thereby do Appeal from the Judgement of the said two Justices [---] of the peace to the Judgement of his Honorable court And crave a Day of hearing

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
