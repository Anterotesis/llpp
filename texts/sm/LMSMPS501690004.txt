Peticon of Richd. Gower

Whitechappell poors rate

refr to Mr. Michel Mr. Dennett Mr. Hayne Mr. Sewell Mr. S: Perry or 2

to ex & cert

To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generll: Quarter Session The humble Petition and Appeal of Richd. Gower of St Mary Whitechappell in ye said County Jorniman Weaver

Sheweth That your petitioner is rated 8d Pmo towards the poor rate that your petitioner is a very poor man and hath a wife and family and is unable to pay the said rate or any part thereof and humbly appeals to this Court against the same

And prays Your Worships will, discharge him from paying ye sd rates or any part thereof And in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
