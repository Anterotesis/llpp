To the Honble Bench of Justices your Pettioner Richd Stanford has Prenatted with his Cprperrall to Loy Down ten Shills for him which is all ye moneys he has which is Half fees beging that you will be pleased to take of the fine and Disscharge him for ye Said money or Else he will Intireley perrish for want of Subsistiance for that money will never be cased for me again I humbley beg your Consideration on my Misfortune and Your Pettioner in Duty Bound will ever pray

Humbley Ley the Benches Answer before my Corperalll goes

Richd Stanford
