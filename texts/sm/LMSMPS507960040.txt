To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of G: Mercer and John Barnfather Equires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of February last past Mary Bailey and Jane her Daughter aged one month and upwards (by the Name and description of Mary Bailey and William her Son aged one month and upwards) were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Bailey and Child; Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray That the Benefit of such their Appeal may be Saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in & for the said County of Middlesex And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the said next Session for the Hearing and determin= =ing of this their said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. will Pray Etc.

Cox Sollr. for Appellts.
