[...] his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of [...] General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands [...] do G. Morav and John Barnfather Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One [...] of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th day of October [...] John Haines was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the [...] of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the order of the said Five Justices there fore humbly Appeal at this Court against the same and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions for the hearing & determining the sd. appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Jno Finklers Thos. Bourdillon Richd. Christmas} Churchwardens

Hassell Hutchins Isaac Frooms} Overseers of the Poor
