Middx To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Warrilo now Confined in Clerkenwell Bridewell

Humbly Sheweth

That Your Petitioner by the Judgement of this Court was on Saturday last Ordered to be publickly whip't from the White horse door in white Cross Street to Old Street Church in the parish of St Luke Middx for the taking One pewter pint Pet the Property of Mr Taylor of the said Parish

That your Petitioner is by Irade a Brewer and has a Wife.

That he has resided a long time in the parish afd and by reason of his Business of a Brewer he can be Imployed with Advantage in the said Trade there the Better to maintain himself & Wife & that the Publick Punishmt afd, if Administerd may tend to the utter ruin & disquist of your Petitioner & Family

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays that the said Judgementor Order may be Varied as to his your Petrs being Publickly Whip't in-to Private Correction & Such Imprisonment as your Worships Shell Judge Adequate to this Crime & the Judgement before mentioned
