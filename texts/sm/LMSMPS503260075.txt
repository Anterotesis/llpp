To the Worshipfull the Chairman & others his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx At their Genl. Quarter Session [...] of the Peace for the Said County Assembld.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of George Jackson of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County Appothecary.

Sheweth That your Petr. Rents a House of Ten pounds a Year in Shoreditch in the Said Parish & has for Some time past been Tax'd & Rated 4Â½d P Week to the poor of the Said Parish.

That tho your Petr. Still Continues under the Same Rent yet he is now Rated Six pence P Week without knowing any just reason for the Same, Several of his Neighbours paying the like Rent being Rated at no More than 2d. or 3d a Week

That as your Petr. humbly Conceives all Overseers of the poor in their Respective Parishes are to Make the Rates which are usually approv'd of by the Inhabitants of the Same and That any person aggreivd thereby may Appeal to the General Quarter Session of the Peace.

Your Petr. therefore Believing himself to be Soaggriev'd as aforesaid Does now humbly Petition & Appeal to this Honble. Court for Such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Shall Seem Meet.

19th. October 1737.

And he Will Pray Etc. Geo: Jackson
