To the Right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace in Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled in & for the County of Midd.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of All allows on [---] London Wall London.

Sheweth.

That by an Order Under the hands & Seales Henry Lowth & Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Mats. Justices [...] of the Peace for the Sd. County (Quorn unus) beareing date the Eleventh day of March last Thomas Stimson & Eliz: his Wife Were removed from the Parish of St. Giles Without Cripplegate in the Sd. County of Middx to the Sd. Parish of Alhallows on London Wall London.

Yr. Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the Sd. two Justices of the Peace, Appeale to this Court & humbly Pray yt. the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Giles Without Cripplegate & All other Parishes concerned May be Ordered to Attend this Court Some day th [...] Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. and Determination of this Court touching the Prmisses.

And yr. Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
