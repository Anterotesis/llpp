To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticion of Ann Jackson Widow of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the County aforesd

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish aforesaid are now indebted to yor. petr. the sum of Four pounds and sixteen shillings for nursing of two penconers both being Lunatick.

That the sd Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor do refuse to pay yor. Petr. her said Arrears and the being a very poor woman must inevitably parish Unless this Honble Bench wilbe pleased to consider her sad Condition.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worsppll to be pleased to order that he may be releiv'd in the sd prmisses

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
