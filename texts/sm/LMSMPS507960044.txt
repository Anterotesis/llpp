To the Right Worshipful his Majesty,s Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Saviour Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Thomas Collins and John Barnfather Esquires two of his Majesty's of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of February 1785 Thomas Wesley aged twelve Years or thereabouts & Catherine Wesley aged nine Years or thereabouts Megitimate Children were removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Dane's in the said County of Middlesex unto the Parish of Saint Saviour Southwark in the said County of Surrey by the name and description of St Mary Overy's in the County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of St Saviour Southwark by the name and description of Saint Mary Overy's to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore Appeal to Your Worships therefrom and humbly Pray that You will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of Your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Vincent Solr for the Appellants
