To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of St Robert Darling Knight and David Wilmot Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County bearing date the ninth Day of January last William Hughes and his wife Mary were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary White chapel in the Said County to the Said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Hughes and his Said wife

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the Said two Justices Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court Against the Same And Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining the Said Apeal

And Your Petition & Shall ever Pray Etc
