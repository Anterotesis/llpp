To the Rt: Worshipfull His mats:Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genl. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

the humble Petition and Appeal of Andrew Fletcher of the Parish of Saint James Clerkwell in the said County

Sheweth

That your Petitioner is charged much more to the Poor and Scavengers Rates in the said Parish for the house lives in In the said Parish of St James Clerkwell then what he ought to pay therefore thinks himself agrieved and humbly appeale to this Court

The Premisses considered your Petr. humbly such Releif as this Court Shall seem meet

And your Petr. shall over pray Etc

Andrew Fletcher
