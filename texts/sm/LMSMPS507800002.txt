To the Worshipful Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Frans Gray

Sheweth That Your petitioner has been regularly brought up to the Surveying Business under Mr James Carr and has been employed by him in the Various Branches of the Business for nine Years. therefore humbly presumes to offer himself a Candidate to be Surveyor and Supervisor for the district of St. James and St. John Clerkenwell

We whose Names are under written being Surveyors and Builders do Certify that Mr. Gray we believe to be in every respect well Qualified for the above office knowing him to have been regularly educated for the Prosession of a Surveyor and Supervisor

James Cart Ezekl. Delight Nathl Wright Long

A Copy only of a petition delivered the last Session

My Father Mr. Saml Gray has been a parishoner in Clerkenwell Upwards of 40 Years
