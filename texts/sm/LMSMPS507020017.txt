To the Rt. Worspl her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds.

The Humble Petition of John Spencer of the Parish of St James Clerke meet in the County

Sheweth That yor Petitioner is Rested Four Pence P Month towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish But for as much as yor Petr being by many Misfortunes and Lossess Reduced of late to a poor and low Condition Which renders him all most Incapable of providing for [...] himselfe and Family And he heeing in a very small House in the sd Parish soe that he is noe waies Capable nor Able to pay any thing towards the said Rate

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays yor Worsple to be Pleased to Grant that he may be wholly Discharged from paying towards the Relief of Ye Poor of the said Parish if your Worshipps soe thinke fitt

And Yor Petr shall ever Pray et

P Tho: Nash
