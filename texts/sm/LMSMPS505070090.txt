To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlets of Mide End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunston & Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Shew That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of January last Anna Maria Rider Single women with a Male Child aged [...] Six Months born upon her Body a Bastard out of lawfull Matrimony were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the Lord County of Middlesex up to the Hamlet of Mide End Old Town aforesaid As the last legal place of St. Clement of the said Anna Maria Rider and her said Child

Therefore your said Petitioners for receiving themselves agrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners Etc.

William Carter Spitalfields Attorney for Appellts. 22d. Febry 1762
