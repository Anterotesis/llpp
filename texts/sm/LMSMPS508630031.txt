To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Present General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney and Charles Friquet Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof in of the Quorum bearing dated the 23d day of April last Ann Robertshaw Wife of Thomas Robertshaw was removed and conveyed from and [...] of the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and other that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Sepulchre may appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of upon Worships touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc. A Gliddon Sold for the appets
