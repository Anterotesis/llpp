To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their general Quarter Session of the Peace held by Adjournment at Hicks-hall in St. John Street within the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Stew Market in the County of Suffolk

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass under the Hand and Seal of Thomas Quarrill Esqr. One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of January 1748 Hannah Pullum Widow A Vagrant was Conveyed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Stew Market in the County of Suffolk Aforesaid,

Yr. Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agreived by the said Order or Pass Appeal Against the same And Humbly Pray Yr. Worships to Appoint some day this present Session to hear and Determine the said Appeal

And Yr. Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

John C Clayter Agent for the Appellant [...]
