To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Alban Woodstreet London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Browne and Jacob Harvey Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorum Unus) bearing date the third day of September last Ann Powell was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said Parish of St. Alban Woodstreet as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeale to this Court against the Same and humbly Pray Your Worships Will be Pleased to Appoint a day to hear and [...] determine the Same

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc.
