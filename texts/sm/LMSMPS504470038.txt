To the Worshippfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox Esqrs. two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the third day of November last Benjamin Thomas and Benedicta his Wife were Removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields to the said Parish of St. Sepulchre as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal and humbly Appeal against the Same and Pray your Worshipps to Grant them such relief in the Premisses as to your Worshipps shall Seem Meet

And your Petitioners (as in duty bound) shall ever Pray Etc

G. Talmash Attorney for the Appellants.
