To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at The Session house on Clerkenwell Green assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Joseph Merceron and Daniel Williams Esquires Two of the Majesty's Justices assigned to keep the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof is of the Quorum bearing date the tenth day of February Instant Ann Ball Wife of Thomas Ball who has deserted (her, their five lawful Children and left than destitute and unprovided for namely Thomas aged Thirteen years or thereabouts who has gained no legal Settlement) in his own right, Eleanor aged Eleven Years or thereabouts who has gained no legal Settlement in her own Right John aged Eight years or thereabouts who has gained no legal Settlement in his own Right, George aged five years or thereabouts and Aaron aged sixteen Months or thereabouts were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Christ Church as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Ball and her said five Children namely Thomas Eleanor, John George and Aaron Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved

And therefore They Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and the said Order And Pray That the benefit of their said appeal may be saved unto them And That the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjoined until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County And That Your Worships will be pleased to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green And all Persons Concerned do then Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination thereof touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

H Parnell Sollicitor for the appellants Church Street Spitalfields [...]
