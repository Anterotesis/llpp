To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace holden for the said County at Hickshall.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That By Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of Bastwick Johnson and Thomas Wen Esqs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 22d. Day of Feby last, John Price and Ursula his Wife were Removed, from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the County of Middx: aforesd. to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe, as the place of their last Legall Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggrieved

Your Petitionrs therefore pray your Worpps to Appoint a Day for hearing and Determining this Appeal (this present Sessions) And that your Petitioners may be Relieved in the premises as to Your Wisdoms shall [---] Seem Meet

And your Petitionrs shall Ever pray Etc
