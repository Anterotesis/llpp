To the Rt. Worpll. His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter le Poore London

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 30th. September 1720 under the hands & Seals of John Milner & John Richardson Esqrs two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor Unus) by wch. said Order One Richard Ayres with Anne his wife & their 3 Children were passed from the parish of St. Giles in the field in the same County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Peters le Poore as the place of their last legall Setlement

That the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter le Poore humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order & Pray an Appeale to this Honoble. Bench

Your Petrs: humbly pray this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing the said Appeale against the said Order

And yor Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.

Middx ss Ad General Quarterial Sesion Dom Regis tent pro Com Middx Per Adjournament apnd Hicks Hall in St John Street in Com prd. die Lune scilt decimo die Octobris Anno regni Dui urd Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Septimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in this County have notice of this Appeale & that they & all Persons concerned doe attend this Court on Thursday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to heare & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

P Cur HarCourt
