To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Martin of Bull Inn Court in the Strand

Sheweth That in consequence of the Misfortunes of Mr Thomas Parry who attended Your Worships as Door keeper at the Session House he is no longer able to attend in that Capacity

That your Petitioner has attended the Court that present Session instead of the said Mr parry [...] prays your Worships to be appointed to Succeed him

That in Case Mr Parry shared be Released from his present Situation and your Worships Shared think proper to Restore him to his former place your Petitioner wod relinquish in his favour without regret

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays Your Worships to appoint him to succeed Mr Parry

And your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray John Martin
