To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds The humble Peticon of Thomas Hose.

Sheweth That your Petr was lately comitted to New Prison in this County where he now Remaines for assaulting & Beating of One Morgan Jones and that your Petr. was for comitted by the Worspll. Justice Bateman But for as much as the sd. abuse being Composed & Ended Between your Petr the sd. Morgan [...] As [...] appeares by Releases given Between them [...] you Ready to be produced

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspp to be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from his sd. Imprisonmt.

P Tho: Nash

And yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc
