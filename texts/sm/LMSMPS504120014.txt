To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks hall in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Ann Cherrington Singlewoman a Vagrant not having a legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Clement Danes was on the 8th. Day of June last found wandering in Labour in the said Parish of St. Clement Danes and being carried to one of the Parish Nurses of the said Parish was on the same Day delivered of a male bastard Child by means whereof the Inhabitants of the said Parish were obliged necessarily to Expend the Sum of Two Pounds Six Shillings & two pence for the lying in and maintenance of the said Ann Cherrington and Child & other incident Expences

And that in pursuance of an Act of Parliament made in the 17th. Year of his present Majestys Reign Intitiled an Act to a mend and make more effectual the Laws relating to Rogues Vagabonds and other idle and disorderly Persons and to houses of Correction your Petitioners conveyed the said Ann Cherrington to one of his Majestys Justices of the peace for this County who Examined the said Ann Cherrington and committed her to the house of Correction Pursuant to the sd. Statute

Your Petrs. therefore pray this Court to Order the Treasurer of this County to Reimburse them the sd. Sum of Two pounds Six Shillings and two pence Expended by them as aforesaid or such other Sum as this Court shall Adjudge a reasonable Satisfaction

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Tho Coombe} Oversr. of the Poor
