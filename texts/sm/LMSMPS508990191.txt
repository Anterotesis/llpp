To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardn and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of R Davies and P. Colquhoun Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirty first Day of May one thousand seven hundred and Ninety four Sarah Lewis Wife of John Lewis and her Child John aged about Eighteen Months were removed and conveyed from and out of that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the the West in the City of London as the Place of the last legal settlement of the said Sarah Lewis and her Child John.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and pray this Court that the benefit of the said Appeal be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray
