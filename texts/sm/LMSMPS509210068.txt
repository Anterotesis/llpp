To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leon as do Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of G. J. Goodenough and Edward Read Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex bearing date the Seventh day of January 1796 Elizabeth King the wife of John King now absent was removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint Mary le strand in the said liberty of westminster and County of Middlesex unto the Parish of Saint Leonard Storeditch in the said County as the Place of the last legal settlement of the said Elizabeth King

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order and do appeal thereto

Your Petitioners therefore meet humbly pray That you will be Pleased to appoint a Day in the next Session for bearing and Determining the merits of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners peace Ever Pray &c.

Jas. Leare To b. for St. Leod. Shoreditch.
