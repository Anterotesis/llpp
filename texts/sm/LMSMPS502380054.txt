To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices for the County of Middx in Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of Mary Cade wife of Richard Cade

Sheweth That your Petr: said Husband preferred a Bill of Indictment this prsent Sessions agt. one Thomas Lane and John Lane for an Assault and taking from him his Staff being a Headborough and called in to part a fray and comand the Peace but before he was Examined before the Grand Jury He hath by the Instigation and persuasion of the said Thomas Lane and John Lane to Some of your Petrs. Husbands Credrs. they have caused your Petrs. said Husband Richard Cade to be Arrested and he now lyes in the Custody of one King a Marshall Court Officer in Union Court Loaded with three Actions Soe that he cant appear to give Evidence on the said Bill of Indictment and the said Lanes think to Evade the intended prosecucon agt. them.

Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps Order to the said King the Officer to bring the said Richard Cade before this Worshipfull Court to give Evidence on the said Bill And that such further Order shall be made as shall be agreable to Justice

And your Petr. shall pray Etc.
