To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled.

Sheweth The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Munday

That your Petitioner is an Inhabitant and Householder in the Parish of Saint Aldgate in the County of Middlesex and as- such rated and charged to the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That by a Rate or Assessment made for the Relief of the Poor of the said- Parish on or about the twenty sixth day of March last your Petitioner is charged One pound four shillings towards the imediate relief of the Poor of the said Parish which your Petitioner apprehends greatly exceeds in Proportion what many other of the Inhabitants of the said Parish are charged by the said Rate and some Inhabitants of the said Parish who ought to have been charged by the said Rate are not charged thereby and that the said Rate is unequal and excessive

That your Petitioner is as he conceives aggrieved by the said Rate or Assessment and doth appeal therefrom and prays such Relief as to the Court shall seem just and that a day may be appointed for hearing the Appeal and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish way attend and produce the said Rate or Assessment at the Hearing thereof

And Your Petitioner will ever prayed

John Beard Attorney for the Appellant.
