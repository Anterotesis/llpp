To the Right Worspll. his maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

To humble Peticon of Garrett [...] Fitz Garrett or the parish of St. Botolph Algate in the said County Victualer

Sheweth That as of Last Sessions your petr. was Convicted for keeping a disorderly house and Ordered that his Lycence should be suppressed That your petr. is a very poore man and haveing a wife & Five Children to mainteyne and haveing some time to ten time in this house and haveing [---] 6 pound due for Quartering Souldiers Herein

yor. petr. therefore most humbly Prayer yor. worspps to be pleased to Grant that be years [...] be allowed to sell such drmbe or to much show of as be hath Mon by him [...] not exceeding such time as shalbe allowed by your worspps

P Tho Nash

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc

pet of your Hatr [...] Gar ett

to see till wet Loss.

May 1697
