To his Majties. Justices of the Peace now Sitting at Hicks Hall in St. John Street.

The Honble Petition of the Chuchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by order under the Hands and seals of Samuell Jones and Phillip Wilshire Esqr two of his Majties. Justices of the peace of the Said County Quor un one Beajamin Harper a blind Man was removed out of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe into the sayd Parish as the Place of his last legall Settlement by wch. order your Petrs. think themselves agreived and appeal against the same

And humbly pray Yor. Worshipps will appoint one Say next Quarter Sessions finally to hear and determine the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
