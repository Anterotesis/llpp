To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in general Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of John Moril

Sheweth That a certain Rate or Taxation for the relief of the Pan of the Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stebonheath otherwise Stepney in the County of Middlesex was made and published on the 22d Day of October 1780 but that George Young Clerk is omitted to be charged in and by such Rate or Taxation for a certain House and Premises in his occupation in the said Hamlet

That your Petitioner [---] Apprehends himself Agrieved [---] by the Inequality of the said Rate [---] for that John Perry and Co holding an House Yard and Premises of the Yearly value of £550 [...] £20 in and by the said rate [...] Batson holding a House Yard and Land over the way a Mould Loft and Smiths Shop of the yearly value of £250 is rated only £7 in and by the said Rate and for that James Manetone holding an House Yard and Premises of the yearly value of £200 is rated only £5..8 in and by the said Rate And for that Simon Pomfret holding an house of the yearly value of £52..10 is rated only £1-7. in and by the said Rate and for that James Pool holding an House and Premises of the yearly value of £50 is rated only 19s.1½d in and by the said Rate and for that John Rogers holding an House of the yearly value of £42 is rated only 14s.3d in and by the said Rate And for that William Tooley holding a House or Premises of the yearly value of £45 is rated only £1.2.6 in and by the said Rate And for that Benjamin Granger holding a House of the yearly value of £30 is rated only £1..1 in and by the said Rate And for that Edward Wood holding a House of the yearly value of £30 is rated only 19s.1½d in and by the said Rate and for that James Jope holding an House of the

Yearly value of £30 is rated only 19s.1½d in and by the said Rate and for that John Higgs otherwise Haggis holding an House of the Yearly value of £30 is rated only 19s.1½d in and by the said Rate And for that Wm Fountain holding an House of the yearly value of £30 is rated only 19s.1½d is and by the said rate And for that George Frost holding an House and Law covered with Water of the value of £30 is rated only £1..1..0 in & by the said Rate

That your Petitioner has a material objection to the said Rate or Taxation and apprehends himself to be aggrieved thereby by reason of the unequal and unjust manner of rating as afsd. and therefore humbly appeals agt. such Rate or Taxation and

Your Petitioner prays that Such Rate or Taxation may be quashed or that your Petitioner may have such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships Shall Seem meet
