To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of The Parish of Saint Alban Wood Street in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a certain Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Mich. Downs and Wm. Gowan two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of May 1785 Susannah Hartsea Singlewoman was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Alban Wood Street in the City of London by the description of the Parish of Saint Alban in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them And that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex.

Chetham Solicitor for the Appellants }
