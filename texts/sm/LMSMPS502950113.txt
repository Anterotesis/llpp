To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seales of Richard Newton and C: Moore Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of September last past Mary Newton Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the same County as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves agrieved by the Judgment and Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem Just

And yr Peticoners will Ever Pray Etc
