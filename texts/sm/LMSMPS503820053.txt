To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Quarrell and Nicholas a Dukinfield Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Dale the Seventh Day of November last past Sarah Burnett Wife of Patrick Burnett (who is at Sea and has been Many Years) was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saml Mary White Chapel in the said County to the Said Parish of Saint Martin in the fields as the Plea of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioner Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace and Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint some Day in this Present Session to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeale

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

J Williams Agent for the Appellants
