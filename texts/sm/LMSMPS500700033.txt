Sheweth The Ann [...] the [...] Churchwardens Ovseers of the [...] of the parish of St Martins in the Feilds in the sd. County

That by Warrant undo the Hands and Seales of [...] of the Matys Justices of the peace for the County. One Mary Smith lately passed from the Parish of St. Anns withhim the City & Libtys of Westmr to the sd. pish of St. Martins in the Feilds as the place of her last Legall Settlemt.

That by Son all Wittnesser, yor. Petr. can make appeare to this Honble Court the Sd. Mary Smith was never Legally Settled in the sd. pish of St. Martins in the Feild they therefore humbly conceive them selves, not lyable to mainteyne & provide for the sd. Mary Smith.

p Tho: Nash. Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Pray yor: Worspps to he pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poore of the sd. pish of St. Anne, Westmr may appearence this Honble Cort. this p Sent Sessions to shew cause why the sd. Mary Smith should not he repassed to the sd. pish of St. Anns in the City & Libtys of Westmr.

The Yor. Per. Shall Ever Pray Etc Will Andrews Overseer
