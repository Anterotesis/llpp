Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dorchester in the County of Oxford

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Hyde and Edward Reed Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the first day of February last past William Hartford Mary his Wife and three of their Children (Viz) William aged about five Years Harriot aged about three Years and Thomas aged about one Year were removed from the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Dorchester in the said County of Oxford as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and Pray that your Worships will appoint same day in the Present Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Hammersmith and all other Persons concerned to appear before you to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the Court touching this Appeal

And your Petititoners shall ever pray Etc
