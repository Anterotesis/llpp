To the Right Hanble. the Lord Mayr. and to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Bench

Sheweth The humble peticon of Richard Prickett Now a [...] your. in Newgate

That your peticoner: is confined for High Erenson in John for feitnig the marrant confed of this Raigdam (when in hath Your peticoner is ned wayes guilty of the Same) and hath his Saittnessed Ready to prove his mircereney

Your Peticoner: therefore most humbly prayes he may be tryed this plsent Sessions and that his peayer may be Entered accordingly as to yor. Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc.

And yor: peticornr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
