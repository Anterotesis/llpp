To the Worshipsfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth

That By Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Pickering and Henry Trent Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 27th Day of July 1748. Sarah Arthur Singlewoman was Removed for the parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Said parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London by the Name and Discripcon of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex As the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant Or Order and humbly Appeal against the Same

Your petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of this present Session That all Parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said appeal

Thos: Hell Fras Albons} Churchwardens

William Hacksworth Tobias Benton} Overseers
