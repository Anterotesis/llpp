To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St. Catherine Cree Church London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or pass Warrant Under the Hands & Seales of Nathaniel Chandler Esqr Andrew osborne Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex One Where of is of the Quorum bearing Date the fifth day of February One Thousand Seven Hundred & Thirty Six In the Tenth year of his present Majestys reign One Albert Valentine & Martha his Wife were removed from & out of the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditct in the said County of Middlesex into the said parish of St. Catherine Cree Church London As the place of the is last legall Settlement

Will Salt Agent for ye Appellans Your Petitioners therefore humbly conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Said Order or Pass Warrant do Appeale Against the same & pray that this Honourable Court will please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch & all parties concerned to appear Some day at this present Sessions to hear & Abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
