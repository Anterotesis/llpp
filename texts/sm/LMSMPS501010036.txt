To the Rt Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Charles Whitby

Sheweth

That One Dorothy Curtis Stand bound before the Worshippfull Justice Preton for her Appearance in this Honourable Court this prent Sessions for raising A Tumalt before yor Petr House And that the said Dorothy Curtis hath by her own meanes or Some- others by her Imployed kept back Yor Petr. Wittnessess fram Appearing in this Honourable Court this prsent Sessions

P Tho: Nash Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that the sd Dorothy Curtis may be Continued bound untill next Sessions If yor Worshipps Soe thinke fitt

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Charles Whitby
