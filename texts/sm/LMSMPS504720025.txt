[...] full his Majesties Justices of the [...] [...] of Middx. in their General Quarter [...] Assembled

The Humble petition & appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint [...] Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said [...]

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under [...] Hands and Seals of George Wrighte and Richard Manley Esqrs. two of his Majesties Jutices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 26th: Day of May last, Mary Kelsey Spinster was removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Andrew, Holborn above the Bars as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petrs: think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices Appeal to this Court against [...] same and humbly pray that Your Worshipful will be pleased to appoint some Majesties this present Sessions for the hearing and determining of the same

And Yor: Petrs: shall ever pray
