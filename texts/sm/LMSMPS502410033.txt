To the Rt. Worpll. the Justices of his Majesty. Peace for the County of Middx in Session Assembled.

The humble Petition of Cathrine Hovey Widdow Reliet of George Hovey late of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourn on behalfe of her Selfe and her two Chilldren

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petitionr. haveing in persuance of an Order of this Honble. Court (bearing date the tenth day of January, in the 8d. Year of the Beigne of her late Majesty Queen Ann,) divers times apply'd her Selfe for releife in the premises, as well to this Honble. Bench, as to the Overseers of the Poor of the Parish wth. out the Barrs to whom the said Order was directed. And as Yor Petr. is well Satisfied that the Overseers. of the said Parish have had a due regard to the Execution of the sd. Order, and taken due Security for what was therein directed for the releife and Support of Your Petitionr.

Yor. Petitr. Therefore humbly Prays, That she may in persuance of sd. Order bereleiv'd and that the Overseers of the said Parish may be directed to a Compliance wth. the Purport of the said Order Accordingly.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray etc.
