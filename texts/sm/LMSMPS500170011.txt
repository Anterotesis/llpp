To the Right Worpl. their Matie Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of the Constable and Severall antient Inhabitants of the Liberty of Newton Folgate als Northon Folley in the sd. County.

Sheweth That the Watch house belonging to the sd. Liberty is at this time very much out of repairne Insomuch that yor. Petr are very fearfull the Same will fall downe.

They therefore most humbly pray yo Worpps to be pleased to Order that they may make an Asessmt. upon the Sewall Inhabitts of the whole Liberty whereby to defray the Charges of repaireing and amending the sd Watchhouse

And yor. Pets shall ever Pray Etc
