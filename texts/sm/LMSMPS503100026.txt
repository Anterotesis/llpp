To the Right Worphipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of Saml Owen

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Wm Booth and Anthony Wroth Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of September last your Petitioner was Adjudged to be the putative Father of a Female Bastard Child born of the body of Mary Walkwood in the Parish of St Luke in the said County of Middx in which Order your Petitioner is Ordered and directed to pay to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish the sum of forty five shillings for the first four Weeks after the birth of the said Female Bastard Child and for the charge of her lying in and also two shillings and six pence weekly for the purposes mentioned and contained in the said order That your Petitioner was not served with a Copy of the said Order till on or about the 22d day of April last and not before

Your Petitioner conceiving himself aggreived by the said Order & Adjuditation of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief against the same

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray

Sam. Owen
