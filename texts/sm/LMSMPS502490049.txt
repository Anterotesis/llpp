To the Rt. Worshippll. his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in th [---] Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Middlx

Sheweth

That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Alexander Ward and John Fuller Esqrs. Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (Quor Unus) bearing date the Seventh day of December last One Sarah Gooding was Removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County of Middlx to the said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney as being the place of her last Legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Humbly Appeal against the same and desire their said Appeal may be saved to them to the next Generall Sessions And that you Worshipps would be pleased to appoint some day in the said next Sessions for the hearing and determining the same

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
