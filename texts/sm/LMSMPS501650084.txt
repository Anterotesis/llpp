To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeal of Charles Feltham of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Brewer.

Sheweth That your Peticoner did appeal against the poors. rate of the said parish at the last Quarter Sessions And that your Peticoners peticon was referred to John Fuller & Isaac Tillard Esqrs: & others his Matys Justices of the peace for the said County to examine & certify their opinions touching the sd Appeal And they having Sumoned the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the said Parish & he annts the said peticon have by their Report certifyed to this Court that severall Difficulties haveing anjen touching the Allegations of your peticoner which the Sd Justices Doe Submitt [...] to the Judgment &. Determinacon of this Court.

Therefore your Petr. Humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a Day this Sessions for both parties to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal. [...]

And Yor: Petr Shall pray Etc.

Charles: Feltham
