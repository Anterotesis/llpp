To the Worshippfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled Dim Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in St John Street in & for the Sd County

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clemt Danes in the Sd County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th day of Febry last Anne Persons Widw was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in this County to the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of the last legall Settlemt. of the Sd Anne Browne where by your Petrs conceive themselves to be agrrived and agt the sd Order Doe Appeale

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray that in Short day may be Appointed this Present Sessions to heare and determine the matter touching the Sd Appeale

And Your Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc

Ro: Chimmow Selt for the Appellts}
