The humble Petion Elizabeth Memorry Begs for god sake humbly To take Compashon on heir and heir poor Children or els they will perish for want of subsistance for she has Been a prisner hear Seven Months to hard Laybor humbley Beegs of the honerable Bench for to take of heir Court fees she having No Frend. to Crave the faver But heir Poor Child and in so Doing your Poor pitcherner will Be Bound in Duty Ever to pray for you and your

Elizabeth Memorry

Tho. Robe
