To the Right Worshipfull the Bench of Justices Now Sitting of Hicks Hall in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of Thomas Delf

Sheweth That Yor Petr: was Committed a Prisoner to Clerkenwell Bridewell By Vertue of a warrant or Mittimas under the Hand & Seal of George Garratt Esqr: one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date 11: February 1750: By which Said Warrant or Mittimns Yor Petr: was charged on the Oath of John Sollis for being a Rogue and Vagabond using Subtle Craft and Pretending to the Fortunes and by Such pretend deceiving & imposing upon the Said John Sollins And Unlawfull obtaining from him one Shilling Contrary to the Statute Etc

That Yer Petr humbly apprehands he is agrieved by the Sd. Warrant on Mittimus and therefore humbly appeals against the Same and Prays that this Court will be Pleased to Order a certain Day for the hearing Ye Sd. appeale And that all Parties Concerned, May then attend the hearing & Determination there of

And Yr petr Shall Pray Etc Tho: Dolf
