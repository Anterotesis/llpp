To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of Thomas Hardwick of the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in ye Sd County

Sheweth That your Petirs this year Raised in the poors Rate of the sd Parish

That sevral of yor Petr Neighbours who are of as good ability as yor petr. are not raised soo much as yor Petr to the poors rate Soe yr Yor petr humbly Conceives him Selfe aggriev'd

He therefore humbly prays suth Relief in the geremser As to this Court Shall be thought Meete.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever prayer Tho Hardwick
