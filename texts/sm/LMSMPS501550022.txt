To the Right Worpll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Sarah Blaine of the Hamble of Spittle feilds in the County of Middx widow.

Humbly Sheweth.

That yor. peticonr in July Sessions last peticoned this Honoble Joint for Releife from the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds in this County whereupon this Honoble Court Refeued it to Samuel Perry Esqr one of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County who ordered that the Church warden & Overseers of the said Hamblett should allow yor petr. [...] Five shillings P month which said order the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett have not complyed with [...] still refuse to allow yor petr the said pension

Yor Petr therefore humbly prayes yor Worpps will be pleased to grant yor petr an Order for the Churchwar- den and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds to attend this Court to shew cause why they have not complyed with the said Justices of the peace order and paid her her pension thereby allowed her And yor. petr shall pray Etc.

Sarah Blaine.

Reciv'd of the Sd Church Wardens two Monts Pay Ten Shillings Recevd in Part for ten. Weeks more three Shillings
