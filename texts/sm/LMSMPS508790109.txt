To the Worshipful William Manwaring Esquire Chairman and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Lewis Wheatley

Sheweth

That on the fifth day of November One thousand seven hundred and ninety two your Petitioner was Convicted before George Story Esquire and John Staples Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex for Receiving Harbouring and Detaining a Spaniel Dog the property of John Ralph Gibson Knowing the same to have been Stolen and was Adjudge by the said Justices to forfeit and pay of the same the sum of Twenty Pounds

That Your Petitioner Conciving himself agrieved by the said Conviction Humbly Pray that your Worships will grant him the benifit of his Appeal and such other relief in the premises as may sam meet and as in duty bound will ever pray Etc
