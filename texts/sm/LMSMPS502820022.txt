To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon of John Tull one of the late petty Constables of the parish of Southmims in this County

Sheweth That at Lady day now last past there was due and owing to yor. petr. the Sume of Ten pounds for one qrters. Salary of the allowance made him (by this Court) for his Trouble, loss of time & Expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worships would be pleased to Order the paymt. of the sd Sume of ten pounds to yor. sd petr.

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.
