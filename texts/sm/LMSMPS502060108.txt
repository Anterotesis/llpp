To the Worll. his Mats. Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Qr Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Sr Margt Westmr. in ye Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. undr the hands & Seals of John Ellis and Christain Cole Esqrs two of his- Maties Justies of ye peace of and for ye City and Libty of Westmr. & Said County of Middx bearing Date ye 24th July last Sara Macklayne Daughter of Thomas & Margaret Maclayne (aged about four Years) was removd out and from the parish of St. James Westmr. in ye said County to ye Said Parish of St Margt Westmr. as the place of hee Lawfull Settlemt.

That Yr Petrs. thinking themselve agrievd by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the Same.

Therfore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to order That ye ChurchWardens & Overseers of ye poor of the said Parish of St James Westmr. - may attend this Court Some Day this Qr. Sesss. to hear and abide the final Detirmination of of this Court touching the Said Appeal.

And Yr Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
