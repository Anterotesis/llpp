To the Right Honble St. William Calvert Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of the Honble the Justices of Gaol Delivery in their General Sessions of Gaol Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition of Samuel Jordan

Sheweth That your Petitioner was committed to Clerkenwell Bridewell on the 16th. Day of May last on Suspicion of forging the Will of John Dunton deseated and publishing the same as true Contrary to the Statute in that Case made and provided And now is in Newgate for the Same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays, That at this present [...] Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate he may be tried Baited or Discharged pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray
