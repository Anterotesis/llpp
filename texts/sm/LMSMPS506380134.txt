Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands & Seals of [...] John Sherwood & B. Campers Esqrs. two of his Maty's Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of October 1773 Margaret Lord Widow of Richd. Lord deced and her Male Bastard Child aged nine Months and upwards, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the sd. County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of St. May at Rotherhith to be the place of then last legal Settlement of the said Margaret Lord.

That your Petrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or order of the sd. two Justices Do therefore appeal to your Worships therefrom & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premes and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Nichos. Hayne John Jarman} Churchwardens.

John Leppard Michael Hedges} Overseers
