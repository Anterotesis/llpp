Peticon of Richard Byfeild to be discharged from paying to the Poors Rate

St. Giles Cripplegate Parish January Qr Sess. 1713

to be hd 22d. Feb: 1713: 9 cl

rate to stand

To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Richard Byfeild of Grubstreet Liberty in the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Callico Printer

Sheweth That yor. Petr. hath a wife and two Small Children to maintaine and dwelleth in a House but of Four pounds a year Rent, haveing nothing to doe in his Trade yet not with standing is assessed eighteen, pence per Quarter to the poors Rate which your Petr is not able to pay

Therefore yor Petr. humbly prays of this Honble Court to be discharged from paying to the said Rate neither was yor Petr. ever rated before.

And yor Petr. as in duty bound Shall pray Etc

Richard Byfeild.
