To the Right Worshipfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of ye: Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bottolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Ralph Harwood & Jos Goddard Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twenty first Day of this instant August, Robert Scofeld an Infant near Eight years of Age, was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the Said County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Bottolph without Bishopsgate to be the Place of his last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoners Conceiving yr: Selves agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore Appeale to your Worps therefrom, & humbly Pray That your Worps will be pleased to hear them in ye. Prmisses, & order That ye: Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a day certain to be by yor. Worps appointed to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale.

And Yor. Peticoners shall Pray Etc.
