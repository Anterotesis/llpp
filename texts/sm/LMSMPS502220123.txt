To the Right Worshipfull His Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of MIddx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Church Wardens- and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Clemt. Danes in this County

Sheweth

That by order under the hands and Seals of Edwd. Grose & R. Dennet two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for this County dated the, 2d Sepr. last Issabella Quarles Widow and her two Children John and George were removed from the parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate to ye said parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor Petrs apprehend themselves to be agreived by the ordr of the said two Justices and Appeale theref [...] to Yor. Worshipps And

Therefore pray your would be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determineing the sd Appeale

And yor Petrs Shall pray Etc.
