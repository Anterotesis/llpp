To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties of the peace for the County of Middx: at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the said county of Midds

Sheweth That Peter Lottery a Poor Child belonging to the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County was by the Parish Officers of the same bound Apprentice to Jacob Valescheur of the Hamlett of Bethnal Green aforesaid Weaver as will appear by an Indenture bearing Date the fourth day of August One thousand seven hundred and twenty five The said Jacob Valescheur notwithstanding the Obligation he laid himselfe under by the sd. Indenture has been gone away from his said Apprentice about three quarters of a year without leaving any Person to Instruct him in his Trade or business or making any Provision at all for him by which means he has ever since that time and now is Burthensome and chargeable to the sd. Hamlett of Bethnal Green We therefore pray that this Court will please take his Case into, their consideration and discharge him from his said Apprenticeshipp in Order that he may be put out to some other Person That will take better care of and make Provision for him

And yor Peticonr shall Ever pray Etc
