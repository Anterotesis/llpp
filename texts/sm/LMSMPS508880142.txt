To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Margaret next the City of Rochester in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by virtue of an order of removal under the hands and seals of A Graham and W Bleamire Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twentieth day of August now last past Jane Smith the Wife of John Smith was removed from the parish of Saint Sepulchre which lieth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Margaret next Rochester the said Justices thereby adjudging that to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Smith

That Your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order and judgment of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal therefrom

Your petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session [...] to hear this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Sepulchre and all parties concerned in and about the premises may then and there hear and abide the determination of your Worships therein

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Geo: Fenner Jno Weller} Churchwardens}

Richd. Marriner } Overseer of the poor} of the parish of Saint Margaret next Rochester
