To the Right Worsppll. her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Country of Middx.

The humble Peticon of William Harvey William Stearmore Cornelius Harvey and Thomas Hyde.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. did serve the office of Overseers of the poor for the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County for the year last past.

That four other persons were at Easter last elected to serve the said office for the said parish for the year ensueing Yet nevertheless the Churchwardens and Vestrymen of the Said parish Pretend to compell yor. & Petr. to pay one Months pencon for releife of the poor this present week altho their said Office is expired and they having noe such moneys in their hands belonging to the poor of the said parish.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps to the pleased to order that they may be discharged from payment of the Same.

And yor. Peter. shall ever pray Etc.
