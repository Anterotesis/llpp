To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of George Reid and John Spiller Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth Day of August one thousand seven hundred and Ninety five Ann Darling Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Greenford in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Darling and your Petitioners were required to provide for her according to Law whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly Pray Relief in the Premises and that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some Day this present Session for the Hearing and determining this their Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Moses Ellis John Copley} Churchwardens

Samuel Reed John Foster} Overseers of the Poor
