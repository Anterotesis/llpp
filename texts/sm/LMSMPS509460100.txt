Middlesex to Wit - To the worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr and the East of the Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition of the churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by our Order under the hands and Seals of John Floud and P: Colquhoun Esqr two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace taking in and for the said County Mary Stevens Widow path been removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex to the aforesaid Parish of Edmonton.

That your Petitioners convie themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore humbly appeal agr. the same and pray this your Worship will peace to appoint same day in this present Session for hearing and determining the said appeal and this all persons concerned do them attend this Court.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray et
