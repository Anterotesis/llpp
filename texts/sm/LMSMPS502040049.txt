To the Right Worshipful his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Assembled.

The humble Peticon and appeal of Daniel Pratt off the parish of St. Martin in the field in the Liberty of wester and County of Midd stationer

Humbly sheweth That by an order or Judgment dated the first day of August last under the hand band and Seals of Leonard streate and william Cowper Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx and Liberty of Wester your peticoner is convicted for selling and exposing to sale two Pamphlets or half sheets of paper, the same not being stampler Marked according to the form of the statute in that case made and provided and thereupon the said Justice have adjudged that your peticoner hath for feiled the sum of £20: vizt. £10: for each pamphlet or half sheet of paper so sold and exposed to Sale as aforesaid, which said penalty of £20: is unitigated to £6: beside £3: for the Informers costs and charges herein

That your petitioner finds himself aggrieved by the order or Judgment of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court against the same.

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships will be pleased & to appoint a day this present Sessions for all persons concerned to attend this court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this court upon and touching the said appeal and that your peticoner may be relived herein according to Law and Justice.

And your peticoner shall pray & Daniel Pratt
