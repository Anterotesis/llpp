Middx to Wit To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Held for the said County

The Humble Petition of John Singer Gent High Conble within the Hundred of Isleworth in the said County

Sheweth That Your Said Petitioner hath Served the said Office for the Space of Almost four Years and hath Accounted with the Treasurer for the said County for the County Rates and Prayeth that he may be Discharged his said Office and Some Other proper person Appointed in his Room and Humbly Recommend to Your Worships, Mr Robert Walcot Mr. Edward Smyth & Mr. William Stacey of the Parish of Isleworth in the said Hundred as fitt & proper persons to Succeed him in his said Office

Octor 17th 1751 Ja: Clitherow Jo: D: Birkhead

John Singer
