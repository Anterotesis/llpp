To ye. Rgt. Worspll: his Matis Justices of ye. peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeale of John Wareinge of ye. parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye. sd County [...] Comb Maker

Sheweth That your peticoner is rated Two peace P: weeke towards ye. rates made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd Pish and that by reason of his poverty which is occationed by ye. great decay of his traid he is not able any Longer to pay to any pish rate whatsoever, he findinge very hard to maintaine himselfe & family by ye Smale busieness which he has at his traide

Your peticonr: therefore humbly prayes Your Worsps. Consideracon in ye. premisses; and to make such Order therein as to Your Worsprs. Shall Seem meet.

And Your peticonr: shall ever pray Etc: John Wareinge
