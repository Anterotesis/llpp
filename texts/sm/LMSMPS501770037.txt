To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Eggham in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of Whitelock Bulstrode Esqr & Sr Richd Reynell Bart two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (quor unus) bearing date the 7th. day of March last past [...] Mary the wife of Francis Boulton and Francis & John their two Sons Francis aged five Years and John aged years were-removed & conveyed from the Parish of Stainvell in the said County of Midd to the said Parish of Eggham in the said County of Surry as the place of the-last legal Settlemt of the said Francis Boulton the husband

Yor Petrs finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt or Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray this Court would be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Pish of Stanwell & all other Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide their Judgmt & determinacon touching the said Appeal

And yor. Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
