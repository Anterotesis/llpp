To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Genl Sessions at Hicks Hall assembled

The Humble Petn: & Appeale of the Church wardens and Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchre's in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of A pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and Robert Hicks Esqrs Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) Bearing Date the Eighteenth Day of November last past Thomas Ryland and Ann his wife was removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said parish of St. Sepulchre's since which the said Ann is Dated & Buried at the Charge of the said Parish of St Sepulchre's the said Thomas Ryland still Surviveing by which said Warrant or Order Yor. Peticoners Conceive them selves to be Aggreived.

There fore they Humbly Appeale to Your Worships Against the Adjudgment of the Said Two Justices And Pray Your Worships will Be pleased to Order that the Church wardens and Over seers of the poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields and all persons Concerned Do attend this Court Some day in this present Session to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal.

And Yor. Peticoners Etc.

Decemr 4th 1732
