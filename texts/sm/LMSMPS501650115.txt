Middx Ss: To the Right Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Holy Trinity within the City of London

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of Samuel Perry and Doyly Michel Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd (Quor Unus) bearing date the 19th day of September last John Caborne and his three Children Vizt William aged about seven years George Augustus aged about two years and two Months and Elizabeth Sophia aged about three Weeks were removed & conveyed from the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds in the parish of St Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Midd to the said parish of Holy Trinity by the name of St. Trinity as the last place of their legal Settlemt.

Yor petrs finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Spittlefeilds and all parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor petrs Shall ever pray Etc
