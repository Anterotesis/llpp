To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeal of Thomas Poole

Sheweth That by order dated the fourth day of [...] Under the hands & seals of John Metcalfe and John pery Esqrs two of her Maties Justices of the peace for this County (quae- unus) your petr is adjudged the reputed Fath [...] of a female bastard child borne on the body of Elizabeth Mills wide in the pish of St Andrew Holborne in this County and thereby your peticon is ordered to pay [...] the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd pish the sume of eighteen peace weekly for & towards the maintenance & educucon of the sd Child for soe long time as the same should be c [...] the said parish

That your petr finds himselfe aggreived by the said order of the said Two Justices and humbly appeals agt the [...]

And prays that this Court would please to appear [...] day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Libty of Saffron hill [...] garden & [...] sd parish of St [...] Andrew Holborne & all the [...] attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgemt [...] determinacon of this Court touching the sd appear

And your Petr Shall ever pray Etc

P J Rolfe
