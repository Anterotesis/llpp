To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House for the said County Assembled,

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westmins -ter in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of D. Wilmot and John Spiller Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh day of March 1789 John Welch, Ann his Wife and their three Children Vizt: Joseph , Hannah and Esther was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of their last legal Settlement, Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that Your Worships will please to Appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned do then Attend this Court.

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Wm. Peale} Church Wardens

Jos. Jacob Henry Amela [...] Swann} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Ann
