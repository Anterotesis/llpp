To the Honorable Bench of Justices at Hickes Hall

The Humble petition of Tho: Yearwood

Exon e N: P:

to att: Wed 10 cl 1721/2

Midds Ss Ad general Session paris Dui Regis tent P Com Midds apnd Hicks Hall in St John street in Com prd P adjorn die Martis scilt vicesimo Septimo die February Anno requi Dui Georgy Dei gra mmc Regis magne Britannie Etc octavo

Itt is ordered by this Court that the within named Yarwood wife of the within named Thomas Yarwood & all other persons concerned doe attend this Court tomorrow att tenn of the clock in the forenoon to answer the peticonrs complaint & Shew cause why the peticonr Should not discharged from his imprisonment for the cause within menconed

P Cur Harcourt
