To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for Je County of middle

The humble Pelicon and appeale Edward Iden & John Crutchfield of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That Your Peticoners being charged for stock or Personall Estates and overcharged as to their rents in the poor and Scavengers rates of the Said parish Appeal age he Same

Yer Peter therefore humbly pray they may be whised in the premises As to this Court Shall seem meet And Yer Peter shall Ever pray etc

Edwd Iden Jon: Crutchfield
