To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of R Butler and Sampn. Wright Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twelfth Day of January now last past Elizabeth Briden Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Pancras adjudging the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Briden to be in the said Parish of Saint Pancras

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Determination of the said Two Justices do humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day of this present Sessions of the Peace for the Hearing of this their said Appeal and that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes and all Parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there attend and hear and abide the final Determination of Your Worships therein

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Thos: Wade Junr. Sollr. for the Applellants
