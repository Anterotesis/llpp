To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middlx.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Martin one of ye. petty Constables of the parish of South- Mims in this County

Sheweth That at [...] Christmas last past there was due and owing to your Petr. the Sume of ten pounds for onr qrters Salary for his loss of time Charges and Expences in Releiving Passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymt. of the said Sume of ten pounds to your Petr. for the sd Service

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Martin
