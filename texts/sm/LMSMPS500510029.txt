To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Anne Morcutt

Sheweth

That as of Last night one Anne Barnett of the Parish of St. Martins in the Feild in this County was robbed of Severall Goods and the person who robbed her was by your petr. apprehended and taken and was carryed before the worspl. Justice Negus and by him comitted to Newgate where she now remaines That the said Anne Barnett hath rent her sd. Goods againe yett nevertheless refuses to prosecute the Theise according to Law but Insists that your Petr. ought to prosecute And forasmuch as your Petr. being a poore maide Servt.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes your worspps to be pleased to Grant this Honble Bench Warrant for bringing the sd. Anne Barnett into this Honble Court this present sessions to answer the sd waller

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
