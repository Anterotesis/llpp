To the Rt Worll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their Generall Quarter Sessions [...]

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Feild in the said County

Sheweth That by an order under the hands & Seals of Felix Feast & John Pery Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quon un) bearing date the 12th Day of December last Mary Issum was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Criplegate in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St Martin in the Feild as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

Your Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by ye Indgmt of the said two Justices humbly Appeale there from And pray this Court Will please to order the Churchwardens [...] Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Giles [...] Carplege without Criplegate and all Parties herein concerned to Attend this Court Someday this Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgemt & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Petrs as in Duly been Shall Ever pray Etc
