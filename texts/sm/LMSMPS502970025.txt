To the Honble: Bench

The Humble Petition of Arthur Gibson

Sheweth That Your honours Petitioner in Confined in New Prison for a Quarrel These Seven weeks, and almost Starved, and willing to Serve his Majesty and an able Seaman being brought up to it Humbly begs to be Discharged having nobody to help him

And Your honrs: Petitioner as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc
