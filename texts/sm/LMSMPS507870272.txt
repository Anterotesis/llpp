To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Quarter Session of the Peace for the sd. County assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrel and Samuel Hawkins Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th day of October 1784 Catherine Twaites Wife of John Twaites as the Widow of the said John Twaites, removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County to the Parish of Saint John at Hackney as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend
