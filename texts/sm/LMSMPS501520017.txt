To the Right Worpll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quartl Sessns:

The humble Peticon & Appeale of Roger Rogerson Rector of Hanwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Yor: Peticonr: is rated & assessed for his Tythes (both great & Small) to ye poors rate in the town of New Brentford in the said parish of Hanwell at the value of tenn pounds P ann

Yor Peticons. thinking himself aggrieved wth. the said rate or Assessmt appeales to this Court for releife And prayes that the Chappellwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said town of New Brentford may be ordered to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
