To the Worshipfull Chairman of this Honourable Bench and the Rest of the Worthy Gentlemen now Sitting

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioners being within the Limitation of his Royall Majesties Most Gracious Act, Most Humbly beg Leave that they may be Ordered to be brought up before the Court, to plead for his Royall Clemency, and the Indulgence of this Honble, Court and in Duty Bound shall for Ever Pray

William Dickinson Arthur Pryor
