To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace of Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Ca Coker and J Girdler Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh Day of this present Month of February Sarah Chambers Widow of William Chambers deceased and her Daughter named Mary aged about seventeen Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all persons concerned Do then attend this Court

And Your Petitr. Shall Pray Etc

Chas. Stace

Charles Storey} Church Wardens and Robt. Desbrow Thos. Key Geo. Chalmers Wm: Hall} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
