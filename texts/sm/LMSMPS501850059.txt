To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of Thomas Hogg Late Beadle of the psh: of St: Leonar'd Shoreditch

Humbley Sheweth That yor: Petr: being formerly Beadle of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch at the yearly Sallery of Â£20 Pr: Ann or 8s Pr weeke was Discharged about 3 Weeks After Lady day Last Past but was not Paid for the 3 Weeks Service by the Late Constable and haveing Demanded it of the Present Constable he Refuse's to Pay yor: Petr. for the 3 Weeks Service

Yor: Petr. therefore Humbly Prays that he may be releived as you in yor. Great wisdom Shall See Meet

And yor. Petr. shall every Pray

Tho: Hogg
