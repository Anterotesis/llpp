To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex In their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph Without Bishopgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant Under to hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex One Whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the 31st. Day of Octr. 1748 Lydia Mann Widow late Wife of John Mann Deceased Was Passed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex To the Parish of St. Botolph Without Bishopgate Landon Aforesaid A Wedging the Last legal Place of Settlement of the said Lydia to be there and Your Petr. Conceving themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly Pray Your Worships.

Burr Attorney for- Bishopgate Parish} Would appoint Some day this present General Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County to hear this appeal and that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens And Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Mary White Chopel And all parties Concerned in and About the premes may then attend hear and abide the final Detemination of Your Worships

And Your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
