To His Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the sd Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Crost Barn father Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and forth City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of October instant Elizabeth Bingenden Singlewoman and Richard her Male Bastard Child aged four Months were removed from the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex So the said Parish of Christ Church in [...] the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Bingenden whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived and against the said Order do appeal.

Your. Petitioner therefore humbly Pray that the said Appeal may be received another heaving and determining thereof adjourned over until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden at Hicks Hall in and for the said County of Middlesex And that Your Petitioners may have such Retiot in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet.

Wm. Cooling Solr. for Appellants.

And upon Petrs. shall pray Etc.
