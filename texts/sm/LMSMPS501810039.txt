To the Right Worll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Hickes Hall

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St Martin in the Fields into the said County

Sheweth That by an order or Warrt. bearing date the Eighth day of August last under the hands & Seales of Thomas Railton & John Leanoft Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County Solomon Rixon was remove From the pish of St Margaretts Westmr. to Your Petrs sd pish of St Martin in the Fields whereby Your Petrs humbly conserve they are wronged & aggrevied

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Appeale to Your Worshipps agt the said Order & Pray Your Worshipps to appoint aday to hear & determine the same

And your Petrs: Shall Ever Pray Etc

Phill: Davis Giles Harris} Churchwardens
