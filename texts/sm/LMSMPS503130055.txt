To the Right Worshipfull and Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of saint Mary of Stratford Bow in the said County

Sheweth That by a warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Martin Lease and John priestley Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorun) bearing date the second day of this Instant December whereby John Morgan Jane his wife and William his Son about one Year Old were removed from the Hamlet of mile end old Towne in the parish of Stepney in the said County to the said parish of Saint Mary of Stratford Bow of the place of their last legal Settlement

By which said to warrant or Order of the said two Justices your petitioners conceive themselves Agrieved and humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such releife in the promised as to your worshipps shall seem meet

And yor peticoners will Ever pray Etc
