To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace Assembled att Hicks Hall

This Humble Peticonr of Thomas Perry of the pish of Stoake Newington in the County of Middx

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Rates 6s-by virture of An Act of Parliamt for Granting to his Maties Certaine Rates or Dutyes upon Houses which your Peticonr Humbly Appeales from to this Court

Your Petconr Humbly prays that he may bee discharged from the Rate of 4s he being Ready to make Oath to this Court that he hath but nine Lighte And your peticonr shall ever pray Etc

Tho: Perry

Tho: Perry discharged
