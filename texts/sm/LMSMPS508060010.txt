To the Worship ful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in there Genl Sessr. of the Peace for the sd. to Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary May dalen in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Edward B Green and N Forster Esquires two of his [...] Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the 2d day of May 1785 Martha Goldsmith Widow and her two Bastard Children namely Richard Best aged about 2 Years and a Half and Elizabeth Ann Munn aged about 7 Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Kensington in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen as the place of their last legal Settlement where by your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved,

Your Petitioners there fore humbly Bay Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc
