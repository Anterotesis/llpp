To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County

The Humble Petition of Ann Ditcher

Sheweth The the place of Matron of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell in this County is now become Vacant by the Death of Jane [...] Cavenagh lately deceased

That your Petitioner is the Wife of John Ditcher who hath for many years attended Your Worships during the Sessions held at Hicks Hall in and for the said County, and is now become infirm and incapable of getting her Living, humbly prayeth Your Worships that you will be pleased to appoint her Matron of the said House of Correction in the room of the said Sarah Cavenagh

And your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray Etc

The Mark of Ann [x] Ditch
