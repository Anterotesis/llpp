To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire and the rest of the Justices for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the County of MIddlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of William Bleamire and T. Aylisse Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the 12th. day of February 1795 Margaret Mackay was removed and conveyed from and but of the Parish of Kensington within the said County to Hammersmith as the Place of his best legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and beg leave to appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Pray that they may be at liberty hands their appeal and that the same may be adjourned to the next Sessions for the hearing thereof
