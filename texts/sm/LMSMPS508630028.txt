To his Majestys Justices of the Peace holding the Quarter Sessions of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Inhabitants of the Parish of East Dereham in the County of Norfolk

Sheweth That by an Order of removal bearing date the 7th. day of March 1791 under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller and William Blackmore Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex George Harvey and Elizabeth his Wife and their four Children namely Peter Arabella George and Mary were removed from the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of your Petitioners

Your Petitioner therefore humbly pray your Worships that inasmuch as they consider themselves aggrieved by such Order of Removal that they may be at liberty to enter an Appeal against such Order and that your Worships will appoint at day in the next Sessions for the hearing thereof

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
