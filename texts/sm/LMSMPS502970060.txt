To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Doddington in the County of Northampton.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seales of John Ellis & M: Harpe Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for [...] and Liberty of Westminster [...] (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of January instant Roger Parker aged nine years and Elizabeth parker aged seven yeares and Ann Parker aged about two Yeares children of John Parker were removed & conveyed from the parish of St James in the Libty of Westminster in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Doddington in the County of Northton (called in the Sd. Order the Parish of Darrington) [---] as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said John Parker & his said three children

Thos: Elmes agent for the Appellants. Yor. Petrs thinking themselves aggreived by the aforesaid Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same to this Court And Forasmuch as your Petrs. cannot be ready to prosecute their sd. Appeal this present Sessions humbly pray that your Worps won'd be pleased to adjorn the hearing and determinine thereof untill the next General Session of the Peace to be held for this County & that a Day may be appointed therein for all parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the Sd Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
