To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Ware in the County of Hertford

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order, of the under the hands and Seals of Pierce Galliard and Merry Teshmaker Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty sixth Day of January 1750 Elizabeth the Wife of Thomas Tyth otherwise Tide with her three Children were removed from the Parish of Endfield in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said parish of Ware as the place of their last legall Settlement Whereby your Petition think themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court wife please to Appoint a time fir hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then Attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc James Windus Attorney for the Appellants.
