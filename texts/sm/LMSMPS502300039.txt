To the right Worll: his Majties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx at a generall Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Alban WoodStreet London.

Sheweth That by an Order under [...] and Seals of Henry Lowth and Richar [...] Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace P [...] the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) of the sixteenth day of July last Elizabeth Lilley wife of Benjamine Lilley removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Alban Wood Street al be [...] the place of her la [...] all Settlement.

That the said Elizabeth Lilley's la [...] legall Settlement was not in the [...] Parish of St. Alban Wood Street.

Therefore yor. Petr. do appeal to the said Order and pray this Worshipll. Bench to appointed a day for the hearing of this Appeal that yor. Petr. may be discharged from the said Order and the said Elizth Lilley [...] not legally settled as aforesaid

And yor. Petr. shall pray Etc.
