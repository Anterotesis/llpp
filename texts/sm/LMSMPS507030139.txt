To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembd their General

The Humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St [...] Southwark in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under ye Hands & Seals of Joseph Newsom and Cadwallader Coker Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx One being of the Quorum bearing date the Fourth Day of August 1769. One Elizabeth Satchwell therein called the Wife of William David Satchwell who was mentioned to be gone from her And [---] to have left her destitute and unprovided for and her Child named Elizabeth aged Seven Months and Upwards were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Saviour Southwark as the place of [...] Settlement Whereby your Petrs. Conceive them selves Aggrieved and Pray relief in the Premes.

Wm. Pearson for the Appellents
