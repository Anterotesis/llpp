To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Stepn. Clarke and Saunders Welch Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the 5th. day of June 1762 Anne Duffy Signlewoman with her two Bastard Children Vizt. Richard aged Sixteen Months and Patrick aged Six Weeks were Removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Svans in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Martin in the fields as the plans of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petr. think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to his Court against the Same

your Petr. therefore humbly pray that this Court will be pleased for appoint same day in this present Session for the hearing & determining the said Appeal.

And you Petr.. will ever pray & Wm. Croft Soll,
