To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knight Chairman and to the rest of the Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their general Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Alder mary in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Girdler Esqr. and Philip Dyot Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) Catherine Pickles Widow of Daniel Pickles Wine Cooper deceased and her Son named John Aged seven years together with Ruth Mary her Bastard Child aged four years were removed from and out of the parish of Saint Mary Islington to the said parish of Saint Mary Aldermary by which your Petrs. conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a day may be appointed for the hearing and determining of their said Appeal and that your Worships would make such Order in the premises as to your Worships shall seem just

And your Petrs. shall ever pray.
