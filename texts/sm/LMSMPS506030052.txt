To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Machin and George Wright Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Sixth day of July last Mary Harrison wife of James Harrison was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of St. James with in the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Harrison

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Chas. Harris W Meades } Church Wardens

John Granage Sams Aris} Overseers of the Poor
