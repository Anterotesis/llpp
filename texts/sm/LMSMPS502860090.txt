To the Rt. Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the Said County

Sheweth

That by a Ward. or pass under the hands & Seals of Thomas Jones & John priestly Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County (Quar. un) bearing date the 15th. day July last Jane Burnett aged about eight years the daughter of Uphan Burnett was removed & Convey'd from & out of- the Parish of St. George in the Said County of the said parish of St. Mary White chappell as to the place of her last legall Settlement

Yr. Petrs. thinking themselves agreived by the said Wan. or pass humbly appeal agt. the Same

Yr. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray that yr. Worsps would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. George aforesd: & all other Psons Concerned may Attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Determinacon of the sd. Court touching the sd. appeall

And yr. Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etca October 9th. 1732. R: Pidgeon Agent [...] Applls.
