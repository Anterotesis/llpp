To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir William Addington Knight, and Thomas Robinson Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th: Day of November last, Susannah Darbin- Singlewomen was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes written the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners- conceive themselves to be aggrieved-

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to his Court against the- Adjudication of he said two Justices and their said Ordered And pray tha Your Worships will please As

to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

W Yarnold} Churchwardens

Isaac Hitchin Alex. Gordon William Reid} Overseer of the Poor
