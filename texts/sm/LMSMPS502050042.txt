To ye: Rt: Worshipll: His Maties Justices of ye: Peace for the County of Midd assembled in their Genall Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of ye. parish of West ham in ye County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Samuel Jones and Philip Willshire Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Midd (Quor unus) bearing Date the 11th Day of July last James Hallum Susanna his wife and their two Daughters vizt Sarah aged eighteen years & Frances aged fifteen years were removed from the Hamlett of Ratcliff in the parish of Stepney in the said County if midd to the said parish of Westham as the place of their last legall Settlemt:

Yor Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by ye Judgmt; of the Sd. two Justices appeal to this Court & humbly pray That ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Hamlett of Ratcliff & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
