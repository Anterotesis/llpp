To ye Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition and Appeal William James of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents within the Parish of St Andrews Holbourne In the said County.

Sheweth That at Easter last your Petitr was by the Inhabitants of the said Liberty Elected and Chosen to serve the Office of Overseers of the Poore for the Year Ensuing.

That your Petitioner is a very poor Man and in Mean Circumstances whereby he is Rendred altogeather Uncapable of Executing the said Office And forasmuch as there are severall Inhabitants in the said Liberty of farr better Ability then Your Petitioner and better Quallifyed to Execute the said Office Wherefore yours Petitioner humbly Conceives himselfe Aggrieved

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays he may Discharged from Serving the said Office And that some other proper person may be Chosen in his Roome As to this Court shall be thought Meete

And Yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc Wm James
