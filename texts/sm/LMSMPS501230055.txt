To the Rt: Worppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poore of the pish of Hanwell in the Sd. County.

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 26th. day of Aprill 1711. under the hands & Seals of Sr: Thomas Francklin Bart. & Ralph Hawfrey Esqr two of her Maties: Justices of ye peace for ye. sd. County (Quor unus) Joan Mould widw. of Henry Would was removed from ye. parish of Hillingdon in the Sd. County to ye. sd. parish of Hanwell as the place of hee last legall Settlement.

That Yor. Peticonrs. Conceivmt themselves to be agrrived by the Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices Due humbly Appral to yor. Worpps. therefrom.

Yor: Peticonrs. therefore humbly prayyor. Worpps will be pleased to Order that the Church: wardens & Overseers of ye. poore of the said parish of Hillingdon same day in this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon three of touching this Appeal.

And Yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc

Thomas Snape Churchwarden
