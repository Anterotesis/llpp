To the Rt Worsppll hils Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Mary Arnold of the Parish of St James Westminster in the sd County Widow.

Sheweth

That your Petitr: was allowed ten Shillings P Month as a Pention from the said Parish But for these three Months the Churchwardens have stop't payment thereof But forasmuch as yor Petitr being troubled with Lamenesse In soe much that she is able to stir and must Starve if not Relieved by this Court

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays she may be allowed her said former Pention of Tenn Shillings P Month & her Arreares paid her

And Your Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Mary Arnold.
