To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens's Etc Overseers for the poor of the parish of St. Dunstans in the West London

Sheweth

That by Warrant undr. the hands and Seales of Jeffery Saunders and John Worley Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justice of the Peace for the sd. County (quor unus) bearing date the twenty fourth day of December last past Thomas the Son of James and Frances Nader (an infant about halfe a year old) was past on yor. Petr. from the parish of St. Clements Danes in the sd. County as the place of the last legall Settlement of the sd. Thomas Nader

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggrieved by the order. of the sd. two Justices and Appeale to yor Worpps therefrom

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And yor. petrs. Shall prayer
