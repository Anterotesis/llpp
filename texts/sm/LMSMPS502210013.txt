To Mr. Walter

The humble Petition of Richard Seddon

[...] hoeth that your poor petitioner having had the misfortune of Coming in Comp [...] with those that took some goods in a another mans name was Committed to Newgate were fore receiving his tryal at Hicks's Hall was ordered to the house Of Correction for hard Laborer for a month's time which being now [...] weeks since the said time Commenced and your Petitioner having been twenty three weeks in Confinement very ill, and in a starving Condition, who having no friends and Saying for his fees, humbly begs, (he being sensible of its Saying in qr. power to Releive him out of this his miserable Confinement which [...] Duty will bind him to prayers for you & your posterity

Richard [...] aged 28:
