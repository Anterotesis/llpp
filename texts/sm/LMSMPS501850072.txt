To the Rt Worpsfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St John at Hackney in the said County.

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of Saml. Benson & Alexander Pitfeild Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (quor unus) bearing date on or about the 2d day of February last Ruth Dymoke wid. was removed from the Hamblet of Bethnal Green in this County to the sd Parish of St John at Hackney as the Place of her last legal Setlemt

Yor Petrs finding themselves agrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court agt the same

And For asmuch as yor Petrs cannot be ready at this Present Quarter Sessions to maintain & make good their said Appeal humbly Pray the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them untill the next Genll Qur Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the sd County and that this Court would appoint a day at the sd next Qur Sessions for all Parties concerned to attend in Order to abide the Judgmt & detminacon of that Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor Petr shall Pray etc

Saml Thornton
