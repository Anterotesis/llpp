To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Session

The Humble petition of Thomas Briggs Constable of the parish of Acton in the sd. County of Middx

Sheweth That your petitioner was appointed Constable in the Easter Week which was in the year of our Lond one Thousand Seven hundred & forty Eight and has Continued the said Office of Constable ever since,

And that your petitioner has Served the Office of Constable above Twelve Months and that the Court Leet have not chose any constable in your petitioner place and stead

And that your petitioners Bussiness calls him abroad which will Prevent your petitioner from Executing the said Office

Therefore your petitioner humbly prays your Worships to appoint another Constable for the said parish of Acton in his Room & stead

your petitioner recommends to your Worships Choice

Thomas Clarke at the Crown at Acton John Emmerson of Acton Butcher George Rose at the George at Acton} And your petitioner duty bound shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Briggs
