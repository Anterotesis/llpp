To the Right Worshipp full his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of William Wiggeth of New Brent ford in the said County.

Sheweth That at Easter last Your Petr: was Chosen by the Inhabitants of the aforesaid place to Serve the Office of Constable for the Year Ensuing That Your Petr: is aged near Sixty Years and very Infirme and not able to Execute the said Office

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Prays he may be discharged from serving the said office and another fit person chosen in his roome

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Wm Wiggeth
