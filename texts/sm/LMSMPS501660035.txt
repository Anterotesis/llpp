To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Sarah Elcock. Wife of James Elcock

Sheweth

That your Petrs: said Husband frequently haveing boat and abused her in a cruell and barbarous manner and having made away with all her Goods & turned her out of doors and refusing to allow her any Mainteynance your Peticoner made her application to the Worshippfull Justices Cragg [...] refused before whome yor Petitrs stands bound his appearance in this Court this Sessions to answer the aforesaid Complaints.

That yor Petitr: by reason of the aforesaid Crulties of her Husband is rendred uncapable of doing anything towards her Subsistence And forasmuch as her said Husband being a taxable of providing for her

Yor Petitr: therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to order her said Husband to allow her such a Mainteynance As to this Court, shall seems Meete.

And Yor: Petitr: shall Ever pray Etc.

Sarah Elcock
