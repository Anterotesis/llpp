To the Right Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Francis Jones Broker

Sheweth That by Indenture of Apprenticeshipp bearing date the 30th. day of October 1702, Elizabeth Bull was putt an Apprentice, to yor. peticonrs to Serve from the date of the Said Indenture for the Space of Nine Years, as by the Sd Indenture may appeare with whom yor. Peticonr recd. noe money

That the said Elizabeth Bull the apprentice has frequently Imbezelled yor Peticonrs. Goods, and divers times deserted his Service without leave, whereby yor. petr. hath been considerably damnified

That yor. peticonr there upon obtained a Warrant from Mr. Justice Negus, who upon non Conformety of the Apprentice, has bound over the apprentice to appe in this Court this prsent Session.

Yor. Petr. humbly prayes yor. Worshipps will be pleas'd to appoint a day this prsent Session [---] that the said Elizabeth Bull may be discharged of her sd. Indenture of Apprentice hood for the rafons aforesaid

And yor petr shall pray Etc. Francis Jones.
