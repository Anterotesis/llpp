Middx ss: To the Rt. Worshppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genal Session assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Edward Shordich in the sd: County

Sheweth That by Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry coloranie & James Brain Esqr. Justices of the peace for the sd: County (Quor unuc) William Brown Ann his wife & their two Children (vizt:) Thomas aged abt. Two Years & John aged about Two Year [...] from the Parish of Tottonham high Cross in the sd. County to our said Parish of St. Leonard Shoredich as to the place of their last legal Settlemt.

Therefore Yor. Peticoner the [...] them selves agreived by the sd: Goor & humbly Appeals to this Honble Court agt; the same

And Prais [...] that Yor; Worshpps would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd: Parish of Tottenham High cross may attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the sd: Appeal.

And yor; Petr; Shall ever pray Etc
