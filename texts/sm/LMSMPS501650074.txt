To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in their Genall quarte Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Patrick Urquart of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the Sd County

Sheweth That your Peticonrs: hath been Nominated and Appointed to be One of the Scavengers in the Said parish of St Giles in the Fields for this present year

That your Peticonr: by reason of a Distemper in His Head is wholly uncapable of Serveing the said Office

Your Peticonr: therefore Humbly prays (the prmises considered) that he may be discharged from serveing the Said Office

And your peticonr: (as in duty bound) Shall pray Etc
