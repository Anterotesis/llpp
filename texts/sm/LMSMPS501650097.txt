To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardns and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant undr. the hands and Seales of Richard Hilton and Richard Bealing Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quor Unus) dated the 5th. day of August 1717. Anne Bowman Singlewoman was past from the parish of St. Clements in the sd. County Upon yor Petrs. as the place of her last legall settlement

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggreived by the ordr. of the sd. two Justices & appeall to yor Worpps therefrom

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly hope yor Worpps wice appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And yor. Petr. shall pray Etc.
