Midlx Ss: To his Majties [---] Justices of the Peace for the sd: County of Midlx: at their Genal Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Mary White Chappel in the sd: County.

Sheweth. That by Order under the Hands & Seals of John Kyrby and Philip Wiltshire Esqr. two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the sd: County (Quor un) bearing Date the 30th: day of August last, [...] Mary Goulston an Infant aged about Eight Years were removed from the Parish of St Paul Shadwell to the sd: Parish of St: Mary White Chappell as to ye: place of her last Legall Settlemt:

That yr Petrs: think [...] themselves agreived by the sd: Order, [...] humbly appeal to this Honble Court against the Same

And prays that yor: Worsppl would be pleased to Order that the Church wardens & Overseers. of the poor of the sd: parish of St Paul Shadwell or some of them [...] attend this Court Same day this Prsent Sessions to hear & Abide ye Judgement & Determinacon of they Court touching ye. sd. Appeal

And yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
