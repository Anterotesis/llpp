To the Worrshipful his Majestys, Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at this General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order of pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Mich. Decons and R. Johnson Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th.. day of November 1792 Mary Beale Singlewoman and her illegitimate Child to wit Edward born the 25th.. day of October last was removed and Conveyed from out of Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey as the place of this last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themseves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present Sessions to hear and determine their said appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever prays Etc
