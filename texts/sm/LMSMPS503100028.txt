To the Right Worptt. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of Edward Watson

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Wm Booth and Anthony Wroth Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of April last your Petitioner was adjudged to be the putative Father of a Male Bastard Child born of the body of Margt. Anne Bourn (now young) on the second day of June 1733. in the Parish of St. Luke in the said County of Middx in which Order your petitioner is ordered & directed to pay to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish the sum of forty five shillings for the first four weeks after the birth of the said-male Bastard Child and for the charge of her lying in and also two shillings and six pence weekly for the purposes mentioned and contained in the said Order That your Petr could not Appeal agt. the said Order before by reason of the said Order being Served upon your Petr. since the last Quarter Sessions held for the sd. County

Your Petitioner conceiving himself aggreived by the said Order & Adjudication of the said two Justices doth humbly Appeal to this Court for relief against the same,

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc Edwd Watson
