To the Rt Worppll his Matys Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Salsbury Constable of South Mims in the said County.

Sheweth

That there was one Quarter Sallary due to yor Peticoner at Christmas last for passing and relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Tenn pounds which yor Peticoner Stands in Need of

Therefore humbly prays Yor Worshipps will be pleased to order of he may be paid his said Quarters Sallary [...]

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc. [...]
