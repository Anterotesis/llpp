To the Worship full his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genal Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon of Russell Farmer Joseph Tewith & John Randall

Sheweth That yor Petrs some time ago Passed Sevl: all Vagrants as appears by Certificates hereunto annexed

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly prayes yor. Worships to Order [...] Scott the High Constables to pay the same

And yor Petrs. in Duty Bound will ever pray Etc

Russell Farmer Jos: Winch John Randall

To the Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace in their Genl all Quarter Sessions Assembled

In Pursuance of the within menconed Order We whose Names are hereunto Sett being a Quorum of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx appointed to take an Account of the Trears & High Constables of this County (to whom the Consideration of the sd Peticon is refe [...] have Perused & inspected the Annexed Certificates & likewise the Report of their Justices of the peace hereunto Annexed (resideing in Ux bridge Division) And Do find by the sd annexed Certificates, of allowances Reports of the Justices & Affid [...] that the Petrs. has Expended & laidout for the passing & releiving of Vagrants the P Sum of light pounds four Shillings with. yor Comittee humbly Certify ought to of Eight Pounds four Shillings wth. yor Comittee humbly Certifye ought to be paid to the sd Petrs all wth: wth: yor sd. Comittee humbly Certifyes to this Court Dated the 22d day of September 1736

Jno Martin Alex Garrett Ansho: Chamberlain Robt Hinde
