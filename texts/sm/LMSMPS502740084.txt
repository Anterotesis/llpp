Middlx ss To the Right Worshipfull [...] Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in Genral Qur Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Old Artillery ground in the sd County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order Dated the Twenty third Day of January one Thousand Seven hundred and Thirty under the hands and Seals of Alexander Garrett and Thomas Exelbe [...] Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd county (Quor un) Elizabeth Forward an Infant Aged about four years Daughter of Joseph Forward was removed from the Parish of Christ Church Spittlefeilds in the sd County to the sd Liberty of the Old Artillery ground as the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitionrs find themselves Agreived by the Warrt. as Order of the sd two Justices and humbly appeal against the same

Middx Yor Petitionrs therefore humbly pray you will be pleasd to Order the ChurchWard and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of Christ Church and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court some Day this prsent Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the sd premisses and that yor Petitioners may have such releif therein as to Justice and Law Doth Appertain

And yor Petitioners Shall pray Etc
