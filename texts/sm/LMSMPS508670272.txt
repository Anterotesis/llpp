To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the twenty third day of September last under the Hands and Seals of John Staples Esquire and Samuel Hawkins Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex James Ralph Ann his wife and their five Children to wit James Aged Nine years Ann aged eight years John Aged Seven years William aged five years and Elizabeth Aged sixteen Months were removed from the Parish or Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner find themselves agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal and humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that you will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish or Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex and all other persons concerned to attend this Court this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the Premises And than Your Petitioners may have such Relief therein as to Justice and Law doth appertan

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
