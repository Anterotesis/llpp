To the right Worpll his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Generall Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Laugley in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Valantine Hilder and William Hayton Esqr two of his Majties.Justices of the Peace to the said County of Midx (Quor nuns) bearing date the the Twenty Fifth day of Septr last past John Ceverly was removed [...] from the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St: Andrews Holborn in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Langley in the sd County of Bucks as the place his last Legall Settlemt.

Yoe Peticrs thinking themselves Aggreved by the Judgemt of ye. sd. two Justices humbly Appeal there from and Pray that this Court will be Pleased to Order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the said Liberty of Sattron Hill Hotton Garden & Ely Rents to Attend this Court Some Day this Sessions to hear and [...] Abide the Judgement & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

and yoe Peties shall ever Pray Etc
