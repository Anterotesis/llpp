Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Session Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch

Sheweth That by an Order dated the Twelfth day of February 1725 under the Lands and Seales of Henry Louth, and John Fuller Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said Court (Quor Un) Anne Mills Widdow was removed out of [...] of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the County [...] Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of the place of her last Legall Settle [...]

That your Peticoners think themselves Aggreived by the said Order & humbly Appeal against the same

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Ptt agt that [...] may be Appointed for the Hearing your Peticoners against the said Order And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate may be Ordered to attend at the same Anne And that the said Orderd to attend at the same Anne Sett aside And that they may be further Relieved as Your Worshipps in your great Wisdom To have Judge to be according to Law

And your Peticoners shall Ever pray Etc
