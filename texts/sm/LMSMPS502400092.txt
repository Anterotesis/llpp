To the Worship full his Majestys Justices of the Peace for [...] of Middx at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street att their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the City of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order an Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorn Unus) bearing date of the twenty Sixth day of October last one Rebecca the wife of Abraham Collyer was removed from the Parish of St Mary White Chappell in the said County of the said Parish of St. Sepulchres adjudgeing the said Parish of St. Sepulchres to be place of the Legall Settlement of the said Rebecca the wife of the said Abraham Collyer

That your Petitoners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices

And humbly Appeal to your Worships therefrom and pray that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Mary White Chappell may attend and hear and abide the determination of your worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray & Etc

Tho: Mayward Hen: Clifton} Church Wardens

Tho: Ward Nathall. Russell} Overseers of the Poor
