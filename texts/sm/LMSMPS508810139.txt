To the Worshifull His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Certain Order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Nathaniel Conant and Philip New Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventeenth day of December one Thousand seven hundred and ninety two Amelia Fitz hugh Singlewoman was removed from and out of the parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the said City of London as the plan of the last legal settlement of the said Amelia Fitzhugh whereby your Petitioners apprehended themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them And that the hearing and determination thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex

W Newman Sol for the Appellants}
