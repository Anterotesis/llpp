To the Right Worsppll. Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Newlin & William Partridge Constables & James Alder & Jeoffry Allerbary Headboroughs of the parish of Kensington in the sd. County

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr. were the last year elected to Serve the said Offices which they have due of executed for the term of one whole Year & there being no Court Leet or any other way of Discharging yor Peticonr of their Sd. Offices & appointing others in their room

Yor Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray that they may be discharged by yr. Worspps. of their said Offices & that the Psons undernamed may be appointed in their rooms, if to yr. Worspps is Shall Seem meet

And yr. Peticonr (as in Duty board) Shall pray Etc

Thomas Begginton Robert Turner } for Constables

Cornelius Holland Thomas Harris } for Headboroughs
