To the Worshipfull his Majesyts Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Aaron Graham and Richard Clark Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of December now last past Rebecca Freeman the wife of Wiliam Freeman (who has deserted her) and their Daughter Susannah Charlotte and Infant of the Age of three years or there abouts were removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely rents in the said County to the said Part of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggreived of the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your worships against the same and request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved to your petitioners until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County, and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely rents may be ordered to attend to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs Etc

Arthur Gliddon Sollr for the Applts.
