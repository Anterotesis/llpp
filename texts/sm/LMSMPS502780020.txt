To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks. Hall assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church- wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Walgrove in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by an order or Warrant under the hands & Seales of Robert Lax & John Priestley Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Quon unus bearing Date the 24. Day of July last Hannah Robinson aged about three Years the Daughter of Rob Robinson Cordwainer & Abigal his late Wife Deced was by the said order removed from the Parish of St. John Wapping in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Walgrove inthe sd. County of Berks as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the Said Hannah Robinson whereby your Petitioners Humbly Conceive they are wrenged & agrived.

Appeal from the St al [...] Your Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day to hear & Determine the said Appeale & your Petitioners may be relieved as to your Worships Shall Seem meet.

And yor. Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Will Silver
