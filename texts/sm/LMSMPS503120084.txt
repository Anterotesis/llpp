To the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their generall Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe in the City of London

Sheweth That your Petitioners have had Elizabeth Weston Mary Weston and Sarah Weston Children of one Michael Weston past from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middx by a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Anthony Wroth and Thomas Robe Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Seventeenth day of [...] to the parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe aforesaid

That your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Pass warrant and Appeal [...] against the same to this Court.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Honoble Court to appoint a Day for hearing the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Samll: Brogdon agent for ye appellants
