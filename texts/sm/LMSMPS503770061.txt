To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace Assembled the Generall Quarter of the Peace holden at Hicks Hall in- and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petitioner and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth
