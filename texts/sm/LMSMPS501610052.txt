To the Right Worpfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Peters [---] Cheap in the City of London

Sheweth That by an order Dated the Sixth day of February last under the hands and Seales of Thomas Rand and John Fuller Esqrs two of his Majtys Justices of the peace for this County (quor Unns) one Susanna Creese was lately removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said parish of St. Peter [---] Cheap as the place of her last Settemt.

That your Peticoners finding themselves aggreived by the said Order or Judgmt. of the said two Justices [---] Appeal to this Court agt. the same

Your peticoners there fore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate and all other psons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal and that yor. Peticoners may be releived here in according to Law Etc

And your peticoners Shall pray Etc
