To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire and others his Fellows his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in their Court of General Session of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green in and for the said County assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of David Levy of Rosemary Lane in the parish of Saint Mary Matfelon otherwise White Chapel in the said County Salesman

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the twenty seventh day of May last upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County aforesaid duty summoned to appear before Rice Davies Esqr. and Robert Smith Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex to answer the Complaint of the said Churchwardens and Overseers for unlawfully be getting a Male Bastard Child on the Body of one Catherine Nathan single Woman, and which said Child is become chargeable to the said Parish.

That your Petitioner in pursuance of such Summons on the thirtieth day of May last did appear before the said Rice Davies Esqr and Robert Smith Esqr the Justices aforesaid when they thought proper to make an Order of Filiation and to adjudge your Petitioner to be the putative Father of the said Child.

That your Petitioner conceiving himself to be greatly aggrieved by the said Order of the said Justices humbly appeals to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Barnett Hyams Solr. for Appells.
