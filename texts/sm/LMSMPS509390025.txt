To the Right Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of New Windsor in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Mich. Downs and Wm. Tyler two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace action in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of June One thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety seven Mary Jones was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex into the said Parish of New windsor as the Place after last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved

Robt Cole Solr for the Appellants} Your Petitioners as therefore humbly appeal against the said Order or pass Warrant and humbly Petition Your Worships that the said Appeal may Conceived and admitted and that the same may be respited until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex and then heard on a day to be appointed by your Worships for that purpose And that your petitioners may have such other relief in the premises as to your Worships Shall same meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
