To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of James Dalbiac

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the thirteenth Day of February last convicted before Nathl. Conant Esq one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex for purchasing a certain Pair of coloured Leather Gloves subject to the Stamp Duties imposed by the Statute in such Case made and provided without the Stamp Ticket directed by Law to be affixed to the same being so affixed as the Law directs contrary to the Statute Etc And your Petitioner was Meland and adjudged to have forfeited for his said Offence the Sum of the Pounds Whereby your Petitioner conceives himself to be aggrieved

Your Petr. therefore humbly begs have to appeal to this honourable Court against the said Conviction and that a Day may be appointed in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

James Dalbiac
