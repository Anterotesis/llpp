To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Sepulctire in the said County

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Rugge & Wm. Hyde Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace & for the City & Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quourm) bearing date the 13th. day of Decr. last Lucy Moss aged Seven Years was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint George hanover Sqr. in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre by the Name of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre without as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for hearing and determining this their said appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Wade Sunr. Sollr. for Appeld.
