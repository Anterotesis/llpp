To The Rt: Worspfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx in Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of White Fryers London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removell under the hands and Seals of Robert Hinde Junr: and Valentine Hilder Esqrs: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx (Quor Un) bearing date the Fifteenth day of September last Mary Dear was Removed from the parish of St. Andrews Holborn above the Bars in the said County to the said Liberty of White Fryers as the place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Petrs. Apprehends themselves to be agreived by the Paid Order of Removell of the Said Two Justices and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petrs: and Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Determination of this Court to [...] the said appeal and that your Petrs: and appellts. may be relieved herein according to Law and Justice

And your Petrs. Shall pray Etc Tho. How Edwd. Cockman
