To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Zacharias Archall Clerk Charles Rivington Broughton John Downman and David Alston

Sheweth That on or about the twenty sixth day of July lost William Humphris late Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex did make and yield an Account of Monies received and disbursed by him as Overseer of the Poor of the West Division of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex from the first day of April one thousand seven hundred and ninety seven to the thirty first Day of March One thousand seven hundred and ninety eight which said Account was verified and allowed before Jacob Leroux and Samuel Foysters two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County on or about the thirteenth day of August one thousand seven hundred and ninety eight

That your [---] Petitioners have [---] material Objections to the said Account in as much as there [---] is charged in the said Account the Sum of forty pounds nineteen Shillings and four pence to have been disbursed as follows (that is to say)

1798 April 18 Paid Mr Seale for Printing one thousand Report of the Committee respecting the Church Lands } 3..8..

May 15 Paid Mr Patten for Entertainments of the Committee appointed to inspect the Accounts relative to the Church Lands } 13..13..4

July Paid for collecting two thousand seven hundred and ninety pounds seventeen shillings and six pence at two pence in the Pound allowed by Vestry} 23..5..

Said Mr. Hill for bad Silver 13..

That your Petitioners apprehended themselves to be aggrieved by the said Account and therefore appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worships will be pleased to take the Premises into Consideration and make such Order therein as to your Worships shall seem just-

Z Stichall Chas. R. Broughton Jno Downman David Alston
