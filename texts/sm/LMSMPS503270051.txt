To the Right worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session at Hick's Hall assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum bearing Date the seventh Day of November last past Nicholas Rigby was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeale to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day this present Session to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeale.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray.

Wm Head Sollr. for the Appellants.
