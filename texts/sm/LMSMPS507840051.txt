To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Halse and Thomas Collins two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 19th. Day of June last past William Hartley Aged Sixteen Years is removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Hartley

Your Petitioners conceive themselfs aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc S. Malbon Sollr. for The said Parish of Shadwell
