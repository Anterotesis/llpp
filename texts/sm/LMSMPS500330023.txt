To the Right Worspl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of Robert Roadon

Sheweth

That in sometime past yor. petr. at the request of the late Overseere of the poore of the parish of St. Martine in the Feild in the sd. County did receive and take into his Howse severall poore Lunatick persons in order for their Durv who were chargeable to the said parish Soe that there is due to yd. petr for such the Entertaineing the said poore Lunatick persons the Sum of Â£26.12s.6d. which the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd pish doe refuse to pay to yor. petr. whereby he humbly conceive himselfe aggreived

Yor. Petr therefore most humbly prays yor. Worsp [...] to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd .parish may appear in this Honble Court this put Quarter Sessions to shew cause why yor. pet. should not be pd. and sattisfyed the sd. moneys soe to him due on aforesd.

And yor. petr. shall ever Pray etc
