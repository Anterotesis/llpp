To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn below the Barrs in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of R Clark and A. Graham Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventeenth day of June instant Elizabeth Barker Wife of John Barker (gone from her) and their two Children Namely John aged upwards of four Years and Charles aged near two Years were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Barker Elizabeth his Wife and their said two Children John and Charles. Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that the Benefit of this their said Appeal may be saved unto them untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Joshua Cox. Hatton Garden Sollr for the Appellants June 27th. 1793
