To the Worshipful his Majesties Just [...] of the peace for the County of Middlesex [...] their General Quarter Session of the peace [...] held in and for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition of Reuben Routledge of Rati [...] in the parish of Stepney in the said County of Middlesex Cordwainer

Sheweth That by Indenture dated the 26th. day of October [...] William Cole aged then about Twelve years (a poor Child left [...] to the parish of Saint Ann in the same County) was by the the [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the same Parish with the Con [...] and Approbation of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace [...] the said County put Apprentice to your Petitioner until he sho [...] attain the Age of Twenty four years to learn your Petitioners Art of a Cordwainer

That the said William Cole is a very loose idle [...] disorderly Apprentice and hath frequently run away from [...] Petitioners Service and embezelled and purloined your Petitioner money and Goods and otherwise greatly misbehaved himself towards your Petitioner and his Family

Therefore your Petitioner most humbly pray [...] this Court will be pleased to discharge him from [...] said Apprentice Or that your Petitioner may relieved as this Court shall see meet

Reuben Routlidge
