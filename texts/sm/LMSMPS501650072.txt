To the Rt: Worpll: his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Joseph Scarlett of the parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the Said County Marriner

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath Serv'd the Government in Severall of his Majestys Ships Since the year 1691. for the Space of 8. Years and upwards, as appears p Certificate hereunto annexed That in the Said year 1691 the received a Hurt in his back on board his then Majestys Ship Sandwich by the fall of a Gunn for which he never received any Pention or Allowance.

That being now 67 years of Age and very Infirme is become unfitt for Service, wholly incapable of getting a Livelyhood and is reduced to great Want. being destitute of Friends to releve him That your petitioner was never Imprest, but alwaies Enterd Voluntary into ye Service

Your petitioner therefore most humbly prays your Worps to grant that he may receive the benefitt of this County Pention Allowed to Maimed Soldjers & Seamen if your Worps So think fitt.

And your poor Petr: Shall ever pray Etc
