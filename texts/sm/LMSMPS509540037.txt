To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth, That by virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of G. Mercer and J. Mercer, Esquires, Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quourm) bearing Date the 10th day of October 1798 Esther Harradine Widow of Matthew Harradine and her Three Children namely Elizabeth aged Seven Years, Ann aged Six Years and Mary aged Three Years and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Marylebone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worship will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

Wm. Lemage, Sol for Appellants No 12 Queen St. Soho
