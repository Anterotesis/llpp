To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Paddington in the Said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of George Mercer Junior and George Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of November Last Mary Miller Wife of Richard Miller (who in the Said Order is said to have deserted and left her) and her three Children Namely Samuel Christopher aged Nine years Margaret aged four year and Mary Anne Aged two years were Removed and Conveyed from and Out of the Parish of Saint Mary lebone in the said County to the Said Parish of Paddington as the place of the Last legal Settlement of the Said Mary Miller and her Said Three Children.

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggreived by the Adjudication of the Said two Justices therefore Humbly Appeal to his Court against the same And pray your Worship will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the Said appeal

And Your Petitioner will Pray

Herbt. Robertson Sollr. for the Appellts.
