To the Rt. Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the pish for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Wraisbury in the County of [---] Bucks

Sheweth That by an ordr. dated the Fifth day of Septembr last past undr. the hands & Seals of Sr. Jonathan Andrews & John Bateman Esqr two of her Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Middlex, [---] William Combs his wife & Children were [...] Removed from the [---] parish [...] [---] Sundbury in the County of Middx[---] the sd parish of Wraisbury in the sd County of [---] Bucks as the place of their last legall Settlement

That yor. petr conceiveing themselves to be Aggrieved by the Judgement of the said too Justices doe humbly Appeale to yor Worshipps there from

Yor. peticonrs therefore humbly pray yor Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Session for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Sunbury [---] to attend this Court to [...] eare & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal.

And Yor petr shall pray Etc.
