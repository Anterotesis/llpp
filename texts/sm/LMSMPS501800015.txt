To the Rt Worshppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Hannah Williams,

Sheweth That at the Goal Delivery of Bridewell this Session Yor Petitr was rni and & to Bridewell for the space of Fourteen daies

That your Petitr is in such a sick and week. condition that her Life is Dispaired of

Yor Petitr therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to considbr. her Deplorable condition and to order her Discharge.

And Yor petr Shall Ever pray et
