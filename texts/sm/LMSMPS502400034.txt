To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices for the County of Middx now in Session Assembled.

The humble Petition of William Sarson & Mary his Wife

Sheweth That your Petrs. stand Indicted for an Assault on one John Lodge To which they have pleaded Not Guilty and given Notice to Try their Traverse on Thursday the 12th. of this Instant January at Ten of the clock in the forenoone And there being a cross Indictmt preferred and found agt the said John Lodge for an Assault on your petrs. Mary Your petrs. humbly conceive It will be the most Agreeable to Justice to have both the Traverses tryed together

Your Petrs: therefore Humbly pray the Order of this honoble Court for the said John Lodge to try his Traverse att the Same time with your petrs.

And your petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Sarson
