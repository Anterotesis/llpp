To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bambury in the county of Oxford

Sheweth That by an order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Charles Friquet and D. Wilmot Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and ford the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty seventh day of March last Bridget Ewer widow of Samuel Ewer deceased and her two Children lawfull Issue namely George aged six Years and Jane aged two Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Banbury as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Pray that your worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions to hear and determine the Appeal and order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all Persons concerned to attend the Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination thereof

Wm Walford

Agent for William Pratt John Pain Richard Haddon} Churchwardens

Charles Wyatt Charles Barker John Cropley Robert Knight} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Banbury
