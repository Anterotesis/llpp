To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Chruchwardens & Overssers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Patrick Colquhoun and Daniel Williams- Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (me being of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th. day of November 1797. Richard Smith and Susannah his Wife were removed from the Parish of Enfield in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Giles will not Cripplegate as he place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That Your Worships will be pleased to grant them such Relief as shall be thought most equitable.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Earnshaw solr. Red Cross Street Cripplegate
