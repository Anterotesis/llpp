To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the new Session House on Clerkenwell Green. Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Charles [...] ssers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under Hanes and Seals of James Chadwell and James Fielding Esquries Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th day of November 1783 Ann Charle [...] Murdifant alias Garden Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster In the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Luke as the Place of her lastlegal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Over and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this Present Session for the Hearing and Determining [...] peal and to Orders the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James Westminster and all persons Concerned to attend this Court

And your Petioners shall ever pray Etc

Rich Jenings Sollr for Appellants
