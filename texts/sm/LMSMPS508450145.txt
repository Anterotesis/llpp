To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at the Sessions House for the County of Middlesex Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and seals of D. Wilmot and Tho. J. Cotton Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date Eighteenth day of April 1789 and directed to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex. and to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex Robert Newbrooke and his Wife and their Child Ann aged two Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch[---] on the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Clement Danes as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal
