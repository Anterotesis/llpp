To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church- warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in the said County of Middx

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under the hands and seales of G Vaughan and W Greenwood Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the first day of January instant Abraham Grasscoomb a male bastard Child aged Eight months was removed from the parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborne above the Barrs alledgeing the same parish to be the place of his legal Settlement

Your Peticoners conceiveing themselves aggreived by the said Order Doe therefore humbly Appeales to your worships that You will be pleased to Order the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden to attend your Worships some day this Sessions in Order to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc
