To

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Barkham in the County of Books

Sheweth

That Sarah Sharp and her three Children namely Thomas William and Sarah one by Order or pass Warrant dated the 13th. Day of March 1797 Under the Hands and Peals of Samuel Wegg and R Lewes. Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex removed and conveyed from the and out of the Parish of Chiswick in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Barkham ill the County of Berks as the Place of their last Settlement.

Your Petitioners and Appellants conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby humbly Appeal to the Judgment of the Honourable Court against the same and pray that this Honourable Court will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may abide the Judgment and Determination thereof and that your Petitioners may be releived in the Premises as this Honourable Court shall seem street

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
