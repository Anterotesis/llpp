To the Rt: Worppfull. her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborne London.

Sheweth That Sarah Briggs widdow. was lately remove [...] conveyed from the libty above Barrs in the parish St Andrew Holborne in the County of Midd by Warrt under the hands Seals of John Metcalfe Esqr & Alexander Marl Esqr two of her Maties Justices of ye peace of the Sd County (Quorum) to the sd parish of St Andrew Holborne London as the last place of the legall Settlemt of her the sd Sarah Briggs

Yor. Peticonr: think themselves agreived by the Judgmt. of the Sd two Justices doe therefore Appeale to this Court, and humbly pray that the Church warden & Ovrseers of ye. poor of the sd. pish of St Andrew Holborne in the libty above Barrs & all other Plons concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions one to hear & above the Judgmt Finall Determinacon of this Court in the Prmisses

P Cur. And yor. Peticonr Shall pray Etc

Char Blackwell Churchwarden of Snt. Andrews Holburn London
