To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace of Ye County of Middx The humble Peticon. of John Agres

Sheweth That yor. Petr. by Indenture bearing date the twenty second day of October in the fourth Year of his Prsent Matys Reign Annog Vm 1717 did become an Apprentice into John Prewett[---] then of the Pish of St. Bottolphs Aldgate in the County of Middx Baker for the term of Eight years concerning from the day of the date thereof As by the sd Indenture will appear.

That Yor. petr. sd Master and his Mistress have left Yor. Petr. destitute of any Imployment for this there quarters of a Year [...] that your Petr. knows not where they are gone.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worspps to order that he may be discharged from his sd Apprenticehood

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
