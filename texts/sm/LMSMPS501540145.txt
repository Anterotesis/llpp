To the Rt. Worsppll His Maties Justices of the Peace att the Qr. Sessions holden at Hickshall for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon of George Tilson of the Hamblet of Wappin Stepney Baker

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated one Shilling per Month towards the releife of the Poor of the Said Hamblet That the having a wife and Six Children to maintaine out of his weekly labour which is but Nine shillings aweek, makes him uncapable to pay the Said rate, Or any part thereof

Therefore your Peticoner most Hambly Pray's Your Worspps to give him Such releife in the premisses As to Your Worspps shall Seem meet

And yor Petr shall ever pray
