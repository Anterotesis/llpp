To ye. Rt. Worpll his Mates. Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx Assembled in the theire Genrall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of Humbly in ye. County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a Warrt. undr: the hands & seales of Two of his Majesties Justices of ye. Peace for ye. sd. County (Quor unus) dated ye. 26th. day of June last Thomas Franklin and Anne his Wife & theire Two Children (vizt) Elizabeth aged abt. 9 years and Martha abt. [...] Eleven Months were removed from the parish of St. Mary Matffellon als White Chappell In the sd. County to ye. sd. parish of Finchly as to ye. place of their last legall settlement

That yor. peticonrs. thinks themselves agreeved by the sd. Warrt. & Humbly Appeale to this Court against the same And pray that [---] yor. Worsppl would be pleased to Ordr. ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poore of ye. sd. Parish of White Chappell and all other parties concerned may Attend this Court some day this prsant Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall ever pray Etc.
