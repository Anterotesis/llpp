To the Rt: Worspfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx at their Genall Quarter Session [...] assembled:

The hble peticon and appeal of James Andrews of the Parish [...] St Mary. White Chappell in the [...] said County

Sheweth That your Peticr. is this Year Rated and assessed the Sume of one Shilling P Month towards the poor of the said Parish and your Petr. neighbours who live in houses of Greater Rent and ability are not Charged so much.

Your Petr. thinking himself agreived thereby Humble appeal against the same

Your peticor: most Humbly pray that your Worsps would be pleased to give him Such [...] in the premises as your worsps shall seem meet

And Your Peticoner shall ever pray

James Andrews
