To the Rt. Worspful his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now Sitting at Hicks hall

The humble petion of Nathaniel Her [...]

Sheweth.

That the last week Yor. petr. was nominated one of the Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields who is wholly ignorant of the sd. parish affairs & not fit to serve ye. sd. office for that is near 70 years of age & his Memory fails & is very infirm in Body Likewife

yr. petr. therefore humbly prays [...] anothr. person may be nomina [...] to serve in his Room

And yr. petr. Shal ever pray Etc

P J Gay

Nath Herbert
