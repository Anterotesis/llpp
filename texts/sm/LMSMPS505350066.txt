To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Edgeware otherwise Edgeworth in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Hallett and John Crosse Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one of whom being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Sixth Day of March last John Cover a Poor than was removed from [...] and out of the Parish of Hendon in the said County to the said Parish of Edgeware otherwise Edgeworth as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this Present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Thos Purtyn on behalf of the Parish of Edgware
