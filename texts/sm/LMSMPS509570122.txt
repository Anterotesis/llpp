To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All hallows London Wall in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of G Goodenough and J Hunter Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 9th: day of November 1798 Martha Daniells by the Name of Mary Daniels aged about 28 Years Singlewoman removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St: Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Allhallows London Wall the said Justices adjudging the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Martha by the name of Mary to be in the said Parish of Allhallows London Wall whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determination thereof may be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Willm: Hughes Solicitor for the Appellants
