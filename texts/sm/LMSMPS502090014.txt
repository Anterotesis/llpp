To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Session

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint [...] holborne above the barrs in the County of the [...]

Sheweth That by a pass warrant or order under the hands and Seales of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs: two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor vuns) bearing date the ninth day of February instant Ann Smith wido: with Ann her daughter were removed and passed from the parish of Saint Giles's on the Feilds in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Saint Andrew-holborne above barrs as the place of them last legall Settlement

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves aggreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the and Pray that all Parties concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching [...] Appeale

And Your Petrs: shall pray Etc
