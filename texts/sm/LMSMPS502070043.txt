To the Rt. Worppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticonr and Appeals of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish fo St. John of Wapping in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Samuel Perry and Robert Dennett Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace (quorum unus) for the said County bearing doth the 20th day of October last past Katherine Kitely Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Metfellon als Whitechapple in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. John of Wapping as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Peticoners think themselves Agrived by the Said Order humbly Appeals to this Honoble. Court. against the same

And Prays that your Worpps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St Mary Matfellon als WhiteChapple and all other persons Concerned may Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeals

And Your Pets Shall ever Pray Etc
