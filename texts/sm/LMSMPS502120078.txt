To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeall of the Churchwardns. & Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Samuel Sanders and Thomas Sett Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middx (quor unns) bearing date the 17th. day of Aprill inst. Elizabeth Clare urdo was past on your Petrs. from the parish of St. Andrews holborne in the sd County of Middx as the place of the last legall settlement of the sd. Elizabeth Clare

That ys Petrs. conceive themselfes aggreived & burthener by the order of the sd. two Justices and humbly appeale to yor. Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor worpps to appoint a Short day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And yor. Petrs Shall Prays Etc.
