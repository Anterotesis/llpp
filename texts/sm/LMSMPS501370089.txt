To the Right Worspps her Majties Justices of the peace for the Countie of Middlx

The Humble: Peticon [...] appeale of Jeremiah Chandler of the parish of St Giles without Cripple- gate in the sd Countie Twister

Sheweth That yor: Petr is rated to the releife [---] of the poore of the sd parish three Shillings pr Quarter hath a Wife & two Small Children to maintane by his once hand Labour And for as much as yor: Petr keeps his Mother from being any wages a charge to the. sd. parish & but of meane circumstance which makes his unable to pay th sd. Rate

Therefore Yor: Petr. most humbly prayes this Honble; Court to take his case in to Considrtion & grant him Such releife as yor Worps shall Seem meet

And yor Petr shall pray Etc
