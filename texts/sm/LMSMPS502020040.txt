To the Worfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Petition of Joseph Wooton and William Gillmore Church Wardens of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County on behalf of themselves and other Inhabitants of the said Parish.

Sheweth That the Highwayes of the said Parish are very much out of Repair and will require a Considerable Sum of Money to repair and Amend the Same Which cannot be raised without the Aid of this Honrble. Court.

Your Petitoners therefore humbly pray that a Rate or Assessmt. not Exceeding three halfpence in the pound may be made upon the said Inhabitants for repairing of the said Highwayes according to the Directions of an Act of Parliament in that Case lately made, to be Collected by the present Surveyors of the Highwaied in the said Parish, And that the Money collected on the said Rate be imployed and laid out as the Justices of the Peace for this County usually meeting at the Speciall Sessions in the said Parish shall Direct and Appoint.

And Yor. Petrs. er. Joseph Wootton Will: Gillmore} Church Wardens
