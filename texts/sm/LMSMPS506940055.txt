To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Pryor

Sheweth That for the Year last past your Petr. served the Office of Constable for the Hamlett of Sudbury in the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in this County and that this Year there being noe Court Leet held & kept for the Mannor of Harrow aforesd. Your Petr. cannot be Discharged from his sd. Office.

P Tho: Nash Your Petr. therefore most humbly prayes your Worspps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd. Office and that Richd. Bliss a fitt and able person may serve the same Office for the Year Ensueing Fyor Worspps for this [...] fitt

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

John Prior
