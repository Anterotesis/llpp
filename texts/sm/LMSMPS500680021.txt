The Humble: Petion: of Morgan Hu [...] a prisnr: ln Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petinr being falsely swane against for washinge of Money, of which Crime he being Innecerit he humly: desires yr Honble wond be Pleaser to Call him to his Tryall, yt he may be Admitted to Baile, or Discharged, according to the Late act of Parliimt:

And as in duty bound He Shall ever pray
