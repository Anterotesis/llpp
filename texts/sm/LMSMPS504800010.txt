To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Court of General Sessions at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent.

Humbly Sheweth Unto This Honourable Court That by Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Walter Berry and Chrisr. Scott Esquires bearing Date the Seventeenth day of Janry last Ann Bradfold Widow of John Bradfold Mariner deced was Removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middx to the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent as the Place of her last Legal Settlement by which We think our Selves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the said Order

We therefore your Petitioners Humbly pray this Honourable Court will be pleased to Grant an Order for all Parties to Attend this Court in Order to hearing the Complaint and Defence of both Parties by Evidence And to make such further Order thereon As the Court shall think proper.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd Hopkins Robt: Shank} Churchwardens

Silk Norman Wm: Stevens} Overseers of the Poor
