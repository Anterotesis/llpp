To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Genll. Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticonr & Appeale of the Church wardens and Over seers for the poore of the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands & Seales of Jno Roughton and Thomas Jett Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the Sd. County of Middx (quor Unus) dated the 29th. day of July 1726 Elizabeth Storer wido. with two Children Vizt Thomas Shute aged about four yeares & Arabella Maria aged three Yeares were past on yor. Petrs from the Parish of St. Andrews Holborne in the Sd. County of Middx as the Place of their last legall Settlement

That Yor. Petr. conceive them selfes aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices one humbly appealed to Yor. Worpps therefrom

Wherefore yor. Petrs. humbly Pray your Worpps to appoint day to heare and determine the Sd. Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
