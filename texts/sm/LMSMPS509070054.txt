To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Matthew Baynes of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hand and Seal of William Addington Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex bearing Date on or about the thirtieth Day of January last whereby your Petitioner was adjudged and ordered to pay the Sum of five Pounds for Knowingly harbouring and Assisting One James Gorton a Supposed Deserted Pursuant to the Act of Parliament for that purpose by which said Order Your Petitioner Conceives himself to be aggrieved

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Justice and his said Order and Prays that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day to hear and Determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioner will ever pray Etc.

Mattw. Baynes
