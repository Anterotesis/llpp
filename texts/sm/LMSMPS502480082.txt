To the Rt. Worsppll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the Parish of Stepney in the Said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Edward Grose and Robert Jackson Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the Peace of the [...] [...] County [...] contrary date the 20th day of October [...] pest Sara Young Widdow and Relict of John Young Labourer deced and her two Children (vizt) Edmond young aged about three Years and John Young aged about One yeare the lawfull Issue of the said Sara Young and her late husband deced were removed from the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the said County to the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney above said as the place of their Last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners think themselves Aggrieved by [...] the Order of the Said two Justices And Humbly appeal to Year Worsps. against the Same

St. Cowper Sols Therefore most humbly pray Your Worsps will be pleased to hear them in the purfoes And Appoint a day in the present General Sessions for the [...] the said Appeal and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John of Wapping and all parties concerned may then Appear and hear and abide the determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Yr. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
