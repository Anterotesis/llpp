To the Rt. Worpll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Johnson [...] [...]

Sheweth

That there is due to your Petr. for Wages from one Mr. Wimble the sum of Â£5. which he Refuseing to pay to your Petr Hands bourn to appear & this were the Same before this Honble Cort this put Sessions.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayer your Worspps to be pleased to Grant this Honble Bench Warrt. for bringing the sd. Mr. Wimble into this Honble Cort this put Sessions to Shew cause why he refuseto pay the sd. Wages to yor Petr.

P Tho: Nash

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.

Thomas Johnson
