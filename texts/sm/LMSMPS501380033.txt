To the Rt: Worsppll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of George Wray of the Liberty of Norton Folgate in the said County.

Sheweth That on Easter monday last your petitr: was by the Inhabitants of the Said Liberty maliciously Chosen to Serve the Office of Scavenger for the Said Liberty That your petitr: is a poor Infirme man and getteth his Living by Driving a Carroome which he Hireffs of another person for the Carriage of Goods & is Obliedged to be frequently from home soe that Itt would tend to the ruin of yor petitr: to Execute the Said Office

That your Petitr: did in October Last preferr his Peticon to this Court thereby Setting forth that in September Last he was maliciously Chosen to serve the Office of Headburrough for the Said Liberty And upon hearing of what was Insisted on by yor. Petitr: your Worspps were pleased to discharge him from Serving the Said Office.

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays yor Worspps will be pleased to order that such Inhabitants of the said Liberty as are usually present at the eleccon of the Publique Officers thereof may attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew Cause why he Should not be Discharged from serving the said Office and that such other person may be Chosen in his Roome As unto yor Worspps Shall seeme Meete And yor Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Geo: Wray
