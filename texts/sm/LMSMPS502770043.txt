To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in General Sessions al Hicks Hall assembled

The Humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Chiswick in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Mercer and Richard Gifford Esqrs. Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing Date the fifth Day of August Instant Susan Owen Widow of James Owen and their two Children (vizt.) Martha aged six Years and Steward Charles aged Eighteen Months were removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Chiswick whereby your petrs. conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they Humbly appeal to your Worships against the adjudgment of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your petitioners shall pray Etc
