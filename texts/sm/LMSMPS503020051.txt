To The Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Clane in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of May last past Mathew Tompson Casandra his Wife with their three Children (That is to say) William Tompson aged ten Years Mary aged three Years and William Tompson aged five Months, were removed from the Parish of St. George in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of St. Nicholas Clave London alledging the said Parish of St. Nicholas Clave to be the place of their last legall Settlement

Anthony Harrison Agent for the Appellants That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do Therefore appeal to your Worships against the same [...] and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. George in the County of Middlesex may appear before yen at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
