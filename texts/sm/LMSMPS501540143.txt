To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and appeale of John Ransom of the hamblett of Wappin Stepney in the said County Corkcutter

Sheweth That your Petr is rated towards the releife of the poor two Shilling P month and forasmuch as your petr. is in mean Circumstances and a family to maintain by his hand labour and thinks himself aggreived by the said Rate and appeales against the Same

Therefore your Petr. humbly prays your Worps. to take his Case into your Consideracon and grant him such releife in the prmes as your Worps shall seem meet

And yor petr Shall pray Etc John Ransom
