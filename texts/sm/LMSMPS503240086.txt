To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of I. Poulson and W: Hayton Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 30th. day of June 1737 Ann Clapham aged about Eleven years Margaret aged about Seven years and Mary aged about four years the Children of Robert Clapham were removed from the Liberty of Session Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. James as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby You Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved

And Therefore humbly Prays this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
