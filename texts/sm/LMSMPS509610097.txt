To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woughton in the County of Buckingham

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Cosser and Joseph Moser Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of March last past whereby Susannah Jennings and Jane Jennings the Children of Thomas Jennings a Private Soldier in the first Regiment of Foot Guards were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the said City and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Woughton as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order

Order of the said Justices and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session so the hearing and determining thereof and that all persons concerned may have notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Thomas Billington Churchwarden Thomas Shoulder Overseer of the Par
