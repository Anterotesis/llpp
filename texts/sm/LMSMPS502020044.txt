To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace of assembled

The Humble petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bartholomews the Less London

Sheweth That by a pas Warranter Order under the hands & Seals of Matthew Hewitt and Thomas Puidar Esqr two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor in us)bearing date the Fourteenth day of February last Elizabeth the Wife of Nicholoas Clifton with William their son were removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx unto the Sd. parish of St. Bartholomews the Less London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That yor Petitioner conceiving themeselves aggreived by the sd. pass Warrantor Order Do humbly appeal to your Worshipps therefrom And pray that yor. Worshipps will be pleased to them them in the plmissed & order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the sd. County may appear at a certain day to hear & able the Indgmt. & Determination of yor. Worpps. touching this Appeal

And yor. petr. shall over pray etc.
