To the Rt Worshippfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the county of Middlx in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of William Allstone.

Humbly Sheweth That the place of Governour of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell in the said County being now vacant oy the Death of Mr Adam Harker late Governour there of And your Worshipps Nomination and Disposall

That your Petitioner hath been Assistant and Turnkey to several late Keepers of New prison And being (as he humbly conveit Quallifyed to serve the said Office of Governour of the said House of Correction and is ready to give sufficient Sureties for his faithfull discharge of the said Office and being a Person well affected to his Majestys Person and Government as will be Attested if required.

Ypur Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Elect and choose him Govenour of the said House of Correction in the Room and Place of the aid Adam Harker Deceaced.

And yor: Petr Cas in Duty bound shall Ever Pray Etc

William Alstone
