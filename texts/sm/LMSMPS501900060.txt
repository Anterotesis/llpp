To the Rt Worpll: his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Allhallows Barkin in the City of London

Sheweth That by a pass warrant or order under the hands and Seals of [...] Fuller and Matt: Hewitt Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midlx (Quorn Unus) bearing date the 16th Day of November last past Elizabeth Clare Single woman, was removed from the parish of St James Clerkenwell in this county unto the said Parish of Allhallows Barkin in the City of London as the Place of her last legal settlement That your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your Worpps there from

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgmt and determinacon of your Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petrs shall ever Pray Etc
