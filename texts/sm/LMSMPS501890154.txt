To the Rights Worpl his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace for the Sd County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Albans Wood Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of Fisher Tench Bart & Thomas Milner Esqr two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the Said County (one being of the Quore) bearing Date the 18th day of June last Sarah Croxford was removed & past On Your Petitconers by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Elykents in the parish of St Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx alleding the Sad parish of St Albans Wood Street to be the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt & Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to Your Worps. therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worps will be pleased to save to them this Appeal & to be next Qur Sessions & hear them in the premisses, And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd [---] Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden & Ely Kents in the Pish of St Andrews Holborn in Middx may attend tem [...] at ye Sd next Quarter Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of the Court touching the said Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
