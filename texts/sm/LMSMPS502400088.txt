To the Rt. Worpfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Barthew by the Exchange in the City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seales of John Fuller & Ward Esqrs. Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Thirty First Day of October last past Elizabeth Davis Wife of Edward Davis & their two Children, vizt Elizabeth aged about Six years & Richard aged about three years, were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Bartholomew by Exchange London, alledging the said Parish of St. Bartholomew by Exchange to be their last legall Place of Settlment.

That Your Peticonrs. Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore appeals to yor. Worps therefrom & humbly pray that your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses and order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain Day to be by your Worps there unto appointed to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeals.

And yor. Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.
