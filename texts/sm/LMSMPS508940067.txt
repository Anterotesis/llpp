To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Sessions of the peace for the said County assembled

The Humble petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which her above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and seals of William Bleamire and Richard Clark Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the First day of February Instant Whereby Thomas Fox, Mary Fox, Charles Fox and Mary Fox were removed and conveyed from and our of the parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and request that you will be pleased to appoint some day in the present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And as in Duty Etc

Arthur Gliddon for Appellants
