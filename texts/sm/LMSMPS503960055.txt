To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex In their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopgate London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant Under the hands- and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the- County of Middlesex One Whereof being of the Quorum made the 12th. Day of April 1749 (and Dated by mistake the 12th. Day of April 1748) Ann Perry Steed Otherwise Singlewoman was- Removed form the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex To the Parish of St. Botolph Without Bishopgate London aforesaid Alledging the last Legal place & Settlement of the said Ann to- be there and your Petrs- Conceiving themselves Agrieved by he Said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly pray your Worship's

Burr Attorney ford Bishopgate parish } Would Appoint some day this present General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County to hear this Appeal and that your Worships Will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Luke and all Parties Concerned in and about the premises may then Attend hear and abide the Final Determination of your Worships

And yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
