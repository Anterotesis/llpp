To the Worhsipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Quarter Session of the Peace assmbled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Barking in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virture of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of N: Conant and John Scott Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the first day of March last, Elizabeth White Widow and her three Children namely Aqnes aged about Nine Years Robert aged Six Years and upwards and Sampson aged four Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Barking in the said County of Essex as the place of their last legal Settlement, Whereby Your Petitioners as as they conceived aggreived.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their and Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining there said Appeal

And your Petitioners will Pray Etc

T Dacre Sollr for the Appellants
