To the Right Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of William Camron apprentice.

Sheweth That by Indenture of Apprentices his bearing date 16th. day of August in the Year 1726 yor: Petr: was Bound an apprentice for the Term of Seven Years to one Thomas Millington a Brushmaker in the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County Middlesex to learn his Art & with after the Marrer of an apprentice to Serve the and seven Years.

That Yor: petrs: Said Master has severall times without any proveration bruelly & Barborously beaten & misused yor: Petr. that he has not been able to Perform his work.

That yor. Petr. said Master has not Instructed Yor. Petr. in the art of a Brush maker according to his Coven and in the said Indenture of appernticeship

Yor: Petr. there fore humbly prays yor wors will be pleased to Discharge [...] from his said apprenticehood

And yor. Petr. that ever pray Etc

William Camron
