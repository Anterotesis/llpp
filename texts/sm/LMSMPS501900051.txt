To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace For the County of Middlx.

The humble peticon of James Cass of the Liberty of Grubstree in the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth That at Michaelmas last your petr. was by the Inhabitants of the said Liberty chosen to serve the Office of Head borough for the year ensuing.

That your peticoner hath a sickly wife to mainteine and your Peticoner being aged above Sixth Years is rendred uncapable of Watching in the night time or Executing his Office.

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to Order the Inhabitants of the said Liberty to attend this Court some Day this Session to shew cause why Your [...] should not be Discharged from serving the said Office and some other fit person chosen in his room and stead.

And Yor. Petr: shall Ever pray Etc

James Cass.
