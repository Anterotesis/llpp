To the Rt: Worsppfull his Maties Justices of the peace fro [...] the County of Middx at their Genal Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of St: Dunstan Stepney in the sd. County

Sheweth. That by Order under the Hands & Seals of Thomas Railton & Gideon Harvey Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd: County & Liberty of Westmr: (Quor un.) bearing date the 26th: day of May last Elizabeth Salmon widow was removed from the Parish of St: Margt: Westmr. in the sd: County & Liberty to our said Hamblet of Mile End Old Town as by the name of the parish of St. Dunstan Stepney als Stebunheath in ye Hamblet of Mile end Old Town as to the place of her Last legall Settlemt:

Wee thinking ourselves agriev'd do humbly appeal to this Honble Court against the same.

And pray that ye. Worspps would be pleased to Order that the Churchwarden & Overseers, of the poor of the sd: Parish of St: Margt: Westmr: and all persons concerned may attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & Determinacon of the Same touching the said Appeal

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc:
