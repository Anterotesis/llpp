To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the [...] Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary of Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of David Walker Esqr and Philip Dyot Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty seventh day of April now last past William Taber and Hannah his Wife and their three Children Namely John Aged Nine years or thereabouts Sarah Aged five years or thereabouts and Thomas Aged Sixteen Months or thereabouts were removed and Conveyed from and not of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth in the said County of Surry as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said William Taber and Hannah his Wife and their said three Children

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Adjucation of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will Appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray etc

John Tucker Sofr. for the Appellants
