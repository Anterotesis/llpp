To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Christopher in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Charles Ackers and John Keeling Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Corum) bearing date the fourth day of November one thousand Seven hundred and fifty Seven. Mary Green was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex. in to the said parish of Saint Christopher.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices. Do humbly Appeal against the same, and pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for the said County and that the Court will appoint a day in the said next Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all other Persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal

Fras. Salt Alley for Appellts.
