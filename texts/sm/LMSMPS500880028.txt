To the Worshippfull her Maties. Justices of the Peace assembled as Hickshall The humble Petition of Samuel Bonwick and a Nathaniel Tarrent late Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Hamersmith

Sheweth

That yor Petitioners were Overseers of the Poor and served that office in the year 1701 And at the end of the year had their Accompts audited by the Vestry and allowed and passed by Thomas Gratwick and Timothy Lannoy Esqrs. two of her Maties: Justices of the Peace for this County resideing in or near the said Hamlett And that the ballance due to yor. Petitiors upon the said Accompts amounts to about five and twenty pounds which sume they have demanded of William Rogers Chappell Warden and Gilbert Pasmore and Robert Kirk overseers of the Poor who refused either to pay, or deliver yor Petrs. their Booke.

Wherefore yor. Petrs. pray That this Court will please to Order the said William Rogers Gilbert Pasmor and Robert Kirk to produce their said Book and pay yor. Petrs. what shall appear due to them on the fort of their said Accompts

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray etc Samuell Borwick Nathanel Tarant
