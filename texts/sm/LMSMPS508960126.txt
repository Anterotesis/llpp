To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of all hallows the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That under and in Pursuance of an Order made by two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex under their Hands and Seals bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of November nor last past Susannah Bullin Wife of John Bullin deceased and their four Children Viz. Elizabeth aged about fifteen Years Susannah aged about thirteen Years, Esther aged about eight Years and John aged about five Years, were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County to the said Parish of All hallow the Great whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray that your Honours will be pleased to receive their said appeal and appoint a Day during the present Session for Hearing and Determining the same

John Jackson Wm. Walduck} Churchwardens

Mattw. Johnson W. Horwood} Overseers
