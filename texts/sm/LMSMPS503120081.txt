To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the General Quarter, Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Mercer and Robert Hucks Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (Where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of July 1735 Jane Delany Wife of Solomon Delany was removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Jane Delany

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day of this present Sessions that all parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and determination of this Court for and touching the said Appeale

And your Petrs. as in duty bound Shall ever pray
