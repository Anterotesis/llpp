To the Rt Worpptt his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of William Simpkin

Sheweth That your Petitioner by his Indenture of Apprenticehood. bearing Date the Fourth Day of September 17 [...] became an Apprentice to Dacimus Price Sw [...] Cutler for the terme of Seven yeares Comencing From the day of the date of the said Indenture As there by appeares.

That your Petitioners said Master is & for sometime past hath been a Prisoner in the Marshalsea prison C [...] in Southwarke & yor petitioner is noe weires taken Care of as to Necessrys or Instructed his busy used And for as much as yor petitrs. Indenture not being Indented humbly Coxtewes the same to be Illegal

Yor Petitr therefore humbly Prays he may be discharged from his Apprentice hood to the Said Dacimus Price.

And yor Peticoner shall Ever pray [...] Wm Simpkin
