To the Right Worsppl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Jeremy Boreman Governr. of the house of Correccon at Clerkenwell in the Said County.

Sheweth. That yor. Worspps were pleased to allow your peticoners Sallary of three Score pounds P An [...] in respect of his said office.

That there was halfe a years Sallary due to your Petr. at Christmass last and he having been at great charges and expences in prosecuting Richard Keele and William Lowther for the murder of Edward Perry your Petrs. late servant and in paying the Surgeon for tureing the wounds and bruises which yor. Petitoner and his Servant received at the time the Said Murder was comitted and your Petr. being at this time in great want of money

[...] Westley Yor. Petr. therefore humble prays your Worspps wil be pleased to order the payment of his Said Sallary together with such allowance towards defraying his said other charges as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
