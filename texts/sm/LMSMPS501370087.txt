To ye: Worspll: her Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeale of Robert Singleton of ye. parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in ye. Sd County;

Sheweth That your peticoner is Rated 3s: p: Quarter to ye. Rate made for ye. releife of ye poor there & by reason of ye. mony Quarters that are ussually made he is not able to pray ye. Sume, and that the he was ye. Last. Yeare by reason of his poverty discharged from payinge to ye. Sd. Rate, and that he is not in better Circumstances then he was ye. Last Yeares

Your peticoner therefore humbly prayes your Worspr. will be pleased to grant an Order of this Honable: that he may be discharged from payinge to ye. Sd Rate or Otherwise as to Your Worspr. shall Seeme meet

And Your peticoners shall ever pray Etc: Robt. Singleton
