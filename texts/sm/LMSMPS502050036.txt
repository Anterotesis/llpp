To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

Tha humble Peticon of the Church wardens and Ovrseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the Sd. County

Sheweth That an Ordr. andl. the hands & Seales of Wm. Gore & Danl. Combe Esqr. two of his Matyes Justices of the peace further County (Qum unus) dated the 23d. day of Augt. last Peter Hill was removed from the pish of St. Martyns in the Feilds in the Sd County to the Sd. pish of Clemt. Danes as the place of his left legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs. think themselves aggreious by the Sd. Ordr. & Ges [...] to make it appeare that the Sd. Peter Hill hath not quines any settlemt. in the Sd. pish of St. Clemt, Danes & [...] therefore apperle from the Sd. Ordr. to the Judgemt. of this Cort

Chimmore Yor Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. worspps will be pleased to appoint all plyes concerned Wethers yor. worshipps some day this prsent sessions to heare & abide by yor Judgemt. on this Appeale

And Yor petrs. shall pray Etc.
