[...] to her that Justice of the peace [...] [...] for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Grimstone of old street Square in the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County aforesaid Glove cutter

Sheweth That your Petr. dwelleth in a House of Six pounds per Ann Rent, hath a wife and four Children to provide for, and is but a Journy-man; and is also in a very poor condition yet nevertheless your Petr. is Assessed 4s:6d per Quarter to the poor's Rate which your Petr: is not able to pay.

Therefore humbly prays of this Honble. Court to be discharged from paying to the said Rate, nor was your poor Petr. ever Assessed before;

And yor Petr shall pray Etc William Grimstone
