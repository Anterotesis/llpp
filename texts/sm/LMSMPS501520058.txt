To the Rt: Worpll: his Majts: Justices of the Peace at their Genll: Quartr: Session for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & overseer of the poor of the parish of St: Martin in the Field is the said County

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands & Seals - of John Milner & William Prestley Esqr: two of his Majts: Justices of the Peace of the said County, bearing date the 23d: day of March last, the Lady Ann Read widow of St: Wm Real Knight deced was removed from the Parish of St. [---] Giles in the Fields in the said County, unto yor: Petr said Parish, to be by yor: Petr: releived, in: whereby yor: Petr humbly think they are aggrieved

And therefore humbly appear to yor: Worps agst: the said order; And pray yor: Worps to Adjoyne the said Appeal to any day the next Session, or to appoint some day this Prsent Session, to hear & determine the same

And Yor: Petr shall ever pray Etc
