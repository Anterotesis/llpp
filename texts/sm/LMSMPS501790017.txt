To the Rt Worppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Jane Pratia Widow

Sheweth. That on Monday last one Alice Powell was comitted to New Prison for assaulting yor petr. in a violent Monmer insoemuch that her Life is Dispaired of now and for asmuchas. Your Peticoner not being able to come to Prosecute her this Sessions

Humbly Prays she may be continued untill next Sessions in New Prison unless good bail be given

And Yor: Pert Shall Ever pray Etc.

J. Pratia
