To The Worptll. Her Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the [...] let of Wapping Stepney in of St Dunstans Stepney in ye County of Middx

Sheweth

That by an Order dated the 27th day of November Last past made under the hands and Seiles of Roger Smith & Robert Constable Esqr two of her Majties Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx (Quor on) one Elizabeth Shepheard widw: was Lately removed from ye parish of St Mary whitchappell in ye said County to ye sd Hamlet of Wapping Stepney as the place of her last legall Settlemt:

That yor. Petr find themselves agreived by the said Order of the said two Justices & Appeal against ye same the said Elizabeth ;Shepheard not haveing any legall Settemt. in the sd Hamlet of Wapping Stepney

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays that [...] Court will order a day in this Session Lysto attrnd and to he

Judgement & determination of this Colart touching ye sd Appeale

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc:

Wm Caunfer} Churchwarden

Anthony Bootes} Overseer of the Poor
