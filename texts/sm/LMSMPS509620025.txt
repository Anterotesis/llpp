To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Willm Addington Knight and Rupert Clarke Esqr Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of Septermber now last past Ansteas Kavanah Wife of Peter Kavanah (who is therein stated to have deserted her) and her Child Ann aged five Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray Etc. Herbt. Robenson Solr. for the Appellants
