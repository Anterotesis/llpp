To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genll. Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers for the poore of the Parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands end Sentes of Thos. Rand and John Whetham Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quor unus) bearing date the 27th. day of February 1718. Robert Lamas with Ann his wife and one Child Vizt. Robert aged abt. 2 Yeares were past on Yor. Petrs. from the parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate in the sd. County as the plan of their last legall Settlement

That Yor. Petrs. apprehend themselfes aggreived by the order of the sd. two Justices & humbly appeale to Yor. worpps therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly hope yor. Worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
