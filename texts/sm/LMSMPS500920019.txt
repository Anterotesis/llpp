To the Rt. Worspr. her Matys. Justice of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and appeare of the Churchwardens in On Pere of the Poore of the Parish of St. Georges in Southwarke in the County of Surrey.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands Stales of Two of her Matys Justices of the Peace for this County One Katherine Butler wife of William Wester was Passed from the Parish of St. Margarett Westmr in the sd County of Midds to the sd Parish of St. George in Southwarke as the place of [...] Legale Settlemt.

That yor. Petr can make Appeare to this Honble Cort that the sd. Katherine Butler was never Legally settled in the sd. Pish of St Georges in Southwarke

P Tho: Nash Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Pray your worpps to be pleased to Order that the Chruchwardens Overseers of the Poore of the sd. Pish of St. Margt. Westmr may appeare in this Honble Corr this Put to Sseeions be Shew cause why the sd. Katherine Butler should not be Repassed to the sd. Pish of st. Margt. Westm.

And Yor. Pet. Shall Ever pray Etc
