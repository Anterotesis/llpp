To the Rt. Worshipll His Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Badley in the County of Northampton

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seales of John Wilson & Richard Lilly Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Midd (Quor unus) bearing Date the eighth day of March last Moses Newman & Elizabeth his wife were remov'd from the parish of Wilsdon in the said County of Middx to she said parish of Badley as the place of their last legall Settlemt

Yor. Petrs: think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray that yor. Worshps. will be pleased to appoint a day of hear & determine the same

Dan Bolton Agent Appt

And Yor. Petr: Shall ever pray Etc
