To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble petition of Benjamin West

Sheweth That your petitioner on or about the 27th day of March 1751 as bound [...] an Apprentice to Thomas George of Cow Cross Cordwainer For the Term of Seven years

That your said petitioner master has obsconded for fear of being Arrested and has left your said Petitioner destitute of all Common Necessaries

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to take his Case into ye Consideration and for the reasons aforesd [...] make such order for the discharge of your petitioner from his sd Apprenticeship as to your Worships shall seem meet and your petitioner as in duty bound shall ever pray

Benj: West
