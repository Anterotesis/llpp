To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The Humble peticon of Thomas Martin one of the petty Constables of ye parish of South mimms in this County

Sheweth That at Christmas last past there was due and owing to yor. petr. the sume of ten pounds for one qrters Salary for his loss of time and Expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants from the further most part of the sd parish of Southmims through this County

Yor. Petr. therefore hmbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymts. of the sd Sume of ten pounds to yor. sd petr.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Thomas Martin
