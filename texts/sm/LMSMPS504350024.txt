To the Worshippfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of Barny Murphy a poor Prisoner

Sheweth. That your Petitioner being a Prosecutor upon a Bill of Indictment Preferred and found some Anne since against Patrick and James O Haro in this honoble Court for a Assault the same came on to be Tryed last Sessions and the Defendants were Acquitted thereon and your Petitioner was upon the said Tryal taken into Custody and Committed to New Prison upon Suspicion of being Guilty of Perjury on giving his Evidence there in.

That the Defendants have this Sessions Preferred and found an Indictment against your Petitioner for the above Offence and Several of your Petitioners Material Witnesses, (without whose Testimony he cannot Safeley Proceed to the Tryal of the said Indictment) being at Present act to be met with

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be Pleased to Deferr the Tryal of the said Indictment untill the next Sessions and that your Petitioner may in the mean time be Admitted to Bail

And your Petitioner (as in duty bound) shall ever pray Etc

Barny Murphy.
