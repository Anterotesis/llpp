To the Right Worshiptull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of [...] the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant on Order under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of February One Thousand Seven Hundred and thirty five James Willson and Ellinor his wife and their Four Children Martha aged about Seven years James aged about Six years Samuel aged about Three years and Mary William aged about Seventeen Months were Removed from the Hamlet of Ratcliff in this County to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved [...] Court against the Same

And pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day this present Sessions that all Parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court for and touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall pray Etc.

Benj: Bass Agent for the Appellants
