To the Right Worshpll: her Maties Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon of Thom Hinton of the parish of St: Andrews Holborne in the Division above barrs

Sheweth: That your peticon is but in mean Circumstances & rents but a house & Stables [...] of Forty pounds a yeare and is charged Eight Shillings a quarter to ye poores Rate when Severall of his Neighbours of good ability rent houses of Eighty and a hundred pounds a year are Charged but Six Shillings a quarter

Wherefore your Petitionr prayes that this Honble: Bench will relieve him the prmisses And your Pet shall ever pray

Thomas Hinton
