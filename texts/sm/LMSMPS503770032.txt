To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace held in and for the said County

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Stenton

Sheweth That by Indenture dated the Thirteenth day of September which was in the Year of our Lord One thousand Seven hundred and forty Four the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London (with the Consent of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City)Did put of [...] your Petitioner Apprentice to Susanna Wood of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the county of Middlesex to Serve the said Susanna Wood till She the said Elizabeth Stenton Should Attain her Age of Twenty one Years

That your said Petitioners said Mistress has most Gruethey Beaten and Abused your Petr at divers times without any stroncation and about Two Months ago your Petr Mistress Struck her with an Iron Spindle and Dislocated your Petr Clbow so that your Petr must have lost the Use of her right hand had it not been [...] for the great Care of the Surgeon of the London Infirmary

That your Petr verily believes that if the Shod Continual with her said Mistress the is so very Passionate and Cruel she will one daly kill or Lane your Petitioner

That your Petr said Mistress is now Married to one Mr. Samuel Bates and he and Your Petr said Mistress have Now Agreed that your Petr may be Discharged from the said Indenture

Therefore your Petr most humbly prays your Worshipp [...] will be pleased to Discharge your Petr from her said [...] Apprentiship

And Your Petr shall ever Pray etc

her Elizabeth [x] Stenton mark
