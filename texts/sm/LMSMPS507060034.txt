To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace at Hick's Hall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St: Anthony oulgarly called St. Antholin's London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Kynaston and Peter Planck Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing date the Twentieth day of October 1769 John Lillebon and Beatrice his Wife were Removed from the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields within the said Liberty of Westminster to the said parish of St: Anthony as the place of their And legal settlement, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore do humbly appeal to this Court against the same and do Pray that your Worships will appoint same day in this present Sessions for hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petr. will pray Etc.

I Nix Soll: for the Appll.
