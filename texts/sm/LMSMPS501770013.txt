To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx:

The Humble Peticon of Richard Rush late petty Constable of the parish of Southmims in the sd County

Sheweth That there is due and owing unto yor. said Peticonr. the Sume of Twenty pounds for halfe a Yeares Service (as a Salary) for his Loss of time, Charges and Expences in Releiving passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County being due at Lady Day last past

And that Yor. Peticonr. is now Discharged from the sd Office of a Constable for the sd parish of Southmims

Yor. Peticonr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble: Court would be pleased to Order the Payment of the sd Sume of Twenty pounds unto yor. said Peticonr: for his said Service

And Yor. Peticr: shall ever pray Etc.

Richd: Rush
