To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace by Adjournment Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of J. Briscoe and Henry Trent Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th. Day of March 1754 Elizabeth Isom and her two Children (to wit) Francis about Seven years old and Andrew about three years old were Removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Thos: Huddle Peter Holmes} Churchwardens

Wm Whitney R Briand} Overseers of the Poor
