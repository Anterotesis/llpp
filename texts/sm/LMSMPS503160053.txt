To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and General Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Rt. Honble. the Lord Carpenter and Richard Whitworth Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 28th. Day of February 1735 John Barnet Ruth his wife And Elizabeth aged Seven years and Mary aged two years their Children were removed from the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster to the Said Parish of St. James as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners thinks themselves Aggrieved

And therefore humbly Prays that this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a time for Hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Saml. Seddon Vestry Cl
