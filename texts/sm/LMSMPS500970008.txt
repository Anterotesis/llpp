To the Right Worsph. her Matye Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of John Butt of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in the Said County.

Sheweth That about four years past one James Gale did put him selfe Apprntice to your Peticoner for the term of Seven years comencing from the date of his Indenture of Apprenticeship As thereby may appeare. That the Said Apprentice hath frequently and by lawfully absented himselfe from his Said Service and hath divers times fraudulently taken away or Stolne your Petrs. goods and in also guilty of divers other crimes & misdemeanors which your Petr. is ready to make appeare to this Honble Court.
