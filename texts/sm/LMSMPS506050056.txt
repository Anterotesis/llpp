To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sesdsions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George the Martyr Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of James Fielding Esquire and Thomas Edwards Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex One of the Quorum bearing Date the Sixth day of October last Mary Ellis Wife of Robert. Ellis then gone from her and their Child to wit Hannah aged Six Months was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr Southwark in the said County of Surrey as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved And against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the matter Touching the said Appeal And Your Petitioner shall pray & C

J Preston Attorney for the Appellants 3d. Decr. 1770
