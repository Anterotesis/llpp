To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Ovseers of the poor of the parish of Chelsea in the said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of daniel Combe and William Gore Esqrs Two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) Margarett Cunningham Aged about Sixty yeares widow of John Cunningham deceased was removed from the parish of St Martin in the Fields in this County to the said parish of Chelsea as the Place of her last Legell Settlement.

That your Peticoners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices and Appeal against the same.

Your petitrs therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day this Sessions for all partys to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc
