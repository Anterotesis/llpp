To the Worshipfull William, Mainwaring Esqr and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Olave Silver Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal bearing date the 25th day of October 1794 under the hands and Seals of R. Davies Esqr, and H. Reynett Esqr. [...] two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex John Holbird his Wife Elizabeth and their two Children namely Elizabeth aged five Years and John aged two Years were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the sd. County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of St. Olave Silver Street as their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal agt the sd. Order of Removal and pray Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the present Session for the hearing and determining thereof

And your Petitioners will ever Pray

W. Berridge 99 Hatter Garden Sollr for the Appellants
