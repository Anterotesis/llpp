To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hick's Hall in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All hallows the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of William Blackborow and I Girdler Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fourth Day of September last Esther Layton and Sarah her Daughter were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows the Great as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Session of the peace to be holden for this County

And Your Petitrs shall pray Etc

Tho Thomas Wm: Dotterill Edwd. Wager R. Owen} Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of All hallows the Great
