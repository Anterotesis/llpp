To the Rt: Worpll: his Majts: Justices of the Peace at their Genll: Quartr: Session for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands & Seals - of John Milner & William Presthey Esqrs: two of his Majts: Justices of the peace of the said County, bearing date the 23d: day of March last, the Lady Ann Read widow of Sr: Wm Read Knight deced was removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County, unto yor: Petr said parish, to be by yor: Petrs: releived. or: whereby yor: Petrs humbly think they are aggrieved

And therefore humbly appear to yor: Worps agst: the said order; And pray yor: Worps to adjoyine the said Appeal to any day the next Session, or to appoint some day this prsent Session to hear & determine the same

And yor: petr shall ever pry Etc
