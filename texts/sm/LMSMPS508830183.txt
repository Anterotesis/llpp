To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant unde the Hands and Seals of Thomas Collins and Richard Paul Joddiell Esqr two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting and for the County of Middlesex (where of one is of he Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty third day of February One Thousand seven Hundred and Ninety three, Charlotte Thomas Single Woman was convayed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex and delivered to your Petitioners the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Fulham in the County of Middlesex the said Justices adjudging the lawful settlement of the said Charlotte Thomas to be in the said Parish of Fulham whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved

Batsford Solr. for the Appellants} Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that you Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in the present Session for the hearing and determination thereof.

Middlesex At the [---] General Quarter Session of the Peace of our of Sovereign Lord the King holden in the and for [...] of Middlesex County at the Session House Clerkenwell Green[---] by adjournment on Tuesday the Ninth day of April in the Thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third King of Great Britain Etc.

It is Ordered that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Saint of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Persons concerned Do attend this Court at the Session House for the said County on Thursday the [---] Eleventh day of April instant at Nine of the Clock in the Morning to [...] hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal
