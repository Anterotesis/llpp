To the Worshifull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of Ann Delamar an Inhabitant in the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middx. Widow.

Sheweth That your Petitioner is assessed in a Taxation made on the Several Inhabitants of the said parish in the Sume of Six Shillings [...] towards the relief of the poor of the said parish.

That your Petr. is in very Low Circumstances [...] and reduced having no business to maintain her and is not able to pay the said rate.

Therefore your Petr. humbly apprehending herself agrieved by the said Taxation humbly Appeals to this honoble Court and that She may be Discharged from the said Tax or otherwise relieved As to this court shall seem meet.

Ann De Lamar

And Etc
