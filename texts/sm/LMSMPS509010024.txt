To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembeld

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woodford in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of R. Davies and H. Reynett two of his Majesty's of Justices the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second Day of July last Mary Partrick Widow and her two lawful Children (Viz) Ann aged nine years and upwards and Celia aged two Years or thereabout were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Mile end New Town in the Parish of St. Dunstans Stepney in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Woodford as the place of this last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners are as they Conceived aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Henry Coole Church Warden

T Daire Sollr.for the Appellant

Overseers} G. Van Voorst Charles Pratt
