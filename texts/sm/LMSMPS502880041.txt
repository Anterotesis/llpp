To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph Without aldgate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order bearing date the Eleventh day of December last under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor uns) Mary DrinkWater widow of Georgy Drinkwater and Hannah their Child aged about two Years and three Quarters was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County to the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Peticoners think themselves agrived by the warrant or order of the said two Justices and Humbly appeals against the same

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray you would be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. [...] Giles in the fields and all other Persons Concerned to attend this Court Some day this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the premises and that your Peticoners may have such Releife therein as to Justice and Law doth appartain

And Ye Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc.
