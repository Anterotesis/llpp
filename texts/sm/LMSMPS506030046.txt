To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden by Adjournment at Hickss - Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Beddington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That Thomas Kynaston and Peter Planck Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid upon Complaint made unto them by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex That Elizabeth Griffis Wife of Edward Griffis (gone from her) and their two Children to wit Elizabeth Aged two Years and upwards and John Aged about two Months lately Intruded and came into the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields and were become Chargeable to the same Did by an Order or Warrant of Removal under their Hands and Seals bearing Date the Twenty first Day of February last Past Order the said Elizabeth Griffis and two Children to be removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields to the said Parish of Beddington as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

That the said Elizabeth Griffis and two Children by Virtue of the said Order have been removed and Conveyed from the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields to the said Parish of Beddington whereby they now remain Chargeable to the same Parish

Your Petitioners Apprehending themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal made by the said two Justices as aforesaid do therefore humbly Appeal Against the same

John Brewster Sollicitor for the Parish of Beddington
