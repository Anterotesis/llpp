To the Right Worshipful His Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled.

The humble Petition of John Turner

Sheweth. That Your Petitioner hath been brought up in the Business of a Watchmaker,

That he hath a Wife and several Children to Provide for

That having heard the Place on Employmt. of Governor or Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell is vacant by the death of William Jones late yor or Keeper thereof Humbly hopes he shall be found Qualified for that Employmt. And that he can have such Recomendations as shall be approved by this Honble. Court.

Your Petitioner therefore prays to be admitted to succeed the said William Jones humbly Promiseing to behave himself with diligence and fidelity in the performance of the duties of the said Office

And your Petitioner as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.

Jno. Turner
