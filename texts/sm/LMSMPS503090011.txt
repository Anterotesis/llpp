To the Rt. Worpl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the Peace for the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Saint Owen

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Wm Booth & Anthony Wroth Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peacefor the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of September last your Petitioner was adjudged to be the putative Father of a Female Bastard Child born of the body of Mary Walkwood in the Parish of St Luke in the sd County of Middx in which Order your Petr. is ordered & directed to pay to the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish the sum of forty five shillings for the first four weeks after the birth of the said Female Bastard Child and for the charge of her Lying in and also two shillings & six pence weekly for the purposes mentioned & contained in the said order, That your Petr was not served with a Copy of the said Order till on or about the 22d [...] day of April last and not before

Your Petr. conceiving himself aggreived by the Order & Adjudication of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief against the same

And Yours Petrs Shall [...]

[---] Cop: of appeale of Samuel Owen agt. an Order of Adjudication in case of bastardy

Mr. Benne for parish of saint Luke App: dismissed Order confirmed Appellt. called By default

5s.
