Middx to Wit To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Generall Quarter Session of the peace held at Hicks Hall for the sd. County Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers & Parishoners of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order bearing Date the 27th day of August last Signed & Sealed by Ralph Harwood & Jos: goddard two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County one of which is of the Quorum Mary Powe Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of St. Leonar [...] Shoreditch to the sd Parish of st. Dunstan in the West [...] which Order your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

Your. Petrs. therefore humbly Prays your Worships will be pleased to Order a Short das for all party's to Attend this Court & Abide by the Judgment & Order of Your Worships touching their Sd. Appeal

Richd Berry Agent for the Appellts.

And they Shall Pray Etc.
