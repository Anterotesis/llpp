To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembed

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Edmonton in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and Charles Friquet Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) [...] date the 2d. day of October last Susanna Parker the Wife of [...] was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Enfield in the said County to the sd. Parish of Edmonton as the place of her [...] legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved [...] appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing & determining the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may then attend

Richd. Hussey & J. Hough Sollrs for the Appeal }
