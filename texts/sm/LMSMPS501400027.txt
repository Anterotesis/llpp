At Worsppll her Matys Justices of the Peace For the County Middx.

The Humble peticon of John Carter Beadle of the parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

Sheweth

That there is Due [...] for passing and wherein of Vagrants the Sum of Nine and Twenty Shillings & Six Pence As appeares by Three Certificates under the Hands & Seales of Robert Dennett Esqr: one of her Maties Justices of the peace of this County here unto Annexed That your Petitr. stands in great want of the And Money

Therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps will be pleased to order yt he may be find the sd Moneys An unto q [...] Worspps shall Seeme Meete

And yor Petitr shall Ever pray Etc

John Carter
