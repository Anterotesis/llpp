To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, in their General Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael, Bassishaw, in Ye City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Warrant under Ye Hands and Seals of William Mantle and William Timbrell, Esqrs, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for ye. City and Liberty of Westminster, in Ye County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date ye. Thirteenth Day of July last past, Elizabeth Rawlings, Widow, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. George, Hanover Square, within ye. Liberty of Westminster, in the County of Middlesex, unto the Said Parish of St. Michael, Bassishaw, in ye City of London, alledging ye, Said Parish of St. Michael, Bassishaw, to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge. Pollr. ye. Appellts } That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrantor Order of ye. Said Two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom, And humbly Pray, that the Benefit of this Appeal be saved unto them, and ye. hearing and Determining thereof, be Adjourned until ye. next General Quarter Session of ye. Peace to be ho holden for ye. Said County of Middlesex; and that the Churchwardns and Overseers of Ye Poor of the Said Parish of St. George, Hanover Square, may appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide Ye Indgment and Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc.
