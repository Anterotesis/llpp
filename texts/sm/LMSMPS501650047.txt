To Ye. Rt. Worshipful his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx I Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of Henry Lee in the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Yor Peticon is rated to the poors rate of the said Parish for Ten pounds pr. Ann for the house he lives in altho': he Rents but Eight pounds pr. Ann

Yor. peticon therefore thinks himself agreeved by the Said Assessment and Appeales against the Same

Yor. peticon therefore humbly pays for Such releife as Your Worshipps shall See meet

And Yor. peticon shall ever pray Etc.
