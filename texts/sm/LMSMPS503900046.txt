To the right worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition and appeal of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Paul Covent garden in the Liberty of Westmister and County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an order of pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Edward Ashe and James Fraser Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (whereof one is of ye Quorum) bearing date the twenty first day of November in the year of our Lord 1748. Elizabeth Poole (by the name and additi [...] of Elizabeth Poole an Infant aged one Year and upwards Daughter of William Poole by Sarah his Wife) was removed from ye. parish of St. Martin in the Fields With in the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid into the Parish of St. Paul Covent garden as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

That your Petitionrs. conceiving themselves aggrievd. by the order of the sd. two Justices Do therefore appeal to your Worshipps therefore and pray yt you will be pleased to appoint a day in this Pr.sent Sessions to hear and determine [...] the sd. Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

J Spranger Att. for the Appell.
