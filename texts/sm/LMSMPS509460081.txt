To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace holden for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of Thomas Rumball being an inhabitant within the Parish of the Holy Trinity Minories in the said County of Middlesex and rated towards the relief of the Poor there.

Sheweth That on or about the twenty fifth day of May last a Rate or assessment was made and allowed for the relief of the Poor within the said Parish for which your Petitioner was assessed at the Sum of Three hundred pounds being an Inhabitant of that Parish.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly appeals against the said [---] Rate or assessment and prays that the same may be quashed or that your Petitioner may have such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet and that your Worships with appoint a day in this present session to hear and determine this his Petition and appeal.
