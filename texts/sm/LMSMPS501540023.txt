July 7-1716

May it plese this Honble Corte

Whereas wee our Names under written stands bound to this Corte for yt apeereance of Wm Cox & ye said Wm Cox is in Corte [...] Submittant

Wee Humbly pray this Honble Corte that we may be discharged & we shall ever pray

John Green Edward Downes

And also: Capn Pottinger & Mr Edwd Dounds prays ye same [...] in behalfe of Neamiah Coggs submittant

Edward Cownes
