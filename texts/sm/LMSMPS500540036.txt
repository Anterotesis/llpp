To the Honoble: Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall.

Sheweth

The humble peticon of Nathaniel Hancock

That your unfortunate Peticoner being convicted for uttering a Guilt Shilling for an Elizabeth peece of broad-Gold, and a Fine of fourty pounds sett upon him by your Honours, your Peticoners present Circumstances being such, that if the said Fine be not Mittigated, it will be the utter Rume and distraction of your Peticoner and his Family, wherefore your Peticoner.

Wm: Leche Scripsit

Most humbly Prays that your Honours will be pleased to Mittigate the same

And your Peticoner shall ever pray Etc

Nathaniel Hancock
