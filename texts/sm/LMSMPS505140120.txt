To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, in their General Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St; Mary Mounthaw, in ye. City of London.

Sheweth; That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of Ebenezer Mussell & Robert Darling, Esqrs, Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace [...] (whereof One is of ye. Quorum) for ye. County of Middlesex, bearing Date ye. Tenth Day of August now last past, Sarah Burridge, Singlewoman, was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Matthew, Bethnal-Green, in ye. County of Middlesex, unto ye. Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw, in ye. City of London, aforesaid alledging ye. said Parish of St. Mary Mounthaw, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge sallr. for ye. Appellts. } That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of ye. said two Justices. Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom, and humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Promisses, and Order, that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of St. Matthew, Bethnal-Green, may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc.
