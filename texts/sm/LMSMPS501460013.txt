To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon & Appeale of Felix Peast Esqr of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petr rents a house and brewhouse in the said Parish And is rated towards the releife of the poor of the said parish £3 P Quarter And that your petr poays but £51. [...] Pound and is charged after the rate of £80: P and to the sd poore Rate: Therefore your petr conceive himselfe to be evercharged in the said Rate And appeales agt the Same.

Your petr therefore prays your Worps that he may be releived in the Prmies as your Worps shall Seem meet.

And he Shall pray etc.

Felix Feast Esqr. Appeale agt the poore Rate of Cripplegate Middx over charged

1715 refr to Mr. Brown Mr. Fuller Mr. Crosbie or 2

to ex & cert Present John Fuller & Richd. Brown Esqr:

to be Reduced from £80 to £60 Pr. Ann.
