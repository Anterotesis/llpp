To the Rt: Worshipl: His Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midx in Quarter Sessions Assembled at Hicks Hall

The humble Peticon of Giles Quinny

Sheweth That upon the death of Captn: Bowrman the Governors place of the House of Correction for the County of Midx is vacant.

That Yor: Petr: hath serv'd the Rt: Honoble the Earl of Clare (and his Father) about sixteen Years, in which service he has always behaved himself very faithfully, as by His Lordship Letter will appear:

Yor: Petr: humbly Prays That Yor: Worships will be pleasd to Elect Yor: Petr: into the said Place of Governor: Yor. Petr: having some Substance, that if he should dye or be remov'd from the said Place, he shall leave enough to maintain his Family, without their being chargeable to the County.

And Yor Petr shall ever pray Etc.
