To the Right Worll. Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Peynard

Sheweth

That in the month of [...] November 1706 your Peticoners was put forth Apprentice by Indenture for the terme of Seven Years to Thomas Vincent of the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in the Said County Baker to learn his Art

That your petr Said Master hath often times gave him Imoderate correction & refuseth to teach him his Trade but Sends him out often to drive plow & cart for wch: complaint hath been made to the Right Worll: Sd Thomas Franklin Bart one of Her Matice: Justices of the Peace for the Said County who upon hearing of the matters bound your Petr said Master to appear in this Court to answer yor Peticonr: Complaint

P J Tyton [...] Your Peticonr: humbly Prayes (the prmises considered) that this Court will be pleased to Order his Master to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to Shew cause why he Should not be discharged of His Indenture of Apprenticehood & placed wth Some other [...] person useing the Same Trade to Serve the residue of the said Term

And your Peticonr: as in duly bound shall en pray Etc.
