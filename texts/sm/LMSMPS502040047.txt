To the Right Worshipful his Majties: Justices of the Peace of the county of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of John Hatchett of the Parish of Bedfont in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is One of the High Conbles : of the Hundred of Spelthorne in this County And hath Contained in his sd Office Upwards of Seven years, But being now very Lame and Infirme is Unable to Execate and Performe the said Office of High Conble

That the Persons Under menconed are Substantial House holders within the said Hundred, and Fifty Qualified to serve the said Office of High Constable.

Your Peticoner therefore Humble Beggs this Court wll be Pleased to Discharged him From his sd. Office of High Constable, And appoint some other Person in his Stead.

And Yor: Peticoner shall doe Prays John Hatchett

Thomas Rolfe of Feltham John Ride of Feltham: Nicholas Sherman of Hampton Court
