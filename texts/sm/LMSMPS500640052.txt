To the Rt. Worsps. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of and appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseer of the poore of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell the sd County

Sheweth

That in Sometime past Jane Williams Joseph Williams and Thomas Williams Three Children of Robert Williams and Amey his wife were by warrant under the Hands and Seales of Two of his matys Justices of the Peace for this County passed [...] from the Precinct of St. Katherines in the sd. County to the sd. pish of St. Paul Shadwell.

That the sd. Robert Williams was an Apprntice to Mr. Wm Rockett a Cordweyner in the sd. pish of St. Paul Shadwell as the sd. rented Warrant mencons but the sd. Three Children were not borne in the sd. pish they being the Children of the sd. Amey Williams before her Intermarriage with the sd. Robert Williams.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray your Worspps to be pleased to Order that the Ovrseers of the Poore of the sd. Precinct of St Katherines may appeared in this honble Cort this prsent Sessions to Shew cause why the sd Three Children should not be repassed to the sd prcinct

And Yr. Pr. Shall evr pray Etc

Thomas Tatnell John Dew } Overseers of the poore
