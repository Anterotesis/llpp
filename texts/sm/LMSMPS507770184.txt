To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace of the Session House Clerkenwell Green in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Upwell in the County of Norfolk.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of J: Slade and Henry Lough Esquires two of it is [...] Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing dale the fifteenth day of November last Mary Middlesex and three of her Children Vizt. Susannah Aged about five Years Edward aged about four Years and William Aged about three Years were removed and conveyed from the Hamlet of Hammer smith in the Parish of Fulham on the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Given at the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Said Order and pray that your Worships will Please to Appoint some Day in this Present Sessions for the Hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned do then attend this Court.

And your Petitioners will pray Etc Bontflower & Phillip son Agents for the said Church Wardens said Overseer of Upwell
