To the Worshipful Bench of Justices, for ye county of Middle, The humble petition of John Anderson

Sheweth;

That yr: petitioner is a very poor man, and have been confind in Newprison and Clarkenwell Bridwell, upwards of five Months, and now lies for fees, and in a miserable starving condition , having no friends in ye world to come near yr Petitioner

Your Petitioner most humbly Prays your Worships to be so pleasd out of ye: Innate Goodness, to Order yr -petitioner, to be set at liberty, without fee, or Else he must inevitably perish, except releif
