To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of Thomas Spratley of the Parish of Hornsey in this County

Sheweth

That your Petr. is charged to the Poors Rate for His House and Lands in the said Parish of Hornsey much more Than what He Humbly Conceives he ought to pay, and therefore Appeals Against the same.

May it therefore please your Worshipps to make Such Order for your Petr. relief in the Premisses, as to this Court shall seem meet.

And your Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc

Thos: Sprattley
