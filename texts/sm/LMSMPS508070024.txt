To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows on the Walls in the City of London.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir Sampson Wright and Wm. Addington Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th day of December part Catharine Need Singlewoman was Removed & Conveyed from & out of the Parish of Finchley in the sd. County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of Allhallows on the Walls in the sd. City of London as the place of her last legal at Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Agreived by the said Order a Pass Warrant of the sd. two Justices Do Humbly appeal against the same and pray that the hearing and determining there of may be adjourned to the next General Sessions of the Peace to be holden in & for the sd. County of Middx & that the Court will be pleased to appoint a day in the sd. next Sessions for the Churchwardn. & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of Finchley & all other persons concerned therein to Attend to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of the then Court touching the sd. Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
