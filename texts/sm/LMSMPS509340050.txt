To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at The Session House on Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies Esquires and the Reverend Henry Reynell Doctor in Diomity Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty third day of December new last past Henry Bridges an Infant aged One Year or there about Son of Henry Bridges was Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Ratcliff as the lawful Settlement of him the said Henry Bridges thereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

And therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudiction of the said Two Justices and the said Order And Pray That Your Worships be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determination of the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George and all Persons Concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

H. Parnell} Sollicitor for the Appellants
