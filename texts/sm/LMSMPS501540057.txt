To the Rt Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx now assembled at Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon & Appeale of Robt Jollimay of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the sd County

Sheweth That Yr Peticoner having a Wife & 2 Small Children to maintain and his trade decaying is under very mean circumstance being scarce able to maintain his charge That Yr peticoner being made chargeable to pay to the poors rate and altogether unable to do the Same is much aggrei [...] thereal [...]

Your Peticoner therefore humbly beggs to be releived herein as to this honble. Bench shall seem meet

And he shall Ever pray Etc.
