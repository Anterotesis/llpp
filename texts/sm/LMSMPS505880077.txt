To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of her Churchwardns and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of Stephen Clarke and I: Girdler Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the day of July 1768, Sarah Edsow Widow was Removed from the Liberty of Safforn Hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County of middlesex to the Parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate London as the peace of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner Conceive themselves aggrived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day of this Present Sessions that all parties may attaned to hear and above the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petr shall Ever Pray Etc

Willm Watts Jos: Nicholls} Churchwardens

Danl. Crockett Saml Smith} Overseers
