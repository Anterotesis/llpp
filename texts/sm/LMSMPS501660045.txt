To the Right Worpll. his [...] Peace for the County of Middx assembled [...] their Generall Sessions

The Humble Peticon to Appeal of the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the sd. County of [...] Middx.

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of John Fuller Alexander Ward Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middx (Quor unus) Elizabeth the wife of Humphrey Hamilton and her three children (vizt) Mary aged about thirteen yeares, William aged about Seven yeares, & Jane aged One year to upwards were by the sd Order (bearing Date the fifteenth day of Novembr. last past) removed for the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt [...] of the sd Justices humbly appeale there from & pray that [...] this Court will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James Clerkenwell to attend this Court some Day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
