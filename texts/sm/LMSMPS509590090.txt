To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of Paul Woolley of Fulwoods Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex Victualler

Sheweth

That Your Petitioner was convicted on the Sixteenth day of February last by John Flood Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County in the Penalty of Twenty Pounds for keeping and detaining a Stolen Dog by which Correction Your Petitioner is aggrieved and begs leave to appeal against the same

Your Petitioner therefore prays that Your Worships will appoint some Day in the present Session to hear and determine the said appeal.

And your Petitioner will ever pray
