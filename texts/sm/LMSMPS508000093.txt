To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Antholin in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of June Instant Mary Willman Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Autholin as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Judgement the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to this Court for such Relief in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
