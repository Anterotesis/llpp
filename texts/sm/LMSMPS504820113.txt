To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace of ye County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessn. of ye Peace held in & for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a pass or Order of Removal under the hands & Seals of Benjamin Cox Thos. Kynaston Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of ye peace of the City and Liberty of Westmr in ye. County aforesd (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth Day of December last Susannah Hester Gallindo Widow was Removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said Liberty & County to the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel as ye Place of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Apprehending themselves to be Aggrieved by the aforesaid pass or Order of Remo [...] of the said two Justices Do Appeal to this Court again ye same

And therefore Your Petrs. & Appellts. Pray that the Court will Appoint a Day this present Session for ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the said Parish of St. Martin and all Persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And that Your Petrs. & Appellts. may be Relieved in the Premisses as to this Court Shall Seem meet

And Your Petrs. & Appellts. will ever pray Etc.

Janry ye. 15th. 1760

Jno. Billington Sollr for Appellts.
