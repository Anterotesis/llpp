To the Worshippfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church warden [...] and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant a Order under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Edmund Byron Esqrs. two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing [...] the Eighteenth day of June last Past Susannah Andery Singlewoman was Removed from and out of [...] of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County aforesaid [...] said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the Peace of [...] last legall Settlement

The Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the [...] Pass Warrant or Order and therefore humbly Appea [...] against the Same and Pray this Court to Grant the [...] Relief in the Premisses as to your Worshipps [...] Meet

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

G. Talmash. Attorney for the Appellants
