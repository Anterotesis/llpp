Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Jenkin Jones Esquire And the Reverend [...] George Booth Baronet two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County Court whereof being of the Quorum I bearing datd the thirtieth day of April last Sarah Ayres widow of Jos [...] Ayres deceased and her five a Children Namely [...] aged about Ten Years Sarah aged about Nine years Thomas aged about Six Years, Jane aged about three years and William aged about Six Months, [...] removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luther in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Sarah Ayres widow and her said Five Children, By which your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved
