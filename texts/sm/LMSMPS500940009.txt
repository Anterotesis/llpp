To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hickss Hall.

The Humble peticon of Thomas Turner

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr is Bayle for the personall appear [...] of Henry Bening of Branford this prsent Sessions & that the wife of the Said Bening now lies Inn, & in a dangerous condicon & he haveing noe person to look after his Shop & other concernes

Therefore yor. Peticoner Humbley prayes yor. Worships to order the sd. Recognizence to be respited till next Sessions and yor. peticoner shall ever pray
