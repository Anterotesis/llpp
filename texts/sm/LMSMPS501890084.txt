To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of Mary Tough of the Precinct of St Katherines in the said County Middx

Sheweth That ye. Petitioner is rated Forty Shilling P Annum towards the releife of the Poor of the said Precinct That Severall of her neighbours who pays much Larger rents are not so high rated in the poor book That she Conceives her self to be very much agrieved and humbly Appeals against the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays she may be relieved in ye Premisses in Such Manner as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc.

Mary Tough
