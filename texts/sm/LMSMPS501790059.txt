To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph Aldersgate London without.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Sr Edward Gould Knt John Metcalfe Esqr. two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor un) Dated the Twenty Third day of May last Mathew Jackman a Young Child aged Eight Years was removed from the Parish of st Andrew Holbourne for the Liberty above the barrs in this County to the said parish of St Botolph Aldersgate as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the sd Mathew Jackman That your Petr: Conceive themselves aggrieved. by the Sd Order and appeal agst the Same.

Yor. Petr: therefore humbly Pray this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a Day in this Sessions for all parties to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs: Shall Ever pray Etc.
