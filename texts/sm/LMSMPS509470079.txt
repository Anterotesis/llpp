To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth,

That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Moser and H. Cosser, Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City and Liberty aforesaid bearing Date the 30th day of December 1797 Jane Forrett, Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray your Worships that the Appeal may be saved unto them and that you will be [...] to appoint a Day to hear and determine [...] said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

W. Lemage Solicitor for the Appellants
