To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of under the hands and Seals of the Worshipful W, Blackborows and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of December 1779 William Hunt was removed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be appoint a Day in the Present Session for the hearing and [...] determining of the said Appeal And that the persons concerned do then directed to attend

And your Petrs. shall ever Prsy Etc

J Lane Soll. for the Appellrs.
