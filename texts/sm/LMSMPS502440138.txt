To the Rt: Worshful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Genl. Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble petion of Peter Brabant John Brabant & Joseph Anthony Crump

Sheweth That or the Eleventh Day of July Instant your Petionrs. were by Justice Ward Comitted to New Prison for an Assault upon Robt. Tulley & others Since which Time has made Sattisfaction and a General Releafe Signd to them

Your Petionrs. therefore most humbly pray your Worsps would be pleased to Order they may be brought to this North Court and Discharged

And your Petionrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc

Peter Brabant John Brabant Joseph Anthony Crump
