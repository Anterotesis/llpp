To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Baker

Sheweth That yor. Petr. did Indict one Matthew Studbridge. last January for an Assault and Battery and upon hearing the Cause yor. Worspps were then pleased to fine him Ten pounds and that he should give yor. petr. satisfaccon Likewise And upon Now payment of his Sd fine yor. Worspps were pleased to Comitt him to Newprison

That yor. Petr. this day went to the Sd Gaol to ask for the Sd Studbridge (not knowing but that he was there in Custody and was told by one Worrall belonging to Newprison that the Sd Studbridge was lately discharged out of the Sd prison and that he had in his Custody the Sd Fine of Ten pounds & told yor. petr. that yor. worspps must do as you thought fit with it.

That Yor. Petr. was assault very greiviously insomuch that he never wilbe his one Man Therefore prays yor. Worspps to pleased to order that he may be releived in the Sd prmisses As to have satisfaccon for so barbaious a fact

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.
