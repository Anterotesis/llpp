To the Right Honble: the Lord Mayr: and to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Bench

The humble Peticonr of Alexius Shad a Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth

That your peticonr: is confused for High Treason in counterfeting the warrant come of this Kingdome And your peticonr: being altogether Ignorant of the Same And haveing his witnesses Ready to prove his innocency

Most humbly prayer that he may be tryed att this Prsent Sessions and that his prayer may be [...] accordingly As to your Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc

And your peticonr: Shall Ever pray Etc

Alexias Shhadd
