To the Rt: Worshipl: his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genoral Sessions of the peace assembled:

Sh [...] [...] land

Humbly Sheweth That Yor: Peticoner in or about the Month of October 1728. took into her Service One Sarah Lycence a poor Girl of the parish of St. Martins in the Fields in the County of Middx who was then turned over to See from Anne wick to serve the remainder of her apprenticeship with her, but the Indenture & assignmt. of [...] to your peticoner is since lost or mislaid

That since that time her said apprentice has several times absconded from her Service the last time for near seven Months, & pisred from her Sundry goods & never Comes home again [...] into her Service until she is almost Naked

She therefore most humbly prayes this Honble Court that the [...] any further Care. of the said apprentice for that there is not fill to be Intrusted N: her Hosue or such other Relief as to yor: Worshipps shall seem meet

And yr. Peticoner as a Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc

Eliz: Cleaveland
