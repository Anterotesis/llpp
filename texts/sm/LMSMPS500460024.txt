To the Pt: Worpll: his Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx assembled in open Sessions

The humble Sessions of Ann Capon

humbly Sheweth That ye Petr. was here att 3s=10d P Annu to Mrs Frances Phillipps with whom [...] bt near halfe a year & [...] e a ye petr demanded some wages of her Sd Mistress She instead of payeing ye Petr. [...] er wages, beat ye Petr. and turned her away without giveing any satisfacton & withelds 34s. which are due for wages

Yor. Petr. the Mr. fond humble prays the wife of this Worpll Bench for obtaning len Sd Mayes

And Petr. Shall pray Etc.
