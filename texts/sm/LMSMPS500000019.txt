St Worpll. the Justices of Peace for the County

The humble Peticon of Thomas Jones keeper of New person and Bridewell

severall Breaches are made in the Tyleing [...] greater damage will happen that unto

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays this honble Bench to be pleased to Order that the said Breaches may be ve need by some of the adjacent Justices of the Peace and that the same and other defects therein may be by your Worspps ordered to be repared in each manner as unto yor. Worspps shall seem meet.
