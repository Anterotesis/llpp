To the Rt. Worl. his Majts. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. now Sitting at Hicks hall in St. John Street

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Milend Old Town in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of John Priestly Esqr. and Sr. Charles Peers Knt. two of his Majts. Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quor un) Jane Knight Widdow and one Child named Anne Knight aged abot. Six Years were removed from the Parish of Stratford Bow in the Said County to the said Hamlet of Milend old Town as the place of their last legal Settlemt. By which Order your Petrs. think themselves aggrieved and appeale agt. the same. to this Court,

And humbly pray your worships will appoint one day in next Sessions finally to hear and Determine the Same

And Yor. Petitioners shall ever pray
