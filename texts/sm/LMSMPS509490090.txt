To his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Court of General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex on behalf of themselves and others the Inhabitants of the said Parish.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of Robert Clarke and John Staples Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County bearing date the Seventh day of April 1798 Catherine Steward was Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middlesex as the place of her legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray.

Jno. Clement Sollr. for the Parish of Saint George
