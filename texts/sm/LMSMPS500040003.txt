To ye Worll. their Maties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Daniel Lowen of Endfield in this County Collr: Maker

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: hath Servd ye Office of an Headborough all ye last year; & also Supplied & ye Office of a Constable, over since ye 13s Mari [...] last in ye room of Jno: Shambrook then lately by Detd for Green Street Quarter within ye pish of Endfield aforesd. That Thomas Munns Wheelwright was. by ye Jury of ye Court Loot held for ye Munnr. of Endfield in May last, Chosen to Suneed yor Peticonr in ye Sd office of Constable for ye year ensuing, but hath hot her to refused to be Sworne, & is now ready to make his Appeal to this Court.

Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly prays. that ye said Tho. Munns may be called to shew cause wh [...] he should not undertake ye said office of a Constable And that ye Worpps: will either Order [...] him or Some of her fitt Pson to be sworn in the room of yor Peticonr. accordingly

And yor Peticonr Shall ever pray
