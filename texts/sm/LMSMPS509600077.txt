To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Kinnard and Jacob Leroux Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the third day of April now last past Edward Charington and Susanna his Wife were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County into the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Edward Charington and Susanna his Wife

That in as much as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order and the removal and conveyance of the said Edward Charrington and Susanna his Wife in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
