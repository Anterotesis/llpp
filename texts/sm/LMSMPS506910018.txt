To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Robert Slape and Daniel [---] Woodcock On the behalf of themselves others the Constables for the Liberty above Barrs in the pish of St. Andrew Holbourne

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. have pursuant to a Late Act of Parliamt. Expernden & Discharged great sumes of Money in passing of Vagrants Etc Which Moneys your Petrs. have Demanded of the High Constable who Insists that he hat be not Moneys in his Hands Sufficient to pay the same whereby your Petrs. humbly tome [...] themselves Aggreived

P Tho: Nash,

Yor. Petrs. therefore meet humbly Pray your Worspps to be pleased to Relieve them in the prmisses as unto this Honble Cort Shal be thought most meet

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc [...] Claye [...]
