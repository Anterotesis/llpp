To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Martin in the fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Stands and Seals of Rob. Pell and Geo. Garrett Esquires the of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Third day of November last Ann Clay was removed from and out of the parish of St.Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of St.Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petr. conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petr. therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said Order and pray that your Worship with appoint a day within present Session for hearing and determining this Appeal

And your Petr. will pray &

Wm Burroughs Soll.
