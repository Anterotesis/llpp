To the Worshipfull His Majties- Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble petition of John Wilkins paviour.

Sheweth.

That your petitioner did by Articles of Agreement bearing date the 16th. day of March 1746. Covenant promise & agree with several of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx in Consideration of the Yearly sum of Nine pounds to be paid unto your Petitioner, well and substantially to repair amend and maintain a certain Bridge called Brentford Bridge with all manner of needfull and necessary Reparations and Amendments when and as aften as necessary I shall be for the Tenn of Eleven Years as by the said Articles will more fully appear

And that there is now due to your petitioner the sum of Nine pounds for One Years Allowance from Michaelmas 1746 to Michaelmas 1747

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to order the payment of the said sum of Nine pounds.

And your petitioner shall ever pray Etc. Jo. [...]
