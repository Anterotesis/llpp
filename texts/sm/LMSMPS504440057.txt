To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the Hand & Seals of James Sawcer & Henry Frent Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing date the 16th. day of July 1755 Elizabeth Reiches widow of Gregory Reiches was removed from the parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court [...] will please to appoint a true for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners, shall ever pray, Etc

Tho. Morris Morgan Gwynn Wm. Bedcott } Churchwarden & Overseers
