To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midd in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clements Ipswich in the County of Suffolk.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and John Priestley Esqrs. two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Midd bearing date the Second Day of October last past Mary Bishop was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of St Paul Shadwell in the County of Midd aforesaid and on or about the Seventh day of the same Month [...] was brought into the said parish of St Clements Ipswich in the said County of Suffolk as her last legal place of Settlement

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves aggreived by the said Order of the Said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the Same and pray this Court will appoint a Day this Prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell aforesaid and all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal and yor Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc.
