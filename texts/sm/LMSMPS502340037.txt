To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Andrew Howard of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticoner is charged for the last half Year of this Present Year 1725 she sume of nineteen Shillings & Six pence towards the Releife of the Poor of the Sd Parish your Peticoner finding himelf aggrewed by she Sd Rate humbly appeals aga [...] the same.

And Prays that your Worps Woud be pleased to Order him such Releife in the Premisses as to your Worsps Gave Wisdoms shall seem meet

And yr Petr as in Duty bound shall pray Etc

Andrew Howard
