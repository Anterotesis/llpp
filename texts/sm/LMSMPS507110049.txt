To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled in

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Bread Street London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hand and Seal of John Sherwood and Wm. Blackmore Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof bring of the Quorum) bearing date the [...] Sixteenth day of December One thousand Seven hundred and Seventy eight whereby Mary Wilkinson Widow of Thomas Wilkinson together with her two Children lawfully by other by her said Husband Vizr. Sarah Aged about Ten years and an half and Elizabeth aged about Eight years and an half more removed from the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Peter le poor in Bread Street London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners concieve themselves aggrieved by the said Order and do appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a short day may be appointed this present Sessions to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
