To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Dorothy Harrison Wife of Benjamin Harrison of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your petrs. said Husband is now in his Matys Service and about three weeks aged was Draughted of and left Yor. Petr in a very poor condicon with two Children the one Sucking at her breast who are ready to perish for want

Yor: Petr therefore humbly prays she may be allowed such relief from the said parish till her said Husband returnes for the Support of herselfe & poor Children As to yor. Worshipps shall Seeme Meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

D: Harrison
