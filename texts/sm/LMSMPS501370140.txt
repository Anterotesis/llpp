The Humble Peticon of Thos: Denman agst: the poor Rate of ye: Parish of St. Gile's without Criple Gate

To the Worpll Her Majties Justice of the Peace for the County of Middx: Etc

The Humble Petition [...] & appeale Thomas Denman

Sheweth [...] our Peter: being an Inhabitant in the [...] St Gile's without Criplegate Humbly [...] over rated to the poores Rate [...] made destress of yor Peter [...]

Yor Peter: Humly: prays that [...] pray be releived, as you [...] great Wisdom shall [...] most proper

And Yor Petr: shall ever pray
