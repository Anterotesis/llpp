To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Ely- Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Jonathan Durden & Nicholas Spencer Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in for the City & Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. day of May inst. George Glibberley & Esther his Wife were Removed & Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the sd. Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents in the sd. Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the sd. County of Middlesex as the Place of the Legal Settlement of the sd. George Glibberley and Esther his Wife, whereby your Petrs. conceive- themselves [...] aggrieved, and against the sd. Order Do Appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray That the Benefit of their sd. Appeal may be Saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be Holden in & for the sd. County of Middlesex and that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some Day in the next Session for the Hearing & Determining their the Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Cox-Solr. for Appellts.
