To the worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dartford in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 19th. Day of April 1753 under the ha [...] and Seals of Thomas Lane and Francis Bedwell Esquires two Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middlesex [...] where of being of the Quorum) James [...] Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish St Dartford as the Place of the last legal Settlement of [...] James Ballenden

And your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day this Sessions for all Partys to attend to hear and abide by the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Tho: Williams Attorney for the Appellants
