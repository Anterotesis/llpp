To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish o St. Margaret in the City of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of William Pickering and Henry Trent Esq two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. Bearing Date the third day of May 1748 Henry Stowers was Removed from the Parish of St. James in the Liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners Conceives themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned may then Attend

Copy. And Yr. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Jno. Ferry Agent for St. Margt [...]
