To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon of Thomas Davis.

Humbly Sheweth

That for the fact where of yor. petr. Stood Justiced in this Court this Sessions Yor. Worshippl were pleased to fine him ten pounds And for Now payment thereof to Comitt him to Now Prison where he now remanis

That Yor. Petr. a poor Soldier & hath a wife & three small Children who as well as himselfe must Inevitably perish if not comiserated by your Worshipps being unable to pay the sd fine

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to remitt the sd Fine.

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc. Tho: Davis
