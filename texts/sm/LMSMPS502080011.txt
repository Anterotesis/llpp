To ye: Rt: Worshipll. His Maties Justies of ye. Peace for ye: County of Midd assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions of ye: peace

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of Francis Sirr.

Sheweth That yor: Petitionr: was convicted by Sr. Charles Peers Knt: one of His Maties Justices of ye: peace for the said County upon the Informacon of one John Snow for receiving & buying two hundred and three quarters weight of. Pensylvania Rice which was clandestinely run and imported knowing the same to be so clandese tinely run & imported and by the said Sr. Charles Peers adjudged to pay the Sume of ten pounds for the Same

Yor: Petr: thinks himself aggrieved by the said Judgmt. of the said Sr: Charles Peers appeals to this Court & humbly prays that the said John Snow & all other parties concern'd may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor: Petr: shall ever pray Etc

Francis Sirr
