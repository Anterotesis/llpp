To the Worshipfull Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their generall Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew by the Wardrobe in the City of London

Sheweth

That [---] Elizabeth Weston and Mary Weston and Sarah Wetson Children of Michael Weston have been past and removed from the parish of St. Luke in the said County of Middx by a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Anthony Wroth and William Booth Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County to the parish of St. Andrew by the Wardrobe aforesd.

That your Petitioers humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrants and appeal from & against the same to this Court,

Samll: Brogdon Agent for the Appeallts

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray this Honoble Court to appoint a Day for hearing the same

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
