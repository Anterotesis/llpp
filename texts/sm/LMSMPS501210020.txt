To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices

The Humble Pettion of Thomas Holberhe Taylor in Forsters Buieldings in White Cross Street in St. Gilses Crippell Gate

St. Humbly Sheweth that the Pettioner hath Six Small Childern: where of the oldest of four his but Six years old and hath given Secuerity to the Parish for them

Death Humble Beg that this Hounerable Court would be pleased to Take of the halfe peney a week that he his Charged with to the poor tell such time as his Charge of Children less and your sarvant shall be in Duty Pray
