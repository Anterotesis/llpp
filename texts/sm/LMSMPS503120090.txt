To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Over -seers of the Poor of the Parish of the Parish of Saint Michael at Quern London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) Dated the [...] Third Day of this Instant October Hannah the Wife of Edward Bodey was removed from the parish of St Botolph with -out Aldgate in the sd County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Michael at Quorn London as the Place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners humbly concieve themselves Aggreived by the order of the said Two Justices

Therefore Humbly Appeale to this Honourable Court for Releife in the premisses and pray Your Worships will indulge them in Appoin ting a Day for to hear and Determine the same

And Yor Petitioners Shall ever pray

Jon Shey Churchwarden

Mills Overseer
