To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Nicholas Corrall one of the petty Constables of the parish of Southmims in this County

Sheweth That by Order of this Court in April last past your Petr. was appointed to pass releive, and Convey the Vagrants through this County at the allowance of Thirty pounds p Annm for such Service

And That there is due and owin'g to yor. Petr. the Sume of Fifteen pounds for halfe a years Salary for the like Service ending at Michmas. now last past

Yor. Petr. therefore most hmbly prays yor. Worships would be pleased to Order the payment of the sd Fifteen pounds to yor. petr.

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc

Zach. Clarke{ Agent for the Petr.
