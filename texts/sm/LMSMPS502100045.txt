To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands & seals of Matthew Hewitt and Thomas Pindar Esqr two of his Maties Justices as the pease for the County of Middlx (Quorn unns) bearing date the fourth day of February last past, Elizabeth Nothingham wife of Henry Nothingham was removed from the parish of St St Sepulchre in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conieveing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to your Worpps there from And pray that your Worgges will be pleased to hear them in the pr misied and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Sepulchre may appear before yor at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And Your Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
