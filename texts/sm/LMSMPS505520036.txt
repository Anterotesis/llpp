To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the [...] parish of Bedfont in the County of Middx

Sheweth that by Virtue an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir Philip Musgrave and Sir John Gibbons Baronets two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifth day of August last past Margaret Brown widow out her two Children (Vizt) William aged about five years and James aged about two years were removed from the Parish of Stanes in the County of Middlesex into the said parish of Bedfont in the said County of Middlesex adjudging the said parish of Bedfont to be the place of the last legal settlement of the said Margaret Brown and her said two Children

That your Petitioner concieving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premiss And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Stanes may appear before you at a Certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray

Chas: Jemmett} Sollicitior for the [...] orders and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Bedfont
