To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Genll Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for the sd. County

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing da [...] the 23d. day of March 1735 Rose the wife of Jno. Mathw. Lemony & Jno. Mathew Lemon her son Aged abt. one Yeare were removed from the Parish of [...] St. Botolph without Aldgate in the sd. County of Middx to the Parish of St. Clemt Danes in the same County as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Peticoners conceive themselves to be Agreived & agt. the sd. Order doe Appeale

Your Petrs. there fore humbly pray that a Short Day may be appointed this Present Sessions to heare & determine the mre touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray

Ro: Chimmow Sollr. for the Appellts.}
