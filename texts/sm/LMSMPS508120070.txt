To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [...] Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Pancras on the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of William Gascoigne and John Machin Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of April last Michael Jones and Frances his Wife with their Two lawful begotten Children to Wit Elizabeth aged five Years or thereabouts and Mary Aged Seven Months or thereabouts Were Ordered to be Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Martin in the fields within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Pancras as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Michael Jones Frances his Wife and their said two children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said tow Justices and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint 8 Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. will Pray Etc

Herbt. Robertson Sollr. for the Appellants
