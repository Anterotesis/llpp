To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled -

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Honorable Catharine Talbot of the Parish of Hillingdon in the County of Middlesex Widow against such Part of the Rate lately made for the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish of Hillingdon as respects the Message or Tenement Outhouses Lands and Premises which your Petitioner now occupies and resides in

Sheweth That the usual Mode of Rating and assessing the Inhabitants in the said Parish of Hillingdon to the Poors Rate which hath been practiced for several Years past is in the proportion of These four the of the Rent of Premisses respectively rated

That the House Land and Premisses occupied by your Petitioner in the said Parish is Precisely the same as in the precxing Years without any Addition or Encrease whatsoever and for which she used to be rated at £95 being the Proportion of Three four the of the Rent of such Premisses in case the same had been let to a Tenant at Will or other wise

That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish have lately made a Rate for the Relief of the Poor thereof and have rated and assessed Your Petitioner to such Rate at £120 P Annum as three fourths of the Rent of her said House Land and Premisses which is greatly more than she concieves she ought to Pay not having made any Improvement thereon for several Years and therefore appeals to this Court against the same

Your Petitioner therefore prays that the benefit of her said Appeal may be saved unto her unto the next general Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County And that your Worships will appoint a Day in the said next Session of the Peace for the hearing and determining her said. Appeal requiring all Parties concerned to attend the same.

Christ Hill Solicitor for the Appellant
