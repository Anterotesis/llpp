To the Worshipfull William Cooper Esqr. Chairman 0& the Rest the Justices of the peace now at the Quarter Sessions of the peace holden at Hicks's Hall,

The Humble Pettn of Henry Adams now a prisoner in new Prison Etc

Humbly Sheweth that yor pettr was this day before yor Worshipp and acquitted, but being a very poor man and hate not one farthing in the World and this [...] day past in prison and now Remanded Back to the said Prison for want of Fees

Most Humbly imploreing yor Worshipps to take yor pettr Accssciable Circumstance unto yor Nature Consideraon by Ordering the Keeper of new prison to Discharge yor pettr

And yor pettr as in Duty Bound will pray
