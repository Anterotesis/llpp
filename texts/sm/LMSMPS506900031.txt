To the Right Worspll. her Maties Justices of the Peace at their Sessions at Hicks hall in and for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Samuell Woodrove

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr. being an Inhabitant of the pish of of Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx aforesaid payes only Thirteen pounds P annum for the Rent of a very Small house, But is Eaxee by the Assesser. of the poore's Rate Twelve Shillings a yeare, wth is much more the he was ever Assessed before till the last year, & as ninth again as Some of his Neighbours (far more able then himselfs) doe pay, the their Rents are higher then his

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore most humbly beyd to be a Releived in the prmisses.

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
