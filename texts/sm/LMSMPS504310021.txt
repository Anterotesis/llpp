To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Francis Bedwell Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of July last Mary Douglas Single- woman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrt. appeal to this Court against the same & humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this Present Sessions for the hearing & determining of the said Appeal And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray

Thos: Parker Robt. Woolridge} Overseers
