To the Right Worll: his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd.

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Saviour in Southwarke in the County of Surry

That Dorcas Shorter Widdow was lately passed from the Hamblett of [...] Bethnall Green in the pish of Stepney in the said County of Midd to the said parish of St. Saviour in Southwarke by Warrant under the hands & seales of Joseph Jorye & Robert Constable Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd thereby alledging that the said Dorcas Shorter was last legally settled in the said parish of St. Saviours where she & her [---] late husband Roger Shorter lived for some years.

Yor. Petrs. thinking them selves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices do Appeal to this Worll. Routh humbly pray taht the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of [...] Bethnall Green may be ordered to attend in this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the finall determinaton of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor. Petr. (as in duty bound) shall every pray
