May it please your Honours Your Worships most humble Petitioner John Vain Parishioner to St Andrews Holbourn, but new living in Bowling Alley, Turn Mill Street but in a very Sick & languishing Condition; humbly pray that your Worships, will be pleased to grant him a pass, that He may be relieved, in order to present his Starving: & shall as in Duty bound pray for your Worships. I am your Worships most Obedient & dutiful Servant Jno: Vain Turn Mill Street 16 Octr 1755.

Your poor Petitioner is now here in waiting
