To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Charles Floyer and Nathaniel Booth Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) dated the Eighth day of April last Mary Rodon the wife of John Rodon aged abt. thirty three years (who as is alledged in the sd. Order) had lately come into the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middx endeavouring to settle there contrary to Law & likely to become chargeable to the same parish was removed from the said parish of St Andrew Holborn above the Barrs to the sd. Parish of Christ Church London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners find themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of removal of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court against the same

Your Peticoners Appellants therefore humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to appo [...] a day for the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn above the Barrs & all other persons concerned to attend this Court this present Sessions to hea [...] & abide the Judgement & determination of the Court upon the said Appeal And that the Appel [...] may be releived herein according to Law

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc

Joseph Marshall Edw. Brackstone} Church wardens

Giles Theye Willm. Payne} Overseers
