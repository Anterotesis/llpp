To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace of the session House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminister in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hand and Seals of N Bond and Tho Vaughan two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex and one of them of the Quorum bearing date the fourteenth day of August last William Matthew East therein said to be the son of John East and Sophia his Wife aged about Eight Years born in the said Parish of Saint James was conveyed from and out of the Parish of Kensington in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James as the places of his lawful Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justice and their said Order and pray that your Worship will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas. Dyson Solicitor for the Appellants
