To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for County of Middx

The humble Petition of Fran [...] Layton on the behalfe of Fra [...] Layton his Son

Sheweth That your Petr: said Son by his Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing rate the Twenty Fifth day of March last became an Apprentice to one John Clarke of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in this County Blacksmith for the terme of Seven yeares comencing from the Date of the said Indenture As thereby appears.

That the sd Master hath not Instructed yor Petr. son his Busyness & frequently abused him To answer which complaint their Master [...] stanes bound by Recognizance before the Worspp Justice ward to appear this present Sessions And forasmuch as the said Indenture not being Indented Yor Petr humbly conceives the Same to be void

Therefore humbly Etc ays this Court will be Pleased to appoint a day this Sessions for the sd Master to attend to shewcause why his said Son should not be discharged from his said Master

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc Fran Layton
