To the Rt. Worspfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Benjamin Niccoll late Petty Constable of Southmims in this County.

Sheweth. That there is due and owing unto your Said Petr. the Sume of Twenty Pounds at Lady Day last past for halfe a Years Salary for his loss of time & Expences in passing and Conveying Vagrants through this County,

Yor. Peticonr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the Paymt. of the sd Sume of Â£20 unto yor. Petr. for his sd Service.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray etc.

Benjamin Niccoll
