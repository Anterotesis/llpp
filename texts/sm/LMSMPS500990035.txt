1708 bench warrt. granted

to answer Compls of Lydia his wife for violently assaulting & beating her in beach of her Maties Peace since the nine he was bound by recognicance taken before Robert Constable Esqr one of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this County to [...] him [...] to answer the complaint of his sd wife for the like offence
