To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Sarah Lane Wife of Richard Lane

Sheweth That your Peticoners said Husband Entred into as Recognizance in this Court for his Appearance here this present Sessions and likewise for his good behaviour for One whole year towards his Wife Which said year Expires this Sessions

That your Peticoner is ready to Make appear to this Court that her said Husband Daily threatens her Life after he shall be Discharged of his said Recognizance Insoemuch that your Peticoner goes in great danger

R: Prettie Sod & Petr: She therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order that her said Husband may attend this Court someday this Sessions to shew Cause cause why he should not be further continued for his good behaviour

And Yor Pet Shall Ever pray Etc Sarah Lane
