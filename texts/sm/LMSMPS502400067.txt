To the Rt. Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx att their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace att Hicks hall Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Botolph without Aldergate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands & Seales of Thomas Pindar and Jacob Harvey Esqrs: Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the Fifth day of January Instant Elizabeth Maybus and Sarah Maybus were by the said Order Removed from the Parish of St. Giles a without Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Peticoners humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggreived

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray Yor: Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and Determine the said Appeale and that yor Petrs: may be releived as unto yor Worships Shall Seem meet

And Yor Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
