To ye Rt Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Oliver Humphreys of ye Parish of St James Clerkenwell in ye sd County

Sheweth. That your Petitr is rated in ye Poors rate of ye sd Parish for ye Sum of two hundred Pds Stock wherefore he Conceives himself aggrievd. and appeals agst. ye Same

Yor Petitr therefore humbly prays such relief in ye Premises as to this Court shall be theught Meet and Yor Petitr. Shall over Pray

Oliver Humphreys
