To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in the General Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition & Appeal of Samuel Boydon of the pish of St. Luke in the County of Middx Victualler

Sheweth

That your Petitioner by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hand and Seal of the Worshipful John Spencer Colepepper Esqr-One of his Matys Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx was Convened before the sd John Spencer Colepepper Esqr-and was Convicted (as he Apprehends) of knowingly Suffering and permitting One Thomas Salmon & One Charles Walker to play at a Certain Game Called Skittles in his Skittle Ground in the parish Afsd-against the form of the Stabile in that behalf

That your Petioner humbly Conceiving himself Aggrieved by the Judgement & Determinacon of the sd Justice doth Appeal to this Honble Court from the Same And prays Such relief in the premisses as to your Worship Shall Seem Meet

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that all Parties Concerned may be Ordered to Attend this Honable Court to hear & Abide the Determination of your Worships in the Premisses

And Your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc

Wm. Watts Sollr. for the Appeal [...]
