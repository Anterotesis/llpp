To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles in Reading in the County of Borks

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of James Clitherow and John Dunkwater Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th day of April last past Olive the wife of John Heath and their four Children one named Sarah aged about nine years another named Mary aged about six years another named John aged about five years another named William aged about two years were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Heston in the County of Middlesex unto the parish of Saint Giles Reading as the place of their last leagal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of their Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing an determining there of may be adjourned until the next Session
