To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Thomas Price

Sheweth That your. Petitioner is a House holder in the Parish of Hampstead Higlor aged Thirty three years is a Married Man and has a wife and two Children and prays a License of this Court to be a Common Badger buyer and Seller of Corn Butter Cheese Pouctry and other dead Victuals for one years

And your Petitioner shall ever prayer Thomas Price

23d. Oct 1768 Reknowl Etc wh. upon the usual Coud
