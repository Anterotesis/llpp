To the Rt: Worpll his Majts Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx at their Genll Quart Session at Hicks hall

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the sd County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hand & Seals of Anthony Mann & Lawrence Sanitle Esqr: two of his Majts:Justices of the Peace of the sd County bearing date the Seaventeenth Day of May last one Mary Goulding Spinster was removed from the parish of St: James in the Liberty of Westmr in the sd County unto ye: Petr said parish whereby your Petr humbly conceived that they are aggrieved

Ye: Petr therefor Appeal to your Worps aganist the said Order & pray ye Worps to appoint a day to here the same

And ye petr: shall pray
