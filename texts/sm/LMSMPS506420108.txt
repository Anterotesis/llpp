To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Quarter Session of the Peace of Hicks Hall in the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition of George Thompson of Portpool Lane in the County of Middlesex Journaman Baker

That your Petitioners Brother Robert Thompson on the; 23d Day of June last became a Lunatic and furiensly Mad so that your Petitioner was necessarily obliged and did put him into a Madhouse at Hoxton kept by one Mrs. Harrison where he has continued ever since and your Petitioner hath paid Ten Shillings and Six Pence for each Week out of his own Pocked his said Brother not having any Money or other Thing whereby he might be supported

That your Petitioner and his said Brother are Oath Natives of Edinburgh where their respective Settlements are,

That your Petitioners said Brother for some Time before and at the Time he became a Lunatic lived with one Alexander Latham a Baker who resides upon Saffron Hill in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middlesex as a Journaman Baker and was paid weekly, and your Petr. did apply to the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish to take care of your Petrs. said Brother but they absolutely refused,

That your Petr. is unable to support his said Brother any longer be being only in Journaman Baker at the weekly Sw [...] of 8s :xno more so that your Petr. must be obliged to order the said Mrs. Harrison to turn his said Brother out of her House altho he hath ever since the Time aforesaid been furiously Mad and so far disordered in his Senses that he will be exceedingly dangerous to be permitted to go abroad

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays that his said Brother may be ordered to be taken care off by such Parish as to your worships shall seem meet

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
