To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Lawrence Old Fewry-in the City of London

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order bearing date the 22d. day of January last past under the hands & Seals of John Fuller & Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Midd (quor unus) Thomas Dewers & Alice his wife were removed & conveyed from the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County of Midd to the said Parish of St Lawrence Old Jewry-in the said City of London [...] (by the name of St Lawrence Jewry) as the place of the last legal Setlemt of the said Thomas & Alice

Yor Petr finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt or Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray this Court would be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell & all other Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide their Judgmt & determinacon touching the said appeal

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
