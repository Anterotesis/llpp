Middx ss To the Rt. Worshipll: her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Leonard Fosterlane London

Sheweth That Jane Everingdine a Child between Sixt and Seven Years old and William Everingdine a Child about seven Years old the Daughter and Son of William Everingdine and Alice his wife were lately removed and convezed from the parish of St. Mary Islington in the County of Middx by warrants under the hands & Seales of Joseph Short and Richard Brown Esqrs. two of her Maties, Justices of the Peace of the Said County (Quorum unus) Dated the 27th: day of September 1711: to the Said parish of St. Leonard Fosterlane London as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the Said William Everingdine the father of the Said Children

Your Peticonrs themselves place appeal [...] the Said two Justices of the Peace doe therefore appeale to this and Humbly pray that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St. Mary Islington and all other persons concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to heare P abide the Judgment and finall determinacon of this Court

And Your Peticonrs Shall pray Etc P Rd. Watkins

John Leven Will Thompson} Churchwardens
