To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assemble

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint George in the County of middlesex on behalf of themselves and the the Inhabitants of the said Parish

Sheweth

That by Value of and Order a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies and I. Merceron Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County bearing date the 12th. Day of September 1797 one John Ponder and his two Legitimate Children vizt elizabeth and Mary Ann were conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of St Dunstan Stepney in the said County to the Parish of saint George in the said County as the place a man Lawful Settlement

That your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order was much as the place of the lawful settlement of the said John Ponder and his said two Children appears to be in the Hamlet of Ratchife

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal agianst the said Order a pass warrant and pray your Worships to app [...] a Day for the hearing and Determining thereof.

Jno Clement Vestry C [...] of St. George Middx
