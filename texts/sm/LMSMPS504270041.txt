To the Right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chatham in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Walter Berry and Christopher Scott Esquirs two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex One being of the Quorum bearing date the eighteenth day of March 1754. Edward Mills Elizabeth his wife and Edward their Son Aged about two Months were removed from the Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Chatham in the said County of Kent Alledging the said Parish of Chatham to be the Place of their last legall Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Worships there from And humbly Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses And Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Poplar and Black wall in the County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the Appear

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Willliam Guyt Richd Cooke Saml Arthur} Church wardens

Wm: Holmden Walsham: Masters Nath Pearne} Overseers

Thos: Tomlyn Agent for the Appellants }
