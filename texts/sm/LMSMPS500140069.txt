To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Mathew Davis

Sheweth That as of the last Sessions yor Worpps were pleased to orders that [...] [...] severall streets in Goodmans Feilds should be paved according to a late Act of Parliamt. in that case made and provided

That yor. Petr. humbly conceive that he is not within the interton of the said Act although he hath some small states in the sd Feilds.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps to be pleased to where him in the prmisses in Such manner as unto yor. Worpp shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. Shall are Pray
