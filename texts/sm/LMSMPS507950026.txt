To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Gouldhall in king Street Westminster in the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said county

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and seals of Jno. Wright and James Croft Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of February last Maram Hobbon Jane his Wife and Sarah their Child aged near Sixteen Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Anne within the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint James as the placed of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray etc

Jas: Dyson [...] Solicitor for the Appellants
