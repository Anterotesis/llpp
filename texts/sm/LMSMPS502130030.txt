The Humble Petr. of Robert Stockdale of the Parish of St. Martins in the Fields in the County of Middx

To the Honourable Bench of Justices

Your Petr. Being a very Poor man & almost Blind & Past his Labour, Begs this Honourable Bench will allow him the County Pention, towards his [...] Subsistence &

Your Petr. in Duty Bound Shall Ever Pray
