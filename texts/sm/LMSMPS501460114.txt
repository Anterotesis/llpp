To the Rt. Worppll. his majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Genl Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseere for the poore of the parish of st. Bridgett London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands one seales of B. Buckridge and I Chamberlaine Esqrs two of his Trugties. Justices of the peace for the sd County (Quors Urns) bearing date the 4th:day of may 1715 Ann Talby widdow of Martin Talby deced was past onyor. petrs. from the parish of st Margts. Westmr. as the P Can of her last legall Settlement

That yor. Petr. apprehend themselves aggreived by the sd. two Justices Order and appeale t yor. worpps therefrom

Yor Petr. therefore humbly hope yor. Worppsll will appoint a day to heare and determine the sd. Appeale

And yr. Petrs. shall every pray to
