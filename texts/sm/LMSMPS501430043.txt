To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Baxter

Sheweth That Your Peticoner did Serve the Office of Petty Constable from Christmas 1713: To Christmas last within the parish of St. Andrew Holborne Abovebarrs in this County

That Your Peticoner has expended and paid in passing Vagrants during the time of his Said Constable Ship the Sume of Twenty pounds & Nineteen Shilling as will appeare by the Severall Certificates of allowance under the hands and Seales of divers of his Maties, Justices of the Peace residing within the Said parish

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays Your Worships to Grant him and Order of this Comt for Mr. John Mills High Constable for Holbornne Division to Reimburse Your Peticoner the Said Sume or otherwise relieve him in the prmisses as to Your Worships Shall Seem meet

And Your Peticoner Shall pray [...]

T Rd. Watkins

Thomas Baxter
