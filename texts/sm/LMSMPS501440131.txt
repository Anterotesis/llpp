To the Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the peace [...] att Hicks Hall in St. John Street

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by order under the Hands and Seales of Samuel [...] Benton and Nathaniel Manlove Esqrs. two of his Majties. [...] of the peace of the said County Quor vn bearing date [...] the 15th. day of February last Gabriell Gillingham with his two Children Gabriell aged about five years and Phillipp aged about two years were removed from the Hamblett Bethnall Green unto the said Hamblett of Ratcliffe by wh [...] Order Your Petitionrs. thinks they are agreived

And therefore humbly pray that your Worp [...] will be pleased to [...] appoint a day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Bethnall Green to attend this Court and hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said appeal

And your Petitionrs. shall ever pray Etc
