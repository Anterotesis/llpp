To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the Sd. County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. under the hands & seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quors Unus) bearing Date the 3d. day of October Instant Richard Oliver was removed from the pish of St. Martyn in the Feilds in the Sd. County to the Sd. pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of his last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs. think themselves aggreived by the Judgemt. of the Sd. tow Justices & humbly appeale to this he able Cort agt. the Same

Yor. Petrs. therefore pray yor Worspps would be pleased to Ordr. the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the Poor of the Sd. pish of St. Martyn in the Feilds & all other Psons concerned to attends this Crt some day this prsent Quarter Sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt. & determinacon of this Cort touching the sd. Appeale.

Chimmow

And yor Petrs. Shall pray Etc
