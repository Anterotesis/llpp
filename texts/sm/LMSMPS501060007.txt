To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Hannah Tring and Thomas Smith

Sheweth

That Sometime past upon Petr. became Baile before the Worshippfull Justice Metcalfe for the Appearance of one Mary Lamb in this Honour Court this prsent Sessions for an Assault As by the Recognizance [...] Appeare

That the said Mary Lamb hath Since beene Arrested for Debt and is now A Prisoner in the Marshalsea Prison in Southwarke

P Tho: Nash

yor Petrs: therefore most humbly pray yor Worshipps to be Pleased to grant that they may be Discharged from the sd Recognizance

And yor. Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc

Hannah Tring her [x] Mrke

Tho: Smith his [x] Mrke
