To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields the County of Middx:

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Jno. Ellis and M: Lambart Esqrs. Two of her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the 14th. Day of December Last Phillis Barnes the wife of Edward Barnes and Edward their Child aged about one Year and an half were removed from the Parish of St. James Westminster the said County of Middx. to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to yor Worshipp [...] agt. the said pass warrant & Judgement of the said two Justices and Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be Saved unto them untill the next General Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the Said County of Middx

And Yor. Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc.
