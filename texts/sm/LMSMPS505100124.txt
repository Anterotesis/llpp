To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Martin in the City of Worcester.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr in ye. sd. County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Eighth Day of April last Elizabeth Briggs Widow of John Briggs and their Three Children Vizt. Ann Aged Twelve Years and Upwards William about Ten Years and Sarah Aged upwards of Seven Years were [...] removed from the parish of Saint James with in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Martin in the City of Worcester the said Justices thereby Adjudging that to be the Place of their last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices and Do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Pray that your Worships

Would be pleased to Order that the Benefit of this their Appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing and Determination thereof may be Adjourned untill the Next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and to make an Order that the Church Wardens and Oversees of the Poor of the said parish of Saint James [...] within the Liberty of Westminster aforesd. And all parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there hear and Abide the Determination of your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

} Churchwardens.

} Overseers of the Poor
