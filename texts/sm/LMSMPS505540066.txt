To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Joseph Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quarom) bearing Date the SIxteenth Day of November last Elizabeth Veal Wife of David Veal (who has deserted her) and Sarah her Daughter aged twenty one Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne by the name and description of the Parish of St. Ann Soho in the Liberty of Westminster) as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said David Veal Elizabeth his wife and Sarah their Daughter where by your Petitioner conceive themselves to be Aggrieved And

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices And Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some Day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James at Clerkenwell and all Persons concerned do then Attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Pettioners Shall Pray Etc

Samuel Smith John Trotter Thos. Thompson Thos. Williamson}

Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Anne
