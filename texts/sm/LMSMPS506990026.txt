To the Right Worsppll. her Matys: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of William Powell.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. did Serve his late Maty King William and also her present Maty till of late he was disabled in her Matys Service on board. her Matys Ship Litchfield Soe that he is now unfitt for the Said Service And being aged 60 yeares and very poor and altogether uncapeable of getting a lively hood.

P Westley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to allow him Such County pencon as unto yor. Worspps Shall Seem meet

And yor. petr Shall ever pray Etc.

Masr. a person of 2s [...] from [...] We whose names are here Subscribed Inhabitants of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Sd County Doe hereby certify your Worspp of the truth hereof

Druel Peadminister

Samuell Bidgeon John Bartrom
