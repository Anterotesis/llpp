To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Mary Gregory

Sheweth That in some time past your Petr. was by the Worspl Justice Woodward comitted to the House of Correccon in this County for attempting to robb one John Woodruffe

That your Petr. is a person who hath allwaces demeaned and behaved herselfe very civilly and honestly and hath lived as a Servant in very creddible places As by a Note now ready to be produced & severall wittnesse now ready in Court can make appeare

P Tho. Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Praye yor. worspp [---] to be pleased to Grant that she may be wholly discharged from her sd Imprisonmt she being now out upon Pray he

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
