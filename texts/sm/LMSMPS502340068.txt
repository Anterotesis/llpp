To the Right Worpfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Clavering in the County of Es [...]

Sheweth

That by an order dated the seventeenth day of January last under the hands & seals of Sr John Austin Bart and Honorat Smyth Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middlx (quor unus) one Mary Ruden with one child named Richard aged about Six months, who were likely to become chargeable to the Parish of Monken Hadley in the Said County of Middlx [...] were removed from the Said parish of Monken Hadley to the Said Parish of Clavering in the Said County of Essex as the Place of their last legal Settlemt

That your peticoners find themselves agreived by the Said warrt or order of the Said two Justices & hope to make it appear to this Court that the Said Mary Ruden & her Said Child have noe legal settlement in the said Parish of [---] Clavering & therefore humbly appeal against the said order

Jo: Rolfe for the Officers of Clavering: Yor Petrs & Appellants humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of Monken Hadley & all other PSons concned to attend this Court to hear & abide the judgment & determinacon of this Court upon the Said appeal & that the Appellants may be releived herein according to Law & Justice

And yor Petrs & Appellts Shall pray Etc
