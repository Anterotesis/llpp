To the Rt: Worpll: his Majties: Justices of Yr Peace for the County of Midlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mildred Bread Street London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Felix Feast and John Fuller Esqr: Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor un) bearing date in Twenty Sixth day of February last Edith Close Wife of John Close with her Three Children Sarah Samuel sd and Robert were Removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Criplegate in the said County of Midlesex unto the said Parish of St. Mildred Bread Street as the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That Your Peticoners Conceiving them Selved aggreived by ye said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do humbly Appeale to your Worships there form And pray that your Worsps will be pleased to hear them in ye premes And Order that ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Criplegate may Appear before you at a Certain day to hear and Abide the Judgemt: and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeale

And Your Peticonrs: shall ever pray Etc.
