Feb 1690/1

Sitting at the [...] Barly

The humble Petition of Rebecca Parks now prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth That Severall Implements relateing to Clipping & Coping, Such as pots, Stamps, Sheeres being found in yor petrs. garden about 3 weekes agoe, yor. petr. was upon suspisicon sent hither to her great Wrong

That shee is very poor, and unable to bear the charge of longer Imprisonmt. here.

And therefore humbly beggs to be accused or Excused this Sessions according to the 31. Statute of Charles the 2d. wth. directs it in like Cases.
