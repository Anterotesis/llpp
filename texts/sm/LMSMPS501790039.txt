To the Gentleman of this honble. Bench

Just now I see ye Star't ye is bound for ye appearance of Michael Lamy, who peace against peter Viel, Philipp Berard, Naamas Gordon, & Margarit Viel, which is now come to petition this Court to have his discharge by reason ye Michael Lamy ye Plaint [...] is fleet non we whose names are here presented do desire yt. he may be [---] [...] , [...] & not be discharged, yt we May have our recovers against him by due Coverse of law.

Philip Berard namas Gordon Peiter viel Margan's viel} refersants of ye Cause
