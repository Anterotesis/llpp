To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the peace

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Heston in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of George Errington & Richd. Chamberlayne Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th. day of August last Jane Cameron Widow and her four Children Vrizt. James aged about twelve Years Jane aged about Nine Years Margaret aged upwards of three Years and Alexander aged upwards of ten Months were removed from the parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Heston as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of the peace Appeale to this Court agt. the same and humbly pray Yor. Worships will be pleas'd to Appoint some day this present Sessions to hear and determine the same

And Yor. Petrs, shall pray Etc

Dan Botton Atty. for the Appellts.
