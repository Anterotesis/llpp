To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminsters in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of James Croft and John Machin Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid bearing Date the Twenty ninth Day of January last Philip Oge and Margaret his Wife and Charlotte their Child aged about Nine Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Anne within the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint James Whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that Your Worships Write be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc. Jas: Dyson Solicitor for the Appellants
