To the Worship full His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of Roger Cutler of the Parish of Edgware in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by the Second rate or Assessment made for the relief of the poor of the said Parish in this present year, that is to say in or about the Month of October last and Confirmed and allowed by two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the same County on or about the Fourth day of November last your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the three several Sums of Two pounds, Seven Shilling five Shilling for and in respect of his occupations in the same Parish.

That he humbly apprehend the said rate or assessment to be illegal unegual and Excessive and particularly for that Robert Harding in the said rate named Stands rated and assessed there by in the Sum of Twelve Shillings only for and in respect of the Land and house belonging to Mr. Sharpe in the said Robert Hardings Occupation

That your Petition conceiving himself aggrieved by the said Rate or Assessment humbly appealeth to your Worships against the same and prayeth such relief in the premises as to your Wordhips in your Wisdom Shall seem meet.

And your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc Roger Cutler.
