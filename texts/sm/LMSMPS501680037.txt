To The Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace Now Sitting att Hickes Hall

The Humble Peticon of Dorothy Goldsmith

Sheweth That whereas William Aldridge is bound over by Recognizance for a Rape Committed this present Sessions & whereas her most Materiall Wittnesses are not be found or Keep out of the way Soe that She can Noe ways prefer her Bill of Indictment this Sessions She most Humbly prayes the Order of this Court that he may be Compelled to Appeare the next Session to give Evidence For the King & that he may be Continued upon the Recognizance

And as in duty bound She Shall ever pray Etc. Dorothy Goldsmith

The Wittnesses for the King is Elizabeth Leigh & Susan Fisher
