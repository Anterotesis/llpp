To the Honble: Bench Of Justices Sitting Att Hicks Hall

The humble Petition Of Sarah Dennison Prayeth she may be discharged Of her Recognizance in Assault & Batterie again Philip Meale He having fled from Justice Twelve Months Since: Yr: Petitioner A poor Woman & in Great Appliction Beg Yr: Honors Discharge hereor,

And Yr: Petitioner shall Duty full Pray

Gentlemen Yr: Petitioner is readie to give Oath yt. she paid 2s:&8d for withdrawing her Recognizance to One Of the Clerks in Court in October last.
