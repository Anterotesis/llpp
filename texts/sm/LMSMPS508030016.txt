To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Alphage near Sion College in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Handsand Seals of Sir Tampson Wright Knight and James Paine Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteeth day of September one thousand seven hundred and Eighty five Sarah Burch singlewoman was removed from the [...] Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint Alphage near Sion College adjudging the said Parish of Saint Alphage near Sion College to be the place of her last legal Settlement wherby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that a day may be appointed by this Court for the hearing and determining this appeal and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes and all Persons concernd be ordered then to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgmt. and Determination of Your Worships in the Premises.

And Petitioners shall ever pray John Evans Sobr. for the Appellants
