To the Rt. Worppll. his Majtyes Justices of the Peace now sitting at Hicks Hall for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticion and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseer of the [...] of the Parish of St: Anns:

Sheweth That by hath [...] Rich: Jn Cord [...] his wife and & holden being it [...] Parish of St: Anne from the Parish of Chiswick That your Peticon have since they being passed to the Parish of St: Anns, Carefully enquired according to the alledged in the sd Pass but can not finde that the sd [...] Hall his wife and children [...] hath any legall Settlemt sd Parish

Your Peticionr therefore humbly prayes that this Honble: Court would ne pleased to grant an Order that the Church: wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of Chiswick doe attend this Court the next Quarter Sessons (some day) to Shew reasons why the sd John Hill his wife and Children ought not to be rep [...] or otherwise Setled as your Worpps [...] upon hearing the cause think fitt

And Yor Peticonr. Shall ever pray [...]

[...] quartial 3 or 2 St Anns & Chiswick

March 1700/1
