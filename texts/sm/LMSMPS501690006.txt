To the Rt. Worshpll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Joseph Satchell & Ann his Wife of the Parish of St. Gils's Cripplegate of the County aforesd.

Sheweth That yor. Petitir. Joseph Satchell is above Seventy Years Old and very infirm and Ann his Wife; above Sixty Years Old, and very Weakly, and have been inhabitants of the abovesaid Parish for above 44 Years, and have Likewise Paid the Duties of the Said Parish for many Years Past, but are now Reduced to Great Poverty, and uncapable of Provideing for them-Selves

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that yor. Worships will be Pleased in tender Compassion to their deplorable Condition make an Order for their Speedy relief, Otherwise yor. Poor Petrs. must Inevitably Perish.

And yor. Petrs. as in duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc.
