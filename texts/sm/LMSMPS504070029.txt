To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices att. Hickes Hall

[...] Humble Petitioner was from Canstable before this Worshipfull bench att Michaelmas Sesions 1749 and to Continue to the aforesaid Sesions 1750 which has been Expired same time but your Petioner is still Detained as Constable to the great Preicede of my self and family: and hope this worshipfull Bench will Take itt into Consideration and Remove your Humble petioner out of his office of Constable and you will very much oblidge your most Humble oblidged and obedient Servant

Richd. Trobridge Decem. 5. 1750 [---] midle Libty St. Johnstreet
