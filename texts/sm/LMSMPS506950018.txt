To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Mary Castine On the Behalfe of Stephen Castine her Son

Sheweth

That Some time past Your Petrs. sd. Son was Comitted to New Prison in this County where he now Remaines upon the Oaths of Grace Archer (One of your Petrs. Late Servts.) that your Petrs. sd Son was the Father of a Bastard Child Borne On the Body of her the sd. Grace Archer

That yor. Petrs. sd Son Did this Morning attend this Honble Court But your Petr. nor noe other of his Friends being then prsent with him

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Beggs your [...] Worspps To be pleased to Order that her sd. Son may this Afternoone be againe brought before this Honble Cort to answer the sd. matter If your Worspps Soe thinke fitts.

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

M Castaing
