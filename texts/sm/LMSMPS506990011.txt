To the Right Worpll: her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Robert Winington Jeremiah Wright and Thomas Cox of the Parish of St Leonard Shoredicth to the said County Victuallers

Sheweth That yor: Peticoners are Severally Indictmt for keeping Gameing Houses And Your Poor Peticon are very Seing for such then offence humbly Submitting them -Selves to this Honble. Court and Promise not to offence against in the like nature

Yor Peticonrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worppll well please to consider [...] the Condition of yor. Peticonrs and their poor Familyes and to grant them such releife in the Premised as your grave Wis [...] Shall be thought meete

And yor Peticonr as in duly bound Shall ever pray
