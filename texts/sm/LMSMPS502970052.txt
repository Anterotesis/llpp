To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westmr in the County of Middlx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of November last Sarah Mason wife of Richd. Mason and their three Children Vizt. Christopher aged Nine Years Anne aged four Years and Rebecca aged Eight Months were removed from the Parish of St. Giles the Fields in the said County of Middlx to the said Parish of St. Anne whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worship against the Adjudgement of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields & all persons Concerned do Attend this Court some Day in this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgement & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Jo: Kendall Agent for the Appellts.
