To the Right Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of John Castle

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petr: was committed to New Prison on Tuesday last for Offending & misbehaving himself before this Worshipful Court

That Your Petr: was at the Time of so misbehaving Over come with Liquor & is heartily Sorry for this great Offence and very willing to make all due Submission & humbly to ask Pardon

That Your Petr: is a poor labourious Person & gets his Livelyhood by setting Herbs & Fish

Therefore Your Petr: most humbly implores this Honourable Court will have Compassion on him & be pleased to Order that he may be discharged from his Imprisonment

And Your Petr: shall ever Pray Etc

John Castell
