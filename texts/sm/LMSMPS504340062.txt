To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Caslon and William Withers Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Ninth Day of October last past Sarah Smeaton, Widow-was removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County to the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos Huddlr Wm: Boorsher} Churchwardens of St. Mary Le Bone

James Price R Benner} Overseers of the Poor
