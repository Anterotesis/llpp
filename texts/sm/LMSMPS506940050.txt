To the Petr. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of middx

The humble Peticon of James Hutchinson

Sheweth That some time past your Petr. being in a Quarrell in the house of One John Barrow in Lymehouse in this County Your Petr. was Bound Over for his appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to answer the same That the Principall person concerned in the sd. Quarrell made his Escape and that Yesterday your Petr. was by his Bayle Surrendred before your worpps and your Petr. being altogether a Stranger in this Country could not proc [...] other Bayle whereupon your Worpps were pleased to comitt him to New Prison in this County where he now Remaines

That your Petr. is now One of the Mitcheife Men belonging to her Matys shipp London and hath been sevall Yeares in her Matys service and allwaies Demeaned & behaved himselfe faithfully & honestly

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. Worspps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from his sd. Imprisonmt.

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc.
