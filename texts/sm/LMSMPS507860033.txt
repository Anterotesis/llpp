To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Hyde and E Kelly Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of August last whereby Sarah Roe Wife of James Roe (gone from her) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc. Jas: Dyson Solicitor for the Appellants
