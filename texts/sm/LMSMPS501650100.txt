To the Worll his Majesties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the sd County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seale of Laurence St. Lo and Ib. Blagny Esqrs. Justices of the peace of and for the City and Liberty of Westmr. and said County of Middx bearing Date the 24th. day of September last Elizabeth Bradley Singlewoman then prgnant with a Bastard Child or Children was removed from & [...] out of the parish of St. Jamess Westmr. in the County of Middx aforesaid to the sd. parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of her lawfull Settlement

That Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves agreived by the Judgement of the sd. two Justices humbly appeal to Yor. Worshipps agt. the same.

Therefore Humbly pray Your Worships would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Oversees of the poor of the sd. parish of St. James's in this County may attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to hear & abide the finall Bridgement and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
