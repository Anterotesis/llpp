To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of George Baxter

Sheweth That by an Order of Adjudicaton dated the 22d day of February last under the hands and Seals of John Milner Esqr and Henry Fetherston Bart, [...] two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one where of being of the Quar) and whiteing with in the Lymitte of the Parish Church of the Parish of St Giles in the feilds in the Said County Seting forth That they haveing mett together & heard the Complaint of the Churchwardens & Overseer. of the poor of the sd parish of St Giles in the feilds concerning A Male Bastard Child born in the sd Parish on the body of Mary Dodson Single woman on the [...] third Day of February in the year of Our Lord And Thousand Seven hundred and fifteen which is Baptized Richard and become chargeable to the Sd Parish and Se likely to continue Did find upon Exaiacon of the sd Mary Dedson taken upon Oath that your Peticoner die be yett out he body of the Sd Mary Dodson the sd Male Bastard Child. They the Sd Ju Fines did Adjud ye your & Peticonr He put a live Father of the Sd Male Bastard Child and die Order and Appoint your Peticonr Forth with upon notice of Such their Order to pay to the sd Churchwardens and Overseers. of the poor or Some or One of them the Sume of fifteene Shillings of law full mony of great Britain by them then already expended [...] my the the sd male, Bastard Child as also that your Peticoner should meeleh, and every [...] be from thence forth pay unto the Sd Church wardens and Overseers of the poor or Some or Out of them the Sume of two Shillings & Six pence of like lawfull merry for and toward the Releife and Mainteinance of the Sd Male Bastard Child for So long time as the Sd male Bastard Child Shall remaine chargeable to the Sd Parish And that he Should of and from the Charge of the Sd Bastard Child at age times there after Save harmless and Indemnifie the Sd parish of St Giles in the Feilds

That your Peticonr conceive my himselfe to be appreived by the Sd Order humbly Appealed there from to his Court

And humbly [...] that this Court will be pleased [...] Certein Time to hear the matt of this your Peticonr sd appeale And to Order the Churchwardens and Over seers of the poor of the Sd Parish of St Giles in the feilds to attend at the hearing & Determinacon there of And further to Stand to abide Such Order and Direccon there in as to third Court Shall Seem Meet

And Yor Peticonr Shall ever pray Etc
