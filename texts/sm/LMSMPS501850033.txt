To ye. Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Robinson of the Parish of Edmonton in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petr being Destitute of Imployment and Aged hath been obliedged to apply to the Officers of the said Parish for relief who refuse to allow him any or to take Any care of him soe that he and his Wife are ready to Perish

Your Petr: therefore humbly. Prays he may be allowed such a Pencon As to this Court shall seem Meet

And Yor: Petr Shall Ever Pray etc.

Jo Robinson
