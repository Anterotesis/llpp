To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seale of Samuel Benson and Alexander Pitfeild Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx (Quorw Unus) bearing date the Nineteenth day of March last past John Potter with Mary his wife and his two Children Mary aged about Six years and Thomas aged about four years were removed from the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Midlx unto the said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do appeal to your Worpps these from And humbly pray that your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch to appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment & determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs Shall ever Pray &ca.
