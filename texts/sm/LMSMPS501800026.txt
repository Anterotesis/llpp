The humble Petition of Jane Nesbett et

Shadwell, for rel

refr to Mr. Kyrby Mr. B: Johnson Mr. Raymond or 1

To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall

1719

Middx ss: Ad General Session pacis Dui Regis tent p Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John street in Com prd. [...] die Martis scilt primo did Septembris anno regin Dui Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Sexto

It is Ordered by this Court that it be And It is hereby recomended unto [---] Robert Kyrby Bastwick Johnson & Hugh Raymond Esqrs Justices of the peace for this County or any one of their to Sumon before them the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of st. paul shadwell in this County, and to examine into the truth of the Petrs. complaint, and to make such Order for the releife of the petr. and her children as to the sd. Justices of the peace or any one of them shall seem meet,

p Cur Harcourt
