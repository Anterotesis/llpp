Midd ss. To the Right Worpfull the Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Peticon of John Keble

Sheweth That yor Peticonr has served ye Office of Constable for ye Parish of Cowley in ye said County by ye space of one year and upward And tyal there has not lately any and Court Leet been Kept in ye said Parish Nor is it Known when any will be

Yor Peticoner therefore humbly prays that Richd Camble who is Unanimously chosen to be Constable by ye Inhabitant of ye said Parish may by [---] Order from this buy be Comanded to attend this Court to take his Oath for ye due Execucon of ye said Office And ye peticonr will old pray.
