To the Right Worpll. Her Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Ye. Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woolwich in ye. County of Kent

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hands Seals of Robert Kyrby & Bastwick Johnson Esqrs & two of Her Majtys Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date ye. Twenty fifth day of April last there was passed & removed into their said Parish from ye. Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the County of Middx One Jane Miller Widdow And yt yor. Peticonrs. by reason pray a Day this Quarter Sessions to be heard Upon ye. sd Appeal against the Order of the said [...] Justices.

And Yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc

Samll. Fletcher William Loyd} [...] Church Wardens

John Osborne Overseers John Shermyam
