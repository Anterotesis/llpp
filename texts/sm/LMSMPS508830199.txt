To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant Esquire and Philip Neve Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of March One thousand seven Hundred and Ninety three Susannah Clancy and Child William were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Susannah Clancy and Child

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices do therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jas. Fisher Sollicitor for the Appellants
