To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Gardn. and Ely Rents, in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn being that part of the said Parish which lies below the Barrs in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of James Spagg and Burf Camper Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st. day of November One thousand seven hundred and seventy four. Richard Keys and Martha his Wife and their two Children vizt. Joseph Keys of the Age of Six years and an half or thereabouts and John Keys of the Age of three Weeks or thereabouts, were removed from the Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents, as the place of the legal Settlement of the said Richard Keys and Martha his Wife, and their said two Children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggriev'd and against the said order to appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that the Benefit of their Appeal may be Saved unto them until the next general Session of the Peace to be holden for the County of Middlesex aforesaid And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the said next Sessions for the hearing and determining of their said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Cox Sollr. for Appettrs.

Exd. J. C
