To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardn. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order the Hands and Seals of Mich Downs Esqr. & Wm. Tyler Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Libty of Westmr in the County of Middlesex bearing rate the 24th. Day of March last Elizabeth Ann Savage Wife of Wm. Savage gone from her, and her Child Ann Elizabeth born 4th. Feby. 1795. were removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate us the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order Do humbly appeal against the same And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions that all parties concerned may attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
