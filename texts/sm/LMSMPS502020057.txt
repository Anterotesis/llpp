To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margarett Lothbury in the city of London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the hands seals of Matthew Hewett and Thomas Pindar Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middle (Quarter Unns)bearing date the Eight day of March last past Elizabeth Lee with Jemina her Daughter aged about nine years were removed from the parish of St. Sepulchre in the County of Middle aforesaid into the said parish of St. Margarett Lothbury in the city of London as the place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitionrs: conceiveing themselves aggrieved [...] the said Warrant and Indgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeale to your Worppd therefrom and pray That your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And order That the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor the said parish of St. Sepulchre may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Indictment and Determinaton of your Wopssal touching their appeale

And your Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
