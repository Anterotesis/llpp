To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Sessions Assembled by Adjournmt.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardns. & Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clement Danes

Sheweth That by an Ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quorum Unns) dated the fifth day of Decemr. last Susanna Heard Widw. of John Heard & Martha her Child aged abt. tenn yeares were removed from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepny in the sd. County of Middx to the Pish of St. Clement Danes as the legall Settlemt. of the sd. Susanna Heard the Mother in her owne right

Chimmon Your Petrs. therefore thinks themselves aggreived by that pte of the sd. Ordr. of the sd. two Justices whereby Martha the Child of the sd. Susanna Heard was removed to the sd. Pish of Saint Clemt. Danes & therefore appeale therefrom to the Judgemt. of this honoble Court And humbly pray that this Appeale may be Adjourned over to the next Sessions & that a day may be appointed for the hearing thereof

And yor. Petrs. shall pray etc
