To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church -Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass warrant under the hands and Seals of [---] Herbt. Mayo and N Black more Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth Day of November last John Wynne was removed and conveyed from the [---] Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saml Mary le Bone as the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal there from

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Hear them in the Premises and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to Hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Etc

Thos Martin Sor for Appellants No:3 Newman street
