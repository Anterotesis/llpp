To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Jno: Ellis & Lawrence [...] St Lo Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (quor Unus) bearing date the fifth day of November 1720: Ann Storey aged about 24. Years was past on yor. Petrs. from the parish of St James within the liberty of Westmr. and County of Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement

That Yor Petrs. apprehend themselves aggrived by the ordr. of the sd. two Justices and appeale to yor Worpps therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore pray yor. Wopps to appoint a day to here and determine the Said Appeall

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
