To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Robert Shenton Scavenger of the Middle Liberty of St. John Cheese and the rest of the Inhabitants of the Same Liberty in the Parish of St. Sepulcher in the sd. County

That Mr. Wainwright the Cryer of this Court is rated 9s.4d P Anne towards cleansing and carrying away the Duct and Soyh from about Hicks Hall and other places of the said Liberty And that the said Mr. Wainwright doth refuse to pay the sd Assessment Alledging that Mr. Hart the late Cryer of this Court did never pay any Such rate.

That the said Mr. Hart was rated us aforesd. and allwares duly paid the Same which wee whose names are here [...] Subscribed are ready to make appeare to yor. Worspps.

Rai to Broodman John Wescott Moses Coldwett Berytton Edward Prishar Richard Coles

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that the sd money Mary be paid by Such Waier and meaner as unto yor. Worpps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
