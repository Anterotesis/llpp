To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx [...] [---] in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Lambeth in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That by Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Railton & Henry Trent Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing Date the Ninth Day of June last past Mary Mason Single woman was removed from the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Lambeth as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Peticoners therefore [...] [...] (thinking themselves agrieved [...] [...] ) most humbly appeal against the said order and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. may be ordered to attend this honble Court to shew Cause why the said Order should not be vacated

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
