To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled their Genll: Sessions-

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew holborne for the Liberty above the Barrs in the said County

Sheweth

That by a Pass warrant or Order under the hands & seales of Sr. Isaac Tillard Knt: & Samuel Benson Esqr:- two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx aforesaid (Quorum Unus) bearing date the twenty fourth day of July now last past Edward Harris and Ann his wife were removed or passed form the [...] Hamlet of Bethnall Green in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney ats Stebun heath in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew holborne above the Barrs as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Edward Harris

Your Peticoners finding themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Bethnall Green and all other Parties concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale
