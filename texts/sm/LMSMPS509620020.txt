To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General [---] Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of N Conant and P. Neve Esquires. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of July 1799. Thomas Hawkins was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

W.S: Lemage Solr for the Appellants:
