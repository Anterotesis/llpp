To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Hester Hedman of the Hamblett of Wapping Stepny in the said County Widow

Sheweth That your Petitr hath applied herselfe to the Churchwarden for relife who refuses to allow her any

That Yor Petitr is afficted with the Palsy and almost blind and troubled with fitts and hath neither lodging or Subsistance and is ready to Perish in the Streets.

Your Petitr therefore humbly. prays your Worshipps will. be pleased to allow her some imediate relief in this her Extremity.

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc H: Hedman
