To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County in their Genll Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Woodbridge Conble of the Township of New Brentford in the said County

Sheweth That Whereas James Clitherow Esqr. Lord of the Mannor of Boston cum Brentford wherein the said Township doth lie hath not lately Held a Court Leet in his said Manner nor is likely Speedily to held one whereby Conbles May be Chosen

And Whereas Samuel Fox Appointed likewise a Conble for the said Township hath been some time Dead

Your Petitioner Having Served the said Office for the year last past Humble prays that he may be discharged form his said Office and that Other Constables may be Appointed in his Room & the Room of the said Samuel Fox A List of Proper persons fit to Serve the said Office being annexed hereto

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray

Willm: Woodbridge
