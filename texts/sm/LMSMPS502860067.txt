To the Right Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeals of the Churchwarden & Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Blooms sbury in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order mider the hands & seals of Richard Manley And Thomas Robe Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quar unus) bearing date the 25th. day of Septer. Last George Joseph with Elizabeth his wife were removed from the Liberty Glasshouse yard in the parish of St. Botolph Aldersgate without, in the said County to the said Parish of St. George Bloomsbury as the Place of their last legall Settlement.

Yor. Petr. think themselves agrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices appeals to this Court against the same & humble pray Yor. Worsps will be pleased to appoint a day for Determining the same.

And Yor. Petrs shall ever Pray Etc

Dan Bolton [x]
