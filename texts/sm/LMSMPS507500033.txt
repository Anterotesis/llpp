Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removed under the hands and Seals of [...] D: Wilmot and J: Ponleuge Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty sixth day of December last whereby Margaret Frammingham Wife of James Frammingham (who is above) and her two Children names Ann aged Five years and upwards and James aged two years, were removed and conveyed from & out of the Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St: James Westminster as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby you Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the Sd. order and Pray that your Worships would appoint some day in the present Session for the Churchwardens and overseers of th Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch to appear before you to hear & abide the Judgement and Determination of their Court touching this Appeal.

Jas: Dyson Solr: for the Appellants
