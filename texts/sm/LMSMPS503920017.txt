To the Rt. Worshipfull His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition & Appelae of Thomas Fielder of Hampstead in the sd. County Coachmaster

Sheweth That Yor Petr. was on the 19th. day of Octor. last Convicted upon the Information of Thos. Grant Gent Exhibited the Sixteenth day of Septemr. then last past before John Poulson and Philip Dyot Esqrs. two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County for having within three Months then last Past made use of one Coach not being a Stage Coach without giving at the next Excise Office such Notice in Writing of the said coach so used & paying such rate for Duty for the same as by the Statute in such Case made he ought to have given and Paid and adjudged to Pay the Sum of Twenty Pounds for the said Offence

Yor. Petr. thinks himself Aggrieved wth. the said Conviction and Judgment Appeals to this Court agt. the same & humbly prays such relief herein as upon bearing the Matter you shall think reasonable to give

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc Tho. Fielder
