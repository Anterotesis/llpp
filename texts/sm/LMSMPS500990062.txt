To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the poor for the Comty of Middlx.

Sheweth The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Sae County of Midds.

That by a Warr under the hands and Seales of Ger: Ireton and John Metcalfe Esqrs. two of her Maties. Justice of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the tenth day of June last past One Mary Bonner a Child of the ape of Ten years was passed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the same County to the Parish of St James Clerkenwell aforesaid: therein alleadging that the Said Mary Bonner was borne there and there her late [...] Edwd Bonner [...] was a Setled: house holder.

Yor. Peticonrs. finding themselves agreeved with the Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace humbly Appeal to this honble. Court against the said Wart. one Judgment of the Said two Justices of the peace And humbly prayes your Worpps will be Pleased to grant an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the St. Parish of St. Andrew holborn and all Partyes concern'd may attend this honble. Court this Prsent Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and finall determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc
