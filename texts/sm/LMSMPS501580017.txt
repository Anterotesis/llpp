To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of William Taylor.

Humbly Sheweth

That for the Fact whereof your Petitr: Stood Indicted in this Court this present Sessions Your Worshipps were pleased to Fine him Twenty Pounds And for Non payment thereof to Comitt him to New Prison in this County Where he now [...]
