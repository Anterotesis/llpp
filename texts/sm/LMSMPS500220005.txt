To the Right Worll: their Maties Justices of the for the County of Middx

The humble Peticonr of the Churchwardens & others the Inhabitants of the pish of Kensington in ye sd County

Sheweth

That by an Order heretofore made in this Court the Towne of Kensington is to be pavee wth gravell or Stone

May it please yor worps the last Summer proved soe well & the towne being very dirty it was Imposible to pforme ye sd Order wthout an Extraordinary Charge

Your petionr therefore pray (the prmises Considered that you would be pleased to respect the Said Order within the [...] next Spring

And ye petr shall on pray Etc.
