To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in & for the sd. County Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wards. & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hand & Seal of Rich Whitworth one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westmr. in the sd County of Middlesex bearing Date the 22d Day of April Instant Mary Manwaring was Passed as a Vagrant from the Parish of St.George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westmr. to the sd Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petr. conceive them selves to be agrieved and agt. the sd Pass Warrant do Appeal

Wm Chimmow agt for Appell Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Short Day man be appointed this present Session to hear & Determine the matter touching the sd Appeal

And Your Petr shall pray Etc

Middx At the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the County afore sd [...] by adjornmt [---] on Tuesday the twenty eight Day of April in the Twentieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain France & Ireland Defender of the Faith

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of the George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westmr. and County of Middx have Notice of this Appeal & that they & all Persons concerned do attend this Court on Thursday [...] next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon [...] to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd Appeal

By the Court Waller
