To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstans Stepney in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Smith and George Garrett Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th day of May 1767 Sarah West Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of Saint Mary White Chaple in the said County of Middlesex unto the Hamlet or Vill of Mile End Old Town in the said County adjudging the said Hamlet or Vill of Mile End Old Town to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices of the Peace - Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying that it may be adjourned over untill the next Sessions to same the benefit thereof and for such other Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever Etc James Harrison Chchwden

Henry Brighton Henry Cole} overseers
