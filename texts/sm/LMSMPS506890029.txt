To the Rt. Worpll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Martha Baker Wife of Edwd. Baker now in her Matys service

Sheweth

That your. Petr. stands Indicted in this Houble Cort for a Riott Entering the house of One Wm. Badham & Assaulting One Mary Malvin As thereby Appeares That yor Petr. is very Poor & cannot try the Traverse On the sd. Indictment but intends to plead guilty to the Same

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays your Worspps to be pleased to Say Such small Fine On her as unto yor. Worspps shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.
