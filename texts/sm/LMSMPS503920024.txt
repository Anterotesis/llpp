To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Jutices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor, of ye. Parish of St. Bartholomew by ye. Exchange, in ye. City of London

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order, or Pass Warrant, under ye. Hands & Seals of James Clitherow & John Awsiter, Esqr, Two of His Majty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middx (one whereof being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty third day of December 1748, Henry Berrow, was Removed from the Parish of Ealing, in ye. County of Middx, unto ye. sd. Parish of St. Bartholomew by ye. Change, London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Bartholomew, to be the Place of his last legal Settlement.

Chas: Baynbridge Agent for Appellts. That your Petitioner conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of ye. sd. Two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to yor. Worships therefrom, & humbly pray, that you will be pleased to save unto them, ye. Benefit of this Appeal, to ye. next General or Quarter Session of ye. Peace, to be holden for ye. County of Middx, And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of Ealing, May appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determinacon of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Peticoners Shall pray Etc
