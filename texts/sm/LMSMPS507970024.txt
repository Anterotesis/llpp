To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the Session House in the said County in their General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Stephen Walbrook in the City of London.

Sheweth

That Joseph Dyson Esquire and John Clarke Esquire Acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex by an Order under their hands and Seals respectively bearing date the nineth Day of June 1785 Directed To the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex Did thereby require them to Remove and Convey Rebecca Blang and her Male Child (to wit) James aged Six Months into the said Parish of St. Stephen Walbrook.

That your Petitioners apprehend and are advised that they are greatly aggrieved by the said Order of Removal. And

Therefore your Petitioners humbly- appeal against the Same and pray That your Worships will please to make such Order therein as shall be just and Reasonable

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
