To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the [...] Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christchurch London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Pass order under the hands & Seals of Felix Feast Esqr and William Cotesworth Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the [...] (Quor unns) bearing date the Eight and twentieth day of May last past Margarett the wife of George parker with her two children William and Mary were removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Christchurch London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom And pray that your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this appeal

And your Petr shall ever pray Etca.
