To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or order Under the hands and Seals of Ebenezer Mussell Esqr. & Thomas Quarrill Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx bearing date the Twenty fifth Day of May last Mary Coiffeau (widow of Abraham Coiffeau her late husband deced) and their three Children viz. John Ages about Six Years Abraham aged about four years and Mary aged about three years were removed from and out of the parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middx into the said precinct of Saint Catherine as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petrs Concerning themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant humbly appeal to this Court against the Same and pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint as day for hearing & determining of the said Appeal

Clare Sollr for the Appellants}

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
