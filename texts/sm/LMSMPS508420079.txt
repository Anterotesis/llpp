To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx of the General Quarter Sessions held at the Sessions House Clerkenwell Green in and for the County of Middx on Monday the 12th. day of Janry 1789

The Humble Petition & Appeal of Joseph Attersoll Matter

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the 8th. day of Novr last was convicted on the Information of one William Boyd before John Slade and Thomas Ayliffe Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middx in the Sum of Â£100 for the your Petitioner had not kept his Com of a certain Steeping under Water for Forty Horces.

That your Petitioner conceiving himself aggrieved by such Conviction is advised to Appeal against such Conviction on the said Information in manner aforesd.

Therefore your Petitioner most humbly Prays Your Worships will be pleased to Permit him to enter his Appeal before your against such Conviction

And Your Petitioner & Appellant will ever pray Etc

Joseph Attersoll 12 Janry 1789
