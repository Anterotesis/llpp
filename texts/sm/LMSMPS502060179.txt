May it please your Honors John Moloney being bound in a Recongnizance to Appear before this Honorable Bench in May last by his Bail to Answer the prosecutor and since is Deceased So that his Bail cannot bring him to Answer the pretended Brice layd to his charge and being lately Sumoned by letter to appear this Sessions to Save such Recognizance from being Estreated us humbly hoped the said John Moleney being Dead the said Recognizance may be discharged and shall pray

Yor humble Supplysant Bryan Flanagan
