To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex at their general [---] Sessions of the Peace held in and for the said County of Middlesex Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Pass or Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of Benjamin Cox and Peter Hanck Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th day of November last past Elizabeth Toole sayed to be the Wife of Thomas Toole a Soldier and gone abroad and their two Children to witt Elizabeth Aged 10 Years & upwards and Sarah Aged 4 Years and upwards were removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell aforesaid as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Toole and her said two Children

By which said Pass or Order of the said Two Justices Your Petitioners & Appellants apprehend themselves Aggrieved and to Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore Your Petitioners & Appellants Pray that this Court will Appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Martin in the fields and all Persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal And that your Petitoners & Appellants may be relieved in the Premises as this Court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners & Appellants will ever pray Etc.

6th. Decr. 1762

Wm Johnson Jno: Christofors} Churchwardens
