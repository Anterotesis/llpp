To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Day the Twenty first Day of February last Mary Charleton Wife of Obadiah Charleton (gone from her) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the Parish of Saint James as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved And

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court.

And Your Petitr. shall Pray Etc

James Dyson Sollr. for the Appellts.
