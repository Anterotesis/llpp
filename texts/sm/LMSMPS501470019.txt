To the Rt. Wppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midx at Hicks Hall

The Humble peticon of Jane the wife of xavarion Harris of litle 2:n Street Limner & painter

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. Husband was one about ye 25 of July Last committed to Newgate for Refusing to take ye Oaths or pay as the Act of parliamt Din [...] That yor: petr: is a poor helpless Woman wth two small Children & nothing to support [...] But her Husbands Labour & by his confinement the & her said Children are in a very miserable condition wanting ye necessaries of Life

Yor Petr: Therefore most Humbly prays yor: Worships to take her poor & necetitons Condition into yor: tender & Compassionate consideration & grant her said Husband his Liberty that he may not parish in prison by Reason of his old age who is now about Seaventy two years

And yor: Petr: as in Duty Bound shall ever pray Etc
