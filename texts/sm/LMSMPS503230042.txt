To the Worshipfull the Chairman and others his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex as their Generall Sessions Assembled The Humble Petition of John Atiller a Prisoner in the House of Correction at Clerkenwell

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was before your Worships on the 12th day of June Last Try'd and found Guilty of a Riot for which he was Find 13s 4d and Committed to hard Labour for the Space of Twelve month's from the Said day and was further Order'd to find Such Sureties for his good behaviour for one Year after the Expiration of the Said Imprisonment as this Honble Court Should approve of

That Your Petrs has the two Person's whose Names and Additions are hence under written, Ready to Enter into a Recognizance for his good behaviour according to the Said Sentence

That the time of Your Petrs Said Imprisonent Expries on the 12th day of June next and this Honble Court not being to Sit (as your Petrs. humbly Apprehend's) till the 4th day of July [...]

Your Petr humble Pray's that your Worship's will Please to Penithian to be Bails before any one of Your Worships or any other of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said County that he May not be Longer confind than the Said Sentence directs, Or order Such other releif to your Petrs in the Premisses as to Your Worships Shall Seem meet

And he will Ever Pray John Miller
