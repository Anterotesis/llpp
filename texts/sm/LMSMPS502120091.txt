To the worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition & appeale of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the Pish of Sherborne in the County of Dorsett

Sheweth That by an Order made by Thomas Railton Esqr and Henry Segar Esqr. two of his [---] Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City & Libty of Westmr. in the said County of Middx bearing date the 29th: day of Aprill Last one Katherine Taylor was removed from St. Margaretts Westmr. in the sd City Libty & County to Sherborne in the County of Dorsett as her last legal place of Settlementfrom Against which order your Peticoners doe [---] appeal [---] unto this Court [---] apprhending. themselves greived thereby

Yor. Peticoners therefore pray this Honble: Bench to appoint a day for hearing their appeal
