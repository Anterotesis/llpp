To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Session [...] Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwn and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bot [...] without Aldersgate in the Said County

Sheweth That Upon an Order under the hands & Seals of [...] Richards & Ri. Farmer Esqrs. two of his Majesties Just [...] of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorn [...] bearing date the 17th. day of September last Thomas C [...] was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell [...] in the said County to the said Parish of St. Bottolph without Aldergate in ye. same County as ye. place of his last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr. thinking themselves agt [...] by the Judgment of the said two Jus [...] of the peace Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray your Worships would be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessio [...] for ye. Hearing & Determining ye. [...] same

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Danl. Prolton agent for the Appellants.
