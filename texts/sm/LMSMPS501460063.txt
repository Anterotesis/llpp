To ye Worshipfull ye Justices of Ye Peace at ye Quarter Sessions held at Hickes Hall for ye County Middx.

The Humble Petition of John Baker Bail for Richd. Seddon

Sheweth That all partys having agreed & a release executed by ye Prosecutor which a witness is ready to make oath on

Ye. Petr. therefore humbly be seeches p our worships that ye Recognizance may be withdrawn being working people that are concerned

And ye. Petr. as in Duty here sd shall ever pray etc.
