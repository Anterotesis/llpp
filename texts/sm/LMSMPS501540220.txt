To the Rt. Worppll his Majtes. Justices of the Peaces for the County of Middx in this Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petitcon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant nndr the hands and Seales of John Cleere and John William Esqrs. two of his Majtes. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quon unns) bearing date the 30th. day of June Anno Did 1716. Frances Elcock Singlewoman was past on yor petrs. from the parish of St. Gyles in the feilds in the sd. County of Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement

That yor Petrs. conceive them Selves aggrieved by the order of the sd two Justices & appeale age the secure to ye worpps

Yor Petr. therefore humbly hope yor worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the Said Appeale

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
