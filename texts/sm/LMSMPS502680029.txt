To the Rt Worspll: his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary le Bone in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt: under the hands & Seales of William Dobyns & Thomas Inwood [...] of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the twentieth day of April last past Th [...] Finch and four Children Vizt: Sarah Eleven years Elizabeth Eight years John three years and William one year & a Quar. old were removed from & out of the parish of Hendon in the said County to the said parish of St. Mary le Bone as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petr: thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale against the same.

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly pray that yor Worsps will be pleased to Order that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hendon may attend this Court some day this present Generall Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said appeale.

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
