To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or pass warrant under the hand and Seal of William Timbrell Esquire one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty Ninth day of March last Robert Emmes is Removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kensington as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Robert Emmes

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the said Justice Do humbly Appeal to this Court for such Releif in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Middlesex At a General Quarter Sessions of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hickshall in St. John Street in the County afsd. on Monday the fourth day of April in the thirty first year of the reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex aforesaid have Notice of this Appeal And That they and all persons Concerned Do Attend this Court at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same Court touching the said Appeal
