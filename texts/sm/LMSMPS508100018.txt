To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their general Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of James Croft and John Barnfather Esquires, two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of April last past Jane Peet aged about fourteen Years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London, as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order; and humbly pray Relief in the premises and that your worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present session for the hearing- and determining this their said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Cox Sold for appellants.
