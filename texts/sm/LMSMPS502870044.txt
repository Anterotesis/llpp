To the Right Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church warden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order the hands & Seals of William Jones & Samuel Grey Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the three and twentieth day of October last James Rowning a Child aged about 7 years was removed from & out of the parish of St. Martin in the fields in the said County to the said parish of St Giles in the fields as the place of his last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr. [...] think themselves agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray yor. Worp will be pleasd to appoint a day for hearing & Determining the owne

And yor. Petr: shall ever pray Etc

Dan Boltory Sollr p y [...] Appell
