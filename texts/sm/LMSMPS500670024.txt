To the Right Worspll. his Maties Justices of Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of William Maryson & William West of the Hamlet of Hamer Smith in the Sd County.

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner did Serve the office of Constable for the said Hamlet for the yeare last past And that your Peticoners have disbursed divers Sumes of money in releiving & passing of Cripples & vayabonds & a Rate having been made whereby to reimburse yor. Peticoners yet nevertheless John Bromley Richard Davenport & Priffin Widow wherhold & possess Lands within the Said Hamlet doe refuse to pay the Severall Sumes of money on them respectively assessed as aforesaid.

P S Westley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that the said John Bromley Richard Davenport & Widow Criffin may be compelled to pay the said Rate Or that they may attend yor. Worspps this prsent Sessions to show cause to the contrary.
