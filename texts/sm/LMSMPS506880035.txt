To The Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace Sitting at Hicks Hall in the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of James Bowman a Prisoner in New Prison.

Humbly Sheweth That Yor: Peticoner was Yesterday by this Honoble. Bench justly comitted to prison for delivering a Noat to one of the Grand Jury

And for as much as yor: Peticonr. did the same Ignorantly he Humbly beggs parden for the same

And prayes he may be discharged From his Imprisonmt.

Yor: Petr: shall ever pray
