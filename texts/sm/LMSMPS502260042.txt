To the Worshipl. his Majties Justices of the Peace in Quarter Sessions Assembled in and for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That Jane Dagger Singlewoman was by Order under the Hands of and Seals of John Fuller and Wm. Catesworth Esqrs. Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx bearing Dated the Nineteenth day of Janry. last removed from the parish [...] St James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx to the Said parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey as the place where the Said Jane Dagger was last legally Settled.

That Your Peticoners do humbly conceave they can make it appear to Your Worships that the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said Jane Dagger is not in the Said parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey.

Therefore Yor. Peticonrs. humbly Appeal against the Said Order And pray your Worships will be pleased to order the X wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Sd. parish of St James Clerkenwell to Attend this Court for the leaving the Sd. Appell Such day as Yor Worships shall think fitt.

Aud [...] Shall Ever [...]
