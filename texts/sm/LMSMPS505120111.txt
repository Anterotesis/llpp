To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church otherwise ChristChurch London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the Handsand Seals of Christopher Scott Esquire and Robert Pell Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (whereof one is of the Quorum) for the County of Middlesex aforesaid bearing date the 25th. day of May One Thousand seven hundred and sixty two, Mary Baker Singlewoman with her Female Child named Elizabeth and aged Eleven weeks born of her Body a Bastard our of lawfull matrimony was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechappel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church otherwise Christ Church London as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Baker and also her said Child for Nurture.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

James Fisher Money for the Appellants
