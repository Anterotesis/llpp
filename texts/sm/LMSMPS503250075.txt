To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their General Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of A Wescombe and John Rathbone Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of August 1729 Joseph Hall and Cartwright his Wife and Elizabeth their Child were Removed from the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County to the said Parish of St. James as the places of their last legal Settlemt Whereby your petitioners think themselves Aggrieved

And therefore humbly prays this Court will be Pleased to appoint a time for bearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may this attend

And your Petrs. Shall Pray Etc

Saml Leddon agent for the Appellts.
