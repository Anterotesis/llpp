To the Honourable Bench

The Humble Petition of William Wynn one of the High Constable of the Hundred of Spellhorn Sheweth that your Petitioner Haveing Serv'd the Office three year Humbly Hopes your Worships will Discharge him and your Petitioner as in Duty Bound shall ever pray

Wm: Wynn

N.B Mr. Richard Blanchett Yeoman of Hampton Middx. is a proper person to serve and Approv'd on by Mr. Justice Goodchild
