To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Qr. Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Margtt Westmr in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Wm. Gore and Wm. Conne Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace of and for the [...] fily and Liberty of Westmr and [...] said County of Middx bearing Date the Sixth day of May last Ann Smith was Remove from and out of the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County aforesaid to the Said Parish of Saint Margarett Westmr. as the place of her Lawfull Settlement

That yr Petrs thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly Appeal to your Worsps. against the Same

Therefore humbly pray your Worsps. would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of Sr. Martin in the Fields in this County may Attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sesss. to hear and Abide the Judgment and final Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
