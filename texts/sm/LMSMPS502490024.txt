To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Martin one of the petty Constables of ye parish of Southmims in this- County

Sheweth

That at Christmas last past there was due and owing to yor. Petr. the Sume of ten pounds for one quarters Salary for his less of- time Charge and Expences in passing and- Conveying of Vagrants through this County-

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymts. of the said ten pounds to yor. sd Petr. for ye sd Service

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Thomas Martin
