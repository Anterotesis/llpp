To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Michaelmas General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint John Horsley down in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and William Quarrill Esquries two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st. day of October In the year of our Lord 1783. Edward Blogg, Maundy his wife, and their two Children vizt. Agnes Ann aged about Eighteen Years and Edward John aged about Nine Years was removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of St John Horseley down in the County of Surrey as the place of their last legal settlement; whereby Your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggreived and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the benefit of this appeal might be saved unto them and that all persons concerned might be ordered to attend at the next Session to abide the determin of the Court there on

And Your Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc
