To the Right Worspr. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Sybella Pepwell Wife of Ephraim Pepwell

Sheweth That this day Yor. Worsppr were pleased to comitt yor. Petrs. the House of [...] Cecrow for being taken out of a Suspected house That your Petr. is Marryed to the Sd. which your Petr. Mother now ready in Court can testily And forasmuch as your Petr. Sd Mother will for the [...] lude take particular came of your Petr.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayer yor. Worspp to be pleased to Grant that She may be discharged from out of the House of [...] Corwrond aforesd

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

P Tho. Nash
