To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Sir James Esdaile Knight and William Blackborow Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty eighth Day of November last Elizabeth Harper Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last Legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices [...] said Order and [...] that [...] Worships [...] the hearing and determining the said Appeal and [...] Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court.

Jno. Dorman Richd. Nowlson} Churchwardens

Banfield, J Wall} and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
