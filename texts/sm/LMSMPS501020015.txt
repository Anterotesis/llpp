To the Honble the Bench of Justices att Hickes hall for the County of Middx.

The humble Petcon: of Thomas Walter Gent

Sheweth That yor. Petr. being indited before this Honble Bench for words reflecting on the Worppfull Ms: Justice Dyott; For which yor. Petr. hath askt the Pardon of this Honble Bench and the Said Me: Justice Dyott and is Sorry for his Said Offence. And whereas this Honble Bench hath fined yor. Petr: in the Same of Five Pounds for the Said Offence.

Yor. Petr. humbly hopes this Honble Bench will [...] att the Said Fine

And Yor. Petr: Shall Pray Etc
