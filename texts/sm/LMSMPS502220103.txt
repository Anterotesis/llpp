To the Rd. Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx all their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace all Hick shall Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Botolph Aldersgate in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Order or Warrant under the hands & Seales of Alexandr Ward and John Hattings Esqr: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of middx (Quor un) bearing date the Third day of September last Elizabeth Butler a Single woman was by the said Order Removed from the Parish of St: Andrew Holborne above Barrs in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of St: Botolph by Aldersgate as the place of the last leg [...] Settlement of the said Elizabeth Butler Where by yer: Petrs: humbly Comeire they are wronged & Aggreived

Your Petrs: there fore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day to hear and Determine the said Appeal & That Your Petrs: may be Reseiveet as unto your Worpps: shall seem meet

And Your Peticonrs: Shall ever Pray & Etc
