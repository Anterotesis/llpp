To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Hathaway Convicted by the Name of John Alloway of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex Baker

Sheweth That your Petitioner was convicted on the fifteenth day of July one thousand seven hundred and ninety six by Thomas Barnard Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County on the complaint Richard Osman for having unlawfully exposed to Sale on the fourteenth day of July last One Quarter of a Pick Leaf of Wheaten Bread deficient in weight Seven three penny Loaves of Wheaten Bread deficient in wright four two penny loaves of Wheaten Bread deficient in wright and Six two penny loaves of Wheaten Bread deficient in weight according to the Assize set for such Bread and your Petitioner was adjudged to pay and forfeit the Sum of three Pounds fifteen Shillings for the same

That your Petitioner conceives himself to be aggrieved by the said Conviction and humbly appeals against the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
