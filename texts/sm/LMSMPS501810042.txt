Middx: ss To the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Psh St: Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx & to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Sattron Hill Hatton [...]

To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx Assembled in Quarter Sessions'

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens' and Overseers of the poore of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents [...] In the parish of St. Andrew Holborne it in the Said County

Sheweth That by a Passe Warrant under the hands' and Seales of Wm. Booth and F. Crosbie Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid (Qu [...] unus) bearing date the 17th day of June last past Eilizabeth Eld [...] Wife of Richard Eldridge deceased with her three Children (viz [...] ed about Eleven Years William Aged about Nine Years' and [...] about two Years were removed and brought from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County to the said Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton, garden and Ely Rents as the last place of their legall Settlement.

Your Peticoners thinck themselves Aggrieved with the Indgment and order of the said two Justices, humbly Appeale to this Court against the Same, And pray that the Churchwardens and Over- seers of the poore of the Said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all other parties concerned may be ordered to Attend this Court Some day this Session's to hear and abide the Judgment and deter in macon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Your Peticoners' at in duty bound shall pray Etc
