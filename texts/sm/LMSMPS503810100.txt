To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of them Village of last Langton in the parish of Church Langton in the County of Leicester

Sheweth

That by order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Thomas Ellys Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (whereof one is of the Quorum) Dated the 21st. day of September 1747 for the removing of Jane Andrews Widow from the parish of St Margaretts Westmr. in the County of Middx to the sd Village of East Langton in the parish of Church Langton in the sd County of Leicester (but called in the sd Order of Removal the place precinct or parish of East Langton in the County of Leicester

That your petitioners humbly Apprehends they are aggreived by the sd Order of Removal and Appeals against the sd Order

Your Petitioner humbly prays your Worshipps to appoint some short day for the sd Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St Margaretts Westmr: in the sd County of Middx and all other Psons concerned to Attend the sd Court and hear the sd Appeal

and your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Frans. Higgs Sollr. for the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of the sd. Village of East Langton in the parish of Church Langton in the sd County of Leicester
