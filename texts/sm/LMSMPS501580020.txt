To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Bennett of the precinct of of St: Katherine in the said County Widow

Sheweth. That your Petr. is an inhabitant in the precinct and has four Small Children and in a very poor and miserable Condicon

That your Petr. some time agoe made her Complaint to the Overseers of the poor of the said precinct for some releife wch: they refused to give yor. Petr. therefore made her application to the Worsppfull Robert Dennett Esqr: who was pleased to send an Order to the said Overseers to allow your Petr: five shillings P Month for the releife of Your Petr: and her four small Children wch: they refuse to pay.

Your Petr: therefore humbly Prays your Worships to Order the Overseers of the poor of the said precinct to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to shew Cause why they refuse to pay your Petr: pursuant to the said Order

And your Petr: shall ever Pray Etc
