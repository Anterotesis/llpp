To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Scott and P. Neve Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of Decr. 1798 Elizabeth Christopher & her female Child named Martha aged (one year and- Ten Months wife of John Christopher then absoud from her were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices Appeal to your Worships therefrom & humbly Pray that is you will be Pleased to hear them in the Premises & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the fields may Appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to Abide the Judgment & Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray
