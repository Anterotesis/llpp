To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matgs Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeals of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of st Martins within the Citty of Worcester

Sheweth

That by Order Dated the fourteenth Day of Aprill Instant under the hands and Seales of hoo of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor urns) One Sarah Lewis wife of Every Lewis Decd was Removed from the Parish of St Anne in the Liberties of Westminster in the County of Middx to the aforesaid Parish of st martin as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Sarah Lewis

That your Petitor: find themselves arrived by these Order of the said two Justices of the pence and Appeal against the same to this Court But for asmuch as your Petitrs: Evidence by reason of the Distante of the Place cannot attend this Court this present Sessions

Your Petitors: therefore humbly pray your Worpps will be to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Anne within the Liberty of Westminster to attend this Court some Day next Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal And yor Petitor: shall over pray etc.
