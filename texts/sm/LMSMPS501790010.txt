To the Right Worpll this Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generals Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of John Messuard

Sheweth That on ye. Sixth day of April in the year 1716 yor Petr was bound Apprntice to one Frederick Richard of St Martin an ye Fields Lapidary for this Tern of Seven Years to Learn ye Art of a Lapidary

That his said Master hath not a flowed ye. Peticon Sufficient Meat drink washing Etc Loading but often times went into the Countrey for his Pleasure & Sometimes continued there for Some months leaving yor Petr a Charge to the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields altho he had Seven pounds paid him by your Petrs father

That upon Complaint made ye Peticonr [...] of his sd. Masters usage to the Worpll. Justice Milner he Granted a Warrant agt. this sd. Master but he end [...] not be taken and is now Clone Peconr in the Fleet Pusion for Debt

Yor: Petr. humble Prayes the Primisses considered thas the May be discharged from his sd Master so that he may serve ye recidue of the sd Term [...] wth. some other person useing the same To ade [...]

And yor. Peticonr (as in Duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc
