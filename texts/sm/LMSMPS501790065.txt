To the Worhippfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. John of Wapping in the Sd County of Middx

Sheweth Bass To yor Worshipps that by an Order under the hands and Seals of Robt. Dennett & Samll. Perry Esqrs. Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the Sd. County of Middx. (Quorum Unus) bearing date ye 14th. day April 1719 whereby Dorothy Holland ye Widd & Relict of Cha. Holland Deced was removed from & out of ye Precinct of St Katharines in ye sd. County to ye parish of St John of Wapping as ye Place of her last legall Settlemt. in right of her sd. late Husband whereby yor. Petitionrs & Appellants humbly conceive themselves aggreived & apply to this Court for releife

Therefore humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to appoint some day this present Quart Sessions, for ye Overseers of ye Poor of ye Sd. Precinct of St Katharines & all parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Determination & Judgemt. thereof touching the sd. Appeal

And yor Petitionrs & Appellants as in Duty bound shall pray Etc
