To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant Esquire and John Scott Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty sixth day of February one thousand seven hundred and ninety three Margaret aged near ten years and Sarah Aged near eight years the Children of David and Ann Clark deceased were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex and delivered to your Petitioners being the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate in the said City of London (the said Justices Adjudging the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Margaret and Sarah to be in the said Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate) whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves tobe aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determination thereof

Wm Chetham Solr. for the Appellants }
