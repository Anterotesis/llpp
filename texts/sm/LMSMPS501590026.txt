To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and appeale of Henry Wood of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth

That your Petr is rated towards the [...] of the poor of the said parish Twelve Shillings and Six peace P ann and forasmuch as you petr rents or Small house of two pounds [...] and fifteen Shillings P ann and is rated after the rate of five pounds for the Same, Therefore your petr thinks himself aggreived by the Said Rate and appeales against the Same

Your petr. prays your Worpr to releive him in the premises as your Worps Shall Seem meet.

And he Shall pray Etc.
