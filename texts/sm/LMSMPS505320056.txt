To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Bottolph Bishopsgate without in the Liberty of the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of on Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir John Fielding Knight and William Kelynge Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Day of 1764 Elizabeth Eades Single Woman was removed from the Parish of Enfield in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Bottolph a Bishopsgate without in the Liberty of the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Eades Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Pettiioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships to Appoint a Day in this Present Session, to hear and Determine their said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

N: Spry-Attorney for the Appellants
