To the Right Worshipfull his Matice Justices of the Peace Assembled at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petion of Daniell Newcomb Gents of the pish of newington in the County of Middx

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Rated 10s upon An Act for granting to his Matie Rates & dutyes upon Houses from which Rate your Peticonr humbly Appeales to this Court

Your peticonr humbly Prays that he may discharged of the said Rate he being onely a Lodger in Madam Thompsens house who is Rated 10s alsoe for ye same And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc

Daniell Newcombe

Peticon of Daniell Newcomb dd
