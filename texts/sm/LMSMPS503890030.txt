To his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

Worshipful Sirs

The Petition of Ann Hornby of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourn in the Said County

Humbly Sheweth

That Whereas your Petitioner is a Prisioner in the new Prison at Clerkenwell for a Fine of Fourteen Shillings levied by your Worshipes the Sessions before this last, and your Petitioner's Husband having laboured under a long Series of Illness, and is now so very infirm, which renders him incapable of paying the said Fine, or even of allow ing me ye common Necessaries to support Life, (having two small Children) & am thereby become a real Object of Pily

Your Petitioner therefore humbly implores the Releasement

And Your Petitioner as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc.

Ann Hornby
