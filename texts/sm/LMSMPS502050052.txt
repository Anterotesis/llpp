To the Rt Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the parish of Chelsea in the said County.

Sheweth That by warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Combe and william Gore Esqr Two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) Margarett Cunningham aged about Sixty years widow of John Cunningham Deceased was removed from the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in this County to the said parish of Chelsea As the place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Petrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and Appeal against the same.

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day this Sessions for all partys to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And yor petrs shall ever pray Etc
