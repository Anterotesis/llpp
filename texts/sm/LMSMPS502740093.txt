To the right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kingston upon Thames in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by W [...] under the hands and Seals of John [...] Milner and Richard Gifford Esqrs. two of the Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) dated the Second day of March last Elizabeth Clarke aged Eleven Years daughter of Thomas Clarke by Elizabeth his wife both deced (who on is alledged in the said Order) hath lately intruded herself into the said Parish of St. Giles in the feilds and was likely to become chargeable to the same, was Remov'd from the said Parish of St: Giles in the fields to the said Parish of Kingston upon Thames as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners finding themselves agreiv'd by the said Warrt. or Order of Removal of the said two Justices, humbly Appeal to the Court agt. the same.

Wm: Heath Agent for the Appellants Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore humbly Pray, that this Court will be pleas'd to appoint a day for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Giles in the Feilds, and all other Persons concern'd to attend this Court this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt. & determination of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal, and that the said Appellants may be Releiv'd therein according to Law.

And Your Petrs. Shall Pray Etc
