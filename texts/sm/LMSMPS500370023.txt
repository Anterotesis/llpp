To the Right Worll: their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the poor of Acton in the [...] County

Sheweth

That by a Warrant under the Hands & Seals of sr John Elwes Knt & Richards Woodware Esqr two of their Maties: Justies of the peace for the Said County Mary Noakes wth Judith Noakes &John Noakes her Children were on the Sevententh day of August last past passed from the pish of Ealing als Zealing to the pish of Acton in the Said County alleadging that John Noakes husband of the Said Mary Noakes & father of the Said Judith & John Noakes in his life time was last Legally settled at the sd pish of Acton

Your peticoner: finding themselves agreived by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humble Appeals [...] to this Honble Bench & pray that you will Order the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of Ealing als Zealing may attend here in this Court on a day certaine to shew cause why the Said Mary Noakes & her two Children should not be [...] repassed to the said pish of Ealing als Zealing

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc.
