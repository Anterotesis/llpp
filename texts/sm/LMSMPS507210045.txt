To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwandens and Overseers af the Poor of the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Notice of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Sherwood and Jasper Clarke Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirtieth day of December now last past Mary Petty the Wife of Giles Petty and her Child were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint George as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Petty and her Child

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same, And pray your Worships will Appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining the Said Appeal, And your Petitioners will every pray Etc.

Major Wright Sollicitor for the Appellants
