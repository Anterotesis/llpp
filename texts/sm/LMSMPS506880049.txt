To the Right Worspl. her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of William Turnor.

Sheweth

That in the year 1697 your Petr. Served the Office of Churchwarden for the parish of St. John Wapping in this County and by an order of this Honble Court made in the thirteenth year of the Reign of his late Maty King William the third Year Petr. was there ordered to pay to Thomas Fryer upper Churchwarden of the said parish the sume of Â£34.15s.11d which Sume in pursuance of the said order your Petr. accordingly paid & tooke the receipt of the said Thomas Fryer for the Same But your Petr. hath not as yet been reimbursed the Same from the Said parish.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspp to be pleased to order that the present Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor the Said parish may appeare in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to shew cause why your Petr. should not be paid and satisfyed the said moneys.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

P Tho: Nash
