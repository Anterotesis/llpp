To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Rickmersworth in the Liberty of Saint Albans in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hand and Seal of Philip Dyot Esquires one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the twenty fourth Day of February last a Person therein called Mary Larken was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish Place or Precinct of Charley Wood in the County of Hertford as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Therefore your Petitioners humbly pray your Worships will appoint a short Day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal.

Henry Wankford Dan Parrett Stephen Satter} Churchwardens

Danl Hower his William [x] Wrainch Mark} Overseers
