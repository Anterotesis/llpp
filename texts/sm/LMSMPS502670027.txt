To the Right Worshipll: His Majestye Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx at she in Genl: Quar. Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble Petition of John Waylett of Gent Street Ward in the parish of St. Gyle's without Cripplegate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That your petitioner in Rated Two pounds ten Shillings P annum for & towards the necessary Releif of the poor of the said parish of Saint Gyle's without Cripplegate for this prsent year 1730

That yor: Petition apprehend he pays on are in Proporton than there it of his Neighbours who are of ye. like or better ability & Circumstances, therefore thinks himself aggrieved by ye. said Rate, and humbly Appeal against ye. same, Pray nigt such Relief in ye. pr in, as to yor. Worpps shall seem Meet

And Yor peticoner shall Ever pray & ca. John Waylett
