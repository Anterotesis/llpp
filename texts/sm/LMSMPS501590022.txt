To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of Hugh Letherd of the Parish of St James Clerkewel in the said County

Sheweth

That your peticoner hath Lived in the sd Parish for these five & years & upwards & was never rated in the poors rate thereof untill this prsent year

That your Peticoner is a poor Labouring man & hath a wife & two Children to mainteyne. by means where of & his poor Circumstances. he is not able to pay to the said poors rate and appeals against the same

Your Petitr therefore humbly. prays he may be wholly Discharged from paying to the sd poor rate.

And Yor Petr shall Ever prayer Hugh Letherd
