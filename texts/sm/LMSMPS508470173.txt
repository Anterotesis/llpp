To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Brides in the City of London.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Sampson Wright and Nicholas Bond Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the Eleventh Day of June 1789 Elizabeth Chambers Singlewoman was Certified from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Brides London as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to appoint a Time for hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may be then Attend.

A. Bradley Sollicitor for the Appellants }
