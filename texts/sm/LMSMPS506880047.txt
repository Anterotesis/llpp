The humble Peticon of William Turner.

Sheweth

That In the year 1697 yor. Petr served the Office of Churchwarden for the pish of St. John Wapping in this County and Gyan Order of this Honble Court made in the Thirteenth Year of the Reign of his Late Maty King William the Third Yor. Petr. was thereby Ordered to pay to Thomas Fryer Upper- Churchwarden of the sd. pish the sum of Â£34.15s.11d. Which sum in pursuance- of the sd. Order your petr. accordingly pd [...] tooke the Receipt of the sd. Thomas- Fryer for the same But your petr hath not as yett been Reimbursed the same from the sd. Pish.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that the prsent Churchwds & Ovseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish may- appeare in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to shew cause why Your Petr. should not be pd. sattisfyed the sd Moneys.

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

P Thos: Nash
