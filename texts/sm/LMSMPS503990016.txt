To the Worshipfull his Majties - Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of John Wilkins

Sheweth That your petitioner having formerly Agreed to amend and keep in Repair the Pavement on a certain Publick Bridge called Brentford Bridge in this County. Your Petitioner in Consideration thereof was to be allowed & paid the sum of Nine Pounds Yearly

That there is [...] due to your Petitioner the sum of Nine Pounds at Michaelmas last.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Worships to make an Order I on Mr. Higgs Treasurer of this County for the Payment of the same

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray

John Wilkins
