To the Rt Worshipsfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Session

The Humble Petition & Appeals of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middx Quor unus bearing date the third day of May instant Mary Robinson Widow was Removed form the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the said County to the Sd Parish of St. Dunstan in the West as the Place of her last Legall Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. think themselves Agreived by the Order of the Sd. two Justices & hope to make it appear that the Sd Mary Robinson hath not any Settlemt. in the Said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West And Therefore as Appeale from the Sd. Order to the Judgmt. of this Court

Your Petr [...] all the pleased to Appeal two day Maj [...] Sessions to was Settlement the mater touching this Appeal
