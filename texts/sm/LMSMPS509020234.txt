To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies and H. Reynett two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midddlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing date the Nineteenth day of September in the year of our Lord One thousand seven hundred and Ninety four Mary Cox Spinster was removed from the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint John at Hackney alledging the said Parish of Saint John at Hackney to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Edmonton in the County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the Appeal. And your Petitioners as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc.

Richard Dann Agent in the Appellants }
