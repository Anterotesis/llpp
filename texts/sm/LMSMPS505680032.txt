Middlesex To the Worship full the Chairman and other his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Daniel Collins of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County Weaver

Sheweth That your petitioner (as appears by a conviction fyled in this Honourable Court) was on the Eleventh Day of this Instant February Convicted before Sir John Fielding Knight One of the Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex of buying and receiving Two Bunces of Silk the Property of Samuel Lawrence of the Parish of Christ church Spital Fields in the said County Master Silk Weaver and by him Delivered to Arnold Sassere to be manufactured

That Your Petitioner thinks himself greatly aggrieved by the said Conviction and therefore Appeals against the same and every Part there of and humbly prays that the said Conviction may be Quashed with such reasonable Costs and Charges as he hath Sustained by reason of the Premisses

And as in Duty bound shall our pray Etc.

Daniel Collin
