To the Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Mansfield

Sheweth That one Hanna Grantland was bound over to appear at the last Sessions of the peace held for this County and then & these indicted for a notorious cheat in defrauding your Peticoner

That the Said Hanna Grantland did not at all appear at the Said Sessions to gave her Baile & to plead to the Said Indictment but hath Since meerly for delay & pure vexation Superseded the warrant or process of this honble Cort. by giving in fresh Baile without any notice to yor. Peticoner which Baile (as yor. peticonr. is credibly informed are insufficient, and the Said Hanna Grantland Still hitherto to Stands out in contempt

Yor. Peticonr therefore most humbly prayes that the Said Recognizance forfeited the last Sessions as aforesaid may be forthwith estreated, and such further order had in this matter as to your wisdom shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Hanna Grantland Jud ult 2 rec ibm 71 rec Jany 77
