To the Right Worspr. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Eliz: Weston of the Parish of Hampstead in the Sd. County Window

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. Joth now [...] receive as a [...] Pencon of and from the Sd. pish the sum of 6d. P weeke But in regard She being now reduced to extreame poverty and aged 72 Yeares and very weake and mary in body and almost dim of [...] Sight and altogether past her Labour And haveing requested the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the sd. pish to allow her further releife but they refuse for to doe Soe that She must meritably pish unless comisserated and releived by this Honble Court

P [...] Luston Westley

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor Worpps to consider the sad and deplorable [...] pndicon wherein She now laugnesth and to he pleased to Ordr. the Churchwardens & Overseers of his Poore of the Sd. pish to allow yor. Petr. Such further pencord toward her releife & mainteynance [...] your worpps Shall go in meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall [...] Pray Etc.

Elizabeth Weston her [x] mke
