To the Honoble Wm. Cooper Esqr Chairman and to the rest of the- Bench now sitting at Hicks Hall in St. John's Street in the County of Middx

The humble peticonr of Edward Bevan

Sheweth

That your petr. was bound to appear this Ensuing Sessions as Baile for Mr. Jacob Tinney for an Assault as by a sumons sent by this Honble. Court

Therefore your poor petr humbly Implore ye your Honor would please to admitt your petr. to be forthwith Called in order to Answer the Recogingzances the affair betug agreed on

And your petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
