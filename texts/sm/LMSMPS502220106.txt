To ye R. Worpple his Majties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx in their genal Quart Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Mary woolnoth London

Sheweth

That by a pass Warrant or order under ye handy Seals of Doyly Mitchell & Alexander Pitfield Esqrs two of his Majties Justices of ye peace for ye County of middx (Quorn unns) bearing Data ye 9th Day of July last past Mary Southern Single woman was removed from ye parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye Sd. County unto ye Sd. Parish of St Mary Woolnoth London as ye place of her last legal Settlement that your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by ye sd Warrant & Judgmt of ye sd two Justices do humbly appeal to your Worshipps these from

And pray that your Worshipps will be pleased to hear them in ye p [...] snes & order that ye Church wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch may appear before you att a certain Day to hear & abide ye Judgmt and Determinacon of your Worshipps touching this Appeal

And Your Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc
