To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens and Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clement Danes

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quorum Unns) dated the fifth day of July last Theodosia Stevens Widw. of John Stevens deced was removed from the Pish of St: Martin in the feilds in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. Pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

Chrinmow

Your Petrs. thereby humbly conceive themselves aggreived & Appeale therefrom to the Judgemt. of this Court & pray a day for Hearing Etc

And Your Petrs. shall pray Etc
