To the Rt. Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlx.

The Humble Peticon of Anthony Buckmaster one of the petty Constables of Southmims in this County.

Sheweth

That at Michmas. last past there was due and owing unto yor Petr. the Sume of Twenty pounds for halfe a years Salary for his less of time Charge and expences in releiving & passing of Vagrants through this County.

That Yor. petr. has been at very great Expences in Faithfully Discharging the sd Office And has a very great Family and Charge of Children to provide for:

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble: Court would be pleased to Order the payment of the sd. Twenty pounds.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Anthony Buckmaster
