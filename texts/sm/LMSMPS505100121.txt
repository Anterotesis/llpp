To the Pet. Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middlesex, in their General Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye. Parish of St. Sepulchre, London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant, under the Hands and Seals of Bartholomew Hammond and Paul Vaillant, Esqrs Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middlesex (one whereof being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye Twenty first Day of April last past, Susanna Flood, Widow of Noah Flood, detd, & their three Children, to wit, Noah, aged five Years, Sarah, aged near three Years, and Mary aged about five Months, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex, unto ye. said Parish of St. Sepulchre, London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Sepulchre, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Chas. Baynbridge Sollr. for Appellants.} That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of ye. said Two Justices, Do Appeal to yor. Worships there from, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & Order that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye said Parish of St. Clement Danes, may appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of yor. Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Peticoners shall pray Etc.
