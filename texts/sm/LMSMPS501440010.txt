To the Right Worpll. His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Joseph Green Cowkeeper

Sheweth

That Yor. Peticonr. rents Land in the Parishes of St Leonard Shoreditch [...] St. John Hackney & the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in said County in each of wch. places Yor. Peticonr. is rated to the Releif of the Poor

That Yor. Peticonr. by the late Mortality among Cattle hath lost almost all his Stock & having a wife & six small Children to maintain &forced to abscond by reason of Debts is thereby made incapable at present of paying what is due at Lady Day last past for the Releif of the Poor of the said parishes & Hamlett

Yor. Peticonr: humbly prays (the prmisses considered) that in consideracon of his great Loss & that he hath not wherewith to buy a Fresh Stock of Cattle this Court will be pleased to releive him by such ways & means as to Yor. great Wisdom shall seem meet

And Yor. Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
