To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Gentll. Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall in the said County

The Humble petiticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. paul Covt. Garden in the County aforesaid

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and Tho: Dummer Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth day of January last Susannah Chapman aged abt. Thirteen years was removed from the Pish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx to the said Pish of St. Paul Covt. Garden as the place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your petitioners think themselves agrieved,- and appeal to this Court against same,

Your Petitioner's therefore Humbly prays this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the sd. appeal & that all persons concerned may attend at the same time

D Hughes Soll for Appel}

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

} Churchwardens
