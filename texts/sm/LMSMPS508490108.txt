To the Worshipful this Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at the the session House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petititon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Feilds within the Liberty of Westminister in the said County.

Sheweth,

That by virtue of an Order of pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of James Fielding and Michael Downes, Esquires Two of this Majesty Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminister (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth Day of July 1789. Elizabeth Huggins Pregnant was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceivd themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therfore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal.

And your petitioners will ever pray Etc

Alex: Gardiner Benjn Ashed George Bride Jno. Maberly Benjn Capper William Butler Robert Dunbarton N Gardner Thos. Middleditch Char. Rymer John Banner} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poors
