To ye Rt Worspll. her Majtys Justices of the Peace For ye County of Midds.

The humble Peticonr of Elizabeth Faconk of the Parish of St Giles in the Fields In the sd County Widow of Cale [...] Jacoce Decd:

Sheweth That for this present year your Petrs Said Husband served the Office (amongst others) of Ovseer of the Poor of the said Parish And that During the time of his said Office He did Disburse and Lay out over and above what he Received Â£90 and upwards And for as much as yor Petr being Inwant of the Said Moneys which appeares to be Disbursed by our Account now ready to be produced And yor Petr having five Children to Mainteyne

P Tho: Nash. The therefore humbly prays Your Worspps will be pleased to Order that she may be paid the sd Moneys As unto your Worspps Shall seeme meete And yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
