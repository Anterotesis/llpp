To the Rt: Worpll: his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeall of the Church: wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Ann Westmr. in ye sd County

Sheweth That Susannah wife of John Hudson with Margaret and John their Children, were by an Order dated March the tenth last past under the hands and seals of John Ward and John Metcalfe Esqrs. Two of his Majtyes Justices of the peace[---] for the sd County of Middx Quor Un [...] , removed from the Libtys of Saffron Hill Hattan Garden in the parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the sd County of Middx to the parish of St. Ann Westmr. and Since their Remove all into the sd parish of St Ann. your Petrs. have carefully required according to the Reasons aledged in the sd Order, but can not finde That the sd John Hudson had ever any legall setlemt. in the sd parish of St. Ann

Your Petrs: therefore humbly conceives themselve agrieved and appeals from the Judgment of the sd Justices to the Judgmt. of this Honble: Court and crave a day of hearing and that the Church: wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St. Andrew Holburn do then attend, so that the sd Susannah Hudson and her sd Children may be so selled as his honble Court in their wise Judgment shall think fitt:

And Your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
