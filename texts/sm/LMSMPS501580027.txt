To the Rt Worpl his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Davis of the Hambt. of the Spittle Feilds Weaver.

Sheweth.

That Your Peticoner is Assessed halfe A Crowne A Quarter towards the Releife of the peace of the sd Hambt. that he is very Poore and is uncapeable to pay the Same

He therefore humbly prays to be Discharged from paying the sd. Rate

And shall pray Etc

John Davis
