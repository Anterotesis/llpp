To the Worshipfull his Majesties of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Martin and William Timbrell Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Nineteenth day of September 1768 John Barwick the Younger was removed from the Liberty of Saffron Hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County of the Parish of Christ Church aforesaid as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order do therefore humply Appeal to this Court against the same Praying that this Court will be pleased to appoint a day in this Present Session for the Hearing and determining there of And that all Persons Concerned may then Attend And that Your Petitioners May have such further relief in the Premisses As to this Court shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

17 October 1768

Carter & Woodgate Attorneys for the Appellants
