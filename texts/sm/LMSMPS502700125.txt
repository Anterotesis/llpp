To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justies of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition of and appeal of the Churchwdns. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ann Westminister in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Mercer and Gifford Esqrs Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (Quor unus) bearing date the 25th. Day of July last James Stephen aged about Thirty One Years was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middlesx to the said Parish of Saint Ann Westminister whereby Your Petitioner conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgemt. of the Saidd. Two Justices and Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Feilds and all Persons Concerned do attend thid Court some day in this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeals

And your Petioners shall pray Etc.
