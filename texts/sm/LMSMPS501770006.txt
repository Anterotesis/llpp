To the Rt: Worsp. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Besswick.

Most humbly Sheweth That yor. Petr. did by one Indenture of Apprenticeship bearing date the Sixth day of September in the second year of his now Matys Reign Annoq, Dom 1715 put himselfe Apprentice unto Peter Steel of the Pich of St. James Westm in the County of Middx Bricklayer for the term of Seven Years comencing from the day of the date thereof As in and by the said Indenture may appear

That yor. petr. said Master doth cause him to make Bricks and doth burn them in his Cellar and hath [...] obliged, yor. petr. this year and on halfe 10 to do. and to empty house of Offices All which is contrary to his Indenture That yor. Peticoner is not able to learn his trade of a Brick layer by reason he is obliged to make bricks in his sd Master house Not only that but has given your petr. in [...] d rate Cha [...] sein. That yor. petrs. Mother has been trickt by his sd Master in the sum of Five pounds in pretence of Curing yor. ptr. of a Ribe heel.

Yor. ptr. therefore prays yor. Worspps to be pleas [...] to be discharged from her sd Master

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.
