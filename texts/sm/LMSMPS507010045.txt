To ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Stevens and Wm. Greenhill

Sheweth

That for that Year Last past Yor. Petrs. Served the Office of Head burroughs for the Parish of Layton in this County and that att the Last Count Leet and Court Barrow held for the Manner of acton there not being a sufficient Number of Jury men to choose were Head burroughs Your Petrs. could not then be Discharges and for a Sume as the sd. Pish haveing Consented for the choyte of the Two Persons under Names If your Worspps for thinks fitt

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly Pray Your Worspps to be pleased to Grant that they may be Discharged from the sd. Office

And Yor. Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc

Tho: Aldridge Senr Tho: Turvin }

John Stevens Wm. [x] Greenhill his mrke
