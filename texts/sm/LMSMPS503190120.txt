To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Sessions Assembled at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the said County the 11th day of October 1736.

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Thames Ditton in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an order or Warrant under the hands & Seals of Thomas Jones & Richard Farmer Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Dated the thirteenth Day of July last past William Page and Anne Page his Wife were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Thames Ditton in the said County of Surrey as the place of their last legal settlement agt which order or Warrant The sd. Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor on the behalfe of themselves & the rest of the Inhabitants of the sd. parish of Thames Ditton Do Appeale to this Court thinking themselves agrieved there by

Your Petrs. Humbly Pray yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint a certain Day of next Sessions for tryall of the Merits of the said Appeale

And your Petrs. as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc.

Richard Restal George Feilder} Churchwardens of Thames Ditton

Thomas Johns} Overseers of the poor
