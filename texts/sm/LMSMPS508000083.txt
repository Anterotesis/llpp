To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petiiton and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Herbut Hayo and William Blackmore Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the eighth day of August last past Cotton Thompson aged thirteen Years. Mary Thompson aged eleven Years Elizabeth Thompson aged nine Years and John Thompson aged six Years the Children of Cotton Thompson deceased were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Cotton Thompson Mary Thompson Elizabeth Thompson and John Thompson

Your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Same and pray your Worships will appoint a day in the present Sessions for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your petitioners Etc

Saml. Malbon Solr. for St. Paul Shadwell
