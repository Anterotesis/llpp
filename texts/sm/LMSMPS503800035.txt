To the Honble: Thomas Lane Esqr: Councelor at Law

The Humble petition of Elizabeth Davidson New at ye Petnt of death in Bridewell

Most Humbly Sheweth your Worship that Your Petitioner being so ill and not being able to be brought before Your Worship at Hicks: Hall on Saturday last but Your Worships Notwithstanding there Your great Clemency and goodness Orderd Your petitioner to be discharged Never the less Your petitioner is Confined and Must Certainly parish Unless Your Worships goodness Will Order Your Petitr. to be Turn'd out

And Your Petitr. as in Duty Bound Shall Ever Pray &

Elizabeth Davidson
