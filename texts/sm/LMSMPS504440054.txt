To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [---] Sessions of the Peace for the County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Keeling; and Fran: Bedwell, two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of July 1755, Gould Winniard was Removed form the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Warrant Judgment of the said two Justices, humbly Appeal to this Court for Relief in the Premisses, And Pray that this Court will be pleased to Appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray.

Richd Norris James Whiften} Church Wardens.

Wm Houghton Bryan Benson} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westminster.
