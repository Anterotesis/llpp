To the Rt Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of John Grimes

Sheweth

That your Petitr. is a Lodger in his Mothers house in the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in this County Not with standing Which your Petitr. is Chosen to serve the Office of Scavenger for this Present year in the said Parish Wherefore your Petitr humbly Conteives himselfe aggrieved

p Tho: Elmes. Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to order. the Churchwardens & Oveseers of the poor of the said Parish to attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew cause why Your Petitr: Should not be Discharged from serving the said Office and Some other fitt person Chosen in his Roome

And Geor. Petitr shall ever pray Etc:

John Grimes
