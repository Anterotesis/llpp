To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justice of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humbly peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the Sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) bear [...] Date the 17th. day of December last Martha Barker was removed from the parish of st. Andrew Holborne above Barrs in the sd County of the Sd. parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her last legall settlemt.

That yor petrs. thinke themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd two Justices & due appeale therefrom to the judgemt. of this Cort

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor wor [...] rs with be pleased to appoint Some day this prsent Sessions to hear & determine the matter touching this Appeale

And ys Petrs Shall pray Etc

Serve Mr Jones Wife with a true Coppy of this Ordr. of Sessions the 13th. day of Janry 1724
