To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of J. Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 16th. Day of November One thousand seven hundred and Seventy Charlotte Vuljohn aged about four Years Daughter of Wm Vuljohn Coach harness maker (who hath deserted her) by Mary his Wife deced was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the said County as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appealts this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present General Session to hear and Determine their said Appeal

Wm Dickins George Shakespear James Shoroell} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
