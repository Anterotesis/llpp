To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middlx

The Humble Petition of William Why then.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner by his Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing date the fifth Day of July Anno Dom 1721 became an Apprentice to Walter Hood of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the said County Perukemaker for the term of Seven Yeares concerning from the date of the sd Indenture as thereby Appears.

That Your Petrs. Master by his blows and hard usages hath almost brought Your Petr to deaths Door Neither is he capable of Instructing him his Trader. To answer all which Your Petr. said Master stands bound by Recognizance for his Appearance in this Court this present Sessions

Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays his said Master may attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew cause why he should not be Discharged from his Apprenticehood to the said Walter Hood

And Ye Petr Shall Ever Pray etc.

William Whitlaw
