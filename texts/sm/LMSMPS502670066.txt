To the Rt: Worspll: His Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt: under the hands & Seales of John Beaver and Samuell Gray Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County bearing date the tenth day of Febry last Elizabeth Jones aged thirty Five years the Wife of William Jones and their two Children Vizt: Jane aged thirteen & Elizabeth aged Nine years were removed from & out of the parish of St: Paul Covent Garden in the said County to the sd. parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of their last legal settlement,

That yor. Petrs: thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale against the same to this Court,

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly pray that yor: Worsp will be please to Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Paul Covent Garden may attend this Court some day this present Quarter Sessions the hear and abide the Judgemt and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeale

And yor: Petr: shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton
