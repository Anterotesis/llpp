The Humble. Petition of William Newel that made his information before your Worship aganst able Gibson and John Marlia

Humbly Sheweth that your petitioner is almost Starvd having no friends to Come near him and humbly beg that your worship will look with am dye of Pitty upon him and send an order for his enlargement or else he must certainly Perish for want being almost Starvd already if your worship will be so good your Petitioner Pray Etc

For Mr Phillips
