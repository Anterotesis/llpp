To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of William Burdon & R: Peirson Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Libty of Westmr: bearing date the 24th day of June last Ann Heaney & William her Child were removed from the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Libty to the said parish of St. James as the place of their last legal Settlemt. Whereby your petitioners think them Selves Agrieved

And Therefore humbly pray this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may attend at the Same time

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray
