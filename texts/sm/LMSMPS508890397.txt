To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace- Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Brettell, John Porker, Thomas Isherwood, and Michael Hodgson Parishioners of the Parish of Hornsey in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a certain Tax Rate or Assessment according to a Pound Rate after the rate of Nine Pence in the Pound made on the 25th. day of March 1793 by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hornsey aforesaid upon the Inhabitants Owners and Occupiers of Houses Lands Tenements Tythes Impropriate and Impropriation of Tythes in the said Parish towards the Necessary relief of the Poor of the said Parish for half a year ending at Lady day being the second rate and other purposes mentioned in the statute made in the 43d year of the late Queen Elizabeth "Intitled an Act for the "relief of the Poor to be paid immediately upon Demand "made by the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of "Hornsey"

Your Petitioners were in and by such Rate or Assessment charged and Assessed in several Sums of Money for the relief of the Poor of the said Parish which they humbly conceive are greater than (for the following causes and objections) this ought to be

First

First For that the said Rate or Assessment hath not been made signed allowed and Published in such manner and form as the Laws made and now in force concerning the making signing allowing and Publishing of Rates for relief of the Poor of the said Parish require

Secondly For that in and by the said Rate or Assessment Your Petitioners are charged and assessed for Houses Lands Tenements and Premises held and Occupied in the said Parish of Hornsey in a much larger Sum than Your Petitioners ought to be so charged and Assessed for the same and above the proportion at which the Houses- Lands Tenements and Premises belonging David Devuluz, Anthony Parkin, and Alexander Anderson, are charged in the said Parish And are greatly Over rated and aggrieved thereby And that your Petitioners are also in and by the said Rate or Assessment Charged and Assessed for Improvements

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the said Rate or Assessment may be quashed And that Your Petitioners may have such relief in the Premises as to Your Worships shall seem meet

John Porker John Brettell Thos. Isherwood Michl Hodgson [...]
