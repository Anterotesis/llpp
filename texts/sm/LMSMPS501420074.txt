To the Right Worshipfull her Majesties Justisses of the peace for the County of Middsex

The humble petion of, William Bedford of the parish of St. Jameses Clarkenwell in the County aforesaid

Your humble Petioner for Seven or Eight and twenty years paid to the Poor and all other duties of the said Pish is now by Reason of great troubles and Losses taken upon him and being in age makes him [...] Incapable of paying any longer your petioner humbly begs [...] In hour wisdom and goodness your will discoarge him of the same and your petitioner Shall always pray
