To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices sitting at Hicks Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon of Thomas Bates.

The humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner having for some years Officiated at Prison as Turnkey, and in the absence of the late (and present) Keeper of the Prison attended this honble: Bench (hee hopes to Satisfaction)

That your Petitioner being, informed, that your Worshipps in [...] to dismiss Mr. Lowthorpe the present Keeper, from his said Office,

That your Petitioner haveing by his constant attendance Turnkey Etc a: (as he humbly conceives) qualified himselfe for the [...] hance of his Duty (if your Worshipps shall please to admitt him

Most humbly prays that this honoble: Bench will be favourably pleased to Elect your Petitioner in the Room of Mr. Lowthorpe (if dismissed) Keeper of your said Prison

And yor: Petitionr: (as in Duty bound) shall ever pray Etc.

Tho: Bates
