To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Walthamstow in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Boulton Mainwaring and John Lawton Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of April last John Taylor Aged between Seven and Eight Years Mary Taylor Aged about five Years and Susanna Taylor two Years Old the Issue of Thomas Taylor deceased and Ann his Wife were Removed from the Hamlet of Popler and Black Wall in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Walthamstow in the County of Essex as the Place of his and their last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the Peace Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and Pray this Court will Appoint a day this present Session for the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlet of Popler and Black Wall and all other Partyes Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall every Pray

Wm: Martin Sollr: for the Parish of Walthamstow
