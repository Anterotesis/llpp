To the Right Worspl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Bedingfeild of the Parish of Hornsey in the said County

Sheweth

That yor. petr. did serve the Offices of Surveyor of the Highwaies and Constable within the said Parish for the yeares 1686 and 1689 That yor. petr. did disburse in the time of the said Offices towards amending the Highwaies within the said parish and passing and releiving Cripples and Vagabond and otherwise the sume of Â£15.16s.1d

That yor. petr. hath often requested the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd. parish to reimburse him the said moneys soe by him disbursed as aforesd. but they refuse soe to doe without an Order of this Honble Court.

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worspps. to be pleased to Order that he may be pd. and sattisfyed the sd. moneys sue to him due as aforesd by such waies & meanes as unto yor Worsp shall seem meet

And yor. petr. shall ever Prayer
