To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Gen Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Richd Wills Sheweth

That your Petitioner was bound an Apprentice to Edwd Jemmett of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx Distiller by Indentures bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of September in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty Seven for the Term of Seven Years.

That on or about the third Day of November last about the Hour of in the Evening your Petitioner said Master without any Provocation whatsoever beat your Petr in a very cruet and severe Manner with a Horse Whip and pushed your Pet along his Shop with great Violence and repeated Blows with the sd Horse Whip and forced your Petr to the Street Door and then Shut and bolted the Door against your Petr. to prevent his coming in again the said Mr. Jemmett using many Oaths & Imprecations that your Petr. shou'd never enter his House again so long as he lived

That your Petr. has ever since been absent from his said Masters House dreading to return to his Service for Fear of being treated in the same cruel Manner of Worse

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays Your Worships to discharge him from his said Indentures of Apprenticeship or to grant him such Relief in the Remes as to your Worships shall seem meet And goure Petr. as in Duty bound will ever pray etc

Richd Witts
