To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order under the hands and Seales of Nicholas Jeffreys and Robert Hinde James Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (quorum unus) being therein mentioned, bearing date the third day of Aprill One thousand Seven hundred and thirty three one [---] Sarah Wilson aged about twenty two yeares was removed from the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County to the said parish of Saint [---] Giles without Cripplegate as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said [---] Sarah Wilson

That your Petitioner find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices of the peace, And humbly Appeales to this Court against the same

Your Petitioner therefore prays this Court to appoint a day this present Sessions for all parties concerned to attend this Court touching the said Appeale

And your Petitioners Shall pray
