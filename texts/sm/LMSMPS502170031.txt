To the Right Worshipll: his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseer of the Poor of the Liberty of Norton- folgate in the Said County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Joseph Hayne and D'Oyly Michel Esqrs two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the Eighth day of January last, John Davenport and Ann his Wife and Ann his daughter aged about Eight years, by a former wife were Pass'd from the Parish of St Clement Danes in the sd. County of Middlesex unto the sd: [...] Liberty of [...] Nortonfolgate as the place of their last legall settlement

That your Peticonr: conceives himself Aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and do therefore humbly Appeal against the same praying that this Court will please to Adjourn this appeal untill the next Genal Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County and Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of It Clement Danes and all other persons concerned Some day that Sessions, to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal
