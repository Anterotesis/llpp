To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth, That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Nich: Spencer, and Same: Person Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 16th: day of January 1767, Mary Denningbrook Wife of John Deningbrook and their 2 Children (Vizt.) John Aged 3 Years and Richard Aged 3 Months, were Removed from the Parish of Saint James as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore Pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal and that all persons & concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc. Thos: Thomas Solr. for Appeallts:
