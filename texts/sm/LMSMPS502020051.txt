To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Jutices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Gabriel Fanchurch in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands, Seals of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx (Quorn Unno) bearing date the Twelfth day of February last past John Vaen with Elizabeth his wife and their male Child about a Week Old were removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Midlx unto the parish of St Gabriel in Fenchurch Street in the City of London

That your Petr conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or order Do humbly appeal to your Worpposr therefrom And gray That your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
