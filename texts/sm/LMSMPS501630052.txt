To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Isaac Ragg John Young Thomas Mills & Sibell Downes on behalf of themslves and Divers others in Newprison in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petitioners and severall others have beene confined in New Prison aftersaid for severall Months to give Evidence against severall Person for severally Burglarys and Fellonys by them Comitted and Divers have beene Convicted upon the Informacon of your Peticonrs. That your Peticoners have Compleated their Evidences and are very poor and ready to Starve in the said Prison.

Therefore humbly Pray Your Worshipps will be pleased to Consider their long Imprisonment & Poverty and to be pleased to Order that they may be discharged from their said Imprisonment

And Yor Peters shall Ever Pray Etc
