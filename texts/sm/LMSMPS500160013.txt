To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Robert Chadd

Sheweth

That in Some time past Richard Dixon and Lancellot Hudson became Apprentices to yor Petr. each of them for the terme of Seaven yeares As by their Severall Indenture of Apprenticeshipp may appeare.

That the said Richard Dixon and Lancellot Hudson doe refuse to serve yor. Petr. aswell by Sea as Land in the And of a [...] Shipwright during the remainder of their said severall Apprenticeshipps according to the Contents of the sd. Indentures whereby yor. Petr. is likely to receive great losses thereby by reason he cannot proceed in the voyage to Sea in which he is engaged.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to orders that the sd Richard Dixon and Lancellott Hudson may serve the residue of their said severall Apprenticeshipps with yor. Petr. according to the Contents of the sd severall Judies.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
