To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said county Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and Philip Neve Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th Day of May instant Elizabeth Ireland Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne Westminster as

as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to the aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to the Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for this County to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Tho Willows Saml. Unsworth} Churchwardens

James Byfeld Jacob Welsh Charles Bond} Overseers of the Poor
