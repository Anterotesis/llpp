To the Rt Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St: Giles in the fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That [...] Order under the hands & seals of Wm Dobyns & [...] Bucknall Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the 8th.day of January last Frances Sutton wife of Thomas Sutton was removed from & out of the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of [---] her last legall Settlement.

Yor. petrs. therefore think themselves agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices appeale to this Court agt. the same & humbly pray yor. Worsps will be pleased to appoint a day for hearing & Determining the same.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton
