To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices

The Humble Petition of Thomas Reynardson Petty Constable of the parish of St. Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petitioner being out of Town in the County of Bucks upon his lawfull business and before he went delivered his Return to Joseph Cox his Headborough (which he thought in humble Submission would he [...] Sufficiently) but being informed that notwithstanding the prosecution that he I had taken, the Court was pleased to Fine [...] me I and hope you will take it into your tender Consideration to Releive me from the Fine

And your Petitioner As in Duly bound will ever Pray

Hicks hall Octor. 24th. 1758
