To the Right Worspp her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeare of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St Clement Danes in the sd County

Sheweth Tho Williams Sol: That by Warrt Dated the 10th day of December last under the hands and Seales of Ger: Ireton and fsr Negus Esqrs two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx aforesd (Quor Unns) That Anne the wife of Joseph Kemp and her Two Children (Vizt) Mary Aged abt Six Years and a halfe and Anne Aged abt five Years were removed from the parish of St Mary le Savoy in the Strand in the sd County to the Said parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middx aforesd. Alleadging that the Sd parish of St Clement Danes was the place of the last legall Settlement of the sd Joseph husband to the sd Anne Kemp & Father of the sd Two Children

That yor Peticonrs conceive themselves Aggrievd by the Judgemt of the sd Two Justices and doe humbly appeal to yor Worsppl therefrom

Yor Peticonrs therefore most humbly pray yor worsppl will be pleased to appointe a day this prsent Sessons, that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the sd parish of St Mary le Savoy in the strand may Attend yor Worsppl and hear and abide the Determinacon of yord worshipps touching this Appeal
