To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for County of Middlesex In their General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cowley and Cadwallader Coker Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifteenth day of November 1766, Elizabeth Marner Singlewoman and her Bastard Child named John were Passed from the Parish Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Christ in the said County Adjudging the last legal Place of Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Marner to be in the said Parish of Christ Church. Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom and humbly pray your Worships for time to Appeal against the said Warrant or Order until the next Quarter Sessions of the Peace which shall be held for this County And for such further Relief in the premisses as to this Court shall Seem meet.

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Carter Attorney for the Parish of

Christ Church
