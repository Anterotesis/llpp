To the Rt. Worshipll. It is Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Aldenham in the Libty of St. Albans in the County of Hertford

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Nicholas Philpott & William Bucknall Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorn Unus) bearing date the 28th. day of February last Past Charles Batman & Frances his Wife were removed from the Parish of Edgeware in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Aldenham as the Place of the last legal Settlemt. of the said Charles Batman

Yor Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeale to this Court and humbly Pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Edgeware & all other Parties concerned may be Ordered to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the Premisses

And Yor. Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
