[...] Right Worsppl. her Maties Justices of the for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Challener of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in the Said County Cordweyner.

That your Petr. is rated 2s. P Month towards the [...] releife of the poor of the said Hamlet for this present year and he having noe estate and his trade [...] much declined and being aged 78 years Soe that he is not able to pay the said rate.

your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to Milligate the said rate in such manner as unto yor. Worspps shall seem meet.

[...]

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
