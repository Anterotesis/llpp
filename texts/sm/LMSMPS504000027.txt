To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Giles Camberwell in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of Gideon Harvey & J: Lediard Esqrs: two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster Dated the 18th: day of October last past John Fry & Mary his Wife were removed from the parish of St: Margaret Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to our said parish of St: Giles Camberwell as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby we think ourselves agrieved

We therefore Humbly Appeal to the said Order & forasmuch as we are not Ready to Try the same at this present Sessions We Humbly pray that the Hearing & Determining thereof may be Saved and Respited until the next Sessions And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Margaret Westminster be Ordered then & there to attent

And Yr: Petitioners in Duty bound shall ever Pray

Thos Shepherd Ch: Warden John Readworth Overseer
