To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in their Generall Quanter Sessions att Hicks Hall Assambled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the said County

Sheweth That by Value of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Williams & J: Brom field Esqrs two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Eighth day of September 1731 Thomas Richins and Eliz his Wife and their two Children Vizt: Elizabeth aged about Eight Yeare, and Thomas an Infant aged about three Months And also John by Margaret his first Wife were directed to be removed and Passed from the Parish of Chelsea in the said County of Middx to the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the said County And Whereas by Virtue of the said Order Elizabeth Richins Wife of Thomas Richens and Elizabeth and Thomas her Children by her said Husband were removed and Passed from the said Parish of Chelsea to the Parish of S Ge [...] ge Hanover Square as the Place of their last legall Settlement Whereby the said Parish of St George Hanover Square think themselves Agrived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will Appoint a day for heareing the said Appeale and that all Persons concern'd may attend as the same time

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

George Carpenter Churchwarden
