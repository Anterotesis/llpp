To the Worshipful his Majestys Justice of Peace for the County of Middlesex in then General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Benjamin Sebbs Kinght and John Collick Esquire two of his Majestys justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County bearing Date the Twenty seventh day of December now last Past Elizabeth Edwards singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the said Liberty and County to the Said Parish of Saint Pancras as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the Said two justices and then said Order and Pray that your [---] Worships will be Pleased Appoint a Day in this Present Session she hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs wile Pray Etc Herb. Robertson Sob: for the Appellrs.
