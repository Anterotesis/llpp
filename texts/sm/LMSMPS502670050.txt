To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon & Appeal of James Edwards of the parish of Edmonton in this County

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands and Seals of James Colebrooke and William Gould Esqrs two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for this County bearing Date the [...] Fourth day of this instant April Yor. Petr. was appointed to be one of the Overseers of ye poor of the sd parish of Edmonton for ye year now ensuing;

That Yor. Petr. is a Servt. to the Honble. the New River Company, and lives in a House wth in ye sd parish belonging to the sd Company, And that he never payd Rent or Taxes for the same (for ten years last past and upwards) only for under behalfe of the sd Company; And that it never has been Customary for any of the sd Company Servants (in ye Capacity of yor. Petr.) to serve any parish or Ward Office:

Yor. Petr. therefore finding himselfe aggrieved by the sd Wart. of Appointmts. humbly Appeals agts the same

And Prays yor. Worsps would be pleased to Discharge yor. Petr. from the sd Office, and that some other person of the sd parish of Edmonton may be appointed Overseer of the poor of the sd parish in the room of yor. petr .

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Zach. Clarke Agent for ye Petr.
