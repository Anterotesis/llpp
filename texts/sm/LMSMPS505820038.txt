To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Stephen Wilson of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex Esqr.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner thinking himself Aggrieved by the last Rate made in Vestry for the Relief of the poor of the said parish of Isleworth Doth therefore Appeal against the same for that the said Rate is Illegal and Unequal and for that he is Over Rated in the same and humbly prays your Worships to make such Order in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet at the next General Quarter Sessions And in the mean time in regard your petitioner hath Demanded a Copy of the said Rate but hath not yet been able to Obtain the same

your Petitioner humbly prays that his said a Appeal may be Entred this present Sessions and that your Worships will be pleased to make an Order that the same may be heard at the next General Quarter Session and that the said Rate together with the Books wherein the same is Entred may be then produced

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Thos: Jessopp

Attorney for the Appellant
