To the Right Worppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Conton in the County of Nottingham

Sheweth

That by warrant or Order under the hands and seals of Henry Norris and Samuel Eysson Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) dated the Eleventh day of March last Rebecca Newgent wife of patrick Newgent and her three Children (Vizt) Elizabeth Matheu Aged seven years, Susannah Aged five years, the issue of the said Rebecca & her late Husband John Machon deced and Katherine Newgent about halfe A year Old the issue of her & her present Husband patrick Newgent (who were the lately come into the parish of St John att Hackney in the said County of Middx She the said Rebecca Newgent haveing no legal Settlement there and that she & her said three Children were become chargeable to the said parish of St John att Hackney were removed from the said parish of St John att Hackney to the said parish of Conton as the place of their last legal legal Settlement the said patrick Newgent being an Frishman & haveing noe legal Settlement in this Kingdome as [...] Alledged in the said Order

That your petitioners said themselves aggreived by the said Warrant O [...] Order of the said two Justices as to the removal of the said Rebecca Newgent & her said Child Katherine Newgent, & humbly Appeal to this Court again the same.

Jo: Rosse Agent on Sette for the Appellts

Yor. peticoners & Appellants humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to Appoint A day for the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St John att Hackney & all Other persons concerned to Attend this Court this present Sessions to hear & Abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal and that the said Appellants may be releived herein according to Law.

And your petrs shall pray Etc
