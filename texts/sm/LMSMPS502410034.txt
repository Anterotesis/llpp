To the Rt. Worpll. the Justices of his Majties. Peace for the County of Middx. att their Semon held at Hicks hall for the sd. County.

The Humble Petition of Cathrine Hovey Widdow in behalfe of herselfe and her two Chilldren

Ordered that the Overseers attend on Sat next at ten o' clock.

25 Feb 1726 Confirm Mr. Booth's ordr [---]

Sr. Dan. Dolins Col Watkins Mr. Troughton Mr Jackson
