To the Honarabell Bench of Testissis

The Humble Petion of Thomas Grace Shath that Year Petisioner Lyes Under A Fine in New Prison Upords of Sixteen Weeks Upon the Account of his Wife Who Keep Cumpany With Another Man and the Honarable Bench thaught Propper to fine him three Marks Wick he cannot Pay for your honers Petisioners Lys Pearish in With Hunger and Could Upon the bair Boards and has Oanly Apore Mother that Washes at the tub for her Lining to Bind him Any thing Wheek I begg Your Worshipfull Bench Will take itt in Consideration his Fine May be takin of

And Your Honours Petisiner as In Duty bound Shall pray
