To the Right Worppfull their Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petion of Jon pe [...]

Sheweth That yor peticonr is and was upon the 28th day of November 1690 a prisonr in Finsbury Goale in the Custody of Symon penne keeper of the Same for debt and being in noe wise able to pay the Same humbly desired the benefitt of two Acts of parliamt made for the whife and discharges of poore prisonr for debt Etc And went before at Justice of the peace to take the oathes appointed by the sd Acts in Order to be discharged but the said Keeper & refused to being yor peticonr to the Quarter Sesss as the said Acts require and is since withdrawen by wth meanes yoe peticonr is still detained

Yor. peticonr therefore humbly prayes to be discharged by this honoble bench he being qualified for it

And yor peticonr shall ever pray
