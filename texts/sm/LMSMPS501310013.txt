To the Right Worpll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Warden Carpenter & Wm: Bills Brick Layer

Sheweth That Yor. Petr: were imployed in reparing & amending this Honble Hall and Bridewell Prison

That Yor. Petr. have compleaty done & finished all the work which was to be done to the said two places and that there is due and owing to Yor. Petr. Warden ye Sume of £120. and to yor Petr. Bills ye sum of £50. that your Petr: have a very great occacon for their Sd Moneys

P Hemman Yor. Petr. Therefore humbly Pray this Honble Bench to be pleased to take if into Consideracon and to order that they may forthwith be paid their said Moneys. And Yor. Petr: Shall ever Pray etc.
