To Their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Etc

The humble Peticonr of Francis Drake & Francis Balard

Humbly Sheweth That Yor: Peticonr are Rakers of ye Streets and was imployed by ye Scavengers of Wappen Stepney in ye said worke and hath July pease dived itt for this halfe yeares and there is due to yor peticoner 5 o odd pounds and Yor Peticoners havinge demanded the aforesaid sume humbly craves yt: the Scavengers may be ordered by this Honoble: Cort: to [...] attend to shew cause why they doe unjustly detaine ye aforesaid money from Yor: Peticoner

And Yor: Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
