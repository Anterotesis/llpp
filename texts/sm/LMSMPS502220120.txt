To the Right Wroshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace in & for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions now assembled

Sheweth The Humble petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye parish of St. Giles without Cripple gate in ye sd. County

That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order bearing date ye Tenth Day of September last under ye hands & Seals of John Ellis & Tho Gouge Esqrs Two of his Matys Justices of ye peace of ye sd. County (Quor Un) Thomas Gibbs Jun & Mary his Wife a Lunatick were removed from that part of the parish of St. Ann within Aldersgate in the Lilbty of St. Martins le Grand Westm to the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in ye County of Middx afd as their last legall place of Settlemt. Whereby your petitionrs do find themselves aggrieved & humbly appeal therefrom.

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly pray this Honble Court to appoint a Day this prsent Sessions that all persons concernd may attend & abide he Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor pests shall ever pray Etc
