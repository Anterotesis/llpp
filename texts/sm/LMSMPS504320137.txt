To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. assembled at the Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace held for the City and Liberty aforesaid on Wednesday the 16th. Day of October 1754

The humble Petition of Ann Chambers Widow

Sheweth That at the General Quarter Session of the peace holden for the City and Liberty aforesd. on Wednesday the 17th. day of October 1753 your Worships were pleased to Grant Licence to Cuthbert Ogle to keep a certain House called or known by the Name of the Concert Room in Dean Street in the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty aforesaid for publick Dancing Musick or other publick Entertainment of the like kind Which Licence was to continue in force until the Present Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace held for the Liberty aforesaid

That the Condition of the said Licence hath all things been performed & fulfilled That the said House is the property of your petitioner

Therefore humbly Prays that your Worships would be pleased to grant a Licence to Your petitioners for the said House for the purposes aforesaid to continue in force until the next Michaelmas Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this Liberty

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Ann Chambers
