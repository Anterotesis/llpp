To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Court of General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall in Saint Johns Street in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Paul Deptford in the Counties of Kent and Surry.

Sheweth. That by Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of John Hale and Thomas Bullard Esquires bearing date the fourteenth day of December last Margaret Monro Widow of Hugh Monro deceased was removed from the parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Paul Deptford in the County of Kent as the place of her last legal Settlement By which we think ourselves aggrieved And Appeal to this Court against the said Order.

We therefore your Petitioners humbly pray this Honourable Court will be pleased to Grant an Order for all parties to Attend this Court in Order to the hearing the Complaint and defence of both parties by Evidence and to make such further Order thereon as the Court shall think proper.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
