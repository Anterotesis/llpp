To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Present General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Rudge and William Phillimore Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th day of February Instant Sarah Newman Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Edgeware in the said County of Middlesex to the said Part of the said Parish of St Andrew Holborn an the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and Pray that you will be Pleased to appoint same day in this Present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that you will Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Edgeware to appear before you to abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc Arthur Gliddon Sollr. pr the Appls.
