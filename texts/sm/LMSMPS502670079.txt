To the Right Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Sepulchre's in the Town of Northton

Sheweth That by Order dated the three & twentieth day of January last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Railton and Richard Farwell Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd and City and Liberty of Westmr in the Said County (Quor Unus) Mary the Wife of Robert Reeves and their three Children Vizt Ann aged 5 years Abigal aged 4 Years and Sibura aged upwards of a Year (who) were become chargeable to the parish of St Margarets in the Said City of Westmr and County of Middx as was alledged in the said Order) were removed from the Said parish of St Margaret Westmr to the Said parish of St Sepulchres in the Said Town of Northampton as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Wife of the Said Robert Reeves and their Said three Children

That Your Peticoners and Appellants find themselves aggrieved by the Said Order of the Said two Justices and Appeal to this Court against the Same And for that Your Peticoners and Appellants cannot hereby with their Witnesses, (Living att such a distance from this Town) to have the Said appeal heard & determined this present Sessions

Jo: Rolfe Agent fn the Appellants Your Peticoners and Appellants therefore pray that the Said Appeal may be saved to them & that the hearing & determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St Margaret Westmr and All other persons Concerned may be Ordered to Attend the Said Court of Sessions on Such day as Your Worpps Shall think fitt to hear & abide the judgment & determinacon of the Said Court in the premisses and that Your Peticoners & Appellants may be releived therein according to Law & Justice

And Yor Peticoners shall Pray Etc
