To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled.

The humble Petition of Thomas Thompson an Apprentice to William Gambier of the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the County of Middlesex Frame Picture [...] Carver

Sheweth That Your Petitioner by Indenture bearing date the tenth day of February one Thousand seven hundred and thirty was bound an Apprentice to the said William Gambier for the Term of sever Years.

That Your Petitioner hath served his said Mater above two years of the said Term during which Time your Petitioner hath behaved himself in all things towards his said Master as an honest and faithfull servant but his said Master being of a Passionate temper hath several times beal your Petitioner with Sun lawfull weapons and Particularly on the day of October last he struck your Petitioner with a Large Iron to her Which very much bruised and hurt your Petitioner.

That Your Petitioners said Master through Misfortunes or otherwise is reduced to a very low state in the world in so much that your Petitioner hath frequently been forced to goe without Victualls for a Considerable time And your Petitioners said Master hath often moved his habitation and his Business hath of late very much fallen off and he hath now but very little if any Business to do in his way of trade so that your Petitioner Cannot be in the least pro [...] d by Staying with and Serving his said Master during the Remainder of [...] the said Term of seven years

That Your Petitioner is willing and desireous to be turned over to another person of the same had & with whom Your Petitioner will serve the Remainder of his said Time.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays your Worships for the reasons aforesaid to discharge your Petitioner from his said Indenture of Apprenticeship and to appoint a day for your Petitioners said Master to attend and abide by the Judgement of this Court.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray.

Thomas Thompson
