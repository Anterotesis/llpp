To the Rt Worsppll her Maties Justices of the Peace for Ye County of Middx

The humble petition of Anthony Buckmaster Constable of South mims in the Said County

Sheweth

That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr: At Lady day last amounting to the Sum of Tenn Pounds for passing & relieving of Vagrants Which Money Your Petitioner Stands in great need of

He therefore humbly prays yours Worshipps will be Pleased to order that he may be paid the said money als unto Yor Worspps shall seeme meete

And yor: Petitr: shall ever Pray Etc:

Anthony Buckmaster
