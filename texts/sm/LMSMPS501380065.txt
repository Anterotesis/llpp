To the Rt. Worpll. her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Middx ss The humble Appeale of the Church: Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St. Peter Le Poore Broadstreete London.

Sheweth That by an Order dated the 26th. day of March 1714 and under the hands of Stephen Hall & Thomas Frampton Two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of middx (Quorum Unus) By which said Order One John Shaw & Sarah his wife & theire Two Children George & Sarah were passed from the parish of St. Mary Matfellon als White chappell in the County of Middx to the said parish of St. Peter Le Poore Broadstreete as theire legall Settlement.

That the said Church Wardens & Overseers of St. Peter Le Poore most humbly conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order And pray an Appeale to this Honoble. Bench.

Yor. Peticoners humbly crave this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing of your Petrs. Appeale against the said Order.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
