To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Catherine CreeChurch otherwise Christ Church London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and John Robinson Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bed ring Date the Eighteenth Day of April One thousand Seven hundred and Ninety five Margaret Marshall the Widow of Alexander Marshall was removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Catherine Cree Church otherwise Christ Church (called in the said Order the Parish of Saint Catherine Gree Church in the City of London) the said Justices thereby adjudging that to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of Removal most humbly Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint same Day in this Present Session to hour this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all Parties concerned in and about the Premises may then and there attend hear and abide the Determination of Your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall our Pray Etc

} Churchwardens

} Overseers
