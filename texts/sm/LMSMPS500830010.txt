The humble Peticon of Daniell Gardner

Sheweth That in Some time past your Peticoner became Apprentice to James Ivers of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the Said County Ropemaker for the term of Seven yeares comencing from the date of his Indenture of apprenticeship As thereby may appeare That your Peticoners Said master hath absconded himselfe for about a Month past & hath removed all his goods & effects (he being much in debt) & he having taken noe care of your Peticoner for that he is likely to be ruined for want of being instructed in his said trade.

P Wessley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from his said apprenticeship.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc. Daniell [x] Gardner his marke
