To the Rt: Worspll: her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx-

The humble Peticon and Appeal of- the Churchwardens & Overseers of the- poor of the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey-

Sheweth

That by Warrant under the hands & Seales of Two of her Maties: Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx One Edward Pennery Son of David Pennery Aged about- Two yeares and Six month was passed from the parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx- to the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey as the place of his last Legall Settlement-

That your Peticonrs: can make it Appear to this Honble: Court that the Said Edward Pennery was never Legally- Settled in the parish of St: George the Martyr in the County of Surrey-

Your Peticonrs: therefore most humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the sd. Parish of St: Gyles wth:out Cripple gate may Appear this Honble: Court this prsent Sessions to shew- cause why the said Edwd: Pennery should not be repassed to the said parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate

And yor: Peticoner shall ever pray Etc.
