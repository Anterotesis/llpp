To the Right Worspl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Buttolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the 24th.. Day of November last past James Colleyson Senr. with Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye County of Middx to the Parish of St. Bottolph without Aldgate London as the Place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Warrant and Judgement of the said Two Justices humbly appeal to this Honble. Court for Relief in the Premisses

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
