To the Rt. Worppll his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poore fo the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London.

Sheweth That by Warrant undr. the hands and Seales of Jno Milner. & William Prestley Esqrs. two of this Majtes. Justices of the peace for these. County of Middx (quon unns) bearing date the 15th. day of May 1716. Ann Bevis Widow was Past on yor. Petrs. from the parish off. Gyles in the feilds in the sd. County of ye Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement

That Yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggrieved by the Order of the sd. two Justices and Appeals therefore to Yor Worpps

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly hope yor Worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the Said Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
