Peticon of Geo: Knapp Fr 10 cl dismissed

To the Honoble: Bench of Justices at their Sessions of the Peace held att Hicks hall for the County of Midlesex.

The Humble petion of George Knap Of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in the said County Feltmaker.

Sheweth

That yor. Petitioner being Old Lame and weake- sighted, and therefore enable to get by his Labour as he has- done Formerly towards mentaynance of his Family and Support thereof, and haveing had a Sister lying long upon his hands Lane and bedrid; The Heads of the parish of White Chapel where he last lived before he Removed hither looked on him as an object of their Compassion, and Exempted him from paying to the poore which Favour cannot be Obtayned here where he now is the- with all due Respect he has made his Aplication, but is answerd by the Overseers, they cannot help it unlesse the Sessions will Releive him.

Yor. Peticoner therefore in all humility Appeales to yor. Honrs. and begs that his Circumstances may be considered, and yor Redresse herein.

And yor. Petitioner as in Duty Bound.

Shall ever pray Etc
