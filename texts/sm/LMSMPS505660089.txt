To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hick's Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty seventh day of October last Eleanor Barnell Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and against the said Order do Appeal to this Court

Your Petitoners therefore humbly pray That your worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc

Saml Agar B Wood T. Ayliffe R Creser} Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
