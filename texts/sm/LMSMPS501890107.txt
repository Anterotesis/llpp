To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for Ye. County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Howett Constable of Endfield in the Said County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary due to your Peticoner at Michaelmas Last for Passing and relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds.

That Yor Petr hath been at great charge and Expence in Passing Vagrants this Last Quarter and stands in Need of his Money.

Yor; Petr therefore humbly prays he may be paid his said Quarters Sallary.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc John Howett
