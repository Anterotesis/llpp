To the Right Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their general Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of James Westbrook Gentleman as Inhabitant of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor and also the Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch as a Meeting held on the seventh Day of August 1799 in the Vestry Room of the said towards the make a certain rate or assessment for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish and for other the Ends and purposes of a certain act of Parliament made and passed in the fourteenth year of the Reign of king George the Third for the better Relief and Employment of the Poor within the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and that they the said Churchwardens and overseers and also the Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch have omitted in such rate or assessment the names of william Humblebee Thomas Sharp John Amos Herbert Gardens one of the overseers James Saltwell William Harrison Samuel Leban Robert Edward Benjamin Dinnage william Raventille John Wills Robert Staff John Willson Robert Armstrong Thomas Seall Josh Parker Peter Edwards John Willson James Duthail Richard Edlin Elizabeth Parrie Edward Barnes Richard Stutter James Bumpstead Robert Edwards Richard Hegeman Henry Barlow The widow Hatch and Samuel Hill for his Factory Inhabitants of the said Parish and liable to be rated and included in the said Rate and to be assessed towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish and who so were at the time of making the said Rate And also the names of several other Inhabitants of the said Parish who are and at the time of making the said Rate or assessment were liable to be rated and assessed towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That the said Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor and also the Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch have by such Rate or assessement wrongly compounded with James Saltwell Robert Stacey one other of the Overseers Thomas Herbert and others Overseers of Houses or other Buildings within the said Parish the annual Rent or value of each such House or Building not Exceeding Ten pounds by having in such Composition remitted more than one third of the Rate by the said Act intended to be laid upon such premises respectively contrary to the Directions of the above named Act and have also compounded with James Matter for his Houses or other Buildings in the said Parish contrary to the Directions of the above recited act The same being respectively above the annual Rent or value of Ten pounds

That the said rate or assessment was on the said Seventh day of August last ratifyed and confirmed by John Nares Esquire and William Brodie Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the Race for the said County of Middlesex

Your Petitioner conceiving himself to be agggrieved by the said Rate or assessment Doth appeal to your worships therefrom and humbly prays that you will pleased to hear from in the premises And that the said Rate may be Quashed and that your worships will be pleased to order that the Church wardens and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch And such others whom it doth or may concern may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioner shall every pray Etc
