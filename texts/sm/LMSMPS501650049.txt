To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Tobias Gibson of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated for the Sum of Two hundred Pounds Stock or Personall Estate in the poor rate of the said Parish

That your Peticoner hath not any Stock or Personall rate in the said Parish & Appeal against the said rate

Yor Petitr: therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day this Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers to attend to shew cause why yor Petitr should not be Discharge from the aforesd Assessmt.

And Yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

T: Gibson
