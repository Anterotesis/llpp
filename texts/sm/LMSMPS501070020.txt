To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Ovseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Giles Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth

That About Five years agoe One John Davison Lived in the said Parish of Cripplegate and Gained A Legall Settlement there Since which time he hath withdrawn himselfe and Left in the said Parish Lydia his Wife And John and Elizabeth his Two Children which new are and Unavoidably will be A Great Charge to the said Parish If not Someway prevented And for as much as the said John Davison stands by A Recognizance bound for his Appreanced in this Hononrable Court this prsent Sessions to Answer the said Complaint.

P Tho Nash Your Petrs. therefore Most humbly pray Your Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that the sd. John Davison may be ordered to Provide for his Wife and Two Children Or Otherwise as unto your Worshipps shall Seeme Meete

And Yor Petr: shall Ever Pray Etc
