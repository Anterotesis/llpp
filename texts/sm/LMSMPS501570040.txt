To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Elizabeth Vyalls.

Sheweth That your Peticoner became bail for the appearance of John Mackneal in this Court in July Sessions last That soone for an assault After the said Mackneals Mother died in the Country where your Peticoner was obliedged to goe Soe that he could not attend this Court to withdraw his Recoginzance which is now. likely to be Estreated

That the said John Mackneal is now in the Country on Extraordinary Busynesse. and cannot attend this present Sessions But your Peticoner is well assured will attend the next Sessions to withdraw his said Recognizance.

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Respite the Recoginzance of the said John Mackneall untill the next Sessions

And Yor: peticr. shall Ever pray Etc: Eliz Vyalls
