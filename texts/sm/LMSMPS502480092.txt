To the Right Worps: his Mats: Justices of the peace of the County of Middx Sitting at Hicks hall

The Humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the peace of the Hamlet of poplar and Blackwall in the Pish of Stepney in the sd: County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Saml: Jones and Stephen Martin Leake Esqrs two of his Mats: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 9th: day of October last Thomas Jackson was on ye 29th: day of November last Removed from the Prih of St. Pauls Shadwell in the said County of Middx to the said Hamlet of poplar and Blackwall at the place of his Last Legall Settlement.

Your Petrs: finding themselves aggreived by the said order Humbly Appeales agt: the same And prays a Day may be Appointed this present Session for ye hearing and Determining of the said Appeale

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
