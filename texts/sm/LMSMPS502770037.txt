To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the [---] County of Midlx in Genall Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Midlx

Sheweth. That by Warrt or Order of Removell under the hands and seals of Thomas West and Robert [...] Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing [...] the [...] th day of this Just Sarah Hastings wife of John Hastings Marnr was removed from the Parish of St. George in the County of Midlx. to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Hastings That your Petitr: think's themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Humbly appeals against the same,

Your Petitrs: Therefore Humbly pray you will be pleased to Order the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. George and all other Persons concerned to attend this Court Same day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Indictment and Determinacons of this Court touching the premises and that your Petitrs: may have such Reliefe therein as to Justice and Law doth Appertain
