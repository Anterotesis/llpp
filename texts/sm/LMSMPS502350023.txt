To the Right Worshipfull His Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the County Middx [...] [...]

The humble quarter Sessions of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Middx of the Said [...] in the [...]

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Samuel & Webb & Stephen Hall Esqrs Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of this instant May John Clay Sarah his Wife & their Six Children, Vizt Elizabeth Clay aged about fourteen Years, William Clay aged Twelve Years, Richard Clay aged Eight Years, Sarah Clay five Years, Anne Clay aged three Years & John Clay aged Nine Weeks, were removed from the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Mildred in the Poultry London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Mildred to be the Place of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticoner are Comencing themselves agreived by the Said Warrant in Order of the Said Two Justices Do herefore [...] herefrom And [...] to hear their in he prd Speed order to the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of hamblett of Bethnall Green in the Said by [...] Middx [...] appear before [...] certain day in year Worsps to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale
