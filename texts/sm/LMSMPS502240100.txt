To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth That Catherine Barber the Widdow of Thomas Barber and her Two Children vizt. Sophia aged about Seven years and Richard aged about Five years were by an Order dated the 2d. Day of November 1724 under the Hands and talis of Sr. Henry Dutton Colt Bart and John Ellis Esqr. Two of His Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (quor unus) removed and Conveyed from the Pish of St. James Westmr in the County of Middlesex to the said Pish of St. Giles in the Fields

Yor. Petr. humbly conceive themselves agrieved thereby and Appeal from the Judgement of the Said Two Justices of the Judgemt. of this Honble Court and crave a Day of Hearing

And yor Petrs. Shall every pray Etc.
