To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon of Thomas Blackman

Sheweth That Yor Petr hath duely served the Office of one of the Petty Constables of Stanwell in this County for the Year last past

That the Steward & Jurors of the Court Leet of the Mannor & of Stanwell have nominated & appointed one John Brown of Stanwell aforesaid to be one of the Petty Constables of Stanwell aforesaid for the year ensueing as by the Paper hereunto annext doth & may appear

Yor Petr therefore prays he may be discharged from his said Office And that this Court would be pleased to Order the said Brown to attend some of his Maties Justices of the Peace [...] for this County Inhiting near Stanwell aforesaid in Order to be Sworn into the said Office for the year ensueing in the Room & stead of yor Petr

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
