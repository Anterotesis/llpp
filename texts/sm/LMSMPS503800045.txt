To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Sd. County

Sheweth That by Vrtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Burdon and Daniel Gach Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the liberty of Westmr in the said County (one where of being of the Quoram) bearing date the 5th. day of August last Richard Savill & Dorothy therein mentioned to be his wife were removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Clement Danes in the said Liberty in the County aforesaid to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of their last legall Settlement

Dan Bolton Agent for the Appellts. Yor. Petrs. think Themselves Aggrieved by the Sd. Order or Pass Warrt. of the Said two Justices of the peace, Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray that Yor. Worships will be pleased to appoin [...] a day in this present Sessions for the hearing & Determining of the same

And Yor Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
