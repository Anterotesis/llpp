To the Rt: Worpll: his Matys [---] Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor and other Inhitants of the Parish of Heston in the Hundred of Istleworth in the sd County of

Sheweth That yor. Petitioners are Rated and Assessed in the rates made to the [...] Marshealsea Kings Bench and Hospitalls & Maimed Soldiers & Mariners & likewise in other Rates larger Sumes than the Inhitants of the Parishes of Istleworth & Twickenham in the said Hundred, who are better able to pay than your Peticoners as appeares by a certain Account hereunto annexed Whereof Your Petrs. humbly Conceive themselves much aggrieved being willing to pay in proportion wth: other Neighbouring Parishes

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray Such releife in the prmises As to this Court Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
