To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Pancras in the County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph St, Lawrence & Benjamin Cox Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster in the Sd. County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Second day of July last Elizabeth Sunderland called the wife of John Sunderland and their four Children Vizt. Ann aged Eight Years and upwards Samuel aged five years upwards Sarah aged three years and upwards and Mary aged Five Months were removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said Liberty of Westmr. in the County aforesd. to the Sd. parish of St. Pancras as the place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the sd. Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace, Appeale to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining of the sd. Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Humphrey Elmes Attorney for ye Appellts.
