To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by and an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrill Esquire and John Staples Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and For the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the twenty third Day of June last Christian George Roodelph and Mary his Wife and Five Children namely George aged about twelve Years Hannah aged about Nine Years Ann aged about Seven Years Thomas Aged about three Years and John aged about Fifteen Months were removed and Conveyed From the said Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex aforesaid into the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the Place of their legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said two Justices Appeal to this County against the same and humbly pray that the said appeal may be heard this Present Sessions and that a time may be now appointed For the hearing and Determining thereof

Christ: Brewer Coll. for the Appealts.
