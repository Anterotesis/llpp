To the Right Honourable Stephen Theodore Janssan Esquire Lord Mayor of the City And to the rest of his Majestys Justices assigned to deliver the Goal of Newgate of the Prisoners therun being at Justice Hall in the Old Baily Assembled

The Humble Petition of John George

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on the Twenty Seventh Day of December last comitted to the Goal of Newgate charged on Oath with having feloniously Stolen taken and carried away a large quantity of Drapery Goods to the Value of Three Hundred Pounds and upwards the property of James Hay

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that he may at this present Sessions be brought to his Trial for the said Supposed Offence or in default there of that he may be Bailed or discharged

And your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc

John George
