To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex General Session of the Peace at the Session house for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Charles Triquet and Dr Wilncot Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Late the 15th day of February instant John Tindles was remove and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order, and Pray that your Worships will Please to Appoint some day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal, and that all persons Concerned do then attend this Court.

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc: Francis Deschamps} Church Wardens

Richd, Reeder John Grieve} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne.
