To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace hold in & for ye said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in ye County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Order of Removal or Pass Warrant and on the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of ye said County bearing Date ye Eighteenth Day of April last Ann Alcock was Removed from ye Parish of Christ Church in the said County to the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners do humbly apprehend themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal or Pass Warrant and therefore Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners Appellts: therefore humbly Pray that this Court will Appoint a Day this Present Session for ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye said Parish of Christ Church & all Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal And that Your Petitioners and Appellants may be Relieved in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners and Appellants will ever pray Etc.

May 2. 1760 Mr. Billington Sert. for Appellts.
