To the Right World. his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Thomas Greaves Churchwarden of the Hamlet of Lymehouse in the Pish [...] Stepney in the Said County.

Sheweth:

That one Elizabeth Hurst the wife of John Hurst is late from the Pish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County to the Aforesaid Hamlet of Lymehouse under prtence that about Seven a Goe her Said Husband was there Settled and paid to the Poor.

Now Soe it is may it please Yor. W [...] that yor. Petr. hath made Strict Inquiry in to the prmises. that the Said John Hurst never paid to the Church of the Said Hamlet and onely Dwelt there for abo [...] the And therefore yor. Petr. thinks himselfe [...] in the Said Removall.

Your Petr therefore most humbly [...] this Honoble. Court to order the Church [...] and Overseers of St. Giles aforesaid to attend here at a Day Certaine that the whole [...] may be further heard And yor. petr. releive as to Justices Appertaineth.

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
