To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph Aldersgate in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of William Caslon and Ebenezer Mussell Esquires of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth Day of December in the Year of our Lord 1750. Deborah Penlington Widow And her Child (lawfull Issue of her late Husband John Penlington deceased) named Richard aged Thirty Seven Years were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceived themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same.

John Jennings Thomas Burt } Overseers of the Poor of the Parish aforesaid

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray That this Court will appoint a time for hearing and determining the Said Appeal, and that all Parties concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray etc.
