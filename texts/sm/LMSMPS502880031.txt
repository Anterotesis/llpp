To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westminster in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order Under the hands and Seals of H: Vincent and Richd Lilly Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the twenty first day of December last Ann Webb widow was Removed from the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of St. James as the Place of her last legall Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

And Therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to Appoint a time for Hearing the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc

Saml. Seeldon Vestry Cl
