To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds the humble Peticon of Thomas Bennett

Sheweth That sometime past your Petr. became apprentice to Michaell Cleaver of the Liberty of Norton Folgate in this County Weaver for the Term of Seaven Yeares as by the Indre of App Enticeshipp Appearer

That your Petr. & Master with being a Roman Catholick and useing rid [...] Endeavoure to drawyorer Petr. away from the Church of England.

Yor Petr therefore most humbly prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Grant that he may be discharge from his said Apprntishipp.

P Tho Nash

And yr: Petr. shall Ever pray Etc Thomas Bennett
