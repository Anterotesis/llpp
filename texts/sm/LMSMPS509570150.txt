To the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now Assembled in Quarter Session

The Humble Petition of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the 18th day of December last under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and George Story Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Jane Mc.Kency, the Wife of John Mc.Kency and her Three Children Margaret Emmila and Charles were removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth, said to be their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving the said Jane Mc. Kency and her said Three Children to have no legal Settlement in the said Parish of Lambeth.

Humbly Pray that an Appeal against the said Order may be entered, and that the same be respited untill the next Session to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex
