To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the the Poor of the parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Thomas Quarrill Esqr. and Sr. Samuel Gower knt. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middex aforesaid bearing date the fourteenth day of September last Jane Crumpler Wife of Thomas Crumpler (who in the said Order is Say'd to be gone away from her and not to be found) and their Child Named Thomas Aged two Years and upwards were Removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the Place of their legal Settlemt.

That Your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves Aggrieved by the aforesaid Order of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore pray that this Court will appoint a Day this Present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch aforesaid and all persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

Jno Billington Attorney for Appellts.

And Yr. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
