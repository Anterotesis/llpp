To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of John Strong

Sheweth That your Petitioner [---] did this day plead guilty to an Indictment against him for making an Assault upon Bridget his Wife and was fined Twenty pounds and ordered to be imprisoned three Months in New Prison at Clerkenwell

That your Petitioner is willing to allow his said Wife Two Shillings a Week for her Support and Maintenance which is the utmost your Petitioner can possibly afford to allow having two Children one of which Your Petitioner pays now and has agreed to pay four shillings a Week for the bringing up thereof and the other your Petitioner intends to bring up in his own Trade of a Buckle Chape maker, Your Petitioner getting no more by his Labour than Twelve or Fourteen shillings a Week, but his Wife insists be allowed 7s. a Week

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worships that the said fine and Imprisonment may be taken off and that your Petitioners said Wife may be ordered to take such reasonable Allowance by the Week as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc

John Strong
