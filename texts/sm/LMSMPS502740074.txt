To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of ye. Peace for ye County of Middx as their Genl. Quarter Sessions as Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of yr Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Rebecca Burncrass Widow aged about Twenty five years a Parishioners of the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square is Chargeable be & releived by ye said Parish being Poor & Unable to Work towards her Maintanance & being reputed to be a Lunatick

That James Walters of St. John Street in yr County of Middx Factor & Father of yr said Rebecca Burncrass is of a Sufficient Ability to releive & [...]

Your Petrs: therefore Pray your Worships would be Pleased to Order yr said James Walters to Contribute towards her releife & Maintainance

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

S: Parry Vestry Cl
