To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions of the peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Richmond in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass warrant under the Hand and Seal of Thomas Lane Esqr. One of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing Date the 28th Day of October last past Grace West was removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Clement Deans in the County of Middx aforesd. to the said parish of Richmond as the place of her last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners Concieve themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to your Worships against the said pass Etc Judgment of the said Justice of the peace, And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto yr. Petitioners and that your Worsh [...] will be pleased Now to hear and dete [...] the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Wm Clark agent for the Appelts.
