To the Rt. Worshipll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in qr. to Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Shenley in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order bearing date the 14th. day of May last under the Hands and Seals of Nicholas Jefferys and Stephen Beckingham Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Mary Handcock was passe'd and conveyed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Shenley in the County of Hertford as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Handcock.

And that Yor. Petrs. finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices doe humbly appeal agt. the same

And pray Yor. Worships would be pleased to Order that the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars doe attend this Court some Day this Prsent. Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray

Robt. Hunks} Church- -warden

Dan: Wilshre [...] Overseer
