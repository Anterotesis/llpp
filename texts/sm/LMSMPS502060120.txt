To the Rt. Worll: his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in the General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks hall Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Feild in the said County

Sheweth

That Ann Cooke aged abt 37 years Singlewoman was by an Order dated the 29 day of September last under the hands & Sealess of John Milner & Jno Richardson Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Qurq un)- removed and [...] Convey'd from the Parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the said County to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Feild, as the place of her last legall Settlemt

Your Petr therefore thinking themselves Aggrived there by humbly Appeale there from

Therefore humbly Pray that Some day this Session this Court would be pleased to order for the Determining the same & that all person concerned do attend.

And you Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.
