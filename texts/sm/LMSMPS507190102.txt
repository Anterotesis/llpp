To the Worshipful his majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of middlesex in their general Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Abbotts Hensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under she Hands and Seals of James Fielding and Thomas Edwards Esquires two of this Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 28th.. day of October last John Domminico Mary his Wife & their five Children (N [...] ) John Samuel aged about Nine years Ann Theirssa aged about Seven years Joseph aged five years & upwards Louis aged three years and Maria Martha aged about eleven Months were removed from the parish of Saint James Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary Abbotts Hensington as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners Apprechend [...] themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to your worships against the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some proper day for hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Ja Benny John Shrimpton} Church Wardens

John Hooper Wm Ro [...] } Overseers of the poor
