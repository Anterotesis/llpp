To the Rt. [...] for the City of London and other the Jufe [...] Sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Baley

The humble Peticon of Richard White

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr: was Comitted to the Goale of Newgate by Warrant from the Warden of the Mint dated the 26th: day of January Charged thereby for deminishing the Current Mild many of this Kingdome and is now remaining a Prisoner in the Said Goale

He humbly prayeth that he may bee tryed this prsent Sessions or that he may bee admitted to Baile as by the law in that case is directed and that this his prayer may bee recorded

And he Shall ever pray Etc
