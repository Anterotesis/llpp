To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Thomas Bennett and Richard Manley Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (me where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of July Instant Elizabeth Ballett Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barras as the place of her last legal Settlement

D: Bolton Attrs. For the Appelts. Your Petitrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Appeal to this Court against the same & Humbly pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint some day in the next General Session of the Peace to be held for the said County for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray
