[...] full His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty & Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of S. Greene and R. Davies Esquires two of His. Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex having date the Twenty third day of January last Charlottee [...] Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish Saint John & Wapping in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint James whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determined the said Appear

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas. Dyson Solicitor for the Appellants
