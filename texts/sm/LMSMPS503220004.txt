To the Rt: Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in a Generall Quarter Sessions.

The humble. Peticon of Robt. Pilkington one of Constables of the parish of Kensington in the Said County.

Petition of Robert Pilkington Constable within the parish of Kensington to be discharged from his Office

Elias Hirons Sworn in Court constable in his Stead

1737
