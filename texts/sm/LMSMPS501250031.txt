Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull her Mayes Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Edward Feilds

Sheweth That by order of Richard Kirby Thomas Hampton & Bastwick Johnson Esqrs three of her Majesties Justices of ye Peace of this County Dated Under their hands & Seales ye 29th. day of September one thousand Seaven hundred & eleaven Wherein they adjudge yor Peticonr. to be the presentive Father of a Female Bastard Child born of ye body of Jane Phillips Wife of Leonard Phillips And yor. Peticonr. hinds himselfe aggrieved appeals order

Yor. Peticonr. humbly appeals against the sd ordr. & humbly prays an order of this Court for ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye Parishe St. Pauls Shadwell in ye Sd County & all pties concerned to attend ye Court att a time certain to avoide the Judgemt. & Determinacon of this Court touching ye Prmisses as to yor Worshipps Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Peticonr. Shall ever Pray Etc
