To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of George Austin.

Sheweth That for the Fack whereof your Petr. stood Indicted in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions Your worshipps were pleased to Fine him yor. and for Nonpaymt. thereof to comitt him to New Prison in this County where he now Remains

That yor. Petr. hath used all the meanes he hath been able by himselfe & Friends to make an Agreemt. with his adversary and hath offered him all he is able to give to that purpose But his demands are Soe unreasonable that he cannot by Reason of his Poor Circumstances by noe meanes Comply with and your Petr. not being able to pay the sd Five and humble on buritting himselfe to the Mercy Goodness of this Honble Court

Yor Petr therefore humbly Prays Yor Worspps to [...] gate the sd [...] Shall V [...]

And your Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc

P Th: Nash
