To the Right Worshipll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genl: Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of John Waylett of Grubb Street ward in the parish of Saint Gyles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx Founder

Sheweth That yor Peticoner is Rated Â£2:10s: per Annum for & towards the necessary Relief of the poor of the said parish of Saint Gyles without Cripplegate for this present year 1730

That yor Peticoner Apprehends he payes more in proporcon than the rest of his Neighbours who are of the like abillity & Circumstances, therefore thinks himself aggreived by the said Rate, And humbly appeals against the same, Praying such Relief in the prmis, as to yor. Worpps shall seem Meet

And yor: peticoner shall Ever pray Etc.

John Waylett
