To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in their Generall Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Wilsdon in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Order under the hands and Seales of George Hampfield & Joseph Rous Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the Said County (Quor unus) bearing date the Fourth day of Aprill last past John Fletcher and Mary his wife were removed from the Parish of Hampstead in the Said County to the Said parish of Wilsdon as the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said John Fletcher

Yor Petr. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly appeale therefrom & Pray that this Court will please to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of Hampstead to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs. as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc
