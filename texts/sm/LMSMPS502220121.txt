To the Rt. Worspl. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Sitting at Hicks hall.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor to the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth

That by Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Kyrby and Bastwick Johnson Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing Date the 2d. Day of July last, John Cornelius and Mary his Wife, and Elizabeth Anne , Martha , Isabell , and John Cornelius their five [...] Parish of St. Paul Shadwell, in the said County of Middx: to the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall aforesaid as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said John Cornelius and his said Wife and five Childn:

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removall of the Said two Justices humbly Appeal agt. the same and Pray your Worsps will be Pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal, this present Sessions and that your Petr. may be Relieved as to your Worpps- Shall Seem Meet

And Your Petitionrs: Shall Ever Pray Etc.
