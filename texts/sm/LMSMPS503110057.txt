To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Mieddx in Generall Sessions assembled,

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the [---] Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the Old Artillery Ground in the sd. County

Sheweth That by a Warrt. or Order of removell bearing date the [---] Sixteenth day of this Instant August under the hands and Seals of Nahll. Chandler and Ralph Harwood Esqrs: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Elizabeth Wilford the wife of Richard Wilford was removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shreditch in the sd. County to the Liberty of the Old Artillery Ground in the County afore said as the place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Peticoners thinks them selves agreived by the Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices and Humbly appeals against the same

G Meidch Altor for the appll. Your Peticoners therefore Humbly pray you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Leonard Shore ditch and all other Persons Concerned to Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the Premises And that your peticone [...] May have Such releife therein as to Justice and Law Doth Appertain

And your Peticoners [...] as in Duty bound Shall pray Etc.

Peter Lekeaux Tho: Teaton} Overseers of the poor
