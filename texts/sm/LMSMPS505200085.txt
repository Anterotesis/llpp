To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows London Wall in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of James Esdaile and Benjamin Cowley Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of November One thousand seven hundred and Sixty two Susanna Holmes Single Woman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows London Wall as the place of her last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal against the same and May that the hearing and Determining thereof May be adjourned to the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be held in and for the said County and that the Court will be pleased to Appoint a day in the said next Sessions In the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all other Persons concerned then to Attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the then Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Deyongh Atty. for the Appellts.
