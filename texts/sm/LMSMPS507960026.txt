To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County at the Session House for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sudbowen in the County of Suffolk.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Settlement or Removal under the Hands and Seals of H. R. Cowitenay and John Griffiths Esqrs. two of his, Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex [...] ing, date the twenty third day of February One thousand seven hundred and eighty five John Mullins Aged about four Years was removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Sudbowen as the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order of the said Justices And Pray this Court to make such Order therein as to them shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners shall ever prays

Wm Browne Agent for the Petitioners
