To the Right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Harefeild in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That Anne Porter was by Order of two of his Majties. Justices of the peace of this County past from the parish of Hillington to the said parish of Harefeild alledging that to be the place of her last legall Settlement.

Jo. Arden Yor. Petr. therefore finding themselves aggreived by the said Order Humbly appeale from the Same to the Judgmt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the same.
