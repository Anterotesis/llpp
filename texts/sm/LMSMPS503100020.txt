To the Honourable The Bench of Justices at Hick's hall in the County of Middlesex Present

The Humble Petition of Susannah Spiers , now a Prisoner in New Prison Clarkenwell;

Sheweth That Whereas the having layn there ever Since the 22d. day of April last there being no prosecution Against her, it being for a pretended assault and having no friends to Assist me, Saving a husband, who is incapable to do it by having lost one of his arms In the Honorbe. the Merchants Service.

Your Poor Petitioner humbly prays your honours would take it into Consideration, & Order her discharge And She (as in Duty bound) Shall Ever Pray Etc

Su Surier
