To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) dated the twenty Eighth day of November last part John Harriath Jane his wife and their two Children to wit Elizabeth aged ten Years and upwards and Samuel aged four Years and upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Martins the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Aldermanbury as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment By the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to the Honourable Court against the same And Pray that their Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved [...] them and that the [...] and Determination thereof may be adjourned until the next Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Rob Holder Sol for Appellts.
