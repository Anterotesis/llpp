To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of Charles Feltham of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the sd County Esqr.

Sheweth That your Peticoners rated in the poors rate of the said parish for the Sum of Fifty pounds rent more. then be pays as he is ready to make appeare.

That yer petr did appeal last year & was relieved notwithstanding which they have this yeare. over Rated your Peticoner Wherefore he humbly. Conceived himselfe aggrieved and Appeals against the same.

Yor Petitr: therefore humbly prays such relief in the prmises as to this Court shall seeme Meet and yor petr shall Ever pray Etc

Charles Feltham
