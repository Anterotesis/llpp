To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in & for the sd. County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of Geo Howard & Ricd. Manley Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the Liberty of Westminster (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23d. Day of Janry last Jane Andrews Wido. was removed from the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the sd. Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt the sd. Order do appeale

from Chimmow agt. the Appellts. Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear & Determine the matter to [...] the said Appeal

And your Petrs: shall pray Etc

Middx At the General Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County aforesd. on Monday the twenty third Day of February-in the 20th. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain France and Ireland Defender of the Faith

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margt. Westmr. in the County of Middlesex have Notice of this Appeal & that they & all Persons Concerned do attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clock in the fore noon to hear and Abide the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal

By the Court Waller
