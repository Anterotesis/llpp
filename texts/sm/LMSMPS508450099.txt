To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session held at the Session House Clerkenwell Green in and for the County of Middlesex on Monday the 20th. day of April 1789.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of William Lambert

Sheweth That Your Petitioner on or about the 20th. days of March last was convicted on the Information of one Thos Mather before John Staples Esqr. one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex in the Sum of Â£20 for that your Petitioner sold one Pair of Gloves without having the stamped Tickett affixed thereto as the Act in that oan directs.

That your Petitioner conceiving himself aggrived by such Conviction is advised to appeal against such conviction on the said Information in manner afsd.

Therefore Your Petitioner most humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to permit him to enter his appeal before you against such Conviction.

And Your Petitioner & Appellant will ever pray Etc

William Lambert 22d April 1789.
