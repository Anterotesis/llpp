To the Right Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Anne Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth. That by an ordr. dated the 22th. day of June last past ordr. the hands and Seales of Ralph Bucknall & Richard Dyott Esqrs: two of her Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. one Elizabeth Flint widow late wife of James Flint was removed from the Parish of St. Anne Westmr. in this County alleaging the Same to be the place of her last legall Settlemt.

That yor. petrs. finding themselves Aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the Said two Justices, doe humbly Appeale to yor. Worshipps therefore;

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Pray yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint a day of that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds aforesaid may attend this Court to heare and abide the Judgemt. and determinacon thereof touching this Appeale:

And yor: Petrs: Shall Pray Etc
