To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwarke in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Dukinfield and Richard Ricards Esqrs. two of His Majesy's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Dated the Thirteenth day of May 1747 William Price and Mary his Wife were Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary White Chappel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave Southwark as the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the said William Price and Mary his Wife

Your Petitioners finding themselves agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court would appoint a Day at this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chappel and all Partys Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

13th. July 1747 And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Knowles Richd: Wilkins} Churchwardens

Thos. Selby Tho Wheelhouse
