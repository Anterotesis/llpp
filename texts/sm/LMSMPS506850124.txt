To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knt. and the rest of his Majestys Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quater Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Peters Cheeshill in the County of Southampton

Sheweth

That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Handsand Seals of John Penleaze Esqr. and David Wilmot ESqr. Two of his majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing dale the 23d. day of Septembr. 1778- Elizabeth Pickernell Wife of William Pickernell and her Three Children name [...] William Aged Seven years and upwards Jane Aged five years and [...] upwards and Margat. Aged about four Months were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Peter Cheeshill in the County of Southampton where by your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore pray that a day may be appointed for the hearing and a dermining such Appeal and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch may be ordered to attend to hear and abide the Judgment of the Court concerning the same Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray etc
