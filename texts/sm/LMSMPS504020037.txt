To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Enfield in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Quarrell Esqrs. Sir Samuel Gower Knight two of It is Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of March last past John Sparks Hannah his Wife & their two Children namely Sarah aged eight Years and John aged eight Weeks were Removed & Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Enfield as the place of their last legal Settlement.

The Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the said Order humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same & Pray that Your Worships will grant them such Relief in the Promisses as shall Seem meet & [...]

And the Appellants will Pray Etc.

G: Talmash Agent for the Appellants
