To the Right Worll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Chappel Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Town of [...]

Sheweth

That lately one Joseph Durrant wth. [...] Warrant under the hands & seals of Nicholas Grive & Francis Merit Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace of the said County from the towns of Southhall in the County of Midd to the said Towne Uxbridge

Yor. Peticonr think themselves aggriest wth the Indgent. of the said two Justices & doe appeale to this Court & humbly [...] premises considered the [...] poor of Southhall & [...] may attend this Court [...] to heare & abide the finall dud & [...] of this Court touching this Appeale

And yor petr shall did pray Etc
