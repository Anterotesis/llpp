Middlex To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Shepperton in the said County

Sheweth that by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Paul Vaillant and Stephen Cole Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty seventh Day of August last past Frances the Wife of James Hartfield and their Three Children mamely Frances of the Age of Five years and upwards James of the Age of Three Years and upwards and Thomas of the Age of Three Months and upwards were Removed from the parish of Twickenham in the County of Middlesex unto the said parish of Shepperton in the said County Alledging the said Parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Frances Hartfield and her said Three Children

That your petitioners conceiveing themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will please to hear them in the premisses and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Twickenham aforesaid may Appear before you at a certain Day to be Appointed to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Rd. Tallemach Sollr the appeallts:
