To William Mainwaring Esquire Chairman of the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

Richard Barnard Fisher Esquire against the Chapel Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the precinct Norwood in the parish of Hayes in the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Richard Barnard Fisher Esquire Appellant

Sheweth That being aggrieved in a certain Rate or Assessment made and published in the Month of June Pursant for the Relief of the poor of the said precinct of Norwood in the psh of Hayes on Account of the Inequality of the said Rate he the said Appellant being overrated in the said Rate and one James whiteley Yeoman in the said Rate or assessment named being underrated in the said rate He the said Appellant humbly entreats that he may be Permitted to lodge his appeal Against the said Rate on Assessment and that the same may be duly examined and enquired into And such Relief given as to the Justices at their present Session Assembled Shall seem meet

And Your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc

L B Fisher June 30th. 1795
