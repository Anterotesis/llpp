To the Rt. Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal. of John Lowe.

Sheweth That by Order Dated the First day of Aprill Anno Dom 1718 under the hands and Seales of Thomas Rand and Isaac Tillard Esqrs. two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor un) Your Petitr was adjudged the Putative Father of a Female Bastard Child Lately borne in the Parish of St Mary Islington in this County of the Body of Jane Benskin of the same Parish Singlewoman

That Your Petr. conceives himselfe aggrieved by the said order of the said Two Justices and Appeals against the same to this Court,

That [...] your Petr: was not untill very lately Served with a Coppy of the said Order of Adjudication which Notice being soe very Short yor Petr: is not [...] [---] able to gott his Wittnesses ready to try the sd Appeal this Sessions.

Yor Petr: therefore humbly prays yor worshipps will be pleased to Reseite his Recognizance. untill the next Generall Quarter Sessions and likewise the hearing of the sd Appeal

And Yor. Petitr Shall Ever pray Etc. John Lowee
