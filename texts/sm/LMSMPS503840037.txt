To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Ja. Clitherow and John Awstick two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 13th. Day of January last Samuel Stedman was removed from the Parish of Littleton in the County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of St. Clement Danes is the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and Determine the matter touching the said Appeal

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appellts.

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
