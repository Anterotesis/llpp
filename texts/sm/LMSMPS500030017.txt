To the hoble. Bench of Justices at Hick's Hall

The humble of Joann Blanco

Humbly Sheweth That your petr. had som goods in his Chamber seizd by the Constable that appre [...] hended him upon a pretended Suspicion of being a priest, wch goods are said to be deposited or the Cloack of the Rate his hand That year Petr. being last to a time Cleard at the Kings Bench Barr by proclamation, and being now sessions to return into Spains his na- tive Country.

The most humbly prays your Honors. to give order that the said goods may be restord. unto your petr. or his St sligm

And he will ever pray Etc
