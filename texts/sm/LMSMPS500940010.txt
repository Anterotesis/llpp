To the Rt Worspll her Matis Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Bullock

Sheweth That your Petr. was Lately [...] Bastard Child of the while Wm Coles to the Reputed Father [...] was Borne in the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in this County and the sd [...] his [...] in this Honble [...] Cort the next Genall Quarter Session But for a [...] the sd Parish without an Order of this Honble Cort Refuseing to a [...] [...] a Sufficient Maintey name towards the releife of her Selfe & Child & paymt. of her [...]

Yor Petr therefore most humbly Prayes your worspps to be pleased [...] to Order her Such Releife from she sd. Parish [...] as unto your worspps Shall Seem meet

P Tho: Nash

And Yor Per Shall Ever prayes
