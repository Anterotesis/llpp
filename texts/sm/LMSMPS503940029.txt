To the Worshipfyull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session of the Peace in & for the sd. County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Peter le Poor London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of John Milner and Philip Dyot square two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one of the Quor) bearing Date the 9th. Day of March last Paul Wedding aged g Yrt.d upwd. was removed from the Parish of St. [---] Pancras in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. Peter lo Poor London afored as the Place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Paul Wedding whereby your Petrs. Conceive themselves agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do Appeal

Batten for the Appell. Yr. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that a short Day may be Appointed this prest. Session to hear and Determine the matter of the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall Pray Etc
