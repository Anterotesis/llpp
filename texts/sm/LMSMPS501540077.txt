To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County. of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of William Medcalfe & Mary Rose of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the sd County.

Sheweth That your peticoners are severally rated in the poors. Rate of the said Parish

That your peticoners are very poor people and live in very Small rents and have large Familys to mainteyne and are not able to pay to the poors rate and Appeal against the Same.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray they may be wholly discharged from paying towards the sd poors rate

And Yor Petr. Shall Ever pay etc.

Wm Medcalfe Mary Rose.
