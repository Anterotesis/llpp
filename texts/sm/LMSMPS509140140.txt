To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of H Reynett [---] DD and John Robinson Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty eighth day of July last Mary Wescott wife of John Wescott and Robert Shaw her Child aged one month and three days were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Wescott and Robert Shaw her Child

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and humbly Appeal against the same and pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in the present Session for the hearing and determination thereof

W L & J. Newman Sollr for Appellants}
