To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Sessions assembled

The Petitioner of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Henley upon Thames in the County of Oxford

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal Under the Hands and seals of Robert Smith and R. Davis Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justice's of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex Hearing Date the nineteenth day of November Now last Past John Stone a Lunatic with Elizabeth his Wife and their From childern (viz) Samuel, thomas Elizabeth and Bethel were removed from the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Henley upon Thomas aforesaid

That Your Petitioners Conceiving the said John Stone his wife and Family are not Parishioners belonging to the said Parish of Henley upon thomas beg leave to Appeal against the said Order of Removal [...] they have given Notice to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the of the Poor of the [...] Hamlet of Ratcliffe, They therefore humbly entreat the Court to order a day in the present Sessions for hearing and determining the said Appeal Thos. Cooper

Henley upon Thames Sollicr. to the Appells 5th. Decemr. 1791,
