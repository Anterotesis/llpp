To the Worshipfull Thos Lane Esqr and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Garrad Vandegutcht one of the Constables of the Parish of Georges Hanover Square in the said County

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner was tired for an assault committed by him in the Executing a Warrant of George Wright Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx and was by the Jury found Guilty and sentenced by the Court to be imprisoned for six Months Your Petitioner most humbly Prays your Worships to take his hard Case into Consideration as Your Petr: family support Intirely depends on the hand Labour & Industry of your Petr. and that if the said Sentence is carried unto Execution it will be the total ruin of his Trade & business to yr with his wife & Child who has no support but through Your Pet. industry.
