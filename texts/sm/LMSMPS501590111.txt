To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the- County of Middlesex in their General quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Mary le Bow in ye City of London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorn) bearing Date the third Day of January instant Eleanor Barnes an infant aged about Five years and an half-was removed from out of the parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the said County unto the said parish of St Mary le Bow, alleading the said- parish to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elenor- Barnes That your Petr conceiving themselves aggrieved by the- said Order of the two Justices do most humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly Pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Giles in the Feilds may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Worpps touching this- Appeal

And they shall ever pray Etca
