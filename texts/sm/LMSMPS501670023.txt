To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quartr. Sessions of the Peace [...] now Assembled

The humble peticonr & Appeal of John Prince of the parish of St. Mary Islington in the sd County.

Sheweth

That yor Petr. being rated to the Poor rate of the sd Parish for 12t P And rent And also rated towards the repairs of the Highways of the sd. Parish much more than he humbly conceives he ought to pay wherefore he Appeals agst. the Same rates

And Prays Such releif in the prmisses as to yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet

And yor. Petr Shall ever pray Etc

John Prince
