To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Natrum Crosley of the Liberty of Holy-well Street in the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County Aforesaid Gent:

Sheweth That your Petitioner was no Monday the Eighth Day of April Instant Elected Constable of the said Liberty for the yeat Ensuing Notwithstand -ing he hath served all other Offices in the said parish hath left of Trade hath the Honour to be in the Commission of Land Tax Therefore Prays that Ye. Worships will be pleased to Order that He may be Excused from serving the said Office of Constable as Yr. Worships were pleaded to Order in the Case and on the Petition of Mr. James Burye of the said Parish Janry. ss 1749.

And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall. Etc

Natrum Crosley
