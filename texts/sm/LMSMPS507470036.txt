Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in their general Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of William Hyde and Thomas Ballard Esquires bearing date the 28th. day of November now last past Elizabeth Eggerton Wife of Richard Eggerton (gone from her) and their Child to wit Charles aged one year and upwards [---] [...] were removed from the [---] Parish of St. Martin in the Fields [...] in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. James Westminster as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the sd. Order or Pass warrant and pray that your Worships [...] will appoint some day in the present Session for the Churchwns. and Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Martin to appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching this Appeal.

And yr. Petrs. shall ever pray

James Dyson Solr. for the Churchwns. & Overseers. of the poor of the parish of St. James Westmr.
