To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex their present General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of St Andrew Holborn which his above the hare in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of W,, L.. Kingsman and J Mercer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifth day of July Instant Thomas Ray and Elizabeth his Wife were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conveiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and humbly request that the benefit of their said Appeal may le David unto them until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone may then be Ordered to attend to abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc

A Gliddon Soll for the Appellants
