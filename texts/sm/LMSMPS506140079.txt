To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace by Adjournment at Hicks Hall in the Said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyoland John Cox Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of October One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy One Peter Morgan was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the said Liberty and County and the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and Appeal to the Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will Please to appoint a time in this Present Session Hear and Determine the said Appeals And that all persons concerned may then attend

George Shakespear Henry Russell James Showell John Hewitt} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Saint George Hanover Square
