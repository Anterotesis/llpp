To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Richard Ford Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th: day of June last past Hannah Dawson and her Child Mary Dawson aged Seven Years or there abouts were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Dawson and Mary Dawson her Child.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and Pray your Worships will Appoint a Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc S. Malbon} Sollicitor for the Appellants
