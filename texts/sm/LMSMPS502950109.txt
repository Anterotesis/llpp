To the Worship full his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Genll. Quar Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Sepulchres in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an order under the Hands and Seales of Tho: Exelbee and Ja Brain Esqrs Two of his Majtys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the tenth day of Septemr Last Bennet Wasdell was removed from the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County to the said parish of St Sepulchre's whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the Adjudgment of the said Two Justices and pray Your Worshipswill be pleased to order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamlet of Bethnal Green and all persons Concerned do attend this Court [...] day in this present Sessions to hear and Abide the [...] Judgment and determination of this Court touching said Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs shall Etc.
