To the Right Worshipfull Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of the Chappell Wardon & Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St. John [---] Baptised in the Savoy in this County

Sheweth That by an Ordr under the hands & Seals of Thomas Railton & Gustavus Sheldon Esqrs. two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the City & Libty of Westmr. Dated the Twenty Eighth day of July last, One Margarrett Camron an Infant Aged about Nine Months was removed & Conveyed by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margarett Westmr. in this County. to Ye Peticoners to be Reced & Provided for by them according to Law

Your Peticonrs therefore humbly [...] aggreived the said Order and humbly Appeale to this Court agt. the Same And pr [...] Your Worshipps will be Pleased to [...] appoint a day for all Partys Concer [...] to Attend & abide the Judgmt. of the Court Touching the sd. Appeal And Your Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc
