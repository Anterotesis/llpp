To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of William Pemberton

Most humbly sheweth

That there being now a Vacancy in Edmonton hundred of an High Constable Your Worspps were pleased last Sessions to nominate George Crockford Edward Hundsdon and your Peticoner in order that one of them should serve the Office of High Constable.

That your Peticoner is above sixty years of age and doth most humbly Conceive himselfe altogether unqualifyed to serve the said Office.

Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to excuse him from serving the Sd Office.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
