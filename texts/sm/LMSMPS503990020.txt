To the Worshipfull his majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of st James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass warrant or order under the hands and Seals of George Errington and James Fraser Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 2d day of November 1749 Jane Davis Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of St. Martin in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St James as the Place of her last legal Settlement when by Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore humbly Prays this Court will appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned May then Attend

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray

Saml Seddon agent for St. Jamee's
