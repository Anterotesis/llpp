The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye Pish of St. Martin in ye fields Middx:

agt. Mary Scott to be hd. 20th Feb next 9 cl

Ref to the Justices of the Tower & such other Justices as shall think [---] Division fil to attend or any [---] two of them to consider of the Petition & upon their Opinions thereon, & Mr. Studley to have notice of the meeting wch is to be at the Courthouse is White Chappel

1s.

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd. P adjorn die Martis Scitt decimo Septimo die Januar anno regui Dui Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc decimo tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that the within named Mary Scott have notice of this peticon, and that she doe attend this Court on Monday the twentyth day of February next at nine [---] of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why she should not allow & pay such weekly sume of money as this Court shall think fit for & towards the releife & maintenance of her daughter & three Grandchildren within menconed in ease of the Inhitants of the parish of St. Martin in the fields in this County,

P Cur Walter
