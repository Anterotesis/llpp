To Sir John Hawkins Knight Chairman and the rest of [...] his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Barwell Brown Esqr

Sheweth That by a certain Rate or Assessment made by the the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex on the Inhabitants and Occupiers of Houses Shops Warehouses Chambers Lands Tenements and other Premisses of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex on or about the 18th. Day of October 1774. for one Quarter of a year beginning the 24th. Day of June 1771. and ending the 29th. of September following for the Necessary Relief of the Came impolent blind and other Poor of the said Parish your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the Sum of 16s. and 8d. for his Chambers in Lincolns Inn for and toward the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That by a certain other Rate or Assesment made as aforesaid on or about the 16th. Day of January 1772 for one other Quarter beginning the said 29th. of September 1771. and ending the 25th. of December following for the purpose above mentioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the Sum of 13s. and 4d. for his sd Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 22d. Day of May 1772 for one other Quarter beginning the 25th. Day of December 1771. and ending the 25th. of March following for the purpose above mentioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 10th. Day of July 1772. for one other Quarter beginning the sd. 25th. Day of March 1772. and ending the 24th. of June following for the purpose abovementioned Your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the like Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made on or about the 25th. Day of October 1772 for one other Quarter beginning the said 24. of June 1772 and ending the 29th. of September following for the purpose above mentioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the Sum of 16s. and 8d. for

his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 15th. Day of January 1773. for one other Quarter beginning the said 29th. of September 1772. and ending the 25th. of December following for the purpose abovementioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 14th. Day of May 1773. for me other Quarter beginning the sd. 25th. of December 1772. and ending the 25th. of March following for the purpose abovementioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the like Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 28th. Day of July 1773. for one other Quarter beginning the said 25th. of March 1773. and ending the 24th. of June following for the purpose abovementioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the like Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 15th. Day of October 1773. for me other Quarter beginning the said 24th. Day of June 1773. and ending the 29th. of September following for the purpose abovementioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the like Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.

That by a certain other Rate or Assessment made as aforesaid on or about the 13th. Day of January 1774. for one other Quarter beginning the said 29th. of September 1773. and ending the 25th. of December following for the purpose abovementioned your Petitioner is rated and assessed in the like Sum of one Pound for his said Chambers for and twards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish.
