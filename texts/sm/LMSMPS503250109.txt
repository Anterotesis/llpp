To the Rt. Worpl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions Assembled.

We the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County Do most humbly beg leave to Certify to this Honoble Court, That Thos. Bunny of the parish of Saint John the Evangelist Westminster, has made satisfaction and indemnified [...] our said parish from a Bastard Child born there and filiated upon the said Thomas Bunny, & for which he stands now upon his Recognizance; wherefore it is our humble request, that this Honoble Court will be pleased to discharge him and his Bail by permitting them to with draw their said Recognizance Dated 5th. Septr. 1737

Thos. Prior} Church Warden

Thos Wilson Rrd Martin Jon: Price} Overseers of the Poor
