To the Rt Worspll. her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Hester Goulding

Sheweth

That On Monday last Yor Petr was Comitted to the House of Correcton in this County being Suspected to be a Loose Idle and Disorderly Person

That Yor Petr is A Young Woman And is heartily Sorry for the Offences She hath Comitted And Resolved for the future to Amend her Life and Conversation And humbly Submitting herselfe to the Goodness and Mercy of this Honourable Court.

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays Yor Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that She may be discharged from her sd Imprisonment

And yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc
