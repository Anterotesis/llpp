To the right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London.

Sheweth. That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of Thomas Lane and John Poulton Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the thirty first day of August last Past, Thomas Halsey (mentioned in the said warrant or order to be Aged about twenty eight years) was removed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the west London as th last legall Place of Settlement of the said Thomas Halsey

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same, and [...] Pray this court will appoint some day in this present sessions for the church Wardens [...] and overseers of the poor of the said Parish St. Clement danes and all other Parties concerned to attend this court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

M; Howard agent for the Appellants
