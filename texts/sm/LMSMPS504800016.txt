To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Tarring in the County of Sussex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass under the hand and Seal of John Horne Esqr. One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx bearing Date the Twenty first day of November one Thousand seven hundred & fifty nine Mary Jefferys Widow apprehended in the parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westmr. in the said County of Middx as a Rogue & Vagabond was Conveyed from the said parish of St. Margaret to the said parish of Tarring as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggreived

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto Yor. Petitioners & that the hearing & Determining thereof may be Adjourned untill the next General Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middx and that this Court will be pleased to Appoint a day in the said next Sessions for the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Margt. & all persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear & Abide the sd Judgemt. Determination of the same Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
