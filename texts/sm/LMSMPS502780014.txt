To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions att Hick's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the said County

Sheweth

That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Williams & Bromfeild Esqrs two of His Matys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date ye Eighth day of September 1731 Thomas Richins and Elizabeth his Wife together with Elizabeth aged about Eight Years and Thomas Intant aged about three months their Children And also John by Margaret his first Wife aged about Sixteen Years were Diserted to be removed and passed from & out of the Parish of Chelsea in the County of Middx to the pacis to of St George Hanover Square in the said County And Whereas there was removed and passed the said Elizabeth Richins and Elizabeth and Thos her said Children from the said Parish of Chelsea to the said Parish of St George Hanover Square as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. Whereby the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square think themselves Agrived

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays this Court will Appoint a day for hearing the said Appeal and that all Persons concernd may Attend at the Same time

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray George Carpenter Cecill Wray
