To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justies of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genlle. Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of John Milner and R: Gifford Esqrs Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the Second Day of October Instant Sarah Slade wife of William Slade and their Child James aged about Three yeares were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the sd. County of Middx: to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgmt. of the Sd. Two Justices, and Pray your Worships will be pleases to Appoint some day in this present Sessions to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc.
