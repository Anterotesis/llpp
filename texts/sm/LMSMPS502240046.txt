To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace in their Genl. Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeale of John Godfrey of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell.

Sheweth.

That Your petr. in Charged to the poor & Scavengers Rates of the Said parish, for the House that He Rents in the [---] Same, much more that He Humbly Conceive He ought to pay.

There for your petr. most Humbly prays this Court will be Pleased to great Such Releif in the Premisses, as to Your Worships [...] Shall Seem meett.

And your Petr. as in Duty Bound Shall Ever Pray. Etc

John Godfrey
