To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church warden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County of Middlesex-

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Ludbey and Oliver Lambart Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of February last past Sarah Johnson Widow of William Johnson was removed from his parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the said Hamlet of Bethnal Green as the place of her last legall Settlement

By which said Warrant or order of the said two Justices of the peace your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved and humbly appeale to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the premisses att to this Court shall seem meet.

And Yor. Peticoners shall Ever pray

Signed by Order of the said Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor

Saml. Sadler their Agent
