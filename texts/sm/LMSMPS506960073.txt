To the Rt Worpfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for County of Middx

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of Paddington in this County

Sheweth That by order dated the 9th day of June last under then hands & seals of Joseph Short & Benjamin Hilton Esqrs two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this County (quord number) one [...] Hugh Edwards a poor impotent pson was lately removed from the Pish of St James Clerkenwell in this County to the sd Parish of Paddington as the place of his last legall Settlemt

That your Petr find themselves aggrieved by the said order of removall & doubt not but to make itt appear to this Honble Court that the said Hugh Edwards hath noe legall Settlement in the sd Parish of Paddington

Your Petrs therefore humbly appeal agt the sd order of the said two Justices & pray that Yor Worpps would be pleased to appoint a day [...] [...] this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd pish of St James Clerkenwell other pties Concernd to attend this Court accordingly to hear & abide the Judgt & determination of this Court upon the sd appeal

And yor Petrs shall pray Etc.
