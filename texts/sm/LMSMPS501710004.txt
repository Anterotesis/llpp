Peticon of Geo: west Cripplegate poors rate

refr to Mr. Browne Mr. Fuller Mr. Tillard Mr. whetham Mr. Rand or 2 to ex & cert

The Humble Petition of Geo: West of ye Parish of Cripple- -gate wth.out

Middx ss: Ad General Quartial Session paris Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John Street in Com prd. die Lune scilt Septimo die July Anno regui Dui Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto.

It is Orderd by this Court that it be, And it is hereby Recomended unto Richard Browne John Fuller Isaac Tillard John Whetham and Thomas Rand Esqrs. Justices of the peace for the said County, or any two of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County, and to Examin into the truth of the Petrs complaint, and to certify their opinions touching the premisses within menconed unto this Court or to his Maties Justices of the peace at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the said County.
