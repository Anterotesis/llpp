To the Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles Without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of Benjamin Cowley & James Esdaile Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth day of January 1764. Elizabeth Allen Singlewoman Was removed to the parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order & humbly appeal against the same

Your petitioners therefore humbly Pray that your Worships Will be pleased to appoint a Day that all parties may attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said appeal And your petrs shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Stagg Sollicitor for the appellts
