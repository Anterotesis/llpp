To the Right Worpll: his Mats: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Sitting at Hicks hall

the Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poore of the Hamlett of Limehouse in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of Bastwick Johnson and Thomas- West Esqrs: two of his Mats. Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (Quor Un) Bearing Date the twenty Seventh Day of November Last Sara Sympson Singlewoman was Conveyed from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the said parish and County to the Hamlett of Lime house aforesaid as the place of the Last Legall Settlement of the said Sara Sympson

Yor: Petrs: Finding themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removall of the said two Justices Humbly Appeale agt. the same to this Court and pray Your Wospl will be pleased to Appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeale this present Sessions and that Yor. petrs: may be Relieved as unto Your Worps shall seeme Meete.

And Yor: petrs: shall ever Pray Etc
