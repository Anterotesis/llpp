To the Rt. Woppfull his Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamblet of Bethnall green in the Parish of St. Dunston Stepney in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That [...] last part under the Hands and Seals of Saml. Benson and John Poulson Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) one Dorthy Glass widow late wife of Robert Glass deced together with her & six sd late husbands Five Children (vizt.) Robert abt thirteen years old, Anne about eight years old, and John about Four Years old, Edward about Three Years old, and Sarah about Six Months old were removed from the Parish of st. Leanord Shoreditch in the said County, to the said Hamlet of Bethnall given as the place of their last legal settlement.

That yor. Peticonrs find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or Ordr. of the said Two Justices, and humbly Appeale agat. the same to this Honble Court,

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray. yor. Wopps will be pleased to ordr. the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all other persons commn'd to attend this Court some day this present sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the said Apeale And that yor. Peticonrs may have such relief herein, as to yor. Wopps shall seem meet

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
