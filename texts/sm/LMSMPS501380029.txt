To the Worfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Edward Boswell and William Thomson Churchwardens of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in behalf of themselves and other Inhabitants of the said parish

Sheweth That the Highwayes of the said parish are very much out of repair, and will require a consider able Sume of money to repair and amend the same which cannot be raised without the aid of this Honble Court

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray that a Rate or Assessment (not exceeding Three half pence in the pound) may be made upon the said Inhabitants for repairing the said Highwayes according to the direccon of the statute in that case provided to be collected by the prsent surveyors of the Highwayes in the said parish, And that the money collected on the said Rate be imployed and laid out as the Justices of the peace for this County usually meeting at the speciall Sessions in the said Parish Shall direct and appoint

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Edw. Boswell Wm Thompson
