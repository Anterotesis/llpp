To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition of William Martin one of the Petty Constables of the Parish of Southmims

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was by an Order of this Court made on Thursday the Tenth day of Mayin the Sixth Year of his present Majesties Reign admitted into the Service of releiving Passing & conveying of Vagrants in & through this County for the Year then next ensueing at the Sallary of Thirty Pounds a Year

That there is due to Your Petitioner Fifteen Pounds for half a Years Sallary Ending at Lady day last

Your Petitioner humbly prays Your Worshipps to make an Order upon Your Worshipp's Treasurer to pay the Said Fifteen Pounds

And Your Petitioner as in Duty bound will ever Pray Etc

William Martin
