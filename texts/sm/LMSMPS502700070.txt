To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices att Hixess Hall Worshipfull Gentillmen may it plase yu

yr umbll pettisoner Sheweth yt I am in Consinment this Considrabel time for A mistimaner & am Destitnd of mony & finds therefore worshipfull Gentellmen I Beg yt yu may take it into Considration & Lett me Be Dischargd for ye procetuter when Shee found yt Shee Could Do more Damage Shee was willing to make it up with me therefore Dr Gentillmen I Beg yt yu may send an order to ye Keeper for my Relesment & I'm Duty is Bound to Pray Mary Wattkins
