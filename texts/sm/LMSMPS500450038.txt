To the Right Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Cannon of this Hamlett Wemley Apperton in [...] Parish of Harrow of Hill in the sd. County

Sheweth

That att Easter 1695 yor Worspps were pleased to order that your Petr. should Serve the office of Constable for the said [---] Hamlett for the yeare then ensuing

That your Petr. did Serve the sd. Office for the sd. [---] Hamletts untill Easter last att which time one John Weedon was elected to Serve the sd. Office for the sd [---] Hamletts in the place and Stead of your Petr.

That the sd. John Weedon did leave his habitacon in the sd. Parish & did remove thereout within the Space of Two or Three dayes next after he was Soe elected as aforesd yett nevertheless the Inhabitants of the said Parish doe Still compell your Petr. to Some the sd Office of Constable for the sd. [---] Hamletts And forasmuch as your Petr. haveing been att great expence and charge in and about the Execucon of the sd. Office Same Easter last and being a very poore Man and haveing a Family to Mainteyne

P Tho Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor Worspps to be pleased to Order that he may be discharged from the sd. Office and that another person may be elected to Serve the sd. Office of Constable for the sd. [---] Hamlett in the place and Stead of your Petr

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

John Cannon
