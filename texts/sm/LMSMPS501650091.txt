To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembl

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardns. and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Michael Wood Street London

Sheweth That Whereas by an Order under the hands and Seals of Alexander Ward & John Metcalf Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of Peace for the said County of Middx dated the 8th day of August last made upon the Complaint of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Andrew Holborne in the Liberty of Saffron Hill in the County of Middx The said two Justices did adjudge Henry Lane and Elizabeth his Wife to have their last legal Settlement in ye: Said Parish of St. Michael Wood Street requireing Yor. Petitioners to provide for them as their own Parishoners, Your Petitioners do humbly apprehend themselves to be agreived by the said Order for that the said Henry Lane and Elizabeth his wife or either of them never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Michael Wood Street

And there In your Petitioners do humbly Appeal from the said Order & pray to be relieved as to your Worships shall Seem meet

And ever Pray Etc.
