To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition & Appeale of William Grane of the Parish of St Mary Islington in the sd County

Sheweth

That your Peticoner is Rated towards the Releife of the Poore of the sd Parish the Sume of Tenn Shillings for Fifteene Pound P And Against which Rate he humbly Appeales to this Court

And humbly prays Such Releife in the Prmisser as to your Worshipps Shall Seeme meet

And yor Peticonr Shall ever pray Etc
