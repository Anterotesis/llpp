To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of 6. J. Kirby and Is St Andrew Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th. Day of July last past Joseph Julion Mary his Wife and Mary Ann their Child aged near Four Years were Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the County of Middlesex Aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the Place of their legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said Two Justices Appeal to the Court against the same And humbly pray that the said Appeal may be heard this present Session and that a time may be appointed for the hearing And determining thereof

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray
