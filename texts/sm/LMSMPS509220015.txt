To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W. Bleamire and A Graham Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex and Liberties thereof (One whereof bring of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th Day of December 1795 Ann Russell and her two Bastard Children Namely Joseph Aged 6 years or thereabouts and Sarah aged three year or thereabouts were removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint SepulchreLondon as the Place of there legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be adjourned to the next Session And that a Day in the next Session may be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof
