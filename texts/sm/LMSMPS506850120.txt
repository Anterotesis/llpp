To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Jant the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Came Peters Cheeshill in the County of Southampton

Sheweth That by on Order or pass warrant [...] the Hands and Seals of John Penleaze Esqr. and David Wilmot Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 23d day of Septr. 1778 Elizabeth Pickernell wife of William Pickernell and her there Children namely William Aged Seven Years and upwards Jane aged five year upwards and Margaret aged about fen [...] months were removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shorditch in the County of Middx to the Parish of Saint Peter Clarwell in the County of Southampton whereon you Petitioners Conceive themselves agrrieved and humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petrs. therefore pray that a Day may be appointed for the hearing and determining such appeal and that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch may be ordered to attend to hear and abid the Judgment of the Court concerning the same Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
