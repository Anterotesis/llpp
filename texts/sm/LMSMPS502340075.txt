To the Right Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quar Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St: Benedict als Bennett Fink London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Nicholas Jeffery's and John Troughton Esqr Two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Seventh day of April Instant Henrietta Hill Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in the County of Middx unto ye. Sd Parish of St. Benedict als Beniett Fink London alledging ye Sd Parish of St. Benedict als Bennett Fink to be the place of her last Legall Settlemt

Therefore your Petrs. Conceving themselves agrieved by p Sd Warrt or ordr. ye Sd Two Justices Do therefore Appeal to Your Worships therefore and humbly Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premes and Orde. that ye Church wardens and Overseers of ye poor of the Pish of Sd Andrews Holborn in the County of Middx May Appear before You at a Certain Day by Your Worships to be Appointed to hear and Abide ye Judgemt And determinacon of your Worships touching this Appeal

And Your Peticoner Shall Ever Pray Etc
