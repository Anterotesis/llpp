To the Rt Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlex;

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sd County Danes in the County of Middex

Sheweth That by an Ordr. dated the 23th day of Janry Last end the hands & Seals of Jno Rotheram & [---] John Richard for Esqrs two fo her Majesties of the Pease of this County (Quor Vnnd) Susannah Maw wife of Thomas Maw was removed from the psh of St Giles in the Field in this County to the Psh of St Clemts Danes aforsd as the place of the last Legall Settlemt of the Sd Thomas Maw & Susannah his Wife

That the yor conceiveing themselves to be Aggreived by the Judgemt of the Sd two Justices doe humbly appeal to this Court there from

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worshipps will be Pleased to appoint a day for the Church Wardens & Overseers of St Giles aforesd to atend to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Petr shall Pay Etc
