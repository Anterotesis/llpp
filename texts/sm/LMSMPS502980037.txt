To the Right Worshipfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomece the Great London

Sheweth Thereby Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Nicholas Jestreys & Stephen Beckingham Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirteenth day of February instant, Dorothy Evans Widow was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Barthelomew to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Cha: Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That yor. Peticonrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yor. Worships there from, And humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Prmes And Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn above the Bars in the Said County of Middx may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgment & Determanacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale.

And your Peticonrs. Shall pray.
