To the Worshipful the Chairman and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks's Hall assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London.

Sheweth

That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Relyngr Esqr. and Peter Blanch Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date 26th. day of June last Hannah Hull Widow of William Hull deceased and Elizabeth her Child of the Age of three Months or thereabouts were removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Peter le Poor in Broad Street London by which said Order Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeal from the said Order to the Judgment of this Court And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions for the Hearing of the said Appeal And that your Worships will be pleased to make such Order therein as to this Court shall seem meet.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

John Batten Sollt. for the Parish of St. Peter to Poor in Broad Street London
