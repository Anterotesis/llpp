[...] Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Jon. Piper

Sheweth That Yor. Petrs. by his Indenture of Apprenticeship bearing date the Thirteenth day of October in the Seventh Year of his now Matys Reign Annoqr Dom 1720 Did put himself Apprentice to Richard Wiseman of Norton Fallgate in the County of Middx for the term of Seven years concerning from the date of the sd Indenture to learn the trade of a Small Coalman As by the sd Indenture will appear being hereunto annexed That [---] the sd Richd: Wiseman dott very of her times beat and above Yor. Petr. unmercyfully Insomuch that he is afraid of his life And for what reason Yor. Petr. Knows not for That the sd Indenture besides is not Indented as the Law directs (as Yor. Petr. is with humble Sessions informed)

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worpps to be pleased to order that his said Master may attend this Honble Court one day this prsent Sessions to shew Cause why her shall not be discharged from his sd Apprenticehood

And Yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
