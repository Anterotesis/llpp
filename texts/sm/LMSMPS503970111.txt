The Petition of Richard Unwin

Humbly Sheweth That on or about the 26 th. day of July Last Your Petitioner was Committed by Henry Feilding Esqrs. one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex for Feloniously taking & Carrying away One Chaldron of Coals the property of Mr. Maring

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays Your Lordships that he May be either Tryed Bailed or Discharged this Present Sessions for the said supposed Offence Pursuant to the Direction of the Habeas Corpus Act

And Your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc

Rich Ann in
