Middx Dr prit. Session 1784} To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removal under the hands & Seals of R. Hearing & Joseph Hacking Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Sd County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty seventh day of March 1784. Elizabeth Davis Singlewoman was removed and delivered from & out of the Parish of St Luke in the sd County of Middx to the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the same County as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitionrs conceive themselves aggrieved.

Wherefore your Petitionrs humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day in this present General Quarter Session to hear & determine their said appeal

I Clare J Bampfield} Sollr for the Appellts in the sd appeal
