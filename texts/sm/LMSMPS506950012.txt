To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justice of the peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The Humble peticon of Mary pelteir and Grace Godley

That yor. Petrs. hath been very much abused and threatned divers times by Joseph- Harrison that he will stop their months and doe their business for them and other theratning lang wage by reason whereof your petrs goe in danger of their lives- And the said Harrison is bound over to his good behavior and to appear at this present Sessions at the Complt. of your petr. Grace Godley Since wth. he the said Harrison hath threatned both yor. petrs. to due their work And your petrs. further shew that the said Harrison is by the Constable indicted for keeping a disorderly house wth. yor. petrs. knows he does

Yor. Petrs. humbly pray yor. Worspps that his Recognizance may be streated and that he may be continued to his good behavior and that your petrs. may have notice of his confession or tryall of the said Indictment

And yor. petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
