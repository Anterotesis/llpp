To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass warrant or order under the hands & Seals of Saml. Grey & Wm. Jones Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City & Libty of Westmr. bearing date the 15th. day of August Instant Martha Taylor Singlewoman Aged about Sixty years was Removed from & out of the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the Libty of the City of Westmr. in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. James As the Place of her last legal settlemt. Whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

and therefore humbly Prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for Hearing & Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may Attend at the same times

And your Petrs. Shall Ever Pray

Saml. Seddon Vestry Clerk
