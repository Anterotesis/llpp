To the Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemnts. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. under the hands & seales of M. Crake W. Conn [...] Esqrs. two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date the Seventh day of Janry Instant Sarah Mitchell wife of John Mitchell was removed from the Pish of St. Martyn in the Fields in the sd. County to the sd Pish of St. Clemts. Danes as the Pish & place of the last legall settlemt. of the sd. John Mitchell & the sd. Sarah Mitchell his wife

That yor Petrs. thinke themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices & hope to make it appeare that the Pish of St. Clemts. Danes was not the place of the last legall settlemt. of the St. John Mitchell and doe therefore appeale from the sd. Ordr. to yor Worpps

Phimmow Yor Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor Worspps will be pleased to appoint a Short day for the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Pish of St. Martyus in the Feilds to attend yor worpps & to abide by such Ordr. as shall be made out bearing the sd. Appeale

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
