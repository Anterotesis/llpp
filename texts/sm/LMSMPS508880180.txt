To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Tho: Collins Esqr. and John Scott Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex and City and liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th. day of August 1793 Mary Gore Wife of James Gore (if living) and their Child Dennis Aged one Year were removed from the Parish of Saint Anne within the liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioner conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a day may be appointed in this present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal and that all parties concerned may then attend

And Your Petitioners shall can Pray Etc

Saml. Wegener Sollicitor for Appellants
