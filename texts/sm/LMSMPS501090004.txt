To The Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Calida Late Wife of James Calida Dead

Sheweth That Your Petr. said Husband Did in his Life time Receive As A Pension From this County the Sum of Five Pounds P Annum And that your Petrs. said Husband Died in May Last And Left your Petr. in A Poore Condition And For a Small As the said Pension being all paid and Discharged to Lady Day Last

P Tho Nash

Your Petr. Most humbly Prays your Worshipps to be pleased to order that she may be paid the Quarters Pension due att Midsumr Last of your Worshipps Soe think fitt

And yor Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc

Eliz: [x] Calida Her mker
