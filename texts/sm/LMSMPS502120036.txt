To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in Their Genl. Qurarter Sessions Assemble

The Humble Petition Peter Cresswell Master or Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell.

Sheweth That Since your Petr. being Elected Master or Keeper of the said House of Correction, The House wherein he lives is become very much out of Repaire so that in Divers places thereof it Raineth into the same. And your petr. further humbly Sheweth, that The Brickwell which parts the said Prison from the Corporation Work House is in great Danger of falling, whereby the Life of some of the prisoners may be Endangered if not Speedily Prevented. and also that that There is not a Sufficient number of Blocks and Beetles wherewith to Sett The Prisoners to work.

Wherefore your Petr. most humbly prays your Worspps. will be pleased to order Such necessary Repaires may be made to the Said prison as that The House may be kept Dry the wall Supported or Rebuilt and a Sufficient number of Blocks and Beetles for the Prisoners to work at:

And your Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
