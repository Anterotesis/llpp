To the Right Worshipfull her Majies. Justices of the peace of the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Jane Dun of the parrish of Stepney in the County of Middx & vid.

Sheweth. That yor. Petr. about two years agoe Nursed one Eleanor Clayberry [...] five Weeks for wch. She has fourteen Shillings due to her And yor. Peticoner haveing often Friendly requested her to pay her the sd money due to her could never get any thing but abuses from her wch. Obliged yor Petr. to crave A Warrt. of the Worspfull. Justice Stewart & had her bound over to Answer the same at this prsent sessions, And soon after yor Petr. was Arrested at the Said Clayberryes Suite in A Sham Accon & has been put to great charges thereupon And yor Petr. being extream poor has not money left to prferr A Bill or further to psente the said Clayberry.

Wherefore she humbly prayers an order of this Honble Court for the said Clayberry to Attend this Honble. Court at A time certain to Shew cause why she refuses to pay the Wages abov sd or otherwise order asto yor. Worps. shall seem meet.

And yor Petr. shall pray Etc.
