To the right honble. Sr. Francis Child Knt. Lord Mayr: and the rest of his Maties Justices att the [...] at the Old Bayly London.

The humble peticon of John Tayler a prisonr. in Newgate.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was Comitted to the Gatehouse the firstday of June last & hath Since been brought to Newgate, for an offence of which he is noe ways guilty

Therefore humbly prayd this honble. Bench that he may bee try'd, admitted to bayle or discharged;

And yor. Petr. Shall pray Etc.

John Taylor
