To the Knight Worpll. his Majties. Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Joseph Bolton of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the Sd. County Taylor

Sheweth That yor Petr. Severall years last past yor Petr. being A Poor Inhabitant in ye Sd Hamlet was rated four pence & Month towards ye. reliefe of the poor of the Sd. Hamlet but now is rated 12d P Month altho' his Circumstances in all respects is worse then hereto fore being Aged near to years & so troubled wth. Convulsions & other Indissasitions that render him incapable of following his employmt. & altogether unable to pay ye Sd. rate or any Part thereof

And therefore Humbly prays that yor Worspr will be pleased to order yt. Yor petr be wholly discharged there from or otherwise order for reliefe of yor Petr. in ye prmised as to yor Worspr Shall Seem meet.

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

reduced to 6d. P Month in ye next Booke.

Joseph Boltons [x] Marke
