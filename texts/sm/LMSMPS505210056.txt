To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Easter Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Stephen Randall of Hammersmith in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your Petitioner was Appointed High Constable of Kensington Division in the Hundred of Osulston at the last Easter Sessions

That he hath performed the Duties of his Office ever since to the best of his Ability and should very Chearfully Excute the same till your Worships, without his application, should please to nominate another in his stead, But that his further continuance in the said Office will be very Detrimental to him in his bussiness which is that of a Butcher as the due performance of the Office necessarily hinders him in his own Affairs.

That there are several proper Persons for the Service of the said Office in his stead residing in the said Division who have not Yet served some of whom are under named for the Approbation of your Worships

John Randall of the Parish of Chelsea Timber Merchant Thomas Bormly of the Parish of Acton Farmer John Clarke of the Parish of Kensington Bricklayer. That your Petitioner hath settled and cleared his Accounts with the County Treasurer

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that your Worships will be pleased to discharge him from his said Offices.

And shall ever pray Etc

Stepn. Randall High Constable
