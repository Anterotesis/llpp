To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Watlington in the County of Oxford

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Geo: Reid and John Spiller two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th. day of February 1795 Mary Leewood and four Children vizt. Richard aged about Six years John aged about four years Thomas aged about two Years and William aged about three Months were removed and conveyed from the Township of New Brentford in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Watlington as the plan of their last legal Settlement wherefore your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Workships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for.

the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Township of New Brentford and all persons Concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners Shale pray Etc

Wm Chaplin John Bracey} Churchwardens

John Hine Thos Stonill} Overseers.
