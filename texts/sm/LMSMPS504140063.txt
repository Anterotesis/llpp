To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Ann within Aldersgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Fan [...] an Seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st day of August 1751 Letitia Edsall [...] Widow of William Edsall deced was Pass'd From the Parish of St. Botolph witht Aldgate in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St Ann within Aldersgate London aforesaid Alledging the last Legal Place of Settlement of the Said Letitia Edsall to be there And your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the Said Order and Judgment of the Said two Justices do Humbly Appeal there from and Humbly pray your Worships

Would Appoint some day at the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden For the said County to heart his Appeal and that your Worships will make an Order that he Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Botolph witht. Aldgate and all Parties concerned in and about the premisses may then Attend hear and abide the Fina & Determination of your Worships

And Your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

Bignall Attorney For Appellants
