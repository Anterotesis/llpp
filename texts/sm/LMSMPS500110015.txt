remaine in prison till he finds suretyes for good behaviour

May 1691

To the Right Worshipfull their Mates Justices for Midx sitting at Hickshall

The humble petion of John Holland als Williams

Sheweth That yr. petr. being indicted for Rideing away wth. a Mare out of Smithfield Market the Bill was found Ignoramus

Yr. petr. therefore prays this worspfull Court to order he may be discharged there being no other matter agt. him
