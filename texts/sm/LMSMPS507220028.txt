To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Old Artillery Ground in the Liberty of the Tower of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir James Edaile Knight And Jenkin Jones Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Ninth day of November last Ann Harvey wife of James Harvey (who is in his Majestys Service as a Sailor) was Removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Old Artillery Ground as the last place of the legal Settlement of the said Ann Harvey whereby your petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved And

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said the Justices and the said Order And pray your Worships that the Hearing and Determination of the said Appeal may be Adjorn'd to the Next Sessions And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish of Saint Luke And All persons Concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
