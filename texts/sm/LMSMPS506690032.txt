To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Cornelius Martin Esquire and William Martin Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace [...] in and for the City and Liberty of westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty first day of December last Past Mary Thompson widow of John Thompson Deceased was Removed from the Parish of Saint James within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth in the said County of Surrey And the said Two last Mentioned Justices of the peace in and by the said Order of Removal Adjudged the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Thompson to be in the said parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth in the said County of Surrey

That your Petitioners are Advised and believe that the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Thompson is not in the said Parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth as by the said order is supposed and Therefore they Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved by the said Order and Determination of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore as well on the part and behalf of themselves as of the other Inhabitants and Parishioners of the said parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth Do Humbly Pray that the said Order of Removal of the said Mary Thompson from the said parish of Saint James to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Lambeth be Quashed and that Your Worships would please to make such further Order therein for the Reimbursing and paying your Petitioners the Costs and Charges they have been put to in Maintaining and providing for the said Mary Thompson in Obedience to the said Order as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

John Tucker Solr. for Appellants
