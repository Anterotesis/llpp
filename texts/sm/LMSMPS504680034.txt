To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and Thomas Kynaston Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) Dated the 19th. day of January 1758 Edward Sumerton Elizabeth his wife and their Child to wit Elizabeth aged about fifteen Months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of their last legall Settlement.

Humphy. Elmes Attorney for the Appellts. Your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices of the peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
