To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middsx

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Varnum of the Liberty above the Bars In ye Parish of St Andrew Holborne In ye County aforesd Widdow

Sheweth

That yr Peticoner has Lived upwards of Fifty years in ye said Liberty, is very Antient and past her Labour, and is Reduced to Extreame Poverty and want, and must Inevitably perish if not Speedily Releived

Humbly Prays This Honble Bench to Order her such speedy Releife as yr Worshipes in yt Great Goodness Shall seem meet

And as in duty Bound Shall Ever Pray Etc
