To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the fiftenth day of April instant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and [...] Henry Dodson Esqrs. County (one being of the Quorum) Margaret Karr an Insant aged about Two years and a half was Removed form the Parish of Saint George in the said County to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as to the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Said Order of Remove all and appeal to this Court against the same,

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that their Appeal against the said Order may be saved over to the next Generall Sessions, And that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day in the said next Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall pray
