To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble petition of John Green of the Parish of South Mims in the [...]

That your petitioner hath dwelt in the said County upwards of three years last past Is a Married Man and an Householder and of the Age of Thirty three Years & upwards

That your petitioner having a Family to maintain is desirous of using the Trade or Business of a Badger, Higler, or Buying & Selling of Butter Cheese Poultry and such like kind of Victuals for their Support.

Your Petitioner, therefore humbly prays your Worships to grant him a Licence for that purpose.

And your petitioner as in Duty bound shall ever pray.

Bentley Heath

The mark of [x] John Green
