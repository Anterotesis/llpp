To the Right Worshipfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mildred in the Poultry in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Anthony Chamberlain & Alexander Garrett Esqrs. Two of His Majestys's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the thirteenth day of October last past, Jane Bellamy the wife of George Bellamy with Eliz: Bellamy her Child aged about six Years, were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Mildred in the Poultry in the City of London alledgeing the said Parish of St. Mildred to be the place of their last legall Settlemt:

& Baynbridge Sollr. P Appellts.}

That Yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrt. or Ordr. of the Sd, two Justices Do therefore Appeall to Yor. Worships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premes, & Ordr. Yt. the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx aforesaid may appeal before you at a Certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide yr. Judgemt. & Determinacon of Yor. Worpl touching this Appeale.

And Yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
