To his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the holding the General Quarter Sessions in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor for the Parish of Binfield in the County of Berks

Sheweth That the Church wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Little Stanemore in the County of Middlesex did by virtue of an order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the sixth day of May last remove. Ann White and her two Children Paupers from the said Parish of Little Stanemore to the aforesaid Parish of Binfield where the said Ann White and her two Children have no Settlement whereby your Petitioners are aggrieved

That on the Tenth day of June Instant your Petitioners caused due Notices to be served on the Churchwardens and overseers of the Parish of Little Stanemore aforesaid of their intention of appealing at the next General Quarter sessions of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex against the said order

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the appeal may be lodged and set down to be heard
