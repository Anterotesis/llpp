To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. in ye. Sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of Saml. Perry and D'Oyly Michel Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace of & for the County of Middx bearing Date the Fifteenth day of May last, Joseph Damsell and Jane his Wife was removed from and out of the parish of St. Mary Mattfellon als White Chapel in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Margt. Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlement

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worships will please to Order, That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary Mattfellon als White Chapel may attend this Court some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide he final Detirmination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
