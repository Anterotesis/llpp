To the Worpll: his Majtys: Justices of the Peace of ye: County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled in and for ye sd County

The humble peticon and Appeal of ye: Church wardens and Overseers of ye: poor Parish of St: Mary Rotherith in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Elizabeth Gale wife of Jno: Gale with her Daughter Mary Gale werby Order or Warrant under ye: hands & Seals of two of his Majtys: Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx beating Date the 23o: Day of January last removed from ye: Parish of St: Paul Shadwell in the sd County of Middx to the Said Parish of St: Mary Rotherith as the Place of their Last Legal Settlement that your Peticoners humbly Conceive that they can made it Appear to this Court that the Place of their last Legall Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Gale and Mary Gale is not in the Said Parish of Rotherith

Your Petioners therfore humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St: Paul Shadwell to Attend this Court for the hearing the Said Appeael. Such time as to your Worships shall Seem meet.

And Your Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc.
