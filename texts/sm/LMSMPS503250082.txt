To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London.

Sheweth. That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Mercer and Robert-Midford Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Eighteenth day of August last past Susannah Bradbury Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West London as the last legal place of Settlement of the Said Susannah Bradbury

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same and pray this Court will appoint Some day in this present Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields and all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Mr. Howard Sel for ye Appellants
