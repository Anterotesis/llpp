To the Right Worspll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Boreman

Sheweth That your Peticoners late husband Jeremy Boreman decd was keeper of the house of Correccon at Clerkenwell And that during his said office your Peticoner by the assistance of servants did cheifly manage the Prisoners from time to time comitted to the said house of Correccon That your Peticoner humbly conceiving herselfe intirely to understand the management thereof can undertake to perform the same with the assistance of servants aswell as the same was done in the life time of her said husband And she being left with a charge of children in a poor condicon.

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to admit her to be Keeper of the said house of Correccon or that she may have such allowance as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yor: Petr: shall ever pray Etc.
