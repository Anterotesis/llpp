To the Worpfull her Majests Justice [...] of the Peace for the County of Middx [...] Quarter Sessions Assembled for the said [...] County.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the parish of Newington in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Mary Butler was by Order under the hands and Seals of Two of her Majts. Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx being Date the first Day of Septemr/1713 removed from the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Newington as the place of her last legal Settlement gained by her Birth in Kent sheet in the said parish of Newington

That yor. Petitioners can make it app [...] that the said Mary Butler was bound apprentice to Mary Smith of Greys Inn Lane in the p [...] of St. Andrew Holbourn and Served the said Mary Smith as her Apprentice for above the Space of Twelve months within the said parish of St. Andrew Holbourn

Your Peticoners do therefore humbly appeal aganist the said Order and pray th [...] this Honoble Court will order the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of ye p [...] of St. James Clerkenwell aforesd. to attend [...] Honble. Court to Shew Cause why the said or [...] Should not be vacated

And your Petion Shall ever pray Etc
