To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hick's Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of William Spinnage and Samuel Waddington Esquires two of his on Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 30th. day of June 1763 Mary Greenwood Spinster was removed from the parish of Saint and Margaret[---] in the City of Westminster to the said parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Greenwood whereby Your petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justicies and pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal and to order that all persons Concerned Do then Attend

And Your petitiones shall pray Etc
