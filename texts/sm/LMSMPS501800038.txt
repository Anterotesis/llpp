To the Rt Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Overseers of the poore of the Liberty of the Rolls in the Said County

Sheweth: That by a Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seales of John Cleeve and John Milner Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid (Quorum Unus) bearing date the 11th. day of June last Mary Bowley the wife of Thomas Bowley was removed from the parish of St Gyles in the fields in the Said County to the Said Liberty of the Rolls as the last place of the said Thomas Bowleys Settlement

Your Peticoners think themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment and order of the said Justices humbly Appeale to this Court against the same and pray that the Churchwardens and Over- seers of the Poore of the said parish of St. Gyles in the fields and all other parties concerned may be ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners as in Duty bound shall pray Etc
