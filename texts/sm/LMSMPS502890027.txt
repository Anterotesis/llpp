To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middx,

Sheweth That Hannah Barrett widow was by order of Pass warrant dated the nineteenth day of January last past under the hands and seals of John Priestley & Thomas Jones Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) removed and conveyed from the parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the same County of Middx to the said parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Peticoners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and appeal from the Judgment of the said two Justices to the judgment of this Worshipfull Court & desire a day of hearing may be appointed this prsent Sessions And that All Persons concerned may be ordered to attend to hear & abide the judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Peticonrs: shall Pray Etc
