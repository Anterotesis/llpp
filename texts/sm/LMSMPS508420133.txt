To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of Ann Miller Wife of Thomas Miller

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hand and Seal of D Walker Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty fourth day of November last your petitioner and her male Child Named Thomas aged about eight weeks were removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint George in the same county as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your petitioner Conceives herself aggrieved and humbly Appeal therefrom

Your petitioner therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to hear her in the premes & to Appoint the Churchwardens & Overseers of the sd. parish of St Giles to Appear at a Certain day to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your petitioner shall ever pray Etc

The [x] Mark of Ann Miller
