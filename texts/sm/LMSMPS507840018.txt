To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margaret in the City of Westminster and County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Tyler and Wm. Gowan Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City & Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 19th: day of June 1784 Hannah Cowell Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. James in the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex to the Parish of St Margaret in the City of Westminster as the place of her last legal Settlement with her Female Bastard Child for Nurture, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal against the Siad Pass Warrant or Order of Removal, and Pray that your Worships will appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the matter of the said Appeal, and that all Parties concerned may attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Hitchin Jams. Toplis} Church Wardens

James Annys Thos. Snow} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margaret in the City of Westminster
