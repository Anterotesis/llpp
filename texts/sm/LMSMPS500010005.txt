To the Rt Worship theire Maties Justices of ye peace for Midlex

The humble Peticon of John Cooke

Sheweth

That theres due to your Peticoner the Sume 40s-& before Dayes workes from one Hugh Cole

That Last Sessions your Peticoner had a warrant from this Hounoble Bench but Could not take him, the which is either Lost or mislaid

Your Peticon therefore humbly Prayes your Warrant to bring him in their Sessions

And yor petr shall ever Prey Etc.
