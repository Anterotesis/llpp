To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition and Appeal of Charles Feltham Esqrs: of this Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner it rates for the Sum of Fiffteen Hundred pounds Stock or personall Estate in the poors rate of the said Parish whereby he Conceives himselfe much aggrieved and appeals against the same

Yors Petitr therefore humbly prays he may be Eased in the said rate As to this Court shall seeme meete.

And Yor. petitr Shall Ever pray Etc Charles Feltham
