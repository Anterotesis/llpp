To yr Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The Humble Peticon at Jane Garton Wife of Edward Garton

Sheweth That As of Yesterday yr Petr Preferr'd A Peticon to this Honble Court there by Setting forth that She had Indicted John Todd together 10th Thomas Douten and Ann his Wife for Assaulting yer Petr That yor Petr Informed the sd Todd and Donten & his wife together will one Margrett Garton have this Day Prferr'd A Bill of Indictmt against yor Petr in this Honble Court for Assaulting them

That ye sd Todd & others are Malicious Persons and of bad Lives and Conversations And Soe farr Aim att ye Rume of yor Petr & her Family that they have caused her said Husband to be Arrested upon A Sham Accon & he is now in ye marshalsea Prison in Southwarke

P Tho: Nash

Yor Petr therefore most humbly prays such Reliefe in ye Prmises form this Honble Court as unto yor Worshipps Shall be thought most Once to

And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc Jane [x] Garton her mrke
