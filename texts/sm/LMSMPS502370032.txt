To the Right Worship full His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Over seers of the poor of the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Orderer Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Robert Jackson & Robert Dennett Esqrs Two of his Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the Fifteenth Day of this instant August Mary Peirson Wife of Thomas Peirson & her four Children (Vizt) Elizabeth aged about Eleven Years, Thomas aged about five years, Daniel aged four Years & Samuel aged about Ten Months the law full Issue of the said Thomas Peirson & Elizabeth his Wife, were removed from the Parish of St. mary White Chappell in the said County of Middx into the said Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls London, alledging the said Parish of St. Gregory to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That Your Petrs. Conceiving them Selves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yor. Worps therefrom And humbly pray that yor. Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmises & Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day to be by your Worps appointed, to hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacond of ye Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc
