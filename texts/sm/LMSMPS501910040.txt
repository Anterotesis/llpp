To the Worshipful his Majties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled in & for the Said County

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Thomas in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Thomas Sparks and Margarett his Wife were (by Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majties Justices of the peace of the Said County of Middx bearing Date the 15th. day of December Anno. Dom 1720) removed from the parish of St. Bottolph without Aldgate in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of St. Thomas Southwark as the place of their last legal Settlement, That Your Peticoners, humbly conceive that they can make it appear to this Court [...] that the place of the last legal Settlement of the Said Thomas Sparks & Margarett his Wife is not in the sd. Parish of St. Thomas.

Yor. Peticoners. therefore humbly pray Yor. Worships will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate to Attend this Court for the hearing the Said Appeal at Such time as to Your Worships Shall Sam meet

And Yor peticoners shall ever Pray
