To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeall of the Churchwarden and Overseer of the poor of the parish of Saint Phillip and Jacob in the City and County of Bristol

Sheweth That by virtue of a warrant or order under [...] hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and William [...] Cross Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex and Bearing date the Nineth day of May last past Elizabeth Esmead widow and Ann Esmead her daughter were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex aforesaid into the said Parish of Saint Phillip and Jacob in the City and County of Bristoll aforesaid as their last legall place of Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said warrant or order of the Said two Justices of the peace humbly appeall to this Court against the Same and pray that this Court will please to appoint a day for the hearing & determining of the Matter of the Said appeall and Your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc dated this 14th June 1737

John Axford{ Churchwarden Wm: Meryman{ Overseer
