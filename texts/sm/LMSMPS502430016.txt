To the Rt. Worspful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their genl Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of William Brown

Sheweth That your Peticoner having been Assaulted And threatned to have his Life taken away by George Littlefield and your peticonr. for that Offence have Caused him to be bound by Recognizance to be of his good behaviour towards yr. Peticoner & notwithstanding has been threatned and Terryfied by the said Littlefield in so much that yr. Peticonr. now apprhends. himself to be in danger of [...] his Life by the sd. Littlefield

Yr. Peticonr. therefore most humbly pray that he may be Continued of his Recognizance

And Yr. Peticonr. Shall Ever Pray Wm. Brown
