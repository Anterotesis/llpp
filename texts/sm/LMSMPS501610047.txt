To The Rt. Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. at their Generall Quartr Sessions at Hickshall.

The humble petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish, of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands & Seales of John Fuller and Tho: Rand Esqrs. Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County (quor and bearing date the Fifth day of Aprill Instant, One Mary Righton an Infant of about the age of Eleaven years was removed from the parish of st. James Clerkenwell in the said County to your [...] Petitioners said. Parish of St. Martin in the Fields. where by your Petr. humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

your Petr. therefore humbly appeale to your Worps [...] that your Worps would be pleased to appoint a day to hear and. determine the same.

And your petr. shall over pray Etc.

James Timberlake: John Bus} Church Wardens
