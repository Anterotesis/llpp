May it please Yor: Honors

Some time since one white was Apprehended by me and is Now in Custody for Robbin the House of one suell nere Covent Garden & there being a Reward for the Apprehender I being the person an Clandestinately Arrested and in Custody at Mr. Hamsteads In Clements Lane in Order to be deproved of nere Temple Barr the Sd Reward, Therefore I humble pray yor Honors: Countenance yt. I may be sent for Who am yor Honors: most Obedient Humble

Octr ye 12th 1708 Scrot. at Comand

Richd. Savile
