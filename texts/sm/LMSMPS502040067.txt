To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the sd County

Sheweth That by Order under the hands & seals of Matthew Hewitt and William Cotesworth Esqrs. two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the Fifteenth day of May last David Cleeve & Rebecca his wife with William their son were removed from and out of the parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the sd County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Sepulchres Middx as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That Your Peticoners think themselves aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices and hope to make it appear that the sd David Cleeve and family have not any legall Settlemt. in the sd Pish of St. Sepulchres Middx and therefore humbly Appeal against the sd Order to your Worspps

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to appoint a day for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Pish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the County of Middx to attend this Honble Bench this present Sessions and to stand to and abide such Ordr. as shal be made upon hearing the sd Appeal

P Henman

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
