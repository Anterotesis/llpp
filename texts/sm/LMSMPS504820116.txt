To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poof of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the sd. County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Caslon and Ebenezr. Mussell two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of December 1759 Mary Oliphant and her five Children namely Jane Aged ten Years, Thomas Aged eight Years George aged five Years, John Aged three Years, and William Aged one Year and upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the place of their last legal Settlement; Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same; And

Therefore Pray that this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal; And to Order that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Jonan. Alderton Luke Pike} Church Wardens

John Warner Will Foden} Overseers of the Poor.
