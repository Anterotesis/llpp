[...] Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the [...] for the [...] of Middx. in General Quarter Session of the [...] at Hicks [...] ll in St. John Street in and for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appea [...] of the Church Wardens and Overseers [...] the Poor of the Parish of St. [...] Danes in the County of [...]

Sheweth That by Order under the hands and seals of John Fielding and Benjn, Cox Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of Septr. 1758. Sarah Quench Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be Appointed this present Session for the hearing and determining the Matter touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
