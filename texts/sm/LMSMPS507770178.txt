To His Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Churchward and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Old Swinford in the County of Worcester

Sheweth

That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Edward Burnaby Greens and Henry Lambe Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of October last Elizabeth Johnston the wife of Samuel Johnston who hath deserted and left her and Ann their Daughter aged about two Years and an half were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Old Swinford in the County of Worcester by the description of the Parish of Swinvert in the County of Worcester as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Day Your Worships to appoint some day in this present Session to hear and determine the said Appeal and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Kensington and all persons concerned attend the Court at the said hearing

And Your Petitioners as in duty bound will pray Etc
