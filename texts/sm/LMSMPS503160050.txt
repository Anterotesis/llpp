To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of St. Vincent and Is Cardonnel Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 8th. day of April 1736 Trueman Davis and his Wife Elizabeth were removed from the Parish of Kensington in the Said County to the Said Parish of St James As the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved

And therefore humbly Prays that this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a time for Hearing and Determining the Said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Saml Seddon Vestry Etc
