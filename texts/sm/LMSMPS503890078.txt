To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Poulson and Francis Hole Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourteenth day of September One Thousand seven hundred and forty Eight Benjamin Jewson was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Luke as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Benjamin Jewson

That your Petitioners find themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of removed and humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray that this Court will appoint some day in this present Session for the Church wardens and Overseers of the poof of the said Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jn. Witton Sollr. for ye Appellts.
