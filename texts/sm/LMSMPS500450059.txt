To the Right Worll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of Isleworth in the said County

Sheweth

That by a Pass warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr Michael Cole Knt & Edward Hous Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the peace of the Said County one Margaret Marloe was passed from the Pish of St Ann to the Said Pish of Isleworth as the last place of legall Settlement of the Said Margaret Marloe

Your Petr thinke themselves aggreived wth the Judgmt of the said two Justices appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the said Pish of St Ann may be ordered to appeare in this Court att on a day certaine to She to heare & abide the Judgment of this Court touching this Appeale

P James Tytons

And yor petr Shall ev pray Etc
