To the Right Honble Sir Crisp Gascoyne Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx. in Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Lydia wife of Oliver Mc.Alester, now a prisoner in this Ma [...] jesty's Goal of Newgate.

Sheweth. That your petitioner on the 22d. Day of August last was comitted to Newprison by the name of Lydia Mc.Alester otherwise Roach , by vertue of the Warrant of Henry Fielding Esqr one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex for Felony expresed in the said Warrant and is now detained thereon in His Majesty's Gaol of Newgate.

Your petitioner most humbly prays this Honble Court that she may be tried bailed or discharged this Session according to the directions of the Statute in such Case made and provided.

And Your pet. shall for ever pray

Lydia Mc:Allester
