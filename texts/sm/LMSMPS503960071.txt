To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices Of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Holden for the said County at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of Pass Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Ellys and Peter Elers Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) Bearing Date the Fourth day of April last past Alice Mason in the sd. order called the Wife of Dennis Mason (who is Absconded and left her) And their three Children (that is to say) Dennis Aged near Six Years Hester aged about three Years and Elizabeth an Infant Aged about three Months were Removed and Conveyed from the parish of Chelsea in the said County to the said Parish of Kensington as the place of their last Legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to Your Worships Against the said Pass warrant or Order and the Judgment of the said two Justices of the Peace And pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint Some Day in the Next General Session to Hear and Determine the Matter of the said Appeal In Regard the persons whom Your Petitioners will be Obliged to produce As Witnesses Upon the Hearing of the said Appeal are now Many Miles distant and Cannot be produced by Your Petitioners Before Your Worships at this present General Quarter Sessions

And Your Petrs. will Ever pray Etc.

John Nicoll Agent For Appellants }
