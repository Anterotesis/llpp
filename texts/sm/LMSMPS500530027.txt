The peticon of George Maxfield in Newgate

To the Right Honble. Sr. Humphry Edwin Knt Lord Mayr: And to the Rose of the Justices of this Honble. Court

Sheweth The humble Peticn. of George Maxfield Now a prsonr. in Newgate

That your peticonr: to confined Upon Suspicon of Fellony and Burglawry And he haveing Wittnessed here in fourth hopeing thereby to prove his innocency of the said Fack

Therefore yor: peticonr: Most humbly prayes that he may be tryed at this prsent sessions And that his prayer may be entered accordingly as to yor Lordpps shall seem meer Etc

And yor: peticonr: shall Ever pray Etc
