To The Worpll. the Chariman & other his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Anne & Agnes in the City of London

Sheweth That Richard Greenhill a Boy of about Sixe yeares of Age is lately become Chargeable to the Said parish of St. Anne & Agnes his Father & mother who lately were Inhabitants legally settled there being both withdrawn to Places unknown to yor Peticoners

That Henry Haley of Hendon in the County of Middx year the Grandfather by the Mothers Side of the said Richard Greenhill is a Person of a very good Estate and sufficient ability to provide for his said Grandson

Yor Peticoners therefore humbly pray that the said Henry Haley (pursuant to the Statute in that behalfe) may be assessed in such reasonable weekly or monthly sume for & towards the Maintainance of the said Richard Greenhill as to yor Worpps Shall seem meet

And ye Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
