To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the Peace of the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Stoke Newington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 12th. day of October 1773 Mary Holmes & Alice her Daughter lawful issue aged 2 years & upwards were removed and conveyed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the sd. County from act of the said Parish to the sd. Parish of St. Mary Stoke Newington by the name of the Parish of Stoke Newington in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Holmes & Alice her Daughter

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the sd. Order or pass warrant humbly appeal to your Worships against the same

and pray relief in the Premisses and that a Day in this present Session may be appointed for the Hearing and Determination of the said Petition and Appeal

Jos: Hatt Turner Sollicitor for the Appellant Middlesex At the General Session of the Peace of our Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hick's Hall in Saint John Street in the said County on Monday the Sixth day of December 1773.

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in this County have Notice of the within Petition & Appeal and that they and all other Persons concerned do attend this Court at Hicks's Hall afsd. on Thursday next at Eight of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court
