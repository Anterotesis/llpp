To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by an Order under The hand sand Seals of St. Thomas Charges Barrt. and Ant: Corbiere Esqr. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the third Day of April Instant Margaret Coussons aged about twenty seven years was removed from the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the same County as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petrs: thinking themselves agreived by the aforesaid Order [---] of the said two Justices of the doe appeale against the same [---] to this Peace County And Humbly pray that the Benefit of an Appeal may be saved to them untill the next General Sessions of the Peace for the said County of Middx

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
