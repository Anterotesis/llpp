To The Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assemble in General Quarter Session in & for the said County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of & Porchester in the County of Southampton.

Sheweth

That by a Vagrant Pass under the hand & Seal of Gabriel Fouase Esqr: one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster within the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twentieth day of February last. Mary Fraser Wido. of Alexander Fraser decd: and her three Children, James, Anne, & Jane, were removed from the Parish of Saint Paul, Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the parish of Porchester (in the said order called Policherster) in the said County of Southampton as the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Fraser & her said three Children, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggriev'd, & against the said Pass do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear & determine the matter touching the said appeal And your Petitonrs: Shall pray & [...]

John Wigg William Hellyer} Churchwardens

John Sutton Robert Waller} Overseers
