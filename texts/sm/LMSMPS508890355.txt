To the Worshipful this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of A. Graham Esqr and The Revd. Herbert Mayo & [...] Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty Ninth Day of August Last (One whereof being of the Quorum) Rachael Jones Wife of Morris Jones who has deserted he and their Two infant Children both in Lawful Wedlock Vizt. Mary Ann aged about Six Years and Rachael aged about Four years were removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate Middlesex as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners concerning themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly Appeal against the same and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day

that all Parties concerned Mary attend to hear and abide the Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal and your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jas. Clare Charles Glynes } Sollicitors for the Appellants
