To the Rt. Honble Sr: Willm Withers Knt. Lord Masr: of the Citty of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of James Collon A Prisonr. in Newgate

Sheweth

That Yor. Petr: was Comitted to Newgate ye. 16th: of September last Charged upon Suspicon for the murder of Jacob Hewson Esqr. of wch: fact yor. Petr. is wholy innocent

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prayes to be Tryed this Sessions or to be Bailee pursurce to the Act of parliamt.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
