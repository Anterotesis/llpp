To the Rt Worspll. her Matys Justices of Ye Peace for ye County of [...]

The humble Peticon of Thomas Bishopp Of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents In the Parish of St Andrew Holbourne In the sd County

Sheweth That Att Easter Last your Petitr was chosen Scavenger For the sd Liberty But for a Swmch As he Being noe Tradesman And having noe other Dependance for a Lively hood Then by being only A Brewers Servantor Herke In the Citty of London And Being aged Sixty Four Yeares And Renting A House of noe more then Five Pounds P Anns In the Said Liberty

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore humbly prays years Worspps will be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from serving the sd Office And that Such other person may be chosen In his Roome As unto Yor Worspps Shall seem meete

And yor: Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Thomas Bishop
