To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Margt. Lunersturup

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof your Petr. Stood Judicted in this honble Cort this Prsent Sessions Your Worspps were pleased to Fine her Â£5 and for Nonpaymt. thereof to comitt her to New Prison in this County where She now Remaines

That yor Petr. is very sorry for the Offence for which she Stood Judicted and Resolves never be offend aqaine in the like nature and for as much as she being very Poor is not able to pay the sd. Fine and humbly Submitteing herselfe to the Goodness & [...] of this Honble Court.

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the sd Fine as unto your Worspps Shall Seem meet

P Tho: Nash

And Yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc.
