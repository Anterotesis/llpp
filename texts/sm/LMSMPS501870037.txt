To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Butenx

Humbly Sheweth That about Four years and a half agoe yours Petitr by Indenture of Apprenticehood became and apprentice to Michael Ennett of St Martin in the Fields In this County Goldsmith for the terme of Seven years

That your Petitioners said Master (without any Manner of provocation) hath frequently beat and abused him in a very Car Carons and Inhuman Manner once by breaking one of his Fingers and at another time by striking him over the head with a large Cam whereby your Petitr fell to the ground and was Seized there upon with Convulsion fitts and his wound occationed by the said Blow being soe very dangerous your Petr was obliedged to Have a Consultation of four Surge one and your Petitr itt some Measure by means of the said Blow is readred uncapable of following his trade To answer which complaint Yor Petrs said Master stands bound by Recognizance before the Worsppll Justice Brice for his Appearance this prsent Sessions in this Court

Yor: Petr the refore humbly prays this Courts will be pleased to Order his sd Master to attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew cause why he should not be discharged from his said Apprenticehood

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

John Butenx
