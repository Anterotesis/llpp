To the Right Worsppll. Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in the Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of William Hall Vicar of Ealing in the said County

That a Rate Tax or Assessmt. hath been made for the Releif of the poor of the said Parish of Ealing for this present Year wherein yor Peticon is very much advanced for the small Tythes over & above his share or Proporcon

That yor Peticoner is always willing to pay an equall proporton with the rest of his Parishioner.

p [...] Tyton Your Peticonr finds himself grieved wth. the sd. Rate Appeals to this Worsll. Bench & prays (the prmises considered) that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Ealing may be Ordered to attend should not be releived in the prmisses

And yor. Peticon (as in Duty bound) shall ever Pray Etc
