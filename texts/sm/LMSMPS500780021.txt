To the Right Worll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Michael Queenhith London

Sheweth That by a Warrant under the hands & Seals of Joseph Short Esqr & Samuel Blackerby two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the said County (Quoqumms) Dina the wife of Richard Burroughs wth: Dina & Margaret their Children were lately passed from the Pish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the Said pish of st Michael Queenhith London as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Richard Burroughs

Your Peticonrs. thinke themselves aggrieved wth: the Judgment of the said two Justices And doe therefore Appeal to this Cort & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Pish st. Giles Clerkenwell & all other Pties concern [...] herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day th [...] to hear & abide the Judgment & finall determination of this Court touching this Appeal.

P J Tyson

And your Peticonrs. shall eu pray Etc
