To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of D Wilmot and B Camper Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23rd Day of April in the Year of our Lord 1770 Susannah Rousett and her three Children Vizt Samuel Aged Six Years & Upwards, Ann Aged three years and upwards, and George aged Six Months and upwards, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the said Order of removal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal he saved to your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this County

And your Petrs. will pray Etc Wm. Burroughs Soll for Appelr
