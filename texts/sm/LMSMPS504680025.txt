To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County of Middx at Hicks hall Assembled

The humble petition of John Wilkins Paviour

Sheweth That your petitioner did by Articles of Agreement bearing date the [...] Sixteenth day of March 1746 [...] ant premise And agree with [...] Several of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx in Consideration of the yearly Sum of Nine pounds to be paid unto your petitioner Well and substantially repair Amend and maintain a certain Bridge called Brentford Bridge with all manner of Need full and necessary reparations and amendments when and as Often as need shall be for the Term of Eleven years as by the said Articles will more fully Appear

And that there is due to your petitioner the Sum of Nine pounds for one Years Allowance from Michas 1756, to Michas 1757.

Your petitioner therefore humbly Prays Your Worships to Order the payment of the said Sum of Nine pounds

And your petitioner will ever pray

Jos Wilkins
