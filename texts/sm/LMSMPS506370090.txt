To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter & Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Wood and Richard Tayler Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty third Day of December One thousand seven Hundred and seventy three Ann King singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Feltham in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all parties concerned may be then directed to Attend.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

John Lane Sollicitor for the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden.
