To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knt. Chairman and other the Worshipful Magistrates at the Quarter Sessions to be held at Hicks's Hall for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of Henry Haslip, Apprentice to Mr. Benjamin Duterreair, Repeating Watch Motion Maker in Rathbone Place

Humbly Sheweth, That Your Petitioner was bound Apprentice to the said Benjn: Duterreair by Indenture bearing date 12th. Febry 1770 and his Friends then paid with him the Sum of Ten Guineas for his being instructed in the said Business.

That from his having been chiefly employed on Eriands by his Master & Mistress, and from his own Incapacity, he has made very little or no progress in the knowledge of the said Business, altho' he has served near Five Years to it. That
