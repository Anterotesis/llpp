To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth The humble Petition of John Sabatier and Jeffery Nash

That For the Year last Past Your Petrs. Served the Office of Surveyors of the Highwaies for the Dutchy Liberty in the Straw Iu the Parish of St. Clements Danes in this County And that During the time of their Said Office they [...] Disburse and lay out [---] Ninety pounds and upwards To Mendeing and Repairing the Liberty Pavemts And new Building Strand Bridge and Repaireing the Caurseway there.

That Your Petrs. produced their (Workemens Bills att the Vestry By Order to have an Order att A Speciall Sessions att Bloomsbury for a Rate But the Churchwardens of the sd. Parish Refused to Signe Such Bills.

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petrs. therefore Humble pray Your Worspps Wilbe pleased to Order that the Churchwardens Way Appear in this Honble Court this Prsent Sessions to Shew cause why they Refused to Signe the sd. Bills

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Sabatiers Jeffery Nash.
