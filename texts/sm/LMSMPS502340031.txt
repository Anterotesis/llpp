To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties; Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon of Nichos Ludford one of ye petty Constables of Southmims in this County

Sheweth. That at a Court Leet holden in and for the sd parish on Saturday last past Yor. Petr. was rechosen Constable for the same Parish for the year ensuing, and then and there sworn into the said Office accordingly

That Yor. Petr. discharged himselfe in [...] such Office so punctually in every Respect during the last years Service, That the parishonrs. of the sd parish, in Regard to such Service, as well as to his numerous family of small Children, have approved and thought fitt to continue him in the Sd Office

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to appoint him to pass and Convey ye Vagrants as he has done for ye year past

And that yor. Worshps would Order him such yrly Allowance for the said Service, as has been customary, or as to yor. Worpll, shall seven just & reasonable

And yor. Petr. shall every pray &c

Nicholas Ludford
