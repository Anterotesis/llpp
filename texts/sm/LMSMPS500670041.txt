Middx ss [...] Right Worppall [...] aseshes [...] of the peace at Hicks Hall assembled

The humble Peticon and appeale of sewall Inhabitants whose names are hereunto Subscribed as well for themselves as other Land holders on within the Hamlett of Stratford le bone and old ford in the Parish of Stepney in the said County

Sheweth. That after a new and unusuall manner a pound rate hath beene lately made for the Maintenance of the poor of the said Hamlett for this prsent yeare one thousand six hundred ninety and nine without respect had to personall Estates contrary to the knowne laws of this Realme and the rules and method heretofore used in makeing the like assessements within the said Hamlett by the inequallity whereof your Peticoners are greived and doe humbly appeale from the same

And pray that the overseers of the poore of the said Hamblett and the Parishioners who were prsent att the makeing the said Rate may be ordered to appeare at a certine day to be appoynted by your Worppl to shew Cause why the said Rate Should not be quashed and a new Rate be made according to law.

And your Peticonrs as in duty bound shall ever pray etc.
