To the Rt Worshipfull His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in the Genll Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth

That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the hands Seales of Tho: Railton and Henry Segar Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date ye 9th: May instant Hannah Wilson a widow was Remov'd from and out of the Parish of St Margtt: Westmr in the County aforesaid to ye Said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her Lawfull Settlemt.

That your Petitioners thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgmt: of the Said two Justices humbly Appeale to your Worships against ye same

Therefore humbly Pray Your Worships would be pleas'd to Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St Margarett Westmr in this County may Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide ye final Determinant of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Your Petr. shall every pray Etc.
