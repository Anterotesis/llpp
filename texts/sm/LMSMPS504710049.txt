To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [---] Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hand and Seal of Francis Jackman Esquire One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County Bearing Date the Eighteenth day of April last Past Nathaniel Lowry was removed as a Vagrant from the Parish of Saint James on the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin is the Fields in the same County as the Place of his last legal Settlement, Whereby Your Petitioners conceived themselves agrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray Your Worships to appoint a short Day this present Session to hear and Determine the Matter touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

W. Croft Agent for St. Martin in the Fields}
