To the Right Worsps. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticors of Henry Welth

Sheweth That Yor. Petr in some time past became Com before the Worspr Justice Lea for your Petrs. appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to answer Joseph Biddle for your Petr. takeing App Entire one William Biddle As by the Recognizance may appeare That the fulre of Apprentishipp to hereby it is to be made appeare that the Sd. William Biddle was actually assigned over to your Petr. is att this time in Stafford Shire

P. Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray your worspp to the pleased to respites the Sd. Re [...] ckhiraure untill next Sessions

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Henry Welch
