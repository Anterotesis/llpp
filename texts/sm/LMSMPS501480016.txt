To ye Right Honoble: ye Justices of ye Peace now Sitting uppon ye Bench

The humble Peticon of Robert Plumpton

Sheweth That yor Poor Peticoner and his wife are Both very Ancient and Almost Past the [...] Labor I Keepe a house of must Confes wch. of make hard shift to Pay yr Rent Itt is but thirty Shillings a yeare, I hope yor hon [...] will take itt into Consideracon and free me for I am nott able to Pay itt

yor Peticoner shall ever be bound to Pray for yor honnori Prosperity
