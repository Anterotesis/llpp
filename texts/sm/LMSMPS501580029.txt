To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Dixon

Sheweth

That in the April Sessions your Peticoner preferr'd a Bill of Indictment against Thomas Flack & three other persons for a Rescue

That last Sessions the said Flackecland estinely came to this Court and Pleaded Guilty to the said Indictment without Giving your Peticoner any Manner of Notice Oath of which is ready to be made to this Court Whereby your Peticoner humbly conceives himselfe much aggrieved

Yor: Peticoner therefore humbly Prays such Relief in the premises the to this Court shall be thought Meete

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

John Dixon
