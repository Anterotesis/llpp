To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County aforesaid

The humble Petition and appeal of the [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of W. Blackmore and Robt. Mayo Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 20th day of July 1785 Hannah Rowlands was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave Sart Street as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will Please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may then attend

James Maitland Thos Holdsworth} Wardens
