Hond; Sir The hble Petition of Elizth. Custil

Sheweth That your Petr. is a poor Woman and goes out to Labour by which she gets a Livelihood & on Fryday last yor Petr preferred a Bill of Indictmt. against Mary Mackinteer for Stealing from your Petr. a Shift to the Value of 3s: the Prisoner was Convicted of sd. Felony this day.

Wherefore your Petr. most humbly prays that she may be Allowed what she has Expended in the prosecuting the Prisoner as she has Expended in the said prosecution upwards of 20 Shillings besides looseing her place at Which she was a Nurse

And Yor Petr. shall ever Pray
