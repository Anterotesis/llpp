To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General quarter Sessions assembled:

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Katherine Creed church in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorn) bearing date the Eighth Day of December last past John Hatton Labourer aged about 36 years together with Mary his Wife and three Children Vizt William aged 16 years, Mary Eight years and Robert four years was removed from the parish of St Pancras in the said County of Middx unto the said parish of St Katherine Creed church London Settlement That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices [...] do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order That the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Pancras may appear before you and hear and abide the determinacon & Judgment of your worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever Pray Etca
