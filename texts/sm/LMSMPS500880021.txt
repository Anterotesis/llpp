To the Right Worsppll. her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Anthony Stokes.

Sheweth

That one Amey Goodey being latly delivered of a female bastard child in the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said- County Yor. Petr. is by an Order under the hands & Seales of Richard Milner Esqr. & Thomas Ellis Esqr. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County adjudged to be the father of the said bastard child.

That Yor. Petr. is not the father of the said bastard child which he is ready to make appeare to this Honble Court.

P Westley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly appeales against the said Order & prays that he may be discharged from the mainteynance of the said bastard child.

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
