To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and Thomas Kynaston Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 2d. day of April 1762 Ann Whitesides Wife of Charles Whitesides and five Children Vizt. Ephraim aged Six Years and upwards Thomas aged Five years & upwards Daniel aged Three Years Hester aged about 15 Months and Nicholas aged about Six Weeks were Removed from the parish of St. Luke Chelsea in the County of Middx to the Liberty of the Rolls in the same County as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said order do appeal

Your Petrs: therefore humble Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing and Determining there of may be adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middx

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
