To the Worll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace of & for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Andrew Osborne & Robert Dennett Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace of and for the said County of Middx bearing date the 26th. day of June 1729 Elizabeth the Wife of Arthur Cox and their Two Children Vizt. John Aged about three Years and Mary Aged about Two Years were removed from And Out of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappel in the Said County to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of their last legal Settlement

That yr. Petrs. thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal to your Worships against the fame.

Therefore Humbly pray your Worst. will please to Order. That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Mary Whitechappell may Attend this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear & Abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
