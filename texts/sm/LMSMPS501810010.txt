To the Pet: Worsppfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx

The Humble Peticonr & Appeal of John Shard of Hee Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in ye sd. County

Sheweth That yr. Petr is this Year charged gd. P week to yr poors Rate of ye sd. Parish when severall of ye. Petr. Neighbours who are of better Ability than himself are not charged so much

Yor. Petr: therefore most humbly prays that your Worspps would be pleased to give her such relief in ye premises as to yr Worspps shall seem meet

And yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
