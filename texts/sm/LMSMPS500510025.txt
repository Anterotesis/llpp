To the Right Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Sarah Millner

Sheweth

That on Thursday Last yor. Petr. Husband Robt Millner was by the Worspl Justice [...] Mundey comitted to New Prison for being an Idle person and a Comon Disturber where he now remaines.

That yor. Petrs sd husband hath brought up tenn Children in the Parish [---] where he lives & hath wpl then from the Parish & hath endeavoured to mainteyne his Hannly according to his abillity And forasmuch as your Petr. haveing Five small Children & she now biyy with Child soe that aswell yor Petr. as her sd Children must Inevitably Perish unless releived by your Worspps

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspps to be pleased to Grant that her sd husband may be Discharged from by sd Imprisonmt

And yor Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Sarah Millner her [x] mke
