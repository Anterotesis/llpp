To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Ovrseers of the poore of the parish of St. Anno Westm in the sd County.

Sheweth

That by Warrant under the Hands & Seales of John Perry and Joseph Shorte Esqrs. Two of her Matys Justices of the Peace of this County [---] Mary Skitsley Wife of Robert- Skilsley who hath left his sd Wife and gone from her was passed from the Pish of St. Andrews Holbourne in the sd. County to the sd. yish of St. Anne Westm.

That the sd. Mary Skitsley is since brought to bedd of a Female Child and forasmuch as your Petr. being creddibly Informed that the Last Legall Settlemt. of the sd Robert Skitsley was in the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be please to Order that the sd. Mary Skiltsley & her Child may be Repussed to the sd. Pish of St. Sepulches London otherwise as unto yor Worspps shall seem most meet.

And Yor. Petr. Shall Eve pray Etc

Richd Duke Wm Green} Churchwardens
