To the Right [...] Worshipful his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition of Hannah Slanus

Humbly Sheweth That your petr. is Comitted to New prison by Anthony Chamberlain Esqr for being a Loose Idle & disorderly person taken on the Grass in Moorfeild in an Unseemly posture with one Richd Oliver And your petr. having at the Same time Charged the said Richard Oliver with having Casual knowledge of her body [---] Without her Consent

Your petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble Court will Grand her an order to be bought to this Court to preferr a bill agt the said Richard oliver for the above offence your petr. [---] [...] having no Friend to Apply for such order in any other Maner being poor & destitute

And Your petr shall Ever pray Etc Hannah Slanus
