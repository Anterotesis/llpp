To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Bortholomon the great in the city of London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Alexander Ward and John Metcalfe Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx (Quour Unus) bearing Date Eighteenth Day of March last past John Ewer and Easter his wife were removed from the parish of St Andrew Holborn in the Liberty of Saffron Hill, Ely Rents and Hatton Garden in the County of midlx unto the said parish of St Bartholomew the Great by the name of great St Bartholomews in the Liberty of the City of London as the place of their last legal Settlement That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices And Do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Therefore your Petrs most humbly pray your Worpps to hear them in the prmisses and That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St Andrew Holborn in the Liberty aforesd may appear before you at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever pray Etc
