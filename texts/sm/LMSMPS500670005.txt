To the Right Worspll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of John Woodford & William Parties ye Surveyors of the Highwaies in the Parish of Fryern Barnett in the said County

That the highwaies belonging to the said Parish are much out of repaire which yor. Petr. are at this time amending but are not able to finish Such their intended worte by reason the Statute worke will not extend to compleat the same.

P Swestley Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yox. worser to Order that a rate may be made upon the Inhabitants of the Said Parish for the repaireing and compleating the said Highwaies.

And yor. Prs. Shall on pray etc.
