To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humbe Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin Vintry in the City of London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Matthew Hewett and Thomas Pindar Esqr. Two of his Matices Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx (Quorn vinus) bearing date the One and thirtieth day of October last past Elizabeth Boell aged about Fourteen Years Daughter of John Boell deceased was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middlx unto the said Parish of St. Martin Vintry in the City of London as the Place of her Last Legal Settlement

That your Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal to your Worppd therefrom And Pray That your Worppd will be pleased to hear them in the Excuised And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may appear before you at a certain Day to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinaton of your Worppd. Something this appeal

And Yor: Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc.
