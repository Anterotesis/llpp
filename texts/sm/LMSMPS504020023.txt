Middx To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Pettion of Henry Walden and Thomas Tipping Constables and John Gearing Headborough of the Township of New Brentford in the said County

Sheweth That Whereas James Clitherow Esqr Lord of the Manner of Boston cum Brentford wherein the said Township doth lie hath not lately Held a Court Leet in his said Mannor and is not likely Speedily to hold one Whereby Constables & Headboroughs may be Chosen in their Rooms And Whereas Your Petitioners have Served the Said Offices the Year last past

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that they may be discharges from their said Office and that the Persons Named in the Lists Annexed are fit and proper persons to Succeed them in their said Offices

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray

April 25th 1750
