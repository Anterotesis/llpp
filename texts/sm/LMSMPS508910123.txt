To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Kitchiner and Edward Naish Esquries two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the [---] Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth day of August one thousand seven hundred and Ninety three Mary Gould Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate in the City of London and delivered to your Petitioners being the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate the said Justices adjudging the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Gould to be in the said Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate.

That your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and therefore Appeal against the same

Wm. Chetham Solr. for the Appellants Your Petitioners humbly Ray that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them And that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex.
