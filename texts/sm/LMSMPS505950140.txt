To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarters Session of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of George Johnston and Thomas Kynaston Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County bearing date the 29th. day of March last Charles Slader was Removed and Conveyed form the parish of St. Leonard Foster Lane in the Liberty of Westminster to the said parish of St Giles in the Fields as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Richd Fletcher Thos: Simmonds W Hilliar John Davis
