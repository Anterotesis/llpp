To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Daniel Gach & William Jones Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventeenth day of September last past Mary Stone Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from & out of the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the aforesaid Parish of Saint Sepulchre as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

The Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order of Removal & pray that your Worships will grant them such Relief in the Premisses as shall seem just & equitable

And the Petitioners will Pray Etc:

G: Talmash Agent for the Appellants
