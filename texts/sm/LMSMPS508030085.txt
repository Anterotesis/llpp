To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Oder or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Rt Towler and James Croft Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof bung of the Quorum) bearing date the 1st day of December 1785 Mary Seadding Spinster aged about 39 Years was removed from the Parish of St Mary le Strand in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster unto the said Parish of St Mary Lambeth in the said County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of St Mary Lambeth to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices, do therefore Appeal to Your Worship [...] therefrom and humbly Pray that you will be Pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Mary le Strand may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Vincent Solicitor for the Appellants
