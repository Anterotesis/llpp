To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Preston

That your Peticoner by his Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing date the Third Day of April Anno Dom 1716 became an Apprentice to John Hibbs of St Andrews Holbourne in this County Sadler for the terme of Seven years comencing from the date of the said Indenture As thereby appears

That your Petitrs said Master hath frequently beat & abused him In a very barbarous and In human Manner and Neglects and refuseth to Instruct him his Busyness To answer which complaints The said Master stands bound for his Appearance in this Court in this present Sessions before the Worshippfull Justice Ward.

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to Order his said Master to Attend this Court some Day this Sessions to shew cause why he should not be Discharged from his Apprenticehood to the [...] Hibbs.

And yor: Petr: Shall Ever pray Etc

Jon Preston
