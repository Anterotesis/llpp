To ye Right Worpll his majtes Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx in their Genal Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye Church wardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under ye hands & Seals of Sr John Austen & H: Smith Esqr two of his Majties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midd (Quorum unns) bearing Late ye twentieth Day of August last past Martha Steward Widdow was removed From ye parish of southmims in ye sd. County unto ye sd parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as ye place of her last Legal Settlement That your Petitioners humbly conceive the [...] teres aggrieved by ye Warrant & Order of ye said two Justices do appeal [...] your Worshipps there from

And pray that your Worshipps will be pleased to hear them in ye prmisses and order that ye Church wardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye parish of southmims may appear before you att a certain Day [...] [...] to hear & alide ye Judgement & Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc
