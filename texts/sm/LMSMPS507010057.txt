To Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of the Governour Deputy Governour Assistances and Guardians - of the poor for the Town of Colchester in the County of Essex.

Sheweth That Ann Wells an infant of the age of Six months was by warrant under the hands and Seales of the Worspplls Wm: Benson Knt and Roger Smith Esqr. two of her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Midds lately passed from the parish of St. Leonards Brumly in the said County to the said Town of Colchester in the parish of St. Magdalen That the said Ann Wells was born at St. Leonards Brumley and hath noe legall Settlemt. in the parish of St. magdalen in the said Town of Colchester wch. your Petrs. are ready to make appear to this Honble Court

Yor. Petrs. therefore doe appeal against the sd Order and doe humbly pray yor. Worspps to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Leonards Brumly may appear before this Honble Court to morrow to Shew cause why the sd Ann Wells should not be repassed to the said parish of St. Leonards Brumly

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray
