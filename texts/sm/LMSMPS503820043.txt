To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in their General Session Assembled

The humble Petition of Richard Rush Gent one of the High Constables within the Hundred of Edmonton.

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath Served the Office of High Constable for that Part of the Division called South-mims and Hadley for upwards of three Years

That your Petitioner hath Paid and discharged all the Publick Rates which hath been Issued forth by any order of this Court

Your Petitioner humbly Prays your Worships to discharge him from the said Office of High Constable and to appoint another in his Room

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Richard Rush

Your petitioner Recommends to your Worships for your Approbation Henry Hill of Hadley Carpenter Francis Lee [...] of Pottars Barr in the Parish of South mims Yeoman and Francis Randall of Southmims Shopkeeper

We whose Hands are hereunto set do Recommend Henry Hill of the Parish of Hadley to be High Constable in the Room of Richard Rush

D Hicks [...] Rd Hassell
