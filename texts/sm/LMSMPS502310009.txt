To the Worshipfull Bench of Justice Now Sitting at Hicks Hall

The Humble petition of John Banking

Sheweth That he has been a prisoner for Debt Ever Since the year 1721 and was Convicted at Hicks hall in August 1724 for an assault and fined by the Court Â£10 which fine is now paid: and that the bearer will Justifie yt your poor petitioner has Summon'd his Preditors three times and in all things Conformed himselfe to the Act of parliament for releife of Insolvent Debtors

He therefore Humble prays the Worshipfull bench will be pleased it Consider his Extreame poverty and order his appearence this Day in order to his being Dischargd

And he will Ever pray & Etc.
