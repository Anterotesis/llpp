To the Right Worspl his MatiesJustices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Spurwell Marriner

Sheweth That yor. Petr. haveing been a Slave att allgires for about Tenn Yeares your petrs. Friends did about Seaven Yeares past vaise and lay downe for your Petr. the fund of Â£450. Which was paid for your petr. Redempcon from Slavery and your petr. haveing had great losses great part of the sd. Money is Still unpaid by your Petr. and your petr. haveing Starre meanes Sufficient to mainteyne himselfe and Family and being an Inhabitant on Stepney Causeway in a house but of 6s. Pannm and hath been and Still is overrated in the Assessmt for the poor of the sd. parish and much hitcher then his Reighbourne of four greater substance and for asmuchas his Neighbourns being rated but att 6s. Pannm for a Single rate and 12 Pannm for a double rate yor Petr. is charged & rated att 20s. Pannm for the one & 40s. Pannm for the other

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to remedy his Aggreicame by Such wares & means as only yor. Worspps Shall Seen meet [...] Tho Nash

And Yor. Pr. Shall ever Pray Etc John Spurrell
