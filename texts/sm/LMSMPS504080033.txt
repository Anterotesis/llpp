To the right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for in County of Middlesex at their general Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churhcwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the fourth day of this Instant January under the hands and Seals of Water Berry Esqr. and Sr. Samuel Gorcer Knt. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Mary Mason aughter of Peter Mason deceased, aged about where Years was amount from the Hamlet of Poplar and Blacks the in this County to the Said Parish of Saint Anne is the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Order of the Said two Justices do appeal against the Same

And humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day this present Session for all Parties to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And the [---] Petr. will pray Etc

G: Talmash Attorney for the Appellants.
