Middlesex to Wit To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chipping Barnet in the Liberty of Saint Alban

Sheweth That Stephen Fields Sarah his Wife and their eight Children namely Susannah aged upwards of nineteen years Charlotte aged upwards of fourteen years Stephen aged upwards of thirteen Years Frances aged upwards of eleven Year Thomas aged upwards of nine years James aged upwards of seven Years John aged upwards of three Years and Sarah aged about sixteen Months were by an Order under the hands and Seals of William Bleamire and Kinnard Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Seventh day of June One thousand Seven hundred and ninety seven removed to the said Parish of Chipping Barnet from and out of that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex as being the Place of their last legal settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships that the said Order of Removal may be Quashed and that they may be reimbursed the Costs and expences which they have in this Case wrongfully sustained

Jos. Rodbard Corns.Cope} Churchwardens

Isaac Newton Wm. Northage} Overseers
