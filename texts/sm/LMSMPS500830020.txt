The humble Peticon of Timothy Owen Brazier

Sheweth That in Some time past your Petr bought a Parcell of Goods Relateing to his Trade and Search being made they were Suspected to be Stolen where upon your Petr. was carryed before the Worsppl Justice Milner who comitted your Petr. to New Prison in this County and Ordered the sd. Goods to be Delivered into the hands of Mr. Partridge One of the Constables of the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in this County where they now remaine But forasmuch as noe Person appearing against your Petr. Your Worspps were pleased to Discharge your Petr. form his Recognizance

Your. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that the sd. Constable may Deliver the sd. Goods to your Petrs

p Tho. Nash

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc Timothy owin
