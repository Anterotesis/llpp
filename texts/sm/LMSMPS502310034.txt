To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the feilds in the said County of Middx

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hand and Seals of Nico: Jeffreys and Jno. Troughton Esqrs: two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 25th. day of November last Mary Roberts Singlewoman was removed from the parish- of St. Andrew Holborn above barrs in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legall Settlement Whereby your Petrs. humbly Conceive themselves to be agrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeale to your Worships against the Judgmt. of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint Some day this present Sessions to hear & Determine the matter touching the Said Appeale

And yor Petrs: Shall ever pray Etc.
