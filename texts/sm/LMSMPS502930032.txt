To the Rt. Worl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now Sitting at Hickshall

The humble Petition of John Simpkins of the Parish of Whitechapl. in the County of Middlesex

Humbly Sheweth That Yor. Said Petitr. about Eight Years agoe being then about Thirteen Years old was bound Apprentice the Said Parish of Whitechappel to one John Chittle a Plaisterer of the said Parish which Said John Chittle uses your Said Petitioner in a barbarous and Cruel manner by immoderately & unreasonably beating him [...] not allowing him Sufficient Meat Drink [...] Apparel

Therefore Yor. said Petr. desires you would take his Case into your Consideration to releive him so as in your Wisdoms Shall seem meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.

The mark of John [x] Simpkins
