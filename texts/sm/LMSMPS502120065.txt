To the Rt. Worshipll, his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx, in their Genll, Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: James Westminster Etc.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Bearing date the Seventeenth day of May 1723, Catherine Burnet Wife of George Barnet was Removed from the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the County of Middx. aforesaid, unto the said Parish of St. James Westminster in the said County of Middx, alledging the said Parish to be the Place of her last legall Settlement. That your Petitioners conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices, Do humbly appeal [...] Worships thereupon

Your Petitionrs. therefore most humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St Martins may appear before your at a Certain day to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships Touching this Appeal.

And Your Petitioner shall every Pray Etc.
