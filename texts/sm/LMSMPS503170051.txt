To the Rt Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions Assembled

The Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands Seals of Gideon Harvey & Richard Manley Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the City of Westmr in the Said County Come being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of May last past Lucey Paulton wife of William Paulton was removed from out of the parish of St. Margarett Westmr. in the Said County to the Said Parish of St Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

Yor. Petrs therefore thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the Said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the Same & humbly pray a day to be Appointed for hearings Determining the Same

And yer Petr Shall ever pray Etc

Dont Bolton Sell for the Appellts.
