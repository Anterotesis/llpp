To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their Generall quarter Sessions assembled

The [...] appear of [...] Several persons whose Names are Subscribed inhabitants within the Ancient limits of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That Henry Howard John Burdett John Godfrey and William Coates have been lately Nominated or appointed by two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said County by their Warrant or Order for that purpose to be Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish for the year Ensueing, but such Nomination or Appointment hath been as your Peticoners humbly Conceive made in an undue manner of Persons not qualityed or lyable to serve the said office.

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves Ag grieved by the said Appointment Warrt or Order doe humbly appeale therefrom & pray to be relived against the same as to your Worships shall Seem meet & that a day may be Appointed for hearing of this Appeale & the said Persons before named then attend with the said Warr or Appointment & that Thomas Harvey [...] served the said Warrant [...] then also attend & [...] Examined touching the same.

And Your Peticoners shall ever pray

Edward Rogers Tho. Richardson Tho. Groye Jos: Jackson

Dun. Whyte William Okely Will Downing Wm Gamble Caleb Colchester
