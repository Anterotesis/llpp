To the Right Worpfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter- Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the- Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of- the Parish of St Ann Black Fryers London

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Two of- his Matys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the Twenty Fifth Day of- August last past whereby Valereen Mann was removed from the parish of St Pauls Shadwell in the County- of Middx aforesaid to the said parish of St Ann Black Fryers London as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Rd: Fitzwater Geo:Atwood} Church Wardens

Wherefore Your Peticoners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the Said two Justices of the Peace therefore- appeale to this Court against the Same Order and humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day to hear and Determine the Same

And Your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
