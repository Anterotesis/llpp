To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Sarah Lane. Wife of Richard Lane.

Sheweth That your Peticoners Husband stands bound [---] by Recognizance in this Court to this present Sessions to keepe the peace towards your Peticoner

That your Peticoners said Husband frequently threatens soe so one as he is Discharged from his said Recognizance That he will be the Death of your peticoner Insoemuch that your Peticoner goes in great Danger of her life Oath of which is ready to be made

Yor Petitr therefore humbly prays your Worsppsll will be pleased to Order that he may be Continued on his said Recognizance

And yor. Petitr. Shall Ever pray Etc

Sarah Lane
