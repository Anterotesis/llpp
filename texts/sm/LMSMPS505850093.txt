To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Girdler Esquire and Sir John Fielding Knight Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th day of June last. A Male Bastard Child named John Aged about three years and an half born in the Body of Ann Kent was removed from and out of the [---] Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Luke

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices of the peace Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray your Worships will be pleasd to appoint some day in this Sessions for hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And Petitioners Shallever pray Etc

J: Jenings Attorney for the Appellants
