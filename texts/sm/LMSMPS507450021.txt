To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hick's Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sweth That by virtue an Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and William Kitchener Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fifteenth Day of September last Mary Query (Wife of John Query who is gone from her) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex to one said Parish of Saint as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Query and Mary his Wife whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will Please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and & determining the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Pancras do then Attend the Court

And yr. Petitr: shall Pray Etc

J Wall Saml Jackman Rd Murthwaite Thos Wellows} Church Warden & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
