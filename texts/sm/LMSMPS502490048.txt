Middx ss To the worspp his Majes Justices [...] for the County of Middx assembled in Qr. Sessions

The humble peticon of the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. Midd [...] Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County [...] bearing Date the Second day of Decr. last John Osborn & Ester his wife were remove from the pish of St. Marty in the feilds in the sd. County to the sd Pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That ye petr. doe think themselves aggrived by the sd ordr. & doe humbly appeale there from to them Cort

Butt in regard the sd. John Osburn hath [...] from his [...] this at prsent [...] [...] Now most [...] that the Heareing of this Appeale adjorne own like [...] Genr. Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County

And yor petr Shall pray Etc
