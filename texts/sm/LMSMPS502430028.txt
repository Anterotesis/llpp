To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of Peace for the County of Middx. now in their Generall Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Peticon of John Wheatley of the Parish of St. Sepulchre Glazier

Sheweth That about Two Years Ago Your Petr. was employed by Mr. Geary then Housekeeper at Hicks Hall to repair and Amend the Windows belonging to the sd. Hall who at that time Informd Your Petr. that the County was to pay him for so doeing upon wee Your. Petr. did the Some to the Amount of about [...] two Pounds ped money and that your petr. has Severall times Applyed him Self to the [...] of the Peace for the Same but could never obtaine the payment thereof

Therefore Yor. Petr. humbly Prays such releife in the princes as to your Worships shall [...] [...] meet

And Your Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
