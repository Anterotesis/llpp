To the Right Honourable Bench of Justices the Humble Petition of Susanah Carnall Wife of Wm Carnell Shewing Your Petitioner haveing latly Married the Said Wm Carnall and finde Since That he hath three Children kept by Clerkenwell Parish and is informed the Officers of the parish will force her to keep them but being impoverished since her marriage is incapable of Maintaining them but would indevour to do it with some Small Assistance from this Honourable Bench

And Your Petitioner Shall ever Pray Etc
