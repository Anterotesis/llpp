To the Right Worspll Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middle assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Full said County

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Sr Thomas Franklin Bart & Ralph [...] Hawtrey Esqr. two of Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the Sd County (Qurd wnus) bearing Date the third Day of October instant Elizabeth Williams aged acor [...] Seven years was passed from the parish of Harrow in the said County to the said parish of St Giles in the & fields as the place of her last legall Settlement

Yor peticonrs think themselves aggrieved wth. the Judgmt. of the Sd two Justices, appeal to this court [...] humbly pray (the premiers considered) that the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Harrow & all other parker concerned [...] erein May be Ordered to attend this Court come day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of that Court touching this Appeal

And Yor peticonrs (as in Duty bound) shall ever pray Etc.

p J [...] Tyton ss
