Middxss: To the right Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace assembled att Hicks Hall

The humble peticon and Appeale of Edward Hopkins Thomas Hopkins Sarah Ingram Widdow Richard Eustace Richard Waley Joseph Evans John Barrett Joseph knowles James Huggett Thomas Merredell and many other Inhabitants of the Hamlett of stratford le Bow and old Ford in the said County.

Sheweth

That your peticoners in behalfe of them selves and other Inhabitants of the said Hamlett did att the Lady day Quarter Session peticon this worpfull Court against the prsent Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Hamlett And did then likewise appeale against a pound rate made for the releife of the poor of the said Hamlett for this prsent yeare one thousand six hundred ninety and nine as unequall unusuall and greivous to the land on Occupiers upon hearing the said appeale that rate was quashed it being adjudged unequall oppressive and contrary to law and a Competent sume of money was ordered to be raised for the necessary releise of the poor of the said Hamblett [...] according to the ability of the said Inhabitants put Etc
