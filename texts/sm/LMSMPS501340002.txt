To her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Joseph Wishall and Edward Boswell Churchwardens of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth That the Highwayes in the said parish are very much out of repair and will require a considerable sume of money to repair and amend the same which cannot be raised without the aid of this Honble Court

Yor Petr therefore humbly pray that a Rate or Assessment may be made upon the Jnticants of the said parish not exceeding three half pend in the pound for repairing the highwayes there according to the direccons of the Act of Parliamt lately made to be collected by the prsent Surveyors of the Highwayes in the said parish And that the money to be collected on the said Rate be employed and laid out as the Justices of the peace of this County usually meeting at the speciall sessions in the said parish shall direct & appoint

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Joseph withall Edw Baswell} Churchwardens
