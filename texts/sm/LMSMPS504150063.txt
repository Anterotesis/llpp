To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass under the hand and Seal of Ts Lediard Esqr. one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing Date the 22d. Day of Novr. last Margt. Forster was passed as a Vagrant from the Parish of St Margaret Westmr. in the County of Middlesex to the aforesaid Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved and agt the said Pass Appeal

Your Petitioner therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session to hear and Determine the matter of the said Appeals

And your Petitioner Shall pray Etc

Wm Massey Saml. Burgess} Ovrs
