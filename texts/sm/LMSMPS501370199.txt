To the Right Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Wm: Austen and Thom Parmey late petty Constables of the pish of Hadley in this County

Sheweth That your Peticonr. have expended sevll. Sumes of money in passing an conveying of Vagrants through this County in Psuance of a late Act of Parliamt.

And That yor. Peticonrs. have sevll: Bills taxed and Charged upon Wm. Prat gent H: Constable in their Division for payment of the sd moneys but that He refuses to pay the same wth. out some Orders from this Court so to doe.

Your Peticonrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worships would be pleased to make such Orders in the prmisses for Releife of your poor Peticonrs. touching this Complaint as to yor. Worships shall seem meet

And yor. Peticonrs. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc.

Wm: Austen Tho: Parmey
