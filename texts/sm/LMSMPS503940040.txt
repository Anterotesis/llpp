To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Henry Feilding and William Jones Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Libty of Westmr (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of February 1748 Barbara Aime Daughter of Elizabeth Aime by Bernard Aime decd was Removed from the Parish of St. Paul Covent garden in the said County to the said Parish of St. James as the Place of her last legal Settlement where by your Petitrs. think them -selves agrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore humbly Prays your Worships will Please to appoint a time for hearing Determining the said Appeal and that all person Concerned may this accept

And your Petits shall ever Pray Etc

S Seddon agent for [...] James
