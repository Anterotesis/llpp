To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Paul Mary le Bone in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of I Girdler and W: Bleamire Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of October 1785 Sarah Borsker the Wife of John Bowler (who is therein mentioned to be gone away and left her) and then Child named Elizabeth aged about five Weeks were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone as the Please of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Bowker and her said Child Elizabeth Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly Appeal there from

Your Petitioners there fore Humbly Pray Your Worships will be pleased to Hear them in the Premises and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke may appear before you at accertain Day to be appointed to Hear and abide the Judgment and determination of Your Worships touching this appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pay Etc

Thos Martin Sor. for the Appeallant No:3 Newman Street
