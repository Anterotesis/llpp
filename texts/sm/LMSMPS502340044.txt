To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden for the Sd. County

The humble Peticon of Sarah Gary of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner rents a House in Hexton Square in the sd. Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and is rated or Assessed towards the releife of the Poor of the said Parish consider ably more then her Neighbours that Occupy houses in the same Square of Greater rents then Yor Petitionrs: She therefore conceives herselfe Agrieved by the Sd. Assessment and humbly Appeals against the same praying such releife in the premisses as to this Honourable Court shall seem Just

And Yor Petionr shall Ever Prays
