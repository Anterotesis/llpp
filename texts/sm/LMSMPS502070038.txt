To the Rt Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St John of Wepping in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Samuel Perry and Robert Dennett Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace (Quar unn) for the said County bearing date the 20th day of October last post Katherine Kilely Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Mary Matfellon ats White Chapple in the Said County to the Said Parish of St John of Wapping as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Peticoners think themselves Agreivd by the Said Order, Humbly Appeals to this Honoble. Court against the Same

And Prays that your Worspps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St Mary Matfellon ats White Chapple And all other persons Concerned may Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Decmr: ye 4th 1722 We ye Church Wardens & our fears of ye Parish of St Mary Whitechappell do a Low of & Consent to this Apeale } John Bartholmew Morgan Davies

a Wittness our hands

Church Wardens
