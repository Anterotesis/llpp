To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon of Mary Cranwell

The King agt. Carpenter

Sheweth That your Peticoner Peticoned This Court on Tuesday Last for relief agt. the Deft and This Court was pleaded to [...] refer it for a further hearing That Justice Lambert and Justice Midford might be an the Bench The Bail being In Suficient and one of these not to be found and the Deft Keeping out of the way [...] Additionall affidts. of the facts being filed

your Peticoner therefore [...] pray the Same may be read and Yr Peticoner Will ever pray and so forth

Mary Cranwell
