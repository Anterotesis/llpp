To the righ [...] his Maties Justices of the Peace at the [...] [...] Session Hicks Hall in St. John Streets in and for the County of [...] Middx assembled

The Humble Peticonr end appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the said County

Sheweth [...] of [...] bearing date [...] the 8th. day of December last past under the hands & Seales of Robt. Jackson and Robert Dennett Esqrs. Justices of the Peace of the said County (Quor unus) Susanna How Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the Said County to the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney abovesaid as the place of her last legall Settlement by Which Order Yr. Pets there themselves Aggreived and Appeale agt. the Same.

Wherefore Yr. Pets. most humbly pray that a day may be Appointed this Prsent General Qr. Sessions by the Honble Court for the determining of the Peticon and Appeale And that all persons concerned may Attend & abide the Judgement of this Court touching thee same

And Yr. Pets. Shall ever Pray Etc.
