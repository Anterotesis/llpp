To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Barking in the City of London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a certain order a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Rice Davies Esquire and The Reverend Henry Reynett Clerk two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of August 1793 Henry Moses who as alledged in the said order was then insane was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Alhallows Barking in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Henry Moses Whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said order or Pass Warrant and Pray that the benefit of the said appeal Mary be Saved into them and that the hearing and determination thereof may ne adjourned until the next general Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex

W. L. Newman Session Appellants }
