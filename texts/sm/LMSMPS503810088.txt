To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order- under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Gach and James Fraser Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in this County bearing date the 17th. day of September 1747 Katherine Burnet, Singlewomen was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County to the sd Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court- against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that- the benefit of the said appeal maybe saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be held for this County

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

S:Parry, Agent for St. George Hanover Square
