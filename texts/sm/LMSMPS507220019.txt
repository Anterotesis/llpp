To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Walford and W. Blackborow Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of January 1780 Mary Ivin the Wife of William Ivin (who is in the East Middlesex Middlx) and her Child Named John aged about Twelve Months were removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of their last Aged Settlement Whereby four petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the Same the same

Therefore humbly pray this Court will be please to appoint a Fime for hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all persons Concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray

I [...] Scarr [...] for appelds. Herton [...]
