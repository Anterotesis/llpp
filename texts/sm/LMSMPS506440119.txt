To the Right worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Third day of June last Thomas Wright and his Three Children Ann aged Nine years Thomas aged Seven years and Samuel aged Three years and a half were removed from the Parish of Trinity in the Minories in the Liberty of his Majesty's Tower of London and County aforesaid to the said Parish of saint andrew undershaft as the Place of their last Legal settlement

Your Petitioners therefore Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to this Court for such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray

Samuel Butler atty. for the appellants.
