To the Rt. Worpll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Martha Pelley of the Parish of Wapping Stepney in the sd County Widow

Most Humbly Sheweth

That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish have Asses'd Yor. Peticoner Eighteen pence P Month towards the Releife of the poor there for the Year ensuing.

That Your Petr. rents but a house of Nine pounds & Ann in the sd Pish and has liv'd therein Thirty Years & never was before asses'd to the reliefe of the sd Poor So that she thinks herselfe very much aggreiv'd by the sd Assessmt.

Yor Peti [...] Therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that she may be releiv'd in the sd prmisses

And Yor. petr. shall ever pray Martha Pelley [x] her Mke
