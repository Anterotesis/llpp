To the Right Worshll. Her Maties: Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary Newington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

Thay by Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of her Majesties: Justices of the peace of this County One Whitethorne a Child about Eleven weeks old was passed from the parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex to the sd parish of St: Mary Newington in the County of Surrey as the place of his last legall Settlement

That Yor. Petioners can made it appear to this Honble: Court that the said Whitethorne was never legally setled [...] the said parish of St: Mary Newington & finding themselves agreived by the said order of removall

J Farrer Yor. peticoners therefore most humbly Appeal against the said Order & pray your Worships to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate may [...] Appear in this Honble; Court this prsent Session to Show cause why the said Whitethorne Should [...] not be repassed to the parish of St: Gyles without Cripplegate

And Yor. peticonrs. shall ever pray etc

friday at 2 o Clock
