To the Right Worll: and Worll: The Chairman and other her Maties Justices of ye Peace of ye County of Middx now Sitting att Hicks hall in St John Street

The humble Peticon of Ann Blow Spinster

Sheweth That yor Petr was Servt maid & one Mr. Mary Blay & had just by due Nine pence & Eight Shillings for wages which her said Mistress refused to pay her Tho: often in humble manner Demand do where upon yor Petr was forced to apply her Selfe To Justice Dyodale date who: granted his Warrtt the 12th: of May last who (upon hear may cause fully on both sided & upon her said Mistressed refuseing to pay yor Petr her said Wages) Bound her over To appear ye Last Session: Butt She not appearing in fine The Court Ordered notice To Bee given to her to attend which notice was given & Then she attended without Champerling her Sollicitor And by Combination & Contiwancd which her Sollititor gott yor Pelir out of Court To ye Appeared in St Jones Lane under Pretence of giveing yor Petr Satiffaction her Mrs. gott into Court & by Mocom precuered her Discharge which ye Court after understanding Ordereed Your Pelir To apply her sesse To another Justice for a new Warrt

Whereupon Yor Petr Obtained Justices Iretons Warrtt agt her and she removeing her Lodgings yor Petr with great truble & Charge att Last Took her who was Bound over by Justice Met called to appeard here This Sissions

That this great Oppression of yor Petr said Mrs. hath been a very great turble Charge & Injury to yor Poore Petr & Tened To her utter & nine

Therefore yor poore Petr in all humility implored this Honoble Court to Order a Short day for the said Mrs. Mary Blay to attend this Court or to grant yor Petir a Bench Warrt Or otherwise to do for tor Petirs Speedy relieife as to yor worshipps shall seem most meet

And yor Petir shall ever pray Etc
