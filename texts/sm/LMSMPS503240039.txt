The Humble Petition of Mitchell Badcock

Sheweth That your Petitioner is a Very Poor woman and haveing lately been Robbed and last all she had to Support her, is now become a Miserable poor Creature and not haveing any Place of Residence Humbly Prays that your Worshipps will take it into Consideration and Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the parish of St Geoerge Middx to Assist her in some manner or other, Otherwise she must Inevitabley Perish

and Your Petitioner as in Duty shall Ever pray Etc

Michell badcock
