To the Right Worsppll her Maty's Justices of the peace [...] of Midds

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Watford in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Warrt. under the lands and Seales of Richard Meryweather Esqr. James Hawley Esqr. and Thomas Cullum Esqr Justices of the peace for the County of Midds aforesd One Elizabeth the wife of John Cogdon was lately past from the parish of Wilsdon in the Said County to the parish of Watford aforesaid

That the Said Eliz: Cogdon hath noe legall Settlement in the said parish of Watford.

P L Weslley Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly appeale against the said Order and pray yor. Worspps to appoint a day this prsent Sessions to hear and determine the Same

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
