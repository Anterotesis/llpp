To the Rt Worpll. his Majties Justices of the peace (Assembled in their General Sessions) for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Walton on the Hill in County of Surry.

Sheweth That by Order or warrt. under the hands and Seales of Bastw. Johnson and Edr byrose Esqrs. her of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx. (Quor Unus) bearing date the 16th. Day of January last past Mary Deacon the widow of Henry Decon deced with her two Children (Vizt) Peter Deacon Aged five years & an half and Henry Deacon Aged three years were removed from the Hamblett of Wapping Stepney in the sd. County of Middx to the parish of Walton on the Hill aforesd. (being called in the sd. Order the parish of Walton in the County [...] Petconrs. find themselves agrieved by the sd. Order or Warrt. of the sd. two Justices, And humbly appeal agt. the same to this Honble. Court.

Your peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worpps. will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Hamlett of Wapping Stepney & all other persons concerned to Attend this Court some Day this Prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court upon & touching the sd. Appeal And that your Petrs. may have such relief herein as to your Worpps. shall seem meet Etc.

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
