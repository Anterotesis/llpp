To the Rt. Worshipll. his Maties: Justices of ye. Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Mary Champlin

Sheweth That your Peticoner is a poor Penticoner in the parish of St. Andrew Holborne in the Liberty above the Barrs in the said County, for these ten yeares past and hath had an allowance of four shillings P Month for her and three small Children which all are at this time a great Charge and trouble to your peticoner for their maintainance being a very poor and infirme Woman unable to Work to support herself or Children, and the same allowance being taken from her by the present Oversees of the Sd. Liberty for some Months past, and onley allow six pence P Week for herself and three small Children and severall of her friends and Acquaintance being Dead and no Relations to help or assist her or her Children whereby she is brought into a very poor and needfull Condicon she and her said Children wanting at this time the Common Necessaries of Life

Yor. peticonr. therefore most humbly prayes this Worshipll Court will be pleased to grant an Order to the Overseers of the "poor of the said Liberty to" pay and allow unto her such weekly allowance for herself and Children, as to your Worshipps shall seem meet

And she as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc
