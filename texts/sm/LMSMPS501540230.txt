To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions assambled

The humble petitions Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Mary Colechurch in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the 16th Day of April last past John Gouldson and Mary his wife four Children vizt John aged about 20 years William aged about Sixteen years Chester aged about Thirteen years and Samuel aged about nine years were removed from the hamlett of Ratcliff in the parish of Stepney the County of Middx to the said parish of St Mary Colechurch London alleadging the said parish to be the place of their last legal settlements

That Your Petitioners do conceive themselves aggreived by the said warrant or pass Order of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to youre Worrpps therefrom

And do most humbly pray that your Worrpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamlett of Ratcliff may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petr. Shall ever pray & Etc
