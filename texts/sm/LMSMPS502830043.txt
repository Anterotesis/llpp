To The Rt. Worshipl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George the Martyr in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That Catherine Evans Singlewoman, aged about Sixty Years, was by Order of Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Milner and Richard Gifford Esquires two of his (Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the Third Day of May Instant, removed and Coneveyed from the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. George the Martyr as the place of her last legal Settlement.

Yor. Petrs. humbly conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and Appeal from the Judgment of the said two Justices to the Judgment of this Honoble Court and desire a Day of hearing may be appointed this Present Sessions. And that all persons concerned may be Ordered to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor. Petrs. Shallever pray Etc
