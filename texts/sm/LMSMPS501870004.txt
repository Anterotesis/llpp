To the Rt. Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon & Appeal of William Addis of the parish of St. Martins in the Feilds in the sd County Taylor:

Sheweth That Yono Petr. on or abt. the 16th of June last past Upon Informacon of Richard Boddington and upon the Oath of One James Brookes was Convicted before Jeffery Saunders Esqr one of his Matys Justices of the peace for this County for Setting on Seven dozen and two Cloth Buttons and Six dozen and Ten Cloth Holes on a Suit of Cloth Cloathes of the sd. James Brooks contrary to the form of the Statute in that Case made & porded.

That your Petr. hath Given Notice by his Attorney or Agent of his intencon to Appeal as of this Genrll Quarter Sessions agt. the said Conviccon.

Yor. Petr therefore humbly [...] Conceiving himselfe agreived by the Said Conviccon humbly Appeals to this Honrble Court against the same and craves Oyer thereof And prays that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day for all partyes concerned to Attend and Abide the Judgmt. of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor petr. Shall ever pray Etc
