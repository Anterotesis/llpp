To the Right Worship full his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Theydon Boyse in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Dukinfield Esqr and John Tall Esqrs Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (where of one was of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of March in the Year of Our Lord One thousand seven hundred and forty six Sarah Mallory (called in the said Warrant or Order Daughter of Phillips Mallory deceased) was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Hamlett of Ratcliff in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Middlesex into the said Parish of Theyden Boyes as the last legal place of Settlement of the said Sarah Mallory

Your Petitioners there fore therefore finding themselves aggrived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court will appoint a day in this present Session for the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliff aforesaid and all persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Wm Chapman Agent for the Appellants
