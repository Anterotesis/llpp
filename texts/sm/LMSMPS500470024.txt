To ye Rt Honble. Sr Edward Clark Knt Lord Majes of ye City of London To ye Rt Worspll Sr S Kovill Knt Recorder of ye City of London & To ye Rest of ye Justices now Sitting att ye old Baily

The humble Peticonr of Arundale Talbott als Dickerson als Arrowsmith als Burchill. A Snsinr in Newgt.

Most Humbly Sheweth

That yor Peticonr was Comitted by Cha. Bucknall Esqr upon ye Information of one Johanna Hurford a person of a Lost Consience haveing no habitation nor Repution, who accused yor Peticonr for Clipping & diminishing ye Cmt Com: of this Realme A Crime wth yor Peticonr is all togeather Innocent off,

Therefore Most humbly Prayes this Honble bench will bee pleased to order yor Peticonr to bee tryed this Sessions or Baild & that this her Prayer may bee Recorded

And yor Peticonr Shall Pray
