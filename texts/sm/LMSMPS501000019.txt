To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Thresher

Sheweth That in July Sessions last your Petr preferr'd an Indictment against Daniell Whitlach a Bayliffe of the Mannor of Stepney for Extorting Twenty Shillings from your Petr upon his Arresting yor. Petitionr wch. Bill was found by the Grand Mry And your Petr. tooke out Five severall Bench Warrants against him before he could be taken And ye sd Witlath was Apprehended & Bound in a Recognizance before Roger Smith Esqr One of her Matys. Justices of ye Peace for this County for his Appearance att the last Sessions of the Peace held for this County But the said Whitlathe forfeited his said Recognizance & hath not yet Appeared to Answer the said Indictment

P Tho: Nash Your Petitioner therfore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that the Recognizance of the said Whitlash & his Bayle may be forthwith Estreated If your Worshipps soe thinke fitt

And your Petr shall ever pray Etc

Tho: Thresher
