To the Rights Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genall Sessions of the Peace now holden at Hicks Hall in and for the Said County

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Spittle Fields in the Parish of St Dunstan Stepney als Stebeenheath in the Said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That in and by an Order bearing Date the twenty Second Day of August last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Masters and Edward gross Esqrs: two of his Majties : Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) George Ransom and his two Children (vizt) Elizabeth aged about Nine years and Sarah Aged about three years the lawfull issue of the Said George Ransom and Elizabeth his wife ;lately deceased were passed from the Parish of St Mary white Chappell in the Said County of Middlesex, unto the Said Hamlett of Spittll Fields in the Said County as the place of their last legall Settlement, by which Said Order your Peticoners conceives themselves Aggrieved and humbly Appeals against the same, praying that your worships wil be pleased to appoint a day Sometime this present Sessions that all parties may attend this Court in Order that this Court may finally hear and determine the Same
