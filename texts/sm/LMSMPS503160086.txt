To the Rt Worpfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Wool noth in the City of London

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Seventh day of January last past Mary Pewell Widow was removed from the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the said County of Middx into the said Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth London alledging the said Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Yer. Petrs. conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the sd. Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yer. Worps there from & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear ym in the prmisses & order that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the said County may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of yer. Worps touching this Appeale.

And yer. Petr. Shall pray Etc

Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants.
