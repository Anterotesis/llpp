Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Sir James Edaile Knight and Jenkin Jones Esquire two of this Majestys [...] of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the 11th. Day of February Instant Joan Hopkins Wife of John Hophins (who is pur away and left her) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the Place of her legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners are aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the A [...] dication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships was please to appoint some Day in the Present Session for the hearing and determining this Appeal And to Order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Luke and all persons concerned do then a Hand this Court
