To the Right Worship full his Majesties Justices of the Peace assembly att the Quarter Sessions Held for the County of Middx att Hicks Hall in St. Johns Street

The Humble Petition of Peter Cresswell Keeper of the House of Correction Clerkenwell

Sheweth That your Petitioner is informed by the neglect of a Servant not attending this Court as he should have done, that your Petitioner lyes under the displeasure of this Court, he humbly Begs this Honourble. Court in their Goodness will pardon this Offence he being utterly Ignorant of his Servants neglect & for the Future he shall take such Care as the like Officer shall never be Comitted any More.

And your Petitioner shall allways pray as in Duty Bound
