To the Right Worpll. Her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the Said County

Sheweth That Elizabeth Pope widow is lately passed from the parish of St Ann within [...] Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Edward Le Neeve & William Conn Esqrs two of her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster Quor unus to the Parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the Said County as the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said Dorothy Pope

Your Peticonrs: think themselves aggrieved wth: the Judgment of the Said two Justices do appeale to this Court and humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Anne within the Liberty of Westm & all other parties concerned herein ma [...] be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & finall determination of this Court touching this Appeal

P Tyson

And yor Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc
