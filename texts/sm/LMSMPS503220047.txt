Middx To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Alhollows in Lombard Street London.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and John Preistly Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Sixth day of March last Susannah Ashing wife of John Ashing Mariner and her child named Mary Ashing aged one year & eleven Months were removed from the Parish of St. George in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of Alhollows in Lombard Street London as the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the Said Susannah Ashing and her Said Child.

That Your Petitioners find themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices of the Peace, and humbly appeal to this Court against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court to Appoint a day this Present Sessions for all Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc

Henry Smart Agent for the Appellants
