To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Dionis Back Church in the city of London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant for Order under the hands and Seals of Alexander pitfeild and Isaac Tillard Esqrs two his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx (Quorn Unus) bearing Date the fourth day of May last past Mary Sweetland Spinster aged about Two and forty years was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Midlx unto the said Parish of St Dionis Back church in the city of London as th Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petrs concerning themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do appeal to your Worpps therefrom and humbly pray your Worpps with be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch may appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall ever Pray Etc
