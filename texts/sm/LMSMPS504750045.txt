The humble petion of Your Poor Prisoner James Hughes Now Living in new Prison

Honerable Sir now my time is almost expired and I have suffered very much and was next to Loosing my Life by ilness and as I understand my feis are more than is in my power to may and my Wife and Child in dange of Perishing for want as every thing wee both had is sold and presoned and nothing Left to releive us as I have not earned one Shiling since I was Confined noe my Wife and if I am Detained Imust actually Jamish for want and all Lies in your honourable brest to sett me at Liberty to Shiwe [...] To relieve myself in so doing your Poor Petioner in duty bound will for ever pray

James Hughes
