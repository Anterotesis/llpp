To the Rt [...] Matys Justice of the Peace [...] of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Regers Hugh Hughes

Sheweth

That [...] petrs became [...] [...] Courtl this Prsent [...]

That the sd Ambrose Sweatand being [...] Custody by convience for Warrants Granted from One of her Matys Justices of the Peace Colthip

Yor. Petr. therefore must humbly pray your Worpps to be pleased to Grant that they may be Discharged Crom the sd. Recognieance

P Tho Nash

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc
