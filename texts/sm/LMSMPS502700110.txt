To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of John Oakey and Anthony Chamberlain Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the 3d day of this Instant October Susanna Claris (who is Menconed in the said Warrant or order to be Aged about Twenty years & to be a Singlewoman) was Removed & Conveyed from the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Christ Church As the Last legal Place of Settlement of the said Susanna Claris

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said warrant or order of the said Two Justices Humbly appeal to this Court against the Same One Pray that this Court would Appoint Some Day at this said Present Quarter Sessions for the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlett of Bethnall Green & all other Parties Concerned to Attend to Hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs: shall Ever Pray etc
