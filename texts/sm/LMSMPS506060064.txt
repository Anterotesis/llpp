To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of shenley [...] in the County of Hertford.

Sheweth That by an outer under the Hands and Seals of Stephen Clarke and Bartholomew Hammond Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for there County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Late the Tenth Day of December last past Ann Dances Widow of John Dances deceased together with her Five Children namely, Mary aged Eleven years, Rebecca Aged Six Years and upwards John Aged near Five Years Francis Aged Two Years and upwards and Joseph Aged Nine Months were removed from the Parish of Saint Pancrass in the said County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of Shenley by the Disruption of his parish of Shenley Hill in the said County of Hertford as the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Dancer and of her said Five Children Mary Rebecca, John Francis and Joseph whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners thereof one humbly Pray, That a Day may be appointed the next Sessions to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray
