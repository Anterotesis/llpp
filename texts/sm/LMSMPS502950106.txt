To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chappell in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass under the hands and Seals of [...] Ralph Harwood and Joseph goddard Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum Diana Johnson Widdow with three Children (Namely) William Johnson aged about Twelve Years Alexander Johnson Aged about eight Years and Elizabeth Johnson aged about five Years the Lawfull Isue of the said Diana Johnson and John Johnson Deceased whereby they were removed from and out of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County as to the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitions thinking themselves agreived by the said Order or pass humbly Appeall against the Same and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Leonards Shoreditch aforesaid and all Other persons Concerned may Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court Touching the said Appeall And your Petitioners shall ever Pray.

October the 8th. 1733

Robert Pidgeon Attorney and Agent for the Petitioners and Appeallants abovesaid
