To the Rt. Worppll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Greenwich in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by warrant or Order, dated the second day January instant, under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones, and William Cross Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of lthe Peace for the said County of Middlesex (Whereof one is of the Quorum) Sarah Wilson aged about Six Year's and Elizabeth Wilson aged about four Years, both the Lawfull Issue of John Wilson and Ann Wilson his wife, were Removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Greenwich, in the said County of Kent, as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Sarah Wilson and Elizabeth Wilson

That your Peticonrs conceive themselves aggreived by the said order of the said two Justices, and Appeal against the same

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray a Day may be appointed this present Sessions for all parties to Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court, touching the said Appeal

And your Peticonrs shall ever Pray Etc

John Webbe John Teulons} Churchwardens
