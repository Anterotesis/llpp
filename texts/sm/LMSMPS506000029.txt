To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Clewer in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Christopher Scott and Charles Digby Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty ninth day of January last Sarah Cross Widow of William Cross Cordwainer deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Battolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Clewer as the Place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and therefore do appeal to this Court against the said Order

And the Petition do humbly pray that this Court will appoint a Day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the Matter of their said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
