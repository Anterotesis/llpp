To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties:Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in then Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poor of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and seales of Richard Newton & I Hayne Esqrs. her of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the county of Middx (quar Vnns) bearing date the fifth day of October 1720. Elizabeth Cookes Singlewoman was past on yr. Petrs. from the parish of St. Clements Danes in the sd. County of Middx as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselses aggreived by the oldr. of the sd. two Justices and Appeale to yor. Worpps there from

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Worpps to appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And yor. Pets. shall Prayer Etc
