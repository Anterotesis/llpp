To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of Saint Andrew Holborn above the barrs in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That John Sykes and Lucy his Wife and their two Children (Vizt.) Hannah aged two years & upwards and Unisus aged 13 months were by an Order or Pass [...] [...] Warrant dated the 18th. day of June Last under the hands and seals of John Milner and John Mercer Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields to the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs as the place of their Last legall Settlement

Your Peticoners humbly conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and appeal from the Judgment of the said two Justices to the Judgment of this Worship full Court and desire a day of hearing may be appointed and that all Persons concerned may be Ordered to attend and abide the determination of this Court

And Your Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc
