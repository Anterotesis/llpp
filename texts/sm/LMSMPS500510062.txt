To the Right Worshippfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace for Ye County of Middlesex

Sheweth The humble Peticonr of the Churchwardens of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in [...] this County

That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Srs. James Smith Knt: and Thomas Smith Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of this County bearing date the Twentieth day of July Last Elizabeth Combes wth: three Children (whoe had been Chargeable to Your Peticonrs:) were passed and conveyed to the Churchwarden or Overseers of the poore of the Parish of Finchley in this County (where they were last legally settled) And were delivered to the Overseer of the poor there as appeareth by Note hereunto annexed wth said Overseer doth Refuse to take care & provide for the said Elizabeth Combs & her said Children But on the contrary Sent them back to your peticonrs. where they now continue.

Dr S J Ingham Therefore Your peticonrs: Most humbly Prayes the Promises considered yor. Worpps will Order all Pties concerned to Attend here at a [...] time to shew cause why they should [...] for the said Elizabeth Combes & her children alsoe to pay the Penalty of five Pounds wth. yor Peticonrs humbly conceive this have for feited for Returning the said Elizabeth Combs & her children to your Peticonrs. wth out Appealing to this Honble. Court or Orders As to your Worpps Shall seem meet Etc

And Yor. Peticonrs: Shall pray Etc

John Framlane Will Horton} Churchwardens
