To the Hounerable Chearman and Worshipfull bench the humble pettiction of mary williams humbly Tipkbring your wise Considerations to Compasante my poor Condistow I being left In a very desolate Condistion haveing three Small Children the Eldest not Eaght years old and have been forst to Sell my bed from under them to Indever to Right my Self and I have nither friends nor money and if you are not Soe Charstable as to be Esey with me I and my poor orphans Shall perish which I hope you with all be Soe good as to Consider for my orphans Sake and I as In Duty Bound Shall Ever pray

Mary Williams
