To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removed under the hands and Seals of Banett and Geo: Stubbs Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirtieth Day of July last William Burton aged Eight Years was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of his last legal Settle- -ment Whereby Your Petitioners conceive Themselves to be Aggreived

Your Petitioners Therefore Pray that this Court will be Pleased to Appoint some Day in This Present Session for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will Pray Etc.

J: Holl Sol. for Appelts.
