To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of Richard Exall of the parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlx

Sheweth That your Petr. on Easter Monday last by a Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Fuller and William Cotesworth Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx (Quor nunc) was nominated and confirmed one of the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell for the present Year 1726.

Your Petr. is very well informed there are divers Inhabitants in the said parish who of right ought to serve as an Overseer of the Poor in the said Parish before your Petr. they having some years ago served the usually Offices which are frequently served before their being nominated Overseers and your Petr. being infirm in his Limbs and only his own hand labour to maintain his family, and having but lately served the Office of Scavenger in the said Parish thinks himself much agrieved and humbly appeals against the said Warrant of the said Justices.

Therefore your Petr. most humbly Prays this Court will be Pleased to make such Order herein for the releif of your Petr. asto this Court shall seem meet

And your Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
