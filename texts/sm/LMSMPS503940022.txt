To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace to the County of Midelesex in Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the Sd. County of Midelesex

Sheweth That Ann Standfast Singlewoman a Vagrant not having a Settlement in the said Parish of Saint Clemt. Danes was on the Seventh Day of March last taken out of the Street in Labour and delivered of a Male Bastard Child in Clemts. Lane in the Sd. Parish of Saint Clement Danes by means whereof the Inhabitants of the sd. Parish were Obliged necessarily to Expend the Sum of Two Pounds one Shilling for the Lyeing in and maintennace of the said Ann Standfast and Child And that in Pursuance of an Act of Parliment made in the Seventeenth Year of his Prest. Majesty Reign Intitled an Act to Amend and make more effecutial the Laws relating to Rogues Vagabonds other idle and disorderly Persons and to houses of Correction the Petitioners conveyed the said Ann Stand fast to one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for this County who Examined the sd. Ann Standfast and Committed her to the house of Correction Pursuant to the sd. Statute

Your Petitioners therefore Pray this Court to Order the Treasurer of the County of Middlesex to pay your Petitioners the sd. Sum of Two Pounds one [...] Shilling Expended by them as afored or such other Sum of money as this Court shall Adjudge a reasonable Satisfaction

And your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
