To the Right worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the October the County of Middlesex in [---] their Genlo. Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition And Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Reynell Barronett and William Harvest Esquire two of his are Majesties of the Peace for the Said County Dated the First day of August.1737. Bartholomew Harper Pallecia (called in the said Order or Warrant) his Wife and Bartholomew Adam George Hannah John and William (called in the Saw Order or Warrant) their Six Children were removed and conveyed in the Month of November more Last Past from the parish of Stanwell is the County of Middlesex to the Said Parish of Saint Olave Southwark as the Plece of the Last legall Settlement of the said Bartholomew Harper

Year Petitioners finding themselves Aggrieved the Said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same And pray this Court would appoint a day at this present Session for the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Stauwell and all Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Indgement and Determination of this Contiune touching the Said Appeal

And Yor Petitioners as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray etc

Ralph Hilditch Church Wardens Christ: Smith: Overseer Samul: Gregory Overseers
