July 31st: 1716 Present John Tiller Thomas Rand Esqrs

Reduced from £8: to £7 pr ann and to nothing for prsonall

To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Bradshaw of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That your peticoner is rated in the poors Rate of the sd. Parish for the Sum of One Hundred pounds Stock and alsoe over Rated as to his Rent Whereby he is much Aggrieved and Appeals against the same.

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays such Relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought meete

And Yor. Petr Shall Ever pray etc.

Wm Bradshaw.
