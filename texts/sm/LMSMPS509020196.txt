To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of that Part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which her above the bars in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Aaron Graham and William Bleamire Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) hearing date the 28th day of October Instant Grace Keen Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the sate County to the said Part of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and request that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre may be ordered to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners Etc
