To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Saunders Welch and John Fielding Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirtyeth day of April one thousand seven hundred and Sixty Elizabeth Shergold Widow of Robert Shergold Deceased and her three Children namely John Aged about four years James Aged about two Years and Elizabeth Aged about two months were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Dunston in the East London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do Humbly Appeal against the same And Pray that this Court will be Pleased to Appiont a day at this Present session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke and all other Persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court Touching the said Appeal.

Fras. Salt Attry for Appellants
