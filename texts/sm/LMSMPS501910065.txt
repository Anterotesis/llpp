To the Right Worshipll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Michael Royall London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a warrant under the hands and Seals of Mathew Hewitt and Richard Browne Esqrs Two of his Maties Justices of the peace within [---] the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the Eight day of November last William Mansey Katherine his Wife with their [...] three Children Vizt. William about Seven Years old Sarah about Five and Hester about One Year and an half Old were removed from the parish of St: Sepulchers in the County of Middx: to the said parish of St: Michael Royall in London alledging the said parish to be the place of the last Legall Setlement of them the said William Mansey Katherine his Wife so Called and her three Children

Your Peticoner Conceiveing themselves agreived by the said Warrant of the two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and pray this Court would appoint a Day in this present Quarter Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of St: Sepulchers and all other parties Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide by the Determinacon of Your Worships touching the said appeale

And Your Peticoners Shall ever Pray
