To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex[---] in their General Sessions of the peace for the said County [---] assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Cranley Thomas Herby and Henry James Pye Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County [---] (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th.. day of August last Mary Gill Widow of William Gill deceased with Charles her Child (by her said late husband) were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster aforesaid to the Parish of Christ Church aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be reserved to them and that a day may be appointed in the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County for the hearing and determining the said appeal and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Margaret Westminster aforesaid be then and there required to attend to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the Court Touching the said appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc Dacie & Stands Solr.. for the Appellants
