To the Rt. Worpl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Halborn above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Mary Haynes Wife of Richard Haynes and her Son Thomas aged about Eight Months were by Order of Pass Warrant Dated the Twenty Ninth Day of November last past under the Hands and Seals of Richard Newton and Thomas Lane Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum) removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Halborn above the Bars as the place of their last Legal Settlement

Yor. Petitioners humbly conceive themselves aggriev'd thereby and Appeal from the Judgment of the said too Justices to the Judgment of this Court; And desire to be heard this present Sessions: And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Clement Danes and all Persons concerned may be required to attend this Court, to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the same Court, touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
