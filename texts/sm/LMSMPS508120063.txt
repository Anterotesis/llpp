To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace assembed.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and seals of Sir George Booth Baronet and W Blackborow Esquire, two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th. day of January last past Mary Patchet Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Patchet whereby Your Petitioners concieve themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray relief in the premises and that the Benefit of this their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Cox Soll for Appellts
