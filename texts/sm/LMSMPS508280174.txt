To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gregory[---] in the City of London.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a certain Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of William Tyler and James Fielding Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the fourteenth day of September one thousand seven hundred and eighty seven Mary Nerssesso and her two Children to wit Charlotte aged thirteen years and William aged nine year were removed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of saint Gregory in the City of London by the description of Saint Gregory by Saint Paul in the City of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Nerssesso and her said two Children whereby your Petitioner apprehend themselves to be aggrieved.

Chetham. Solr. for the Appellants} Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and pray that the benefit of the said appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determination thereof may be adjourned unto the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex.
