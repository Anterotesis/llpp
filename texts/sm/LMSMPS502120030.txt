To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of Robert Powney (one of the Church- wardens of the parish of St. Mary le Strand als Savoy in the sd. County Robert Nicholson William Soley Robert Sparke John Verdon William Wilson Francis Saul Charles Singleton John Morris James Fuller [...] Henry Green Andrew Parnell Robert Shelley Thomas Nicholls and John Walsh all of the same parish and divers other Inhitants

Sheweth That it hath been the custome of the said Parish time out of mind for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor there together with such Inhitants who are of the Vestry to assemble on Ascencion day in order to make a rate or assessment for the releife of the Poor in the said Parish after previous notice first given

That contrary to the custome of the said parish as a aforesaid One of the present Churchwardens with the Overseers have without notice first given as aforesaid to such Inhitants who are of the Vestry proceeded to make a rate for the releife of the Poor there for this present year in a very [...] equall as also unprecedented and illegall manner and have since the making of the same rate by false misrepresentacons obtained the confirmacon thereof by two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for this County

Therefore your Peticoners conceiving themselves aggrieved therein humbly appeale against the said Rate or Assessment as being unequall & surreptitionsly obtained and Pray that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions with the sd. Rate or in the mean time to have a Copy thereof as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticonrs. shall Pray Etc.

Robt. Powney Rob: Nicholson Will: Soley John Terdon Rob Sparke Wm Wilson Td Fuller Henry Green

Churchwarden

In Morns Andrew Parnell John Walsh Thos: Nichol Robert Shelley Fra: Santr
