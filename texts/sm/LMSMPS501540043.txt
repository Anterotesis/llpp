To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeal of Sarah Osgood of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County Widow

Sheweth That your peticoner is rated in the poors Rate of the said parish for the Sum of One Hundred & Fifty pounds stock or personall Estate.

That your peticoner having Left of the greatest part of her Busynesse humbly Conceived herselfe aggrieved and Appeals against the same.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays Your Worshipps will be pleased to order that she may be Exempted from paying any thing for Stock or personall Estate.

And Yor. Petr: shall Ever pray etc

Sarah Osgood
