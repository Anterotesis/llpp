By J Merrill

Middlesex: At the Genal Sessions of the peace of our Lord the King held at Hick's Hall in St: John Street in & for the County of Middx on Monday ye. 25th: day of August 1735 and in the ninth yeare of the reign of our Sovereign Lord George the second King of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in this County have notice of this Appeal, And that they & all persons Concern'd do attend this Court on Thursday next at nine [---] of the Clock in the forenoon to heard and abide ye: Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching ye: Appeal Within written

By the Court Walter
