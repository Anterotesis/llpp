To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Stoke Newington in the said County

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of Charles Sheppard and R. Davies Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 19th. Day of September 1783. Sarah Hawk Wife of Job Hawk who is absent from her was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Mary Stoke Newington as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing an determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend
