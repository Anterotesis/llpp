To the Right Worpll: her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Joseph Mason of ye Parish of St. Giles Cripplegt. in ye sd. County Weaver

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr is rated four shillings a quarter to ye. Poores rate & having a wife & five Children to maintain & hath lately Sustained very great losses Yor Petr: [...] being also 60 yeares of age & in a Sickly weak condicon & his said wife being lame

Yor Petr: being not able to pay the said Rate humbly Prayes to be releived therein as to your Worps. great wisdom Shall Seem meet

And Yor Petr: as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc

Joseph Mason
