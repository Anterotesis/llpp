To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The humble Appeal of John Lewis Destraz of Grevil Street in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County Baker

Sheweth That your Petitioner (as appears by a Conviction filed in this Court) was on or about the 16th. Day of January last convicted before Thomas Kipraston Esquire one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for this County of Selling and exposing to sale two Quartern Leaves of Bread defecient on Weight six Ounces and was thereupon adjudged to pay and forfeit the Sum of thirty Shillings

That your Petitioner conceiving himself greatly aggrieved by the said conviction and adjudication entered into a Recognizance to prosecute Worships in the Premises

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeals to this Court against the said Conviction and Adjudication and every part thereof and prays to be heard thereon by his Council and that such conviction may be Quashed and that he may be Ordered his reasonable Costs and charges in the Premises

And Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

David Coupland Solicitor for the said John Lewis Destraz
