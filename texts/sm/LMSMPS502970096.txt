To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for the sd. County

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th day of Decr. last Elizth. Findall Widw of Jno Findall & Wm his Child by a former wife deced & her three Children by a former husband Jno. Higgenbotham deced (Vizt. George Aged 10 yrs. Sarah Aged 8 Yeares & Jno. Aged 6 Yearee were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County aforesd. to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of their last Legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be Agrieved as to so much & such part of ye sd. Order as relates to the Settlemt. of the sd. George & Sarah two of ye Children by the former husbd. as in the sd. Order menconed your Petrs. Appeale therefrom.

Therefore your Petrs. most humbly pray that a Short day may be Appointed this Present Sessions to hear & determine the mre touching the sd. Appeale

And your Petrs shall ever pray

Ro: Chimmow Sold for the Appellts.
