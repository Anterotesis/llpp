To the Rt: Worpll his Majts Justices of the Peace at their Genll Quart Session at Hicks hall

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Martins in the Fields in the County of Midd

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seale of Thomas Sewell and Doyly Michell Esquires two of his Majt: Justices of the Peace of the said County bearing date the third day of July Jydgemt Jane Evans wife of Robert Evans and her two Children Ephraim Evans aged about fourteen Yeares and Jane Evans aged about two years the lawfull Issue of her said husband were removed from the Parish of St: Mary Motfelon ats White Chaple in the said County unto were petr said Parish of St: Martin in the Fields whereby ye Petr: humbly Concern the are wrengeld & aggrieved

Yor: Petr: therefore Appeal to yr Worpp agst:the said Order And pray ys: Worps to appoint ad to hear the same

And yor: Petr shall pray Etc
