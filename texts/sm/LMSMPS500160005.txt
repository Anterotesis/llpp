To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of James Val of the parish of St. Giles in the Feild in the said County

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. hath lived in the sd. parish and County these 20 yeares and upwards And hath not as yet been chargeable there unto But in reyard yor Petr. being very poore and aged almost 60 yeares and altogether past his labour and having a Wife and Foure Children to mainteyne And having requested the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish for releife they refuse see to doe Soe that aswell yor Petr. as his sd. Wife and Children are likely to parish Unless comisserated and releived by this Honble Court.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worpps. to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd. parish may allow him Such pencon towards the releife Etc mainley name of himselfe & family as unto yor Worpps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall over pray Etc
