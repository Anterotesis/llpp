To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Stephen Clarke Esqres. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 15th. day of March 1764 Catherine Sargeant Widow of Richard Sargeant deceased was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Anne as the Place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved And against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that this Worshippfull Court will be pleased to Appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining this Appeal And that all Partys concerned may then and there Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Edwd Searle [...] Thos: Chamberlain John Sagnier Osborn Thornton} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Anne
