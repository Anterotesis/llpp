To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of sd Philip Dyot and John Fielding Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 11th. Instant Ann Scott Widow of John Scott decd. was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. George the Martyr as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Humy. Elmes Atty. for Appelts. Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves Agggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrt. of the said Two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same & humbly pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some day in this Present General Quarter Session of the Peace for the hearing and determining of the same

And Yor. Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
