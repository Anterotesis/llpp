To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry James Pye and J Warner Esquires bearing date the twenty second day of April now last past Elizabeth Casey the Wife of Thomas Casey was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Andrew by the Wardrobe as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved by the said Order of Removal and do humbly Appeal against the same and pray that the benefit of the said appeal be saved unto them and that the hearing and determination thereof may be determined at this Session And pray such Relief in the Premises to this Honorable Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
