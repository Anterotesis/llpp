To the Worshipfull The Justices of the Peace for The County of Middelsex

The Humble Petition of John Burland Sheweth that your Petitioner being an in habitant Peace old Street In the parish of Cripplegate and payeth but four pound Six Shilling P annum Rent for the house he non liveth in and is not with [...] take Charged att five pounds p Annum to ye. Poors Rate in which error Your Petitioner Entreats your Worships to be Relieved in

John Burland

Feb 7:1715
