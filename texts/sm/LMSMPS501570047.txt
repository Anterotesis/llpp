To the Rt: Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Francis Gearey heretofore Keeper of New Prison in the said County.

Humbly Sheweth That sometime agoe there were severall Orders made by this Court to Reimburse your Petitioner the Sum of Eighty Three Pounds Three Shillings and Three Pence Moneys by him disbursed on Account of his being heretofore Keeper of the aforesaid Prison As by the sd Orders Appeares.

That your Petitioner hath Received in part of the aforesaid Sum of Â£83: 3s: 3d: the Sum of Thirty Six Pounds Six Shilling and Eight Pence Soe that there remaines Due and unpaid to your Peticoner the Sum of Honrty Nine Pounds Sixteene Shillings and Seaven Pence Which your Petitioner Stands in Need of.

Your Petitr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to Order that he may be paid the Remainning Sum of Fourty Nine Pounds Sixteen Shillings & Best Seaven Pence If yor. Worshipps soe thinke fitt

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

Fran: Gearey
