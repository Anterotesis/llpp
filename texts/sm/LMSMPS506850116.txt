To the Worshipful his Majestys. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace. holde mat Hicks's in Saint. John. Street Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & seals of W. Blackbeiew & J. Girdler Esquires. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the Ninth day of November 1778. Elizabeth Bates Singlewoman, was removed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal. Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Therefore, humbly pray this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal, And that All. Persons. Concerned may then attend And your Petitioners will pray Etc

John Barnard [...] John Joison Wm. Clarke,} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
