To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at [---] the Sessions House Clerkenwell in and for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of he Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the fourth day of October 1797 under the hands and Seals of W Bleamire and A Graham two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) John Gafford aged about 48 Years was removed and conveyed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County as and for the place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Gafford Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved,and against the said Pass Warrant or Order and Removal of the said- John Gafford Do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Pray your Worships that the benefit of

the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining the same may be adjourned untill the next General Session of the Peace to be held at Hicks's Hall in the County of Middlesex

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc

Robt: Mann John Minnett} Church Wardens of St. Margaret Westmr. in the County of Middx

William Dawes Henry Doughty} Overseers of the said Parish
