To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kington Saint Michael in the County of Wilts

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of John Adam Frederick Hesse and John Cox Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th. day of January 1773. Mary Taylor and Elizabeth Taylor Infants Vizt. the said Mary of the Age of Twelve years and the said Elizabeth of the Age of eight years Daughters of Thomas Taylor were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Paddington in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kington St. Michael in the County of Wilts by the name of Kenneton St. Michael in the said County of Wilts as the place of their last legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and humbly Appeal against the same

Therefore your Petitioners Humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day in this present Session to hear and Determine this their Appeal

And your Petitioners shall every pray Etc

Saml: Tapscott. Soll. for the appelants
