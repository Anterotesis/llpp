To the Right Worshipfull his maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Genral Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble peticon and appeal of Henry Sibley of the parish of St. John Wapping in the sd County

Sheweth That your Peticoner rents a House but of Six pounds a year and is rated Six pence a month besides Overates towards the releif of the poor of the sd Parish that Your Peticoner hath a Wife big wth Child and [...] four Small Children to maintain and is unable to pay the sd rate or any part thereof and humbly appeals agst. the Same

And prays to be releived in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoner shall ever pray and soforth [...] Henry Sibley

G Meidle Attorney
