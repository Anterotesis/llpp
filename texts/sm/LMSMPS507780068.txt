To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace as the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Peter Greene and Robert Smith Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty sixth day of January last John Lacon with his Wife Elizabeth and their four Children reign John Aged about six years Daniel Aged about five years Catherine aged about two years and Elizabeth aged about one year were removed and Conveyed from the Stepney in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of saint John of Wapping as their last legal place of Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day of this present Session to hear and determine the matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Tudman & Wegener Sollrs. for the appellts
