To the Rt: [...] Justices of the Peace [...] of Middlx

The Humble Peticon of Anthony Burkmin [...] one of the petty Constable of Soutunmms in this County

Sheweth That at Lady Day last past there was due and [...] wing to yor Petr ten pounds for afters Salary for his Less of time and Expences in passing & Court eveing Wagrants through this County.

Yor Petr therefore most humbly prays this Honble Court would be pleased to Order the paymost [...] the sd Ten pounds unto yor said Petr.

And Petr that ever pray Etc

Anthony Buckminster
