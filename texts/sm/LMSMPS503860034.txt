To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Aldenham in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Vertue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable the Lord Carpenter and Lewis Chas. Montolien Esqr Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the [...] Liberty of Westmr in the said County of Middlesex (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty sixth day of April last past Elizabeth Ingalton (the widow of Edward Ingalton) and William her Child by her said Husband aged about eight Years were removed & Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Aldenham in the said County of Hertford as the place of their last legal Settlement

The Petitioners think themselves aggreived by the said Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same & Pray Your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premisses as shall to your Worships seem meet

And the Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

G: Talmash Agent for the Appellants
