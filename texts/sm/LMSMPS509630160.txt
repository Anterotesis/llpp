To the Worshippel his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the country of Middlesx in General session of the Peace for the said on County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of this Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gregory Old Fish street London.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal or Pays Warrant in under the Stands and seals of William Kinnard and William Car [...] Esquires two of this Majesty's Justice of the Peace in and for the Country of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the nineteenth day of August One thousand seven hundred and ninety nine Sarah Westgarth was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Ealing in the County of Middlesex and delivered so your Petitioners being the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gregory Old Fish Street London (the said Justices adjudging that the Careful Settlement of the said Sarah Westgarth was in the said Parish of Saint Gregory old Fish Street London by which said Order of Removal or Pass Warrent your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against the said Order of Removal or Pass Warrent and pray such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet And that the Benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the Hearing and Determination thereof away ne adjourned untill the next General Quarter session of the Peace to be holder for the said County.
