To the Worshipful the Chairman & the Rest of the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex, in General Session assembled

The Humble Petition of Richard Armstrong

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petitioner officiated as Messenger to the Commissioners under an Act of Parliament for Building a New Sessions House for this County, from the Passing the said Act till the Building Was finished, in the Whole Four Years, or thereabouts

That, during that Time, there were a great Number of Meetings of the Commissioners, for all which your Petitioner had Summons to deliver, at different Parts of the Town, Advertisements to insert in the different Newspapers, & Likewise constantly attended at every such Meeting to do whatever Should be required of him

That when Your Petitioner undertook the said Office he understood he was to have a Salary or Gratuity for the Same, but hath not as yet received any

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays Your Worships would take the Matter into Consideration & allow such Recomponse as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioner (as in Duty bound)

Shall ever Pray, Etc.

Rd Armstrong
