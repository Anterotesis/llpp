The Peticon of Tho: Barlow To ye Right Honble ye Lord Mayor

To the Right Honble: Sr John Houblon Knt: Lord Mayor of the City of London; And to the rest of ye Justice on ye Bench at the Old Bailey.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Barlow, now Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth,

That yor Peticoner stands now chargd for a Robbery comitted on the High way near Threes Years since; And that having been but lately releasd from Imprisonmt. in France; where he endurrd very great Hardships, has not where withal to maintain himself in Prison; but must Perish for Want, unless he be speedily releasd. from this his Confinemt.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly begs, That he may be brought to his Tryal this Session, or admitted to Bail, according to the Statute in that Case made and provided; Or that he may be Inlisted in His Majesty's Service, if this honble Court shall think fit.

And. yor Peticoner shall ever pray Etc
