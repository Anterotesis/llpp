To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poor of the Parish of St. [---] Mary Magdalen in Old Fish Street London.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated the fourth Day of August last under the hands & Sealed of Felix Feast & Alexander ward [...] Esqrs. two of his Matys Justices of the peace for this County (Querunnl) Mary Gillis wife of Thomas Gillis with her Two Children Thomas Aged about two yeares & John Aged. about Tenn Months were Removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County to the said Parish of St Mary Magdalen in Old Fish Street As the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Gillis & her two Children

That yor Petrs. Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the sd two Justices & Appeal against the same

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray Your Worspps will be pleased to appoint a day in this Sessions for all partie to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touthing the sd Appeal And Yor Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc.
