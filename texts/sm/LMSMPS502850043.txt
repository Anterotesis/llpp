To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx assembled in Generall Sessions at Hicks Hall in ye sd County.

The humble peticon & appeale of ye Church wardens & Overseers of ye poor of ye parish of St. Clemt. Danes in ye County aforesd.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrent under ye hands & Seales of two of his Majestyes Justices of ye peace for ye County of Middx (Quor Unns) bearing date ye Seaventh day of July last Jno. Shaw & Elizth his wife were removed from ye parish of st. George Hanover Square [---] in this county to ye sd. parish of St. Clemt. Danes as ye place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your petrs conceive themselves to be agrieved.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worshipps wl. be pleased to appoint some day this prsent Sessions to hear & determine ye matter touching the sd. Appeale

And your petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
