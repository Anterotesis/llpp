To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeale of Michael Bland of the Parish of St Mary Islington in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitioner by virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seal's of Jacob Harvey and John Whetham Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing Date the Twenty Second day of Aprill One Thousand Seaven hundred and Twenty was Constituted one of the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Mary Islington for this present Year although your Petitioner is no Inhabitant or house Keeper in the said Parish whereby your Petitioner thinks himselfe aggreived and humbly appeals against the, said Warrant or Confirmacon

And humbly prays to be discharged from the said office of one of the Overseers of the said Parish for the Reasons above- mentioned.

And yor Petr shall Ever pray etc
