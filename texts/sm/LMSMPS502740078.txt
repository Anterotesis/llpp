To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace assembled in General Quarter Sessions held in & for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poor of the Parish of Christchurch in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Nathaniel Cramphorn & Mary his wife were by Order or Warrt: under Hands & Seales of G Bourn & H Gough Esqr: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the 22d Day of February last removed from the Parish of Enfeild in the said County of Middx to the Parish of Christchurch in the County of Surry [...] place of their last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners humbly concerne they [...] it appear to this Court that the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Nathaniel Cramphorn is not in the sd: Parish of Christchurch in the County of Surry

Your Peticoners therefore humble pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Enfeild may attend this Court for the hearing the said Appeal at such time as your Worships shall appoint.

And Your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
