To the Honorale Bench of Justices

Sheweth that wheeras Hannah Newton Widdow Hath Been an Inhabitant in Grubstreet In the parish of Criple gate without thirty years and hath paid all dutys Belong to the Same parish and payeth But Six pound ten Shill yearly But the Colectors forceth her to pay after Rate of nine pound a year which Causeth her to Ma her Appeal to this his Honourable Court for Releif and your peticioner shall ever pray

Janr: 12: 1715
