To the Worsppfull his Majties: Justicy of the [...] for the County of Midlx.

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of ye. poor if the Parish of St. Botolph without aldgate in the sd: County.

Sheweth

That by Order undr: the Hands and Seals of Robt: Kyrby & Bastwick Johnson Esqr: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the sd: County Quor. un.) bearing date the 22nd: day of December last Henry Hallom aged abt. nine years George Hallom aged. abt. Seven years Ruth Hallom aged about five years and Joseph Hallom aged about Three years all Infants the lawfull Issue of William Hallom Victualler deseed and Ruth his late Wife also deseed have been [---] removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in this County to our said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as to the place of their last Legall Settlemt.

That yr. Pet I think themselves agrieved by the sd. Ordr. & humbly Appeal to this Honorable Court agt. The same

And pray that yr. Worspps would be pleased to Ordr. that the Churchward: and Overseers of the poor of the sd. Psh of St. Paul Shadwell & all other persons concerned may attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt. & determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal

And yr: Petr: Shall Ever Pray Etc.
