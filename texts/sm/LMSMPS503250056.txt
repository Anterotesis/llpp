To the Worshipfull Justices now assembled in their Gentl. Sessions of the peace at Hick's Hall

The Humble petition of John Turner

Sheweth That your Petr. was bound Apprentice to Samuell Randall now of the Parish of St. Clements Danes in the County of Middx Clogmaker for the Term of Seven years to learn his Art & Trade of leather Clogmakeing and that your Petr. has served his said Master upwards of three years and has never been taught or Instructed by his said Master or any of his Servants in the said Business as and Apprentice ought to be but instead there of When ever your Petr. Desires his said Master to Instruct him and learn him his Trade as he ought to do he Swears and Curses at and Dam's your Petr. and often hicks and beats your Petr. in a barbarous manner without any just Provocation [---] where by your Petr. must beat Inevitably mined for want of learning his said Trade unless he can be Turned over to some Proper Master that will Instruct him there in

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays that your Worships will take his Case into Consideration and doe what to your Worships shall seem meet for yor. Petr. Releife.

John Turner
