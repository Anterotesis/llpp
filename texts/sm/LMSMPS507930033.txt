To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Orden of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W. Blackborow and Themas Cogan Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and of the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quarum) bearing date the Twenty Eighth day of September new last past Phillis Knott Widow was Removed and Concerned from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Phillis Knott Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Order and Adjudication of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will Appoint a Day for the hearing and Determination of the same Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Major Wright & Sollicitor for the Appeallants
