To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and appeale of the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Bottolph without Aldgate in yr said County

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrt: under them hands and Seals of Edward Grose and John Cooper Esqr. two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for this County (Quorum urns) bearing date yr 27th: day of June last Dorcas Fandy the wife of William Fandy with three Children (vizt:) Laboure Elderton aged about three years, Millacent Elderton aged about Seven months the Lawfull Issue of Edward Elderton deceased and of Dorcas Fandy His Widdow and relict, and William Fandy aged about Nineteen Months Son of William Fandy the second husband of the said Dorcas who was the Widdow and relict of Edward Elderton deceased were removed from and out of the precinct of st: Katherine in this County to yr said parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate as to the place of their last Legall Settlement.

Your petrs: finding themselves agreived by the Judgement of the sd two Justices humbly appeal to this Honble: Court agt: the same and pray that your would be pleased to Order the over Seers of the poor of the precinct of St: Katherine to attend thy Court son day this Quarter Sessions to heare and abide the Judgement and finall determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale

And yor: peticoner shall ever pray etc.
