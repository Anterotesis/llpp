To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden for the Said County

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church- -warden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Spittle Fields in the Said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of D'Oyly Michel and Robert Dennett Esqrs. two of his- Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the 6th. day of August last. Jane Cleer the wife of Michael Cleer was pass'd and conveyed for the Liberty of Nortonfolgate in the said County, unto the sd: Hamlett of Spittle Fields as the place of her last legall Settlement

That your Peticonrs: conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the Said two Justices, do therefore humbly Appeal against the Same praying Such Belief in the Premisses as to Your Worships Judgment Shall Seem- Just

And your Peticonrs: Etc
