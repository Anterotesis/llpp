To ye Right Worshipll ye Bench of Justices Sitting at Hixes: Hall

The humble Peticon of Elizbth: Wither

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner is a very Poore Woeman A Widdow and Almost Past her Labor being above three score yeares of Age and have A Poore Child to bring up, and Pay but fifty Shillings a Yeare rent and Now Am Seased in ye Poores booke wch: I am not Able to Pay: I Beg most humbly yt yor Worships will take itt into Consideracon yt I may be Freed and [...] shall ever be bound to Pray for yor Worships Prosperety
