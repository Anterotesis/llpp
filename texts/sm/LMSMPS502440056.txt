To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardns & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx:

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Bast. Johnson and Ed: Grose Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx: (Quor unus) bearing date the 5th: day of September last, Elizabeth Rash the Wife of Tobias Rash with her two Children (Vizt.) Ann Rash aged [...] two years and an half and John Rash aged one Yeare were removed from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the said County of Middx: to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legall Settlement, Whereby your Petrs: Conceive themselves to be Agreived

Therefore they humbly Appeale to your Worships agst. the Adjudgemt. of the said two Justices And pray your Worships will be Pleased to order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney & all Persons Concerned do Attend this Court some day in this Present Sessions to hear & Abide the Judgement & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

And yor. Petrs: shall Pray Etc
