To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Court of Middlesex at their General- Quarter Session of the Peace- assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarders and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Ann in Black Friars, London,

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex one where of being of the Quorum bearing Date the twenty ninth Day of December last Past Elizabeth Sears aged forty five Years, was removed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the- said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Ann in Black Friars, as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices, humble appeal to this Honourable Court against the same

And Pray that this Honble Court will Please to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determination of the said Appeal and to make such Order therein as shall be just.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc Rob [...] ers Sol.
