To the Right Worship full his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace now Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Chertsea in the County of Middlesex Surrey

Sheweth That by Order under the hands & Seals of John Huggins and John Highmore Esqrs. two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the 28th. day of this Instant June, whereby Sarah Butler wife of Jasper Butler was removed and conveyed from the Hamblett of Hampton wick in the Parish of Hampton upon Thames in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Chertsea as the place of [...] the said Sarah Butler legall Settlement

That your Peticonrs. conceives themselves Aggrieved by the order of the said two Justices and do therefore humbly appeal against the same praying such relief in the prmisses as to your worships Judgment shall seem just
