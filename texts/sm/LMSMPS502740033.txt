The Humble petitionr of Elizabeth Smith spouse to Thomas Smith To your worships humbly Sheweth as for takeing the Cheild away was upon noe bad desyne but going to deverte my selfe with the Cheild happend to meitt with some aquaintance and being right Could not feind my way home again and haveing gott A Lille drink more then Ordinary was the Occassione of my Not Returning back with the Cherld Soe your Worships poor distrest Petitioner humbly Begs for Gods sake to Sett me at Liberty for I thank God that I was Never in prisione before Nor noe bodil Could Ley nothing to my Charge and in Seting me at Liberty your Worships humble petitioner Shall Ever pray for you white I am

Elizabeth Smith
