To The Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

Midlx: The Humble Petition of Saml. Redford of the Parish of Feddington one of the High Constables of Stanes Division in the Hundred of Spelthorne in the Sd. County.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner hath Served the Office of High Constable for the Said Division for [---] two years and Upwards and is desirous of being Discharged from the Some and he doth return John Hatchett of the Parish of Bedfont in the Sd. County and Division Yeoman to serve the sd. Office in his room.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that he may be discharged from the sd. Office [---] and that the said John Hatchett may be appointed in his Stead

Samuel Redford

Midlx: to Wit Wr His Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the Sd. County and Division Do Approve of the above Petition and return of the said John Hatchett

P Masgrave Thomas Wood John Gibbons Nath: Hudson [...] oorbond J. Ronell.
