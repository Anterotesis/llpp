To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Hicks Hall in the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of James Fielding and G. Wrighte: Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing Date the Tenth day of February 1774 Christian Graves Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said Liberty as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same

Therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may then Attend.

Trevor [...] S: Dayrolles Js. Jones} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Saint George Hanover Square.
