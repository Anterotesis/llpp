To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ealing alias Zealing in the County of Middlesex [...]

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under [...] and Seals of John Machin and Thomas Rea Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of this Instant October Ann Newby the Widow of John Newby late of the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middlesex Labourer Deceased was removed from the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Ealing alis Zealing in the said County of Middlesex Alledging the said Parish of Ealing als Zealing to be the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to Your Worships therefrom And humbly pray that you Will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex amy appear before [...] day to be appointed to hear and Abide the Judgmt. and Determination of Your Worships touching the Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Robert Fells Samuel Wilson} Churchwardens.

Peter Thorn John Falkner} Overseers.
