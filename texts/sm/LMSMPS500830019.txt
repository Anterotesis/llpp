To the Rt. Worspll her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of the prsent Churchwardens & Overseer the Poore of the pish of Chelsey in the sd. County

Sheweth That John Driver One of the Late Churchwardens of the sd. pish (the others being Dent) did in the time of his sd. office receive Sevll Sumes of money as Churchwarden of the sd. pish for the use r Benefitt of the Poore thereof and Refuseing to come to any accompt with your Petr. for Such moneys.

P. Tho. Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that the sd. John Driver may appeare in this Honble Cort this put Sessions to shew cause why he Refuse to pay such moneys to your Petrs. as Shall made appeare he did receive in the time of his Churchwardenshipp for the sd. pish for the use of the Poore there Or which was rent by the other Late Churchwarden thereof

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

John Manfeald Robert Munden} Churchwds
