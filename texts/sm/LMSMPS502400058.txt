Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Alexr. Pitfeild and Ralph Harwood Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Twenty first day of December last past Mary Shittle Wife of John Shittle and one Child (Vizt.) Sarah aged about Six years the lawfull Issue of the said John Shittle and Mary his Wife were removed from the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx to the said parish of St. James Clerkenwell as the place of their last legall Settlemt

Your petrs. therefore humbly think themselves aggreived and appeal agt. the said Order of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will order a day of hearing and determineing thereof

And your petrs. shall ever pray Etc

To prove that Settlement of John Shittle to be in Brewhouse Yard in the parish of St. Andrew Holbourn Call } Mary Pinching
