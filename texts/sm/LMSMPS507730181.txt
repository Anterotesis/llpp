To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the new Session House on Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Olave in the Borough of Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Staples and Samuel Hawkins Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th. day of September 1783 Ann Habersham Widow of James Habersham deceased and her three Children Vizt. >James Aged about five Years, Ann Aged about three Years & a half And >William Aged about fourteen Months was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapple in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Olave as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore Humble Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their a said Order and pray That Your Worships will please to Appoint some day in this Present Session for the Hearing and determining this Appeal and to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish Saint Mary Whitechapple and all persons Concerned to Attend this Court.

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc
