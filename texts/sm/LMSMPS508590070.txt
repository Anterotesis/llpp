To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Robert Smith and Peter Greene Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the sixth day of December thousand seven hundred and Ninety Lucey the Wife of Samuel Barret was removed and conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Yours Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present Sessions to hear and determine their said Appeal

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Carter & Simpson Solr. for the Appellts.}
