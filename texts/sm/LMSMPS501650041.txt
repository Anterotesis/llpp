To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Eliza Fry and Sarah Mason

Sheweth That your Peticoner beene [...] Months in the [...] Correction at Clerkenwell at yr. complaint of Sarah Ryland for being taken in prd with for combind And this at ye Goale [...] Delivery yor Petr. were recomended to the said Pson thereto remaine till next Session [...] That yor Petr are heartily sorry for the said offence and resolve [...] Offend againe in the [...] Nature And for as much as the Complt again [...] Desire they should be [...] discharged being [...] ready to starve

Yor Petr therefore humbly pray this [...] Court will be pleased to considerate them & order their Discharge forthwith

And yor peticonr shall ever pray Etc Eliz Fry Sarah Mason
