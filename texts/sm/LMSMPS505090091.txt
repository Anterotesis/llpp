To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Parish of Hatfield in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or broker under the hands and- Seals of Joseph Keeling and Samuel Wegg Esqrs. two of his Majesties- Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of April 1762. Susan Norris Widow with her two Bastard Children (Vizt.) William Aged between five and six Years & Henry aged between two and three years were removed from the Parish of Acton in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Hatfield in the County of Hertford as the place of their last Legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against them

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that this Court will make an Order for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Acton and all parties concerned to attend this Court on Friday the 23d. of this Instant April to hear and abide the Indgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc. John Palmer} Churchwardens Overseers
