To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Session of the Peace

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and John Fielding Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of August last [---] Winifred Baker aged Fifteen Years Daughter of [...] William Baker Deceased by Winifred his wife was removed and conveyed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the Order or pass Warrant of the sd. two Justices of the Peace, appeale to this Court against the same and humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day in this Present Sessions for the hearing and Determining of the same

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton Attornery for ye Appellts.
