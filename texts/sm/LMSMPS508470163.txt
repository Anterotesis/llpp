To the Worshipful his Majesties of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their [---] Quarter Session of the Peace at the New Session House Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Staples and Samuel Hawkins Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty third day of June last Thomas Danes, Alice his Wife, and their three Children to wit Mary aged Eleven Years Thomas Aged Four Years and Elizabeth aged about Twenty one Months were removed from the Hamlet of Popular and Blackwall in the County aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden on the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Pray this Court that Your Worships will be Pleased to save the benefit of their Appeal and to adjourn the hearing and determining thereof until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex at the New Session House aforesd And that all Persons concerned may be then directed to Attend

And Your Petitioners ahall Pray Etc

Church Wardens Richd Christmas Robt Parkridge

Overseers J Lloyd Thos. Lonsdale }
