To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of David Walker and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth Day of August last Elizabeth Charker the Wife of Edward Charker was (by the Name and description of Elizabeth Charter Wife of Edward Charter a Guilder who hath deserted and left her) removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and there said Order and Pray that Your Worships will please to appoint same Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining the Said appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Pancras and all persons concerned do then attend this Court.

And Your Petitrs: shall pray Etc Thos Brooks John Dorman Thos Shetton} Churchwardens and

Robt. Jefferson} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
