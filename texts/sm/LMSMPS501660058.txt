To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Nathaniell Chandler.

Sheweth:

That your Peticoner Did Serve the Office of Constable for the years 1716: within the parish of St. Gyles in the fields in this County:

That your peticoner has expended and paid in passing of Vagrants during the time of his Said Constable ship the Sume of Six pounds five Shillings and Six pence as may appeare by the Severall Certificates of Allowance under the hands & Seales of divers of his Maties, Justices of the Peace residing within the Said parish

Your Peticoner most humbly prays your worships to Grant him an Order of this Court for reimbursing your Peticoner the Said Sume or otherwise relieve him in the promisses as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

And your Peticoner as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc
