To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon of William Sterman Thomas hyde Cornelins. harvey and William harvey of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoners Served the Office of Overseers of the poor of the said Parish for the year 1713 During the time of which said Office they Did did burse and Layout in paying the poore there of the Sum of £25 : £10 : £07 more then they Received. or their Booke amounted to.

That your Peticoners were promised payment there of by Mr Tobias Gibson the Succeeding. Church warden and other andient Inhabitants from time to time But have beene putt of with frivolous Excuses and for as much as. your Petitrs Standing in want ye the Same.

Therefore humbly Pray they may be Reimbursed their and Money As to this Court shall [...] me meete

And yor. Pets Shall over pray Etc.

Wm Sterman Tho Hyde Corneluis Harvey Wm Harvey
