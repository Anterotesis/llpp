Middx ss To the Right Worsppfull her Maties Justices of the peace assembler at the Generall Quarter sessions held of Hicks Hall in & for the Sd. County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeare of the Church War [...] & Overseers of the poor of [...] St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth Th [...] & seales of St. Master & John Jenyas two of her [...] of the peace for the Sd. County of Middx (Quoy unus) sar [...] Conoway a Single woman was removed from the pish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in the sd. County of Middx to the pish of St. Clemt. Danes on the County of Middx aforesd. alleadgeing that the sd. pish of St. Clemt. Danes was the place of the last legall settlemt. of the sd. Sarah Conoway

That yor. Petrs. doubt not to prove that the sd. Sarah Conoway hath not a legall settlemt. in the sd. pish of St. Clemt. Danes whereby they conserve themselves aggreived by the Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices & humbly appeal there from to yor Worspps

Chimnow Yor. Petrs therefore humbly pray yor worsp will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Andrews Holborne above the Barrs may attend, yor Worspps to hears abide the Judgemt. of yor worspps [...] con touching this [...] yeale

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
