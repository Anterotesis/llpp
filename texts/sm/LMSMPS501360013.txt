The humble petition of [...] Tho Burrell & Joan h [...]

to att: sat 10th

45s the post in Court

Middx Ss: Ad General [...] Session pacis Dne Regine tent & Com Midd apud Hicks Hall in St John Street in Come prd P adjorn died New Scill undecimo die Decembr Anno regin Due me Anne Die yra [...] Regine magne Britannie Etc Duodecimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Tottenham is this County have notice of this peticon, and that they doe attend [...] Court on Saturday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to answer for their contempt in not paying the sume of fourty five shillings unto the within named Thomas Burrell &Joan his wife for the arreares of their pencon, & in not provide P for & delivering unto them shifts & streets pursuant to an Order made at the Generall Session of the peace held for this Co [...] by adjornmt. on the Sixth day of September in the tenth of the reign of her present matie. Queen Ann as the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor will answer the contrary at their peril.

P Cur H [...]
