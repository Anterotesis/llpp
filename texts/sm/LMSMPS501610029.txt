To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for [...] County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Hewett. Constable of Endfield in the said County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary due to your Peticoner at Lady Day last amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds for passing & relieveing of Vagrants

That your Peticoner hath been at great charge & Expence this last Quarter in Conveying Vagrants. and stands in Need of his said moneyes

Your [...] Humbly prays he may be said. his said La [...] eters Sallary.

As to this Court shall Seeme meeter

And yor Petr: shall ever pray Etc

John Hewett
