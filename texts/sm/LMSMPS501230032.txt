To the Rt. Worspfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humbly Peticon fo the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Alhallows Barking in the City of London

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands and Seals of Alexander, Pitfield and Joseph Short Esqrs. two of her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx, bearing date the Seventh day of June last Katharine Mooris Single woman was lately passd from the parish of St. Buttolph Aldersgate in the said County to the said parish of Alhallows Barking.

That the said Katharine Morris never had any Legal Settlement in the said parish of Alhallows Barking

P Westley

Your petrs therefore humbly Appeal against the said order, and pray your Worsps. to be pleased to Appoint a day this present Sessions for Determining thereof.

And your petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Copi
