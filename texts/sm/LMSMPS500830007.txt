To [...] Mary Jas [...] of [...]

The humble Peticon of John Tilliar on the behalfe of Ricd Tilliar his Son

Sheweth That for the Fack whereof yor. Petr. sd Son Stood Indicted in the Honble Cort this pnt Sessions your Corpps were pleased to Fine him wth. And for Nonpaymt. thereof to comitt him to Newgate where be now remain

That yor. Petr sd. Son is poore Lunatick Person & is very Sarry for the Fact For which he Stood [...] bed and humbly Submitting himselfe to the [...] erty of this Honble Cort.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayed yor. Worspps to be pleased to mittigate the sd. Fine as unto your Worpps Shall been meet.

Tho. Nash

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc. John Tillear
