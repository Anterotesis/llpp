To ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Petition of John Skidder

Sheweth That about 24e. June. 1700 Your Petr. was by the then Ovseers of the Poor of the Precinct of the Artillary Ground in the Liberty of the Tower of London By and with the Consent of Two of her Matys Justices of the Peace of the sd. Tower Liberty Putt and placed an apprntice to John Hurrey of Mile End New Towne in the Parish of Stepney in this County Weaver untill your Petr. should Accomplish his full age of Four and Twenty Yeares According to the Statute in that Case made and provided.

That your Petr. hath for Want of Comon Sustey name been Ready to Perish and for Want of Apparell is almost Naked And for as much as the Circumstances of your Petrs. sd. Master Obleidging him to Confine himselfe allwayes to his house and goes, not out from thence but On Sundays.

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly Prayes your Worspps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from his sd. Apprntishipps If. your Worspps soe Hicks fitt.

And yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Etc.

John [x] Skidder his Marke
