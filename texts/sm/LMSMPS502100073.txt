To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genll: Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Andrew holborne above the Barrs in the said County

Sheweth That by as Pass warrant or order under the hands and seales of Alexander Ward and Matthew Hewitt Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the fourteenth day of March last past James Pritchard was removed and passed from the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton garden and Ely Rents within the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborne & County of Middx to the said liberty above the Barrs as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said James Pritchard

Your Peticoners finding themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and Pray that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the said liberty of Saffron hill Etc and all other Party's concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs: shall Pray Etc
