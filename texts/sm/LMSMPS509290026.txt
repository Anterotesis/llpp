To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex at the General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of William Whittington and George Foreman Two of the Inhabitants of his Majestys Royal Palace or Fortress of Tower of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Thomas Barnard Esqr and Henry Reynett D. D. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th day of April now last past Mary Ann Warner Widow of George Warner deceased was Removed from the Hamlett of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex and delivered to your petitioners as Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter ad Vincula in the Liberty of the Tower of London that being the Place of her Settlement

That your petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and Humbly Apppeal against the same

Your petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day that all parties may attend to hear and abide the Judjment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc
