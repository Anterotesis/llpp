Wapping Stepney The humble Petition and Appeale of Henry Turner 1717

Poors rate

ref to Mr. B: Johnson Mr. Raymond Mr. West Mr.Kyrby or 2 to ex & cert

Middx Ss Ad General Quarterial Session paris Din Regis tent P Com Middx apud Hicks hall in St John Street in Com prd & Adjorn die Martis Scilt vicesimo quinto Die February Anno regin Din Georgy nuns Regis Magne Britannie Etc Quarto

Itt is Ordered by this Court that itt be And itt is hereby recomended unto Bastwick Johnson Hugh Raymond Thomas West & Robert Kyrby Esqr Justices of the peace for the Said County or any two of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St John Wapping in this County And to Examin into the truth of the petr complaint And to Certify their Opinions touching the primes within menconed unto his Maties Justices of the peace att the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the Said County
