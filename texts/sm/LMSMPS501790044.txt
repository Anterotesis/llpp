To the Rt. Worshppfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County.

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing date ye 23d. day of June last. Frances Tatham Widow was removed from ye Parish of St. Clement Danes in the said County to ye. sd. Parish of St. Mary White Chapell as to the Place of the last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs think themselves agrieved by ye Judgemt. of the sd. two Justices & Humbly Appeal to this Honrble: Court agt. ye same.

And pray that yor. Worspps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Clemt: Danes and all other persons concerned to Attend this Court some Day this present Quarter Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determinaton of this Court touching ye sd. Appeale

And yr: Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc.
