The Humble Petition of Samuell Harrison

St. Leonard Shoreditch further rel

ref to Sr. J: Tillard Mr Pitfeild Mr Michel Mr Harwood or 1

Middx ss: Ad Genal Session Paris Din Regis tent P Com Middx apud Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com Prd die Lune scilt vicesimo octavo die February Anno un Din Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Duodecimo

It is Ordered by this Court that it be and it is hereby recomended unto Sr. Isaac Tillard al knt. Alexander Pitfield D'Oyly Michel and Ralph Harwood Esqr Justices of the Peace for this County or any one of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in this County And to Examine into the truth of the Peticoners complaint And to make such Order for [...] the further relief of the Peticoner and his Wife as to the said Justices or any one of them shall seem meet.

P Cur Walter
