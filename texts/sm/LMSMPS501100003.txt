To ye honrble ye Bench of Justices for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Gillingham Sheweth

That Yr Petr was fined the Sume of ten pounds for Assaulting James Lewis a Butcher That yr. petr hath made Satisfaction to ye said James Lewis pursuant to ye Order of this honrble Bench

Yr Petr therefore humbly prayes that his fine may be miti- gated as Yr honrs shall think fit And yr petr shall pray Etc.
