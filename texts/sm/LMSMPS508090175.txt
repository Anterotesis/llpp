Middlesex To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices for the said County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Clipton in the County of Oxford.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry Lambe and T. Ayliffe Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the fourthday day of the Instant April Ann Collens wife of Richard Collens and his and her five Children John aged twelve years Ann aged eleven years Richard aged seven years William aged five years and Elizabeth aged ten month were removed from the Parish of Chiswick in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Clifton in the County of Oxford as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order or pass Warrant and pray that your Worships will appoint same day in the present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chiswick and all other persons concerned to appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching this appeal.

And your Petitioners shall over pray Etc.

Robt. Lovegrove

Solicitor for the Churchwarden & Overseers of Clifton
