To the Worshipfull Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bennetts Paul's Wharfe in the City of London

Sheweth

That your petitionr hath had one Judith Billingsley & her Two Children past from St. Giles in the Fields in the Sd County of Middx by a pass under the hand and seale of John Milner and Jno: Ellis Esqrs Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx to the parish aforesd. That your petitioners Humbly Conceives themselves agreived by the said pass and appeale [---] the same

Your Petiors: Therefore humbly pray this Honable Court to appoint Tuesday next for hearing the Same

And Your petionr will for ever pray Etc.
