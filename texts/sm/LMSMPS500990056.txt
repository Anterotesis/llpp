To the Rt. Worpll her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middsx.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Christ Church London.

Sheweth That by Warrant dated the Thirtieth day of Aprill last under the hands & Sealed of John Steuart & Robert Constable Esqr two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this County of Middx (quor unus) one Hannath Chaddock widow was lately removed from the Prcinct of St. Katherines in the Said County to the Said Parish of Christ Church London as there Place of her last Settlemt.

That yor. petrs. finds themselves aggreived by the Said Order of Removall & humbly Appeale against the same And pray that this Court would please to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the said prcinct of St. Katherines & all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmet. & determinacon thereof upon & touching the Said Appeale & that yor. petrs may bee releived herein

And yor. petrs Shall ever pray Etc
