Middx: ss. To the Rt. Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Edmonton in the sd County

Sheweth That by Wart under the Hands and Seales of Bernard Halpenny and Geo: Bourne Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for this County One Sarah Baker was lately passed from the parish of Endfield in this County to the parish of Edmonton aforesd as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

That Yor. Peticonrs. finding themselves aggreived by the sd Wart. or Order of the said two Justices doe humbly appeal against the same.

And pray yor. Worships would be pleased to Order that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of Endfield aforesd may appear in this Honble: Court this present Sessions to shew Cause why the sd Sarah Baker should not be repassed to the sd parish of Endfield

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall ever pray Etc.
