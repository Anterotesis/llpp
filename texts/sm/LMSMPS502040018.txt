To yr: Right Worshippfull His Majesties Justices of ye: Peace for tor ye: County of Middx: att. a Generall Quarter Sessions held att Hix's Hall

The Humble Petition of Tho: Beckett Headborrough in ye: Hamblett of Wapping Stepney being verry Lame and Unable to Serve ye: said office Most Humbly desires to be discharged from serving ye: office
