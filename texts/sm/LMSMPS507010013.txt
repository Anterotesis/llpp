Midd ss: To the Rt: Worppfull her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The peticon of Edwd. Briggs and John Sweatbone of Arton.

Sheweth That yor: Peticonrs: have Served the Offices of petty: Constables in the sd Parish for the Span of one whole year & upwards & that there hath not bin a Court Leet held there att Easter last nor her do be yor. peticonrs: Know when there will be a Court Leet held there to elect & choose Constables there.

Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly pray yor. Worpps. to grant yor. Order to Thomas Lott & Wm. Wilkinson of Arton aforesd to be Sworne Constables in the Roome & stead of yor Peticoners.

And yor. peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
