To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of ye: Peace of ye. County of Middx

The humble petition of Nicholas Langdon of ye: Parish of St. John Wapping Etc

Sheweth That he is now Rated and charged in the Rate made this present Geare for the reliefe of ye: poor of ye: said parish the Sume of two Shillings P month, which is a Same too large for your petr: to pay by reason of his pourty, therefore he ratios reliefe of this Honble Court, to be reduced to the former Sume charged on him, being one Shilling and Six pence P month and as in duty bound Shall for ever pray Etc

Nicholas Langdon

9th. Sept: 1715.
