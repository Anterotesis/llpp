To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Stevenage in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Lister Selman and Robert Darling Esquires Two of his majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of April last past Elizabeth Corrial and Mary her Daughter was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Stevenage in the said County of Hertford as the last legal Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Corrial and Mary her Daughter Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal

And your Pettioners shall ever pray Etc.
