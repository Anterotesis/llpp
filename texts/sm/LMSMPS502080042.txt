To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace in Quarter Sessions Assembled in and for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That Anne the Widow of Benjamin Clee was by Order under the Hands & Seals of Thomas Masters & Samuel [...] Newton Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx bearing date the 13th: Day of November last removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey as the Place where the said Anne the Widow of Benjamin Clee was last legally Settled.

That your Petitioners do humbly conceive they can make it appear to your Worships that the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Anne Clee is not in the said Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey

Therefore your Petitioners humbly Appeal against the said Order and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Mary White Chappel to attend this Court for the hearing the said Appeal such day as your Worships shall think fit

January 15th. 1722

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
