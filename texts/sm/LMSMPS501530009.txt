To the Worshipfull Justices of his Majts: Pease

The humbl: Petition of Eliza: Brett

Sheweth That yor honrs. Petitioner is now Came and hath lost the use of her Right hand by the Evell and is now destiti [...] of getting her Bread and her Substance is now gone and in a miserable & poore Condition

Humbly pray's yor: honrs: that she may be ordered to the parish of St: Matri [...] in the feilds where she was last a Servant that she may [---] by the Charity of the parish have Sume Small releife wherby She may be kept from Starveing and yor: honr: Petitioner as in Duty bound

Shall Ever pray
