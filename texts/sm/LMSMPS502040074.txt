To the Right Worshipll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at the Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The Humle: Peticon and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael Royall London.

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Matthew Hewitt and Thomas Pindar Esqrs Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace within the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the 15th day of March last William Field and Elizabeth his wife with their Two Children Vizt. Grigory about Four Years and William about Three Weeks Old were removed from the Parish of St: Giles without Criplegate in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St: Michael Royall in London alledgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of them the said William Field Elizabeth his wife and their Two Children.

Your Peticoners Conceiving themselves Agreived by the said Warrant of the Two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court would appoint w Day in this Quarter Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of St: Giles and all other Parties Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide by the Determination of Your Worships Touching the said appeal

And Yor: Peticoners Shall ever Pray Etc.
