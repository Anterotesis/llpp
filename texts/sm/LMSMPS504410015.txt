To the Worshipful his Majesty's his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace held in and for the said County Assembled The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a pass or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Thomas Bennet and Richard Manley Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster (Where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third Day of May 1755 Sarah Matts & her two Children vizt. Sarah Aged upwards of five years and half and John Aged upwards of three years were Removed from the Parish of St Margaret in the said City of Westminster to the said Parish of S [...] Mary White Chapel as the Place of their last legal Settlement By which said pass or Order of the said two Justices Your petitioners & Appellants apprehend themselves Aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners & Appellants therefore humbly pray that this Court will appoint a Day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said par [...] of St. Margaret and all persons concerned to Attend this [...] Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determina [...] of this Court touching the said Appeal And that Your Petrs. Appellants may be Relieved in the Premisses as this Court [...] Shall See meet

And Your Petitioners & Appellts. Shall ever pray [...]

[...] Jno Billington Attorney for Appellts.
