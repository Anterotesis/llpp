To the Worshippfull the Bench of Justices for the County of Middx now in Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of William Thomas and William Rex

[...] Sheweth That your Petr. in April Sessions last were Bail for one Thomas Pert on an Indictment then Prferred agt. him Soon after which He the said Pert Entred himselfe Soldier in Coll Pitts's Regiment of Horse and went out of Town and hath not been Seen by yor. Petr. at any time Since but your Petrs. are credibly informed and beleive that he still belongs to the said Regiment and that the Troop in which he rides is quartered in or near Coventry in Warwickshire as by the Affidt. annext appears

That your Petr. William Thomas hath had notice that the said Recognizance Soe Entred into by yor Petr. is for feited and will [...] Estreated into the Exchequer unless care taken to stopp Proceedings this Sessions and your Petrs. being ignorant of the consequence of such affaires humbly here they shall receive such indulgence from this Court as shall be agreable to Equity and Justice

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray this Worshipfull Bench to grant your Petrs a Respitt Sine die of the said Recognizance and an Order for the releife of yor. Petrs. as to your great Judgments shall Seem meet

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
