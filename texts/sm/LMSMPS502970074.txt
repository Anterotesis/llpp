To the Rt Worpll: his Ma: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Andrew Undershaft in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seals of W. Booth & Thos: Robe Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx one of the Quorum bearing date the thirtieth day of November last past John Abchurch together wth: Elizabeth his wife & Hannah a Daughter aged about two Yeares & a half & John a son aged about one Yeare & a Quarter were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middx to the Parish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London alledging therein that the said Parish of St Andrew Undershaft is the place of their last legall settlemt.

Thos: Elmes Agent fro the Appeallants Yor Petrs being aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same & pray that your Worps: would be pleased to appoint some Day this prsent Sessions for all parties concerned to attend [...] to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
