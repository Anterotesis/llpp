To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Stevens of the Parish of St Pancras in the said County.

Sheweth That your peticoner hath applyed himselfe to the Churchwardens of the sd Parish for relief who refuses to allow him any That yor Petr: is above Ninety years & past his Labour and hath an Aged wife who as well as himselfe are ready to perish for want

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prays he may be allowed such a pention in his old Age As to yor Worsppll shall Seem Meet

And yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc.

J Stevens
