To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of John Williams and John Simmonds Petitioners of the Hundreds Elthon Spelthon and Isleworth as maimed soldiers & Mariners,

Sheweth That Your Petitioners has not received any of the Pension that this Court was pleased to give us for the loss that Wee received in Her Late Majesty's Service for the Year 1733 and 1734 By reason there has been no [---] Rate made for those two yeares past

Therefore Your Petitiioners most Humbly Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to consider our Condition And Order that We shall be Paid our Pensions in Arrears

And Your Petitioners as in Duty Bound shall ever Pray Etc

Jno: Williams John Simmonds
