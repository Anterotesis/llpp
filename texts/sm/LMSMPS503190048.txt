[...] .Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon of Joseph Downs one of the [...] Rakers of the parish of White Chappell in the said County.

Sheweth That yor Petr. was bound by Recognizance in Forty pounds before Clifford William Philips Esqr to prosecute John Lewis otherwise whitaker) in last July Sessions for a Nusance.

That Yor. Petr. was prevented prosecuting the said Lewis at th said Sessions, for which Reason your Petr. is now under the Forfeiture of his said Recognizance But as the said Lewis still continues upon his Recognizance and Proceedings agt. him not stopt thereby; Yor. Petr. has at this prsent. Sessions prferred. and found his Bill agt. the said Lewis, and has taken a proper process thereon

Yor. Petr. therefor most humbly Prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Discharge his from the forfeiture of his sd. Recognizance.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Jos: Dowm
