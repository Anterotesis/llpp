To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Present General Quarter Session of the Peace foe the Said County Assembled-

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N. Conant and John Scott. Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County ( one whereof- being of the Quorum ) bearing date 22d. October Instant. Sarah Batten Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr (by the description of the United Parishes of Saint Andrew Holborn above the bars and Saint George the Martyr) as The Place of her last legal Settlement bringing with her a Female Bastard Child named Rebecca aged about Six Weeks. which said Female Bastard Child is not mentioned in the said Order.

YOUR Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the Same and.

request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County and that all parties concerned may be then ordered to attend to abide the judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc Arthur Gliddon Sollr for Petitioner
