Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kingston upon [...] in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of under the Hands and Seals of George Reid and William Hyde Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County one whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the thirty first Day of May last Sarah Andrews was removed from the Parish of Saint MaryleBone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kingston upon Thames as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and Humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray your Worships that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint MaryleBone may appear before you at a Certain Day to be Appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc
