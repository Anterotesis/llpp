To his Majesties Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at Hickss Hall in Saint John Street in the said County at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeall of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras Soper Lane in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quorum nuns therein mentioned) [---] bearing Date the Nineteenth day of February One thousand Seven hundred and thirty two, Mary Storey Singlewoman Aged Seventeen Years was removed from the Parish of Saint George Hannover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Pancras in the said City of London as the place of her last Legall Settlement by which Your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be Pleased to Hear and determine the Matters touching this Appeall and give their such Relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall Seem Just and meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

John Cooke Agents for the Appellants
