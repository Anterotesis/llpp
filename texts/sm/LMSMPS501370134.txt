To the Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Robt: Champ

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner being an Inhabitant of the parish of St. Giles Cripplegate without is assesed To the Poores Rate att 9s:10d: Eli which by reason of great Losses and other Disapointments in Trade he iss uncapable to pay but the officers threatning [...] distrain of his Pdads for ye same

Yor peticonr Theirfore humbly pray that he may be releived as you in yor. greate Wisdom shall Think. fitt

and yor peticonr: shall Evr. pray
