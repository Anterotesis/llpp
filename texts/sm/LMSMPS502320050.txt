To the Right Worsppfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Sitting at Hicks Hall.

The Humble Peticon of and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseer of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in yr County a for sd:

Sheweth That By Warrt: or Ordr. undr. ye: Hands & Seals of Bastwick Johnson & Thomas West Esqrs: Give of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the sd: County (Quor. un) bearing date the 1st day of November last Prosperouse Wonnell was by the sd Ordr. removed from ye: Parish of St: John of wapping in the sd: County to the sd parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate [...] place of her last legal Settlement. Where by ye petrs. humbly Conceived they are li [...] onged and Agrieved.

Your Petr: therefore [...] humbly Pray ye Worspps would be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and determining of yr: sd: Appeal of this Prsent Sessions and that yor: Petrs: may be Relieved as to ye Wisdoms shal seem meet.

And yr: Petr: shall ever pray Etc
