Peticon. of Edward Standly & Francis Charity Pillory remitted Feb 1690/1

To the Right Worshipll then Maties: Justices of the Peace sitting at Hickes Hall for the County of Middx

The Humble Petitions of Edward Standly and Francis Charity belonging to Major Fox Company in the Regiment of the Right Honorable Colonell Robert Hodges

Sheweth That yor Petrs: were never Shame Press Masters. till this time and Young men not considering the [...] they were doeing but now are very Sensible of the Same and heartely by Sorry for what they have done and doe Solemnly promise never to committ the like offence and are ashamed to being their Parents & themselves to Such Disgrace

Yor Petrs most humble craves & beseeches this Honble: Bench that they may not Stand in the Pillery to be Exposed to merry of the Rabble & the Scandall of their friends

And yor Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
