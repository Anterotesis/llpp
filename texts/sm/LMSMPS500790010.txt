To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Prcinct of St Katherines near the Tower of London.

Sheweth

That by warrt dated the Seaventeenth day of May last under the hands & Seale of Nicholas Wolstenholme Esqr one of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County one Mary Ferguson wife of Alexander Ferguson was (pursuant to a late Act of Parliament made in the eleaventh & twelfth year of his now Maties reigne Entituled an Act for the more effectuall punishmt of Vagrants & Sending them whether by Law they ought to be Sent) removed from the Pish of Enfeild in this County to the Said Prcinct of St Katherines as the place of her legall Settlemt (where her Sd Husband is a Soldier) to be taken care of.

That the said Alexander Ferguson & his said wife never had any legall Settlemt in the said Prcinct of St Katherines as your Petrs hope to make appear to this Honble Court.

Jo: Rolfe Your petr therefore humbly appeal agt the order & prays this Cort would appoint a day this prsent Sessions for all Pties to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the said appeal.

And yor petrs Shall ever pray Etc
