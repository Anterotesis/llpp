To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Mile end New Towne in the parish of St: Dunstan Stepney in the said County.

Sheweth. That [...] by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Alexander Pitfield and Samuel Benson Esqrs. two of his Majts: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorum unus) bearing date the 23d. day of July last Edward Stone with Rebecca his wife and his two Children John aged about eighteen Months and Joseph a young Child about a Month old were removed from the parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in this County to the said hamblett of Mile End New Towne as to the place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petrs. think themselves agrieved by the said Order and therefore humbly appeale to this Honrble: Court against the same.

And pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all other persons concerned to attend this Court some day this present Generall Session to heare and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said appeale.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
