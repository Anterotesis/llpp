To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. in ye. sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of Felix Feast and Matt. Hewitt Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the said County of Middx bearing Date the One & Thirtieth [...] day of May last, Eleazer Gibbon and Mary his Wife with his four Children Thomas , William , Mary , and Eleazer was removed from and out of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlemt.

That your Petrs. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worshipps agst. the same

Therefore humbly pray your Worships will please to Order, That the Church- -Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate may attend this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Detirmination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
