To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Wales in the County of York

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands and Heals of Samuel Webb and Ralph [...] Harwood Esqrs. two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing Date the 29th. Day of November last past Robert Belt Hannah his Wife And Mary their Daughter were removed from the Hamlett of Bethnell Green in the said County of Middx. to the said Parish of Wales in the Sd. County of York as the place of their last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioner humbly Conceive themselves Agreived

Therefore They humbly Appeale to Your Worships against the Judgement of [...] of the sd. two Justices And pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some Day this present Sessions to hear and determine the Matters touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Middx ss Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Din Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com Prd die Mercur in Sep [...] P & post Festum Epiphanie Scilt undecimo die Januar Anno regin [...] Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britaunie Etc decimo tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that the Church Warden & Overseers of the poor [...] of the Hamblett of Bethanll Green in this County have Notice of this Appeale and that they & all person Concerned doe Attend this Court on Fryday next at ten of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale

Exr

P Cur Walter
