To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genl. Quarter Session

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Town of Hampton in the said County

Sheweth That by An Order bearing date the 13th. Day of October last past under the Hands & Seals of Gideon Harvey & Richard Manley Esqrs. two of His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middx One whereof being of the Quorum Francis aged Eight Years & John aged Seven Years two Children of Charles Parsons were Removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the said City & Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middx to the said Town of the Hampton as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Charles Parsons and his said two Children

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same

And Pray that this Court will Please to appoint some Day this Session to hear & Determine the same

Jas Tyton Agent [x] for ye Appellts

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
