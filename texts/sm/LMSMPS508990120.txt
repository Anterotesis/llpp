To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of July Instant James Dunning Hannah his Wife and their five Children namely Elizabeth aged upwards of Twelve Years Joseph aged near eleven Years Ann aged about Nine Years, Hannah Aged upwards of five Years and Mary aged upwards of two Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the said County of Surrey as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises And that your Worships will be pleased to respite the hearing and determing. this their Appeal until the next General Session of the Peace

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Carpenter King Arm Yard Sollr. for the Appellants
