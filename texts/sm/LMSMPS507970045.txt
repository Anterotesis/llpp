Middlesex April Sess 1785}

To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Pease for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Herbert Mayo Doctor in Divinity and William Blackmore & same return for the Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the Said County one whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the Twenty Second day of March one thousand seven hundred and Eighty five Richard Edmondson Mary his Wife and their ten Benjamin of the Age of Nine Years were Removed and Conseyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners Concerning me themselves to be Aggrieved by an the said Order and Adjudication Humbly appeal to this Court from the same and pray that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto the in that the hearing and determining there of may be adjourned untill the next General [---] Session of the peace to be held in and for the said County That all parties may be then ordered to Attend and that your petitions may [---] have Such relief in the Premises as may be then thought meet

And your Petrs will pray Etc

Jno. Skutt Sol. for appellants

Goodmans Fields 9th April 1785
