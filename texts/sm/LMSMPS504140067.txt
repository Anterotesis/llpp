To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Chamberlayne and Peter Elers Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventeenth day of August 1751 John Tallent and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Chelsea in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived and against the said Order do Appeal.

W: Croft Agent for St. Martins. Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that a Short day may be Appointed this present Session to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
