To the Worshipll. Bench of His Maties Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx, now Sitting at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon of Six Poor Neibouring Inhabitants in Red Bull Yard in Clerkenwell Parrish.

Who For Many Pears Past hane Lined Quietly and Peacebly For Many Years Past

But now Being in A Continiall Grieveance of Mary Rowbottam a Lodger in the neibourehood

Who by Virtue of her money is Continlly at Law with her neibours

We humbly Beg Your Worshippl that we may have An end put to it this Sessions in order that we may Line in Peace the Attorneys. Being Paid

And Your Petions Shall ever Pray
