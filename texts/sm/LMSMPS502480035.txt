Middx ss: To the Rt: Worsppfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genal Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon & appeal of ye: Churchwardens & Overseers of ye: poor of the Parish of St: Leonard Shore dich in the sd County.

Sheweth. That by Order or Warrant under the Hands & seal's of Henry Coleranie & James Brain Esqrs: Justices of the peace for thesd: County (Quor unr.) William Browne Ann his wife & their two Children (vizt:) Thomas aged abt: Two Yeare & John aged about One Year were removed from the parish of Tottenham High Cross to our sd: parish of St: Leonardshore ditch as to the place of their last Legall Settlemt:

Therefore yor: Petr: thinks them selves agreived by thesd: Order & Humbly appeals to this Honble Court agst: the same

And prays that yor: Worspps would be pleased to Order that the Churchwns & Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of Tottenham High Cross may Attend this Court some day this prsent sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt. and determinacon of this court touching the sd: Appeal

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
