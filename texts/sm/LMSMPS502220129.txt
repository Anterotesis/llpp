To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace as their Genal Quarter Sessions now holden for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the Said County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Ralph Harwood and D'Oyly Michel Esqrs: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the 22d day of August last, Mary Johnson the Widdo: of John Johnson was pass'd and Conveyed from the Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex unto the Said Hamlett of Bethnall Green as the place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Peticonrs. conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the Order and Judgment of the said two Justices, do therefore humbly Appeal against the Same praying Such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships Judgment Shall Seem just
