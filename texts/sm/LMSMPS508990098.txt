To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the bars in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of George Mercer Junr. and I Mercer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of June last Mary Ann aged Eight Years the illegitimate Child of Elizabeth Randall Singlewoman was removed and conveyed with the said Elizabeth Randall (for Nurture) from and out of the parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of Her last legal Settlement

Your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and pray that you will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that you will Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Mary le bone to appear before you to abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc

Arthur Gliddon Sollr for the Applts
