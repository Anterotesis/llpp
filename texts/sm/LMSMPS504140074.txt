To The Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peaces assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of her in the County of Buckingham

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Kemoveal bearing date the Tenth day of July in the year of Our Lord One thousand seven hundred and Fifty one under the hands and Seals of Hammond Crosser Esquire, and Jacob Harvey Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the peace of the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) John Dewberry as therein in called and named Elizabeth therein mentioned to be his wife and William aged about fourteen years Hannah Aged about eleven years and Jane aged about eight years therein mentioned to be their three children were Removed from the Parish of Hornesey in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Iver in the said County of Barks as the Place of their last Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order of Removed and Humbly appeal to this Court against the same And Pray your Worships to take the premisses in to Consideration And to Appoint a Day for the Hearing and determining the said appeal And that all, Partys Concerned may then attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Jabez Holdar ajent for Iver
