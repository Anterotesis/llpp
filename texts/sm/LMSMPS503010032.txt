To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace of the County aforesaid assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Saithes London

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex & (whereof one of the Quoram) bearing date the 22d. day of May last past Instant Benjamen Jackson with Mary his wife and Two Children (namely) Ann Jackson aged. about three Years and Benjamen Jackson aged about One year wee passed From the parish os St. Leonard Shereditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of St. Haithsas their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioner humbly Conceiveing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgement of the said thee Justices appeal to this Court for Relieve in the premisses and your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
