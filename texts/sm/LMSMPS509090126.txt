To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Marylebone in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Handsand Seals of Edmund Pepys and Samuel Foyster Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County bearing date the 9th. day of February 1795 Ann Watkins Wife of George Watkins (gone from her) ws removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Marylebone as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the Said George and Ann Watkins Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore do appeal of this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Humbly pray that your Worships will hear them in the Premises touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners will Worship Etc Thos. Martin Sol. for Appellts. No.4 Newman Street
