To the Rt. Worpll Her Mats Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Martin Constable of South mims in this County

Sheweth

[...] Where is due to your peticoner the Sum of Tenn Pounds for one Quarter Sallary for passing of Vagarants from Lady day Last past till Midds and Last That your Petioner is in great want of money to discharge Severall persons to whom hee Stands Indebted

And therefore most humbly prays an Order for the said Sum of Ten Pounds to bee paid him

And yor petr shall ever pray
