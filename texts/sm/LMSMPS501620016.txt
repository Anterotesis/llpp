To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon [...] of Alexander Killgore Bray Lane William Collier Thomas Bastin Thomas Gibson and To [...] Birch on behalf of themselves and the rest of the Inhabitants of that Liberty of St John of Jerusalem in the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said Countys

Sheweth That in Easter Weeke last your Petitioners with severall other antient Inhabitants of the said Liberty according to the Custome thereof Did Elect Nominate and choose Mr Stephen Sedgwick an Inhabitant therein to Serve the Office of Headborough for the said Liberty for the year Ensuing And on last Ascention Day Presented the said Stephen Sedgwick to the Lords of the Manner of St John of Jerusalem aforesaid. to be sworne in order to Execute the aforesaid Office But open such Presentment the Stewards of the aforesaid Lords of the Mannor Ctho' without shewing any Manner of cause to your Petit: and the rest of the Inhabitants) Refused to swear the said Sedgwick but Discharged him from serving the same Whereby Your Petitrs: humbly conceive themselves much aggrieved And for as much as. there being noe other person chosen to Execute the said Office which will tend much to the Detriment and prejudice of the said Inhabitants

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray yor Worshipps will be pleased to order the said Stephen Sedgwick to attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew a just cause why he should not Execute the aforesaid [...] Legally chosen And that yor Petitioners may be: relieved As to this Court shall seeme Meete

And Yor. Petrs: shall Ever pray Etc

Tho Bastin Constable Bray Lane
