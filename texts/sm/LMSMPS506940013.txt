To the Right Worpll: set Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of William Page of Golding Lane Liberty in the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Laborer.

Sheweth. That your Peticoner pays near twenty Shillings per Annum to the Poors Book of the Said Parish but is now reduced to Soe great Poverty that he is not able to pay the Same haveing five Childen, and an industrious Laborious wife tho not able to bear up, under soe great a charge, neither hath your Peticoner had three Days work this twelve moneth being almost blind neither would your Peticoner complaine if not under soe great necessity

Therfore humbly prays that this Honble Court, the Pruces considered will pleace to discharge your Peticoner from paying to the Sd Rate upon hearing the Church Wardens and other ancurt [...] Inhabitmts. of the Sd Parish at Such time as Yor Worpps. Shall Pleane to appoint

And your Peticoner as bound in Duty Shall our pray Etc.

William Page
