To the Right Worspll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens of the [...] parish of 5o. Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Order of this Court made at the Genrall Quarter Sessions of the peace held for this County by Adjournment On Tuesday the Eighteenth day of Aprill last A Rate not exceeding three halfe pence in the pounds was made for repaireing the highwayes in the said parish

That Morris Evans Jonas Powell Jonathan Thisleton John Driver William Hall & Richard Huggins Surveyors of the Highwayes in the said parish for the Year last past have collected & seced divers Sume of money of the Inhabitants there for that purrpose

That the said Morris Evans Jonas Powell Jonathan Thisleton John Drivers William Hall & Richard Higgins & every one of them have in contempt of the said Order refused to Account for the monies by them seced althe they have been genrall tunes sumoneds so to do by Order of His Maties: Justices of the Peace Inhabiting in the said parish

Your Peticoner: therefore pray that the said Morris Evans Jonas Powell Jonathan Thisleton John Drivers William Hall & Richard Huggins may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to the intent they may be compelled to give an account of the monies by them reced for the purpose aforesaid

And yor: petrer:(as in Duty bound) shall ever pray etc
