To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Johnson and Michael Downs Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said Liberty of Westiminster (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of August last Sarah Grant was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the said Liberty and County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices, and Pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining thereof, and that all Persons conerned may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever Pray

Jas Barber

Osmond Gills William Ricds
