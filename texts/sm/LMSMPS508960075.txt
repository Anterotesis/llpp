To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street in the City of London.

Sheweth Tath by Virtue of a Pass Warrant of Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Wickham Esquire and the Reverend Henry Reynett Clerk two of his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fourteenth day of November One thousand seven Hundred and Ninety three Hannah Lane the Wife of Robert Lane (lately impressed in his Majestys Service) And her Child Mary aged about Ten Months the Lawful Issue by her said Husband were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex into the said Parish of Saint Olave Hart Street as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Lane and her said Child

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Passd Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Relief is the premises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jas: Fisher Sollicitor for the Appellants
