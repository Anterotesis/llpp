To the Right Worpll her [...] Justices of the peace for this County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of John Selby of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth That yor: poor. Peticoned is rated 18s: per Quarter towardes the Releife of the poors of the sd parish And for as much ord yor Petr is a Sickly Consumtir. perrose & that by reason of his poverty he is not Alle. to pay the St Assesment he haveing nothing to maintane his Family but his Trade which he cannot fellow by reason of his Sickness.

therefore humbly prays this Honble: Court to dischargd. him from paying the Sd Assesment or otherwise as to yor; worspps shall Seem meetes

And yor Petr. Shall pray Etc Jno Selby
