To the Right Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The Hble Peticon & appeal of the Church -Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chelsea in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the Hands & Seals of In. Ellis and T. Woodcock Esqrs. two to his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Un) dat. the twenty first day of June last Mary Wood Widdow of Wm. wood deced & their two Children Vizt. John aged abt. 13 Years & William aged 10 Years or thereabouts was Remove from the Parish of St. James within the Libty. of Westmr. in the County of Middx. To the sd Parish of Chelsea in the said County of Middx as the last legal Settlemt. of ye said Mary Wood & her said two Children whereby the said Parish of Chelsea think them- selves aggreived

Your Peticonrs. therefore most Humbly prayes this Court will appoint a day for hearing the sd. appeal and that all Psons concerned my attend at ye same time

And your Peticonrs shall pray Etc.
