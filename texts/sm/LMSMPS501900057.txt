To the Right Worppll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Ely Reats wth: in the Pish of St Andrew Holbourn in this County.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the Hands & Seals of John Metcalf & Nicolas Jeffreys Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County Midds (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth Day of November last Margret Ault Widdow wth: her three Children (vizt.) Thomas aged eight Yeares William aged, Six Yeares & Sussanna aged three Yeares were Removed from the Pish of St Andrew Holbourn above Barrs to the aforesd Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents as the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

Yor Peticoners being aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the sd two Justices Humbly Appeal against the same And pray that your Worshipps would be pleased to appoint some Day this Prsent Sessions for all parties to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc.
