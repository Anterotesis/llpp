To the Right Honourable Sir William Calvert Knt. Low Mayor of the City of London and to the rest of the Kings Justices of Gaol Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Edward Wrench

Humbly Sheweth. That your Petitioner on the third day of July instant was Comitted to the said Prison of Newgate for the Offence mentioned in the Comittment and Detainer annexed

That your Petitioner is Informed that no Indictment is as yet preferred or found against your Petitioner for the supposed offence he is Committed for

Where fore your Petitioner humbly prays your Lordship and this Honble. Court That he may be tryed at the present Session upon any Indictment to be found agt him without your Lord ships or this Honble Court's Jurisdiction or in default thereof that your Petitioner may be admitted to Bail or discharged or be otherwise relieved in such manner as to your Lordship and this Honourable Court shall Seem fitting

And your Petitr. shall ever Pray Etc

Edward Wrench
