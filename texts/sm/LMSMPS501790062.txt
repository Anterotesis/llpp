To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bushey in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Alexander Ward & John Metcalfe Esqrs two of His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (Quor unus) bearing date the third day of July instant Mary the wife of Richard Perriman & Amy their daughter were passed from the parish of Finchley in the Said County of Middlesex to the Said parish of Bushey as the last place of the Legall Settlement of the Said Richard Perriman

Your Peticonrs: thinke themselves Aggrieved wth: the Judgment of the Said two Justices do Appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Finchley & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & detrmination of this Court touching this Appeal

And Your peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc
