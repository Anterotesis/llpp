To the Rt: Worspll full the Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlx at their Generall Sessions for the said County at Hick's hall Assembled.

The humble Petition of Thomas Bowley

Sheweth That yor: Peticoner on the Complaint of the Overseers of the liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlx made to the Worpllfull Justice Saunders that yor. Petr. threatned to leave his wife and children on the said Liberty Was by the said Mr. Justices Saunders comitted to the Goal of New Prison for want of Suretys where yor. Petr. has been Kept Clase Prisoner near a fortnight

That the Overseers of the said Liberty haveing appealed to yor. Worshipps in respect of yor. Petitioners wife being passed on them as the place of yor. Petrs. Settlemt. the said Appeal was yesterday alowed of and determined in favour of the said Liberty So that yor. Petrs. family can have no releife there

That yor. Petr. is freindless and must mevitably Starve unless yor. Worships Please to Discharge him but of Custody to Endeavour to Work for himselfe & family

Wherefore yor. Petr. humbly Prays yor. Worshipps That he may be discharged out of Custody without paying any Fees

And yor Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc
