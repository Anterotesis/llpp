To the Rt: Workshipfll: his Majties. Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx.

The Humble: Peticon of Benjamin Niccoll one of the petty Constables of Southmims in this County

Sheweth That at Michmas last past there was due and owing unto yor. Peticoner Twenty pounds for his Less of time and Expences in passing and Conveying Vagrants from Southmims aforesaid through this County, being for halfe a year then last past, And that yor. Petr. has been at very great Expence and Charges in P forming that Service.

Yor: Petr. therefore most Humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the payment of the sd Sume of Twenty pounds unto yor. sd Petr.

And he [...] ever pray Etc Benjamin Niccole.
