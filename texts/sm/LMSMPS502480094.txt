To the Rt. Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon & Appeal of the Church= =Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Martin Ludgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Thayer & John Troughton Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the 26th day of September last past Katherine Westbury menconed in the said Order to be the Widow of Robert Westbury daced with two Children one named James aged about four years and the other named Mary aged about Seven Months were removed & conveyed [...] on the Twenty Seventh day of September last from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above Barrs in the sd. County of Midd unto the parish of St. Martin Ludgate aforesaid in the said City of London as the Last Legal place of on Settlemt. of the said Katherine Westbury

Yor. Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved: by the said Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray this Court will appoint a day at this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St Andrew Holborn above barrs and all other parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the sd. Appeal

Jno: Chaplyn John Beckington

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc. Henry Sisson. Tho: Mosely

Wm. Southonte Wm. Holaday
