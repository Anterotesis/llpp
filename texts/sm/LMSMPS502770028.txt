To the worshipfull Bench of Justices of the County of Middx.

The Hble Petition of John Griffiss

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. is Detain'd a prisoner in New Prison for want of paying his Fees only which he is not in Anywise able to pay and being entirely Destitute of Friends and Money must suffer the Utmost Discuss Unless Yor. Worship shall think fitt to Order his Discharge by Which means he can be able to provide for himself

He therefore beggs Yor. Worships would take this his Deplorable Case under your Consideration & grant him a Discharge without paying his Fees.

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall Ever Pray Etc
