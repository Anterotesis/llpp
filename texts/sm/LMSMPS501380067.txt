To the Rt. Worpll. her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

Middx ss The humble Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Peter le Poore Broadstreete London.

Sheweth. That by an Order dated the 26th. day of March 1714 & under the hands of Stephen Hall & Thomas Frampton two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) by which Said Order One John Shaw & Sarah his Wife & theire Two Children George & Sarah were passed from the parish of St. Mary Matfellon als Whitechappell in the County of Middx to the said parish of St. Peter le Poore Broadstreete as theire legall Settlement.

That the said Churchwardens & Overseers of St. Peter le Poore most humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order And pray an Appeale to this Honoble. Bench

yor. Petrs. humbly crave this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing of your Peticoners Appeale against the said Order

And yor Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.

Middx ss Ad Genal quartial Session pacis Dne Regine tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Streete in Com prdict P adjorn die Martis Sexto Die April Anno rni Dne Anne mmc Regine Magne Britannie Etc Decimo tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Mary Matfellon als Whitechappell in this County have notice of this Appeale & that they & all other persons concerned do attend this Court on Thursday next at time of the Clock in the forenoone to heare & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

P Cur Harcourt
