To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the Court & General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green in and for the said County

The Humble Petition of Stephen George Dordoy of Bow in the parish of Saint Mary Stratford Bow in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was on the Twenty Second day of September last Summoned on the Information of one William Rudd to appear before John Staples Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County to answer the Complaint of the said William Rudd for that Your Petitioner then using the Business of a Pawnbroker old in Bow aforesaid on the Seventh day of September One Thousand seven hundred and ninety one take of one Elizabeth Jewell by Way of Pledge a Skirt and did lend there on the Sum of three Shillings of lawful Money of Great Britain and upon the Redemption of the said pledge on the seventh day of September aforesaid did take and receive of and from the said Elizabeth Jewell the Sum of one penny of lawful Money of Great Britain by Way of Profit upon a Loan of the said sum of three Shillings being an Intermediate Sum Between the Sum of two Shillings and six pence and the Sum of forty Shillings by your Petitioner then to fore made the said Sum of one Penny being more than at and after the Rate of four Pence for the Loan of twenty Shillings ley the Calendar Month including the Current Month in which such Pledge was Redeemed Contrary to the Statute &c

That your Petitioner in Consequent of such Sammons did on the twenty fourth day of September last Appear before the said Justice who thought proper to convict your Petitioner in the Penalty of five pounds

Wherefore your Petitioner conceiving himself aggrieved by the said Convictions of the said Justice humbly appeals to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the premises as to you may seem meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray

Middlesex At the General Quarter Session of the Peace of our Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex
