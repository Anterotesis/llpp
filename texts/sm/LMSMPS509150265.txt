To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of G. Mercer and J. Mercer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of Jane Instant John Gage was Removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners concerning themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices Appeal to your Worships against the same and humbly request that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of saint Mary le Bone may them be ordered to attend to abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

Arthur Cliddon Sollr. for the Appeallants
