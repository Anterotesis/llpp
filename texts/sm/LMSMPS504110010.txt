To the Worshipfull his Majtis. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Genl: Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Jno Fry

That your Petitiener in the Month of April 1750 was Elected and Sworn in Constable for the Parish of Kingsinton in the said County of Middx:

That your Petitioner's year Being Expired for some Weeks past, and having a substantial House holder to Serve in his Room

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to accepet of the said whose Name is Under Written

Sworn in Court. the 21st. day of May 1751.

Jno Newbould
