To the Rt. Worshpp full of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Gent: Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblet of Bethnal Green in ye Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in yor Sd. County

Sheweth That by Order under under the Hands & Seals of D'oyly Michel & Ralph Harwood Esqr. Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the Sd. County Quor uns bearing date the 15th: day of December last Robert Bradshaw was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shordich in the Sd: County to the Sd. Hamblet of Bethnal Green and to the place of [...] Legal Settlemt.

Yr. Petr. therefore thinks themselves agrceived by the Sd. Ordr. & humbly appeal to this Hamblet Court agt. he same

And pray that yr. Worshipps would be pleased to Ordr. that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Leonard Shordich & all other persons concerned may attend the Court some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgmt & determinacon of the Court touching ye Sd. appeal

And yor Pets. shall ever pray Etc
