To the Right Worshipfull the Chaireman And the Rest of the Worshipfull Bench of Justices att Hicks hall the Sessions held for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Martha Bettey wife of Richard Battey deced.

Sheweth That your Petitioners late husband Richard formerly an Inhabitant of the parish of St. Pauls Shadwell in the County of Middx Joyner deceased and your Petitioner being his Relief and representative and your petitioners Husband having deposited in the hands of the former Churchwardens of the said parish a very Considerable sume of mony but how much Cannott Discover and takeing the Advantage of your petitioners poverty refuse to let your petitioner know the Amount thereof

Wherefore your Petitioner under a regard of her poverty Humbly Intreats this Honble. Bench to Order that the pressent Church Wardens of the said parrish might to Compelled to Appeare before this Honbe. Court and then and their Discover upon their Oathes what Assetts or Effects as are remaineing in their Custody or power or where Use to their knowledge or beleif

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
