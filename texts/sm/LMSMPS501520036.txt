To the Rt. Wpl his Mties Justices of the Peace at Hicks's hall Assembled.

The humble Peticon & appeale of Andrew Hoatson of the Psh of St. Mary Islington

Humbly Sheweth Whereas your Peticoner is Rated after the rate of £54. P Ann to the Poors Rate in the Said Parish, and hath not during the last year nor doth not now Rent any more than £31. P Ann which your. Peticoner is ready and willing to make appear.

May it therefore please yor. Wops to take yor. Peticoners Case into Consideracon and Grant him Relief in the Sd. Prmisses

And yor. Peticoner as in Duty bound I Shall ever pray Etc.
