To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

To the humble peticon of Stephen Dippan of the pish of New Brentford in the sd County

Sheweth That yor. petr. was lately allow'd (by ordr. of the Justices in ye devision) four shillings p Month towards the Releife of the said petr. and his wife.

That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd pish have lately taken of from yor. peticoner sd pecon 2s p month So that he and wife him at. inevrtably perish Unsess allow'd Eight shillings P Month (Yor. petr. having lately broke his Are.

yor. petr. therefore prays yor. Worspps that he may be resorv'd in ye sd prmisses

And yor. shall ever pray Etc.
