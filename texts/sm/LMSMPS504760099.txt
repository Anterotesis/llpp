To the Worship his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street In and for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Wimbleton in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of G: Wrighte and Richd: Manley Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 3d: Day of Octr: 1758 Mary Winter Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Wimbleton as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved And against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the sd: Appeal may be saved unto And that the Hearing and Determination thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middx or untill such Day as the Court shall think proper to Direct or appoint.

Thos. Pepper Sollitor for Appellants
