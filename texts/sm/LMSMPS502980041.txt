To the Right Worsph: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions Assemble

The humble Petitionr Appeale of the Churchwardens & Answer of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the field in the and County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hand & Seals of Hor Jones & Rob. Sam. Esqr. two of his Maties Justice of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventeenth day of January last Katherine Lister the widdow of George Lister doced with three Children (that is to say) Elizabeth Lister aged Eight Years Katherine aged 7 Years and George Lister aged two Years were removed from & out of the Parish of St. George in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields or the place of their last legall Settlement

Dan Bolten Yor. Petr. therefore thinking themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices Appear to this Court against the same & humbly pray yor. Worsps will be pleased to appoint a day to hearing & determining the same.

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc

Midd. At the General Session of the Peace of our Lord the King held at Hicks Hall in St John Street in & to the County of Midlesex & on Monday the twenty Fifth day of February in the Seventh Year of the Regin of our Sovereign Lord George the Second by the Grace of God of Great Britain Prance & Ireland King Defender of the P Smith

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St George in this County have notice of this Petition & Appeal & that they & all persons Concerned to attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clerk in the forenoon to hear & a bide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeals.

By the Court Walter
