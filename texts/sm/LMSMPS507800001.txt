To the Worshipful Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Frans. Gray

Sheweth that your Petitioners has been regularly brought up to the Surveying Business under Mr. James Carr and has been employed by him in the Various Branches of the Business for Nine Years therefore humbly presumes to Offer himself a Candidate to be Surveyor and Supervisor for the District of St. James & St Johns Clerkenwell

We whose Names are underwritten being Surveyors and Builders do Certify that Mr. Gray we believe to be in every respect well Qualified for the above Office knowing him to have been regularly Educated for the Prosesscon of a Surveyor and Supervisor

James Carr E: Delights Wright Jn. Long
