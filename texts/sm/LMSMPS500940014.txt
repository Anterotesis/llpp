To the Right Worsppll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That Isaac Long with his wife and Four Children were by warrant under the hands and seales of the Worsppll. John Crosbie and Joseph Short Esqrs. two of her Matys Justices of the Peace for the Said County lately passed from the Parish of St. Gyles Cripplegate in the Said County to the Said parish of St. Sepulchres.

That the Said Isaac Long hath use legall Settlemt in the Said Parish of St. Sepulchres wch. yor. Petrs. are ready to make appear to this Honble Court

Yor Petr. therefore humbly appeale against the Said order of the Said two Justices and pray the Honble Court to be pleased to appoint a day this present Sessions for determining thereof

P L Westery.

And yor: Petr. (as in duty bound) Shall ever Pray Etc.
