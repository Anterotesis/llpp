To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the said County (by Adjournment) Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That Samuel Clarke of Five Fields Row in the said Parish Bricklayer and Plaisterer and Editha his Wife, and Five Children, Namely Lucy Aged 12 Years Clara 10 Years, Editha 8 Years, Harriott 4 Years, and Charles 3 Years, are become Chargeable to the said Parish.

That the said Samuel Clarke and his said Wife and Children, have not any Provision wherewith to Maintain them other then from the Labour and Employ of the said Samuel Clarke [...] Mat the said Samuel Clarke neglects to provide for his Family whereby they are become destitute of support.

That the said Editha his Wife has applied to Your Petitioners for Relief and has been relieved by them.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray this Court that the said Samuel Clarke may be compelled to Maintain his Family or that such other Order may be made in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet.

Js. Jones Wm Clarkey Jas. Goding Geo. Martin Joshua Owen} Churchwardens and Overseers
