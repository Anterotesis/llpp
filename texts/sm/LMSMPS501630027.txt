Appeale of Joseph Eastman

to att wed 10 cl

Chwden. & Ov: in Cort

The Humble Peticion and appeall of Joseph Eastman Carpenter

reduced to 9d P moth.

Middx Ss Ad genal Quarter Session [...] apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd. die Lune Scilt [...] quinto die July anno regin Din Georgy mmc Regis Magne [...] Britaunie Etc tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in this County have notice of this appeale, and that they doe attend this Court on Wednesday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the Petr. should not be eased in the Rate made for releife of the poor of the sd. Parish,

P Cur Harcourt
