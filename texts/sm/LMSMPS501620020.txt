To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary White Chappell in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Bastwick Johnson and Robert Kyrby Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 16th: day of May last Elizabeth Forrest widow and her two Children (Vizt) John and William were conveyed from the hamblett of Wapping Stepney in the parish of St: Dunstans Stepney in the said County to the said parish of St: Mary White Chappell as to the place of their last Legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners think themselves agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and humbly Appeale to this Honrble: Court against the same.

And pray that your Worshipps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the sd: Hamblett of Wapping Stepney and all other parties concerned to Attend this Court someday this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of this Court touching the said appeale.

And your Petr: shall ever pray Etc
