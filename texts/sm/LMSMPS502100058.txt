To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the Peace now holden for the sd: County

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney als Stebunheath in the Said County

Sheweth That By an Order bearing Date the Second day of March last under the Hands and Seals of Felix Feast and Thomas Pend [...] Esqrs: two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) Mary the Wife of Joseph Syer with her two Children Anne and Mary were Pass'd and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County unto the said Hamlett of Bethnall Green as the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Said Peticoners conceives themselves Aggrieved by the order of the sd: two Justices, do therefore humbly Appe [...] against the same praying Such Relief in the Premisses as in Your Worships Judgment Shall Seem just
