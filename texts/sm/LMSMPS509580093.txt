To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant and or the Hands and Seals of John Nares and [...] Rice Davies Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Thirteenth day of February instant [...] Mary Tomlins Wife of William and her three Children lawful issue namely William aged about Fourteen Years, Ann aged Twelve Years neither of know have gained any legal Settlement in then own right and Sarah aged Seven Years and upwards were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Tomlins and her said Three Children

That inasmuch as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and the Removal and Conveyance of the said Mary Tomlins and her said three Children in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
