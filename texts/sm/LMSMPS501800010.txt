To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their General Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Henry Lanone

Sheweth That one Michael L: amy having given Informacon before Conn Esqr one of his Maties: Justices of the peace for this County agt:Berard Gourdon & Peter Vieil for speaking Seditious words, Your Peticoner was desired to enterpret between them & the Justice (they being Forigners) & the sd Justice requireing the sd L: amy to find Surety's for his apearance the last Sessions to prosecute on the Sd Informacon Your Peticoner was prevailed with to become his Surety.

That the sd L: amy has with drawne himselfe & not with standing the strickt enquiry & search made by your Peticoner he had not been able to find him as appears by the Afidavit annexed.

Your Peticoner most humbly prays Your Worships [---] that in regard he became bound for the Sd L: amy at the request of the sd Justice thinking thereby to Keep him to prosecute as aforesaid to respite the sd Recognizance sine die or otherwise relieve him in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Peticoner as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Henry de Hanout
