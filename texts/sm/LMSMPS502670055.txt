To the Rt. Worshipfll. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genl. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Peticon of William Johnson now a Prisoner in the House of Correccon in the said County

Sheweth That abt. Four Yeares ago yor. Peticonr. was bound an Apprentice to Thomas Neale of the parish of St. Clements Danesin the said County Distiller for the Bruce of Seaven Yeares to learn the Art and business of Distillers and yor. Petrs. said Mar. reced with yor. Petr. Five and Forty Guineas

That dining the time of Petrs Servitude he has reced very little influence from his said Mar. in the way of his Trade but has mett with [...] owse and Severe usage from his said Mar. in Order to induce him to desert his Mars. Service and has often sinces been Comitted to the House of Correccon by this said Mars procuremt withouts any Just or reasonable Cause

That yor. Petr. was abt: a Mouth since [...] Comitted against to the said house of Correcon by his said Mar. pocurent: without any Caafe where he nown remaine

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes that yor. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day for the said Mr. Neale to attend this Court to shew Cause why yor. Petr should not be discharged from his & Apprenticeship And that yor Petrs. may be further received in ye prmisses acto yr. Worshipps shall seem meet

And yor Petr in duty bound shall pray Etc William Johnson
