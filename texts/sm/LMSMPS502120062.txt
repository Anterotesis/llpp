To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That Anne Bacon wife of John Bacon with their young Child named John aged abt. time Months were by ordr. dated the 31th day of May last undr. the hands & seales of two of his Matyes of the peace for the [...] County of Middx (Quor unns) removed from the pish of St. Giles in the feilds in the sd. County to the sd. Pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of their last legall settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly conceive themselves to be aggreived by the sd. order. of the sd. two Justices & appeale therefrom to the Judgmt. of this Cort and pray that this Cort will appoint appoint a day for Hearing Etc

And yor Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.
