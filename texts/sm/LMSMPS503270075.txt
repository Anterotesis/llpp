To The Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in Generall Sessions at Hick's Hall in and for the said County.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County.

Sheweth. That by an Order Under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County, (one of the Quorum) bearing, Date the twenty first day of October last Susannah Graham aged about twenty Eight Years Wife of John Graham and their Daughter Elizabeth Aged about two Years and Six Months were removed from the parish of Saint Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in this County to the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of their last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and do Appeale against the said Order.

John Witton Sollr. for the Appellts. Your Petitioners therefore pray that a short day may be Appointed this present Sessions to hear and Determine the Matter touching, the said Appeale.

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc.
