To the Right Worpll; his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon & Appeale of John Corker of the parish of Sd Giles without Cripplegate in the County afore said Silk frain work (witter)

Sheweth That yor: poore peticoner is Assessed two Shillings to two pence per quarter by a pound rate towards the reliefe of the poore of the St. parish haveing a wife & two small Children & she bigg with a third & to be maintained by his One hand Labour And for as much as your Petr pays but forty Shillings per Annum therefore humbly prayes to be discharged from paying the Same as to your Worpp: shall seem meet

And yor. petr. shall pray Etc

John Corker
