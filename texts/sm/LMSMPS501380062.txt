To the Rt: Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Andrew Holborn London.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or Ordr. dated the tenth day of March last past and [...] the Hands and Seals of John Metcalf Esqr. & Sr. Strencham Master two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (Qnor unus) Anne Turner widow of James Turner with two Children (to wit) John aged about Eleven years and Thomas about five years old children of the said James Turner and Anne his wife were removed from that pt of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn which is above the barrs in the said County to that part of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn which is in London as the place of last legal Settlemt. of the said James Turner.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Ordr. of the said Two Justices and humbly appeal agat. the same to this Honble Court.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to Ordr. the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the sd. Parish of St. Andrew Holborn which in above the barrs in the sd. County & all other Psons concerned to attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & deteminacon of this Court upon & touching the sd. appeales and yt. yor. Peticonrs. may have such releif herein as to yor. Wopps shall seem meet Etc

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
