To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the New Sessions House Clerkenwell in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Whitchurch in the County of Oxford.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Collins and John Bosworth Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth day of April 1785 Henry aged seven Years the Male Bastard Child of Mary Ely was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Whitchurch aforesaid as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this Session for the Hearing and Determining this Appeal And to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc: Fras: Lockey Solicitor for the Appellants

To the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex

This is to give Notice to you and every of you that we the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Whitchurch in the County of Oxford do intend at the next Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex to commence and prosecute an Appeal against an Order of Thomas Collins and John Bosworth Esqrs: two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex for and concerning the Removal of Henry aged Seven Years the Male Bastard Child of Mary Ely to our said Parish to WhitChurch, Witness our Hands this twenty first day of June One thousand seven hundred and eighty five

Benjamin Dodd Thomas Clark } Churchwardens.

Benjamin Dodd Joseph Sorall } Overseers.
