Middx ss To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genl. Qur. Sessions now Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church warden and Overseers of the Parish of St. Olave Silver Street in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and R: Midford two of his. Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unuc) bearing date the twenty thir day of Septemr. last. Ann Bray Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx to the Said Parish at St. Olave Silver Street London as the Place of her last legal Settlement By which Order of the Said two Justices Your Petrs. Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved and humbly Appeal there from

And therefore your Petrs. pray that the Church wardens & Overseers of the Said Parish of Sd. Giles in the Fields and all other Persons Concern'd may Attend this Court this present Sessions [...] Stand to and Abide the Determination of this Court touching the Meritts of this Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray. Etc.

Cha: Bignall Atter for the Appellants
