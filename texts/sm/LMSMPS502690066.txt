To the Rt: Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden for the said County at Hicks Hall.

The humble Petition of Richard Matthews, late Constable of the Parish of Uxbridge in the said County for the yeare 1723

Sheweth That your said Petitioner, in his said Office, hath Disbursed Divers Sums of Money for passing and Relieving of Vagrants, Amounting in the whole to the Sum of five pounds and Seven Shillings, and may appear by a Certificate under the hands and Seals of Robert Vyner, James Rogers, and Francis Byng Esgrs. three of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County, Dated the 9th Day of April Ano. Dni 1724: which said Sum your Petitioner is out of Pockett.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays this Court that he may be paid and Reimbursed the said Sum as to your [...] Widows shall seem Meet,

And Your Petr: shall Pray Etc

Richard Mathews
