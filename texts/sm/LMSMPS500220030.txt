To the Rt. Worppll. their Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

Sheweth The humble Peticonr of the Inhitants of the pish of St. Pancras in the County of Middx.

That yr Sd Inhitants were abt. 12 Months since Indicted for not repaireing the highwayes in the Sd pish & by reason whereof sevall Issues have been returned against the Sd Inhitants. Now for as much as the Sd Highwayes have been repaired & are now in reasonable repaired & will be Sufficiently repaired & amended if yor Worppll will be pleased for grant yor. peticonr a further time for the doeing thereof.

Yor. Petr. therefore humble pray yor. Worppl to Respite the Extecuteing the Sd Issues & to make [...] [...] other ordr. in the prmises. or to yor. Worpplfull seeme meet

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc.
