To the Right Worsps. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of the Inhabitants of that part of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell which is within the Liberty of Ishington in the said County.

Sheweth

That yor. Peticoners doe Severally Contribute and pay to the Watch within the said parish Which Watch is Constanly kept at or neare the upperend of St John Street within the Said parish Which place is remote from your Petrs. habitacons Soe that yor. Petr. have noe benefit by the said Watch.

That Severall Burnlaries & Robberies have been attempted in and neare your Petrs. Neighbourhood & they being Still in danger there of for want of a Sufficient Watch.

P S Westley Yor. Petrs therefore humbly pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may be discharged from contributing and paying to the Said Watch within the said parish and that they may have liberty and power to imploy and pay one or more Watchmen in the Said Liberty of Islington for the better Safeguard & Security of yor. Peticoners.

Edwd: Hitchcock

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Hen: Hankin Charles: Clarke

James Miles John Booth

Wid Gawton George Licoris
