To the Right worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Richmond in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands And Seals of William Pickering and Francis Burton Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (One of them being of the Quorum) for the Liberty of Westminster bearing date the Twelfth Day of October 1750 Mary Farrow the Widow of Thomas Farrow and Mary her Child by her said late Husband deced were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said Liberty of Westmr. to Richmond aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Therefore they Humbly Appeal to your Worships against the said Order and Judgment of the said Two Justices of the Peace And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto your Petitioner, And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in Next Sessions for the hearing and Determining the said appeal And Yr. Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.
