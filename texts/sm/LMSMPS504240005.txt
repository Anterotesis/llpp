To the Right Honble, the Ld. Mayor of the City of London & other His majesty's Justices, of Goal Delivery of Newgate for & County of middlesex

The humble Petition of Richard Banks a Prisoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor Petitioner stands & Committed to His Majesty's Goal of Newgate for Felony exprssed in ye Warrant, by ye name of Richard Benks otherwise Banks

Yor. petitioner most humbly prays the Honble. Court that he may be tried bailed or discharged at this present sessions according to the form of the Statute in small case made a provided.

the mark of [x] Richd. Banks
