To the Right Worpfull his Majisties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall

Sheweth The humble Petition of Nicholas Crawforth.

That your Petr. by Indre bearing date the 8th. day of October. in the Year of our Lord 1744 was Bound Apprntice to William Pridham of the Parish of Saint Becolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex Clock and Watchmaker for the Term of Seven Years

That your Petrs. said Master has often Beat and Bruised him in a Barbarous and Inhuman Manner with a Great Stick over his Head and at other times Beat him with files over his Head till they broke in his Hand

That your Petrs. said Master frequently comes Home Drunk at unseasonable. times of Night and has tore your Petr. out of his Warm Bed and Horse Whiped him in a most Barbarous manner and which he frequently does

That yr. Petrs. Master when he comes Home Drunk as aforesd. Beats your Petr. in an Unmerifull manner and often turns your Petr. out of Doors Where yr. Petr. has been either [...] obliged to sit at ye Door or Walk the Street the Remainder of the Night

Your Petr. therefore hbly prays Your Worps will please to Order the said Wm. Pridham to attend this Honble Court this present Session to Answer Your Petrs. complaint and Hear & Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the Promises and to Show Cause why your Petr. should not be discharged from his said Apprenticeship

And your Petr. shall Pray Nicholas Crowforth
