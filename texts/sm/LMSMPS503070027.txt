To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majties. his Justices of the peace for the County by Midlx in ye Sessions Assembled.

The Hble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Paul Shad well in the sd. County

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order of Removall under the hands and Seals of Robert Sax and Thomas as Jones Esqrs.. two of his Majties. Justices- of the peace for the said County bearing date the thirteenth day of January last Hannah Sanders the wife of John Sanders was- removed form the parish of Saint George in the said County to the Parish of St. Paul Shad well in the said County as- the place of her Last legall Settlement in Right of her sd. Husband

Your Peticonrs. think themselves agreived- and humbly appeals against the said Warrant or order and prays that your Worship will appoint a day this present Sessions for hearing and determineing- the same And that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the- said parish of St. Geerge and all- other persons concerned be Sumone'd to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Peticonrs. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

W: Martin Attorn for the Appel

Walter Berry and William Bennett} Church Wardens of Shadwell
