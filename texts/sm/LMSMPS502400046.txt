Middx ss To the Worshipfull This Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Generll: Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary Woolnoth London

Sheweth That by order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Pindar and Simon Michell Esqrs: two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: (Quor Unus) bearing date the 12th: of October 1726. Winifred Mason aged abts: four Years and Johanna Mason aged abts: three Years the Children of Joseph Mason who is absconded: were removed & Conveyed from the parish of St: James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx: to the said parish of St: Mary Woolnoth London

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly think themselves aggrieved and Appeal agst: the order of the said Justices and pray that yor: Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for All Party's to Attend & abide the Indgement of this Court.

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
