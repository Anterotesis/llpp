Session House Middlesex February Session 1786. To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session Assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mathew Bethnal Green in the sain County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal bearing one date the 19th. Day of January now last past under the Hands and Seals of George Booth and W. Blackborow two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlesex Charlotte Jones Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mathew Bethnal Green as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same And pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex and all other Persons concerned to attend this Honorable Court some Day this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the Premises

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray etc.

Josh, Cadogan Robl Cri [...] G [...] to or} Church Wardens &

Jonar.. Sunderson Wm Beverly} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mattw. Bethnal Green
