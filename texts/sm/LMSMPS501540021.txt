To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Cook Spinster

Sheweth That your peticoner Served Mr Samuel Pumpillions Wife As Chamber Maid near a year and Quarter at £5 per Annr Wages, And the said Mr Pumpillion Mother dyeing near Five Months Since the said Mr Pumpillion gave your Peticonr Mourning for his said Mother

That your peticoners Mistresse told your peticoner that if your peticoner did not Goe from her in A Month or two your Peticoner should have the said Mourning.

That your Peticoners Mistresse has turned away your Peticonr Without giveing your Peticoner A Months Warning which was her Agreement And retains £3..10s..0d due to your peticoner for Wages And All your Peticoners Cloaths Unlesse your Peticoner Will give Back her said Mourning Not with standing your Petr has Worn her said Mourning near 5 Months in their Servite.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays your Worshipps Willbe Pleased to Order your Peticoner her said Cloaths and Mourning together with the three Pounds tenn Shillings due to her for Wages And Also her Months Warning.

And your Peticoner Shall Pray Etc.
