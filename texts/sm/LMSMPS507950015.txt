To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for th County of a Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of West Drayton in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass warrant under the hands and Seals of Sir Sampson wright and william Addington Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County (one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty sixth day of February last Hannah Gunning (wife of James Gunning a Taylor absent from her and her male Child by her said husband named James of the age of two years and four months or thereabouts were removed and conveyed from the said Parish of Stanwell in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of West Drayton as the place of their last legal settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved in that part of the said Order which adjudges the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Gunning and James Gunning her son to be in the said parish of west Drayton.

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said order and Humbly pray relief in the premises And that your worships will be pleased to appoint some day in the present Session for the hearing this their said Apopeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray etc.

Geo. Clark, Ux bridge Sollr. for the Appellants.
