To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saith Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of MIddlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the 27th. day of December last under the Hands and Seals of D. Wilmot and John Spiller Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of MIddlesex Henry Le Vallain, James Le Vallain and Jane Le Vallain three Children (who have gained no legal Settlement on their own Right) lawful Issue of John Robert Le Vallain & of Ann his Wife who have deserted and left them destitute and unprovided for were removed and conveyed from and out of the the Parish of Saint Mary Edmonton in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the Same And pray that your Worships will be pleased to order the Churchwandens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Edmonton and all other person concerned to attend this Honourable Court some day this present Session to hear and abide the [...] Judgment and determination of the Court touching the Premises

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Ph: Ja: May Solr. for the Appellts.
