To the Right Worspll: Her Majties: Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands and Seales of two of her Majties: Justices of the peace of this County One Simon Bradley and Sarah his wife were passed from the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Christ Church in the County of Surrey as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Peticonrs: make it Appeare to this Honble: Court that the Said Simon Bradley and Sarah his wife were never legally Setted in the Sd. Parish of Christ Church And finding themselves agrieved by the said order of Removeal

Your Peticonrs: therefore most humbly pray yor. Worships to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney may Appear in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to Shew cause why the Said Simon Bradley and Sarah his wife Should not be repassed to the Said Hamlett of Poplar & Blackwall in the Parish of Stepney.

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc
