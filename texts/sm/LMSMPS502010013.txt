To the Rt: Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of John Cooper one of ye High Constables of ye Hundred of Edmonton in ye. sd County

Sheweth That yor. Petr. has duely and Faithfully served his Majty. as one of the High Constables of the sd Hundrd. for /almost/ three Years now last past

And that yor. Petr. has for sometime past and now is afflicted wth. the Stone Gravel and Gout to such Degree that he is not able to Ride Travel or otherwise attend the publick and constant Buisness of his Office any longer

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble: Court to Discharge him from the sd Office, And that one of the Psons here under named /may be very well qualifyed/ may be appointed High Constable in the room of yor. Petr.

And he shall ever Pray Etc

Edward Bridgman John Jones John Rumball George Waller Thomas Howard} of Edmonton

John Cooper
