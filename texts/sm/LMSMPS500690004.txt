Sheweth

That in some time past yor. Petr. became bound for the appearance of & me John Norton in this Honoble Court att the Session of there holden in May last dis by the Recognizance may appeared

That yor. Petr. can maked appeare that he hath all his Owned prayer cost [...] charger travelled above Three Hundred Miles to seek for the sd Norton It order to bring him to Justice but the sd. Norton connot be heard of and for as much as ye Henry Mackness the adversary promiseing to proceed [...] farthed against the sd. Norton for the Fact For which he grand, Indicted.

P Tho. Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Prayes yor. Worspp to be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd. Recognizance

The Yor. Petr. Shew Ever Pray Etc John Bevin
