To the Worpll. her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and [...] ed of John Rovier of Spittlefields markett in the Parish of [...] in the County of Midds Victualler

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Adjudged to the Father of a Male Bastard Child-born on the Body of one Barbara Ady a Single woman by the Worppll: Robert Kirby Esqr Thomas Frampton [...] Bastwick Johnson Esqrs tenre of her Maties. Justices of the peace for [...] County (Qour unus) Setting forth that the Sd Child hath been [...] chargable to the Parish of St Paul Shadwell in the Said [...]

Now your Peticonr findeth himself agreived by the Order of the Said tene Justices of the peace [...] humbly appealls from the Judgment of the Said Justices, and prays [...] heare this Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall by his Councell, in order to be releived, against the Said Judgmt at Such time as this [...] Shall think fitt

And Yor Peticonr Shall pray Etc

John Rovier
