To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace and Oyer and Terminer for the County of Midlx

The Humble Peticon of William Taylor now a Prisoner in New Prison at Clerkenwell

Sheweth That Your Peticoner was tryed and Convicted this present Sessions for a Riot and Misdemeanor in flinging Dirt and Stones at the house of Thomas Leister and breaking his Windows on the first day of August last being illmuniated on on Aut. of his Maties. happy Auossion to the Crowne

That Your Peticoner is very Sorry for being concerned in the said Riot and is resolved carefully to avoid (for the remainder of his life) giving any cause of Offence to the Government in like or any other cases

That Your Peticoner is incapable of paying a Fine of twenty pounds Sett on him (being a poor Mann) having a life and Children who must [...] Suffer in Want of Comon necessaries, if Your Peticoner is continued in Custody for Non payments of the Said Fine

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays Your Worships to Mitigate and reduce the Said Fine

And as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc

Wm. Taylor
