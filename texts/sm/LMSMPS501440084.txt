To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Joseph Whitney of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated four shillings and Six pence P month towards the releife of the Poor of the said parish for this present year And he having Susteyned great Losses in the way of his trade is not able to pay the said rate.

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps wilbe pleased to mittigate the said Assessment as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

P Swestley And yor: Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
