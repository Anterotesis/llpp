To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Margt Day of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County Widow.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated for Stock or personall Estate in the poors rate of the said Parish

That your Peticoners Stock Doth not Lye in the said Parish but is rated Else where wherefore she conceives herselfe aggrieved and Appeals against the same

Yor. Petr therefore humbly prays Such relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought meete

And Yor. Petr Shall Ever pray etc.

Mary Day.

July 31st: 1716 Present John Fuller Tho Rand and Isaac Tillard Esqr Not Releived [...]
