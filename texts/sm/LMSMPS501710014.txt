To the Rt: Worsppll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticonr & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Goldington in the County of Bedford.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated the Twenty Eighth day of June last under the hands & Seals of Robert Wallis & Butler Esqrs two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx and Liberty of Westmr: (Quor unus) Mary Watts wife of Thomas Watts was removed from the parish of St Margarett Westminster to the said parish of Goldington as the place of [...] hers last legall Settlement.

That your Petitrs: Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices & appeal against the same

That your Peticoners were Ignorant of the Sessions beginning here this Weeke and therefore did not appeal soe soone as they ought to have done.

Wherefore Your Petitrs: humbly pray this Court will be pleased to save to them the beneffitt of their said Appeal untill next Sessions And Yor Petrs. Shall Ever pray Etc

Robt. Claiton Tho: Joy } Churchwdns.

Tho: Walford John Wiffin} Overseers.
