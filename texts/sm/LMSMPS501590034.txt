To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Sampson Hodges

Sheweth

That Robert Moore now in Newprison hath Committed a Robbery in your Petr house Therefore your Petr provs your Worps to Continue him in New prison untill next Sessions by reason your Petrs evidence his out Towne & that your Petr cannot prosecute this present Sessions for that [...]

And your Petr shall ever pray Etc.
