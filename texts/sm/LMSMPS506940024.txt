To the Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Thomas Butcher of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn in the County of Middx Labourer

Sheweth, That one Alice Cook having for about the Space of two years last past lodged in yor. Petitioner's house in Purple Lane in the Said Parish did there depart this life about three weeks ago and left there a little child a Daughter of hers of about three years old without any manner of subsistance save what Yor. Peticoner out of pure Comiseration hath hitherto afforded since the death of the said Alice Cook the mother, but Yor. Peticoner being a very poor man and having a great charge of children of his own is not able any longer to maintain the said child

Yor. Peticonr. there fore humbly prayes Yor. Worships to take such order for the Relief of the Said child, & such consideration of Yor. Peticoners charges here [...] as to yor. wisdom shall Seem meet.

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
