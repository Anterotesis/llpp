To ye Worshipfull ye Justices of ye Bench how Sitting Att Hicks Hall in St. John Street

Humbly Sheweth

That yor: Petitioners Mary Williams Ann Johnson haveing A Bill of Indictment four day [...] them for An Assault upon one Mary Tomkins yor Said Petitioner Mary Williams being Every Sick in Bed as will appear by her Husband now present

Humbly Begs of yor Sand Worships to Grant yor Sand Petitioner a Repute tell next Session

And yors Petitioners in Duty Bound Shall ever pray
