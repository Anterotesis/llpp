To the Rt: Worsppful his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at there General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Norton folgate in yr: sd: County

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Midlx (Quor un.) bearing date the 5th. day of October last John Faircloth was removed from the Hamblet of Spittle fields in the sd: County to our said Liberty of Norton folgate as to the place of [...] his last Legal Settlemt.

That yr. Petrs. think themselves agrievd by the sd: Ordr: & humbly appeal to this Honble Court agt. ye same.

Your Petr. therefore most humbly pray that yr. Worspps would be pleased to Order that yr. benefit of ye: sd: Appeal may be saved unto yr: Petrs: until the next General Sessions of the peace to be held for the sd. County And that the Churchwns & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Hamblet of Spittle fieles may be Ordrd. to Attend some day the sd. sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt. & determinacon of the Court touching ye. sd. Appeal.

And yr: Petrs: shall Ever Pray Etc.
