To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary White Chappell in the said County

Sheweth That by and Order under the Hands and Seales of Samuel Jones and Edward Johnson Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the 5th: day of this Instant January one Christian Hill was removed from the Hamblett of Ratcliff in the parish of Stepney in this place of her last legall Settlement

That your petrs: think themselves agreived by the said Order and humbly appeale to this Court agt the same

And pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Ratcliff to attend this Court some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your petr: shall ever pray Etc

A true Copy
