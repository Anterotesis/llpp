To the Worppfull his Maties Justices of the peace at their Genrall Quarter Sessions of the peace at Hicks Hall in St Johns Street in and for the County of Middx now Assembled;

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of Edward Prince

Sheweth That at the Complaint of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in this County an Order or adjudicacon [---] bearing date the Eighteenth day of August last under the hands and seales of Oyly Mitchell & Ralph Harrwood Esqr Two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County (Quor nuns) whereby Your Peticoner is Adjudged to be the putative Father of a Male Bastard Child born of the body of Lucretia Wharton Widdow in the said parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch & then was and its still likely to become Chargeable thereto By wch: Order or adjudicacon of the said Two Justices your Petr. conceives himselfe to be Aggreived and humbly Appeales there from

Yor. Peticoner therefore prays your Worpps to Appoynt a day the next Quarter Sessions to be held for this County and Order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the sd parish of St Leonard Shoreditch to attend this Court and Abide the Determinacon thereof touching the merrietts of this Peticon and Appeale

And your Petr shall ever pray Etc
