To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Ricards and Ebenezer Mussell Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty first day of January last Mary Underwood Wife of John Underwood (who is gone away from her to Sea) was removed from the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnall Green in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of of the said John Underwood and Mary his Wife

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of this present Sessions That all parties may Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

James Bonner John Caterer} Churchwardens

Tho Carleton} Overseers
