To the Rt: Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of William Goodwin Constable of End field in the said County

Sheweth That there were one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr at Christmas last for passing & relieving of Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds Which Moneys your Petitr by reason of his Family stands in Need of

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to order that he may be paid the said Moneys As unto your Worspps shall seeme meet

And Yor Petitr: shall ever pray Etc Wm Goodwin
