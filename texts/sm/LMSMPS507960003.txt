To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex in their Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of West Ham in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of John Staples and William Quarrill Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the sixth day of May one thousand Seven hundred and eighty five Ann Allam Widow of Peter Allam deceased and their three Children Vizt. Ann aged about Six Years John aged about three Years and Elizabeth aged about eighteen months were Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex Settlement

Your Petition thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order Do Appeal against the same and humbly Pray that a day may be appointed to hear and determine thing said Appeal And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Parties concerned do attend this Court on Thursday next often of the Clock in the forenoon of the same day to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court to touching the said Appeal
