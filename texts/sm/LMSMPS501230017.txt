To the Worshipfull the Vestry for the Parish of St Leonards Shorditch the Petition of Elizabeth Cole widdow humbly Sheweth

That Yor Petitioner is Reduced to Extreame Poverty & Want She beeing of the Age of Sixty & Six years and is allmost [...] blind therefore yor Peti most humbly prayes that yor worships will take her deplorable Cafe into yor Indicious Consideration and afford her Yor Charitable Releife She beeing an Inhabitant in the Parish this 26 Years and Yor Petitionr as In duly bound shall Ever Pray

Elizabeth Cole
