To the Right Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Bromley in the County of Kent

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Colby jun and John Smith Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd (Quor anus) bearing date the fifth day November last past Willm Bunting late of the said Pish of Bromley a Lunatick was removed & conveyed from the parish of Kensington in the said County of Midd to the said parish of Bromley as the place of his last legal Settlement

Yoe petrs finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt or Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the Same And pray this Court would appoint a day this pe sent Quarter Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Kensington and all parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor petrs shall ever pray Etc.
