Middlesex To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled at their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County by Adjournment on Monday the twenty eighth day of October 1793.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Downham Market in the County of Norfolk

Sheweth That on the twenty second Day of June last an Order was made by Charles Sheppard Esq and the Revd. Thomas Lockwood Clerk two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex for the Removal of Marianne Coles Single woman and her Female Bastard Child namely Lucy Sarah of aged three years for Nurture from the Parish of Saint Mary lebone in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Downham Market

That on or about the sixth day of July last the said Pauper and her Female Bastard Child were removed accordingly.

That the last Place of legal Settlement of the said Pauper Marianne Coles as your Petitioners have been informed and believe

is in the Parish of Denver in the said County of Norfolk.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray the Consideration and Judgment of your Workship in the Premises and that your Petitioners may have such Relief as to your worships shall seem meet and their Case may require.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
