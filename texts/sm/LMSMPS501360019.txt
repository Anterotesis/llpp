To the right Worp [...] Bench att Hicks. [...]

The humble Petition of John Andrewes of the Parish Leonard Shore ditch.

hambly sheweth That the poor Petitir. being an ancient Parishconr. and above threescore and Seaven Years old, and nowise able to get to himselfe any mainte nance, is reduced to great poverty, So that unlesse charitably relieved by the Church wardens of the Parish aforest. he must inevitably parish, being turned out of his habitation by his Land Ld. for nonpayment of rent.

Therefore in all humillity imploreth yor. Worps. will please to order him such reliefe & comfort as to Yor. misdomes shall be thought fit.

And the Petr. shall ever prays
