The King agt. Judith Wright} upon ye prosecution of Thomas Charlesworth for perjury

To the Worshipfull the Chairman & the Rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at Hicks Hall

The Humble petition of the Deft.

Sheweth

That Your Petitioner cannot Safely go on to Tryal in the above Cause for that a very Material Wittness for the Petr. is Sick in Bed as appears by the Affidt. annexed

Therefore Your Petr. most humbly prays her Recognizances may be respeted till the next Sessions when she will go on to Tryal & well hopes to make her Innocence appeal to this Horble Court
