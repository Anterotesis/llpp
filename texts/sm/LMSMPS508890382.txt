To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hackney in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John scott and Philip Neve Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th. day of September last Sarah Turnbull Wife of Robert Turnbull alias Johnston was received and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Hackney as the place of her last Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne within the liberty of Westminster may appear before you on a Certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners Etc. Wm Nead John Adamson} Church Wardens

Wm. Arnor James Griffiths} Overseers
