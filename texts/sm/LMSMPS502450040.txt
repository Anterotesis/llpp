To the Rt. Worppfull his Matis Justices of the Peace for the County [...] Genal Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblet of Spittlefeild in the Parish of St. Dunstan stepney [...] said County

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seales of John Fuller& William Cotesworth Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd: County bearing date the 30th. day of this instant August; Joseph Wood & Ann his wife were removed from the Parish of St: James Clerkenwell in ye. sd. County to our said Hamblet of Spittlefeilds us to the Place of their [---] Legall Settlemts.

Wee therefore thinking [...] lves Agriev'd do humbley appeal to the [...] Honble Court against the [...]

And pray that yor. Worspps would be pleased to Order [...] that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish [...] other [...] may Attend this Court [...] Prsent sessions to hear & abide ye Judgemt. of the same touchg: ye: sd: Appl:

And Yor. Petr shall ever pray Etca:
