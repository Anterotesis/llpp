To the Rt. Worpll. His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Peter [...] Poor London

Sheweth. That by vertue of an Order dated the 4th. day of April 1730 Under the hands & Seals of Wm Young & Jacob Delamot Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) [---] one Mary Brown aged abt. forty years (Widow of Saml. Brown deced) [...] John her Son aged about three years were passed from the parish of St. Ann Westmr. in the County of Middx to the said parish of St. Peterle Poor London as the place of their last legall Setlement

That the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Peter le Poor humbly conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order & therefore Appeal to this Honoble. Court agt. the same,

[...] Agent & sollr for the Appellants

And humbly pray this Honoble. Court to appoint a day for hearing the said Appeal agst. the said Order at the next genall Sestions of the peace to be held for the County

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
