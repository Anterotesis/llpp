[...] Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth, That by vertue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrt. undr. the Hands and Seales of Sr. Jonathan Andrews & Stephen Hall Esqr. two of her Maties Justies of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quorunns) bearing date the 12th. Day of January instant Robert Alcock & Mary his wife & Anne their Child were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the sd. County to yor. Peticonrs. sd. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of their last legal Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Ordr. or Pacs Warrt. of the said Justices, and humbly appeale agat. the Same to this Court.

Wherefore yor. Peticonrs. humbly Pray that you would please to ordr. the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Mary White Chappell & all other persons concerned to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear the Judgmt. & determinacon of yor. Wopps upon & concerning the said Appeale.

And yor. Pedticonrs. shall Pray Etc
