To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Peter Mellish Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th day of August 1797 Mary Smith was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Hamlett of Popular and Blackwall in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court agt the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray & [...]

S. Malbon Sollr. for the Appell [...]
