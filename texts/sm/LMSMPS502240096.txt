To the Rt. Worspl. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden at Hickshall in and for the said County

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the pish, of Stepney in the said County

Sheweth That By Warrt. or Order under the Hands and seals of Bastwick Johnson and [...] Robert Kyrby Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 23th Day of December last Mary Heath the Widdow of David Heath Deced was Removed from the Parish of St. John Wapping in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe as the place of her Court legall Settlemt. whereby your petitionrs humbly Conceive they a [...] wcon gad and [...] aggrieved

Your petitioners therefore humbly Prays your Worpps would appoint a Day for the hearing and determing the said Appeal this Present Sessions, and that your Petr: may be relieved as unto your Worpps shall Seem meet [...] .

And yor: Petr: shall Ever pray Etc.
