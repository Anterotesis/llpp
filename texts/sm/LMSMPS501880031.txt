To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Francis Oliver Constable of the Liberty of Glasshouse yard within the Parish of St Botolph Aldersgate in this County.

Sheweth That there is due to your Petitr. for Conveying of Vagrants by Order of the Worsppll Justice Fuller and Justice ward the Sum of One Pound Eight Shillings As appears by the five severall Certificates here unto Annexed.

That your Petitr stands in Need of the said Money.

And Humbly prays this Court will be pleased to Order he may be paid the said Money As to yor Worshipps shall seem Meet

And yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

Fran: Oliver

Haveing Excused in to the truth of this petition find the Sumes all right being five in Number P Amounts in the wholl to 1:01:0

J Ward
