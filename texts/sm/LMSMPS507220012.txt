Middlesex To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Edgware in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by the Order under the hands and Seals of Wm. Quarrill and John Staples Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of July 1780 Sarah Clark the Wife of Nathaniel Clark was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Bromley in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Edgware as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Nathaniel Clark and Sarah the Wife whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved and against the said Order Do appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Day may be appointed in the Present Session to hear and determination the Matter taking the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Claridge Solr. for Appellts.
