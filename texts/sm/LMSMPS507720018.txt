To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in & for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands & Seals of John Sherwood Esquire and William Blackmore Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighteenth day of September last Barbara Alexander of the Age of Twelve Years or there abouts Margaret Alexander of the Age of nine Years or there abouts John Alexander of the Age of Seven Years and upwards and Elizabeth Alexander of the Age of Five Years or there abouts Children of John Alexander therein said to have deserted them were removed from the Parish of Saint George to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Barbara Alexander Margaret Alexander John Alexander and Elizabeth Alexander

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the sd. Order & do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a [...] may be appointed of this present Session to hear and determine the matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Tudman & Wegener Sollrs. for Appellants
