To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace in and for the Said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Staples Esqr. and The Revd. Herbt. Mayo [---] Dr. in Divinity two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the eighteenth Day of January last, William Debnam Eleanor his Wife and Margaret their Child were removed from the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and do appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be appointed in this present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Saml Wegener Sollicitor for Appellants
