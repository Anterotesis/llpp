To the Right Worpfull Lane Esquire Chairman at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street And To the Rest of the Worpfull the Justices of the Peace for the said County at their Sd. General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Holy Trinity London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twentyeth day of February 1733 One Hannah Edwards Widow was removed from & out of the Parish of St George in the Said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Trinity (meaning the Parish of Holy Trinity) in London as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Yor Petrs. By the said Order humbly con [...] e themselves Aggrieved therefore Appeal Against the Same And Pray a Day may be appointed this present sessions for the hearing & determining the Same And that all Persons Comerned may be ordered to Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Cot. touching the said Appeale

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
