To the Rt. Worspll: his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Generll Quarter Sessions of the peace for the said county. at Hicks hall assembled.

The Humble petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of D'Oyly Michel and Samuell Perry Esqrs. two of his Matt. Justices of the peace for the said County (Quar un) bearing Date the 12th. Day of May last Mary Green the Widdo. of Peter Green was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Matfellon as white chappell in the said county of Middx. to the Hamlett of Racliffe where by your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged and aggrieived

Your Petitioners: therefore hum [...] by appel agt. the said order of Removall of the said two Justices and pray your Worrpps: will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determination of the said appeal this present Sessions and that ypur Petrs: may relieved as unto your Worshipps shall Seem meet.

And Yr. Petrs: shall Ever Pray Etc
