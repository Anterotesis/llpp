To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (whereof one of the Quorum) bearing date the thirty first day of October last Mary painter wife of Giles painter the Younger was removed from the parish of St Luke in the County of Middx to the parish of St. Boloph without Bishopsgate London as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

Your petitioners conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Order of Removall Humbly Appeal to this Court for relief

And Your petitioners shall ever pray

Jno North Soll for the Appellants
