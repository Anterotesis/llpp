To The Honble: Court Whom this May Consern

The humble Petition of John Burt Mariner humbly Shew't that he has receav'd the County Pention Setled by Act of Parliment from the Yeare thirty Eight to the Yeare forty One I being four score Years of Age nott able to help my Self, Now it would be of great Service to me, Please to lett me Know when I Shall receave Same

Your Petitioner Shall has In duty bound for ever Pray
