Middx Instices of the prove for the County of

Sheweth The humble peticon of the late Overseers of the poore of the parish of St Martins in the Feilds in the sd County

That yor. Pet. were overseers of the Poore of the sd Parish the yeare 1695 and the time of their sd. Office Your Petr. Account Bulebbed upon Bond the paid of 450lb. or there abouts white was distributed for the use of the Poore of the sd Parish

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor worspps to be pleased to releive them in the manned as unto your Workspps

and ye. Petr. Shall enter Pray Etc
