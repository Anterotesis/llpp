To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of Francis Jackman and William Pickering Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 4th. day of February 1758 Richard (said to be) a Male Bastard Child aged about Six years born on the Body of Hannah (therein called) Atkinson Singlewoman, was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty & County as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

Wm: Bedcott Richd Evans} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square

Danl. Hancock Chas. Smith
