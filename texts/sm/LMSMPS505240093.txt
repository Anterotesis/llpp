To the Worshipfull the Justices at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of Tracy Tutt

Sheweth That he stands indicted for an assault and has get a Release from the Prosecutor but having been very much afflicted with an Ague and fever and being in a consumption to such a Degree that he has been unable to work at his Trade for a long Time past and is new a patient of Westminster infirmary

That he would willingly plead to the said Indictment but by reason of his extream poverty in totally unable to bear the expences

Therefore he Desires the hounourable Court will order his fees to be given him and that he may be pardoned the indictment and his recognizances withdrawn he having received A notice that they will otherwise be carried into the Exchequer

And your Worships Petitioner will ever pray
