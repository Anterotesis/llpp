To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex At their adjourned Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Mich: Downs and Samuel Thorpe two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of March last Susannah Caldon Wife of Jeremiah Caldon (who hath deserted her) and their two Children Vizt. Mary Ann borne 23d. of February 1787 and David Jeremiah born the 4th. of April 1790 was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. James Within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth as the place of their las legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

I: Carter Sollr. for the Appellts.}

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall- ever pray Etc
