Sepr: 17: 1778

To His Worship Justices Hawkins

Sheweth that Your Humble Petitioners Daniel Emson

Now Standing at the Bar Declare Himself Innocent of The thing be is Now accused with But if your Worships and the gen them of the Jury Shall Think (it to as) liot Me with amy Punishment I hope your Worship will be So Kind as to tell me go to Sea as I ham a Seamen and her Served his Majesty in that Capasity Nine years and Shall be glud So Save his Majesty this Ware if your Worship thinks fitt and at the same Time Shall Ever Be Bound to for your Worship from your Petitioner

Daniel Emson
