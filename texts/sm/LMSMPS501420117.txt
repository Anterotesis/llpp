To ye. Rgt. Worspll: his Maties: Justices of ye. peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye. church- Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of parish of Brentwood in ye. County of Essex

Sheweth That by a Warrtt: or order under ye. hands & Seales of [...] Cooper & Robert Kyrby Esqrs: Two of his Maties. Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date ye. 16th. day of October 1714: Elianor Pickett ye. wife of George Pickett wth their Two children Wm: aged about Eight Years, & Sarah aged about four Yeares were removed & passed from ye. Hamlet of Wappin Stepney in ye. parish of St. Dunstom Stepney in ye Said County of Middx & to ye. Sd parish of Brentwood in ye Sd County of Else & therein Setting forth, ye. Sd George Pickett was Last Legally Setled in ye. Sd Pish of Brentwood;

Your peticoner therefore findeinge themselves agreived wth. ye. Judgmt. of ye. Sd Two Justices of ye. peace humbly appeales to this Honable: Court against ye. Sd Warrtt. Order, and prayes Your Worsps. with be pleased to grant an Order, That ye. church Wardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Sd Hamlet of Wappin Stepney may attend this Court Some days this Genrll Quarter Sessions of ye. peace, to heare & abide Judgmt: & determinacon of this Court touching this appeale;

And Your peticonrs Shall ever pray Etc
