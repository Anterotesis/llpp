To the Right Honble William Bridgen Esquire Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majesties of General Goal Delivery now Sitting at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The humble Petition of Richard England

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner Stands Charged with Stealing a Silver Spoons three Gold Rings and a quantity of Wearing Apparel and therefore your Petitioner Prays that he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged at this present Session and your Petitioner as in Duty Bound Shall for ever Pray Etc

Richd. England
