To Mr. Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Session Assembled on Monday the 14th. day of July 1794

The Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Staining London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hand and Soul of P. Colqhoun and Wm. Browne & Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Thirtieth day of June last Wm.. aged six years and upwards the Illigitimate Child of Sarah Jones wife of Richard Jones (late Sarah Jackson Singlewoman) was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Mary Staining London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Illigitimate Child

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by such Order of Remove and therefore pray your Worships to examine into hear and determine the circumstances of the Birth and settlement of the said Illigitimate Child And that the said Order of Removal of the said P. Colquhoun and Wm. Browne may be Quashed and that a day may be appointed in this present Session for hearing and determining this Appeal
