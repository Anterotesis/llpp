The humble petition of Samuel Greenhill one of the High Constables of the Hundred of Hoxton over Dated Aprill [...] 16th- Ann Dom 1694

Crete are to Certifie the Honourable Bench of their Maties Justifee of the peace seting at Westminster. That your humble petitioner; hath served the office aforesaid faithfulley for three yeares Last past, And their being Able and fitt men to Serve the Said Office your Humble petitioner peareth to bee Released of the Said office

The Names of substantiall inhabitans of the aforesaid Hundred & Fitt men to Serve theire the Said office

Mr John Page of Wembly in Harrow pish Mr [x] Handall Page of Pinner
