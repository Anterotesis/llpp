To the Right Worpfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Gregory by St. Paul London

Sheweth

That by Vertue of an Order Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of William Jones & Samuel Grey Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth Day of August 1732 Elizabeth Bride Single woman was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Said County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Gregory by St. Paul London alledging the Said Parish of St. Gregory to be last legall Place of Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Bride.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yor. Worps [---] against the same and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, & Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the sd. County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed Sometime during the next Generall Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the Said County, then & there, to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
