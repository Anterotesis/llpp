To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall in the said County

The Humble Petition of Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of I: Trevanion and Thomas Cotton Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th day of May last past Elizabeth Edwards Widow of William Edwards and Ann Edwards aged about Eight Years Daughter of the said William Edwards by his first Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist in this County to the said parish of Saint George Hanover Square as the place of their last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships would be pleased to Appoint some day this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

S: Parry-} Agent for the parish of St George Hanover Square
