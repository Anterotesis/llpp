To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Clarke and Saunders Welch Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of June last Catherine Gilbert Singlewoman aged near Forty Years was removed and conveyed from that Part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlememt Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Wherefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to Your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for this County And that Your Worships will Please to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex do then Attend this Court.

And Your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc

Gs Bradshaw Tho: Greening John Nind Jas Young} Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
