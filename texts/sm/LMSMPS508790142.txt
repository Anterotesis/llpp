To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire and the rest of the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmenton in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of sd R Davies Esqr and H R Requett Esquires Clerk two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County searing date the Eighth day of December One thousand seven hundred and ninety two Sarah Currell and Five of her Children namely Jane aged about twelve Years ann aged about ten Years Mary aged about eight Years Lydia aged about Six Years and James aged about four Years were by the said Justices Order removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Bromley in the said County and adjudged to have their Lawful Settlement in the said Parish of Edmonton and did thereby require your Petitioners to receive and provide for them as Inhabitants of the said Parish

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Justices And therefore

Most humbly Pray your Worships that the said Order may be quashed And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining this Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever Pray etc
