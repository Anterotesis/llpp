To the Rt Worsppll his Majes. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeal of Thomas Watts of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth

That last year your Petitr: was rated tenn pence p Month towards the relief of the poor of the said parish besides Over rates and that this present year Yor Petitr is raised to Fourteen pence p Month.

That your Petitr is aged near Seaventy Yeares due hath Susteined great Losses & past his Labour Wherefore he humbly Conceives himselfe opprest Â£3 p An

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays Yor Worspps will be pleased to relieve him As unto yor Worspps Shall Seeme Meete

And Yor Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Tho: Watts
