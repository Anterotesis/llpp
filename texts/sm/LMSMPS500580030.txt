To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of Chelsey in the County

Sheweth That by order dated the 29th day of June [...] last under the hands & Seals of Sr Roger Lansley Kt & Thomas Railton Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County (Quord unns) whereby one Dorothy Ayres als Edwards als [...] Chipp was lately removed from the parish of St Margarets Westmr in this County to the said parish of Chelsey as the place of her last Settlemt where She now continues chargeables

That yor Petrs are able to make itt appear to this Court that the said Dorothy [...] Ayres als Edwards als Chipp hath noe legall Settlemt in the said parish of Chelsey & find the [...] agreived by the removall of the said Dorothy Ayres als Edwards als Chipp to their said Pish

Rolfe Yor Petrs therefore humbly appeal agt the Said order & pray that a day may be appointed for the hearing & determineing of the said appeal & that the Chur [...] wardens & Overseers of the poor of [...] the said parish of St Margarett Westmr doe then attend to hear & abide the judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the Sd appeal

And Yor peticonr Shall pray Etc
