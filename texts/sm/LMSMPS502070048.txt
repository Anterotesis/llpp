To the Right Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeals of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Ordr. undr. the hands & Seales of Sr. Danll. Dolius Knt.& Samll. Webb Esqr Two of the Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 17th. day of Novr. last Katherine Walkinton widw. of Joshua Walkinton was removed from the pish of St. John at Hackney to the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

That ye. Petrs. thinks themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices & doe appeale there from to the Judgemt. of this Cort.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray ye Worspps to ordr. the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Pish of St. John at Hackney to attend ye Worpps some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment of this Cort touching the sd. Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
