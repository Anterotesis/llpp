To the Right Worspll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of the Inhabitants of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch whose names are hereunder Subscribed in the behalf of themselves and other Inhabitants of the Same Parish aggrieved

That the Prsent Ovrseers of the Poore of the sd Parish In Combinacon with Some of the Antient Inhabitants thereof have unequally rated the Contributions of money for the releife of the poore of the parish for the Yeare ensueing without haveing regard to the rateing of personall Estates in Goods browne but have proceeded in the [...] Taxation according to a pound Rate on Rent referred only by which your Petr. are greived for that every person (by the Lawes of this Realive) ought to be rated according and in proporcon to his Justice Sab Stained within the sd. parish aswell as by the pound Rate

P Thos Nash [x] Wherefore yor. Petr. humbly Pray that the Sd. Ovrseers and others prsent all the makeing of the sd. Rate may be warned to attend this Court to Shew cause why there Should not be a new Rate made with respect by person all Estate & not collect by a Pound Rate only.

And Yr. Pr. (as in duty bound) Shall ever Pray Etc

Thomas Beesley John Atkinson Richard Ayres will Francis

Sarah Corslers her [...] mark Anne Bannister
