To ye Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The Humble Petition of Dianna Hussey

Sheweth That About the 26th of June 1706 yor Petr Became an Apprntice to Stephen Gore and Elizabeth his Wife for the For me of five yeares to Learne the Art of Childs Coat making And that. the sd Mr and Mrs Having both Abused yor Petr A Hearing was had thereof in this Honourable Court about July last And yor Worspps were then pleas'd to order that yor Petr should be Received by her sd Mr & Mrs But they have ever since Absolutely Refused soe to doe

P Thos: Nash Your Petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that she may be discharged from her sd Apprnticeships She having been tenderd before wittness

And yor Petr shall ever Pray Etc
