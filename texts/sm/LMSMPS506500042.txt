To the Chairman

and the Rest of the Majestrates at the Sessions Hicks Hall

Gentlemen Yor Worships were Pleased & Pass Sentence of 3 Months Imprisonment [---] on Thos-Gibson Last Sessions for Asalting Me on My Duty he his a wife & four Children and Nothing but his hand Labour & Support them and they are in great Distress I hope Your Worships will Excuce My Freedome in [...] You will be Pleased to the lease him out by Prison as he has been Six Weeks

I am Your Worships Most Humble Servt to Comand Wm Murden

Constable of St John Wapping

Feby 15-1775
