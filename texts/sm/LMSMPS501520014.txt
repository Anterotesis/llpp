To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Robert Archer Constable of Endfield in the sd County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallery Die to your Petitr. at Lady day Last amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds for passing and Relieving of Vagrant That your Petitr hath this last Quarter been at great Charge and Expence in relieving of Vagrants therefore Stanes in want of his sd. Money.

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays. your Worsppl will be pleased to order that he may be paid the sd Moneys. As to this Court Shall be thought meete.

And Yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc. Robt Archer.
