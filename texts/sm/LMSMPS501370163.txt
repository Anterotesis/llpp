To the Worshipll: her Majtes: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church -Warden and Overseers of the Poore of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in this County

Sheweth That by an order Dated the 19th day of October last under the hands and Seales of John Fuller and Felix Feast Esqrs two of her Majtes. Justices of the Peace for this County (quor Unus) Bridget Manson Wife of Thomas Manson (now at Sea) with her two Children vizt Jane and Thomas Manson were removed from the Parish of St Gile's Cripplegate in this County to the Said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney as the place of the Last Legall Settlement of the Said Thomas Manson

That Your Peticonr finde themselves agreived by the said Order of removall and Humbly appeale against the Same and Doubt not but to make it Appeare to this Worshipfull Court that the Said Thomas Manson never gained any Legall Settlement in the said Hamlett

Your Peticonrs therefore Pray this Court would please to Order and appoynt a day this present Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Said Parish of St Gile's Criplegate and all other parties Concerned to Attend this Court to heare and abide the determination of this Court on ye Said Appeale

And your Peticonrs Shall pray Etc.
