To The Rt: Worspll: her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County Middx

The humble Petition and Appeale of [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Islington in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Order dated the Third day of this Instant June under the have [...] Seales of John Fuller and Felix Feast Esqrs: two of her Maties: Justices the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unns) Elizabeth Ruff was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate to the sd Parish of Mary Islington as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Ruff.

That yor: Petitrs: find themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of the Peace and Appeal against the Same The said Elizabeth Ruff not having any Legall Settlement in the said Parish st Mary Islington.

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays Yor: Worspps will be Pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles Cripplegate to Attend this Cout Some Day the Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Yor: Petitr: Shall ever pray Etc
