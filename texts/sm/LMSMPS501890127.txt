To the Wors. His Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County Of Middx. in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westminster in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and Sl Parry Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the Said County of Middx. bearing Date the Sixteenth day of September Edward Thompson was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. Margaret Westminster as the Place of his Lawfull Settlement

That Your Petitioners thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the same

Therefore humbly pray Your Worships will Please to Order, that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of st. Giles in the Feilds may attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determi nation of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
