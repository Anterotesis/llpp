To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Joseph Burnthwaite

Sheweth That your Petitioner is an Inhabitant and Householder in the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and as such is rated and Charged to the relief of the poor of the said parish

That by rate or Assessment made for relief of the Poor of the said parish is Charged for his Dwelling house in the said parish at and after the rate of the Yearly rent of Fifty eight pounds and your Petitioner only pays the yearly rent of Forty Five pounds as will appear by his Lease so that your Petitioner is Aggrieved by the said rate

That your Petitioner is as he Conceives Aggrieved by the said rate or Assessment and humbly Appeal therefrom and pray such relief as to this Court shall seem meet and that a day may be Appointed for hearing the Appeal and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish may attend and produce the said rate or Assessment at the hearing thereof and your Petitionr shall over pray Etc

Jos Burnthwaite
