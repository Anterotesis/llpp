Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition [---] and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and [...] Christopher Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the 13th Day of January last past Elizabeth Edwards and her Female Bastard Child not yet baptised were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancrass in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the Rearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray.

Jas Dyson Soliciter for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westminster.
