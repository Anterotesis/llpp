To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Spranger Esquire being an Inhabitant within the Hamlet of Pinner in the said County of Middlesex and rated towards the Relief of the Poor there

Sheweth That on or about the fifth day of December last a rate or Assembled was made and allowed for the relief of the Poor within the said Hamlet for which your Petitioner was rated at the sum of One Hundred & fifty Pounds being an Inhabitant of the said Hamlet

That your Petitioner Conceives himself aggrieved by the said Rate or Assembled for the Reasons hereafter mentioned

First forth that at your Petitioner House and Lands in the said Hamlet are rated beyond the Real and just Value thereof.

2d. For that the same House Persons and Lands are rated mequally with respect to the property of other persons in the same Hamlet

3d. For that the Property of the Persons in the same Hamlet is underva [...] in the same rate or Assessment

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly appeals against the said Rate or Assessment and prays that the same may be Quashed or that your Petitioner may have such Relay in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem Meet and that your Worships will appoint a Day in the next Sessions to Hear and Determine this his Petition and Appeal
