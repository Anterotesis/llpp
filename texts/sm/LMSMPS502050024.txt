To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Jonathan Jackson

Sheweth That yor. peticoner did imptey this prsent Sessions one Mr. Willm. Wreathouse as a Sollicitr. to this Court And that the Sd Sollicitr. did only attend this Court for to put in Bail for yor. Petr. to Traverse the Indictmt. till next Sessions.

That the Bill hereunto annexed delivered to yor petr in this matter being very extravigant (as yor. petr. humbly conceives) for doing no more business

Therefore humbly Pray yor. Worspps that the Sd Bill may be Taved by the Clerk of the Peace

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray etc.
