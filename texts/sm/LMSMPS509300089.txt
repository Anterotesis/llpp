To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace for the said County assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Bucklebury in the County of Berks

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N Onant and Pr. Nave two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty ninth Day of June one Thousand Seven hundred and ninety six Whereby Richard Turner together with his wife Sarah and their Seven Children Videlicet Francis aged about fourteen Years Mary aged about Thirteen Years William aged about Ten Years Jane aged about Eight Years Richard aged about Four Years Daniel aged about Two Years and James aged about six Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Bucklebury as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said order pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and Determine the said appeal

And your Petitioners will Ever pray Etc

John Minall Junr. William Clargo Nathan Goddard Richard Full} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor
