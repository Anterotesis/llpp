To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace For the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition [...] and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of J. Girdler and Charles Sheppard Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and For the County of Middlesex one where of being of the Quorum. ring Date the Eighteenth Day of October One they and seven Hundred and Eighty Five Sarah Gibbs together with he Male Bastard Child Aged abt. Eight Weeks For Nurture were removed and Conveyed From the said Liberty of Saffron Hill, Hatton Garden, and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the Place of their legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the Same and thereby Pray that the said Appeal may be hand this present Sessions and that a time may be now Appointed For the Stearing and Determining there of

Richd: Brewer Sollisitor For the Appealants
