To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman and the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the [---] County of Middlesex General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County.

That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the [...] Beete and S: Leroux Esquires two of His Majesty's [...] for the said County bearing date the 19th day of Febry [...] Joseph Sherwood Ann his Wife & their Child named Mary Aged about [...] & Conveyed from & out of the parish of Saint Luke in the [...] to the said parish of Saint Mary White Chapel [...] legal settlement of the said Joseph Sherwood and his

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the Order & Adjudication of the said two Justices Humbly Appeal to this Court from the same & pray relief in the Premises and that a day may be appointed this present Sessions for the hearing & Determining thereof and that all parties concerned may be then Ordered to attend

And A four petitioner will pray Etc

Jno. Skutt Solr. for appellants

Goodmans Fields 23rd. February 1784
