Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assaulted

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the Peter George Booth Bart. and William Blankburrow Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid Cone of whom being of the Quorum bearing Date the fifth Day of January instant Thomas Allibon and his Wife Lydia were removed and Conveyed out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Martin Ludgate London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices do appeal to Your Worships

then from and humbly pray that you will please to hear them in the Premises, and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc.
