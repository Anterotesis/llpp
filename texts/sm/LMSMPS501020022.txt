To the Rt. Worsps, her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Sr. Thomas halton, Sr. Edward Wills, Charles Anthony, Stephen Mason, Walter York Inhabitants of the Parish of Islington

Sheweth That the Poors rates on the inhabitants of said Parish have Been (as they conceive) frequently made irregularly.

Therefore Yor. Petrs. humbly pray that your Worships wilbe pleased to give the necessary directions for making the poors rates for the said Parish to prevent the like for ye future

And Yor. Petrs. Shall Ever pray Etc

Edw Wills Tho: Halton Cha: Anthony Walter York He. Mason
