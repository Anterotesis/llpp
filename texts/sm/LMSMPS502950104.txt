To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Burnham in the County of Bucks.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Booth & Thos: Thayer Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of October Instant Richard Callcutt Elizabeth his wife and their two Children to witt Thomas Aged about seven Years and John aged about fourteen months were removed & conveyed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the sd County of Middx to the said Parish of Burnham in the County of Bucks alledging therein that the sd Parish of Burnham is the place of the last legall settlemt. of the sd. Richard Callcutt Elizabeth his wife & their two Children & whither by Law they ought to be sent.

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants. Yor Petrs. thinking themselves aggreived by the sd. Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal against the same to this Court And forasmuch as your Petrs. cannot be ready to prosecute their sd. Appeal this sd sent Sessions Humbly pray that your Worshipps wou'd be pleased to adjorn [...] the hearing & determining of the sd Appeal untill the next Generall Sessions of the peace to be held for this County And appoint some day therein for all partieng concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And yor Peticoners shall pray Etc.
