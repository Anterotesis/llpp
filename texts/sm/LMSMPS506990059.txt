To the Rt. Worpll. her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the parish of Padbury neare Buckingham in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & seals of Robt. Constable & Joseph Jory Esqrs. two of her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quop unus) beareing date the ninth day of Decembr. last Thomas Inns & Martha his wife were removed from the Hamblett of Bethnall Green the parish of Stepney in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. parish of Padbury in the County of Bucks as the place of the last legall settlemt. of the sd. Tho: Inns,

That neither the sd. Tho: Inns or his wife have any legall settlemt. in the sd. Parish of Padbury, and yor. Petrs. therefore conceiveing themselves aggreived by the sd. Order of removeall humbly appeale agt. the same but their witnesses liveing remote in the Country they cannot be ready to psecute their appeale this Prsent Sessions,

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the sd. appeale may be saved to them, and that the heareing & determining thereof may be adjorned untill the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County, and that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the sd. hamblett of Bethnall Green & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to attend her Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County at the sd. next Quarter Sessions to heare & abide their Judgmt. & determinacon touching the sd. appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
