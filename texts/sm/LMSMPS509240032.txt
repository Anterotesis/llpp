To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwards and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Pt. Colquhoun Esqr. and Daniel Williams Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the First Day of February last (one whereof being of the Quorum) Rachael Williams Single woman was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day this present Sessions that all Parties concerned may attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
