To the Right Worll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St Pancras in ye sd County

Sheweth That Frances Shaw a your Child was lately passed from the pish of [...] Leonard Shorditch in the said County to the said pish of St Pancras by [...] under the hands & Seals of William Withers Esqr & Alexander Pitfeild Es [...] two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quor [...] thereby alleadging that the said young Child was last legally sen [...] at the said Pish of St Pancras by reason Richard Ward who marryed Mary the mother of the said Child lived eleven years at the pish of St Pancras

Your Petrs thinke themselves aggrieved wth the Judgment of the Said two Justices Do Appeal to this Worll Bew [...] & humbly pray (the premises considered) that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish Leonard Shorditch & all other pties concerned therein [...] be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Session to hear & abide the Judgment & finall determination this Court touching this Appeal

And Pr Petr (as in duty bound) shall on pray
