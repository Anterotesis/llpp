To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of Ye: Peace for ye. County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of Ye. Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls in ye.City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under ye. Hands & Seals of S. Bever and Benjamin Cox, Esqrs., Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, whereof one is of ye. Quorum; bearing Date ye. Nineteenth Day of January last past; Ann Davis a Child of about 7 Years old, & Thomas Davies, about 5 Years old, the Grand children of Thomas Davis deceased, were Removed from ye. Parish of St. Luke Chelsea in ye County of Middlesex, unto ye said Parish of St. Gregory by St. Pauls, London alledging ye. said Parish of St. Gregory, to be ye. Place of their last legal Settlement.

Cha Baynbridge, Sollr. for ye. Appellts.} That your Petitioners concerning themselves agrieved by ye said Warrant or Order of ye. said two Justices, Do Appeal to yor. Worships therefrom, and humbly pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, & Order that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of St. Luke, Chelsea, may appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petitioners shall pray Etc.
