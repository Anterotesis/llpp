To the Right Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of All Hallows the great London.

Sheweth That by Warrant on order dated the Eighteenth day of April last under the Hands and Seales of John Cleeve and Richard Milner Esqrs. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (Quor unns) Elizabeth Horbon wife of Daniel Horton and Susanna a Young Infant daughter of the Sd. Daniel & Elizabeth were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the said County to the Said Parish of All Hallows the Great as the place of last legall Settlemt. of the said Elizabeth Horton.

That Yor. Peticonrs. find themselves aggreived by the sd. Warrant or Ordr. of the said Two Justices, and humbly appeale agat. the Same to this Honble Court,

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to Order the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Sd Parish St. Giles in the Feilds and all other psons concerned to attend this Court Some day this Prsent Sessions to heare and abide the Judmt, and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the Sd Appeale, and that yor. Peticonrs. may have Such releife herein as to Yor. Wopps shall Seem meet Etc

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall Pray Etc

Charles Mason Samll: Tilie [...] } Churchwarden

Jno: Kenrick [...] } Overseers of ye Poore
