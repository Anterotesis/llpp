The Peticon of Jacob Burton Constable of the [...] of Saint Pauls Covent Garden Humbly Desires The Opinion of This Honourable Bench

That Some Days Since he had a Warrant brought him by an Informer Against one Ven a Victualler of the Said Parish for Selling of Short Measure And your Petitioner hath Since been to Make Distress in order to Satisfie the Said Warrant and could not find any thing Thereon to Answer one half of the Charge Without the Breaking of Doors which the Warrent Doth not Impower. the Said Informer Doth Daily Threaten your Petitioner That if he Doth not Immedately Pay ye Money That he will give him Trouble, your Petitioner Begs your Opinion and That you would be Pleased to move that hardship from him.
