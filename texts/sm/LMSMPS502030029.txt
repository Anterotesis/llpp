To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The Humble Peticon and Appeals of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Poplar and Blackwall in the Parish of Stepney in ye. sd: County.

Sheweth. That by Order undr: the Hands and Seals of Philip Wilshire and Saml. Jones Esqrs: bearing date the 14th: day of April last Hannah Compiano Widow of Joseph Compiano dececd a Forreigner whose Settlemt: could not be found was removed from the Hamblett of Lime house in the said Parish and County to our said Hamblett of Peplar & Blackwall as to the Place of their last Legall Settlemt:

That yr: Petr: think themselves agriev'd by the said Ordr: and Humbly Appear to this Honble: Court agt, the Same.

And Pray that yr: Worshpps would be Pleased to Ordr: that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamblett of Limehouse and all other Persons concerned may attend this Court some day this Present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement j& determinacon of this Court touching ye. sd: Appeal.

And yr: Petrs: Shall Ever Pray Etc.
