To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwdns. & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Ann Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of John Ellis and Oliver Lambart two of his Majestyes- Justices of the peace for the City and Libty of Westminster (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twenty third day of June Instand William Forrister aged about Sixty Eight Years was removed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Ann whereby your Petitioners conceive them selves to be Aggrieved.

There fore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudgement of the said two Children and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Church wardens- and Over seers of the poor of the said parish of Saint James Westminster and all person concerned do attend this Court some day in this present sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And shall ever pray Etc. Jo: Kendall Sol P Appelth.
