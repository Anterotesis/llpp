To the Rt. Worspll his Majties Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Martin one of the petty Constables of Southmims in the County

Sheweth That at Midsumr [...] last past there was due and owing to yor. petr. the Sume of ten pounds for one qrter. of a years Salary for his loss of time and Expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the payment of the sd Sume of ten pounds to yor. sd Petr for such his sd Service

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

Thomas Martin
