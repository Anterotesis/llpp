To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Old Street in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant- under the Hands and Seals of the Revd. Sir George- Booth Bart. and William Blackborow Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (and one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th. day of May 1786 Elizabeth Lee (Wife of Peter Lee and their Son Peter were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell to the said Parish of Saint Luke Old Street as the place of the Lawful Settlement of the said Elizabeth Leo and Peter the Son.

That in as much as your Petitioners do Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant & the Removal & Conveyance of the said Elizabeth Lee and her Son Peter in Pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly may relief in the Premisses And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing & determining This their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall over Pray Etc.

Morton Soll for the Appeal
