To the Right Worspph her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of Thomas Davison

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. did serve the office of Constable for the liberty of Cherhouse Lane in the parish of St. [...] Sepulchres in the said County for the year last past and that the Inhabitants of the said liberty did elect John Cadwell to Serve the Said office for the year ensueing and that yor. Worspps were Pleased to discharg [...] the said John Cadwell from serving the Said office where upon your peticoner hath applyed himselfe to the Inhabitants of the Said liberty to proceed to a new choice the Said Inhabitants refuse see to doe alledging that they doe insist upon their said eleccon of the said John Cadwell which electon was legall and that the said John Cadwell quallifyed to serve the said office.

yor. Peticoner therefore humbly Prays yours Worspps wilbe pleased to grant that he may be discharged from the said office and that one of the persons here under named may be elected to serve the said office.

John Cadwell Thomas Aymscome} Persons fit to succeed

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
