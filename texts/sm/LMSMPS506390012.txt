To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knight and the test of the Worshipfull Justices Assembled to hold the Genl. Sessions of the peace at Hicks Hall Saint John Street in and for the County of Middlesex on Tuesday the Twelfth Day of April 1774

The Humble Petition of Thomas Anderson

Sheweth That your petitioner was bound in Recognizance to Appear and give Evidence at the last October Session Against James Riley in a Case of Felony

That your petitioner Neglected Appearing pursuant to his Recognizance thro A mistake of the Day on which he was to Appear and not with any Instant to be favourable to the Culprit or Sorene him from Justice, so your petitioners recognizance became forfeited

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Implores your Worships to Consider the premis & Order your petr Recognizance to be [...] Respited

And in Duty bound Will pray

Sworn in open Court at Hicks Hall in Tuesday the 12th. day of April 1774 to the Truth of the above petition

Thomas Anderson

W:
