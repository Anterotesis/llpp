To the Right Worship full his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St: Bridgett ats Brides London.

[...] That by Warrant under the hands and [...] Ward Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (quoz unus) bearing date the Nineteenth day of February last Thomas Gilles with Mary his wife and their three Children (vizt.) Thomas John and Mary were past on your Petrs: from the parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate as the place of their last Legall Settlement.

Tha your Petr. conceive themselves Aggreived by the order of the said two Justices and appeale to your Worpps therefo [...]

Your Petrs: therefore humbly pray your Worpps will appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeale.

And yor Petrs: shall pray Etc
