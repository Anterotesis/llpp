To the Right worshipful the chairman and other his Majesty's Justices assigned to keep peace in and for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller Esquire and T.T. Cotton Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date on or about the twenty eighth day of January last past and made on the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ church in the same County they the said Justices did adjudge the Place of the last legal Settlement of Catherine Collins to be in the said Parish of Saint Olave and did require the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Christ church to Remove the said Catherine from their said Parish of Christ church to the said Parish of Saint Olave and did also require your Petitioners to receive and provide for her according to Law.

That your Petitioners did receive and do now provide for the said pauper but humbly conceive that they are aggrieved by the said Order and that the last legal Settlement of the said Pauper is not in the said Parish of St. Olave Your Petitioners then fore humbly pray that they may be permitted to enter an appeal against the said Order at this Court and that this Court with be pleased to appoint a day for hearing the Same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

John Britain James Gassey} Churchwardens

Rich Dendy} Samuel Barrow} Overseers
