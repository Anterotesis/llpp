To the Right Worsph her Maty Justices of the Peace of the County of Midd.

The humble peticon & appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Sepulchres in the Said County.

Sheweth

That Jane Birch widow was by warrant under the hands and seales of Joseph Short & John Fuller Esqr. two of her Majesty. Justices of the Peace for the said county lately passed from the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said county to the said parish of St. Sepulchres

That the said Jane Birch never had any legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Sepulchres which your Petrs. are read to make appeare to this Honble Court.

P Wessley Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly appeale against the said order and pray your Worsps to be pleased to appoint a day this pdte Sessions for determining thereof.

And yor. Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc.
