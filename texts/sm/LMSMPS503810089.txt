To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humbled Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London.

Sheweth

That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John- Poulson and Francis Hole Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one was of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of September last Alice Wicks Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx into the said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgersgate London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of he said Alice Wicks

Benj. Drayton Agent for tho Appell Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the Peace humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court that the hearing and determining of the said Appeal may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this County and that the Court will appoint a day in the said next Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn and all other persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
