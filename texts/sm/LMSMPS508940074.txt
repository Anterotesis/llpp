To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and John Scott Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of January last Ann Lechman otherwise Ann Mc. Cann was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Ealing in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of the last legal settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

They therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication and order of the said two Justices and pray that your worships will appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining thereof and that all persons concerned therein may have notice to attend the same

And your petitioners will ever pray.

George Barber Joseph Fisher Jno Metteby Richd Willan Tranus [...] ssip N T Hodgson
