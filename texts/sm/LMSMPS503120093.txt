To the Right Worpll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genl: Quarter Sessions

That Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Town of Hampton in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands & Seals of N Boothe & Nicholas Jeffreys Esqr. two of His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County (where of One if of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of July last patt Elizabeth Mist wife of William Mist with Ann Mist their Child aged about two Months Were Removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middx to the said Town of Hampton as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Mist and Ann Mist her said Child

Jas Tyton Agent For Ye. Appells. Yor Appellts. thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeale against the same And Pray this Court will please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs And all Persons Concerned to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor. Appellts. Shall ever pray
