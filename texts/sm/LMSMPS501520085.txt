To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the hands & seales of Alexander Ward & John Matcalfe Esqrs: two of his Maties of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) Benjamin Ward his wife & two children (vizt) Tamasin aged eight Yeares & Thomas aged four yeares were removed from the Liberty of Saffron hill in the Pish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the fields as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Peticonrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said Justices do appeale therefrom to pray that this Court will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Liberty of Saffron hill in the parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
