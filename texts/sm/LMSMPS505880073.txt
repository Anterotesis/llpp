To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirtieth Day of August 1768 Mary Dorothy Brown Single woman (with John her Bastard Child aged thirteen Months) was removed on the tenth day of September last from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth London aforesaid adjudging the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Dorothy Brown to be there And your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do Humbly appeal therefrom and humbly pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worshipe shall seem Meet

Therefore Your Petitioners humbly pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session to Hear and determine their said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos: Moy Sollr. for the Appellts.
