To the Rt. Worshippfull her Maties: Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon Mary Guest in behalfe of her husband John Guest

Sheweth That yor Peticonr husband is Over Rateed to the Poor of the Prcint of St. Katherines. he renting only a house of four pound P ann & they charge yor. Peticonr. husband one & Twenty Shillings a Yeare to the poor. That Your Peticonr hath made application to the Vestry for Releife and they refuse to Releive yor. Peticonr herein altho: they are not of ability to pay the said some Soe Assessed upon yor. Peticonrs: husband

[...] S [...] Ingham Therefore yor. Peticonr humbly prayes an Order of this Homble. Court for the Overseers of the Poor to Attend here to Shew cause Why Yor Peticonr. husband Should not be Eased of the said assessment or otherwise Order as [...] to your Worpps Shall seem meet Etc.

And Yor. Peticonr. Shall pray Etc

Mary guest
