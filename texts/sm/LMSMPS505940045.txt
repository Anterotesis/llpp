To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary AlderMary London

Sheweth That by Virtue an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex ( one being of the Quorum) dated the twenty ninth day of March last past Mary Browning the Wife of Samuel Browning was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary Aldermary as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgement of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court against the same And

Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determining the same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Robt. Holder Solr. for the Appellants
