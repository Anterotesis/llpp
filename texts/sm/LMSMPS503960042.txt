The the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of St: Honbl. the Lord Carpenter and John upton Esqr. Two of His Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 14th day of april 1749[---] Frances Trevors widow of Peter Trevors was Removed form the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County to the said Parish of St James as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be Please to Appoint a time for hearing and Deter- mining the Said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may then attend

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc

Danl. Seddon as out for St. James's
