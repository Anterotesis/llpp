To the Rt Worpll. her Maties Justices of the. Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appearl of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poore of the Hamblett of Spittle Feilds in the Parish of Stepney in the County aforesd

Sheweth That One Edward Smith was Warrt: under the hands & Sealer of the Worpll. Roger smith Tresr & Rob Baleman Esq [...] of her Maties Justices of the Peace for there County lately removed from the parish of St Mary Whitechappell to the sd Hamblet under Colour of being A Servt: therein Your Peticonerd hope to prove the sd Edward Smith Did never Attaine an [...] Settlemt in the sd Hamrt

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray that all psons concived may Attend this Honble Court on a Certeyme day to a [...] Your Worpps Determinacon of the Prennted

And Your Peticoner as in Duly bound shallen pray Etc

Jno: Knowles [...]

Tho: Wilkes Churchwden Richard Coloroft William Robinson} overseer of the poore
