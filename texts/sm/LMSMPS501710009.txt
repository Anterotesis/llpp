To the Rt. Worpll, his. Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Genal Quartl. Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble peticon & appeal of John King of the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the sd. County Taylor, Thomas Allen and Henry Barnes of the Same parish Cheesmongers.

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. are Severally rated much more for & towards the releif of the poor of the said parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch for this prsent Year One Thousand Seven hundred and Eighteen than as they Severall humbly Conceive they respective ye ought to pay and Appect agt. the Said rate

Yor. Petrs. therefore Severally Pray Such releif in the Justices as to they Court Shall Seem meet

And yor. petrs. Shall pray Etc.
