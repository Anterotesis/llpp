To the Worshipfull ther Majties. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace held for the sd County

The humble Petition of James Knowles of the Hamblet of Wappin Stepney

Sheweth That yor petitionr is rated 10d p Month to the poor of the said Hamblet and 1s only a Lodger at the house of Mrs. Mary Daniel a Widdow at 3s. P week his Lodging being ready furnished and he noe waies an Inhabitant.

Therefore humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to concnr the premisses and make such Order for yor. petitionrs discharge as to yor Worshipps Shall Seem meet

And Shall pray James Knowles
