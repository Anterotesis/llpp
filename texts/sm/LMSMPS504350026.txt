To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Roger Wright

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seals of Jacob Harvey Richard Chamberlayne Hamond Crosse Wm. Withers Esqrs. His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) Inhabiting in Finsbury Division where in the Parish at Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County doth lye bearing date the twentieth day of king instant April Did Nominate & appoint Your Petitioner as a Substantial House holder in the sd Parish to be one of the Overseers of the Poor there for one year th

That the Petitioner is not a House holder within the said Parish and Submits to this Honourable Court That he is not liable to Serve the Office of one of the Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish

The Petitioner thinks himself aggreived by the Nomination & Appointment aforesaid and therefore humbly Appeals to this Court against the Same and Prays your Worships to discharge him from the sd Office

And the Petitioner will ever Pray Etc

G: Talmash Attorney for the Appellant
