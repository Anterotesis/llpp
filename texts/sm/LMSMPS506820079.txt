To His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street on Tuesday the 14th. day of July 1778 Assembled

The Humble Petition James Smith Petty Constable of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County

Sheweth That Your Petitioners about a week since received a Summons from Mr. John Wild High Constable of Holborn Division ordereing him to attend at Hicks Hall in St. John Street on Tuesday the 14th. day of July 1778

That Your Petitioner intending obeying the said Summons but a Gentleman coming into his Shop to buy a Quantity of Good and having no other person to do the Business but himself was unavoidably prevented from attending so soon as he otherwise intended to have done, and that as soon as the Gentleman departed from your Petitioners Shop Your Petitioner set out fer Hick Hall and same there about a Quarter of an hour Past Ten O Clock and was then informed that the Court been fined him 20s. for not attending sooner

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays Your Worships will be pleased to remit the said Fine as your Petitioner was so prevented from attending and Your Petitioner is very willing if Your Worships shod. so Order to attend at any future day

And your Petitioner as in duly bound will ever pray

Js. Smith
