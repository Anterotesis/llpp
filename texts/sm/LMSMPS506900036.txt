To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of David Richards of the parish of St Gyles Cripplegate in the said County Victualler.

Sheweth That it is alledged yor Petr. became [...] Vaile before John Bond Esqr. one of her Matys Justices of the peace for the said County for the appearance of one Elizabeth Wootton in this Court at the Sessions there holden in december last And it is likewise alledged that yor Petr. became baile before John Perrey Esqr. one other of the Matys Justices of the peace for this County for the appearance of one Anne Wicksteade in this Court at the Sessions there also holden in December last As by the Severall Recognizance may appear That yor Petr. did not became baile for either of the said personalie being an absolute Stranger to each of them See that some ill person or persons unknowne to yor. peticoner heth personated yor. Petr: therein

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly Prays yor. Woripps to be pleased to order that he may be discharged from the said two severall Recognizance,
