To the Worshipful Justices now Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Burf: Camper and John Sherwood Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of April last Dorothy Hammock the Wife of Edward Hammock and her Two Children in Law (which the said Edwards Hammock had by a former Wife)Vizt. Edward aged fourteen Years on there abouts and Hannah aged Seven Years or there abouts were Removed from the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order humbly Appeal against the same and pray such Relief as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.

Wm. Drew Sollisr. for. Appellants.
