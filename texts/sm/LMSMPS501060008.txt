To the Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the Peace in Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of Nicholas Cormack

Sheweth

That your Petitir. was an Hyred Servant to one Colonel Thomas Kennady for Several yeares past, after the Rate of Seven pounds per Annum: and that there is due to your Petitir. for his said Service about Eleven pounds, which Said sum the said Colonel has often promised to pay unto yr. petir. But now absolutely Refuseth to do the same

yr. petr. therefore humbly prays Releife in the premisses, and as in Duly bound he shall ever pray Etc
