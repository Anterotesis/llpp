To the Right Honble. the Lord Mayr: And to the Recordr. & the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Bench

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Charles Egglestone a Prisoner in Wood Street Compler

That yor peticonr: hath been an Evidence against Willm Unitt Henry Manfield John Bevin & Elizabeth Wright for High Treason the last Sessions & all of them were convicted & three Exemted

That yor peticonr. is almost Starved for Want & hath noe friends to Assist him

Therefore yor peticonr most humbly prayes that he may be Bailed by Order of this Court as to yor Lordpps Shall Seem meet Etc

And yor peticonr Shall Ever pray Etc
