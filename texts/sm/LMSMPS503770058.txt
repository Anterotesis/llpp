To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter [...] Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Athallows in Lomlard Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the Hand and Seals of Thomas Quarrill Esqr and Samuel Gower Knt. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date of Nineteenth day of January 1746. Mary the Wife of George Richards (who is called in the said Order or Pass Warrant Mary Richards Singlewoman) was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary white Chappell in the said County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Alhallows in Lombard Street London; The Sd Justices Adjugment the said Parish of Alhallows in Lombard Street London to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners concerning themselves aggrieved by the Order or Pass Warrant Do Appeal to your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet; and that you worships will be pleased to grant an Order for the Church [...] Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White chappell to Attend this Honourable Court and ab [...] by the determination of the same touching this Appeal

And your Petr. shall Pray Etc

Frasalt Agt. for Appeal
