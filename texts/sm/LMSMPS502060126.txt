To ye. Rt: Worspfull this Maties Justices of ye: Peace for ye. County of Midd assembled in their Genall Quorter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Chapell. Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye: Pricnet of St: John Baptist in the Libty of ye Dutchy of Lancaster in the Strand in ye County of Midd

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands & Seals of William Gore & Stephen Brice Esqr: two of His Maties Justices of ye: peace for the Sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 24th Day of September last Mary Jones Wife of Charles Jones was remov'd from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said prcinct of St. John Baptist as the place of her Cast legall Settlemt:

Yor: Petrs: think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices Appeale to this Court & humbly pray That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Martin in ye Fields & all other parties concern'd may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

I Nicholas Sweeting one of the Churchwardens of ye parish of St. Martin in ye field to consent to allow this appeale Witness my hand

Nicholas Sweeting
