To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overssers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of St. George Walter Knight and Jno. Meard Esquire Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof beings of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of this Instant June Ann Payne Daughter of Robert Payne Deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Ann Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Wm. Head Agent for St. Martins

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the Said Two Justices and Pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint Some Day this present Sessions to hear and determine the Matter touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray
