To the Right Worsppl his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St Pancras in the Said County.

Sheweth

That Nathaniell & Caleb Stephenson (Sons of Benjamin Stephenson) were by Warrt under the hands & Seales of the Worsppl Sr Charles Lee & Thomas Ellis Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the Said County lately passed from the parish of St Giles in the Feilds to the said parish of St Pancras

That the said Benjamin Stephenson or his Said Sonns never had any legall Settlement in the said parish of St Pancras which your Peticoners are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court

Yor Petr therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poore of the Said parish of St Giles in the Feilds to appeare in this Court to Shew cause why the Said Nathaniel & Caleb Stephenson Should not be repossed to the Sd parish of St Giles in the Feilds

And yor petr Shall ever pray Etc
