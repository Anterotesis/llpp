To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter [...] Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chippenham in the County of Cambridge

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant Under the hand and Seal of Merry Tishmaker Esquire One of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Dated the Fifth day of January last Erasmus Minett was removed from the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Chippenham in the said County of Cambridge As the place of his the said Erasmus Minetts last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant of the said Justice Merry Tishmaker Esquire Appeal to this Court against such Order And Humbly pray Your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some Day in the Generall [...] Sessions for the hearing and Determining the same

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray and so forth
