To the Right Worshipfull her Majts. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in the sd County of Middx

The humble Petition and appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth

That Elizabeth Nutthall was by an Order Bearing date the One and Twentieth day of Aprill last under the hands and Seales of R: Smith Esqr. and John Stewart Esqr. Two of her Majts Justices of the Peace for the said County [...] removed from the Parish f St. Mary White Chappell in the said County unto your Petitioners said Parish of St Martin in the Fields to be maintained and provided for by which order your Petitioners (with humble Submission) think themselves wronged and aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore do humbly Pray Your Worships to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners as in duty bound shall ever pray. J: C

Will Orum} Church Warden

John Tilford} Overseers of the Poor
