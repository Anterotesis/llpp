To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace held by Adjourmnt in and for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a pass or Order of Removal under the Hands and seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of May last past William Bruit and Mary his Wife were Removed from the parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green aforesaid as the place of their last legal settlement whereby Your Petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Count against the same

Therefore Your Petitioners and Appellants pray that this Court will appoint a Day the next session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Edmonton and all persons concerned to Attend the said next session to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal And that your petitioners and Appellants may be Relieved in the premisses as this Court shall see meet

July 1763. And Your Petrs. & Appellts. shall ever pray Etc. Wm Slade} Churchwardens

John Padgham John Hawkins John Atkinson Oversrs. poor
