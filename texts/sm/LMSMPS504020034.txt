To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks hall in St. John Street in & for the sd. County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth in the County Surry

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands and Seals of James Croft and Gilbert Fouace Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of March last Elizabeth Pulsford Widow was remove from the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden [...] County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

waterfeild Attorney for The Appellants Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session To hear And Determine the Matter Touching the said Appeals

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
