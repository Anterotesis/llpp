To th Rt. Worshipfull His Majestys, Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That Rebeccah Barnoness Widow aged about twenty five Years a Parishioner of the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square is Chargeable to and Releived by the said Parish being poor & unable to work towards her maintenance being reputed to be Lunatick

That James Walter of St. John Street in the said County of Middx Factor and Father of the said Rebeccah Barnnoness is of a Sufficient Ability to releive & maintain her

your Petitioners therefore pray Your and Worships would be pleased to order the said James Walters to Contribute towards her Releif and maintenance

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

S. Parry Vestry
