To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of ye Peace for the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Willm. Rippon one of ye petty Constables of ye Parish of South mims in this County

Sheweth That in July last Past yor. Petr. obtained an Order of this Court [...] on William Cotesworth Esqr for ye Paymt. of Ten Pounds due to yor. Petr. at Midsumer then last Past for one qrter. of a years Salary for his loss of time and Expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants through this County; But yor. Petr. has not reced the said Sume or any Past thereof:

And That there is now due to yor. Petr. the Further Sume of Ten Pounds for another qrters. Salary (for the like Service) ending at Michmas. last Past

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worsps. would be pleased to make such Orders for the speedy Paymt. of the sd Sums; And for the Releif of yor. Petr. in the Prmisses, as to yor. Worsps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Zach: Clarke

Agent for the Petr.
