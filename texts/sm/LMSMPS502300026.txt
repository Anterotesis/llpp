To ye: Rt: Worshipll His Maties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Peirson Widow

Sheweth That [---] John Peirson yor: Petrs, late Husband was a Pentioner of this County at three pounds P ann for many years last past & dyed on the 24th. Day of August last

That Yor: Petr: is upwards of fourscore years of Age & left in a very poor & miserable Condicon

Yor Petr: therefore humbly prays Yor: Worships would be pleas'd to Order the paymt: of the Michaelmas Quarter pencon to ye Petr.

And Yor. Petr Shall ever pray Etc Eliz Peirson
