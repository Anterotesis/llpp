To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace of ye county of Middlesex at their Genl. Session of the peace held in for ye sd. County Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St. Mathew Bethnal Green in ye County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Order of Removal or pass Warrt. under the hands and seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of ye peace of the said county of Middlesex bearing date the thirteen Day of February last Susannah Debroader Wife of William Debroader and their two Children namely Mary Aged two Years and upwards and William aged Seven Months and upwards were Removed from the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County do the said parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green as the place of their last Legal Settlemt.

That Your Petrs. humbly Appleand themselves to the Aggrived by the said Order of Removal or Pass Warrt. and do therefore Appeal to this Session against ye Same

Your petrs. and Appellants therefore humbly pray that the Matter of their said Appeal may be taken into consideration And that a Day may be Appointed for the Judgment and Determination of the next Session touching the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may be Ordered then and there to Attend to hear and abide by the said Judgement

And that Your Petrs. and Appellts. may be Relived in ye premisses as the Nature of their said Esquires

And Your Petrs. and Appellts will ever pray Etc.

March 1st. 1760 Jno. Billington Soln. for St. Matthew Bethnal Green Appellts. }
