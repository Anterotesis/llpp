To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Mary Austin.

Sheweth That at the last Sessions of Goal Delivery of Bridewell your Petr: was remanded to hard Labour in Bridewell for the space of one p Month

That your Petr: hath been above five weeks in the said prison and hath several holes in one of her legs and two in hew head wch. for want of looking after are likely to ganger and your Petr: it Deteined will inevitably loose her leg and being ready to perish

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to Order that She. may be Discharged from her said Imprisonmt.

And Yor petr Shall Ever pray et:

Mr Austin
