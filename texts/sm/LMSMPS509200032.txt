To the Worshipful the Bench of Justices at the Quarter Session holden in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Holy Rhood in the Town and County of the Town of Southampton

Sheweth That by an Order bearing Date the Eighteenth day of December One Thousand Seven hundred and Ninety five under the Hands and Seals of William Jones and Robert Johnson Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) made on the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid Mary Randall the Wife of Robert Randall (gone from her) and their three Children to wit Mary Ann born 25th August One Thousand Seven hundred and Eighty Seven Elizabeth born 29th. May. One Thousand Seven hundred and Ninety one and Ann born 14th. January One thousand Seven hundred and Ninety three were removed

removed and conveyed from the said Parish of Saint James to the said Town of Southampton and delivered to your Petitioners the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of Holy Rhood Parish there

That your Petitioners conceive they are greatly injured aggrieved and oppressed by the said Order and therefore

Your Petitioners most humbly pray this Honorable Bench will quash the said Order and that you will be pleased to permit your Petitioners to lodge and respite an appeal for the Purpose

Wm. Woodford Robt Willies} Churchwardens

John Morse Mr SIlvester} Overseers
