To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the Said County.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney and Charles Friquet Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof is of the quorum bearing date the 23 day of April last Ann Robertshaw wife of Thomas Robertshaw was removed and convey- ed from and out of the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Sepulchre may appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the Said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc A Gliddon Sold for the Appets
