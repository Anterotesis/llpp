To the Right Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions held for the said County

The humble Petition and Appeal of Henry Turner of the Hamblet of Wapping Stepney Tobacco Pipe- -maker

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petitionr. was by order of Bastwick Johnson Esqr one of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx to pay but 1s & Month to the use of the Poor of the said Hamblet yett notwithstanding your Petition stands now rated 1s..10d P Month which he is not able to Pay being but a Poor lan bouring man and hath 4 Children to maintaine

Therefore yor. Petitionr humbly prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to make such order for your Petitioner releife in the prmisses as to yor Worshipps Shall Seem meet

And Shall Pray Etc
