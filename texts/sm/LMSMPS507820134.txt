To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chosely in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the fifteenth day of April now last past Richard Cornwall and Elizabeth his Wife were on the Nineteenth day of the same instant. April removed from the Town of Uxbridge in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Chosely in the said County of Berks as the last legal place of their Settlement By which your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray your Worships will be [...] leased to hear and determine the Matter touching this Appeal and give them such relief in the Premises as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
