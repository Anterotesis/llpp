To the Worshipfull Councellor Lane and therest of his Majestys Justices of this Honble Bench

The humble Petition of Catherine Taylor

Sheweth That your Petitioner has attended Several times on this Honble Court to withdraw her. Recognizance but being very all weak, and not able to wait, and being unacquainted with the Custom of the Court missed her Call

Therefore your Petr. most humbly Prays that your Worships in your tender Goodness will consider her, and order her to be called in Order to have her Recognizance to be with drawn

And yor Petr. as in Duty bound shall pray Etc
