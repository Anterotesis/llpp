The King is Plt & Andrew Maxwell Deft} To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices Of the Peace now Sitting at Hickshall

The humble peticon of Andrew Maxwell

Sheweth That your peticoner stands Indicted for an Assault upon Ann White to Which Indictment your peticoner Traversed and put in Bayle the last Sessions.

That your Peticoner Intended to try the said Traverse this present Sessions But your Peticoner is Advised It is not safe for your peticonr to try the said Traverse Two of your Peticoners Most Materiall Witnesses being at Present not to be found As by your Peticoners Affidt Annex't Appeares.

Your peticoner therefore humbly Prays an Order of this Court to Deferr the Tryall of his said Traverse untill the next Sessions.

And your Peticoner shall ever Pray Etc
