To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the peace holden at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County of Middlesex Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Burnett Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order under the hands and seals of Thomas Lane Esquire and Paul Vaillant Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of May 1767 your Petitioner was appointed overseer of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes

Your Petitioner conceiving himself aggrieved by the said order of the said two Justices of the peace Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray that this Court will appoint a day in this present General Session of the peace for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal and that the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes and also other persons concerned may they Attend this Court to hear and Abide [---] the Judgment and determination thereof Bucking the sd. Appeal

Wm. Bray for the Appellant
