To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Pancras in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable George Lord Carpenter and Francis Burton Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the fourth day of December Instant Peter Turvie Singleman aged about forty four years was removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Mary le Bone in the said County to the said parish of St. Pancras as the place of his last legall Settlement

Humphy Elmes Sold for ye. Appellts. Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the peace, Appeale to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this prevent Sessions for the hearing and Determining of the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
