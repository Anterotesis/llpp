Middlx To the worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and Benjamin Burroughs Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (one being of the Quorum) the County of Middlesex bearing Dale the twelfth Day of May last past Elizabeth Wills aged about fourteen Years was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint Some Day in this present Session to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeale

And Yor. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Wm. Head Soll [...] .for the Appellts:
