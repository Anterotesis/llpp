Middx ss

To the Worshipfull Wm: Cooper Esqr: and the rest of his Maties: Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx now Assembled att Hicks's Hall St: John Streett

Sheweth The humble peticon of Mary Barber als Ruffitt

That your peticoner stands Indicted upon the Comission of Oyer and Terminer for the County of Middx aforesd. in May or June Sessions last for keeping all Ill Governed and Disorderly House and for Want of Bail was Comitted in Custody of the Keeper of Clerkenwell Bridewell where she is now and hath been about Sixteen Weeks Confined in a very Deplorable Condition

Wherefore your petitioner most humbly prays your Worships Orball Order to the Keeper of Bridewell aforesd. to bring your Petr: before your Worships in Order to Appear and plead to the said Indictment and that upon Notice given to the Prosecutor that your Petr. might be at Liberty to Try the Travers of the sd. Indictment upon Fryday next at the Sitting of the Court

And your Petr: shall ever pray Etc

Dat 28the Aprill 1731
