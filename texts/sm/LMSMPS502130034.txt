To the Rt. Worshpll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Saviours Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Order under the hands Seals of [...] John Offley & [...] [...] James Clifferow Esqr two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Twenty first day of this instant month of August Mary Topping & Bland Topping Children of Benjamin & Com Topping were removed from and out of the Parish of Heston in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Saviours Southwark in the County of Surry as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoner think themselves Aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices & hope to make it appear that the said Mary & Bland Toppinghave not any legall Settlement in the sd parish of St. Saviours Southwark in the County of Surry And therefore humbly Appeal against the sd. Order to this Honble Bench.

Therefore Yor. Petr. humbly pray Yor. Worspps to be plased to appoint a day for the Churchwardens & Over seers of the Poor of the Pish of Heston in the County of Middx aforesd to Attend this Honble Bench & to stand to & able such Order as shall be made upon hearing the sd Appeal

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
