To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Robert Midford Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx. (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twenty fourth Day of August last Arabella Abbott Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx: to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the Place of the last legal-Settlement whereby Yor. Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeale to yor. Worships against the Judgemt. of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeale.

And your Petrs. shall ever pary Etc.
