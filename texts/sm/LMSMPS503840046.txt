To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the peace by Adjournment Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth: That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Ludbey and William Pickering Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th. day of December 1747 Anne Brogden, Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same.

Therefore Humbly pray this Court That the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto your Petitioners and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for this County

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.

S: Parry Agent for Appellants
