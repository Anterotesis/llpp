To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Anne within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Pickering and Hen Trent Esqrs. two of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the City and Libty of Westmr. (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of June last Thomas Wells and Elizabeth his wife were removed from the parish of St James within the said Liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St Anne whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the said Pass warrant and the adjudgment of the said two Justices & pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St James within the Libty of Westmr. and all persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yr. Petitioners shall pray Etc Agent for the Appells

Dan: Jones
