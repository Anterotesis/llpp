The Humble: Petition of Nichol: Stoker new Prison aprill ye 14th 1716

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner having been a prisoner here three weeks & my Adversary not appearing nor any bill being found against me I hope your Worships will take it into to Consideration and to remitt my former Sentence to going to Bridewell, I having a wife and family being almost Starved through my Confinement already and your Petitioner as in Duty bound

Shall Ever Pray

Nichls. Stoker
