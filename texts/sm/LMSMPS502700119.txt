To the Rt Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ganesbrough in the County of Lincoln.

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of William Gore & Robt Stiles Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Midd (Quor unus) bearing date the twentieth day of July last Past Ann Green (who is menconed in the said Warrt or Order to be aged about 63 years and to be the Widow of Robt Green deced) was removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the sd. Parish of Ganesbrough as the last legal place of an Settlemt of the said Ann Green

Yor. Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt. or Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray the Court would adjorn the hearing of this their Appeal untill the next General or General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for this County and would appoint some day at the same [---] Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Fields aforesd. and all other Party's concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And Yor Petrs shall ever Pray Etc

Wm Morland Soll for the Appellants.
