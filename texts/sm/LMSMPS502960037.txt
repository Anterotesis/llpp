To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County October the 13th: 1733.

The Humble Petition of John Humble of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner is Charged towards the releif Of the Poor of the said Hamlet four Shillings P Quarter, besides Additional rates, Your Peticoner being Aggreived by the said rate Humbly Appeals against the same, And Prays Yo Worships to Grant him Such releif as to you shall seem Meet And yo Petioner Shall Ever Pray Etc.

John Kimber
