To the Worshipful the Chairman & the Rest of the Justices of the peace now sitting as Hicks's Hall in & for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of John Busby Hatter

Humbly Sheweth That James Shorts has been this Morning tryed & convicted for feloniously stealing Beaver & Cony Wool, to the Property of yr. Petr.

That yr. Petr. has at several times lost a great Quantity of such Wool, but has not been able to detech any of the persons who stole the same before this Time.

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays ye. Worships That what ever Punishemt. ye Worships are pleased to inflict on the sd. Short, that, in Terror of others, it may be executed as near the place of ye Petrs. Abode, wch. is near Bell Dock in Wapping, as to ye Worships shall think fit.

And yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
