To the Honble the Bench of Justices

The humble Petition of Isabella Code

Sheweth That your Petitioner was a hired Servant to Elizabeth Osborne (who is house keeper to Sr Henry Bellassis) and that Sr Henry Bellassis Lady ordered the sd Elizth Osborne to Sausfie her for her to Service which thee refuseth to pay the thee faithfully promised your Petr £6 P Ann and now would putt her of with £5 P Annum

Your Petr therefore most humble Prays yor Wpps to order the said Elizth Osborne to pay her the sd Wages your Poore petr haveing foure Children to maintaine, and Cechassand being now in their Mates Service

And yor. Petr shall ever Pray Etc.
