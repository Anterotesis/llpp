To the Rt Worsppll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Howett Constable of Endfield in the sd County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary Due to your Petitr: at Christmas Last amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds for passing & Relieving of Vagrants:

That yor Petitr: nowstands indebted to her Majesty for Excise in a large Sum of Money Which he is not able at present to pay Therefore stands in great want of the said Money

Yor Petitr: therefore humbly prays. your Worshipps will be pleased to order that he may be paid the sd Moneys As unto yor Worspps Shall Seeme meete

And Yor Petitr: Shall Ever pray Etc:

John Howett
