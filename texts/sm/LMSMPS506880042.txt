To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx May it please Your Worships

The humble petition of Widdow Smith inhabitant St Andrews Parish Holborne that your Worships will be pleased to take [...] into your consideration ye under written matter viz That unfortunately takeing a child to nurse, the sara Early daughter of John Early Glover, of the said Parish he being gon from his habitation & the Mother being dead the child lyes wholly upon the charge of their abovesaid Widdow, therefore she humbly intreats your Worships to consider her circumstance haveing a child of her own & nobody to take care of it but herself she therefore, with sub- mission to your Worships better Judgement presumes the Parish better able to maintain this than her self
