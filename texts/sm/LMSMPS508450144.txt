To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of The Chuchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Corsham in the County of Wilts

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Stephen Cole and William Heckford Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and one of them of the Quorum bearing date the thirty first day of March in the Year of our Lord One thousand seven hundred and Eighty Nine Isaac Taylor with his Wife named Elizabeth and his Child named Sarah aged Eight Months were Removed from and out of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Corsham in the said County of Wilts as the Place of the lawful Settlement of them the said Isaac Taylor and his said Wife and Child whereby your Petitioners apprehend themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant and Pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned untill the next General Session of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex

Chetham Solicitor for the Appellants}
