To ye: Worpll: Lane Esqr: Chairman of ye Sessions one of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye: County of middx & to ye: rest of his Majties Justices of Peace there

The Humble Peticon of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the County of Kent

Humbly Sheweth That by Order under the hands & Seales of Tho: Jones Esqrs and Henry Dodson Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of ye: Peace for the said County of Middx for the Hamlett of Ratclift in the sd: County of Midddlesex to the said Parish of Saint Nicholas Deptford in the said County of Kent as the Place of their last legal Settlement, Against which Order & Adjudication yor: Peticoners do Appeal as being agreived and prejudiced thereby

Wherefore your Peticoners do most Humbly pray that yor: Worships will Appoint a day for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal some time this Session

And they shall ever pray Etc. T [...] [---] Robert Crundwell John Wescott} Church wardens James Humpy Isaac Stacey} Overseers
