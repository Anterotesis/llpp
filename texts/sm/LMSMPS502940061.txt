To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex now in their Sessions at Hick's Hall:

The humble Petition of Mary Morrin

Sheweth That your Petr. and her husband unfortunately differing your Petr. procured him to be Confined in New Prison at Clerkenwell on the Second of August last

That throug his long Confinement there he had the Goal Distemper and now is in a Languishing and ill State of health

That your Petr. and her said husband are reconciled and hope to Continue so

Your Petr. therefore most humbly beggs that your Worships will Order her said husband to be released from his said Unhappy Confinement

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
