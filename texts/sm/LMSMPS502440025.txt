To the Rts Worshipll. His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of John Martin and John Fortescue Churchwardens of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County on behalf of themselves and the rest of the Inhabitants of the said Parish

Sheweth That Dorothy Hale an Inhabitant of the said Parish is a Poor Antient Woman unable to Maintain herself whereby She is become Chargeable to the said Parish and So likely to continue

That Thomas Hale of the same Parish Chandler Son of the said Dorothy Hale is a Person of Sufficient Ability to Maintain his Said Mother at his own Charge

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Pray that the said Thomas Hale May be Ordered at his own Charge to Maintain and Provide for the said Dorothy Hale his Mother in such Manner as to Yor Worships Shall seem Meet

And Yor Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.

John Martin John Fortescue
