To the Right Worps the Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth The humble Peticon of James Feild of the parish of Finchley in the Said County.

That your Peticoner hath lived in the Said parish and County the sd Fourscoore yeares and upwards and the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore there refuseth to allow your Peticoner any thing towards his releife and mainley name And he being very poore and aged Fourscoore yeares & upwards and altogether past his labour is libly to perish unless comisserated and releived by your Worpps Charity.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays your Worpps to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Said parish may allow your Peticoner such pencon towards his releife and mainley name as unto your Worpps shall Seem meet

And your Peticoner Shall ever Pray Etc.
