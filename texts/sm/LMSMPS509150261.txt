To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Oveseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Wood Junior and Thomas Scott Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Nineteenth Day of September now last past Thomas Revel and Elizabeth his Wife and their Three Children vizt. Maria Ann aged about eight Years Elizabeth aged about four Years and Ann aged about five months were ordered to be removed and conveyed and the said Elizabeth the Wife of the said Thomas Revel and the said three children before named were accordingly removed and conveyed from the Parish of Laleham in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave Southwak as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Revel and Elizabeth his Wife and their said three Children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining this their said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc

Sherwood Canterbury Square Solr.

Octr. 26th. 1795.
