To ye. Rigtt: Worspll: his Maties: Justices of ye. peace for ye County of Middx:

The humble peticon & appeale of Nathll: Hoskins of ye. parish of St: [...] ard Shoreditch in ye. Sd County

Sheweth That your peticoner is rated Three Shillings Three pence P: [...] weekly to ye. rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. Sd parish & that by reason of his poverty she is altogether unable to pay ye. Same & that he finds it very hard be maintaine himselfe & his wife;

Your Peticoner therefore humble prayes out Order of this Honable: Court that he may be releived in ye. premisses as to your Worsps: Shall Seem meet.

And Yr: peticoner Shall ever pray Etc:

Nathll: Hoskins

Middx ss: Pursuant to ye. Order of ye. Genrll: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace wth: written We whose names are hereunto Subscribed Two of his Maties: Justices of ye. peace in ye. Sd Order named haveinge taken upon vs ye. heareinge & examinacon of ye. Appeale & Complt: of ye. peticoner Nath: Hoskins wthin menconed ye. churchwardens Overseers of ye. poor & Severall other of ye. Inhitants of ye. Pish of St: Leonard Shoreditch beinge prsent; and upon heareinge of all partyes & duely Consideringe ye. peticoners Complt: as alsoe ye. reasons & allegacons given & made on all Sides the report our oppinions to this Honable: Court; That ye. peticoner Nathll: Hoskins ought to be eased & abated Three halfe pence P: weeke to all rates made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. Sd parish, & that he ought to pay Three halfe pence P: Weeke to ye. Sd rates & noe more all partyes accquiessinge, Given under our hands this 18th: day of Augt: ano Dond 1720;

Alex: Pitfeild Sam Benson
