To the right Worppfull his Maties Justices [...] Peace of the County of Middx at their Genall Quarter Sessions now Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Henry Street Benjm Tillard and William Cope Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in this County for the year 1726

Sheweth That Your Peticonr have Passed their Accounts as Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish in a full Vestry according to the Custome of their Predecessor made Applicacon ton to the Justices of the Peace at their Petty Sessions held at Edgworth in this County for a Confirmacon and Passing of your Petr said Accounts but the said Justices refused to doe the same and ordered your Petr to Apply to this Court for your Worpps direccons herein

Yor Petr therefore most humbly Pray that this Court will give such direccons in the Prmisses as to your Worpps shall Seem meet

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Henry Street Benj: Tillyard William Cope
