The humble: Peticoner of Mary Rust of the Pish of St Marg Islington in the Sd Countie

Sheweth. That yor Petr: husband was bourne & lived all his life time in the sd Pish But since this Three yeares last hath two small Children her husband haveing a very sonh. Legg & hath not Whare with to Subsist hath been with the Church wardens & Overseers of the poore of the sd pish of St Mary Islington & received but Two shillings of six pence for about in months Subsistence.

Yor: Petr: therefore prayed yor. Worpps to be Pleased to [...] allow her W [...] entry tell her husband is able be worke as yor Worpps shall Seem meete

And yor Petr shall pray Etc

Mary [x] Rust her mark.
