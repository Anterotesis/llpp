To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thos. Preston and Jno. Bosworth Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the Twelfth Day of April last Edward Woodbridge was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceve themselves aggrieved thereby and humbly appeal to the Judgment of this Court against the same And pray that this Court will be pleaded to hear and determine the said Appeal And that all persons concern'd may abide the Judgment and Determination thereof And that Your Petitioners may be relieved in the premisses as to this Court shall seem Meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Wade Junr. Sollr. for Appellts.
