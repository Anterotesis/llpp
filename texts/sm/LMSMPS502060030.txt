To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions held for the sd County of Middx at Hicks Hall in St. John Street wth. in the Sd. County on Thursday ye 11th: day of January 1721

Wee the Church:Wardens and Overseers of the poor and other the substantiall Inhabitants of the Parish of Ealing als. Lealing in the sd. County of Middx Do humble Certifie unto this Court That James Evered having Servsd her late Majty Queen Anne and his Prsent Majty King George as a Soldier for Seven Years & upwards is non disabled from getting a Livelyhood for himself and Family that he hath a Wife and Children unprovided for and that he is an Object of Compassion and a persons who highly deser [...] the pension of this County. In testimony whereof we have hereunto Subscribed or Hands the Eight day of January Anno Dem 1721.

John Freind Robert Browne} Church:Wardens

John Harvest Richard Osborn} Overseers of ye poor

Darell Deak S Anderson William neatharwood Tho Salsever Thomas neatharwood Joseph Hillier William Duckat Hen Browne Robart Cheesman Saml proise

Richd: Overs Jno Sviett James Barrett Charles Cheesman
