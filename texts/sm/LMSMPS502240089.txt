To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace fro the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the Sd County

The Humble Petion and Appeal of the Chuchwardens And Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Buckingham In the County of Bucke

Sheweth That by Order or Warrant of Removall Under the Hands And Seals of John Milner and John Richardson Esqr. two of his Majestices Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlesex Elizabeth Webster Aged About Twelve Years And Mathew Webster Aged about Seaven Years Children of Thomas Webster were Removed from the Parish of St Gyles in the Fields in the Said County of Middlesex To th Parish of Buckingham in the County of Bucke by which Order Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

And therefore Humbly Appeal Therefrom and Pray a Day for hearing the Said Appeal
