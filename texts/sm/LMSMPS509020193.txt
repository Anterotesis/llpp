To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Helen in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Herbt. Mayo and John Staples two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty second day of July last Frances Lipman Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the said Parish of Saint George in the sd County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Helen as the Place of the lawful Settlement of the said Frances Lipman

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order

Your Petitioners therefore pray that the said Appeal may be head and determined at this present General Quarter Session of the peace and that your Petitioners may be relived against the said Order

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc
