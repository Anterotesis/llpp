To the Worship ful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the Said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Fawley in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Timbell and William Rugge Esquires two of his Majesty's [...] Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one being of the Quourm) bearing date the twenty fourth day of June last Susannah Prestage the Widow of Joseph Prestage with their two Children to wit Elizabeth aged 6 Years and Joseph aged 4 Years were removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Fawley in the County of Bucks as the Place of their last legal Settlement the Fawley in the Order is called in the County of Oxford whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

[...] to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in the next Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square and all Persons concerned do then attend this Appeal

And your Petitioners shallever pray

Thomas Cooper Junr

Solicitor for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Fawley in the County of Bucks
