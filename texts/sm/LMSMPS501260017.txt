Desember ye 7 in 1711

Sur this is to A quaints your Worship that the parish of Saint tander holborn hath seased all my Creaks for ye Se [...] of my Childern for that Thane won Son that has bin bound prentis About Six mon's and his master has Run Away from him and Left to the wide world Soe that the Church wordiens of the Same parrish will tak no manner of Care Exsept your worrish Speadey Care and in Soe dewing your pettishner will cuer

Jury Simon Rabaskey
