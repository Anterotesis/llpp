To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St.Luke Chelsea in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Joseph Morer and H. Cosser Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid ( one when of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Second day of january 1797 Peter Burnett was removed and Conveged from and out of the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster to the said County to the said Parish of St. Luke Chelsea as the Place of the last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudiction of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray the Your Worship will be pleased to Appoint a day [...] for the hearing and Determining their said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Herb Robertson Solr. for the appellants.
