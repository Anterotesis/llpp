To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of Farmer Casse

Sheweth That Your Petitr. lyes under your Worship's pleasure for a Nuisance in Boyling of Horse Flesh and as he was bayld out of New Prison I mis last Sessions to traverse his Indictmt. on the sd. Nuisance

That your Petitrs. Landlord Suris the Eali [...] wanted nothing but to be rid of your Pentr. to improve the Tenemt. And to make the benefit another way

Your Petitr. most humbly desires that he may be call'd to the Barr to traverse his indictmt. [...] And to answer such Accupations as may he alleadgd agt him

And Your Petitr. Shall ever pray Etc.

N:B Notice was given to the prsecuters as may be Sworn [...] .
