To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Blackmore and Herbert Mayo esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of July last past Thomas Conroy aged five Years and Simon Conroy aged three Years and six Months the Children of Simon Conroy were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Conroy and Simon Conroy.

Your Petitioner conceived themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a Day in the present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your petitioners Etc

Saml. Malbone Solr. for St. Paul Shadwell
