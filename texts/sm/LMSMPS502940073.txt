To the Right Worshipfull his maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwar -dens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

Sheweth That William Martin a poor Child of the said Parish was bound apprentice by the said Parish to Henry Price of the sd Parish of Aldgate Taylor to Learn his Art but the said Henry Price is since run away above this Eight Months and left the said Apprentice William Martin a Charge to the said Parish which Continues a great Charge thereunto

Your Petitioners most Humbly prays your Worships would be pleased to Discharge the said Indenture that the said Appren- tice may be bound to another master

And your Petrs: as in Duty bound shall ever pray and so forth
