To the worpfull her Maties Justices of ye peace for the County of Middex

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Ippolites in the County of Hertford

Sheweth

That Elizabeth Ramsey Widdow together with three of her Children vizt Elizabeth, Ephraim & Jane were by a warrant under the hands & Seale of Charles Bonython Esqr. & Sr. John Woolfe Knt. two of her Matiet. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx, bearing date the Seven & twentieth day of February last, passed from & out of the parish of Fryerne Barnett in the said County of Middx to the parish of Ippolites aforesd

Script [...] Yor. Peticonrs. finding themselves agrieved with the Judgment of the Said two Justices, Humbly Appeal to this honble Court, And beg Yors. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint on a day for the Church wardens & Overseers of the poore of Fryerne Bennett aforesaid to attend this- honble Court to hear & abide the final End & determinacon of this- appeal

And Yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray & Etc.

Nicholas Marshall James Mardell
