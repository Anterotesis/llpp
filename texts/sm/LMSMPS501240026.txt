To the Right Worpll. her Maty. Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Edmund Aspernall

Sheweth That one Mary Kevill and Eliz: Hart are now bound over in this Honble Court this present Sessions for buying and selling of Stolen goods they knowing them to be Stolen.

That Jane Kevill the princepall witness cannot appear this seserd Session in order to prosecute the sd Kevill and Hart

Yor. Petr Therefore humbly Pray. yor Worshpps to be pleased to order that the sd Mary Kevill and Eliz: Hart may not withdraw their Recognizance this prsent Sessions but to respite the same hill next Session

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
