To the Rt. Worsip full His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in the County of Middx & new in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth That Mary Jeffreys an Infant aged about Four Years was by Order Dated the 15th. day of December 1725 under the Hands and Seales of Henry Lowthy and Jacob Harvey Esqrs. Two of HIs Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (Quor unus) removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary Islington in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields

Yor. Petitioners humbly conceive themselves agrieved thereby and Appeale from the Judgemt. of the Said Two Justices to the Judgemt. of this Honble Court and Crave a Day of Hearing

And Yer. Petrs. Shall over Pray Etc
