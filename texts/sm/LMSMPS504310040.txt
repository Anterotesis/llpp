To the Right Worshipful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Richard Lewis Apprentice unto John Lewis Lamatte of the Parish of Saint Giles In the County aforesaid Jeweller

Most Humbly Sheweth. That your Petr. was By Indenture Bearing Date the Seventh Day of September In the Year of Our Lord One Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty eight Bound apprentice unto the abovenamed John Lewis Lamatte To Serve him for the Spawand Term of Eight Years relation being thereunto had by the Indenture Annexed may more fully appear During which Time your Petr. hath Faithfully Serve his Said Master Notwithstanding your Petrs. Said Master hath Cruelly Beat and Misused your Petr. whereby He is much hurt threatned your Petrs. Life at Divers times and with Dangerous Weapons Attempted the Same.

Septemr 10th. 1754 [...] Attorney for the Apprentice Your Petr. therefore most Humbly Pray your Worsps would Be Pleased To Order that his said Master John Lewis Lamatte he being Bound by Recongnizance Before Justices Sr. Laurance One of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County To Answer the Complaint of his Said Apprentice to attend Some Day this present Session to [...] Shew Cause why Your Petr. should not be Discharged from his Said Apprenticeship And that all persons Concerned Do Attend this Court to give Evidence of and concerning the premisses.

And Yr. Petr. Shall Ever Pray Richard Lewis
