To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order Under the hands & Seals of Sr. Thomas Cross Bart. and Thomas Railton Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing Date the Fifth day of Febry. last Susannah Lincoln Aged about Nine years was Removed from the Parish of St. John the Evangelist in the City of Westmr. in the County of Middx Aforesd. to the said Parish of St. James As the place of her last legall Settlemt. Whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved

And therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the sd. Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petr. shall may Etc

Saml. Seddon Vestry Cl
