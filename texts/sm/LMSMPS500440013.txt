To the Honor Bench at Hicks Hall the Peter of Mary Browne Widdow of St Andrewes houlbourne in the Liberty Above bars humly

Sheweth That your Petir is a person that has Lived very Well and bin an Ancient house Keeper in ye the Saide Liberty and for Many years hath pade Scott and Let to the poore but by many Misfortunes [...] besides ye infirmities of Ould Agge being Above [...] Eight years of Agge and being [...] poverty Pone Petr [...] of said Liberty [...] [...] from [...] and yor Petr

That She Majty have [...] what the hath forme [...] linn A Lowed or otherwise what this Honarable Court Shall think fitt and your Peter as in duty bound

Shall Ever Pray
