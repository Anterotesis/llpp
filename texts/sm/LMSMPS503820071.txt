To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Henry Trent and G. Vaughan Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing date the 13th. day of October 1747 Sarah Walker Wife of John Walkers (gone from her) and their Child named William aged about one Months [...] removed from the Parish of St. James Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc Parry Agent for St. George Hanover Square
