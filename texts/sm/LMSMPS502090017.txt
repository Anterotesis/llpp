To the Right Worspll. His Majties Justices of & Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Session.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of ye Parish of Chels [...] in ye said County of Middx

Humbly Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Gore & Stephen Brice Esqr. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor. Un) Dated ye 26th day of December 1722. Anne Coffee Wife of William Coffee and their Child Anne Aged almost 6 Years was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Chelsea as the last legal Settlement of the sd.Anne Coffee and her said Child, whereby the sd. Parish of Chelsea think themselves aggrieved

Your Peticonrs. therefore humbly prayes this Court will appoint a day for hearing the said Appeal and that all Psons concerned may attend at ye same time

And your Peticonrs shall ever pray Etc

J Holmes Clk Vest. [x]
