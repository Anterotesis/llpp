To the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourn'd General Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant where the Hands and Seals of J: Hale and William Hyde Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth Day of September 1779 Susannah Poynee Wife of John Prince (now in his Majestys Sea Service) and their two Children to Ms Elizabeth aged four Years and Sarah aged two Years were removed and conveyed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid from and out of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields to the said Liberty of the Rolls as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant humbly appeal against the same to your Worships and Pray refief in the Premises And that a Day in this precent Session may be appointed for the hearing and determining the said Appeal
