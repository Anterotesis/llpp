To the Worppll: her Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds att their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon and Appeale of the ChurchWardens & Overseers of the poore of St Bridgett als Brides parish London

Sheweth that by Warrant under the hands and sealed of Joseph Short and Richd. Browne Esqrs. two of her Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Midds (Quor unus) dated the fourty day of September Anno 1708. Francis plenty a Child about eight years old Son of Richard Plenty & Mary his wife was past on Yor. petrs. as the place of his last legall Settlement from the parish of St. Sepulchers in the said County of Midds

That Yor. petr. cannot find out any Settlement that the said Francis plenty hate in the sd. parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London and therefore humbly conceive themselves agreived by the order of the said two Justices

Yor. petr. therefore humbly Appeale to yor. Worpps and hope the said Warrant shall be reverted.

And yor. petrs. shall pray Etc.
