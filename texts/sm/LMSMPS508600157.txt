To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and William Quarrill Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh Day of January Instant Sarah Chetham Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn at the place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be reserved to them until the next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County for hearing and determining the same And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel be then and there required to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Arthur Gliddon Sollr. for the Appellant
