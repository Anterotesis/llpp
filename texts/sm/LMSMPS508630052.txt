To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Charles Friquet and Joseph Faikney Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 2nd Day of April 1791 Martha Shearman, Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Count against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners as in Duty Bound will ever pray

Stephen Wright E Berry Smith Thos Egerton J Massive J Stately Jno Matthew G: Collett R Bowen} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor
