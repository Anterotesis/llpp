To the Worll. her Majts: Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr: in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon of Johanna painter

Sheweth.

That yor. petr. bound her Son Richard painter an Apprtice to one William peirson of Westmr. aforesaid Lordweyner to learne his Art on the 18th. day of May last, Notwithstanding which his said Master refuse's to maintain his said Apprtice or to learne him his Art, pretending that he has not work to provide him with.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray's yor. Wops. to grant yor. Warrant to bring the said William peirson before this Worll. Bench to answer the Complaint of yor. Petr.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
