To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of [...] the County of M [...]

The humble Petition and assault [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Part of St. Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney and Chas. Triquet Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middx (one who of being of the quorum [...] date ye. 22nd: days of Sepulchre 1791. One Magaret Handling Singlewoman was removed and Convey'd from and out of the sd. Par. of St. Sepulchre Middx to the Part. of St. Sepulchre London afd. as the Place of her last lagal Settlement

Your Petrs.. thinking themselves aggrieved by the sd. Order or pass Warrant of the sd. two Justices appeal to the Court agst the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be heard at [...] Quarter prd [...] Session of the Peace [...] holden and for the sd. County of Middx
