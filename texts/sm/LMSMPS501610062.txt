To the Right Worpll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & appeall of the Church Wardens & Overseers [...] the Parish of Ashford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by an order under the hands & Seales of Henry Box & Timothy Laney Esqrs, two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Thomas Benjamin Labourer Martha his wife & their three children (Vizt) Jacob aged tenn yeares & upwards Thomas aged about nine years & Christian aged about two yeares were on the fourth day of february last part removed from the parish of Chiswick in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Ashford in the County of Kent as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. thinking themselves aggrieved by the order of the said Justices humbly appeald there from & pray that this Court will be pleased to order the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Chiswick & all other parties concerned to allend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this appeale

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall prays
