To the Rt. Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall.

The humble Petition and Appeal George Sherwin of the Parish of St. John Baptizt, in the City of London

Sheweth

That By Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Hayton and Robert Tothill Esqr nor of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the [...] County of Middx (Quor un) bearing Date the 12th Day of February last your said Peticoner is by the said Warrt. or Order Charg'd and Adjudged to be the reputed father of A Female Bustard Child born of the Body of Easter Wakeling, in the parish of St. James Clarkenwell in the said County on the 19th Day of October last [...] And Orderly to pay Divers Sums of Money for and towards the Maintainance and keeping of the said Female Bastard Child, By which sd Warrt or Order your Petr. humbly conceive he is Wronged and Aggreived

Therefore humble Appeal agt. the same unto this Court And prays this honoble Court to appoint same Day in the next Gent Quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for the said County; And that he may be Relieved in the Premises. As to your Wisdoms shall seem Meet

And your Pet: hath Pray Etc

Strokes Sob: of Petr
