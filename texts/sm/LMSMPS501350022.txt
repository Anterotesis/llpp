To the Rt Worspll Her Mats Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of James Misson Esqr.

Sheweth That your Peticoner Rents a House in the Parish of [...] in the said county of Middx But is Rated for the Poores Assess =ment much more then in Proportion to the Rent which hee Pays

That your Peticoner finding himselfe aggreived by the said Poores Rate as being much more Charged there in any of his Neighbors in the like [...]

Therefore your Peticoner humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to grant him such Releife in the Premises as in your great Wisdom shall seem meet and Convenient

And your Peticoner Shall Ever pray Etc
