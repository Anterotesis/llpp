Middlesex To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the rest of the Justices in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of William Pentlow Keeper of New Prison Clerkenwell

Most Humbly Sheweth that the said Prison is and for some Time Past hath been greatly out of Repair and if not very soon Repaired will become much Worse

Therefore hopes Your Worships will take the Matter under Your Consideration and Order such Repair to be done as Your Worships may think Newparys

And Your Petitioner will wer Pray Wm. Pentlow
