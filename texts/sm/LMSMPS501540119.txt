To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Medstone

Sheweth

That yor. Peticoner is assess'd by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County two shillings and six pence P Quartr. towards the Releife of the poor of the said parish for the year ensuing Which said Assessment is after the Rate of Five pounds p Annu (at two shillings in the pound) Wch. said Rate is charg'd upon the sd parish towards the Releife aforesd

That yor. Peticoners Rent is but Three pounds P Annu Therefore he thinks himselfe very much agreiv'd by the said Assessmt.

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps to order that he may be releiv'd in the said prmisses

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
