To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Harrow in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Hassell Junr. and Paul Mombray Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth day of January last [---] Elizabeth Perry Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Great Stanmore in the said County to the said Parish of Harrow as the Place of her last Legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrived

Your Petitioners therefore therefore Appeal to this Court against the said Order and humbly pray that your Worship's will be pleased to Appoint some day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal and to Order that all Persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

[...] Thos. Gibbs Samll. Slaughter} Churchwardens

Jno. Edlyn Overseers Wm Ratcliff Thomas Prier
