To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in the Genl Sessions Assembled at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James Westminster

Sheweth

That James Dibble Mary his wife and his two Children to wit Sarah Aged One Year and Daniel About three Months were Removed from the Parish of Twickenham in the County of Middx by Virtue of A Pass Warrant or Order Under the hands And Seals of Jo:D:Birkhead & James Children Esqrs Two of [...] his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the [...] said County of Middx to the said Parish of St James as the Place of their last Legal Settlement Whereby yr Petitioners think themselves Agreived

Therefore humbly Pray that this Court wills Please to Order a Day for the hearing and Determining the Said Appeal and Yr Petitions: will Ever Pray

The Pickles Edmd Hayes Thos Heaford
