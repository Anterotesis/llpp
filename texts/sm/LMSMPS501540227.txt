To the Rt Worpll his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Buttolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Thirtieth day of June last past one Thomas Hinton was removed and passed from the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx unto the said parish of St Buttolph without Bishops gate London alleadging the said parish to be the place of his last legal Settlement That your petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or pass Order and do most humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom:

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear in the prmisses And order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County may appear before you and hear and abide the determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petr Shall ever Pray & Etc
