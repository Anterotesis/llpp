Middx To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Gen Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Petition of James Ashley and John Priest Constables of the Township of New Brentford in the said County

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioners have served the said Office of Constable in the said Township in the Years 1753 & 1754 and Whereas James Clitherow Esqr. Lord of the Mannor of Baston cum Brentford in which the said Township Doth lie hath not lately Held or is likely to Held a Court Leet wherein Constables may be Chosen to Succed them

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that this Court will Discharge them from their said Office, And Appoint Two other fit persons of the said Township Named in the List Annexed or such Others as Your Worship shall think proper to Succed them in their said Office

April 25th 1754.

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray

John Priest James Ashley
