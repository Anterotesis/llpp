To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall in St. John Street

The humble Petition of Martin Haynes

Sheweth That your Petr. was by Indenture of apprenticeship a Counter part of which is hereunto annexed bound apprentice to John Pearson of the parish of Saint George in the East in the County of middlesex Whitesmith to serve him for the Term of Seven Years

That your Petrs. said Master hath sice absconded for Dept and as your Petrs. is informed is gone to Ireland

That your Petr. is an Infant but about the Age of fourteen Years and unable to provide for himself and your Petrs. Mother is a Widow of mean Circumstances having four small Children and unable to provide for your Petr.

That yor. Petr. must inevitably be ruined unless discharged from the said apprenticeship by reason severall Tradeomen who are willing to take yor Petr apprentice refuse so to do untill he is discharged from his former apprenticeship

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worships to take his Case into Consideration and order that yor. Petr. may be discharged from his said Apprenticeship that he may be at Liberty to go Apprentice to some other Master

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound will ever pray

Martin Haynes
