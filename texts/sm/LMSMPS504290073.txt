To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Francis Bedwell Luke Robinson Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of this instant July Elizabeth Drew aged about fourteen years Daughter of William Drew by Rachael his late Wife deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the bars in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the place of her last legal Settlement

The Petr. think themselves Aggreived by the said Order and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same and Pray Your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premisses as shall be thought meet

And the Petrs. will ever pray Etc

G. Talmash Attorney for the Appellants
