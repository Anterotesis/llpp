To the Worshipful This Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Pease for the said County on Clerkenwell Green

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Murray of East Smithfield Pawn broker

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on is about the fourth day of March last convicted in the Penalty of Five Pounds by the Rev Henry Reynett me of the Justices of the Peace in and for the said County upon an Information charging that your Petitioner did on the twenty eighth day of February last take and receive of one Solemon aaron more Money by way of Profit upon a certain Pawn or Pledge than by the Stampt allowed Contrary to the Statute Etc.

That your Petitioner conceives himself aggrieved by such correction and is advised to appeal against the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Worships will be Pleased to Permit him to unter his Appeal so that the same May be heard at this present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County and that your worships will appoint a day for hearing and determining the same

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
