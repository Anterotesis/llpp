To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assambled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mathew Fryday [...] Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant Ender the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Dated the 24th Day of May last Elizabeth Collings Single Woman was passed from the Parish of Christ Church in your Said County to the Said parish of Saint Mathew Fryday Street as the place of her last legall Settlement

Wherefore your Petitioners humbly concieve themselves Aggreieved by the Said order or Pass warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices Therfore humbly Appeale to this Court for Releife in the premisses and pray a Day for the hearing and Determine ing the Same

And your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.

C: Robinson sollt by order of the said Churchwardens and overseers
