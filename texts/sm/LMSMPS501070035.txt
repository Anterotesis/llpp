To the Worfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens of the pish of St Giles in the fields

Sheweth

That a Rate or Assessment hath lately been made for cleansing the Streets and carrying away the Ashes die & soyle out of the said parish But the Justices of the peace of the said parish have refused to allow and approve of the said Rate

J Arden Yor Petr. therefore humbly pray to be releived in the prtnisses

And they shall ever pray Etc

Bruce Randall Geo: Byland

Henry Lane Holborne Red
