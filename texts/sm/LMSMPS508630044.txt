Middx (ss) To the Worshipfull William Mainwaring Esquire and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Genral Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition of William Chapton of Sand Yard in the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County Coach master

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of the Reverend Sir George Booth Baronet and Rupert Clarke Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in for the said County bearing date the 12th. day of February 1791 whereby they have adjudged you Petitioner to be the reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child and have ordered your Petitioner to pay certain Sums of money for the weekly maintenance and support of such Bastard Child

That your Petitioner conceives himself aggrieved by the said Order and appeals to your Worships against the same and

Humbly prays your Worships that the said Order may be quashed and that or day may be appointed in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioner Etc.
