To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Worshipfull Cornelius Martin and William Martin Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirtieth Day of January One thousand seven hundred and seventy seven Ann Warring Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint James within the said Liberty and County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all persons concerned may be then directed to Attend.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Rich Ravenshaw Jno: Tinkler Jerh Hawkes} Churchwardens.

C Goulding Thos: Singer} Overseers of the Poor.
