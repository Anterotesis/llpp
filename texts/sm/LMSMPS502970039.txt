To the Rt Worspll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Severall Persons whose Names are hereunto Subscribed Inhabitants of the parish of Sheperton in the Division of Staines on the said County on behalf of themselves & the rest of the Inhabitants of the said Parish

Sheweth That pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided on the twenty Sixth day of December last the Constables Headboroughs Churchwardens & Inhabitants of the said parish Assembled together did make a List of a Competeit Number of the Inhitants of the said Parish qualified to Serve the Office of Surveyors of the Highways in the said parish for the Year ensueing to wit Thomas Stone Alexander Battell Henry Rosewell & Prin Thomas which said List they did return to severall of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County at a special Sessions held for the said [...] Division at the BushJune in Staines in the said Division on the 11th day of January then next following

That notwithstanding such return the Justices present at the said Special Sessions did appoint by warrant under their Hands & Seales one John King an Inhitant of the said parish who was not returned in the said List to be a surveyor of the highways in the said parish

Yor. petr. think themselves aggrieved by such Appointment and Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray that the said John King may be Discharged from the said Office of Surveyor of the Highways & that one of the other persons named in the said List may be appointed in his roome

And Yor. Petrs: shall ever pray Etc

the mark of the John Warner: Constable Robert Roswell headborou will good in Churchwarden James Tolwell Alex: Battle Rich Thomas Overseers George Roswell Henry Rosewell the mark of John Hatchet
