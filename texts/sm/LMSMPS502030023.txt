To the Rt Worspll. his Matys Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Francis Tenison

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner stands now Indicted in this Honble Court for Assaulting one John Fitzpatrick That Yor. Peticoner at this present time is Confined in the Kings Bench Prison as a Prisonr. So that he Can't appear in this Honble Court this Sessions to try his Traverse upon the sd Indictmt.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased that he may Traverse the sd Indictmt. till next Sessions upon the Reasons abovesaid.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
