To The Honble. Bench of Justices att Hicks's Hall.

The Humble Petition of Robert Robinson

Humbly Sheweth that your Hon [...] Petitionr. about a Month ago was Sent to New Prison for a Quarrell and about a fortnight ago was Discharged and now Lieth for his fees in the said Goale.

Your Honrs. Petitionr. humble Prays in consideration having a wife and three smals Children ready to starve for Want and Your Peticon having no Friend in the World to Pay the Money to fetch him out, humbly Prays Your Honls. [...] lieth in Your breasts to do that he may be Releaced From his Long Confinement.

And Your Honrs. Petitionr. as in Duty bound Wild Ever Pray Etc.
