To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General [---] Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- -Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Ann Westminster in the sd County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seales of John Miller and Robert Hicks Esq: Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty eighth day of October last Henry Underdown Ann his Wife and George their Child Aged about Seven Months were removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Ann Westminster Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived

Therefore they humbly Appeal to Your Worshipps Against the Adjudgement of the said Two Justices and Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned doe Attend this Court some day in this present Sessions to hear and Abide the the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall Pray Etc
