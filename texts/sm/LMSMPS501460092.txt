To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of giles Quincy

Humbly Sheweth That your Petr. was at the generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace held at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County of Middx on Thursday the 13th. day of January last past Nominated Elected and appointed governour and Keeper of the House of the Correction at Clerkenwell in this County in the room and place of Jeremy Bowman deced late governor and Keeper of the said House of Correction to have hold Execute and Enjoy the said office or Keeper of the said house of Correction with the like Sallary of sixty pounds P Ann as was allowed to the said late Keeper to be paid him according as this Court should Order and Direct.

That your Petr. has been governor and Keeper of the said house of Correction and Executed and performed the said Office for the space of six months at Midsumer last and hath been at great Charges in maintaining and releiving the prisoners Committed to his Custody.

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order the halfe yearly payment of 30th. due at Midsumer last to be paid to be your Petr. As to your worspps shall seem meet.

And your Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray etc.

Giles Quinny
