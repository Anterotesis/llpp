To the Rt Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace. for the County of Middlesex

The humble petition of Elizabeth Osborne Wife of Frances Osbourne of the Hamblett of Bethnall Greene in the Parish of Stepny in the said County-

Sheweth

That about a month agoe your Petitrs said Husband went away and left her with Two small Children to Mainteyine. Which she by reason of her Poverty is not able to Doe without a Pention from the said Hamblett

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays she may be allowed such a pention from the sd Hamblett As unto yor Worspps shall Seeme Meete

And Yor Petitr Shall ever pray Etc Eliz Osbourne her R Mkes
