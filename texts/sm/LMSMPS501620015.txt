Mary Islington

The humble Petition & Appeale of Wm: Dobson Aged 80 & odd yeares

Refr to Mr. Browne Mr. Fuller Mr.Tillard or 2 to ex & cert 3 Geo I 4 June 1717

[...] 1717

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John street in Com prd P adjorn die Martis scilt quarto die Juny anno regui Dui Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that it be And It is hereby recomended unto Richard Browne John Fuller & Isaac Tillard Esqrs. Justices of the peace for this County [...] any two of them to sumon before them the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Islington in this County, and to examin into the truth of the Petrs. complaint and to certify their opinions touching the premisses within menconed unto his Maties: Justices of the peace at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County.

P Cur Harcourt
