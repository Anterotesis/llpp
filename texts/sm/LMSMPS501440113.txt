To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Benedict als Benefink London

Sheweth That by Order Dated the Tenth day of February Anno Dom 1714 under the hands and Seales of Samuel Benson and Nathaniel Manlove Esqrs: two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor unnc) Robert Thomson with Mary his Wife and his five Children were removed from the Hamblett of Bethnall Greene in the Parish of St Dunstan Stepney in this County to the Parish of St Benedict als Benefink aforesaid as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Robert Thomson

That your Petitrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd order of the said two Justices and Appeal against the same to this Court

They therefore humbly yor Worspps will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Dunstan Stepney to attend this Court some day next. Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd Appeal Yor Petitrs: most materiall Evidence being out of Towne & cannot attend this Sessions

And Yor Petitrs: shall ever pray Etc

Samll Kempskr Danll Gwesdon} Churchwdns
