Midd Ss To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Att Quran London

Sheweth That by Order under the hands and Seales of [...] Thomas Masters and Samll. Newton Esquires two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quorum unus) bearing date the Sixteenth day of Aprill Anno Dom 1723 Samll. Morley was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Michael Att Quern London as the last legall place of his Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore humbly their themselves Aggreived and Appeall against the aforesaid Order of the said Justices unto this Court and pray that your worshipps will be pleased to appoint A day for all partyes to attend and abide the Judgement of this Court

And yor. Petitrs shall ever pray
