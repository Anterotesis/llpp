To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Pangborne in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of John Spillire and George Reid Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty sixth day of February now last past Mary Liewood otherwise Livard and her four Children one named Richard aged seven years another named John aged five years another named Thomas aged three years and another named William aged one year were removed and conveyed from and out of the Township of New Brentford in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Pangborne in the said County of Berks as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves aggreived and do hereby humbly Appeal against the same Order

James Redit Sollr. for the said Appellants }

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worship will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. will ever pray Etc
