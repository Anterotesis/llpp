To the Right Worship full to is Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Stratford View in the County of Middlesex.

Humbly sheweth That by Virtue of a warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Rirch Esqr. Serjeant at Law and Narcissns Luttrell Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twelfth day of January last past One Samuell Payne an Instant aged abt. three years was conveyed from the Parish of or Hamblett of Hamer smith in the said County to the said parish of St. Mary Stratford Bow by the name of the parish of Bow in the said County As the place of his Legall Settlemt.

Yor. Peticonr finding themselves aggrieved by the said warrant or Order humbly Appeal against the same and pray that a day may be appointed this present Sessions for all partys concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor peticoner shall ever pray Etc.
