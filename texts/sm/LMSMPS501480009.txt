To ye Worshipful ye Justices of ye peace of ye County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Ricd Tomson sheweth that your Petitioner being an Inhabitant in Old Street in ye Lordship part of Ye Parish of St. Giles Creplegate; and is a poor laboring in an having four Children upon [...] hands; for wch. reason ye. Petitioner entreats ye Worships ye he may be excused from paying to ye Poors - rate

& yr Petitioner shal ever pray Richard Tomson
