Middlesex To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of The Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of The Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of The Church Wardens & Overseers of The poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Vertue of an order pass warrant under the hands and seals of Ralph Harwood & Nath Chandler Esqrs Tu [...] of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of The Quorum) bearing Date the 24 day of March Last Elizabeth Johnson singlewoman was Passed from ye Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County to the said Parish of Christ Church as the Place of her Last Legall Settlement & the said Elizabeth Johnson is Delivered of a Male Bastard Child in the Workhouse of Christ Church aforesaid aged one Month and Baptired by the Name of William

Wherefore Your Petitioners humbly Conceive Them- Selves aggrived by the Said Order or Pass Warrant and Judgment of the Said Two Justices Therefore humbly Appeale to this Court for Reliefe in the Premisses and Pray a Day for the hearing and Determining the same

and your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray Etc

Richd: Carter Ch: Warden Edward Grangl Overseer

Abr:Davis Vestry Clerk Sellr.
