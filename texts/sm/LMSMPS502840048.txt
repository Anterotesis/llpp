To the Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx: in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bow in the City of London

Shew That by order the Sixteenth day of May 1732. Under the hands & Seals of Jno. Oakey and Jno. Martin Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Sd. County (Quorn Nuns) Hannah Bynes was removed & Conveyed from the [---] Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the Sd. County of Middlesex to the Sd. Parish of St. Mary Le Box

Yor. Petrs. humbly conceiving themselves Aggreived thereby Do Appeal from ye Sd. Judgmt: of the Sd. two Justices of the peace to the Judgmt of this Honble. Court & Crave day of hearing

And Yor. petrs. shall ever pray Etc
