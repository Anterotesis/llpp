The Humble Peticon of Mary Burn

wapping stepney poor's rate

refr to Mr. B: Johnson Mr. Kyrby Mr. West Mr. Poulson Mr. Cooper or 2 to ex & cert

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial session paris Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John street in Com prd. die Mercur scilt due decimo die Junuar anno regin Dui Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc primo

It is Ordered by this Court that it be And It is hereby [...] recomended unto Bastwick Johnson Robt. Kyrby Thomas West John Poulson & John Cooper Esqrs. Justices of the peace for this County on any two of them to sumon before the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the hamblett of wapping stepney in this County, and to examin into the truth of the Petrs. compliant, and to certify their opinions touching the premisses within menconed unto this Court [---] or his Maties. Justies of the peace at the next Generall Quarter sessions of the peace of to be held for this County.

P Cur Harcourt
