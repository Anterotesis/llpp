To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Spittle Fields in the parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of Robert Dennett & D'Oyly Michel Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for ye said County (Quor unus) bearing date the 20th: day of August last whereby Mary Dervean Singlewoman was removed from the Hamblett of Mile End new Towne in the parish and County aforesaid to the said Hamblett of Spittle fields as to the place of her last Legall Settlement.

Your Petrs: therefore think themselves agrieved by the said Order and Humbly Appeales to this Honrble: Court against the same.

And prays that Yr: Worships would be pleased to Order that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Mile End new Towne may Attend this Court same day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt: & determinacon of this Court touching ye said Appeale.

And your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
