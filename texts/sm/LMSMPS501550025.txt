To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Mary Seawell

Sheweth.

That your Petr: was last Sessions convicted for a Misdemeanour in receiveing of goods stole from one William Jordan for wch: she was fined ten pounds and Comitted to his Majts: Goale of Newgate for three Months.

That your Petr: has Since made the prosecutor (who is now in Court) full Satisfaction.

That your Petr: is an antient woman and is at this Time so very Ill in the said Goale that if your Worshipps in your great goodness and mercy does not think fitt to discharge her she must Inevitably Perish.

Your Petr: therefore humbly Prays that you would be pleased to discharge her from the said Comittment and to Mitigate the fine in such manner as to your Worships shall Seem meet.

And your Petr: shall ever Pray Etc.
