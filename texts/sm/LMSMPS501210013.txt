To the Worsh her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hartington in the Said County

Sheweth That about the 10th: of September last Mary the [...] Widow of Xtopher Davis dyed Intestate in the Said Parish & left for or Children

That Richard Sparks & Bowyer beins neerly related & Creditors as they pretend took out letters of Adminstracon to the Intestate & not possession of all the effects & estate to a considerable value yet about three month past the Said Sparks & Bowyer brought two of the Children & left them to the charis of the parish where they Still to continue

Yr Peticonr therefore humbly pray your worspl will be pleased to order [...] That the Said Spark & Bowyer may accompt with your Peticonr & out of the effects of the said Jutestate pay for the maintence of the Said Children or Shall make Such order herein as to your worsp Shall Seem meet

And your Peticonr Shall ever pray etc

Elisha Combes Natell Buckland
