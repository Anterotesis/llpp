To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Petition of Andrew Bever.

Sheweth That your Peticoner sometime agoe Imployed one John Gary a Sollictor of this Court to Preferr a Bill of Indictment against one Bradwell Davy for an Assault Which was agreed between Prosecutor on [...] Defendant before any Tryall or Hearing Notwith -standing which the said Gary hath Delivered [...] your Petitioner a Bill of Two Pounds Seven Shillings and Two Pence Charges Whereby Your Peticoner humbly Conceives himself much aggrieved And Appeals to this Court

And humbly prays such relief [...] the premises As to this Court [...] shall seem Meet.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc Andrew Bever
