To the Right Worpll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Job Rawlins of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Midds aforesd.

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner is assess'd by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of this sd parish towards the Releif of the Poor therein for this prsent Year

That Yor peticoner by late Losses is not able to pay towards the sd Relief And therefore does think himselfe very much aggreiv'd by the sd Assessmt.

Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps that he may be releiv'd in ye sd pr [...]

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Middx ss Pursuant to ye. Order of ye Genell: Quarters: Sessions of ye peace wth: in wr'tten We whose named are hereunto Subscribed Two of his Maties. Justices ye. peace for this County. in ye sd Order named haveinge this Day taken upon or ye. hearinge & examinacon of ye. Complt. of ye. Peticoner Job Rawlins abovenamed; ye. churchwardens Overseers of ye. poor & other Inhitants of ye. sd Pish of St. Leonard Shoreditch being prsent; & upon heareinge of all Ptyes & duely Considering ye. reasons & illegacons given & made on all Sides, We report our oppinions to this Honable: Court that ye. sd Job Rawlins by reason of his pooer [...] ought to pay not more then one penny P: Weeke to ye. rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd Pish Given under our hands this 8th. day of October Ano Dom 1719:

Alex: Pitfeild Sam Benson
