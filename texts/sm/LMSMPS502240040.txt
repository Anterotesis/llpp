To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their Gene Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble petition of Jane Low of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate Widdow.

Sheweth That your Petr. Rents a small House in the Said parish and is charged to the poor and Scavengers Rates for the Same.That your Petr. is upwards of Sixty of age and very Infirm and by Great Losses and other misfortunes is Reduced to a poor Low Condition, and in no wife Capable of paying to the Said Rates.

May it therefore please your Worships to Commiserate your Petrs. poor Condition, and to make Such Order for Her Releif as in your Wisdoms your Worships shall think meet.

And your petr. as in Duty Bound shall ever pray Etc.
