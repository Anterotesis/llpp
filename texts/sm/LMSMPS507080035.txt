To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Mary hill in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Newson and Benjamin Cowley Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteeth day of November last past Elizabeth Colson Widow and her son George aged about Seventeen months were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said parish of Saint Mary hill in the City of London as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said order and pray that the benefit of this their Appeal may by saved unto them and that the hearing and determining there of may be Adjourned unto the next Session of the peace to behold for this County to hear and determine the same And that your Worships will be pleased to make your order for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all persons concerned then to attend.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

William Elliot Sollr. for the Appellts.
