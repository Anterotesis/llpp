To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the Rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ingatestone in the County of Essex

Humbly Sheweth That by Vertue of an order under the hands & Seals of Henry Feilding & Daniel Gach Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex bearing date the 22d day of September last One James Mitchell an Instant Aged about 20 Months was removed from the Parish of St. Clements Danes in the County of Middlesex to the Sd Parish Ingatestone in the County of Essex aforesaid as being the last legal place of Settlement of the Sd. James Mitchell

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be greatly Aggrived by the Judgment and Determination of the Sd. Two Justices do Humbly Appeal to Your Worships for relief in the Premisses & that Your Worships would be Pleased to make Such order therein as your Worships in Your discretion shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners as in duty Bound shall ever pray &
