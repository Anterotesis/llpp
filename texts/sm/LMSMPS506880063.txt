To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon [---] and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the hamblett of Wapping- Stepney in the parish of [...] Stebunheath als Stepney in County

Sheweth

That by order dated the fourth day of Apri [...] the hands & seals of Robert Bateman Esqr and Roger Smyth Esqr [...] her Maties Justices of the peace for this County (Quor unus) o [...] Elizabeth the wife of Edward Trusted (who was the gone to sea & their Child Richard Trusted aged about three years were rem [...] from the parish of St Mary Matfellon als Whitechappell in the County to the said Hamblett of Wapping Stepney of the place the last legall settlemt of the said Edward Trusted, and [...] Elizabeth Trusted Since her said removall dyed in the said Hamblett Wapping Stepney & was bined att the charge of the Inhabitants of Hamblett

That your petr. find themselves aggreived by the sd order of remov [...] & humbly appeal agt. the same and doe not doubt but to make appear to this Worpfull Court that the sd Edward Trust [...] settlemt in the sd Hamblett of Wapping Stepney

P I Rolfe Your Petrs therefore [...] pray that this Court [...] to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers o the poor of the of St Mary Matfellon als White chappell all other concerned [...] to attend this Court to hear & abide judgmt and determinacon of this Court upon & t [...] the said appeal

And yor Petr shall pray Etc
