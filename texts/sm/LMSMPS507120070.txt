Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That the said parish of Saint paul Covent Garden is charged to the County Rate by a Rate which was made at the General Sessions of the peace held by adjournment at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street on Thursday the 18th. day of February last with the Sum of £69..5..7½ altho the Annual Rents or Value of the Lands and Tenements therein charged or Assessed to the poors Rate doe not amount to the Sum of £32,000

That the parish of Saint Mary le bone in th said County of Middlesex was assessed to the same County Rate at the Sum of £7:5s:10d. only altho the Annual Rents or Value of the Lands and Tenements in that parish Charged with the poor Rate amount to £140,000 or thereabouts Wherefore your petitioners humbly conceive that the said County Rate so made upon them as aforesaid is unequal and partial And Declare themselves grievously oppressed thereby

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal against such part of the said Rate as affects the said parish of Saint paul Covent Garden And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of this Appeal And that your Worships will give your petitioners such relief as seems meet to you

And your petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Jane agent & Sollr. for the sd. Parish of sd. Paul C [...] }

B Byam [...] } Church wardens

Thos. Bourdillon} Overseers of the poor
