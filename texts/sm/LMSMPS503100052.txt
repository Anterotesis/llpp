To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex now assembled at Hicks's Hall

The humble petition ans appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Anne in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John priestly and Thomas Jones Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the second day of May last Elizabeth Cole Widdow and her three children were removed from the hamlet of popular and Blackwal in the parish of Stepney in the said County to the said parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement

And Your petitioners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal Do appeale against the same

And humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Quarter Sessions of the peace for hearing and determining your petitioners Appeale and Order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said hamlet of popular and Blackwal and all persons concerned attend and hear and abide by the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the same

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc
