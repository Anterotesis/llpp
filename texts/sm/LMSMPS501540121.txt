To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Spencer.

Sheweth

That the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the County aforesd have assess'd yor. Peticoner Eight Shillings a Quartr. towards the Releife of the Poor there for the year ensuing Which said Assessmt. is after the Rate of Sixteen pounds P Annu (at two shillings in the pound) which said rate is charg'd upon the Inhabitants of the said parish towards the Releife of the poor aforesaid.

That Yor. Petres. Rent was never more than Eleven pounds P Annu So that he thinks himselfe very much aggreiv'd by the said Assessmt. As by the Receipts hereunto annexed may appear.

Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to ordr. that he may be releiv'd in the said prmisses.

P W Henman

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.
