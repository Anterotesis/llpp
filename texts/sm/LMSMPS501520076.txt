To the Worpll: his Majtys: Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled and for the said County.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Mary Rotherhith in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Elizabeth Gale wife of John Gale with her Daughter Mary Gale wereby Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majtys. Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx bearing Date the 23d: day of January last removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Mary Rotherhith as the place of their last legall Settlement That your Peticoners humbly Conceive that they can make it appear to this Court that the place of the last legall Settlement of ye said Elizabeth Gale and May Gale is not in the said parish of Rotherhith.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleaded to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Paul. Shadwell to Attend this Court for the hearing the said Appeal Such time as to your Worships shall seem meet.

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
