To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kintbury otherwise Kimberry in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry Lambe and Thomas Gilbert Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirteenth Day of September One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty four Mary Hawkins and her three Children to wit Robert aged thirteen Years or thereabouts William aged nine Years or thereabouts and James aged five Years or thereabouts were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Ealing in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kintbury otherwise Kimberry in the said County of Berks as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that this Court will please to appoint a Time for Hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend
