To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St. Michael Woodstreet London

Sheweth That whereas by an Order under the hands and Seal's of Alexander Ward and John Metcalfe Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex dated the eighth day of August last made upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Andrews Holborne in the Liberty of Saffron hill in the County of Middlx The said two Justices did adjudge Henry Lane and Elizabeth his Wife to have their last legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Michael Woodstreet Requiring your Petitioners to provide for them as their own [...] Parishoners Your Petitioners do humbly apprehend themselves to be agrieved by the said Order for that the said Henry Lane and Elizabeth his Wife or either of them never had any legal Settlement in the said Parish of St. Michael Wood street

And therefore your Petitioners do humbly Appeal from the said Order and pray to be relieved as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

And ever pray Etc
