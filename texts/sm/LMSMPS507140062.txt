To the Worshipful Justices now Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Wm. Quarrill and W. Blackmore Esquires of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fifth Day of June last Sarah Bowyer Wife of Thos. Bowyer who hath deserted and left her and her four Children Vizt. Thomas aged about fourteen Years Joseph aged about Eight Years Rosamond aged about three Years and Ann aged about three weeks were Removed from the Parish of Saint Mary White chapel in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order humbly Appeal against the same And pray such Relief as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Wm. Drew. Solr. for Appellants
