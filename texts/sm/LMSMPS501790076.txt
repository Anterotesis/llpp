To the Rt: Worship full his majetys Justices of the Peace for the County of middx att their Generall quarter Sessions att Hickshall

The humble Petition and appeall of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: martin in the Fields in the County

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant bearing date the sixth day of June last under the hands and Seals of John millner and John Richardson Esqrs: Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quos un) Elizabeth Greenwood was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields to your Petr: said Parish of St: martin in the Fields as the place of her last legall Settlement, Whereby your Petr: humbly Conceive they are Wrong'd and agrieved.

Yoour Petr: therefore humbly appeall to your Worships and pray your Worships to appoint a day the next Sessions to hear & determine she same

Phill Davis Giles Harris Church Wardens

Adm Long Wm Nind Wm Bridges Overseers of ye poor
