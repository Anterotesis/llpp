To the Right Worpll his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petiton & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Acton in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the Hand & Seal of st Wm. Osborne & Cliffd: Wm Phillips two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 15th Day of March last James Farley Ann his wife & John their son were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Mary White Chappell in the County of Middx to the Parish of Acton in the sd County alledging therein that the said Parish of Acton is place of their last legall Settlement.

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants. Yor Petrs. Finding themselves aggreived by the order of the sd. two Justices humbly appeal against the same.

And pray that your Worps: wou'd be pleased to appoint same Day this present Sessions for all parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

And your Petrs shall pray Etc
