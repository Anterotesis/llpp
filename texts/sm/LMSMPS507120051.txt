To The Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Mr. Blackmore and Parkr. Rowland Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date Twenty Second day of July last Martha Simpson Wife of Andrew Simpson with her two Children (Vizt) Elizabeth aged about Eight Years and Margaret aged about two Years were Removed from the Parish of Saint Johns of Wapping in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners are Advised and believe that the last legal Settlement of the said Martha Simpson and her said two Children are not in the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as by the said Order is Supposed and therefore they Conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and do humbly appeal to your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore as well on the Part and behalf of themselves as of the other Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell do humbly pray that the said Order of Removal of the said Martha Simpson and her said two Children may be Quashed and that your Petitioners may be further and otherwise Relieved in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners and Appellts. shall ever pray [...]

Saml. Malbon Sollr. for the Appellts.
