To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of John Forster Esqr of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That your petr is rated for his Stabling Etc in the poor and Scavengers rates of the said parish us are then he paw and appeals agt the Same

And Prays such relief as to this Court Shall Seem Meet And Yor Peter Shall Pray Etc

John Forster
