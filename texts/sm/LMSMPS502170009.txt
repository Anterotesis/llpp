To the worshipfull bench of Justices Now sitting on the bench att Hicks's Hall

The Humble petition of Robeca taper Prisoner att ye barr

Sheweth

That your petitioner must confess She is Guilty of ye Allegations made against her by the Prosecutor John Blanon and your petitionr being destitute of Friends or any support Hum- -bly prays her discharge without fees as to your Worships shall seem meet Etc

The Premisses tenderly consider'd And In as much as your petitioner being far remote from any friends And noe way able to pay her fees Hum- -bly Implores She may be discharg'd & she as in duty bound will Pray
