[...] To the Right Worpll: her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx P [...]

The humble Peticon & appeale of Joseph Pidter of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Countie of Middlx & Hirkenman

Sheweth That yor: Petr is Rated nine Shillings pr quarter towards the. Releif of the poore of the sd parish payes but Six pounds tenn Shillings pr Anum for his house And for as much as your Petr; hath had many great losses and want of Trade make, him unable to pay the sd Assesment

Therefore humbly: prayes yor: Worpps to take his Care into Consideration & grant him Such releif herein as in yor: Wisdom Shall Seem meet

And as in duty bound shall pray Etc
