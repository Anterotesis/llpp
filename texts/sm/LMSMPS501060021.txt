To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for of County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Ann Printon of the Parish of St Paul Shadwell in the County Widdow

Sheweth

That About Six yeares part your Petr. Husband Died And Left her with Two Poore Children to Mainteyne For the Mainteynance of whome as well as herselfe She hath Struggled in the World Soe Long As She hath beene Able Without being Burthen some to the said Parish But She being now become very Poore Is not Able Longer to Subsist without Relief

P Tho: Nash

Your Petr therefore most humbly Prays your Worshiipps to be Pleased to order of She may be Allowed Such penscon From the sd Parish As unto your Worshipps Shall Seeme Meete

And yor Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc

Ann Printon
