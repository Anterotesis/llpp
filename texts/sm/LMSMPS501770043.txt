To the Worppfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in the Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor office Parish of [...] allows Lombard street in the Citty of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of Thomas Rand & John Whetham two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County (Quor Unus) bearing Date the 24th: Day of March last One Elizabeth Ebrall Widdow with 2 Children vizt Josiah & William Ebrall were removed from the Parish of St. Gyles without Cripplegate to the Said Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street Alledging that the Said Parish is the Place of the last Legall Settlement of this Said Elizabeth Ebrall

That Your Peticoners hope to prove that the Sd Elizab Ebrall had no Legall Settlement in the Said Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street whereby they Conceive themselves Aggreived by the Judgment of the Said two Justices And Do humbly Appeale to Your Worpps thereon

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Pray Your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmises and Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. Gyles without Cripplegate to hear [...] abide the Determinacon [...] Worpps touching this App [...]

And Your Peticoners Shall Ever Pray Etc
