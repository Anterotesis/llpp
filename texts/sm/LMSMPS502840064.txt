To the Rt. Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Qur [...] Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrant on Order Dated the 10th: Day of June last past under the Hands and Seals of Andrew Osborne and Thomas Exelbew: Esqrs, two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor Un) John Dutfield (since decely) and Catharine his wife, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the said County to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and Appeal to this Honoble Court agt. the same.

Wherefore your said Petitioners most humbly pray your Worshipps to appoint a Day in this present General Quarter Sessions for the hearing and Determining their Petition and Appeal against the said order

And Your Petrs: Shall Pray Etc
