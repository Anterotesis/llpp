To the Rt. Worshipfull is Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genll. Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Chandler & Anthony Wroth Esqrs. Two of is Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fifth Day of September last past, William Stevens was Removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St: Botolph without Aldersgate London, Alledging the Said Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate to the Place of his last legall Settlement.

Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That Your Peticonrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Justices, Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom, & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear ym. in the Prmisses, and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the sd. County of Middx may appear before you [...] at a certain day of be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
