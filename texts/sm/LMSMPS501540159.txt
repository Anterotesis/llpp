To ye. Right Worshipll ye Bench of Justices Sitting att Hixs Hall

The humble Peticon of Willm. younge

Sheweth That I am in yeares and have a Wife and two Chilldren wch: I finde a very hard Matter to Maintaine My Calling is very Meane in so much you Canott be Sensible in what [...] a Loe Condition I am in and I am Coated to Pay to ye Poores booke wch: I am not able to Pay I Beg yor Woships will be Pleased to Free me from itt

Yor Poore Peticoner Shall ever be bound to Pray for ye Woships Prosperrity
