To the Rght Worrll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genll Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St Martin in the feild in the sd County

Sheweth That by an order or Warrt. bearing date the 14th day of September last and the hands & Seales of Lawrence St Loe T. Woodcock Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County William Thrift & Mary his Wife & John their Son aged about Six Years were removed from the pish of St James Westmr to Your Petrs. said pish of St Martin in the Feilds whereby Your Petrs. humbly conceive They are wronged & Apprived

Your Petrs. therefore him they Appeale to your Worshipps ag the said Order & Pray Your Worshipps to appoint a day to hear & determine the Same

And your Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc:

Phill: Davis Giles Harris} Churchwardens
