To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Mary Fouch of the Parish of St John Hackny in the said County Wife of Thomas Fouch

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was allowed Eighteen pence P. Weeke as a Pention from the said Parish But for this Month past the same has been wholly taken away from her

That Yor Petrs. Husband is a Poor Labouring Man and Destitute often of Imployment and Your Peticoner hath Seaven Children to Mainteyne Who as well as herselfe are ready to perish for want

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly prays the may be allowed Such a Pention from the Parish As. to yor. Worshipps Shall Seem Meet

And Yor: Petr: Shall Ever pray Etc.

Mary Fouch
