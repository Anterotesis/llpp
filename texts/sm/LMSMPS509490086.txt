To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Court of General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex on behalf of themselves and other the Inhabitants of the said Parish.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of M. Buckley Clerk and Thomas Collins Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of bearing date the 24th. day of February last Richard Weston Elizabeth his wife and their two Children namely Elizabeth aged 11 years and Mary aged 9 Years was Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Le bone in the said County to the said Parish of Saint George in the said County as the place of their lawful Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the Premises and that your worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray
