To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary Le Bone in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Ludbey and Henry Trent Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 21st (day of January 1747 George Outing Hannah his wife and their Child (to wit) George aged four years were removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will plea [...] to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then accend

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Thos South Thos: Huddles} Churchwardens

Wm: Malin Thos: Whitehead} Overseers of the Poor
