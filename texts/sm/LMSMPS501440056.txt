To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the p [...] for the County of Middlx.

The humble peticon and Appeale of Jane Peters of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Midds Widdow.

Sheweth That your Petr. is rated four Shillings and Six pence P quarter towards the releife of the poor of the said parish And forasmuch as your petr hath had severall losses in the world and but very Small trade which makes her unable to paij the said Rate or Assessment

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays your Worps to be pleased to order that She may be discharged from paymt to the poors Rate of the Said parish.

And your petr Shall ever pray Etc

Jane Peters Peticon agt the pavis Rate of Cripplegate parish

1714
