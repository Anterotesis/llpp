To the Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That that Jane Clarke widw. Daughter of John Sheppard of the Pish of St. Clemt. Danes hath been for fifteene Weekes last past and now is chargeable to the sd. Pish and that the sd. John Sheppards her Father is a person of Ability & capable of providing for her & ought soe to doe in ease of the Inhabitants of the sd. Pish

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worspps that the sd. John Sheppard may be Ordered to allow & pay unto the sd. Jane Clarke his Daughter such Weekly Sume of money as this Cort shall thinke fitt in ease of the Inhabitants of the sd. Pish he being a Pson of Suffict. Substance & ability soe to doe

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc

W Cornelius W Buttan} Overseers of the poor
