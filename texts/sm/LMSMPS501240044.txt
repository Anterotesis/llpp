To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of All saints in the Burrough of Hertford

Sheweth That by warrt or order dated the Ninth day of July last Under the hands & Seales of Roger Smyth and John Stewart Esqrs two of her Maties Justices of the peace for this County (quor [...] vuus) one Dorcas Maul wife of Thomas Maul and their Child named Hester aged about two months were removed & conveyed [...] from the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds in the parish of Stebunsheath als st [...] in the County of Midds to the said parish of All saints in the sd Burrough of Hertford as the place of their last legall settlement [...] That your peticonr find themselves aggreived by the said order of the said two Justices and humbly appeale against the Same

J Rolfe Yor peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worpps will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Hamblett of Spittlefeilds & all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judjmt & determine [...] of this Court upon & touching the said appeal, And that your Petrs may be releived herein As your Worpps Shall seem meet

And yor Petrs shall pray Etc
