To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Horner of the Parish of Kensington in the sd. County

Sheweth That your Petr. was Elected by the Court Leet One of the Headburroughs of the sd. Parish for the Mannor ofarles Court Kensington att Easter 1704 and that since there hath been noe Court Leet hole for the sd. Mannor.

That the Vestry of the sd. Parish have Returned One James Nichollson to Serve the sd. Office of Headburrough for the sd. Mannor

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspps to be pleased to Grants that he may be discharged from the sd. Office of Headburrough and that the sd James Nichollson may be Sworne in his Roome

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc

Thomas Horner
