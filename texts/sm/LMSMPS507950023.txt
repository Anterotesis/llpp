To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in this General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Church Gresley in the County of Derby

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of Henry Lambe and Edward Burnaby Greene Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th day of December last a Man a Woman and these Children named and described in the said Order as Follows vizt." Thomas Chapman. Elizabeth his "Wife & Sarah their Daughter aged about 8 years Elizabeth their Daughter "aged about 6 years and Thomas their son aged about three Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Church Gresley as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceived themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray etc

Herbt Robertson solicitor for the Appellants
