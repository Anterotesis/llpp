To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

We whose hands are hereunto Subscribed and set the present Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint John at Hackney in the County of Middlesex Do hereby Certify that William Franks of the parish aforesaid Coachman who was bound by Recognizance before Henry Norris Esquire one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County to appear at this present General Quarter Session of the peace to be held for the said County to answer our Complaint for begetting Sarah Bigenden Single Woman with Child of a Male Bastard Child born of her Body in the said parish and Chargeable to the same hath full paid and satisfied the changed and Expences that he have been at and hath given Bond to Indempnify the said parish from any future Expence and therefore we pray that he may be Discharged form his said Recognizance Wittness our hands the Eighth day of July 1748

George Clear John Demant Sam Sadleirs} Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. parish
