To the Rt: Honble Sr: Charles Duncombe Knt. Ld: Maj. of the City of London & to the Rest of the Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Justice Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Andrew L'Apostre A Prisonr in Newgate

Sheweth That yor. Petr was the 12th: day of March last Comittee to this prison by the Rt: Honble the Earle of Sunderland Charg'e for High Treason of wth. Arnne yor. Petr is inocent

Yor. Petr therefore humbly prayes to be Tryed this Sessions or that this Honble Court would be pleased to admit him to Baile or that he may be otherwayes discharged as this Honble Court shall thinck fit

And yor petr. shall ever pray Etc
