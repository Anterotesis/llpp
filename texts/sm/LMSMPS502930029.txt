Honble. Sr. The humble Petition of Elizth Smith & Elizth: Garland

Sheweth That ye. Said Elis Smith & Eli [...] Garland is Mother & daughter and has been a long time Confin'd in Prison and has a large familly and is intirely destitute, therefore humbly beggs ye. favour of your Honl. to Consider her deplorable Case, and release her without paying Court fees as she is intirely unable, being a large Starv'd and

Your Petitioner as in Duty bound will ever pray
