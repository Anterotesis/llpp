To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace, Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of G Mercer and Mr. A. Taylor Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the sd. County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of November last. Ann Flaxman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Martin in the fields in the sd City and Liberty to the sd Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceived themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worhisps will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Richd Christmas Jno French James Sidgwith} Churchwardens

Gould Chaplin} Overseers of the Poor
