To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the Peace at the Session's-House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney and N Conant Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the the 30th. day of October last Elizabeth Mary Williams the Wife of John Williams Seaman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will he pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Jas: Dyson Solicitor for the said Appellants
