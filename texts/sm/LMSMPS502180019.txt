To the Rt: worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of [...] Poor of the parish of St. Botolphs without Aldgate Landon

Sheweth That by Virtue of a warrant under the Hands and Seals of Phillip Wilshire & Samuel Jones Esquires two of his Maties Justices of the Peace [...] for the County of Middsex & (Quor unus) bearing date the 11th day of March last Margaret Hammond Widdo. was sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamelet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middlesxe to the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London aleadging the said Parish to he she place of her last legal Settlemt

That Your Peticoner: conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrt Judgmt of the sd two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Worps there from

Your Petr therefore most humbly pray Your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and appoint a day for ye hearing & determining ye sd Appeal and that the Churchwardens & Overser's of ye poor of Ye sd Hamblet of Ratcliff Day then appear & abide the [...] of this Court hearing sd paper [...]

And they shall pray ye
