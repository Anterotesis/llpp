Middx ss: To the Rt. Worppfull. her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Midd.

The humble peticon of Thomas Stoneof Shepperton.

Sheweth That yor. peticonr: hath Served the Office of petty Constable in the sd. parish for the space of two yeares & upwards & that there hath not been a Court next held there neither doth yor. Peticonr: Know when there will be a Court held there to elect & choose a Constable there

Yor. Peticonr. there fore humbly to prays yor: Worpp. to grant yor. Order to Richd Coombs of Shepperton aforesd to be Sworne Constable an the Roome & stead of yor. Peticonr.

And Yor. Peticonr shall pray Etc.
