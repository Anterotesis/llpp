To the Right Worshipfull Maties Justices of the [...] Sitting at Hicks Hall in the County of Middx

The humble Petition of James Morris Victualer in ye pish of St. Giles without Criplegate

Sheweth That whereas yor. Petr. preferring a Bill of [...] Indictment wth was found and this Worshipfull Bench was pleased to grant ye Petr. Ho: Bench Warrant upon the same not with standing yor. Petitioner was prsently taken & [...] rrced [...] New Prison but suport what account yor Petrs. Knoweth not

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly pray that he may be brought this night before this Worshipfull Bench that he may Know beth his accuser & his Crime

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
