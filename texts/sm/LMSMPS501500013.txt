To yr Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for yr County of Middx

The humble Petition of Thomas. Williams and William Froggett

Sheweth That about Three Sessions agoe your Petitrs became bail for the appearance of William Smithson in the Court to Answer what should be objected against him by one Richard Rudd

That the said William Smithson has beene Confined a Prisoner of Debt in the Kings Bench. for these two Months last past soe yr Your Petitos are apprehensive trouble will Ensue. And for as much to as they being poor Men

Humbly Pray they May be Discharged from their Said. Recognizance

And Yor Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc Tho: Williams Wm Froggett
