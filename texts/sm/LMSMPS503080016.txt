Middx To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in General Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Petition & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Aylesbury in the County of Bucks.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fourth day of March 1734 Barbara Brandon wife of William Brandon & their four Children (to wit) [---] Mary aged almost Nine Years & [...] [...] William aged Six years Elizabeth aged four years & Henry aged almost two years were removed & conveyed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the Said parish of Aylesbury in the Said County of Bucks as the place of the last legal Settlemt. of the Said Barbara Brandon & four Children.

That Your. Petrs. finding themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices of the peace humbly appeale to this Court against the Same.

And forasmuch as your Petrs. cannot be ready to prosecute their Said appeale this present Sessions humbly pray that Your Worships would be pleased to Adjourn the hearing of the Said Appeale untill the next General Sessions of the peace to be held for this County And that Some day may be appointed therein for all Parties concerned to Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the Said Appeale.

Clement Agent for the [...] Appellants

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
