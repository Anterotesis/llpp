To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middx at the General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the St. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of [...] Mr Countnay [---] and Jno. Griffiths Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westmr. in the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of Sepr. 1784. Joseph Holland a Singleman was removed to Conveyed from the parish of St. George Hanover Square within the sd. Liberty of Westmr. [---] the [---] County of Middx afsd. to the sd. parish of St. Mary le Bone or the place of his last legal Settlement

That Yr. Petrs. conceive of themselves aggrieved by ye. sd. Order or passwarrt. Do therefore Appeal to yr. worships therefrom And Humbly pray that you will please to Hear them in the premes And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Psh of St. George Hanover Square may Appear before you at a Certain day to be Appointed to Hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching this said Appeal

And Yr. Petr. shall ever Pray Thos. Martin Solr. for the Appellants Newman Street
