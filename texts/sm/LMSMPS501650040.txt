To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Christopher Bannister

Sheweth That as of last Sessions a Bill of Indictmts. was preferred against your Peticoner in this Court for an Assault upon Richard Rudd and John Cutting and Yor Peticoner then pleaded to the Sd

That your Peticoner this present Sessions preferrd Si [...] against the said Rudd and & Cutting for an Assault on yor Petitr: which Bill is found But the said Rudd & Cutting have not pleaded to the Same and abscond

Whereof Yor Petitr humbly prays an Order of Wch. Court that the [...] Traverles on ye. said Indictments may be. tryed togeather next Sessions

And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc:

Cr Bannister
