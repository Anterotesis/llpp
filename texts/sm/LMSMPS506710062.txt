To Sir John Hawkins Knt. and other his Majesty's Worshipful Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex at a Quarter Sessions of the Peace held at Guild Hall, in King Street Westminster.

The most humble Petition of John Hughes of Enfield in the County of Middlesex aforesaid.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner hath for Three years past performed the Office of one of the Chief or High Constables for the Hundred of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex, but by great Affliction with the Gout is rendered in-capable of his Duty therefore most humbly Prays to be excused from serving that Office any longer, and your Petitioner will ever be bound in Duty to pray for Etc.

I recommend [...] Saint Goldwin of Baker Street Enfield Appraiser to be high Constable

Thos. Brooksbank

Jno. Hughes
