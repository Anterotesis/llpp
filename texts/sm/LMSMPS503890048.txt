To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County Middx at their Genl Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sr Margaret Westminster in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals. of Gabriel [...] ace and William Jones Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Dated Septemr: the 13th. 1748. Elizabeth Aldersey Singlewoman was Removed from the parish of St Clement Danes in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the parish of St Margaret in the City of Westmr: in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Yor Petrs. Conceive themselves aggreived and Against the said Order do Appeal.

Your Petitrs therefore humbly Pray that Day may be Appointed this Present Sessions to hear and Determine the matter touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs shall ever Pray Etc.

J: Feary Agent for Le Margts:
