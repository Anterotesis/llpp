To the Rt. Worspful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their general Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Joseph Hows of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoredich in the said County

Sheweth That your Peticonr. on or about the Eleventh day of July Instant was Chosen to Serve the Office of Scavenger for the Liberty of Holywel Street in the Parish of St. Leonard Shoredich aforesaid altho on the fourth day of April being Tuesday in Easter week last Thomas Dee Solomon Silby was Chosen by the Constable Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor & other Ancient Inhabitants of the said parish to Serve the Office of Scavenger for the Liberty of Holywel Street aforesaid and that they did exte Etc of the said Office and employ'd Thomas Sykes to be Raker for the said Liberty

Your Peticonr. thinking himself agreived thereby humbly appeals to this Honble Court and pray that your Worsps would be pleased the Order that the Constable Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor & other Ancient Inhabitants of the said parish & all other persons concerned may attend some day this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgemt. & determination of this Court

And your peticonr. Shall Ever pray Etc Joseph Hows
