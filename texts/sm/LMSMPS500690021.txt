To the [...] Righers of the [...] County of Midds [...]

Sheweth The Humble Peticon of C [...]

That yor. Petr. is rated the sum of £1.5s.0d which is after the Rate of 6d. in the Pound [...] towards Reparieing the Highwayes within the parish of St. Mary Islington in the County [...] as haveing an Estate of £50. pound within the sd. Parish Over and above which Rate your Petr. is obleidged in respect of the sd. £50. pound to find & provide a Team & [...] Horses to worke in Repaireing the sd. Highwayes.

That yor. Petr. can safely by make appeare to this Honoble Court that he is actually [...] possessed with or Intituled to noe more then yor. P and within the sd. parish whereby he humbly Contriver himselfe agreived.

P Tho Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly Prayer yor Worspps to be pleased to Willigate the sd. Rate as unto yor. Warspps shall been meet

The yor Petr Shall [...] Pray Etc Ch: Gery
