To the Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Thomas Martin Constables of South Mims in the said County.

Sheweth

That there was One Quarters Sallary one to your Petitr. [...] Passing and Relieving of Vagrants at Lady day Last amounting to the Sum of Tenn Pounds

That your Petitr: hath beene at great expence in Conveying [...] divers Vagrants and Stands in need of the said Money

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to [...] orders that he may be paid the said Money

And your Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Tho. Martin
