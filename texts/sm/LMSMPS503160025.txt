To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Thomas Topping Constable of the Parish of Littleton in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your petitioner having Served the Office of a Constable in the said Parish of Littleton for four Years and about a half is very desirous to be put out of the same And there being no Court Leet holden in the Manor and Parish of Littleton for several Years so that your Petitioner hath no Opportunity of Getting Out from being Constable but only by an Order from your Worshipps

Therefore most humbly prays your Worshipps would be pleased to put him out from being Constable And in his Room he doth Nominate Richard Nicholls of the said parish of Littleton Labourer or Thomas Child of the same place Carpenter or Richard Dell Labourer

And he shall ever pray And so forth

Thomas Topping April 29th 1736

Thomas Sopping April 29th 1736
