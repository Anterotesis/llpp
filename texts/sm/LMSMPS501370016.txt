To the Rt: Worsppll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Anne Ridge &: John Ridge.

Sheweth

That upon the Fifth of December last your Petitrs: Convicted. [...] Mary Harris before John Fuller Esqr: one of her Matys Justices of the Peace for this County for Selling a Half Penny Wheaten Roll: Wanting One Ounce and Eight Drams of the Assize then Sett forth pursuant to the direccons of a late Act of Parliament in yt Case made.

That the said Mary Harris hath this Sessions brought an Appeal In this Court against the said Conviction And that Last Night at Nine of the Clock Notice was Left at your Petitrs: houses: to Try the said Appeal this Morning at Tenn of the Clock Which Notice being soe very Short, and yr Wittnesse out of the way your Petitrs. are not able to try the said Appeal this Morning

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray Your Worshipps will be Pleased to Respite the said Appeal untill Tuesday Morning next If your Worspps Soe thinke fitt

And Yor Petrs: shall ever Pray etc

Anne Ridge John Ridge
