To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the Said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Philip Neve and Thomas Gordon Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 16th. day of October 1793 Penelope Heacock the Wife of John Benjamin Heacock and their two Children namely Penelope aged Two years and upwards and Susannah aged Seven Months and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the same.

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

Thos. Wallis C Richardson Willm. Morris Thos Gleaves Ferdinand Ballhorn Joseph Whitaker Thos Cockings Jno Dutton Richd Himpley} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor
