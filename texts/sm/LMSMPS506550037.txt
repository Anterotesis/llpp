To the Worshipfull his Majesty Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace assembled I The Humble Petition, and appeal, of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of Hayes in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That By Vertue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of girdler Esquire and John Cox Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date then seventeenth day of april in the year of our Lord one thousand seven Hundred and Seventy fives Ann Evans single woman was removed from and out of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn about the Barrs in then said County of Middlesex to the parish of Hayes aforesaid aledging the said parish of Hayes be the place of her last legal, Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves agrieved by the saint order or pass Warrant and Humbly appeals against the same and pray your Worships to grant them such Releife in the premisses as shall be thought Just and Equitable and that the Church wardens and overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint andrew Holborn above the B [...] s may appear befores your Worships at a day to be by you appointed to hour and abider then Judgement and determination of your worships touching this appeal

and your Petitioners shall Ever Paray Etc Jab [...] Subsistor for the parish of Hayes
