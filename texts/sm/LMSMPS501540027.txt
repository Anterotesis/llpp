To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Salsbury Constable of South Mims In the said County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary one to your peticoner for passing and Relieving of Vagrants at Midsummer last amounting to the Sum of Tenn pounds.

That your peticoner hath been at great Charge this last Quarter in eassing Vagrants and stands in Need of his said Money.

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays Your Worshipps will be pleased to Order that he may be paid his said Quarters Sallary

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

John Salsbury
