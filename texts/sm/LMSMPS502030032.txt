To the Right Worpl his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Anne Black fryers London.

Sheweth That By Virtue of a Pass Warrt under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor & unus) bearing Date the Ninth day of January last Peter Decote Anthony Decote Mary Decote and Anne Decote four Children of Peter and Mary Decote lately deceased were on the Tenth day of Janry[---] at or said Sent and delivered by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx unto the Church -Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd parish of St Anne Black fryers London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legall Settlemt

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to Your Worps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly pray your Worps will be pleased to Adjourn this their Petition & Appeal to the next General Sessions and appoint a day for hearing and determining the Same.

And Your Petrs Shall Pray Etc
