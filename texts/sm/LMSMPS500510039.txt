To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of Jane Edgerton of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourne in the sd County Widow

Sheweth

That some time past yor. petr. tooke to nurse out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill within the sd. parish one David Foster son of William Foster and was promised paymt. for nursing the same by the sd. William Foster

That the sd. Wm Foster is now in Flanders the mother of the sd. Child being dead Soe that yor. petr. hath not as yett been paid for nursing the sd Child

That the sd. Child was borne within the sd. Liberty where the sd. Wm Foster his Father was an Inhabitant for the space of nine yeares or thereabouts And forasmuch as the Overseers of the poore of the sd. Liberty about Two Months past promised to pay yor Petr. from thereforth forwards for nursing the sd. Child but they now refuse Soe to due And forasmuch as yor. Petr. being very poore & not longer able to keepe the sd Child

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr therefore most humbly Prayer yor. Worspp to be pleased to Order that the sd Child may be mainteyned att the charge of the Overseers of the sd Liberty & that they may pay yor. Petr. for keeping the sd. Child Since the time of their promiseing your Per Paymt. for the Same

And yor Petr shall ever Pray Etc

Jane Edgerton her [x] mke.
