To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Petition of Katherine Symonds

Sheweth That there being Due to yor Petr for Wages the Sum of Â£8:7S:6D from one Mr Charles Boott And he Refusing to pay the same Stands Bound before the Worsppll Justices Tulley for his the said Bootts Appearence in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions That yor Petr hath Attended this Honble Court since Monday last And for as mach as she being a Poor Servant and in great want of the sd Wages

Yor Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to order that She may be paid the said Wages as unto yor Worshipps Shall Seeme meete

And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc
