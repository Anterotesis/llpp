To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the same County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Michael Downs and William Jones Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and liberty of Westminster (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 11th. day of June 1794. Arabella Duflos (Insane) was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

P Richardson Jas. Birchall John Griffith Fredk. Bock John Lewis John Gordner James Chalk Gn Boulter Wm Watson Wm Corbett John Jenkins} Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor
