To the Worship full his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwdns. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westmin the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Jas. Fraser and Wm. Jones Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Thirteenth Day of November last Ann Watersaged about Thirty years Wife of William Waters was removed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middlx to the said Parish of St. Ann Westm whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgement of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwdns and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields and all persons Concerned do Attend this Court some day in this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitionrs. shall pray Etc

Jo: Kendall Agent for the Appellts
