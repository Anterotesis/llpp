To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Wrexham in the County of Denbigh

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Paul Pechell and D Wilmot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof beeing of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfthday of January last Robert Simon Charity his wife and their children vizt. William about 12 years Thomasin aged about Eleven Years Sarah aged about Eight Years Mary aged about five Years and Philip aged about six months were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Edmonton in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Wrexham in the County of Denbigh as the place of their last legal Settlement that Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal and humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining this their Petition and Appeal and to make an Order for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Edmonton and all persons concerned to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court upon the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc

P. Parry Agent for the said Parish of Wrexham
