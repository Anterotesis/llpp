To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows in Lombard Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Benjamin Cowley and Merry Tishmaker Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of September 1762. Mary Hayward and Wilmer her Son aged near two Years were removed & Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Alhallows as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal agt. the same and Pray that the hearing & determining thereof may be Adjourned untill the next Genl. Session of the Peace to be held in and for the said County And that the Court will be Pleased to Appoint a day in the said next Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard and all other Persons Concerned then to Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the then Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs will Pray Etc Fras. Salt Attry for appellants
