To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace Assembled in Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order Dated the 14th: of December last- Under the hands and Seals of Tho: Rand and A. Ward Esqrs. two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx. (Quor Unus) George Green. Was removed- from the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx. to the said parish of St: Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last Legall on Settlement.

That Your Petrs: find themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices, And humbly Appeal Against the Same to this honble. Court.

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray Yor: Worpl. will be- Pleas'd to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the- Poor of the said Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate And All Other Persons Concern'd to Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court Upon and touching the said Appeal. And that Your petrs: may have such Reliefe herein As to Your Worps. Shall Seem meet Etc.

And Your Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.

9 o. Janry: 1716:
