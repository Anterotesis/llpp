To the Right Worshipfull His Majtys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Christopher in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of on Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Nicholas Jeffreys and N Booths Esqr. Tweo of this Majtys Justices of the peace [...] County of Middx (Quor un) bearing Date the Twenty- second Day of January Anno Dui 1724. Ann Hallding Widdow was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above Barrs in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Christopher London, alledging the said Parish of St. Christopher to be the place of her last legall Settlement.

[...] your Petitioners conceiving them Selves aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeale to yer. Worships there from And humbly pray That your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses, & order That the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above Barrs in the County- of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain Day by your Worps to be app [...] to near & above by the Friday [...] Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale

And yor. Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
