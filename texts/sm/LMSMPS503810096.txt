To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace For the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Gabriel in Ten- Church Street in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of William Withers and Francis Hole Esqrs two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace (whereof one is of the Quorum For the County of Middx bearing date the 3d day of September 1747 Alice Cropp a Lunatick was remov'd from the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middx to the Parish of St Gabriel in Fenchurch Street in the City of London, Adjudging that to be the last Legal place of Settlement of the said Alice Cropp, by which said Order of removal we Apprehend one selves Aggrieved and Humbly Appeal [---] against the same to this Court

Your petitioners therefore Pray Your Worships to Appoint a day For the Hearing of the said Appeal & Pray to be Relieved in the Premisses And Your Petitioners with Pray

Libert Dorrien George Sumner} Church Wardens
