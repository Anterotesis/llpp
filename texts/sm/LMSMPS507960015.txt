To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Sampson Wright Knight and Thomas Gilbert Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth Day of April last John a Male Bastard Child aged Ten Weeks (therein said to be deserted by his Mother Elizabeth Hughes Singlewoman) was removed and conveyed form the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby you Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas. Dyson Solicitor for the Appellants
