To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Page

Sheweth That the Year last past Your Petr. served the Office of Constable for the Hamlet of Royetts In the Parish of Harrow On the Hill in this County and that there being this Year noe Court Leet held and kept for the Mannor of Harrow aforesd. Your Petr. cannot be Discharged from the sd. Office

P Thos: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. worspps To be pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the sd. Office and that Ralph Higgins a fitt and able Person may for the Year Ensueing serve the same [...] your worspps soe think fitt.

And yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc.

Thomas Page
