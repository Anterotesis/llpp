To the Rt: Worshipll His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd Assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by are Order under the Hands & Seales of Stephen Brice Esqr & St. John Colbatch Kt. Two of His Matics Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth Day of April Instant Joseph Gillet aged about Eleven Weeks was removed from parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the Said Pish of St. Giles in Fields as the Place of the last legall Settlemt. of the said Joseph Gillet.

Yor. Petr. think themselves agg [...] by the Judgemt. of the Said two Justices of the Peace Appeale to this Cort. & humbly pray that the Church- -wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Pish of St. Martin in the Fields and all other parties concerned may be Ordered to Attend the Court Some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
