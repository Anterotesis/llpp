To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in

General Quarter Session at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth,

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Milner and William Sloane Esqrs: two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Eleventh day of March 1725 last Past, Mary Wary Widow was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields, Whereby Your Petitioner Conceive themselves to be Agrieved.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices, and Pray your Worships will be pleasd to appoint some day in this Present Sessions to hear & Determine the matter touching the said Appeal.

And your Petrs: Shall ever Pray Etc
