To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Chettenham in the County of Gloucester

Sheweth That by an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and Philip Neve two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middlesex (One whereof was of the Quorum)bearing date the twelfth day of January One thousand Seven hundred and Ninety Six was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex to the [---] Parish of Chettenham in the County of Gloucester as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Young

That your Petitioners Consider themselves aggrieved by the said Order and are advised that the same is Contrary to Law

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships that they may have leave to lodge their Appeal against the said over so as the same may come on to be heard before your Worships during the presents Sessions and that all persons Concerned may be ordend to attend

Hill & Meredith Solicitors for they Churchwardens and Overseers of Chettenham
