To the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor and the rest of his Majestys Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Goal delivery of Newgate held for the County of Middlesex}

The Humble Petition of John Harvey

Sheweth That your Petitioner on his coming to London in April last was apprehended and Commited to Newgate on Suspicion of his being one of the persons required to Surrender by his Majestys Order in Council published in the London Gazett & of the 17th of Janry last for Smuggling

That your Petitioner was on the 22d. of June last brought to the Kings Bench And Tryed there as a person Convicted and Attainted by verdict and Judgment of Felony But It appearing to the Court that the Act of Parliament had not been Strictly pursued your Petr. was acquited Yet he has been ever since Confined in Newgate

That your Petr was and is Intirely Inocent of the fact wherewith he is Charged

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays this Honourable Court will be pleased to Order that Your Petr may be Tryed for the sd Fact or be discharged from his Imprisonment this present Sessions or Make such other Order therein as this Honourable Court Shall think fit

And your Petr Shall ever pray
