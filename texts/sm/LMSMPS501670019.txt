To the Rt Worshippll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of the Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of Jospeh Steers of ye Liberty of Norton Folgate in ye sd County

Sheweth That yor Petrs: rents a house of Six pound P Ann in ye said Liberty & is charged 12d: & Month to the poors rate besides Overasey when others of better ability and who live in house of Greater Rent do not pay so much Wherefore yr Petr thinks himselfe agrieved & humbly appeale to this Honrble Court against the same

And prays that yor Worshipps would be Pleased to give him such Reliefe in the premises as to yor Worshipps shall seem accept.

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
