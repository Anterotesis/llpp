To the Worshipful his majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said County

Sheweth That by and Order under the hands and Seals of Wm. Prigg [...] & Wm. Hyde Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City & Liberty of Westr. (one where of of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. day of Decr. last. Lucy Moss aged seven Years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Sqr. in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre by the Name of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre without as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to the aggreved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray Relief in the premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions for bearing and determining this their said appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray

Wade Junr. Sollr. for Appeld.
