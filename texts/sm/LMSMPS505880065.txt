To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hick Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the twentieth Day of July last Mary Evans Widow of John Evans Baker deced:and her two Children namely Mary aged Seven Years and upwards and Katherine aged near six Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County To the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of the last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal May be saved to your Petitioners and that the hearing and Determining may be Adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this County

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Richd Aynge Lewis Perier John Allison Francis Deschamps} Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
