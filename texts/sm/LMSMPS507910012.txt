To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of his Majesties Tower & of London and County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble & Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the Eighth day of October instant under the hands and Seals of Wm Quarrill Esqr. and John Maptes Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of his Majesties Tower of London and County of Middlesex Mary Franklin the Wife of Thomas Franklin a Mariner now Abroad beyond the Sear was removed from the Parish of Trinity in the Minories in the Liberty of his Majesties Tower of London and in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners find themselves Aggrieved by the said Order a Warrant of Removal and humbly Appeal against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Trinity in the Minories in the Liberty of his Majesties Tower of London and in the County of Middlesex and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court this Sessions and to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the premisses And that your Petitioners may have such relief therein as to Justice and Law doth Appertain

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
