To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition of Samuel Fielding on the behalfe of Thomas Fielding his Son.

Humbly Sheweth That by a certain Ordr. & under the Hands and Seals of Mathew Hewitt and Thomas Pindar Esqr. bearing Date. the 2d Day of August last Your. Petitrs. said Son was Adjudged the Pntative. Father of a Female Bastard Child borne on or about the 22d Day of November 1718 on the body of Ellen: Robertson Singlewoman And there by Ordered to pay severall Sums therein. mentioned Which said Sums Yor. Petrs said Son by reason of his poor Circumstances and. being out of all manner of busyness was no waies able to pay Soe that your Petitr by his Industry from time to time hath pried the same and given Security to the Churchwardens of St. James Clerkenwell for Indempnifying the same parish That your Petr not only Labours under the Hardshipp of paying the Mother Two Shillings and Six Pence P Weeke for the Mainteinance of the said Bastard Child but freguently his Family meet with severall Insults front her

That the said Child being aged neare Five Years. and Yor: Petr being very Desirous of having the Educating of the same and where the said Mother or any other person may have recourse to it.

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly hopes this Court will be pleased to consider his hardshipps and to relieve him in the prmises as to this Court shall Seem Meet And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

S: Fielding
