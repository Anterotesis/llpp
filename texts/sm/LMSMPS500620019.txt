To the Right Worspl: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Sarah Diston of the Pish of St. James Clerkenwell in the sd. County widow.

That Some time past Yor. Petr. tooke in one Francis Bromfeild as a lodge That the sd. Fran: Bromfeild being aged 105 Yeares and upwards and noe ways capeable of helping himselfe Soe that he is became Very troublesome to Yor. Petr. And he being a Penconer belonging to the Said Parish Yor. Petr. hath often requested & desired the Church: :warden & Overseer of the Poore thereof to take the paid Francis Bromfeild from Yor. Peticoner which they refuse to doe.

P Livestley Yor. Pr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. worps to order that the said Churchwarden & overseers of the Poore may take the Paid [...] : Fran: Bromfeild from yor. Petr. [...]

And Yor. Pr. Shall er pray Etc. Sarah Diston
