To the Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Robt Stephens

Sheweth That your Poor Petr. and wife are so reduced thro his long Confinement that he not only lyes Starving in Goal but his Poor wife and Children are also Starving at home for want of your Petrs. daily Labour they having no other dependance for their bread

Yr. Pet. therefore beggs that your Worships will Order your Petr. to be discharged without Fees otherwise he must lye and Starve in Goal for want

And your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
