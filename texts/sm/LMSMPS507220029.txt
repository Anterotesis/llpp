To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Court of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Stephen in Colemar Street London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one concerned being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second day of December last past Catherine Pitman Widow of John Pitman deceased, and the child named Catherine aged about two Years, were received from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Stephen in Coleman Street, as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners concerning themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices, humbly appeal to this Honourable Court against the same.

And Pray that this Honourable Court will please to appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determination of the said Appeal and to make such Order therein as shall be just.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

W: Holder Ses: for the Appellants.
