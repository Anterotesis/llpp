To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quar [...] Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Ant. Wroth & Wm. Booth Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the 27th. day of Sept. last for the removed al of Elizabeth Hill otherwise Scot from the parish of St. Ann & Agness in the Liberty of Westmin- -ster to the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx. as the place of her last Legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Order do humbly Appeal therefrom & prays to be releived against the same as your Worships shall seem meet And that a day may be Appointed for hearing of this Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray

Eliz-Hill Otherwise Scot taken into St. James Clerkenwell Worke house 9 Novr-1737 & brot. to bed of a Bastard Child the 26 of Same Month-

Wm Browning Charles Ashley Jno Airag.
