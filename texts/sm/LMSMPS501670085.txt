To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties: Justice of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genall Quarte [...] Sessions Assembled

The Humble peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: James Westmr. in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorum unus) bearing gate the 16th: day of October last Sussan Yates wife of Jeremiah Yates was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in this County to the sd Parish of St: James Westmr as to the place of the last Legall Settlemt: of the sd Jeremiah Yates.

That your Petrs. think themselves agreived be the said Order and Humbly appeale to this Honble: Court agt: the Same

And pray that your Worships would be pleased So Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St: Giles in the Twelve and all other persons concerned to attend this Court Some day this prsent Quarter Sessions to [...] and abide the Judgmt: and determinacon of this Court touching the said appeale

And your petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
