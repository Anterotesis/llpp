To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their present Session [---] assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Tomnoship of Eaglesfield in the Parish of Bringh in the County of Cumberland

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Spencer Colepepper and James Girdler Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex Ann Harrison and six of her Children Vizt. Elizabeth aged thirteen Years Joseph aged Eleven years Mary aged nine years Edward aged seven years Sarah aged five years and John aged three year were removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the said Tonnship of Eaglesfield in the Parish of Brigham aforesaid as the Place of their Last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be reserved untill he next Quarter Session

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc

I. Jenings Accomey for the Appellants
