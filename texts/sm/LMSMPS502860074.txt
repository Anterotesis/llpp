To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace of And for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petitioner and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Henry Norris and Samuel Tyssen Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Second day of October instant James Bishop was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Jno at Hackney in the said County to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westminster he the place of his Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petitioners thinking themselves agreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the same.

Therefore humbly Pray your Worsp. will please to Order, that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St John at Hackney may attend this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yr. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
