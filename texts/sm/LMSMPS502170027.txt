To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their General Sessions.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth, That by Vertue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of John Milner Esqr and Sr John Gonson Knt[---] two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorum Unus) bearing date the 19th. day of July last Owen Phillips and Ann his Wife were removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices.

Humbly appeal therefrom And Pray this Court will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Giles in the Fields and all Parties herein concerned may Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of his Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs: (as in duty bound) shall ever pray
