To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Egham in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Ellys and George Howard Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the [...] County of Middlesex one Whereof is of the Quorum bearing Date the Third Day of October one Thousand seven Hundred and forty seven (which Order was not Served on Your Petitioners till the Twelfth Day of the said Month of October which was after them Commencement of the General Quarter Sessions at that time assembled for the County of Middlesex by Reason Whereof Your Petitioners were prevented from [...] [---] Appealing to the said General Quarter Sessions) One Elizabeth Bagley Widow of Michael Bagley lately Deceased and Catherine her Child were removed from Out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster into the said Parish of Egham in the County of Surry as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Bagley and Catherine her Child

Your Petitioners therefore humbly conceiving them selves aggreived by the said Order do Appeal against the same and pray that this Honourable Court will be pleased to Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. Margaret Westminster and all the Parties concerned to appear at the next General [...] Sessions of hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeals

Harvey Acton Sollr for Appellants
