To the honourable the Justices of the peace for the County of Middsex

The humble petition of William Web[---] an Inhabitant in the Pish of St James Clarkenwell in this County of Middsex Labourer who hath a wife and three Children he being of a long time sick and not able to work is Reduced to great want and can get Nothing from the Said pish to Support him in his great Necessity, yor poore peticioner humbly beggs that you would be pleased to take into consideraccon this yor poor peticioner to make an order that the Sd pish may alone to help time in this his great Necessity and yor petioner should Cuer pray
