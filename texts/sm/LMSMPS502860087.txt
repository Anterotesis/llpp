To the Right Worpfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Gregory in ye. City of London

Sheweth

That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Richard Manley & Thomas Robe Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth Day of September 1732. a Person by the Name and- Addition of Elizabeth the Wife of Thomas Taff, with her Sen Thomas aged Six Weeks, were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County of Middx, unto the Said Parish of St. Gregory in the City of London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Gregory to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Peticonr. conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices, Do therefore appeale to your Worps therefrom, And humbly pray, That you will be pleased to hear them in the prmes, & order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County of Middx may- appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticonrs. Shall pray Etc.
