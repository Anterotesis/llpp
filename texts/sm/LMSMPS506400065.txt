To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Helen near Bishopsgate in the City of London.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Burfd. Camper Esqr. and Jno. Sherwood Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of April last Mary Hays Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Helen as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order.

Your Petitioners therefore pray that their said Appeal may be heard and determined at this present General Session of the Peace And that your Petitioners may be relieved against the said Order.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

John Colcraft Thomas Houston} Church Wardens

W [...] Clark James Stone} Overseers.
