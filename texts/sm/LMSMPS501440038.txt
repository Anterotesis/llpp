To the Rt Worsppll his Matiss Justices of the Peace for the [...] of Middx

Middx. ss To the Rt. Worspll: his Majties. Justices of the peace for ye sd County

The humble Peticon of William Prat gen High Constable of South: Mims and Hadley wth: in the Hundred of Edmenton in ye sd County

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr: has duely and Faithfully served the said Hundred as one of the High Constables thereof for the space of three years last past & upwards

And That your Peticoner being no Scholar the sd Office has been a great Burden & Charge yor Peticonr who humbly als [...] discharged from the sd Office

And prays yor. Worships would be pleased so appoint one of the persons her undernamed (being very well qualified) to serve the sd Office instead of yor. sd Peticonr

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc

Wm. Clarke of Hadley

[x] Hen: Gladman Phill Bocket of S Mims
