To the right worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and seals of William Bleamire and Henry Kinnard Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the third day of October one thousand seven hundred and ninety eight Harriett Jarrett with her two Children Vizt. John aged two years and William aged eight Months have been removed from the parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the aforesaid parish of Saint Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore humbly appeal against the same and pray that this worshipful Court will please to appoint some day in the present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned do then attend this Court.

And your petitioners Etc.

Middlesex At the General Quarter Session of the peace of our Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County on Monday the 22d. day of October in the 38th. year of the reign of our Sovereign Lord George the third King of Great Britain Etc

It is ordered that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all persons concerned do attend this Court at the Sessions house aforesaid on thursday the 25th. day of this instant October at nine o' Clock in the forenoon of the same day to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court Selby
