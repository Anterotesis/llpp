To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Olave in Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth. That by a Warrant or order of removal dated the ninth day of September last under the hands and Seals of William Blackmore Esqr. and [...] The Reverend Parker Rowlands clark Sarah Harris the Wife of George Harris (who by such Warrant is said to have lately deserted and left her) and her two Children Vizt Mary Harris aged four Years and Esther Harris aged Eight Months are removed from the parish of Saint George in Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Olave in Southwark, where they are now Chargeable

That the said George Harris the husband of the said Sarah Harris and father of the said two Children Marry Harris and Esther Harris having rented a house in Fox's Lane in the parish of Saint paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex your petitioners humbly Apprehend their last legal Settlement to be in such parish and not in the parish of St. Olave Southwark and therefore that the said parish of St. Olave Southwark ought not to be Burthened with the Charge and expense of Maintaining the said paupers

Wherefore Your Petitioners humbly Appeal against the said Warrant or order of removal and pray that the same may be discharged and that your petitioners may be paid for Maintaining the said paupers together with the Costs of this Appeal by the Church Wardens and Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St. George Middlesex or that your petitioners may have such other relief in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem Meet.

And your petitioners will ever pray Etc
