To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Mary Athill in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Browne and Jacob Harvey Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx (Quorm Unus) bearing date the 27th day of June last past Elizabeth the wife of Phillip Riley was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County unto the said Parish of St Mary Atthill in the city of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Riley-

That your Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom And Pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in this County may appear before you to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
