To the Rt. Worpll His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of the Thomas thorowgood & George Warner [...] Head boroughs of the town of New Brentford [...] [...] County

Sheweth That yor. Petrs. for the Year last past have Executed the Office of stead boroughs wth in the said Town

That James Clitherow Esqr Lord of the Mannor of Bostan cum Brentford [...] wch Mannor the sd. Town of New Brentford doth lie hath not too many years last past holden [...] likely to hold any Court Leet for the said Manner whereby new stead boroughs may be Appointed in their Rooms

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pay to be Discharged from the said Office & that John Derry & Richard Halls inhitants of the said Town & Persons approved of by the sd James Clitherow may be Appocased Hend boroughs in their Rooms

And Yor Petrs. as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Thomas Gower Wm. Fimmock} present

Charles Finch Nicolas Hawkins} in their place
