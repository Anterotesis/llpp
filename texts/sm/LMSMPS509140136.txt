To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Blamire and R, Clark Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 5th day of June 1795 Thomas Bracken aged about 5 Years was removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Margaret as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjuration and order of the said Two Justices and pray that your Worships will appoint a Day in this present Session for hearing and determining thereof and that all persons concerned therein may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

Jon. Smith} Church Wardens

Robt. Hooker Cha Mills} Overseers of the Poor
