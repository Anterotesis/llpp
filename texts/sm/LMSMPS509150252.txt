To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Collick and John Hatchett two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth Day of September instant Mary Holmes and her three Children Vizt. Elizabeth aged about Eight Years, Richard aged about five Years, and James aged about fourteen Months, was Removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to the Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden in and for the County to hear and determine the said Appeal according to Law

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc [...] Sydenham Rd Murthwait Wm Main Byfeld
