To the Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Wace Joseph Griffin on the behalfe of themselves and other late Constables of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the sd County.

Sheweth

That Yor. Peticoner have in the whole disburs'd the sum of Four pounds eight shillings and six pence upon the Accts of passing of Vagrants the Years 1718. and 1719 when they were Constables which sd money they have been out of pockett ever since

Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to Order that they may be releiv'd in the sd prmisses

And yor petr. severally (as in duty bound) shall ever pray Etc.
