To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the county of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the city and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the eleventh day of October instant Ann Lyon is removed from the Parish of Saint James with in the Liberty of Westminster in the county aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Under shaft as the place of her last legal Settlement

Wm. Nash Sollr. for the Appellants } Your Petitioner there fore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices Do Humbly appeal to this Court for such Relief in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
