May it Please your Worships

My Husband Robert Robinson a prisoner in New prisson Lyeing Only for his Fees Which I am not able to cause haveing three small Children most humbly Prays that your worships would be Pleased to Order him his Liberty & as in Duty bound shall everpray

Martha Robinson

Octo 19. 1730 To the Worshipfull the Justices on ye bench at Hicks Hall
