To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Benedict alias Bennett Fink London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant of Order under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and William Bleamire Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of December one thousand seven hundred and eighty five Fanny Winslow Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Benedict alias Bennett Fink as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Fanny Winslow Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing and determining thereof adjourned unto the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
