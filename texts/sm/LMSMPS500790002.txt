To the Rt. Worspl his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of Grace Appleyard

Sheweth

That there is due to your Petr. for Wages [...] from Sr. Charles Inglefeild Barot. the Sum of Four by and Six pounds and Two shillings which yor. Petr. hath often Demanded of the sd. Sr. Charles Inglefeild but he from time to time neglects & refuses to pay the same and forasmuch as yor. Petr. being in great want of the sd. Wages.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspps to be pleased to Grant this Honble Bench Warrt for bringing thesd. Sr. Charles Inglefeild into this Court this pnt Sessions Soe that yor. Petr. may be paid her sd. Wages

And Yor. Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Grace Appleyard
