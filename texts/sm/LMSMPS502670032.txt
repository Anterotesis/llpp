To the Worshipfull The Chairman and The Best of the worshipfull the Justices at the Generall Sessions at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County of Middx

The Humble Petr. of Benjamin Hadley Cloggmaker

Sheweth That your Petr. became bound in a Recognaze for Thomas Lewin of St. George in the East in Ratcliff highway before the worshipfull Thomas Evant Esqr. one of his Majtes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx for his personall apperance before this Honrble Court in feburary Last and That the said Thomas Lewin has absconded from his Habitacon being then a Erenent to your Petr. and your Petr. not being able to finde the sd. Thomas Lewin and Your Poor Petr. being fearfull of Comeing into Truble by the said Recognanze

Therefore Your Petr. most humbly prays your Worshipes in your known Goodness will be pleased To order your Petr. to be Discharged from the said Recognanzes in [...] such manner as to your Honours shall Seem meet

And Your Petr. us is Duty bound shall Ever Pray

Benj Hadley
