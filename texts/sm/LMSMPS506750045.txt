To the Worshipfull bench of Justices or whom it may Concern.

The humble Petition of Thomas Waters, Smith Eastsmithfield in the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate; Concerning the prosecution of Daniel Empson in July Sessions last held at Guildhall in King Street Westminster. And Whereas your Petitioner was wrong informed concerning the Commencement of the said Sessions where by Recognizance is become for feited and am liable to be Estreated in the Court of Exchequer for levying Twenty Pounds on my Goods and Chattels

And your Petitioner being very Poor and not able to pay such a sum, being not worth Five Pounds in any wise so ever Humbly desireth that he may be permitted to with draw his Recognizance and your Petitioner as in duty bound shall pray.
