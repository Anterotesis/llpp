To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of R. Clark and F Mathue Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirtieth day of April last Jeffery Hamett Oneale a Singleman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London as the Place of the last legal, Settlement of the said Jeffery Hamett Oneale

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Ward or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and pray this Court that the Benefit of the said Appeal be saved unto them until the next General Session to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all Persons Concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will ever pray
