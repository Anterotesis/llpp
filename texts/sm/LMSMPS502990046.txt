To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petiticon and appeale of the Churchwar- dens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James Garlick hith London

That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the Said County of middx one being of the Quorum bearing [...] the fourth Day of March last One Mary Howard Singlewoman was passed from the Parish St Leonard Shoreditch in the County of middx to the Said Parish of st James Garlick hith London as the Place of the Last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conserving them selves aggrieved by the Judgement of the Said two Justices Humbly appeale to this Court for Releise of the Premisses

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Robmson agent for the appellants}

Served a Copy upon Mr Goswell's Maid Church- Wardens of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch 23d: April 1734} P Jnv: Littioth
