To the Rt. Worpll: [---] Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of new Brentford. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of Henry Vincent & Richard Lilly Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 27th Day of October last past Hannah Beedon the wife of John Beeden Junr. Labourer was removed & conveyed from the parish of Kensington in the sd County to the Hamlett of new Brentford in the parish of Hanwell in the County aforesd alledging therein that the sd Hamlet of new Brentford in the sd parish of Hanwell is the place of the last Legall settlemt. of the sd Hannah Beeden.

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants yor Petrs being aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices humbly Appeal against the same.

And pray that your Worps. wou'd appoint some Day this prsent Sessions for all parties concern'd to attend this honble Court to hear & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeals

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
