To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Isleworth in the sd: County

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Griffiths and George Reid Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of August 1785 Harriet Herne aged Eight years and George Frederick Herne aged six years the two Children of John Herne were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlemt:

That your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order and do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that a Day may be appointed of this present Session to hear and Determine the matter of the said Appeal and that all parties concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc. Michell. Sollr. for Appellants
