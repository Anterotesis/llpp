To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Place in and for the said County assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Isleworth in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of B: Kennett, doctor in divinity, and Mich: Downs Esquire [---] , two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of February One thousand, Seven hundred, and ninety one, Martha Drew the Widow of John Drew deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster, in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Isleworth in the said County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order, and do appeal against the same

Your Petitioners, therefore humbly pray, that a day may be appointed, of this present Session, to hear and determine the master of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Michell: Sollicitor for Appellants
