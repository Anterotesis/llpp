To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Ann Westm in the said County of Middlx.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Grey and Thomas Deveil Esqrs. two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the city And Liberty of Westm bearing date the Fourteenth day of July last Mary Allen aged about 35 Years wife of John Allen gone from her and their three Children (that is to say) Elizabeth aged about 13 years Henrietta 8 years and Ann upwards of two years were removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the City of Westm in the said County of Middlx to the said Parish of St. Ann whereby your Petitionrs. conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgment of the said two Justices & Pray your Worships will be pleased to order that the Churchwardns and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields & all persons concerned do attend this Court some Day in this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeals.

And shall Pray Etc.

Jo. Kendall Sol for Appellts.
