To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoredich in the sd. County.

Shewth. That by Order under ye. Hands & Seals of Felix Feast & John Fuller Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of ye. peace for the sd. County (Quor. un.) bearing date ye. 18th: day of May last Sarah . ye. Wife of Robert Moakes with her two Children Martha aged about three years and James aged about three Months were removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the sd: County to our Sd: Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch as to the place of their last Legall Settlemt:

That yor: Peticonrs: think themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Humbly appeal to this Honble: Court against the same

And prays that yor: Worshpps would be pleased to Order that the Church- -wardens and Overseers of the [...] poor of the sd: Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate and all other Psons Concerned may attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt: and determinacon of this Court touching the sd: appeal

And yor: Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc:
