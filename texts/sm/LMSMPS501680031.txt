To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the rest of the Justices Assembled in Sessions

The humble peticon of Job Baker and Sarah his wife

Sheweth That yor Petrs through the Judgement of this Court are in Custody in Newgate for crimes by them committed and covicted of there to remaine for the space of one Month and likewise under the Fine of Twenty pounds.

Your petrs therefore humbly prayes your worships to grant Liberty to Sarah his wife their house haveing noe body to look after itt which will be to their utter ruien and humbly prayes a mittigation of the said Fine they being but young beginners and but of low circumstances they pronnseing for the future needs to committ the like offences and yor petrs shall for ever pray Etc

the mark of Job [x] Baker Sarah baker
