To the Right Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx,

The Humble Peticon of Appeale of Samuel Bolton

Sheweth That yor. petr. was by ordr. undr. the hands & Seals of Bastwick Johnson & Thomas Frampton Esqrs, two of her Majties. Justices of the peace of this County dated the 2d day of July last adjudged the reputed Father of female bastard Child bane in that part of the parish of Saint Dunstan Shoreditch als Stepney called or known by the Humble of Wapping Stepeny in this County [...] of the body Martha Payne being Charge & to pay the Sevall Sumes of money therein expences by the sd ordr appears;

That yor petr concerneing himself to be Aagreivd by the Adjudicacon of the sd two Justices doe humble Appeale to this Court therefrom;

Yor petr. therefore humbly prays yor Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the [...] sd Hamblett of Wapping Stepney to attend this Court to was & abide the Judgmt. thereof touching this appeale

And yor Petr Shall Pray Etc.

Saml Bolton
