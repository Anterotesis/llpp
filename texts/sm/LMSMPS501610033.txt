To the Right Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter

Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Walter Collett of the parish of St Giles in the fields

Sheweth That Yor: Petr: served the Office of Headborough in the [...] parish the Year last past & is appointed one of the Overseers of the Poor in the said parish for this present Year.

That yor. Petr. hath not money to expend & lay down to the use of the Poor of the said Parish & his wife also lieth very ill of the Rheumatism So hath no Servant to takes Care of his business in his absence

There fore humbly Prayes the Premisses [...] Considered he may Discharged from [...] said Officer.

And Yor Petr as in Duty bound Shalled [...]
