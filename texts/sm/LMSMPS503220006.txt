To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of William Newman an Inhabitants of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County

Sheweth

That on Easter Tuesday being the Twefth day of April last At a Publick Vestry held in the Church of the said Parish Your Petitioner was Chosen Nominated and Appointed one of the Scavengers of the said Parish for the Year ensuing and Accordingly by a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County bearing date the Eighteenth day of May Instant was so Confirmed

That your Petitioner has paid his Time to one of the late Churchwardens of the said Parish to Exempt him from Serving all Offices in the said Parish by which said Choice your Petitioner Apprehends himself agreived and humbly appeals to this honourable Court

Therefore your Petitioner prays that he may be discharged from Serving the said Office or be otherwise releived as this Court shall see meet

And Etc

Willm. Newman
