[...] Justices of the Peace for ye County.

The humble Petition and Appeal of George Barber of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Petitr: is rated to the poors Rate of the said [...] Peace of Land he Rents in the said [...] the Rate of Foncscore pounds & Anun.

[...] payes noe more then Sixty Pounds P for the said Land as appeares by receipt [...] and of his Landlord Wherefore he [...] ceive [...] selfe aggrieved and appeales [...]

And Your Petitr: therefore humbly. [...] prays such Relief in the premies as to this Court shall be thought meete

And yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc. Geo: Barber
