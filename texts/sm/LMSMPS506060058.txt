To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That [...] order and [...] of James Esdaile and Cadwallader Coker Esquires two of his Majestie's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eleventh Day of December Arst Ann Jones Widow of William Jones deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all Persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc.

Gs Bradshaw Thos Greening Jas Cutter [...] Robr Baldey Henry Wicks [...] Jas: Young} Church Wardens and Overseers of [...] of the said Parish of Saint Anne
