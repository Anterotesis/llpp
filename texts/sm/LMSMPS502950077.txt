To the worshipfull Bench of Justices Now Siting at Hickeses Haull in St Johns Street

The Humb Petition of John Slagg Carpinter

Sheweth That yr worships petitionr had his Left Eye Struck out by Them Treining whereas Cast the Last Sessions and fined by yr worships fifty pound settle refuseth to Com to any Complayance with yr petitions or to make him any satisfaction at all for the Grate damig dun to yr worships petitioner

[...] yr Worships poor petition humbly prayer this Honerable Court to Continue him in Gale untill such time as he shall make Satisfaction & as In duty Bound shall Ever pray [...]

John Slagg
