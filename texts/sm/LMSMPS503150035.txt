To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Gent Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for the sd. County

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of Nico: Jeffreys & Ste: Beckingham two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of Janry last Joseph Cowper Aged abt. Seaventy Eight Yeares was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of his last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be Agreived and agt. the sd. Order doe Appeale

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this prsent Sessions to hear and Determine them as [---] touching the sd Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray

Rob: Chimmow Sollr. for the Appellts }
