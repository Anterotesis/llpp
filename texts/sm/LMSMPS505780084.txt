To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of St John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of November last, Grace Parker and Mary her Daughter aged thirteen Years and upwards were removed form the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Pars in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. John of Wapping, the said Justices adjudging the last legal Settlment of the said Grace Parker and Mary her Daughter to be in the said Parish of St. John of Wapping That your Petitioners conceiring themselves to be Agguered by the said Order and determination of the said Justices do humbly Appeal therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day of this present General Session of the Peace for the hearing of this their said Appeal And that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn and all Parties concerned may then and there hear and abide the final Determination of your Worships therein,

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Joseph Tinkler John Jones} Churchwardens

John Horsley Bergtham} Overseers of the Poor
