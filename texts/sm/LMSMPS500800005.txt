To the Right worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of James Morris.

Sheweth That yor Pr stands bound for appeareance in this Honble Court this present Sessions to prosecute Samson Hodges for forgery and wilfull perjury As by the Recognizance may appear That the Records and originall papers whereby the said Offences may appeare are not in the power of yor. Pr. but are in the hands of John Read L Tho: Priggs who have promised to produce the same in this Honble Court the next Sessions wth yor. Pr. with severall wittnesses is now ready to make oath of before yor. worses.

P Sweetley yor. Pr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worsps to be pleased to respite the said Recognizance till the next Sessions.

And yor. Pr. shall ev pray Etc Ja Morris
