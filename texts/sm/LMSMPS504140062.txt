To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hornsey in the sd County

Sheweth That a Rate was lately made by Yor. Petrs for the relief of the poor of the said parish & Allowed by Jacob Harvey & Hammond Crosse Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County

That by a Mistake unknown to Yor. Petrs. at the time of making the said Rate the Names of Several House holders and Occupier of Lands in the said parish which ought to have been charged therein were Omitted and left out of the said Rate, so that Yor. Petrs. cannot Collect the Monies intended to be raised by the said Rate for the necessary & relief of the poor of the said parish

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly pray that Your Worships will be pleas'd to Quash the said Rate, & Order & direct Yor. Petrs. to make a New Equal Rate, or give such other relief to Yor. Petr. as to Yor. Worships shall seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Humphy Elmes Atty. for the Appellts.
