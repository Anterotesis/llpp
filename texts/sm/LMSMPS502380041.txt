To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the said County of Middx:

Sheweth That Rebecca Shore a poor Child of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County was by the parish Officers of the same bound Apprentice to John Ditto of the Hamlett of Bethnal Green aforesaid as will appear by an Indenture bearing Date the Seventh day of July One thousand seven hundred and twenty five The said John Ditto notwithstanding the Obligation he paid himselfe under by the said Indenture has been gone away from his said Apprentice about a Year without making any provision at all for her by which means she hath ever Since that time and now is Burthensome and chargeable to the said Hamlett of Bethnal Green We therefore consideration and discharge her from her said Apprenticeshipp That she may be put out to some person that will make better provision for her

And yor peticonrs shall Ever pray [...]
