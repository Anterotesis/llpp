Middx To the Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the per [...] for the County of Middx in General Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Town of Uxbridge in the Said County of Middx

Sheweth

That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Sr. George Cooke Knt. and George Cooke Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the Eighteenth day of April last one John Stonell the Younger was removed from the parish of Hillingdon in the Said County to the Said Town of Ux bridge as the last legal place of Settlement of the Said John Stonell the Younger

That your Petrs. find themselves aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices of the peace and humbly appeal to this Court against the Same

Bloums Your Petrs. therefore pray this Court to appoint a day this present Sessions for all parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the [---] Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your Petrs. Shall pray Etc
