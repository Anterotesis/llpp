To the Worll. her Mats. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions at Hickshall

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Margtt. Westmr. in ye County aforesaid

Sheweths. That Elizabeth the Wife of John Oldham Vintner decd by Warrant Signed by James Dewey and Gideon Harvey Esqrs. two of her Mats. Justices of the peace [...] for the said County was passed from the parish of St. [...] in ye Feilds to the said parish of St. Margt. Westmr as the pl [...] of her last legal Settlement

That yor. petr. find themselves Aggreived by the [...] of the said two Justices.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Appeal to yor. Wops and pray your Order for the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the said parish of St. Martins in the Feilds to appear before this Worps. Bench to answer ye said Appeal.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
