To the Rt. Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genll. Quarter Session, at Hickshall.

The humble petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands & Seales of Thomas West and Robert Kyrby Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County (quor me) bearing date. the Sixteenth day of July last, Winifred patteloe wife of John Patteloe was removed from the Hamlett of Lymehouse in the parish of Stepney in the said County. to your Petr. said parish of St. Martin in the Fields. Whereby your petrs. humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeale. to your Worps agst. the sd. Order, And pray your Worps to appoint a day to heare & determine the same.

And your Petrs, shall ever pray Etc

Jms Timberlaker: John Bird } Ch:wardens
