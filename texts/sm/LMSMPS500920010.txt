[...] the Worshippfull the Bench [...] [...] Of Justices att Hicks's Hall.

[...] humble peticion of Phillipp Delanaway

That your Peticioner being by this Honoble [...] Coure Fined Four Nobles and being now in Newprison in every poor Condicon and is not able by any meanes to raise so mnos money to pay his Fine and Fees

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Goggs your Worshipps to Consider his Case And take of his Fine he haveing agreat Charge of Children.

And your Peticoner as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.
