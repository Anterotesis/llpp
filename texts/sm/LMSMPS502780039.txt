To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for this County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Glass house yard in ye parish of St. Botolph wthout. Aldersgate in the sd County

Sheweth That by Wart. or Order under ye hands and Seals of Richard Manley & Robt. Midford Esq two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middlx (Quor unus) beuring date the 15th. day of 7 ber last past, one William Steventon wth. Margret his wife were passed from the parish of St. Giles wthout. Cripplegate in the said County. to the sd Liberty of Glass house yard in the sd parish of St. Botolph wthout. Aldersgate in the sd County. as the place of their last legal Settlemt.

And yor. Petrs. finding themselfe aggrieved by the sd Order of the sd two Justices doe humbly Appeal agt. the same

And pray that the Church- wardens and Overseers of ye poor of the sd parish of St. Giles wthout. Cripplegate in this County may be Sumoned to attend this Court some Day in the next Genll. Sessions of the Peace to be held for this County, to hear & abide the Judgmt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Wm Rogers Francis Oliver} Over Seers
