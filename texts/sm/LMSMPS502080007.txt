To the Rt: Worpll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Gentl. Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticonr & Appeale of William King Dr. of Lawes

Sheweth That Yor Peticonr: is rated & Assessed for & towards the Releife of the Poor of the parish of Ealing in the said County for the Year 1721 [...] the Sume of tenn pounds tenn Shillings for the great Tythes ariseing within the said parish and also the Sume of Sixteen pounds Seven Shillings & Six pence towards the Releife of the poor in the said parish for this present Year for the said Great Tythes

That Yor Petr: is no Inhitant wth. in the said parish neither is Yor Petr. the Occupier of the said Tythes

Therefore humbly Conceives himself to be & aggrieved wch. the said Rate

Jas Tyton

And Prayes that the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Ealing for the year 1721 And also the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Ealing for this present Year may be Ordered to attend this Court Some Day this Sessions to Shew & Cause why Yor. Petr. Should not have Such releife in the Premises as [---] Yor. Worships great Wisdom shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray
