May it pleasd yor. Worpps.

That your a former Order of this Court yem Worpps were pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Ratliffe Should [...] not preed dallow John fire and penson for and towards his releife and mainteynance by reason his wife was a papist & also that he should affred. this Honble Court as of this morning which accordingly he is now ready

He therefore most humbly prays ye. Worpps to be a pleased to grant that his Sd. penson may not be taken away he having small Children to mainteyne who are likely to parish

And to Shall ever pray Etc.

to he paid as by Order
