To the Honble the Bench of Justices

The Humble Petition of Samuel Tricker and Margret Preston

Sheweth That your petitrs. appeared before your Honors on acct of a Prisoner and you ordered your petitioners to attend and your Petitioners being poor people humbly beg you will be pleased to order your petitioners might be releived Mr Justice Deveil was in Court Yesterday and your petitioners were ready but no opportunity to move the Court and humbly beg your honors will order your petitioners recognizance to be Discharged being poor people not having time to attend And Your Petitrs. will as in Duty bound Pray Etc

Saml Tricker Margt. Preston
