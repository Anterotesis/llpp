The Rt: Worpll: her Maties Justices of Ye: Peace (Assembled Sessions att Hicks's Hall) for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeale of Ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That is Warrant or Order Dated the 22th: day of this Instant June under the rand and Seale of Richd: Harnage Esqr: One of Ner Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx Elizabeth Holmes was removed from the Parrish of Isleworth in the said County to the Pish of St: Paul Covent Gards aforesaid, as the Place of her last Legall Settlement That Yor: Petr: and themselves Agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said One Justice And humbly Appeale against the Same to this Honble: Court.

Your Ptrs: therefore humbly prays Yor: Worpp's will be Pleas'd to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of Islerworth and other Persons Concern'd to Attend this Court Some day this Prsent Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court upon and [...] touching the said Appeale; And that yot: Petr: may have Such Releife here in as to Yor: Worpps Shall Seein meet Etc

And Yor: Petr: Shall ever pray Etc
