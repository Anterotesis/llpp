To the Worshipful Justice now assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session's House on Clerkenwell Green

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and seals of John Staples and Saml. Hawkins Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the sixth day of July instant Jane Watkins Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dionis Backchurch aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order Appeal against the same and pray such Relief as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Edward Tut [...] Thos. Powell} Church Wardens

Thos. Hudson Jams. Eley} Overseers
