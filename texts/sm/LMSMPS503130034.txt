To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. Att their Genl: [---] Session of the Peace held at Hicks Hall in & for the said County.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens. Overseers of the Poor & Parishioners of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Farmer & Andrew Osborn Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th. day of November last past George Buck. Mary his wife together with George Aged thirteen years and Thomas their Son Aged Nine years were Removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapell in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London as the place of their last Legall Settlement by which Order and Removall your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray Your Worships will be pleased to Order a Short day for all parties to Attend your Worships on this Appeal & to abide by the Order & determination of your Worships concerning the same

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Richd Berry Sollr for the Appellts.
