Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the [...] of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace

The Humble Petition of Henry Bell of No. 229 High Holborn in the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields within Holborn Division in the said County Shoemaker

Sheweth That your Petitioner being informed of the intention [...] Mr John Wild to resign the office of High Constable of [...] said Division and having been mary years an Householder in the said Division and conceiving himself capable of executing the Duties of that offce she applied to the Magistrates residing and acting in the said Division requesting to be nominated by them as a fir Person to serve the same and has had [...] which will appear (as your Petitoner is informed) by the Minutes of a General Meeting of the said Magistrates holden on the 15th.. day of May Instant in which your Petitioner is returned to the present Session for the approbation of your Worships

That your Petitioner is ready to give the Security which he has been informed the said Magistreates have recommended to be required from the Person to be chosen into the said office

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Prays that your Worships will be pleased to elect him into the said office and if so fortunate us to meet with your Approbation it shall be his constant on dearsour to deserves a Continuance thereof by a faithful and diligent discharge of the Duties of the office

And your Petitioner will ever pray etc

Henry Bell
