Midlx Ss: To the Rt Worpsl: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon of Sarah Esson Apprentice to William Reed of the Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County. Weaver.

Sheweth. That yor: Peticonrs: said Master hath Frequently beat & Misused yor: Peticonr. in a Barbarons maner without any Lawfull Cause and hath not provided her Sufficient Necessaries, So that yor: Peti [...] is unable to Continue in her Service.

Therefore yor. Peticonr: most Humbley pray's that yor: Worps would be pleased to Order the sd: William Reed, to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to Shew cause why yor: Peticonr: should not be Discharged from her Apprentice Ship.

And yor: Peticonr: Shall Ever pray Etc: Sarah Esson.
