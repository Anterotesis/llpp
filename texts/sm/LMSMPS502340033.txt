To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Howard Gent one of the High Constable wth. in ye Hundred of Edmonton in this County.

Sheweth That yor. Peticoner has served his Majty. in the Office of High Constable wth. in the said Hundred for the space of three years now last past

That yor. petr. has given constant Attendance at all publick Sessions & Meetings of the Justices Wth. in his Sd Hundred, and punctually Discharged all the Publick Levies and Demands on him relating to his said Office

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worsps. That (in pursuance of a late Order of this Court) You would be pleased to Discharge him from his said Office; And that one of the persons hereunder named [...] (being very well qulifyed) may be appointed High Constable in the room & place of yor. Petr.

And yor. Petr. shall pray Etc Thos. Howard

John Rumball John Jones Robt. Barns}

nominated to serve of Edmonton gent.
