To the right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Genl. Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty ninth day of July in the [...] Year of our Lord 1762 Martha Pricket Widow of John Pricket was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex adjudgg. the last legal Place of Settlement of the said Martha Pricket to be there and your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices Do humbly Appeal there from

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships wou'd be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions And that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James & all parties concerned in and about the premisses may then and there hear and abide the Final Determination of your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

[---] John Longland Churchw [...]

Wm: Killingworth Fras. Fergusson Peter Mellish} Overseers of the Poor.
