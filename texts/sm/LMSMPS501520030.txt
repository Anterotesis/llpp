To the Worfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Willm Lewis and Henry Wright Churchwardens of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County on behalf of themselves and other Inhabitants of the said parish

Sheweth That the Highwayes of the said Parish are very much out of repair and will require a Considerable Sume of money to repair and amend the same Which cannot be raised without the aid of this Court

Yor Petrs therfore humbly pray that a Rate or Assessment not exceeding three half pence in the pound may be made upon the said Inhabitants for repairing of the said Highwayes according to the direccon of an Act of Parliament in that case lately made, to be collected by the prsent Survey yors of the Highwayes in the said parish And that the money collected on the said Rate be employed and laid out as the Justices of the peace for this County usually meeting at the Spetiall Sessions in the said parish shall direct and appoint

And yor Petrs Shall ever pray Etc. Willm Lewis Hen Wright} Churchwardens
