To the Rt Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the field

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of John Milner & John Richardson Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the one and twenteeth day of August last Mary Lambe wife of Joseph Lambe was removed from the Parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the Said County to the Said Parish of St Martin in the Feilds as the place of the Last legall Settlemt. of the said Joseph Lambe

That your Petr Thinking themselves aggreived by the order of the said two Justices of do appeal there from to the Judgemt of determinacon of this Court

Your Petr therefore humbly pray our Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this prsnt session to hear & determine the matter touching this Appeale

And your Petr Shall be to pray Etc
