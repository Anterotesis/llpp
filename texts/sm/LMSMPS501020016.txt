To the Worpfull. her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon of John Bedall of the parish of At. Giles in the Fields Taylor

Sheweth That yor. Peticon. workes at his trade for the most part of the Year in the Countrey & dureing his absence his wife lodged an ill woman in his house unknown to yor. peticoner for wch. yor. peticoner Stands Indicted in this Court for keeping a disordered house

Yor. Peticoner being a very poor man & not having where wth. all to traverse the said, Indictment humbly Submitts to this Worpfull Bench, And prayes that you'l be pleased to be favourable to him in the Fine, And for the future he never will Offend in the like kind

And Yor. Peticoner will ever pray Etc.
