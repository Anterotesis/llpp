To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex Assembled at their General Quarter Session of the Peace begun and holden the 27th day of October in the 34th year of the Reign of King George the Third

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Charchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seal of Herbert Mayo DD and William Blackman Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 11th. day of October 1794 Susannah Mitchell Widow and her Child named Elizabeth Mary aged 11 years and upwards were Removed from the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Said Clement Danes as the Place of their last legal Settlement and delivered to your Petitioners to be Provided for by them according to whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and Humbly Pray Relief in the Premises And that your Worships will be Pleased to Order that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and that the Hearing and Determining thereof may be adjouns until the next General Session of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex and that some day in the Session may be Appointed for that Purpose

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
