To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex in their general Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwns. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth. That by an order under the Hands & Seals of Thos. Bullard & William Hyde Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the sd County of Middlesex (one bring of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twentieth day of November last past Frances Angel Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Frances Angel Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Order do appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the Hearing and determining of their said Appeal And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Cox Sollr for Appellts:

Turn over
