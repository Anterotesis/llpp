To the Worshipfull Thos: Lane Esqr. Chairman [...] of the Sessions of the peace holden at Hickes [...] Hall for the county of Middlesex and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace there assembled.

The Humble Petition of Henry Grove

Sheweth That your Petr. was Committed to the Gate house Westmr. by Mr. Justices Deveil for Perjury and has ben since admitted to Bail before for the said Justice.

That your Petr. is advised sevt. weighty Matters depend upon your Petrs. Trial and will be attended with ill Consequences to your Petr: as well as others Concerned therein should the same come on before Michas Term next and that your Petr. will have occasion for sevl. Witnesses who are at Prevent in Worcestershire and are Labouring Persons & employed in the Harvest and can't attend your Petr. Trial but to the great Detriment of their Families.

Your petr. therefore most humbly Prays your Worships that the Recognizance entered into by your Petr. & his two Bail may be respited untill the Sessions next after Michas Term. And your Petr. as in Duty Bound shall ever Pray.

Henry Groue
