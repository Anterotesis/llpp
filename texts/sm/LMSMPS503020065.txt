To ye. Rt. Worshipll: His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeales of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Bloomsbury

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Oliver Lambart & Miles Harper Esqrs two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the sd County one being of the Quorum be a ring Date the 27th day of May last past Richard Williams & Susan his wife were remov'd from the parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the said Parish of St. George Bloomsbury as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt: of the said two Justices of the peace Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray that Yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this Sessions to hear & determine the same

And Yor. Petrs: shall ever pray Etc.
