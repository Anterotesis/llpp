To the Rt: Worshipll: the Justices of the Sessions held at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of Elizth Wilbrake [...] of Eagles Hall in Staffordshier [...]

Sheweth That your Petr: being a poor disconselate Woman [...] and great distance from Home and being unfortunately [...] drawn into trouble the matter being now make upon [...] agreed is by reason of her extream poverty and [...] of Friends become unable to pay the incidnt charge [...]

Therefore most humbly prays that [...] yours Worshipps would be pleas'd to agre [...] her of the Fees and yourd Petitir as in [...] duty bound shall ever Pray Etc
