To the Rt: Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in General Quarter Session of the peace by Adjournment

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George a Hanover Square within the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of James Fraser and Daniel Gach Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing date the 30th: day of March 1750 Thomas said to be amale Bastard Child aged about Nine Months was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the said Liberty and County as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to the Court against the same

Your Petitrs. therefore mass Humbly prays this Court the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto the next Quarter Session of the peace to be held for the said County

And Your Petitrs. Shall ever pray Etc.

Fra: Burton Wadm. Wyndham} Churchwardens

Wm: Barkham Samll: Wilson H: Southey} Overseers of the Poor
