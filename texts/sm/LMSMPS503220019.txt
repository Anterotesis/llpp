To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the precinct of Saint Catherines in the County of Midlx

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or Order of removall Dated the 26th. day of February last under the hands and Seals of Ant. Wroth and Thomas Robe Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Elizabeth the wife of John Halse, Mary aged about Five Years and Elizabeth aged about two Years their Lawfull Children, was removed from the parish of Saint Luke in the said County To the Precinct of- Saint Catherines in the sd. County as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That your Peticoners find themselves agreived by the Warrt. or Order of the sd. Two Justices and Humbly Appeals against the same

Your Peticners therefore Humbly [...] Pray you would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Said Luke and all other persons Concerned to Attend this Court Some Day this Prsent Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the premises, And that your Peticoners may have Such releife therein as to Justices and Low Doth Appertain

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc. John Charriot Overseer of the poor
