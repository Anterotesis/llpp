To the right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret next the City of Rochester in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of A Graham and W Bleamire, Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twentieth day of August now last past Jane Smith the Wife of John Smith was Removed from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre which lieth in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Margaret next Prosecutor the said Justices thereby adjudging that to be the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Smith

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgement of the said two Justices and do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that Your Worships would be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session to hear this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish Saint Sepulchre and all Parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there hear and abide the determination of Your Worships therein

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Geo: Fenner Jno. Weller} Churchwardens

Richd. Marriner } Overseer of the Poor

{of the Parish of Saint Margaret next Rochester
