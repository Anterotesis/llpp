To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of Sir W: Gibbons Bart and P Henderson Esquire [---] Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County Dated the 11th: day of Septemper last. Ann Porter and her Child named Sarah aged four Years were removed from the Parish of Staines in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kensington as the place of the last legal Settlement of her the said Ann Porter and her Child Sarah Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and do appeal to your Worships against such Order.

Wherefore your Petitioners humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing & determining the said Appeal and that all Panties conceived may be Order'ed to attend and

and to abide and perform such Order as Your Worships shall please to make and this you Worships will please to give Your Petitioner such Relief in the promises as shall by just and your Petitioner shall pray

William Smith Jonathan Hamston -} Churchwardens

William Balls George Tunks} Overseers of the Box
