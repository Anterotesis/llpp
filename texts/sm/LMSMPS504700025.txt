Middx To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County in their General Quarter Session Assembled this 6th day of April 1758

The Petition of Benjamin Gage one of the High Constables of Edmonton Hundred in this County

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner being by this Court Elected one of the High Constables for the Hundred of Edmonton in this County did an Notice thereof Take his Oath for the Executing the same and hath According to the best of his Cunning and Understanding faith full Executed the sd Office for the Space of Two years & upwards which is a longer time than many of your Petitioner's Predeussors have Continued in that office. That your Petitioner hath no Public Money in his Hands And the Continuing in the sd office being very Inconvenient and Prejudicial to your Petitioner in the Transactions of his Business. Your Petitioner Therefore humbly desires to be discharged from the sd Office and That one of the persons hereunder Mentioned may be Appointed in his stead and Place. And

Your Petitioner as in duty bound Shall Ever Pray Etc

Benjamin Gage

Richard Harvey the Younger of South Mims Francis Sarjant of the Same Henry Hill of Hadley

We whose Names are hereto Subscribed his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd County Residing Near the sd Parishes of South Mims & Hadley do Recommend the above Names Richard Harvey the younger as a Proper person to Serve the office of High Constable for the sd Hundred of Edmonton

P. J [...] Rich. Hassell Jun [...]
