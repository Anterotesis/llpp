To the Worshipfull ye: Justices of the Peace for ye. County of Middx

The humble Petition of Usly Allen Widow of John Allen Deceased

Sheweth That Whearas yor. Petitioner aforesd. Husband was a Pentioner to ye. said County & Departed this life on the 13th. March last and not able to attend ye. Clerk for ye. last halph Year by reason of Sickness your Petitioner humbly beggs therefore

Yor. Worships ordr. for Paymt. to your Petitioner as due before his Decease

And in Duty Bound Shall Pray
