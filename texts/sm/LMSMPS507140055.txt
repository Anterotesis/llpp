To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Bustry in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Cadwallader Coker and John Walford Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and of the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty third day of June last Sarah Bone Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Bustry as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices And pray that yor Worships will please to appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petr will pray Etc

Herbt Robertson Sollr. for the appellants
