To the Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the peace to [...] at the Generall Quarter Sessione of the peace holden at [...] in and for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Dagenham in the County of Essex

Sheweth

That by an Order bearing Date the 6th. day of July last One Jane- Osborne was removed from the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx to the parish of Dagenham in- the said County of Essex as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Yor. Petitionrs. finding themselves Aggreived by the sd. Order Appeale to this County agt. the same And that yor. Wopps would please to Appoint a day for hearing thereof as to yor. Wopps Shall Seem meet Etc:

And yor. Petitionrs. as in Duty bound shall ever Pray etc.

Thos: Cresswell James white
