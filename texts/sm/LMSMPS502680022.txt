To the Right Worspll. his Matyes Justices of the peace Now Assembled in Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of Wm. Wilson

Sheweth That by an Ordr. under ye hands & Seales of John Mercer & John Milner Esqrs. two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing Date the 22d. day of Aprill last your Petr. was Adjudged to be ye putative Father of a Female Bastard Child borne in ye Pish of St. Gyles in the Feilds in ye sd. County of Middx on the body of one Mary Willard Widw. on ye 25th. day of March 1727 & baptized Susanna, and was ordered to pay several sumes of money in the said Order menconed on account of the said child.

That your Petr. thinks himselfe aggreived by the sd. Ordr. & Appeales there from to the Judgemt. of this Cort

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays that your Worspps will be pleased to Ordr. all ptyes concerned to attend this Cort some day in the next Sessions in Ordr. to hear & determine the Matter of this appeale

And your Petr. shall pray Etc
