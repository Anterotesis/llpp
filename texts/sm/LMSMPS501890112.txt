To the Right Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at ye Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Addis of the Parish of Saint Martins in the Feilds in ye said County

Sheweth That Your Petr on or abt. the 16th of June last Past upon the Infomacon of one Richard Boddington and upon the Oath of one James Brookes was Convicted before Jeffery Saunders Esqr. one his Matyes Justices of ye peace for ye said County for setting on Seven dozen and two Cloth Buttons and Six doz and tenn Cloth Hales on a Suit of Cloathes of ye Said James Brookes contrary to ye Statute in that made & Pvided

[...] [...] That Your Petr. did accordingly Appeal agt ye said Convicon unto this Court [...] [...] which Appeal was appointed to be heard [...] by this Court on Wednesday ye 13th.day of July instant That Your petr could not possibly bring on the same by Reason his attorney or Agent was Obliged to Attend ye Comrs. on a Comiccon de Lunatico Inquirendo - That Your Petr. being greatly aggreived by the said Convicon [...] humbly prays that ye. said Appeal may be heard by yor. Worships on Saturday Morning next or any other time your Worships shall please to appoint.

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
