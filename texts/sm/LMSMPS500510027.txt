To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

To humble Peticon of Robert Walker

Sheweth

That about Twelve Months past yor. Petr. became bayle for the appearance of Robert Dart in this Honble Court As by the Recognizance may appeare that the said Robert Dart some time past now your Petr. he was discharged from the sd. Reognizance for that he had withdrawne the Same & paid all charges Incident thereout but the said Robert Dart hath not appeared in the Honble Court in discharge of the sd Recognizance See that your Petr is fearfull the same wille steated into his Maties Court of Chequer And forasmuch as yor. Petr. being a very poore Man & haveing a wife & Severall sume Children to mainteyne who must Inevitably Perish unless releived by your Worspps in the Prmisses

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Grant that he may be discharged from the said [...] Recognizance the sd Dart being in New Prison.

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
