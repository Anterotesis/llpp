To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Rogers Gent. High Constable of the Hundred of Isleworth within the said County

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath served the said Office of High Constable for the term of three years and upwards He therefore Prayeth to be Discharged from His said Office and Humbly Recomendeth James Wood, John Jorden or Thomas Ealing of the Parish of Twickenham in the said Hundred as proper persons to Succeed him in His said Office

Middx approved of James Wood by R Bulstrode

John Rogers
