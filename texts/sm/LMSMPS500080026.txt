To the Rt Worppfull their Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petion of Tho: Lockington

Sheweth That yor petionr is and was upon the 28th day of November 1690 a prisonr in Finsbury Geale in the Custody of Symon pen keeper of the same for debt and being in no wise able to pay the same humbly desired the benifitt of two Act of partiamt made for the whife and discharge of pease prisonr for debt And went before a Justice of the peace to take the Oathes appointed by the sd Arts in Order to be discharged but the said keeper neglected to bring yer petionr to the Quartr Sessr. (tho often these to requested:) As She said Acts require and is since 18th drawn by wth meanes yor petionr is still detained

Yor petionr therefore humbly prayed to be discharged by this honoble. Bench he being quallified for it

And yor petionr shall ever pray
