To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeale of Ralph Lambert of the parish of in this County Hornsey, in this County.

Sheweth

That your Petr. is Charged to the Poor's Rate for this House he lives in, In the said parish of Horney, much more than what He Humbly Conceives he ought to pay, and therefore Appeals Against the same.

May it Therefore please your Worshipps to make Such Order for your Petrs. Releif in the Premisses as to this Court shall Seem meet

And your petr. as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc

Ralph Lambert
