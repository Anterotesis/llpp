To the Right Honnoble: and Rt: Worppll: this Majts: Justices of the Peace Sitting on the Bench at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of Chipping Barnet in the County of Hertford:

Sheweth

That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor [...] Pancras By virtue of a warrt: undr: ye hands & seales [...] Sd; Charles Lee and John Herbert Esqr: two of his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx Quar [...] unns Have Lately sent to us Randall Smith Alledging that the said Randall Smith is an Inhabitant belonging to our said parish of Chipping Barnet, Whereas in truth hee never had any Legall Residence there

Your Peticonrs: Therefore most Humbly P [...] That this Honnoble: Bench will be play [...] to Order the said Churchwardens and Ove [...] of the Poore of the St. Pancras to appear [...] & Shew Cause why they shall not ab [...] the Judgemt: of this Court touching [...] Setlement of the said Randall Smit [...]

And your Peticonrs: shall ever pray [...]
