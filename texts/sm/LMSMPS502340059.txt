To the Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the peace of the County of Middx now assembled in their Quarter Sessions held for the said County

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the Sd. County

Sheweth

That by Order under the hands & Seal of the Honble. George Carpenter and Thomas Scott Esqr two of his Majties. Justices of the peace of the yr. County Quor Un dated the 15th. day of this Instant April Johanna the Wife of Daniell Thorne aged 32 years and Johanna her Daughter by her Sd. Husband Daniell aged 3 months were removed from the Parish of St George Hannover Square in the Sd. [...] County unto the Sd. [...] Parish of St. Paul Shadwell as the place of their last legall Settlemt. by which order Your Petrs. conceive themselve agreived and appeal against the Same.

And humbly pray that yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint a day this present Quarter Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. George Hannover Square to attend this Court to hear the Judgmt. & finale determination thereof in & about the Premisses

And [...] shall ever pray Etc
