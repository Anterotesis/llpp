To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East in the City of London

Sheweth That ley Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Benjamin Cowley and James Esdaile Esqs two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty second day of March 1764.. Jane Stratford Single woman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Dunstan in the East as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Yoe. Peter Conceiving themselves Aggrieved thereby Do humbly Appeal against the Same And pray that this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a day at this present Session of the Peace for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Luke and all other persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment land determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Peter will Pray Etc

Fras. Salt Attly for Appellants
