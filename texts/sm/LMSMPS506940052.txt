To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Wm Aldwin Constable of the Hamblett of Weald in the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in the sd County

Sheweth That your Peticoner hath served the office of Constable for the sd Hamblet above one whole yeare And their is noe Court Leet Held for the sd Mannor of Harrow this prsent yeare by reason whereof your Peticonr cannot get discharged of his sd office of Constable

Your Peticonr therefore humbly prayes that he may bee discharged of his sd office of Constable & that your Worshipps will appoint Benjamin Tilliard Michaell Page & Nicholas Fletcher or some or one of them may bee sworne Constable in the Roome of your Peticonr

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc

Wm: Aldwin
