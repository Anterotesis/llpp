To the Rt. Worspll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Humphrys

That Yor Petr. did become bail for one John Yerbury in September Sessions in the Year 1719 for an assault That the sd John Yerbury has ever since the sd time absconded. That Your Peticoner does now Know that the sd Yerbury is now in Custody at a place called Chessey in the County of Surry That your petr. has now taken out a Warrt. for the apprehending of the sd Yerbury wch. is hereunto annexed

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor Worspps to be pleased to respite the sd Recognizance the next Sessions or to discharge Yor petr. he paying for the same Or to order such other way as unto yor. Worppt shall seem meet

And Yor. petr. shall ever pray.
