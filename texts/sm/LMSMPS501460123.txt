to the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Orpington in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by Order Dated the 17th Day of June last under the hands & Seales of John Milner and John Richardson Esqrs: two of his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of middx (Qnor unrs) One Mary Bodenham was Removed from the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the sd County of Middx to the aforesaid parish of Orpington As the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Mary Bodenham

That your Petitrs humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices and Appeal against the same the said Mary Bodenham not having any Legall Settlement in the sd Parish of Orpington But for as much the said mary Bodenham being now very Sick and Weak cannot attend this Sessions As is now ready to be attested upon Oath to this Court

Your Petitrs: therefore humbly pray yor Worspps will be pleased to order a Day next Sessions for all parties to attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal And yor Petitrs. shall Ever pray etc
