To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary At Hill in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order on Pass Warrant under the Hand & Seales of Sir Isaac Tillard Knt. & Alexander Pitfield Esqr. Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quon unus) bearing Date the Eleveth of February last past Richard Watts & Anne his Wife were removed from the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney als Stebunheath in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Mary At hill London alledging the said Parish of St. Mary At hill to be the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That your Peticoners conceiving them- selves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore Appeale to yor. Worps there from And humbly pray That yor. Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses & Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the sd. County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day by yr. Worps to be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of year Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
