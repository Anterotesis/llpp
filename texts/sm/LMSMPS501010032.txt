To the right Honble her Majties: Justices of the peace of the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of John Lawes of the parish of St. John Wapping Porter in this County.

Sheweth That yor Peticoner is Over rated to the poor of the said Parrish of St. John Wapping And yor Peticoner haveing Applyed himselfe to the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parrish to be Eased of the said rate they refused to doe it & yor Peticoner is no wayes or able to pay the same.

Therefore humbly prayes an Order of this Honble Court for the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parrish to Attend this Honble Court to Shew cause why yor Peticoner should not be Eased of the said rate or otherwise order as to yor Worps. Shall Seem meet.

And yor Peticoner Shall pray Etc.
