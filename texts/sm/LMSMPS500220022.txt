To the Rt: Worpll: their Majties Justices of the peace for: the County of Middx assembled in Qr: Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poore of ye [...] Parish of St. Martin in the feilds

Sheweth

That Mary Clerke als Hewet was by Warrt: beareing date the 24th: day of August last under the hands & Seales of Ralph Marshall Esqr: and Andrew Lawrance Esqr hereof their Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx past from the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden to the Sd pish of St: Martins therein incerting that the said Mary Clerke als: Hewett was last Legally Settled

That Since yor: petirs: find the said Mary Clerke als Hewett to be a Married woman whose husband is a Soldier that since Marriage she Lived 3 qrs. of a yeare a Servt: in St Martins parish & last of all lived a Servt: for 6 Months space in the pish of St: Paule Covent Garden for wth. reason yor Petirs: are Aggreived And

Therefore humbly appeale to this Worpll: Bench for releife & pray a day of heareing & that the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poore of St Paul Covent Garden may Attend & Shewcause why the said Mary Clerke als Hewett Should not be repased to them

And yor: Petirs: Shall pray Etc
