To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Thomas Braint of the parish of Hendon in the sd. County.

That at Easter last yor. Petr. was chosen One of the Ovseers of the Poore of the sd. Parish But forasmuch as your Petr. being aged (64) Yeares or thereabouts and very weake in Body and being much afflicted with great Disorders in his Head which causes a Violent Burning heat in his Eyes [...] that your petr. humbly conceives himselfe wholly uncapeable of Serving the sd Office.

P Th: Nash Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Whorspps to be pleased to Grant that he [---] may be Discharged from Serving the sd. Office and that the Inhabitants may proceed to chase such other Poor in his Roome to Serve the same as unto your Worspps grave wisdomes & Discretcons shall be thought most meet

And Yor. Petr. shall Even pray Etc:

Tho Braint
