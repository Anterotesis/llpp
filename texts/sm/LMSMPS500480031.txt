Appeal allowed [...] Appeal of Clerkenwell

To the Right Worshippfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for ye County of Middlesex

Sheweth The humble Peticonr & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers for the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in this County

Whereas Elizabeth Fulford wife of Urian Fulford with two small children were by Warr. under the hands and Seales of two of his Maties: Justices peace for this County passed from the Parish of St. John at Hackney to the pish of St. James Clerkenwell where the said Elizabeth Fulford with her said Children have been Chargeable Ever since That yor. peticonr. are able to prove the said Urian Fulford husband to the said Elizabeth Fulford to be last legally settled in the pish of St. John at Hackney as a hired Servt. by the space of three months or upwards

Therfore yor. peticonrs: most humbly appeales agt. the said Order of two Justices And prayes Yor: Worpps Order for all pties to appeare at his Sessions And that your peticonrs: [...] ard in the matter And to abide Such Orders as this Court shall think fitt in the prmisses As to yor. Worpps Shall seem meet Etc

And yor. peticonrs; Shall pray Etc

Dr p Jon: Ingham [x]

John Proudlove Will: Horton} Churchwardens
