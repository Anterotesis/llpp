To the Right Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Publick Fallon of Edwards Street Portman Square in the Parish of Saint Mary lebone Hatter

Sheweth That by an Order or Adjudication under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Collins and Thomas Robinson Esquire two of His Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the First day of June one thousand seven hundred and ninety nine Your Petitioner was adjudged to be the reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child which was then chargeable to the said Parish of Saint Mary lebone and of which Honora Carroll singlewoman was delivered on the Nineteenth day of July One thousand seven hundred and ninety seven And was thereupon Ordered forthwith upon Notice of the said Order to pay or cause to be paid to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone or to some or one of them the sum of four shillings weekly and every Week commencing the fifteenth day of May then last past for and towards the keeping sustentation and maintenance of the said Bastard Child for and during so long time as the said Bastard Child should be Chargeable to the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone

That Your Petitioner Conceiving

himself Aggrieved by such Order and Adjudication humbly appeals against the same and prays such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem Meet.
