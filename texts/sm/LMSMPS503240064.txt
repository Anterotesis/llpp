To the Right Worpll. His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genl Quarter Sessions

The Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Ealing [...] in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands & Seals of John Mercer & Robert Hucks Esqrs: two of his Maties; Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st. day of May last past Thomas Rest was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Ealing a the place of his last legal Settlement

Yor Petrs: thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order Appeal to this Court

And Pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields & all Persons concerned may be Ordered to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination thereof touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

S Anderson Ch Warden

Jno: Andrews Overseer of ye Poor
