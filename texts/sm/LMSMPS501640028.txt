To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of David Lopes Peregra.

Sheweth That as of last Sessions your Peticoner preferred a Bill of Indictment in this Court against one Thomas Glanister for a Misdeamenour for having found in his Custody two yards of Tycia Gold Cleathe which with other Goods was Stold form your Peticoner To which Indictment the said Glainster pleaded the Act of Indempuity and was [...] allowed the same

That the said Cloath is in the hands & Custody of one William Harrison in Drury Lane one of the Constables of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields who refuseth to Deliver the same to your Peticoner without an order from this Court

Your Petr therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order the said William Harrison to attend this Court some day this Session to Shew Cause why he refuseth to deliver the said Cloath to yor petitr:

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

David Lopes Peregra
