To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of R Davis Esqr. and H Reynett [---] at D. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 7th Decr. last Mary Swift Wife of Edward Swift who is [...] Sea and their Child Carolina were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiveing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order de Humbly appeal against the same And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Sessions that all parties concerned may attend to hear and abide the judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
