To the right Worspll their Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon up Joseph White

Sheweth

That in August 1691 yor petr became Apprentice to Lawrence Sullyvan Marnr for the Terme of Eight Years as by his Indenture of Apprentice shipp may appeare That petrs said Master was Slaine in an Ingagemt attlea with A French Privateere in August last one that Edith his relint doth since refuse to assiyne yor petrs said Indenture of Apprenticeshipp to any other pson exercising the said Act of A Moore Whereby yor Petr may be instructed in the said Act or otherwise discharge yor petr for his said Apprenticeshipp

Yor petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshpll to be pleased be Order that the said Edith Sullyvan may assayne yor petr to some other peson exercisinge the said Act of A Moore or otherwise that he may be discharged from his said Apprenticeshipp

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc.
