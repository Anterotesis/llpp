To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pay Warrant under the hands and Seals of David Wil [...] and Charles Triquet Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County (on whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the nineteenth day of May last Judith Hancock Widow was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the same County as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Judith Hancock Widow By which Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said Order and Adjudication of the said Two Justices And pray your Worships will Appoint a day in the present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

Qn: Tearr [...] Sollr. or appellants Hottan Town And your Petitioners will pray Etc.
