To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [---] Sessions of the Peace for the sd. County at the Session House for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the [...] Abbey Parish of Saint Albans in the County of Hertford.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Settlement or Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Rudge and Edward Gray Esquire two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Midsex bearing date the 17th. day of May 1784. James Painter and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Edgware, Middlesex to the said Abbey Parish of Saint Albans as the place of their last Legal Settlement, whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order of the sd. Justices And pray this Court to make such Order therein as to them shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
