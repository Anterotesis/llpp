To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of John Tull one of the petty Constables of the parish of Southmims in the said County.

Sheweth That by Order of this Court bearing Date the thirteenth day of January now last past your Petr. was appointed and admitted into the Service of releiving passing and conveying of Vagrants in and through this County in the room & place of Thomas Martin one of the late petty Constables of the said parish deced, at the rate of ten pounds for a qr.ter of a year for such Services

That your Petr. has strictly performed the sd Office from Michmas. 1731 and has been at a great Expence and Charge in doing the same

That there is now due and owing to yor. Petr. the sume of ten pounds for one qr.ter of a years allowance made him for his Trouble and Expences in the sd Service ending at Christmas last past;

And That yor. Petr. being a very poor man & having a great family of Children.

He most humbly prays Yor. Worships would be pleased to Order ye Paymt. of the sd sume of ten pounds to Yor. sd Petr.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Zach. Clarke { Agent for ye Petr.
