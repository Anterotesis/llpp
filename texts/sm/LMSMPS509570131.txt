To the Right Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in Southwark in the County of Surrey.

Sheweth

That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of G. Goodenough and J Hunter Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventh day of December now last past Christopher Gainford was removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint George Southwark in the said County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of Saint George Southwark to be the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to Your Worships therefrom and humbly Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premises And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray etc.

Townshend & Russell Southwark Solrs. for the Appellants
