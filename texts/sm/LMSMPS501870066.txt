To ye. Rgt: Worspll: his Maties: Justices of ye. peace for ye: County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeale of ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in ye Sd County

Sheweth That by a Warrtt: or Order under ye. hands & Seales of Doyley Michell and Samll: perry Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd County (Quor unus) Peareinge date ye. Seth: day of May 1720 Richard Cooper & Mary his wife & their Two: Children (Viztt:) Elisabeth aged about four Yeares & Deborah. aged about Two [...] Yeares, were removed from ye. precinct of St: Katherine in ye. Sd County of Middx to ye. Sd Parish of St: Leonard Shoreditch; as ye. place of their Last Legall Setlemts:

Your Peticoners findeing themselves agreived wth. ye Judgmt: of ye. Sd Two Justices; humbly appeales to the Honable: Court against ye. Sd Warrtt: or Order and prayes Your Worsps will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Genrll: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace for ye. Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Sd Precinct of St: Katherine to appeare to heare & abide ye. Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touchinge ye. Sd Appeale

And Your peticoners shall ever pray

John Cann Jeremiah Wright} Church Wardens

Benj: Holloway John Ceverts} Overseers of ye. poor.

Middx ss: William Burton of ye. Pish of St: Leonard Shoreditch in this County Weaver Maketh Oath that on ye. Twelfth day of this Just. July he Served a Coppy of ye. peticon & Appeale above Written & alsoe a Coppy of ye Order of ye. Genrll. Quarter Sessions wth. in Written on Thomas Holland one of ye.Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Precinct of St. Katherine in this County

Jur in Cur 14. die [...] Julij 1720 }

Harcourt

Wm. Burton
