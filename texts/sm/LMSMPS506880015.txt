To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Joseph Feild the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the sd. County.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. is Rated for this prsent Year towards the Releife of the Poor of the sd. Parish the ound of 30 [...] . Or there abts. But in Regard your Petr. being or Poor mann haveing a Wife & Five Small Childrens to mainteyne is not able to pay the sd. Rate.

Yor. Petr therefore most humbly prayes Such Releife in the prmises from this Honble Cort as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

Jos. [x] Feild his mk

P Tho: Nash
