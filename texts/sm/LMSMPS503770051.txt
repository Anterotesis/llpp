To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Aldermary in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of Geo: Errington & Tho: James Esqr. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the Sd County of Middx Cone whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st. day of March last Jane Ambridge fourteen years & Thomas Ambridge aged twelve Years were removed Conveyed from the Parish of Hampstead in the said County, to the said parish of St. Mary Alder Mary as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petr. think themselves Aggrieved by this Sd. pass Warrant or Order of the Sd. two Justices of the Peace, Appeale to this Court against the same & humbly pray your Worships to hear and Determine the same

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Dan Bolton Agent for Appelts [...]
