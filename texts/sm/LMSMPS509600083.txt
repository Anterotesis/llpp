The Worshipfull Sir John Hawkins Knight Chairman and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth in the City of London.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal Dated the 7th. day of March last under the Hands and Seals of William Blemair, and William Kevinard Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex Edward Morris was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex, to the said Parish of Saint Mary Woolnoth, as the place of his last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant and Appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray this Court that they may be heard at this present Sessions of the Peace touching the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may attend and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the same.

And your Petitioners, shall ever pray Etc
