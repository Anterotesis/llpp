To the Rt. Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Mathew Hewett and Thomas Pindar Esqrs two of his Maties Justiced of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quord Unno) bearing date the Sixteenth day of February last past Ann the Wife of Peter Ward was removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex aforesaid unto the said Parish of St. Piled & without Cripplegate London as the place of her Last Legall Settlement

That your Petitionrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal to your Worsppd there from And pray That your Worppd will be pleased to hear them in the excuised and order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County may appear at a certainday to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of your Worppd touching the Appeal

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
