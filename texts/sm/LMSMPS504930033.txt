To the Worshipfull his Majtyes. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter in January 1761 Assembled

The Humble Petcon of John Wilkins Paviour

Sheweth That your Peticoner is now in Contract with yor. Worshipps for Keeping Brentford Bridge in Repair for a Term of years yet to come to Nine pounds a year

That there is due to yor. Peticonr Nine pounds for one year Contract due at Michas last past

Therefore humbly prayes yor Worshipp to order yor Trear & to pay yor Peticon the sd. sum of Â£9

And yor Peticonr shall ever pray Etc

John Wilkins
