To the Right Worsppl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & other Inhabitants of the parish of Fryern Barnet in the Said County.

Sheweth

That at a Court Leet holden for the Mannor of Fryern Barnet under the Deane and Chapter of St. Paul the 20th of May Last Mr. John Nicholls was then elected to Serve the Office of Constable for the Said parish for the yeare ensueing, whereupon applicacon was made by severall of the said Inhabitants to the Steward of the said Mannor for granting his Warrant in order to Sweare the said Mr. John Nicholls into the said Office of Constable Who refusing to grant such Warrant, Mr. Serjeant Bonython One of his Maties Justices of the Peace for this County did without the knowledge of the said Inhabitants cause one Robert Newman to be Swerne into the said Office of Constable in the place & Stead of the said Mr. Nicholls.

That the said Robert Newman being very poore & much indicted to divers persons hath Since absconded himselfe from his habitacon Soe that he cannot execute the Said Office of Constable.

P Westley.

Your Petrs. therefore most humbly pray your Worspps to be pleased to Order that the Said Mr. John Nicholls may be compelled to Serve the said Office of Constable for the sd parish for the yeare ensueing.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Coppock Chwd
