To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session at Hick's Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sam Thresher and Willm. Timbrell Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. Day of July last, Sarah Baldwin Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint George, Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the place of her last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners conceive Themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices, And Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square and all Persons concerned do Attend this Court some Day in this present Session to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc.

[...] J:V: Mr. Francis Haynes
