To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman and the rest his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The Humbled Petition and Appeal of John Ogle

Sheweth That on the twenty fourth day of April One thousand seven hundred and ninety three your Petitioner was Convicted before Patrick Colquhoun Esqr. and Daniel Williams Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex for that he being hired and Employed to Weave and Manufacture certain Silk Materials into a peice of Stron Black Silk for John Crallan a Master Silk Weaver did afterwards take in Materials for Manufacture from another Master and before the Completion of the Work first taken as aforesaid

That Your Petitioner Conceiving himself agrieved by the said Conviction Humbly Pray that your Worships will grant him the benefit of his Appeal and- such other Relief in the Premises as- may seem meet

And as in duty bound will ever pray John Ogle
