To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Session of the peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of that part of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Edward Read and Thomas Gordon Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof is of the Quorum bearing date the 16 day of November last Ann Gould the Wife of James Gould and her two Children Vizt Charlotte Aged Nine Years and Elizabeth Aged Two Years were removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Chiswick in the County aforesaid to the said part of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to Your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Chiswick may appeal before you on a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners Etc.

Arthur Gliddon Sollr for the Appellants
