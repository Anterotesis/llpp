To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dartford in the County of Kent

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of N. lonants, and Philip Neve Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid bearing date the thirtieth day December 1793 Sarah Edwards aged about eight Years and Hannah Edward [...] aged near tow Months the Children of John Edwards gone from them, were removed and conveyed from and out of that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Dartford in the County of Kent, as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said John Edwards and his said two Children Sarah and Hannah, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and

Therefore humbly pray your Worships to receive their appeal and to adjourn the hearing thereof until the next general Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and in the mean Time that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex may have Notice thereof and that they may attend to hear and abide the Determination of the said appeal

Wm: Pennington Solr. for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Dartford abovement.
