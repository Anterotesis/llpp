To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and Appeale of Sarah Istead of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That Your peticoner is rated for Stabling in the poor and Scavengers rates of the Said parish more then She Conceived She ought to be and Appeals against the Same.

And Prays to be relieved in the premises As to this Court Shall Seem meet And Yor. Petr Shall Pray Etc Sarah Istead
