To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and Appeale of Dorothy Bawler of the Pish of St James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx Widdow

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Rated towards the Releafe of the Poore of the sd pish our shilling p Month & that youl Peticonl is a very poor woman & not Able to pay towards the Releafe of the poore of the said pish

your Peticoner humbly prays she may bee discharged from paying to the Releife of the Poore of the sd Parish

And your Peticonr shall ever Pray Etc
