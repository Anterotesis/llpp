To the Right Worpfull His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth

That by Virtue an Order or Pass [...] Hands & Seale of Two of His Majesty's Justices Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing- Date the Twelfth Day of this instant January John Corsbey & Katherine his Wife were removed from the Hamblet of Spittle fields in the County of Middx unto the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London, alledging the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate to be the last Place of their legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore Appeale to yor. Worps therefrom, and humbly pray That you will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and order That the Churchward wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said [...] Spittle fields [...] appear before you at a certain Day appointed, to hear & abide the Judg [...] Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners shall pray Etc.
