To the Right Worspfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Richmond in the County of Surry.

Sheweth That by Warrt dat the 31st day of October last past under the hands & Seals of Sr Godfrey Kneller Knt. and Thomas Vernon Esqr, Anne Ellott was removed & conveyed from the parish of Twickenham in this County to the parish of Richmond aforesaid as the place of her last legal Settlemt.

Yor petrs finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order humbly Appeal against the same and pray a day may be appointed this prsent Sessions for all parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor petrs shall ever pray Etc
