To the Right Worpfull His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of Ye Parish of St. Bartholemew ye: Great London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order on Pass Warrant under [...] Hands and Seales of Richard Manley & Valentine Hi [...] Esqr. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor nunc) bearing Date the Tw [...] Second Day of July last past Edmund Carlile with his [...] four Children, Vizt. Lettisse nine Years old, Margaret ag [...] Seven years, Elizabeth aged about five years & Edmund aged about three years & an half, were removed from the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate without the Said County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Barthew & [...] Great London, alledging the Said Parish of St. Barthew the Great to be the Place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor Peticonrs. Conceiving themselves agrieved by the Said Warrant or Order of the sd Two Justices Do therefore appeale to your Worps therefrom, & humbly pray that your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses; & Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate ye sd. County of Middx may appear before you, at a certain Day to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
