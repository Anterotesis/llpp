Middx Ss; To the Rt: Honble; and Rt: Worship full his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx In their Gennerall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church=Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Woollwich in the County of Kent.

Sheweth; That by Warrt: or Order under the hands and Seals of John Kyrby and john Smith Esqrs; Two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) and dated 28; day of September last past, Mary York Widow and five Children (vizt) Mary aged about twelve Years, Elizabeth aged near tenn Years, William aged Six Years, Sarah aged four Years, and Anne York aged about two Years were removed or conveyed from the Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx to the sd, Parish of Woolwich in the said County of Kent, as the Place of their last Legall Setlement.

That yours said Petrs: most humbly conceive themselves aggreived by the said order of the said Two Justices and appeale against the Same

Therefore yours sd, Petrs: most humbly Pray a Short day to be appointed by this Honble; Court for the hearing and determining this theire Peticon and Appeale.

And your Petrs; Shall pray Etc.
