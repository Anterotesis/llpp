To the Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex

The Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Flewer in the County of Northampton

Sheweth That under and by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Collins and Samuel Hawkins Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the 11th. day of July last Samuel Faulkner has been removed from the Parish of Finchley in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Flewer as the Place of his Lawful Settlement

That your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved by such Order and Removal and they therefore pray

That they may be allowed to lodge an Appeal at this present Session against the said Order that the Hearing of such appeal may be respited till the next Sessions
