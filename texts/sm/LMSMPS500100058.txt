To the Honrable theire Mats Justices of the Peace at the Genrall Quarters Session assembled at Hicks Hall the 25th of April 1691

The Humble peticon of Elizabeth Turners

Humbly Sheweth That whereas Suzana Stanley & Katharine Morice was bound overs to appe at this Sessions For Robbing & beateing of yd House prove Peticon before Justices Robins.

The Promisses tenderly considered yor House pave Peticonr Humbly waves this Honorable Bour the Keepe & continue the Said Suzana Stanley bound who does and hath Sevrall times now of late threatened ye doath of yor peace peticonr and yor Peticonr. as in duty bound will door pray for yor bonds health and happiness
