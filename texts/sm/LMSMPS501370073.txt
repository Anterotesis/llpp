To the Right Worshipfull her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Cristopher Carey of the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the Sd County.

It is Orderd by this Court that it be [...] hereby recomended & referred to Samuel Benson Joseph Jorye Stephen Hall Robert Dennett & John Steuart Esqrs Justices of the peace for this County or any two of them [...] ex Sumon before them the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in this County, and to examine into the truth of the petis compll, and to certifie their Opinion touching the to [...] ne within menconed unto this Court at the Genal quarll Sessions of the peace to be holden for this County by Adjournmt on Monday the twenty second day of Febry next at nine of the clock in the forenoon

P Cur Harcourt

Middx ss In Psuance of the Order of Sessions above Written wee Sumoned the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of St: Leonard Shoreditch before us to examine into ye truth of the petr comitt: and upon hearing what could be sayd on either Side wee are of Opinion that the petr: ought to be discharged from ye. payment of Parish Taxes.
