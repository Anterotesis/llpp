To the Right Worpll his Matis Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and appeale of Francis Coxson of the hamblett of Wappin Stepney in the said County Victualler

Sheweth That your Petr is rated one Shilling and Six pence P month towards the releife of the poor of the said hamblett and forasmuch as your petr hath a wife that is very Sickly and in mean and low Circumstances which makes him unable to pay the said Rate

Your petr therefore prays your Worps to releive him in the prmes as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And your petr Shall pray Etc

Francis Coxson
