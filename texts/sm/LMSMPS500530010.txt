To the Right Worll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Chappellwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Town of Uxbridge

Sheweth

That lately one Katherine Cary bigg wth Child was passed by warrant under the hands & seals of St John Elwes Knt & Richard Woodward Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace of the said County from the pish of Fulham in the said County to the Town of Uxbridge in the said County thereby [...] dging that William Cary husband of the said Katherine was last County settled there as an Apprentice

Pr Tyson your Peticonrs: think themselves aggrieved wth the Judgmt. of the said two Justices & do appeale to this Court & humble pray the premises considered that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of Fulham & all other pties concerned may attend this Court some [...] day this sessions to heare & abide the finall end & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And yor petr shall did pray Etc

George Battey Robard Hilman
