To the right Wrspll. his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Sam: Pope of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in ye Sd. County of Middx Gardner.

Sheweth That yor Petr. by reason of Lameness in one hands & ye Palsie is incapable of following his employmt. & by the Creditt of Some Friends hath raised money whereby to keep an Alehouse but being rated to ye. poor 2s:& 6d P Moneth is really unable to pay it

And therefore Humbly Prayes yor Worships will be pleased to order A Mittigation of the Sd rate or otherwise order for reliefe of Yor Petr. in yr. Premises as to yor Worspr. Shall Seem meet.

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

Not to be relieved
