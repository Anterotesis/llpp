To ye Rt Worshipll: His Maties Justices of Ye: Peace for the County of Midd assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands & Seales of Stephen Brice Esqr & St. John Colbatch Knt: two of [...] His Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date the ninth Day of April Instant Joseph Gillet aged about eleven weeks was remov'd from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of the last legall Settlemt: of the said Joseph Gillet

Yor Petrs: think themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt: of the sd. two Justices of the peace, Appeale to this Court [...] & humbley pray that the Churchwardens [...] & Overseers of the poor of the Sd parish [...] of St. Martin in the Fields & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to [...] attend this Court come day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
