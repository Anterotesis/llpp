To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys his Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of October last Sarah Griffith. Wife of George Griffith (gone abroad ) is removed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Under shaft as the Place of her last Legal Settlement

Your Petitions therefore Conceiving themselves Agreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal to this County for such releif in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall over pray

Samuel Butler Atty. for the Appellants
