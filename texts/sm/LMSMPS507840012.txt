To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Year General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Neots in the County of Huntingdon.

Shew That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Wilmot and Charles Triquet Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesed bearing date the twenty eighth day of June One thousand seven hundred and eighty four Lucy Blayne Singlewoman and her three bastard Children for Nurture namely Daniel Aged five Years, Lewis aged four Years and John Aged eleven Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Neots in the County of Huntingdon as the place of the said Lucy Blaynes last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore humbly Pray your Worships to Appoint a day for the next General Quarter Sessions to hear and determine their said Appeal

John Park Solr. for the Appellants.
