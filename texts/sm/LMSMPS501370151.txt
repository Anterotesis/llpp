To the Worshipful her Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions assembled for the said County.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christchurch in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Jane Shaw wife of Patrick Shaw was by an order under the hands and Seals of Two of her Majtys Justices of the peace of the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Seventh day of December 1713 removed from the Parish of St: Andrews Holbourn above the Barrs in the sd County of Middx to the parish of Christ Church in the said County of Surry. as the place of last legal Settlement gained by her said Husband Patrick Shaw living as an hired Servant by the year (whilst a Singleman) for one whole year and upwards with one Mr Denson in the said parish of Christ Church about Six years agoe

That your Peticoners can make it appear unto this Honble: Court that the said Patrick Shaw was only a weekly Servant with Mr. Denson and that he did not continue in Mr. Densons Service for the Space of One year

Your Peticoners therefore do humbly appeal against the said order and pray that this Honble: Court will order the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Andrew Holbourn to attend this Court to shew cause why the said order Should not be vacated

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
