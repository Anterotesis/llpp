To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwarderns and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint John in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the Re [...] his George Boothy Barned and Charles Triquet Esqrs two of his Majestis Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty third day of Jane one thousand seven hundred and eighty nine whereby Mary Glazbrooke the wife of Thomas Glazbrooke sawyer, and their two Children namely William aged four year and upwards and Mary aged Six Months or thereabouts [...] were removed from the Parish of Saint james Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to our Parish of Saint John Southwark aforesaid as the place where said Mary and her two Children were and are legally settled by which order your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your petitioners therefore most humbly pray your worships have to appeal against his said order of Removal

And your petitioners shall ever pray
