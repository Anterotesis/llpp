To the Rt. Worshipfll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. at the genll. quartr. Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of John Milner and John Ellis Esquires two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (quor unus) bearing date the 12th. day of January last Elizabeth Stone and John Stone were removed from the Parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London at the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Stone and John Stone.

That your Petrs. Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the same but Cannot be now ready to have the said Appeal now heard by reason of the absence of some of their Material Witnesses

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray that the said Appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing and determinacon thereof may be adjournd to the last day of this present one quarter Sessions And that all parties Concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal And that your Petrs. may be releivs herein according to Law and Justice

And your petrs. (col induty bound) shall ever pray Etc Tho: Buck} Churchwardn.

Jno Evans John Bickerton} Overseers
