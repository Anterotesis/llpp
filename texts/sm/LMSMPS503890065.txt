To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Magnus in the City of London

Sheweth That By Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Lawton and N. Dukinfield Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County to Middlesex (whereof One is of the Quorum) bearing Date the 27th. day of July one Thousand Seven hundred and Forty Eight John Osgood and Mary his Wife together with Thomas Osgood (his Son by a former Wife) aged about Twenty One years and their Son William Osgood aged about two years were Removed from the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Saint Dunston Stepney In the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Magnus in the City of London as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the Same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of this present Session That all parties may Attend to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the 1d. appeal

T. Gillmore G. Ismay } Churchwardens

Mr Backs Chris Hurad} Overseers
