To the Worll. his Majesties Justies of the Peace of and for the County of Midx in Qr. Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westminister in the sd County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrt. under the hands and Seals of John Fuller and Abraham Ward Esquires Justices of the Peace of and for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of October last Mary Haydon[---] an Infant of the Age of three Years or thereabouts was removd from and out of the Parish of St. Giles's without Cripplegate in the County aforesaid to the sd. Parish of St. Margaret Westminister as the place of her Lawfull Settlement

That Yor Petrs. thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgment of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal to yor. Worps. against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray yor Worps. would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in this County may Attend this Court Some Day the Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and final Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
