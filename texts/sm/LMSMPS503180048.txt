To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbor [...] above the barrs in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Lane and Richard Newton Esquires two of his Majestys Justices for the Peace for the said County (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the eleventh day of May last Past whereby Mary good singlewoman aged 22 years was removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above barrs in the same County as the Place of her last legall Settlement

Rd. Walkins, Agent for the Appellts. Your Petitionrs think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices of the Peace appeal to this Court against the same and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to app [...] as day for the hearing and determining the same

And Your Peticoners Shall were Pray Etc
