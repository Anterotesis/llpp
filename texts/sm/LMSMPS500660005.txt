To the Right worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Solomon May & John Birket of the Parish of Islington in the sd. County.

That Yor. Petrs. did Serve the Office of Constables for the sd. Parish for the Yeare last past & did disburse in the time of their Said Office the Sum of Â£25.4s:9d in Passing & releiveing Vagabands and Crippler & otherwise As by yor. Petr. Account may appeare

That Yor. Petr. have demanded the Said moneys of the Churchwardens of the sd. Pish but they refuse to pay the Same to Yor. Prs. without an order of this Honble Cort.

P SWestley

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly prays yor worspp to Order that the sd. Churchwardens may pay Yor. Prs. the sd. moneys Soe due to them as aforesaid.

And yor. Prs. Shall ev Pray Etc

Salomon May John Birkett
