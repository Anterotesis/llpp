To the Right Worpll his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their General Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon of Thos Clowser of Ripley in the County of Surrey Malster

Sheweth That on or about the 29th Day of August last past your Peticoner Freighted five Quarter of Malt in a Barge of Wm Wells of Sunbury from Ripley in the County of Surrey in Order to be conveyed to London that in its passage from Ripley to London the sd Barge was unfortunately sunk in the River Thames in the County of Middx near Westmr. & all the sd Malt spoilt.

That your Petr hath pd the Duties for the sd Malt amounting to twenty Shillings

And hath also given Notice to the Collector of the sd Duties that he wou'd apply to this Court this prsent Quarter Sessions for an allowance for the sd Damage & Loss.

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays such an allowance in respect of such Damage & Loss as to your Worps shall seem meet

And yor Petr shall pray Etc.

Thos Clowser
