To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of Christopher Scott and Robert Pell Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of March 1764 Mary Pound an illegitimate child aged about eight months was removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave as the Place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Pound whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships to appoint a Day in this Present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.
