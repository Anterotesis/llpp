To the Right Worspl His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the Said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Ricards & Richard Farmer Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Sd County of Middx [...] being of the Quor) bearing date the fifth day of February [...] Elizabeth Brawn Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St Mary White Chappell in the Sd County of Middx to the Said Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves Aggreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the sd Two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief

Jno North Agent for St Bot witht. Bishopsgate
