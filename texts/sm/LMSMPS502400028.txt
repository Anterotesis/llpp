To the Rt: Worshipll: his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genll: Quarter Sessions at hicks hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in their fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth That William Bodle Elizabeth his Wife and their three Children Vizt. Thomas aged about Eleven years Robert About five years And Catherine about three Quarters of a Year Parishioners of the said Parish of St. Martin in the fields an Chargeable to and by the said Parish being Poor & unable to [---] towards their maintenance

That Mrs: Mary Scott of Hackney in the sd: County of Middlesex Widow and [...] Mother of the said Elizabeth Bodle and Grandmother of the said three Children is of a Sufficient Ability to releive and maintain them

Your Petrs: therefore pray your Worships would be pleased to Order the said Mrs: Mary Scott to Contribute towards their Releife and Maintenance

And your Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc
