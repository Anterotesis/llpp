To the Rt: Worsppr his Maties Justices of the Peace for County of Middx

The humble Peticon Gary Mason of the Parish of st es Clerkenwell in the sd County wo

Sheweth That your Petitr was allowed Eighteen Per Weeke [...] of the Parish Nursing a poor child belonging there on to But Sim Easter last the same had Ceene wholly taken away further but for wt Reasons yor Petitr doth not Know

That your Petitr is every poor woman and hath there Children to maintaine and is in Get want of her Arears.

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays such reliefed to the Court shall be right most meete

And yor Petitr all over prayer

Mary laton
