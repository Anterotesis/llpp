To the right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor at the Parish of Allhallows London Wall

Sheweth That By Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the [...] and Seals of J: Girdler and Support Clarke Esqr save of his Majesty of [...] the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) being date the 17th day of May Instant Sarah Chipps a Single Woman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrews Holbourn above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows London Wall as the place of her last Segall Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrived by the said order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do Humbly Appeal against the same and pray that the Hearing and determining thereof may be Adjuourned to the next General or Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex and that the Court Will be pleased to appoint a day in the said next Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holbourn above the Barrs and all other Persons Concerned the Attend to hear and above the Judgement and [...] of the then Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
