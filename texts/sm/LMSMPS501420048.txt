To the Right Worfull his Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx

The humble Petition of James Robinson of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the County of Middx aforesaid Hempdresser

Sheweth That your Petitioner served seven years as an Apprentice to the Trade of a Hempdresser and has ever Since followed the said Trade, and did for severall years serve the house of correction at Clerkenwell with materialls for the imploying of idle and disorderly persons sent thither to work And the Office or Place of Governor of the said house being now vacant by the death of Capt Boreman the late Governor

Yor Petr [...] therefore humbly prays yor Worpps to take the premisses into yor consideration, and to admitt him into the said Office or place he being as he humbly conceives a person very fitly quallified for the same

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc
