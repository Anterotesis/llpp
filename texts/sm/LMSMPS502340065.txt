To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Robert Jackson and Edward Grose Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Eighteenth day of March last past William Mosely was Removed from the Parish of Saint Mary While Chappell in the County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London Alledging the said Parish of St. Botolph without Britropsgate to be the Place of his last legall Settlemt.

Therefore Your Petrs: Conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore Appeal to Your Worships there from And humbly Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to hear them in the Premisses and Order yt the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in the County of Middx May Appear before You at a Day certain by Your Worships to be Appointed to hear and Abide the Judgmt. And determinacon of Your Worships touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. Shall Ever pray Etc
