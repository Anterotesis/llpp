To the Rt Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session's of the Peace, at Hick's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Chruch Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order under the hand's and Seals of Valentine Hilder & Peter Guerin Esqrs: two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: bearing Date the thirty first day of October last Frances James aged fifteen Year's was removed from the parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of her last legall Settlement whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved [...] & Appeald this Court agt. ye Same

Your Petitioners therfore humbly prays this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal & that all Persons concerned may attend at the same time

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

J: Parry Agent for St. George Hanr. Square
