Peticon of Churchwardens of Shepperton and [...]

To the Right Wartt. their Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of the Church- wardens & Overseers of the poore of Shepperton in the Said County

Sheweth

That about the begining of November last past one William Tench wth: his wife & Child was passed from Chertsey in the County of Surrey to Shepperton aforesd by warrant under the hands & feath of two of their Maties. Justices of the peace for the Sd County of Surrey thereby alleadging that the Sd William Tench had conffessed before them that he was last loyally settled at Shepperton aforesaid

May it please yor worps the Said William Tench was never selled at Shepperton but since the Said pass hath absconded & left his wife & Child chargeable to the Sd pish

That yor petrs. are Informed that ye Sd. Williams Tench married his wife at Haswell in ye County of Surrey where ye Sd. Child was Borne & as is reported allowed the mother a weekly pention

yor petr therefore humbly appeals to this Worll. Bench to be releived in the primises

And they shall ever pray Etc
