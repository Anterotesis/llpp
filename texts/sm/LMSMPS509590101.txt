To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the County o Middlesex in Session assembled

The Humble Petitioner of Richard Murthwaite

Sheweth

That your Petitioner was on the 3d day of January 1799 convicted before Henry James Pye and Joseph Moses Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex upon the Oath of William Settle in the Penalty of Five Pounds for demanding and receiving of the said William Kettle one penny as profit upon two shillings lent by your Petitioner upon a Pawn of a Waistcoat to the said William Kettle in the name or under the Description of Mr. Catel one Penny being moe than at the rate of one half penny for the Loan of any Sum of exceeding the Sum of Two Shillings and six Pence by the Calender Months including the Current Month in which the said Pawn was received- Contrary to the Statute Etc. by which Conviction your- Petitioner conceived himself agrieved

And therefore Your Petitioner Appeals to this Court against the same and prays that your Worships will hear thereon and grant him such Relief as his case requires and that a day may be appointed for the hearing and determining the same

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc

Richd Murthwaite
