To the Right Worrph: their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Thomas Jones Keeper of New Prison and the Howse of Correccon in the said County.

Sheweth That severall poore Persons being from time to time comitted to the said severall Prisons, Many of them are there visited with sickness which hath occaconed severall such persons to dye in the said Prisons, and severall poore women being soe comitted are there frequently delivered of Children whereby your Peticoner hath susteyned great expence and charge both in releiving and burying such poore persons.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays your Worspps to consider the premisses and to be pleased to order that he may be reimbursed such his expense and charge aforesaid as unto your grave wisdomes Shall Seem meet.

And your Peticoner Shall ever Pray Etc.
