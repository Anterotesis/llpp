To The worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peters in the Liberty of Saint Albans in the County of Hertford.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and George Colson [...] Smith Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th: day of November 1774. Mary Howell Widow and Daughter Ann were removed from the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Peters in the Liberty of Saint Albans in the said County of Hertford as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order humbly appeal there from and humbly beg your worships to give your said Petitioners such relief in the Premises as to your worships shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners shall over Pray Etc.
