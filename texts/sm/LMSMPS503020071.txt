To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble petition and Appll: of the Overseers of the poor of the Precinct of St Catherines in the sd County

Sheweth That by Warrt: or Order of Removall under the hands and Seals of Robt. Sax and Thos Jones Esqrs: Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum Johanna Huxford Wido: was removed from the Parish of St George in the said County to the Precinct of St Catherine in the same County as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Johanna Huxford That Your Petitioners think themselves agrieved by the sd: Order and Humbly Appeals against the same And prays that your Worships will appoint a day this Sessions for the hearing and determining of the same and that the Churchwardn: and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St George and all other persons concerned may be Summoned to Accond this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the same

G: Meidel Attend for the Applls:

And your Petitionrs: as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Geo: Shudwick Jos: Gascoyn} Overseers of the poor
