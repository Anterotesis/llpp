To the Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled att Hukes's haltn

The humble peticon [...] of John Mainsfeild Shooemender

That abt: three Sessions's since your peticonr: was Caite for Sarah Perry alias Broadwater, and your peticoner was last Sessions surrendred her into Custody in discharge thereof

That the said Broadwater is since discharged out of Custody wth. out payeing the fees of such surrender

Your Peticonr: humbly prayes yt: he may have such recognizance discharged wth. out payeing the fees of ye Same,

And yor Petr, shall pray Etc
