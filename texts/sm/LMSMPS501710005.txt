To ye Rt. Worsppfull ye Justices of ye Peace at ye Sessions Holden at Hixes Hall for ye County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of George West Sheweth That Yor. Petitioner Lives in a Victualling House and is assessed at two shillings Six Pence in the pound to ye Poor's Rate and also for thirty pounds Stock to ye Poor and He through want of Business & Misfortunes being reduc'd to Poverty not having twenty pounds in ye World his Debts Paid Humbly prays that he may be Relieved in this Case as ye Law Directs or as it shall seem Just to Yor. Worspps

A Yor. Petitionr. shall Evr Pray Etc:
