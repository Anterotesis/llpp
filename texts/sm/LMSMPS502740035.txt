To the Rt: Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their Peneral Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall

The Humble Pencion and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ralcliffe in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth

That By Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Robert Sax and Thomas Evans Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorums) bearing date an or about the first day of March last, Sarah Parsons Widdow of William Parsons, decd, and her two Children (Vizt) James Parsons aged four Years and Mary Parsons aged ten Months, were Removed from the Parish of St. George in the said County of Middx to the said Hamlett of Ralcliffe as the place of their legall settlement whereby your Petitioners most humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrievely

Your Petitionors therefore humbly Appeal agt. the said Order of Removall & Pray this Court to Appoint a Day this present Sessions for the learing and Determining of this Appeal and that they may be relieved in the Premises, as to your Wisdoms shall seem meet.

And your Petrs shall Pray Etc [...] Soli
