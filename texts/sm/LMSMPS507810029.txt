To the Worshipfull William Manwaring Esqr. Chairman and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woodford in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Ordered under the hands and Seals of N. Conant and Jenkin Jones Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said Ordered That George Fowler and Mary his Wife lately come into the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex endeavoring to settle there contrary to Law, and likely to become Chargeable to the said Parish And Adjudged that the said Parish of Woodford in the County of Essex is the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said George Fowler and his said Wife Whereby Your Petitioners and Appellant are very much Aggrieved

Your Petitioners and Appellants therefore most Humbly pray Your Worships to hear and take their Case into Consideration against such Order and that the same may be quashed and to grant such other Releif in the premises as to your Wisdom shall meet

And your Petitioners and Appellants as in duty bound shall [...] over pray Etc.
