To the Worshipll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Mary Burn Widow of the Hamblet of Wapping Stepney in the Sd. County of Middx.

Sheweth That yor Petrs. late Husband Garret Burn deced was Master of a Man of Warr & in his life time yor Petr. & Familyhood very reputably & paid all Such Taxes to the poor as were Assessed upon them But now yor Petr. being left A Widdow wth. two Children to maintain & & nothing whereby to Support them bur what She getts by her own Industry & hard Labour & being rated two Shillings P Moneth to the poor of the Sd. Hamlet humbly appeals agt the Sd. rate being altogether unable to pay it or any part thereof.

And therefore in Consideracon of the Prmises & forasmuch as yor Petr. by reason of her poverty is not able to pay ye. Same prayes yor Worships will be pleased to order that She may be wholly discharged from ye Sd. Assesmt. or otherwise order for reliefe of yor Petr. in ye Premises as to yor Worships in yor goodness Shall Seem meet.

And Yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc.

To be reduced to 12d. P Moneth as from Xmas last.

Mary Burn
