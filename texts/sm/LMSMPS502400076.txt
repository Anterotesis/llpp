To the Rt: Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx alt their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace att Hicks hall Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands & seales of William Cotesworth and John Fuller Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the Eighth day of December last Mary Smith Widow was by the said Order Removed from the Parish of St: Giles Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of St: Botolph without Aldersgate as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Smith whereby yor Pt: Sd humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggreived

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly Pray your Worsps will be pleased to Appoint a day to hear & Determine the saw Appeal And That your Peticoners may be Releived as unto yor Worpps shall seem meet

And yor Petrs: Shall ever Pray & Etc
