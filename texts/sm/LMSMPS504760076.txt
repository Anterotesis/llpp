To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster Assembled at the Michaelmas Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for the City and Liberty aforesaid on Wednesday the 18th. Day of October 1758.

The Humble Petition of Ann Chambers Widow

Sheweth That at the General Quarter Session of the Peace holden for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid on Wednesday the 19th. day of October 1757. Your Worships were pleased to grant License to Your petitioner to keep a certain House called or known by the name of the Concert Room in Dean Street in the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty aforesaid for the Publick Dancing Musick or other Publick Entertainment of the like kind which License was to continue in force until this present Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace held for the Liberty aforesaid

That the Condition of the said License hath in all things been performed and fullfilled.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays that your worship would be pleased to grant a License to your Petitioner for the purposes aforesd. to continue in force until the next Michaelmas Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this Liberty.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.

Ann Chambers
