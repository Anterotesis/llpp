To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled

The humbled Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist in the City of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Mich. Downs and R. Johnson Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices if the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. day of November 1793. Charles Corderoy aged near fourteen Years was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John the Evangelist Westminster as the peace of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said order and pray that your worships will be pleased to appealed a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

John Ansell John Fenwick} Church Wardens

Thos Jackson} Overseer of the Poor
