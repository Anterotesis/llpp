To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace by Adjourment Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George hanr. Square in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Cox and Philip Dyot Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st.. day of February 1764 Hannah Potter Wife of William Potter, was removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petritoners therefore humbly Pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearin and determining the said appeal and that all persons concerned may be directed then to attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc. Robt Morg an Jos Hincklitt & Mark Wareup Adam Price Thomas Vardy} Church wardens and Overseers of the poor
