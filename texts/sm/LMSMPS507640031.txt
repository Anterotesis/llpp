To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order on Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Jno. Machin and G. Mercer Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one of them of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fifteenth Day of January last James Caruthes Eleanor (there in called Eleanor his Wife and their two Children namely Robert aged twelve Years and John aged Eight Years were removed and conveyed from the Town of Uxbridge in the Parish of Hillingdon in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved in that part of the said Order which adjudges the last legal Settlement of the said Eleanor and the said two Children to be in the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray Relief in the Premisses and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining this their said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Thos. Wade Junr. Sollr. for the Appellents
