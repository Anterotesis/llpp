To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now Assembled at Hickss. Hall

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County (One whereof is of the Quorum bearing Date the 24th. day of May Last William Walker was removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County to the Legal Settlement

And Your Petrs. finding themselves aggrieved by the Said Order of Removal Do appeale agt. the same and humbly Pray that your Worships will appoint a day in this Present Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the hearing & Deter- -mining of your Petitioners Appeale and order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all Persons Concerned to attend and hear and abide by the Judgement and Determination of your Worships touching the same

And Your Petiotrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
