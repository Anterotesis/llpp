To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petitions and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thos. Parker and N. Forster two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) hearing date the 15th. day of October last Ann Eddy Chiney Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the said City and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint James as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their Said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

Jas. Dyson

Solicitor for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James
