To the Knight Worshipll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Margaret Green of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in the Sd. County of Middx Widdow.

Sheweth That yor Peticonr for Eleven years last past being A Widdow & Ancient Inhabitant in the Sd. Hamlet & A due reguard had to her circumstances was rated no more then five pence & Month to ye poor of the Sd. Hamlet but is now Assessed one Shillings & Six pence & Month wth. is far above her Ability & more in pporcon o the rest of her Neighbours & thinks her Selfe much aggrieved & wronged thereby.

Therefore yor Petr. humbly prayer yor Worships will be pleased to order her to be reduced to her former rate or otherwise to A Mittigacon thereof as to yor worships in yor great Wisdom & Justice Shall Seem meet.

And yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc Margertes Greene

To be continued upon report of ye Inhabitants of She hate an Estate follows A beneficiall employmt.
