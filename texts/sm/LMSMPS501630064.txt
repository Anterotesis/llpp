To the Right Worpll: his Maties Justices of ye Peace (Assembled in Quarter Sessions att Hick's Hall for ye: County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish Covent Garden in the said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated the 19th: day of June last, Under the hands and Seals of R Dennett and Samuel Perry Esqrs: Two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor. unus) Richard Scutt and Thamar. his Wife and Elizabeth their Daughter were removed from the Hamblet of Spittle: Fields in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx. to the said Parish of St: Paul Covent Garden As the place of their last Legall Settlement.

That Yor: Petrs: find themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices and humbly Appeal against the Same to this Honble: Court. And That for as much as yor: Petrs: Cannot be prepared to prosecute their said Appeal this present Sessions.

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray Yor: Worps. will be pleas'd to Order. That the benefit of their said Appeal may be Saved unto them And that the hearing & Determineing thereof be Adjourn'd, Untill some Day the next Generall Sessions of the Peace to be held for the said County, And that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamblet of spittle: Fields (Upon Service of a Copy of an Order of this Honble: Court) And All other Partyes Concern'd may Yr: attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court, Upon and touching, the said Appeal, And That Yor: Petrs: may have such Relief herein as to Yor: Worps. shall seem meet Etc

And Yor: Petrs: Shall Pray Etc

Dat. 15th: July 1717.
