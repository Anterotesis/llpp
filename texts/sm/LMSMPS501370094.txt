To the Right Worspps her Maties Justices of peace for the County of Middlx

The honble. Petican and appeale of John Allen of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Sd. Countie Tayler

Sheweth That yor: poore. Petr is rated to the releife of the poore in the sd parish 3s per quarter and pays but St pr annm for his house & of meane Circumstances [...] trade which makes him unable to pay the sd. rate

therefore humbly prayes to be releifed in the prmises by this Honble Court

And yor Petr Shall pray Etc
