To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Katherine Coleman in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of the [...] and D. Wilmot Esquires tow of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Rate the fifth day of February 1785 Elizabeth Taylor Widow was removed from the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Katherine Coleman in the City of London adjudging the said Parish of Saint Katherine Coleman to be place of her last legal Settlement whereby you petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court agt. the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint a Day for the Hearing of this Appeal and to order that the Ch. wrdns & ovrsrs of the poor of the parish of Saint Leonard in Shoreditch afsd. and Psons concerned do them attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships in the Premises And your Petitoner shall ever pray

John Evans Sobr. for the Appellants
