Middlesex, ss To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Upminster in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Rob: Smith and John Staples Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 4th: day of November last past Henrietta Clarke Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Upminster the Adjudication of the said Justices being that the legal Settlement of the said Henrietta Clarke was in the said Parish of Upminster

That in as much as the last legal Settlement of the said Henrietta Clarke is not in the said Parish of Upminster Your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do Appeal against the same And pray that your Worpships will be pleased to Order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all other Persons who may be concerned do attend this Court some day this Present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and determination there of And that your Petitioners may have such relief in the Promises as to your Worships shall Leem meat Dated this 12th: day of December 1785
