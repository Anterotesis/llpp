To the Worshipfull his Majestys of the peace for the County of Middlesex now in General Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woughton in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Cox and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty Sixth day of July last past James Major was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Woughton in the County of Bucks as the place of his last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal against the said Pass Warrant or Order

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and Appeal to the Judgment and determination of this Court against the same And humbly prays your Worships to appoint a day this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the Same

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Clarke Sollicitor for the Appealants
