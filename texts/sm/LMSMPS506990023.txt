To the Right Worspl her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Bates

Sheweth That one Jane Price als FitzGerall stands bound for her appearance in this Hononr Court this Prsent Sessions for abusing your Petitr soe that she went in danger of her Life As by the recognizants may appear

That ye Petitr is still apprehensive of further Abuses from the said Price for that she is a Woman of a Wicked Life & Conversacon

P Tho: Nash

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays yr. Worspl to be pleased to grant that the sd Price may be continued Bound till next Sessions

And ye Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Ann [x] Bates her Marke
