To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Midlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hendon in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Alexander Strahan and George Bampfield Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Midlesex (Quorum unus) upon the Complaint of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hampstead in the said County Mary Randall Widow of Edward Randal deced was removed from the said Parish of Hampstead to the said Parish of Hendon

That Your Petr. conceive the said Parish of Hendon agreived by the said Order and doe appeall therefrom and humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint Such Time for the hearing this Appeals as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Pettr. Shall ever pray Etc
