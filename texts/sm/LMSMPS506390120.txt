To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Jutices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace holden at Westminster in and for the County of Middlesex Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Hassell Esqure and Sir John Fielding Knight at two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth Day of February One thousand seven Hundred and seventy four Sarah Rumball Singlewoman [...] removed from the parish of Hadley in the County [...] and to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden [...] place of her last legal Settlement. Whereby [...] our petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal [...] to this Court against the same:

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may be then directed to Attend:

And your petitioners shall pray Etc:

John Lane Sollicitor for the said parish of Saint Paul Covent Gardens.
