To the Honble: Bench at Hicks Hall

The humble peticon of Thomas Frampton Church warden & other Officers of the parish of St. Jon. Wapping on the behalf of themselves & other parishioners of the said parish.

Sheweth

That Mary Stephenson being a hired Servant by the year [...] [...] the parish of St Mary [...] White Chappell in Com Midd Edge [...] in yor: Petr: aforesd parish Yor. petr: Frampton fearing the Mary would become chargeable into the sd. parish of St. John Wapping Ordered James Stacey the Beadle to apply [...] to John Webber Esqr for a pass to convey the sd. Mary to the [...] Psh of White Chappell But instead thereof the sd: Mr Webber passd the sd. Mary Stephenson unto Well Close within the Liberty of the Tower of London.

That some part of the sd: Close being within the said parish of St John Wapping, And the sd: Pass bring agst. your petr. Order & intention, & likely to bring a trouble & detriant to the sd. parish,

Yor Petr most humbly pray that the Constable Overseers of the Poor, & other Officers of the sd. place called well Close may be Ordered to attend this Court with the said Pass, And that the said Mary Stephenson may be removed to White Chappell parish as of right She ought Or that yor petr may be otherwise relieved as to Honrs [...] & Goodness shall seen meet,

Script P G Fisher

And [...]

[...] Copy

Thomas Frampton Jno: Nicolls.
