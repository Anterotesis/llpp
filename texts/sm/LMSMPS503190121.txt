To the Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Over seers of the poor of the parish of St. Thomas the apostle in the City of London

Sheweth

That by Order of Removal Dated the Seventh day of September last under the hands & Seals of Thomas Cotton & Thomas Mulso Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the peace (whereof one is of the Quorum) for the county of Middx [---] Ann Staples Wife of Thos. Staples & their Child Named William aged abt. 14 Months who are therein mentioned to have lately intruded & come into the parish of St. Anne in the Liberty of Westminster in the said County thereto Inhabit as parishoners contray to the Laws relating to the Settlement of the poor were from thence removed to the sd parish Saint Thomas of the Apostle as [---] the last legall place of Settlement of the said Ann Staples & her sd Child [...] [---] [---]

That yor petitoners are aggreived by the sd. Order of Removal & Appeal agt. the same this Court

Your petrs. humbly prayes yor Worshipps to make an Order That the sd. Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of St: Anns Westmr. in the sd. County of Middx & all other psons concerned Do attend this Court some day this Session to hear the final. Determination & Order of this Court in relation of the premisses

Wm. Hoggard} Church wardens

James Peyro [...]
