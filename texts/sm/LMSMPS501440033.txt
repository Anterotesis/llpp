[...] Rt. Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for [...] County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Wells of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth

That at Easter last your petitr was Chosen to Serve the Office of Scavenger for the upper Liberty of St John Street in the said [...]

That your Petitr: hath lived but a short time in the sd Parish [...] and divers of your petitrs: Neighbours who have Lived many [...] eares therein and are of farr better abilitys then Yor Petitr that have not served the said Office.

And for as much as your petitr: being a poor Man and having a Sickly Wife to mainteyne is not able to serve the said Office

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays. he may be Discharged from the said Office and that some fetter person may be Chosen in his Roome.

And Yor Petit shall ever pray Etc.

John Wells.
