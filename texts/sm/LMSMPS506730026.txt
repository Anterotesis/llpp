To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knt and the rest of the Justices in their General Quarter Session assembled

The Humble Petition of Joseph Wood Contractor for the repairs of New Prison and Clerkenwell Bridewell

Humbly Sheweth That your Worships Petitioner hath done Work in the repair of New Prison and Clerkenwell Bridewell pursuant to his Contract to the full amount of two thousand two hundred and twenty six pounds 11s/5¾d and there now remains due to him upon the Ballance of the said Account the Sum of one thousand four hundred and seventy five pounds11s/5¾d.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that your Worships would be pleased to order the said sum of £1475..11..5¾ to be paid him out of your Worships Treasury

& Your Petitioner will ever pary Etc

J Wood
