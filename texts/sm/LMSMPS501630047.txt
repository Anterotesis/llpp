To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of Elijah Clarke of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County.

Sheweth That your Petr is rated toward the releife of the poor of the Said Parish for Seaventy five pounds psonall Estate and that your Petr was rated last year for fifty pounds Psonall Estate to the poor, and that your Petr is of no better ability this Present yeare but reather worse Therefore your Petr think himselfe aggreived by the Said rate and appeales agt the Same

Yor. Petr Prays your Worps to releive him in the Promises as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And your Petr Shall ever pray Etc
