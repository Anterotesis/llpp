Middx Ss To the Worshipful his Maties Justices of the Peace assembled att their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for this County

The Humble Petion of John Dance

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. being of the age of fifteen Yeares contrary to the will of his parents who are now Living did bind himself an Apprentice to George Millis of Hampton wick in this County Waterman for the Terme of Seven Yeares

That Yr. Petr. hath Served his Said Master for abt. the Space of Nine Months and during such time, his said Master who is bound over to Appeare att th is Present Sessions to answr. Yr. Petr. Complaint hath several times given yr. Petr. Immoderate Correccon upon the Slightest occasion

That Yr. Petrs. Said Master hath not Supplyed Yr Petr. wth. necessary food and Clothing for want of wth. Yr. Petr. is almost Starved and naked altho his said Master is Oblidged thereto by the said Indenture

That Yr. Petrs said Master Since he was bound over as aforesaid has in a present Manner beat & bruised Yr. Petr. by kicking him on the belly face & other parts of his Body

Yr. Petr.therefore humbly prays Yr worships will be pleased to Discharge Yr. Petr. from his said Apprenticeship by reason of his said Masters violent usage towards him

And Yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
