May is Plean your Worship

Your Petitioner Edmond Murphy being [...] son'd by a Cruel and wicked wife for Upwards Six months in the new Prison and having allowance in the goal has forced me to Cloaths to Support with & at this time [...] either Cloaths or victuals to Eal and have [...] and able to Assist me therefore I most humbly [...] at your worship in gods name to Use your [...] most in deavours with his worship Justice Fielding for me to be acquited otherwise I must [...] avor dably Parish and in So doing I Shall be [...] Over bound to Pray and am

[...] Your Worships most Humble and

Distressed Petitioner [...] 6- 1761 Edmond Murphy
