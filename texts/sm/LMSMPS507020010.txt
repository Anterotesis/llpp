To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of Ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Petition at Thomas Fields

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof your Petr stood Indicted in the Honourable Court this present Sessions from Worshipps were pleased to Fine him Fine Marke And for Nonpaymt. thereof to Committ him to New Prison in this County Where he New remaines

That yor Petr. an Appentice and hath but a short time to Serve And his Master being att present under some Circumstance in the World the whole Dependance of his Busyness most by on your Petrs Managmt And yor Petr humbly Submitting himself to the Mercy of this Honourable Court

The therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleaded for Mitigate the sd Fine as unto your Worshipps shall seem meete he haveing Agreed With the Prosecutor

And ye Petr shall ever Prays etc

P Tho: Nash
