To the Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlesex

The humble petition of John Jackson a Parrishoner of St: Mary Whitechapple

Humbly Sheweth that your Petitioner is very antient and past his labour and by reason of sickness and lameness he cannot support himselfe and his Antient Wife who are in a starving Condition Yr. Petitr. humbly prayes your Worships. to order him some releife from ye. Parish and Your Petitr. as in duty bound shall for ever pray Etc.
