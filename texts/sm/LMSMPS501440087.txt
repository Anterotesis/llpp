To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & appeal of Anne Barker [...] of the parish of st James Clerkenwell In the said County Widdow.

Sheweth

That your petitr: is rated two Shillings and [...] Three pence p Month (besides Over Rates) towards the relief of the poor of the said Parish That your petitr: rents a House of noe more then Four pounds p Ammus and hath Susteyned great Losses By meanes whereof She is now Reduced to a poor and lowe Condicon and is not able to pay the said rate

Your Petitr. therefore humbly prays such a Mittigation of the said rate As unto your Worspps Shall seem Meet

And yor Petitr. shall ever pray Etc.

Anne Barker
