To ye. Rgt: Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middx:

The humble peticon & appeale of ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. parish. of Brentwood, in ye. County of Essex &

Sheweth That by a Warrtt: or Order under ye. hands & Seales of P: Cooper & Robt. Kyrby esqrs. Two of his Maties. Justices of ye. peace for ye. Sd County of Middx (Quor unus) bearinge date ye. 16th: day of October. 1714: Elianor Pickett ye wife of George Pickett; wth: their Two children Willm. aged about Eight Yeares, & Sarah aged about four Yeares, were removed & passed from ye. Hamlet of Wappin Stepney in ye. parish. of St. Dunston Stepney in ye. Sd County of Middx: for ye. Sd parish of Brentwood in ye. Sd County of Essex. therein Setting forth that ye. Sd George Pickett was Last Legall Setled in ye. Pish of Brentwood

Your peticoners therefore findeinge themselves agreived wth. ye. Judgmt. of ye. Sd Two Justices of ye. peace humbly appeales to this Honable: Court against ye. Sd Warrtt: or Order [...] & prayes Your Worsps. will be pleased to grant on order: chat ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Sd Hamlet of Wappin Stepney may attend this Court Some day this Genrll. Quarter Sessions of ye. peace, to heare & abide ye. Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this appeale

And Your peticonrs. shall ever pray Etc [...]
