To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens, and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminter in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seal of Robert Baker and W. Shennard Esquires [...] of his Majestys Justice of the Peace for the Said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of February instant whereby James Philpot, and Mary Copland his Wife were rempled and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbour above, to the Said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of theeir last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication a,d Order of the Said two Justices, and pray that your Worships will appoint a day in the present Session for the heaving, and determinning thereof

thereof, and that all persons concerned may have Notice to attend the same.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

E Allen Solicitor for the -} Churchwardens

and

} Overseers of the poor
