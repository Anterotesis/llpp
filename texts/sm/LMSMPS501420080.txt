To ye. right Worshipll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Lucreetia Petrie of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in ye Sd. County of Middx.

Sheweth. That yor Petr. is but this Quarter come to Inhabitt in ye Sd. Hamlet & that She is rated 18d: P Month towards ye. reliefe of the poor of the sd. Hamlet wth. is double to ye rest of her Neighbours in like Circumstances & yt. yor Petr. is really unable to pay ye Sd. rate & thinks her selfe much Aggrieved thereby.

And yor Petr. therefore Humbly prayes that yor. will be pleased to order A Mittigacon of the Sd. rate or otherwise for reliefe of yor Petr. in the Premises as to yor Worsps Shall Seem meet.

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.

reduced to 12d: P Month

L: Petrie
