To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under he hands and Seals of Sir William Addington Knt. and Edward Read Esqr. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. day of March 1799 Stephen Stringer Hannah his wife and their Two legitimate Children Vizt. William aged about 12 Years and Daniel aged about 11 years were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le Strand in the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of the Rolls as the Place of their Ewst legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices Appeal to your Worships there from & humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Strand May appear before you on a Certain day in the next Sessions to be Appointed to Abide the Judgment & Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner will ever Pray
