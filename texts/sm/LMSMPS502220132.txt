To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth

That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Vizt Alexander Pitfeild and D'Oyly Michel Esqrs (Quorn Unus) bearing date [...] 21st day of August last past Thomas Purchas aged about Fifteen Years was removed from the parish of st Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middx unto the said parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the Place of his last legal Settlemt- That your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Worrpps there from

And Pray that your Worrps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses and Order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petitionrs: Shall ever pray Etc
