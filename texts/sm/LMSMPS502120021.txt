The humble Petition of John Cowper, Tho: Waterson and John Bass, Scavengers of The Liberty of Grubb Street, in the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate

to be hd Fr: 10cl Mr Theed for Petrs

Refr to Mr. Fuller & Mr. Feast

to order & settle such Rate as Shall think fit.

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd. die Lune scilt octavo die July anno regni Dui Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Nono

It is Ordered by this Court that the Constables Churchwardens Overseers of the poor surveyors of the high ways & other ancient Inhitants of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County have notice of this Peticon, and that they doe attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to Shew cause why the Rate complained off by the Pet Should not be vacated, and why such a just & equa [...] Rate should not be made to defray the charges & pay wages of the scavengers or scab Rakers & other officers empl [...] in the ensuing the streets & carrying away the ashes dirt fitt [...] Joyle within the Liberty of [...] Street in the said parish, as this Court shall think fit,

P Cur Harcourt
