To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of poor for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and appeale of Maudline Beazant of the hamblett of Wappen Stepney in the said County Widdow

Sheweth That your Petr is rated towards the releife of the poor One Shilling P month And forasmuch as your petr is very poor and in mean and low Condicon which makes her unable to pay the said Rate

Your petr prays your Worps to discharge her from paying to the said poors Rate or otherwise releife her in the prmes as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And yor petr Shall pray Etc

Maudline Beazant
