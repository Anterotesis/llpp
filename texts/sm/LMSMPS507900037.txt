To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace, at the Session House for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands Seals of St Beck and Jno Griffiths Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d day of November last Margaret Rotondi and her two Children to wit John aged Four Years and Elizabeth aged two Years were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining of the said Appeal, and that all Persons concerned do then Attend this Court.

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc.

Geo: Jas Soward Francis Deschamps} Church Wardens

Lanct: Fell James Barber John Hand} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
