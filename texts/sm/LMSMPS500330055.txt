To the Right Worspl. this Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Kensington in the sd. County

That Jane Mitchell was by Warrant under the hands and seales of three of their Maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County lately passed from the- parish of Fulham on Hamersmith side in the sd. County to the sd. pish of Kensington

That the sd. Jane Mitchell did dwell and Inhabit in the sd. pish of Fulham on Hamersmith side for the peace of Eight yeares past where she rented whose of Â£12. P annu and pd. all taxes and parish duty & imposed on her see that yor. petr. humbly conceive themselves not lyable to provide for the & Jane Mitchell.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor worspp to be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & ovrseers of the poore of the sd pish of Fulham may appeare in this Honble Court this put Quarter Sessions to shew cause why the sd Jane Mitchell should not be repassed to the sd pish of Fulham

And yor Pr shall ever Pray etc.
