To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Heaviside of George Street in the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex Surgeon.

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the 26th day of June last convicted in the Penalty of Ten Pounds by Cranley Thomas Kerby Esquire one of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County upon an Information exhibited and prosecuted by Joseph Sewerby for using and weaing Hair Powder as an Article of Dress not having clamed a Certificate thereof contrary to the Statute, And also four Shillings for Costs.

That your Petitioner conceives himself aggrieved by such Conviction and is advised to appeal against the same

Your Petitioner humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to permit him to enter his appeal so that the same may be heard at this present General Session and that your Worships will appoint a day for the hearing and detemining the same

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.
