To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Paul Pechell and Wm. Breton Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 2d: day of April 1785 Agnes. Reid and her Female Bastard Child named Charlotte were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Margaret within the said Liberty and County as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that the hearing and determining of the same may be Adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Thos Hitchin James Toplis} Church Wardens of St: Margaret Westminster

Thos Hunt John Ansell} Overseers of the Poor of the Same Parish
