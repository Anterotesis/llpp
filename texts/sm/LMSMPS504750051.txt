To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Session assembled

The humble petition of John Stone

Sheweth That your petition on was bound Apprentice about the Space of two years ago to John Tompkins Waterman and that Diving the said Term he has been ill used by his said Master by beating and assaulting him at Divers Times Whereby he is readed incapable of following any Manner of Business

Your Petr Therefore humbly prays Mr. Worships that he may be Discharged from his said Apprenticeship or otherwise as to your Worships shall Seem meet

And Yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
