To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Lane and John Barnfather Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the sd County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th. day of May 1784 Sarah Duffin & her four Children to Wit Ann Aged 9 Years Mary Aged 7 Years Sarah aged 5 Years and Frances aged 2 Years wee were Removed and conveyed from the Parish of East Bedfont in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Olave Southwark in the County of Surrey aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

July 5th. 1784 Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend
