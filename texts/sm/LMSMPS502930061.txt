To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled-

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the county of Middlesex

Sheweth.

That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Jno. Martin and Andrew Osborn Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing Date the twelfth Day of April last Charles Pyke was removed from the Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwal in the parish of stepney in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of his last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Wm. Head Agent for St. Martins

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some Day this present Sessions to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
