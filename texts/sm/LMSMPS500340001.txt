[...] Major of the City London & other their Maties: Justices Sitting att Justice Hall in Old Baley

Sheweth

The Humble Peticon of Francis Bridges

That yor: Peticonr: was by Warrant under the Hand and Seale of Ralph Marshall Esqr Out of their Matites: Justices of day of said Instant May Comitted to their Maties: Coale of Newgate on Suspicon of Counterfeiting the Current Coins of this Realms and Fill remaines a Prisoner in the Said Coals

He humbly prayeth that he may bee tryed this Sessions or admitted to baile according to this Act of Parliament in that case made and provided, And that this said prayer may bed Recorded

And he shall ever pray Etc:
