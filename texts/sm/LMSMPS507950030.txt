To the Worshipfull his majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Baverstock in the County of Wilts

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Pass warrant under the Hands and Seals of J. Reading and W: Addington Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing data the 7th. Day of February One Thousand seven Hundred and Eighty five Phoebe Lewis Daughter of Christopher Lewis aged about Eighteen Years was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Clement Danes is the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Baverstock as the place of her last legal settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or Pass warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that the hearing and determining of the said appeal may be adjourned untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be hath in and for the said County of middlesex and that the Court will be pleased to appoint a day in the said next Session for the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes and all other Persons concerned therein to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the said Court touching the said appeal

John Turnes, Attorney for Appellants
