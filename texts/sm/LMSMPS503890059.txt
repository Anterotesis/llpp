To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall is St. John Street in General Quartar Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Danl. Gach & Jas Traser Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 9th. Day of Septr. last Mary Pritchard (since De [...] the [...] Wed. of Andrew Pritchard deceased) and her two Children to wit Saml. aged Nine Years & upwards and Joseph aged Seven Years and upwards were removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes aforesd. as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that a short Day may be appointed [...] this present Session to hear and Determine the matter touching the sd. Appeal

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appel [...] .

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
