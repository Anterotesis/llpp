To the Right Worsppll Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & [...] of the Poor of the parish of st Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands & Seals of William Conn & John Roydhouse [...] two of Her Maties: Justies of the peace for the city & Libty of Westrn in [...] County (Quor unus) bearing Date the twenty fifth Day of September past Elianor Low Singlewoman was passed from the parish of St Martin the Fields in the said County to the said parish of st Giles in the Fields the place of her last legall Settlemt

P [...] Tyson Yor peticonr think themselves aggrieved wth. the Judgm [...] the said two Justices, appeal to this Court & humbly pray [...] prmises considered) that the Churchwardens & Overseers [...] the poor of the parish of st Martin in the Fields & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court some Day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt & Determinacon of this Court touching Appeal
