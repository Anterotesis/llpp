To the Rt. Worspll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of Wm Meson Esqr. in behalfe of himselfe and Severall of his tenents (vizt) Wm Walker, Joseph Oswyn, Peter Peru, Peter Dethawyte and Widdow Bremond all of the Parish of St. Mary White: Chapel in the Said County

Sheweth That your Peticoner and his Said Severall Tenants are Rated for Severell peices or Platts of Garden: Ground contiguous to their Severall houses Severall Sumes of money for and towards the necessary reliefe of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church Spittlefields in the Said County

That the Said Severall Peices or Platts of Garden Ground have for Severall years last past allways been rated towards the reliefe of the Poor of the Hamlett of Mile End New Towne in the Parish of Stepney in the Said County, And not to the Hamlett of Spittlefields, which Said Hamlett within ten months last past hath been made parochial and call'd the Parish of Christ Church Spittlefields

Therefore Your Peticoner and his Said Tenants think themselves aggreiv'd by the Said Poor's Rate of Christ Church Spittlefields and humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same [...]

W Mason And most humbly pray this Honoble Court to Appoint Some day in this present Generall Quarter Sessions for all parties concerned to Attend to hear and abide the determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Yr. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc
