To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Val Hilder Esqr. and Sr. James Thornhill Knt. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex one of them being of the Quorum bearing Date the Twenty Fifth Day of May last Elizabeth Hill was removed from the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said Liberty of Westminster in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Wm. Head Agent for St. Martins

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the Said Two Justices and pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint Some Day this Present Sessions hear and Determine the Matter touching the Said Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray
