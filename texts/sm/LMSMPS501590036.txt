To your worshipfull Jostis Fuller I begs you will show my poor unfortened husband Compashon to deschard him out of this Dismall and Deplordable place And of never to Committ the same fort again being it is the first fund And of pray

and beg your goodness will pordon my boldness in intudeing upon your worship And your poor pittist com shall be ever bound to pray Elisbeth exton
