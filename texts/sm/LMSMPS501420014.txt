To the Rt: Worsppll his Majtes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of Giles Quinny Governour of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That the Dwelting House belonging to the said House of Correction is very ruinous and Decayed and the Foundation thereof very defective Insoemuch the same is ready to fall Soe that your Petitioner cannot live in the same unlesse speedily Repaired And for as much as the said Prison being very Insufficient (The Convicts comitted thither having frequently broke out and Escaped)

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays Your Worspps will be pleased to order that a view may be taken of the said Dwelling house and Prison and that the same may be effectually Repaired

And Your Petitr. shall ever pray Etc.

Giles Quinny
