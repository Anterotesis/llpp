To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of William White and Thomas Simple in Church Wardens And Thomas Smith and Henry Alder Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eleventh Day of November last Ann Williamson was Removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Chelsea Adjudging the last Legal Settlement of the said Ann Williamson to be in the said Parish of Chelsea

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves to be greatly Aggreived by the said Order and determination of the said two Justices do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners Therefore do Pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be Saved them and that the Hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned Untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster And all Partys Concerned May Attend the Court of the said next General Quarter Session of the Peace to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of the same Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc
