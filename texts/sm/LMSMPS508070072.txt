To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor & Parishioners of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrt. or Order under the hands and Seals of Beese & D Walker Esqr two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County bearing date the 11th. day of Jaury 1786. Ann Asking Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the field in the said County to the said Parish of Kensington as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That by Virtue of one other Pass Warrt or Order under the hands and Seals of the sd Beets & D Walker Esq, two of his said Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in & for the said County bearing date the said Eleventh day of Janry 1786 Mary Flynn was removed from the sd. Parish of St. Giles in the Fields to the said Parish of Kensington as the Place of her last Legal Settlement

By which said two several Orders of Removal Your Petitioners Conceives themselves aggrieved & Do therefore appeal to Your Worships agaust the sd two several Orders

Your Petitioners therefore Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day Proper for the hearing & determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

John Bardin} Church Wardens William Smith} Overseers of the Poor of Kemr.
