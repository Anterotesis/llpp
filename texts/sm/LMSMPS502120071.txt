To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majetys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quoru) bearing date the third day of May now last past one Elizabeth Ford was removed from the Parish of St. James Clerknwell in the said County to the said Parish of St. Sepulchres London adjudgeing the said Parish to be the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elizabeth Ford.

That your Peticoners doubt not no make it appear that the said Elizabth. Ford had no legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Sepulchers London whereby they Conceive themselves aggreived by the Warrant & Judgement of the said two Justices and do humbly appeal to you Worships therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and order the Churchwardens and ovrseers of the poor of the said parish of St. James Clerknwell to attend; and hear & abide the determination of your Worships touching this appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.

Throp: Joyner Ch: War Willm Bott [x] Ch: Warden
