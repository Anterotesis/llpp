To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Quarter Sessions off the Peace

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor off the Parish off Saint Paul Shadwell in the County off Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue off a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals off William Blackmore and Peter Greene Two of his Majesties Justices off the Peace Acting in and for the said County off Middlesex (One whereoff being off the Quorum) bearing date 15th day for September last Past Peter Cattenhorn the age of Sixteen Years or thereabouts was removed and Conveyed from and out off the Parish off Saint George in the said County off Middlesex to the said Parish off Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place off the last legal Settlement off the said Peter Cattenhorn

Your Petitioners conceive them selves Aggrieved by the Adjudication off the said Two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court again of the same

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

S. Malbon Sollr.

For the said Parish off Shadwell
