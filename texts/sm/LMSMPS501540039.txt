To ye Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition & Appeale of Anthony Mills of ye Parish of Charken=well

Humbley Sheweth that your Petitioner Anthony Mills aforesaid is become very poore & necessitus and same and unable to help or feed himselfe and thereby reduced to great strates and Humbly prays to be releived from paying to ye Poore in such manner as your Worshipps shall think fitt

And your Petitioner shall Ever paray Etc

July ye 6th=1716

Anthony Mills
