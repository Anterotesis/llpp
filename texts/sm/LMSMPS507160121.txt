To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session [---] of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition & appeal of the churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands Seals of Cad Coker & W. Blackborow Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one being of the Quorum bearing date the nineteenth day of August last Ann Rolfe Wife of John Rolfe (who is impressed into his Majesties Service) & her three Children namely Elizabeth Aged about nine years John aged about Six years & Catherine Aged about two years were removed from the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of their last legal settlement

That your Petitionrs Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the sd Warrant or order so humbly Appeal against the same And pray that your Worships Will be pleased to appoint a day this present Session that all parties Concerned may Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd appeal and your Petitionrs shall ever pray Etc

Clare & Bampfield Sollr for the Appellts

Middlesex. at the General Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in st John street in the County aforesd by Adjournment on Monday the Thirteenth day of September in the 19th Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third king of Great Britain France & Ireland Defender of the Faith Etc

It is ordered by this Court That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex have notice of this appeal And that they & all Persons Concerned do Attend this Court on Thursday next at nine of the clock in the Forenoon to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd Appeal

By the Court Selby
