To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Saviour Southwark in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That Ann Tomlinson widow & her son William aged about Eleven Years was removed by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Ant. Wroth & W: Booth Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Ninth day of January 1737. from the Parish of St Luke in the said County to the Parish of St. Saviour Southwark in the County of Surry as the place of their last legall settlement by which said Order your Peticonrs. conceive themselves to be aggrieved and Humbly Appeal therefrom

Yor. Peticoners therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleas'd to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Luke may attend this Court for hearing the said Appeal at such time as Your Worships shall appoint And Your Peticonrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

John Wight Churchwarden & Thos. Neldrett & Overseer
