To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Teversham in the County of Cambridge

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Dated the Twenty Second day of August last past Mary the Wife of Thomas Gurney, and Thomas their Son aged about Four Months were passed of the Parish of Enfield in the County of Middlesex, to the said Parish of Teversham as the place of their last legall Settlement

That [---] Your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Two Justices and Therefore humbly appeal to this Honorable Court against the same for relief in the Premisses and pray Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determining the Same

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Edwd. Hancock Willm. Foot} Overseers
