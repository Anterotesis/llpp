To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their General Session

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order pass Warrt under the Hands & Seals of George Errington & Saunders Welch Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of January last Robert Holmes aged about forty two Years Mary his Wife aged about forty nine Years Mary A Daughter aged Eighteen Years Robert a Son aged thirteen Years Isaac a Son aged twelve Years Elizth. a Daughter aged ten Years Hannah a Daughter aged Seven Years and Wm a Son aged five Years were removed & conveyed from the Parish of Hampstead in the said County to the said Parish of St. James Clerken- well as the place their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said, two Justices of the Peace, Appeal to this Court agt. the same & humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing & determining

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

Wm Lambe Attorney for the Appellt.
