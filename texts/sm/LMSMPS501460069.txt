To the Rt: Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon of William Marshall Constable of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County and Francis Oliver late Head bore of the Liberty of Glass house yerd in the Parish of St. Buttolph Aldersgate in the sd County

Sheweth That there is due to Yor. Petrs the Severall Sumes of money to their respective Names here under Odded for their Loss of time trouble and expences in conveying of Vagrants from the said parishes and through the said County as by Severall Certificates allowed under the hands and feales of Severall of his Matics Justices of the peace for this County will Appear

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly pray an Order of this Court that they may be reimbursed the said severall Sumes due to them as aforesaid by Such ways as to this Court Shall Seem meet.

And yor Petrs. Shall ever pray etc
