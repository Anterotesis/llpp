To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul, Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster [...] County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Geo: Reid and James Fielding Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d Day of March 1780 Andrew Pollock Aged 77 Years and upwards was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the said Liberty and County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last legal Settlement By which Order your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session to hear and Determine the said Appeal And to Order that all Persons concerned may be directed to attend

J. Lane King Street Covt. Garden Sollr. for the Appellts
