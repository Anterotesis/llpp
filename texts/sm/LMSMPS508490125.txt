To the Worshipful the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint John Baptist in the City of Peterborough in he County of Northampton

Sheweth

That by an Order dated the Seventh day of August last under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller and Thomas I Cotton Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Second Ann Marriott Wife of John Marriott who had deserted her and her three Children namelyCharles aged Nine years Mary aged Three year and Samuel aged about four Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to our said Parish of Saint John Baptist as the Place of their last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners consider themselves aggrieved and humbly after this Appeal and

Your Petitioners Pray that you the said Justices will please to fix a Time for the hearing and determining their said appeal and that all Parties concerned may them a Hand.

On the Behalf of the Appellants.

Peart & Miller Solls.
