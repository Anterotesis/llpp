To the Right Honourable Six Mathew Blakistone Knt. Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Rest of his Majesties Justices of Oyer Terminer and General Goal Delivery now held for the County of Middlesex at Justice Hall in the Old Baily within the Suburbs of the City of London.

The Humble Petition of Samuel South now a a Prisoner in his Majesties Goal of Newgate.

Most Humbly Sheweth.

That your Petitioner Stands Charged of having Committed certain Felonies, Wherefore he most humbly Prays, that he may be either Tryed, Bailed, or Discharged from the Same, at the now Present Session, according to the Form of the Statute in that behalf

And your Petitioner as in Duty Bound will for ever pray Etc.

Samuel South
