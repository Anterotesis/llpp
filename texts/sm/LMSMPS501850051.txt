To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justises of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and appeale of John Whetham of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County Esqr.

Sheweth That your Petrs is rated to the Poores rate & Scavengers rate and Churchwardens Rate of the Said parish of St James Clerkenwell much more in proporcon then he ought to be Therefore your Petr thinks himselfe aggreived by the Said Rates and appeales against the Same.

Your Petr therefore prays your Worps to releives him in the prmes as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And your Petr Shall pray etc.
