To the Rt. Worshipfull her Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the parish of St. Maryle Savoy in this County.

Sheweth. That by Warrt. dated the 24th. day of this inst May under the hands and Seales of Ralph Bucknall & Richard Dyott Esqrs. two of her Majties. Justices of the peace of this County (Quor unus) One Elizabeth Suger a Child of near two Years old, was removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in this County to the parish of St. Maryle Savoy aforesaid; as the place of her last legall Settlement.

That yor. petr. conceiveing themselves Aggrievd by the Judgemt of the sd. two Justices doe humbly Appeal to yor Worships therefrom

Yor petrs. therefore humbly pray yor Worships will be pleased to appoint a day, that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of St. Giles aforesd may attend this Court, to heare & abide the Judgement thereof touching this Appeal;

And yor petr. shall pray Etc;

Robt Loveday Wm. Symonds} Ch. Wardens.
