To the Worpll: Her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Petition of Abigal Gervis of the Hamblet of Spitle feilds in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That your Petitioners Husband is in the British Train of Artilery in the Company Comanded by Capt. Gilmorgen in flanders and She having two Small Children has not where with all to Subsist but must unavoidably Perish unless She has Some Releif from the Sd Hamblet of Spitle feilds which is utterly Denyed her

Your Petitioner Most Humbly prayeth that this Honble Bench will take it into your Consideration that She may be Releived therein

And She as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.

Middx ss: Ad General session paris Due Regine tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks [...] in St. John street in Com prd. p adjorn die Sabti Scilt octavo die Septembrs. anno regin Due Anne mmc Regine Magne Britani [...] [...] decimo.

It is Ordered by this Court that it be And It is hereby recomended a [...] referred unto Joseph Fory Roger Smith & John Steuart Esqrs. Justi [...] of the peace for this County or any one of them to sumon before them the Churchwarden & overseers of the poor of the hamblett [...] Spittlefeilds in this County, and to examin into the truth of [...] the Petrs. complaint, and to make such Order for the releife of the Pet [...] and her children as to the sd Justices of the peace or any one of them shall seem meet on hearing of the parties.

P Cur Harcourt
