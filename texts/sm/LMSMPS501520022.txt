To ye Right Worsppll; Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Parker of the Liberty of the Rolls in the parish of St. Dunstan in the West

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was lately allow'd by the Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty 2s.6d p Week towards her Releife

That yor. petr. was allow'd by Yor. Worspps upon hearing her complaint last August 2s. p week & did receive it of the Overseers ever since that time till now, but the said Overseers do refuse to allow yor. petr. any more them 1s.6d p Week Wch. yor. petr. is not able to insist upon such a Small allowance She being very helpless and altogether past her Labour

Yor. petr. therefore humbly prays This Honble Bench to be pleased to order that She may have her former person of 2s.6d p week

And yor. petr Shall ever pray Etc.
