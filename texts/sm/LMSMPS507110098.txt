To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden, and Overseer, of the poor of the Parish of saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Edward, and James, Fielding Esquire, tywo of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixteenth day of February last Margaret Mitchell Singlewoman aged about twenty two Years an Ideal was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of he: last legal Settlement whereby the Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Agrieved

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justice, and their said Order and pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to your Petitioners and that the Hearing and determining there of may be adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for this County.

And Your Petitrs. Shall pray Etc.

Isaac Baldwin Richd Caeser Adam Dunford Jos: Fisher} Churchwardens and Oversers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Anne
