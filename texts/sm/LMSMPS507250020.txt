To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Jacob Fletcher and William Hyde Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eighth Day of April last a Woman and two Children in the said Order called and described by the Names and Descriptions of Susanna Vanno Widow of Peter Vanno deceased and their two Children to wit Peter aged Five Years and upwards and John James aged two Years and upwards were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that Your Worships will please to Appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining this Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court.

And Your Petitrs: shall pray Etc

Jno: Dorman Richd: Nowlson} Church Wardens and

Thos. Banfield John Wall} Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
