To the Honorabell Bench now Setting at Hixis Hall the humbell pettishon of Elizbeth Warren

Humly Shwth That your pettishoner being a very poor and wanace haveing no frinds to Subsisties and her child it not Relive by this Honourbell Court must in nesaty parsh for want She not being abell to pay the fine that your honour have ben pleas to lay upon her

This is thefore humly to insptor

This Honourble Court out of
