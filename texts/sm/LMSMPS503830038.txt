To the Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Great Yarmouth in the County of Norfolk

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hand and Seale of John Milner Esquire One orf his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the thirtieth day of November last Ann Briand Widow of Edward Braind deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles's in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Great Yarmouth as the place of her last legal Settlement

Thomas Henzell Agent for the Appellants } Yor Petitioners therefore thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said Justice Appeale to this Court against the same and humbly pray Yom Worships will Appoint a day for hearing and Determining the said Appeale and that all persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And Yor Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
