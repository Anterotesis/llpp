To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Martin on of the Petty Constables of Southmims in this County.

Sheweth That at Midsumr. last past there was due & owing to Yor. Petr. the Sume of Ten pounds for his less of time and expences in releiving passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County, being for one qrters Salary, for the sd Service, ending at the same time

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymt. of the said Ten pounds to yor sd Petr.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Thos: Martin
