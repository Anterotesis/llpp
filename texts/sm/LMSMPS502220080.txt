The Humble Petition Of Gyles Fortin agt. Tho: Plumer petr discharged being bound only for 4 ys & an halfe

St. H colt

Mr. Milner Sr H. Bateman Sr Isaac Tillard

or be hd Fr: 10 cl

[x]

Sr D. Dorlins Mr Fuller Mr Tothill Cap Lambell

Middx Ss: Ad General Quarterial Session Pacis Dui Regis tent p Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd. P Adjorn die Mercur scilt decimo quarto die Octobris anno regni Dui Georgy. nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Undecimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the within named Thomas Plumer have notice of this peticon and that he doe attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the Petr. Should not be discharged from his apprenticehood to the said Thomas Plumer, and [---] be releived according to the prayer of his peticon, and that the sd. Thomas Plumer doe produce the deed of apprenticehood in Question before this Court at the same time,

P Cur Walter
