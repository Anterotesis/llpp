To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of M Harper and Jn Ellis Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorum unus) as therein is menconed bearing date the[---] Sixth day of March last Sarah Blundall Widow of John Blundall was removed from the Parish of Saint [---] James Westmr in the said County to the said Parish of saint [---] Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to their Court against the same and desire to be releived therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray [---]
