To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of I Miller and Nicholas Spencer Esquires Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 25th. day of June 1767 John Smith his Wife Elizabeth and their Child William aged 2Â½ years were removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster in the said County to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty and County as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore Humbly may this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Caleb Atkinson Tho French John Edridge Thos. Clough Wm Kirby} Churchwardnes & Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
