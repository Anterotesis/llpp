To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Country of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmenton in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of George Garratt and John Smart Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of January last One Samuel Adley and Susannah his Wife and their three Children Mary aged five years Susannah aged three years and Samuel aged Six months were removed out of the Parish of Saint Mary White chapple in the said County of Middlesex into the said Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant Do Appeal against the same and Pray that this Honourable Court will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of White chapple and all Parties concerned to Appear some day at this present Session to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

I Smart Sold for Appellts

Middsx At the General Session of the Peace of Our Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in the said County on Monday the Sixteenth day of February in the Seventh year of the Reign of Our Sovereign Lord George the third King of Great Britain

It is Ordered that on notice hereof to be given for thirth to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapple that they and all Persons concerned do Attend the Court on Thursday next at nine of the Clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court Waller
