To the Worpl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace [...] of Middx at their General Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticonr and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the Said County

Sheweth That by a Pess warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Milner and Robert Hucks Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the Said County of Middx One being of the Quorum bearing date the 20th day of January 1735 Benjamin Lord was removed from the Parish of St. Pancras in the Said County to the Parish of Saint George abovesaid (commonly calld the Parish of St. George in the Easter as the place of his last legall Settlement

That your Peticonrs thinks themselves Aggrived by the [...] Order and Judgment of the Said two Justices And humbly Appeale to this Court against the Same

Therefore humbly pray your worspsr will be pleasd to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Pancras way Attend this Court Some day this General Sessions to hear and abide the final determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And yr. Petrs shall ever Pray

Sam Cowper Att [...] the Appettants
