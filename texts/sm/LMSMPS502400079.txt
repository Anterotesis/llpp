To the Worshipll. His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of his Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middx aforesaid

Sheweth That Joseph Merim about Seventy Five Years of Age was by an Order Dated yr 16th: day of December last past under the Hands and Seals of H: Blngny and Allen Hollen Esqrs. two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd (Quor Unus) removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. [...] Anne in the liberty of Westmr. to the said Parish of St Giles in the Fields

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly conceive themselves Aggrieved thereby do Appeal from the said Order of the said two Justices of the Peace to the Judgment of this Honoble Court And Crave a day of Hearing

And yor. Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.
