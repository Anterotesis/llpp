To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall [...] Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborne above barrs in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass warrant or order under the hands and seals of John Milner and John Ellis Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of July last past whereby Elizabeth Upton widdow was removed from the parish of Saint Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above barrs as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Peticonr: conceiving themselves aggrieved by the order of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same And pray that Your Worships would be pleased to appoint some day this Sessions when all Persons concerned may be order'd to attend in order to hear the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the sd. Appeale

And Your Peticonrs; shall Pray Etc.
