To the Worshipful Justices now Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Edwards and George Wright Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the sixth day of September last Moves May was removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry

Yours Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Orders humbly Appeal asained the same and Pray such Relief as to your Worships shall seem Meet. [---]

And Your Petitioners shall ever prays

Wm. Drew Sell for Appellts.
