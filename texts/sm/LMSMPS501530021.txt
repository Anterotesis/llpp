To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Northolt in the said County

Sheweth That Robert Carter & Sarah his wife by warrant bearing date the eighteenth day of Aprill Last under the hands & Seals of Whitlock Bulstrode & Rurpert Brown Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quor unus) were passed from the parish of Greenford in the Said County to the Said Parish of Northolt (by the name of Northall) as the place of their last legall setlemt

p J Tyson Your Peticonrs: thinke themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices do Appeal to this Court & humbly pray (the premiser considered) that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Greenford & all other parties concerned may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And your Peticonrs: (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc
