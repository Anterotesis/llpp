To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition of Hannah Attersoll Widow of Samuel Attersoll late Keeper of the House of Correction at Tothill Fields Westminster.

Humbly praying that your Worships will please to allow the Account of Money distributed by Samuel Attersoll her late Husband for the Subsistance of Vagrants at Tothill Fields Bridewell herewith delivered

and your Petitioner Shall ever pray Etc

Witness Richd. Goodwyn

the Mark of Hannah [x] Attersoll
