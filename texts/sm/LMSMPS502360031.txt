To the Rt. Worshipll: the Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Peticon of Stephen Cole.

Sheweth That by Indenture bearing date ye 19th. day of June 1723 Yor. Petr. was bound an Apprentice for the Term of Seven Years to Sarah Dennis of the Parish of St Martin in the Fields in the said County Widow to learn the Art of a Brewer

That in Consideracon of the Sum of 100 Guineas given with Yor. Petr. & that yor. Petr. had some knowledge in the Manner of keeping Brewers Accounts yor Petrs. Said Mistress did Agree to learn Yor. Petr. his Trade in 5 Years and at the End thereof to discharge Yor. Petr. & that during his whole Apprentiship he Should dine Etc. with his sd. Mistress.

That of late for reasons unknown to Yor. Petr. he hath been turn'd from his Said Mistress's Table & out of the Compting House from taken of Yor. Petr. to Instruct him in his Art wch. as he is inform'd is very difficult to be learn't in the two Years now to come of Yor. Petrs. time nor is Yor. Petrs. any wise emply'd by his said Mistress but left at Liberty wthout. any direccon or Instruccons wch may be of dangerous Consequence to Yor. Petrs. altho: he hath frequently desired to be employ'd

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays That he may be discharged from his said Mistress or otherwise relieved herein as to Yor. Worsps. Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
