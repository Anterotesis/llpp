To the Right Worshipfull The Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble petition of Cecilia Fiennes an Inhabitant of the parish of St. Jamess. Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth. That your petitioner humbly apprehends herselfe to be agreived by the taxation or Assessment Assessed or taxed on her by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St. Jamess. Clerkenwell in pursuance of the authority given [...] them in & by the Statute made in the three and [...] the reign of Queen Elizabeth for that they have not [...] taxed your peticoner according to a pound rate of the re [...] the house wherein she Inhabitts in the said parish but have also taxed her for her store for which as your peticoner is advised & humbly apprhends they the said Churchwardens and Overseers have not the least foundation or colour of Authority either by the said before menconed Act or by any other Subsequent Act whatsoever

Wherefore yor. peticoner Appeals from the said Sesse or tax unto such Order and determination therein as by your Worshipps on the hearing of your peticoners case shall be thought convenient and reasonable And humbly prays your Worshipps to appoint a short day for to hear your peticon. & the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said parish in the premisses & your peticon shall ever pray Etc.

Celia Fiennes
