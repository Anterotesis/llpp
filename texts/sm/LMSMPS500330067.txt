To the worshippfull their Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now- assembled at their Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Church- -wardens and other the Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Clement Danes within the said County.

Sheweth

That by an order under the hands and Seales of Martin Ryder and Richard Milner Esqrs: two of their Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx Francis Golding his wife and Six children were passed from the parish of St. Mary le Bone to the said parish of St: Clements Danes.

That your Peticonrs: are advised that the said Francis- Golding and Family have no colour or pretence of legall settlement within their said parish and therefore de appeal to your worpps: for releif. agt: the said order. And do

Therefore humbly pray your Worpps: will please to appoint a time for the Churchwardens of St Mary be bone & your Petrs to attend in- this Court to argue the said Appeal.

And your Petr: shall ever pray etc.
