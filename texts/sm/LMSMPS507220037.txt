To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition [...] and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hemel hempsted in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Stallett Esqrs. and John Rude Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth Day of September One thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy Nine William Gates and Mary his Wife and their Six Children namely Daniel about Fourteen Years of Age William about thirteen Years of Age Ann about Nine Years of Age Joseph about Seven Years of age Thomas about three years of Age and James about Eighteen Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Primer in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Hamel hampsted in the County of Hertford as the place of the last legal Settlement of William Gates and Mary his Wife and their Six Children

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said

Warrant or Order of Removal Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such Relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Cowper Sollr. for the appellants
