Middlesex to wit to the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire and the rest of the Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the Said County of Middlesex in Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Floud and William Brodie Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County Elizabeth Cardiff hath been removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Stoke Newington in the said County of Middlesex by the name of Elizabeth Boards to the aforesaid Parish of Edmonton

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said order and therefore humbly appeal against the same and pray that your Worships will please to Appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
