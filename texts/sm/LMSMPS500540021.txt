To the Worpfull Benche of Justices at Hicks's Hall

The humble Peticon of Wm Powell of the parish of St. Giles Creeplegate[---]

Humbly Sheweth That your Peticoner is but a poor man and is so lame that he cannot work and hath A Charge of Children So that he is not able to pay what the Parisheners Assess him for they make him pay almost four pence A week to the Poor wth he is not able to do therefore humbly intreats yor Worpps would be pleased to consider his Condition & be pleased to take him off of this great Charge and in so doing your Peticonr Shall ever Pray Etc.
