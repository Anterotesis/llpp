To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justice of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon Appeal of ye Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor & other inhabitants of the Parish of Deptford in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Lyme house in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx have lately obtained an Order and Adjudicacon under the Hands & Seales of William Johnson Robt Kirby two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd County (quor Unus) dated ye 14th of Novembr last for the Removeing and Conveying of one John Holland & Anne his Wife with Robt and John Holland their Children indigent poor persons from the said Hamlett of Lyme house to the said parish of Deptford adjudging the said Parish of Deptford to be their last legal Settlemt. (the Contrary of wch yor Petrs and appellants doubt not to proves And the sd Churchwarden & Overseers of ye Poor of ye sd Hamlett have accordingly conveyed to & left with yor Petrs. & Appellants the sd Wife of ye said John Holland and their said two Children to the great Burthen and greivance of the sd Parish of Deptford which Adjudicacon & proceedings thereon (as yor Petrs and appellts are advised) are contrary to Law & Justice

Yor Petr. & Appellts there the humbly pray yor Worshipll to apposed a certain day in this prsent. Sessions to hear & determine this their Peticon & Appeal and to Ordr such Releife therein as to yor Worshpps shall Seem meet

And yor Petrs & Appellts shall ever pray Etc
