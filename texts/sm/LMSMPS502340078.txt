To the Right Worpfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Geoernor Deputy Governor Assistants & Guardians of the Borought of Sudbury in the County of Suffolk.

Sheweth

That by a warrant or Order Dated ye 8 Day of February last under the Hands & Seals of John Milner & William Hicks Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middx (Quor Unus) One Elizabeth Shaw widow the the wife of Samuel Shaw and Mary her Daughter (Aged about five Years) the Lawfull Issue of the Said Samuel Shaw & Elizabeth his wife (as is altedged in the Said warrant or Order) were lately removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Gyles in the Fields in the Said County of Middx to the Said Borough of Sudbury in the Said County of Saffolk as the place of the last Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Shaw & her Said Daughter.

The Appellants finding the uselees Agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices Appeal to this Court agt. ye Same

Amb: Kedington Morng for the Appelell. And Humbly Pray this Court will please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye Poor of ye Said Parish of St. Gyles in the Fields & all other persons Concerned to attend this Court this Preset Sessions to hear & Abide ye Judgment & Determinacon of this Court upon & touching ye Said Appeal & that ye Appellants may be Releived herein according to Law

And yr. Piticoners Shall pray Etc
