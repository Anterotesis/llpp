To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled, now holden for the said County at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Ratcliff in the Parish of Stepney in the said County

Sheweth That by Warrant or order under ye Hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and William Cross Esqr; two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County bearing date the Third day of March last; James Ferguson Son of William and Sarah Ferguson, as named in the said Order; was removed from the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County to the said Hamlet of Ratcliff as the place of his last Legall Settlement.

Your Petrs; humbly Conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Order of Removall and pray a day this present Sessions for hearing the said Appeal

And Your Petirs; shall pray Etc.

Thos, Martin Agent for Appellant
