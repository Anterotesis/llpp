To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knight Chairman- and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alton in the County of Hants

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and James Penleage Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof of the Quorum) bearing date the tenth day of June last Elizabeth Pickernell Wife of William Pickernell and her three Children Namely William then aged Seven Years and upwards, Jane then aged five Years and upwards and Margaret then aged about four Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoredicth in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Alton in the County of Hants as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day for the hearing & determining the said Appeal and to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of saint Leonard Shoreditch and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

and Your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc
