To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden said Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Rupert Clarke and Edward Naish. Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th. day of September 1799. Mary Mc. Namara Spinster and her illegitimate Child, named William for Nurture were removed and conveyed from and out of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Strand within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

W. S. Lemage, Solr. for the Appellants.
