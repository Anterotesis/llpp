To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks hall in St. John Street in & for the sd. County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bridget otherwise Brides in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands and Seals of John Milner and Philip Dyot Esqrs: Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of March last Robert Bunney and his Two Children Elizabeth aged almost Four Years and Frances aged about Fourteen of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Bridget otherwise Brides London as the place of their last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agrieved And against the said Order Do Appeal

Wm: Gason Attorney for the Appellants } Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray that a Short Day May be Appointed this Present Session To hear and Determine the Matter Touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall pray Etc
