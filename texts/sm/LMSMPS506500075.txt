To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Mongwell in the County of Oxford.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Miller and Henry Lambe Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth Day of December last Sarah Axalar and her Female Child aged about two Years were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Chiswick in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Mongwell as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Axalar and her said Female Child Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that the benefit of this Appeal may be saved to Your petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Sessions of the peace to be holden for this County.

And Your Petitr. shall pray Etc. Thos: Dyson Sollictor for the Appellants
