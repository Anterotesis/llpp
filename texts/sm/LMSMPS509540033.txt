To the right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of William Bleamire and Henry Kinnard Esquires two of his majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the third day of October one thousand seven hundred and ninety eight Harriett Jarrett with her two Children vizt. John aged two years and William aged eight Months have been removed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the aforesaid Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and therefore humbly appeal against the same and pray that this worshipful Court will please to appoint some day in the present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned do them attend this Court.

And your Petitioners Etc.
