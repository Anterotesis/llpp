To his Majesty's Justices of the peace forth County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Inhabitants of the parish of Chelsea in the County of Middx whose Names are hereunto Subscribed on behalf of themselves and divers other Inhabitants of the said parish

Sheweth

That a Rate hath been lately made by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chelsea upon the Inhabitants and Occupiers of Lands [...] and Tenements in the same Parish for the Relief of the Poor thereof in this- present year 1747, which your Petitioners are advised is unequal oppressive and unjust, For that Several of the Inhabitants of the said Parish who are Persons of great Substance and Ability are under charged in the said Rate And that Several other Inhabitants of the Same Parish of less Circumstance are over assessed in the aforesaid Rate, and for that Several other Inhabitants of the said Parish of Considerable ability are not assessed but left out of the said Rates

That your Petitioners are very much aggrieved by the aforesaid Rate- and humbly appeal to this Court against the same

And pray that the said Rate may be quashed and vacated, and that the said Poor's Rate may be produced before this Court at such time as the Court shall appoint for the hearing and determining the Matter of the said Appeall.

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.

Henry Meriton Alexr. Small V Warner Claude Bennett

Juane Ln [...]
