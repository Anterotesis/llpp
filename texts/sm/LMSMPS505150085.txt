To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices Now Assembled at Hicks's Hall

The Humble Petition of James Braybrook.

Humbly Sheweth That your Worships Petitioner being Sentenced by this Court in a fine of Fifty Pounds and Six months Imprisonment, and your Petitioner's Time being now Expired but not being able to Discharge himself by Reason of the great fine Laid upon him, Your Worships Petitioner humbly Prays your Worships will grant him Leave to Draw his Recognizances this Sessions. Humbly Hoping Your Worships will not order them to be Estreated as it will be the Utter Ruin of him, he having been Seven months Allready in Confinement Therefore Humbly Praying you Worships will Take his Unfortunate Case into Consideration Your Worships Petitioner as in duty Bound will Ever Pray Etc:

James Braybrook

21 Oct 1762
