To the Rt Worsppll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of John Skip with of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in ye said County.

Sheweth

That your Peticoner was Sumoned to attend this Court this present Sessions to Serve on ye. Grand Jury.

That your Peticoner being an Ignorant person did not attend pursuant to his Summons. And for as much as your Petitioner being by trade a Cooper and keeping a Shopp and having noe body at all to Manage his busynesse in his Absence would have. leaded to his Utter ruin to have Attended. That yor petitr: for his Non appearance is fined Fourty Shillings which by reason of his. poverty he is not able to pay And being heartily sorry for ye said Offence and willing to serve next Sessions

Yor Petr humbly prays yor Worspps will be pleased to Remitt the said Fine

And yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc John Skipweth
