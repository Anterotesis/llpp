To the Worshipfull Thomas Lane Esqr. Chairman and the rest of the Worshipfull Bench of Justices now Assembled at Hicks's Hall

The Humble Petition of William Pentlow Most Humbly Sheweth

That your Worships Petitioner. Having been Appointed on the 20th. of May Last, as Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell, Till another was Chosen; Continued as Such untill the 20th. of September

That your Worships having been Pleased to Allow A Yearly Salary of Fifty Pounds to the Keeper of the House of Correction, Your Worships Petitioner Humbly Begs your Worships would be Pleased to order The Treasurer To pay the Salary due to Him, for the four Months he Officiated as Keeper

And as in duty Bound

Your Worships Petitioner

Will Ever Pray Etc

20 October 1763

William Pentlow
