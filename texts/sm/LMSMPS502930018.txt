To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Francis against the poors Rate of the parish of Saint Mary White Chappell in the Said County

Sheweth

That your petitioner is this year Rated an Assessed the Sume of One Shilling and Six pence per month towards the poors Rate of the Said parish notwithstanding your petitioner last year appeald and by the Justices to whom it was referred him to Seven pence per Month and your Petitioner being very poor is unable to pay the said Rate

Your petitioner therefore most humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order that he may be wholy discharged from paying the Said Rate or Such Other Releif- in the premises as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

And your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray
