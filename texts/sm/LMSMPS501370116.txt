To the Right Worsppll her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Peter Vastelly of the parish of St Giles without Cripple gate in the Sd Countie Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated three Shilling a quarter towards the releife of the poors of the sd parish and for as much as yor: Petr hath a wife & 2 Children & William Harris his Son in law is Jan a May and left his wife & 4 Small Children to be maintaned by your Petr, & by reason or old Age decay of trade & losses is reduced Soe that he is uncapeable of paying the St rate

therefore humbly, prayes yor Worspp to discharge him of the sd Rate

And yor; Petr: shall pray Etc
