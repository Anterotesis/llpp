To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seals of Nicholas Jeffreys & Robt: Hinde Junr Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the 20th Day of December last David Parry aged about Sixty Six Years and his wife Elizabeth Parry aged about Sixty Six Years were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the Sd County to the Sd. Parish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London alledging therein that the sd Pish of St Andrew Undershaft is the place of the last legall Settlemt. of of the Said David Parry and his wife Elizabeth.

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants Yor Petrs. thinking themselves aggreived by the sd. Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same to this Court And forasmuch as your Petrs. cannot be ready this prsent Sessions to psecute their said Appeal humbly pray that the hearing & determining therof may be adjorn'd untill the next Genll Sessions of the Peace And that your Worpl: wou'd be pleased to appoint some Day therein for all parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt. [...] & determination of this Court touching the said Appeals

And yor Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
