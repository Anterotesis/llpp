To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of John greenwood of the Liberty of Old Street in the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Victualler.

Sheweth That Your Petr. has been lately continued by two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County one of the Overseers of the poore of the said parish for the yeare ensueing

That Your Petr is very pass adversed in yeares being about Seventy four, haveing his Eyesight very much impaired being at left utterly void of all mannor of schollarship, And in meane Circumstances, Which makes him incapable of serveing the said Office

Your Petitoner therefore most humbly prays. your Worpl. will be pleased to order the Churhwardens and Overseers of the poore of the said parish and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court some day this present Sessions to shew Cause why your Petr. Should not be discharged from serveing the said Office of Overseers of the poore

And Your Petr. Shall ever pray etc

(to attend or Salisbury J. W
