To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That to Virtue of a Pass Warrant a Order under the Hands and Seals of William Timbrell and William Mantle Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and, Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Ninth Day of February Instant Mary Clifford Widow was removed and conveyed from the Parish, of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in this County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore the Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order And pray that the Benefit of the said may be saved unto Your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this County.

And Your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Benjn Gee John Dorman Richd. Nowlson John Milbourn} Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
