To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarterial Sessions [...] Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the [...] the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Judeicture of Apprentice bearing dated 10th day of June 1725 Joseph Heughs a poor Child of the Parish of Wood ford in the County of Essex was bound to One Andrew Deverdine of the said Hamlett of Bethnal Green to eaver tiee he should accomplish his full Age of twenty four Years who in a short time after the said Indenture was Exeated went away from his said Apprentice without making any provision for him of leaving any person to Insturet his in trade or business there by he is likely to become a great Charge to the sd. Hamlett

Yor. Peticonr [...] most his ever prays that this Court said be pleased to take him Case into their Consideration and discharge him from his sd. Apprenticeshipp in Order that the may he bound on by the Officers the said Hamlett of Bethnal Green to some more fir person

And as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Christop. Platt Churchwardens
