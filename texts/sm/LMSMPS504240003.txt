To The Rt. Honble. The Lord Mayor and the rest of the Worshipfull his majestys Justices in their general Session of Gaol Delivery for the County of Middlesex Assembled

The Humble Petition of Thomas Cole

Sheweth That your petitioner on the 28th. day of December 1753 was Committed to his majestys Gaol of Newgate for the County of middlesex by Henry Fielding Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County being charged before him on the Oath of Jonathan monkhouse for feloniously selling a certain peice of counterfeited milled money resembling a moidore not cut in peices for a less Value than by its Denomination the same did Import and was counter feited for Contrary to the Statute

Yor. petr. therefore most Humbly prays that He may at this present session of Gaol Delivery holden for the county aforesaid, be either Tried Trailed or Discharged for the Offence aforesaid pursuant to the statute in such case made and provided

Thomas Cole
