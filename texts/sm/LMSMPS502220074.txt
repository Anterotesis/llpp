To the Rt. Worshipll. His Matie's Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd in their Genll. Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of Stephen Stepto

Sheweth That yor Petr. is Noiated & appointed one of the Surveyors of the Highways in the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County for this prsent Year

That yor. Petr. is ancient & very much Afflicted with the Plithisick and not able to execute the said Office

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray's that yor. Worships will be pleased to Discharge him from the said Office

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound Shall pray Etc.

Stephen Stepto
