To the Right Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon of Edmund Skinner

Sheweth That yor. Petr. was lately elected to Serve the office of Constable for the parish of Endfeild in the County aforesaid, And yor. Petr. having lived in the said parish for the space of two years past & there being divers more ancient Inhabitants there who ought to serve the said office before yor. Petr. and his presence being dayly regnized in the City of London for the managemt of his trade there

P Mc Aley Yor. Petr. therefore humble prays yor. Worsps to be pleased to order that he may be discharged from serving the said office of Constable and that some other fit person may be elected to serve the same.

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc Edmund Skynnore
