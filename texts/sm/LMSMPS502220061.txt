The Humble Peticon of Rose Cosin Widow

The petr. & son to attend on the Adjournmt day 9 cl

Middx Ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John street in Com prd. P adjorn die Lune scilt decimo nono die Octobris anno regni Dui Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Undecimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the within named John Cosin have notice of this peticon, And that he doe attend this Court on Wednesday the second day of December next at nine of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why he should not at his owne costs & charges releive & maintaine the Petr. his Mother,

P Cur Walter
