To the Rt. Worpll. His Maties. Justices of ye. Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of James Robinson Ferdinand Shrimpton & Harmen Vanheerd & Edward Linney late Conbles of the parish of st. Giles in the Fields in the sd. County.

Sheweth That Yr. Petr. during the Execucon of their sd. Office have laid out & expended severall Sumes of their own money for the passing of Vagrants as by Certificates & Receipts may appear wch they have not been reimbursed by the High Conble of the Division to wch. Yor. Peticonr. belong as by Law they ought to have been

Yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly pray that Yor. Worsps will be pleased to Order the High Conble. of Holborn Division in the sd. County to pay to Yor: Peticonr. the Sumes of money wch they have laid out for the purpose aforesd. & for wch. they have Certificates & Rect.

And Yo. Petr. shall ever pray & Etc.
