To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Deall for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Cox Esqr. and Saunders Welch Esqr. two of his Majestys. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of October one thousand seven hundred and Sixty, Jane Thorley Wife of Samuel Thorley and their Child named Susannah aged about Seven Weeks were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint-Botolph without Aldgate Longon as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Samuel Thorley and his said Wife and Child

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices of the Peace, Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same, Praying such Releif in the Premises as to this Court shall seem meet.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jas. Fishore Attorney for the Appellants
