To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for the County of Middlesex, in their General Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth, London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. Hands & Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox, Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye. Peace for ye. County of Middlesex, (One whereof being of ye. Quorum) bearing Date ye. Twenty eighth Day of February last past, Elizabeth White, Singlewoman was Removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex unto ye. Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth London, alledging ye. said Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth, to be ye. Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by ye. said Warrant or Order of ye. said Two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom & humbly Pray, that you will be pleased to hear them in ye. Premisses, and Order that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. said Parish of St. Giles in ye. Fields, may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide ye. Judgment & Determination of your Worships, touching this Appeal.

Cha Baynbridge Ally for ye. Appellants.}

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc.
