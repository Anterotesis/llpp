To ye Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Susanna Evans of the Hamblett of Lyme house in ye Pish of Stepney Widow

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamblett have assessed yor. Petr. for this present year One & twenty shillings towards ye Releife of the Poor of ye sd. Hamblett That yor. Petr. [...] a very poor woman and has four Children to maintain So that [...] is not able to pay the sd assessmt. And thinks herselfe very much aggreiv'd thereof

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that she may bee releiv'd in ye sd prmisses

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray etc.
