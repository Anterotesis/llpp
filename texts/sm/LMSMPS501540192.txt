To the Rt: Worpll: his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Petition of Samuell White of the Parish of St Paul Shadwell in the Said County of Middlx Cordwainer

Sheweth That your Petitioner is Chose one of the Headborroughs of the Said Parish for this Present year, That he is a Deafman and hath Severall Informities upon him in his head but more espetially occasioned by his setting up in the Watch house and Serving the said Office, That your Petitioner has applyed to the Inhabitants of the Said Parish to releive him from the Said Office, but they refused so to doe, unless your Petitioner would pay his Fine But forasmuch as your Petitioner has obtained Sr: Edward Northeys opinion that this Honourable Bench may discharge your Petr. and appoint an other person to serve the Said Office in his roome.

Yor. Petr. most humbly prays your Honors: will discharge him.

And he Shall ever pray Etc

Samll: White
