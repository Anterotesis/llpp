To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church- -Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woolwich in the County of Kent.

Sheweth. That by vertue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of Robert Sax & A: Osborn Esqrs two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of August last there was passed & removed into their said Parish from the Parish of St George in the County of Middx Sarah Norman the widow of Richard Norman deced with her two Daughters (vizt) Elianor Norman Aged 22 Years and Mary Norman Aged 19 Years And that Yor Peticoners by reason thereof find themselves agrieved & humbly Pray a Day this Quarter Sessions to be heard upon the said Appeal against the Order of the said two Justices.

And Yor Peticoners shall Pray Etc.

Robt: Austin Churchwarden Jon. Andrews Overseer
