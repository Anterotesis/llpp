To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Sessions of the peace.

The Humble petition and appeal of Francis Donnall of the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex Victualler.

Sheweth That Your petitioner by virtue of a warrant under the Hand and seal of the Worshipful John Spencer Colepeper Esqr one of his Majesties Justices of the peace For the County of middlesex was convened before the said John Spencer Colepeper Esqr and was convicted (as he apprehends) of Knowingly Suffering and permitting one William Mills to play at a certain Game called Skittles in his Skittle Ground in the parish aforesaid against the Form of the Statute in that behalf.

That Your petitioner Humbly conceiving himself aggrieved by the Judgment and Determination of the said Justice Doth appeal to this Honourable Court From the same and prays such Relief in the premises us to Your Worships shall seem meet.

Your petitioner therefore Humbly prays that all parties concerned may be ordered to attend this Honourable Court to hear and abide the Determination of Your Worships in the premises

And Your petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Francis Donnall
