T To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace by adjournment Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Francis Burton and Thomas Rea Esqrs. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th. day of January 1714 John Ogden was removed from the Parish of St. George Hanovers Square in the said County to the said Parish of St Maty Le Bone as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the Same

Therefore Humbly Pray this Court that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto your Petitioners and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holder for this Country.

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc. J Austin Agent for St. Mary Le Bone
