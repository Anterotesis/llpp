To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Poor for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of Jno Dawson Humbly Sheweth

That Your Worships Petitioner we Committed to the House of Correction at Clerkenwel on the 5th. Day of July last by J Croft Esqr. one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex on the Complaint if the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Pancras with having be gotter one Susannah Field with Child or Children of wch the was their pregnant That your Petitioner is [...] destrist Situation has no Money of his own nor me friend that will assist him has been in Prison now upwards of Six Months & during the time hath Suffered a seven fit of [...]

That your Petitioner is every much inclined to beleive the said Susannah Fields is not with Child, as at the time your Petitioner was appretended the Score she was four months gone and as is now upwards of Six Months Since your Petitioner has been in Confinemt makes it a longer time their Persons in that situation [...] ally [...]

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Prays your Worships to take his case into consideration and grant him such relief as in your Wisdom shall seem meet

& Your Petitioner Will ever pray Etc

Clerkenwell Bridewel 12th. January 1786.

John Dawson
