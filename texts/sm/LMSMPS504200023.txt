To the Rt. Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Pea [...] the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the P [...] Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden [...] Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Hansover Square [...] County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under th [...] Hands [...] Seals of Robert Paul and Henry Fielding Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Jus [...] for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twelfth day of July 1753 [...] Singlewoman was Removed from the Liberty of Session Hill Hatton [...] Rents in the parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County to the parish [...] George Hanover Square in the County aforesaid as the place of her legal Settlement Where by your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said [...] and that all persons concern may then accend.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

John Wharton Joseph Kettle} Churchwardens

John Shearwood John Warner} Overseers of the Poor
