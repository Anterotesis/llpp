To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of Robert Archer

Sheweth That some Yeares past ye Petr became an Apprentice to One John Archer a Gardner And forasmuch as the said John Archer having not Instructed Your Petitioner in the said Busyness of a Gardner nor Endeavoured soe to doe Neither hath he Provided for him Such Necessary as by the Covenants of his Indenture he was Obliedged to doe But hath frequenth Beate and Abused him

P Thos Nath bound by Parish Your Petr therefore most humbly Bays Yor Worshipp to be pleased to Grant that he may be discharged from his said Apprenticeshipp

And ye Petr. shall ever pray Etc
