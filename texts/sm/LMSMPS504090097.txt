To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrantor Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of William Young and Martin Clare Esqrs Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the Said County of Middlesex bearing Date the Twenty first day of January 1750 Elizabeth Dickson Widow was removed from the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore Pray this Court will be Pleased to Appoint a time for hearing and Determining the Said appeal and that all Persons concerned do then attend

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Henry Steelon Wm. Watts} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars
