To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Edmonton in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removall under the hands and Seals of George Garratt and John Smart Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of January Last One Samuel Adley and Susannah his Wife and then there Children Mary aged Five Years Susannah aged three Years and Samuel aged six Months were removed out of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapple in the said County of Middlesex into the said Parish of Edmonton in the said County of Middlesex as the place of their Last Legal Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceiving themselves Agrreived by the said Order or Pass Warrant Do Appeal against the same And Pray that this Honorable Court Will be Pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor Of the said Parish of Whitechapple and all Parties Concerned to Appear same day at this Present Session to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court Touching the said Appeall

I Heart Soll. for Appellt:
