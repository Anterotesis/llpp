To the Worpfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petr: of Sarah Temple Spr

Sheweth That your Petr. was placed out as an Apprentice by the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County aforesaid, in the Year 1740 unto William Southwick and Margaret his wife of the same Parish and County, to learn the Art & Business of A Beads Maker,

Your Petr: behaveing Humbley as Afaithfull Apprentice, and Notwithstanding which, she has been almost Starved, Whiped beat with Hott Glass by her said Master and Mistress and has dureing her whose Apprenticeship not had A Bed fit to ly on

Therefore Your poor Petr. most humbly prays that she may be discharged from the said Apprentices, yr. poor Petr. in duty bound will ever pray

the Mark of Sarah [x] Temple
