To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Benja [...] Cox Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of [...] Middlesex bearing date the thirteenth Day of March now last legal Edward William Johnson was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (as a Rogue and Vagabond vizt. wandring and begging in a loose idle and disorderly Manner contrary to the Statute in that Case made and Provided) to the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex adjudging that to be the last legal Place of Settlement of the said Edward William Johnson And your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said Justices do humbly Appeal thereform

Your Mrs. therefore most humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint Some day in this present Sessions and that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Martin and all Partys concerned in and about the premisses may then and there hear and abide the final Determination of your Worships therein

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.

John Long land Peter Armstrong Wm Brymer} Churchwardens

Saml: Hunt Dan Martin Wm. Cairns} Overseers of ye. Poor
