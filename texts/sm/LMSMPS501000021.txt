To the Rt: Honble Sr. Wm: Withers Knt. Lord Majr: of the Citty of London & to the rest of ye Honble Bench of Justices Sitting at Fisher Hall in the old Bailey

The Humble Peticon of Alexander Valiers A Prisond. in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth

That yor Petr. was in January last Court to Newgate on Suspecon of High dreason & hath beene ever since [...] a close prisoner without any prosecucon

Therefore Humbly pray [...] to be tryed or Gerwayes [...] defrgarard as yor Lipp shall think fitt

And Etc
