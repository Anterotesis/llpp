To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General [...] Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Pell and Burford Camper Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourth Day of July last James Shaftor was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Mile End New Town as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceives themselves to be Aggreived And.

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices And the said Order And Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint George and all Persons Concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
