To the Right Worsph. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon of Thomas Potts of the Hamlet of Lymehouse in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated One pound Sixteen shillings towards the releife of the poor of the said Hamlet for the year ensueing.

That your Peticoner hath susteyned great sesses whereby he is much reduced and is forced to betake himselfe to be a day labourer for the support of himself and family Soe that he is not able to pay the said rate.

Prwissley Your Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to mittigate the said assessment as onto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
