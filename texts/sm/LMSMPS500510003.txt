To the Right Worspl his Matie Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Robert Caldicutt

Sheweth

That for the Fact Whereof yor. Petr. stood Indicted in this Honble Court this Present Sessions your Worspps were pleased to Fine him 1s. Ane for new paymt. thereof to comitt him to the Bayle Doct where he now remain That the sd. Fine together with your Petr. Fees amount to 9s. yor. Petr. haveing paid 3s.6d in in part of the same And forasmuch as your Petr being very poore and being a Drooper in the Lord of Oxfords Regimt. & being willing to Submitt himselfe to the mercy of this Honble Court

P Tho Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Praye yor. Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the remaineing part of this sd. Fees as unto your Worsppl shall Seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc

Robert Calldecoat
