[...] Worsppll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Jacob Astley

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr. by his Indenture of Apprentices his bearing date the twenty sixth day of October in the Eighth year of his now Matys Reign Annoqr Dom 1721 Did put himself Apprentice unto Henry Vaughan of New Brentford in the County of Middx Poulter for the term of Four years comencing from the date of the sd Indenture As thereby may appear That Yor. Peticoners Master did receive her pound from your Peticoners father upon Executing the sd Indenture.

That Yor. Peticoner is in a very ill state of health having a great Consumpcon upon him in somuch that he not able to follow his said Master business (nor has not for some time) as severall persons will Justify to this Honble Bench & besides Yor. Peticoner (to all humble Submission to your Worpp.) looks upon the sd Indenture to be void in Law being bound tent for four Years.

P W Henman Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that his sd Master may appear one day this prsent Sessions to shew Cause before Yor. Worspps why he may not be discharged from his sd Apprenticehood.

And Yor.Petrs. shall ever prays Etc.
