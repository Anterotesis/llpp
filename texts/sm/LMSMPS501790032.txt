To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of Anne Walter of the Parish of St Andrews Holbourne in the said County Wife of Robert Walter

Sheweth That your Petitrs said Husband is Gone away from her and his left her with four small Children and her Land lady has turned her out of doors for Nonpaymt of her rent Insoemuch that she and her Children are now Perishing in the Streets

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays she may be allowed such Imediate relief From the sd Parish As to yor Worspps shall Seeme Meet

And yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

R: Walter
