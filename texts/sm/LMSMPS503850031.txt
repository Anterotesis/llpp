To the right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent garden in the Liberty of Westminster.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of removal under the hands and Seals of Thomas Lane and William Jones Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the pace for the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 5th. day of February last Dorothy Wilmot was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the Liberty aforesaid to the said Parish of St Paul Covent garden as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petr. therefore apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said order of the said two Justices do appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleasd to appoint day in this prsent Sessions to hear and determine the said appeal

And your petr. shall pray Etc

J Spranger Agent for St. Paul Covent galden}
