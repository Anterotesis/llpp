To the Honourable his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kirtlington N. E. of Woodstock in the County of Oxford.

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of H. K. Courttnay and Jno. Griffiths Exquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex, (one whereof being of the quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of February 1785 Thomas Subtree a single man was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Kirtlington (therein described Kirkington) as the place of his last legal settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices do humbly appeal against the same: and pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next general Sessions of the peace to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex and that the Court will be pleased to appoint a day in the next Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George Hannover Square aforesaid and all other persons concerned then to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the then Court touching the said Appeal
