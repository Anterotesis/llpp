To the Right Worshipfull His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands & Seales of Oyly Michel & Robert Jackson Esqrs. Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor nunc) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of October last past Sarah the Widow of George Whaley was removed from the Hamblett of Spittlefields in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate London alledging the said Parish of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate to be the Place of her last legall Settlemt.

That your Peticoners Conceiving Ym: Selves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justicess Do therefore appeale to your Worships therefrom And humbly pray that your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses, & Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamblett of Spittlefeilds in the said County may appear before you at a certain Day to be by year Worships thereunto appointed to hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacon of yor. Worps touching this Appeale.

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall pray Etc.
