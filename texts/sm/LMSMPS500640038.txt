To the Right Worll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace of the County of Midds

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St Giles in the Feilds

Sheweth

That lately William Froggett an Infant of about the age of Six months was passed from the pish of St Andrews Holborn in the said County to the said pish of St Giles in the Feilds by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Sr Salathiel Lovell Knt & James Mundy Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the Said County (Quoris unns) alleadging that William Froggett was born in the said pish of St Giles in the Feilds & that he is legally settled there

I Tyton [...] Your petr think themselves aggreived wth the Judgment of the said two Justices doe therefore appeale to this Worll. Bench & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Andrew Holborn & all other pties conserned herein may be Ordered to appeare in this Court some day this sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & finall determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor Petror (as in duty bound) shall evr pray
