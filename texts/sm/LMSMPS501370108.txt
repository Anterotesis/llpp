To the Worshipfull Her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon & appeale of Edward Longstaff of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middlesex Gardner

Humbly Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated nine Shillings P Quarter towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish, which is double the Sum he was ever rated before, altho your Peticoner is no better Circumstances, but a great deal worse than ever having a Sick Wife and six small Children [...] but most of whom are now Sick, also that others of better abilities are not rated so much therefore Your Peticoner humbly prays your Worships to be Discharged from paying the said rate.

And your Peticoner as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
