To the Right Worpll: her Maties. Justices of the pease for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of Francis Delate of Grub street Liberty in the Parish of St. Giles wth out Cripplegate in the said County

Sheweth That yor: Petr: hath had very great sesses as is well Known wch hath reduced yor Petr. to a poor Condition yor. Petr also being old and hath a great Family to maintaine get not withstanding yor Petr is Asessed Thirteen Shillings and six pence per Quarter to the poors Rate of the Sd. Parish besides the Churchwardens Over Rate which yor petr is not able to pay

Therefore yor Petr humbly prays of this Honble Court to be eased in the said Rate

And Yor Petr. Shall pray Etc. Francis Delate.
