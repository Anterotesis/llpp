To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices Peace in and for the County of Middlesex General Quarter Session of the peace asses [...]

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Ga [...] and Ely Rents in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Philip- Dyot and David Walker Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth day of September Instant Sarah Gilbert Widow of John Gilbert was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Elizabeth as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said [...] Gilbert, whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved, and against the said Order do appeal.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly [...] the Benefit of their appeal may be said and [...] until the next General Session of the peace to for the said County of Middlesex and That your Worships will be please some day on the said next Session for the and Determining this their said Appeal

Cox Soll for Appeall:
