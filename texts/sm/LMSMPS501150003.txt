[...] Honble: Bench of Justice

The humble Petition of William Davis

Sheweth

That Your Petr: having putt [...] an Apprentice to John Brograve [...] Dancing Master, with him [...] serve for the space of seven years, and to [...] instructed in the Art of Dancing, [...] his said Master instead of Teaching him, [...] and finding him his victuals, as by Inden [...] he is obliged to Doe, neither [...] instructs him, or gives him sufficient [...] Sustenance, or necessaries.

Now yr Petr: most humbly prays that you will take it into your Consideration and that you would Bee Pleased to discharge him from his Said Master,

[...] Petr: (as in duty Bound) will ever pray Etc
