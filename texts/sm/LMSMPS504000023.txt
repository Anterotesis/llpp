To the Right Worshipful His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlesex At their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition Of David Dyer Now Laying In Prison In Clerkenwell In the County above mentioned

Most Humbly Sheweth That Your Petr. Being Convicted of the Crime Charged against him By Bird Coalmerchant In Eastsmithfield In Midlx aforesaid for Stealing Coals upon Which Your Petr. was thereupon Convicted And Your Petr. Being Sensible of his facet & Crime hereby Premiss fishers Amendment In his Life or Conversation whereby He may Gain an Honest Livelihood Therefore

January the 19th. 1749.

Most Humbly Pray that In Stead of the Judgement of Publick Punishment Your Petr. may Receive Private Punishment Having Now A Wife and Three Small Children who May Bear the Reflections of Many People from Such Publick Punishment

David Dyer
