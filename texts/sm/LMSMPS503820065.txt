To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Rea Esquire and the [...] Honble. the Lord Carpenter two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 24th. day of November 1747 Ann Moring the wife of Francis Moring was removed from the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County to the Said Parish of St. James as the place of her last legal Settlement tohereby your Petitioners think them selves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a tine for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may Attend

And your Petitioners Shall Pray Etc Sam Seddon Agent for the said Parish of St. James.
