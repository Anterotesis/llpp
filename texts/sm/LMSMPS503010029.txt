To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Aseembled in genll session at Hicks Hall in and for the Sd County

She humble petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Clemt Danes in the Sd. County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order Under the hands& seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the Sd. County of [...] Middx (of wch one being of the Quorum) bearing onto the St. day of this Instant May Mary the wife of thos Kick was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr toys parish of St. Clemt Danes as the place of her last legall settlemt whereby your petrs. conceive themselves to be agreived & agt the Sd. Order doe Appeale

[...] : Chimmow- Sollr forthe Applts} Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a short day may be Appointed this Prsent session to heare & determine the matter touching thes Sd Appeale

And your Petrs shall pray Etc
