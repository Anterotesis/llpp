To the Worshippfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Genll. Session Assembled at Hicks Hall in & for the sd. County

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Jsutices of the Peace of sd. County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of this Instant Augt. Mary the wife of Saml. Branklin & Hannah their Oane were removed from the parish of St. John of Wapping in this County to the parish of St. Clemt Danes as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd, Saml. Branklin whereby your Petr conveive themselves to be Agrieved and agt. the sd, Order doe Appeale

Your Petr therefore humbly pray that a Short any may be Appointed at the Adjornment of this present Session to heare & determine the ure touching the sd Appeale

And your Petr Shall ever pray and Soe forth

Robt, Chimmow. Sollr. for the Appellts.
