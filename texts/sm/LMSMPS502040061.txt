To the Rt. Worspll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County at Hicks hall assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Kyrby and Bastwick Johnson Esqrs: two of his Mats. Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorun) bearing Date the 10th Day of May last Benjamin Barwick was removed from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middx: to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe whereby your Petrs. humbly Conceive they are Wronged and aggrieved .

Yor. Petrs: therefore humbly Appeal agt. the said And yt of Removall of the said two Justices and pray Your Worpps: will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining of the said Ap: -peal this present Sessions and that your Petrs. may be relieved as unto your Worrpps: Shall seem meet

And Yor. Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc
