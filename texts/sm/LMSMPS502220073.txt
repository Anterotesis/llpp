To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices now Sitting at Hicks Hall

the Humble Petition of Simon Thonis

Sheweth that your poor Petitioner lies Dangerously Ill of a feaver and to attend the Court would Endanger his Life.

He therefore prays the Court will be pleased to Exuse his not being able to attend and Defer the trial till the next Ensueing Sessions and he will allways pray Etc.
