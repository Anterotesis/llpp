To the Right worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of [...] Middx.

The humble Peticons Henry Hankin of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell [...] said County

Sheweth. That your peticoner is rated Seven Shillings P month towards the releift of the Poor of the said parish for the yeare Ensuing and your Peticoner being over rated humbly conceives himself much aggreived.

P L Wessley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to mittigate the said [...] as unto your Worspps Shall Seem meet.

And Yor. Petr shall ever Pray Etc.
