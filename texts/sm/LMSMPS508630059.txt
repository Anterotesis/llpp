To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humbe Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney and Chas: Friquet Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middelsex (whereof one is of the Quorm) bearing date the 1st day of June 1791 Mary a female Bastard Child born of the Body of Mary Pratt a Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of Saint Mary Newington to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, do therefore Appeal to your Worships thereform and humbly Pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Promises and Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the County of Middlesex may appear before you at a Certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Pearson Solicitor for the Appellants
