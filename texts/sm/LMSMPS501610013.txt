The humble Petition of Alexandr Jack on behalfe of Jon. Jack to Henr Thompson of ex aford there 10sd

on oath of Jon. Gery that on 1st. May he ser cop : of petr & Order on wife of Hen Thompson Jon Jack Sworn

Neither Thompson or wife appeared

Appr to be dischd

intr. Cod pery Mr Venner Capt Brown Mr Burdus Mr Crosbie

Middx Ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Din Regis tent & Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John Street in Com prd. adjorn die Martis Scilt tricesimo die [...] Aprils anno regin Dur Georgy [...] Regis Magne Britannie Etc tertis

It is Ordered by this Court that the within named Henry Thompson & his wife of one of them doe attend this Court on Thursday next at ten of the Clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the within named John Jack should not be discharged from his apprentice hood to the said Henry Thompson,

P Cur Harcourt
