Middx Easter Session 1768} To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd County in their General Quarter Session Assembled this 13th day of April 1768

The Petition And Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Totteridge in the County of Hertford

Humbly Sheweth Whereas by An Order under the Hands and Seals of Willm: Hallet and I. Rudge Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Bearing date the fourth day of this Instant April Ann the Wife of William Cashbolt hath been Removed from the Parish of Harrow on the Hill in the sd County of Middlesex to our sd Parish of Totteridge as the Place of her Lawfull Settlement-Therefore We the sd Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of Totteridge thinking Ourselves Agreived by the sd Order & Removal Do Appeal to this Court Against the same And do Desire that the Hearing and Determination of this our sd Appeal may be Postponed And Respited Untill the Next General or Quarter Session to be then Heard & Determined by this Court And

Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc: John Miller Church Warden William Sheppey Overseer of the Poor
