[...] Rt Worshippfull his Majtes: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Joseph Whitney of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth

That your Petitr: was by several of the Inhabitants of the said Parish last Weeke malltiously chosen to serve the Office of Scavenger therein But Forasmuch as your Petitr: having [...] ed but Two yeares in the said Parish and being aged above Sixty yeares and very Infirme and Diverse of his Neighours who have Lived many yeares in the said Parish not having as yett served the said Office Soe that your Petitioner humbly Conceives himselfe aggrieved

Your Petitr. therefore humbly prays your Worshipps will be pleased to order that he may be Discharged. from serving the said Office And that such other Person may be Chosen in his Roome As unto yor: Worspps shall seeme Meete

And yor: Petitr: shall ever pray Etc

Jos: Whitney
