To the Rt: Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace att their General Quarter Sessions holden for the County of Middlx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Humble wapping Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by win Order date the 26th day of March last past under the hands and Seales of Robert Dennett and Edward Grose Esqr [...] the [...] Samuel Aged four Years the Lawful Issue of [...] [...] (quorum [...] (quorum ) [...] Mary Lugg the wife of Robert Lugg and her two Children (Vizt) Thomas Aged about Eight Yeare And Samuel Aged about four Yeares the lawfull Issue of them [...] the Said Robert and Mary Lugg were Removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the Said County to the Hamlett of wapping Stepney abelesaid as the last Legall Settlement of the Said Mary Lugg and her Said two Children Whereby Your Petr think themselves age [...] and Humbly Appeale against the Said Order

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Pray Your Worsppll to [...] the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Said Parish of St. Bololphs Aldgate and all other persons Qucornd to Attend this Court Some day this present Quarter Sessions to her & abide [...] the Judgement and determine con [...] of this [...] Honoble Court on and touching the Said Appeal [...]

And Your Petr Shall [...] Pray Etc
