To the Right Worshipfull His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seales of John Milner & John Ellis Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Ninth Day of January instant Sarah Sherwood Singlewoman, was removed from tha Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx unto the Said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the Said Warrant or Order of the Said Two Justices Doe therefore Appeale to your Worships thereform, & humbly pray That, your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, And Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain day to be by your Worps appointed to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of your Worships touching this Appeale.

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
