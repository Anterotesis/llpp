To the Rt Worspll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Blundell

Sheweth

That for the fact whereof your Petr: Stood Indicted in this Honourable Court this present Sessions Your Worshipps were Pleased to Fine him Tenn Pounds And for Nonpayment thereof to Committ him to New Prison in this County where he now Remains

That your Petr is very Sorry for the said Offence And having Wife And Severall Children to Mainteyne And humbly Submitting himselfe to the Goodness and Mercy of this Honourable Court

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays Yor Worshipps to be pleased to Mitigate the sd fine As unto Your Worshipps Shall Seeme meete

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.
