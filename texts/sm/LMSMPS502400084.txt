To the Right Worp full His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St.Vedast als Foster London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hand, & Seales of Samuel Grey & Jo: Colbalch Esqrs. Two of His Majty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twentyeth Day of December last past That one Mary Ailay Widow aged about Forty nine years was removed from the Precinct of the Savoy in the said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Vedast als Foster London, alledging the said Parish of St. Vedast als Foster to be the Place of her last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners Conseiving themselves agreived by the said warrant or order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeals to yor: Worps therefore, & humbly pray That your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, & Order That the Chapple Warden & Overseer of the Poor of the Precinct of the Savoy in the said County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day to be by yor. Worp appointed to hear & abide the Judgement and Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale.

And yor. Petrs. Shall Pray Etc.
