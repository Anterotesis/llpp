To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their their present General Sessions of the peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the the bars in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order of pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of G. Mercer, and J. Mercer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County lane whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of November Instant, Susannah Bowen was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the County afore aforesaid to the said part of the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the same and request that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that the Churchwadens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone may be Ordered to attend the Court to hear and abide the determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal.

and your Pettioners Etc

Arthur Gliddon} Sollicitor for the Appellants
