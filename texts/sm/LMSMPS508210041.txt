To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex

The humble petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an order of Removal under the hands and Seals of The Reverend Sir George Booth Baronet and William Blackbourne Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 3d. day of February 1787 Mary Javens Singlewoman aged Nineteen years was removed and conveyed from the sd Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex aforesaid unto the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of his legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removed of the said Two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session And that a Day in the next Sessions may be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof

R Brewer Sollr. for Applts
