To the Rt. Worppll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of John Eadey of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticoner is Rated Foure Shillings and Six [...] pence & Quarter to the poores Rate of the said Parish besides all other Overrates.

That your Peticoner having sustained great Losses by his Trade is Due reduced that he is there by [---] almost become uncapable of paying the said Rates.

Therefore your Peticoner humbly prays Your Worpps that Considering the premises You will be pleased to discharge him from paying the said Rate or mitigate the same As to yours Worshpps. shall seem meet.

And Your Peticoner As in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc
