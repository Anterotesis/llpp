To the Rt. Worspl. he Matys Justices of the Peace for the County: of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Clarke.

Sheweth That for the Fact whereof Yor. Petr. Stood Indicted in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions Your Worsps were pleased to Five him 20s. and for Nonpaymt. thereof to comitt him to New Prison in this County where he now Remaines

That your Petr. in very Sorry for the sd. Fact and being very Poor and haveing a Wife and Four Children to mainteyne is not able to pay the sd. Fine and humbly Submitteing himselfe to the Mercy & Goodness of this Honble Court

P Tho Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most Humbly prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Mittigate the sd. Five To unto yor. Worpps shall seem meet

[...] Petr. Ever Pray Etc
