To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of Moses Scrofton of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That your Peticonr: hath been Nominated and Appointed to be One of the [...] Surveyors of the High wayes in the Said parish of St Giles in the Feilds for this present year

That your Peticonr: humbly conceives he is not obliged to Serve the Said Office by reason he is noe house keeper but a Servant to his mother who is the house keeper & drives a Trade of Selling Old Goods his father being lately dead

Your Peticonr: therefore humbly prayes (the prmises considered) that he may be discharged from Serveing the Said Office

And your peticonr: (as in duly bound) Shall pray Etc
