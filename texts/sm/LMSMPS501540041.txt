To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeal of Stephen Sedgwick of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth. That your peticoner is rated for the Sum of One Hundred pounds Stock or personall Estate In the poors Rate of the said Parish

That your peticoner humbly Conceives himselfe aggrieved by the said Rate and Appeals against the sames

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays such Relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought Meete

And Yor Peticoner shall Ever pray et [...] Stephen: Sedgwick

July 31st: 1716 Present John Fuller Tho Rand and Isaac Tillard Esqr

Not releived
