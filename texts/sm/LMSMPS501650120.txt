To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties. Justices of the Peace for County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble pticon and Appeall of the Church wardns and Overseers for the poor of the parish of St Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant undr the hands and Seales of John Milner and John Cleeue Esqrs two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quor Unus) dated the 17th. day of August 1717. Joseph Trevors and Elizabeth his wife were past from the parish of St. Gyles in the feilds in the sd. County to yr Petrs. as the place of their last legall Settlemt.

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselfes aggreived by the ordr. of the sd. Justices and appeall to yr Worpp therefrom

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly hope Poul Appoint a day to heare and determine the sd. Appeale

And Yor Petrs. Shall pray Etc
