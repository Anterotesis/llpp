To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County Midds

The humble Peticon of Abraham Tribett

Sheweth

That your Peticoner is assess'd by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County four shillings a Quartr. towards the Releife of the poor of the said parish for the year ensuing Wch. said Assessmt. is after the Rate of Eight pounds p Annu (at two shillings in the pound) Wch. said Rate is charg'd upon the Inhabitants of the said parish towards the Releife aforesaid

That yor. Peticoners Rent is but four pounds ten shillings p Annu So that he thinks himselfe very much aggreivd by the said Assessmt

Yor. Peticoner therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that she may be Releiv'd in the said prmisses.

And yor. Peticoner as in Duty Bound Shall ever pray etc.
