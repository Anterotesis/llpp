Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull the Justices of Peace for the County of Middlesex att Hickx's hall Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Smith Victualer

Sheweth That Yor. Peticoner has Served the Office of Constable for the Parish of Kensington in the said County by the Space of one whole Yeare & upwards And there is noe Court Kept wherein a Constable can be Chosen not like to bet. of some Considerable time

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly prayeth that he may be discharged And that Thomas Smith a Timber Merchant Unanimously Chosen by ye Inhabitants of the said Parish may by Order of this Court be Sworn in his Place and your Peticonr. will ever pray.
