To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx.

The Humble Peticon of Henry Mackerith Constable of the Parish of Enfield in the sd. County

Sheweth That your Petr. has been Constable of the sd. Parish for ye. space of two years last past and has been at a great deal of Expences & trouble in Passing Vagrant wch: have been brot. into ye. sd. Parish by Passes from distant Counties And your Petr: upon making Application to George Bourne Esqr. one of his Majties: Justices of ye peace for the sd. County to be reimburse his sd: Expences was promised to be allowed the same, as this Predecessor in ye. sd. Office was Allowed by Order of his Majties: Justices of the peace for ye. sd. County in Sessions Assembled wch. was Sixty Pound P ann.

That yr. Petr. has not been reimbursed the sd: Expences or any part there of nor had any Satisfaction or Allowance fro ye. same

Yr. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that yr. Worspps would be pleased to ordr: that he may be paid the sd: Sixty Pounds P ann for the time he has been in ye. sd. Office or give him such other relief in ye. premises as to yr. Worspps shall seem meet

And yr. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc

Henry Mackris
