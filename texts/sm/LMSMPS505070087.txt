To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace, assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overssers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanr. Square in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Saml Peirson & Nichs. Spencer Esqrs two of Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of West (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of January 1762. Anne Headland, a Bastard Child born of the Body of Judith Dennison Spinster; was removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in this County as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners Humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day for the bearing and Determing of the said appeal and that the persons concerned may then attend.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Wm: Perrice John Spencer Wm. Cobbell John Jackson} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square
