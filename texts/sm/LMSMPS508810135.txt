To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of N. Conant and Philip. Neve Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 28th day of December 1792 Elizabeth Elef and her two Children were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid, to the said Parish of Saint Pancras as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived and humbly appeal therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners Shall pray Etc

Herbt Robertson Solr. for the Appellts.
