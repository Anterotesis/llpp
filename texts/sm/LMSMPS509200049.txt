Middlesex January Session 1796} To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices Assembled to keep the Peace Etc in and for the County of Middlesex at this present Session of the peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of removal under the Hands and Seals of R. Davies and Thomas Barnard Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of January instant Timothy Berry and Elizabeth his Wife were removed and conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Poplar and Blackwall in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Luke as the Berry and Elizabeth his Wife

That in as much as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or pass Warrant and the Removal and Conveyance of the said Timothy Berry and Elizabeth his Wife in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said pass Warrant or Order of Removal and humbly pray relief in the Promises And that the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing & Determination thereof be adjourned until the next General Session of the peace to be holden in and for the County of Middlesex

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
