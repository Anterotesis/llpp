To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The humble peticon & appeal of Isaac Henriquise of London Merchant

Sheweth That by order dated the 17th day of Aprill instant under the hands & seals of Sr Salathiel Lovell Kt Recorder of the City of London & John Hungerford Esqr two of her Maties Justices of the peace for this County (quor [...] ) your petr is adjudged the Father of a female bastard child born on the body of Dorothy Seward widd within the Liberty of the Rolls in the pish of St Dunstan in the west in this County which is become chargeable to the said Liberty

That thereby yor Petr is ordered forthwith to pay or cause to be paid to the Overseers of the poor of the said Libty the sume of three pounds tenn shillings & tenn pence being by them expended in pvideing necessarys for the said Dorothy Seward & the said female bastard Child to the date of the said order & from & after the day of the date of the pd order to pay or cause to be paid weekly & endy week the Sume of three shillings of Lawfull mony of England to the Overseers of the poor of the sd Liberty for the fine being for & towards the releife & maintenance of the said female bastard child Soe long as the same shall remaine chargeable to the sd Liberty & alsoe that your petr shall forthwith gie sufficient security to the Overseer of the poor of the sd Libty to pforme the sd order or else to appear this prsent Sessions & abide such order as this Court shall make in the prmes That your Petr finds himself aggreived by the said order or judgmt of the said two Justices & humbly appeals agt the same to this Court

Yor Petr there fore humbly prays that your Worpps will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions for the Overseers of the poor of the said Liberty & all other Psons concerned to attend this Court this prsent Sessions to hear [...] abide the judgmt & determinacon of this Court upon & touching the sd. appeal & that your Petr may be releive in the Prmes according to Justice

And yor Petr Shall pray. Etc.
