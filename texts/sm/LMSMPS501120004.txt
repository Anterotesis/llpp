To the Rt. Worpfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Eleanor Mills

Sheweth

That there is due to your petr from Ralph Merideth the Sume of One & twenty Shillings for Wages, for Non paymt. whereof he Stands bound by Recognizance to appear in this honble Court this prsent Sessions

That the said Ralph Merideth hath not yet appeared pursuant to his Said Recognizance

Yor petr. therefore humbly prays this Court would be pleased to grant a Bench Wart against the Said Ralph Merideth.

And yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc

Eleanor Mills

Pauper
