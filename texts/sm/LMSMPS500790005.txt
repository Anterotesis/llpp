To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Chapple [---] Wardens and Overseere of the poor of the Precinct of the Savoy in the County of Middx

Humbly sheweth

That by virtue of an Ordr dated the 20th. day of May last past, under the hands and Seales of Gideon Harvey Esqr and Richard Rider Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace of this County (Quor unus) Eleanor Witherly was removed from the parish of St Martins in the Fields in this County to the Precinct of the Savoy aforesd alleadging that the Same was the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Elianor Witherly

That yor. Petrs. hope to prove to yor. Worshipps that the said Elianor Witherly never had any legall Settlement in the Precinct of the Savoy aforesaid, wherefore yor Petrs. conceive themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the said two Justices doe humbly appeale to yor Worshipps therefrom, And

Wm. Balch Sollr Yor Petr (the premisses considered) humbly pray yor. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day this present Session that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of the parish of St Martins aforesd may attend yor Worshipps, and heare and abids the Judgemt of the Court herein.

And yor petrs Shall pray Etc

Christopher: Riley Chapple Warden Robt Bampton Overseer
