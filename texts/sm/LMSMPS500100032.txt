Peticon of Matthew Ferne

To the Rt. Worpll. their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd.

The humble Peticon of Mathew Ferme one of the High constables of the Hundred of Pore wthin this County

Humbly Sheweth That yor. Petr: hath faithfully Executed the sd office of Highconstable this the Hundred aforesaid for the Space of three yeares last past to the great expence & trouble of yor: Petr:

John Streete Samll: Greenhill John Page Yor: Petr: therefore humbly prayes yor Worps will be favourably pleased to discharged yor: Petr. from tye execucon of the sd office of Highconstable as aforesd And to grant yer: Pet. a warrt: from this honble: Bench for the bringing (one of the Psons hereunto Subscribed or some other fitt pson to this Honble: Court in order to be Sworne for the due execucon of spd Sd. office of Highconstable wt in the sd Hundred of Pore in the roome & place of yor: Peticonr:

And yor: Petr: (as in duty bound) Shall pray Etc
