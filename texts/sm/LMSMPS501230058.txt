To the Rt: Worspll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Ovseers of the poor of the Parish of Chesham in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Ordr. Dated the 25th day of June 1711 Under [...] hands & Seales of Richard Milner and John Milner Esqr Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (Quar Unus) James Palmer Aged neer Fourteen Yeares and Henry Palmer aged Eleaven Yeares Children of James Palmer were lately removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in this County to the Said Parish of Chesham as the Place of the last Legall Settlement of the Said James Palmer and Henry Palmer

That yor. Petrs. find themselves Aggreived by the Said Order of the Said Two Justices of the Peace and Appeale against the Same

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petrs. therefore Pray that this Crt. will Order a day in this Sessions for all Parties to Attend and to heare & Abide the Judgemt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the Sd Appeale.

And Yor. Petrs. shall Ever pray Etc.

Exd a time Copy
