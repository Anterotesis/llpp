To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of John Stephens

Sheweth. That Your Petitioner has lived with his Grace the Duke of New Caste for near Nine Years and dureing all the said time has behaved himself with Sobriety fidelity and honestly.

That the Office or Place of Master or Keeper of the House of Correction of Tothill feilds is become vacant on the Death of Samuel Attersoll

That Your Petitioner is fitt and Qualified to Execute the Trust reposed in such an Officer

Therefore humbly Prays Your Worshipps to Choose Your Petitioner to be Master or Keeper of the said House of Correction in the room and Place of the said Samuel Setters all deced

And Your Petitioner in Duty bound shall ever pray.

John Stephens
