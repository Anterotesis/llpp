To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of R. Abington and Joseph Moser Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quourm) bearing Date the twentieth Day of October Instant Mary Leicester and her Child Martha born on the 22d. day of May last were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City and Liberty of Westminster to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and Pray this Court that the Benefit of the said Appeal be Saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc.
