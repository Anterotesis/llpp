To the right Worpl. his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of William Platt of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell

Sheweth That the Petitioner hath for many years payed all parish taxes and duties, but now being Seaventy three years old & very lame and past his labour and haveing little or no trade is reduced to great straights & necessity, is no longer able to pay any taxes not being able to get to himselfe a maintenance

The poor Petitionr. therefore humbly prayeth Yor. Worps will please to grant him Such reliefe in the premisses as to your Wisdomes shall be thought fit, And the Petr. shall ever pray etc.
