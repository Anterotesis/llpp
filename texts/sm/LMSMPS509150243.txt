To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller and George Reid Esquires two of His Majestys, Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Sixth day of August One thousand seven hundred and ninety five Sarah White (whose Husband George White as alledged in the said Outer, hath Run away from her) and her two Children to wit Elizabeth aged two years and six months and Samuel aged Eleven Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Isleworth in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the City of London (by the description of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the County of Middlesex) as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah White and her two Children whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

W L & J Newman Sollrs. for the Appellants 14 Thavies Inn } Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Pass warrant and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determination thereof.
