To the Rt. Worpll: his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of James Short

Sheweth That your Petr. was this Day tryed & comitted of petty Larceny in stealing asmal Quantity of Breaver & Coney Wool That year Petr. hath a wife & four smal children & nothing to maintain them but his hand Labour & hath been there six weeks confined in Goals whereby your Petrs. Family is reduced to great hardships & almost starving.

That if your Worps: should order your Petr. publick punishment it will be the alter andoing him so that no Body will employ him or if continued much longer in Goals must be reduced to a starving Condicon.

Therefore your Petr. most humbly prays that your Worps: would be pleased to order that your Petr. may be privately punisht as to your great Wisdoms & goodness shall seem meet.

And your Pertr as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc

James Short.
