To the Rt. Worppll. his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Thomas Bridgman of Ye. Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in ye. County of Middx

Sheweth

That your Peticoner is Rated towards the Releife of ye. poore of ye said Parish One Shilling and Six pence P Bound for Twelve Pounds P Annum for halfe a Years and Seaven Shillings and Six pence P Hundred Pound for Psonall Estate for ye. same And that your Peticoners dwelling home and premisses he Possesses is onely Tenn Pounds P Annum And that your Peticoner can make itt appeare that there are a great many person in the said Parish of much better Ability in Psonall Estate is not Rated soe much as our Peticoner

Your Peticoner humbly Prays such Releife in the Prmisses as to your Worpps Shall seem meet

And your Peticoner shall Ever Pray Etc
