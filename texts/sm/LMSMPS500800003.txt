To the Rt: Honble: Sr. Thomas Abney Knt. Lord Mayr of the Citty of London & to the Rest of the Justices of this Honble. Court

The humble peticon of Judith Inglesby a prsoner in Newgate

Sheweth That yor peticonr: is confined for coyning of false money And she being inocent of the said Fact And She haveing wittnesses here in Court hopes thereby to prove her Innocency

Therefore the peticonr: humbly prayes she may be tryed at this prsent Sessions And that her prayer may be entered accordingly As to yor. Lorspps shall Seem meet Etc

And yor. peticonr: shall pray Etc
