To ye Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Richard Oldridge and John Lane

Sheweth That yor. petr. lately became bayle for the appearance of one William Joy in this Honble Court the last Sessions and the said William Joy then being in her Matys Service could not appeare in this Honble Court Wherefore yor. Worspps were pleased to respite the said Recognizance till this prsent Sessions

That [---] the said William Joy is Still in her Matys Service in parts beyound the Seas

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to respite the said Recognizance untill the next Sessions

P L Westley

And yor Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
