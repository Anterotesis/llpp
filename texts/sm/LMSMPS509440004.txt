To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire Chairman and the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of James Cook

Sheweth That Your Petitoner was or the thirteenth day of November last adjudged (by George Story Esquire and John Staples Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex) to be the reputed Father of Female Bastard Child born of the Body of Isabella Collier in the Parish of St. George And the said Justices did Order Your Petitioner to pay to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St- George the Sum of One Pound six shillings for and towards the lying in of the said Isabella Collier And did- further Order Your Petitioner to pay to the said Churchwardens and Overseers the Sum of Two Shillings weekley for and towards the Sustentation and maintenance of the said Bastard Child during so long time as the same is chargeable to the said Parish

That Your Petitioner conceiving himself agrieved by the said adjudication and Order Humbly pray that Your Worships will grant him the benefit of his Appeal and such other relief in the premises as may seem meet

And as in duty bound will ever pray
