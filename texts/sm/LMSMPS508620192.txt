To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in & for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Cogan Esqr and Rupt. Clarke Esq Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting & for the said County bearing date the Eleventh day of December now last Charlotta Margaret Read Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St Mary Islington in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Kensington as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Charlotta Margaret Read Whereby your petitioners Conceive themselves aggreived and do therefore appeal to your Worships against the said Order

Wherefore Your Petitioners humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the Hearing & determining of the said appeal and that all parties concerned may be Ordered to attend and to abide such Order as your Worships shall please to make and that you will 'Give' such other releif in the Premes as you shall see Just And Your petitioners shall pray Etc

James Cowmeadow} Church Warden

Richd. Davis John Annis} Overseers
