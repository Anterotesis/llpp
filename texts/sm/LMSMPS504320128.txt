To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex In their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Chruchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 20th. Day of August 1754 Darcus Bendall (called in the sd. Order the Wife of Peter Bendall) was removed from the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London aforesaid Alledging the last legal Settlement of the said Peter and Darcus to be there And Your Petitioners Concerning themselves agrieved by the said Order [---] and Judgment of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal therefrom And humbly pray Your Worships.

Burr Attorney for Bpsgate Parish. Would appoint someday this present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex to hear this Appeal And that Your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green and all Parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there attend hear and abide the final Determination of Your Worships.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
