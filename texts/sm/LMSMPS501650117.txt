Midd: ss To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of ye. Peace for ye County of Midd: Assembled in Quarter Sessions.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Oversseers of the Poor of the Parish of Holy Trinity within the City of London

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Samll: Perry & Doyley Mitchell Esqrs: two of his Majties: Justice of the peace for ye Said County of Midd: (Quor Unus) bearing date the 19th day of Sepr: Last, John Caborne & his three Children (Viz) Willm: aged about Seven Years Geo: Agustus Aged about two Years & two months & Elis: Sophia Aged about three weeks, were removed and Conveyed from ye Hamblett of Spittle fields in the parish of St Dunstan Stepney in the said County of Midd: to ye said parish of Holy Trinity by ye Name of St Trinity as ye Last place of there legall Settlement.

Your Petrs: finding themselves Agreived by the Said Order yt the Said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court Against the same And pray this Court will be pleas'd to Appoint a day this present Sessions for ye Church Warden & Oversseers of ye Poor of the said Hamblett of Spittle fields, And all parties Concern'd to Attend this Court to here and Abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeale

And Yor: Petrs: Shall ever Pray
