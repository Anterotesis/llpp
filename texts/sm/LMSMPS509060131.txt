To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their [---] Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Little Munden in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and George Story Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing Dated the eleventh Day of November last past Mary Pooley the Widow of James Pooley and her Children videlicet Edward Pooley Susannah Pooley Charles Pooley and Mary Pooley werd removed from and out of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Little Munden whereby your Petitioners find themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to the Court against the said Pass Warrant or Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Benjn Cherry Solr. for the Parish of Little Munder
