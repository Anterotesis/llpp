[...] to the Right Worshipful her Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at this General Quarter Session of the [...] Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by a Warrants or Orders under the Hands and Seals of Benf. Camper Equire and John Sherwood Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex from whereof a of the quorum bearing Date the 13th day of October 1773. Thomas Carder who in Insane and Mary his Wife and then Child Thomas Aged three Year upwards. [---] were removed and conserved from the Parish of St. Mary What Chapel in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Christs Church in the sd. County of Sury as the place of there last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order. of Removal do humbly Appeal to this Count, againsts the same, praying such Relief in the Premises as to the Court shall seem meet

And Your Petitoner shall every May. Etc

Jn Pardon Solr.

33 OCTOBER 1773
