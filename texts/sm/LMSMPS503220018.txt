To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the Said County

Sheweth

That by a Warrt or ordr. Dated the Twenty Fourth day of Febry one thousand seven hundred & thirty six under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Chandler and Anthony Wroth Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of this peace for the Said County (one being of the Quorum) Francis Eleanor was removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County to the parish of Christ Church aforesaid As the place of his last legal Settlement

That Yor Petrs. conceive themselves Aggreived by the said Warrt or ordr of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court against the Same

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray that yo will be pleased to Save to them the Benefit of the said appeal and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjorned in the next General Sessions of this peace to holden for the said County of Middx and that Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish of St. Leond Shoreditch and all persons Concerned may be ordered to attend the Cour of the said next General Session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middx hear & abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching the sd. appeal and yor Petr may have such further releif the premes as to Justice & Law doth appertain

And yor Petrs Shall ever pray

A. Davis Att for Appellants
