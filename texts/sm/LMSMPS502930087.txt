To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for the said County.

The Humble peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Over seers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County aforesd.

Sheweth

That by an Order Under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (One of them being of the Quorum) dated the 13th. Day of this Instant June Ann Wilbourn was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn on have the Barrs in this County to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her last Legall Settlemt. Where by your petrs. conceive them selves [...] be agreived.

Your Petrs. there fore Humbly pray Your Worships we- be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions to hear & determine. the matter touching the sd. Appeale.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pray
