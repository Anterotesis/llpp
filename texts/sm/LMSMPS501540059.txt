To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and appeale of Mathew Feild of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County [...]

Sheweth. That your petrs rated towards the releife of the poor of the said parish ten Shillings P quarter and for as much as your petr rents a house of Twelve pounds P ann, and that he is charged. Twenty pound for the same Therefore your petr. thinks himself aggreived by the said Rate and appeales against the Same.

Your Petr prays your Worps to releive him in the prmes as unto your grave wisdom Shall Seem meet

And he Shall pray etc

Mathew Feild
