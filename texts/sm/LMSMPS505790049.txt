To the Worship full his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of [...] Hands and Seals of John Brettell and Elisha Biscoe [...] two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Ninth Day of January instant Thomas Cooper was removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bart in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden at the Place of his last legal Setting [...] by Your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggriev against the said Pass Warrant or Order do Appeal Court.

Your Petitioners therefore most humble [...] that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint Same Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of [...] Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars and all Persons concerned Lo [...] then attend this Court.
