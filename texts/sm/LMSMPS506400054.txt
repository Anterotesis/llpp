To the Rt. Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace by Adjournment Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under that Hand and Seals of Robert Pell and John Shewood Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th day of March 1774. Richard Hawker and Margaret his Wife with their two Children and lawfull Jone Namely Mary aged about two Years and half and John Aged about One Year and half were Removed form the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in this County, as the Place [---] [---] of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners humbly pray this Court that the benefit of the said Appeal be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that all persons concerned may then Attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the a Court touching the said Appeal And your Petitioners shall for pray Etc.

S: Daynnes Caleb Atkinson J: Jones John Noyrove} Churchwardens & Overseers
