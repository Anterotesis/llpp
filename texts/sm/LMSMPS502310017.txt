To the Rt. Worspll. his Majtes. Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middlsx.

The Humble Peticon of Nicholas Ludford one of the petty Constables of Southmims in this County.

Sheweth

That at Michmas. last past there was due and owing to yor. petr. the Sume of ten pounds for one qrters. Allowance for his- [---] trouble and Expence in passing and Con- -veying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymt. of the sd ten pounds unto yor. sd petr. for the sd Service

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Nicholas Ludford
