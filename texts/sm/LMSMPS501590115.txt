To the Worshipp full his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at the Generall Quarter Sessions held for the Said County.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St John of Wappin in the said County of Middx

Humbly Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands and Seals of John Poulson and Bastwick Johnson Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) one Mary Allen the wife of Thomas Allen with 2 Children Vizt. Major aged about Three years and Susanna aged about Six Months were lately removed and passed from and out of the Hamblet of Wappins Stepney in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St John Wappin as the place of their last legall Settlement whereby said parish of St John of Wappin- think themselves very much aggreived & that they have not any legall Setlement there

S: Bass Therefore humbly pray your Worshipp will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Quarter Sessions to hear and determine the said Appeal as in your just Judgemt. shall seem right

And your Petitionrs Shall Pray Etc
