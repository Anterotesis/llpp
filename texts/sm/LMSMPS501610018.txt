To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of Edward Percivall of the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petr: is an Inmate having only a Cellar in the said parish for which he pays Seven pounds ten Shillings P Annum

That your Petr: hath for some time last past been rated thirteen Shillings P Annum towards the releife of the poor of the said Parish besides Overrates your Petr: there fore thinks himself agrieved and Humbly Appeals to this Honeble. Court against the same.

And prays that ye this shipps would be pleased to Order that he may be wholly discharged from paying any parochiall Rate he being very poor and of mean Circumstances.

And your Petr: shall Ever pray Etc
