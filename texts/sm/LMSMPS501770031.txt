Middx ss To the right Worpfull his Maties. Justices of the peace now Assembled at their genall Quarter Sessions at Wesmr in this County

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of little Brickhill in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order bearing Date the Sixth day of February last under the hands and Seals of Thomas Burdus and Francis Sorell Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County of Middx (Quor unus) Christian Bonn wifeof & John Bonn was lately removed from the parish of Saint Margt; Westmr in this County to the parish of litle Brickhill aforesd. as the place of the last legall Settlement of [...] send John Bonn being soe Adjudgd. by the sd two Justices By which said Adjudicacon & Order your peticoners humbly conceive themselves to be Aggreived and doe Appeale to this Court against the same and therefore

Your peticoners most humbly pray that this Court will Appoynt a day this Sessions for ye Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St Margts. Westmr. & all other psons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the sd Order & this Appeale & that your petrs may be thereby releived

And they shall ever pray Etc
