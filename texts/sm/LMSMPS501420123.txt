To the Right Worsppfull his Majetys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the Said County

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Clemt Danes in the said County

Sheweth That by Warrt. under the hands and Seales of J: Cooper & J: Poulson two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the sd. County bearing date the First day of January instant Lettice Draper Singlewoman was removed from the parish of St. John of Wappin in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of her last legall Settlemt. Whereas your Peticoners hope to prove that the said Lettice Draper hath wee legall Settlemt. in the sd. Pish of St. Clemt. Danes & humbly appeale from the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices to yor worpps

P Chim0mow & St.Clemt.

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray your Worspps would be pleased to [---] Sumon before you the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. John of wapping to Shew Cause why the said Lettice Draper Should not be againe repassed to their said parish & the Order of the Said two Justices vacated And yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
