To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of Joseph Mansfield.

Sheweth That your Petitioner or Sundry other Persons to your Petitioner utterly unknown called the Governors are rated and assessed and taxed for or on the Account of Saint Lukes Hospital for Lunaticks in the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middlesex for One whole Year beginning at Lady Day 1757 after the Rate of £2..13..4 by the Quarter & of £10..13..4 by the Year.

Whereas your Petitioner hath no Sort of Estate Title Interest or Occupation of in or to the House called St. Lukes Hospl. or any Part thereof Or of in or to the moveable Goods & Chattells within the said House or any Part thereof And as Your Petitioner hath been informed and believes No other Person or Persons whatsoever have any absolute Interest Estate or Occupation of or in the Same by reason of which either your Petitioner or any other Person is by any Law or Statute liable to be rated and assessed and taxed or bound to pay any Sum or Sums of money whatsoever to the Relief of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. Lukes.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly conceiving himself aggrieved by the sd. Sess & Tax doth Appeal against the same And Prays that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day at the next Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. Luke And All Persons concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioner shall Pray Etc.
