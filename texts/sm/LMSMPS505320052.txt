To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Allhalows the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thos. Smith and Geo: Garrett Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of February 1764 Mary Gear Widdow was removed from the Parish of St. Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex aforesaid unto the said Parish of Allhallows the Great as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to the Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and Pray. That Your Worships will be pleased to Order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St: Matthew Bethnal Green and all persons concerned do Attend this Court some Day in this present Session to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And Your petitioners shall pray Etc

{Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Allhallows the Great.

Church Wardens {Timy Budworth John Kennet

Overseer John Darlow
