To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N Conant and John Scott Esquire two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County bearing date the 15th. Day of September 1797 Elizabeth Elliott (Wife of James Elliott) and their two Children namely James aged Six Year and William aged about eleven Months were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James within the City and Liberty of Westminster aforsaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the

the said Order and humbly Pray Relief in the Premises and that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a Day for that Purpose.
