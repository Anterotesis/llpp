To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. John's Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of W: Blackmore & P: Rowlands two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd: County of Middlesex bearing Date 13th day of Novr: 1778 Whereby Mary Barnwell Single Woman was removed from the Parish of St, George in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to our said Parish of St John Southwark aforesaid as the place where the said Mary Barnwell was & is legally settled by which Order your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships leave to appeal against the sd Order of Removal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thomas Heorne Saml. Saloway Andrew Newland} Church Wardens

Wm. Meymott Thos. Cox Thos. Jackson Joseph Frogg Danl. Wigmore} Overseers
