To the Rt Worpll his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church London

Sheweth Thos: Elmes Agent for the Appellants That by Warrt. or Order under the hands Seales of John Milner & Wm Hucks. Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 4th day of December last Arthur Harris aged Eighty Yeares & [...] Ann his wife & Elizabeth the Child of the sd Arthur aged eight Years by Elianor his former wife deced were removed & conveyed from the pish of St Giles in the Feilds in the sd County to the Parish of Christ Church in the City of London alledging therein that the sd Parish of Christ Church London is the Place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd Arthur Harris & Ann his wife & Elizabeth the Child of the sd Arthur by Elianor his former wife deced.

Yor Petrs. finding themselves aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices Humbly Appeal against the same And pray that yor. Worps wou'd be pleased to appoint some Day this Psent Sessions for all parties concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
