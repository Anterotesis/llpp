To the Right Worpll. his Maties: Justices of the for the County of Middx assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of John Cook of West Horsley in the County of Surrey Malster

Sheweth That on or about the 29th Day of August last past your Petr Freighted in a Barge of William Wells of Sunbury from west Horsley in the County of Surrey ten Quarter of Malt in Order to be conveyed to London that in its passage the sd Middx Barge was Unfortunately sunk in the River Thames in the County near Westmr & all the sd Malt spoilt.

That your Petr hath paid the Duties for the sd Malt amounting to forty Shillings

And hath also Given notice to the Collector of the sd Duties that he wou'd apply to this Court this Prsent Quarter Sessions for an Allowance for the sd Damage & Loss

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays such an allowance in respect of such Damage & Loss as to your Worps shall seem meet

And yor Petr shall pray Etc.

John Cook
