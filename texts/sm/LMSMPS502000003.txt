To the Rt: Worpll: his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds

Sheweth

That by an Order and the hands of Seales of John Mercer & John Richardson Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing date the third Day of January Instant Henry Comfort with Margaret of his Wife and John their Young Child aged at fourteen Months were removed from the parish of St. Gyles in the Fields in the said County to the said parish of St Martin in the Fields as the place of their last Legall Settlemt.

Your Petrs. thinking themeselves Aggrieved by the Judgemt of the said two Justices humbly Appeale there from And pray that this Court Will please to Order the Churchwardens & Overfeers of the poor of the said parish of St Gyles in the Fields And all parties herein concerned to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear and A bide the Judgemt. & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Petr. (as in Duty bound) Shall Ever pray Etc.
