To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Generall Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in ye said County

Sheweth That by warrant or order Dated the 11day of Augt. Instant under the hands and seales of Peter Gifford and John Mercer Sqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye county for Middx (Quor un) Elizabeth Shepard widow of Joseph Shepard and Paul their Child aged almost two years were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in ye County of Middx to the Parish of Christ Church in ye said County as the Place of their last Legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners find themselves agreevied by the Warrt. or under of the sd Two Justices and humbly appeal against ye same your Petitioners therefore humbly pray you will be Pleased to Order to Church wardensa and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds and all other Persons concerned to attend this court Some day this Present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinats of the Court Touching the Premisses and that your Petiticon may have such Relief therein as to Justice and Lw Doth apperta [...]

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc
