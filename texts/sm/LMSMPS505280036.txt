To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petiton & appeal of the Church- -Warden & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Cox & Philip Dyot Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 3d. day of October last Mary Elderfield Widow of Benjamin Elderfield deced. was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said Two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased in this present General Quarter Session to hear and determine the said appeal

Nathanl Redhead } Ch:Warden

Thos Burch John Blew } Overseers
