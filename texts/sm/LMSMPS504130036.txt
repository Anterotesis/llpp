To the Worshipfull his Majesty; Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middx aforesaid.

Sheweth. That by a pass dated the 16th day of August Cast under the hand and Seal of Thomas Lane Esqr one of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx Ann Row aged about thirteen Year therein said to be a Vagabond was removed and Conveyed Rom the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the sd County to the sd: parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the place of her birth.

That your Petitioners and Appellants apprehending themselves grieved by the said pass of the said Justices humbly appeal to this Court againt the same.

Your Petrs. and Appellants therefore Pray this Court will please to Order the Constable Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the sd. parish of St. Clement Danes and All persons concerned to Attend this Court some day this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the premisses and that ye Petrs. and Appellants may be relieved therein as this Court shall see meet

And ye Sepr. Etc

William Carter Atty for the Appts
