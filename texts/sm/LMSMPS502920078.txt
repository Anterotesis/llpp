To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of Joshua Walker Keeper of New prison At Clerkenwell

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petr. is commanded by an Order made by this Worshipfull Court to shew Cause why he has not Paid the Sum of Twenty pounds P Ann. to the Treasurer of this County ever since his Entrance in the said Office

That Your Petr. did pay the Sum of Thirty pounds for the first Year and half after he was admitted Keeper The Fees Profitts of the said Place being then very considerable to what it has been since Which Your Petr. humbly Submits will appear by two Accounts The one for the first Year since Your Petrs. admittance and the other for the last Year, ready to be produced to Your Worships

That Your Petr: is at great Charges providing Necessarys for the said Prison and is obliged to continue his usual number of Servants for the security of it The Arts and mischeivous Practices of Prisoners [...] almost daily increasing

That Your Petr: hath not had any great Complaint made against him Since his being admitted Keeper and as much Care has been taken of the Prison and Prisoners he humbly conceives as sould be possible There hav [...] above Two prisoners dyed in Eight Years either in the said Prison or [...] being discharged from the same and not one Dyed nor any Escape hath been made f [...] upwards of tow Years Neither hath there been any Oppressions laid to his or his Servants Charge

Therefore Your Petr: most humbly [...] and [...] Premisses considered Your Worships will be plea [...] him with such releif as to Your Worships in your [...] and Clemency shall seem meet

And Your Petr: (as in Duty bound) Shall [...]

Joshua: Walker
