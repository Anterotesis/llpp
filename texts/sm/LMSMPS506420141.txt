To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the Peace of the sd. County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Stoke Newington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or pass warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (on being of the Quorum) bearing date the 12th day of October 1773 Mary Holmes and Alice her Daughter lawful issue aged 2 Years upwards were removed & concerned by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County from & out of the Sd. parish of the said Parish of St. Mary Stoke Newington by the name of the Parish of Stoke Newington in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Holmes & Alice her Daugh -ter

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said order or pass warrant humbly appeal to your Worships against the same and pray relief in the Premisses that a Day in this present Sessions may be appointed for the hearing and determination of the said Petition and Appeal

Jos: Hatt Turner Solicitor for the Appellants}
