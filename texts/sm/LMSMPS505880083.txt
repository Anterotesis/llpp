To the Worshiful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in ad for the County of Middlesex Assembled at their General Quarter Session Holden at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant on Order of removal bearing Date the Twenty eight day of September One thousand Seven hundred and sixty eight under the hands and seals of William Timbrell and William Mantle Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one being of the Quorum) Susannah Johnson Single woman was removed from the parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the Said Parish of Saint John of wapping as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Pass warrant or Order of removal do humbly Appeal Against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your worships will be Pleased to Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County and all other Persons Concerved to Attend this Court some day this present Session to here and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal and that your Petitioners may have such releif therein also Justice and Law doth Appertain

And your Petitioners Will ever pray Etc.

Willim Grasby Fra P Anderson Robt Stinson} Church Wardens

Thos. Clark Jno. Badnall Thos. Pike} Overseers of the poor
