To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Andrew Enfield in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Ordered made under the hands and Seals of Aaron Graham and William Bleamire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Tenth day of September 1793 Richard Rathbone Singleman was removed and Conveyed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Enfield as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved-

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the David Order and humbly pray relief in the premises and that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining this their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
