To the Worshipll: His Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That a Male Infant Child Named William Aged about five years was by an Order dated the fifth day of September last under the hands and Seals of Rob Jackson & Robert Dennett Esqrs. two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the said county of Midds (Quor Unns) removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds

Yor Petrs. therefore humbly conceive themselves Agreived thereby do Appeal from the said Order and Judgement of the said two Justices of the Peace to the Judgement of this Honoble Court And crave a day of Hearing

And Yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
