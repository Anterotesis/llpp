To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St John Street in and for the County of Middlesex assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint George in the Borough of Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Tho. Smith & John Smart Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one of ye Quorum) bearing date the 10th day of March last Mary Harris Such Wife of Thomas Harris Such (who is gone away from her) & her Child by the said Thomas Harris Such named John and aged one year last May was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in ye County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint George in the Borough of Southwarke as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitionrs conceive themselves agrieved & agt. ye sd. Order do appeal

J. Preston Atty. for ye Appelants 28th April 1767} Your Petitionrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear & determine the Matter touching the said appeal

And yr Petitionr. shall pray
