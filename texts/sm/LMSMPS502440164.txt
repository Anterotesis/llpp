To the Right Worpl his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the peace of St Benedict als Bennet Fink in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & Seales of [...] Grey and Willm Ellis Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Tenth day of June last 1727. Mary Swanton aged about Twenty Years was Sent and delivered by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Pauls Covent Garden in the County of Middx to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Bennedict als Bennet Finck London Alledging the said parish to be the place of her last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgmt of the Said two Justices and do most humbly appeal to your Worships therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That yo [...] Worships will be pleased to save to them the benef [...] of their Appeal and appoint and a day within the next General Sessions of the peace to be holden for ye sd County for hearing the said Appeal And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Pauls Covent Garden [...] [...] and all [...] & hear & abide the Judgment determinacon [...] touching the Said Appeale

And She Shall ever pray Etc.
