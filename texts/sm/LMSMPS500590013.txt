To the he Worfull. the Bench of Justices now Sitting at Hicks Hall at John's Street

The Peticon of the Church Wardens of St Mary white Chappell in the County Midx Humbly Sheweth

That your Petrs. Since the Inditme of the [...] of the late Overseers of the poor of the said parish are informed of Severall Errors in his Said Accounts and your Petr are fully Satisfyed of Severall omissions

There fore your Petrs. humbly desire that the former Auditt of the said Overseers Accounts may be set by [...] Keth Warrant may be quainted to auditt them

And your Petr is in doth to Steven prayr

[...] Wm Crawley} Church [---]
