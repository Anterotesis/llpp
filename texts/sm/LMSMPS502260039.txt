To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their Geo [...] Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden in and for the Said County

The humble Peticon and appeal of De [...] sharrow of London [...]

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Henry Lowth and Oyly Michel Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (Quor unus) bearing Date the Seventeenth day of February, last your said Peticoner is Adjudged to be the Putative father of a Male Bastard Child born in the Parish o St: Giles with out Cripplegate of the Body of mary Watson Widdo.

That your Peticonr: con [...] elves himself Aggrieved [...] the Order and Judgment of the said two Justices and doth there fore humbly appeal against the same praying such relief in the- Premisses as to your worships Judgment shall seem just

And your Peticonr: Shall ever pray Etc
