Middlx Ss To the Rt: Worsppfull his Majties: Justices of the peace for the County. of Middlx, at the General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Peticon of Sarah Esson Apprentice to William Reed of the Hamblet of Bednal Green in ye said County.

Sheweth. That yor: Peticonrs. said Master hath Frequently beat and Misused yor. Peticonr in a barbarons maner without any Lawfull Cause and hath not provided her Sufficient Necessaries, So that yr. Peticonr is unable to Continue in her Service.

Therefore yr: Peticonr most humbley prays. that yr Worspps would be pleased to Order ye: sd: William Reed to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to shew cause why yor: Peticonr. should not Discharged from her apprenticeship.

And yor: Petr. shall ever pray Etc Sarah Esson.
