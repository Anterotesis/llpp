To the Worshipfull the Justices of the peace for the County of Middelsex.

The Humble petition of Randall Mosse being an inhabitant peace. Old Street in the parish of Crippilgate: Sheweth that Hee being a Lodger and Hireing a shop of thirty Shillings P year he is Charged to the poors Rate More then his said Rent and your Petitioner being old: and [...] A bell to work as formerly Humbly Indicats that He may be Dischard from ye said poors Rate and your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray

Randall Mosse
