To the Worshipll her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Flower Brooke Widdow.

Sheweth

That your Peticonr is a very poor Widdow with Foure small Children the Eldest being but Nine yeares old,) who was some time since past out of yorkshire with her sayd 4 Children to the parish of St. Andrew Holborne in the Liberty above the Barrs in the County of Middx and the late Overseers there allowed your peticonr. 5d. a Week for some time: but is now reduced to 4d. which your peticonr is not able to subsist on her said Children being all small and your Peticonr. out of Work in a strange place without any Relacons or friends to Assist her or Contribute to her Releife

Yor. Peticonr there fore most humbly prayes your Worshipps would be grationsly pleased to take her poor and distressed Condicon into your tender and Compassion at Consideracon And grant an Ander from this Worshipll. Court to the present Church- -Warden and Overseers of the poore in the parish and Liberty aforesaid for a further allowance from them; for the better releife of your poor Peticonr. and her sayd foure Children who are [...] a Miserable Distress for want there of as to the [...] Worshipll. Court shall seem meet:

And your Peticonr will ever pray Etc
