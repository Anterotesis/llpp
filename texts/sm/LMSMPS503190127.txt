To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Luke in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth.

That by Warrant or Order dated the 27th. day of September last under the Hands and Seals of Robert Hacks & William Hacks Esqrs. two of his Majestyes Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof is of the Quorum)one Catherine owen Aged about ten years was removed & Conveyed from the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of St. Luke as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

That your Petitioners Apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the said order of remove all of the said two Justices & appeal to this Court agt. the same.

Jno: Witton Sollr: for the Appellts.

Your Petitionrs. & Appellants therefore pray this Court will please to Appoint a day in this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields & all other persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgement & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal And that ten your Petitionrs. & appellts. may be releived therein According to Law & Justice.

And yor. petitrs. & appellts. shall ever pray Etc.
