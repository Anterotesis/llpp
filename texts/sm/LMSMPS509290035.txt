To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Peace at the Session House Clerkenwell Green assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Trinity Little Minories in the County of Middlesex

Shew That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or order trader the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and A Graham Esqrs Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of July 1796 Francis Cahill was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Leonard in the Liberty of Saint Martins Le Grand in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Trinity Little Minories as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Francis Cahill whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrived

Therefore they appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and the said order and prays that your Worships will be pleased to adjourn the hearing of the said appeal and appoint some day in the next Sessions for the hearing and Determination of the said appeal and to order that the Church Wardens and overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard and all Persons Concerned do the attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray.

Chas: Martin

Sollr. for the Appellants Haydon Square
