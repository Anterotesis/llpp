To the Right Worpll: Her Matis: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the Said County

Sheweth That on the eighteenth day of September last Frances Thomas Spinster [...] from the parish of St Mary White Chappell in the Said County to the Said [...] of St Giles in the Fields by a Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Ste [...] Roger Smith Esqrs two of her Maties: Justices of the peace for the Said County (Quor [...] unns) as the place of the last legall Settlement of the Said Frances Thomas [...]

Your Peticoners think themselves aggreived by the Judgement of the Said two Justices and Appeal to this Worpll Bench & humbly pray [...] that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Mary [...] White Chappell and all other persons concerned may attend this Court [...] Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgement & finall determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

P J Tysons

And your Peticoners as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.
