To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Richard Billing.

Sheweth That Yor. petr. is assesd by the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St [---] [...] in the sd. County Three shillings a Quartr. towards the Releife of the poor there Wch. said Quartely Rate or Assessmt. is (at two thillings in the pound And at the Rate of Six pounds P Annn

That Yor Worspps Petr. rents but a house of Three pound 10s P Annn So that he thinks himselfe very much aggreved thereby

Yor petr. therefore most humbly prays Yor. Worspps. that he may be releivd in the sd prmisses

And Yor Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
