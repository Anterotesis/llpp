The humble Petition of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of St James Westmr.

to att: Mo: 3 cl

On oath of Robt. Austin that on the 1st. of March. 1717 he served a to p: of this peticon & Order upon Jon. Ball, and Ball not attent Cort Ordd. that Cess: & cess: be taken off

Midd ss: Ad General Session pacis Dni Regis tent p Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd P adjorn die Sabti Scilt primo die Marcy anno regni Dni Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto

It is Ordered by this Court that the within named John Ball have notice of this peticon, and that he doe attend this Court on Monday next at three of the clock in the afternoon to shew cause why the Cess at processus for stay of proceedings Upon the Indictmt. within menconed should not be taken off and why the said John Ball should not be prosecuted thereupon for the reason within menconed

p Cur Harcourt
