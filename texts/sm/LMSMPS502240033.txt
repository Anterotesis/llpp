To the Right Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Roberts and ann Caine & Elizabeth Hith of the parish of St James Clerk work in the Said County

Sheweth That your Peticoners are rated to the poor and Scavengers rate of the Said parish But being aged and very poor are not able to pay to the Said rates and Appeal against the Same

Yer Peter therefore humbly pray they may be discharged from paying to the Said rates

And Yor: Peter Shall Ever pray Etc Wm. Roberts Ann Caine
