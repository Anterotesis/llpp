To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of William Grayham

Sheweth

That in May last Your Petitioner became bail for one Stephen Winston (then a Servant to your Petitioner) before the Worshippfull Justice Mercer for his Appearance in this Court the next Sessions

That the said Stephen Winston hath ever suite absconded and Yor peticoner hath been at great Charges in Endeavouring to Apprehend him But the same hath proved Ineffectuall

Your Pet therefore humbly pray Your Worshipps will be pleased foresaid the said Recogniance Sune Die

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc.

Wm Grayham
