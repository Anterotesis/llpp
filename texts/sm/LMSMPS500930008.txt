To the Worshpll: her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition of Sarah Pinckney of the parish of St: Andrew Holborne in ye Division above barrs

Sheweth That your petitioner paid the last year to the pares Rate but two Shillings and Six pence a quarter but is charged for this prsent year four Shillings and Six pence a quarter.

Wherefore your Petitionr prays that She may bee releived herein And your Petitionr will ever pray ye Sarah Pinckney
