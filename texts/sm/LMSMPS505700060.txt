To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in their Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That on the Eighth day of April 1767 James Laird then one of the late Overseers of the poor of the said parish came before Robert Pell Esqr one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex and upon his oath verified the Truth of his Accounts as one of the Overseers for the said parish in the year 1766.

That the said James Laird hath not in his said Account given the said parish Credit for all the Sums of Money to hath received or might have received and as such your Petitioners Do apprehend the said Account is Erronious and unjust

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the same and humbly pray such Relief therein as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

James Praech Attorney of the Appellants
