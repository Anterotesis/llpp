To the Rt: Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Jacob Wakelin late Constable of St Mary Stratford Bowe in the said County.

Sheweth That there is due to your Peticoner for Passing of Vagrants the Sum of three Pounds & Five Shillings as appears by the Several Orders [---] which your Peticoner Stands in need of

your Peticoner therefore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to order that he may be paid the said moneys

And your Peticonrs: Shall ever Pray Etc

Jacob Wakelin
