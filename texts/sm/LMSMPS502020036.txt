To The Rt Worshppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of John Bowes of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner by order Dated the third Day of April Instant under the Hands of John Filler and Mathew Hewitt Esqrs. two of his Matys Justiced of the Peace for this County was approved of and Confirmed to be One of the Scavengers of the aforesaid parish for the year Ensuing as being an Able Tradesman That Your Petr humbly Conceived himselfe much approved by the Confirmacon of the said two Justiced as being an person ( pursuant to the Art of Parliament in that Case made and provided) Excluded from Serving the said Office.

Your Petr. therfore humbly prays he may be Excused from Serving the said Office and another fit person chosen in his Room

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray etc John Bowes
