To the Honrable Bensh of Justis is Belong into the Honrable Cort [---] of Exsis Hall The Humble petecion of Fransis Exton Your per peticioner

Humbley Saieth that you would be pleased for to Consider my deplorable Condecion for I have bin a long time under Confind [...] and have bin verey ill Ever sens i have bin under this Confindence and no frind for to give me aney releve for my wife and cheldren are verey ill and not able for doe aney thing for me I humbley beg your honrable faver for to grant me the fees of the gole for i am not able for to pay them and if not i must ley hear and perish and my poor fameley all so and your poor petecioner as in Duty shall for Ever pray
