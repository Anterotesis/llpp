To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [---] Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Joseph Faikney Chas Triquet Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty eighth day of April now last past or 1791 Mary Ann Mansell aged near 4 years was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London as the place of the last legal settlement of the said Mary Ann Mansell

That your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and pray this Court that the Benefit of the said appeal be saved unto them untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your petitioners will ever pray
