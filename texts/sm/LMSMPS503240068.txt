To the Worship full his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Session at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in and for the Sd. County.

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands & Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th. Day of June Last Anne the wife of Jno. Lary was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapell. in the sd. County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Clemt. Danes[---] as the Place of the Last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Anne Lary whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be Agreived & agt. the sd. Order Doe Appeale to this Court.

R: Chimmore Agent for St. Clemt. Danes} Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed next Genll. Session to hence & Determine the matter touching the sd. Appeale [...]

And your Petrs. shall pray
