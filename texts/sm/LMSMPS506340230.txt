To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Michalemas Session of the Peace assembled for the Year 1773.

The Humble Petition of William Davis

Sheweth That your Petitioners predecessor John Wilkins deceasd was in contract with his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex for keeping the Pavement of Brentford Bridge in Repair [...] the Yearly allowance of Nine pounds and since his death the same hath been repaired by your Petitioner for the same annual allowance

That there [...] therefore Due to your petitioner at Michael mas last the sum of Nine Pounds for keeping the said Bridge [...] in Repair for the space of One Year.

Wherefore your Petitioner humbly prays your Worships will be pleased to Order Mr. John Benson the County Treasurer to pay unto your Petitioner the said sum of Nine Pounds as the yearly allowance [...] for the Repairing the said Bridge

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.
