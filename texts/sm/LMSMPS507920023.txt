To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace in the County of Middlesex at the General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Barnfather John Wright Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the the said County of Middlesex (One where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of November new last Past Mercutio Hill was removed and Conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mercutio Hill whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Order and Adjudication of the said two Justices and may your Worships will Appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

Major Wright Solliciter for the Appellants
