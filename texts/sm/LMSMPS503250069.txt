The humble Petition

Geo: Blackiston Grocer

agt. Edward Lloyd his Apprentice

to be hd. Sat 10 cl Mr. Theeds for Petr. Petr. in Court

Appre dd Father of the Appre in Court Master to deliver & Cloaths to Ov: of the poor Ov: of the poor in Court .Mr. Lane .Mr. Chandler .Mr. Miller .Mr. Booth .Mr. Margetts

Middx At the General Session of the peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middx at Hickshall in St. John street in the County aforesaid on Monday the fifth day of September in the eleventh year of the reign of our sovereign Lord [---] George the second King of Great Britain Etc

It is Ordered by this Court that notice of this petition & Order be given to the within named Edward Lloyd and also unto Robert Oliver & William Oxtoby now or late Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in this County, And that they and all persons concerned do attend this Court on Saturday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the matters contained in the said petition,

By the Court Walter
