To the worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in this present Session assembled

The humble petition of the Churchwarden of the side of the Parish of Fulham called Hammersmith and Overseer of the poor of the Hamlet of Hammersmith

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the hands and Seals of William Addington Esq and Archibald Mitchell Esqr two of his Majestys of the peace acting in for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22nd day of June 1793 Frances Poole was removed and conveyed from and out of the [---] Parish of St. Paul Covent garden within the said Liberty to the said Hamlet of Hammersmith as the place of the last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner concerning themselves aggrievedg: the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your worships against the same and humbly request that the benefit of the said appeal may be reason to them and that a day may be appointed in the next general Session of the peace to be holden for the said County of Middx for the hearing and determining of the same and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden by the them a deced to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said appeal

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
