To the Worshipfull Her Majties: of the peace for the County of Middx:

The Humble Petetion of Joseph Mills

Sheweth That Yor. Peter: being an Inhabitant in the Parish of St: Giles without Cripple gate is assest to the poores rate at 15: in ye: [...] [---] which is more then other persons of the same abillity is Charged to pay and the officers threatening to [---] make distraint of Yor: Peters: goods

Yor: Peter: therefore Humble: prays that he may be releived as you in Yor: great Wisdom shall Think meet

And Yor: Petr: shall ever pray Etc.
