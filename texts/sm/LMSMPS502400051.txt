Middx ss: To the Rt: Worsppful his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at their Genal Quar [...] Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Botolph wth: out Aldg [...] in the sd: County

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Johnson & D'oyly Michel Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd: County bearing date the 28th: day of October last past, Rebecca the Wife of Paul Char & her four Children (Vizt:) Mary aged about Nine Years Thomas aged about five Years Sarah aged about four Years. & Paul aged about tenn Months the Lawful Issue of the sd: Paul Charr & Rebecca his sd: Wife were removed from the Parish of St: Mary White Chappell to ye Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate as. to the place of their last Legal Sett [...]

That Yor: Petrs: think themselves & humbly Appeals to this Honble: Court against the Same

And pray that Yor: Worspps would be pleas'd to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers. of the poor of the sd: Parish of St: Mary Whiteth Chappell may Attend this Court same day this Pr [...] Session [...] & there to hear & Abide the Determinacon [...] of this Court touching [...] . Appeals

And Yor: Petr: Shall ever pray Etc:
