To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County

Middx

The humble peticon of John Wells of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That at Easter last your Petitr was Chosew to serve the Office of Scavenger for the Upper Liberty of St John Street [...] Parish in the sd County

That your petitr hath Lived but a short time in the said parade and Divers of your petitrs Neighbours who have Lived many Yeares therein and are of farr better abilitys then your petitr: that have not served the said Office And for a Smuth as yor Petitr: being a poor man & having a Sickly Wife to Mainteine Is not able to serve the said Office

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays he may be Discharged from the sd Office and that some fitter person may be Chosen in his Roome

And yor Petitr: shall ever prayer

John Wells
