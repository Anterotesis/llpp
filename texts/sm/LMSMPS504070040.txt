To the Worshipfull His Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Sessions of the peace

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the sd. County

Sheweth That on or about the 24th. day of September last as one Elizth Jones Single woman was Wandring and begging in the said Parish she was Delivered of a Male Bastard Child Whereby she & her said Child became Chargeable to the said parish

That Yor. Petr. did detain the said Woman in Custody until the 29th. day of Novr last when Yor. petr. Conveyed her before George Errington Etgr. one of His Maties Justices of the peace for this County who Examined her and Committed her to the House of Correction at Clerkenwell where she now remains

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray that yor. Worships will be pleas'd to Order the Frear of this County to pay Yor. petrs. such a Sum of money as you shall Adjudge a reasonable Satisfaction for the Charges the said parish has been put unto on Accot. of the said Elizth. Jones & her sd Child

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Jas Bolton [...] for the sd. parish
