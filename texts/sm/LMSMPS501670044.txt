To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Henry Dolman

Sheweth

That your Peticoner exhibited his Peticon unto this Court at the Last Generall Quarter Sessions held Setting forth that last year he Served the Office of Scavenger for the Liberty of Holy well Street in the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in this County And that during the time of his said Office he had Disbursed more then he received the Sum of Eight pounds and. Twelve Shillings And Praying he night be reimbursed the said Money This Court was pleased to referr his Case to the Worshippfull Justice Petfield & Justice. Benson to relieve your Peticoner as to them Should Seeme meete But the said two Justices have not relieved your Peticoner And for as much as Your Peticoner being a poor Man and having a Wife and five Children to Mainteqne stands in great want of the sd Money.

Yor Petr. therefore humbly Prays he may be reimbursed ye: sd Money As to this Court shall Seeme Meete

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc Hen: Dolman
