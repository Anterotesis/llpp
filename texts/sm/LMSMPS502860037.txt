To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices Assembled at the Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace held at Hickes Hall for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Susan Wright Widow

Sheweth That your Peticoner was bound for the Appearance of one Charity Freeman in December Sessions last at Hickes Hall but Freeman not appearing your Peticoners Recognizance is Forfieled

That your Peticoner was altogether Ignorant of the Necessity of Dischargeing the Recognizance untill received the Notice hereunto annexed

That your Peticoner is informed the Fees for dischargeing the Recognizance are Augmented from 2s:8d to 14s:8d care not being taken in time and all through the Ignorance of Your Peticoner

That Your Peticoner is a very Poor woman and hath two Children to provide for So that she is altogether unable to pay the Fees for discharging the Recognizance, and if Estreated she and her Family is utterly ruined

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worships to take her unhappy Case into your Consideracons and remitt her Fees or lessen them whereby her Recognizance may be discharged or otherwise to releive your Peticoner herein as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Peticoner (as in Duty bound) shall ever Pray Etc

I am Creditably Informed that the Contents of this Peticon Is true & therefore recommend it as an Act of Charity & humanity Dat 14th 8ber 1732

Wm Billers
