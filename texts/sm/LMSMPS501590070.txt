To the Right honble his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Church wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Clemts. Danes in the sd County

Sheweth That by Order. undr. the hands & Seales of Sr. Wm Ashurt [...] Mar: Bealing Esqr two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) [...] bearing Date the Eighth Day of [...] December last Robt. Monday with Ann his wife & their Six Children Mary aged 21 yeares Susanna 8 yeares Robt. 7 yres Johanna 4 yres John 3 yeares & Edwd. one yeare were removed [...] from the pish of St. Pancrass in the sd. County to the sd. Pish of St- Clemts. Danes of the place of their last legall Settlemt

That yor Petrs. doubt not but to make it appeare that the sd Robt. Monday wth. his wife & Children hath not any legall Settlemt. in their sd. Pish & humbly appeale from the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices unto yor Worspps

Chimmow Yor. Petrs. Therefore most humbly pray yor Worspps will be pleased to appoint a short day for the Church wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Pancrass to attend yor. Worspps & to abide such Ordr. as shall he made upon hearing of this appeale

And Yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
