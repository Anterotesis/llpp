Middslx To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in this General Session of the Peace (holden by adjournment) assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the Overseers of the poor of the Hamlets of Ham in the Count of Surry

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an order of also warrant under the Hands & Seals of Stephen Cole & W Stechford Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the third day of May instant John Grove & was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Twickenham in the County of Middlesex aforesaid unto the said Hamlet of Ham as the place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or order of the Majestices Justices Do appeal to your Worships therefore and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses & Order that the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Wickenham may appear before you on a certain Day to be appointed, to hear and abide the Judgement & determination of your worships touching this [...] appeal

May 23d:1785

And your Petitioners shall pray

Rd. Tollemach Sollr futhr appellts
