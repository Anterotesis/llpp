To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of the Parish of St. Christopher in the City of London.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of July last past, Elizabeth Hill Widow , was removed from the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Christopher London alledging the said Parish of St. Christopher to be the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Your Peticoner Concerning themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeale to yor. Worps therefore & humbly pray That your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed, & order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in ye County of Middx aforesaid may appear before you at a certain Day by Yor. Worps to be appointed to hear & abide the Judgment and Detrminacon of Yor. worps. touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners & shall pray Etc.
