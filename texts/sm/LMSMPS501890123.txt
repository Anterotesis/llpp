To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Asembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Andrew holborne for the Liberty above the Barrs

Sheweth Ye Ann That by a Pass warrant under the hands & seales of Whitlock Bulstrode N Mason Richard Bulstrode & George Vaughan four of the Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unns) bearing date the Sixteenth day of september last Robert Seacole Anne his wife with Joseph Seacole John Seacole & Mary Seacole their Children were Passed from the Parish of Tuddington to the Parish of St. Andrew holborne above the Barrs as the Place of the last Legall settlement of the said Robert Seacole

Your Peticoners find themselves aggrived by the Judgment of the said Four Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Tuddington & all other Parties concerned here in may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide ther Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Your Peticoners (as in duty bound) shall pray & Etc
