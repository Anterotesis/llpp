To the Right Worspll: their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Gerard Selbey of the Hamlet of Wapping Whitchappell in the sd County.

Sheweth That at Easter last your Peticoner was elected to Serve the office of Scavenger in the said Hamlet for the yeare Ensueing.

That your Peticoner hath been a Lodyer in the dwelling house of one William Great in the said Hamlet about three yeares Past and never was a Housekeeper in the said Hamlet Neither hath there been at any time any parish dutys taxed on your Peticoner Soe that he humbly conceives himselfe not lyable to serve the said office.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from serving the said Office and that the Inhabitants of the said Hamlet may elect some other person to serve the same.

And Yor. Peticoner Shall ever Pray etc.
