To the Rt: Worsppll: his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The humble peticon of William Wigley of the Parish of St Leonard shoreditch in the said County

Sheweth That as of last Sessions of your Peticoner Exhibited his Petition unto this Court Setting forth That he was a very poor man and had lost his Eye sight and had two small Children to mainteyne and could not longer Subsist without relief from the said Parish your Worshipps were pleased to referr his Case to Three of his Matys Justices of the Peace resideing in or near the said Parish Who upon hearing your Petitioner Ordered him a pencon of Twelve pence p Weeke But your peticoner by reason of his Family and Misfortunes cannot Subsist without a further Allowance.

Yor Peticoner therefore humbly prays such an Augmemtacon of his said Pention As to this Court shall be thought meete.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.
