To the Rt Worpl her Mats Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Anthony Buck [...] Constable of South Mims in this County

Sheweth That there is due to your Petioner the Sum of Ten Pounds for passing of Vagrants To Michelmas last past That your Peticoner has very great and Pressing Occassions for money In Consideracon where of

Your Petitioner most humbly prays this Honble Bench wilbee Pleased to order the Payment of the said Sum of Tenn Pounds out of The money Appropriated for that use

And yor Petr as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc
