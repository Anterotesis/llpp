To The Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of Thomas Lynall of the Parish of St Mary White Chapple in the County of Middx Cook.

Sheweth

That Yor. Petr. Rents a House of Twelve Pounds a Yeare in the Said Parish and is Charged Two Shillings and Two pence Pr. Month for and Towards the necessary Releife of the Poor of the Said Parish That yor. Petr. hath a Great Family- to Maintaine and in, Very Low Circumstances and- hath, nothing but what he Getts by his Labour and- Industry whereby he is Intirely unable to Pay the- Said Rate and yor Petr. further Settsforth, that he- Never was Assessed More than One Shilling and- Six Pence P Month, And that Severall of his- Neighbours who Live in Houses of Greater Rents and in better ability do not pay So much as he Now Stands Assessed.

Therefore Yor. Petr. thinks himselfe Aggreived and humbly Appeals- against the Same And prays to be Releived in the Premisses as Yor. Worships Great Wisdom, & Justice Shall Seem Meet.

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc

Thomas Lynall
