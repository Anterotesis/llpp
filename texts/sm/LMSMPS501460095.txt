To the Rt Worspp his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx/

The humble Petition of Hannah Cooper of the Parish of st James Clerkenwell and the sd County

Sheweth That your Petitr: was allowed Six pence & Week as a Pention from the said Parish But for these Three months past the same has been wholly taken away from her

That your Petitr is a very poor and sickly Women and is threatned to be turned out of Doors for Non payment of her Rent and being past her Labour Is not able & Subsist without her sd Pention

your Petitr. therefore Humbly Prays your Worspps will be pleased to Order yr she may be allowed her said Former Pention of Six Pence & Weeke and her Arreares paid her

And yor Petitr Shall Ever pray etc

Hannah Cooper.
