To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex.

Humbley Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrent or order under the hands and [...] Seals of Ant: Wroth. and N. Booth Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 6th day of October 1737 Sarah Jones wife of John Jones was removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. James as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrived

And Therefore humbly Prays that the Court will be pleased to appoint a time for bearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons Conce [...] do Attend as the sometime

And your Petrs. Will Pray Etc

Saml. Seddon} Agent for the said Parish of St James
