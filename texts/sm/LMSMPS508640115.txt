To William Manwairing Esqr. Chairman and others His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled at the Session House for the said County

The Humble Petition of George Williamson

Sheweth That Your Petitioner's last Father was appointed by the Court the Jobbing Smith to the Session House, for the County and the Goals of New Prison and Clerkenwell Bridewell That on the Fourth day of July Instant Your Petitioner's Father died and has left an Aged Widow who is entirely destitute of any way to get her livelihood but what may arise from her late Husbands Business

That your Petitioner has for some Years Past conducted the Business with, and done the different work at the abovementioned Places for his Father

Your Petitioner therefore humbly hopes that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint him your Jobbing Smith in Room of his late Father for the benefit of himself and Mother

And Your Petitioner will ever Pray & George Williamson
