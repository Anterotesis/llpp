Middx Ss To the Rt. Honble: Sr. Edward Clark Kt. Lord Mayor of the City of London & the rest of Justices of [...] & terminr. now Sitting in Sessions for the City of Londo. & County of Middlx

The humble Peticon of Wm. Mathews now a Prysonr in Newgate

Sheweth That on Tuesday last yor, Petr. was Comitted by Isaac Newton Esqr. upon the ann Saconr. of one Hill yor Counter [...] the Current of Coyne of this Realn [...] of wth fact, yor. Petr. is altogether a Stranger to [...] divers or Di [...] will [...] his reputacon

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly Pray that he may be prosecute for the Said Offence this present Sessions, or for want of Such prosecution he may be admitted to Baile

And yor. Peticonr. Shall Pray Etc

The Peticonr of Wm. Mathews
