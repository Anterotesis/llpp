To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of ye peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary le Bone in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seales of Bowater lemon and James Stewart Esqrs two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the twenty first day of December last Charles Disney aged Seventy five Years was Removed from the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County to the said Parish of St Mary le Bone as the place of his last legall Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concern'd may Attend the Same time

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Thos: Gilbert John Lane} Church Wardens
