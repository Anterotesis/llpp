To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Ann Lubonisky

Sheweth

That As of Last Sessions Yor Petr preferred a Bill of Indictmt. against Susanna Nayler for Assaulting Yor Petr And Beating her Which Bill new found against the said Susanna Nayler But She Did not Answer to her Call though She was then in Court Whereupon Yor Worshipps were then pleased to Grant this Honourable Bench Warrant against the sd Susanna Nayler Who by vertue thereof was Apprhendedx taken And Stands bound before the Worshippfull Justice Ellis for her [...] Appearance in this Honourable Court this prent Sessions And forasmuch as She having not as yett Appeared Allthough She hath been Called

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays such Order in the premises from this Honourable Court As unto your Worshipps Shall be thought most meet

And Yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Ann Luboniskey
