To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ickenham in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands and Leales of William Conne & Richard Bealing Esqrs. Two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Eight & Twentieth Day of September last P [...] Jane Groves Wife of Jeffery Groves a Soldier gone on the late Exped [...] & William Groves & Ann [...] Groves his Children were Removed from the Parish of St. Martins in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Ickenham in the County of Middx aforesaid as the Place of their last legall [---] Settlement

Your Petitioners thinkeing themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the said Two Justices Humbly Appeale [...] there from And pray that this Court will be please [...] to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Pe [...] of the said parish of St. Martins in the Feilds to Attend this Court Some Day this Sessions to her and Abide the Judgement and Determinacon this Court touching this Appeale.

And your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
