To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Petition of Margrett Berry

Sheweth That about the Thirtieth Day of January 1706 Your Petrs became an Apprentice to Thomas Butterfield of Wapping in Stepney Parish Cane Chaire maker & Sarah his Wife for the Terme of Six yeares Comensing from the day of the date of the sd Indenture of Apprntice- -hood as thereby may Appeare

That the said Mastere Mr having barbarously Beate and Abused your Petr And not Allowing her common necessarys

He thereupon Stands bound for his Appearance in this Honble Court this present Sessions.

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humble Prays your Worshipps to be pleased to [...] Order that the said Thomas Butterfield Susinte may Appear in this Honourable Court this present Sessions to Shew cause why the sd Apprntice should not be Discharged from her sd Apprentice hood

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
