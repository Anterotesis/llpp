To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Henry Broadhead Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventh Day of June last Susan Woodward Widow of Isaac Woodward with their Two Children Vizt. Thomas Aged near Twelve Years and Dorothy Aged near Ten Years were removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars which by Mistake in the said Order or Pass Warrant is called the Parish of St. George the Martyr) as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Hen: Binfield agent for the Appelts. Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved by the Order of the said Two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court against the same & humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Present Sessions for the hearing and Determining of the same

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc a.
