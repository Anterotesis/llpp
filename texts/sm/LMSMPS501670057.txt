Middx ss To the Right Worshipfull the Justices of the peace att then Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble petition and Appeale of Thomas Sarson of Hounslow in this County Inn keeper

Sheweth

That Yor. Petr. as Charged by an Order Under the hands & Seales of Robt Wallis is and Thomas Butler Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County to be the Reputed father of a Female Bastard Child born of a Body of Mary Apple by Singlewoman in the Parish of St: Margarett Westmr: in this Court [...]

That Yor. petr. Conerives himself Agreived by the Said Order and humbly Appeales to this Court against the Same

Yor Petr therefore humbly prayes yt Gr. Worshipps would be reason to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of Saint Margarett Westmr and all other Psons contained to attend this Court some Day this prsent Quarter Session heare and abide the Judgemt & Determinacon of this Honble Court touching the Sd Appeale

And Yor Petr Etc
