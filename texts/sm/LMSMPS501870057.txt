To the Rt Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx in their general quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripple gate in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of [...] Matthew Hewitt and Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx (Quorne Unns) bearing date the 22d: Day of June last past one Katherine Field was removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the City of London by the name of the Parish of St Giles within Cripplegate in the City of London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petrs conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom And pray That your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the donnty of Midlx may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etca
