To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Petition and Appeale of John Fuller of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middx Esqr

Sheweth

That by the Severall Rates made this present Year for Releife of the Poor of the said Parish and also for defraying the Charges and paying the Wages of the Scavengers Rakers and Other Officers within the said Parish for Carrying away the askes Dirt Fifth and Soil Your petitioner is charged in the said Rates up to the Almost Rent which he pays, notwithstanding many Inhabitants in the said parish are greatly Under Rated, Whereby Your Petitioner thinks himselfe Aggreived and Appeales against the Same unto this Court

And pray's that this Court will be pleased to appoint the same to be heard in this Court at the next Generall Quarter Sessions on Thursday the Elleaventh day of October next and that the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell and also ye be Ordered then to Attend to hear and abide the determination of this Court

And yor Petitioner shall ever pray Etc. John Fuller
