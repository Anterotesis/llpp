To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Staining in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and W Kennard Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifteenth day of June in the Year of our Lord one Thousand seven hundred and ninety nine Edward Morris was removed and conveyed from and out of that Part of the parish of Saint Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allhallows Staining as the place of his last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them And that the bearing and determination thereof may be adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County

W L & S Newman Sollicitor for Appellants}
