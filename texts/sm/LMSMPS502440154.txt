The Humble Petition and Appeals of Thomas Clements agt.

The Parish of St. Giles in the feilds in the County of Middx

Ordr confirmed

Co: both sides M Milner Sr. Danl Dolins Col Dobyns Col Ellis

Mr S. Michell Coll Ellis 5s.

Middx ss Ad General Quarteral Sescon pacis Dni Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd die Lune Scilt tertio die July Anno Regni Dni uri Goergy scdi Dei gra nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc primo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the feilds in this County [---]have Notice of this Appeal and that they & all persons concerned doe Attend this Court on friday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to here & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the said appeal

P Cur Walter
