To the Rt: Worship full his Majesties Justices of the Peace for Middlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assem [...]

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Sepulchre in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant of removall under the hands and Seals of John Kirby and Bastwick Johnson Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx (Quor unus) bearing date the Eighth day of January instant One George Wood was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County to the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County aforesaid as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said George Wood

That your Petr. apprehending themselves agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore your Petr. most humbly prays this Court will be pleased to appoint a day sometime this present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sa [...] Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all other parish concerned may attend this Court to hear and abide the final Arbitrament and Determination of this Court touching the Matter of this Appeal

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
