To the Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Langley in the County of Bucks

Sheweth

That by Order under the hands & Seales of Thomas Rand & John Fuller Esqrs. two of his sd. Matyes Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing date the 23d day of January last One George Green was removed from the pish of St. Giles's without Cripplegate[---] in the sd. County of Middx to the sd. parish of Langley as the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. George Green

That the Parishioners of the sd. pish of Langley humbly apprehend that the sd. pish of Langley is not of right the place of Settlement of the Said Geo: Green & appeal agt the sd ordr

Therefore Pray that yor. Worships wod. be pleased to assigne [---] Some Convenient Day for ye hearing of their Appeale from the sd. Order

And your Petitionrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
