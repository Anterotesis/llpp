To the Prs. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Joseph Treadwell

Sheweth That by an order [---] under the Hands and Seals of Francis Bedwell and Bartholomew Hammond Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the Second day of august last Your Pettr. was adjudged to be the Brapched Father of a Male Bastard Child Com of the Body of Sarah Mackey and Baptized by the name of Joseph which Child is become Chargable to the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County

That your Pettr. by the said Adjudication is ordered to pay or Cause to be paid into the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish for the time being or to Some or one of them the Sum of two Shillings and Six pence by the week weekly and every Week from the Date of the said order for and towards the Keeping Sustentation and Maintainace of the said Child for and during so long a time as the said Male Bastard Child shall Continue to be a Charge on the said Parish

Your Pettrs. apprehending himself agrieved by the said order of the said two Justices of the Peace Humbly Appeals to this Court agt. the Same and Prays that this Court wills [...] a day this Present Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes and all Persons Concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of his Court touching the said appeal

And your Pettrs. Shall ever Pray

Jos: Treadwell
