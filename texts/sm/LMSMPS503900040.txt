To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anne within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Philip Dyot and John Milner Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of October last Elizabeth Floyd Singlewoman was remov'd from the parish of St. Giles in fields in the said County of Middlx to the sd. Parish of St Anne, whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggriev'd

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgment of the said two Justices, and pray Your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the fields and all persons concernd do attend this Court someday in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And of Petitionrs. shall pray Etc Dan:Jones Agent for the Appelts.
