To the Worshipful Justices now assembled in their General Session of the Peace at the Session House in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of P. Colguhoun and R. Davies Esquires, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Midlesex, one where of being of the Quorum bearing date the twenty first day of November last, Bathia Oakley was removed from the Parish of St. James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Dionis Back church as the place of her last legal Settlemnt

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrived by the said Order, Appeal against the same, and pray such Relief as to your Worships shall seen meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc

Jno Tombs Wm Lampkin} Church Wardens

Thos Lancaster Mattw. Gibbs} Overseers
