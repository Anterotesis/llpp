To the Right Worpll. His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & appeale of Thomas Nicholson of the Libty of the Rolls in yr County of Middx

Sheweth That your Peticoner is Nominated & appointed one of the Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty for this present year 1717

That your Peticoner is not house holder therefore humbly conceives that he is not [...] able to Serve the said Offices

Yor: Peticonr there fore humble Prayes that the later Overseers of the poor of the said liberty & all other parties Concerned may be Ordered to Attend the Court Some Day this Sessions to Shew Cause why your Peticonr. should not be Discharged

And yor Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
