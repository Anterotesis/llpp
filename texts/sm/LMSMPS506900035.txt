To the Rt. Worspll. Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Sarah Green of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the sd. County Widow.

Sheweth That yor. Petr. is allowed 2s. P month as a Pencon from the sd. Parish But in Regards your Petr. being a very Poor Woman and haveing Two male Children to Mainteyne and the poor Same Creature not able to gett a Livelyhood for the Mainteynance of them, Soe that she is not able to subsist On such her sd. pencon (the same Only paying her Rent) But that aswell herself as her sd. Two Poor Children must Inevitably Parish unless Releived in the prmisses by this Houble Cort.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Beggs your Worspps to be pleased to Order that she may have such pencon from the sd. Parish towards the better Releife of herselfe & Poor Children as unto this Honble Court shall be thought most meet

and yor. Petr. shall Ever Pray Etc

P Tho. Nash
