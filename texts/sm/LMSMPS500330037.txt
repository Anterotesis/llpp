To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices The humble peticon of Ann Wake

Sheweth

That Whereas your peticoners husband doth from her abscond leaving her being poor & indigent to maintaine her self & Child which she is notable to accomplish she had a Warrant granted her which was served upon her said husband by and Mr Barrow who after sett him at liberty without your peticoner in without releif

Therefore humbly raies the assistance of this Worshipfull Bench

And yor peticoner ad in duty bound shall ever pray etc

discharged

Peticon of Ann [...] of [...] Conss on [...] oath of pets to attend Sat 2 sd

Higgens discharged

ap 6 Wr c M 1694
