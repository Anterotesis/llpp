To the Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middle at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace held at Hicks Hall for the said County

The humble petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parrish of St John of Wappin in the County of Middlx aforesaid

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands and seals of Whit Bulstrode and Alexander Ward Esqrs. Two of her Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx bearing date the 16th. day. of March 1713 one Joseph Carr was removed from the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden & Ely rents in the parrish of St: Andrew Holborne in this County to the said parrish of St John of Wappin, and your Petitionrs. think themselves to be aggreived thereby

Bass Therefore humbly pray yor. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint some day this present Quarter Sessions for the hearing and determining the said appeal as to yor Worshipps shall seem must

And Shall pray Etc
