To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Alban in Wood-Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of the Right Honourable the Lord Carpenter and Francis Burton Esquire Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing Date the Sixth day of July instant Susanna Coxon Single [...] Woman was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Alban in Wood- Street in the City of London as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Susanna Coxon

Your Petitioner finding themselves aggreived by the said Warrant Order of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court will appoint some Day in this present Sessions for the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone and all other Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Edwd Benton junr. [...] ler. for Apellts }
