To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Session House for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of James Croft and G.S. Bradshaw Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th. day of July last Benjamin Cooper and Frances his Wife were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the said Liberty and County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Jas. Young Geo. Smith} Church Wardens

John Nalder Thos. Read} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne Westminster
