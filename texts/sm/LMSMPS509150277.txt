To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petitioner and appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of North Muskham in the County of Nottingham

Sheweth

That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of John Scott and T: Were esquired Two of his a Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of August last, Rachael Archer wife of Joseph Archer a Soldier together with her four Children Vizt Mary aged upwards of Twelve Years Joseph (aged upwards of Eight Years Ann aged upwards of Four Years and Elizabeth aged upwards of six Months were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the said county of Middlesex to the said Parish of North a Muskham as the place of their legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggreived by the said order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal May be heard His Present Session and that a Time may to appointed for the the hearing and determining there of

Jos Benj Smith Sollicitor for the appellants
