To the Rt: Worshipfull His Mats: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of Peter Creswell Master or Governor of the house of Correction at Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petitioner upon viewing the want of repairs in the said house of Correction doe fined that the Roof of the Prisoners Wards and the Tyling over the gateway into the said house as also the Roof of his dwelling house is very much out of Repair and may be very detrimentall to him in regard of the Prisoners comitted to this Charge. And further that the Surveyor of the Quakers Work house having informed your Petitioner that the Party Wall between ye said Quakers work house and the said house of Correction would soon fall down whereby the life of some of his Prisoners and your Petr. and family may be in great danger if the said Wall be not speedily Repaired with a Butress or some other Strength to the part of it next the Work shop in the said house of Correction and also that the sd. Wall had sunk into the ground above five inches within this two Years past.

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays this Court Grant an Order for the speedy repair of the Prmisses above mentioned in such manner as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
