To the Right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Loughton in the County of Essex.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order dated the tenth Day of March last under the hands and Seals of John Oakey and Alexander Garrett Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx (Qoorum Unus) One Edward Hemmings (a poor impotent person) was lately removed from the Hamblet of Bethnall Green in the said County of Middlx to the said parish of Loughton in the said County of Essex as- the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Edward Hemmings

That Yor peticoners find themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court against the same

Thos Elmes Agent for the Appellants Yor Peticoners and Appellants therefore humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions for the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Bethnall Green and all other persons Concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon thereof upon & touching the said Appeal And that the Appellants may be releived herein according to Law and Justice

And Yor Peticoners shall pray Etc
