To the Right Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace forthe County of Midds

The humble Peticon of James Johnson and James Lovett

Sheweth That in some time past yors. Petrs. became baile before the Worspll. Justices Perryman for the appearance of one Jane Brilionson in this Honble Court this present Sessions for assaulting one Daniell Bushey As by the Said Recognisance may appear. That the Said Jane Brilionson is Since become Lunatick So that the cannot appear in discharge of this said Recognisance And the sd John Bushey having agreed the Said matter with the Sd Jane Brilionson wth. your Petrs. are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court by Magdalen Johnson & Mathew Boucher upon their Severall Oaths.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to grant that they may be discharged from the Said Recognizance

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
