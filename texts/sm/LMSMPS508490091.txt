To the Worshipful His Majestis's Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petion and Appeal of the Churchwarens and overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Peter in the Town of Derby

Sheweth

That by an order under the hands and seals of N. Conant and J. Barnfather Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th day of June 1789 Mary Swindall was removed and Conveyed from the parish of St. Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Peter in the Town of Derby as the place other last legal settlement whereby your petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray they this Court will please to appoint a Time for hearing and determining the said appeal and that all persons concerned may them Attend

And your Petitions shall ever pray Etc.
