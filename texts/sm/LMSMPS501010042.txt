To the Worpll. the Chairman and the rest of the Justices of the peace at the Sessions of Peace at Hicks Hall for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon of John Hooper. of St. Saviours Southwarke[---] Sawyer.

Sheweth That Thomas Ward of St. Andrew Holbourn in the County of Middx Sawyer being and indebted to your petr. one pound Sixteen Shillings and six pence for sawyerd wages And refuseing to pay the same Your petr. obtained Mr. Justice Iretons wart. agt. him And the sd Thomas Ward being brought before the sd Justice [...] thereupon he was bound over to appear before your Worps. this Sessions to answer the Complt. of your petr. touching the prmissed.

Now forasmuch as the sd Thomas Ward had given out in Speeches that he will not appear but Suffer his Recognizance and to become forfeited And your petr. wanting his money very much Humbly prays yor. Worps. Order That the sd Thomas Ward may appear psuant to the said Recognizance your petr being disabled from working by reason of lamenesse on his right hand and haveing Fue small Children to maintaine

And your petr. shall ever pray Etc.
