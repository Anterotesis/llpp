To the right worshipfull his maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church: Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows Lombard Street in the City of London

Sheweth that by vertue of a Warrant under the hands & seals of two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quoh) bearing date the fourth day of June last past an Infant therein called by the name of Anne Gearing aged about nine or tenn months was removed from the Parish of St Gyles Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Alhallows Lombard street in the City of London alleadging the said parish is the place of the last legal Setlement of John Gearing who in the said Warrant is called the father of the said Anne;

That yor. Peticonr. doubt not but to prove that the said Anne had no legal Settlement in the said parish of Alhallows Lombard Street in the City of London, whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices, And doe humbly Appeale to your Worships there from;

yor. Peticonr. therefore humbly pray yor. Worshipp will be pleased to hear them in the Dr. Misses and order the Church: Wardens & Overseers of the Poor the sd. parish of st Gyles Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex to appear here before you & hear abide the determination of yor. Worships. touching this Appeale.

And yor. Peticoners shall over Pray etc. Charles Page Church Warden
