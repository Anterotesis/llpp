To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticonr of Claudius La Frong That some time pass your Petr preferred a Bill of Indictmt. in this Honble Court against John Green for Receiving certaine Goods which were purleyned and taken from your Petr: by his Apprntice

That the sd Greene gave notice to your Petr that he would Appear this Morning att Nine of the Clock in this Honble Court and plead guilty to the sd. Indictmt.

That the sd. Greene hath this Morning appeared but is gone and forasmuch as your Petr: with his Wittnesses haveing Attended this Honble Court all this Morning.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Grant that the Recognizance of the sd. John Green may be EStreated Gr that your Petr. may have fresh notice It your Worspps Soe thinke fitt.

And Yor. Pet. shall Ever pray Etc.

Claudius [x] La Frong His wife
