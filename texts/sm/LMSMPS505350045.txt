To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of saints Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order dated the 28th. day of February now last past under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot Esqr. and John Cox Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) Mary Cryer Widow of Edward Cryer deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London as the Place of her last & legall Settlements

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices, Do therefore Humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such Releif in the Promises as to this Court shall Seem meet

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Jno. Fisher Attorney for the Appellants
