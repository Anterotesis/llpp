To the Worshipfull Chairmen of the worthy Bench of Justices Att Hicks Hall

The humble petition of Henry Tempest of St: Giles in the Fields Sheweth that yr. Petitiner Paid into the Hands of Mr: Wilkerson The Sum of Nine Shillings and Four pence for Drawing the Recogniances of Bridget the Wife of James Brown the first Day of last Sessions and left her a Release in Court Expecting to have the Recognian ces Drawn which Money was paid For Drawing the said Recogniances Hopeing that that Your Worships will take into Consideration to have Your poor Petitener heard and he As in Duty Bound shall Ever pray

Henry Tempest
