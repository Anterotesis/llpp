To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Child of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said Countys

Sheweth That your Petitioner is Over rated in the poors rate of the said Parish for the house he Dwells in and alsoe for Stock or personall Estate Wherefore he appeals against the same

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays such Relief in the prmises as to this Court shall be thoughts Meete.

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

Wm. Child.
