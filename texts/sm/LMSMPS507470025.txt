To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows the great in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of J. Girdler and W: Gregson Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty seventh Day of November last Ann Gee otherwise Ann Tyce was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Allallows the great as the place of her last Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that the benefit of this appeal may be saved to the Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County

And your Petitr: Shall Pray Etc

Ewd. Wagor Saml Clark.} Church Wardns

Thyad Bitt James Saunder} & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Allhallow, the great
