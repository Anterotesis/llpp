To the Rt: Worship full His Mats Justices of the peace for the County of middlx in their Genl: Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of William Watkins of the parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That your Petitioner Discharged much more to the poor and Scavengers rate in the Said parish for his house he lives in and other his Tenements in Clerkenwell Close then what he ought to pay therefore thinks himself agrieved and humbly Appeals to this Court

The Premisses considered your petr therefore humbly prays such releif as this Court Shall Seem Meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

Wm: Watkins
