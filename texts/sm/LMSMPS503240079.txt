To the Right worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in General Quater Sessions Assembled

The Humble petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant Under the hands & Seals of Peter Guerin Esqr. & Val: Hilder Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for ye sd. County of Middx one whereof is of the Quorum bearing Date the 10th day of May 1736 In the tenth Year of his prsent Majestys Reign One Eliz: Lee widow was removed from & out of ye Pish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the Libty of westm. in ye County of Middx into the sd. Pish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London as the place of her Last Legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Conceiving them- selves Aggreived by ye sd. Order or pass Warrant Do Appeale Agt. ye Same & pray that ys. Honrble Court will Please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of ye sd. Pish of St. Paul Covt. Garden and all parties Conceived to appear Some day at this prsent Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgmt. & determination of this Court touching ye said Appeale
