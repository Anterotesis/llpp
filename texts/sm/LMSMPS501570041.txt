To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Gutteridge

Sheweth That your Peticoner became bail for the Appearance of John Hughes in this Court as of July Sessions last [...]

That the said John Hughes did attend accordingly. and was Called & paid his Fees as Oath is now ready to be made But your Petitioner is Informed by Mistake the Recoginzance was not Discharged not being Marked in the Clorke of the Peaces Booke and is now. likely to be estreated

Yor Petr. therefore humbly prays such Relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought Meete

And Yor Petitr shall Ever pray Etc Jon Gutteridge
