To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their general quarter session Assembled

The humble peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Walter Berry and Richard Ricards Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace of the said County (one being of the quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of September last Anna Maria Humston wife of Hugh Humston was removed from the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County to the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate as the place of her last legal Settlement.

James Sare Sollr. for the parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate } Your Petrs. conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said pass Warrant or order of the said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court will appoint a Day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Paul Shadwell and all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the judgment and Determinacon of this court touching the said appeal

And yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
