To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx. in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble peticon of John Oder one of the Overseers of the poor of the parish of Chelsea in this County

Sheweth

That yr. Petr. with one Walsingham Heathfeild was appointed Overseer of the poor of the said parish of Chelsea and hath acted as Such above and by himself the Said Heathfeild having purchased a Certificate Exempting persons from Serving Porchial Offices) Since Easter Last

That yor. Petr. hath ever Since paid the poor of the said parish who are very Numerous and hath Expended the Same of Vinety pounds and upwards towards their necessary releife

That the Vestry have given and allowed to ye. petr. Rate to collect his Said Expended money but the Justices of the said parish on Appliacon made to them by yor petr. have and doe refuse to Confirm the Same.

Therefore Ye. petr. humbly prayes the Direccons of this Court in the prmises

And yor. Petr Etc.

John Oder
