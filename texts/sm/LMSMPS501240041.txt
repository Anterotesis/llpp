To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justies of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Frances Bolton a Prisoner in Newprison

Sheweth That your Peticonr was this prsent Sessions Fr [...] Forty shillings for Assaulting Rutter which he is not able to pay xth in a imserable condition And lyes [...] the Boords & must perish If this Court have not Compassion [...] him

Your Peticont humbly prays that this Court will [...] gate the said Fine & your Peticonr will ever pray Etc

Franses Boallcon
