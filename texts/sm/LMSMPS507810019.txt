To the worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order [...] the Hands and pass of William Hyde and Thomas Bullard two of [...] Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County of [...] one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 19 Day of March [...] John Thompson and Sarah his Child by Ann his late Wife deceased [...] removed and Countyed from and out of the Parish of [...] in the Liberty of Westminster in the said [...] Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place [...] Aged Settlement of the said John Thompson and his said Child

Your Petitioner [...] aggreived by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court Against the same And Pray your worships will appoint a Day in this Present session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

S Malbon Sollr.

for the said Parish of Shadwell
