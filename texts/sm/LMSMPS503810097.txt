To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County St Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition & appeal of the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of the parish of Christ Church in the Said County

Sheweth

That by Warrant or order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Quarrill and N Dukinfield Esquiers Two of this Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the quorum) bearing Date the Twelfth day of august Last past Whereby Mary Clements Wife of Thos. Beason Clements & their Child Named Joseph aged about Three years was removed from the parish of St. Mary White Chapple. in the Said County to the parish of Christ Church Middlesex as the place of their Last Legal Settlement

your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the Warrant or order of the Said Two Justices of the peace do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Same praying Such relief in the premisses as to this Court Shall Seem Meet

and your petitioners Shall ever pray Etc,

Wm. Lane Lewis Gilbert} Church Wardens

Claud Croque Joseph House} Overseers the poor
