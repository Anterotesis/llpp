To the Right Worpll: her Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble. Peticon & appeale of John Smith of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegat in the St. County Weaver

Sheweth That yor Petr is rated 2s.6d. per Quarter towards the releif of the poore of the St parish hath a wife & 2d Children to maintane and yor Petr is a weabe & Sickly man & a Small Trade and hath not Whare with to pay the sd rate by reason of said poverty

Therefore humbly prayes yor Worps to be discharged from paying the sd Assement

And yor Petr shall pray Etc

John Smith
