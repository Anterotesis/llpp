May ye 13/1730

Two the Honourable Bench

The Humble Pertition of Richard Evans now Confind. In Bridewell humble Sheweth that your poor Pertitinor is in a St [...] vin Condition and Same in all his Limbs and not able two Get his bred whereof was obliged Two Go too the Sade >Parish of hackney for Relife haveing All his Affects Ceas'd upon and went to the Churchwarden To ask Relife whereof was sent to Bridewell and since Discharged and has not hat Bred Since Last Friday and Begs that the Honourable Bench too [...] set at Liberty Your poor Pertitiner who is in Deauty Bound to Pray

Richard Evans
