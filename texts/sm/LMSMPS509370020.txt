To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of N Conant and John Scott two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the the 29th day of April last past George Tanner was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said George Tanner

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a day in the present Session for the Hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Etc

Saml. Malbon Sollr for St. Paul Shadwell
