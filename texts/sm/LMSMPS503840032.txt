To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margaret Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of James Fraser & Daniel Gach Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing Date the 28th. day of January 1747. Mary Absworthy Widow was Removed from the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the Liberty of Westminster to the Parish of St Margaret Westmr: aforesaid as the place of her Last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggreived & Appeal to this Court against the James

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray this Court will please, to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal & that all Persons Concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitrs Shall ever Pray Etc.

I Feary Agent for St. Margts:

Middlesex At the Genll Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid on Monday the 22d day of [...] February in the 21st. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain Etc.

It is Ordered by this Court That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St [...] Martin in the Feilds in the Liberty of Westmr: in this County have Notice of this Appeal And that they and all Persons Concerned Do Attend this Court on Thursday next at ten the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

By the Court Walter
