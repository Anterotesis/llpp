To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westmr in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Aaron Lamb and Benjamin Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. bearing date the 14th. day of August 1762 Esther Bocky Spinster aged about 35 Years was Removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. Martin in the fields within the said Liberty and County as the place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Your Petrs. humbly pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal.

And your Petrs. will pray Etc

Wm Croft Sollr. for the parish of St. Martin in the field
