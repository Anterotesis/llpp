To the Right Worpll His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. James Dukes Place London

Sheweth That one Mary Lewis the wife of Isaac Lewis was lately passed by Warrant under the Hands & Seals of Robert Kirby & Bastwick Johnson Esqrs two of His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid (Quogmms) bearing date the second day of August last past from the parish of St Paul Shadwell in the said County to the said parish of St James Dukes Place London as the place of the last Legall settlent of the said Isaac [...] Lewis

Your Peticonrs thinke themselves agrieved wth. the Judgmt of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court & pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Paul Shadwell & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court upon & touching this Appeal

P J Tyton

And yor. Peticonr: Shall ever pray Etc
