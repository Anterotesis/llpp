To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant on Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Henry Feilding and Gabriel [...] Fouace two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City & Liberty bearing Date the fifteenth day of May 1751 Mary Clarke Spinster was removed from the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Libty of Westmr: in this County to the said Parish of Saint Margaret Westmr. as the place of her last Legal Settlement Whereby Your Petrs: think themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same

Crofts Attorney for Appellts: Your Petitr. therefore Humbly Pray, that this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Parsons concern'd day then attend.

And Your Petitr: Shall ever Pray Etc
