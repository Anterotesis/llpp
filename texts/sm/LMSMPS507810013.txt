To His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace [...]

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of West Drayton in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands one Seals of Henry Lambe and Thomas Gilbert of [...] her of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said [...] one being of the Quorum bearing date the 22d day of [...] last Ann Youd Singlewomen was removed and [...] from and out of the Parish of Fulham in the said county to the said Parish of West Drayton in the said [...] as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your [...] themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal to this Honourable Court against the said Order of the said two Justices and pray that a day may be appointed for the hearing thereof and that all persons concerned may be Ordered to attend at that time

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray
