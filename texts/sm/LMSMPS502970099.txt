To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices [...] of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County

Sheweth That By Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and [...] of Richard Ricards & Andrew Osborn Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Whereof are [...] of the Quorum) Eleanor the Wife of Robert Wenwood and her two Children that is to say Joseph aged about four Years And James aged about two Years the Lawfull Issue of the said Robert We moved & Eleanor his Wife Were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the sand County, to the parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County, As the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggreived by the Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Honble Court for Releif in the premisses.

And Your Peticoners shall Ever pray and so forth
