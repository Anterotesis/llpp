To the Worshipfull his Majesty's justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square with in the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order and or the Hands and Seals of James Fielding and George Wright Esquires two of his Majestys of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing date the 31st. Day of October 1770 Elizabeth Williams Wife of Richard Williams if living now gone from her and their three Children, to wit Catharine aged Eleven Years Mary aged five Years and John aged Eighteen months were removed from the Parish of St. James with in the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty and County as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing & determining the said appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend

Wm :Dickins George Shakespear James Showell} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
