To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Petition of Henry Arrundle and James Walch Constables of New Brentford in the Said County and William Pereivill Head borough for New Brent ford aforesaid for the Year 1747

Humbly Sheweth That Whereas James Clitherow on Esqr. Lord of the Manor of Boston Cum Brentford wherein New Brentford Aforesaid doth lie hath not lately Held a Court Leet in his said Manor and is not likely Speedily to Hold one whereby Constables and Head boroughs May be Chosen in their Room's and Whereas the said Petitioners have Served the Said Offices for the said Year last past Do Humbly Pray that they may be Discharged from their Said Offices and Do Return the Persons Named in the Lists Annexed as fitt and proper persons to Succeed them in their Said Offices

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray
