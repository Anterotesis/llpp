To the Right Worsppll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon of William Turnor

Sheweth

That Yor. Petr. did Serve the office of Churchwarden for the parish of St John Wapping in the Said County for the yeare last past.

That year Worspps were the last Sessions pleased to order yor. Petr. to [---] Pay [---] the Sume of £34.15s.0d [---] to Thomas Fryer late Churchwarden of the Said parish towards his reimbursment.

That yor. Prs. accompts relating to the said office are not as yet audited but [...] he computes there wilbe [...] the Sume of [...] fifty pounds & upwards due to him from the Said parish besides the said Sume of £34.15s.0d.

P Sweffley Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to order that his said [...] Accompts may be speedily audited and that the Churchwarden Overseers of the poore & other theife Inhabitants of the Said parish may forthwith make a Rate within the said parish whereby to reimburss your Petr. such moneys as shall appeare to be due to him at the foot of his accompt.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Will Turner
