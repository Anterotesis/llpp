To the Worshipfull William Manwairing Esqr and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petion and Appeal The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of removal bearing date the Seventeenth day of July one thousand seven hundred and eighty Nine under the hands and Seals of Thomas Cogan Esquire and Charles Trequet Esquire Catherine Lawson Singlewoman hath been removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex into the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldersgate aforesaid as to the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive and Apprehence that the last legal Settlement of the saw Catherine Lawson is not in their sd Parish, And therefore conceive themselves aggrieved last the said Order And humbly appeal against the Same
