To the Right Worspl. the Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Judith Warden

Sheweth

That some time past One Anne Carter upon Complaint made by her Husband before the Worspll. Justice Perry was comitted to New Prison where she now remaine That yor. Petr. hath been soe much beaten & wounded by the said Anne Carter Insenmuch that her life was much dispaired of the haveing been under the hands of Chirmegeon for some time past the Charge of which will amount to a considerable sum of money And forasmuch as your Petr. being very poore and her husband in his Maties Service in Flanders And fearing that in case the sd Anne Carter should this Sessions be discharged from her Imprisenmt. she would beate & abuse your Petr. as she hath done heretofore.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor Worspp to consider the Charge she must susteyne towards persenting her Curre And to be pleased to Grant that the sd Anne Carter may be continued in New Prison aforesd untill wet Sessions

And yor. Pr. shall ever Pray Etc

Judith P Walden her [x] mke
