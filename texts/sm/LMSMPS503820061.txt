To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session at Hicks Hall assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Anne Within the Libty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Ludbey and Wm. Pickering Esqrs. two of his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libty (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th. day of November last Mary Burroughs Widow was removed from the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Westmr, in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Anne whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrievd

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgment of the said two Justices and Pray your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James and all Persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yr. Petitionrs. shall pray Etc Dan Jones Agent for the Appellts.
