Middx ss. To the Rt Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlex.

Humbly Sheweth The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseer of the poor of the parish of St. Clement Danes in the County aforesaid.

That Elizabeth Watton Singlewoman was by Warrant order the hands and Seales of German Ireton and Joseph Offley, Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace of this County removed from the Rolls liberty on the psh of St Dunstand in the West London in this County to the parish of St. Clements Danes aforesaid alleadging the sd Elizabeth Watson was last legally setled therein, where about two years abd halfe since was a hired yearly servant to on. Mr. Harper wth. whom she continued alt a year.
