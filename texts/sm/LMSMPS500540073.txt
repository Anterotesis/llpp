To the Rt Honble: and Rt. Worppll his Majties Justices of the peace Sitting on the bench att Hickshall in St John street Etc.

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney.

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe. By an order under the hands & Seales of John Webber & Robert Bateman Esqr two of his Majties Justices of the peace of this County have lately Sent to us one Richard Thurlewind a distracted person, aledging that hee is a settled Inhabitant of our Said Hamlett, whereas in truth hee never had any legall Settlement there.

Your Peticons therefore most humbly Pray That you will be pleased to Order the sd Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of Ratcliffe afforesd to appeare here, And shew cause why they Shall not abide the Judgment of this Honorble: Court touching the Settlement of the said Richard Thurlewind.

And yor. Peticons shall ever Pray Etc Richard Martin chwarden

This writen by John Mousleh Clark to the townd
