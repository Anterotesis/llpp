To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue [...] Warrt. under the hands & Seals of John Ellis & Oliver Lambert Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing Date the 25th. day of March last Ann Jennings Widow of John Jennings and her Twins David and Thomas Aged about three Quarter of a Year was removed from and out of the parish of St. James Westmr. in the said County to the Said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petrs. Thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgement of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal to your Worsps. against the same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worships will please to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James Westmr. may attend this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc.
