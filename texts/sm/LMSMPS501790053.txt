To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St: Andrew Hubbard in the Citty of London.

Sheweth That Value of A Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Bastwick Johnson and Robert Kirby Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorm unus) bearing date the 22d: day of Aprill last Past Mary Lee a Singlewoman was removed from the Hamblett of Wapping Stepney in the Parish of St: Dunstan Stepney in the Said County unto the Said Parish of St: Andrew Hubbard in the Citty of London as the Place of her last legall Settlement.

That Your Petrs: Conceiveing themselves Aggreived by the Said Warrant and Order of the Said two Justices do Appeal to your worshipps therefrom And humbly pray Your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the Prmisses And Order that ye Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Said Hamblett of Wapping Stepney may Appeare before You and hear and Abide the Judgment and determinacon of Your Worpps touching this Appeal.

And they Shall ever Pray Etc.
