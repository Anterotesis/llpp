To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dudley in the County of Worcester

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Tho: Quarrill and Rob Pell two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 19th: day of October 1759. Elizabeth Broynton aged about seven Years and Alice Broynton aged about four Years Children of Thomas Broynton and Anne his Wife then both gone away from their sd Children were removed from the Parish of St Mary White Chapel unto the said Parish of Dudley in the said County of Worcester that being adjudged by the said Justices to be the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Thomas Broynton and Anne his Wife and their said Children.

That your Petitioners concerning themselves agreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said Two Justices Do therefore appeal to Your Worships therefrom And humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the Premisses, And order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex may appear before You at a Certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc.

Wittness [...] Sixty.

3 Church Wardens Ferdinando Cardall

Overseers{ Benjn Price Daniel Pitt Wm. Geast Joseph Bond
