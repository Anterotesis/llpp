To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their general Session of the Peace for the sd: County assembled the Humble Petr. & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pots of St. Mary in the Town & County of Southampton

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hand & Seals of John Floud & Daniel Williams Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 3d: day of July 1797 Mary Bolton Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from & out of the Psh of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the sd: Cor.. of Middx to the Psh of St.. Mary in the Town & Cor.. of Southampton afsd: as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petr: conceive themselves aggrieved

Yr.. Petrs.. therefore humbly pray that the benefit of their sd: Appeal may be reserved to them And that a Day may be appointed in the next general Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the sd.. Cor.. of Middx for the hearing and determining the sd: Appeal And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd: Psh of St.. Leonard Shoreditch be then & there required to attend to hear & abide the Judmt. & Determine of the Cot: toudsy. the sd: Appeal

And Yr. Petrs.. shall ever pray Etc

Duill & Matthews Solrs.. for the Appellants
