To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and seals of Sir Robert Taylor Knight and James Croft Esquire two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fifth day of August last Hannah Casey singlewoman and her female Bastard Child namely Elizabeth aged seven weeks were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Casey and her said Child whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

And your Petrs. will pray Etc

Herbt. Robertson Solr. for the Appellts.
