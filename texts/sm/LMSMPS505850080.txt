To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace as Hicks Hall assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden: and Overseer: of the Poor of the Parish of Stow Bardolph in the County of Norfolk

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order Under the hands and Seals of George Garrett and Richard Hare Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the sixteenth day of March last Past Martha Carter in the said Warrant or Order Called Martha Carter Widow of John Carter in the said Warrant or Order named Was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of New Bardolph by the Name and Description of the Parish of Stowe in the said County of Norfolk as the Place of the last legall Settlement of the said Martha Carter in the said Warrant of order named

That your Petitioner Apprehend themselves Aggrieved by the said Warranter Order of Removal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to the said Court Against the same and Pray that this Court Will Appoint some day in this Present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint John at Hackney and all other Persons Concerned to Attend the Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc

Francis Rising} Churchwarden for the said Parish of Stow Bardolph

The Mark of [x] John Hall} Overseer of the poor of the said Parish
