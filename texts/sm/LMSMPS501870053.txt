To the Right Worshipfull his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of the Chappell Warden and Overseer of the Poor of the Precinct of St John Baplist in the Savoy in this County.

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seales of Thomas Railton and Francis Sorell Esqrs two of his Matys Justices of the Pease for the City and Liberty of Westmr. dated the Third Day of June last one Margaret Camron an Infant Aged about Seven Months was removed and Conveyed by the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr in this County to Yor Petrs by the Name of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Strand als Savoy in the Said County

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Conceive themselves Agreived by the Said Order and humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same and pray Your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint a Day for all Partyes Concerned to Attend and Abide the Judgmt of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs shall pray Etc
