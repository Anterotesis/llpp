To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of James Esdaile and Benjamin Cowley Esquire, two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of September last ann Nash Wife of Joseph Nash Peruke maker ( who is run away and left her and not to be bound) and their Child Joseph aged eighteen Months and upwards were removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn above the Barrs in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Anne as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Joseph Nash Ann his Wife and Joseph their Child whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and against the said Pass Warrant or Order do Appeal to this Court.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that Your Worships will be Pleased to appoint some day in the Present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order that the ChurchWarden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitrs; Shall pray Etc

B Wood Saml Agar Thos Brooks Richd craser} ChurchWardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Anne
