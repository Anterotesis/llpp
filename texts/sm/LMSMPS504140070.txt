To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Session Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Bisham in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the Hand and seal of Philip Dyot Esqr. One of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of July last Mary Hester Widow of west Hester deced (with Sarah their Child Aged Two Years) who was apprehended in the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx as a Vagabond Vizt. wandring and begging there [...] was removed and conveyed from the said parish of St. Giles in the Fields to the parish of Bisham called in the said Warrant or Order Bissum in the County of Berks as the place of the last legal Settlemt. of the said Mary Hester and her said Child

That Your petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the aforesaid warrant or order of the said Philip Dyot Esqr. and humbly Appeal against the same

Your petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of the said appeal may be saved unto them and that a day may be appointed in this present Quarter Session of the peace to hear and Determine the Same and to grant Your petitioners Such other relief in the premisses As to your worships Shall Seem meet

And your petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Hely Attorney for the Appellants
