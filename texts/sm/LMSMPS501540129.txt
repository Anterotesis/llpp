To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Davison, John Feilding, Richd. Cole William Welstead, Thomas Clark, Ann Emerton Joseph Prince, & Mary Burton

Sheweth That yor. Peticoners are severally Rated and Assess'd towards the relesfe of the poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the sd. County for the year ensuing

That Your petrs. are all in general very poor and live in low Rents and are not able to pay their severall and respective Rates So that they think themselves very much aggreiv'd by thereby

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may severally be releiv'd in the sd premisses

And yor. petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
