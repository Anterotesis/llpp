To the Worfull his Maties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblett of Mile End New Town in the pish of Stepney

Sheweth

That Thomas Smith with Joseph and Mary Smith his Children were by Warrt under the hands and Seales of John Tully Esqr and Phillip Howard Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace of this County past from the pish of St James in the Liberty of Westmr to the said Hamblett of Mile end New Town alledgeing that to be the place of his last legall settlement

Yor Petrs therefore finding themselves agreived [---] humbly appeal from the said Warrt and desire a day to be heard and that all parties concerned may then attend to abide by the determination of this [...] Court touching the same

And yor Petrs Shall ever pray Etc

Aroon
