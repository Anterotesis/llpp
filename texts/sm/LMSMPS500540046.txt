To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Over Seers of the poor of the Parish of St. Olave Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That on the Second of Novembr last was passed by Virtue of a a Warrant under the hands and Seales of Sr John Elwes Knt. and Francis Regus Esqr. two of his Mates: Justices of the peace for the County of Midds from the parris of St Martins in the Feilds in the said County into [...] Parish of St. Olaves Southwark aforesd, Ralph Scott and Richard Scott two of the Children of Ralph Scott, a Brewer Servt.

Yor. Peticoners think themselves agreeived with the Judgment of the said two Justices And Doe Appeale to this Cort And humbly pray the Prmises considered that the Over Seers of the poor of the Parish of St Martins in the Feilds in the County And all other Parties concerned may askend this Cort Some Day this Sessions to heare & abode the finall end determinacon of this Cort. touching this Appeale

And yor. Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc

Humfrus Elwes [...]

Church wardens of St Olaves {Richard Parkins John Roberts

The Peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens of St. Olave South wark

10 Decembr 97
