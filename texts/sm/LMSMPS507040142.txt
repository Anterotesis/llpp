To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrt. under the Hands & Seals of John Lane & George Wrighte Esqrs, Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in & for the County, of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th day of September last, Robert Willett a Male Bastard Child Aged Nine Months was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Mary Le bone in the said County to the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitrs. thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrt. of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and Humble pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day in this present General Quarter Session to hear and determine the sd. Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Wade Junr. Atty. for the Appellts
