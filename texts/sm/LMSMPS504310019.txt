To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of [...] John Lewis

Sheweth That your Petitioner is committed to New Prison charged with carrying Fire Arms contrary to the Statute

Yor. Petitioner therefore humbly prays that he may be tried bailed or discharged at this present Session for the said Supposed Offence for which he is committed as aforesaid.

And your Petr. shall ever pray

John [...] Lewis
