To the Rt. Worshipful His Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter session by Adjournment Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Maryle Bone in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass waremnt or [...] Order under the Lands and Seals of Thomas Lediard and Richard Manley Esqrs. Two of this majesties Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminister (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 22d. Day of December 1753 Katharine Lucas, Widow of David Lucas, Deced and their thee Children (namely) Isaiah aged Ten years Anne aged Seven years and half and Mary aged three years were removed from the parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster to the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of middlesex as the Place of their last legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court aganist of the same

Your Petitioners therefore Here by may that the benefit of the said appeal may be saved unto then and that hearing and determining thereof May be adjourned until the next general Quarter Session of the Peace to be hold for this County.

And your Petitioners shall every Prayer.

Tho:Huddle Peter Holmes} Church wardens
