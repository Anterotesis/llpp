To the Right Worpl the Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of George Collison

Sheweth That in Some time past your Peticoners tooke Apprentice William Chandler And Soon after the said william Chandler became Apprentice [...] your Peticoner as aforesaid your Peticoner was informed that was bound to another Master.

your Peticoner therefore most humbly beseecheth your Worpps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged of the said William Chandler soe that he may not be oblerdged to come under any trouble by his former Master

And yr. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
