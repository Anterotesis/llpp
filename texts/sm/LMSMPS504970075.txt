To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Isleworth in the sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Derbyshire Birkheadand James Clitherow Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 28th. day of February last James Belworth Margaret his wife, and his five Children (to wit) John aged ten Years, Sarah Eight or nine Years, Ann Seven Years, Margaret Six Years, and James about two Years were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Heston in the said County to the said Parish of Isleworth as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the sd. two Justices of the Peace appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining the same

Humphy Elmes Atty for the Appellts.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray
