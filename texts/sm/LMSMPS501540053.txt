To the right Worp. his Majties. Justices of the peace of the County of Middx.

The humble petition of Simon Barkwill of the parish of St James Clerkenwell

Sheweth That yor. Petr. being almost Sixty & five years old and past his labour was by reason of his poverty formerly discharged from paying to the poor rate, yet notwithstanding he is reduced to great Straights is now rated to pay towards the relief of the poor of the parish aforesd. and being Bursten & deafe and much afflicted with paines & other distempers and helpless is altogether unable to pay the Same

Therefore the Petr. humbly prayeth yor. Worps. will please to grant him Such relief in the premisses as to yor. Worps shall be thought fit

And the petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Simon Barkwill his Petition to be discharged form paying to the poor
