To the Rt: Worshpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx att their General Quarter Sessions of the peace att Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands & Seales of Thomas Pindar & Jacob Harvey Esqrs. Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the [...] day of James Instant by virtue of a Certificate under the hands and Seales of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the said Parish of St Botolph Aldersgate in the City of London and allowed of by Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said City of London Joshua Hannah and Mary Aldersey Children of George Aldersey Were by the said Order Removed from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of St: Botolph without Aldersgate as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Joshua Hannah and Mary Aldersey Whereby Your Petrs: Humbly Conceive they are wronged & Aggrieved

Yor: Petrs: Therefore humbly Pray Yor Worshps will be pleased to Appoint a Day to hear & Determine the said Appeal And that Yr. Petrs may be Releived as unto yor Woships shall Seem meet.

And Yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
