To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of his Peace for the said County of Middlesex assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Edmund Popps and Richard Holbrook Esquires two of his Majestys Justices by the Peace in and for the said county of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the [...] days of May was last past Elizabeth Law Wife of Thomas Law and her two Children (vizt Thomas aged Nine Years and Elizabeth aged Six Years were removed and conveyed from and out of [...] the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex until the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Law and her said two Children

That inasmuch as your Petitioners humbly apprehend themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order and the Removal and Conveyance of the said Elizabeth Law and her said two Children in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the same and pray your Worships will be pleased to take the Premises into Consideration and make such Order therein as to gain Worships shall seem just
