To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le Strand in the Said County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sr. John Gonson Knt: and Joseph Hayne Esqr Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the Twentieth Day of September last John Bowran and Mary his Wife with their Child named Mary about four Years agoe ever removed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in this County to the Said Parish of Mary Le Strand as the Place of their last legal Settlement

your Petitioners thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the Said Two Justices of the Peace) Appeal to this Court against the Same and Desired to be relieved therein

David Hughes And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc.
