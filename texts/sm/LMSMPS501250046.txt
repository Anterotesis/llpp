To the right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions held for the Sd. County at Hicke's Hall

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Aldermary in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Prcept made the 26th. day of Septr. last by Nathaniel Manlove and Alexander Pitfeild Esqrs. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County (quorum unus) directed to the Constable, Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County of Middx they the Sd. Constables Church Wardens & Overseers were required on Sight thereof to Pass remove and Convey John Grice and Anne his Wife and Miriam and Isaac his Children from & out of the sd. Parish of St. Giles Without Cripplegate to the sd. Parish of St. Mary Aldermary and them to deliver to your Petrs. or to Some or one of them together with the sd. Prcept to be Provided for by your Petrs. as for other Poor of your Petrs. Parish that for asmuch as the sd. John Grice his Wife and Children neither had nor have any legall Settlemt. in your Petrs. sd. Parish [---]

Yors. Petrs. humbly appeale to yor. Worship [...] agt the sd. Ordr. or Prcept and from & agt. [...] the Providing for the sd. John Grice his [...] Wife and Children and humbly pray the [...] sd. Order or Prcept may be discharged [...] to yor. Petrs. and their sd. Parish and [...] Such Costs s your Worships shall [...] think fitt

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray [...]
