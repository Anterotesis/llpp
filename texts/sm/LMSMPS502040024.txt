To the Right Worshippfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx In Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of John Geary.

Humbly Sheweth. That the Place of Governour of the House of Correction at Clerkenwellin the said County being now vacant by the Death of Mr Adam Harker late Governour. thereof and in your Worshipps Nomination and Disposall.

That your Petitrs Father having sustained great Losses. in the World is soe reduced that he is not in sufficient. Circumatances of providing for him And For as much as your Petitioner being (as he humbly conceives) Quallifyed to serve the said Office of Governour of the said house of Correction and ready to give sufficient Security. for his Faithfull discharge of the said Office and being a person well Affected to his Majestys Person and. Government

Your Petitr: therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be pleased to Elect and Choose him Govenour of the said house of Correction in the Roome and place of the said Mr.Adam Harker Deed

And Yor: Petr: (as in duty bound) shall Ever pray Etc

John: Geary
