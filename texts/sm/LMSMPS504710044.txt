To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the peace held by Adjournment in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or order of removal under the Hands and Seals of Richard Ricards and Thomas Quarrill Esquires two of [...] his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the ninth day of March last Rebecca Whitehead Widow of John Whitehead deceased and their two Children, vizt. Elizabeth aged upwards of eleven Years and William aged upwards of ten Years were removed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement,

That your Petitioners thinKing themselves apprieved by the said pass Warrant or order of Removel do therefore appeal against the same And humbly pray you Worships to make such order concerning the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And you Petitioners shall ever pray

April 4th. 1758 James Praed Attorney for the Appellants
