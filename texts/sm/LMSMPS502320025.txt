To the Worshipful his Majtes Justices of the Peace Assembled in General Quarter Sessions held in and for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwadens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Saviour Southwarke in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Thomas Nibbs son of Thomas Nibbs was by Order or Warrant under the hands & seale of two of his Majties Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx bearing date the 24th: day of November last removed from the parish of St. Mary Islington in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Saviour Southwarke as the place of his last Legall Settlement.

That your Peticoners humbly Conceive that they can make it appear to this Court that the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the said Thomas Nibbs is not in the said parish of St. Saviour [...]

Your Peticoners therefore humbly prays your Worship will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the said parish of St. Mary Islington may Attend this Court for hearing the said Appeal at such time as your Worship shall Appoint

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
