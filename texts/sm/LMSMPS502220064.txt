To the Right Worpll his Majesties of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of Robert Bent of the parish of Edmonton in the said County Gentleman

Sheweth That your Peticoner is charged towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for the Great Tyths and other his Estate there, much more then your Petr thinks he ought to pay Therefore appeales against the said Rate

your Petr prays your Worps to appoint Some day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor to attend this Court with their Rates for the releife of the poor for ths present yeare to hear and abide the Judgment & determinacon of the Court touching the said Appeale or otherwise to releive your Petr as your Worps Shall think meet

And your Petr Shall pray Etc. Robt: Bent
