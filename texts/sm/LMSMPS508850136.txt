To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Maria Wife of James Dalbiac.

Sheweth That your Petitioner was on or about the 13th. day of February last Convicted before Nathaniel Conant Esquire one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex for purchasing a certain pair of Tan Leather Gloves Subject to the Stamp Duties Imposed by the Statute in such Caw made and provided without the Stamp Ticket directed by Law to be Affixed to the same being so Affixed as the Law directs Contrary to the Statute Etc. And your Petitioners was declared and Adjudged to have forfeited for his said Offence the Sum of Ten Pounds Whereby your Petitioner conceives himself to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioner therefore [---] humbly begs leave to Appeal to this honourable Court against the said Conviction and that a day may be appointed to this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc

Maria Dalbiac.
