To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their General Quarter Session in and for the same County Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order bearing Date the 16th: Day of March last Under the Hands & Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid Two Childn: Born of the Body of Susannah Pearson out of Lawful Matrimony the One Named Mary Aged about 5 Years and the Other Named Susanh: Aged three Years (their Mother as therein Alledged having run away and Deserted them) were Removed opt of the Parish of Edmonton in the said County to the said Parish of St. Mary as to the place of their Legal Settlemt

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order Do Appeal against the same and pray that the Court will please to Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Edmonton and all Others Concerned to attend the Court some Day the present Session to hear and abide the Judgmt. and Determination of the Court touching this their Appeal

1767 April ye. 28.

Bittin Stone Sol. for Appellts.

And your petrs: & Appellts: will pray
