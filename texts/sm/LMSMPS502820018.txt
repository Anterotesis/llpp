To the Rt. Worpll his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Samuel Wilkinson Cook

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Jn: Ellis & M: Harper Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) dwelling in the Parish of St James in the Libty of Westmr. in the sd County your Petr. is adjudged the reputed Father of a Female Bastard Child born in the sd parish on the Body of Martha Full single woman on the 27th day of March last.

That thereby the sd Justices did Order that your Petr. should forthwth. pay or cause to be payed unto the then Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd parish or to some or one of them the Sume of 3 pounds of Lawfull money of Great Britain for & towards the laying & one Months lying Jn of the sd Martha Full of the sd Female Bastard child other extraordinary [...] concerning the sd Bastard child And further that your Petr. should likewise pay or cause to be payed unto the sd Churchwardens & Overseers or to some or one of them the Sume of two Shillings & six pence weekly & every week from & after the Expiracon of one Month to be computed from the sd 27 day of March for & towards the keeping Sustentacon & maintenance of the sd Child for & during so long time as the sd Child should continue & be a burthen on the sd pish And Lastly that your Petr. should put in securing for the performance of the sd Order or to appear at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the sd County then & there to abide such Order as the Justices of the peace or the Major part of them should make in the premises.

Yor. Petr. thinks himself aggreiv'd by the Judgmt. of the sd. two Justices of the peace and Appeals to this Court against the same & humbly prays that your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear & Determine the same.

And your Petr. shall pray Etc

Samuel Wilkinson
