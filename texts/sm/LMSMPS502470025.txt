The Rt. Worspful his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx at their Genl Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Faith Caseon

Sheweth.

That your Peticonr. is bound by Recognizance to Answer an Indictment for an Assault upon Joseph Parfett and Since which Indictment have made him Sattisfaction and that the sd. Joseph parfet is deceed whereby you Peticonr. Cannot give him Notice of Pleading Guilty as appeals by an affidavit hereunto Unexed

Yor. Peticonr. therefore most humbly Pray your Worsp would be pleased to give her Such releif in the prmits as yr. worsps Shal Seem meet

And yr. Peticonr. Shal Ever Pray Etc Faith Caseon
