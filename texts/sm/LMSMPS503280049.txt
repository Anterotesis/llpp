To the Right Worpll. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Sessions

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Stoke Poges in the County of Bucks.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Order under the hands & seals of Mr Frecker and William Miller Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) bearing date the thirteenth day of December last past Mercy Perryman & [---] two Children John Perryman aged seven years and Thomas Perryman aged three years in the said order mentioned to be her Children were removed from the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Stoke Poges in the said County of Bucks as the Place of their last legal Settlement

N. Blount Agent for the Appellants Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the said two Justices humbly Appeal there from and Pray that this Court will be Pleased to Order the [---] Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of Fulham to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
