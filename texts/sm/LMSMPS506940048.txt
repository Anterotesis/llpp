To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Mary Walker wife of Joseph Walker

Sheweth That your Petr. sd. husband heveing very frequently Abused and Barbourously aged your Petr. she thereupon made her Complaint to the Worspll. Justice Ellis Before whom her sd. Husband stands Bound for his Appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions As by the Recognizance appeares.

That your Petr. sd. husband still continues his like barbourous usage to your Petr. [...] Insomuch that she goes in Danger of her Life and he Refuseing to Allow her any Mainteynance or to Deliver her Cloaths Soe that she is Ready to Perish for want of Releife.

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Grant that her sd. Husband may be continued Bound untill next Sessions

and Yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc

mary walker
