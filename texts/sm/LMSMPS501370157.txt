To the Right Worpll. her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Alphage London

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Wart. under the hands acct Seates of the Worpll John Fuller & Benjamin Hilton Esqre. (Quor unus) Bearing Date the 24th of December last there was one Martha Gammidg widow received from the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the County aforesaid into the said Parish of Sd Alphage London abovetant: seting forth that St Alphage was the place of her last legal Date who And that your Petre. think themselves agrewed wth the Judgment of the said Justice of the peace And [---] Appeal against the said order and pray a day to be heard this present Quarter Sessions, and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd Parish of Ste Giles Cripplegate may also attend to hear and abide the Judgmt and final-determinacons of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor Petr Shall pray Etc

John Bicknell John Chapman} Church-warden

Willm. Farrow John Benson} Overseers of the poor
