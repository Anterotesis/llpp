To the Rt. Worspful his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx: at their Genl Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Henry Chapman

Sheweth. That your Peticonr. was bound by Recognizance for the Appearance of Wm. Bennet to answer an Indictmt. for an Assault upon Johanah Wright and that he hath made dilligent Search after the said Bennet & Cannot find him at appears by an affidavit hereunto Unexed

Your Peticonr. therefore most humbly pray your Worsps would be pleased to Respitt the Recognizance and give him Such releif in the Prmisses your Worsps shall Seem meet

And ye. Peticonr. Shal Ever Pray

Henry Chapman
