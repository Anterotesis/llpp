To The Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of C. Martin and W. Martins Esqrs two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 3oth of January 1777 Anthony Losson Single Man aged Twenty two Years was removed from the parish of St. James in the said Liberty and County to the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty and County aforesaid at the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Anthony Losson whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may then Attend.

G. Ragsdale Ja: Fisher Jno. Stone Wm. Richards.} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square
