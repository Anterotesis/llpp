To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden's and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of George Mercer and John Spinnage Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th. day of November 1769 Sarah Forster the Widow of Ralph Forster was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time in this present Session for hearing and Determining the said appeal and that all persons concerned may then attend.

And Your Petitioners shall over pray etc.

John Barnard James Showell Thos. Smith} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. George Hanover Square
