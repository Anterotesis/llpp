To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in General [---] Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westminster in the sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seals of William Gore Esqr. and Sir John Colebatch two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the 28th day of October Anno Dui 1727. Elizabeth Young Aged about Eight and Twenty Yrs. Wife of Thomas Young was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the Said County of the Said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petr. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Same.

Therefore humbly Pray your Worsps will Please to Order That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields may attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
