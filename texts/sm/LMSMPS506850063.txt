Middlesex October Sessns. 1778

To the Worshipfull Sir John Hawkins Knight Chairman and the rest of the Majestrates Assembled at the Quarter Sessions Holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in & for the said County of Middlesex

The Petition of Thos. Allen Senr. Thomas Allen Junr. and Thomas Jones Creditors of William Philps an Insolvent Debtor who in September last took the bennifit of an Act of Parl. Passed in the 18th year of the reign of His present Majesty Intitled an Act for the relief of Insolvent Debtors & for the relief of Bankps. in Cetain Cases

Sheweth That your Petitioners are the Principal Creditors in Value of the said Willm Philps Concerned together by Publick Notice for that Purpose given in the Dailey Advertizer of the 26th. day of Sepr. (thereunto annexed) That your Petrs. met pursuant to the said Advertizment And these Thomas Allen Senr. of Tottenham Court road in the said County of Midx Corn Chandler one of the Creditors of the said William Philps to be Assigned to all Estate and Effects mentioned in the Schedule delivered by him when Seared from His Confinement

And your Petr. in Duty bound will ever Pray Etc.

Thos. Allen Senr Tho Jones Thomas Allen Junr
