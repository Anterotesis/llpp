To the Worshipfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genll. Quarter Sessions of the peace held by Adjournment

The humll. Petn. of John Turner

Sheweth That your Petr. by Indenture of Apprentiship bearing date the 27o. June 1729 bound himself Apprentice to Phillip Maytrew of the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex Barber Chirurgeon for the term of Seven years from the 29o. September 1729 to learn his said Art and allso to allow him sufficient Meal Drink, washing lodging and Apparell during ye. sd. term

That your Petrs. said Master has not work to employ him and has been absent three Months to ye. and neither left any Pson to leach him his business nor took any care of your Petr., so that your Petr. was oblidged to apply to his friends for dyett and lodging and Cloaths and to endeavour to gete himself another Master

Your Petr. therefore most humbly prays that your Worships will be pleased to take his case into your wise Considn. and to discharge him from his said Apprenteship or to give such other Releife as to you shall Seem meet

And yr. Petr. as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc

John Turner
