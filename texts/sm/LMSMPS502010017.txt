To the right Worship full the Bench of Justices for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Chatham in the County of Kent

Shew That the said Church Wardens being Served will an order under the hands and Seals of two of his Maj [...] Justices of the said County of Middx Quorum Unus to maintain [---] Charles Carey and Mary his wife and one Child belonging to the Hamlett of poplar and Black Walls in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx aforesaid [---] and that the said Persons by vertue of the said order were removed to the said parish of Chatham and that your petrs [---] conceiving themselves agreived thereby appeale to this Court [---] agt the said [---] order

And humbly pray yor Worshipps to appointe a short day for determining of the said Appeale

And yor petrs shall ever pray
