To the Right Worpl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the Said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing Date the 24th. Day of November last past Elizabeth Colleyson the Wife of James Colleyson Junr. with Mary Colleyson her Child aged about two Years were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middx to the Sd. Parish of St. Botolph with- out Aldgate London as the Place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices doe humbly Appeal to this Honble. Court against the same and Relief in the Premisses

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
