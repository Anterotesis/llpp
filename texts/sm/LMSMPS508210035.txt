To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saffron Walden in the Town of Saffron Walden in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Tyler and Michael Downs two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth Day of December 1786 Susannah Cornell Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James's within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saffron Walden aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against such Order of Removal and pray that a Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the same
