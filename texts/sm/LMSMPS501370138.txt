To the Worshipfull her Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: Etc

The Humble: Petetion and appeale of Daniel Waine

Sheweth That Yor: Peter: being an Inhabitant in the parish of St. Gile's without Cripplegate is assest at 6s: & 9d in the pound to the poores rate (and more then other persons of the same abillity are assest to pay) but he by reason of great Losse's and decay of Trade is unable to pay the same but the officers haveing made distraint of Yor: Peters: goods

Yor: Peter: therefore Humbly: prays that he may be releived as you in Yor: Great wisdom shall Think meet

And Yor: petr: shall ever pray
