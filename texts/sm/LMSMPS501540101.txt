To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition & Appeale of Wm Haddon of the Parish of St Gyles without Cripplegate in the sd County

Sheweth That your Peticonr is Rated towards the Releife of the Poore of the sd Parish the sume of Thirty seaven Shillings & Sixpence for three Hundred Pound Stock & psonell Estate, And that he can make it Appeare that their are many Inhabitants within the sd Parish of much better Ability in Personall Estate that is not Rated soe much

Your Peticonr humbly prays such Releise in the prmisses or to your. worshipps shall seeme meet

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
