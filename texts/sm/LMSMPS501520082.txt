To the Rt. Worpll his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Michael Bassishaw within the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Tenth Day of March last past Elizabeth Fenton Widdow of William Fenton lately deceased was removed from the parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate in the said County to the said parish of St Michael Bassishaw London alleadging the said Parish to be the place of her last legal Settlement That your petrs conceiving them selves aggrieved by the said Pass Order do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly prays your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St Buttolph without Aldgate may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And they shall ever Pray Etc:
