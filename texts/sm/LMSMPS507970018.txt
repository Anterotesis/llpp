To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of The Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Edwd. Webster and R Heaviside Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Qusorum) bearing date the Seventh day of June instant Elizabeth Holmes and her female Bastard named Mary and two Years (for nature only) were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint James at the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Holmes whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudications of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas: Dyson

Solicitor for the Appellants
