To the Right Worshipful His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition of Elizabeth Flint

Sheweth That your Petr: hath been this present Sessions Convicted of a Petty Larceny for stealing two Pishes the property of Fisher & hath been Sentenced by this Honble: Court to be Publickly wip't round Spittle Fields Market

That Your Petr: hath two small helpless Children is a Widow & gets both her & their Maintenance by her Hand labour

That Your Petr: is exceeding Sorrowful for this Offence and will for the future behave Honestly & Industriously But if she shall be publickly Punish't will be in [...] tably Ruined and her Children brought to the Parish

Therefore Your Petr: most humbly implores and intreats Your Worships will be pleased to Order that the above Sentence to be [...] altered to a Private Punishment

We the under written humbly recommend the Petitioner as a Proper Object of Compassion and humbly desire her request may be Granted.

And she as in Duty bound will ever pray Etc Eliza: flint
