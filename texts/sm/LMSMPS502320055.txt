To the Worspull. the Chairman & other his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Lawrence old Jewry London

Sheweth That by a Warrt or Order under the hands & Seals of John Fuller and Thomas Robe Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor Vn) one Samuel Boote was lately removed from the parish of Hornsey in the Said County of Middx to the said parish of St Lawrence old Jewry as the place of his last Legall Settlemt.

That your Peticrs. find themselves agreived [---] by the said Order of the said two Justice dated the 20th of December last and humbly appeale to this Court agt the said Order

Your Peticonrs & Appellts. therefore pray your Worspps will be pleased to appoint Some day to hear the matter of the Appeale and that the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of Homsey and all other persons concerned may attend to hear & abide by the Order & determinacon of this Court in the prmises.

And yor. Peticonr. Shall ever pray Etc
