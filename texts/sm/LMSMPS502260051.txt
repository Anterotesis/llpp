To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx [...] their Gent: Quarter Sessions of ye. Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Sepulchre [...]

Sheweth That by a Warrant of Removall under the Hands [...] Seals of Thomas Pindar and Simon Michell Esqrs. Two of this Mats. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing date the 17th. day of February 1724 Richard Croker with Elizabeth his wife and their four Children vizt Hannah aged about Seven years and an half Elizabeth aged about five years an half Anne aged about two years and an half and Mary about Sixteen weekes were removed from the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre as the place of the [...] Settlement That your Petitioners apprehending themselves [...] the Order of the said Two Justices humbly Appeals their Court against the same.

And Pray that this Court will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell and all other parties concerned may attend this Court Some day this Present of Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination and Judgment of this Court touching the Premisses

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
