To the Rt worspll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Henry Nobes of Hamer Smith in the Said County.

Sheweth That yor. Pr. did Serve the Office of Churchwarden for the Said Parish these Three yeare last past and did disburse more then recd in the time of his Said Office the Sund of Â£15.2s.9d. upon accte. of the Said Parish.

That yor. Pr. accompt was Audited and allowed of by the Inhabitants of the Said Parish and at the Same time did make an Order that the present Overseer of the Poore for the said Parish Should reimburse yor. Pr. his said moneys And for that purpose did Anyment their Assessmt. Yet nevertheless Yor: Pr. is unsatisfyed his said money.

P S Westley

Yor. Pr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worsps to Order that the Said Overseers may pay him the Said moneys Soe due to him as aforesd

And yor. Pr Shall ev pray etc Henry Nobes
