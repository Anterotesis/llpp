To the Worshipfull his Majs. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now Setting on the Bench at Hick's Hall.

The petition of Samuel Sansbury now in Clerkenwell Bridewell

Humbly Sheweth

That your petitioner was convicted [...] last Sessions by his Worship Justice Barey for 6 Months for Uttering false Counterfeit Guineas imitateing the Courant Coin of Great Brittain

Your Petitioner has been 6 Months Confined and am in a miserable Condition having lost one of my Eyes since my Confinement & having a Wife & 6 Small Children humbly begs Your Worships would please to order his Discharge

And in so doing Your Petitioner will ever Pray
