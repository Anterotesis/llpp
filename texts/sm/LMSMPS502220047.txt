To the Rt: Worpll His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genlls Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Michael Purse, of Houndsloe in the County of Middx Carpenter

Sheweth That yor Petr: by the Order & Direccon of Whitelock Bulstrode & John Offley Esqrs Deced & Severall other of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County attended On the Severall Dayes & times menconed in the Bill hereunto Annexed to Survey the work done at Chertsey Bridge in the said County being Two & twenty Dayes & agreed wth the said Justices to have for his less of time trouble & expence therein tenn Shilling per diem which Amounseth in the whole to Eleven pounds

That the said Sum of eleven pounds Stills remains due & unpaid to yor Petr.

yor. Petr. therefore humbly Prayes Yor Worpps will please to make An Order for the paymt thereof

And yor Petr as in Duty Bound Shall ever pray Etc

Michael Purse
