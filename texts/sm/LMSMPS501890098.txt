To the Rt: Worspll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Mary Jones wife of Rodrick Jones

Sheweth That John Loyde is yor. Petr. husbands Apprentice That the sd Loyde has divers times abused your petr and likewise his master ruining away from them often times and calling yor. petr. Bitch divers times and assaulting his Mater

That the sd Loyde is a very wicked person & is now in Bridewell That yor peticoners husband is now out of Town.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that the sd Loyde may be continued in Bridewell till next Sessions Or to referr the matter to the Worspll Justices as comitted him

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc
