To the worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All Hallows the Great London

Sheweth That by an order or pass Warrant, under the hands & Seals of Ebenezer Mussell Esqr. and Sir Samuel Gower Knight two of his majesties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twelfth day February last William Atwood Elizabeth his wife and their Child named Richard aged eight years were removed from the parish of Saint Leonard in Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Said parish of All Hallows the Great in the said City of London as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgement of the Said two Justices appeal to your Worships against the said order and pray that you will be pleased to appoint a Day to determine the Same and make such Order thereon as to your Worships shall seem meet

And ye Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Mr: Robinson Attorney for the Appellants.
