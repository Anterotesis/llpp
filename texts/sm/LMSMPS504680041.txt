To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace assembled at their General Sessions of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Weybridge in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Frederick Baronet and J Rowles Esqr. I [...] n of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex Dated the 23d. day of January 1758 James Ward was removed from the [...] Parish of Littleton in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Weybridge in the said County of Surry By which order Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships that you will be pleased to appoint a Day of hearing the said Appeal and your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc Etc Etc
