To the Right Worsppll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon & appeale of George Bell of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated 2s.4d. P Month towards the releife of the Poor of the said parish for this present year.

That your Peticoner only paying Â£6d P Anna rent for his dwelling house and he [---] having a wife & two small children to mainteyn and being aged three Score years & having nothing to mainteyn his said family but his own Labour and industry See that he is not able to pay the said assessment.

Yor. Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worspps wilbe pleased to grant that he may be discharged from payment of the said rate.
