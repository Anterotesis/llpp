To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margaret Westmr. in the County of Middx aforesaid.

Sheweth That Elizabeth Wilson Wido. of George Wilson aged about Sixty Years was removed by a pass Warrant bearing date ye 27d. of July last under the hands seals of Miles Harper & John Ellis Esqr. from the Parish of St. James Westmr. to the Said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the Place of her last regal Settlemt.

That Yr. Petrs. [...] conceive themselves agrieved by the Adjudgment of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to Yor. Worpll. against the same.

Therefore humbly pray that the Churchwarden & Overs. of the Poor of the Sd. Parish of St. James Westmr may be Ordered to attend this Worspll. Court someday this Qr. Sessions, then & there to abide by the Judgment and final determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
