To the Worsfull his Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Prater and William Whitehurst Churchwardens of the parish of St Giles in the fields in the said County on behalf of themselves and other Inhabitants of the said Parish

Sheweth That the Highwayes of the said parish are very much out of repair and will require a considerable sume of money to repair and amend the same which cannot be raised without the and of this Court

Yor Petrs therefore humbly pray that a Rate or Assessment not exceeding three half pence in the pound of may be made upon the said Inhabitants for repairing the said Highwayes according to the direction of the Act of Parliamt. in that case made, to be collected by the present Surveyors of the Highwayes in the said parish And that the money collected on the said Rate be imployed and laid out as the Justices of the peace for this County usually meeting at the speciall Sessions in the said parish shall direct and appoint

And yor Petrs shall ever pray Etc

John Prater Will Whitehurst} Churchwardens
