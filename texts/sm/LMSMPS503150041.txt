To the Right worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd in General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Great Marlow in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Thomas Reynell Bart. and Brent Reynell Spiller Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd bearing date the Fourth day of Feburary instant William Smith was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Shepperton in the County of Midd aforesaid to the said Parish of Great Marlow in the said County of Bucks as the legal Place of Settlement of the said William Smith

Your Petitioners concerving themselves aggreived [...] by the said Order of the said two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray this Court will appoint a Day this present sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Shepperton aforesaid and all other parties concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Jonathan Hammond one of the Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Great Marlow
