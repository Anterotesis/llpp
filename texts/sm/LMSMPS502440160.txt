To the Right Worpl his Majtys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Allhallows the Less London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrt under the hands & Seals of Robt Jackson and Robt Dennet Esqrs two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 6th day of June last 1727 Elizabeth the wife of Abraham Willis & Anne her daughter were Sent & delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in ye County of Middx to the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Allhallows the Less London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt & Judgment of the said two Justices and do most humbly Appeal to your Worps. therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worps. will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Save to them the benefit of this Appeal and appoint a day in the next Sessions for hearing the Sd Appeal And Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Mary White Chappel in ye sd County & all other parties concerned may then Appear & hear & abide the Judgment Determinacon of this Court touching ye sd Appeal.

And they shall ever pray Etc
