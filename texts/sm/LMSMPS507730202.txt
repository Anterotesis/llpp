To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor off the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Samuel Hawkins Two off his Majesties Justices off the peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (One where off being of the Quorum) bearing date 8th. day of October last Past Mary Dolder Wife of James Dolder (who is now at Sea and her Infant Child) Vizt. (Jasper Aged about Five Years were removed and Conveyed from and out off the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of the Last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Dolder and her said Child

Your Petitioners conceive them selves Aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same

And your Petitioners will ever pray

S. Malbon Sollr for the said Parish off Shadwell
