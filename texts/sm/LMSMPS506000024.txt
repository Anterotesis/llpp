To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hick,s Hall in St. John Street in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant Under the Hands and Seals of Sr. Robert Darling Knt. and D: Wilmot Esquire Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th day of February last Ann Howard and her instant were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex To the said Parish of St. Andrew Holborn London (by the Name and Description of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the place of the Last Legal Settlement of the said Ann Howard and Child whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Joshua Cox Solld. for yr. Appellrs Your Petitioners therefore Humbly appeal to this Court against the said Order and pray that the said Court will be Pleased to Order that the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chapel and all persons Concerned Do attend this Court some day in the next General or Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court Touching the said Appeal and that the Benefit of hearing the said appeal at that Sessions may be saved to your Petitioners and that Your Petitioners may be otherways Relieved in the in the premises as the said Court shall see Fit.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
