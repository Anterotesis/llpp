To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Sr James Esdaile Knt & W. Blackborow Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx one being of the Quorum bearing date the Twenty Sixth day of April last William Savage who was Sick and infirm and Sarah his wife were removed from the Parish of St Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Botolph without Aldgate as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitionrs Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the sd Warrant or order do humbly Appeal against the same And Pray that your Worships Will be pleased to Appoint a day this Present Session that all Persons Concerned may attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal And Your Petitionrs Shall eve pray Etc Clare & Bampfield Sollr for the appellants

Middlesex. At the General Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the County aforesd by Adjournment on Tuesday the ninth day of May in the Twentieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third King of Great Britain France & Ireland Defender of the Faith Etc

It is ordered by the Court That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex have notice of this Appeal & that they and all Persons Concerned do Attend this Court on Thursday next at Nine of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear & abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

By the Court Selby
