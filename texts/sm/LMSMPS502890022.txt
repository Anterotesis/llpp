To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the peace assembld

This Humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of William Hucks and John Hadley Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Nineteenth Day of January last William Spencer (Since Deceased) and Mary his Wife ware Removed from the parish of St. Giles's in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St George Hanover Square as the place of their last legall Settlements whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concernd may attend the same time

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Rich Whitworth} Church Warden
