To the Worshipfull of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in & for the sd. County Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church wardens & Oversrs. of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of John Milner & Philip Dyot Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 5th. Day of June last Ann Lee Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the sd. Parish of Saint Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and against the sd. Order do appeale

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appellts. Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that a Short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and Determina the said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Middlx At the General Quarter Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hick's Hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid on Monday the thirteenth Day of July in the Twenty first Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Great Britain France & Ireland Defender of the Faith

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchward [...] & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles in the feilds in the County of Middx have Notice of this Appeal & that they & all Persons concerned do attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clock in the fore noon to hear & abide the Judgmt. & Determination of this Court touching the sd. Appeal

By the Court Waller
