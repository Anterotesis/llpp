To the Rt. Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their Genall Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated the fourth Day of November last under the hands & Seales of John Milner and Richard Gifford Esquires two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx (Quor Unus) Elizabeth Vergis the Widow of John Vergis was removed from the Parish of St: Giles in the Feilds in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of St: Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners conceive themselves Agreive the Said Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices And humbly Appealing against the Same to this Honble Court

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray Your Worps will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of St: Giles in the Feilds and all other persons Concerned to Attend this Court Some day this Present Sessions to hear [...] Abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Co [...] Upon and touching the Said Appeal, And that Your Petrs may have Such releife herein as to Your Worps Shall Seem meet Etc.

And Your Peticoners Shall ever pray Etc.
