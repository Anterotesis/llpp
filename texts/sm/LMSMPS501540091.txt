To the Worshipful His Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County off Middlesex att ye Session new holden att Hickes Hall for the Sd County. The humble Peticon of Richd Evans most humbly Sheweth.

May it please Yor: Worsps

Your Peticonr who is an Inhabitant of ye. Parish of St Gyles's Cripple Gate without. and being now Rated to pay to the Poor of the Sd Parish and he being by Age render'd Incapable to Support himself & Family is also Incapable to pay the Sd Rate

Yor Peticonr therefore humbly Prays Such relief in this Case as Yor Worsps in Your great Wisdom & goodness Shall see meet

And Yor Petr

Will Ever pray
