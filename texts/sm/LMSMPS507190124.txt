To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir James Esdaile Knight and Cad Colter Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 23d. Day of October last Mary Colburn Wife of Benjamin Colburn (who is gone away and left her) and her Child named William aged about seven Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of the Peace appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Thos. Wade Junr. Sollr. for the Appellts.
