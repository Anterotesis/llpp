To the Rt: Worspfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of Edmond Suson of the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the said County:

Sheweth That Your Peticoner is this Year Rated and assessed in the Sume of two Shillinges P month towards the poor of the said Parish and your Peticoner Renting In house but a Small Shop in the said Parish your Peticoner being in Very mean Circumstances whereby he is unable to pay the Same Rate

Your Peticonre thinking himself agreived thereby Humbly appeal against the Same

Your Petr. most Humbly pray that your Worsps would be pleased to give him such releif in the Premisses as your Worsps would seem meet

And Your Peticoner shall ever pray Etc

Edmond Suson
