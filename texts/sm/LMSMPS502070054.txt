To the Worshipfull his Majties. Justices of the peace to be Assembled at the Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace holden at Hickes Hall in and for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Barking in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order bearing Date the Second day of October last under the Hands and Seales of Two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx one Lydia Ball Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Mary Mattfellon als White Chapple in the said County to the said parish of Barking in the County of Essex as the place of her Last Legall Settlment

Yor. Petitioners finding themselves aggreived by the said Order Appleale to this Court against the same and pray that their Appeale may be lawed and Adjourned to the Next Sessions and that your Wopps would please to Appoint a day for hearing thereof as to yor. Wopps Shall Seem Meet Etc:

And yor. Petitionr. as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc:

J Weldy Ri. Fidor} Churchwardens

William Harris William Pavell} Overseeres.
