To the Honoble. Bench of Justices now Sitting at Hicks Hall The Humble Petition of Thos: Sinkins

Sheweth That your Petitr: hath been Confined in new Prison ten weeks and being very poore bying on the bare Boards Humbly desires your Worship will be pleased to take it into Consideration & let your Petitr. be discharged he having given his Prosecut, simely notice of tryall and will not attend, wch. of petitr Continues another Sessions in prison will Infallably Starve and Perrish his Poverty being soe Greate having no Friends to Subsist him and Innowat of what he is accused off

And your Petit as in Duty is bound Ever to pray Etc

Thomas Simkins
