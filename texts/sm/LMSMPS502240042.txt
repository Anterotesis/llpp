To the Right Worshippful his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition and appeal of John Bowes of the Parish of st James Clerkenwell in the. Said County.

Sheweth That Your Petitioner is rated in the poor & Scavengers rates of the said Parish for Stock a Personall Estate whereby the conceives. himself Aggrieved and appeals agt the same

And Prays he may wholly Discharged from the same And Yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Jo Bowes
