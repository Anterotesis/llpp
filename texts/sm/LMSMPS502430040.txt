To The Right Worpll. the Chairman & other her Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Anne &Agnes w lin Aldersgate London.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands & Seals of John Ellis Esqr & the Right Honble. the Lord Wm. Powlett Two of his Maties Justices of the peace (Quor unus) for the County of Middx & Liberty of Westmr. dated the tenth day of this instant May, Mary the wife of Richard Baskett was removed from the Parish of St. James Westmr to ye peticoners sd. Parish of St. Anne & Agnes wth in Aldersgate London, by which Warrant or Order of Removall Yr. Petrs humbly Conceive themselves to be aggrieived

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Appeale to Yor. Worpps agt. the sd. Warrt. or Order & humbly pray that yor Worpps will be pleased to appoint a short day to heare & determine the same, And that in Regard the sd. Warrnt. or Order was granted on the ball of the sd. Richd Baskett the husband (who is unknown to yr. Petrs) the Officers of the sd. Parish of St. James Westmr may be obliged to produce the sd. Richd. Baskett at the time of such bearing

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
