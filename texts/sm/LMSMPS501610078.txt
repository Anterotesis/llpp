To the Rt Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Sessions at Hickshall

The humble petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under [...] hanes [...] Richard Holmes and Edward Leneve Esqrs Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (quorun) bearing date the Twelvth day of this Instant Aprill Mary Pitt an Infant aged about Eight years the Daughter of Thomas Pitt and Mary his wife was removed from the Parish of St. Anne in the Libty of Westmr in the said County to your Petrs said Parish of St Martin in the Fields, Where by [...] petr. humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Appeale to your Worps and pray that your Worps would be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the same.

And your petr. shall ever pray Etc.

[...] the Court to adjourn this Appeale

[...] disty Pray lett this appeal be read & adjourned to the next Sessions the poor people are upon us & wee cannot gett our proofs till then [...] & you will save my Coming to Court & Obl [...]

Yr d. darnall
