To the Worshipfull Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the City of London

Sheweth

That by an Order or Pass under the Hands and Seals of Charles Dodd and Stepn. Clarke Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (and one of them of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of October in the year of our Lord 1773 Mary Woodworth was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Sepulchre in the City of London as the place of her legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and Humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved and that a day may be Appointed at this Present Sessions for the hearing and Determining thereof

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

R. Brewer Atty: for Appealants
