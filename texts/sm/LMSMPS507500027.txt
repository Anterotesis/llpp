To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Will Haggard and Thomas Gilbert Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of December 1781 Hannah-West Singlewoman was and Conveyed from the Parish of St. James in the said Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah-West whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the Said two Justices and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some Day in this Present Session for that hearing and Determining this appeal and that all Persons may be then Ordered to Attend And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Jno Skiett Sol for Appellts Goodmans Fields 7th Jany. 1782
