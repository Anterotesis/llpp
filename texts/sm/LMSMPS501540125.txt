To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition & Appeale of Ann Barlow Widdow of the Parish of St Sepulchers in the sd County

Sheweth That your Peticonr in Rated towards the Release of the Poore of the said Parish two Shillings p Pound for the House she now Enjoyes & that she hath nine Children the youngest being about tenn weeke old & that her Husband left her in meane Circumstances by reason whereof she is not able to pay towards the Poors Rates

Your Peticonr humbly prays such Releife as to your Worshipps shall Seeme meet

And your Peticonr shall Ever Pray Etc
