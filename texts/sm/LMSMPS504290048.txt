To the worshipful hid Majseties [...] Justices of the Peace assembled at their Session of the Peace at Hickss. Hall in the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Twickenham in the Said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the hands & seals of the Right Honourable the Earl of Kinnoull and John Goodchild Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of Peace for the said County of Middlesex Mary Bawsom was removed from the Parish of Teddington in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Twickenham by which order your Petitioners apprehend the said Parish of Twickenham to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing of their said Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
