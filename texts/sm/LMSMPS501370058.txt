Right Worspph. her Matys Justices of the for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Edy Johnson of the Parish of Stepney in the Hamlet of Bethnall Green in the County of Middx Widow

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated two shillings and two pence P Month towards the releife of the Poor of the said Parish.

That your Peticoner only paying Nine pounds P ann rent and she being aged Eight & fifty years or thereabouts and nothing but her own labour and [...] stry to mainteyn herselfe Soe that she is not able to pay the sd. assessment.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps wilbe pleased to grant that she may be discharged from paying of the said assessment.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
