To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborne in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Anthony Wroth and William Booth Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid bearing date the nineteenth day of August One Thousand Seven hundred & thirty [---] Seven George Brooks and Joseph his Son aged about eight Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Sepulcher in the County aforesaid to the said Liberty in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holbourn aforesaid [---] as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioner conceiving themselves aggreived by the Said Order of the Said Two Justices of the sd peace humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same and pray this Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Sepulchre aforesaid and all other parties concerned [...] to attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said appeal

And Your Petrs shall ever pray Etc

Joseph Higginson [...] Philip Forester [...]
