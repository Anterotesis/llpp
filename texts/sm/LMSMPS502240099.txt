To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at them Genll: Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Chapell warday and Overseers of the Poor of the Towne of [...] ew Brentford in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of James Clitheroe and John Venner Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex Elizabeth Longland widow of George Longland decd: with her three Children Elizabeth Longland aged abt: ten years, George Longland aged abt: Nine years and Catherine Longland aged abt: Five years were Removed from the precint of Norwood in the said County of Middlesex to the said Towne of New Brentford as the place of their last legall Settlemt. That your Peticoners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the said Justices and Dox Appeall therefrom

Your Petitionrs: therefore Humbly Pray the Court will be Pleased to make such Order for their Releife in the prmissed as to yr: Judgemts: shall Seen meet,

And yor: Petr: Shallevr. pray
