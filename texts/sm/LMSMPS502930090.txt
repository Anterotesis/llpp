To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in and for the said County-

The Humble petition and appeale of the Church Wardens and Over seers of the poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the County aforesaid-

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (one of the being of the Quorum) dated the Second day of May One thousand seaven hundred and John [...] was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Bars in this County to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the place of his last Legall Settlemt. whereby Your petrs. conceive them selves to be agreived

Your Petrs. there fore humbly pray your Worships would be pleased to appoint Some Day this present Sessions to hear & determine the matter touching the said appeale-

And yours Petrs. shall ever pray.
