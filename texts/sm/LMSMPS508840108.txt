Middlesex To the Worshipful his majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Session of the Peace in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the ChurchWardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Abbotts Kensington in the County of Middlesex.

That by an Order of Removal under the Stands and Seals of Rupert Clarke and I. Floud [---] Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County (one Whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the first day of May last Ann Warner and here two Children Vitzt. Mary Ann Aged one Year and William aged One Month were Removed from the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the Said Parish of Saint mary Abbotts kensington as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceives themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and do Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be Appointed o this present Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitoners shall ever Pray Etc.
