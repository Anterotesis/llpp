To the Right Honble Sr William Calvert Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of the Honble the Justice of Gaol Delivery in their General Sessions of Gaol Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Bullock

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was Committed to New Prison Clerkenwell on the 13 Day of June last for Assaulting one John Keeton and Committing Sodomy upon him and new is in Newgate for the Same

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Pray's that at this Present Sessions of Gaol Delivery of Newgate he may be tryd Bailed or Discharged pursuant to the Statute in that Cate made & provided

And Your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray
