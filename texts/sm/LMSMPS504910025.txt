To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the Present General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of William Pentlow

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner being Charged on an Indictment for felony and Murder was by your Worships Suspended from his Office of Keeper of New Prison Until Etc. That your Petitioner having been Tryed at the Present Session at the Old Bailey upon the said Indictment he was acquitted no Evidence appearing in the least to Affect him.

Wherefore your Petitioner most humbly prays that your Worships out of your great Candour and goodness will be pleased to restore him to his former Office of Keeper of the said Prison He promising that he will make it his constant Studdy to deserve your Worships favours by a faithfull discharge of his Duty in the Due Execution of his Office Etc.

And your Petitioner as in Duty Bound will for ever pray Etc

Wm Pentlow
