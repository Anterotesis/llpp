To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hand & [...] of Jas. Fraser & Danl. Gach Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 11th: Day of January last Ann Babington (aged abt. three Years & half) the Daughter of Thomas Babington by Sarah his Wife both deceased was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the feilds within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to thesd. Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Agrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that a Short day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appellts.
