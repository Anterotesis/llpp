To the Rt. Worsppll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Thomas Parker of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath lived four and Thirty Yeares in the said parish and never Received any Relief therefours

That your Petitr. is aged Sixty Two yeares and hath a Wife aged. Sixty four yeares to mainteyne which he is not able to doe being past his Labour and very poor And for as much as the Churchwdns of the said parish refusing to allow your petitr. a peation without an order from this Court

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays Your Worspps will be pleased to order that he may be allowed such a pencon from the said Parish As unto your Worspps Shall Seeme Meete

And Yor Petitr. shall ever pray Etc Tho: Parker
