To the Worppful his Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Mary Liddiard

Sheweth That yor: petr: is convicted for a Rescue Sworne against her by Robert Patrick and fined Tenn Pounds your Petr: haveing Seven Children and in a very poore Condition [---] her goods being seized upon by an Execucon And yor. petrs. husband haveing absented himselfe from his place of aboade ever Since Aprill last which makes yor: Petr: atterly uncapable of paying the said Fine and what your Petr: did at that time was only in Defence of her Husband.

Your Petr: therefore humbly Pray's that your Worpps will be pleased to take her yor: Petrs: Case into your most Serious Consideration and to Mitigate the said Fine.

And yor: Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
