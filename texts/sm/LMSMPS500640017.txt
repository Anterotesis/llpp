To the Right Worspps. his Maties Justices of Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of Thomas Allen.

Sheweth

That in January 1694 yor. Peticoner became Apprentice to Edward Gregory of the parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the said County for the terme of Seaven yeares from the date of his Indenture of Apprenticeship As thereby may appeare.

That the said Edward Gregory with not allow yor. Peticoner any wearing apparell fiting for him Soe that he is likely to be Started with Cold and the frequently beating & abusing yor. Peticoner Soe that he hath been often in danger of his life.

P S Westley yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to grant that he may be discharged from his said Apprentice ship.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Thom Allan
