To the Right Honble. her Majties. Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Oliver Atkey.

Sheweth That yor. Peticonr in the Yeare 1707 served the office of Overseer of the Poor in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn about Barrs in this County:

That yor. Peticonrs partnrs. one Thomas Dell another of the Oversrs. of the Poor of the Said Parish, received from time to time dureing that Yeare the Moneys ariseing by the Booke for the Poors= rate wch. amounted to Eighteen Pounds of money more then was by him disbursed for the necessarie Reliefe of the Poor there, as he himselfe hath often acknowledged, to yor. Petr. & others of the Inhabits

That yor. Peticonr. hath often times applyed himselfe to these Thomas Dell his partnr. to come to an Account, with the prsent Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish, & to pay over to them the money So remaineing in his hands as aforesd, to the end that yor. Peticonr. might be discharged there from, and not be lyable to any demand of the Parish in that behalfe, but the Sd Thomas Dell refuses to doe the Same;

Yor. Peticonr. (the prmisses Considered) humbly prays yor. Worships be pleased to ordr. the Said Thomas Dell to pay over unto the prsent Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish, the Said Same of Eighteen pounds odd money Soe remaining in his hands, and that yor Petr. may be discharged from the Same, the Sd money Soe Collected being all recd. by the Sd Thomas Dell yor. Petr. not in the least intermedling therein

And yor. Petr. Shall pray Etc Oliver Atkey
