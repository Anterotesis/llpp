That yor. Petr conceiveing themselves Aggreived by the Judgement of the sd. two Justices doe humbly Appeale to yor Worshipps therefrom,

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day, that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary White- Chappell, may attend this Court, to hear & abide the Judgement & determinacon of the Court touching this Appeale.

Jno. Waring John Thompson} Churchwardens

Balch Soler.
