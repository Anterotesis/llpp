To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions att Hick's Hall assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of ye Parish of St Mary Bone in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Milner & Robert Midford Esqr two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Tenth day of September 1731 Elizabeth. Wilkinson. aged about ten years was Removed & Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Giles in the fields in the County of Middx to the Parish of St Mary le Bone in the said County as the Place of the last legall Settlement Whereby the said Parish of St Maryle Bone think themselves Agrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court mill please to Appoint a day for hearing the Said Appeal and that all persons concernd may attend at the sametime

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Edward Dewfall: Overseers of the poor
