To the Right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon and appeale of the Overseer of the poor and Inhabitants of the Parish of St: Peter ad Vincula in the Liberty of the Tower of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seats of Bastwick Johnson and Robert Kyrby Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quorumunus) bearing date the 20th: day of this Instant February Frances Suest Widow was removed from the Hamblett of Wappin Stepney in the Parish of St: Dunstan Stepney in this County to the said Parish of St: Peter ad Vincula on to the place of her last legall Settlemt:

That your petrs think themselves agreived by the said Order and Humbly appeale to this Honrble Court agt: the same

And therefore humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to Order the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Wappin Stepney and all other persons concerned to attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to heare and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale.

And your petrs: Shall ever pray Etc
