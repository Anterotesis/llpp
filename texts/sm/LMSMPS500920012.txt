To the Right Honoble. the Bench of Justices [...] Sitting at Hickse Hall:

The humble Peticon of Wm Sparrow [...] of the Hamles of Bow & Old ford

Hambly Sheweth That your Petr. in Allowed Some Small Matter from the Said that for and towards the Maintainance of him selfe and aged [...] being both Ready to Narve, That year Petr. find an order frost Justices ford for a father Allowance which the Churchwarden refused to Obey

[...] for most humbley pray pt. homos. to Grant him Such be wife as in your wisdom Shall Settle weeke

And your Petrs Shall pray Etc
