To his Majts. Justices of the Peace now Sitting at Hicks Hall in St. Johns Street

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Hamlet of Milend old townes in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Samuell Tyssen and Henry Norris Esqrs Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) Henry Reynolds and Elizabeth his wife were removed out of the parish of St. John at Hackney in the Said County to the said Hamlet of Milend old Town as the place of their last Legal settlements By which Order your Petrs. think themselves aggrevied and appeale agt. the same

And humbly pray your Worships will appoint a Day in this present Sessions finally to hear Determination Same

And Your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
