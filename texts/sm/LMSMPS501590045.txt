To the. Rt Worpl his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petecon of Richard Hathaway prisoner in Newprison

Sheweth That Yr Peteconer being Very poor and friend lis, is all most Starved and we body to Com neer him to Sisist him, must perrish in Gole unless Yr Worships takes him in Consideracons and discharge the Court fees Gratis

That Yr humble peteconer hath Been Confind three Months and upwards in this prison for the said fees

he therefore most humbly [---] implors- Yr Worships Comiseracon and that you would order his Reeleasment

And he in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
