To the Right Worspp his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of William James of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents within the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourne in the sd County.

Sheweth That at Easter last Your Petitioner was by the Inhabitants. of the said Liberty Elected and Chosen to Serve the Office of Overseers of the Poore for the Year Ensuing

That your Petitioner is a very Poor Man And in Mean Circumstances whereby he is Rendred altogeather uncapable of Executing the said Office And forasmuch as there are Severall Inhabitants in the said Liberty of Farr better Ability then your Petitioner and better Quallifyed to Execute the Said Office Wherefore your Petitioner humbly Conceives himselfe aggrieved

Your Petitr. therefore humbly Prays he may be Discharged from Serving the said Office And of Some other proper person may be Chosen in his Roome As to this Court shall be thought Meete.

And yor. Petr shall Ever pray Etc. Wm. James.
