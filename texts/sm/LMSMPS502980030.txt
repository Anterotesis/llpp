To The Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James Within the Libty of Westmr in the County of Middx

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Beckingham and Barwell Smith two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Ninth day of February instant dorothy Simons was Removed from and out of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars in the said County to the said Parish of St. James as the last legal settlemt of the said Docothy simons Where by your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

And Therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concerned may Attend at the same time

And your Petitioners Shall Ever pray Saml. Seddon Vestry Clerk
