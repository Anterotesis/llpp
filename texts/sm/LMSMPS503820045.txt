[...] Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General [...] Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition of Frances Blandy

Sheweth That by Indenture of apprentiship bearing date the 28th day of June in the Year 1742 Yor. Petr. was bound apprentice by the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County to Robert Lane of the Parish of St. James Westmr. Leather Dresser to Serve his said Master until he should attain the Age of Twenty four Years.

That about Eighteen months since Yor. Petrs. said Master fail'd & went away by Night from his Habitacon and took Yor. Petr. with him as far as Newcastle where he left Yor. Petr. without any Person to take care of him, And Yor. Petr. was almost Starved before he could get back to Town to his Mother with whom he hath her ever since

That Yor. Petr. cannot learn where his sd. Master is gone to but believes he is in some part of Scotland & there being no Probability of his Return Yor. Petr. will be inevitably ruined unless Discharg'd from his said Master

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays that yor. Worships will be pleased to discharge him from his said apprentiship

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc. Francis Blandy
