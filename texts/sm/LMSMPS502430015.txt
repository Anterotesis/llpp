To the Worshipl. Bench of Justices

The Humble Petition of Wm. Nailer

Sheweth That yr. Petr. being a prisoner now In New Prison dischargd and lyes for his fees

Therefore yr. Petr most humbly prays That yr. Worships will order him to be discharg'd without fees he being In a most deplorable Condition & yr. Petr will most humbly Pray Etc
