To the Worshippfull her Majties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for the sd County

The humble Petition of Mary Daniel Widdow of the Hamblet of Wappin Stepney

Sheweth That yor Petitionr is out of all manner of business and notwithstanding is charged 2s:6d P Month to the Poor besides the Overate and by reason of many Losses is much reduced in her circumstances and not able to pay the same

Therefore humbly Prays yor Worshipps will be pleased to miligate her said Assessmt as to yor Worshipps Shall Seem meet

And Shall Pray

Mary Daniel
