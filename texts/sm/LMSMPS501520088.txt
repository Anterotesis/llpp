To the Rt Worpll his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Mary Bothaw in the City of London.

Shewth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex Bearing date the 23d Day of February last past Elizabeth Lane Widdo of Samuel Lane deceased with Samuel aged Eleven years and Thomas aged a year and an half Children of the said Elizabeth Lane by her Said late husband was removed from the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middx to the said parish of St Mary Bothaw alleadging the said parish [---] to be the place of their last legall Settlement That your Petrs doubt not but to prove that the said Elizabeth Lane never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Mary Bothaw whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Pass order and do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly prays your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmised and that the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls may appear before you and hear and abide the Determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall Ever Pray Etc.
