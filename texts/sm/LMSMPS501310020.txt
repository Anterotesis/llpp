To the Right Worpfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poore of the Hamblett of Hamton Wick in the parish of Hampton in this County

Sheweth That by an order Dated ye 20th. day of January Last under the hands and Seales of John Bateman and Leonard Hammond Esqrs. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this County acertaince male infant Child Was lately removed from that part of the Said Parish of Hampton called the towne of Hampton to the other part of the said Parish called Hampton Wick as the Place of the Last Legall Settlement of the said Childe

That your Peticons finde themselves aggrieved by the Said order of Removall of the Said male infant Child as aforesaid and humbly appeal to this Court agt. the Same

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray your Worpps would be pleased to appoint a day this Present Sessions for the Churchwardens Overseers of the poore of Hampton towne aforesaid pall other persons comenced to attend this Court this heare and abide the Judgment determinacon of this Count upon & touching the Said appeale

And Your Peticoners Shall pray etc
