To The Worshipful Bench of Justices Assembled for the County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition of James Saunders Sheweth that he being Bound an Apprentice to Joseph. Phillips Watch Maker Late of ye Parish of St. Mary Islington has been Deserted and Left Destitute By Yr. Said Joseph. Phillips for the Space of Six Months & upwards and My Uncle Living in ye County pd. not till now Endeavour to Procure my Discharge which it is hoped your Worships will take into consideration as shall to your Worships Seem most fill. the Mark of me James Saunders

A
