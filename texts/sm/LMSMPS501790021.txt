To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Ann Dowdall of the Pish of St. James Westm in the County aforesd.

Sheweth That yor. Petrs husband James Dowdall was bound over by the Worspll: Justices Saintlore to appear before this Honble Bench the last Quarter Sessions for not providing necessarys for yor. Petr. (the bring his wife) for keeping Compny wth. other women.

That yor. Worspps have been pleased to continue him upon his Recognizance ever since, and doth now remain upon the same That yor. Petr. is a very poor woman and must inevitably be a great Charge to the sd. Pish of St.James Westm Unless releiv'd by yor. Worspps.

yor. Petr: therefore most humbly pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to continue her sd husband upon his Recognizance untill such time As the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of St. James Westm shalle indempnifyed and said harmless from yor. petr. ( the being likely to be Chargeable.

And yor. Petr. Shall Etc pray
