To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by virtue of an order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Collins and William Bleamire, Esquires, Two of His Majesy's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of September 1796 Sarah Evans, Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggreived.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this County against the adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will bepleased to Appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will pray Etc.

Will Butler Josh Stutely John Moir Saml Instone Thoms Grangir Dignold Geo Stitchcock Thos Taylor Chr Gill Wm Vernon John Green} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor
