The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens had Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London

That by virtue of the Warrant or Order and the hands and Seals of [...] ph Hodgson Esqr and Saunders Welch Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace (Where of One is of the Quorum) for the County of [...] ddlesex aforesaid bearing date the Sixth day of November [...] thousand Seven hundred and Sixty, Mary Silvester Wife [...] Thomas Silvester was removed from the Parish of Saint [...] ulchre in the sand County of Middlesex to the said Parish of [...] saint Botolph without Aldqate London as the place of her [...] ot legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore humbly appeal against the Same Praying such Releif in the Premises as to this Court Shall seem meet
