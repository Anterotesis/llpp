To the Rt Worspll to her Matys Justices of Peace For the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Petition of Clement whereof the Parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the sd. County

That by Order of this Honble Court Your Petrs. by Reason of his Age and Condicon was formerly discharged from an Over Rate to the Poor of the sd Parish Yett new the less Your Petrs. Goods were Lateby distrained for the Over Rate by the Churchwarden Notwith Sanding he was Requested to the Courtrary by the Worspl Bastock Johnson Esqr. And On Fryday last Yor. Petrs. Goods Were Sold.

P Tho. Nash Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prayes Such Releife in the Prmisses as unto Your Worspps Shall Seem meet

And Your Petition shall ever Pray Etc
