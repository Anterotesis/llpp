To the Rtt: Worpfull his Maties: Justices of the Peace (Assembled in Sessions at Hicks's Hall) For the County of Middx:

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of ye. Churchwardens and Overseers of ye. Poor of ye. Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated ye. 11th: of this Instant January under the hands and Seales of Felix Feast and John Pery Esquires two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) Ann Scott Spinster was removed from the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as ye. Place of her last Legall Settlement. That your Petrs. find themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant Order of the said two Justices, And humbly Appeal against the same to this Honble: Court.

Your Petrs: therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps: wilbe pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of ye. Sd: Pish of St. Giles without Cripplegate, And all other & Sons Concerned to Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and Abide the Judgmt: & Determinacon of this Court upon and touching the y [...] Appeal And that Yor: Petrs: may have such Reliefe herein As to yor: Worps. Shall Seem meet Etc.

And yor: Petrs. Shall pray Etc.
