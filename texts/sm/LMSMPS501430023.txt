To the Worship full his Majties: Justices of the peace of the County of [...] now Sitting at Hick's Hall in St: John Street

The Humble Petition of William Wade of the Hamblett of Ratcliffe in the sayd County

Sheweth

That Yor. Petitioner it rated thirty two Shilling P Ann [...] to the poor of the said Hamblett of Ratcliffe which is more [...] proportion then any of his Neighbours pays and he being a verry poor man is Unable to pay the same

Wherefore yor. petitioner humbly pra [...] that your Worshipps will Order Such [...] releife in the premises ab to yor. worshipps shall seem meet And yor petitioner shall ever pray Etc.
