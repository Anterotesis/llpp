To the Honble. Bench of Justices

The Humble Pettr. of Henry Crane

Sheweth That yr. Pettr. is Indictmted and Most humbly begs that ye Honble. Bench will be Pleas'd to have his Tryal brought or having Given due Notice to his Prosecuters otherwise ye Pettr. must Lye Confin'd in a Deplorable Conditt Untill next Session. [---] and yr. Pettr as in Duty bound will for Ever Pray

Henry Crane
