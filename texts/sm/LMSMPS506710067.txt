To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions at Guildhall in King Street Assembled

To humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of James Paine and John Hall Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st of Febry 1777. Lucy Sapsard Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the Liberty of Westminster & County of Middlesex to the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty & County as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same.

Therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all persons concerned may then attend

Js Showell Jno Stone Ja Fisher Wm Richards} Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Han Square
