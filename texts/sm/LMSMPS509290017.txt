To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [---] Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens an Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Marylebone in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Wm. Tyler and R: Johnson Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County bearing date the 27th day of July one thousand seven hundred and ninety six Frances Horton aged near five years and Mary Horton aged near these Years Orphan Children were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the said Parish of Saint of Saint Marylebone as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in the present Session for the Hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners with pray Etc
