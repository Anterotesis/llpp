To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justice of the Peace for the County of Midds The humble Peticon of Joanna Jackson

Sheweth

That about June 1701 Yor Petr. [...] an Apprntice to Frances Farrand and Elizabeth Farrand of the [...] St. Giles in the Feilds in this County Mantua and Childs [...] for the Term of Seaven Yeares as by the Indre of Apprentice [...] appeares.

That about Six Months [...] yor. Petr. soe becauce an apprntice as aforesd. the sd. Frances F [...] whom your Petr. cheifly depended for instructon in the above [...] left her Habitacon and for as much as the sd. Elizabeth Farrand have in but very little business and refuseing to learne your Petr. the sd. Trade of a Mantua & Childs Coate makers you put be the intent of the sd Indre.

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes your Worspps to be pleased to Order that the sd. Eliz: Farrand may appeare in this [...] onble Cost this prsent Sessions to shew cause [...] your Petr. should not be Discharged from her sd. Apprnticehood

And your Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

P Tho: Nash
