To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Aylesbury in the County of Bucks

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order under the hands & Seales of St: Thomas Francklyn Barrt: & Ralph Hawtrey Esqr. two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the Sixteenth Day of November last past Thomas Higgins was removed from the Town of Uxbridge in the parish of Hillingdon in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Aylesbury in the said County of Bucks as the place of his last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs: thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgmt: of the said Two Justices humbly Appeale therefrom & pray that this Court will be pleased to order the Chappell wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Town of Uxbridge to attend this Court some Day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor: Petrs: shall ever Pray Etc
