To the: Rt: Worpl his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble peticon of Richd Hathaway Senr

Sheweth That your peticoner Did traverse an Indictmt Last Sessions and was bound by Recognizance to try it this prsent Sessions

That he hath beene Employed this prsent Sessions about his Sens Tryall for Murder which hath much Hundred his time & Cost him money See that he is reduced to pennry att Prsent

See therefore Humbly Craves his Recognizance may be refered till next Session Etc

p Jno: Knowles
