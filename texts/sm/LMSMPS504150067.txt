To the Rt Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General [---] Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Margt Westmr in the said County

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of the Right Honourable George Earl of Kinnoul and John Goodchild Esq two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the twenty seventh day of July 1751 William Lane and Mary his Wife were Removed from the parish of Stanes in the said County of Middx to the parish of Saint Margt Westmr aforesaid as the place of their last Legal Settlement Whereby your Petrs think themselves aggreiv'd and Appeal to this Court against the same

Crofts Attorney for Appellts Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all Persons concernd may then attend

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc

Middx} At the General Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middx at Hicks hall in St John Street in the County aforesaid on Monday the 9th day of September in the 25th Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the 2d. King of Great Britain Etc

It is Order'd by this Court That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Stanes in the County have Notice of this Petition and Appeal and that they and all Persons Concern'd do attend this Court on Friday next at Ten of the Clock in the forenoon to hear and Abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

By the Court Waller
