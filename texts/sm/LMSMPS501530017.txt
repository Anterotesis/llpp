Middx Ss To the Right worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of Sr. Clemts. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by Ordr. undr the hands & seales of John Cleeve & [...] dlon Esqrs too of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) waring [...] 18th - day of Aprill last [...] Robt. Knight wth. Sarah his wife & their four Children Alice , Jane , Robert & Dorcas were removed from the pish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the sd. County into the sd. pish of st. Clemts. Danes as the place of the last legall Settlemt of the sd. Robt. Knight Sarah his wife & their sd. four Children That yor. Petrs. doubt not to make it appeare to worspps that the pish of st. Clemts. Danes is not the place of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Robt. knight, his wife & four Children & doe thinke themselves aggreived by the Ordr. of the sd. two Justices & doe therefore most humbly appeale therefrom to yor. Worspps

Chimmor Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor Worspps to appoint a short day for the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. pish of St. Giles in the Feilds to attend yor worspps & to abide by Such Ordr. and shall be made touching the sd. Appeale

And yor Petrs. Shall pray Etc
