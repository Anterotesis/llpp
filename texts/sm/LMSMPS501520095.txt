To the Rt: Worppll: his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardns: and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dunton in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by Warrt: or Ordr: dated the 11th: day of January last under the hands and Seales of William Dobyns and Nicholas Phillpott Esqrs: two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx One Benjamin Foscott wth: his wife and Children were removed from the Parish of Harrow on ye Hill in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Dunton as the place of the last legall Settlemt: of the said Benja: Foscott

That Yor Petrs: find themselves aggrieved by the said Judgemt: of the said two Justices and appeale to this Court agst: the same, And Yor: Peticonrs are advised by their Councill that the said Warrt: or Ordr: of the said two Justices is defective in forme & in sufficient in Law

Yor: Peticonrs therefore pray that Yor. Worpps will be pleased to appoint a Short Day this prsent Sessions for the Church Wardns: of the Poor of the said Parish of Harrow to Attend this Court to Shew cause why the said Ordr should not be Quashed for the insufficiency thereof, and for all Partyes to attend Yor Worpps to heare and abide the Judgemt: and determinacon of this Court upon & touching the said Appeale and that the said Ordr: may be Quashed for insufficiency [---] or that Yor Petrs: may be otherwise releived according to Law & Justice

And Yor: Peticonrs: shall Pray Etc.
