To the Right Worshipfull His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx In their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quouum Unus) bearing Date the 17th Day of November last Mary Drinkwater Widow of George Drinkwater and their child Hannah aged about two years and three quarters were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London as the Place of their last legall Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving them selves aggreived by the Warrant and Judgement of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court for Relief in the Premisses

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
