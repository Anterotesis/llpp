To Yor Rt. Worshipfull His Maties Justices of Ye Peace for the County of Middx in their Genll Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon of Charles Fairchild & Thomas Beere Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Giles in the Fields in the said County on behalf of themselves & the rest of the Inhitants of the said Parish

Sheweth That Catherine Kemp widow of John Kemp Deced is a Poor impotent person not able to Work to maintain herself whereby Tho is become chargeable to the sd Parish And is so Likely to continue

That George Stafford of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County Pewterer Son of the said Catherine by a former husband Deced is a person of sufficient ability at his own Charge to releive & maintain his said Mother but refuses so to do

Yor Petrs: therefore humbly Pray that Yor: Worships will be Pleased to Order the said George Stafford at his own Charge to releive & maintain his said Mothe in such manner & according to such rate as to Yor Worships Shall seem meet to Assess

And Yor Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc Chas. Fairchild Thos Beere
