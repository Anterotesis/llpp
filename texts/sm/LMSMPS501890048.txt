Middx To the right Worship full his Majesties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx aforesaid at their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition of John Sutton of the parish of St. Giles Cripple gate in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitioner is charged much more stock to the Poor and Scavengers rates of the said parish than he humbley con [...] he ought to pay whereby he thinks himself agrieved and appeales against the said rates

Your petitio. therefore prays such relief in the prmises as to this Court shall seem meet

And your petition. as duty bound shall for ever pray Etc
