To ye Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Howeth Constable of Endfield in the said County

Sheweth That There was one Quarters Sallary due to your Petitr at Lady day last for passing and relieving Vagrants amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds

That yor Petr hath been at great charge and Expence this last Quarter and stands in need of his said money

Yor: Pet therefore humbly prays Yor: Worshipps will be pleased to order he may be paid his said Quarter Sallary

And Yor: Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Joh: Howett.
