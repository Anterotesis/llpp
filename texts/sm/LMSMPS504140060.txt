To the Rt. Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Blades of the Parish of Ashford in this County Constable

Sheweth That your Petitioner hath Served the Office of Constable in the sd. Parish for five Years. And that there is no Likely hood of a Court Leet being held in the Maner And he hath no way of being Discharged from his said Office but by an Order from Your Worships

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays to be Discharged, and he doth return John Palmer and Daniel Simms of the sd. Parish Farmers to serve the Office of Constable in his Stead; And begs an Order for one of them to be Appointed Constable of the sd. Parish; And as in Duty bound he Shall ever Pray Etc.

We His Majestys Justices of the Peace for this County Acting in and for Stanes Division Do Approve of the above Petition

Kinnoull Jno. Goodchild

William Blades
