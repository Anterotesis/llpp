To the Rt. Honble. The Lord Mayor of the City of London & other His Majesty's Comrs. of Goal Delivery for the County of Middx

The Humble Prayer of Arthur Davidson a prisoner in Newgate Sheweth

That your Petitioner stand comitted for sd. Goal for felony expresed in the said Warrt.

Yr petitioner humbly prays the benefit of the habe as Corpus Act and that he may be bailed tried or dissetroyed this Session

Arthur Davidson
