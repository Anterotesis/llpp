To the Right Worshipful his Majesties Justices of Peace for this County of Middx in their Genl. Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon of Christopher Martin & Francis Frye Constables and James Silver & John Coggin Headbouroughs of the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonrs. have served in their respective Offices of Constables and Headboroughs in the said Parish for these Two yeares last past & upwards

And that during all that time there has not been any Court Leet hold in the sd Parish neither is there any one likely to be held for two or three Yeares to comet as yor. Peticonr. are Credibly informed the Lord of that Mannor being a Minor

Yor. Petrs: therefore most humbly pray that they may be discharged from their respective Offices & others putt into their Roome

And yor Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Christopher Martin Francis Fry} Constables

James Selvester John Coggin} Headborough
