To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the. County of Middlx.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church- -wardens and Over Seers of the poor of the Parish of St Gregories by St. Pauls in the City of London

Sheweth.

That on the 12th of Septembr last there was one Edith Wade Widow was passed from [---] out of the Parish of St Mary Lee Savoy To ye Peticoners by virtue of a Was undr the hands & Seales of ye Worpll Ralph Marshall & Jermyn Joseton Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace of the County of Midds. by which year Peticonrs doe take themselves to be agreeved. And humbly Appal to this Honble Cort for releife [...] & pray a day to be heard

And yor Peticonr Shall pray Etc.

[...]

Jos [...]

Appeale of Churchwardens Etc of St. Gregorys. of St Maryle Savoy Ordr Confirm'd

12 Oct 1696
