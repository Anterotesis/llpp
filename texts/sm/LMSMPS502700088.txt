To the Rt Worshipfull his Majties Justices of Ye Peace for ye County of Middlesex in Generall Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of Edward Cave of ye parish of St. James Clerkenwell in ye Said County, printer

Sheweth That your Petr is Charged towards the Relief of the Poor and Scavenger Rates of ye Said Parish, much more than he ought to pay Therefore he thinks himself aggriev'd by the Said Rates and Appeals agt the Same

And prays yr Worships to appoint Some day this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor and Scavengers of the Said Parish to attend if Court to hear and abide ye Judgement and Determination of ye Court touching the Said Appeal

And Yr Petr Shall pray Etc Edwd Cave
