To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in general Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the said County

Sheweth. That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Char: Berex Aluced Popple Esqrrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Sixth day of March Last past Mary Spillet aged about 48 years was removed from the Hamlet of Hamersmith in the Pish of Fulham in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden where she is by Certificate there in recited owned & acknowledged to be Settled Inhitant Parishioner

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Agrieved by the Order of the said two Justices of the Peace Appeal to this Court, agt. the same, and desire to be releived therein

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

David Hughes Agent for the Parish of Covent Garden
