The humle Petition of Jane Trew eck of Broad Street Wappin in the Parish Stepney Widdow

Humbly Sheweth that your petitionr being now Aged & Sickley is not Capable of paying the Poors rates as formerly Therefore humbly beggs the Consideracon of this Honble Court of the Said rates may be taken off And.

Yor Peittionr as in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc:
