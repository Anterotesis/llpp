To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Richard Merrideth of the parish of St. Botolph Aldgate in the sd. County.

That yor. petr. is now a Penconr. of the Chest at Chatham on accompt of the losse of One of his Leggs and is Taped to all the Parish Rates in Dutys of the sd. parish But forasmuch as your petr. being a very Poore man and being Rendred wholly uncapeable of getting a Lively who ad is not able to pay the sd. Taxes

P Tho. Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayers yor Worspps to be pleased to Order that [...] pay for the future be Discharged from Paymt of any taxes or Dutys in the sd. Pish

One Yor. Petr. shall ever pray etc.

Richd. RM Merrideth his mrke
