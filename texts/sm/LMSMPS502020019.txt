To the Right Worpll the Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition of Frais Reynolds & John Hyrons Constables of the parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth That Your Petrs. have Served the Office of Constables in the said parish of Kensington for one whole year last part and the Lord of the Manner [...] for of abbots Kensington Earles Court in wth. said Mannor the said Parish of Kensington doth lie hath not for some time Past holden any Court Lees for the said Mannors And as by a Certificate under the Hand of John Diserote Steward of the said Mannor hereunto annexed is Dosh appear that the said Lord will hold no Court peel for the said Mannor this present year

Your Petr. therefore humbly pray to be Discharged at from their said Office And that Thomas Rice and William Penford two Substantiall house holders in the said Parish who are [...] Willing to accept the said Office may be Sworn in their room and Stead a [...]

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty be and shall sd pray etc
