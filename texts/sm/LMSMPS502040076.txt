To the Rt: Worpll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Sd: County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrt: under the Hands & Seales of William Gore & Danll. Combes Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for ye: Sd. County of Middx (Quor Un) bearing date the 15th. Day of June 1722: John Wilson Aged About 70 Years was Paston Your Petrs. from the Parish of St: Martin in the fields in the Sd: County as the Place this last legall Settlement

That Yor: Petrs: conceive themselves Aggreived by the Order of the Sd: two Justices & Appeale to your Worps: therefrom

Your Petrs: therefore humbly pray Your Worps: to Appoint is day to hear and determinate the Sd: Appeale

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
