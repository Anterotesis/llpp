To the Worshipfull His Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & overseers of the poor of the parish of Bristops Hatfeild in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant under the hands & seale of Bourne and Richard Darby Esqrs. Two of this Matys Justies of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (Quor Anno) Bearing date the Twentieth day of April last Thomas Taylor Elizabeth two wife and Joseph their son were Removed from the parish of Endfeild in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Bishop Hatfeild as the place of their last Legal settlement Whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be agreived

Therefore they humbly appeal to your worships against the Adjudgement of the said two justices and pray your worships will be pleased to order That the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Endfeild and all persons Concerned to attend this Court some day in this present session where and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said appeal Jno: Wills agent for the appealante And Yor. Petrs Etc.
