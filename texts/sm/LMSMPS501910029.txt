To the Right Worpll his Maties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon & appeale of Daniel Burford

Sheweth That your Peticonr: is rated and Assessed at the rate of Three Hundred Pounds Rent P ann to the Poores rate in the Parish of Chiswick for this prsent Year

Yor. Peticonr thinking himself aggrived w [...] the said Rate (by reason other his nighbours are not Charged to the height of their Rent) as your Peticoner is humble appeales therefrom

And Prayes that this Court will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of the said parish of Chiswick to Attend this Court some Day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petr as in Duty bound shall ever Pray Etc
