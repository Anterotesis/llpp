To the Right Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds in Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Petison & appeal of William Boyce

Humbly Sheweth That by an Order dated the 3d. day of December last under the Hands and Seals of Nicholas Jeffreys & Nathaniel Booth Esqrs: two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor nuns) Your Peticoner is adjudged to be the Putative Father of a Female Bastard Child named Martha lately born in the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of the body of one Susannah Lamb Single woman And thereby Your Petr. is Ordered to pay or cause to be paid unto the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish [...] some or one of them the sume of £2-10s-0d for the releife of the Sd. Format Bastard Child for the first four weeks after the birth thereof and for the charge of the Sd. Susannah Lambs lyeing in.

That your Petr. also shall weekly & every week after the exapiracon of the Sd. first four weeks pay or cause to be paid to the Sd. Churcwarden &Overseers of the Poor or to the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish for the time beinger their Successers moor one of them the sume (£0:2s-6d for & towards the releife & maintenance of the Sd. Female Bastard Child for so long & at such times as she shall be chargeable to the Sd. Parish untill she shall attain the Age of Eleven years (if she shall so long live) at which time your Petr.Should pay or cause to be paid to the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish for the time being to some or one of them the sume of £4-0s-0d for & towards the [...] putting forth Apprentice the Sd. Female Bastard Child to some lawfull calling and that your Petr. Should pay & discharge the Sd. Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor And the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Sd. Parish for the time being & every of them of & from all Debts damages & demands already demanded or contracted or that may hereafter be demanded or contracted of or by them or either of them for or by reason of the birth bringing upon maintaining the Sd. Female Bastard Child in any Case whatsoever.

And Lastly that Your Peticoner should find Sufficient Sureties to the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish or to some or one of them for the true performance of the Articles & Clauses contained in the Sd. Order

(turn over)

That your Petr. finds himself aggreived by the Sd. Order & humbly appeals to this Court agt. the Same.

Your Peticoner therefore humbly Prays that his Appeal may be Saved to him & adjorned to the next Generall Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for this County & that the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish & all other concerned may be ordered to attend the Court to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of the Court upon & touching the said Appeal & that Your Petr. may be releived herein according to Law & Justice.

And Your Petr. shall pray

S Lowth Attorny for the Appellant
