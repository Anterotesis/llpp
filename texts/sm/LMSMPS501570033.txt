To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of Richard Prettie of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That your Petitr. is rated & Assessed the Sum of Six Shillings P Quarter for and towards the relief of the poor of the said Parish

That your Petr doth not rent any house or lands within the said Parish & is only a Lodger in the house for which he is rated Which is very well knowne to the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish Who doe not withstanding threaten to Distregne yor Petr Goods for Non payment of the said rate

Your Petr: therefore finding himselfe aggrieved by the said rate & Assessmt. humbly appeals to this Court agt the same And prays a Day may be appointed this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Ovseers of the poor of the said Parish to attend this Court to hear & abide the Indgment Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeale

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc. R. Brettie
