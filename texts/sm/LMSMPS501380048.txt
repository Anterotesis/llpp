The Humble Petition of Edmund Parkin of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx humbly Presented to this Honourble Bench

Sheweth That your Petitioner keeps an Inn in the Parish And that on or about the First of January last A strange man and Woman came to your Petitrs: house with a young Child And enquired for Lodging Which they were Supplied with one Night P your Petitioner

That the Said ma and Woman went away From your Petitrs: House unknowne to him or any of his Family and left the Said Child behind them who hath ever Since been a charge to your Petitioner Who thereupon Applied himself to the Churchwardens of the Said Parish to Provide for the [...] same But they Refuse soe to doe Without an order from this Court.

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays that the Churchwardens of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell may attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew Cause why they should not provide for the said Child

And Your Petitr: shall ever pray Etc

Ed Parkin
