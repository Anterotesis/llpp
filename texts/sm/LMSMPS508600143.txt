To His Majesties Worshipfull Justices of the Peace for the [---] County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Geo: Stubbs and W Barrett Esquires two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the Eighth day of November 1790 Martha Reed Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster to the Parish of St. Mary a Lambeth in the County of Surrey as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves a aggrieved and hereby beg leave to appeal against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore a humbly pray that your a Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the Hearing and determining your Petitioners said appeal.

Carter & Simpson Sollrs. for the Appellts. No. 15 Bishopsgate
