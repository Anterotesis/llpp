[...] peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and appeale of Fowle of the parish of St Mary Whitechappell in the said County

Sheweth That your petr is rated towards the releife of the poor of the said Parish One Shilling P month And forasmuch as your petr hath a wife and two Children (one a Cripple) to maintain and in very mean and low Condicon which makes him unable to pay the said Rate

Your petr therefore prays your Worps to discharge him from paying to the poors Rate or otherwise as your Worps shall seem meet

And he shall pray Etc
