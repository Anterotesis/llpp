To the Rt: Worship full his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in their Genl. Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of James Gillespie of the Parrish of St Giles with Cripplegate in the County of Middlx.

Sheweth. Pannum; That your Petitioner rents a house in the said Parish and is charged to the Poors rate double in Proportion to his neighbours and also for one hundred Pound Stock altho it is very well known your Petitioner hath no such Stock for which he is charged fifteen Shillings and that your Petitioner conceiving himself agrieved humbly Appeal to this Court against the same [...]

Therefore Prays this Court will be Pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish and all Parties concerned may attend this Court some day this Prsent Genl Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching this Appeall

And your Petr. shall ever Pray Etc James Gillespie
