To ye Rt: Worshipll: His Maties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midd assembled in their General Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Wilmot John Sharp Charles Hussey William Duckett & Joel Iles Constables of the parish of St Giles in the Fields for the year 1724

Sheweth That there is due to yor: Petrs for passing of Vagrants during their Serving the said Office as by Cert [...] under the Hands of severall of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County may appear the severall Sumes of Money following vizt: To yor Petr Thomas Wilmot the Sume of One pound three Shillings & six pence to yor Petr. John Sharp the Sume of One pound Nine Shillings to yor Petr Charles Hussey the Sume of eighteen Shillings to Yor. Petr: William Duckett the Sume of One pound four Shillings & six pence to Yor. Petr. Joel Iles the Sume of one pound

Yor. Petrs: therefore humbly pray that yor Worships. will be pleased to Order such Sumes to be repaid to Yor Petrs.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Thos Wilmot John Sharpe Chas Hussey Wm Duckitt Joel Iles
