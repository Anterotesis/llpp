To the Right Worsppll. Mr Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Strutton

Sheweth That yor. Petr. stands now indicted in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions for keeping a disorderly house in St. Katherines

That yor. Peticoner being Extream Poor is not able to traverse the said Indictmt. therefore she is willing to confess the same and yor. Petrs goods are seized by her Landlord and had been gone from her sd house every since Christmas last. Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to consider her sad condicon and to lay such small fine upon yor. Petr as unto your Worspps shall seem meet.

And yr. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.
