To the Worshipful Sir John Hawkins Knight, and to the Worshipfull other Justices of the Peace & Oyer & Terminer at Hick's Hall.

The Humble Petition of William Hearn of Group Inn Lane Tallow Chandler.

Humbly Sheweth That your Petr: is most heartily concern'd to have incurred the Displeasure of this Honble, Court by not attending his Duty this Day as a Jury Man and hath been fined by this Honble. Court for his Neglect.

That your Petr. did on Tuesday duly attend his Duly but by an Unforeseen Accident was this Day a few minutes beyond his time of being impanelled and Sworn on the Jury.

That your Petitioner is ready if your Worships Please to be examd on Oath touching the truth of this Petition.

And humbly Prays that this Hon- Court will be Pleased to take off the Fine or mitigate the same in such manner as to your Worships Shall seem meet And your Worships Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.

Wm Hearn 3d. day of June 1778
