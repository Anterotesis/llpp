To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Gabriel Fenchurch Street London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot and Burford Camp Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Thirtieth day of July One Thousand Seven Hundred and Seventy James White and his Wife Jane were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Gabriel Fenchurch Street as the place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceived themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore do humbly appeal to this Court against the same and do pray that your Worships will appoint same day in this present Session for hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray John New Sollicitor for the Appellants.
