To the Right worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire & the rest of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dursley in the County of Glocester

Sheweth

That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of R Ford and [---] Bond Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in & for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the thirtieth day of September 1796 whereby Elizabeth Harvey was removed from and out of the parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the said County of Middlesex to the parish of Dursley in the said County of Glocester as the place of her last legal Settlement by which your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be appointed for hearing & determining of Cheer said Appeal and that your worships would make such order therein as to Justices appertains

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
