To the Rt. Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition and appeal of the several persons whose Names are here under written of the Parish of St Johne Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That Your Peticoners are Overcharged for ye rent of then houses in the poors rate and also for one Quarter more then they have Lived in the. same whereby they conceive themselves aggrieved and Appeal agst the same

And pray such relief in the premises As to this Court shall seem Meet And Yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Wm Humphrys Tho: Heankin Wm Moor
