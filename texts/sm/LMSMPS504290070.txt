To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middx in their general quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or order Under the hands and Seals of Richard Ricard & Thomas Jervis Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx bearing date the Twenty Second day of June last Martha Ingram[---] (an Infant of the Age of two years) was removed and Conveyed from & out of the parish of Saint Mary White chappell in the County of Middx into the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Martha [---] Ingram

your Petr. Conceiving themselves aggireved by the said Order of the Said Two Justices of the peace humbly appeal to this Court against the Same and pray this Court will appoint a day this present Session for the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the parish of St. [...] Mary White chappell aforesaid and all other parties Coutemas to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petr. will ever pray Etc

I. Clare Sollicitor for Aldgate Parish}
