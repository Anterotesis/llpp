To the Rt. Worpfull his Majts Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seales of N: Collins and George Murray Esqr Two of his Majests. Justices of the peace for the Citty and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middx bearing date the Fourteenth day of December [...] last past Whereby Anne Heron apoor woman was removed from the parish of St. Margarett Westminster in the said County to your petrs; said parish of St. Martin in the Feilds. And your Petrs: humbly conceive they are wronged and Aggrieved thereby

And your Petrs. [---] humbly appeale to your Worps to appoint a day to hear and determine these me

And your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
