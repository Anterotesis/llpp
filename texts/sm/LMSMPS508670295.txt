To the Worshipful Justices now assembled in their general Sessions of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dionis Backchurch London

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of John Spiller and T. T. Cotton Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex, one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the seventh day of September instant Sarah Wood Widow and her two Children, lawful Issue namely Joseph aged Six years and upwards and William aged there Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Dionis Backchurch as the place of their last legal Settlement Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order. Appeal against the same, and pray such Relief as to your Worships shall seem meet, and your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Dod Thos Lancaster} Overseers

Maidw Gibbs Robert Morris} Church Wardens
