To ye. Rt. Worshippll. his Maties Justices of ye Peace for the County of Midd Assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of John Perry

Sheweth That by indenture hearing Date ye: 7th Day of August 1723 Yor: Petr: was bound Apprentice to James Edgerton of ye parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the sd. County Cordwainer

That Yor: Petrs: said Master hath very much beat & abused Yor Petr: & some time since conveyed yor: Petr: on board a Ship in Order to go to Ireland but being driven back by Stress of Weather left yor. Petr: at Liverpoole in Lancashire without anything to Subsist

That Yor: Petrs. said Master went from there to Ireland & hath not been heard of since

Yor Petr: therefore humbly prays that this Court will be pleas'd to discharge him from his said Apprentiship

And yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc.
