To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Writen in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass warrant or Order under the hands & seals of the Gifford John Mener Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peas for the said County of Middx bearing date the 20th. day of July last Whereby Charles Scotchyfur and Elizabeth his wife & Jane the [...] Child aged about Six years were Removed from & out os the Paris of Saint Giles in the Feilds in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. James As the place of their last Legal Settlement By which your Petitioner think themselves Agrieved

And Therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for Fearing & Determining the said Appeal and that will persons Concerned may Attend at the Same time

And your Petitioners Shall Ever Pray

Saml. Seddon Vestry Clerk
