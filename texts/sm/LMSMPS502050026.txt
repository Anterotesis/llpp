To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genall Quarter Sessions of the peace now holden in and for the Said County

The humble Petition of Neals Kencher of the Hamlett of Spittle Fields in the Said County Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticoner [...] is one of the Sureties of Henry Royer who Stand indicted for breaking the windows of Nathaniel Sprurgeon and who traversed the Said Indictment as the [...] genall Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden for the Said County of Middlesex in Order to try the Same this present Sessions

That the Said Henry Royer has been ill of a feaver for this three Weeks last and has kept his Bad and is now So very weak thas he can Searely Stand and tis to be fear'd that he should be exposed to the Air it might end anger his life or occasion a Releph of his Said Distemper which may be attended with very ill Consequences, the truth of which your Said Peticoner is ready to make appear by a Proper Affidavit

Your Said Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worships will be please to resquit his Recognizance a [...] that the tryall on the Said Indictment may be pai [...] off untill the next Sessions

And ye Peticoner Shall ever pray Etc
