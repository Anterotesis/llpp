To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hayes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order Under the Hands and Seals of N. Conant and John Scott Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 13th. Day of May last past John Kite and Sarah his Wife and Mary this Infant Daughter Aged eleven Years were removed from the Parish of [x] Acton in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Hayes in the County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant to Appeal against the same

Edward Knibb

One of the Churchwardens for the Parish of Hayes on behalf of himself and the other Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Hayes
