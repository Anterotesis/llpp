To the Rt. Worspl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon And Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the sd County

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the Said County (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Second day of July 1737 Charles Williams Aged about Six year's and Mary Williams Aged about four year's were removed on the 11th day of the Said Month of July from the Parish of St. Paul Shadwell in the Said County to the Parish of St. George abovesaid as the place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Peticoners think themselves aggriev'd by the Said Pess Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices And humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same

Therefore humbly pray Your Worships to Appoint a day in this General Quarter Sessions that all Parties may then Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yr. Petrs Shill ever Pray

Sam: Cowper Attorney for the Appellants
