To the Rt. Worspl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Qr. Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Thomas Jones and William Cross Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d day of February 1736 William Robinson an Infant about the Age of two years was removed from the Parish of St Paul Shade [...] in the Said County to the Parish of St. George abovesaid as the place of his Last Legal Settlement

That your Petr think themselves aggrieved by the Order & Judgment of the said two Justices and humbly appeal to the Court against the Saml.

Therefore humbly pray your Worsps will be pleasd to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Paul Shadwell may attend this Court Some day this present General Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Yr. Petrs shall ever pray Etc

S: Cowper Attorney for the Appellants
