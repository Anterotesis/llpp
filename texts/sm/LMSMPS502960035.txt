Petitioner of Ellenor Wright otherwise Curly [...] { To the Worshipfull the bench of Justices Now [...] att Hicks hall

Sheweth That yr. Petior. is Now in Custody Charges by Mr Wright her husband for Creakens of his Windows to which hee pleads Guilty am for herselfe Saith that thee is one person in law with him and therfa [...] not Account able

Therefore humbly pray the favor of this Worshipfull Court to be Discharge and shee will pray Etc

by over (Ellenor Wright other wise Curly
