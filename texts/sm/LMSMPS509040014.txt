To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of H. I. Pye and I Warner Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in [...] and for the County aforesaid one whereof being of the Quorum bearing Date the twenty ninth day of October last Past Elizabeth Codd Wife of Emery Codd Soldier in the 80th. Regiment of fort then abroad was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County to the Said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Emery Codd and Elizabe [...] his Wife Whereby Your Petitioners conceive [...] selves to be aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that Your Worships Will be pleased to Appoint a Day in this Present Session for [...] hearing and Determing the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will Pary etc

Herb & Robertson Sobr. for the Appellants}
