To the Worsll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. in the Said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of John Ellis & Christian Cole Esqrs. two of his Matie Justices of the Peace of & for the City and Libty of Westmr. and Said County of Middx bearing Date the twenty fourth day of July last Sarah Macklaine Daughter of Thos. & Margt. Macklaine (aged about four Years) was removed [...] out [...] and from the Parish of St. James Westmr. in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. as the place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves- agrieved by the Judgment of the paid two Justices- humbly appeal to you Worsps against ye. Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worsps will be pleased to Order, That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. James Westmr. may attend this Court Some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal.

And Your Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
