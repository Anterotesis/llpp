To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of J. Merceron and Danl. Williams Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twentieth day of July 1798 Edward Knight was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Liberty of the Rolls as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said Two Justices Appeal to this Court against the same and humbly Pray that that the said Appeal may be heard this present Sessions and that a time may be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof

And your Petitioners will ever Pray
