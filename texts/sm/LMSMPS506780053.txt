To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. George Bloomsbury in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Cox and Thos. Preston Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirteenth day of February Instant Richard Wakefield was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Hendon in the said County to the said parish of Saint George Blooms bury as the place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will pray Etc

Herbts Robertson Sol. for the Appellts.
