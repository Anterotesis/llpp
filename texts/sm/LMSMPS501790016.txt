To the Rt: Worshippll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of James Chetwood

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petitr hath been confined in New Prison in this County an Evidence for these Two Sessions past And that last Sessions Your Petitr convicted two persons for Felony and Burglery and compleated his Evidence

That Your Petitr is ready to Starve in Prison having noo Friend to assist him in the World nor any Further Discovery to make.

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays Your Worshipps will. be pleased to Discharge him from his said Imprisonment And Yor: Petitr Shall Ever pray Etc

J. Chetwood
