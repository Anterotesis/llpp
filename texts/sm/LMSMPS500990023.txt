To the Rt. Worppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of John Moulton of Spittlefeilds in this County Vict

Sheweth That some time past Your Peticonr: tooke Jane Square a poor child of the Liberty of Norton Folgate (by the consent of the then Overseer of the poor there) to be An Apprentice Allowed by two of her Maties: Justices of the Peace of this County, But See it is the Said Apprentice hath Divers times Pilserred Sevall parcells of money from the peticonr: & hath bought which the Confessed the bought wth the peticonrs money to his great damage & will be his Ruein if not discharged from her Indenture of Apprenticehood

Dr & J Ingham Wherefore Your peticonr. humbly prayes an Order of this Court for the Overseers of the poor of the Liberty of Norton Folgate to Attend here at a Certain day to Shew Cause why the Said Apprentice of Ye: Peticonrs Should not be discharges from Yor. Peticonr. Service As to Yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet Etc.

And Your Peticonr. Shall Ever pray Etc John Noulton
