To the Rt Worpll Her Maties Justices of the Peace for [...] County of Mid [...]

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Basnett

Sheweth That your Peticoner Rents a House in the Parish Eling in the said county of Middx And is Rated for the Poores [...] Assessment much more then in Proportion to the Rent which shee now pays being likewise more than any of her Neighbours is rated in the like case

That your Peticoner finding herself Aggni [...] by the said Poores Rate

Humbly prays to bee related Premises as to your Worsps great Wisdom Shall seem meet and Convenient

And yor Petr as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
