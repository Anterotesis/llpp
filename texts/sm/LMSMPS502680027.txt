To the Rt Worspll: his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary le Bone in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt: under the hands & Seales of William Dobyns & Thomas Inwood Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing date the twentieth day of April last past Thos: Finch and [...] four Children Vizt: Sarah Eleven years Elizabeth Eight years John three years & William one year & a Quar: old were removed from & out of the parish of Hendon in the said County to the said parish of St. Mary le Bone as the place of their last legall Settlemt

That yor: Petr: thinking themselves aggrieved By the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale against the same.

Yor: Petrs: therefore humbly pray that yor Worsps will be pleased to Ordery the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of [...] Hend on may attend this Court some day this present Generall Sessions to hear & abide ye Judgement & Determination of this Court touching the Said appeale

And yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton
