To His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of Robert Paddon St Brentford in the County of Middlesex Draper

Sheweth

That Your Petitioner was appointed to the Office of one of the High Constables for the Hundred of Eltherine in the said County at the General Session of the peace held for the said County in the Month of June 1780 and hath Continued to serve the Office ever since

That the said Office is very Irksome to Your Petitioner and a great hindrance to his business and that he hath applied to the sevl. Magistrates acting in the sd. Division for their Consent to his being discharged which they readily agreed to

Your Petitioner therefore Prays that Your Worships will be pleased to discharge him from the sd Office & make and Order for the attendance of One of the persons named in the list here to annexed to take upon him the sd Office

And Your Petr. will Ever Pray Etc
