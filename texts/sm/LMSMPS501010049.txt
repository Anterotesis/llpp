To the Rt Worspll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Prsinct of St Katherines in the said County

That Your Petr are Over rated in the Rate for the Mained Soldiers and Marriners Queens Bench & Hosapitall And for Passing of Vagrants

That the sd Prsinct is very Small and Usually Inhabited by People of low Circumstances soe that Yor Petrs: are not Able to pay the sd Rates

P Tho: Nash Your Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor Worshipps to be pleased to Relieve them in the prmises as onto this Honourable Court Shall be thought most meete

And Yor Petr Shall ever pray Etc
