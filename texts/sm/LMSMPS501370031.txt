To the Rt: Worshipfull her Maties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ann Bagster

Sheweth That yor: Petioner is Rated 1s:6d: P quarter to ye Poors rate in the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney and 7s:6d to ye. Over rate, that your petr is very poor and having three Children to maintaine is utterly unable to pay ye. same

Your Petr: therefore humbly prays that you would be pleased to order that She may be discharged from ye payment of the said rates

And yor petr shall ever pray Etc
