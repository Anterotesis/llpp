To the Worshipfull Farwell Esqr.

The hble peticon of John Bennett the Younger

Humbly Sheweth } That yor. Worships Petitioner having been bound over in a Recognizance and no Bill being found-And being a poor Soe and I not hired to a place as Postillion to James Dashwood Barrt. and being obliged to go out of Town tomorrow or quit his place which will be of very great damage to him

Humbly prays yor. Worships leave to withdraw his Recognizance this day

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc
