To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices Peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Peticon of. John Slegg

Sheweth

That there being Due to your petr for Wages from one Henry Guy of the Parish of St Mary White Chappell in this County Carpenter The Sum of One Pound Six Shillings & Three Pence wth he absolutely refuseing to pay stands bound before the Worshipp [...] Justices Constable for his the sd Guys Appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Session

That yor Petr is A Poor Servente And hath been putt to yr Charge of abt Thirty Shillings by the sd Guy so recover ye said wages And for as much as the sd Guy being A Trouble some Letigions Person and Endeavours to yor Petr out of his sd Wages as long as in him Lyes

P Tho: Nash

Your Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that he may be paved his sd Wages as unto yor Worshipps Shall Same meet

And yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc John Slegg
