To the Worshipful Justices now assembled in their General Sessions of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Dionis Back church London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Rich. Ford Esquires, two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex dated the sixteenth day of August last, Elizabeth Pullen and her Child James Pullen aged thirteen months or there abouts were removed from the Parish of Saint George Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dionis aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order, Appeal against the same and pray such Relict as to your Worships shall sum meet And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Wm Lampkin John Mathew} Church Wardens

John Dod Jon Tombs} Overseers
