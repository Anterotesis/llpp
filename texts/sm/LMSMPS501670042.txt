To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx Now Sitting Etc Hick hall

Sheweth

That Peter Boursat of the Parish of St. Leonard Shore-ditch Did Petition this Honble. Court Octor. 19th. Anno: 1717 Seting fourth his Poverty as not being Able to Pay Any-thing towards the Maintenance of the Poor therefore Your Petitioner Humbly prays this Honble. Court will be pleased to A point A Day when his Cause may be Determined & Your Petitioner Shall Ever Pray (Etc)
