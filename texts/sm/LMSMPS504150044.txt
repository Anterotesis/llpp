To the worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace held in & for the said County assembled

The humble Petition of William Davis of Poplar Shipwright

Sheweth That [---] William Wilkins by Indenture bearing date the 29th: day of September 1749 did put himself Apprentices to your Petitioner for Seven years to learn his said Art of a Shipwright

That the said [---] William Wilkins very grossly misbehaves himself towards your Petitioner by frequently running away and abcenting himself from your Petitioners duly & family for a Month or More at a time

Therefore your Petitioner most humbly prays this Court will be pleased to discharge him from his said Apprentice or that your Petitioner may be relieved as this Court shall see meet

Wm Davis
