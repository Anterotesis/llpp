To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of th Peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 16th. day of June 1794 Jane Girdler, Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the said Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the said County of Surrey as her last legal settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises And that your Worships will be pleased to respite the hearing and determining this their appeal until the next General Session of the peace

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Carpenter, things Arms Yard Solicitor for the Appellants
