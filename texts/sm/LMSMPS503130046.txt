To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of W: Hayton and Si: Michell Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (whereof one is of the Quorum) for the County of Mddlesex) bearing Date the thirty first Day of October last past Mary Knox Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of her last legal settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices and pray your worships will be pleased to appoint some Day this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioner shall ever pray

Wm. Head agt. for the Appellts.
