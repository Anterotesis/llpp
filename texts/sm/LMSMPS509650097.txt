To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell.

Sheweth. That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Collick and T: Hunter Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of November 1799. Sarah Matter wife of John Matter who has deserted her was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and that all persons concerned may then attend.
