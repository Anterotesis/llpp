To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hick's hall Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth, That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant bearing date the 24th day of Septr. under the hands & [...] of Robert Jackson and Ed: Grose Esqr: Jno of he Majesty's Justices of the said Peace for the said Cunty of Middx (Quor Unus) John Cadwell was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappel in the said County of Middx to the Said Parish of St: Martin in the Fields as the place of his last legall Settlement, Whereby your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves to be Agrieved

Therefore your Petr: humbly Appeale to your Worships against the Judgement of the said two Justices, And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this Quarter Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Mary White Chappel to attend this Court, to hear and Abide the Judgement & determination of this Court tou [...] the said Appeale.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
