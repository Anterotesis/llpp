To the Rt. Worpfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Overseers of the poor of the Rolls Liberty in the parish of St. Dunstans in the West in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order bearing date the tenth day of Febry 1725 under the hands and Seales of Rico Jeffreys and N: Booth Esqrs then two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quorum Unus) whereby Sarah Gadbury Widow was removed from the parish of St. Andrew Holborne above the Barrs in the County of Middx into the Said Liberty of the Rolls in the Said County as the place of her last legall Settlemt. and your Petrs. were by the Sd. Order required to received to receive her & provide for her as their own parishoner (as by the Said pass Order may appeare)

That your Petrs. finding themselves aggreived by the Said Order do humbly appeale to this Courts and pray that a day may be appointed for the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Sd Parish of St. Andrews Holborne above the Barrs to attend this Court upon the Same

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc.
