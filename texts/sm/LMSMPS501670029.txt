The humbly Pettetion of Johanah Herring to the Woorshipful bench for John Sering and Oliver fann Wm poore Distresed Prisoners whom your worships having Discharged Your Pelletioner being a Very poor Woman and haveing nathing to help my Self nor where withall to pay his faces To Do humbly desire ye Woorships to ReLease him for he never was Guilty of any fact In his Life and If he Lays for Ever I can no way Pay and I hope yr Woorships will take Itt Into Consideration and yr Pelletioner Shall be for Ever to pray for You

Johanah Herring
