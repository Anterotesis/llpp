To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Order under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Williams and W Blackmore Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace (one whereof being of the Quroum) bearing Date the Twenty Second Day of September last Ann Horn Widow was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Christ Church in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the City of London as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly pray Relief in the premises and that the Benefit of this their said Appeal may be saved unto them till the next General Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County.

And your Petitioners shall every pray Etc. Joshua Cox Sollr for Appellts.
