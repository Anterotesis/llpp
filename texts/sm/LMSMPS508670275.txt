To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present Quarter Session of the Peace (by Adjournment) assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals The Reverend Dr. Brackley Kennett and Edward Read Esquire Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace acting in & for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day October 1791. Thomas Pooler was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear & determine the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

James Dyson

Sollictor for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint James Westmr.
