To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Waltham holy Cross in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of Burf Camper and John Sherwood Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 30th day of January last Ann Liptrap Singlewoman was removed from and out of the Hamlet of Mile End Old Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstans Stepney in the said County of Middlesex To the said Parish of Waltham holy Cross as the Place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal have therefore Appealed against the same and humbly Pray your Worships to make such Order in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc. John Jessopp Attorney for the Appellants.
