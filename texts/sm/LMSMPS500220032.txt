To the right Worp: their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of James May

Sheweth

That yor Petr: was put out apptice by the Churchwarden of the hamlet of Lyme house to one Fonas May, but now may It pleasd yor. Worp. the sd. Fonas is absconded & his wife in Newgate, and yor, Petr. hath now where to goe

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly before heth Yor: Worpl: to order his discharge from his said Mar: that yor: petr: may have [...] liberty to Seeke for another Mar:

And yor: Petr: Shall pray Etc
