To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of John Barnjum of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County Carman.

Sheweth

That your Petr. is rated towards the releife of the poor of the Said parish much more then he is able to pay, be reason of his great age & past his labour. and haveing a wife very old, which makes him unable to pay the Said Rate.

Therefore most humbly pray your Worps to discharge him from paying the Said Rate or otherwise releive him in the premises as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And your Petr Shall pray Etc John Barnjum
