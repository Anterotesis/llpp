Worpll: his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the [...] of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions ass [...]

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Bartholomew by Exchange London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and [...] of Richard Woodward and Moses Goodyeare Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (Quorn unns) bearing date the Sixth day of October Ano Din 1718 Mary Sheffield Widdow of Robert Sheffield deceased was removed from the parish of Chelsea in the said County unto the said Parish of St. Bartholomew by Exchange alleadging the said Parish to be the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petr conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said two Justices do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom And Pray That Your Worpps will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chelsea to appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And they Shall Ever Pray Etc
