To Sir John Hawkins Knight and the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Session Assembled

The Petitioning Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Hamlet of Brentwood in the Parish of South Weald in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal bearing Date the nineteenth Day of August last under the Hands and Seals of Sampson Wright and George Stubbs Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex[---] Smart (a Lunatic) with Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the said City of Westminster to the Parish of Brentwood in the County of Essex as the Place of the last legal Settlement of Joseph Smart and Elizabeth his Wife by which your Petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved

Wherefore your Petitioners humbly appeal against the said Order of Removal and pray your Worships to appoint a Time for hearing the same

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jno: Hawkins Brentwood Sollr. for the Appellants
