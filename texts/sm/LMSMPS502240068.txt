To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices now assembled at Hicks Hall in the County of Middx

The humble petition of Francis Geary husband of Judith Geary

Sheweth

That by an Order of this Court dated ye. Eleventh day of July 1724 it was ordered that Mrs. Frances Seymour Should pay unto Mrs. Judith Geary upon Monday then next following halfe of the Court Fees of the then last Generall Session of the peace & halfe the Court Fees of that present Session and alsoe the Sume of fifteen pounds before the then next Sessions for halfe a Years Allowance payable at Midsumer then last past

And That the sd. Mrs. Seymour has in Obedrence to the sd. Order payed unto Your petitioners Wife all the Fees Soe Ordered to be paid as aforesd. But refuses to pay [---] hir [---] the sd. fifteen pounds soe ordered as aforesd.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly prays that the sd. Mrs. Seymour may be Ordered by this Henoble Court to pay unto Your petitioners wife the Sd. Sume of fifteen pounds forthwith

And Yor. peticoner shall ever pray Etc.
