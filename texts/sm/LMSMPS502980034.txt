To the Worshipfull His Majestis's Justices of the peace for the bounty of Middlesex Assembled in Generall Sessions at Hick's Hall in the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le Bone in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by Vertue of a Pass Warrant of Order Under the hands and seals of Miles Harper and John Ellis Esqrs: two of His Majisty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty Fifth day of January last Hannah Minns Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone as the place of her last legall Settlement Where by Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Bays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons Concernd may Attend at the same time

And Your Petrs: Shall ever pray and so forth

Thos: Gilbert John Lane} Church Wardens
