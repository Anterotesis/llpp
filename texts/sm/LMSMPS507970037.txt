To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Amesbury in the County of Wilts.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order or Pass Warrant- under the Hands and Seals of sir Sampson wright- Knight and William Addington Esquire two of his- Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County- of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the eighth day of April 1785 Phoebe Lewis Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Amesbury as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices do humbly appeal against the same and pray that the hearing and determining of the said Appeal may be Adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held in and for the said County of Middlesex and that the Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in the said next Session for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes and all other Persons concerned therein to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the said Court touching the said appeal

John Turner. Attorney for Appellants
