To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Bloomsbury in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of H. Cosser and Joseph Moser Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County bearing date the thirtieth day of July now last past Eliza Brassinden Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret in the said City and Liberty to the said parish of Saint George Bloomsbury as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of of the said Two Justices and their said Order And Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the Hearing and determining the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners will Pray Etc

Herbt: Robertson Sol: for the Appellants
