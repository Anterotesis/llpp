To the Worshipful The Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Your General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Dartford in the County of Rent

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of George Hubbs and W: Barrett Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex bearing date the sixth day of December one thousand seven hundred and seventy nine Jane Bayles aged two Apears and upwards was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Dartford in the County of Rent as the place of the said Jane Waylis's last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved

Therefore humbly pray Your Worships to appoint a Day in this present General Quarter Session to hear and determine their said Appeal
