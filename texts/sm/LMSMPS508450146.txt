To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Faith under St. Paul in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W: Blamire and John Crest Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex and City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Seventh day of March last, Robert Griffis was removed and conveyed from the said Parish of Saint Anne within the said Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Faith in the City of London as the place of his last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and therefore appeal to this Honorable Court against the same,

And pray that this Honorable Court will please to appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to make such Order therein as may be first.

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

W: Bennet Solicitor for the Appellants.
