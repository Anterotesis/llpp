To the Rt Worpll her maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Tho: Wilkes High constable of the Tower Division in the County of Middx

Sheweth That your Peticoner served the sd office for Nine yeares & upwards but is now Incapa [...] ted by Infirmity of Body any longer to officiate

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays to be Discharged of the said office and humbly presents the persons here under menconed for one of them to succeed him

Mr Raphael Dubois Mr. John Furmis Mr. Tho: Miller

And your Peticoner shall ever pray etc

Tho: Wilkes
