To The Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows London Wall in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and seals of J. P Shard Esqr. and John Crosse Esqr. two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eighth day of May 1755. Elizabeth the Wife of Henry Kimpton and Elizabeth their Daughter aged three Years Were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the said County of Middx into the said Parish of Alhallows London Wall as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petrs conceiving themselves aggreived. by the said Order or Warrant of the said two Justices of the Peace Do humbly Appeal against the same and Pray That the hearing and Determining of this Appeal may be Adjourned untill the next Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for the said County of Middx and that the Court will Appoint a day in the said next Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty of Saffron hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents and all Persons Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

Fra. Salt Attry. for Appellts.
