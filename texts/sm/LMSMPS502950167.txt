To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in the Generall Quarter Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Sepulchres London

Sheweth That one Elloner Freeman by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace within the said County of Middx (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Sixth day of this Instant October was removed from the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the parish of St. Sepulchres London aforesaid Alleadging the said parish is the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Elloner Freeman; That your Petitioned doubt not to prove that the said Elloner Freeman had no Legall Settlement in the said parish of St. Sepulchres London whereby they Conceive themselves agreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices, And do humbly Appeal to your Worships thereupon

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day to hear them in the premises and Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middlesex to hear and Abide the Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Abraham Page Ch Wdn Isaac Harris Ch wden Francis Adam Overseer
