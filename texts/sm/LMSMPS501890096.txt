To the Rt: Worspll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Higginson on the behalfe of Daniel Dellavell.

Sheweth That the sd Danl. Dellavell Stands indicted in this Honble Court for Assaulting one William Roberts.

That the sd Dellavell is now become a Bankrupt as by the Gazeette hereunto annexed will appear So that [---] the sd Dellavell is not able to come before this Honble Court in Order to withdraw his Recognizance

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble Bench to be pleased to Respite the [...] Dellavell Recongnizance till next Sessions.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.
