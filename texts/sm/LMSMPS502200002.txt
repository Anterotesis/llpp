To the Rt. Worpfull her Majts. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in the said County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth

That Eliz. Barker was by an Order bearing date the Twenty Seaventh day of June last under the hands and Seales of Richard Milner Esqr. and John Cleeve Esqr. two of her Majesty of the Peace for the said County removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County unto your Petitioners said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields to be Maintained and Provided for by which Order Your Petitioner (with humble Submission) think themselves wronged and aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore do humbly Pray your Worships to hear and determine the same

And your Petitioners as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Ed. Sanderson
