To the Rt. Worshipful his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middle sex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of Joseph Scot of Islington in the said County Victualler

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Mathew Hewitt & William Cotesworth Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor- unus) bearing date teh 5th. day of September last past Your Petitioner was Adjudged the putative Father of a- Male Bastard Child born on the Body of Judith Southby Single woman within the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County, and by the said Order, amongst other Things, Your Petitioner was Ordered to Pay divers Summs of Money towards the relief & and Mantainance of the said Male Bastard Child in teh Said Order of Adjudication particularly Express'd and Contain'd.

Your Petitioner finding himself Aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices of the peace, humbly Appeals against the Same to this- Honourable Court, and Prays a day may be Appainted this present Quarter Sessions for the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. James Clerkenwell and all other Persons concern'd to Attend this Court, to hear and Abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc.

Joseph Scot
