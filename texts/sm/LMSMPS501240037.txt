To the Worpful her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Smalbone of the Pish of St Giles Creeplegate

Humbly sheweth That your Peticonr aged above Eighty years & my wife above 60 years & not able to help our Selves are almost Starved for want of Succour, for the Officers of the Said Pish have taken 21s 6d of the Pension we had formerly So that there 6s of the months pension abuted, therefore he hum- bly implores your Worpps to order yt they may have it restords to them again

And in So doing your poor Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
