To the Right Worpfull his Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Thomas the Aposlle in the City of London

Sheweth,

That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seales of Robert Jackson & Edward Grose Esqrs. Two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor un) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of February last past Mary the Wife of Thomas Pearson with four Children (Vizt) Elizabeth aged about Eleven Years, Thomas aged about five years, Daniel aged four years & Samuel aged about Ten Months the lawfull Fine of the said Thomas Pearson & Mary his Wife, were removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapple in the said County unto our Said Parish of St. Thomas the Aposlle as the Place of their last legall Settlement.

Therefore your Peticoners conceiving themselves agreived by the said Order, do humbly appeale to your Worps therefrom, & pray that your Worps will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in this County & all other Persons concerned may attend this Court Some day the next Generall Sessions of the Pease to be holden for this County, to hear and abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeale.

And yor. Petieners Shall ever Pray Etc.
