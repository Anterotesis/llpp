To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in Generall Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble peticon and appeale of the Chappellwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Town of Uxbridge in the Said County of Middlx

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or Order bearing date the 27th. day of March last under the hands and Seales of Charles. Bere and Lockyer Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for this County That Ann Irman an Infant aged about Six yeares Elizabeth Irman an Infant aged about four yeares & Sarah Irman [---] an Infant agad about Eighteen Months Children of James Irman & Mary his wife were removed from the parish of Ealing in this County to the Said Town of Uxbridge ad being the place of their last legall Settlement Therefore your petrs thinks themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant or Order & appeales against the Same

And prays your Worpll to appoint souce day this present Sessions to hear & determine the Said appeale & all persons Concerned to. attend this Court

And your Petrs shall pray Etc.
