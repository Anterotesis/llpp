To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order dated the fifth day of March One thousand Seven hundred & thirty two under the hands and Seales of Richard Ricards & Andrew Osborne Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said County (quor unus) Katherine Walkinton Widdow was removed & Conveyed from the Hamblett of Bethnal Green in the Said County to the Said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate as the place of her last legall Settlement.

That your Petr apprehend themselves to be aggreived by the Said Order of the Said two Justices And doe appeale to this Court against the same.

Your Petrs therefore pray this Court would be pleased to appoint Some day in this present Sessions for the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Said Hamblett of Bethnall Green and all other persons concerned to attend this Court touching the said Appeale And that your Petrs may be releived therein according to Law.

And your Petrs shall pray
