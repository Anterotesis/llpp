To ye. Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Phillis [...] Rainsford

Sheweth That your Peticoner about a Month agoe was comitted to the House of Correccon in this County for Selling Ale & Beer without a Lycense.

That the next Day after your Peticoners comittment one Anne Thompson who Dwelld in the House with her & broke her house Robbed Your Peticoner of Money and Goods to the vallue of Fifty Pounds For which the said Thomson is now in Newgate.

That there is a Bill of Indictment preferred against the said Anne Thomson for the aforesaid Burglary & Fellony But your Peticoner by reason of her Confinement is altogeather Uncapable of giving her Evidence without An order from this Court

Your Petr therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to grant an Order of this Court Directed to the Governour of the aforesd Prison yt she may be admitted to Goe to the Old Baily to Give her Evidence agt the said Thompson On Thursday at ten of the Clock in ye forenoone.

And Yor: Petr Shall Ever pray Etc
