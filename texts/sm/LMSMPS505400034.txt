To the Worshipful the Chairman And other his Majesty's Justices of the peace in Session assembled.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Hopkins Keeper of Clerkenwell Bridewell

Sheweth That the House in which your Petitioner lives in Bridewell wants some Repairs in the Siling, she Rain having come in and beat down the Ceiling, and is damaging the flooring

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays your Worships that you would be pleased to Order that the County Bricklayer may repair the same and do such further little Jobs as may be found really necessary for the preservation of the House and [...]

And your Petitioner shall ever pray

Thos. Hopkins 18 Oct. 1764
