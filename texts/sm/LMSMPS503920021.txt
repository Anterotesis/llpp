To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Michael Le Queen London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and seals of two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th day of November 1748. Adrian Honnor Margaret his Wife and their Child named Adrian aged about Fifteen Months were pass'd from the Parish of St Mary White Chappel in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Michael Le Queen London aforesaid Alledging the last Legal Place of Settlement of the said Adrian Honnor Margaret his Wife and their said Child Adrian to be there, and your Petitioners Conceiveing themselves Agrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices Do Humbly Appeal therefrom and Humbly pray your Worships

Bighall Attorney For the Parish of St Michael Le Queen {

Would Appoint some day this Present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden For the said County to hear this Appeal and that your Worships will make an Order that the Chuch Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St Mary White Chapel and all Parties Concerned it and about the Premisses may then Attend hear and abide of he Final Determination of your Worships

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc
