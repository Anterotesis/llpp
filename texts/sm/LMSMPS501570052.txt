To the Rt: Worshipfull his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwarden's and Overseers of the Poor of ye parish of West Draiton in the Said County of Midlx

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Francis Alexander and Francis Byng Esqr, two of his Maties, Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quorum Vnus) bearing date the Sixteenth day of July last, Mary Oxton as Foy was removed and passed from the parish of Hillingdon in the Said County to the Said parish of West Draiton as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Peticoners think themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices, Humbly appeale to this Court and pray that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Said parish of Hillingdon and all other parties comerned here is may be ordered to Attend this Court Someday this Sessions to heare and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court upon and touching this appeale

And Your Peticoners (as in duty bound) Shall pray Etc

P Rd. Watkins
