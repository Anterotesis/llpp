To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Session house for the said County Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty [...] Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Chas: Triquet (and D. Wilmot Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 10th day of January last Elizabeth Lewis was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of her last legal settlement where by Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order, And pray that your Worships will Please to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal, and that all persons Concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners Shall pray Francis Dischan [...] y Robert Baldery} Church Wardens

John Grieve Jacob Woodward } Overseer of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
