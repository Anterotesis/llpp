To the Worll: his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in [---] General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seals of Bask. Johnson and Robt. Jackson Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the 15th. day of November Ano. Dui 1727. Sara Sumner Aged about Four Years and Nine Months the Daughter of James Sumner and Jane his Wife was removed from and out of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the said County of the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the Place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves Agrieved by the Judgment of the said two houses humbly Appeal to your Worships against the same.

Therefore humbly Pray your Worsps. will please to Order, That the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney may att end this Court some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
