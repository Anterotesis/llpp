To the Worsll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth.

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt. under under the hands and Seals of Henry Harpur Esqr. and Sir John Colebatch Knt. two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the said County of Middx bearing Date the Nine & twentieth day of July last. Mary Withers Aged about two Years was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County [...] the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. a the place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petr. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray your Worsps. will be please to Order; That the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields may attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Detemi- -nation of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
