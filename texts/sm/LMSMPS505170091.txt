To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Gen [...] Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 24th. day of September in the Year of our Lord 1762 James Manson and Eleanor his Wife & were removed from the Parish of St. Mary Whitechaple in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex adjudgd the last legal Place of Settlement of the said James Manson and Eleanor his Wife to be there And your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices do humbly appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships would be pleased to appoint some Day in this Present Sessions And that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Mary and all Parties concerned in and about the premisses may then and there hear and abide the final Dece [...] of your Worships therein.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Thomas old John Long land} Churchwardens

Wm. Killingworth [...] sson} Overseers of the Poor
