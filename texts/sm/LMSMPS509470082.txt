To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seal of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex one of whom is of the Quorum Bearine date the eleventh day of december last Hannah Atkins Widow of Abraham Atkins was removed and conveyed from the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the Parish of Fulham in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint George Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said Order and humbly Pray relief in the premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day for the Hearing and determining their said appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Jno Clement Sollr, for the Parish of St George Middx
