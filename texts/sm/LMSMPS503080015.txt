To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of th peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint John at Hackney in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Exelbee and Richard Farmer Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the first day of March last past [...] Thomas Hilliard Ann his wife and [...] their three Children (that is to say) Thomas aged Sixteen Years Elizabeth aged three years and Sarah two Years old were Removed from the Hamlett of Bethnal Green in the said County of Middlesex to the sd. parish of Saint John at Hackney as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Peticoners conceiving themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to this Court against the same Praying such relief in the premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And Your Peticoners Will Ever Pray Etc
