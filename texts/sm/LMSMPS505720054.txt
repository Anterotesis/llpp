To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor and Parish of Newbury in the County of Berks

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hand and Seal of George Wright Esquire One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of February One Thousand Seven Hundred and Sixty Seven Ann Payne was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex To the Town of Newbury in the said County of Berks as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order And Appeal to this Court against the same And humbly Pray Your Worships to hear and determine the same

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Td: Bury Sollr. for the Appellants.
