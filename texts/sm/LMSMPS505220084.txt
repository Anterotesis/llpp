To the Worshipfull this Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Phillip Dyot and Stepn Clarke Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of May 1763 Sarah Hill Widow of John Hill deceased was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of her last legal Settlement; Where by your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved, And Appeal to this Court against the same

Wherefore your Petitioners Pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal; And that all persons concerned may be Ordered than to Attend. And your Petrs. will Pray Etc Saml. & Seddon Solicitor for the Parish of St. James Westmr.

Pickadilly Opposite St. James Church 17th. May 1763.
