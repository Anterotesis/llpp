To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Mary Berry or the Pish of St. Paul Shadwell in the County aforesd Widow

Sheweth That the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the Pish aforesd have assesed yor. poor Peticoner Eighteen Shillings to [...] the Releife of the poor of the sd Pish for this prsent year

That yor Peticoner is a very poor woman and rerts but a house of four pounds P Ann and is likewise charg'd to the Scavenger and other P prochiall Taxes and having a Child to maintain is not able to pay the sd Taxes So that the thinks herself very much aggreived thereby

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly prays your Worspps to be pleased to order that She may be discharged from all the sd assessments

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
