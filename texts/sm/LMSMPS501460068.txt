To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of William Good win Constable of Endfield in the sd County

Sheweth That there was one Quarter Salbary Duck your Petit at Midsumer last amounting to the Sum of Fifteen pounds for passing and Relieving Vagrante That your Petitr is a Poor man and hath a Family to mainteyne and hath beene at great charge and expence in Conveying Vagrants

Your Petitr. therefore humbly prays Your Worspps will be pleased to order that her may be osLeslly paid the said Moneys As onto yor Worspps shall feeme meete

And yor. Petitr. shall ever prayer

Wm Goodwin
