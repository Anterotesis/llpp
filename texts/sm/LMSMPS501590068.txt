To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers. of the poor of the parish of Greenwich in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated the Ninth Day of November last under the hands and Seales of Robert Dennet & St Perry Esqrs: two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (Quor unus) Kathwell Vaughan with her three Child on were removed from the Parish of St Botolph with out Aldgate in the sd County of Middx to the said Parish of Greenwich as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Rachaell Vaughan & her Children

That your Peticoners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices & Appeal against the same.

Your Petitrs therefore humbly pray a Day may be appointed this present Sessions for all parties to attend to hear & abide ye Judgments & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

John Bell Tho Norman} Churchwdns
