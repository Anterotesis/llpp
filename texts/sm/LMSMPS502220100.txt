To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Church warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parishe of Saint Andrew Holborne above barrs in the sd. County

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and seales of John Fuller and William Cotesworth Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unns) bearing date the twelfth day of September last Martha Caule relict of Timothy Caule decd was removed and passed from the Parish of Saint [...] Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middx to the said parish of Saint Andrew Holborne above barrs as the place of her last Legall Settlement

Your Peticoners finding themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and Pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate and all other Parties concerned here in may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Your Peticonrs: Shall Pray Etc
