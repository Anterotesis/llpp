To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices now setting at Hick's Hall

The humble petition of agnes Cooper

Most humbly sheweth That Yor. poor Petitior: is now in New prison by yor. Worships, fining her forty Shillings which is more money than your Petitis: hath or can any way raise, she having already pawn,d all the Cloaths the hath by which means yor. petitis: is unable to C Coath herself fitt for any Service And Unless yor. Worship,s in yot. great goodness doth think fitt to mittagate her fire, she much lye in prison, or come to the Parish

There fore humble beggs you would consider her condition and she as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc
