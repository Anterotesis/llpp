To the worshipfull his majties Justies of the peace in Genall Session assmbled

The Hble petion of John Theoderick of the parish of White Chappell

Sheweth That your petioner was bound over in a price Sessions last to answer Timothy Roll for the peace before Justice Brain That your Petioner hath waited ever Sessions Since and Cannot hear his Name Called and is informed his Recognizance is not returned

Your Petioner most Hbly pray's your worships would be pleased to Order the Clerks to Search if the recognisance is returned (or not) that your petr. may be Discharged from it

And your Petr. as in Duty bound Shall pray

John Theoderick
