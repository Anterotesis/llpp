To the Right Worspl. Sr. Edward Clarke Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London and others his Fellow Justices of Goale delivery now Sitting att Justice Hall in the Old Bayley

The humble Peticonr of Sarah Walker new a prisoner in his maties Goale of Newgate

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. was in July last maliciously Indicted for High Preason Clipping the Current Coyne of this Realms

That your Petr. being Sick in the Country could not come to Town in Order to take her Tryall but See Soon as She was able did [...] herselfe before German Ireton Esqr. one of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

That yor Petr. is att this time very Sick and weave and if continued in Prison will Tens to her utter rime

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes your Worspp to be pleased to Grant that She may be tryed this next ensueing Sessions or otherwise be admitted to bayle

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
