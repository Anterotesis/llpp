6 Oct 1768

To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Michaelmas General Quarter Session of the Peace in the Year 1768.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Lowe

Sheweth That your Petitioner was favoured with your Worships Licence the last year for continuing the publick Entertainment at Marybone Gardens, which hath been used for several years past in the Summer Season for Publick Musick and Licenced by your Worships according to the directions of the Act of Parliament for regulating places of publick Entertainment.

That whilst your Petitioner shall be so happy as to be favoured by your Worships with the continuance of that Licence he shall Study to preserve good Order and Decorum therein in Strict conformity to the said Act, And your Worships directions in that behalf.

Your Petitioner therefore makes this his humble Application to your Worships to Grant him your Licence for the year ensuing.

And as in duty bound he shall ever pray Etc.

Thos Lowe
