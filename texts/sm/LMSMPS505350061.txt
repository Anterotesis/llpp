To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles Without Cripplegate London

Shew That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Christopher Scott & Walter Berry Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) Rachael Minus the Widow of John Minus deceased was Removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant Or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray that your Worships Will he pleased to appoint a Day of this present Session that all Parties may Attend to hear & Abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

Thos. Stagg Sallr. for the Appellts
