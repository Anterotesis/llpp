To the Rt. Woppf [...] of the [...] for the County [...]

The humble Peticon [...] of the Churchwarden Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn London.

Sheweth, That by Warrt. or Ordr. dated the Twenty fifth day of February last past andr. the hands & Seals of Robert Kyrby & Thomas Frampton Esqrs. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) Ruth Pettit wife of Edward Pettit & their three Children Elizabeth Pettit aged about Seven years Ruth Pettit aged about six years & Anne Pettit aged about seven weeks were removed from the Hamlet of Ratcliffe in the said County to yor. Peticonr. Said parish of St. Andrew Holborn London as the place of their last legal Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs. com [...] themselves aggrieved by the said Warrts. or order of the said two Justices, and humbly appe [...] agat. the Same to this Honble Court.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to ordr. the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Hamlet of Ratcliffe and all other psons [...] to attend this Court some day this present sessions, to hear & abide the Judges [...] detelimnaw [...] this Court upon & touching the sd [...] Appeal, and that yor. Petitions. may have such relief herein into yot. Wopps Shall seem meet Etc

[...] Peticonr shall pray Etc.
