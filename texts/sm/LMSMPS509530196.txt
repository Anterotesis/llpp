To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of [---] Rice Davis and C Rohde Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Twenty third day of June now last past Jane Graham Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the said County unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Jane Graham

That inasmuch as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and the removal and conveyance of the said Jane Graham in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships to Grant them such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
