To the Rt. Worshippfull her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the pish of St. James Clerkenwell in this County

That by Warrt: or Order under the hands & Seales of Jos: Jorry & Roger Smith Esqrs two of her Maties: Justices of the peace for this County bearing date the 23rd. day of March last Thomas Ellitt & Martha his Wife & Richard Sarah & John Ellitt theire children were passed from the Hamblett of Bethnall green to the Pish of St: James Clerkenwell of Bethnall green to they pretend to be Settled & your peticonrs. thinking themselves agreived

Dr [...] J: Ingham Most Humbly prayes an Order of this Court for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblett of Bethnall -green & all parties concerned to Attend this honble: Court at a certaine time & to abide the finall Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal or otherwise order as yor Worpps shall seem meet Etc

And yor. peticonr Shall pray Etc Robt Bellson Edmond Howard} Churchwardens
