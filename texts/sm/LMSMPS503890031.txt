To the Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the peace in their Genral Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble peticon of John Wilkins Paviour

Sheweth

That by Articles dated the 16th. day of march 1746 yor petioner Contracted with yor. Worships to Repair amen'd & maintain the payment of Brentford Bridge for the Term of 11 yeares and yor Worships Covenanted to pay yearly the sum of Â£9 on every 29th day of September yearly in every year

That there is due to yor Petioner the sum of puse pounds on the 29th day of September last

Humbly prays yor worships to order the payment of the same
