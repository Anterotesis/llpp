To the Rt Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church wardn. and Overseers of the Poore of the Hamblette of Spittle fields in the Parish of Stebun heath ats Stepney in the County of Middx

[...] mbly Sheweth

That by vertue of a Warrant here unto annexed beareing date 22d: day of July last past under the hands and Seales of Two of his Maties: Justices of the peace of the said County One Alice James was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County aforesaid unto the said Hambt there to be provided for according to law That the last Settlemt: of the Said Alice James was in the said Parish of St. Clement [...] as Your Peticoners can make Appeare.

Your Petitconrs therefore most humbly entreats your Worpps that you would be pleased to Order the said Alice James to be reconveyed to the Said Parish of St Clemt Danes unlesse the Church wardens and Over seers there of can Shew just cause to the Contrary

And they as in Duty bound Shall Pray Etc
