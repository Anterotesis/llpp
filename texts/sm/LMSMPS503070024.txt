To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of Thomas I feild of the parish of Ewell in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That by an order or adjucation under the Hands & Seales of George Andrews Esqr. & Sr Thomas Reynell Bart. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Dated the tenth Day of January last past Yor. Petitioner was Adjudged the Putative Father of a Female Bastard Child born on the Body of one Elizabeth Hawkins at Hampton in Middx by which said order or Adjucation he thinks himselfe agrieved & Doth appeale agt. it. [---] which said order or Adjudication was Served on your Petitioner on Saturday last the 22d. day of February instant & not before

Yor. Petitioner therefore humbly prays yor. Worships to appoint a Day for hearing & trying the Merits of the said Appeall

And yor Petitioner as in Duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc

Thos Highfield 26th Febry 1734
