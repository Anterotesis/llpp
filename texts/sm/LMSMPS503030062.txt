To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall [...] Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of James Fraser and Thomas Deveil Esqrs two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the Said County (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of July last past Ann Senior Singlewoman aged about eighteen yeares was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the Said County to the Parish of Saint Luke alledging therein that the Said Parish of Saint Luke is the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petrs being aggrived by the Order of the Said two Justices of the peace humbly Appeales against the Same & pray that your Worps would be pleased to appoint Some day this present Session for all concerned to attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of the Court touching the Said Appeale

And your Petr Shall Pray Etc

Samll Mansfeild Agent for the Appellants.
