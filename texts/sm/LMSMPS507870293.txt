To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of D: Walker and Joseph Beete Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the twenty third day of July last Jane Whiteheadhead a Female Child Aged about seven Months was removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Female Child Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved And

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order and Pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determination of the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all Persons concerned do them attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
