To the Right Worshipfull his Majestices Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace assembled

The Humble petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Bethnal Poor in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Jones and Richard Farmer Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the thirteenth day of March last past William Garden and Jane his wife were removed from the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Bethnal Green in the said County as the place of their last Legal Settlement

By which said Order and Judgment of the said two Justice your Petitoners conceived themselves Agrieved and Humbly Appeale to this Court against the same praying such relief in the Premisses as to your worships shall seem meet

And yor Petitioners will ever pray Saw Sadlen Agent for the said Churchwardens and Overseers
