To the Right Worsppll her Matys Justices of the peace for ye County of Midds.

The humble peticon of Jeremy Boreman.

Sheweth That the Office or Imploymt. of Keeper of New Prison being vacant by the Death of Captain Abel Weeker And yor. petr. having noe Impoymt. or incuarbrance whatsoever that may hinder him from discharging his Duty in the Said Office.

p Middley Yor. Petr. Therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to bestow on him the said office or Imploymt. wherein he will faithfully discharge that Ernst with all duty and Reguare to yor. Worspps.

And yor Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc.

Jeremy Boreman
