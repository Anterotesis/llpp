To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chadlington in the County of [...]

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hand and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Third Day of March Last John Smith and Tabitha his wife were removed from the Parish of Cranford in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Chadlington in the said County of Oxford the said Justices there by Adjudging that to be the Place of their Last Legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Two Justices and Do humbly Appeal there from.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly Pray that Your Worships would be Pleased to Appiont some Day in this Pleasent to Session to hear this their Appeal and to make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of

the Poor of the said Parish of Cranford in the said County of Middlesex and all Parties concerned in and about the Premisses may then and there hear and Abide the Determination of Your Worships therein

And Your Petitioners shall Ever Pray Etc.

}Churchwardens.

}Overseers of the Poor.

Ja: Kedwell Agent for the Churchwardens and Ou' [...] of As Poor of Chadlington
