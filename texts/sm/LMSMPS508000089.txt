To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the new Session House Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of James Croft and Williams Gascoign Esquires two of his Majesties of the peace Acting in and for the County aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of August one thousand seven hundred and Eighty five Elizabeth Athey the wife of Pearce Athey with her three Children namly Susan aged Eleven years Frances aged Eight years and William aged Seven years were removed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Cowet will be pleased to Appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And that all all Persons concerned may be then directed to Attend

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd Christmas Jno French } Churchwardens and

G. C Dalmaine John Lucas } Overseers of the Poor

A Morritt }
