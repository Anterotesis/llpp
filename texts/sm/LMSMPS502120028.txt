To the worshippfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx now in Sessions assembled

The humble Petition of John Blain [...]

Sheweth That your Petr. [...] Entred into Recogn before Mr. Justice Newton for the Appearance of one Robert Keyne the Cast Sessions to Answer the Complaint of Juggy Murphey who hath not prferred any Indictmt. agt. the sd. keyne either last Sessions or this Prsent as Yor. Petr. is informed and the said Robert keyne is since withdrawne be that your Petr. cannot tell where to find him

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly prays yor. Worshipps that he may for the reasons aforesaid have Leave to withdraw the said Recognizance or that your Petr. may be kept from any damage that may happen on Accot. of the same till he can find him out

And Yor. Petr Shall pray Etc.
