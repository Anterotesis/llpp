To the Right Worshipfull his majties Justices of the Peace for the County of middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of wapping in the County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass warrant or Order under the Hands and seals of Richard Richards and Richard Farmer Esqrs Two of his majties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one of the Quorum) bearing date the 3oth day of July last past John Feathers and william Feathers the lawfull Issue of John Feathers and Mary his wife were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary WhiteChappel in this County to the said Parish of Saint John of wapping as the place of their last legall settlement

That your Petrs Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal to this Court against the same

And pray that your worshipps will be pleased to Appoint a day this present Session that all Parties may attend to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And shall pray Etc

Benj : Bass agent for the appellants.
