To the Worshipful Thomas Lane Esqr Chairman & the rest of the Justices of the Pea [...] at the General Quarter Session of the peace of our Lord the King now holden for the County of Middx at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County Aforesaid

The humble petition of the Stephen Bennet

Humbly shewth. That the Petr. by Indentures of Apprentiship bearing D [...] the four & twentieth day of Augst.1732 was bound to one Thomas [...] of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the County of Middlesex Silver spinner for the Term of seven years from thence next ensuing to tern the Art. Trace or Mistery of a silver spinner, by which Indentures yr. petr. master the St. Thomas Horn covenanted to find & [...] Meat Drink Apparel Washing lodging [...] during the sd. term.

That yr. Petr. St. master hath not performed his sd. Covenant in the sd. Indentures mentioned during the Send yr. Petrs. Served his s [...] Master at his House and also refused yr. Petr. any Support while yr. Petr. was at his instance in Custody in Bridewell tho y [...] was then in a starving Condition & destitute of any support so the yr. Petrs. sd. Master hath Frequently without any just proves beat yr. petr. in a most ment & barbarous manner

Your Petr. therefore Prays that for the Causes aforesd. he may be discharged from her sd. Apprentiship and have such other Relief as to yr. Worships shall seem Meet.

And yr. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc

his Stephen Bennet Mark
