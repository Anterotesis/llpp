To the Worshipfull It is Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in their Generall Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St George Hanover Square in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant on Order under the hands and Seales of John Milner and Robert Hucks Esqr two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the twenty Sixth day of October 1732 Constance Hilton was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of St Giles's in the Fields in the County of Middx to the parish of St George Hanover Square in the said County as the place of her last legall Settlement Whereby the said parish of St George Hanover Square think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays this Court will Appoint a day for hearing the said Appeal and that all pson concern'd may attend at the Same time

And Your petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

S Parry Vestry Etc
