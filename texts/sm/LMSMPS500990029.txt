To the Right Worpll: her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of James Dunkerley of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticonr: Son. William Dunkerley is bound an Apprntice to one Robert Cole of the Parish of St Mary White Chappell in the Said County Weaver, for the Terme of Seaven years by a Writeing Bearing Date the 23d of February 1705 And that the said Robert Cole is now and hath beene for a year and a half last past a Prisoner for Debt in White Chappell Gaole, and is not ways capeable to provide for his said Apprntice, who is now maintaine by your Peticonr.

Your Peticonr therefore humbly prays that the Said Willm. Dunkerley your Peticonr. Son. may be discharged from his Said Wlaster for the reason aforesaid. And for that the sd. Writeing called in Indenture is not Indented, which your Peticonr hath here ready to Produce

And yor Peticonr. Shall Pray Etc James Dunkerley

James Dunkerleys Peticon for his Son Wm Dunkerley to be discharged from his Apprenticehood Wed 10 cl
