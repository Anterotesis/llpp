To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Margaret in the City of the Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of I Girdler and Stephn. Clarke Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth Day of November last, Joseph Jarvis, Jane his Wife and their Child George aged about Eleven Weeks were Removed and conveyd from and out of the Liberty of Saffron Hill, Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbourn in the said County to the said parish of St Margaret as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Joseph Jarvis, Jane his Wife and their Child George.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray Your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeal.

And Yr. Petitioners shall Pray Etc.

Jno. Burford Titus Hilson} Churchwardens

Jno Cartwright William-Ride} Overseers.
