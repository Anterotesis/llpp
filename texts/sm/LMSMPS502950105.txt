To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled at their Generall Quarter Sessions for the said County at Hicks Hall in St: John Street

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Ann's within Aldersgate London

Shew That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N: Booth & Robt: Hind & Esqrs: Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of July last Thomas Hopkins Elizabeth his wife & their Daughter named Mary Jannie Aged about Five Yeares were Removed from the Parish of St. Andrews Holborne above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of St: Anne within Aldersgate London as the place of their last legall Settlement By which order of the said Two Justices Yor. Petrs: Conceive themselves to be aggreived & humbly Appeal therefrom to this Court against the same

And therefore your Petr: pray that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the said parish of St: Andrew Holbourne above the Barrs in the sd County of Middlesex & all other Persons Concern'd may Attend this Court this present Sessions and stand to & abide the Determination of this Court touching the Merritto of this appeall

And your Petr: shall ever pray Etc

Jno Adams John Makepeace Henry Niblett John Crick} Church wardens Overseers
