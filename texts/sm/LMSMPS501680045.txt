To the Rt: Worpll. his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx in their Genal Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Ovrseers of the poor of the parish of St. Butolph without Aldersgate in the County of Middlx.

Sheweth That by ordr. under the hands & Seals of Thomas Rand and John Whetham Esqrs. two of his Maties Justice of the peace for the Said County (Quor. un) bearing date the Twentieth day of February instant Richard Hawkshead, Elizabeth his wife & their three Children (vizt.) Mary, John & Sarah were removed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said parish of St: Butolph without Aldersgate in the County of Middx aforesaid as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. think themselves aggreived, by the ordr. of the said two Justices & hope to make it appear that the Said Richard Hawkshead Elizabeth his Wife & their Said three Children have not any Settlemt. in the Said Parish of St. Butolph without Aldersgate in the Said County And therefore appeal from the Ordr. of the Said two Justices to yor. Worpps

Yor. petrs. therefore humbly prays yor. Worpps to appoint a day for the Churchwardns. & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County to attend yor. Worpps & to abide Such ordr. as Yor. Worpps Shall make on hearing the Sd. Appeal

And yor. petrs. Shall pray Etc.
