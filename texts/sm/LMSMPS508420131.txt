To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary lebone in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of J. Ayliffe and W. Bleamire Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th. Day of December [...] one Thousand seven hundred and eighty eight Sarah Evenden Widow of James Evenden deceased with her three Children namely Sarah aged five Years Esther aged two Years and upwards and Ann aged about one Month were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Mary lebone as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly appeal therefrom.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will be pleased to hear them in the Premises and to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Thos. Martin Solr. for the Appellants
