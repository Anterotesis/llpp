To ye: Rigt: Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye. peace, for ye: County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeale of ye. churchwardens and Overseers of ye. poor of ye. Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditchs in ye. sd County.

Sheweth That by a Warrtt: or Order under ye. hands & Seales of John Fuller & Felix Feast Esqrs: Two of his Matis. Justices of ye. peace for ye. sd. County (Quor unns) beareinge date ye. 29th: day of January 1714: one Anne Geareinge an Infant about Six Months old was removed from ye. Parish of St. Giles wth. out Cripplegate in yee. Sd. County to ye. Sd Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch Therein Settinge forth that ye. sd child was house there: and that John Geareinge & Ellenor his wife father & Mother of ye. sd child then Lodged there.

Your peticoners therefore findeinge themselves agreived wth: ye. Judgmt: of ye. Sd Two Justicies of ye. Peace humbly Appeales to this Honable: Court agt: ye. sd Warrtt: or Order and prayes that ye. Benefitt of this appeale may be Saved to ye. Appell unto this Genrll: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace, and that Your Worsps with be pleased to grant an Order that ye. churchwardens & Overseers of ye. poor of ye. sd parish of St: Giles wth: out Cripplegate may attend some say ye. next Generall Sessions of ye. peace to be holden for ye. sd County to heare & abide ye. Judgmt. & determinacon of ye. Court touchinge this appeale;

And Your peticoners shall ever pray Etc:

John Bowyere John Horsefeild} Churchwardens
