To the Rt. Wpfull the Justices of the peace at their Genl. Quarter Session Holden at Hickes hall in & for the County of Middx Assembled. on day of April 1732

The hble peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Bennets Sherehogy in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & Seals of Nicholas Jefferyes & Robt Hind Jim. two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing Date ye 11th day of Janry last one Elizabeth the wife of George Brooks & George their sone was pass'd from the parish of St. Andrews Holborn above the barrs in the County of Middx To the parish of St. Bennets Shere hogy London at the place of their last Legall Settlemt.

Your Peticoners humbly Conceiving themselves Agreived by the sd Warrt & Judgmnt. of the said two Justices Therefore humbly Appeal to this Wpfull Court for relief in the premisses

And your Wps peticoner shall Each pray Etc

No. 1
