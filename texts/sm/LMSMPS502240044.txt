To the Rt Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeale of Henry Ratcliffe of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That your Petitioner Rents a House in the said Parish for which he is Charged to the poor and Scavengers Rates much more then the real Rent which he pays whereby he thinks himselfe aggrieved and humbly appeales against the same

Wherefore Your Petitioner most humbly prays this Court will be pleased to grant him such releife in the Promises as to Your Worship's shall Se [...] meet

And Your Petr Shall ever pray Etc

Hen Ratcliffe
