To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order of removeal under the Hands & Seals of Ralph Harwood and Nathaniel Chandler Esqr. two of His Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing at the Twe [...] fith day of May 1735 Hannah Wing [...] the Wife of [...] fied with William Wing field their Son aged about Eighteen Months were removed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the Said County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the said County of Surrey as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners think themselves agrived and humbly appeal against the Said Warrant Etc Order and Prays that your Worships will appoint a day this Sessions for the hearing and determining the Same and that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all other Persons concerned be Summoned to attend this Court to hear and abide by the Judgemt: & Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

Nath: Byntell Agent for the Appellents}
