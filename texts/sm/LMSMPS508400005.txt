To His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Edmund the King in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Robert Butler and David Walker Esqrs two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth day of December 1788 Mary Daniel therein described to be of the Age of Thirty Years was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County to the said parish of Saint Edmund the King in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Removal

And therefore Pray that your Worships mee appoint a Day to hear and determine this then Petition & give them such Relief therein as to Your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners will Ever Pray
