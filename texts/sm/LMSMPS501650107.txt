To the right Worshipfull his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the parishoners of the parish of Stanmore Magnae and the sd. County

Sheweth That by an order under the hands & Seales of Rupert Browne & Nicholas Philpott Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the sd. County (Quor Unus) Hugh Peplo his wife & Children are removed from the parish of Harrow on the Hill in the sd County to the sd. parish of Stanmore as the place of their last legall Settlemts.

That the sd. Parishoners of Stanmore conceive themselves aggreived by the sd, Order of the sd. [...] Justices & doe humbly appeale there from & pray a day may be appointed att that next Sessions for hearing the sd. Appeals

And Your Peticoners Shall our pray Etc.
