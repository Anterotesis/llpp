To the Rt. Worpfull her Majts: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall quarter Sessions at Hickshall.

The humble Petition & Appeale of Lewis Dubois.

Sheweth. That by an Order of Adjudication under the hands & Seales of John Milner and George Hadley Esqrs. two of her Majts. Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing date the Twenty Seventh day of November last the said two Justices have adjudged your Petr to be the reputed Father of a Female bastard child born in the Parish of st. Gyles in the Fields in the said County on the body of Mary Corbett Singlewoman And the said Justices have also ordered your Petr. to pay to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of st. Gyles in the Fields the Sume of Forty Shillings for the lying in of the said woman Etc. whereby your Petr. humbly conceives he is wronged & aggrieved

Your Petr therefore humbly Appeals to your Worps against the said Order of the said two Justices & pray your Worps to hear & determine the same.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
