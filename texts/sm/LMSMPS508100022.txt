To The Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petitions and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the sd County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Warrant an order under the Hands and Seals of John Collick and N. Forster Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County [---] (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 19th day of May Inst. Elizabeth Lynch Widow was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint Martin in the fields in the said City and Liberty to the said Parish of St Paul Covent garden at the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggreived by the Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said Order and pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing & determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd. Christmas Jno. French Geo Dalmaine Gear Leigh John Tom's} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor
