To the Rt. Worshipll. His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd Assembled in their Genll. Quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition of Bernard Barnes

Sheweth That by Indenture bearing Date the 26th. Day of March 1722 Yor. Petr. was bound Apprentice to John Perrin of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn in the said County Baker.

That your Petrs. said Master does frequently without Reason beat and abuse yor. Petitioner in a very Barbarous & cruell Manner for which he hath been formerly conven'd before one of His Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County before whom he promised for the future to for bear notwith= =standing which he did afterwards barbarously beat & briuse yor. Petr. for which he is bound to appear before yor. Worshps. at this prsent Sessions

That Since his being bound as aforesaid he Continues to threaten yor. Petr. So that yor. Petr. is really in danger of Life

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays that he may be discharged from his said Apprenticeship

And he shall ever pray Etc.

Bernard Barnes
