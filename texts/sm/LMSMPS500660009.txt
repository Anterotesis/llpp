May 1699

To the Rt. Worspl. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Richard Norton One of the Baylists of the Hundred of Ossulston in the sd. County.

That your Petr. not being in Court to answer to his Returnes in Summoneing the Grand Jury for this County this prsent Sessions your Worspps were pleased to Fine him Tenn Pound

That your Petr. comeing to Towne to answer to his sd. Returnes mett [...] with a Fisherman against whom your Petr. had the Ld. Mayors Process for a misdemeanour which Fisherman being very difficult to be taken your Petr. was obleidged to attend upon him to execute his sd Process.

That yor. Petr. is very Sorry for the sd offence and resilver never to offend in the like nature and being a Young officer & Submitteing himselfe to the Clementy of this Honble Cort.

Tho. Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayer your Worspps to be pleased to Nittigale the sd. Five as unto yor worspps Shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc

Richard Norton
