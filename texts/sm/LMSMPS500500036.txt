To the Rt. Honble: St Edwd Clarke Rwt Lord Majes To ye Rt Worspll St Kovell Rwt Recorder } of ye Citty of London

To ye Rest of ye St: Justices Now Setting att Justice Hall in ye old Baily

The humble Peticonr of John Dewin a Prisoner. in Newgate.

Humbly Sheweth, That your Lords Peticonr was Comitted to ye Goale of Newgate ye 31 May Charged with High Treason in Counterfeiting ye Coyne of this Kingdom & putting of Counterfeit money a Crime wer yor Peticonr is all togeather Innocent of

Now forasmuch, as ye yor Peticonr hath, wasted his Substance & haveing most Nothing List to Subsist withall

Therefore in all Humilty yor Peticonr most Humbly Prayes that her may be tryed. Bailed or discharged this Sessions And that this his Prayer may be Registered

And yor Peticonr Shall Pray.
