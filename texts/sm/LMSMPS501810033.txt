To the Right Worshppfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Newbottle in the County of Northton

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrt: under the Hands & Seals of John Milner & John Ellis Esqrs: two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing Date the 29th Day of June 1719 Penelope Flowers Widow of Tho: Flowers Deced with her Three Children (Vizt) Anne Flowers Aged about 7 Years & a halfe, George Aged 3 Years and Eliz: Aged about Eleven Weeks were removed from the Parish of St.Gyles's in the Fields in the Couty of Middx to the said Parish of Newbottle in the said County of Northton as the Place of the Last Legall Settlement of the said Penelope Flowers, By which sd Order or Pass Warrt: of the sd: Two Justices of the Peace, Your Petr: conceive themselves Agrewed & humbly Appeale against ye Same

Your Petrs: therefore pray yor: Worspps will be pleased to appoint a Day this present Sessions for the hearing And Determineing of the sd: Appeale

And Yor: Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
