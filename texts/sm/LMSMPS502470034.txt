To the Worshipfull the Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx at their Genal Quarter Sessions of the peace now Assembled

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Churcwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Hamersmith in ye. County of Midlx.

Sheweth That By virtue of an Ordr. or pass Warrt. undr. the Hands & Seales of R: Marriott & Geo: Howard Esqs Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the sd. County (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twentyeth day of September last past Martha Aged Sixteene yeares Richard. Aged Thirteene years Thomas Aged Ten years Rebecca Aged Eight years & Sarah Aged Six years the Children of Richard Dutton & Sarah his Wife were removed from ye Parish of Fulham[---] on Fulham Side in this County to the sd. Hamlet of Hamersmith where fore ye. Petrs. think themselves to be Agreived & Humbly appeale therefrom

Yr. Petrs. therefore Humbly prays yt. ye be Churchwardens & overseers of the Peace of ye sd. Parish of Fulham (on Fulham Side) may Attend this Cort the next ensueing Sessions to here & abide the Determinacon thereof Touching the Merritts of this Appeale

And yr. Peticoner shall ever pray Etc.
