To the Right Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County

Sheweth That William Martin a poor Child of the said Parish was bound apprentice by the said Parish to Henry Price of the said Parish of Aldgate Taylor to learn his Cort but the said Henry Price is Since Run away above this Eight Months and left the said Apprentice William Martin a Charge to he said Parish which Continued a Great Charge there unto

Your Petitioners most Humbly Pray's your worships would be pleased to Discharge the said Indenture that the said apprentice may be bound to an other Master.

And your Petrs. as in Duty bound shall ever Pray and so forth

Church Wardens{ Tho: Williams Thos: Barnett

Overseer{ Charles Covey
