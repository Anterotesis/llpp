To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint John Southwark in the County of Surry

Sheweth

That by an Order or Warrant of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Henry Fielding and Peter Elers Esqrs: two of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One where of being of the Quorum) Dated the tenth day of May last- Catherine & Ryder widow aged forty Years and her two Children Vizt.- Charles Ryder aged fourteen Years and Nicholas Ryder aged twelve Years She Lawfull Issue by Emanuel Ryder her late Husband- Deceased were Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Chelsea in the County of Middlesex to our said Parish of Saint John Southwark as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal of the said two Justices Humbly appeal against the same and pray this Court will be pleased to Appoint day in this Present Sessions for the hearing and determining the same and Order the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chelsea and all other Persons Concerned these in to attend at such time

And Yr. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Geo: Warren James Cooper John Burnitt} Church wardens

Wm. Finkell Thos: Poultney} Overseers of the Poor

July 2d: 1751
