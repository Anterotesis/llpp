To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church= wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Heese otherwise Hays in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of James Clitherow and John Drinkwater Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17 day of January last John Tarrant and John his Son aged about four Years and an half were removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of Heston in the said County of Middlesex To the said parish of Heese otherwise Hays as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal have therefore Appealed against the same and humbly pray your Worships to make such order in the premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
