To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated the 22d. day of November last under the hands & Seales of Ralph Harwood and Joseph Goodard Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (quor unus) one Elizabeth Black was removed & Conveyed from the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the Said County to the Said parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate as the place of her last legall Settlement

That Your Petrs apprehend themselves to be aggreived by the said Order of the Said two Justices and doe appeale to this Court agt the Socine.

Your Petrs therefore pray this Court would be pleased to appoint Some day in this present Sessions for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch and all other persons concerned to attend this Court to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale, and that your Petrs may be releived therein According to Law.

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
