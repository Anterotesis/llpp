To the Right Worspt his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticond of Margery Morein wife of Robert Morein

Sheweth That Yor Petr Sd. husband hath Kept company with one Hannah Nablock for some time past and about Seaven Yeares past had a child by the said Hannah and will not now allow your Petr. but very Small mainteynance and threatens to be the death of your Petr. Soe that the gar in danger of her life

That of late Yor. Petr. [...] Strikeing the Sd. Hannah Nablock Yor. Petr. stand Jusitces in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions for the Sd. Offeine And [...] forasmuch as your Petr. being a very poore Woman and haveing Three Children to mainteyne And at Knowledged the Fath is willing to phead quilly to the Sd. [...] Indictmt. law Submill herselfe to the Mary of this Honble Court

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly prays your Worspps to the pleased to pray such Small Fine upon herd as unto your Worspp shall Sern meet

And Yor. Pr. Shall ever Pray Etc. Margery [x] M Merein lord markes
