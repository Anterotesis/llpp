To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden by Adjournment at Hicks's Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Whiteparish in the County of Wilts

Sheweth That James Chitherow and John Drinkwater Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid upon Complaint made to them by the Township of New Brentford in the County of Middlesex That Hannah Hewlet and her four Bastard Children Vizt. John aged about Six Years Hannah aged about five Years, Ann aged about five Years and Thomas Aged about Eight Weeks born in New Brentford aforesaid had come to Inhabit in the said Township of New Brentford aforesaid not having gained a legal Settlement there nor produced any Certificate owning her to be Settled elsewhere and that she the said Hannah was become Chargeable to the same Township Did by an Order or Warrant of Removal under their hands and Seals bearing date the third day of May instant Order the said Hannah Hewlet and her said four Bastard Children to be removed and Conveyed from the said Township of New Brentford to the said Parish of White parish as the place of their last legal Settlement and did require the said Churchwardens and Overseers of the said Parish called Whiteparish to receive and provide for the said Hannah Hewlett as an Inhabitant of the said parish and also to receive and suffer to remain with her for Nurture the abovenamed four Bastard Children

That the said Hannah Hewlet and four Bastard Children by Virtue of the said Order have been removed and Conveyed from the said Township of New Brentford to the said Parish of Whiteparish whereby they now remain Chargeable to the said Parish

Your Petitioners apprehending themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal made by the said two Justices as aforesaid Do therefore humbly Appeal against the same.

John Blake Sollr for the said Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Whiteparish in Wilts
