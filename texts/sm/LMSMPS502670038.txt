To the Wpfull the Justices of the peace at their Sessions held for the County of Middx at Hick's hall in St Johns Street on Tuesday the 7o. ap: 1730.

The Humble petition of Mary Jones now a prisoner in the House of Correction by order of this Wpfull Bench as by Copy thereof annext.

Sheweth that yr peticoner hath under gone the punishmt. Inflicted by the said order of Court & is ready to put in Sureties as by the said order required.

Therefore humbly Pray that this Wpfull Court will Grant her an order to be brought into to Court To put in Sureties accordingly

And She Shall Ever pray Etc Mary Jones
