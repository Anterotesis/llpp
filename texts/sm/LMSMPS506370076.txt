To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of David Wilmot Esqr. And John Walford Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of December 1773 Mary Fox a Lunatic was removed and Conveyed from out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex To the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Mary Fox whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly pray your Worships to Appoint a day in this present Session to Hear and Determine their said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

J. Carter Attorney for the Appellants}
