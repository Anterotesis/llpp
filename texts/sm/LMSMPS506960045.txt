To the honble. The Bench of Justices at Hicks hall

The humble Peticon of Alice Davison and Katherine Jones.

Sheweth That yor Petr. are malisously bound over to the Peace by Elizabeth Smith of Aldgate Londo. althos. without Course and being very poore.

Your humbly begg their Acct forzanes may be drawne for Security of there Baile bein brot able to pay the Charge having noe other Support but there dayly labour

And shall pray Etc
