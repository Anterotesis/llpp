To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Waltham Holy Cross Otherwise Waltham Abbey in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of James Esdaile and Benjamin Cowley Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Twenty fifth day of August last Ann Melton Singlewoman and her Bastard Child named John aged About five Months were removed from and out of the parish of Saint Luke in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Waltham Abbey as the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the the said pass Warrant or Order have therefore Appealled Against the same humbly pray your Worshipps to make such Order in the premises as to your Worshipps shall seem meet

And Your petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos: Jessopp Attorney for Appellants
