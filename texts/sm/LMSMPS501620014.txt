To the Worsll: his Majties: Justices of the Peace now Sitting at Hickshall in St Johnstreet

The Petition Appeale of Wm: Dobson Aged Eighty odd yeares

Humbly Sheweth That yor. poor Petr hath lived above fifty yeares in the Parish of St Mary Islington, And thro' Losses & old age he is become very poor and almost Blind, And an aged Wife alsoe to Meynteyne: And the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Said Parish hath Seized yor Petrs Goods for ye Poors Rate, Soe that unlesse yor Petrs Goods be Restored and he Releived from ye Said Rate he & wife must Starve & come a Charge to ye Said, parish

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays to be Releived accordingly as to yor Worsps pleasures it may Seeme meet

And yor Petr shall pray Etc

Wm: Dobson
