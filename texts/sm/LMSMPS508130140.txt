To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of The Parish of Saint Leonanrd Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex.

Shewith. That by virtue of a warrant or order under Hands and Seals of John Barnfather and James Croft Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County o Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty seventh. Day of July last past Peter Dagworthy was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Leonard shoreditch in the said County as the place of his last legal Settlement.

That your petitioner conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said warrant or order do humbly Appeal against the same And pray that you worship will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session that all parties Concerned may Attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of their Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioner shall Ever pray Etc.

Jno Searr Solr. for Appellant
