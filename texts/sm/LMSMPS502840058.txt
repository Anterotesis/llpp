Middx ss To the Worspll: His Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: Assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the Sd, County of Middx:

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Richard Brown & Esqr Two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middx: (Quor unus) bearing Date the fourteenth Day of June 1732, One Mary Ray Widow was removed from the Parish of St. Gile's without Cripple Gate in the Sd, County of Middx: to the said Parish of Kensington as the Place of the Last Legall Settlement of the said Mary-Ray.

That Yor Peticonr, find themselves Agrieved by the said Warrant or order of the said Two Justices of the Peace, And humbly Appeals is their Court against the same

Yor. Peticonrs: therefore pray this Court to Appoint a day this present Sessions for all Parties Concerned to Attend this Court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the Sd. Appeals

And Yor. Peticonrs. Shall ever Pray Etca
