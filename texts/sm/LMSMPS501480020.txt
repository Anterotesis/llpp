To the Right Worshipfull [---] Masjties. Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middx:

The humble petition of Jane Cridendon the [...] of the parish of Sat. Giles Cripplegate Sheweth that the petitioner renting a house of five pounds P Annum and is raited at seven pounds P Annum thorough the hard ness of the times not in ability of paying To the said rate.

Therefore humble pray that your Worships would be pleas'd To take into consideration the hardship that the said petio: labour under in being chard'g in the poor rate
