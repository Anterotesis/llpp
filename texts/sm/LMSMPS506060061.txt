To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All allows the great in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hand and Seals of David Willmot and Burford Camper Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourteenth Day of December last Daniel Cardell was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of All hallows the great in the City of London as the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that your Worships will Please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all persons concerned do then Attend this Court

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc the [x] Mark of Joseph Towke Frans Wynant} Church Wardens

Edwd Emes} Overseers
