To the Worshipful His Majesty Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in there General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The humble petition and appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue a pass warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of J. Miller and John Machin Esquires two of his Majestys Jus- -ties of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th day August last Lucretia the wife of Patrick Harrison whose Settlement is unknown to her was removed porn the parish of Ken- -sington in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved and appeal to your Worships against the said order

Your petitioners therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Sessions for the bearing and determining the said appeal and your petitioners shall ever pray Etc

John Villers D. Davis} Church Wardens
