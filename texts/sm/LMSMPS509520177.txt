To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Court of General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex on behalf of themselves and other the Inhabitants of the said Parish.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Williams and Rice Davies Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the fourth day of July last Thomas Budge was removed from the parish of Westham in the County of Essex to the parish of St. George aforesaid as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal agst. the said Order and Humby Pray relief in the Premises and that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners Shall ever pray. Etc

John Clement Vestry Clk of St. Geo' Middx.
