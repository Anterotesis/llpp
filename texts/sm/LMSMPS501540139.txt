To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx

The humble peticon & appeale of Joseph Boulton of the hamblett of Wappin Stepney in the said County.

Sheweth That your [...] Petr is rated towards the releife of the poor Six pence P month and for as much as your petr is very poore and in mean and low Condicon which makes him unable to pay the Said Rate

Your Petr prays your Worps to discharge him from paying to the said poors Rate or otherwise releive him in the Prnes as your Worps Shall Seem meet

And yor Petr Shall pray Etc.

Jos. Boulton
