To the Rt. Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for ye. County of Middx at their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon and appeale of the Overseers of the poor of the precinct of St. Kathine in the said county.

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrt: under the hands and Seals of John Cleeve and J Lidell Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for this County (Quorum Unus) bearing date ye. 6th: day of March last one Robt: Leatherton als Crouch illegible/>an Infant of the Age of two Years was removed from the Liberty above the Barrs in the Parish of St: Andrew holborn in this County to the parish of St: Kathine by the Tower in the Said County as to ye place of his last Legall Settlemt and left with your Petitioners

Your Peticonrs thinking themselves agreived by the said Order humbly appeale to this Court against the same and pray that your Worshps: would be pleased to Order the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty above the Barrs to attend this Court some day this Quarter Sessions to heare and abide the Judgement & determinati on of this Court touching the said appeale

R: W:

and yor Petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
