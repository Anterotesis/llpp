To the Right Worshipfull in his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx att the Sessions of the Peace att Hicks Hall Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldersgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Robert Hucks & John Merter Esqr. Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the 21: day of April last past Margarett Wager wife of David Wager and Two Children (vizt) Mary aged about Four year & Richard about Two Years old were by the said Order Removed from the parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the said County of Middx to the Parish of St. Botolph with out Aldersgate aforesaid as the place of their last legal Settlement Where by Yor. Peticoners humbly Consive they are wronged & agreived

Yor. Petrs. There fore humbly Pray yor. Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a Day to here & Determine the said appeale and yor. Petr. Releived as the Case shall require

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc.
