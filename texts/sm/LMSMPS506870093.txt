Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint James Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That [...] by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of I Girdler and Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of November last Hannah Reed Single- Woman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of, Saint Andrew Holborn above the Bars to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceived themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly as appeal to this Court request the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said or Order and pray that your Worships will Please to appoint some Day in this present Session for the Hearing and determining of this Appeal And to order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Holborn above the Bars and all Persons [...] concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Jds Dyson Sol for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Westminster
