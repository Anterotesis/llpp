To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for ye. sd. County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Eling in the County of Southampton

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order of Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Scott and N. Conant two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in in for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorrum) bearing date the seventh day of November 1794 Anne Hewlett and her Infant Son William were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Eling aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the benefit of their said Appeal may be reserved to them And that as Day may be appointed in the present general Quarter Sessions of the Peace o be holden for the said County of Middlesex for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And that the Church wardens and Overseers of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square be required to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc for the Appetts. Duill Solr.
