To the Worshipful his majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of Saml. Hawkins and John Staples Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Tenth day of November last past Rose Russell Wife of John Russell who was then Sick in Saint Bartholomews Hospital and their Two Children Vizt. Mary aged about Seven years and Catherine aged about five years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covant Garden as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves agreived by the Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said Order And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Richd. Christmas Thos. Byrnes Thos. T. Tutt P. Pressey James Baugham} Churchwardens and Overseers
