To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulcher London

Sheweth

That by a Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W. Bleamire and W. Kennard Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 6th Day of February Instant Carolina Ward with her two Children of Lawful Issue (Vizt) Hester aged 9 years and James aged 5 years were Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of then last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal to the Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be adjourned to the next Sessions and that a Day in the next Sessions may be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
