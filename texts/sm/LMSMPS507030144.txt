To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace holden at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition & appeal of the ChurchWardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Kensington in the County of Middlesex aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands & Seals of William Mantle Esquires & William Timbrell Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in & for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing dates the twenty third day of August last Elizabeth Harley Single Woman Aged about 20 years was removed from the Parish of the Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Midx to the said Parish of Kensington as the Place of her last Legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices And pray that Your Worships Will be Pleased to appoint And to Order that all Persons Concerned Do then Attend

And your Petitioners Shall Pray &c

Wm. Simpson Wm. Dalley } ChurchWardens

John Thresher Wm Bacon} Overseers of the Poor
