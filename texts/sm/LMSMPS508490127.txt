To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices x=x of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents in the said County.

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace (one of whom is of the Quorum) for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty seventh day of August now last past Elizabeth Pentycross singlewoman was removed and conveyed from that part of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents, as the Place of her last Legal Settlement; whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order and humbly pra relief in the premises and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint same day in this present Session for the Hearing and determination of their said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Cox Soll for Appellants Septr 7th. 1789.
