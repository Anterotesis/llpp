To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeale of Christopher Jefferson of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Petitr: is rated in the poors rate of ye sd. Parish for the house he dwells in after the yearly rent of four pounds Whereas Yor. Petr. pays noe more then Six and Fifty Shillings P Ann rent and Appeals agt Ye same That your Petitr: being an Aged man and past his Labour and having an aged wife & being very Poor.

Humbly Prays he may be wholly Discharged from paying to ye said poors rate

And Yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc:

Chris: Jefferson
