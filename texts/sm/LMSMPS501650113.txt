To the Right Worspfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Trowbridge in the County of Wilts

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order dated the 5th day of June last under the hands & Seals of Samuel Pery and Joseph Hayne Esqrs. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midd (quor unus) Elizabeth Saunders wife of Joseph Saunders was removed & conveyed from the parish of St. Mary Whitechappell in the said County of Midd to the said parish of Trowbridge in the County of Wilts aforesaid as the place of the last legal Setlemt of the said Elizabeth

Yor petrs finding themselves aggrieved by the said Warrt or Order of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And in regard yor petrs material Witnesses are at a remote distance Whereby they cannot & Execute their Appeal at this prsent Quarter Sessions. Yor petrs humbly pray the benefit of this their Appeal may be saved to them and that the hearing & determinacon thereof may be adjourned to the next Genal or quarter Sessions of the peace to be holden for this County And that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Mary Whitechappell and all in the said next Genal or quarter Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt & determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And yor petrs Shall ever pray Etc.
