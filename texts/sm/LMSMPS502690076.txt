To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the, County of Middx at their Generall Quarter Sessions held at Hicks's Hall in the Said County the 26th. day of Augst. 1730

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church, Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order under the Hands & Seals of John Mercer and Richd Gifford Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twelfth day of this instant August Anne Lewis Singlewoman, was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Dunstans in the West London as the place of her last Legall Settlement

That your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of Removall & do appeal to this Court against the same,

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly Pray your [...] Worshipps will be pleased to Order a Shortday for the Hearing & determining the Appeal of your Petrs agst. such Order

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
