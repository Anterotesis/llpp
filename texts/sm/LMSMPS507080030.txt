To the Worshipful Justices now assembled in their general Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of St. Girdler and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the Ninth day of November last Ruth Hayward Spinster was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surrey as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that they may at this Present Session Exhibit this their Petition and Appeal against the said Order of removal And that the benefit of their said appeal be saved unto them And that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for this County

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Wm. Drew Sollr. for the Petitrs.
