To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Session of the Peace

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ealing in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of or order under the Hands & Seals & Seals of Js: Neeld & Thomas Mogg Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 20th. day of June Last whereby Kesia Weller Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Margaret in the Liberty of Westmt. Aforesaid to the said Parish of Ealing as the Peace of her last Legall Settlement

Your Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the said order Appeal against the same & humbly Pray that the benefit of the [---] Appeal be saved to your Petrs. & that the hearing and Determining therefore be Adjourned to the next General Sessions of the Peace to be held for this County

And Yor. Petrs Shall ever pray Etc
