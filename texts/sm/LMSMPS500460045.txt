To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midd

The Humble Peticon of Else Currey

Sheweth That your Petitioner for the fact She Stood indicted in this Honorable Court, as of this present Sessions, was fined the sum of three Shillings and four pence and for non payment thereof was commited to new prison, where She now [...] ren aines being almost ready to parish for want of releife, her husband being dead and left three Small Children for her to take care of She haveing nothing to maintain herself and her three Children but her daily labor, humbly begs being not able to pay the fine inflicted her to Submit herself to the money of this Honb Court.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly begs your worships to consider ye Sad and deplora -ble condicon wherein She now langwish -eth and to mitigate the said fine, as unto your worships Shall Seem meet.

And your Petitioner as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc.

Else Currey
