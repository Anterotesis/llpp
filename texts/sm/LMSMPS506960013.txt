To ye Rd. Honble sd Robert Bedingfild knt. Lord Majr. And To ye Rest of ye Justices now S: King att ye Old Bayley

The humble between, of Elizabeth, Powell a Prisonr in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor, Petconr: was Comitted by Tho. Cullum Esqr accused by Charles Moor for Severall felonies and Burglaw

Your and yr Petionr Most Humbly Prayer To betryed Bailed or Discharged this Sessions. And that this [...] Prayer may be Registered

And yor Peticonr. Shall Pray
