To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appear of Anthony Bouch of Wapping Stepny in the Said County

Shweth

That Your peticoner is rates Two Shillings & Six pence P Month besides Over Rates in the Poors rate

That Your peticoner is Acced and past his Labour and hath an Acced Wife and having susteines great Losses Is not able to pay the said rate, and Appeals against the Same

Yor Petir therefore humbly prays, he may be Eased in the sd rate As to your Worshipps Shall seem Meet

And Yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc.

A: Bouch
