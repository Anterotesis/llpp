To the right Worpll. his Mates: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx.

The humble Peticor of Anne Clarke of Hendon in this County Vistualler.

Sheweth

That your Petitioner was Licensed to beep in Ale. hons in Open Court att Serdal Quarter Sessione in Octobr last not wth the opposicon made against her but that your Poor Peticonere Liceace is Since ordered to be Support [...] att yor last Henowings of hole for Hendon division

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays than thre honble: Court will please to apaul her a New Likenae or that her [...] may be penewes for that noe misrule or disorder in your Peticonre house wry dar charges agt. her.

Humfrus Elmes [...]

And yor Peticoner Shall pray Anne Clarke
