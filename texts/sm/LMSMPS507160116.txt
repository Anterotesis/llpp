To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Sessions of the Peace in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an order under the Handsam Seals of J. Penleaze and Edmund Pepys Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the seventeenth day of July One thousand seven hundred and seventy nine whereby Samuel Young Mary his Wife and their four Children lawful Issue namely Eleanor aged fifteen Years Elizabeth aged about seven Years Ann aged about five years and Sarah aged three Years and upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and do appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a short day may be appointed of this present Sessions to hear and determine the matter of the said Appeal

Robt. Tudman Sollr. for the said Appellants} And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
