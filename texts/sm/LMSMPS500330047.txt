To the Right Worspl. their Maties Justices of the Peall for the County of Miidds

The humble peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the parish of Finchley in the sd. County.

Sheweth

That Emy Arthurbury was by Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of their maties Justices of the peace for the sd. County lately passed from the parish of St. Mary Islington in the sd. County to the sd. parish of Finchley

That the sd Emy Arthurrbury never had any legall Settlemt. within the parish of Finchley which yor. petrs. are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor Worsppl to be pleased to order that the Churchwardens and Ovseers of the poore of the sd. pish of St: Mary Islington way appeare in this Honble Court this put Quarter- Sessions to shew cause why thes Emy Arthurrbury shoud not be reppsted to thee pish of St. Mary Islington

And yor Petr I Shall ever pray etc
