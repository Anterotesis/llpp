To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Giles Quinny Governour of the House of Correccon in the said County.

Humbly Sheweth That at Christmas last there was in Arreare to your Petriconr the Sume of One hundred and twenty Pounds for the last two years Sallary allowed him by this Honoble Court, and to be paid to him as this Court Should order and direct,

That yor Peticonr having now in his Custody agreat Number (to vizt) 36 of Convicts to maintaine and Provide for which is So extraordinary a charge to yor Petr that he is and has been for some time Past oblidged to Pay interest for money.

Yor Peticonr therefore humbly Prays your Worpps will be Pleased to order the Said two years Sallary due at Christmas last to be paid to yor Peticonr as to yor Worpps Shall Seem meet.

And yor Petr: as in duty bound Shall ever Pray Etc.
