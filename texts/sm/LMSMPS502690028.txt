To the Rt. Worspfull his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & appeal John Jackson against the poor Rate of the psh of St. Botolph Without aldgate in the sd County

Sheweth That Your Peticonr. is This poor Rated & assussed the Sume of one Shilling and Six penne Month toward the Necessary Releife of the poor of the Sd. Parish Your Peticonr. Not Renting any house with in the sd psh And being in mea [...] Circumstance is Uncapible of paying the sd Rate

Your Peticoner therefore Most Hbly Pray that Your Worpps would be please to Order that he May be wholy Discharge [...] from paying the same or Such Other releife in the premises as to Your worpps Shall Seem Meet

And Your peticonr Shall Ever Pray Etc.

John Jackson
