To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices for the County of Middx now in Session

The humble Petition of Robert Taylor Gardiner

Sheweth That your Petr. is a poor working Gardiner and Stands Indicted in this Honoble Court for a Trespass and taking of Some Colly flower plants out of the Ground of Jno. Iswell which sand Indictmt. your petr, Traverse last Sessions and was to Try the Same this present Session but your petr. being a poor ignorant Man and Unaquainted with the Rules and Orders of this honoble Court did neglect giving the prosecutr. notice of Tryal in due time for this Sessions as he is now informed he ought to have done.

Your Petr. therefore humbly Prays that this homble Court willbe pleased to Respitt his Recognizance and give your petr. time till next Sessions to try his said Traverse

And your petr. shall pray & Robt. Taylor
