To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the peace Assembled in General Quarter Session held in & for the County of Middx:

The humble peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey in the County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Mary Baker & her four Children were by Order under the hands & Seals of two of his Majties Justices of the peace of ye sd. County of Middx bearing date ye Nineteenth day of December last removed from ye Pish of st. Clements Danes in the sd. County to the said Pish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey as the place of their last legal Settlement.

That yor Peticonrs. humbly conceive they can make it Appear to this Court that the pace of the last legall Settlemt. of the sd. Mary Baker and her four Children. is not in the said Pish of St. Mary Magdalen Bermondsey.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly pray yor Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Clement Danes- may Attend this Court for the hearing- the sd. Appeal such time as yor: Worships Shall Appoint.

And Yor Peticonr. shall ever pray Etc.
