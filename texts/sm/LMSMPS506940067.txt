To the Right Worshipful the Justices of peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwardens & overseers of the poor of the Parish of Isleworth in the sd. County

Sheweth That by a pass Warrt or Order under the hands & Seales of John Bond Esqr & John Coggs Esqr [...] Two of her Majtyes Justices of the peace for the sd County quor [...] Anne Earneans all Brewer was sent from the Parish of St Clements Danes in the sd County to the sd Parish of Isleworth as the place of the last Legall Settlemt. of the sd. Anne Earneans als Brewer [---] by which [...] order of the sd Justices yor said Peticoners are agreived the sd parish of Isleworth not being the place of the last legall Settlement of the sd Ann Earneaus als Brewer

Yor. Peticonrs therefore appeal from ye said Order and humbly pray a day may be appointed to hear & determine the said matter

And Yor Petionrs. will ever pray
