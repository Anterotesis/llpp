To the Rt Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Katherine Creechurch London

[...] eweth That by a pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seales of Oliver Lambart Esqr. and Sr John Colbatch Knt two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quonr Unus) bearing date the the 27th day of June last past One Elizabeth Goodred aged about Thirty Four Years wife of John Goodred was removed from the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the said County unto the said parish of St Katherine Creechurch London as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners humbly conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant & Judgment of the said two Justices do humbly appeal to your. Worpps therefrom And Pray that your Worpps will be pleased to order and appoint a day to Swer and Determine this Appeal
