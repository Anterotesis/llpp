To the Right Worshipful her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Petition of Edward Dawson and Deborah his Wife of the Parish of St. Dunston Stepney in the sd. County

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. James Indicted in this Honble Court this psent Sessions for keeping a Disorderly house But forceforth as Your Petr Not now keeping a Publick house & for all of them Neighbours being now Ready to Justify the Reputacond of your Petrs

That the Prosecutor will not appeard agst. Your Petr. no Oath can be made thereof to your Worships and for Cause as your Petr pseing the Seal this Wife having [...] layne in of as Dead Child about Fortnight pase

Yor. Petr. therefore Most humbly Pray your Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that they may be Discharged from the sd Indictmt.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

P Thos: Nash
