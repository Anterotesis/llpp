To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and Stephen Clarke Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty fifth Day of April last John Hales aged about Thirty four Years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of his last Legal Settlements Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to your Petitioners and that the hearing and Determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this County

And Your Petitr. shall Pray Etc

G S Bradshaw Thos Greening Jas. Cullen Jas Young} Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
