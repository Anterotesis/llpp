Middlesex To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church-Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Wm. Quarrill and John Staples Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 21st. Day of December 1781. Sarah Bourne Single Woman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitchapel in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary le bone aforesaid as the Place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefore And humbly pray that you will hear them in the Premises And order that the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Whitchapel may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed to Hear and abide the Judgement and determination of your worships touching this Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray etc

Thos. Martin Solr. for the Appellants
