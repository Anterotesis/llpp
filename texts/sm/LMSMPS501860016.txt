To the Right Worshippfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The Peticon and Appeal of Alexander Urquhart Overseer of the poore of the parish of St. Peters in Bonds in the County of Middx

[---]

That by Order under the hands & Seals of Isaac Tillard & D'Oyly Mitchell Esqrs. two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the said County (Quorum- unus) bearing Date the fourteenth day of May- Anne Dui 1720 Frances Bell of the Age of Tenn Years Daughter of Nathaniell & Mary Bell was- passed & Conveyed from the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the parish of St. Dunstans Stepney als Stebunheath in the sd. County to the parish of St. peters aforesaid as the place of her last legall Settlemt.

Your peticonr. findeth himselfe- agreived by the Judgmt. of the said two Justices humbly Appeals to this Court agt. the same & prays that the Church warden & Overseer of the poore of the said Hamlett of Bethnall Green & all other partyes concerned herein may be Ordered to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt & Determinations of this Court touching the sd. Appeale And your peticonr. shall pray Etc.
