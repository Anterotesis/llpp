To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the sd County assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Tho. Burdus & Wm. Jones Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westmr. (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 9th. Day of Febry Instt. Ann Wolfe Wife of Sheppard Wolfe who hath absconded from her was removed from the Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westmr aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear and determine the matter touching the said Appeal

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appell:

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc
