To the Rt. Worshipfull her Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Elizabeth Vaughan

Sheweth. That your Petior. attended the Grand Jury yesterday upon an Indictment against one Simon [...] Broome [...] Broome his Wife for an Assault and your Petior. was Examined and [...] Wittnesses on the Sume Indictment and that the Jury was Sattisfied, But there being one Mr. Driver on the Jury [...] told your Petior. that the Bill should not be found he being an acquaintance of the said Broomes Soe that the Bill was not brought into Court with the rest of the Bills.

Your Petior. therefore most humbly prayes that she maye be hand and her wittnesses before this Court.

And your Petior. shall ever pray Etc.

Elizth Vaughan
