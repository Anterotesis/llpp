The Humble Petition of James Docherdy

Sheweth That Your Honour was pleased to Order my [---] Tryal on Fryday last that I should be Discharged on payment of One Shilling

I beg leave to represent to you it was at Hicks Hall and as I have a Wife and three small Children to provide for Cannot pay that Sume

Your Petr. most humbly Implore Your Honours Order for my Discharge and as in Duty bound shall ever pray
