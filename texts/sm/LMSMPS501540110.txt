To ye: Rgt: Worspll: his Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye. County of Middx:

The humble peticon & appeale of Joseph Laxon. of ye. pish of St: Leonord Shoreditch in ye. sd County Weaver.

Sheweth That Your peticoner is rated Six pence p: weeke to ye. rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd pish, and that by reason of ye. great Losses he has Lately had & ye. greate decay of his traide he is unable to pay ye. Same

Your peticoner therefore most humbly prayes an, order of this Honable: Court, that the may be releived in ye. premisses as to your Worsps. shall Seem meet

and Your peticoner shall ever pray Etc:

Jos: Laxon

Middx ss. Pursuant. to ye. Order of ye. Genll: Quarter Sessions of ye. peace wth in written. We whose names are hereunto Subscribed Two of his Matis: Justices of ye. peace for ye sd County in ye. sd Order named. haveinge taken upon as ye. heareinge & examinacon of ye. appeale & Compll. of Joseph Saxon ye. peticoner & appeltt. above named, ye. Churchwardens Overseers of ye. poor & Sevll. other Inhitants of ye. sd pish of St: Leonard Shoreditch beinge prsent, and upon heareinge all ptyes & dully Consideringe ye. reasons & allegacons given on all Sides, We report our oppinions to this Honble. Court, that ye. sd Joseph Saxon ought to be eased & abated one penny p weeke to ye. Last Rate made for ye. releife of ye. poor of ye. sd pish; upon his payinge all arrears to any former rate made for that purposs. & for ye. future to pay five pence p: weeke & are more, Given under our hands this 31 day of May: ano [...] ond 1716: all ptyes accquiessinge

Alex: Pitfeild. Sam Benson
