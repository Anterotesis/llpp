To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Genll. Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for the sd. [...]

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemts Danes in the sd. County.

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 21th. Day of In by Last [...] Brown & Frances Brown his wife Richd. Browne aged abt. two yeare's & Sarah Brown Aged able Four Months (since deced ) his Children Were removed from Parish St. Luke this County to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the [...] their Last Legall Settlemt. where by your Petrs. conceive themselves to be agrieved & agt. the sd. Order doe Appeal

Rob Chimmond Sellr. for the Appellts} Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that [...] day maybe Appointed this Present Session to hear & Determine the matter touching th [...] sd. Appeale

And you Petrs. shal Pray etc
