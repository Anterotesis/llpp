To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex & now Setting at Hickes Hall in Saint John Street

Humbly Sheweth That yor Petitioner Ann Brooks was bound over before justice Milford for An Assault upon one William Brooks her Husband then being noe Bill found the desires to be discharge of her recognizances She being a Nurse to a Newman who [...] have expect every Moment to be desired

And the Duty bound Shall for ever pray
