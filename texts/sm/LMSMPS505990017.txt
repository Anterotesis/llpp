To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Sir John Fielding Knight and Peter Planck Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster[---] (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty Ninth Day of December last John Lendborn and Beatrice his Wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in this County to the said Parish of Allhallows the great as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the Said Order And Pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved to Your Petitioners And that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for this County.

And Your Petitrs. shall Pray Etc

T: Ellis Sollr. for the Appellts:
