To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the Grace of the County of Middx.

The humble petition and Appeal of Caesar Adamson

Humbly Sheweth That by order dated the Eight day of October last past under the Hands and Seales of Francis Negus & Gideon Harvey Esqr. two of the Maties Justices of the Peace for this County (quorunns) is adjudged- the Reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child bound of the body of Mary Goodford single woman in the Parish of St Anns (by the name of Caesar Adams) And there by your petitoner is Ordered to pay to the Church wardens or Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish for & towards the Charges of Lying in of the sd. Mary Woodford the sume of Eleaven Shillings & Six Pence by the work for the first four weeks from the birth of the sd Child & that after the expiracon of the first four weeks [...] to pay weekly & every week the sume of Two shillings six pence towards the releife & maintenance of the said Male Bastard Child for see long time as the same shall be Chargable to the sd. Parish And to give sufficient security to the Churchwardens or Overseers of the sd Parish to & formed the order & Indempnify the sd Parish from the sd Male Bastard & Child [...] the sd Order will appeare

That your Peticonr finds himselfe aggreived by the sd Judgmt & order of the sd two Justices of the Peace and humbly Appealed against the same

Your Peticoner there fore prays your Worshipps will Please a Appoint a day this & Genl sessions for the Churchwardens Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Anns & all other son [...] nce to Attend their Court Accordingly to peace & abide the saint determinacon of their Court upon & touching to is Appeale
