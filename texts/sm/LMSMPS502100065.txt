To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wdn and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Margt Westminster in the Said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Richard Lestock and Stephen Martin Leokes Esqr two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace of and for the Said County of Middx bearing date the Eighth day of March last. Ann Francisco Widow of Sebastian Francisco and their two Children Vizt Sebastian aged four Years and a female Child not there Baptized aged Seven Months were Removed from and out of the Hamlett of Lime house in the Parish of Stepney in the Said County to the parish of St. Margt. Westmr as the place of their Lawfull Settlement

That your Petr thinking themselves agreived by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the Same

Therefore humbly pray your Worships will please to order, That the Church Wardns & Overseers of the poor of the Said Hamlett of Lime house in ye said parish of Stepney may attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

And your Petr Shall ever Pray Etc
