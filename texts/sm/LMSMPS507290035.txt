To the Worshipfull It is Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishopgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Sir James Esdaile Knight and William Blackborow Esquire two fo His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the first day of Septr 1780 Elizabeth Gamald Widow of Alexander Gamald deceased said her five Children namely George aged about in nine year mary aged about seven Years Sophia aged [...] Years Alexander aged [...] from out of the Parish of Saint [...] Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Botolph Bishop the City of London as the [...] Whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day in this present Sessions to hear and Determine them and Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Carter & Simpson Sollr. for the Appellants
