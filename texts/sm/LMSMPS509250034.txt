To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the Session House Clerkenwell Green assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of The Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Trinity Little Minories in the County of Middlesex

Shew That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Barnard and R. Davies Esqrs.. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth day of April 1796 Rachael Holt Widow of Theophilus Holt deceased and their Child Jane were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel to the said Parish of Trinity Little Minories in the Liberty of the Tower of London as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Rachael Holt and Child whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived

Therefore they appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order And Pray that your Worships will be pleased to Adjourn the hearing of the said Appeal and appoint some day in the next Sessions for the hearing and determination of the said Appeal and to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray

Charles Martin Solicitor

For the Appellants Haydon Square
