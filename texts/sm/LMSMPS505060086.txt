To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- wardens of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Edmund Byron & John Lane Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 23d. day of November 1761. Mary Peirson Singlewoman, was removed from the Parish of St. Mary le Bone in the County aforesaid to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court agaist the same.

Your Petitioners Humbly pray this Court that the benefit of the said Appeal be saved unto them until the next General Quarter Session to be holden for the said County of Middx and that [---] all persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the same Court touching the said appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray etc. John Spencer John Jackson Jno Trig} Churchwardens & Overseers.
