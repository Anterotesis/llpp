Middx Ss To the Worshipfull the Bench of Justices Now Sitting att Hickshall in the Said County

The petitior: haveing Qualifyed himselfe to his Majestie King George and Now Prsent and ready to take the Oaths on that Occassion

Humbly prays That he may be Called on and admitted

And he will pray Wm Camden

For a Bruise

Take a pint of good Clarett, an Ounce of New Bran Camomile Flowers & Elder Flowers half an Ounce Each Beyle them together abouts five Minutes Strain it and Bath the part and Then make a Poultis and putt it to the parts all Night And Make a Treakle possett to Sweat.
