To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for Ye County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers the poor of the parish of St Michael Bassingshaw in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Pass Order under the hands and Seale of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the Fourteenth Day of June Last [...] Elizabeth Greed with her two Children Vizt George Greed aged Eight years and Elizabeth Greed aged Ten years was removed from the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County unto the said parish of St Michael Bassingshaw London alloadging the said parish to be the place of their last legal Settlement, That your petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant pass order of the said two Justices Do most humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses and Order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St James Clerkenwell may appear before you and hear a [...] abide the determination of your Worpps touching the Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
