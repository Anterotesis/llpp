To the Worshipfull Her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Wandsworth in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That by an Order or Warrant granted under the Hands and Seales of Thomas Crosse and B. Buckridge Two of Her Matye Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx and Liberty of Westminster (Quorum Unus) for removeing Mary David and John David from the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr: to the said Parish of Wandsworth, Which said Order or Warrant beare date the Tenthday of Aprill 1714 And Your Petitioners intending to Appeal from the said Order [...] nt

Therefore Humbly Pray Your Worshipps to grant them an Order for the giveing notice of the said Appeal to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr:

And your Petitioners shall Pray Etc.
