To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majestys. Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx: in the their General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middx:

Sheweth-. That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seales of Oliver Lambart and John Ludbey Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: bearing date the Seventh day of December last Budget Mattison Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St James Westmr. in the said County to the said Parish of St. Mary Le Bone, as the place of her last legal Settlement, whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the Same.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays this Court will be pleased to save them the Benefit of this Appeal untill the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County.

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

John Perry John Willis}Churchwarden } Overseer of the Poor
