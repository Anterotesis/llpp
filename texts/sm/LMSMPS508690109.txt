To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justice of the Peace for the County of a Middlesex at this General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers of the Poor the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrent under the Hands and Seals of Geo Booth and Rupt. Clarke Two of his Majesty's Justice's of the Peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 3d.. day of November 1791 Elizabeth Fisher was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of saint James Clerkenwell in the County of middlesex to the Parish of Saint Mary at Lamberth in the County of Surry as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbley Pray your Worships to appoint a day in this prevent Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall over Pray Etc.

J: Carter So [...] : for the Appellants}
