To the Rt. Worsppl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Genrall Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the County of Middlx

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Ryres and Edward Grose Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace (Quorum Unus) for the Said County bearing date the 26th day of October last past Anne Ridley widdow of Ralph Ridley deced with her four Children (vizt) Anne,aged about four teene years Mary Aged about Eleaven years Ralph Aged about Six years And John Aged about two years and [...] halfe were removed from the Parish of St. Pauls Shadwell in the Said County to the Said Hamlett of Wapping Stepney as the [...] place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Peticoners think themselves Agrieved by the Said Order humbly Appeals to this honobld. Court against the Same

And Prays that Your Worspps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. Pauls Shadwell and all other persons Concerned may Attend this Court Some day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeale

And Your Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc
