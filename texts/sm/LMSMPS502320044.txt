To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon of Appeal of ye Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Edgeworth in the said County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Houghton and Thomas Scot Esqrs: two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace of the said County of Middx. (Quor un) Mary Rider Widow & Sarah her Daur are by the said Warrant required to be removed from the parish of St George Hanover Square to the said Parish of Edgeworth as the place of the last legal Settlement of ye said Mary Rider and Sarah her Daur

That your petrs. apprehend themselves by the said Warrant & do appeal there from and humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day for the Hearing of the Matter of this peticon

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc.

Middx. ss. Ad genal quartial Session pacis Dui Regis tent p Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St John Street in Com prd Adjorn Die Sabti scilt decimo quinto Die Jannary anno Rui Dui Georgy Dei Gra nunc Regis Mague Britannie Etc duodecimo

It is ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St George Hanover Square in this County have notice of this Appeal and that they and all persons concerned do attend this Court on Tuesday next at ten of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and abide the Indgmt. and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

[x] Cur Walter
