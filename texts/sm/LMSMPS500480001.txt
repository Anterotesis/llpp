To the [...] worspL his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middsx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & other Sentient Inhabitants of the parish of St John Wapping in the sd County

That in the yer 1694 Alburt Alburson & Francis Bristo did serve the Office of Scavenger for the sd: parish And that in the yeare 1695 John Gill & Anthony Dowrich did also serve the sd. Office for the sd. [---] parish That the sd. Scavenger in the time of their sd Office did receive severall Sumes of Money which now remaine in their hand & which they will not give an Accompt for to your Petr.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray yor Worspps to be pleased to Order that fair Scavengers may appeare in this Humble Court this prsent Sessions to show cause to the tailrary

P Tho. Nash

And yor Petr shall ever pray Etc Tho: Fryer
