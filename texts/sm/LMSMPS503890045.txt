To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in and for the sd. County Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Oversrs. of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Mary Saunders Singlewoman a Vagrant not having a Settlement in the sd. Parish of St. Clement Danes was on the 20th. Day of August last in the Strand in the said Parish of St. Clement Danes by means whereof the Inhabitant of the sd. Parish were Obliged necessarily to Expend the sum of Two Pounds for the Lyeing in and maintenance of the sd. Mary Saunders and Child And in Pursuance of an Act of Parliament made in the 17th. Year of his present Majestys Reign Intitled an Act to Amend and make more effectual the Laws Relating to Regnes Vagabonds and other idle and disorderly Persons and to houses of Correction Your Petrs. Conveyed the sd. Mary Saunders to one of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for this County who Examined the sd Mary Saunders and Committed her to the house of Correction [...] pursuant to the sd. Statute

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships will Order the Treasurer of the County to pay your Petitioners the sd. Sum of Two pounds Expended by them as aforesaid or such other Sum of money as your Worships shall Adjudge a was on able Satisfaction

And your Petrs. shall even pray Etc

Abra Mombray John Perins} Church ward [...]

Robt Burton Drake Pollord} Oversrs. of the poor
