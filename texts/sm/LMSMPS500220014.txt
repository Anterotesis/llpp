To the Rt: Worspll: their Maties: Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx assembled in Cr. Sessions.

The humble Peticon of ye Churchwardens and Overseers of ye poor of ye pish of St. Margaret Westmr.

Sheweth

That one Hannah Gives & her 2 Children were by Warrt: bearing date ye 19th: day of July last under ye hands & Seales of Theophilus Eyton & Saml. Buck Esqrs. hereof their Maties: Justices of ye peace for this County of Middx past from ye pish of St: Andrew Holborn above Barrs to ye Pish of St: Marget Westmr. therein incerting yt ye Sd Hannah Gives & her 2 Children were there last legally Setled

Whereas Such upon Strict Inquiry made [...] petrs: can find noe legall Settlemt: either She or her husband ever had in St: Margaret Parish for which reason ye Petrs adjudge themselves aggrieved & appeall to this Worpll: Bench for the life in ye prmises.

And therefore humbly pray a day of heaving & yt ye Churchwardens & Overseers of ye poor of St: Andrews pish may attend to Shew Cause to ye Contrary

And ye Petrs shall pray Etc.
