To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble petition of Richard Browne for and on the behalfe of Samuel Browne his Brothere

Sheweth

That by Indenture of Apprenticeshipp bearing Date the Due and Twentyeth Day of June Anno Dom 1714 your Petitrs: said Brother became an Apprentice to one Thomas Savage of the Parish of St. Brides Watchmaker for the tenn of Eight yeares comencing from the day of the date of the sd recited Indenture As thereby may Appear Which Indenture your Petitr humbly Conceives to be Illegall

That the said Master refuseth to allow yor Petitrs. said Brother comon susteinance according to the Covenants of the sd Indenture

P R: Prettie Your Petit therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps will be pleased to order the said Thomas Savage to attend this Court some day this Sessions to shew Cause why his said Brother should not be Discharged from his said Indenture of Apprenticeshipp

And yor Petitr. shall Ever pray Etc

Richrd. Browne
