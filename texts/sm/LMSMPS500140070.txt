To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Mathew Davis.

Sheweth That as the last Sessions yor. Worpps were pleased to order that [---] [...] Severall Streets in Goodmans Feilds should be paved according to a late Act of Parliamt in that case made and provided

That yor. Petr. humbly conceives that he is not within the intenton of the said Act. although to hath some small states in the sd Feild.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps to be pleased to relieve him in the prmisses in Such manner as unto yor. Worpp Shall Seem meet

And yor. Petr. Shall are Pray.
