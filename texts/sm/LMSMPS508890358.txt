To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in then Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the Said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Orders under the Hands and Seals of Thos. Gordon and N. Conant Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second day of October 1793 Catherine Stoner Singlewoman, aged about twenty two years removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Fulham in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to his Court against the Adjudication of the Said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appeal a day in this Present Session for the hereby and determining the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will Pray Etc

Herbt: Robertson Solictor for the Appellts.}
