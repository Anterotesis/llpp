To the Honbl. Bench

The Petition Most Humbly Shewet That Your Poor Petitioners being in very Mean Circumstances & haveing Great Familys to Maintain being Security for Hannah Regally of ye. Parish of Chelsea She Dying in Shrewsbury in ye. County is of Shrop Shire, (Informed by People Come from thence) Beg ye. Favour of ye. Most Honble: Bench ye. Wee ye. Poor Petitioners may Nott bee enthralled or Come to Trouble ye. Party Abovemention'd being Deceased, And ye. Humble Petitioners Will ever Pray for You & Yours

Wm. Hunt Edw. Harding
