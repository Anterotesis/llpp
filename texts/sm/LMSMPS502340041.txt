To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlesex at their Genall Quarter Sessions now assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of John Peters of the Liberty of Hexton & Church End in the Parish of Shoreditch Weaver

Sheweth That your Peticonr is rated this Year above what he formerly was for & towards the necessary Releif of the poor of the said Liberty for this present Year 1725

That he apprehends he pays morein Proportion than the rest of his Neighbours, who are of like Ability & Circumstances, there fore conceives himself aggreivd by the said Rate and humbly appeals against the same, praying such Releif in the Premises, as to your Worship's Judgment shall seem Just.

And your Petionr shall ever Pray ETc
