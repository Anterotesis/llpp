To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of and appeal of Martha Jones of the Parish of St Mary Whitechappell in the said County Widow.

Sheweth That your Petitioner is rated Twelve pence P Month besides Over Rates in the rate made for the relief of the poor of the said Parish.

That your Petitr rents only a small Apartment of a House which was never before charged in the poors rate And for as much as yor Petitioner having three Children to Main keyne and having of late been very In time and rended uncapable of Doing any thing towards her Subsistance See that she is not able to pay the said rate or any part thereof and Appeal against the same

Your Petitr. therefore humbly Prays your Worshipps will be Pleased to order that she may be wholly Discharged from paying to the said poors rate

And Yors Pets Shall Ever pray Etc. Martha Jones
