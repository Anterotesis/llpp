To the Rt. Worshipfull the Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon of Edward Dalton

Sheweth That Your Poor Peticoner last December was Fined by this Worshipfull Court Twenty Five Pounds, is now in a very poor and deplorable Condition: has a Wife & six Children, which must inevitably perish without this Worshipfull Courts Favour

Most humbly Prayed in pitty to his poor Wife & Family youl Favourably be pleased to remitt his Fine and your Peticoner as in Duly bound shall Ever pray Etc
