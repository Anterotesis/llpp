To the Right Worspr. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Elizabeth Garey and Anne Skuller

Sheweth That in Some time past yor. Petr. became bound for their appearance in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions to answer Anne Spratlin for an Assault his by the Recognizance may appeare

That yor. Petr. did not assault the sd. Anne Spratlin And forasmuch as your Petr. being very poore woman and haveing familys to [...] mainleyne who are almost ready to parish for want of releise And the Sd. Anne Spratlin being a person of a wicked life & conversacen

P the: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most Pray yor. Worspp to be passed to Grant that they may be discharged from the Sd. Recognizance

And yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc.

Eliz Geney her [x] marks

Anne Skuller her [x] marks
