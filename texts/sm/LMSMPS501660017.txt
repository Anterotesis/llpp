To the Right Worsppfull his Matyes Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in Sessions

The Humble peticon & Appeale of the [...] Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of the pish of St. Clemts. Danes in the Sd. County

Sheweth that by Ordr. undr. the hands & seales of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqrs. two of his Matyes Justices of the peace for the sd. County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 12th. day of November last Hannah Burford was removed from the Libty of Glass house yard in the pish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate in the sd. County to the pish of St. Clemts Danes in the Sd. County as the place of her last legall settlemt.

That yor Petr. think themselves aggrieved by the Ordr of the sd. two Justices & hope to make it appeare that the Sd. Hannah Burford hath not any settlemt. in the sd. pish of St. Clemts Danes & therefore appeale from the Ordr. of the Sd. her Justices to yor Worspps.

Phimmow Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray Yor. Worspps to appoint a day for the Church- Wardens & Ovrseers of the poor of [---] Glass house yard in the Sd. pish of St. Buttolph without Aldersgate to attend yor Worspps & to abide such Ordr. as shall be made on hearing the Sd. Appeale

And yor Petrs. shall pray Etc
