To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for County of Middx in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of Henry trent and William Pickering Esqrs: Two of His Majestys Justices of the peace for the Liberty of Westmr: bearing date the Second of August 1749 William Christmas an Infant aged upwards of Seven years Son of John and Catherine Christmas both deceased was removed from the Parish of St. James in the Liberty of Westmr. to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitionrs: therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitrs: shall every pray Etc:

Harry Agent for St. George
