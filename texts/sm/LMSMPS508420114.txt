To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Swining in the County of Berks.

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal bearing date on or about the fifteenth day of December One thousand seven hundred and Eighty eight under the hands and Seals of Mich Downs and William Gowan Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex whereby William Stevens Ann his Wife and their six Children to wit Mary and Joseph Aged Ten Years Charles aged Nine Years Sarah aged Eight Years Rose aged Six Years and Hester aged five Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James in the City and Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of [...] as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners think themselves much Aggrieved by the said removal and thereby Pray leave to appeal to the same and that a Day may be appointed in the present Session for the hearing and determining thereof
