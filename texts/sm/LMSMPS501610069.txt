[...] the Rt. Worship full his Maties: Justices of the peace forthe County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Mary White Chappell in the said County.

[...] That by an Order under the Hands and Seales of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for the peace for the said County (Quor anns) bearing date the 17th: day of Aprill Abraham Storer was removed from the parish of St: James Clerkenwell in this County to the said parish of St: Mary White Chappell as to the place of his last Legall Settlement

That your Petrs: think them selves agrieved by the said Order and Humbly Appeale to this Court against the same and pray that ye Worshipps would be pleased to ord [...] and Overseers [...] Clerkenwell and all [...] attend this Court some day this present [...] Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgemt: and determination of this Court touching the said Appeale

And Your Petrs: shall ever pray Etc
