To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session now Assembled

The humble Petition of Wm Martin

Sheweth That your Petitioner by Order of Court Dated the Tenth day of May 1733 was appointed to releive pass and Convey the Vagrants in and through this County for the Year now next ensuing at Thirty Pounds a Year

That there is due to your Petitioner Fifteen Pounds for half a Years Sallary due at Michaelmas last

Your Petitioner humbly prays your Worshipps to Order the sume of fifteen Pounds for his said half Year Sallary to be paid by your Worshipps Treasurer

And your Petitioner in duty bound Shall ever pray

William Martin:
