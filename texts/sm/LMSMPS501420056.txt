To the Right Worspl. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble peticon. of Mary Cowley of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated two shillings P month towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for this present year.

That your Peticoner conceives herselfe aggreived by the said assessment For that she is rated 6d P month more then she paid the last year which is more in proporcon then divers of the Inhabitants of the said parish are now rated.

P S Westley Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays your Worsps wilbe pleased to mittigate the said Rate in Such manner as unto yor: Worsps shall Seem meet.

And yor: Petr: shall ever pray Etc.
