To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace assembled at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the County of Middlesex

The humble petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Egham in the County of Surry.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of on Order under the Hands and Seals of Jo: D: Birkhead and Samuel Tufnell Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of Peace for the said County of Middlesex Judith Webber Widow was removed from the Parish of Twickenham in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Egham in the said County of Surry by which Order yor Petrs Apprehend the said Parish of Egham to be Aggrieved

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that your worships will be [...] pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing of their said Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
