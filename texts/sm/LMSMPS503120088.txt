To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John Zachry London.

Sheweth That on the Complaint of the Churchwardens and overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Gyles in the Fields in the County of Middx that one Ann Miles Widow of Charles Miles was lately come into the said Parish endeavouring to Settle there contrary to Law Benjamin Burroughs and John Milner Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the. Peace for the said County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) made an Order under their hands & Seals bearing Date the 29th: day of August 1735 Whereby [---] the said Ann Miles was removed from [---] the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields into the said Parish of St: John Zachry requiring the Chruchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of St John Zachry to Provide for the Sd. Ann Miles according to Law.

That your Petr: humbly conceive themselves agreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and Pray that a day may be appointed in this present General Sessions for vacating the sd: Order & that all persons concerned may be ordered to attend.

And yor Petr. as in duty bound shall ever Pray Etc

Wm. Moore Sellr: for the Appellants.
