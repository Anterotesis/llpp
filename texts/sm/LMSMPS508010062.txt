To His Majestys Justices of the Peace of the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions holden at in and for the said County on Wednesday the 14th. Day of Septr. 1785.

The humble Petition of The Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of and under a certain a certain Warrant a Order of Removal under the Hand and Seal of D Wilmot Esqr. one of His Majestys Justices of the Peace of the sd. County of Middlesex dated the 25th. Day of June last, Edward Harrison Alice his Wife and their two Children named Martha aged 12 Years or thereabouts & James aged six Years & upwards, were removed from the Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the sd County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street as the place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners humbly conceive and are Advised that the sd. Parish of Allhallows Lombard Street is not the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Paupers and your Petitioners do accordingly intend to Appeal against the sd. Order of Removal But in as much as your Petrs. are not at present prepared to Argue the sd [...] Appeal.

Your Petitioners humbly pray yr Honors that their Appeal from the said

Order of Removal may be entred of Reced in the Proceed ss. of this Honble Court And that the Heoering thereof be postponed until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the sd County of Middlesex

And upon Petrs. as is duty bound will ever pray Etc.
