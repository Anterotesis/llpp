To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Townsend of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex Pawnbroker

Sheweth

That by a certain conviction under the Hand and Seal of John Nares Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing date the twenty sixth day of March 1799 Your Petitioners was convicted as therein alledged for that on the twenty first day of March Instant at the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid your Petitioner who then and there used and Exercised the Trade and Business of a Pownbroker did demand receive and take of and from Hannah Gogay on reduming the Pawn and pledge thereafter mentioned the sum of One Penny as and for and by way of Profit upon and over and above the sum of One shilling and six pence theretofore to wit on the sixteenth day of February last last and advanced by your Petitioner upon a certain Pawn and pledge that is to say three Cotton Window Curtain to the said Hannah Gogay the said Sum of One Penny so demanded received and taken us aforesaid being more than at and after the rate of four Pence for the Evan of Twenty Shillings by the Calender Month including the Current Month in which such Pawn and pledge was reduced altho' such Month was not then Expired Contrary to the form of the Statute in such came made and provided and the said Justice did adjudge Your Petitioner to pay and Forfeit for the same the sum of Five Pounds of Lawful Money of Great Britain

That your Petition conceives himself aggrieved by the Judgment of the said Justice and therefore

Your Petitioner doth appeal to your Worships at this General Quarter Sessions of the Peace against the Judgment of the said Justice and doth pray such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

B. Bishop Solr. for Appellant No 2 Middlx Temple Lant

Thos: Townsend
