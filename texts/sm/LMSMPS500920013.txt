To the Pr Worsp her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx The humble Peticon of Joseph Lee

Sheweth That in august 1702 yor. petr became apprntice to Luke Delahoyd of the Pish of St. Martin in the Feilds in the sd County Barber Perriwigsmaker for the Term of Seaven Yeares do by the [...] of Apperntishipp more at large appeare

That Yor. Petrs. sd: Master hath frequently Braten abused your Petr. and hath not allowed him such necessarys as are fittings for an Apprentice And the sd. Luke Delahoyd standing come for his appearane in this Honble Cor this Present Sessions

Yor Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes Your Worpps to be pleased to grant that be way he Discharged from his sd Apprentshipp

And Yor. Petr shall Ever pray Etc

Jos Lee his mke

P Tho: Nash
