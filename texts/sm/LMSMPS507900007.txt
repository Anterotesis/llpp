To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace, Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Paul Covent Garden in the said County.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of G. Mercer and M. A. Taylor Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 8th. day of November last. Ann Flaxman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St Martin in the fields in the sd City and Liberty to the sd Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same; And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Session for the hearing & determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Richd Christmas Jno French James Sidgwith} Churchwardens

Chaplin Gould.} Overseers of the Poor
