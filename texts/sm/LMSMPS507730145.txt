To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace in the County of Middlesex at the General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John in the City of Exeter

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands & Seals of James Fielding and William Hyde Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in & for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty sixth day of July now last [...] Ann Hitchfield Singlewoman was removed and conveyed out of and from the Parish of Saint James in the City of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint [---] John by which your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Order and Adju- -dication of the said two Justices and pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Sessions for the Hearing and Determination of the said Appeal And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jos: Burrow Sollicitor for the Appellants
