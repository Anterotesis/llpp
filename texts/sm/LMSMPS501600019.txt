To the Rt. Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Sessions at Hickshall

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seales [...] John Fuller and Tho: Rand Esqr Two of his Majts. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (quor un) bearing date the Seventeenth day of January last past, Whereby Ralph Capper and his two children William aged about Tenn years and Sarah aged about Five years. were removed from the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County to your Petr said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields whereby your Petrs. humbly conceive they are wronged and aggrieved.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly appeale to your Worps, That your Worsps would be pleased [---] to appoint a Day to hear and determine the same.

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Gerrard Smith Overseer
