To the Right Worshipfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands and Seales of John Tarver and John Venner Esqrs. Two of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the said County (one of them being of the Quorum [---] ) Bearing date the Seventh day of March Last Abigail Hayes Single woman was Removed from the Parish of Twickenham in the said County to the said Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County as the Place of her Last Legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners thing them Se [...] Agreived by the said Order and Judgment of the Said Two Justices of the Peace doe [---] Appeale to this Court against the Same and Humbly Pray your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint a day the next Sessions to hear and determine the Same

And your Petitioners as in duty Bound Shall ever Pray
