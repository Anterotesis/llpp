To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bridget otherwise St. Bride in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Francis Bedwell and Bartholomew Hammond Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 31st. day of March last Mary Ives aged upwards of Eight years was removed from the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the Liberty and County aforesaid to the sd. Parish of St. [...] otherwise St. Bride as the Place of her last legal Settlement [...] Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be [...] [...] Do Appeal

Wm. Gason Attorney for the Appellants Your Petitioners therefore Humbly [...] that the Benefit of the sd. Appeal [...] Saved Unto them And that the Hearing and Determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be held for the sd. County of Middlesex
