To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Aaron Graham and Rupert Clarke Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County bearing date on or about the first day of April Instant Margaret Lonnon aged Forty and her four Children Vizt. Helena aged Fourteen John aged nine Ann aged Six and Willim. aged three years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John Hampstead in the said County to the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices appeal to Your Worships against the same and request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the peace of be holden for the said County and that all Parties concerned may be then ordered to attend to abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And as Etc. Arthur Gliddon Sollr. for Appellants
