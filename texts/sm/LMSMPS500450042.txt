To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Richard Balderston

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. stand Indicted in the Honble Court for assaulting of on Mary Hoare As thereby may appeare

That your Petr. caused notice to be left att the usuall place of abode of the sd: Mary Hoare the person who left the sd. notice in writing Saeing now ready in Court to testify the Same which notice was for the sd. [---] Hoare to appeare this day in this Honble Court And forasumch as your Petr. being a very poore Men And the sd. Mary Hoare not appearing in this Honble Court all this Sessions your Petr conteines She will not now appeare

P Tho Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to Grant that he may be discharged from the sd Indictmt.

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc

Rich: Balderson
