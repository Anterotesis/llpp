To the Worshipfull his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions of the Peace at the Sessions House an Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Robert Wild of No 33 Nicholas Lane London Gentleman

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Cosan and William Blackbarrow Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the Third day of September One thousand Seven hundred and Eighty five your Petitioner was adjudged to be the reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child born of the body of Mary Tolks Single Woman in the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County and your Petitioner was also ordered to pay to the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Clerkenwell or some or one of them Five pounds thirteen Shillings and Four pence for and towards the lying [---] of the said Mary Tolks and the Maintenance of the said Bastard Child to the Thirtieth day of August last past Whereby your Petitioner concieves himself aggrieved And

Therefore he appeals to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order And pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determination of the said Appeal and to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex and all Persons concerned do attend this Court

And your Petitioner will ever pray Etc
