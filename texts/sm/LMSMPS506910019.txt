To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens other antient Inhabitants of the pish of St. Mary Islington in the [...] County

Sheweth That some time past att a Vestry held for the sd. Parish Itt was (amount other things) Ordered that a Settled Watch should be mainteyned & Kept in the sd. Parish for the better Guard & Security of the Inhabitant thereof

P. Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray [...] Your Worpps tho be pleased to Grant an Order from this Honble Court for the better [...] & Confirming the above sd. Order of Vestry If yor. Worspps [...] Soe thinke fill,

And Yor. Petr. Ever pray Etc
