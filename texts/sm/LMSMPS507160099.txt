Middlesex To the Worshippl his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Faith under Saint Paul in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warant or order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of David Wilms and John Spiller Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date on the Twenty sixth day of August last past Mary Bathurst and her son William Batturst of the age of Seven Months and upwards by Thomas Basthurst her Husband who hath being impursed into his Majesty's Justices was removed from the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Faith on the City of London as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices and therefore appeal to this Honorable Court against the same

And pray that this Honorable Court will Please to appoint a day for the hearing and determining [...] appeal and to make such Order therein as may be just.

And your Petitioners will ever pray

John Greenaway Soll. for the appellants 13th Sepr 1779.
