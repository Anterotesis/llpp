To the Right Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens of the Parish of St. Michael Battishaw London.

Sheweth, That by Warrant or Order dated the first day of September last under the Hands and Seales of Thomas Railton & T. Boteler Esqrs. Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx & Libty of Westmr. (Quor unus) Elizabeth Greed wife of John Greed and George her Son were removed from the Parish of St. Margart Westmr. in the Said County to the Said Parish of St. Michael Battishaw, as the place of last legal Settlement of the Said John Greed.

That yor. Peticonrs. find themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Ordr. of the Said Two Justices, and humbly appeale against the same to this Honble Court.

Yor. Peticonrs. therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps will be pleased to order the Church- Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Sd. Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. and all other psons concerned to attend this Court Some day this prsent Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. and determinacon of this Court upon and touching the Said Appeale, and that yor. Peticoners may have such releife herein as to yor. Wopps shall Seem wee [...]

And Yor. Peticonrs. shall Pray Etc

Wm: Young William Keirby} Church Wardens
