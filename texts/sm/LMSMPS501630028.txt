To the Rt. Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be holden for this said County of Middlx

The humble Petition and appeal of Wm. Bezer John Ibbott Richard Kent John Hales Wm. Pumphry. Frances Chittenden Charles Harrison and John Beezely with many other of the Inhabitants of Wappin Stepney in the said County of Middlx

Humbly Sheweth

That your Petitioner are all of them charged the poor Rate in the said Hamblet more then they are able to pay and bear and are charged much more in proportion then many other of their Neighbours by which they think they are very much aggreived

Therefore humbly pay that as the said rate hath been made ad charged upon them by such persons as are not charged equall to some of them nor the rest of yor Petitionr in proportion to them that you will be pleased to appoint some day this present Quarter Sessions for the Church Warden & Overseers of the poor of the said Hamblet of Wappin Stepney to attend this Court to abide by the Judgemt. of the same as yor. Worshipps in yor. wife Judgemt shall soe meet

And shall pray Etc
