To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Quarter Sessions at Hicks's Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of George Walter and Thomas Mulso two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing date the twenty third day of May last Elizabeth Tucker Singlewoman was Removed from the Parish of St. Anne Westmr: in the said County to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves agrieved.

Your Petitionrs. therefore humbly pray this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal & that all persons concerned may Attend the same time

And your Petitionrs. will ever pray Etc

S: Parry Sellr.

John Blakesley Jos: Lyster} Overseers of the Poor
