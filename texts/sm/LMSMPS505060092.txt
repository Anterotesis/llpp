Middx To this Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of [...] Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Walton in the Said County

Sheweth That the [...] John Foster Singleman was reme [...] from the parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Libty of Westminster in the County of Middx by Warrant under the Handsand Seals of John Upton and William Timbrell Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx afd to the sd parish of Walton in the County of Surry as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby your petitioners apprehend themselves to be aggreived be not being now ready with their Witness to have the Merits of the said Appeal fully heard and determined

Therefore your Petitioners pray this Court will be pleased to receive the [...] an Appeal against the Said Order and respit the Same to the next General Session of the peace to be holden in & for the Said County of Middx

And your petitioners will pray Etc
