To the Rt. Woppfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London,

Shew That by virtue of an Order of Pass Warrt. bearing date the thirteenth day of August last past andr. the hands & seals of [...] two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for this County (Quor unus) one Elizabeth Goldsmith with her two Children (vizt.) Anne aged about 8 years & Stephen aged about 2 years were removed and Conveyed from the Parish of St. Sepulhrs. in the sd County to yor. Peticonrs sd Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London wch. was adjudged by these Justices to be the place of their last legal Settlemt. in regard that Edward Goldsmith husband of the sd Elizabeth (who was lately absconded from her) was bound an Apprentice there to one Henry Parsons a Currier & Seved him as such for the whole terme of Seven Years.

That yor. Peticonrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd. Warrt. or Ordr. & Judgmt. of the sd. Justices & humbly Appeale agat. the same to this Honble Court.

Yor. Peticonrs therefore humbly Pray yor. Wopps wife be pleased to Ordr. the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the sd Parish of St. Sepulchrs. & all other [...] concerned to attend this Court some day this [...] sent Sessions, to hear & abide the Judgmt. [...] acon of this Court upon and touching the Appale, and that yor. Petrs. [...] have such [...] as to yor. Wopps shall seem meet Etc

An [...] Peticonrs. shall Pray Etc
