The Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Lowlayton in the County of Essex.

Sheweth That by virtue or a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of James Spagg and John Sherwood Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of April last John Bilbie and Susannah his Wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Bromley Saint Leonard in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Lowlayton as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved,

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Bromley Saint Leonard and all Persons concerned Do then attend this Court

And Your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.

Adam Straey Heath Ellis} Churchwardens

and

Jno. Sbrye Saml. Davis} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of, Lowlayton.
