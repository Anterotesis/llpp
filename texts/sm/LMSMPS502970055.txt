To the Rt. Worshipl. his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the several Parishes of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs and Saint George the Martyr in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Elizabeth Jenkins Singlewoman was by Order of pass Warrant dated the thirteenth Day of December last past under the Hands and Seals of Robert Dennet and Clifford William Philip Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) removed and conveyed from the Parish of Trinity Minories (in the Liberty of the Tower of London) Middle to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs as the place of her last legall Settlement

Rd. Walkins Sell for the Appeallts. Your Petrs. humbly conceive themselves aggreived thereby and appeal from the Judgment of the said two justices to the judgement of this Honoble Court and desire a day of hearing may be appointed this Sessions And that all persons Concerned may be Ordered to attend to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
