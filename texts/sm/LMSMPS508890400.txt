Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of William Perry- of Hillingdon in the County of Middlesex Doctor of Physic

Sheweth That a certain Rate was made on or about the 23d. day of May last for the relief of the Poor of the Parish of Chiswick in the County of Middlesex which said Rate was not published in the Church until Sunday the 16th. day of June instant.

That your Petitioner verify believes that several Inhabitants of the said Parish who ought to contribute and be rated to the maintenance of the Poor of the said Parish are totally omitted to be charged in the said rate and others of the said Inhabitants are rated considerably less than they ought to be in regard to their property within the said Parish whilst your Petitioner who holds considerable property and Estate rateable to the Poor of the said Parish and others within the said parish are there by overrated and aggrieved.

Your Petitioner therefore appeals against the aforesaid Rate and prays that the same may be quashed and that your Worships will appoint a day in the present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chiswick and all other Persons concerned to appear before you to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Court touching the appeal.

And Your Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc.

Saml Boarn Sollicitor for the Appellant
