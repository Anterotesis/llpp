To the Worshipfull the Justices of Peace at the Sessions held at Hicks Hall for ye. County of Middx

The humble Petition of Joseph Cocks head

Sheweth That your Petr. hath served one William Bannister of Westmr. as his Apprentice for yr. Space of 4 Yeares & upwards, but now the sd. Bannister hath causes your Petr. to be pressed & sent into ye savey where her new remains confined in order to be sent forthwith [---] into her mates. Service.

That the sd. Bannister notwithstanding presents a Right to the remainder of ye Petrs. Time for which he was bound to him & refuses to give him up his Indenting & also detains ye Petr. Clothes.

Yor. Petr. therefore pray's the sd Bannster may deliver was his Indentures & defection him of & Remaindr. of his time, and also restore him his Clothes.

And you Petr. shall ever pray Etc
