To the Right Worll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Giles in the Feilds

Sheweth That one Elizabeth Parker als Perkes was lately passed by warrant under the hands & [...] seals of St John Elwes Knt & John Worley Esqr two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County from the pish of St Clement Danes in the said County to the said pish of St Giles in the feild thereby alleadging that she was legally Settled at the said pish of St Giles in the Feild

P J Tycon

Your Peticonrs: thincke themselves aggrieved wth: the Indgm [...] of the said two Justices And to therefore Appeal to this Worll. Bench And humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said pish of St Clement Danes may be Ordered to appear in this Court some day this Sessions to heare & abide the small end & determination of this [...] touching this Appeal

And Yor. Petionrs: as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc
