To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Adjourned Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth. That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of William Rugge and Thomas Edwards Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Eleventh day of July Instant Mary Francis Singlewoman aged Thirty three Years was Removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the said County of Middlesex as the Place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved And Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore Pray this Court will be Pleased to appoint a day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned may then attend.

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.

Rt. Tudman Soll for the Appellt.
