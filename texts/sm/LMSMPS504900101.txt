To the Worshipful His Majestys, Justices of the Peace for the County Middlesex Assembled in their General Session of the Peace.

The Humble petition and appeal of [...] Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Richmund [...] County of Surry.

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of John Cox and Saunders Welch Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the county of middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 24th. day of October last Elizabeth Martel wife of John Martel a Foreigner (said in the said Order to be gone from her and not to be found) was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of Richmond as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Martel

Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the [...] two Justices of the peace, Appeal again the same and humbly pray your Worships to Appoint some day in the present General Session of the peace for the Rearing and Determining of the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. will ever pray Etc William Lamb Attorney for the Appellts.
