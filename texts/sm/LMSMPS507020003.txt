To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Clough of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County Widdow

Sheweth That your Petirs Allowed Twelve Pence [...] Month as a Pension from the Said Parish But in regard She being very poor & haveing Three Children to Maintenance And by her hard Labour and Industry for the Mainteynance of them She hath almost Lost the use of all her Limbs Soe that your Petr. is now rendred wholly Incapable of Getting a Lively hood And for as much as Yor Petitioner Husband Loosing his Life in her Matys Service And yor Petitioner being not longer Able to Subsist on the said Pension And the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish refuseing to Allow her any Poor without an Order of his Honourable Court

P Tho: Rash

Your Petr therefore most humble prays your Worships to be pleased to Order that her Said Persons May be Augmented as unto yor Worships Shall Seem meete

And yor Petr Shall ever pray etc
