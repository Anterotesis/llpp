To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at the Session House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Certification of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Feddington in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Feddington by Virtue of an Order or Warrant in to sitting under the Hands and Seals of Peter Handers or an [...] John [...] Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing Date the first Day of March last directed to them the said Churchwardens and Overseers resiting that Complaint and due proof & upon Oath hath been Made onto and before them the said Peter Honderson and John [...] as such Justices as aforesaid by the said Churchwardens and Overseers that William Mills late of the same Parish Laborer had sometime some deserted his Wife and Children an that to his Wife was since dead and also that the said two Children namely Ann Mills age at three Years and William P Mill & aged two Years are know a Charge to the said Parish of Loddington being the plase of their last legal Settlement And along [...] that the said William Mills had last property in the said Parish of Teddington, Comiting of Shop Goals and Household Gaol and [...] are the said Parish of the said Charge in whole or in part the said Peter Handerson and John [...] Justices as aforesaid did Authorize and Command them the said Churchwardens and Overseers to seized and take possession of the said Goods and Chattles

Chattles of him the said William Mills at [x] Teddington aforesaid and that the said Churchwardens and Overseers should detain and keep them in their possession until further Order shall be made in the promises at the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County and the said Churchwardens and Overseers did further Certify that by Virtue of such Warrant they the said Churchwardens and Overseers on the second Day of the same Month of March last siezed and took Possession of the said Goods and Chattles of him the said William Mills mentioned in the said Warrant amounting to gether in the who be in Value to the Sum of Ninety three pounds and Fifteen Shillings as by Inventory taken thereof referrence being thereunto had will more fully appear and last them under the Care and protection of Thomas Kennett the Brother in Law of him the said William Mills to be Applied and dispeaced of for the providing and [...] up of his said two Children in John Mannner as the Court may in their Direction Seem meet and in to repecting sames pursuant to the Statue & in such Cases made and provided

Your petitioners therefore pray this Court will be board to Confirm the said pursuant and make an Order for them the said Churchwardens and Overseers to dispose of the goods and Chattles of the said William Mills by Sale or otherwise agreeable to the purposes of the Acts Subjects to Account And your petitioners shall pray &

James D [...] Martin Lemon} Churchwardens

Thos Perry John Clark} Overseers of the Poors
