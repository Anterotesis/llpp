To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and Thomas Balack Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 26th. day of April 1764 Mary Byfield Widow of Robert Byfield deceased was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Anneas the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved And against the said Order do Appeal to this Court

And humbly Pray that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto Your Petitioners untill the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for this County And that your Worships will be Pleased to Appoint some day in the said next Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that all Persons concerned may then and there Attend

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Edwd Scarlett Thos Chamberlain John Tagnier Osborn Thornton} ChurchWardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: Anne
