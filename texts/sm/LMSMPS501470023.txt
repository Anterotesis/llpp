To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticonr Appeale of William Bale of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the Counties afore said Blacksmith

Sheweth That yor:poore petr is Assesed two Shillings and two pence a quarter towards the releife of the poore of the said parish have ing wife and two small Children to be maintained by his own hand Labour And for as much as yor petr: payes but 35s: per Ann: therefore humbly prayer to be discharged from paying the same as to yor Worpp shall Seem meet

And yor: petr shall pray Etc

William Bale

the peticon of William Bale of the parish of St Giles Cripplegate

Middx

About the poore Rate

Ch: quer Ally
