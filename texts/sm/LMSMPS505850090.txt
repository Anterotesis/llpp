To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Spinnage and George Wrighte Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twelfth Day of March least Amos payne Singleman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby Your petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and the said Order And pray that the Benefit of this Appeal may be saved into Your Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for this County and that your Worhsips will please to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary le Bone and all persons concerned do then Attend

And Your petitioners shall pray Etc.

Benjn. Gee W. Williams Jas. Tayne John. Allison John Swann} Church Wardens and Overveers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
