To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justice's of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace at Hick's Hall Assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the county of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and John Cox Esquires two of his Majesty's Justice's of the Peace Acting in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the third Day of November One Thousand Seven hundred and Seventy four Sarah Hadley Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields within the said County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and do Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may be then directed to Attend.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

John Lane Sollicitor for the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden.
