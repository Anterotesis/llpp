To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands & Seals of Richard Manley and Samuel Waddington Esqr. Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City & Liberty of Westminster in this County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th. day of August 1763 Anne Rossington, Spinster aged 20 Years the Daughter of George Rossington and Sarah Rossington both deced: was removed from the Parish of St. Margart in the City of Westminster to the Parish of St. Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex as the Place of his last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly Pray this Court will Please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.

John Manwell Benjn Gooden} Churchwardens.

Wm. Southall} Overseers of the Poor
