The Humble Petition of Joseph Satchell and Ann his Wife

Cripplegate for rel

Mr. Fuller Mr. Browne Mr. Tilliard & Mr. Whetham or 1

Middx ss: Ad General Quartial Session pacis Dui Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks hall in St. John Street in Com prd. adjorn die Martis Scilt vicesimo Secundo die Aprilis Anno regni Dui Georgij mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto.

It is Ordered by this Court that it be, And it is hereby Rocomended unto Richard Browne John Fuller Isaac Tillard and John Whetham Esqrs Justices of the peace for the said County or any one of them to Sumon before them or any of them the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County, and to Examin into the truth of the Petrs: complaint and make such order for the Releife of the Petr and his wife as to the said Justices of the peace or anyone of them shall seem meet.
