To the Rt: Worpll: his Maties: Justices of the peace of the County of Middx assembled in Quarter Sessions.

The humble Petition of ye Churchwardens & Overseers for ye poor of the Parish of St: Ann within the Libty of Westmr in ye County aforesd.

Sheweth That By Warrt. dated ye tenth ye October last under the hands & Seales of ye Rt: Worpll: Sr Charles Lee and Thomas Ellis [...] two of his Matis: Justices of ye peace[---] [...] Mary Gardner Since dead & Gerrard [...] Pish [...] [...] of St. And as being Settled these [...] are [...] & [...] for 31 qr Last year paying 13s: p Anno.

Now Since upon Shirt Inquiry yr Petrs. find that the fair Mary Gardners Mother was an housebury and She only lived with her in ye Nature of a Lodger & had noe after Settlemt. in ye Sd Pish. Byeling yr Sd Humbly conceive themselves aggressive & appeal Setle Judgemt. of this Worpll Bench for Receipt & craves a day of learing and that ye Churchwardens & overseers of ye poor of ye Pish of St Giles in ye feild may attend to Shew cause.

And yr Petr Shall pray Etc

Overseers{ Tho: Harriat John Jackson
