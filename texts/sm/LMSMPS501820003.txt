To the Worsppll. the Chairman & the rest of his Majts. Justices of the peace (assembled

The humble: Petition of Isabella Williams

Sheweth That yor. Worspps Petr. not with standing she hath put upp abundome of abuses, and Violent assaults by the husband John: William who has kept a Vile woman Under her Nose Senall Years and spence his Substance on her & when Comes home with Knives & other weapons has Cut & wounded Yor. Petr. that her Life is in Continuall danger

Humbly Craves this honble: Bench would Continue him. Yor petr not being safe or out of danger or otherwise as you shall Seeme Meet

and as bound shall pray Etc Isabella Williams
