Wapping Stepney The humble Petition of Severall of the Inhabitants thereof

to att: Th: 10 cl

Bezar not retend Ibbot not releived Kent discharged of the wholetax Hales not releived Pomfrey reduce to 7d p mo Chittenden releivd to 9d p mo [...] Harrison non [...] Bezeley - rel [...] 1s p mo app: un hd. adjd till Fa: 10 cl

Midds Ad genal quartial Session Paris Din Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St John Street in Com Ped p adjorn die Marte Scilt decmio sxto die July Anno regin Din and Georgy Dei gra mmc Regis mane Britaunie Etc Tertio

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the hamblett of Wapping [...] Stepney in this County have notice of this appeale and that they doe attend this Court on Thursday next at ten of the clock in the forenoon to shew cause why the Appeallants should not be releived in the Premisses within menconed

P Cur Harcourt
