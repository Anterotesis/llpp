To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the sd. County

Sheweth That on or about the fourteenth day of March last as me Ann Fox wife Peter Fox was Wandring and begging in the said parish she was Delivered of a Female Child baptized Jame which deped about ten days after her Birth, whereby she and her said Child became chargeable to the said parish

That Yor. Petr. did Detain the said Woman in Custody untill the 9th. day of April now last [...] when Yor, Petrs. Conveyed her before John Milner Esqr. one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for this County who Examined her and Committed her to the House of Correction at Clerkenwell where she new Remains

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly pray that yor. Worships will be pleased to Order the Frear of this County to pay Yor. Petrs. such a Sum of money as this Court shall adjudge a reasonable Satisfaction for the charges the said parish has been put unto on Accot. of the said Ann Fox and her said Child

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Danl. Bolton Atty. for ye. Petrs.
