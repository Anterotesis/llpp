To the Worshipsfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks hall Assembled

The humble Petition Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Rolls in the same County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Boulton Manwaring and John Lawton Esqr. two of his Majestys Justice of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 7th. Day of December 1750 Danl. Chas. Croydon was removed from the Parish of St. John Wappin in the Sd. County of Middlesex to the Liberty of the the Rolls adforesd. whereby your Petrs. conceive themselves agrieved and agt. Order do Appeal

John Reeves Henry Smith } Ovrs. of the Poor Your Petr. therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be appointed this present Session to hear & Determine the matter of the sd. Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall pray Etc

Middx At the General Quarter Session of the Peace of our Sovereign Lord the King holden for the County of Middx of at Hicks Hall in St. John Street in the County aforesaid by adjourment on Tuesday teh 15th. Day of Janry in the 24th. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second King of Grt. Britain etc

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. John Wappin in this County have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Persons concerned do attend this Court on Friday next at ten of the Clock in the fore noon to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the Said Appeal

By the Court Waller
