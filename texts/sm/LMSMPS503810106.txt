[...] Rt Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for [...] County of the Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Thomas Smith Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in this County bearing date the 30th. day of September 1747. Jane Davis, Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to the Parish of St. George Hanover [...] peace in the County of Middlesex as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will please to appoint a time for bearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray

S: Parry Agent for St. George Hanover Square
