To ye Worshipfulls. their Maties: Justices of yr Peace:.

At ye Seashons: holden at Hickss: Hall: in Com Middx: Humbly Sheweth.

That yor Worships Petioner. John Branwood. Carpender had a warrt. Sirued uppon On Henry Johnson: Carpender for dayes worke done: & bound him Over to this Prsant Seashons; Not withstanding ye Said Henery doth not Appeare:. Where uppo. yor poore Petionr. humbly Begs yor Worsps to grant him a warrt. to fetch him Into Cort.

And yor petionr: as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
