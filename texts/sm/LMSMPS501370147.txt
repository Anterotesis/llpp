To the Right Worpll her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London.

Sheweth That by vertue of an Ordr. or Pass Warrt. undr. the Hands and Seales of Sr. Jonathan Andrews & Stephen Hall Esqr. two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorunns) bearing date the 12th. day of January instant Robert Alcock & Mary his wife & Ann their Child were removed & conveyed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the sd. County to yor. Peticonrs. said Parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate London as the place of their last legal Settlemt.

That yor. Peticonrs. conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Order or Pass Warrt. of the said Justices, and humbly appeale agat. the Same to this Court.

Wherefore yor. Peticonrs. humbly Pray that you would please to ordr. the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of She Said Parish of St. Mary White Chappell & all other persons concerned, to attend this Court Some day this present Sessions, to hear the Judgmt. & determinacon of yor. Wopps upon and concerning the Said Appeale,

And yor. Peticonrs. shall Pray Etc
