To the Rt. Worshipl. his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the ChurchWarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and- Ely Rents in the Parish of St. Andrew Holbour in the said County of Middx

Sheweth

That Thomas Howard with Hannah his Wife and- their two Children (vizt.) Elizabeth aged two Years & Thomas aged Thirteen Months were by an Order or Pass Warrant Dated the Twenty Sixth Day of September last under the hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Thomas Robe Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quorum Unus) removed and conveyed from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said Liberty of Saffron-Hill Watton Garden and Ely Rents in the said parish of St. Andrew Holborn as the Place of their last Legal Settlement

Your. Petrs. humbly conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and Appeal from the Judgement of the said two Justices to the Judgment of this Honoble Court And desire a Day of hearing may be appointed this present Sessions And that all persons concerned may be Ordered to Attend to hear and abide, the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
