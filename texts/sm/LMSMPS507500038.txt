To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of James Spagg and William Blackmore Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Eighth day of November now last Catherine Bowerman an was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Hamlet of Rattcliffe in the said County of Middlesex to be said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell the Place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Catherine Bowerman

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever Pray etc.

S. Malbon Sollr. for the Said Parish of Shadwell
