To the Right Worpll. his Maties. Justices of the Pruce for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appral of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Olave Southwork in the County of Jurry.

Sheweth

That on the Second of Novembr. last was Passed by virtue of a wart. under the house and Stales of Sr John Elwes. Knt. are Francis Negus. Esqr. two of his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx from the Parish of St Martins in the Feilds in the said County into the said Parish of St Olave Southwork Foresaid Ralph Scott, and Richard Scott two of the Children of Ralph Scott a Brewer Servt.

Your Peticonrs. think themselves agreived with the Judgment of the said two Justices. And doe Appeal to this Court And humbly pray the Promises comidered that the Over Seers of the Poor of the Parish of St Martins in the Feilds in this County. And all other Parties concern'd may attend this Cort. Some day this Sessions to hear and abise the finall one and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor. Peticoners shall ever Pray Etc

Humfred Elwes Script.

Richard Parkins John Roberts} Churchwardens of St. Olaves.
