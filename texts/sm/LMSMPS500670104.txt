To the Rt. Honoble. Sr: Francis Child Kt. Lord Mayor of the Citty of London; And to the rest of the Honoble. Bench of Justices sitting in the old Bayly

The Petition of Anne Hawkins and Elizabeth Tompkins prisonrs. in the Gonle of Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That yor: peticoners being Accused for suspicion of Felony; They being aloegeather Innocent of what they stand charged; and having there friends about them to prove there Innocenty

Humbly peace this Honobles: Court they may be tryed ; or Admitted to Bayte

And yor. peticoners as in duty bounds Shall ever pray Etc.
