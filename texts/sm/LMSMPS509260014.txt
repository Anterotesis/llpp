To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of R. Davies and [...] Reynett Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the [---] County of Middlesex bearing date the 14 of May 1796 Gilbert Wardlow and his Wife Eleanor Frances Wardlow and their three Children Namely Mary aged 8 Years or thereabouts and Gilbert aged 14 Months or thereabout were remov'd from the Parish of Saint George Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Gilbert Wardlow his said Wife and Children

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order and humbly appeal against the same

And Your Petitioner Pray Etc

S. Malbon Sollicitor for the appellts.
