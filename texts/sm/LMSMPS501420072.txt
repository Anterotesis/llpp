To the Right Worsph. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of Jane West of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said county widow.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated one shilling P month towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for this present year.

That your Peticoner hath susteyned divers losses in the way of trade Soe that she is not able to pay the said rate.

P S Westley Your Peticoner therefore humbly Prays your Worspp to be pleased to mittigate the said rate as unto your Worspp shall seem meet.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
