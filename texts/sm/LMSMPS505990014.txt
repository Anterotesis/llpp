To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals of Peter Planck & Thos. Kynaston Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the sd. County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 12th. day of Decr. last 1769 Elizabeth Wood Singlewoman was removed from and out of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid in the said County to the sd. Parish of St. George the Martyr as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitrs. think themselves Aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the same

Therefore your Petrs. humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present General Quarter Session of the Peace for the hearing & determining the sd. Appeal.

And your Petrs. will ever pray Etc.

Thos. Wade Junr. Solr. for the sd. Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor

8th. Janry. 1770.
