To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace in Sessions assembled at Hicks's Hall in St. John Street in & for the County of Middx

The humble Petition St John Griffith of the parish of St. Sepulchres without in the County of Middx Cordwainer

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. liveing in Redly in Court in [---] the parish of St. Sepulchres afsd; where he rents a house of the annuall rent of 17th. P ann One tnr. Hall & one in Bush overseers of the Pish came abt. fortnight agsd to yor. Petr house & there made a distress & carryed away severall things [...] of vallue under pretence of Seizing the same for want of yor. Petrs. paying a poor to which your Petr. humbly a pprehends was only an Imposition on him the & Severall Neighbours who pay equally in their rents & live in the same court & parish paying nothing at all towards Church poor [...] Scavengr. & as your Petr. can make appear by the affids. of sever. Psons Inhabitants there although severall persons who rent houses of greater rent & there that yor. petr and are per [...] of for greater worth than yor petr are excused from paying any dutys to the poor

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worship's eill be pleased to order the overseers of the sd. parish to attend yor. worsp. in this honoble Court to shew Cause why the sd. goods may not be restored to your petr. or that yor. petr. make otherwise releived in the prmes as to yor Worsps shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc

Jn. Griffith
