The Humble Petition of George Creesey now a Prisoner in Bridewell

Sheweth That your petitionr was by Order of Court Sent to Bridewell for Six weeks. and you Petitioner has been very Sick ever Since his confinement And your. Petitioner is Miserable poor and Most humbly begs you will for give him the Court Fees

And your Petitioner in Duty bound shall Ever Pray
