To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in their Generall quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Wm: Kroger of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That Wm Crampton and Abraham Theobridge were appointed Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish for this Present Year and had a rate made an confirmed for therefore of the Poor in the said Parish contained (as usual in two Books, each Book including a distinct district or part of the said Parish, one of which Books was collected by the said Crampton and the other by the said Theebridge.

That Your Petitioner hath been lately appointed one of the Overseers of the said Parish for the remaining part of this Present Year in the stead of the said Wm Crampton who is lately removed out of the said Parish, and by the Warrant under the Hands and Seales of five of his Majesties Justices of the Peace dwelling in the said Parish (by which he is appointed an Overseer as aforesaid) is commanded and required to take upon him the Execution of the said Office, which [---] Your Petitioner was and is ready and willing to do, But is disabled and him [...] the said Abraham Theebridge[---] who hath gott into this custody both the said Books and refuses to deliver either of them to Your Petitioner per to permit him to receive any of the Moneys thereby assessed.

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Prays [---] that the said Abraham Theebridge may attend this Court with the said Books or rate at such time as your Worships shall think fitt and that this court will then make such Order in the Premises as to them shall seem meet

William Kroger
