To ye Right Worppfull theire Maties: Justices of ye peace for the County of Middx

Humbly Sheweth

The humble Peticon of Henery May of ye Hamblett of Mile End New Towne in ye pish of Stepney in this County

That Yor. peticonr was Churchwarden of ye said Hamblett about One Yeare Since, where Yor peticonr. Expended in his said Office Forty Seaven pounds & Four Shillings wch. he was out of pockett when his Accts: were passed-That Yor peticonr. had a Rate made to Reimburse him the said Forty Seaven pounds & Four Shillings But the Prsent Churchwarden Rufuses to Collect the Same or to make Yor peticonr. Sattisfaccon for the said Money

Therefore yor peticonr. humbly prayes the prmises considered Yor. Worpps will be pleased to Order the prsent Churchwarden of the said Hamblett to Attend here to shew cause why she will not Collect the said Rate made to Reimburse Yor. peticonr. Or otherwise Order as to yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet Etc

And yor peticonr Shall pray Etc [...] of to pay 20s 7d
