To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Grace Jones on the behalfe of her Kinsman Mathew Moore

Sheweth

That in Some time past the sd Mathew Moore became apprentice to Richard Hepthrow of the parish of St. Martins in the Feilds in this County Engraver for the Terme of right yeares comencing from the Day of the date of his Indenture of Apprentishipp As thereby may appeare.

That since the sd. Mathew Moore Soe became Apprentice as aforesd. the sd. Richard Hepthrow hath abused him in a very prosse & harbourous manner by shipping & beating him very Severely when upon upon Complaint thereof made before the Worspl. Justice Ellis the sd Hopthrow stands bound for his Appearance in this Honble Court this prdt Sessons Yett newtheless since that time the sd. Richd. Hepthrow hath wpl the sd Mathew Moore a prisoner in a Close Roome and hath otherwise barbourously abused him.

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes yor. worspp to be pleased to Grant this Honble Bench Warrant for bringing the sd. Richd. Hepthrow into this Honble Court this put Sessions to answer Such things as shalle there alledged against him & likewise to be dealt with according to Law & Justice.

And yor. Petr. shaller pray Etc

Grace Jones
