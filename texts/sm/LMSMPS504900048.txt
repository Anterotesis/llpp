To the Right Honble Sr. Matthew Blakiston Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and the Rest of his Majesty's Justices of Oyer and Terminer and Goal Delivery for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Patrick Grayham now a Prisoner in Newgate

Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner stands charged upon the Oaths of Thomas Hudson and Charles Mead upon Suspicion of feloniously receiving a Quantity of Iron and Lead the Property of the said Thomas Hudson and Edward Cole his Copartner well knowing the same to be Stolen

That your Petitioner doubts not but to prove his Innocence of the Crime charged agt. your Petitioner in his Commitment

Therefore your Petitioner humbly prays that he may be Tryed Bailed or Discharged this present Sessions

And your Petitioner as in Duty bound shall every Pray Etc

Pat. Grayham
