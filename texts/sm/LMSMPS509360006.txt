To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace of our Lord the King holden for the said County

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Shaw of No. 104 Hare Street Bethnal Green in the said County Weaver

Sheweth

That your Petitioner was on the Twenty third day of March last Convicted before Daniel Williams Esquire Rice Davies Esquire and the Reverend Henry Reynett D. D. three of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County for having in his Dwelling hour situate at No. 104 Hare Street aforesaid a Quantity of Unwrought Silk Suspected to have been purloined and embezzled not having given any Account how your Petitioner came by the same

That your Petitioner Conceives himself aggrieved by the said Conviction

Your Petitioner therefore Appeals from the said Conviction and humbly prays that your Worship will be pleased to Appoint a day in this present Sessions to hear and determine the said Appeal

Thos Shaw
