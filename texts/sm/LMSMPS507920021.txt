To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace in and for the sd. County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the hands and Seals of William Quarrill Esqr. and John Staples Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace Acting in and for thesd County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of November last Ann Meckley Wife of Robert Meckley who was then as sea and their Infant Child (viz) Mary Aged about five Months were removed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex to thesd. Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and do Appeal agt. the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a day may be Appointed of this present Session to hear & determine the Matter of thesd. Appeal and that all Parties concerned may then Attend

And Yr. Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Wegener Sollr. for Appellts
