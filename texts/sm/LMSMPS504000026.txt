To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Anne Black Fryers London

Sheweth That by An Order Under the hands and Seals of William Withers and Henry Fielding Esquire two of this Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex to hereof One is of the Quorum bearing date the twenty Eighth Day of December last Mary Glinson Widow & two Children Abraham Aged About five Years And Joseph Aged about two Years were removed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the Said County of Middlesex to the Said parish of Saint Anne Black Fryers in the City of London As the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves Aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices of the Peace Appeal to your Worship's Against the Said Order And humbly desire the Court Will Appoint a Day for the hearing and determining hereof And make Such Order in the premisses as to your Worship's Shall Seem Meet

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

C: Robinson Attorney for the Appellants.
