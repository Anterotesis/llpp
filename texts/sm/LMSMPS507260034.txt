To the Worship full the Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of the Roles in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Charles Marsh and Williams Hyde Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 31st day of May 1780 Martha Greensmith was Removed and Conveyed by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the said County of the Middlesex from and ret of their said [...] of saint George then [...] to the said [...] of the [...] as the Peace [...] her last [...] Settlement

Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said order on Pass Warrant humbly Appeal against the same to Poor Worships and Pray Releif in the Premises and that a day this Present Sessions may be appointed to the Hearing and Determining the said appeal

And Your Petitioner Shall ever Pray Etc
