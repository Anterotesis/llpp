To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General [...] Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Richmondin the County of Surry

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of T: Miller and Nicholas Spencer Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of november: last Elizabeth Langley Spinster aged 17 years was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Richmond as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Elizabeth Langley

Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall Pray Etc

Henry Careless Charles Webster John Barnsley John Bond Job Gardner Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor
