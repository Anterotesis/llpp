The Humble petition & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of St. Bennetts Pauls Wharfe London agt.

St Giles in the Fields

to be hrd. Monday 10

Co: on both sides

App: alld: as to Woman Order confirmed as to children 5s.

14th. Octr. 1727

Served Mr Martin and Mr Fortescue Churchwardens of the parish of St. Giles in the Field in Com Middx with a true Cop of the within petion and order.
