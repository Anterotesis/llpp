To His Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint- Bridget otherwise Saint Bride in the City of London

Sheweth That Sarah Charters[---] was by- Order or Pass Warrant dated the 10th.. day of October 1796 under the hands and Seals of R. Davies Esq and H. Reynett [---] DrD. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of middlesex removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the- said County to the said parish of Saint Bridget otherwise Bride London aforesaid as the place- of her last Settlement

Your Petitioners and Appellants- conceiving themselves aggrieved thereby humbly Appeal. to the Judgment of this Honorable Court against the same and pray that this Honorable Cot..

Will be Pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may abide the Judgment and determination thereof And that Your Petitioners may be relieved in the Premises as this Honorable Court Shall Seem meet

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
