To the Worshipll: Her Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon of William Austin of the parish of St Giles without Cripplegate in the said County of Middlesex Farrier

Humbly Sheweth That your Peticoner was rated 4s.6d. P quarter in the first rate made for the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish for this present Year, but is since rated Nine Shillings P Quarter altho Your Peticoner is in no better Circumstances now than in the beginning of the Year when the first rate was made, but has had divers Losses since, therefore Your Peticoner humbly prays your Worships to be releived against the said Rate

And your Peticoner as induty bond Shall ever pray Etc
