To the worshipful William Mainwaring Esqre. & the other his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex.

The humble Petition of Thomas Webb, Surgeon and Apothecary, Chenies Street, Bedford Square.

Induced by the very respectable support which he received upon a former occasion, your Petitioner again presumes to solicit your votes for the vacant place of Surgeon & Apothecary to the Clerkenwell Prisons . He Flatters himself that a long course of successful practice in the arts of Surgery and Pharmacy will recommend him to

Your favor; and he begs leave to assure you, that should he be deemed worthy of your choice; he will mani: fest the deep sense he has of the honor conferest upon him, by his humane & unremitting attention to the objects under his care.
