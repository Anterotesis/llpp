To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chesham in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seales of John Preistley and Thomas Jones Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County of Middlesex (where of One is of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth Day of May last James Jackson Elizabeth his wife and their three Sons James John and William was by the said Order Removed from the Hamblet of Ratcliff in the Parish of St. Dunstans Stepney otherwise Stebonheath in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Chesham in the County of Bucks as last Place of the legal Settlement of the Said James Jackson and Elizabeth his wife and the sd: James John and William their Children whereby your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged and Agreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day to hear and Determine the said Appeal and your Petitrs. may be Releived as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

And your Petitionrs Shall ever Pray Etc.
