To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the [---] the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Towcester in the County of Northampton

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order Under the hands and Seals of James Pettitt Andrews and Cranley Thomas Kerby Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Liberty (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 26th. day of December last Sarah Corey Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret within the said Liberty and County to the said Parish of Towcester in the said County of Northampton as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices and Pray that the benefit of this appeal may be saved to the Petitioners and that the hearing and determining thereof be adjourned to Next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for this County [---] to hear and determine the said Appeal According to Law

John Hill [...] John Cleaver } Church Wardens

John Pinikara William Kendall William Inns} Overseers of the Poor
