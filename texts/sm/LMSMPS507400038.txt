To the Worshipfull William Mainwaring Esqr Chairman and other his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at Hicks's Hall in the Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Cartwright Tottenham Court Road in the County of Middlesex Butcher.

Sheweth That your Petitioner on the 18th. day of May inst we, Sworn in to the Office of Constable for the Parish of St Giles in the Field in the said County of [---]

That your Petitioner's Business consists of Burging L [...] Cattle and in crossing his Attention for These days in each Week to sapport a Large Family of a Wife and Children of White there is Six in Fer [...] and has not been [...] to Three Years in He send Parish and the support of his Family inter [...] y depend on your Petitioner

That your Petitioner is Prepared with a Substitute to Hill the said Aff [...]

Your Petitioner humbly prays that your Worship be pleased to Order your [---] Petitioner to be discharged on Producing a Substitute who is a Housekeepeer in the said Parish for Farteen Years and pays all Taxes and will duly Qualify himself if the Court bell [...] Hett
