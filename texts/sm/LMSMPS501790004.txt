To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Kenshall

Sheweth That your Peticoner by Indenture bearing date the 24th: of December 1716: was bound an Apprentice to Katherine Stafford of Holborne Sempstriss the wife of John Stafford Gent to serve from the date thereof for the Terme of four yeares to learn the Art of plain work and your Peticoners friends were to find and provide for her all wearing apparell for the first three yeares and for the last years Service your Peticoner was to have fifty shillings from her said Mistress

That your Peticoner is not instructed in the said Art and has been imodorately corrected by her Recognizance taken before times for which she is bound by Recognizance taken before Sr. Edward Gould Knt. to appear in this Court this present Sessions to answer the same and since which time the said Catherine Stafford hath beaten your Peticoner without any just Cause

Your Peticoner therefore humbly prays Your Worshipps will be pleased to order the said Catherine Stafford and all other Parties concerned to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to Shew Cause why your Peticoner should not be discharged of her said Indenture of Apprenticehood

And Your Peticoner as in Duty bound shall Pray Etc
