To the Right Worshipful his- Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition of the Chuchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter at Pauls Wharf, in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex, one where of being of the Quorum, bearing Date the twenty second Day of November last Past, Elizabeth Jones, Wife of Thomas Jones, was removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of- Saint Peter at Paul's Wharf as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That Your Petitioners conceiving themselves to be aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices, humbly appeal to this Hooruable Court against the same

And Pray that this Honoruable Court will Please to appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeal, and to make such Order therein as shall be just

And your Petitioners shall over Pray Etc

Danl. Gunston Sol: for appell's
