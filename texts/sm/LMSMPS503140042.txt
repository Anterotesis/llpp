To the Worshipfull the Chairman And the rest of his Majesties Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the peace Now Holden for the County of Middx.

The humble Petition of Robert Chester

Humbly Sheweth.

That Yor. Petr, has Served the office of Constable in the parish of Kensington in the Sd, County Upwards of two years past, And that Henry Mitchell Mason, Thomas Traver Vict, and Edward Vendor Victr are proper persons and approved of by us Two of his Majties: Justices of the Peace in Kensington Division in the said County whose Names are hereunto subscribed and that either of them are fit to Serve as Constable in Yor, Petrs. Room

That yor Petr, has Expended about ye Sum of Â£4:5s:0d in passing Vagrants from the Officers of the City and Liberty of Westmr: through the sd. County as will appear by Certificates of Allowance Signed by two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County, which Expences Still remain unpaid to Yor, Petr.

That it yor, Petr: is Continued any longer in that Office it will be every great Detriment to him & his Family

Yor. Petr therefore humbly prays Yor. Worps: will be pleased to order That the Treasurer of the Sd, County may Pay Yor, Petrs, his Expenses, And that yor. Petrs, May be Discharged from his Said Office, And that One of the Above hamed persons approved of may be Sevorne into the same in Yor Petrs, Room.

And Yor. Petr, Shall ever pray Etc

Robt: Chester

January 16th: 1735 Approved of by us.

Henry Vincent Alwed Sopple

Robt: Chester
