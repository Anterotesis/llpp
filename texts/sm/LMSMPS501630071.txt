To the Rt. Worspll His Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St. Oswold in ye: City of Chester

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seals of Robert Dennett & Joseph Hayne Esqrs: two of His Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quorn unus) bearing Date the fifth Day of June last past Rachael Vaughan widow of Edward Vaughan Deced & her three Children Vizt: Samuel aged about ten years Edwardaged about Six years & William aged about two years were removed from the Parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St. Oswold as the place of the last legall Settlemt: of the said Rachael Vaughan & her said there Children

P James Tyton Yor. Petr: think themselves aggrceved by the Judgmt: of the said two Justices Appeal to this Court & humbly pray that ye. Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Sd Parish of St. Botolph Wth: out Aldgate & all other Parties concern'd may be Ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear & a tide ye Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor: Petr: shall pray Etc
