To the Rt: Worph. his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Genall Quarter Sessions of the Peace now holden At Hicks shall in and for the said County

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Deptford in the County of Kent

Sheweth That by Warrt: or Order under the hands & Seales of Bostwick Johnson and Robert Dennell Esqr two of his Majties Justices of the Peace for the ad County (Quor Unus) bearing da [...] the 2d of Novembr: last Mary Lewis Wife of George Lewis was Removed from the Hamblett of Wappin Stepny in the County of Middx aforesaid to the said Parish of Deptford as the Place of her last Legall Settlemt: Whereby your Peticonerrs handly Conceive they are Wronged and Aggreived

Your Peticoners therefore humbly Pray ye Worpps would Appoint a day for the hearing & determining the said appeal this present Sessions & that yor Peticoners may be Releived us unto your Worpps Shall seem Meet

Middx ss Ad General Quarterial Sess: pcis Dui Reg tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd per Adjorn die Mercur Scilt Decimo tertio [...] die January Ano [...] Georgy nunc Regs Magnor Britan Etc Undecimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the pa [...] of the hamblett of Wappin Stepney within [---] this County have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Pers one Concerned do Attend this Court on Thursday next being the fourteenth day of this Instant January by tenn of the Clock in the Forenoon to hear and A [...] de the Judgment and determination of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal

P Cur. Walter
