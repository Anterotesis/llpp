To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Hampstead in the sd. County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seals of Boulton Mainwaring & John Lawton Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 6th. day of January Instant Grace Forster Widow of Thos. Forster Deced and her son Anthony aged two years were removed & Conveyed from the parish of St. George Middx in the said County to the said parish of Hampstead as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor Petrs. think themselves Aggrieved by the Order of the said two Justices of the peace Appeale to this Court agt. the same & humbly pray that your Worships will be pleas'd to appointer day for the hearing & determining of the same

And yr. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Dan Bolton Attorney for the Appellts.
