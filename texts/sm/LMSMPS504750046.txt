To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition of George Mc Coy.

Sheweth. That your Petitioner was Bound Apprentice to William Wilson of Saint Baul Shadwell Line maker on the 29th. day of June 1751 and regularly served his Apprenticeship for the space of seven years being the time mentioned in the said Indenture

That your Petitioner was during the said Term very Ill treated by the said William Wilson by [...] Assaulting and beating him, and has since extorted fresh Articles from your Petitioner for the further Term of 5 years

Your Petitioner therefore [...] humbly prays your Worships that [...] he may be Discharged from the Same [...] Agreement or otherwise as to you Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

The Mark of [x] Geo: Mc.Coy
