To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace Assembled in General Quarter Sessions held in and for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Ovrseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Mary Newington in ye. County of Surry.

Humbly Sheweth That Phebe Smith Widow of William Smith and her three Children Wereby an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Samuel Webb and Stephen Hall Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 3d Day of March last Removed from the Parish of St. John at Hackney in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of St: Mary Newington in the County of Surry as the Place of their Last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners Humbly Conceive they can make it Appear to this Court that the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Phebe Smith is not in the Parish of St. Mary Newington

Your Peticoners therefore Humbly pray Your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Ovrseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St. John at Hackney may Attend this Court for the Hearing the said Appeal at Such time as Your Worships Shall Appoint.

And Your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc.
