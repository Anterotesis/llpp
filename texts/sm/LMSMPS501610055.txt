To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Midlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St: Bridgett als Brides London.

Sheweth. That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Thomas West and Samuel Jones Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Midlesex (Quor unns) bearing date the tenth day of Aprill Instant James Oliviro son of Emanuell George Oliviro deced Aged about Nine yeares was past on your Petrs: from the Hamlett of Limehouse in the parish of Stepney as the place of his [...] last legall Settlement.

That Yor. Petrs: conceive themselves Aggreived by the order of the said Two Justices and appeale to your Worshipps there from

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray your Worshipps will appoint a day for the hearing and determining the said Appeale

And yor Petrs: shall pray Etc.
