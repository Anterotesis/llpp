To the Right Worpll: his Maties: Justies of the peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Sundon in the County of Bedfords

Sheweth That lately John Keys & his wife & two Children Johnathan and Elizabeth were lately passed from the parish of Southmims in the County of Middlesex to the parish of Sundon in the County of the Bedford (as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said John Keys) by warrant under the hand & Seale of Sr David Hechstetter one of his Maties. Justices of the peace for sd County of Midd

Your Peticonr: find themselves agrieved by the Said warrant of removeall do Appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Southmims & all other parties concerned may be Bedford to appear in this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determintion of this Court touching this appeale

And your peticonrs: as in July bound Shall ever pray
