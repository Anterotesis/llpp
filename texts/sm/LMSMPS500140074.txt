To the Right Worpll the Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon of James Singlehorse of the parish of St Mary White chappwell in the said County

Sheweth That your Peticoner upon the complaint of Elcana Dixon before the Worpll Justice Cane Stands bound for appearance in this Court as of this prsent Sessions As by the Recognizance may appeare And for as much as the matter in difference betweene the said Dixon and your Peticoner being aqreed

He humbly prays your Worpps to grant that he may be discharged from the sd Recognizance

And your Pr. (as in duty bound) shall ever pray Etc
