To the Rt Worpll: his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general quarter Sessions assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Mary Abchursh in he City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or pass order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx and Liberty of Westmr: bearing date the Third day of this instant April John Cleeveley was removed from the parish of St Margarett Westminster in the said County and Liberty unto the said parish of St Mary Abchurch London alleadging the said parish to be place of his last legal Settlement That your petrs doubt not but to prove that the said John Cleeveley never had any legal Settlement in the said parish of St Mary Abchurch whereby the conceive themselves aggrieved by the said pass Order and do humbly Appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Your Petrs therefore most humbly prays your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmissed And That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Margarett Westmr: may appear before you and hear and abide the determinate of your Worpps therein

And they shall ever pray Etc
