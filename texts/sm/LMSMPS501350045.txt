To the Honble: the Justices as Hicks Hall

The humble Request & Petn. of Majr. Hughes

Sheweth That the Petr. has used all Endeavours to comply with your Worships Order of paying Mr. Owen the Summ of £20 in 48. hours. And has found to his Sorrow he is incapable of revising even Half of it;

That as the Petr. hopes and will find Sureties To pay Mr. Owen £10. in a reasonable time to

The Petr. humbly prays your Worships to Mitigate the Imposition and to order Mr. Owen to accept of his utmost Power and condition to perform (without perishing.)

The humble Petn [...] of Major Hughes
