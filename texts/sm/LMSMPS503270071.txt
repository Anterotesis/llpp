To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middsex in their Generall [---] Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Pancras in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of John Mercer and John Milner Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Fifth day of October last past Ann Dent Widow was Removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Pancras Alledging the said Parish of St. Pancras to be the place of her last legal Settlement.

Hely Agent for the Appellants That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said Justices Do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and Humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and Order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middx may Appear before you at a certain day to be Appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of your Worships touching this Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc
