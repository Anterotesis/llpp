To the Rt Worshppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of David Harris of Hoxton in the Parish of St Leonard Shoreditch in the said County:

Sheweth

That at Christmas last your Petitioner was chosen one of the Surveyours of the High ways of the said Parish for the Year Ensuing

That your Petitr is Aged Seventy Years and much Afflicted with the Gout and by reason of a Fall he sometime agoe received from a horse Is rendred soe very Infirme that he is not able to walke nor is he capable of Serving the said Office

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays he may be discharged from serving the said Office and another fitt person chose in his room and stead

And yor: petr Shall Ever pray Etc

David Harris
