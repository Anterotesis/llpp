To the Right Worll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex assembles in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church warden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Hammersmith in the Said County

Sheweth That by warrant under the hands & Seals of Richard Woodward & Moses Goodyear Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorunns) bearing date the Seven & twenteeth day of August last one Martha Crosby wth her three Children Martha Elizabeth & Susan were passed from the parish of Fulham [...] in the Said County to the Said Hamlet of HammerSmith as the place of their last legall Settlement

Your peticonrs thinks themselves aggrieved wth the Judgment of the said two Justices appeale to this Court & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Fulham & all other parties concerned herein may be Ordered to attend this Court Some day the next Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court upon & touching this Appeale

And yor Peticonrs: as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc
