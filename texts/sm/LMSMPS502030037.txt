To the Rt Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their general Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churhwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate in the City of London

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of [---] George Walter and Jacob Delamot Blagney Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middlx (Quorn Unus) bearing date the three and twentieth day of April last past Ann Consens an infant aged about two Years daughter of John Blakesly Consens was removed from the Parish of St Ann in the Liberty of Westmr: in the said County unto the said parish of St Botolph without Bishopsgate London as the place of her last legal Settlement And Your petrs concerning themselves aggrived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do humbly appeal to your Worpps therefrom

Therefore your Petrs: humbly pray That your Worpps will be pleased to hear them in the prmisses And order That the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St Ann Westmr may appear may appear before you at a certain day to hear and abide the Judgment & determination of your Worpps touching this Appeal

And your Petr Shall ever pray Etc
