To the Right Worspll. their Maties Justices of Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of John Dunsteere of the Hamlet ye Ratcliffe in ye said County

Sheweth That upon Peticoners former Peticon this Honble Court, was pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and, Overseers of the poore of the said Hamlet should pay ye peticoner Eighteene pence P week for his releife

That your Peticoner and his wife are very aged and marcy in body soe that they are libety to perish for want, of releife yet notwyhstanding the Churchwardens and, Overseers of the poore of the said Sander will not, [...] allow ye Peticonr any. thing towards the releife of him, and his wife

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Cesee their Worpps to be pleased to Order that the Churchward and Overseers of the poore of the Said Hamlet may allow & pay yor peticoner the said weekly peticon of Eighteene [...] towards the reley [...] wife

And ye Peticor shall ever pray
