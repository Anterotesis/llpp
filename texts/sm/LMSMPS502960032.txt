To the Worshipfull the Chairman and the rest of his Matyes. Commissioners of Oyer and Terminer assembled att this present Sessions of Oyer and Terminer holden att Hicks's Hall in St. John Street in and for [...] County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Elizabeth Dawson widow the Presecutrix of this Indictmt.

Sheweth That your Petr. in September Sessions last past found a Bill in this Honoble Court agt. the said Deft. Mathew West for theating and defrauding her of the Sume of eleven pounds and eight Shillings by false Specious and deceit full Pretences.

That the said Defends. then and still being a Prisoner in the Fleet Prison London for Debt your Petr. could not (at the the used her utmost Endeavours for that purpose) procure the Said Defent.to give Baile for his Appearance att this present Sessions of Oyer and Terminer to answer unto this Said Indictmt. till very lately the Condicon of which Said Recognizance the said Defends. hath not hither to in any wife performed he haveing as yett not appeared in this Honoble Court to answer to the said Indictment pursuant to the ten our thereof to the very great delay of Justice and hinderance of your Petr. lawfull Presecution of this her Said Indictmt.

Your Petr. therefore humbly prayes your Honours that the Defendants said Recognizance may be Estreated into his Matyes Court of Exchequer and that your Petr. may have Such further and other reasonable Releif in the Prmisses as to your Honours in your great Wisdom shall seem meet

And your Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray

Eliz Dawson
