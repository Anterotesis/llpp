To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Sessions of the Peace for the said County assembled

the humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overssers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Crooked Lane in the City of London on Behalf of the said Parish

Sheweth That liy an Order under the Hands and Hals of G Mercer & I Merce, Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in & for the County of Middlesex made the 15th. Day of April; last for and Concerning the settlement of Ann Cilavan the Wife of michael Cilavan (which gone from her) and her Child namely Michael aged three weeks upon the complaint of the Church Wandens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County of Middlesex it was adjudged that the Parish of Saint Michael Crooked Lane was the place where the said Ann cilavan and her said Child were last legally settled And it was therefore ordered that they should he removed from the said Parish of Saint Mary le bone to the said parish of Saint Michael Crooked Lane and delivered to the Church Wardens and Overssers of the poor there who were than by required to remain and provide for them weading [---] Law

That in pro [...] of the Said Order in the said 15th. Day of April, the Said Ann Cilavan and been said Children were Removed and delivered to the Chelrk [...] and Overseers of the from of the said Parish of saint Michael Crooked Lane who have ever since provided for them

That your petitioners appehending the said parish of Saint Michael Crookedd Lane to be aggrieved by the said Order-Therefore humbly appeal to your Worships against the same And pray Relief in the promisses-And that your Worships will be pleaced whear and determine this your petitioners appeal at this present General Sessions of the peace

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

John David Town Sollr. for the Appellants}
