[...] To the Rt Woppl: his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poore of the Hamblet of Spittle Feilds in the parish of St Dunstans Stepney in the Couny aforesd

Sheweth That by Warrt. under the hands & Seals of John Cleave and John Richardson Esqrs Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd county Dated the 7th Day of March last Mary Allen Singlewoman was paned from the parish of St Giles in the Feilds in the sd County to the sd Hamblet of Spittle Feilds the sd Hambt: being Adjudged the last legall Settlemt of the sd Mary Allen but your: Appellants hope to prove to the Contrary

They therefore humbly pray that all Pties concerned may Attend this Honle. Court to Abide the Determinacon of this Appeale

And Your Appellants Shall ea pray Etc.

[...] Wilks Churchwarden
