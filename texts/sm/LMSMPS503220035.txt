To the Worsp [...] his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their General Qr. Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the Said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Pass warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of John Priestley and Richard Farmer Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace of the said County (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the 15th day of April 1737 [...] Elizabeth Swan the widdow and Relict of John Swan marriner deced was remov'd from the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the Said County to the Parish of St. George abovesaid as the place of her last Legal Settlement

That your Petr think themselves aggriev'd by the Order & Judgment of the Said two Justices and humbly appeal to this Court agt. the Same

Therefore humbly pray Your Worsps will [...] pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and [...] Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Sr. [...] of Wapping may Attend this Court Some day this General Qr. Sessions to hear and abide the final determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeals

And yr Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc

S: Cowper Attorney for the Appellants
