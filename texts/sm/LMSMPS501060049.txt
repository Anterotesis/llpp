Next Door to the Ball in Clements Lane

To the Rt: Worshippfull her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St Mary Le Savoy als Strand in the Said County

Sheweth That by Warrant dated the 30th day of May last under the hands and Seales of John Coggs & John Bond Esqrs Two of her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx aforesaid (Quor Unus) That Frances Boston x"x"x was removed from the Pish of St Clement Danes in the Said county to the Pish of St Mary Le Savoy [---]in the County of Middx aforesaid alleadgeing that ye Said Pish of St Mary Le Savoy was the place of the last Legall Settlemt: of the Said Frances Boston

Tho Williams Sold That yor ptrs Doubts not but to prove the Said Frances Boston has not a Legall Settlemt: in the Said [...] Pish of St Mary Le Savoy als Strand whereby they conceive themselves Agrieved by the Judgement of the Said Two Justices and humbly Appeales to yor Worshipp therefrom

Yor petrs therefore humbly pray yor Worshps will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Said Pish of St Clement Danes may attend yor Worshps & heare & abide the Judgement of yor Worsps determinacon touching this Appeale

And as in Duly bound will pray Etc
