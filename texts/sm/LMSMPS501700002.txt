To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Frances Wayes of the parish of St Giles wthout Cripplegate in the said County.

Sheweth That your Pertr: was allowed Three Shillings P Weeke as a Pention from the said Parish But the Churchwarden now refuseth to allow your Petr: Any relief

That yor Petr: is aged above Sixty Years and past her labour and very sickly and ready to Perish for want

Yor: Petr: therefore humbly pray she may be allowed such relief from the sd Parish As to yor Worshipps shall seem Meet.

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

Fran: Wayes
