To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of & for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of ye Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Margt Westmr in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrt under the Hands and Seals of Henry Lowth and D'oyly Mitchell two of his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the Said County of MIddx bearing Date the Eleventh day of April instant Hanah Sheild Wife of James Sheild with her two Children Samuel Aged abt Twenty Months and Hannah Aged abt Fourteen Weeks were Removed from and out of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the said County to ye Said Parish of St Margt Westmr as the place of their Lawfull Settlement.

That your Petrs. thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgemt. of the said two Justices humbles Appeal to your Worships against the Same

Therefore humbly Pray your Worships will be please to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of St Giles without Cripplegate may Attend this Court Some day this Quarter Sessions to them and Abide the final Determinations of this Court touchg the Said Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc
