To the Right Worspll. His Majtys. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Woolwich in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant under the Hand and Seals of Robert Kyrby and Samuel Jones Esqrs. two of His Majtys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor. Unus) bearing date the One and thirtieth day of May last there, was passed and removed into their Said Parish from the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx Gabriel and Phillip Gillingham Children of Gabriel R [...] Gillingham and that Yor. Petitionrs. by reason there of find them Selves agrieued and Humbly pray a Day this Quarter Sessions to be heard upon the Said Appeal against the Order of the said two Justies.

And yor. Petitionrs. Shall pray Etc.
