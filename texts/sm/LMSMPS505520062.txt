To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of George Wrighte and Thomas Edwards Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty sixth day of August last William Cartwright and Mary his Wife were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices And Pray that Your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. James and all Persons concerned do Attend this Court some day in this Present Session to hear and Abide the Judgements and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Samel Smith Peter Denny Thos Thompson Jas Thomas} Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
