To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of Saint Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of H. Messiter and Edmund Pepys. Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum hearing gate the Fourth day of September Instant Penelope Lambe was removed from the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County to the said Precinct of Saint Catherine (by the description of the Hamlet of Saint Katherine by the Town in the said County) as the Place of her last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order and humbly Appeal against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day this present Session that all Parties may attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the said Appeal and your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Clare Soll for the Appellants
