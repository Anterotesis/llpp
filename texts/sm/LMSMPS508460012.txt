To his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul in the City of Canterbury

Sheweth That by Virtue of Warrant or Order under the Hand and Seals of D Wilmot and John Spiller Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Second Day of May last Rebecca of the Age of Three Years and upwards the illegitimate Child of Sarah Mackenzie now the Wife of Richard Mackenzie was removed and conveyed from and at of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Paul by the Name and Description of St Paul Longford otherwise St. Paul Longfort in the City of Canterbury as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices Therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray that a day may be appointed in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray

Henry Arnold Solr. for the Appellants
