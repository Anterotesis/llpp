To the Right Worshipful the Justices of the Peace at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Now Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London.

Sheweth

his Mistake Waived by [---] both sides That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of November last past Gilbert Thompson, Margaret his Wife and their Two Children Namely William aged near fourteen Years and Margaret aged near three years were removed from and out of the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster in the County of Middlesex unto our said Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great London (by the name title and description of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew the Great in the County of Middlesex) as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitrs. therefore humbly Conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant, and humbly Appeal agt. the Same

And Yr. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.

Ben: Parkes for the Appellants in little Bartholomew Close
