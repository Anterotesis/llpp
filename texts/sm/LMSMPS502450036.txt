To the Rt: Worpfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: in [---] Genall Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Le Bow London

Sheweth That by Ord: Dated the Fifteenth day of July 1727 Under the hands & Seales of Bast [...] : Johnson &. John Smith Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County (Quor Unus) Anne White & Elizabeth White Infants were removed & Conveyed from the Hamblet of Bethnal Green in the said County to the sd. Parish of St. Mary Le Bow

Sadler Cl to Sr Danl Dolins 29 Aug 1727 Yor. Peticonr: Humbly conceiving [...] themselves Agrreived thereby Do. Appeal from the sd. Judgmt. of the sd. Two Justices of the Peace to the Judgmt. of this Honrble. Cort &. Crave a day of hearing

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
