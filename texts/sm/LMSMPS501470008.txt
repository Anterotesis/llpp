To the Right Worpll his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of Ann Munday of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the Said County Widdow.

Sheweth That your poor petr. hath lived in the said parish about fifty pones, And in her husband fine paid all dutys but now haveing nothing where with to Subsist upon, being came on her left Side, and being about Seavety eight pones of Age, and not able to gett any think to maintaine herselfe, and- pay her Rent and if not received must undoubtedly- pensh

Therefore humbly prays your Worps to- order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish to allow her a monthly penson ad to your Worps- Shall Seem meet.

And your petr. Shall Pray Etc.
