To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the Peace for the same County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Chapel Warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of the Savoy in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass under the hands and Seals of Philip Dyot and George Errington Esqrs two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for ye sd. County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the 18th day of March last Elizabeth Dutton (therein alledged to have lately come [---] into the Parish of Saint Giles in the Feilds in the County aforesaid enddavouring to settle and likely to become chargeable there) was removed from the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Feilds to the said precinct of the Savoy as the place of her last legal Settlement [---] (in and by the said Order adjudged so to be) which Yor Petr. apprehend and Alledge it is not and therefore conceive that they are aggrieved by the said Order and do appeal against the same

Yor Petrs therefore humbly Pray Yor Worships to Appoint a time during this present Session of the Peace to hear and determine this their said Appeal and that the said Order may be discharged and vacated

And Yor Petrs shall pray Etc

R Bygrave Vestry Clerk
