To the Rt: Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petition of Anthony Fryer

Sheweth That your Petr served the Office of Constable last year in the Parish of St Giles in the fields in this County And that there is due to your petr. Six and Twenty Shillings and Six pence for conveying Vagrants As appears by four order under the hands of four of his Matys Justices of the peace

That your Petr stands in Need of the said Money

And humbly prays this Court will be pleased to order that he may be paid the same.

And yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc

An: Fryer
