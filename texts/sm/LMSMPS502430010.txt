To the Rt. Worsppfull his Majesties Justices Etc

The humble peticon of Elizabeth Have prisoner in New prison

Sheweth That your peticoner has been in prison for or about [...] Weeks last past upon a pretended Fraud-That your petr take her Tryall this present Sessions and was acquitted

That your petr during her long confinemt is redused to very low Circumstances notwithstanding her Husband & Family are supported by a Weekly Maintenance from the Parish of St Andrews Humbly pray in commeseracon to [...] Condicon & poverty may be dishcarged without Fees

St. Andrews
