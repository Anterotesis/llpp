To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Ann Aldersgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Kynaston Esquire and Sir John Fielding Knight two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth Day of June last William Smith a Male Bastard Child aged three Years and upwards (deserted by Catharine Smith Singlewoman the Mother thereof) was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Ann Aldersgate as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby Your petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of he said two Justices and the said Order And pray that Your Worships will be pleased to hear and determine the said Appeal and grant them such Relief in the Premises as to this Court shall seem Meet

And Your Petitioners shall Pray Etc

Jacob Beers} Overseer of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Ann
