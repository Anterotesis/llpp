To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of Robert Long of ye Pish of St. Giles Without Criplegate in ye County aforesaid

Shewith That your Peticon is Chousen Constable for ye Liberty of Grubb Street In ye Said Psh, Butt being above Sixty Years of Age & haveing been Very Infirme for Severall Yeers and not being Capeable so he Serve In ye Said office

Yor Peticon prays to be Excused from Serving ye Said office

And Yor Peticon Shall Ever Pray Etc
