[...] the Rt: Worspp his Matys. Justices of the Peace for ye. [...] County of Middx

The humble peticon of Anthony. Greene of the Parish of St Leonard. Shoreditch in the Said County.

Sheweth

That at Easter last your Peticoner was by the last. years Overseers Nominated to be one of the Overseers of the poor of the said Parish for this present year.

That your Peticoner is a Phisitian Surgeon Licensed by his Grace the Arch Bishopp of Canterbury As by his Deploma Appeard where fore your Peticoner humble, Conteives him Selfe aggrieved & appeald against the said Nominacon

Your Petit there fore humbly prays this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Session for the said late Overseers & all partis concerned to attend this Court to shew cause why Your Peticoner should not be Discharged from serving the said Office & some other person Chosen in his Roome.

And your. Petitr: shall ever pray Etc:

Anthony Greene
