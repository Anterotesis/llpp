To the Rt. Worspls her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Peter Barker One of the prsent, Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary Islington in the Sd. County.

That lately a Rate hath been made On the Inhabitants of the Sd. Parish of Seaven Months Rate to pay the Poor thereof for the Remaineing part of this prsent year.

That your Petr. (with others) have Inspected in into the sd [...] Rate and the Poor of the Sd. Parish are all paid and for the Remainded of the Sd. Year Three Months Rate will pay the Sd. Poor.

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes you worspps to be pleased to Grant that another Rate for Three Months may be made It your Worspps Soe thinke fitt

And yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc. Peter: Barker
