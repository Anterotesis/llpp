To his Majties Justices of the Peace row Sitting at Hicks hall in St Johns Street

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Church -wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall in the Parish of St. Dunstans Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands & Seals of John Robinson & John Preistley Esqrs Two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quar Un) And Sarah Gilbert Spinster was Removed out of the Parish of St. John in wapping in the Sd. County of Middx unto the Said Hamlett of Poplar and Blackwall as the Place of her last Legall Settlement By which Order your Petrs think themselves Agrieved and Appeal agt. the Same to this Court,

And humbly pray your Worshipps will appoint One day next Sessions Finally to hear & Determine the same

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc
