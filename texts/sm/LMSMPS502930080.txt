To the Right Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Over seers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Oliver Lambert and Miles Harper Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one where being of the Quorum bearing Date the Nineteenth Day of June Mary Thorn Daughter of Ambrose and Amy Thorn both deceased Aged about Ten Years was removed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place her last legal Settlement whereby your petitio- ners Conceive them selves to be Aggrieved

Wm. Head Sollr. for St. Martins Therefore they humbly Appeal to your Worships against the Judgement of the said Two Justices and Pray your we [...] will be plaeased to appoint Some Day [...] present Sessions to hear and Delivered the Matter Touching the Said Appeal

And Your petitioner Shall ever pray Etc.
