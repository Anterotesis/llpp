To the Right Worspll. the Justices of Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Miles Preston Carpenter

Sheweth That in the yeare 1687 your Peticoner was imployed by Mr. Hart since decd to doe severall worker in the way of his said Trade in and about Hickshall For which said worker there is due to your Peticoner the sume of Â£0.16s.0d As may appeare by the Bill here unto annexed And your Peticoner being very poore and at this time much afflicted with a broken key and in great disbress for want of necessaries in such his miserable Condicon.

Your Peticoner therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worsps to be pleased to grant that he may be paid and satisfyed the said moneys doe to him due as aforesaid.

And yor. Peticoner Shall ever Pray Etc.
