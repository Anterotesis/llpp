To the Right Worspll: His Majties. Justices of ye Peace for the County of middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chelsea in ye sd. County

Humbly Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the Hands & Seals of Richard Farwell & Henry Segar Esqrs. Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor un) Dated the 26th. day of Octor. 1722. John Caldwell was removed from the Parish of St. Margarett Westmr. in the said County to the sd. Parish of Chelsea as the last legal Settlement of the sd. John Caldwell, whereby the said Parish of Chelsea think themselves aggrived

Your Peticonr. therefore humbly Prayes this Court will appoint a Day for hearing the sd. Appeal and all Psons concerned may Attend at ye same time

And your Peticonrs shall Pray Etc

J Holmes

Clk Test.
