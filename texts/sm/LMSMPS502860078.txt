To his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall quarter Sessions

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Thomas a Beckett in the Clift in the County of Sussex

Sheweth. That by and Order under the hands and Seales of John Ellis Esqr. and Oliver Lambart Esqr. two of his Majestices Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the 16th Day of August last. Mary Swallow [...] and her 2 Children Jane and Elizabeth were Removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint James Westmr. in the said County of Middx to the said parish of Saint Thomas a Beckett in the Clift in the said County of Sussex as the last legall place of their Settlement And your peticoners were Ordered to receive and provide for the said Mary Swallow and her Said Children

That your Peticoners do apprehend themselves Aggrived by the Said Removeal and Order And do therefore appeal to this Court against the Said Order of Removeal And pray that this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day for hearing the Matter of this appeal.

Jona: Evendon Agent for the appellants.
