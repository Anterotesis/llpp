[...] he Worll: his Maties Justices of the [...] Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of John Milner & John Richardson Esqrs. Justices of the Peace of & for the said County [...] of Middx bearing Date the 25th. Day of January last past, Mary Hardy Wido. of George Hardy Dead was removed from and out of the [...] Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westminster as the place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That Your Petrs. thinking themselves Agreived [...] the [...] Judgment of the said two Justices humble Appeal to your Worships against the Same.

Therefore humbly pray yor. Worsps. would be pleased to Order that the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Feilds in this County Attend this Court Some Day this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the final Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeal

And Yor Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc April 25th. 1715. 16 eyd vewis
