To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Hanover Square in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seales of Samuel Grey and Robert Stiles Esqrs: two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the fourth day of January Instant Elizabeth Batting aged about Sixty Seven Years Widow of Richard Batting was Removed from the parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of the last legall Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly prays this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may Attend the Same time

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray

Rich Whitworth} Churchwarden
