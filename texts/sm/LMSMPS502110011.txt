To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Petition of Richard Webb.

Sheweth That your Petr by his Indenture of Apprenticehood bearing date the Nineteenth day of September Anno Decr 1719 became an Apprentice to William Wills of Dinton in the County of Bucks Jersy Weaver for the terme of Seven years.

That your Petr served the said William Wills one year and was than turned over to one John Taylor of St Mary Islington in this County Linnen weaver for the remainder of his time

That the said John Taylor for these Sixteen Weekes past or there abouts hath absconded by reason of Debt soe that yor Petr is not allowed provision or Instructed his Trade and hath been Obliedged to his friends for Support

Yor. Petr there fore humbly prays he may be Discharged from his Apprenticehood

And yor Petr Shall Ever pray Etc

Richd: Webb
