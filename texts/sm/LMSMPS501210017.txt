To the Rt Worsppll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Midds:

The humble Peticon of Anne Vernon

Sheweth That as of Last Sessions yor Petr [...] pleaded not guilty & gave bail to try [...] Traverse upon a Bill of Indictment then prefered against her In this Honble: Court by George Anno & Mary Phillips for a her passe & Misdemeand As by the Indictment [...] Appeares

That yor. Petr being A poor Woman cannot obtaine Moneys to try & the Same untill next Sessions

P Tho: Nash Yor Petr therefore humbly prays yor: Worspps will be pleased to respite the sd Traver s [...] till next Sessions [---] And yor Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

Ann Vernon herd [x] [...]
