To the Right Worspd. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the parish of St Bridgett als Bride London

Sheweth

That William Sheppey was by warrant under the hands and seales of Samuell Buck Esqr. and Theophilus Eaton Esqr. two of their Maties Justice of the peace for the County lately passed from the parish of St. Andrew Holbourne in the sd. County to the said parish of St Brides. That the sd. William sheppey never had any Legall Settlemt. in the said parish of St. Brides which your peticonere are ready to make appeare to this Honble Court

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray yor Worppd to be pleased to order that the sd. William Sheppey may be repassed to the sd. parish of St. Andrew Holbourne

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
