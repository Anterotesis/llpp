To the worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the Said County

Sheweth That by on Order under the hands & Seals of Wm. Rugge and Wm. Hyde Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the in and for the City & Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 13th. day Decr. last Lucy Mess aged seven years was removed and conveyed from the parish of Saint George Hanover Sqr. in the Said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre by the Name of the Parish of St. Sepulchre without as the place of her last legal Settlement where by your petitions conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore appeal against the said order and humble relief in the premises and that your Worships will pleased to appoint some day in this present Session for hearing & determining this their said appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray etc Wade Junr. Sollr. for Appelltn.
