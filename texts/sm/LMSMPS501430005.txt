To the Rt: Worspplls. his Matys Justices of the Peace For the County of

Middx The humble Petition of Thomas Martin Constable of South Mims in the said County

Sheweth

That there was one Quarters Sallary due to your Petit at Christmas last for Passing and Believing of Vagrants. amounting to the Sum of Tenn Pounds.

That your petit & hath been at great Charge and Expence in passing and wherein Vagrants the aforesaid Quarter & Stands in great want of the said Money

Your Petit therefore humbly pray [...] Worspp will be pleased to order. that he may be paid the said Quarters Sallary

[...] Petit [...] allever prayer. Tho: Martin
