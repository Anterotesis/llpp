To the Worshipful his. Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of John Moxsey of Mill End Road in the County of Middlesex Baker

Sheweth That your Petitioner was convicted on the twenty eighth day of July one thousand seven hundred and ninety fix by the Reverend Henry Reynett D.D. one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County on the complaint of Richard Osman for having unlawfully exposed to sale on the Twenty seventh day of July last fifty one there penny loaves of Wheaten bread deficient in wright your Petitioner was adjudged to pay and forfeit the Sum of Seventeen Pounds three shillings for the same

That your Petitioner conceives himself to be aggrieved by the said Conviction and humbly appeals against the same

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc
