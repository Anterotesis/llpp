To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble peticon & Appeal of Thomas Sharpcliffe of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your peticoner is this year rated fourteen Shilling towards the relief of the poor of the said Parish

That your peticoner is an Aged man and past his Labour and hath mett with great Losses in the world whereby he is reduced and having three Children to Mainteyne is not able to pay the said rate or any part thereof and Appeals against the same

Your Peticoner humbly prays. he may be wholly discharged from paying towards the said poors rate

And Yor: Petr shall Ever pray Etc. Tho Sharpcliffe
