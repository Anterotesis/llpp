To the Rt: Worpll his Majts: Justices of the peace for the County of Midd at their Generall Quarter Session at Hickshall

The humble Peticon of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: Martin in the Fields in the sd County

Sheweth. That by an Order dated the 22d: of Aprill Instant, under the hands & Seales of John Cooper & Samuell Penny Esqrs: two of his Majts: Justices of the peace of the said County Thomas Boulty aged about Eleaven Yeare was removed from the Parish of St: Mary Whitechaple in the sd County unto your Petrs: said parish by wch yor: Petrs: humbly concern the are agrieved

Yor: Petrs: humbly Appeal to yor: Worps agst: the sd Order And for as much as the sd Order was Sewed in them but the day before this prsent Quarter Session Yor: Petrs: humbly pray Yor: Worps to [...] to appoint a day in the next Generall Quarter Session to hear the sd Appeal
