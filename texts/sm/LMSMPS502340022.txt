To the Rt: Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx. in the Genl. Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition of John Honer and William Collins Surveyors of the highways of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That your Petr. have been at great expence and Charge in [...] Repairing a Comon bridge at Hockly hole in the said Parish and other large repaire of the highways and another [...] bridge in the said Parish being now ready to fall hath and will occasion large Sume of money to be expended And the Statute Work done and to be done by the Inhabitants of the said Parish being not Sufficient to make good the great expence as aforesaid being Computed the whole will amount to the sume of Sd and upwards

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Prays this Court will be pleased to Order a Rate to be made thro [...] said Parish of St James Chivell according to the Statute in that case made and Provided for raising and collecting a Sume Sufficient to answer the purpusses aforesaid

And your Petr. shall ever pray etc. William Collins Jno Honour
