The humble Peticon of Anthony Ball

Sheweth That yor. Pr. is Poseessed of a house in Bartholomew Square in the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate in the said County where he did formerly Inhabit but hath Since had noe residence there for above those two yeares past.

That yor. Pr. hath therein placed a poore person to take care of & to let the Said house & other house, there to adjoyning Soe that the Inhabitants of the Said Parish have rated yor.
