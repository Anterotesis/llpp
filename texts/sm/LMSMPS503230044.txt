To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Genll Sessions at Hicks Hall in & for Ye Sd. Count

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Pan [...] of St. Clemt Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order Under the hands & Seales of two his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middlesex of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th day of May Instant [---] Anne the wife of Jno & Lary was removed from the Parish of St. Mary White Chapel in the County & to the sd. Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of her last legall Settlemt whereby your Peticoners conceive themselves to be Agreived and agt. the Sd. Order Do Appeale

R: Chimmow Sollr. for the Apellts} Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that as Short day may be Appointed this Present Session to hear & Determine the matter touching the Sd. Appeale

And your Petrs. shall pray
