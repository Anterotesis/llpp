The Humble petition of Jno Tuner to your

Worship hoping that as I am Sencible of my Misbehaviour, after you wass so kind is to Replace me into that Imploy which wass not Only for my Support but my wife and Children, who are all in Great Distress at present, and must so Continue without your Worships Goodness in Replacing me who being so Sencible of my Error, and Seeing the Great Distress it is likely to bring us all into, I Humbly beg that your Worship will with the Rest of the Worship full Justices of the peace have a mind of tenderness unto a family in the Greatest Distrest, who will be in Duty bound to take the Greatest Care to Obey Every Command you shall be pleass, to Lay On me

Who are your Humble Petitioner Jno. Turner
