To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of & for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal at the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Margt. Westmt. in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of Pass Warrt. under the hands and Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County bearing date the 14th day of Septemr. last Hannah Drayden aged abt. thirty Eight Years & her three Children Vizt. Ann Aged upwards of four years Catherine Aged upwards of two Years and Mary aged a Quarter of a Year were removed from the Parish of St Martin in the Feilds in the said County to the said Parish of St. Margt. Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlemt.

That your Petrs. think themselves aggreived by [---] the sd. pass warrant, and appeal against the same to this Court

Therefore your Petrs. humbly pray that their Appeal may now beadmitted against the said Order of Removal to be heard at such time as this Worll. Court will please to Order & direct the same in the next sessions,

And your Petr Shall ever pray Etc
