To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in & for the Sd. County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Danl Gach & Jas. Fraser Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westmr. (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 1st. Day of Decr. Instant Margaret Lewis the Wido. of Mathew Lewis was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Feilds within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St. Clement Danes as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agrieved and agt. the Sd. Order do Appeal

Wm. Chimmow Agt. for the Appeldts.

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session to hear and Determine the matter touching the said Appeal

And Your Petrs. shall pray Etc.
