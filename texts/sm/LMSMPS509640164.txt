To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition of Robert Jones

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was in the Twenty third day of this instead October convicted before Henry James Pye Esquire one of the Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex for playing at a certain fraudulent Game called Hazard contrary to the form of the Statute on an Information exhibited by one Samuel Clark

That Your Petitioner hath reason to deem himself aggrieved by the said Conviction as he is advised and your Petitioner hath in consequence given Notice of his Intention to appeal to this Honorable Court

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays that the Magistrates assembled in and holding the Quarter Session for the Peace for the said County of Middlesex will allow and hear your Petitioner said Appeal

And Your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever Pray Etc.

Robt. Jones
