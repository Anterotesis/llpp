To the Rt: Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of Abraham Depomare of the Hamblett of Spittle Feilds in the sd County Boxmakr

Sheweth That Your Peticoner is lately come in to the said Hambt: notwithstanding he is rated to the Poore which Accesmt he is not able to Defray haveing A wife & 5 Children & in very meane Circumstances

He therefore humble hopes he may be Exempted from paying the sd Accesmt

And Your Peticoner as in Duty bound Shall ever pray Etc

Abraham despommare
