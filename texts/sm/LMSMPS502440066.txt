To the Rt. Worspl his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their Generall Quarter sessn: of the Peace holden for the said County at Hickshall.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and, Overseers of the Poor of the hamlett of Poplar & Blackwall in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Order or Warrt. under the Hands and seals of Sr. Charles Peers Knt, and John Smith Esq: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 23d: Day of May last Jane Knight and One Child a female aged about six Months were Removed from the Parish of St. Mary Stratford Bow in the said County to the said Hamlett of Poplar & Blackwall as the Place of their last Legall settlement, whereby your Petitionrs humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggrieved

Your Petitionrs therefore humbly Pray your Worpps would be Pleasd to Appoint a Day this present sessions for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal and that your Petrs. may be Relieved in the Premies as to your Wisdoms shall Seem Meet

And Yor Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc
