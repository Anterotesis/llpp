The Humble petition of Jane Low of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate Widdow to be Discharged from paying to the poor and Scavengers Rates.

refr to Mr Brown Mr Fuller Mr Colesworth sen Mr Pindar Mr Harvey Mr Colesworth Jun Mr Kober Mr Tothill Mr Michel

or [---] 2 to Etc & cert

all the referrees to have notice

not relive erd

Midd ss: Ad Genal Quartial Session pacis Diu Regis tent P Com Midd apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd p adjorn die Lune scilt due decimo die Octobris Anno rni Dui Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc undecimo.

It is Ordered by this Court that it be and it is hereby recomended unto Richard Brown John Fuller William Colesworth senr Thomas pindar Jacob Harvey William Colesworth junr Thomas Robe Robert Tothill and Simon Michel Esqrs Justices of the peace for this County or any two of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor and Scavengers of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in this County and to examine into the truth of the peticoners complaint and to certify their opinion touching the premises within menconed unto his Maties Justices of the peace at the next General Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for this County and that notice is to be given to all the said Referrees of the time and place of the meeting for the purpose aforesaid

P Cur Walter
