To the Right Worll: their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of ye poore of the pish of St Giles in the Feilds in the said County

Sheweth

That Sarah Muns a Young Child Was lately [...] from [...] St Ann wth. in the Liberty of Westminister in the said Com [...] [...] feiles in the Said County by warrant under the Hands & Seals of Sr Mit [...] [...] Knt & Edward Bons Esqr two of their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the [...] County alleadging that the said Sarah muns was last legally settled there [...] the place of her Brith

May it please this Honble: Bench the Parents of the Said Sarah Must never had any Legall Settlement in the Said pish [...] of st. Giles therfore your peticoner: humbly conceive the [...] said Child can have noe Legall settlement there by reafo [...] [...] Brith thereof as is alleadged

Your Peticoner. finding themselves aggrieved by Judgment of the said two Justices Doe appeal [...] this Honble: Bench & humbly pray that your be [...] pleased to gramt an order for the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Ann wth in the Liberty of Westminster to appeare here on a day certain to show cause why the said Sarah Muns should not be repassed to the said pish of st Ann wth: in the liberty of Westminster

And yor Petrs shall every pray Etc.
