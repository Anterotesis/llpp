To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church- wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples and Samuel Hawkins Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said county of Middlesex bearing date the 19th. day of November last Hannah Wardley Widow of Samuel Wardley deceased was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therfore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and parish that your Worships will he pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

Jas: Dyson Solicitor for the said Appellants
