To the Right Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of John Fletcher and Dulcibella Atherton Widow

Sheweth

That the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchers in the Said County have assess'd yor. Peticoners severally and respectively Nine shillings a Quartr. towards the releife of the poor of the said parish for the year ensuing Wch. said Assessmts. are after the rate of Eighteen pounds P Annu (at two shillings in the pound, Which said pound rate is charged upon the Inhabitants of the sd parish towards the Releife of the poor of the sd parish

That yor. Peticoners severall and respective Rents were never more than fourteen pounds p Annu So that they severally think themselves very much aggreiv'd by the said Assessments

Yor. Peticonr. therefore most humbly Pray yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that they may be severally releived in the sd. prmisses

And yor. Peticonr. shall ever Pray Etc.
