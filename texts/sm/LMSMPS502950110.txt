To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex att their Generall Quarter Sessions att Hicks's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St Hellens London

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the hands and Seales of Richard Newton[---] and Charles Moore Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the peace (Quorum unus) for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 22th: day of September 1733 Ann Price Singlewoman was by the said Order remand from the Parish of Saint Clements Danes in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Great Saint Hellens in the City of London as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the said Ann Price Whereby Your Petitioners humbly Conceive they are wronged

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray [...] Your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a day to hear and determine the said Appeale And Your Petitioners may be releived as unto Your Worshipps shall seeme meete

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Avery Hobbs Saml Longuet} Church warden

Christor Astley Wm Poole} Overseers
