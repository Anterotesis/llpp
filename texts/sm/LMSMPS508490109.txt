To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at The Session House on Clerkenwell be Green- Assembled,

The Humble Petitions and Appeal of The Churchwardem and Overseers of the Poor of The Hamlet of Mile End New Town in the Parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of William Quarrill and John Staples Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof Being of the Quorum) bearing Date the twenty sixth Day of August last Charlotte Seaman aged about Eleven years and William Seaman aged about Nine Years The Children of William Seaman deceased were removed and conveyed from the Parish of saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex to the said Hamlet of Mile End New Town as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Charlotte Seaman and William Seaman Whereby yours Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

And therefore they Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and the said Order And Pray. That Your Worships will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present session for the Hearing and Determination of the said appeal and to Order That the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor. of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel and all Persons conceined do then attend this Court

And your Petitioners shall every Pray Etc
