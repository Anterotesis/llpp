To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of W Bleamire and J Leroux Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 27th. Day of August last Ann Bardand aged about Twenty there years Singlewoman and her illegitimate Child Vizt James aged about Three Years and an half were removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be heard this present Sessions And that a Nine may be appointed for the hearing and determing thereof

And your Petitioners will ever pray
