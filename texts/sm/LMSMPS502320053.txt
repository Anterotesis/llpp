To the Right Worpfull his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the Parish of St. Anne within Aldersgate in the City of London.

Sheweth That by a Warrt. or Order dated the Seventh day of December last under the hands and Seales of John Milner Esquire & William Huck Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (quor Unus) One William Warner Elizabeth his Wife & their two Children Elizabeth Aged Nine Years and hannah abt fourteen Months were removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of St. Anne within Aldersgate al the place of their Legall Settlemt. which Order was not delivered to yor. peticoners till Saterday the Eighth day of this Instant January

That Yor. peticoners find themselves aggreived by the said Order of the sd. two Justices & humbly Appeale to this Court against the Same

Yor. Peticoners & Appelants therefore pray that this Court will be pleased in regard of the Short Notice Yor. Peticoners have had of the said Order to Appoint a day att the beginning of the next Sessions or att the Adjournmt. of this present Sessions for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poore of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields and all other persons concerned to Attend & to heare and a bide by the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court Upon and touching the Matter of the said Appeale & that Yor. Peticoners & Appellts. may be releived According to Law and Justice

And Yor. Peticoners shall pray Etc
