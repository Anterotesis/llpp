To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of John Goff

Sheweth. That your Peticoner on Wednesday night last was taken up and Comitted to New Prison for being concerned with others in making a Disturbance in the Street and that upon the Goal Delivery this Morning your Worshipps were pleased to Comitt him to the house of Correction for the Space of Fourteen daies.

That your Peticoner hath a wife who Lies in a dangerous and weak condition and Should yor Petr. be confined as aforesaid the same would very much Endomger his Wife Life whereby Your Petitr: would be utterly ruined

Yor Petr therefore humbly pray yor Worshipps will be pleased to reverse the said Order and Discharge him from the said Imprisonmt.

And yor petr Shall Ever pray Etc. John Goff.
