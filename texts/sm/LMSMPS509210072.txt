To the Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex

The Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Coleshill in the County of warwick

Sheweth That under and by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of J. P. Andrews & H. I. Eye Esqr. two of his Majesty's of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing Date the 22d. day of December 1795 Jane Anstey the Hidow of Charles Anstey deced with Jane her child by her said Husband aged about three Years & Eight Months have been removed from the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Coleshill as the Place of their lawful Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive

themselves aggrieved by such Order & Removal & they therefore Pray

That they may be allowed to lodge an Appeal at this Present Sessions against the said Order and that the Hearing of such Appeal may be respited till the next Sessions &

Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
