To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their General Sessions at Hicks's hall Assembled

The humble Petition and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the County of Middx.

Sheweth, That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Richard Manley and Robert Midford Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the Twenty fourth day of August last Arabella Abbott Singlewoman was removed from the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate without in the said County of middlesex to the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legall Settlement whereby yor. Petrs. conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Therefore they humbly appeale to your Worships against the Judgment of the said two Justices and pray yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint some day this present Sessions to hear and determince the matter touching the said Appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
