To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex aforesaid

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Besford Camper and David Wilmot Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County [...] September 1770. Frances Fores and [...] Children namely John aged about Six years and for Ja [...] Aged about four Years were removed from the said Parish of Christ Church to the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster to get [...] with the said Frances Fores as the place of her [...] legal Settlement for Martur That no Appeal wa [...] had to the said Order That Never thereto by a [...] Warrant or Orders under the hands and Seals of George Stubbs and William Kelynge Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid bearing date the 27 day of Chil [...] 1770 John and James the Bastard from the said Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster to the said Parish of Christ Church be the place of their Legal Settlement That your Petitioners think themselves Aggrieved by the last above Recited Order of Removal

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly [...] Appeal against the last reacted Order of Removal And Pray that this Court will please to appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and all persons Con [...] ned may then Attend [...] .

And Your Petitioners all ever Pray Etc

Carter & Woodgate

Allyd. for Appellts
