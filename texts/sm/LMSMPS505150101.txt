To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Alhallows London Wall in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of Benjamin Cox and Thomas Balack Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middx (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 31st. day of August 1762. Sarah the Wife of Job Stokes and Joseph their Child aged about Six Weeks were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Alhallows as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices Do humbly Appeal agt. the same and pray that the hearing and determining thereof may be Adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be held in and for the said County and that the Court will be pleased to Appoint a day in the said next Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint James and all other persons Concerned then to Attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the then Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petr. will pray Etc.

Fras. Salt Attry for appellts.
