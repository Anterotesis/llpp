To the Worshippful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of th Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate London

Sheweth Thay by virtue of a Warrant or Order of Removal unde the Hands and Seals of Edmund Pepys Esquire and Philip Dyot Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing date the Thirtieth day of September One thousand Seven hundred and Seventy nine Penelope Lambe Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Penelope Lambe

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the [...] said Warrant or Order of Removal of the said two Justices do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same praying such relief in the Premisses as to this Court shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

Jms: Fisher & Son Sollicitors for the Appellants
