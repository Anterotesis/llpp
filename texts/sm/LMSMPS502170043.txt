To the Rt Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled at their Genl [...] Sessions

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens and Oversrs: of the poor of the parish of Thame in Oxfordshire

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Daniel Dolins and Samuel Webb Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor un) bearing date the 30th day of January last Mary Brown Widow of James Brown deced and her two Children (vizt) [...] [...] Year and a halfe were Removed from the Parish of St John Hackney in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of Thame in Oxfordshire as the place of their last legall Settlemt:

Your Petrs: thinking themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said two Justices humbly Appeale therefrom And pray this Court will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Oversrs: of the Poor of the Parish of St John Hackney in the Said County of Middx and all Parties herein Concerned to Attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgmt: and Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Petrs as in Duty Bound That ever Pray Etc
