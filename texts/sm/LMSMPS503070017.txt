To the Worshipfull the Chairman and others of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Petion of Robin and Amy Howard

Sheweth That Your Petionrs were indicted in December Sessions for a pretended assault on on anne Hill in Hog law Victualler

23 Dec That Your Petionr and her Bail have attended three Sessions to her your Petionrs immense charge and loss of time and given regular Notice to ye Prosecutrix of her att [...]

That Your Petionr is very poor and unable to fee Counsell

Wherefore most humbly prays this Worshipfull Court to given her leave to withdraw her Recognisances

And as in duty bound Shall ever pray Etc

Amy Howard
