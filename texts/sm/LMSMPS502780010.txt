To the Worspll. his Matyes Justices of the Peace Assembled in Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Ovrseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Clement Danes in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of two of his Matyes Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx bearing Date the first day of October Instant [...] Jacob Mail aged abt. Nine Yeares Some of John Mail deced by same his wife was removed from the Pish of St. Gyles in the Feilds in the sd. County to the sd. Pish of St. Clement Danes as the place of the last legall Settlement of the sd. Jacob Mail

That your Petrs. think themselves [...] aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices doe Appeale therefrom to your Worshipps & humbly Praya Short day may be appointed for hearing the Same

And your Petr. shall Pray Etc
