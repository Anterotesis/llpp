To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Genl. Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of M: Blackmore and P: Rowlands Esqr Two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 26th.. Day of April last Mary Ann Gundy and her Legitimate Daughter named Harriott of the Age of Two Years and Nine Months were removed and Conveyed from and our of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Ann Gundy and her said Doughter whereby Your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said Two Justice and that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing and determing thereof be Adjourned untill the next General Quarter Session of the Peace be held for the said County

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc.
