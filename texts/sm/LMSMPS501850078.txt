To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon and appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Mary le bone in the said County

Sheweth That by a warrant under the hands & seales of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quorum unus) bearing date the eleventh day of March last Elizabeth Hicks Sp [...] was removed from the Parish of St. Gyles in the Feilds in the said County to the said Parish of St. Mary le bone as the place of the last legall settlement of the said Elizabeth Hicks

Your Peticoners find themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeale to this Court against the same and pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Gyles in the Feilds and all other Parties concerned herein may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And your Peticoners (as in duty bound) shall Pray etc
