To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of P: Rowlands and N: Blackmore Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (One Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th. day of September last Sarah Duke was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the said County as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Duke whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said Two Justices and that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and the hearing and determining thereof be Adjourned until the next General Session of the Peace to be held for the said County

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc.
