Middx To the Worshipful his Majesty Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their marker Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Petition and Appeal of In Philip Newgrave of Hanworth in the said County Baronet

Sheweth That by a date or Assessment made for the relief of the Poor of the Parish of Hanworth in the said County Intilled Hanworth Septr: 4th 1778 At A Vestry then held a rate was made for the relief of the Poor at One Shilling in the Pound full rents your Petitioner stands Charged at the sum of Six Pounds Tens Shillings which is for beyond what he ought who taged at and that many of the Parishioners of the said Parish are therein Charged and Assessed at much lower Samthan they ought to be that your Petitioner conceiveing himself Aggreived by the said rate or Assessment Doth therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and Prays that you will Please whear him in the Premisses and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of Poor of the Parish of Hamorth in the said County may Appear before your at a certain Day to be Appointed whear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worships were this Appeal and your Petitioner shall Pray Etc

[---] Petr. on 1778
