To the Rt. Worshipfull ye. Chairman & the vest of his Majaties Justices on the Bench att Hicks Hall

The Humble. Peticon of Elizabeth wife of Zacharia Adson a Naylor:

Sheweth That yor Petr Indicted one John Austin last Sessions for a most barberous & in human assault comitted by the Deft on yor Petr. And yor Petr further sheweth that the Deft gave notice in writing to come into Court on Wednesday the 26 instant to plead Guilty to be said. Indictmt. & your Petr. attended each of every day this Sessions with her Councell Attorney & Witnesses but the Dept never came into Court so to do, & now prtends to allow or Lodge his writt of Certiorarie

Wherefore. Yor Petr most humbly Prayes ye prmess Considered, that yor Petr may be pd her Cost before ye sd Dept allowes his sd writ of Certiorarie.

And your Petr. as in duty bound shall ever Pray Etc.
