To the Right Worll: their Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & appeal of the Churchwardens & overseers of the Poor of the pish of St Giles in the feilds in ye sd. County

Sheweth

That Samuel England & Sarah his wife were passed by a Warrant under the hands & Seals of Ish [...] Webber & Robert Constable Esqrs two of their Maties: Justices of the Peace of the said County from the Hamlett of Spittle Feilds in the Said County unto the said pish of St. Giles in the Feilds alleadging that the Said Samuel England was last legally settled there

May it please this Honble: Bench the said Samuel England mener had any legall settlement in the said pish of St. Giles in the Feilds as hath been alleadged

Your Petitons. therefore finding themselves aggrieved by the Judgment of the Said to Justices Doe appeal to this Honble Bench humbly pray that youh be please [...] grant an Order for the Churchwaden & Over- -Seers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Spittle feilds to appeare here on a day certain [...] Shew cause why the said Samuel England [...] his wife should not be repassed to the Said [...] of Spittlefeilds

And yor or petrs Shall on pray Etc.
