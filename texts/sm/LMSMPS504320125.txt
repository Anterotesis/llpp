To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in & for the said County assembled

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church-Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden & Ely Rents in the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the hand and Seal of Fran: Bedwell Esqr one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing DAte the 27th. day of September 1754 Thomas Hasledine was removed or conveyed from the Parish of Saint Clement Danes in the County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill in the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Hasledine whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggreived

Therefore they humbly appeal to this Honourable Court and pray that the said Court will be pleased to order That the Ch: Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the sd. Parish of Saint Clement Danes & all persons concerned do attend this Court same day in this present Sessions to hear & abide the Judgmt. & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal Etc

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
