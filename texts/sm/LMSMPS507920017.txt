To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew by the Exchange in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass Warrant bearing date the 3rd. day of December 1784 under the hands and seals of Sir Sampson Wright Knight and Wm. Kitchiner Esqr. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in & for the said County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) Solomon Leach and Ann his Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Mary le Strand in the said County to the said Parish of St. Bartholomew by the Exchange in the said City (by the description of the Parish of St. Bartholomew the little) as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the said Order or Pass Warrant of the said two Justices humbly appeal against the same

And therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary le Strand aforesaid and all persons concerned to attend this Court some day next Sessions to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the Court touching the premises And that your Petitioners may have such relief therein as to your Worships shall seem meet
