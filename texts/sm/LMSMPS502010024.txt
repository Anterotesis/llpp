To the Right Worpll. his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genll: Sessions

The humble Peticon. & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Pish of Isleworth in the Said County

Sheweth

That by Order under the hands & Seales of William Lloyd & John Crookshants Esqrs: two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the Said County (Quor unus). bearing date the Seventh day of February instant David Fitzwater & Susanah his wife were removed from the parish of Twickenham in the Said County to the Said parish of Isleworth as the place of their last legall Settlement

Yor. Petrs; thinking themselves aggrieved by the judgment of the Said two Justices humbly Appeale therefrom & Pray that this Court will please to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Said parish of Twickenham & all parties concerned to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the judgment & determinaco of this Court touching this Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. (as in Duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc
