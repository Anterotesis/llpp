To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Nathaniel Conant and Philip Neve Esquires, two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Twenty fifth day of July now last past Sarah Stewart Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary le bone in the said County unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Sarah Stewart

That in as much as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and the removal and conveyance of the said Sarah Stewart in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet
