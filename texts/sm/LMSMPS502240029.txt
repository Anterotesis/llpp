To the Rt Worshipfull his maties Justices of the peace for the County of middx in this General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeale of William Sadler of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said Comly

Sheweth That your Petitioner lives in the Said parish and Rents a Smale House in the [...] for which he is charged to the poor and Scavengers Rates therein Although it is very Known your petr is a very poor man and hath aguat charge of children which notwithstanding all his Industry he hath much trouble to bring up

Wherefore your Petr most humbly prays yor Worship's will be pleased wholley to discharge him from paying to the Said Rates

And your Petrs Shall ever pray etc Wm. Sadler
