To the Rt. Worspl. his Matis. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: at their Generall Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Hicks hall Assembled

The Humble petiton and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the pish. of Stepney in the said County

Sheweth That by Wart. or Order under the Hands and Seals for Sr. Isaac Tillard Knt: and Alexand: Pitfield Esqrs. two of his Mati. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 11th Day of November last, Anne Mercing Widdow & Relict of Jno: Mercing Deceds and Anne Elizabeth Mary and Abigall Mercing, her four Children were by the said Order Removed from the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in the said County, to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe as the place of their last legall Settlement, whereby your Petrs. Humbly Conceive they are Wronged and Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays your Worpps would be Pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determing the said Appeal this present Sessions, & that your Worpps petrs. may be relieved as unto your Worpps shall seem meet.

And Yor Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc

Middx: ss Ad General Quarterial Session pcis Dui Regis tent P Com Midx apnd Hickshall in St. John Street in com prd Die Lune Decimo Tertio Die January Ano Rni Dim mi Georgy Dei gra Magne Britan Etc Regis Decimo

It is Ordered by this Court that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Bethnall Green in this County, have Notice of this Appeal, and that they and all persons Concerned do Attend this Court, on Fryday next being the 17th Day of this Instant Month of Jany, by ten of the Clock in the Forenoon, to hear and Abide the Judg- :ment and Determination of this Court upon and touching the said Appeals

per Cur Harcourt
