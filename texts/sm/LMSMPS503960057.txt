To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the said County

Sheweth

That by an Order under the hands and Seals of John Milner and Jos. Hacks two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (one of the Quorum) bearing Date the 18th. Day of April 1749 Elizabeth Neale (therein said to be the Widow of John Neale deced) was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County to the Parish of St. Clement Danes aforesd. where by your Petr. conceive themselves to be agrieved and against the sd. Order do Appeal

Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that a Short Day may be Appointed this present Session to hear and determine the sd. Appeal

And your Petrs. shall pray Etc

Chimmow Agt for the Appealts.
