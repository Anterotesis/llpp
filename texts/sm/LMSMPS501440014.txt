To the Right Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx

The Humble Petition of Elizabeth Simonton on the behalfe of Rebecca Stafford.

Sheweth

That sometime agoe Your Petitr: said Rebecca Stafford was bound over before the Worsppll Justice Fuller to appear in this Court this present Sessions for keeping a Disorderly house

That the said Rebecca Stafford is now out of Towne on [...] extraordinary busynesse & cannot Appear this Sessions to answer the sd Complaint the truth of which your Petitr is ready to attest upon Oath

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays the Recognizance of the sd Rebecca Stafford may be respited Untill next Sessions

And your Petitr: shall ever pray Etc

Eliz. Simonton
