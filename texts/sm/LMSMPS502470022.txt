To the Rt: Worspll. his Majties. Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Thomas Martin one of the Petty Constables of ye parish of Southmims in this County

Sheweth

That at Michmas. last yeast there was due and owing to yor. petr. the Sume of Ten pounds for one qrters. Salary for his Loss of time Charges and expences in passing and conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor: Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble: Court would be pleased to Order ye paymt. of the Sd Ten pounds to yor. sd Petr.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Thos: Martin
