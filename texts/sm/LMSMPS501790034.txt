To the Worshipfull Bench of Justices of the Peace of the County of Middx now sitting at Hicks Hall

The humble Petition of Peter Bowsett of the Parish of St. Leonards Shoreditch

Sheweth That your Petitioner did Petition this honble: Bench on ye.16th. of July Anno: 1717 And again on ye. 19th. of october following in the same Your & again on ye.18th. of January Anno: 1718 / 9 uppon which the Justices made their Returns

Your Petitconr therefore humbly desires this honbl: Bench will please to grant him Coppys of ye. said Returns made to the Court: as also of every of Petrie Order made upon ye. said Returns

And Yor. Petr: (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc
