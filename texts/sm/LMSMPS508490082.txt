To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled.

The Humble petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint George in the Said County.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an order a page warrant under the hands and seals of John Spiller and William Blackmore Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the peace acting in and for the County aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty fifth day of August last Jane Oyens Widow was removed and conveyed from the Hamlet of Mile and new town in the parish of Saint Dunstan Stepney in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint George as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your petititoners Conceive themselves aggrieved and humbly Appeal therefrom

Your petitioners therfore humble pray your worship will be pleased to hear them in the premises and to order that the Churchwardens and overseers of the poor of the and Hamlet of Mile and new Town may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of you worships touching this appeal

And your petitioners shall ever pray Etc M: Wright Sollr for the Appellants
