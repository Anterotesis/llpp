To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in General Quarter Sessions at Hick's Hall in and for the sd. County.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of [...] Parish of Chelsea in the sd. County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of John Ludbey and Wm Pickering Esqrs. two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the sd. County bearing date the 16th. day of November last Clare Rankin Wife of John Rankin was removed from the parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middx to the Parish of St. Luke Chelsea in the sd. County, as the place of P last Legall Settlement of the said Clare Rankin Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Agrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Jn Holmes Agent for Chelsea Parish.} Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be [...] Sessions to hear and determine the same Appeal And that all Parties concerned may Attend.

And Your Petitioners shall ever Pray Eqr.
