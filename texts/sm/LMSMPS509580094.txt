To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Nares and Willliam [...] Forssteen Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County bearing date the Sixteenth day of February instant [...] Dorothy Parker aged Eight Years, Frances Parker aged five Years and William parker aged three Years and upwards the Children of Catherine Parker Widow who hath deserted and left them were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of Saint Luke as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Dorothy Parker Frances Parker and William Parker

That in as much as your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and the Removal and Conveyance of the said Dorothy Parker, Frances Parker and William Parker in pursuance thereof

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships to grant them such relief in the Premises as to your Worships shall seem meet

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
