To the Rt: Worseflh his Matys. Justices of Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition Jack Carter. Son of John Darter.

Sheweth That your peticoner by his Indenture of Apprenticehood. Bearing Date the Second Day of June Anno Dom 1711 became an Apprentice to Israell Stratford of the parish of Aldgate in this County Cooke for the terme of Seaven ye ares comenting From the Date of the said Indenture. Its thereby may Appears

That your Peticonrs said Master Lorth frequently beat and leryed your peticoner in a barbarons & cenell manner but especially about three Weekes agoe. that your Peticoner hath for some time past beene under the Care of a Surgeon do answer which Complaints the said Israell Stratford Stanes bound for his appearance in this Court this present Sessions before the Ever Shippfull Justice Souls [...]

Your Peticoners therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to order his said Master to attend this Court some Day [...] Sessions to shew Cause why he should us [...] e Discharged from his Indenture of Apprenticehood

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray etc Jacke Carter
