To the Rt. Worspl: his Mats: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: Sitting at Hicks hall.

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwdn: and Overseers of the Poore of the humlett - of Ratcliffe in the Parish of Stepney in the County of Middx:

Sheweth That Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of D'Oyly Michell and Samuell Perry Esqrs: two of his Mats. Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx: (Quor un) bearing Date the 21st: Day [---] of August last John Austin and Jane his Wife were Conveyed from the Precinct of St. katharine in the said County of Middx: to the said humlett of Ratcliffe as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said John Austin and Jane his Wife.

Your Petr. finding themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removall of the said Two Justices humbly Appeale against the same and Pray your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining of the said Appeale this Present Sessions and that your Petrs: may be Relieved as unto your Worshipps Shall Seem meete.

And your Petrs: Shall Ever Pray
