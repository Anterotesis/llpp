To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of Ye Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of Elizabeth Smith of the Parish of St Pancras in the sd County Widow

Sheweth That for Paymt of yor Petr rent and for her Subsistance She was Allowed from the said Parish Two Shillings P Weeke

That for Three & Twenty Weekes past Yor. Petr. hath been denyed the Payment of the said Two Shillings P Weeke By means Whereof And having no Friend To Assist her She is ready To Parish and is forced to Lye in the Streetes Being wholly destitute ofer Lodging

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to order that the Arrears of her said Pension may be paid her And that the Same Pension may be Continued to her if your Worshipps Soe thinke fitt

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Eliz: Smith her [x] mke
