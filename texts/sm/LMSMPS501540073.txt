To the Worshpfull Benche of Justices

The Humble Peticion of Mary Rose of the parish of st Giles Cripplegate

Sheweth. That your Poore Peticioner Mary Rose Widdow is verry Indigent and not able [...] pay soe much as she is Rated at and the She does maintaind. 2 Poores grand Children and keeps them from the Parihs She being Age threes [...] and Seaven She hopes your Honours will bee Soe Pleased as to Consider her Request as to Release her from Daying the same

And your Peticioner Shall for your Worshipps ever Pray
