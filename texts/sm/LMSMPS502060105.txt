To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justies of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardern: and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett- als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands & Seales of Masters & Phillip Willshire Esqrs. two of his Majties:- Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (quor Unus) bearing date the 15th day of September 1722 Josiah Snowden & Ann his wife and two Children Vizt. Edward aged abt. four years & an halfe and Elizabeth Snowden aged about two yeares were past on yor Petrs. from the Hamlett of Limehouse in he parish of Stepney as the place of their cast legall Settlement

That yor. Perrs. apprehend themselfes aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices & Appeale to Yor. Worpps therefrom

Your Petr. therefore humbly pray your Worpps to appoint a day to heare &- determine the sd. Appeale

And your. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
