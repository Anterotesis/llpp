To the Right Worpl: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their Genll. Sessions

The Humble Petition of Gideon Sanders One of the High Conbles: of the Hundred of Elthorn in The said County

Sheweth That Yor. Petr. hath Executed the said Office of High Conble within his Division of the said Hundred for Upwards of three Years last past And hath Collected Reced all the Publick moneys upon the Several Rates & Orders to him sent And is ready to Account for the same wch. the Publick Trearer For this County

That Yor. Petr. hath For Several months Past Resided out of the said Hundred of Elthorn And the Executing the said Office being therefore become very Prejudicial to him in his Own Affairs

Humbly Prays Yor. Worps will please to Discharge him from the said Office And appoint some other Person to Execute the same (A List of the Names of Persons fit & proper being hereunder written)

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc Gid: Sanders

Thomas London of New Brentford Currier William Grigson of the same Distiller appointed high Const in stead of the Petr. John Ockley of the same Cheesmonger [---]
