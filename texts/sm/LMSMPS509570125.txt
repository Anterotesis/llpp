To the Worshipful His majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James in the Liberty of the City Westmr. in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of No Conant and John Scott Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex bearing date the 22d Day of October 1798 Elizabeth [---] Harris togr. with her four Children namely Mary aged about 12 Years by her past Husband John Ferquson and George aged about 7 years Sarah aged about 6 Years and Catherine aged about 2 Years by her late Husband John Harris were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the said County to the Parish of St. James in the Liberty of the City of Westminster aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore himself Appeal to this Court against the said Order and humbly pray relief in the premises And that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day of that purpose And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
