To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Thomas Procter of the Parish of Andrews Holbourne in the sd County

Sheweth That Your Petr as formerly Allowed Twelve Pence Weeke as A Pension from the sd Parish But of late the Churchwarden and Overseer of the Poore of the sd Parish have wholly taken away the Same from yor Petr And for as much as he being very Poor and Ayed Seventy Two years And all together past his laborer must Inevitably Parish unless received in the Prmises by this Honorable Court

P Tho: Nash Yor Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be pleased to [...] order that he may be Allowed Such Person from the sd Parish As unto this Honorable Court Shall be thought most meet

And yor Petr Shall Ever Pray Etc Tho: [x] Procter his mrke
