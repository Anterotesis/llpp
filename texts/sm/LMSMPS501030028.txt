To the Right Worppll. her Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Midds in their Genall Qrter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeall of the Churchwardns. and Overseers of the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Joseph Shore and Benjamin Hilton Esqrs. two of her Majties Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Midds (Qurg) Vuns dated the 6th day of January [...] 1708. Herbert Cadd with Sarah his wife and then two Children vizt. Mary Cadd and Ann Cadd were past on yor. Petrs. as the place of the Cast legall Settlemt. of the sd. Her Cert Cad from the parish of Hornsey in the sd. County of Midds

That yor. Petr. humbly conceive themselves aggreived by the order of the sd. two Justices and Appeall to yor. worpps concerning the same

And humbly hope the same shall be reversed

And yor. Petr. shall pray Etc. on ye other side

January 1708/9
