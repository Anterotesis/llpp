To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St: Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hand and Seal of Benjamin Cox Esqr: one of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster bearing Date the twenty fourth day of June last Sarah Powell was removed as a Rogue and Vagabond from the Parish of St: James within the Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of St: Anne as her last Legal place of Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to Your Worships against the said Pass Warrant and Pray Your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St: James and all persons concerned do Attend this Court some day in this present Session to hear and Abide the Judgement and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc:

Will Wright Jno Hind} Churchwardens

Rey. Grignton Jno Gerard Wm Malpas Wm. Stedman} Overseers of the poor
