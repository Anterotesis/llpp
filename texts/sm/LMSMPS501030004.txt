To ye. Rt: Worspll. her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Richard Sanderson of the parish of St Leonard Shorditch in the sd County

That yor. Petr. Stand Indicted in this honble: Court for Keeping A Game house

That your Petr. a very Sorry For the St. Fact And Resolves never to Offend againe in the Like Nature And For as. much as be being A very Poore Man And Aged Sixty Yeares and Upwards And having A wife of that Age and A Large Family to N for inteyne And Humbly Submitting himselfe to your Worshipps Great Goodness and Mercy

P Tho Nash Robert hemin Churchwarden Edmund Smith Jos: Harewell James Nash Robert Wise William Cooper Edward Allington

Your Petr. therefore most Humbly prays your Worshipps to be pleased to Grant that he may be discharged from the sd. Indictment

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever prayer Etc Richard Sanderson
