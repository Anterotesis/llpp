To The Honble Bench of Justices sitting at Hicks Hall

The Humble Petition of Jane Cook of that parish of St. Margarett Westmr.

Sheweth

That your Humble Petitioner having Issued out a Warrant against Thomas Watkins and Edward Underwood of the parish of St. Giles's to the Feilds for assaulting & Violently Beating & abuseing the said Jane Cook in such manner that She Despaired of Loe Sing one of her (yes) and her Husband being at the same Time abused in an In human manner Comeing to her assistance and now Lyes danger ously Ill so that your humble petitioner by reason of her poor and mean Circumstances Cannot prefer a Bill

Your Humble petitioner Implores the honble Bench to Continue the Said Thos: Watkins and Edward Underwood on their Recognizance till She is able to proceed against them and as in Duty Bound will Ever pray Etc

Jane Cook
