To the Worsps. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

To Humble Peticonr of Jane Smith Widow

Sheweth That your Petr. now mainteyne and keepes Two Poore Orphans belonging to the Hamlett of Ratcliffes in the Parish of Stepney in this County and there is now due to your Pet. for keeping the sd Two Poore Orphans att the Weekly case of 3s. 4d. the fund of Five Pounds.

That Yor. Petr. hash ofter demanded the 8d. Moneys of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poore of thosd. Parish but the cannot receive the Same they alledging that the Names of the 8d. two Orphans are crossed out their Poore Booke & that your Petr. receives mainteynance for the sd. Two Poore Orphans from Some other Psons.

Yor Petr. therefore most Humbly Prayer yor [...] to be pleased to Order Hill she may be pad & Sattisfyed the the sd. Five Pounds, soe due [...] aforesd. & yor Petr will then Mainteyne the 8d. Two Orphans att con Over proper [...] Charged & [...] discharged the sd. Parish from [...] charge [...]
