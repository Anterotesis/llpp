To the Right Honourable Sir Samuel Fludger Baronet Lord Mayor of the City of London and To the Rest of the Commissioners of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery of Newgate holden for the said City of London and for the County of Middlesex in Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition of Thomas Flinn now a Prisoner in Newgate.

Most Humbly Sheweth

That Your Petitioner was Yesterday Committed to the said Gaol of Newgate by Sir Thomas Chitty Knt. one of the Aldermen of the said City of London Charged upon the Oath of Abraham Abrahams upon Suspicion, only, of Stealing a Gold repeating Watch said to be the Property of one Belpre, of which fact Your petitioner is intirely innocent.

And Therefore Most Humbly Prays this Honourable Court that He may at this Present Sessions be Tried Bailed or Discharged of the Supposed Offence above mentioned according to the form of the Statute in the Case made and Provided.

And Yor. Petitioner shall ever Pray Etc Marke [x] of Thomas Flinn
