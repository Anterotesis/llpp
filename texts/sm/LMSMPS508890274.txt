The Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace king in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled

The humble petitions and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Mary at Newington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth Thatby Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of the revd. H Reynett and Rupt. Clarke Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex one or hereof being of the quorum bearing date the 28th day of August last Anne Richards, Mary Richards and William Richards her Children were removed from the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Mary at Newington in the said County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of St. Mary at Newington to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners receive themselves to be aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, do therefore Appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them on the Premises and Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. James at Clerkenwell may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

J S Pearson

And your Petitioner shall will Ever Pray Solicitor for the Appellants
