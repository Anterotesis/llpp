To the Rt Worppfull their Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble petion of James Stacy

Sheweth That yor petionr is and was upon the 28th day of November 1690 a prisonr in Finsbury Geale in the custody of Symon pen Keeper of the same for debt and being in noe wise able to pay the same humbly desired the benifit of two Acts of parliamt made for the whifed and discharge of poore prisons. for debt Etc Ann went before a Justice of the peace to take the oathes appointed by the sd Acts in Order to be discharged but she sd Keeper neglected to being yor petionr to the Quartr Sesss. as the said Acts require and is since with drawn by which meanes yor petionr is still detainer

Yor petionr therefore humbly prayes to be discharged by this honoble. bench he being qualified for it

And yor petionr shall ever pray etc
