Middlesex July Session 1783} To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor St Parish of St Botolph with Aligate in the County of Middlesex.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or order under the hands & Sealed Rice Davies & Samuel Hawley Elgnires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for this County of Middlesex one being of the Quorum bearing date the fifth day of July instant Elizabeth Bennet (wife of Robert Bennett) together with her [...] Sophia aged about two years for Nurture were removal from the Parish of St John Wapping in the County of Middlesex to the San Parish of St Botolph with Aldgate as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the Said Elizabeth Bennett.

That your Petition & Conceing themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or order do humbly appeal against the Same and pray that our Worships will be pleased to appoint a day this present Session that all Parties Concerned may Attem to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touch [...] the Sd Appeal And yr Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc

Clare & Bamptick Sellr for Appellg
