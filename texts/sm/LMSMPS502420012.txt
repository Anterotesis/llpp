To the Right Worpl his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of [...] Middx at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Dionis Backchurch London.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Johnson and Edward Grose Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Date the 10th day of March last 1726 Sarah Bushby Wido of John Bushby with their two children Henry & Sarah Bushby were Sent and delivered by the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepney in the County of Middx[---] to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St Dionis Backchurch London Alledging the Said parish to be the place of their last Legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Said Warrant and Judgment of the Said two Justices and [---] do most humbly Appeal to to Your Worps. therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore [...] humbly Pray your Worps will be pleased to hear them in the premisses and appoint a day in this present Sessions for hearing the Said Appeal And Order that the Church Warden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamlet of [...] Wapping Stepney & all parties concerned then Appear & hear & abide the Determinacon of this Court touching the Said Appeal.

And they shall every pray Etc
