To the Rt. Worsppll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Alice Warnes

Sheweth That for the Fact where of your petitr stood Indicted in this Court this present Sessions your Worshipp were pleased to Fine her Tenn Pounds And. for Now payment there of to Comitt her to New prison in this County Where She now Remaines.

That your petitioner is a very poor Woman & hath Four Children to mainteyne and now big with Child and her Husband a poor Grave Digger who by reason of his Sircumstances Is at present Obliged to Abscond. And your petitr. hath divers times made application to the Prosecutor in order to agree But she Insists upon such Unreasonable Demands. that your Petitr can noe waines Comply with And for as much as your Petr. being very sorry for the said Offence and not Capable of paying the said Fine

Your Petitr: therefore humbly. prays your Worspps will be pleased. to Comiserate her Deplorable. Condition and to Mittigate the. said Fine As on to yor Worspps shall seeme Meete

And yor: Petr: Shall ever pray Etc Alice: Warner,
