To The Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Peticon of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Low Layton in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands & Seals of two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing date the 27th. day of August last Past One Richard Denton an Aged Poor man was Passed from the [...] Liberty of Norton- folgate in the County of Middx to the Parish of Low Layton in the County of Essex as the Place of his last legal Settlement.

Your Peticoners humbly conceiving themselves agreived by the Warrant & Judgmt. of the said two Justices humbly Appeal to this Court for relief in the Prmisses

And Your Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc
