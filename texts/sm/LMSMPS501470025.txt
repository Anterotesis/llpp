To the Rt. Worpfull his Majts. Justices of the Peace for [...] the County of Middlesex at thir generall [---] Sessions at Hickshall

The humble petition and Appeale ofthe Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields in the said County of middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of Phillip Howard and John Ellis Esqr. Two of his Majtys. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (quor unus) bearing date the Five and Twentieth day of July Last Elizabeth Colton Widow of Orbert Colton and their Three Children (Vizt.) Mary aged about Eight years Elizabeth aged about Five years and Ann aged about Two years were removed from the Parish of St James in the Liberty of Westminister in the said County of Middx to your Petitioner. said parish of St. Martin in the Fields whereby your Petrs. humbly conceive they the wronged and aggrieved.

Your Petr. therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day to hear and determine the same.

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
