To the Right Worshipfull Her Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon And Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St: George Southwark in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That by Warrant under the Hands and Seales of two of her Majties: Justices of the peace of this County One Sarah Walmsley was passed from the parish of St: Andrew Holborne in the County of Middlesex to the parish of St: George Southwark as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your peticonrs: can made it Appear to this Honble: Court that the said Sarah Walmsley was never legally setted in the said parish of St: George Sourthwark and finding themselves Agreived by the said Order of removeall

Yor. Peticonrs: therefore most humbly Appeal against the said Order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of St: Andrew Holborne may appear in this Honble: Court this Prsent Sessions to shew cause why the said Sarah Walmsley should not be repassed to the said parish of St: Andrew Holborne

And your Peticoners shall ever pray Etc
