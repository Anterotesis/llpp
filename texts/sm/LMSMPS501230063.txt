To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Giles in the Feilds in the Said County

Sheweth That Dorothy Thompson widow with Her four Young children (vizt [...] Thomas william & Elizabeth Thompson were lately passed from [...] parish of St James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex by Warrant under the hands & Seals of Philip Howard & John Tully Esqrs two of Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Quorr unus to the Said parish of St Giles in the Feilds as the last place of the legall Settlement of the Saw Dorothy Thompson & her children

Your peticonrs: think themselves aggrieved wth: the Judgment of the Said two Justices do appeale to this Court and humbly pray that the Church wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St James within the Liberty of Westminster & all other parties concerned herein [...] be Ordered to attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & final determination of this Court touching this Appeal

p J Tyson

And yor: Peticonrs: Shall ever pray Etc
