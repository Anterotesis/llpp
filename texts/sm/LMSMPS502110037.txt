To the Rt. Worspll: his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx at their Generall Qr. Sessions of the Peace at Hicks hall Assembled.

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church warden and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the Pish of Stepney in the County of Middx

Sheweth That By order or Warrt. under the Hands and Seals of Samll. Perry and Stephen Hall, Esqrs. Two of his Mats. Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor [...] ) bearing Date the 27th. Day of this Instant Month of May Williams Thompson and Esther his Wife and their two Children Vizt. William aged about Two years and Paul aged abot. Six Months were by the sd. Order Removed from the Pish of St. Mary Matfellon als White chappell in the said County to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe as the Place of their last Legall Settlemt. where by your Petitioners Humbly Conceived they all wronged and aggrieved

Yor. Petrs. there fore Humbly Prays Your Worshipps to Appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal this Present Sessions and that your Petitionrs. may be Relieved as unto your Worshipps shall Seem Meet.

And your Petrs. shall Ever Pray Etc
