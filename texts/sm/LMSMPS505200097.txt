To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereby being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Second day of January in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven Hundred and Sixty three Sarah Garland Widow was Removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping Adjudging the last legal Place of Settlement of the said Sarah Garland to be there And Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said Justices do humbly Appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray that your Worships would be Pleased to Appoint some day in this Present Sessions And that your Worships will make an Order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch and all Parties Concerned in and About the Premisses may then and there hear and Abide the final Determination of your Worships therein

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc,

Thomas Old John Long Land} Churchwardens

Wm. Killingworth Willm Lacon} Overseers of the Poor,
