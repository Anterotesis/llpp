To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Hester Butterworth wife of John Butterworth

Sheweth

That for the Fact whereof your Petr. Stood Indicted in this Honble Court this prsent Sessions your Worspps were pleased to Fine her Five pounds

That your Petr. is reduced to extreame poverty and having nothing to relive herselfe but the hard labour of her said husband And She being now big with Child is willing to Submitt herselfe to the Clementy of this Honble Court And your Petr. haveing made Agreemt. with her Adversary pursuant to your Worspps diretcons

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays your worspps (in regard of her poore and miserable condicon) to be pleased to Chilligate the sd Fine unto Such degree as unto your Worspps shall Seem meat

And yor. Pr. Shall ever pray Etc

Hester Butterworth her [x] marke
