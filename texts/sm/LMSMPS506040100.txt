To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in there General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Giles in the fields in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of George Wrighte and James Fielding Esquires two of His Majestys Justi= =ces of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of October last Elizabeth Harrison Spinster was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields as the place of the last legal Settle= =ment of the Said Elizabeth Harrison

Your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same And pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for hearing and determining the Said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc.

Chas. Harris Wm. Leech Jas. Bull John Cranage} Church Wardens & Overseers
