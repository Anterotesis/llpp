To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session's of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Christ Church Newgate Street in the City of London

Sheweth That by virtue of an order or pass warrant under the hands and seals of John Scott, and Philip Neve, two of his Majesties Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 3d. day of July last Elizabeth Crampton was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of westminster in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Christ Church as the place of her last legal settlement whereby your Petitioner's conceive themselves aggrieved in that part of the order which adjudges the last legal settlement of the said Elizabeth Crampton to be in the said Parish of Christ Church

Your Petitioner's therefore appeal against the sd order and humbly pray relief in the Premises & that your Worship's will be pleased to appoint same day in the present Session's for the hearing and determining this their sd. appeal

And your Petitioner's will ever pray Etc.

Dacre and Hands

appellants Solicitors Warwick Square Saint Pauls & Mark Lane
