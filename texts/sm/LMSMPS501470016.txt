To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon of William Rowney.

Most humbly Sheweth That yor. petr. is ordered this day to be whipt in Bridewell by yo. Worspps for imaging a Song about the Take of Orinond

That yor. petr. is heartily sorry that he should be guilty of such a Fault and doth assine yor. Worspps that he design'd no harm thereby

yor. petr. therefore prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that he may be discharged from the said Correccon

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc
