To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Edward Wastfeld.

Sheweth That the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the County aforesaid have affest yor. Petr. Fifteen shillings a Quartr. for his dwelling house towards the releife of the Poor there for the year ensuing and ten shillings besides for psonal Estate for the said whole year. That yor. Peticoner is willing to pay the said Sum of 15s. a Quartr. charg'd as aforesd. but thinks himselfe very much aggreiv'd by the said Psonal Assesmt. he following no manner of Employmt:

P Wm. Henman. Yor. Peticonr. therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps to be pleased to order that he may be discharg'd from the said Psonal Assessmt.

And yor. petr. (as in Duty bound) shall ever pray Etc.
