To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx. in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwardens & overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of John Milner and John Mercer Esqrs. two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the sd. County (quor unus) bearing date the eleaventh day of March 1719. William Fenton Son of Thomas Fenton & Margt. his wife deced were past on yor. Petrs. from the Parish of St. Gyles in the feilds as the place of the last legall Settlement of the said Thomas Fenton.

That Yor. Petrs. apprehend themselfes aggreived by the order of the sd. two Justices and humbly appeale to yor. Worpps therefrom.

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor.Worpps to appoint a day to heare & determine the sd. Appeale

And yor Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
