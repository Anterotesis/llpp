To ye Right Worshipfull her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Robt. Gaskins

Sheweth That one Ann Harris als Harrison is bound over before ye Worshipfull Justice Cleeves for robing your Peticoner

That yor. Petrs. Wife now lyes in.

Yor: Petr. therefore humbly Prays Yor. Worspps to be Pleased to Continue ye sd Harris als Harrison Recognizance till next Sessions by reason of his wifes bring brought to bed

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
