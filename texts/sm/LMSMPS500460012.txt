To the beryht Marpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Rose Field & Eliz Hyden of the Pish of St. Botolph Aldgate in yt. sd. County

Sheweth That Yor. Petr being Judicted the last Quarter Sessions for a Riot comitted on the Bodys of Tho: Sidwood and Hannah his wife the sd. Pits. and Yor. Petrs. haveing agreed the Same.

That yor. Pets being very poore People and lately been in Prison & reduced to extreame Poverty and very Sorry for the sd. fact and altogether unceapeable of paying the Cort Fees and other charges which may arise concerning the Same.

P L Wistley

Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly prays yors. worsps to consider the sad and deplorable condicon which your Petrs. now languisheth in and be pleased to discharge the sd. Crt. Fees and the sd. Fine.

And yor Petrs. Shall end pray Etc.

Role [x] feild her mke

Elizabeth haden
