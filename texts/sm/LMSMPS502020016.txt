To the worshipell Bench of Jesties humble at Shreth that your power petitioner haveing binn disobent to my mother has acosand her to put me Severl times in prison witch has Linn a hendrand in my prisner and my husband being a hreud I have no frind in the middlx to a sist me I here confind in this prison a forenight and all most Married to deth I humbeley bag your weers haps goodnes to Chear Mee this time and of I don Aney [...] ing A Mid to my Mother ye humbley as the parding of her and Shall be [...] be bound to pray for Your honurable bench

Mary Bisett
