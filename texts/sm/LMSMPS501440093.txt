To the Rt Worsppll his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeale of other Richard Soud of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That your Petitr is rated two Shillings and Three Pence p Month towards the relief of the Poor of the said Peticoner

That your Petitr: is a poor man [---] and [...] able to pay the said rate

Therefore humbly prays such a Mittigation thereof as onto your Worspps Shall Seem Meete

And Yor: Petitr shall ever pray Etc

Alice Bond
