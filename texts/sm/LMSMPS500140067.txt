To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Richard Bryan

Sheweth That as of the last Sessions yor. Petr. and his Wife stood bound for their appearance in this Honble Court for assaulting Anne Barnett As by the sd Recognizance may appeare

That yor. Petr. business then calling of him into the County soe that he could not appeare as of the said Sessions in discharage of the sd. Recognizance

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to lay such small Charge upon yor. Petr. for his Non appearence as unto yor. Worpp shall seem meet

Ar yor Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
