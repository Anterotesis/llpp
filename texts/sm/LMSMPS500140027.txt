To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of the [---] Overseers of the Poore of the Precinct of St. Katherine near the Tower of London.

Sheweth That of late Christopher Hoare and Joseph Hoare were by Warrant under the hands and Seales of Sr. John Cutler Knight and Nehemiah Arnold Esqr two of their Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County passed from the Parish of St. Margrett Westmr to St. Katherine aforesd.

To prove the time when She left St Katherines Jos: Bloom Ann Mansell Eli: Haly That the said Christopher Hoare and Joseph Hoare Togother wth. John Hoare their Father did in or about Febry 1684 inhabitt in the sd. parish of St. Margrett Westmr In which sd. parish the sd. John Hoare with his sd. Children continued till the death of the sd. John Hoare which was about 18 Months past And from themeforth the sd. Children remained in the sd. Parish of St. Margrett Westm till about 5 weekes past Att which time their Mother left the sd. & Children to be chargeable to the sd. Parish of St. Margrett Westmr who (as ye Petr. humbly to conceive) ought to provide for them accordingly.

Parliamt begun the 19th day of May 1685

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly pray yor. Worpps to be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore of the sd Parish of St. Margrett Westmr to attend this Court to Shew cause why they should not provide for the sd two Children

And yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
