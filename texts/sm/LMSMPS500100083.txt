To the Right Worshipfull there Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex:

the humble Petition of Christopher Foster inhabitant of Highgate in ye Parish of St Pancras.

Sheweth. That wereas your Petitioner being an Infirme man. retired from the City of London to live in Highgate. The Parishiners of St Pancras hune chose him Constable for this present yeare Which office, by Reason of his indisposition he is not capable of serving. There being another Nominated wth him more fist.

humbly desires this Court that he may be discharged from becoming the office of Constable and as in duty bound that ever pray etc
