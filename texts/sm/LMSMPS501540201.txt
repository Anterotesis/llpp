To the Rt. Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Genll. Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett all Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant andr. the hands and Seales of Thomas Sewell and W. Wickham Esqrs. two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the sd. County of Middx (quor vnus) bearing date the 5th. day ye June Anno Dui 1716. Sarah Gates the wife of Richard Gates wth. three Children vizt. (Mary Gates aged about 11 Yeares, Sarah Gates aged about five yeares and Lydia Gates aged about tenn Months were, past on yor. Petr. from the parish of St. Mary White Chappell in the sd. County of Middx as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That yor. Petrs. conceive themselves aggreived by the ordr. and determinacon of the sd. two Justices and appeale there from to yor. Worpps

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly hope yor Worpps will appoint a day to heare & determine the sd Appeale

And Yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
