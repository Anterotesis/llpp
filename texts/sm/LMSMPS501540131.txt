To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Jane Woodward Widow.

Most humbly sheweth That yor. Peticoner is assess'd by the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the said County four shillings P Quartr. towards the Releife of the Poor of the said parish & the Year ensuing Wch. said Assessmt. is after the Rate of Eight pounds p Annu (at two shillings in the pound) Wch. said Assessmt. is charged upon the Inhabitants of the said parish towards the Releife aforesd.

That yor. peticoners Rent is but Seven pounds p Ann So that he thinks himselfe very much aggreiv'd by the said Rate or Assesmt. of four shillings p Quartr.

yor. petr. therefore most humbly Prays yor. Worspps that he may be releiv'd in the said prmisses

And yor. Peticoner shall ever pray Etc.
