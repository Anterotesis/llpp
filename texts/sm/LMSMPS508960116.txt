To the Worshipful His. Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session assembled.

The Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of East Barnet within the Liberty of Saint Alban in the County of Hertford.

Humbly Sheweth That Ann Lawford (Widow of William Lawford deceased) and her two Children namely John aged six Years and upwards and William aged one year and upwards were by an Order under the hands and Seals of Edmund Pepys and Samuel Foyster Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the seventeenth day of March One thousand seven hundred and ninety four Removed to the said Parish of East Barnet from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex as being the place of their last legal settlement: whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worship that the said Order of removal may be quashed and that the said Church Wardens and Overseers of the said Parish of East Barnet may be Reinbursed the Costs and expences which they have wrong fully in this case Instained

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

John Adamson} Church John Benedicte Durade Wardens Thomas Chandler} Overseers
