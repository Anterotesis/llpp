The Humble Peticon of Charles Gery

Mr. Northey for Petr Surveyor. in Cort

intr to att: Fr: 10cl

Peticon discuissed

This Deponant Charles Gery of Stoak newington Middx Maketh Oath: That he left atrne Copy of his Petition, and the Original order of this Court thereupon, with Uriah Rasbury one of the Surveyer for the Highways of the Sd. Parish (St. Mary Islington) on wednesday the 12th this instant October

Jur Cur 14 o die Octobris 1720}

Harcourt

Ch: Gery
