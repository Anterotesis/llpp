To the Rt. Worspll. His Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition & Appeale of Thomas Perkin

Sheweth That on or about the Eleventh day of June last your Petr. was Convicted before Valentine Hilder Esqr. one of his Maties Justices of the peace for the City & Liberty of Westmr in the said County upon the Oath of Hugh Davis a Master Shoemaker for Suffering himself to be retained & employed by another Person before he had finished a pair of Shoes for which he was retained I employed by the said Hugh Davis to make

That [---] by ye Adjudication of the said Justice of the Peace yor. Petr. was Adjudged to be Comitted to the house of Correction to hard Labour for the Space of Fourteen days

Yor. Petr. therefore thinking himself aggrieved by the said Conviction & Adjudication of the said Justice of the Peace Appeales to this Court against the same

And yor. Petr. Etc

Wm. Reynolds Acent for the Appelltt.
