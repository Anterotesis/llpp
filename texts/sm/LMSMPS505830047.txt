To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Hitchin in the County of Hertford

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the hands one [...] its of Robert Pell and [...] hard [...] two of his majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) [...] date the Thirteenth day of April last Elizabeth Barnes and her male Bastard Child named Daniel Bennett aged about four years and upwards were removed and coveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Hitchin in the County of Hertford as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves greatly Aggrieved by the said Order of Removal and humbly appeal against the same

Therefore your Petitioners Humbly pray that your Worships will appoint some day in this present Session to hear and determine their said appeal and

Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Will Crawley Settl. for the appellants
