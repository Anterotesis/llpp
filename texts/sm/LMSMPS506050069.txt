To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Streatham in the County of Surrey.

Sheweth That by an order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Browne and Stephen Clarke Esquires, two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex and dated the first day of November last Robert Thackerre a Cordwainer, Martha his Wife, and their five Children vizt. Robert aged eleven Years, Ann aged nine Years, James aged Six Years, John aged three Years, and Nathaniel aged two Months, were removed from the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn which his above the Barr, in the County of Middlesex, to the parish of Streatham in the County of Surrey, as the place of their last legal Settlement By which Removal your Petitioners think themselves aggreived.

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships, That they may have Liberty to Appeal to the said order of Removal And that the Court would be pleased to appoint a short day for hearing the Same.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
