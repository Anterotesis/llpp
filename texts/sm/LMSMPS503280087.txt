To the Right Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Session assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church= wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Bartholomew ye. Great London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under ye. hands and Seals of Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (whereof one is of the Quorum) bearing Date the Second Day of November last past Charles White, Mary his Wife and John aged about four years & an half their Child, were Removed from the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middx, unto the Said Parish of St. Bartholomew the Great London, alledging the sd. Parish of St. Bartholomew to be the Place of their last legall Settlement.

Ch: Baynbridge Agent for the Appellants. That your Peticoners conceiving themselves agrieved by the Sd. Warrant or Order of the Said two Justices, Do therefore Appeal to yor. Worps therefrom & humbly pray that you will be [...] hear them in the Premisses & Order yt the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middx may appear before you at a certain Day to be appointed, to hear & abide the Judgmt. and Determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeal.

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc
