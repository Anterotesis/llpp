To the Right Worshippfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the county of Middlesex in Quarter. Sessions assembled.

The humble Petition of Peter Creswell.

Humbly Sheweth That the Place of Governour of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell in this County Vacant by the death of Mr Adam Harker And in your Worshipps Nominacon and Disposal And your petitioner being well Affected to his Majestys Person and Government and ready to give sufficient Security for his faithfull discharge of the said Offices

Your Petitioner there fore humbly prays your Worshipps will be Pleaded to Elect and Choose Sunr Governor of the House of Correction in the roome and Place of the said Mr.Adam Harker.

And yor Petr shall Ever pray Etc. [...] Peter creswell
