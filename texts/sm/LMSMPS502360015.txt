To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties Justices of ye Peace for the County of Middx.

THe Humble peticon of Anne Ludford widw. relict of Nicholas Ludford deced one of the late petty Constables of Southmims in this County

Sheweth That as Lady day last past there was due and owing to yor. Petrs. late Husband the Sume of Thirty pounds for 3 qrters of a yrs Salary for his loss of time and Expences in passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County

That yor. petr. has a great Number of small Children and Sat present, nothing to Support them wth but the Dependance on this Thirty pounds as due to yor petr. sd late Husband

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to Order the paymt of the sd thirty pounds to yor sd Petr.

And yor Petr. shall ever pray Etc.
