To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

Sheweth The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Thundridge in the County of Hertford.

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Merry Tesmaker and Pierce Galliard Esqrs: two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said County bearing date the 15th. Day of May 1751 (whereof one is of the Quorum) Elizabeth the Wife of Thomas Tyth otherwise Tide Whose maiden Name was Elizabeth Clarke with her three Children Vizt. John Thomas and Micheal were removed from the parish of Endfield in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Thundridge in the County of Hertford as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves Aggreived & Appeal to this Court agt. the same

James Windus Attorney for the Appellt. Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray that this Court will please to Appoint a time for hearing & Determining the said Appeal And that all Persons concerned may then Attend

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.
