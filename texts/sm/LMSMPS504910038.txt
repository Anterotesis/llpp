To the Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace att Hicks Hall

I Richard Grunsell do Certify that about the 20th of Decemr Last did go to Mr Brassett with Joseph Churchill your Petitioner on Account of the said Master Mr Brassett Beatting your Petitioners & very Ill using of him, he the said Mastar promising that he would Never use him so again, he the said Master suffering his Lodgers & his Fellow Prentice to Use him Ill Also,

I would have waited an Your Worships but being Oblidge to go Outt of Town with Twenty Children belonging to Chists Hospital going to Hertford

Yours to Command Beadle of Chists Hospital Richd Grunsell
