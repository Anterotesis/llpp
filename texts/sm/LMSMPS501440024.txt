Midd ss: To the Right Worppfull. his Maties: Justices of ye peace for the County of Midd.

The humble Peticon of Robert Turner & Thomas Beckington petty Constables of the parish of Kensington in the County of Midd.

Sheweth.

That yor. Peticonrs: have severally served the Office of petty: Constable in the sd. parish of Kensington for the space of one year last past, & upwards & they not Knowing when there will a Court Leet there

Yor. Peticonrs. humbly pray yor Worpps [...] to Order Abram Browne & Robt. Watts both of the same parish to be sworn Constables of ye. sd pish in the roome & stead of yor: peticonrs: and to Discharge yor. Peticonrs. of & from the sd Office.

And yor: Peticonrs. shall pray Etc
