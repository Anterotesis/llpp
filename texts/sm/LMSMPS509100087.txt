To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex held by adjournment on Tuesday 19th.. May 1795

The Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Chepping Wycombe in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal dated the Eighteenth day of April 1795 under the Hands and Seals of John Hatchett and George Reid tur of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex Mary Rogers Widow of John Rogers and three of their Children Vizt. Ann, aged about Seven Years, John, aged about five years and Thomas aged fifteen Months Inhabitants of the Hamlet of Hammersmith in the Parish of Fulham in the said County of Middlesex evere removed to the said Parish of Chepping Wycombe as the place of their last legal Settlement by which Order the said Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Chepping Wycombe thinking themselves aggrieved do appeal therefrom to Your Worships at the present adjourned Sessions and pray Such relief in the premises as to your Worships Shall Seem meet

Isaac King Samuel Manning Thomas Cox
