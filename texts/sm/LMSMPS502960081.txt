The Humble Petition of Robert Chandler of Holliford in the Parish of Sunbury in the County of Middlesex

Your humble Petitioner having Serv'd Constable in Holliford afforesaid for this last year; and for want of a Court holden in the said Mannour for the Choosing in a New, and discharging me: your Petitioner humbly intreating your Worships to chuse one of the Persons here under named, to serve for the Year Following.

[---] {Robert Gibbings Will: Smith Will: Asloth Will: Langton} Tho Ward of Robt. Chandler Petitioner

The Petrs. have a great way to goe this Hight I most Humbly begg to be dispatched
