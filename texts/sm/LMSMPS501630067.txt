To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poore of the Parish of Hornesey in the said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant bearing date the Twelfth day of July Instant under the bonds and Sealee of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqr two of his Master Justices of the Peace for the said County Quorum unuc John Page and Elizabeth his Wife & their there Children (Viz) John Aged about Fower yeares Elizabeth aged about one yeare & Robert aged about one quarter of A yeare were Conveyed from the Parish of St James Clerkenwill in the said County to the Parish of Hprnesey aforesaid as the place of their last Legall Settlemt & your Peticonrs thinking themselves agrtevrs there by humbly appeare to this Court & prays a day to bee heard

[...] such other Releife in the [...] Premisses as to your Worshipps shall seeme meet

And Your Peticonrs shall ever pray Etc
