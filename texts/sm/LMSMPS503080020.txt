To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled [...] Genll. Quarter Session [---] at Hicks Hall in & for the Said County

The humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church- Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes in the sd. County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seales of two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the sd. County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the 25th. day of March last Sash Harris the Widow of Jno. Harris & Anne her Child aged abt. 9 years were removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Feilds in this County o the Parish of St. Clemt. Danes as the Place of their last legall Settlemt. whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agreived and agt. the sd .Order doe Appeale

Robt. Chimmow Sellr for the Appellts } Your Petrs. therefore humbly pray that Short day may be Appointed this present Session to hear & determine the matter touching the sd. Appeal

And your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc.
