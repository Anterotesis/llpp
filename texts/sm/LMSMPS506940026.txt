To the Pr. Worsph. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Ralph Key of the Parish of St. Sepulchres in the Said County.

The att Easter Last Your Petr. was Chosen Scavenger for the Upper Liberty of St. John Streete within the sd. Parish County for the Your Ensueing.

That Your Petr. is aged (76) Years or there abts. and is very Dim of sight and hard of Hearing and haveing not been Three Years of an Inhabitant in the Sd. Parish

Yor. Petr. there fore most humbly Prayes Yound Worspps to be Pleased to Grant that he may be Discharged from the Sd. office and Yoe. Petr Shall Ever Pray Et.

P Tho: Nash
