To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex now in Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Katherine Leach Spinster was by Order of Pass Warrant dated the Twenty seventh day of September last past under the hands and Seals of John Priestley and Henry Dodson Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint John of Wapping in the same County to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the Barrs as the Place of her last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners humbly Conceive themselves aggrieved thereby and Appeal from the Judgment of the said two Justices to the Judgement of this Honourable Court And desire a Day of Hearing may be appointed this present Sessions And that all persons Concerned may be Ordered to Attend to hear and about the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray and so forth
