To the Worshipful his Majestices Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order [...] Seals of Robert Davis and Robert Smith two of his [...] Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof even of the Quorum bearing Date the 31st. Day of [...] Elizabeth Rowland the illegitimate Daughter of Richard Rowland [...] Elizabeth Gardner Aged about twenty years was removed and Conveyed [...] and out of the Parish of saint John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex [...] said parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of the last legal [...] the said Elizabeth Rowland.

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices and therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court against the same And Pray your Worships will appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

S. Malbon Sollr.

for the said Parish of Shadwell
