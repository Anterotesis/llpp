To the Rt : Worshipfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble peticon and Appeale of William Powell of the parish of St Mary Matfellon als White Chappell in the said County [...] Corn chandler

Sheweth That by a Judgement or Order under the hands and Seales of Thomas Sewell and Samuel Perry Esqr, two of his Maties: Justices of the peace for this County (Quorums) bearing date the nineteenth day of Aprill last Your Petr: is adjudged the reputed Father of a Male Bastard Child born in the said Parish on the fifth day of December last on the body of Mary Duncomb Widow That your Petr: there by thinks himself agrieved and humbly appeals to this Honeble: Court against the same

And prays that your Worshipps would be pleased to Order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Said Parish of St: Mary Mat fellon als White Chappell in the said County and all other parties concernd to Attend this Court some day this present Sessions to hears and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the said Appeale

And your Petr: shall ever pray Etc
