To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Session of the Peace in and for the said County assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Bishop and Edward Hilliard Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifteenth Day of January last John Coxall and his wife named Hannah and their Four Children vizt Mary aged Twelve Years, Charlotte aged Six Years, John aged Two years and a Male Child near a Year old not yet christened were removed from the Town of Uxbridge in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell as the Place of his the said John Coxall's legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and against the said Order do appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that a Day may be appointed in the next General Session to hear and determine the Matter of the said Appeal.
