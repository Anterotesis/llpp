To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of An Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Sherwood Esquire and Burford Comper Esquire Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one Being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Third Day of March now last past George Cooper and his Two Children and Lawfull Issue by his late Wife Elizabeth Cooper deceased Namely Richard Cooper aged Four Years and Joseph Cooper Aged about Twelve Months were Removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish Saint George And the said Two Last mentioned Justices of the Peace in and by the said Order of Removal Adjudged the Last Legal Settlement of the said George Cooper and his said Children to be in the said Parish of Saint George

That your Petitioners are orderised and believe that the last Legal Settlement of the said George Cooper and his Said Children is not in the said Parish of Saint George as by the said Order is Supposed and therefore they Conceive themselves to be Aggreived by the said Order and Determination of the said Two Justices and Do Humbly appeal to your Worships against the same

Your Petitioners therefore as well on the Part and Behalf of themselves as of the other Inhabitants and Parishioners of the said Parish of Saint George Do Humbly pray that the said order of removal of the said George Cooper and his said Children from the said parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate to the said Parish of Saint George may be Quashed And that your Worships would Please to make such further Order therein for the Reimbursing and paying your Petitioners the Costs and Charges that they have been Putt to in Maintaining and Providing for the said George Cooper and his said Children in Obedience to the said Order as to Your Worships shall seem Meet

And your Petitioners will Ever Pray Etc.

Major Wright Sollr. for the Appelants
