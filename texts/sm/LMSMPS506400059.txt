To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in general Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden [...] and Overseers of the Poor the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County aforesaid

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of T. Treslove and John Goodchild Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the City ad Liberty of Westminster (one whereof of being of the Quorum) bearing date the third Day of May Instant Mary Shade Wife of Henry Francis Shade (gone from her) and their Child to wit William aged seven Years and upwards was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the [...] of the last legal Settlement of the said Mary Shade and Child Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that Your Worships will please to appoint some day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal and to Order That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Martin in the fields and all Persons concerned do then attend this Court

And Your Petitrs: Shall Pray Etc

Charles Storey Churchwarden

and

Geo. Chalmers Thos.. Key Robt. Desbroad Wm Hall} Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
