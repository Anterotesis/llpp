To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County [...] of Middlesex in their General Session of Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Monken Hadley in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and [...] Thomas Brooks bank and William Coleman Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty Second day of May instant. Hannah the Wife of John Cooper and her two Children namely Hannah Aged about Two Years and Elizabeth Aged about thirteen Months was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Enfield in the said County to the said Parish of Monken Hadley as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Cooper and her said two Children whereby your Petitioners are as they Conceive Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this Pressent Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal

And Your Petrs. will pray Etc

[...] Robertson [...] for the Appellants
