To the Right Worshipfull her Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Midds.

The humble peticon of the Churchwardens of the parish of St Mildred BreadStreet in the Citty of London

Humbly Sheweth

That by order dated the 29th day of June last past under the hands & Seals of St Charles Dencombe Knt Lord Mayor of the Citty of London and Sr Robert Bedingfeild Knt one of the Aldermen of the Citty of London, two of her Maties Justices of the peace for the said Citty One Sara Standish Wife of James Standish together with her three Children vizt Mary Standish Elizth Standish & James Standish were conveyed from the parish of St Mary Magdalen Old Fifthstreet London to the said parish of St Mildred Bread Street as the place of the last Legall Settlemt of the sd James Standish the Husband

That yor. petr. hope to prove to this Court that one John Standish of Hatton Gardon in the County of Midd Tayler Father of the said James Standish the Husband, and Grand Father of the said Children as of Good Estate & sufficent Ability to allow a Maintanance towards the Relife & Support of the said Sara Standish & her said Children Is Prvent their being chargeable to the sd pish of St Mildred Bread Street

Yor petr. therefore humbly pray this Court to appoint s day this prsent Sessions for the sd John Standish to attend this Court to shew cause why die should not paids for the said Sara Standish & her said Children as by Law he ought to doe

And yor. petr. shall ever pray Etc
