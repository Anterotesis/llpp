To the Rt. Worspll. his Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The Humble Peticon of Edward Bridgman one of the High Constables of Edmonton Hundrd in the sd County

Sheweth That yor. Petr. has duely and faithfully served his Majty. as one of the High Constables of the sd Hundred for the space of one whole year last past and upwards

That yor. Petr. is a Carpenter by Trade has a Multitude of Buisness on his hands; and not capable of performing the publick Duties of his Office any longer, without a very great Neglect of his own private Affaires, and to the prejudice of himselfe and family

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court to Discharge him from the sd Office, And that one of the person under named (being much better qualifyed than yor. Petr) may be appointed High Constable in his stead

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc

Thos: Howard John Rumball George Waller} of Edmonton

Edwd. Bridgmt
