To the Right Worspll: her Majties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon and Appeall of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Pish of High Barnett als. Chipping Barnett in the County of Hertford.

Sheweth That by Warrant under the Hands & Seales of two of her Majties. Justices of the peace for this County of Middx: One Sylvester Webb wth: Elizabeth his wife & two Children named Mary & Anne [being Infants under the Age of Six years were passed from ye Pish of Southmims in the sd County of [...] to the Pish of High Barnett als Chipping Barnett in the County of Hertford as the place of their last legall Settlement:

That yor: Peticoners can make itt appear to this Honble: Court that the sd Sylvester Webb and Family have no legall Settlemt: in the aforesd Pish of Barnett, as by the sd Warrt: is Suggested

Yor: Peticonrs: therefore most humbly Appeal agnst. ye Ordr: & Pray yor: Worships to be pleas'd to order that the Churchwardens and Over seers of the poor of the sd Pish of Southmims may appear in this Honble: Court this prsent Sessions to shew Cause why the sd Sylvester Webb and family Should not be repassed to the sd Pish of Southmims

And Yor. Petitionrs. Shall ever Pray Etc

Cornelius Chapman Overseer of ye poor}
