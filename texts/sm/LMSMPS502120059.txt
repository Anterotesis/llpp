To the Rt: Worsppfull his Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Midlx at their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of ye. Parish of St. Botolph wth: out Aldgate in the sd. County.

Sheweth. That by order under the Hands and Sealy of John Milner and John Ellis Esqrs: of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor. un.) bearing date the 28th. day of June last Ann Beverley widow of James Beverley deceed was removed from the Parish of St. Giles in ye. Fields in the said County to our said Parish of St: Botolph without Aldgate as to the place of her last Legall Settlemt.

That yr. Petrs: think themselves agrieved by the sd. Order and humbly appeal to this Honble Court agt. ye. same.

And Pray that yr: Worspps would be pleased to order that ye. Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd. Parish of St. Giles in ye. Fieles and all other persons concerned [...] may attend this Court some day this prest. Sessions to hear & abide ye. Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching ye. sd. appeal

And yr: Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc.
