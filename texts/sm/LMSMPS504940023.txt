To the Worshipfull Thos. Lane Esqr. and the Rest of the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in General Sessions of the peace assembled.

The Humble Petition of Wm: Jones Servant to Mr: Henry Wallbanck late Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex,

Most Humbly Sheweth. That Your Petitioner has Lived as Servant or Under Keeper to the said Mr. Wallbanck upwards of Sixteen Years and during almost Nine Years of the time has had the Chief Care of the Goal on Account of Mr. Wallbancks Indisposition.

That Your Petitioner humbly hopes that he has always behaved himself in such a manner to your Worships and the Prisoners, as to entitle him your Worships Favour.

Therefore Your Petitioner humbly hopes your Worship's will by pleased to appoint Your Petitr. Keeper of the said House of Correction who if should be so happy as to Succeed, will take Care to Discharge the Duty which your Worships shall be pleased to Entrust him with, with the Greatest Care and Diligence and fidelity

And Yours Petitioner as in Duty bound will ever Pray Etc

Wm: Jones.
