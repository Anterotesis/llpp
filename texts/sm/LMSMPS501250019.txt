To the Right Worshipfull her Majesties Justices of the peace assembled at the General Quarter Session for the County of Middx

The humble petition of Peter Bishop of Wheeler Street in the County of Middx Baker

Saith That he was informed against by one Joseph Browne an informe for not having master her name upon a loaf And a Warrant of Conviction being granted against him by the Worshipfull what There are by ye for levying of a fine of forty Shillings whereby yor petitioner hum- bly conceiving herselfe of to be aggrieved he did notice his appeale And prays the Worshipfull Court to alsoe order for hearing and determining he said appeale

Said And to [...] humble petitioner shall ever pray
