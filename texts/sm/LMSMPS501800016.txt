To the Worshipfull The Bench of Justices Sitting att Hicks Hall

The Humble Peticon of Anno Tayler

Sheweth That your Petitioner was Indicted Last Sessions For an Assault and Rescue to wth Indictment She Pleaded not Guilty and Entered into a Recoginzance to Traverse the Same this Present Sessions But by Reason of a Fitt of Sickness and not Knowing what notice to give She has not Veea able to give Regular notice or to Attend the Traverse

Yor. Petr. Therefore humbly Prays yor. Worshipps to Respite the Sd. Recognizance untill next Sessions

And Yor. Petr. Shall Ever Pray et
