To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Petition of Daniel Waite Esqr. one of the Inhabitants of the Parish of St. Sepulchre within the Hundred of Assulston in the said County

Sheweth That Robert Bent haveing lately Obtained a Judgment in his Majestys Court of Comon pleas at Westminster against the Inhabitants within the Hundred of Ossulston aforesaid for Seventy pounds damages by reason of a Robbery committed him within the said Hundred the said Sum of Seventy pounds was on the twenty Sixth of May last by Virtue of his Majestys Writt of Fieri facias issueing out of the said Court Conyed upon your Petitioner Which Sum you petitioner ought to be reimbursed by the other Inhabitants of the sd. Hundred

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays that this Court will be pleased to make an Order of recommendation to two of his Majestys Justices of the peace of this County Inhabiting within the said Hundred to make a Rate or Assessment pursuant to the Act of Parliament in such case made upon the severall Parishes Hamletts and places within the severall Divisions in the said hundred for raising of the said Sum of Seventy pounds and to Issue forth their Warrants for that purpose under their hands and Seales to the High Constable Petty Constables and Headboroughs of each Division [...] within the said Hundred to the intent that your Petitioner may be reimbursed and paid the said Sum of Seventy pounds

And your Petitioner shall pray Etc. Danl. Waite
