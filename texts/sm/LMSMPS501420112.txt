To the Right Worshipll: his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt. under the hands & Seals of the Worshipll: Samuel Benson & Nathaniel Manlove Esqrs two of his Maties. Justices of the Peace for the sd County of Middlx (Quoum unus) bearing date the 25th day of Novemr. last past their was removed from the parish of St Leonard Shoreditch into the sd Parish one Frances Hooke Spinster who is now Chargeable to the sd Parish of St Botolph wth.out Aldersgate None your Peticoners find them- -selves agreved by the Order of the sd two Justices and humbly appeal against the same and pray a Day to be heard this present Quarter Sessions & that all Persons concerned may attend your Worshipps to hear & abide the Judgment & Determination of this Court touching the sd Appeal.

And yor Peticoners shall pray Etc Robt Bly Richd Walter Churchwardens Jno Faram Overseer for the Poor
