To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Walthamstow in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of P. Colquhoun and John Spiller Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty third day of February last Lydia Hardcourt Single Woman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Walthamstow as the place of her last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners are as they Conceived aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said order and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining their said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc

J Dacre Sollr for the Appellants
