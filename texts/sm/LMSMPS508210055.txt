To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the Hands and Seals of H. R. Cowdenay and Jno. Griffiths Esquires Two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty Westminster in [...] the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Nineteenth day of February Instant Mary Gillett. was removed and Conveyed from and out of the parish of Saint George Hannover Square within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieived by the order of the said Two Justices therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the said order And pray year Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall pray Etc

Richd Christmas Theos. T. Tutt Thos. Bourdillon} Churchwardens

Geo: Leigh John Toms} Overseers
