To the Right Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Sessions, assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Saint Margaret Westminster in the same County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Richard Hassell and D Hechstelter Esquires two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County aforesaid bearing Date the Eighteenth day of February 1750. William Tarrow Elizabethhis Wife and their Two Children were removed from the parish of Little Stanmore in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of their last Legal Settlement

Whereby Your Petitioners think themselves aggreived and Appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Pray this Court will please to appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all Persons Concerned may then attend.

And Your Petitrs shall ever Pray Etc.

Crofts Attorney for St. Margts.
