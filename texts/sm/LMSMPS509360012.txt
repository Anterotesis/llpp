To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn above the bars in the said County

Sheweth

That by and order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of John Reeves and Samuel Edwards Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof is of the Quorum bearing date the ninth day of March last Judith Brady Wife of Patrick Brady and her Son Dennis were removed and conveyed from and out of the Liberty of the Rolls in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Holborn as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said two Justices appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and order that the Overseers of the Poor of the said Liberty of the Rolls may appear before you on a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of your Worships touching the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc

A Gliddon Solr for the Applts.
