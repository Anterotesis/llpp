To the Right Worpll his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon & appeale of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Richard Winnington aged about eleven yeares Sarah Winnington aged about five yeares and Margarett Winnington aged- about two yeares p a half (all the lawfull Issue of Francis Winnington Marriner & Jane his wife) by Virtue of a Warrant under the hands & seales of Nov of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlx (one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 29th. day of October last [...] were removed from the parish of Saint Paul Shadrwell in this County to the parish of Saint Luke aforesaid Alledgeing the said parish is the place of the last legall A settlemt of [...] said Richard, Sarah, & Margarett, That yoen Petrs doubt [...] to prove that the said Richard Sarah & Margacett had no legall settlement in the said parish of Saint Luke whereby- they Conceive them selves aggrived by the Judgment of the said two Justices And doe humbly appeale to your Worps there upon

Your Petrs therefore humbly pray your Worpt. will be Pleased to appoint a day to hear them in the premiser And order the Church wardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell to hear & abide the judgment & determinacon of your Worps touching this Appeale

And your Petrs shall Pray Etc.
