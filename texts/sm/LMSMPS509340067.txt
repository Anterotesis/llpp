To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Holy Rhood in the Town and County of Southampton

Sheweth, That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of the Reverend Herbert Mayo DD and William Chappeson Esquire two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the sevententh day of November One thousand seven hundred and ninety Six Sophia Frazher Wife of John Frazher now at Sea and her two Children namely John aged three Years or thereabouts and Sophia aged eight Months or thereabouts were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint George in in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Holy Rhood aforesaid as the Place of their legal Settlement, whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be reserved to them and that a day may be appointed in the next general quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George be then and there required to attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Quill, Solicitor for the Appellants
