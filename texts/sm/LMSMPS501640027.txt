To the Worspll the Chairman & rest of the of the Justices of Peace in the Honoble. Court of Hicks Hall Most humbly Sheweth

That yor Poore Petitionr having born almost Elleauen Weeks Prisson in Clerkenwell in a very poor & Destitute Condition not having had any thing but ye jayle allowance to Subsist her, And through Greve for ye Death of her Husband & Children togeather is options little better then rauing Madd and violently troubled wth Fitts

Wherefore yor. Petition most humbly Prays this Honorle. Court in Pritty to her poor Condition to Discharge her and she will ever pray for this Honbl Court as becomes

Yor. Poor Petitior Susanna Griffin
