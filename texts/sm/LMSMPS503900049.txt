To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the peace of the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the peace at Hicks Hall [...] Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Hendon in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That By Virtue or Colour of a Warrant or Order under the Stand and Seal of Henry Fielding Esquire One of His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex which Warrant or Order [...] not dated but was delivered to your Petitioners Some or One of them on or about the twenty third or Twenty fourth day of November last, Hannah the Wife of Thomas Henson was Removed and Conveyed from the parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty of Westminster aforesaid as a Vagabond [...] apprehended in the said parish of Saint Ann to the said parish of Hendon as the place of the last Legal Settlement of the said Hannah Henson

That Your Petitioners Apprehend themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of Removal

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Appeal to this Court Against the same But in Regard Your Petitioners are not at present ready to prosecute their said Appeal Your Petitioners humbly Pray that this Court will Appoint Some day in the next General Quarter Session of the peace to be holden for this County for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Saint Ann and all other persons concerned to Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching thesa [...] Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall Ever pray Etc.

Jno. Nicoll Soll for Appellants-}
