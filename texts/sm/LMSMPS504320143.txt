To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Ingram

Sheweth That your Petitioner was bound an Apprentice to Edward Day of the parish of St Luke in the County of Middx Cabinet Maker by Indentures bearing date the Thirtieth day of October in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Seven hundred and forty eight for the Term of Seven Yeares.

That your petitioners said Master frequently [---] since his said Apprenticeship absented himself for a Considerable time together from his Business and left your petitioner quite unemployed and without necessary provisions to Subsist on during hid absence so that Your petitioner was oblidged to Maintain himself at his own Expense

That your petitioners said Master is absconded from his House for fear of being Arrested and will not Instruct him in the said Business by reason whereof he is greatly afraid that he shall [---] hereafter by destitute and unskilled in the said Trade to procure himself Subsistence

your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worships to take the premisses into Consideration and to discharge your sd petitioner from his said Master service or to Great him such other release as to your worships shall seem meet

And your petitioner shall ever pray Etc

John Ingram
