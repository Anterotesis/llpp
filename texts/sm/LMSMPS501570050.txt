To the Rt: Worpll his Maties. Justices the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessns.

The Humble Peticon of Francis Howard of the parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the said County Barber

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr: hath been chosen & appointed one of the Scavengers of the parish aforesaid for this present Year

That yor. Peticonr is a very Poor man hath a wife it four small children & his trade so Small that he cannot afford to keep a Servant

Humbly Prayes the Premises considered th [...] this Court will be pleased to Discharge him [...] from the said Office

And Yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray
