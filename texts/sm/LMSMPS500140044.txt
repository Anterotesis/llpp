To the Right Worshipfull the Justices of Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of Sarah Shorte

Sheweth

That your Petr. was bound over by Mr. Justice James to appeare in this Court as of this prsent Sessions to answer to the Compll. of Elizabeth Deale as she alleadges for servts. Wages As by the Recognizance therefore taken may appeare But now Soe it is may it please Yor. Worpps the said Eliz: Deale never was a Servt. to Your Petr. And forasmuch as Yor Petr. gave the said Deale notice in writeing She would appeare this day to answer the Same & the said Deale will not appeare.

Yor. Petr. therefore humbly beseecheth this Honble- Bench (the premisses considered) to be pleased to grant that She may be discharged from the said [...] Recognizance.

And Yor Petr. (as in duty bound) Shall ever pray Etc.
