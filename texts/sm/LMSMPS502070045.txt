To the Right Worppfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Generall Sessions of the Peace

The humble Petition & Appeal of the Church: Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Westham in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Isaac Tillard and Samuel Benson Esqrs: two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the Fourteenth Day of November last Ann Grey Single Woman was removed from the Hamlet of [...] Bethnall Green in the Parish of St. Denston Stepney als Steburnheath in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Westham as the Place of her [---] last legall Settlement

Your Petitioners therefore thinking themselves aggreived by the Order of the said two Justices appeal to this Court and humbly pray That the Church: Wardn: &Overseers of the Poor of the said Hamlett of Bethnall Green and all other Parties concerned may be ordered to attend this Court some day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners shall ever [...] pray Etc.
