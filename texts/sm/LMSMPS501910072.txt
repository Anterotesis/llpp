To the Rt. Worspll. his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Sepulchres in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Order under the hands & seals of John Fuller & Matthew Hewitt Esqrs. two of his Matys. Justices of the peace for the sd County (Quorum Unus) bearing date the Twentieth day of December last Ann Bell was removed from and out of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the sd County of Middx to the sd Parish of St. Sepulchres Middx as the place of her last legall Settlement

That Yor. Peticoners think themselves aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices & hope to make it appear that the sd Ann Bell hath not any legall Settlement in the sd parish of St. Sepulchres Middx and therefore humbly appeal against the sd Order to this Honble Bench

Therefore Yor. Petrs. humbly Pray Yor. Worspps to be pleased to appoint a day for the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St James Clerkenwell to attend this Honble Bench & to stand to & abide such Order as shall be made upon hearing the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
