To the Right Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of Peace assembled

The Humble Penton and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Giles Camberwell in the County of Surrey

Sheweth

That by virtue of an Order or Pass warrant under the hands and Seals of the Barnard and R Davies Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex are whereof being of the quorum bearing date the 30th. day of December last Martha Walker wife of Matthew Walker who hath deserted and left her and their four Children Martha aged above six years Margaret aged about five years, Matthew aged about four years, and Hannah aged about ten months were removed from the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Giles Camberwell in the said County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of Saint Giles Camberwell to be the place of their last legal Settlement

That the Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices, do therefor Appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be pleased to hear them in the premises and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Whitechapel may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Cross & Green for the Appellants
