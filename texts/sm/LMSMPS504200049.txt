To the Right Honourable Sir Crist Gascoign Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London William Moreton Esqr. Recorder and other their Fellows Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery of the City of London at Newgate and County of Middlesex assembled together at Justice Hall in the Old Bailey

The Humble Prayer of Martin Wright now a prisoner in the Gaol of Newgate

Prayeth That he the said Martin Wright may be Tried Bailed or discharged this present Sessions now hold at the said Justice Hall in the Old Bailey this 18th. day of July 1753

Martin Wright
