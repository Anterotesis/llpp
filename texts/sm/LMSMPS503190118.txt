To his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of William Middls one of the High Constables for the hundred of [---] sorther

That Your Petition has Part to Several Petty Constables Sume of seven Pounds Three shillings and Six Pence [...] Appears by Several Accounts Certificates and Acceipts [...] here unto annexed.

Your Petitioner therefore most humbly prays Your Worshipfull to allow Your Petitioner the said Sume of Seven Pounds Three Shillings and Six Pence out of the Vagrant Rate for this Present Year Your Petitioner having allowed the same to the Several Constables of the Several Parishes and Places in Your Petitioners Division by order of [...] Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said county of Middlesex out of the Vagrant Rate for this present year

And Your Petitioner in Duty bound will ever Pray Etc:

Witt Nicholls
