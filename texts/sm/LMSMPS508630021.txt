To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Surrey

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of W Blackmore and John Staples Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (whereof one is of the Quorum) Waring date the fifth day of May 1791 Mary Newman and William her Son aged five Weeks were Removed from the Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex into the said Parish of Saint Mary Newington in the County of Surrey alledging the said Parish of Saint Mary Newington to be the Peace of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices do therefore appeal to your Worships therefrom and humbly pray that you will be Pleased to hear them in the premises and order that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint George in the County of Middlesex may appear before you at a certain day to be appointed to hear and abide the Judgement and Determination of your Worships touching the Appeal

And your petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Pearson Solicitor for the Appellants
