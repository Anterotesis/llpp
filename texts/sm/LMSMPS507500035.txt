Middlsex To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices for the said County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace assembled.

The humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Warborough in the County of Oxford.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands & Seals of William Hyde and William Rugge Esquires hearing Date the Twenty Ninth Day of November 1781 now last past Susannah Rymes was removed from the parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex to the Parish of Warborough in the County of Oxford as the place of Her [...] last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said Order or Pass Warrant And pray that Your worships will appoint some Day in the present Session for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square to appear before you taken and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching this appeal

And Your Petition Shall ever pray etc

Robt. Langrove Sollr. for the Parish of Warborough.
