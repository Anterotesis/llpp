To the Right Worp full His Maties Justices [...] of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble Peticoners peace of [...] the Churchwardens & Overseers of ye [...] Poor of the Parish of St Dunstans in ye West in the City of London

Sheweth That pray Churchwarden Order or Peass Warrant under the H [...] seale of Johes Fuller and Samuell Parry Esqr Two of His [...] Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx (Quon mmc) bearing Date the Sixth Day of March last past Edward Hughes was removed from the Parish of St.Giles in the Fields in die said County of Middx unto the said Parish of St. Dunstan in the West London Alledging that of St. Dunstan in the West to be the Place of his last legall Settlement

That your Peticoners Conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant on Order of the said Two Justices Do there of [...] your Worps there from And humbly pray That yor Worps will be pleased to hear them to the Premissed & Ordered That Ye Churchwardn. and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Giles in the Fields in the County of Middx aforesaid many appear before you at a certain Day by our worps to be appeainted and hear & abide the Judgement [...] Determainacon of Your Worps touch [...] desppeale

And Ye Petr Should ever pray Etc
