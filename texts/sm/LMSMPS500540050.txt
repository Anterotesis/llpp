To the Right Worll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midd

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the [---] Towne of Hodsdon in the County of Hertford

Sheweth

That [---] one Jane Styles wth a yong Child was lately passed from the Pish of St Andrew Holborne in the Said County to the Said [---] Town of Hodsdon by Warrt. under the hands & Seals of Samuel Buck Esqr & Thomas Smith Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the Said County of Midd alleadging that the Said Jane Styles was last legally Settled att the Said Pish of Hodsdon

Yor Petrs thinke themselves aggrieved wth the Judgmt of the Said two Justices & humbly pray that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of St Andrew Holborne & all other Pties concerned may attend this Court Some time this Sessions to heare & abide the finall end & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale

P Tytons

And yor petr Shall ev pray Etc

Walthus Chevey John Boreham
