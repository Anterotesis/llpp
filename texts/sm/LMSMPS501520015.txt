To the Right Worpll: His Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in the Generall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon & Appeale of Roger Rogerson Rutor of Stanwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Yor. Peticonr. is rated to assessed [...] for his Tythes & Glybe Land lying in the said parish of Hanwell, at the rate of one hundred pounds p ann for & towards the releife of the poor there

Yor. Peticonr. thinking himself aggrieved wth. the said Rate or Assessmt. Appeales to this Court for Releife And Prayes that this Court will be pleased to order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the said parish of Hanwell to Attend this Court Some day this Sessions to hear & abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching this Appeale.

And Yor. Petr. shall ever Pray Etc
