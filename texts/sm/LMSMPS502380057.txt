To the Right Worshipll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their Generall Quarter Sessions.

The humble Peticon and Appeall of the Churhwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County of Middlx.

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass under the hands & Seales of John Fuller and Thomas Robe Esqrs. two of his Majesties Justice of th Peace for the sd. County (Quorum Unus)bearing Date the Twentieth day of September last past Nicholas Robinson & Rose this Wife were removed from the Parish of St. Giles Criplegate in the said County to the said Parish of St. James Clerkenwell as the place of their last legall Settlement.

Yor. Petrs. think themselves Aggreived by the Judgemt of the said two Justices Appeall against the Same and humbly Pray a Day of hearing and Determineing yr of

And your Petrs. shall Pray Etc.
