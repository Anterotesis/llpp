To Theire May Justices of the peace at ye Quarter Sessions of the peace lield al Hicks Hall Assembled the 25th if Aprill 1691 [...]

The Humble peticon of Benjamin Arnold Senior

Humbly Sheweth That whereas yo [...] tocon poor peticonr was bound over before Justices Robin to appse at this genrall Quarter Sessions of the peace & theire being Name wpsemle agt your peticonr.

The Prmises Tenderly Considered your peace peticonr Humbly traver that hee may bee called & discharged hee haveing now psecular & Your peticonr on in duty bound will ever pray For yours [...] health pray prily & happiness
