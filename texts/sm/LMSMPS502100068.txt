To the Right Worpl his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in then General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Clements East Cheap London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrt under the hands & Seals of the Right Honble the Lord H Coleran [...] and Hugh Smithson Esqr two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (Quor unus) bearing Dates Second day of April Anno Dui 1723 James Million was Removed and passed from the parish of Tottenham in ye Sd County unto the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. Clement East Cheap London Alledging the Said Parish to be the place of ye last Legal Settlement

That Your Pettionrs conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd Warrt and Judgmt of ye sd two Justices and do therefore most humbly Appeal to your Worships there from

Yr. Petitioners therefore most humbly prays Your Worps. will be pleased to hear them in them premisses and Order that the Church Wardens Overseers of the poor of ye sd parish of Tottenham may Appear before Your Worps. on Such day as the Court shall appoint and hear & abide the determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal
