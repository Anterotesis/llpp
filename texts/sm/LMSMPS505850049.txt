To the Worshipfull John Hawkins Esqr Chairman and to the rest of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlsx in their generall Quarter Sessions Assemld

The Humble Petition of John Ridge

Sheweth That your Petr by the Consent of his Father John Ridge of Taunton in the County of Somerset Timber merchant was bound as an Apprentice to Henry Thompson and Wm Thomas of St Mary Le Bone in the County of Middlesex Cabinet makers Joiners and House builders to Serve them after the manner of an Apprentice from the first day of June 1766 unto the full end and Term of Seven years.

That the sum of Twenty one pounds was given as an Apprentice Fee with your Petitioner

That the said William Thomas some time in or about the Month of April last Absconded and left his bussiness and hath not since been heard of

That about the 20th of April last Henry Thompson[---] was arre [---] sted for Debt and hath continued a prisoner in the Kings Bench ever since that Time

That the Business of your Petr said Masters hath Since that Time and now is totally discontinued

Your Petr. therefore humbly prays that he may be Discharged from his and Apprin tiship and that his said Master may be Order'd to return all or some part of the money & given

With your Petr and that your Worships woud be pleased to make Such order in the premisses as to your Worships Shall Seem, meet And your Petr Shall ever Pray Etc

W Johnston Sol for the Petr

Middx At the general Quarter Session of the peace of our Lord the King holden for the County of Middlesex at Hicks Hall in St John Street in the said County on Monday the fourthday of July in the Eighth year of the reign of our Sovereign Lord George the third King of great Britain Etc It is Ordered that the within named Henry Thompson and William Thomas do Attend this Court on Thursday the Seventh day of July instant [...] on the Matter of the within Petition to hear and abide the Determination of this Court touching the same and upon reading the affidt, of, the Petr. his further ordered that leaving a Copy of the within Petition and of this Order at the last place of a bode of the said William Thomas shall be deemed good Service on him the said William Thomas

By the Court Waller
