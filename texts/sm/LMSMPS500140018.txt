To the Right Worpl their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble peticon of Rebecca Tucker the Wife of William Tucker of the Hamlett of Wapping Stepney in the said County Marriner.

Sheweth That yor. Petr hath lived in the said Hamlett for about Three yeares past and doth pay for her dwelling house there the annuall rent of 4l. That the Inhabitants of the said Hamlett have allwaies rated yor. Petr. 1s P Quarter toward the releife of the Poore of the said Hamlett till of late they demand of yor. Petr. 5s P Quarter for the Poore thereof Which She is noe waies able to pay she having only her dayly labour to releive herselfe and one Poore Child her said husband having been these Two yeares past in their Maties Service at Sea where he still remaines.

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays yor. worpps to orders that she may be reduced to the said former rate of 1s PQuarter tow and the releife of the Poore of the sd. Hamlett

And yor. Petr Shall over Pray Etc
