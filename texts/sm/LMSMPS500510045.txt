To the Right Worsppl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens of the parish of St John Wapping in the said County.

Sheweth

That in the yeare 1694 Albert Alberson did Serve the Office of Scavenger within the said parish And that upon auditing his Accompts there appeares to be remaining in his hands the sume of Nine pounds or thereabouts which he doth refuse to pay to your Peticoners.

P Westley

They humbly therefore Pray your Worspps to be pleased to Order the said Albert Alberson to appeare in this Court this prsent Sessions to shew cause why he should not pay your Peticoners the Moneys [...] remaining in his hands as aforesaid.

And yor. Petrs. shall ever Pray Etc.
