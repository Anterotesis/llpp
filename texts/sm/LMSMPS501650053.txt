The Humble Petition of Jane Dawson

[...] Urst Fushres

Mr. West Mr. B: Johnson Mr. Raymond Mr. Kyrby or to re & wrt poors rate

Middx Ss: Ad Genal Quartial Session pacis Dni Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hicks Hall in St. John Street in Com prd P adjorn die Lune scilt decimo quarto die Octobris Anno regni Dni Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc quarto

It is Ordered by this Court that it be, And it is hereby recomended unto Thomas West Bastswick Johnson Hugh Raymond and Robert Kyrby Esqrs. Justices of the peace for the said County or any two of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens and Overseers the poor of the in this County and to Examin into the truth of the Petrs complaint, and to certify their opinions touching the premisses within menconed unto this Court or to his Maties. Justices of the peace at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the said County.
