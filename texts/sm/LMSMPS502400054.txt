Middx ss: To the Rt: Worpsl: his Masties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx: at their General Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Peticon & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of St: Mary White Chapel in the sd. County.

Sheweth That by Order under the Hands and Seals of John Colbatch and Samuel Grey Esqrs: two of his Majties: Justices of the peace for the sd: County bearing Date the 29th. day of December last Sarah Munns Singlwoman, aged about Twenty Six Years was removed from the Parish of St: Martins in the Fields in the Sd: County To the Sd. Parish of St: Mary White Chappell as to the place of her last Legal Settlemt:

That yor. Peticonrs. think themselves Agrievd and Humbly Appeals to this Honble: Court against the said [...] prays Yor: Worps would be pleased to Order that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the sd: Parish of St: Martin in the fields may Attend this Court Some day this Prsent Sessions then and there, to hear & abide the Determinacon [...] of this Court touching ye. sd. Appeal

And Yor. Peticonrs: Shall Ever pray Etc:
