To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition, and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the said County.

Sheweth

That by virtue of a Pass Warrant on Order under the hands and Seals of B.J.Hosby and G Pye Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Bare Acting in and for their County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth Day of March last past Timothy Johnson and tomorrow and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea as the Place of his last legal for the whereby your Petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieve

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining the said appeal

And your Petitioners will Pray

Herb Robertson Sol. for the Appellants}
