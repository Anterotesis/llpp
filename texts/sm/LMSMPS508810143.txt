To the Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents, in the Parish of St Andrew Holborn in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of sd Colquhoun and Wm. Gascoigne Esqrs two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the fifth day of January 1793 Ann Fox Singlewoman was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Christ Church in the sd County of Middlesex to the said Liberty of Saffron Hill Hatton Garden and Ely Rents as the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Ann Fox. Whereby your Petition as conceive themselves aggrieved and against the said Order do Appeal

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That a day may be appointed in the present Session to hear and determine the matter touching the sd Appeal.

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Joshua Cox Hatton Garden Sollr for Appellts
