To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of John Horne of Angel and Porter Alley in the Parish of St Luke Middx.

Sheweth. That by an order under the hands and Seals of Thomas Robe and Anthony Wroth Esqrs: two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second day of March 1734 Your Petitioner Stands adjudged the Father of a Female Bastard Child born of the Body of Sarah Hudson in the Parish of St: James Clerkenwell in the said County which sd. Order was not Served on yor. Petr. til the first day of Septr. constant

That your Petitioner humbly conceives, himself agreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and doth appeale to this Court [...] same and Prays that a day may be appointed in this present General Sessions for Vacating the said Order and that all Persons concerned may be Ordered to attend

And yor. Petr: as in duty bound shall ever Pray. Etc. John Horne
