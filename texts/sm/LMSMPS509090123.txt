To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in there General Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of Westminster in the Said County.

Sheweth

That by Cirlue of a Pass warrant or Order under the hands and seals of A Graham and R Clark Esquires two of his Majestes Justices of the Peace for the [...] County of Middlesex aforesaid one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty sixth day of January last Sarah Hardy was removed and Coveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved and do appeal to this Court against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worship will be pleased to save unto them the benefit of their appeal and to adjourn the hearing and determination thereof until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex at the New Session House on Clerkenwell Green and that all Persons concerned may be directed them & there to attend End your Petitioners shall prayly

[...] eo Leigh Peter Paul Hassile Hutchins}

Churchwardens & Overseers}

Saml Sheen Robt Lawson
