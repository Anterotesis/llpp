To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Generall Sessions Assembled

The humble Peticon and appeal of the Churchwardns and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Christ Church in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by a Warrant or order Dated the Eleventh day of July one thousand Seven hundred and thirty four under the hands and Seals of Richard Ricards and Richard Farmer Esqrs. Two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx (one being of the Quorum) Elizabeth the Widow[---] of Robert Kealing was removed from the parish of Saint Mary White Chappel in the County of Middx to the Parish of Christ church in the County of Middx as the Place of her Last Legall Settlement

That your Peticoners find themselves aggrieved by the Warrant or order of the Said Two Justices and humbly Appeal against the Same

A Davis Agent for ye Appellants Your Petrs therefore humbly Pray you will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chappell and all other persons concerned to Attend this Court Some Day this present Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment are Determinacon of this Court touching the premisses one that your Peticoners may have Such releife therein as to Justices and Law Doth appertain

And your Petrs Shall ever Pray Etc
