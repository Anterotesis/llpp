To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch London

Sheweth That by a Warrant or Order of Removal bearing date the Tenth day of November One thousand seven hundred and fifty five under the hand & seal of Samuel Bever Esquire one of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Elizabeth Inwood and her Female Bastard Child aged about four years were removed from the Parish of Kensington in the said County of Middx to the said Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch in London as the place of their last legall Settlement.

That your Petitioners find themselves aggrieved by part of the said Order or Warrant whereby the said Elizabeth Inwoods Female Bastard Child is removed from the said Parish of Kensington to the said Parish of Saint Mary Abchurch and humbly Appeal against that part of the said Order or Warrant

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray That you will be pleased to Order the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Kensington and all other persons concerned to attend this Court this present Sessions and to hear & abide the Judgment and determination of this Court touching the premises And that your Petitioners may have such Releif therein as to Justice and Law doth appertain

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

Thos. Palmer Jona Whebell} Church Wardens

W Tyson Junr.} Overseers.

Richd Maxey
