To the Right Worpll. his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon of John Taylor & Francis Oliver Constables of the Liberty of Glass house yard in the parish of St Botolph without Aldersgate in the Said County.

Sheweth That your Petr. John Taylor dureing his Serveing the Said Office of Constable, Conveyed three Severall persons from the Said Liberty, to the Towne of Chipping Barnett in the County of Hertford for which he was allowed for his trouble & Charge by three Certificates under the hands & Seales of Severall of his Maties Justices of the peace of this County the Sume of Seventeen Shillings & Six pence and that the Said Francis Oliver dureing his Serveing the Said officer, he Conveyed Seventeen Severall persons from the Said Liberty to the Towne of Chipping Barnett in the County of Hertford[---] And one person from the Said Liberty to the parish & Westham in the County of Essex for which he was allowed for his trouble & charge by eight Certificates under the hands & Seales of Severall of his Maties Justices of the peace for this County the Sume of Two pounds nine Shillings & Six pence [...] which Said Severall Sumes your Petrs are not Paid

Therefore Prays your Worps [...] the Payment of the Same Or otherwise [...] therein the premiss as your Wors [...] that [...] next

[...]

John Taylor Francis Oliver
