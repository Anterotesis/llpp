To the Right Worship [...] of the Peace of the County [...] their General Quarter Session [...] holden for the said County [...] Assembled

The Humble [...] Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Gascoyne and King Gould Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventeenth April last past John Garlick and Mary his Wife and their Six Children One Named John Aged about fourteen Years one other N [...] James aged about Eleven Years One other named Mary aged about Eight Years One other Named William aged about Years one other Named George aged about four Years And one other Named Robert aged about Two Years were removed and Conveyed from [...] the Parish of Kensington in the said County to the said Parish of St. [...] in the Fields as the Place of their last legal Settlement whereby Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved

Therefore [...] the Judgement of the [...] Worships will be Plea [...] to hear and determine [...]
