To your Worshipps his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex

The humble Petition of Thomas Topping Constable of the Parish of Littleton in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That your Petitioner having Served the Office of a Constable in the said parish of Littleton for about one whole year and three Quarter is very desirous to be put onto of The same, and there being no Court holden in the said Parish and Manor of Littleton in Several Years, hath no Opportunity of Getting Out of his said Constableship there

Therefore most humbly prays your Worshipps would be Pleased to put him Out of his said Constableship and in his Room he doth Nominate and Appoint Robert Smith or Thomas Child the Younger of the said Parish of Littleton

And he shall ever pray, an so forth

The Mark of Thomas [x] Topping Petitioner

October 6th 1733 Approved by [...]

P Reynell G Andrewes
