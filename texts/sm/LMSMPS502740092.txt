To the Worshipfull his Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx in General Quarter Sessions at Hicks hall Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St. Paul Covt. Garden in the County of Middx

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands and Seals of Theod: Smith and H: Blagny Esqrs. Two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing Date the Seventeenth Day of March last Ursula Harris Widow aged about Sixty years was removed from the parish of St. Ann Westmr. in the said County of Middx to the said parish of St. Paul Covent Garden in the Same County as the place of her last legall Settlement

Your Petrs. thinking themselves agreived by the aforesaid Order [---] of the said Two Justices of the Peace and appeale to this Court against the same Humbly pray that the Benefit of an Appeal may be saved to them untill the next General Session of the Peace for the said County of Middx

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
