To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex

Sheweth, That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Gordon and John Wright Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the City and liberty of Westminster and County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twentieth Day of May instant John described as a Male Bastard Child born of the Body of Margaret Dowling Singlewoman was Removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Ann within the liberty of Westminster aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the Place of his legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners are as they conceive aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Justices and their said Order and pray your Worships that the Benefit of the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that a Day may be appointed in this present Sessions for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petitioners will Pray Etc

Fredk. Booth Sol for the Appellants}
