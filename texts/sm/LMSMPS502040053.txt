To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the Parish of st Nicolas in the Citty of Rochester in the County of Kent.

Sheweth That by Warrent or order Dated the Sixteenth Day of June last under the Hands and Seals of John Fuller & Fr Crosbie Esqr two of his Matys Justices of the peace for the said county (Quor un) Ruth Hollyman Wife of William Hollyman with their two Children Mary Six Years old & Withers aged two Months were removed from the Parish of St Sepulchre in this County to the said parish of St Nicolas being called the said Warrent or order the parish of St Michale. in the Citty of Rochester as the place of their Last legall Settlement.

That Your Petr: Conceive themselves aggrieved by the sd order of the said two Justices & Appeal against the same.

Your Peticoners therefore humbly pray. this Court will be pleased to appoint all partys to attend this court this Sessions to here & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yor. Petrs: shall Ever pray Etc.
