To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Sessions of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Lockwood and I Dalrymple Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 30th day of June 1798. Ann the Wife of Josephus Noortwyck (gone from her) was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Marylebone in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said Two Justices and their said Order and pray the said Appeal may be saved unto them and that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the same And your Petitioners will ever Pray

W. Y. Lemage Sol for Appellant No. 12 Queen fr. Son
