To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace For the County of Midd's

Sheweth The humble Petition of Charles Harall Constable of South Crims in the sd. County

That at Midsomer last there was a Quarter due to Your Petr: as Constable for Passing and Releiving of Warrants Which Quarter is wh. And Your Petrs. wanting the sd. Moneys

P Tho: Nash He therefore Humbly Prayes an Order for the Same [...] Your Worspps Soe thinke fill And Your Petr. Shall Ever pray Etc

Charles Harrald.
