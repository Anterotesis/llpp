To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of the Church: Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Margaret Westmr. in the said County

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant Under the hands and Seals of Ja Jenyns Ja Clitherow Esqr. two of his- Maties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx bearing Date the 4th day of January Anno Dui 1727. Elizabeth Herring was removed from and out of the Parish of Heese in the said County to the said Parish of St. Margaret of Westmr. as the Place of her Lawfull Settlement.

That your Petrs. thinking themselves Agreived by the Judgment of the said two Justices humbly appeal to your Worships against the same.

Therefore humbly Pray your Worsp. will Please to Order That the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of. the said Parish of Heese may attend this Court some day this Quarter Sesss. to hear and abide the final Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petrs. Shall ever Pray Etc
