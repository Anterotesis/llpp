To the Rt. Worspll: his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition Ahppeale of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlets of Hammersmith in the Said County

Sheweth That by an Order under the hands & Seales of Henry Barker & John Tarver Esqrs. of his Maties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the thirteenth day of February last past Elizabeth Bullock late wife of Thomas Bullock Deceased with her Son Thomas Bullock aged about Seven years were removed from & out of the parish of Cheswick in the Said County to the said Hamlett of Hammersmith as the place of their last legal Settlement

Yor. Petr. [---] thinking themselves Aggrieved by the aforesaid Order of the Said two Justices Appeale to this Court against the Sames humbly pray Yor Worps will be pleased to appoint a time for hearing and Determining the Same

Danl. Bolton Sollr. for the Appells.

And Yor. Petr. Shall ever pray Etc
