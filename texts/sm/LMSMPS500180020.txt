To the Right Worpl. their Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

Sheweth The humble Peticon of Robert Mills paviours

That about a yeare and a halfe past your Pecr was by Order of yor. Worpps imployed to repaire: the pavement in the street adjoyning to the West Side of Hicks Hall Soe that there is due to yor Pecr. for Such his said worke the Sume of Foure pounds Seaven Shillings and Six pence.

Yor. Pecr. therefore most humbly prays yor. Worpps to be pleased to Order that he may be paid and Sattisfyed these moneys Soe to him due by such waie & meane as unto yor. Worpps Shall Seem meet

And yor Pecr. Shall ever Pray Etc
