To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The Humble Peticon of Jane Garton wife of Edward Garton

Sheweth That As of Last Sessions Your Petr: Preferred As Bill of Indictment against one Thomas Douten and Anne his wife for Assaulting your Petr And that Sometime before Yor Petr preferr'd another bill of Indictment against one John Todd for alsoe Assaulting yor Petr in her own Shopp

That by the Instigation of the sd Donten & Todd or One of them yor Petr [...] said Husband was Arrested And he is New A Prisonering Marshalsea Prison in Southwarke

That the Todd & Donten his wife are Letigcons People and have caused her Husband to be Arrested on Purpose to Putt her to Further Charge And her most Materiall Witness being att Prsent out of Towne

P Tho: Nash

yor Petr therefore most humbly prays yor Worshipps to be Pleased to respite the sd Indictmt untill next Session

And yor Petr Shall ever Pray Etc Jane [x] Garton her mrke
