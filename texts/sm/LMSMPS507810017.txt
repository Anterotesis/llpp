To Anthony Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace Assembled in their General Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the County of Middlesex in April Session 1784

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Bemfleet in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by an Order or Warrant under the Hands and Seals of Henry Lamb & Philip Dyot Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the [...] and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 18th. [...] day of February last past Geo: Shepperd aged about 35 Years [...] his Wife were removed from and out of the Parish of [...] wick in the sd. County of Middlesex to the sd. Parish of [...] Bemfleet in the sd. County of Essex as being their last legal place of Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and apprehend that the Settlement of the said George Shepperd and Elizabeth his Wife is not in the said Parish of Bemfleet

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your worships that they may be at liberty to enter & try the said Order at the Present Sessions holden in and for the County of Middlesex

And your Petitioners shall ever pray
