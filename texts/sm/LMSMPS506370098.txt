To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth. That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of John Phillips and Willm. Timbrell Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty second Day of September last Anne Wood Widow of Tobias Wood Aged upwards of Thirty years and her five Children by her said husband Vizt. Anne Aged Eight Years, Joseph upwards of Six years, Thomas upwards of four years , Mary Aged three years Elizabeth and aged Seven Months were removed from the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid to the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields aforesaid as the Place of their last legal Settlement by which your Petitioners Apprehend themselves Aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Pray That this Court will be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petrs. will Pray Etc

J. Holl. Sollr. for Appelts.
