To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace to the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace at the New Session House Clerkenwell Green Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or order under the hands and Seals of St. Ayliffe and Edward B. Green Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace Acting in and for the County of middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Eleventh day of January one thousand seven hundred and Eighty five Thomas Croswell was removed from the parish of Kensington in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of St. Paul Covent Garden as the place of his last legal settlement where by a [...] Petitioners think themselves aggreived and do appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore pray this Court that your Worships will be pleased to save the benefit of their said appeal and to adjourn the hearing and determining thereof until the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden in and for the said County of Middlesex at the New Session house aforesaid and that all persons concern use may be then directed to Attend.

And your Petitioners shall pray etc

Richd Christmas Jno French Jas Sidg wick } Church wardens

A Chaplin Gould } Overseers of the poors
