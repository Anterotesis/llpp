To the Rt. Worpl: his Maties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx Sitting at Hicks hall

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the poor of the Hamlett of Ratcliffe in the parish of Stepney in the County of Middx:

Sheweth

That by Warrt. or Order under the Hands and Seals of Robert Jackson and Edward Groce Esqr. two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County (Quor un) bearing Date the 20th Day of November last Alice the Wife of Samuel Hamblin was by the said- Order removed from the parish of St. Mary Whitechappel in the said County to the said Hamlett of Ratcliffe as the place of her legall Settlement whereby your petitionrs. humbly Conceive they are wronged and Aggrieved

Your Petitionrs: therefore humbly pray your Worpps would be [...] pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal this persons Sessions and that your petitionr may be Relived as to your Wisdoms shall Seem Meet

And your Petitionrs. shall Ever Pray Etc
