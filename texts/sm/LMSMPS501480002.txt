To the Rt: Worshipll: His Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx: Mett at Hix's Hall Etc:

The Humble Petition & Appeale of Francis Ives in the Parish of St: Giles without Cripplegate

Sheweth That Yor: Petitioner is a poore man and Lives in apart of the Appart: of Mr: John Rose That the said John Rose hath always paid all parochal Tax's for the said Appartment it being at all Times Esteemd as part of his Premrs: That the Said John Rose is at this Time Assest at [---] for the whole rent at a pound rate to the Poores rate wherein Yor: Petitioners Appartment Is Included

But Yor: Petr: never the less is assest to the said rate for four pounds Pr: ann: which at Three [...] shillings [...] Pound is Twelve Shillings Pr: ann: and Yor: Petr: being unable to pay the Same

Yor: Peter: therefore Humbley prays that he may be relieved as Yor: Worships in Yor: great Wisdom Shall see meet

And Yor: Petr: shall ever pray
