To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of William Child of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That yor Peticoner is Over rated for the house he Dwells in & likewise for Stock in the poors rate of the said Parish Wherefore he Appeals against the same

Your Petitr therefore humbly prays such Relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought Meete.

And Yor Petr shall Ever pray etc

Wm Child

July 31st: 1716 Present John Fuller Tho Rand and Isaac Tillard Esqr Not releived
