To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of Edmund Pepys and Samuel Foyster Esquires two of his Majesty/'s Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty eighth Day of November last, Sarah Sealey Singlewoman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved

They therefore humbly Appeal to this

this Court against the Adjudication and Order of the said two Justices and prays that your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining thereof and that all Persons concerned may have Notice to attend the same

And your Petitioners will every pray Etc

[...] Sydenham Mr Murthwaite} Churchwardens

Byssel Wm Main L Bond} Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Anne

James Ranken
