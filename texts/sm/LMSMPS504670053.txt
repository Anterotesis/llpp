To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of [...] for the County of Middlesex in their [---] General Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the Parish off Camberwell in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by a pass Warrant, or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Francis Jackman and Henry Trent Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster within the said County of Middlesex, bearing date the fifth day of November last past, Ann Campbell aged about twenty two years and her Child Peter aged about fourther months were removed from the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said parish of Camberwell as the place of their last Legal Settlement, Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggreived.

Your Petitioners therefore most Humbly Appeal against the said Pass Warr [...] or Order of Removal And pray That your Worships will be pleased to appoint a short day for the Hearing thereof

And your Petrs. shall ever Pray [...]
