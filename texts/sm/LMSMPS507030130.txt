To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of All hallows the Great in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Cadwallader Coker and J Newton Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Ninth Day of this Instant June Joseph Rowland was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of All hallows the great as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Therefore they humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order And Pray that the Benefit and that the hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next General Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex

And your Petitr: shall Pray etc

John Packman Thos Perkins James Bennett Frans Wynanty} Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Allhallows the Great
