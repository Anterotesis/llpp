To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace Assembled at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace holden at Hickss Hall in and for the County of Middlesex

To Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Lewisham in the County of Kent

Sheweth That Thomas Quarrill and Thomas Smith Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid upon Complaint made unto them by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel in the said County of Middlesex That Susannah Phillips Singlewoman lately intruded and came into the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel and was likely to become chargeable to the same did by an Order or Warrant of Removal under their hands and Seals Dated the Seventeenth day of August last past Order the said Susannah Phillips to be removed and Conveyed from the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel to the said Parish of Lewisham as the place of her last legal Settlement

That the said Susannah Phillips by Virtue of the said Order hath been removed and Conveyed from the said Parish of Saint Mary White Chapel to the said Parish of Lewisham where She now remains Chargeable to the same Parish

Your petitioners Apprehending themselves Agrieved by the said Order or Warrant of Removal made by the said two Justices as aforesaid do therefore humbly Appeal against the same

Wm Arrowsmith Tho Tanders} Churchwardens

Paul Valentine Francis Harvey} Overseers
