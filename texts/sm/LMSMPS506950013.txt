To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Thomas Finley

Sheweth

That your Petr. being Tender of his Reputaced and being [...] Did offened this- Honble Court in being Obstinate was not willing to Expose himselfe to presente Eliz: Hackett for Pisk [...] his Pockett of 16 of Â£17. But being sensible of his Errand in not answering this Honble Cort. was Comitted for Compounding Fellony and is Bayled out though he never Did doe any such Act and is now willing and Ready to prosecute and hath Feed Councell to make a Vigerous Prosecution and the sd. Hackett is to be Tryed to Morrow Morning and comes is allowed to be an Evidence for the Queen against Hackett by the Motion of your Petrs. Councell

Yor. Petr. [...] your Worspps to Discharged him from his Recognizance & further attendance On this Honble Court

And yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

P Thos: Nash

Thomas Tinlin
