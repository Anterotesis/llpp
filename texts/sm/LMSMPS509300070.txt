To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Paul Shadwell in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of C.T Kirby and J Warner two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the said County of Middlesex one where of being of the Quorum) bearing date the 17th day of October Fors 1796 Mary Wright the Widow of William Wright and Jane their Child aged Nine Months together with her said Husbands two Children by his former wife deceased (Vizt) John aged Twelve Years and William aged ten Years were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint John the evangelist in Westminster to the said Parish of Saint Paul Shadwell as the Place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner conceive themselves aggreived by the adjudication of the said two Justices therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same and pray your Worships will appoint a Day in the present Session for hearing and determining of the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Etc Saml. Malbon Sollr. for Saint Paul Shadwell
