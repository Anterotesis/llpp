To the Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in the General Quarter Sessions at Hicks Hall in and for the said County

The Humble petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Neets in the County of Huntingdon

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middlesex (where of one is of the Quorum) bearing date the twenty seventh day of February in the year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and thirty five James Maguire and Elizabeth his Wife and their two Children (that is to say) Thomas aged eleven Years & Elizabeth aged seven years the Lawfull Issue of the said James Maguire and Elizabeth his Wife were removed from the Parish of Christ Church in this County of the Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Neatts in the County of Huntingdon as the peace of their Last Legall Settlement where by your Petitioners Conceive themselves agrieved and against the said Warrant or Order do appeall to this Court

Samll. Harris sold for the Apellants

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray that as Short day may be appointed this present Sessions to hear & determination the Matter touching the said appeal

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
