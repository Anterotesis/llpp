Middx ss To the Worspll: His Majties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in Generall Sessions

The Humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Kensington in the Said County of Middx

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of John Meard &Jacob Delamot Blagney Esqrs: two of his Majties Justices of the peace for the said County of middx (Quar anns) bearing Date the fourteenth-day of July 1732 one Grace E [...] ess was removed from the parish of St Ann Westminster in the said County of Middx to the Said parish of Kensingten as the place of the last Legall Settlement of the sd Grave E [...] css.

That yor; Peticonrs: find themselves Aggrieved by the sd Warrt or order of the said two Justices of the Peace, and humbly Appeale to this Court Against the Same

Yor: Peticonrs: there fore pray this court to appoint a Day this present Sessions for all parties Concerned to attend this court to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the Said appeale

And yor: Peticonrs Shall ever Pray Etc
