To the Rt. Worspll. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Anne Buttery of the parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the sd. County

Sheweth

That As of Last Sessions a Bill of Indictmt. was prferred agst. yor. Petr. in this Honble Count by One Mr. Craine One of the Constables of the sd. Parish for keeping a Disorderly house As by the sd. Indictmt. appeares.

That your Petr hath Lived in the sd. Pish these (30) Yeares past and upwards and both herselfe & husband allwaies Demeaned and behaved themselves very civilly & honestly and forasmuch as your Petrs. husband being near (80) Yeares of Age and very crazy in Body

P Tho: Nash

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prayes yor. Worspps to be pleased to Remedy her Aggreivance as unto yor. Worspps shall Seem meet

And yor Petr shall ever Pray Etc
