Middlesex ss To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Faith under St. Paul in the City of London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Jacob Seroux and William Bleamire Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date of the Second day of January Instant James Boulton was removed and conveyed from the Parish of St. Luke in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Faith in the City of London as the place of his last legall Settlement

That Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggreived by the said Order of the said two Justices and thereupon appeal to their Honourable Court against the same

And Pray that this Honourale Court will be pleased to appoint a day for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal and to make such Order therein as may be just

And Your Petitioners will ever Pray Etc

Chs. Pinker Sollr. for the Appellant Warwick Court, Warwick Lane
