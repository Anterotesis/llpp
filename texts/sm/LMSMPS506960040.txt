To the Rt. Worspll her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon of Mary Crilley.

Sheweth That in August Last your Petr. because Bayle for the appearance of One Rebecca Com att the thew next Expensing Sessions for assaulting and Beating of Our Judith Camovin do by the Recognizance may Appear

That the sd. Rebecca Com before Christmas last was arrested for the sd Fast is untill this time hath been Confined a Prisoner in the Queens Bench

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petr therefore most humble prayes yor [...] To be pleased to Grant that the may be Discharged from the sd. Recognizance.

And yor. Petr shall Ever pray Etc

Mary Cailly
