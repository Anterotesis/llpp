To the Rt Worspll. his Majtys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeale of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Woolwich in the County of Kent

Sheweth

That by Virtue of a Warr. or Ordr. under the hands & Seals of Robt. Dennett & Joseph Hayne Esqs two of his Majtys. Justices of the peace for the County of [...] Middx (Quor unus) bearing date the 26th day of October last there was passed and removed into yor. peticonrs. said parish from the parish of St. Botolphs without Aldgate in the County of Middx One Elizabeth Allen wife of Thomas Allen And that yor. petitionrs. by reason thereof find themselves agrived and appeal agst. the same Ordr.

Yor. petitionrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Worsps. to appoint a day this prsent Genal Sessions for the hearing & determining this their said appeal.

And yor. peticors. shall pray Etc.
