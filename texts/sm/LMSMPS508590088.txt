To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Bartholomew by the Exchange in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order or Pass warrant bearing date the twenty seventh day of October 1790 under the hands and seals of John Spiller and Rupert Clarke Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one where of being of the Quorum) Whereby Ann Kemp Single woman was removed and conveyed from and out of the parish of saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the said parish of saint Bartholomew by the Exchange as the place of her last legal settlement

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggrieved by the side order or pass Warrant of the said two Justices Humbly appeal against to the same

And therefore pray that your Worships will be pleased to order the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex aforesaid and all persons concerned to attend this Court some day this present Session to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching the premises and that your Petitioners may have such relief there in as to your Worships shall seem meet

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
