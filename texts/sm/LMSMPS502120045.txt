To the Rt: Worpl. his Majtyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx now in Genall Quarter Sessions Assembled:

The Humble Peticion and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers: of the poor of the parish of St: Ann in the Libtye of Westmr within the sd. County of Middx

Sheweth That Mary Hutchinson Widow, late wife of William Hutchinson deceased, and Two Children (vizt.) Thomas aged about Eleven Years, and Willm Hutchinson aged about Eight Years were by Order, dated the 24th: day of June last 1723: Under the hands and Seals of Phillip Willshin and Stephen Martin Leakt Esqrs: two of his Majtyes Justices of the peace for the sd County of Middx (Quor Unus) removed from the Hamlet of Lime house in the parish of Stepney in the Sd County, to the aforesd parish of St: Ann in the sd Libtye of Westmr:

Your Peticionrs: thereby humbly conceives themselves agreived, and appeals from the said [...] Ordered to the [...] saw Justices of the peace to the Judgment of this Honble: Court and crave a day of hearing so as the sd Mary Hutchinson and her sd Two Children may be so setled as upon hearing this Honble: Court shall think fitt

And your Petrs: Shall every pray Etc

Vera Copra
