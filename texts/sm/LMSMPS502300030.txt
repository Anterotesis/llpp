To the Right Worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeale of the Overseers of the Poor of the Liberty of Glass house Yard in the Parish of St Botolphs without Aldersgate in the said County

Sheweth That by a Warrant of Removall under the hands and Seales of John Fuller and Alexander Ward Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the Peace for County of Middx (Quor Unus) bearing date the first day of Septemr One Thousand Seven hundred and twenty five, Joseph Mecum and Mary his wife were removed and Convey'd from the Parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middx to the said Liberty of Glasshouse Yard as the Place of their last Legall Setlement

That Yor Petitioners Apprehending themselves Aggreived [...] [---] aforesaid warrant of the said two Justices humbly Appeale to this Court against the same

Therefore Your Petitioners most humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a day sometime this prsent General Quarter Sessions of the Peace and to Order that the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of St. Sepulchre and all other Parties concerned may Attend this Court to hear and Abide the Final Arbitrament & Determinacon of this Court touching the Matter of this Appeale.

And your Petrs shall ever pray Etc.
