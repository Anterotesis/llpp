To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Sepulchre London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Mr. Addington and Thomas Bishop Esquires Two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex aforesaid (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twenty fourth Day of April Instant William White was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields in the County of Middlesex aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Sepulchre London as the place of his legal Settlement

Your Petitioners thinking themselves aggrieved by the said Order of Removal of the said Two Justices appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that the said Appeal may be heard this present Sessions and that a time may be appointed for the hearing and determining thereof
