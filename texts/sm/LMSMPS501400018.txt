To the Right Worsppll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace of the County of Midd assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions

The humble Peticon of the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St Giles in the Fields in the said County

Sheweth

That Richard Paxton Son of Cornelius Paxton Deced hath been chargeable to the said parish for these [...] months last past

That Evan Williams Blacksmith inhiting in the parish of St James in the Libty of Westmr. in this County Grandfather to the said Richard Paxton by the mothers Side is a person of Sufficient Ability & ought to releive & maintain the said Richard Paxton as your Peticonrs: humbly conceive

P J Tyton Yor. Peticonr: therefore humbly pray that the said Evan Williams may be Ordered to attend this Court some Day this Sessions to shew cause why he should not take care to releive & maintain the said Richard Paxton

And yr. Peticonrs. (as in Duty bound) shall ever pray Etc.
