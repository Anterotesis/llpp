To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of Saint Giles Without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Mercer and Robert Midford Esquires two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one being of the quorum) bearing Date the second Day of November 1737 Hannah Saunders Widow of Thomas Saunders deceased and Anne her Child aged about 8 years were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the field in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That Your Petrs. conceive themselves aggreived by the said Warrant or Order & humbly Appeals against & the same

Your Petrs. therefore humbly Pray that your Worships will be Pleased to Appointt a Day of this Present Sessions that all Parties may Attend to hear and abide the Judgement and Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And Yr. Petitioners shall ever Pray

Stephen Butler Phill Adams} Churchwrs.

Wm. Cocking Simon King} Overseers
