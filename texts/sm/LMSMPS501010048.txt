To the Rt Worspll her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Midds

Sheweth The humble Peticon of William Page of the Parish of St Giles Cripplegate in ye to County.

That yor Petr is Rated One Penny & half Penny P Weeke towards the Relief of the Poore of the said Parish But forasmuch as yor Petr being a Poor Man and having a Large Family to Mainteyn And part of his Goods being now Distrayned for Nonpaymt of the said Rate And others threatned soe to be next Weeke soe that Yor Petr is not Able to pay the said Rate

P Tho: Nash Your Petr therefore most humbly prays Yor Worshipps to be pleased to Mitigate the sd Rate as onto Yor. Worshipps Shall Seeme most meete

And Yor Petr shall ever pray Etc
