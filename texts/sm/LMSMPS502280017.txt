Middx ss To the Churchwardens & Overseers of the Poor of the

To the Righ Worship full his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter of Sessions of tho Peace Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish Waddsworth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order of removall under the Hands and Seals of Saml. Grey Esqr and Sr. John Colbutch Knt. two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Ann Beveridge Widow was removed from the parish of St Martins in the fields in the sd. County to the parish of Wandsworth in the County of Surry as the Place of her last Legall Settlement Yor. petitionr thinking themselves Agrieved by the Order of the sd Justices humbly Appeal against the same and desire this Court will please to appoint some day in this prsent Sessions to hear and determine the same

And Yor Petition as in Duty bound thall Ever Pray

Thos. Boverick Thos Skinner} Churchwardens Rob. Melcham [...]
