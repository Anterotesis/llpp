To the Worshipsfull Bench of Justices at Hicks Hall the Humble Petition of Isaac Sympson

Sheweth that he is now a Prisoner in Clerken- -well Bridgewell for a fine of Ten pounds which Fine it is not in your poor Petitioners power to pay He having a Wife & a Small Familey & Deprived of the Power of Supporting Himself & his Poor Familey therefore Humbly hopes your Worships will take his Deplorable Case into your wise Consideration of your Great Goodness to mittigate his Fine which will prevent the intire Destruction of him and his poor Wife and Children therefore your Petitioners Humbly implo [...] Mercy and a in Duty Bound will ever Pray

Isaac Sympson
