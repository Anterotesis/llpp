To the Rt. Worspll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Dennie Boyle of the Parish of Monken Hadley in the sd. County

Sheweth

That yor. Petr. keepes an Inn in the sd. Parish In which place he hath Lived these 40 Yeares and pd. all Taxes and Pish Dutys and still Doth soe farr as his Capacity Permitts.

That yor. Petr. stands Indicted for not Receiving his Lycence att the Last Renewall of Lycences for this County Notwithstanding your Petr. did send his Lycence for that purpose and forasmuch as your Petr. being aged 94 Yeares past his Labour every dim of Siyat

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayes Yor. Worspps to be pleased to Grant him such Releife in the Prmises as unto this Honble Cort shalle thought most meet

And yor Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.
