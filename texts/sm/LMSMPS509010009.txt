To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Kensington in the said County

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of T. Floud and Edw. Read Esqr. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace Acting in and for the said County Dated the Third Day September one Thousand seven hundred and ninety four Jane Brett a Singlewoman was removed from the parish of Saint Martin in the field within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County to the said parish of Kensington as the place of the last legal Settlement of her the said Jane Brett Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved And do Appeal to your Worships against the said Order

Wherefore your Petitioners humbly pray your Workships will be pleased to Appoint a Day in the present Sesion for the hearing and determinging the said Appeal and that all parties concerned may be ordered to attend and to abide and perform such Order as your Worships will please to give your Petitioners such Relief in the Premises shall Pray

Eleanr. Gibbon Church Wardens

S Beare Saml. Chancellor E Brown George Tunks} Overseers [...]
