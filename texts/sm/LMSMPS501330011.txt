To the Right Worsppl her Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The humble Peticon of Jeremy Boreman

Sheweth That the office of Keeper of New Prison at Clerkenwell being vacant and your Peticoner being desireous to execute the said office which he is resolved faithfully and diligently to perform and he being Willing to relinquish his Sallary of three Store pounds P Aunm granted by your Worspps as Governour of Bridewell.

Yor. Peticoner. therefore humbly prays yor. Worspps wil be pleased to grant that he may be admitted into the said office of Keeper of new Prison aforesaid.

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray etc.

Jeremy Boreman
