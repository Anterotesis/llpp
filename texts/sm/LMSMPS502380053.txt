To the Right Worshipfull the Gentlemen of the Bench of Justices at Hick's Hall

The Peticon of Elizbth: Harris

Sheweth That: your Petir: was bound over by Jane Oldman for words spoken in passion and has been close confin'd in the King's Bench Prison Southwark now fifteen weeks all Differences between the said Jane Oldman, and your Petitioner being made up.

Your Petir: humbly prayes that your Worships will be pleas'd to gives Order for the Discharge of her Recognizances.

And your Petir: as in duty bound shall ever pray Etc.
