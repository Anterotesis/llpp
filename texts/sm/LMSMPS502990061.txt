To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Midddlesex in their Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Hmble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwardends and Overseers of the poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the Same County

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands and Seales of Robbert Dennett and Richd. Ricards Esquires two of his Majesties Inst: ices of the peace for the Said County (one of them being of the Quorum) bearing date the [...] Eightteenth day of this Ins tant Aprill 1734, Ann Garrett, Was Removed from the Precinct of Saint Katherine in the said County to the said parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the Said County as the place of her Last Legall Settlement

Your Petitioners Think themselves Agreivd by the Judgment and the said [...] Order of the Said two Justices of the peace, And Appeale to this Court Against the Same, And Humbly Pray your Worships will be pleased to Appoint Some Day next Sessions to heare and Determine the same

And your Petrs. Shall Ever Pray
