To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their present General Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of th Poor of the Parish of St. George the Martyr in the said County

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of N Conant. and Philip Nave Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County Middlesex (One whereof is of the Quorum) bearing date the 1th. day of January instant. Sarah Price (Wife of James Price gone from her) and her Two Children namely Sarah aged Eighty years, and Mary aged Three Years and upwards were removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Pancras, in the County of Middlesex, to the said Parish of Saint George the Martyr as the place of their last legal Settlement.

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices, Appeal to your Worships against the same and humbly request that the Benefit of their said Appeal may be saved unto them until the next General Session of the Peace, to be holden for the said County and that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor, of the said Parish of Saint Pancras May be Ordered to attend to abide the Judgment and Determination of the Court touching the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioner Etc Arthur Gliddon Sollr. for the Applts.
