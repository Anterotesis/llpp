To The Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Quarter Session of the peace assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens. and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Giles without Cripplegate London

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Nicholas Spencer and Samuel Peirson two Of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date The Third Day of February 1764 Elizabeth Hunter Spinster was removed from the parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster To the said parish of Saint Giles Cripplegate in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order and humbly appeal against the same

Your petitioners therefore humbly pray that your Worships Will he pleased to appoint a Day of this present Session that all parties Concerned may Attend to hear And abide the Judgment & determinacon of this Court touching the said appeal

and your petrs Shall pray Etc

Thos. Stagg Sollr. for the appellants
