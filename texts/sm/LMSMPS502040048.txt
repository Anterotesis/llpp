To the Right worshipfull his Maties Justices of the Peace for the county of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions assembled.

The humble Peticon and appeal of Daniel Pratt of the Parish of st. Martin in the Fields in the Liberty of Westmr & County of Midd stationer.

Humbly Sheweth That by an Order or Judgment dates the First day of August last under the hands and seals of Leonard streate and William cowper Esqrs two of his Maties Justices of the peace for the said County of Middx and Liberty of Westmr your Peticoner is convicted for selling and exposing to sale two pamphlets or half sheets of paper the same not being stamped or Marked according to the from of the statue in that case made and provided and there upon the said Justice have adjudges that your petitioner hath forfeited the sum of £20, vizt. £10 for each Pamphlet or half sheet of paper so fold and exposed to sale as aforesaid, which said penalty of £20, is miligated to 6th, beside £3 for the Informed cosls and charges herein. That your Petitioner finds himself aggrieved by the said order or Judgment of the said two Justices and humbly appeals to this court against the same.

Your petitioner therefore humbly prays your Worps will be pleased to appoint a day this present Sessions for all persons concerned to attend this court to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of this Court upon and touching the said Appeal and that your peticoner may be relieved herein according to Law and Justice.

And your Peticoner shall pray Etc. Daniel Pratt
