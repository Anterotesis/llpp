The humble Peticonen of Archibald Clifford

Sheweth That yor: Peticonr: was Comited to the Goale of Newgate by Warrt: From yi Bateman Esqr bearing date the 22th: day of June last Charged by Eliza: Church for Marrying her, his Ferince wife Sarah being a live

That yor. Peticonr. is an infirme man and much Pr judiced in his heath by his long Confidmt:

Humbly prayeth that he may bee [...] tryed this Sessions for the Offence he stands accused off Or that he may bee admitted to Baile of the Act of Parliamt: in that behalfe directs
