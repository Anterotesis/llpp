To the Rt: Worsppful his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Midlx at their General Quarter Sessions assembled

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwarden & Overseers of the poor of the Hamblet of Mile end new Towne in the Sd: County

Sheweth

That by Order under the Hands & Seals of Robert Jackson & Robert Donnett Esqrs. two of by Majties: Justices of the peace for the Sd. County (Quor unc) bearing date the 8th: day of February last Rebecca the Wife of Henry Carter and lur three Children (Vizt:) Ellinor aged a tent five Years Henry aged about four Years & Rebecca aged about four N Louth the Lawful Iffne of the Sd. Henry Carter & Rebecca his Wife were removed from the Precinet of St: Katherine in the Sd: County to our Sd: Hamblet of Mile end were Towne as to the Issac of their last Legal Settlemt.

That yr: Petr: think themselves agriev'd by the Sd: Order & humbly Appeal to this Houble Coart agt: the same

That one of yr: Petrs: Evidences being Sick and thereby rendred incapable of appearing in this Court this present Sessions.

Yr. Petr. therefore most humbly Pray that the benefit of Appeal may be saved unto yr: Petrs. untill the next General Sessions of the peace to be held for ye: Sd: County

And yr: Petrs: Shall Ever Pray Etc:
