To the Rt Worsppll: her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition of John Hewitt Constable of Endfield in the sd County.

Sheweth That there was one Quarters Sallary due to your Petiticoner at Lady day Last amounting to the Sum of Fifteen Pounds for Passing and Relieving of Vagrants

That your Petitr: stands Indebted in Severall Sums of Money to her Majestie for recise And is in great want of the Said Money

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays your Worspps will be pleased to Order that he may be paid the said mony As unto yor: Worspps shall seeme meet

And Yor Petitr: shall ever pray Etc

John Howett
