To the Right Worshipfull His Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace holden for the said County at Hicks Hall assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter and Paul in the City of Bath in the County of Somerset

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox Esquire and Bartholomew Hammond Esquire Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex aforesaid (One whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Twenty Eighth Day of January One Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Four made for the Removal of Jeffery Hunt and Betty his Wife and their two Children William and Anne in the said Pass Warrant or Order mentioned from the Parish of Chelsea in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Peter and Paul as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners apprehend themselves aggrieved by the said Pass Warrant or Order and the Judgment of the said Two Justices and Do therefore humbly appeal to this Court against the same

ex

Your Petitioners Therefore humbly pray That this Court will be pleased to appoint some Day in the next General Quarter Sessions of the Peace to be held for this County to hear and determine the Matter touching their said Appeal

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc.

Sloper Sollr for the Appellts.
