To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Devaynes & John Collier Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County (one Whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 22d day of June 1799 Elizabeth Collins was removed & conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint James at Clerkenwell in the said County as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly appeal against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in present Session for the Hearing and Determining the said Appeal.

And Your Petitioners will pray Etc

Rhodes Cook & Handley Selrs.. for the Appelts }
