To the Worshipful William Mainwaring Esquire Chairman and the said of the Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace held for the said County in April Session 1753 assembled

The Humble Petition of Stephen Boult Jun of Staines in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was appointed one of the High Constables for the said County in the Hundred of Spelthorne at the Session of the Peace held for the said County in the Month of April Sess. 1782 and hath continued to serve the said Office [...]

That Your Petitioner hath annexed the Sumes of several Persons residing in the said Division who are fit and Proper Persons to serve the said Office

Your Petitioner therefore Humbly Prays Your Worships will make an order for the attendance of one of the said Persons to take upon him the said Office in the stead of Your Petitioner who humbly Prays to be discharged therefrom

And Your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever Pray Ect.

Michael Crew of Stanwell John Chandler of Laleham John King of Ashford George Winch of Shepperton William Corbett of Staines

Stepr Boult Junr.
