To the Right honorable Sr. William Ashurst Kt. Lord Major, and the rest of the honorable bench

Most humbly sheweth that whereas Jeane harris now a prisoner for fellony but haveing compownded with her adver sary purposes to have a bill of ignoramus throwen against her in order to acquit her, but your petitioner findeing her not before this day and not haveing time to pr. for a bill against her for Robing of her humbly begs yould be pleased to take it into consideration either to detaine her untill next sessions or else to hould her to sufficient baill that we may be satisfied wheare to find her or the baile, in to doeing youre petitioner will as in duty

for ever pray Whilst Jam Mary Jones
