To the Right Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex In their General Quarter Session Assembled

The Humble Petition of John Ditcher

Most Humbly Sheweth That your Petitioner having Been a Servant to your Worships In this Honble Court for the Space of Thirty years and afrwards In the Capacity of Bagg Bearer of the Records of this Court Humbly hopeing your Petr. hath Behaved himself In stand honest In his said Trust and Being now Advanced In years most Humbly hope As the Place of Keeper of the House of Correction at Clerkenwell In this County Being now Vacant By the Deathes Jones that your Petioner may Be Admitted Into the Said Office

June 25th: 1761

And your Petr. Shall Ever Pray John Ditcher
