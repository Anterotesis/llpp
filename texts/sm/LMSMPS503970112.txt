To the Rt. Honble. The Lord Mayor & others the Comds of Goal Delivery of Newgate for the County of Middx at the Session of Goal Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition of James Burt Comitted by the name of Benjamin otherwise Jas. Burt

Sheweth That your Petitioner stand comitted to his Majtes Goal of Newgate for being armed Wth. fire Arms & other Hensive Weapons in the runing Landling & carrying away uncustemed Goods & not surrending himself within forty day after the first sublicacon in the London Garette

Your Petitioner Most humbly Prays this Honble. Court that he May tryed bailed or discharged of the said offence according he the form of the [...]
