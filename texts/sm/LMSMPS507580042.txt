To His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace holden for the said County in the month of September 1782 assembled

The Humble Petition of John Boddington of Cow Cross Street in the parish of Saint Sepulchre in the County of Middlesex Baker

Sheweth That your Petitioner understanding that Mr Thomas Hurford High Constable of Finsbury Division wishes to be discharged from the said Office your Petitioner begs leave to after himself as a fit and proper person to succeed the said Thomas Hurford as Hugh Constable of the said Division and you Petitioner is induced to make this application from his knowledge of the Duties of the office having assisted Mr Hurford therein Ten Years now last past

That Your Petitioner having applied to the acting Magistrates of the said Division for their recommendation has subscribed the same to this Petition

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays your Worships to appoint him to the said Office

And Your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever Pray Etc

J Boddington.
