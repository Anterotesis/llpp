The Humble Petition of Francis Lutern Nailer

To be heard to more on morning upon notice to the officers

Middx ss: Ad General Quarterial Session pacis Dni Regis tent P Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St. John Street in Com prd. die [---] Martis Scilt due decimo die Januar anno regin Dui Georgy nunc Regis Magne Britannie Etc undecimo

It is ordered by this Court that the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell in this County doe attend this Court on Saturday the sixteenth day of January instant at eleven of the clock in the forenoon to heare & abide the Judgmt. & determinacon of this Court touching this peticon and appeale,

p Cur Walter
