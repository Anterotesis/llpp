To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Session of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Daniel Gach and James Fraser Esqrs. Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 11th. day of March 1747 Charles Mc Cartney was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Field within the Liberty of Westmr. in this County to the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the said Liberty and County as the place of his last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioner think themselves aggrieved and appeal to this Court against the Same

Therefore Humbly pray this Court will pleace appoint a time for hearing and determining the said Appeal and that all persons concerned may then accend.

And Your Petitioners Shall ever pray

S. Carry agent for St George Hannover Square
