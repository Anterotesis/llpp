To the Rt. Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Petition & Appeale of the Churchwds. & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the Said County.

Sheweth That by an Order Under the Hands & Seals of I Poulson & Ant, Wroth Esqrs, two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the Said County (one of them being of the Quorum). bearing date the Second day of March last Elizth. Lloyd Aged about four years, was Removed from the Parish of St. Sepulchers Middlesex to the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the [---] County. As the Place of her last Legall Settlemt.

Yor. Petrs. think themselves aggrieved by the Judgemt. of the Said two Justices of the Peace & Humbly pray yor. Worships will be pleased to Appoint a day this Sessions to hear & determine the Same.

And yor. Petrs. Shall ever pray Etc

Thos. Aneell Sollr. for the Appeants.
