To the Rt. Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx assembled in Generall Quarter Sessions.

The Humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Pish of St. Giles in the Fields in the Said County.

Sheweth

That by an Order under the Hands and Seales of Fealix Feast and John Fuller Esqrs. Two of His Maty's Justices of the Peace for the sd County (Quor unus) bearing date the 11th. day of September 1723. Eliza. Quartermain Widdow of William Quartermain was removed from the pish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the said County to the said Pish of St. Giles in the Fields as the Place of her last legall Settlemt.

Your Petrs. think themselves agrieved by the Judgemt. of the sd Two Justices, and Appeal to this Court and Humbly pray That the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poor of the said Pish of St. Giles without Cripple- -gate and all other Parties Concerned may be Ordered to attend this Cort. Some Day this Sessions to hear and abide the Judgemt. of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
