To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx Assembled in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Ealing in the said County

Sheweth That on or about the thirteenth day of Novemr. last as one Ann Derrick otherwise Ann Bromley Singlewoman was wandring and begging in the said parish she was Delivered of a Male Bastard Child there, whereby she & her said Child became Chargeable to the said Parish

That your Petrs. did detain the said Woman in Custody until the twenty Sixth day of Decemr last when they Conveyed her before King Gould Esqr one of It is Majesty's Justices of the peace for this County who Examined her and Committed her to the house of Correction at Clerkenwell Pursuant to the Statute in such Case made, where she now Remains

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to Order the Treasurer of this County to Pay Yor. Petrs. such a Sum of money as you shall adjudge a Reasonable Satisfaction for the Charges the said parish has been put unto on Accot. of the said Ann Derrick otherwise Bromley and her said Child

Dan Bolton Atty. for the Petrs.

And Yor. Petrs. shall ever pray Etc
