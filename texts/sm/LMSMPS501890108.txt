To the Rt. Worspfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County Of Middx:

The Humble Peticon of Henry Hodge one of the Constables of Southmins in this County.

Sheweth That at Michmas. last past there was due and owing unto Yor. Petr. the Sume of Twenty Pounds for halfe a years Salary for his Loss of time Charges and Expences in Passing and Conveying of Vagrants through this County

Yor. Petr. therefore most humbly prays this Honble. Court would be pleased to make such Order for paymt. of the Sd Sume Of Twenty Pounds unto Yor. Sd Petr. as to this Court shall Seem meet

And Yor. Petr. shall ever pray Etc Henry Hodge.
