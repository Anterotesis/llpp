To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall in the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden and Overseers, of the poor of the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of Westminster in the said County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a pass Warrant or Order under the hands and Seals of Stephen Clarke and Philip Dyet Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the second Day of May last Esther Orchard Wife of Thomas Orchard (gone from her) and their three Children Vizt. Esther aged four Years Elizabeth aged two Years and Sarah aged Six Months were removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County to the said Parish of Saint Anne as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Thomas Orchard and his said Wife and three Children Whereby your petitioner conceive themselves to be aggrieved.

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that Your Worships will please to appoint same Day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal And to Order that that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields and all persons concerned do then attend this Court.

And Your Petitr.: shall pray Etc

Charles Storey Churchwarden

Robt. Desbron Wm. Hall Thomas Key } Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Anne
