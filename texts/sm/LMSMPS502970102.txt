To his Majesty's Justices of the Peace now sitting at Hicks hall in St. John Street

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Churchwarden and Overseer of the poor of the hamlet of Milend Old Town[---] in the County of Middx.

Sheweth

That By and Order Dated the 11th. of Janry. ry: 1733. under the hands and Seals of Andrew Osborne and Ri. Farmer Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace for the said County (one being of the Quorum) Mary Mullet and her son named John aged about two Years were removed out of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappel in this County to the said hamlet of Milend old Town as the place of there last legal Settlement By which Order your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and appeale agt. the same to this Court,

And humbly pray your Worships will appoint a Day in the next General Quarter Sessions finally to hear and Determine the same

And Your Petrs. Shall Ever Pray Etc
