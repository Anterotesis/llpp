To the Right Worspl. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds

The humble Peticon of William Chambers of the parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in the sd. County

Sheweth

That yor. petr. being a penconer belonging to the sd. parish is by the Churchwardens and Overseers of the poore thereof allowed £18. P weeke towards the releife & mainteynance

That your petr. is a Lodger in the house of one Katherine Vales a poore Woman who has a great charge of Children to mainteyne soe that the sd. £18. P weeke will not pay for your Petr. Lodging & mainteynance And for as much as your Petr. being very aged & very Dim of Sight & altogether past his labour soe that he must Inevitably perish unless releived by this Honble Court and the sd. Churchwardens & Overseers. of the poore promiseing to pay the sd. Katherine Vales for Lodging your Petr. over & above the sd. Pencon of £18. P weeke but they now refuse soe to doe

P Tho: Nash.

yor. Petr. therefore most humbly Prayer yor Worspps to be pleased to Order that the sd. pencon may be Augmented as unto your Worspps shall Seem meet otherwise your Petr must in a Short time become destitute of any habitaton

And yor. Petr. Shall ever Pray Etc
