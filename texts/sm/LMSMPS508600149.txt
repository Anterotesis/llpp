To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Lambeth in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by an Order or pass Warrant under the hands and seals of Edward Read and Thomas Golden Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 13th. of November 1790 Charlotte Pullein aged about Eighteen Years of age of Single Woman was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Chiswick in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of St. Mary Lambeth in the County of Surry as the place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggreived

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day in this present Session to hear and determine their said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Carter & Simpson Sollicitors for the Appellants}
