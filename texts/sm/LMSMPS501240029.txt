To the right Worpl. her Majties. Justices of the peace for the County of Middx.

The humble petition of Grace Andrewes of the parish of St. James Clerkenwell widow.

humbly sheweth. That whereas the Petr. being rated to pay towards the reliefe of the poor of the said Parish, she being reduced to very meane and poor circumstances, cannot pay the Same, Soe that unlesse yor Worps. will please to discharge her from paying parish taxes, she in all likelyhood will be pained

The poor Petr. therefore humbly prayeth Yor. Worps. may be pleased to grant her such reliefe in the premisses at to yor. Wisdoms shall be thought fit

And the Petr shall ever pray Etc.
