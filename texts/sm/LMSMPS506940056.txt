To the Rt. Worspl. her Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Midds.

The humble Peticon of John Hatch Robert Beak and William Garraway

Sheweth That as of Last Year Your Petr. Hath served the Office of Constable for the Hamlett of Pinner in the Parish of Harrow On the [...] Hill in this County and that your Petr. Beak and Garraway Did for the same time serve the Office of Head burrough for the sd. Hamlett in the sd. Parish

That there being noe Court Leet held and kept this prsent Year for the Mannor of Harrow aforesd. Your Petrs. cannot be Discharged from their sd. Respective Offices

P Tho: Nash Yor. Petrs. therefore most humbly pray your Worspps to be pleased to Grant that they may be Discharged from their sd. Offices and that the Persons hereunder Named or some of them as yor. Worpps shall thinke fill may for the Year Ensueing serve the sd. Offices in theire Sleads

and yor. Petr. shall Ever pray Etc.

John Hatch Roberd beake William Ganaway

For Constable{ Robt. Hanburrow Sen Jno Clifford

For Headburrough{ Jos: Bodyman Tho: Lowton
