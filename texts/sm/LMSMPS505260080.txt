To the Worshipfull His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their General Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fellow in the County of Middx

Sheweth That at by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Benjamin Cox and G. Wrighte Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster one where of being of the Quorum bearing date the 17th. day of August last Katherine Wellwin ages Sixty years Wife of Andrew Wellwin of Living who is gone from he was Removed from the Parish of St. James within the Liberty of Westmr. in the County of Middx to the Said Parish of St. Martin in the fields as the place of her last Legal Settlement where by your Peter. apprehend themselves aggrieved and Appeal to you Worships against the said Order

Your Petr. therefore pray that your Worships wife be pleased to appoint some day in this prevent Sessions for the hearing and Determining the said Appeal

And your Petr. Shall pray etc

Wm Croft Sell. for Appt.
