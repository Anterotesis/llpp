To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of William Francis Sarah Holt and Simoon Betts of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the Said County

Sheweth That your Peticoners are rated morefor their Rents in the poor and Scavengers rates of the Said parish then they conceive they ought to be and Appeal agst. the same

And Pray they may be rated in the Said rates As to this Court Shall Seem Meet.

And Yor Petrs shall Ever pray Etc Wm Francis Sarah Holt Sims Betts
