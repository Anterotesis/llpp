To the Right Worshippfull his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth That by Warrant or Order Dated the Twelfth Day of February last under the Hands and Seals of Richard Browne and Jacob Harvey Esqrs: two of his Matys Justices of the peace for this County (Quor unus) Susanna Davis Daughter of John Davis was removed from the Parish of St. Mary Islington in this County to the aforesaid Parish of St. James Clerkenwell as the place of her last Legall Settlement

That Your Peticoners conceive themselves Aggrieved by the said Order of the said, Justices And appeal against the Same?

Yor. Petrs: therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to Appoint a Day this Sessions for all Partys to Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And Yor. Petrs Shall Ever pray Etc.
