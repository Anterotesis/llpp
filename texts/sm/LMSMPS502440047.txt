To the Right Worpll. his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London.

Sheweth That by Warrt. or Order under the hands & Seals of John Ellis & Christian Cole Esqrs two of his Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx & Libty of Westmr (Quor unus) bearing Date the fourth Day of May last past Ann Feerne Widdow of John Feerne was removed & conveyed from the Pish of St James in the Libty. of Westmr. to the Pish of St Andrew Undershaft in the City of London Alledging that to be the Place of her last legal Settlemt.

Yor Petrs thinking themselves aggreived by the Order of the sd two Justices humbly Appeal against the same And pray that yor Worps wou'd appoint some Day this Prsent Sessions for all parties concerned to attend to heare & abide the Judgmt. & Determinacon of this Court touching the sd Appeal

And yor Petrs as in Duty bound shall Pray Etc.
