To the Worshipful Wm. Mainwaring Esqr. Chairman and the Rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex at their general Session assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwarden & Overseer of the Poor of the Parish of Lewisham in the County of Kent.

Sheweth

That by a certain order of Removal or pass Warrant bearing date the 22d day of September now last past under the Hands and Seals of Paul Pechel Esqr & W. Heckford Esqr two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum Mary Russell the Widow of William Russel late of the Hamlet of Sydenham in the Parish of Lewisham in the said County of Kent deced was Removed from and out of the Parish of Twickenham in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Lewisham in the said County of Kent as the Place of her legal Settlement whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved and appeal therefrom

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray your Worships that a Day may be appointed for the Hearing and determining of their said Appeal and that your Worship will make Order therein as to Justice appertains

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.
