To the Right Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Brides in the City of London

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Sir Robert Darling Knight and Christr. Scott Esquire tow of hs Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Seventh Day of November Elizabeth Framand Singlewoman was Conveyed from the parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex to the said parish of Saint Brides London as the Place of her Legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves Aggreived And Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly Pray this Court will please to Appoint a Time for Bearing and Determining the said Appeal And that all Persons Concerned may then Attend.

A Bradley Sollicitor for the Appellants}
