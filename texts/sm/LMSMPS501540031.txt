To the Rt Worsppll his Matys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The humble Petition and Appeal of William Allison of the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County.

Sheweth That your Peticoner is rated in the Poors rate of the said Parish for the House he Dwells in after the yearly rent of Seaven pounds Whereas your Petitioner payes noe more then four Pounds of Annn rent As appeares by Receipts Wherefore he Appeals agst the same

Your Petitr: therefore humbly prays such Relief in the premises As to this Court shall be thought Meete

And Yor petr Shall Ever pray etc. Wm Allison
