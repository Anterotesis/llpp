To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Quarter Session of the Peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Sant Dunstan in the West in the City of London

Sheweth. That by virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals or William Blomier and Richard Clerk Esquires Two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one Whereof being of the Quorum) hearing date the Eleventh day of December 1797. Hannah Allen Wife of James Allen was removed and conveyed from and out of the Parish of saint Andrew Holborn which lies above the Bars in the County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Dunstan in the West in the City of London as the place of her last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Allen Wife of James Allen.

That your Petitioners Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and pray this Court that the Benefit of the said appeal be saved unto them until the next General Sessions of the Peace to be holden for the said County of Middlesex and that they and all Persons concerned may then attend to hear and abide the Judgment and determination this court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petition will ever Pray.
