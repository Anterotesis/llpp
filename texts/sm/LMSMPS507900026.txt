To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at the Sessions House on Clerkenwell Green in the said County assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Westminster in the said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order under the Hands and Seals of Rt. Taylor and James Croft Two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the 25th. day of November last Charles Maillard aged upwards of Forty Years was removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County to the said Parish of Saint James Westminster as the place of his last legal Settlement whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therfore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and their said Order and pray that your Worships will he pleased to appoint a Day to hear and determine the said Appeal

And Your Petitioners will ever pray

Jas: Dyson Solicitor for the said Appellants
