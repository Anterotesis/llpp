To the Worshipfull Justes Fuller

Whose as your Worships poor petitioner & is oath miller who was Commited Six years worship Humbly Griplors years & Worships Royall good mas Pd two grand me my discharge Seeing of Have Commited no Crinil afircus to any parson for duy then gusing my Bread in me Honest way: then for Honored St & your Worships poor patitinor Humbly Impilars your Royall goodnes to discharge your petitinor nor Esqr St will Be my Ruin for & never was many such place Bufer: an in so doing & your Worshipp poor petitinor shall for Ever Be Barish in Duty

Elizabeth Miller
