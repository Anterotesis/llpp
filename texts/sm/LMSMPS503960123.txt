To the Right Honble St William Calvert Knight Lord Mayor of the City of London And the rest of the Honble the Justices of Gaol Delivery in their General Session of Gaol Delivery Assembled

The Humble Petition of Benjamin Lander

Sheweth That Your Petitioner was Committed to Newgate on Sunday 2d July Instant for being Concerned in a Riot in the Strand Contrary to the Statute in that Cate made and Provided

Your Petitioner therefore humbly prays That at this present Session of Gaol Delivery of Newgate he may be tryed Bailed or Discharged Pursuant to the Statute in that Case made and provided

And Your Petitioner shall pray Etc
