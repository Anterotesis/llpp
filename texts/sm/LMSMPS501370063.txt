To the Right Worpll: Her Maties: Justices of the peace for the County of Middx assembled in their Genall Quarter Sessions

The Humble Peticon of William Davis

Sheweth That your Peticonr: rents a house of five pounds rent P ann in Virginia street in the parish of St. Dunstan Stepney in this County & has hither to paid all pish Taxes in the said Parish

That yor. Peticonr: is now in a very poor Condicon & not in ability to pay the Said Taxes any longer & therefore prays yor. Worps: well be pleased to give him Such Releife as to your great Wisdoms Shall seem meet

And yor. Petr. as in Duty bound shall ever pray Etc Wm: Davies
