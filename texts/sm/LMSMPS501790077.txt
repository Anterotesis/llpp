To The Right Worship full his Maties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Churchwarden & overseers of the Poore of the Parish of St Peter in the Downe & County of Redford

Sheweth That one Mary Watts the Wife of Thomas Watts was by Warrant under the Hands and Seales of Gustavus Sheldon and Alexander Hardine Esqrs two of his makes Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middx Quor unus dated the third day of June Anno Dui 1719 Conveyed from the Parish of St Margrett Westmr in the Said County of Middx so the said Parish of St Peter as the place of the last Legall Settlemt of the said Mary Watts Against which they humbly Appeale to this Court

And humbly pray such Releife in [...] Promisses as to your worshipps shall Seeme meet

And your Peticonr shall ever pray Etc
