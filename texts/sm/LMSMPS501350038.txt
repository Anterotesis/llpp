To the Rt: Worppll: Her Maties: Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx.

The Humble Peticon of Margarett Hill of the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in yr. said County Widdow.

Sheweth. That Your Peticoner Rents a house of Five Pounds P Anns. [...] and noe more and is Rated to the poores Rate of the said Parish an half penny & Weeke (besides all Overrates)

That by reason of her Age being neare Seaventy and living in a poore Neighbourhood whereby she hath sustained great Losses in her Trade She is soe reduced that she is altogeather become uncapable of paying the said Rate

Therefore Your Peticoner most humbly prays yours Worpps that you will be pleased to take the premisses into Consideracon and [...] discharge her from paying the said Rate.

And your Peticoner As in Duty bound Shall Ever pray Etc.
