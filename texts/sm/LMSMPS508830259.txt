The Humble Petitioner Joseph Hayward, Declares that he has Lost the under Named things, in the Late Fire in the Qaukers Workhouse Clerkenwell, with Thankfully receive the Smallest donation, of any Ladie or Gentleman to relieve him in this Great Want.

Viz a Stove Grate Tendor Tongs and poker three Chairs three Candlesticks a Copper Saucepan three Shirts two Neckcloths three pair of Stockings two Towels Sunday Earthonware a Pair of Glass Salts Snd Different other Articles
