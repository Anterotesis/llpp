To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for County of Middlesex in their Generall Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mary Guildford in the County of Surry

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant dated the Tenth Day of August last under the Hand and Seal of Thomas Lediard Esquire one of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the City of Westminister in the County of Middlesex William Hackman therein said to be a Rogue and Vagabond with Mary his Wife were Past as Vagrants from the Parish of Saint Margaret in the said City of Westminister to the said Parish of Saint Mary Guildford as the Place of their last legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners apprehending themselves aggreived by the said Pass humbly appeal to this Court against the same And Pray that the benefit of the said Appeal may be saved to them and that the Hearing and determining thereof may be adjourned to the next Generall Quarter Session of the Peace to be held for the said County of Middlesex.

And your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc.

Davd: Waterfeild Attorney for the Appellants
