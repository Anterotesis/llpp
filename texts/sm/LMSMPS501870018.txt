To the Honble yor Bench of his Majties Justices of the peace at Hicks Hall

The humble Petision & Appeal of Sr John mordaunt of the parrish of St James Clarken well

Humble Sheweth

That yor: Pettnr: hath Been Assest & time [...] a year for yr Release of yr poor of the sd sd parrish for stock of monys all which Little I haveJs In yr Goverments handr I have att most Lost half yr p presetts of Wt by yr falling of yr [...] Therefore Pray you will be pleased To Take my hard Case Into yor Considerations

That yor Pettnr: May Be Released of that tat and yo Pettr: shall Ever Acknowledge Ma Favour
