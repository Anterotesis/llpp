To the Rt: Worppll. his Majties: Justices of the Peace for the [...] of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions assembled

The humble peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers for the poore of the parish of St. Bridgett als Brides London

Sheweth That by Warrant under the hands and Seales of John Fuller and Alex Ward Esqrs two of his Majties. Justices of the peace for the said County (quor unus) bearing date the fifth day of February 1719. Mary Cock wife of Robert Cock with Nathaniel about one yeare and an halfe old and Mary about three yeares and an halfe old the Children of the sd. Robt. Cock and Mary his wife were past on yor. Petrs. from the parish of St. Giles without Cripplegate in the sd. County as the place of their last Legall Settlement

That yor. Petrs. apprehend themselfes aggreived by the order of the sd. two Justices and humbly appeale to yor. Worpps therefrom

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray Yor. Worpps. to appoint a day to heare and determine the sd. Appeale

And yor. Petrs. shall pray Etc.
