To the Worll. his Maties Justices of the Peace of and for the County of Middx in Qr. Sessions Assembled.

The humble Peticon and Appeal of the Church Wardens. and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster in the Said County.

Sheweth That by Virtue of Pass warrant under the hands and Seales of John Metcalfe and A: Ward Esquires Justices of the Peace of and for the Said County of Middx bearing Date the 30th. day of May last James Beck aged Nine Years Richard Beck aged Six Years and Roger Beck aged three Years the Children of Richard Beck were removed from and out of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn above Barrs in the County aforesaid to the said [...] parish of St. Margaret Westmr. as the place of their Lawfull Settlemt.

That Your Petr. thinking themselves agrieved by the Judgment of the said Two Justices humbly appeal to your Wospl. against the Same.

Therefore humbly prays your Worships would be pleasd to Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Said Parish of Saint Andrews Holborn above barrs in this County may attend this Court Someday this Quarter Sessions to hear and abide the Judgment and Final Determination of this Court touching this Appeal.

And Yr. Petrs. Shall ever pray & Etc.
