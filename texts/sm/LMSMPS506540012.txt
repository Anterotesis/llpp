To the Worshipful His Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace at Hick's Hall in Saint John Street in and for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Thomas Joyce of The Parish of Saint Luke in the County of Middx Chandler

Sheweth

That Your Petitioner was on the 21st. day of [---] March last Convicted before John Sherwood Esquire one of His Majesty's Justices of the peace for the Said County for unlawfully Selling four Leaves of Wheaten Bread not marked with a Roman Wand was adjudged to pay and for feit for the same the Sum of Eight pounds

Your Petitioner there fore humbly Appeals to this Court against the said Convictions and prays that this Court will be pleased to Order that All Persons concerned do attend this Court some day in this present Session of the peace for the said County of Middlesex to hear and abide the Judgement and determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal And that Your Petitioner may be otherwise Relieved in the Premisses as the said Court Shall see fit

And Your Petitioner shall ever pray Etc

J: Jenings Sollicitor for the said Thomas Joyce
