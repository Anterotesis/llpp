To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of St. Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of John Staples Esqrs. and Robt. Batson Esqr. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex bearing date the Eighth day of August last, Briggs Wickett and Sarah his Wife and their Two Children lawful issue (Namely) Elizabeth aged about Ten Years and Mary aged about Eight Years were removed from the Parish of St. John of Wapping in the County of Middlesex to the said Precinct of Saint Catherine as the place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Warrant or Order Do humbly appeal against the same and pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day in this present Sessions, that all Parties concerned may attend to hear & abide the Judgment and Determination of this Court touching the said Appeal

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
