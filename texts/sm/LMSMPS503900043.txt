To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Exton in the County of Bucks

Sheweth That by vertue of a Pass Warrant or Order Under the hands and Seals of James Clitherow and John Awsiter Esqrs. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlesex (one where of being of the. Quorum) bearing date the twelfth day of October last past John Edwards Ann his Wife Ann his Daughter aged five Years & John his Son aged about Two Years were Removed & Conveyed from & out of the Precinct of Norwood in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Eaton in the said County of Bucks as the place of their last legal Settlement

The Petitioners think themselves aggreived by the said Order of Removal & therefore humbly Appeal to this Court against the Same & pray Your Worships to appoint a day for the hearing and determining the matters thereof And to grant the Petitioner. such relief in the premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet

And the Petitioners will ever pray Etc.

G: Talmash Agent for the Appellants
