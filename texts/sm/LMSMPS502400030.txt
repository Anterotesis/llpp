To the Right Worshipfull his Majties: Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The humble Petition of Millett Yeatman and Henry Porter Present Surveyors of the Highways of the Parish of Chelsea in the said County.

Sheweth That by order made the 7 day of January last by Henry Barker Esqr: Sr. Richard Gough Knt; Reginald Marriott I Wood and George Howard Esqrs: Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quorum Unus) att a Sessions or meeting of the said Justices for the Highwayes of the sd: parish It is expressed that the said Justices did peruse the accounts of Henry Anderson and Thomas Herbert late Surveyors of the Highwayes in [...] the sd: Parish, and found the Since of twenty eight pounds & Eleaven shillings and a halfe penny due from the sd. late Surveyors to the sd: Parish.

Hen: Denton Soltr: } Your said Petrs. most humbly pray an order of this Worshipfull Bench that the said Sume of twenty eight pounds eleaven shillings and a halfe penny may be forthwith paid to your said Petrs: And your Petrs: shall pray Etca:

Millet Yeatman Henry Porter
