[...] Honble. Houblon [...] Major of [...] and the Rt [...] Honble. the Pd. Chiefe Justice and the rent of the [...] at Justice Hall in ye sd County

The humble Peticon of Patrick Hughes

Humbly Sheweth

That your Petr. stands Committed in Newgate for High Treason Since the 30th. day of Septembr. last

May it therefore please yor. Lpps to order that your Petrs. Prayer may be Entred in this Court, that your Petr. may have the Benefitt of the Act for the Liberty of the Subject

And yor. Peter. as in duly bound Shall ever pray Etc
