To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Gene- -ral Session of the Peace assembled

The humble Petition and appeal of the Church -Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St George Bloomsbury in the said County

Sheweth That by virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Cadwallader Coker and Benjamin Cowley Esquires two of His Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixteenth day of April last Thomas Dook was removed and conveyed from and cut of the Parish of St. Luke in the said County to the said Parish of St George Bloomsbury as the Place of his last legal Settlement

Your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved by the Adjudication of the said two Justices thereof humbly appeal to this Court against the same And Pray your Worships will appoint a day in this present Session for the hearing and determining the said Appeal

And your Petr Shall pray Etc Rd Russall Wm Plughes } Church Wardens

Tho Halford Geo Sough for } overseers of the Poor
