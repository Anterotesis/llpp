To the Worshipful his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx in their General Session Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Overseers of the Poor of the Precinct of Saint Catherine in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by virtue of a Warrant or Order Under the hands and Seals of Ebenezer Mussell Esqr. and Thomas Quarill Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx bearing Date the Third Day of May instant Mary Coiffea Wido. (in the Said order Name Elizabeth Coiffea) and her three Children vic John aged Near Six Years Abraham ages upwards of four Years and Mary near Three years were removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Matthew Bethnal Green in the said County into the said precinct of St Catherine as the place of their last legal Settlement

Your Petrs. Conceiving themselves aggrieved by the said Order Humbly appeal to this Court against the same and humbly pray that your Worships will be pleased to appoint a Day for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal.

And Your Petrs. shall ever pray Etc

Clare Sollr for the Appellants.
