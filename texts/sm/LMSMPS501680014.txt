To the Honourabell Bench of Justisis Now Seting att hixis hall the Humbell pettishon of Elizbeth waren

Humlly Shweth That your pettishoner being a very poor woman and haveing nothing to Subsist Shee and her Child with but by her one in Endever and being nor above Fivtty years old and att most Reduce to poverty by these Spitfull Enadces who as Endevring to bring us to be Charabell to the parsh

This is therfore humbly to imptour your great Wsdoms and goodness to consider my poverty and I live my self to your Justis and good and Conctines Shall think that and and your pettishner shall ever pray
