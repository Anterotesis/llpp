To the Worshipful sir John Hawkins Knt. and the rest of the Justices there

The Humble Petition of Samuel Bolton

Sheweth That your Petitioner is new Confined in New Prison Clerkenwell for Healing a Handkerchief on monday the Nineteenth Instant for which your Petitoner is found guilty of the totally Innocent of the Crime laid to his Charge

That your Petitioner being so found Guilty and having an amount mother whose main [...] depends on the Labour and Industry of your Petitioner

Your Petitioner therefore most Humbly Prays your Worships to take his most unfortunate case into consideration and hope your worships will be pleased to grant in such Relief as in your great Wisdoms shull seem meet

And your Petr. shall ever pray Etc
