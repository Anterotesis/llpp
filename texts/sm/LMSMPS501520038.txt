To The honble: The Bench of Justices at Hix's hall

The Humble peticon of Thomas Lawrence

And please yor honors

Tho: Laurence and Horoard being Bayle for ye Appearance of Tho: Youde, and appeard for him before this honble: Bench, the former Sessions, (and at that time respitted to this psent, Sessions) humbly prays that yor: honors: will be pleas'd to Discharge them The sd Tho: Youde now being a Prisoner, for Debt, and at this Prsent: in the Kings Bench

Yor: honors: most humble and obedit: Servant Thomas Lawrence
