To the Right Worshipfull his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Sessions of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael att Quern in the City of London

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seales of two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace of the said County of Middx (One whereof being of the Quorum) Dated the Eighteenth day of July last pash Margerett Sutton and John her Son were passed from the Parish of Saint Luke in the Said County of Middx to the Said Parish of Saint Michael att Quern in the City of London as the place of their last legall Settlements

Whereby your Petitioners humbly conceive themselves Aggreived by the Said Order or Pass Warrants and Judgements of the said two Justices Therefore humbly Appeale to this Court for Releife in the Premisses and Pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint a Day for the hearing and determining the Same And your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
