To the Rt. Worshipfull her Majties Justices of the peace for the County of Middx:

The humble peticonr & Appeal of the Chappel-Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the Town of New: Brentford in the parish of Hanwell in this County.

Sheweth That by an ordr. dated the 30th. day of Novembr last past undr. the hands and Seals of Thomas Cullum and Rd. Merewether Esqr two of her Majties. Justices of the peace for this County; Richard and Sarah Yates, Children of Thomas Yates, were removed from the parish of Ealing in this County to the Town of Brentford in the parish of Hanwill aforesd, as the place of then last legall settlement

That yor. petr. conceiveing themselves Aggrieved by the Judgement of the sd tato Justices doe humbly Appeale to yor Worshipps thereforem

Yor. Petrs. therefore humbly pray yor. Worships will be pleased to appoint a day this prsent Sessions that the Church warden & Overseers of the poor of the parish of Ealing may attend this Court to heard and abide the Judgemt & determinacon thereof touching this Appeale,

And yor. Petrs Shall pray Etc.

Chris: Clithero H Dinnok} Chapple wardens

John Payne Alex: Haynes} Overseers of the poore
