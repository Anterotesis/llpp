To the Right Worshipfull His, Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx in their Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Warden & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by a Pass Warrant or Order under the hands & Seals of Thomas Bailton and Gideon Harvey Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx bearing date the 22 day of June last John Watson Mary his Wife & their three Children Vizt Grace aged Eight Years Angel aged five years & Mary aged about 16 months were Removed from the Parish of St. Margarets Westmr in the County aforesaid to the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square as the place of their last legall Settlement whereby your Petitioners think them selves agrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore humbly Prays this Court will Please to Appoint a time for hearing the Sd. Appeal and that all psons concerned may attend at the same time

And Your Petitioners Shall ever Pray Etc
