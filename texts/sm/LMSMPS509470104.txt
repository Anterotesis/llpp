To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace fort the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Olave in Hart Street in the City of London.

Sheweth

That by Virtue of an Order of Removal or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Joseph Merceron and Daniel Williams Esquires two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Sixth day of November 1797. Hannah Laybank Widow was Removed and Conveyed from the Parish of Saint Mathew Bethnal Green in the County of Middlesex and delivered to your Petitioners being the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Saint Olave in Hart Street the(the said Justices adjudging that the place of the last legal Settlement of the said Hannah Laybank was in the said Parish of Saint Olave in Hart Street in the said City of London by which said order of Removal or Pass Warrant Yor Petitioner apprehend themselves to be aggrieved.

Wm. Chetham Sobr. for the Appls taken Square} Your Petitioners therefore humbly Appeal against the said Order or Removal or Pass: Warrant and Pray such Relief in the Premises as to Your Worships shall seem meet and that your Worships will be Pleased to appoint a day in this Present Session for the hearing and Determination thereof.
