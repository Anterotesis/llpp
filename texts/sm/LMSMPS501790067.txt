To the Worshippfull his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlx at their Genrall Quarter Sessions of the Peace held for the said County

The humble Petition and Appeale of the Church -wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the parish of St John of Wapping in the said County of Middx

Sheweth To your Worshipps that by an Order under the hands and seales of Robert Dennett and Samuel Perry Esqrs. Two of his Majties. Justices of the Peace for the said County of Middlx (Quorum unus) bearing date the 14th. day of April 1719 whereby Dorothy Holland the Widdow and Relict of Charles Holland Deced was removed from and out of the Precinct of St. Katharines in the said County to the said parish of St John of Wapping as the Place of her last legall Setlement in right of her said late Husband, whereby your Petitionrs. and Appellants humbly conceive themselves aggreived and apply to this Court for releife

Bass Therefore humbly pray your Worshipps will be pleased to appoint some day this Present Quarter Sessions, for the Overseers of the Poor of the said Precinct of St Katharines and all Parties concerned to attend this Court to hear and abide the Determination and Judgement thereof, touching the sd. Appeal

And your Petitionrs & Appellants as in Duty bound shall pray Etc
