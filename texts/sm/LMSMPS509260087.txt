To the Worshipful His Majesty's Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex in General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County Assembled.

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Joseph Atkinson of the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the County of Middlesex Gentleman

Sheweth That by a certain Conviction under the Hand and Seal of Aaron Graham Esquire One of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex bearing date the twenty first day of May One Thousand Seven hundred and Ninety Six made on the Information of one Theophilus Bell is of the Parish of Saint Pancras in the said County of Middlesex Gentleman Your Petitioner was convicted as therein alledged for that your Petitioner on the fifteenth day of April One Thousand seven hundred and Ninety six in the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden in the said County of Middlesex did set up maintain and keep a certain Frandulent Game to be determined by the Chance of Dice under the Denomination of the Game of Hazard against the form of the Statute in such Case made and provided and the said Justice did adjudge Year Petitioner to forfeit for the Offence supposed to be Committee by your Petitioner the Sum of Two Hundred Pounds

That your Petitioner thinks himself aggrieved the Judgment of the said Justice and therefore

Wm. Chetham Solr. for the Appellant} Falcon Square Your Petitioner doth Appeal to your Worships at this present General Quarter Session of the Peace against the Judgment of the said Justice and doth Pray such Relief in the Premisses as to your Worships shall seem meet;
