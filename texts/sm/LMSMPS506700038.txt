To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at the General Sessions of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint John of Wapping

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of Philip Dyot Esquire and Stephen Clarke Esquire two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the 29th. day of January 1777 Lunareah Green Widow of Edward Green deceased and her female Child by her said late Husband (Vizt.) Elizabeth aged one year and upwards were removed from the Parish of Saint Giles in the Fields in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping as the place of their last legal Settlement Whereby your Petitioners think themselves aggrieved and Appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioners therefore humbly pray this Court will be pleased to appoint a Day in the next Quarter Sessions for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal and that all Persons concerned may then attend

And your Petitioners will ever pray Etc

Robt Tudman Sollr. for the said Parish of Saint John of Wapping
