To this worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in the General Session of the Peace assembled.

The humble Petition of the Churchwarden and Overseers of the Peace of the Parish of Saint John the Baptist in the City of Herford

Sheweth That by a certain Order in writing luarcing dark the 26th Day of August last under the Hands and Seals John Staples Esquires and Herbert Mays Doctor in Divinity two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex one whereof being of the Quorum William Brumson was ordered to be removed and conveyed from the Parish of Saint Anne in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint John the Baptist in the said City of Hereford the said Justices adjudging the said last mentioned Parish to be the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said William Brumter whereby your Petitioner conceive themselves aggrieved

Your Petitioner therefore humbly appeal against the said Order and Pray that the Hearing and determining of their said Appeal may be adjourned unto the next General Quarter Session of the Peace to the holden for the said County of Middlesex

And your Petitioner will ever Pray Etc.
