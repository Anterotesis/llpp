To the Right Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Mathew Friday Street London

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) dated the Eleventh day of February Instant Charlotte Smith aged about Nine years John Smith aged about seven Years and George Smith aged about fourteen Months Childrens of George Smith were removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Aldersgate in the liberty of Glass House Yard in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Matthew Friday Street as the Place of their last legal Settlement

That your Petitioners conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order and Judgment of the said two Justices And therefore humbly Appeal to this Honble Court against the same And

Pray that the Benefit of their Appeal may be saved unto your Petitioners and that the Hearing and Determination thereof may be adjourned unto the next Quarter Session of the Peace to be holden for the said County

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc

Robt: Holder Sol
