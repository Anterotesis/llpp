To the Right Worshippfull his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in their Generall Quarter Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of Robert Negus of Brewhouse Lane Cooper

Sheweth That by an Order of Adjudication under the Hands and Seals of Robert Dennett and Clifford William Phillips Esqr. Two of his Majesties Justices of the Peace for the said County (One being of the Quorum) bearing date the Fifteenth day of May last your Petr is Adjudged the Putative father of a Female Bastard Child born of the Body of Hannah the wife of William Canterbury in the Parish of Saint George in the said County And that your Petitioner was served with a Copy of the Order of Adjudication of the said [---] Two Justices but on Friday last being the Fifth day of July Instant and your Petr. Conceiving himself Aggreived by the Order and Judgement of the said Two Justices humbly Appeal against the same

And prays that as Order may be made for the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. George in the said County (being the Parish wherein the said Hannah Canterbury was delivered of the said Female Bastard Child) and all other Persons Concerned to Attend this Court on a certain day this present Quarter Sessions or the Adjournment thereof to hear and Abide the Judgement and determination of this Court touching this Appeal

And yor. Petr. shall ever pray.

Robt. Negus
