To The Worshipfull His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County Middlesex in their General Quarter Session of the Peace for the said County assambled

The Humble Petition and appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Peter in the Town of Derby

Sheweth

That by an Order unde the Hand and Seals of N. Conant and J. Barnfather Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace in and for the said County and where of being of the Quorum being Date the 24th. Days of June 1798 St Mary Sandwell Susanna Wigley Widow of James Wigley was removed and conbeyed from the Parish of Saint Mary le Bone in the said County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Peter in the Town of Derby as the Place of her last legal Settlement whereby your Petitioner think themselves aggriesed and appeal to this Court against the same

Your Petitioner there fore humbly Pray that this County will please it appoint a Time for hearing and determining the said appealed and that all persons coni [...] may then attend

And your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc
