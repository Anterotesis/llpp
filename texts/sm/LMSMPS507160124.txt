Middlesex to wit} Petition on Behalf of the Parish of Saint Michael Com Hill London against Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their General Session of the Peace at Hicks's Hall Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint Michael Com Hill London

Sheweth That by an Order of Removal under the Hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Vizt. D Wilmot and Jasper Clarke Esquires and dated the Ninth day of July last Judith Petten Singlewoman and her two Bastard Children Namely Catherine aged four years and upwards and William aged fourteen Months and upwards were Removed from the Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County of Middlesex to the Parish of Saint Michael Com Hill aforesaid as the Place of the last legal Settlement of the said Judith Potten by which your Petitioners think themselves agrieved

Your Petitioners therefore most humbly pray your Worships that they may have Liberty to Appeal to the said Order of Removal and that the Court would be pleased to appoint a Short Day for hearing the same and your Petitioners shall ever pray Etc.

James Morgan [...] for the Appellants
