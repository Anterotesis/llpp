To the Rt Worspp his Matys Justices of the peace for the County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Phillip Rowsam of the Parish of St. Mary Islington in the sd County

Sheweth

That yor petr was allowed a penson of Six Shillings P Month but they have reduced the same to Four Shillings P Month Which yor pert by reason of his old Age is not able to Subsuston

Yor Petr therefore humbly prays such An Augment was thereof As to yor Worspps shall seem Meet

And Yor petr Shall ever pray Pl: Rowsam
