To the Worshipful his Majesty's Justices of the Peace in and for the County of Middlesex in their General Session of the peace Assembled

The Humble Petition of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the County of Middlesex

Sheweth That by an Order under the Hands and Seals of John Spiller and Edward Read Esquires Two of his Majesty's Justices of the peace in and for the County of Middlesex (one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the Fourteenth Day of April last John Porter was removed and Conveyed from and out of the Parish of Saint Luke Chelsea in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of Saint Leonard Shoreditch in the said County as the place of the last legal settlement of the said John Porter whereby your petitioners Conceive themselves to be aggrieved

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal against the said order, and humbly pray relief in the premises and that your worships with be pleased to appoint some Day in this present Session for the hearing and Determining this thier said Appeal.

And your petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

Jno. Scarr [...] Solr. for Appellts Hoxton Town
