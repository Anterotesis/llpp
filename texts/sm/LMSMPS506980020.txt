To the Right Worsph her Matys Justices of the Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticonr of Lawrence [...] son at the Parish of St James Clerkenwell in the said County

Sheweth

That yor Petitr rated Six Pence P Month towards the Relief of the Poor of the said Parish But forasmuch as yor Petitr being a Poor Man & having no other Dependency for ye Mainteynance of himselfe and family then only being in Journeyman Taylor And having never before been Rated towards the Relief of the sd Poor [...] yor Petr. being Aged Fifty Five yeares is not able to Pay ye said Rate

P Tho: Nash Your Petitr therefore most humbly Prays yor Worships to be pleasd to granty she may he Discharged from the said Rate

And yor. Petr shall ever pray Etc
