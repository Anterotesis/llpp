poors rate

refr

Middx ss: Ad General Quartial Session pacis Dui Regis tent pro Com Middx apnd Hickshall in St John Street in Com prd die Lune scilt Secundo die July Anno regin Din Georgy mmc Regis Magne Britannie Etc Secundo

It is Ordered by this Court that it be, And it is hereby Recomended unto

Esqrs Justices of the peace for the said County or any two of them to Sumon before them the Churchwardens & Overseers of the poor of the in this County, and to Examin into the truth of the Petrs complaint and to certify their Opinions touching the prmisses within menconed unto this Court or to his Maties Justices of the peace at the next Generall Quarter Sessions of the peace to be held for the said County.
