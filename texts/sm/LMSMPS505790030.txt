Middlesex (to wit) To the Worshipsfull John Hawkins Esqr and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at their Quarter Sessions of the Peace held at Hicks's Hall in and for the said County

The Humble Petition of Francis Fox son of John Fox of Hanover Street Long Acre in the County aforesaid Staymake

Sheweth That your Petitioner in and by certain Indentures of Apprenticeship bearing date on or about the sixteenth day of March in the year of our Lord One thousand seven hundred and sixty four was bound Apprentice to Richard Morphew of Maiden Lane near Covent Garden Jeweller to learn his Art or Trade and with him after the manner of an Apprentice to serve from the day of the date of the said Indenture to the full end and Term of seven years and by the said Indre the said Richard Morphew in [---] consideration of the sum of thirty five pounds to him paid did not only Covenant to teach your Petitioner the said Art or Trade of Jeweller but also to find and provide for your Petitioner sufficient Meat drink Washing and Lodging for and during the said Tenn

That your Petitioner entered into the service of the said Richard Morphew and faithfully serv'd him as his Apprentice under the said Indenture untill on or about the end of November or begining of December last past when the said Richard Morphew was Arrested and soon afterwards moved himself by Habeas Corpus into his Majesties Prison of the Fleet and doth now remain a Prisoner in the Custody of the Warden of the said Prison charged with three Actions only issued out of the Marshalsea Court and the total sum swore to in those three Actions amount to no more than twenty three Pounds seven Shillings and six pence

That Your Petitioner continued in the faith full service and discharge of his duly as an Apprentice with the said Richard Morphew untill the said Richard Morphew was Arrested and went to Prison as aforesaid when your Petitioner was obliged to go home to his Parents and is in manifest danger of receiving no benefit or advantage from the said Indenture to follow the said business or have any Part of the consideration money returnd.
