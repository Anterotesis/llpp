To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session of the Peace at Hicks Hall Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parishes of St. Margaret and St. John the Evangelist in the City of Westminster in the County aforesaid.

Sheweth That by Virtue of a Pass Warrant or Order under the Hands and Seals of Thomas Balack and Thomas Kynaston Esqrs. two of His Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City and Liberty of Westminster (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the 14th day of November last, John Daniel Robinson was removed from the Parish of St. Martin in the Feilds in the Liberty of Westminster to the said Parish of St [---] John the Evangelist as the place of his last legal Settlement, Whereby Your Petitioners conceive themselves to be Aggrieved.

Your Petitioners therefore Appeal to this Court against the Adjudication of the said two Justices and humbly Pray that your Worships will be pleased to Appoint some day in the present Sessions for the hearing and determining of the said Appeal And to Order that all Persons concerned may then Attend.

And Your Petits. shall pray Etc.

Thos. Monk} Churchwarden

Willm. Ever Hum Hughs} Overseers of the Poor
