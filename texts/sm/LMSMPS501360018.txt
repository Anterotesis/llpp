To the Rt: Worshipfull Her Majesties Justices of the Peace now sitting at Hicke's Hall in ye County of Middlesex.

The Humble Petition of David Thompson Soldier

Sheweth That Upon the Complaint of Katherine Walsh yor. Petr. was bound over by Mr. Justice Wand, & that he has agreed [---] with the Sd. Katherine Walsh, where fore yor. Petr. prays that yor. Worsps will please to discharge him He being now immediately to returnd, to his Duty on Guard.

& yr. Petr. will ever pay Etc
