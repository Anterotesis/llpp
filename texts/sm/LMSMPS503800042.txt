To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in General Session at Hicks Hall Assembled

The humble Petition & appear of the Church wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. [...] within the Libty. Wesmr. in the County ye Middx

Sheweth That by virtue of a pass Want. or Order Under the hand & Seals of Hen & rent and Empry Esqrs. two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the City Libty of Westmr one whereof being of the Quorum bearing date the 29th. day of July 1747 William Cosbey and Mary his Evise were removd from the Parish of St. James within the Libty of Wes [...] in the County of Middlesex to the said Parish of St Anne Whereby your Petitioners conceive themselves to be agreed

Therefore they humbly appeal to your Worships against the Adjudgemt. of the Said two Justices, and pray Your Worships will be pleaced to Order that the Churchwardens Overseers of the poor of the Sd. Parish of St. James and all persons concernd do attend this Court some day in this present Session to hear and abide the Judgemt. Determinacon of this Court touching the said Appeal

And ye Petitionrs. shall pray Etc.

Dan: Jones Agent for the Appelets.
