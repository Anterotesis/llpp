To the Right Worshipfull his Majestie's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Quarter Sessions Assembled

The humble Petition and Appeal of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London

Sheweth that by Virtue of a Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of Baswick Johnson and Thomas West Esqrs. two of his Majestie's Justices of the Peace for the said County (Quor unus) bearing Date the 31st. day of October Anno Domini 1726 Mary Sunderland the Widow of William Sunderland deced is passed from the Hamlett of Wapping Stepny in the County aforesaid to the Parish of St. Michael Bassishaw London before mentioned alledging her last legal Settlemt. to be there

Your Peticonrers conceiving themselves agreived by the said Warrant and Judgment of the said Two Justices do humbly appeal therefrom

And humbly pray that your Worships would appoint a Day this Sessions hear this Appeal and that your Worships will make an Order that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Hamlet of Wapping Stepny in the County afores. and all Parties concerned in and abt. the Premisses may then attend hear and abide by the final Determinacon of your Worships

And your Peticoners Shall ever pray [...]
